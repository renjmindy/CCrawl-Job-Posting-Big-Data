 Qualifications 
A degree in Business Administration, Commerce, or Economics and a Chartered Professional Accountant (CPA) designation is required. 
At least 4 years of experience with financial analysis, statistical and economic analysis, and revenue forecasting is required. 
Advanced skills in Excel and Business Intelligence tools are required. 
Success in this position also required strong interpersonal, problem solving, and communication skills.
