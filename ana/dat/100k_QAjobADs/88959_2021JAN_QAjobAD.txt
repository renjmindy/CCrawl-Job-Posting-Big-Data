 Required Qualifications: 
Completion of an accredited Psychiatric nursing education program. Active or eligible for registration and practice permit with the College of Registered Psychiatric Nurses of Alberta (CRPNA). Current Basic Cardiac Life Support - Health Care Provider (BCLS-HCP). 
 Additional Required Qualifications: 
B.Sc.N, or RN/RPN. Training or education specific to the addiction and mental health needs of correctional clients. Experience in the addiction or mental health field is essential, preferably in a correctional setting. Extensive knowledge of addictions and mental health. Specialized expertise around counseling and therapy with concurrent disorders. Knowledge of community resources. Knowledge of criminal justice system. Understanding of correctional facilities and population served. 
 Preferred Qualifications: 
Experience working in a correctional facility.
