 Essential Qualifications 
 Undergraduate degree in a related field or equivalent combination of experience and education. 
Certificate in supply chain management through the Supply Chain Management Association or equivalent organization. 
Minimum seven years of proven experience in procurement, including two years working on complex procurement projects and requests for proposals in the field of construction, renovations and building maintenance. 
Knowledge of the various types of contracts used in the field of construction (CCDC, OAA, ACEC). 
Demonstrated ability to conduct difficult negotiations, developed through extensive experience.
