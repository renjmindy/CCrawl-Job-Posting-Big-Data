 Qualifications: 
 1. Formal education an asset: Ontario Certified Teacher, Post-secondary degree in Education, or combined equivalent of education and experience 
 2. Current or university graduates with a degree in Education or related fields with good academic standing 
 3. Previous teaching/tutoring experience and/or experience working with youth preferred 
 4. Ability to work both independently and as a team member 
 5. Love working with children and adolescents, passionate about teaching
