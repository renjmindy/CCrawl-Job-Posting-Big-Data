 The Mobile Crane Operator is responsible for operating cranes to lift and haul loads and observes rigging to determine safety of loads. Duties include, driving equipment to and from worksites, inspecting rigging to ensure load safety, and documenting all related information as appropriate. Class A CDL and NCCCO or NCCER Crane Certification required. Applicants must be 18 years or older. High school diploma or equivalent preferred. Benefits include Competitive Wages, Paid Health/Dental Coverage, Retirement Plan, Uniforms and Paid Time Off. Pre-Employment Drug Testing is required.
