 Qualifications and Requirements: 
 - Bachelor of Nursing with current registration with the Association of Registered Nurses of Newfoundland and Labrador; 
 - Five years’ long- term care experience; 
 - 1-2 years’ experience in management preferably in long- term care; 
 - Proven ability to supervise, support, and guide staff; 
 - Proven ability to work independently and exercise considerable judgment and initiative in performing duties;
