 Qualifications 
 Specific training and job experience required for this position includes: 
 Post-secondary diploma/degree in publishing and/or experience in publishing. 
 Knowledge of InDesign. 
 Competencies & Skills: 
Competencies and skills required to perform this job include excellent writing and grammar skills, functional knowledge (publishing, editing, typography), keyboarding skills, a critical eye for detail, ability to concentrate, ability to work independently and as a team player, and ability to meet critical deadlines.
