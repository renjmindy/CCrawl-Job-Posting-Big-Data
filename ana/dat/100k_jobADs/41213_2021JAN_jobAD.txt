 Description 
 
 Description 
 An online employment screening test is a part of the hiring, or recruitment, process.This is a type of employment testing that typically accompanies or follows a job application, usually preceding a phone or formal job interview. With this assessment agencies are able to allow their customers to practice without copy-write issues. 
 The Job-Readiness Assessment  is comprised of various questions and scenarios in a four-part format (Multiple Choice, True/False, Scenarios and Scaling). This allows users to examine situations that are job-related and determine how they would handle themselves in such a situation.
