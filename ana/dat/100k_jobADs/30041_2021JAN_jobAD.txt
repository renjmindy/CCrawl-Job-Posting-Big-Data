 World Class Extractions and its subsidiaries needs people to help it achieve its vision of becoming an international leader and developer of advanced industrial scale extraction systems, technologies and processes in the production of high-margin, quality cannabis products and premium consumer brands. 
 We are a Canadian publicly traded company with vertically integrated subsidiaries specializing in cannabis and hemp extraction, processing, and product development.
