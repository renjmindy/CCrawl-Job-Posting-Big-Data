 Welder - MIG experience 
 About the business 
 Work for Australia's leading manufacturer of reinforced concrete pipes. 
 About the role 
 
 
 General labouring 
 
 
 Mig welding Reo bars and other products 
 
 
 Repetitive manual handling 
 
 
 Heavy lifting 
 
 
 Benefits and perks 
 
 
 Immediate start 
 
 
 Ongoing Role 
 
 
 Supportive management Team 
 
 
 Skills and experience 
 
 
 Hard working with a positive attitude 
 
 
 Proven excellent reliability checkable via referees 
 
 
 Previous welding experience in a pre-cast environment 
 
 
 Concrete experience an advantage 
 
 
 MUST be able to pass a Pre-employment medical 
 
 
 MUST be able to pass the Mandatory Drug and Alcohol testing 
 
 
 MUST HAVE OWN TRANSPORT
