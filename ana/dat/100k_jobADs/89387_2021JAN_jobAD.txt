 Can You Believe It?! it's a Full-time job 
 Get your parents off your back with a job as an Art Director at Hedley & Bennett in Vernon, CA, USA. We're a very fun bunch. 
 About 
 At Hedley & Bennett, our mission is to inspire and empower creativity and confidence in the kitchen. We make superfunctional, pro-grade aprons and kitchen gear for people who love to cook — right here in Downtown Los Angeles, CA! We're a growing group of dedicated and creative people and we're committed to relentless and constant improvement, fostering a community of amazing makers, going above and beyond to serve our customers, and uniting form and function in our products. We are seeking an Art Director to join our quickly expanding team. 
 The Art Director will set the direction for Hedley & Bennett creative assets and campaigns, including, but not limited to: email, social media, advertising, print collateral, packaging, photoshoots, copy, and website. The Art Director will be a key leader in creating and managing the Hedley & Bennett brand look and feel and voice. This role will directly supervise designers, both in house and agency and have a dotted line to indirectly supervise copy writers and video producers/editors, both in house and agency. The Art Director at Hedley & Bennett role is that of a player/coach role — both managing the design group and producing content themselves. 
 Experience: 
Our ideal candidate has 5+ years' experience in design and creative leadership 
Experience working with Adobe Suite, required 
Skill in creating copy along with art is a big plus 
Experience with UX and UI design (skills in Sketch a plus) 
Experience with motion graphics is a plus 
Experience working with Figma is a plus 
Please include link to digital portfolio or website on your resume submission. Additional links/materials may be emailed to careers at hedleyb dot com
