 Deli Department Manager 
 Freson Market Ltd./Freson Bros. 
 __Stony Plain, AB 
 Apply Now 
 Freson Bros. an Alberta owned and operated fresh food retail chain  invites an experienced restaurateur or delicatessen operator who is passionate about food to pursue a rewarding career as a Deli Department Manager to deliver a Unique Alberta Food Experience in Stony Plain Alberta and surrounding communities. 
 WHY FRESON BROS. 
 
 Drive:  To make it onto our team, it starts with focus, commitment and your own source of fuel. 
 Integrity:  Like it or not, we’re open and honest. Team members do what is right, they’re upfront and turn feedback into progress. 
 Growth:  Remember, personal development is at the core of what we do. We bring on those who love learning because we’re asking them to inspire others to feel the same way. 
 
 WHAT ARE THE QUALITIES WE ARE LOOKING FOR 
 As the Deli Department Manager, you will be responsible for overseeing the growth of sales and profitability and overall operations of the Deli Department including the implementation of all programs, ordering, receiving, inventory, merchandising and displays, work schedules, staff training and coaching, quality, service, cleanliness and safety standards   . 
 WHAT WE BELIEVE 
 
 The Craft:  Our Artisans are Masters at their craft. Butchers, Sausage Makers, Chefs and Cheese Makers thrive at Freson Bros. The Craft will never become a lost art… it is our heritage! 
 **QSC +XP: **We adhere to the highest operational standards in Quality, Service, Cleanliness and ‘Xciting Promotions. These are the pillars of our operations to achieve customer satisfaction. 
 Our Commitment:  Since 1955, Freson Bros. continues to support the values of Family, Community and our Alberta Heritage. 
 
 WORKING AT FRESON BROS. 
 Founded in 1955, Freson Bros. currently operates fifteen supermarkets employing over 950 full time and part time Team Members. With a tradition deeply rooted in family, community and heritage, Freson Bros. envisions to be Alberta's unique food experience destination. Our stores have been recognized by the Canadian Federation of Independent Grocers (CFIG) in 2015 and again in 2019 with the National Awards as Canada’s Top Independent Grocer of the Year in all three store surface categories. 
 To the successful individual, we offer an attractive compensation package including competitive salary rate, benefits plan (for full time hours), family grocery discount.
