 Are you a Sustainable Procurement expert? Do you have strong project management skills? Are you self motivated and happy to work from home? If so, this role could be perfect for you. 
 Working for a a well known utilities business you will have responsibility for setting up a sustainability program plan, project planning activities and developing and delivering strategy. You will work with the procurement department to ensure that sustainable procurement is embedded in how they work moving forward. Along with this you will also take responsibility for one of this organisation's key procurement projects. 
 In order to carry out this role you will need the following skills and experience: 
 
 Experience of working on and delivering sustainability projects 
 Experience of driving environmental performance within a procurement and supply chain arena 
 Strong influencing and engagement skills 
 The ability to work in a stand alone role 
 
 This role is a 6 month fixed term contract and will be home based. In return you will receive a salary of £60,000 - £65,000 (pro-rata'd) plus benefits. If you meet the above criteria, please do not hesitate to apply 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
