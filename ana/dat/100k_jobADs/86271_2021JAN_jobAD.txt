 Job Description 
 Are you a qualified Anaesthetic Technician/Operating Department Practitioner? 
 Do you have a minimum of 2 years’ experience? 
 Do you dream of living and working in New Zealand and enjoying a great work-life balance? 
 Extensive opportunities exist in both private and public health for AT’s / ODP’s in New Zealand and we are welcoming international applications now. 
 
 All applicants must be registered OR eligible for registration with the Medical Sciences Council of New Zealand* 
 Have current Anaesthetic Technology knowledge and skills 
 Experienced in all surgical specialties in a theatre environment 
 Good communication skills and customer service practices 
 Fluency and comprehension in English to be able to communicate effectively (written and verbal) with patients, colleagues, and other health professionals 
 Able to work under direction and independently 
 
 *Guidance with your NZ Registration is available from our team.
