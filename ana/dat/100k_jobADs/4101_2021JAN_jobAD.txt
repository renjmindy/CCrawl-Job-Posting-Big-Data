 Front-End Developer LOCALPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 Studio Treble is growing, and we are looking to hire an experienced front-end web developer to work across our profile of projects. 
 Role 
 We're looking for an experienced front-end web developer with at least 3 years of commercial experience to work across our portfolio of web projects. You should be able to take design files and technical specifications to fully functional, responsive websites that work across our supported platforms. 
 We are looking for someone confident in working across a wide variety of projects for both new and existing clients, as well as on internal projects, experiments and creative outputs. 
 You should have a keen interest in design and development, as well as new technologies and experimentation. 
 Essential Skills 
 
 HTML5, SCSS, and JavaScript (ES6). 
 Modern JavaScript frameworks including VueJS or React. 
 Integration with content management systems and frameworks, including Craft CMS/Wordpress/Laravel/Express. 
 Front-end tooling including Webpack and NPM. 
 Version control (Git). 
 Technical consultancy and estimating across project phases. 
 Good communication, including internal and client interaction across all project phases. 
 A strong design sensibility, with a keen eye for detail. 
 History working with design teams and an interest and experience in the creative industry. 
 
 Nice to Have 
 
 Object-oriented PHP. 
 Server administration and dev-ops (Linux/Heroku/AWS). 
 Database experience (MySQL/MariaDB/PostgreSQL). 
 E-commerce experience (Craft Commerce/WooCommerce/Shopify). 
 Experience in creative technology platforms e.g. WebGL/GLSL/ThreeJS/Unity/OpenFrameworks. 
 
 About Studio Treble 
 Studio Treble is a multidisciplinary design practice: combining technical expertise with beautiful, collaborative design, we create work for those who care about detail and craft across digital, design, and moving image.
