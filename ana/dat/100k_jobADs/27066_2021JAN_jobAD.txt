 One of Gleeson's long standing clients is currently looking for a Commercial Finance Operations Controller to join their team on a 12 month FTC. The business is based in Shropshire and is a global business within the technology space. The role is business critical, looking after their biggest account. 
 The role 
 
 Partnering with internal and external stakeholders 
 Delivering team KPI analysis to senior management 
 Drive profitability and cost savings across the account 
 Key contract management with delivery teams 
 Apply continuous improvement within the finance team 
 Produce regular and adhoc report in addition to detailed analysis 
 Mentoring and coaching junior members of the team 
 
 You will ideally be ACCA, ACA or CIMA qualified and have the gravitas to implement change and nurture and develop relationships internally as well as externally. 
 For the successful Commerical Finance Operation Controller, they are looking to pay between £50,000 to £55,000 DOE 
 If you are interested in this role please click apply or give Emily Mitchell at Gleesons a call.
