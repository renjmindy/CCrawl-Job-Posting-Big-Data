 Crane Supply is Canada's premier distributor of quality pipe, valves, fittings and piping accessories. Our key market segments include Industrial and Commercial Contractors, Industrial Maintenance, Repair and Operations (MRO), Fabricator, Engineering Procurement and Construction firms. 
 CAMIONNEUR CLASSE 3 
 CLASS 3 TRUCK DRIVER, LASALLE 
 
 Competitive Wages starting at $20.50 P/H 
 Hours: 5:00 AM to 2:00 PM (Monday to Friday) 
 Company Paid Pension Plan 
 Family Health & Dental Coverage 
 Career Advancement Possibilities 
 
 We are looking for an energetic, results oriented individual who has the ability to prioritize and think on their feet. Our ideal candidate is a team player with a positive attitude and someone who is reliable, safety conscious, willing to learn, and demonstrates excellent communication and interpersonal skills, and shares a passion for exceptional customer service. Must be flexible and willing to work overtime as required. 
 The Class 3 Truck Driver is responsible for the timely delivery of all scheduled orders, vendor pickups, and second run deliveries in a safe and efficient manner. 
 Key Delivery Responsibilities: 
 
 Verifies with packing slips that the correct quantities of bags, crates, and bundles of pipe are on his/hers assigned truck 
 Ensures load is safely secured to truck and completes a safety circle check before leaving yard 
 Able to quickly and efficiently read road maps for directions to delivery sites 
 Must be certified to safely use crane boom on truck for unloading various sizes of pipe 
 Must report any safety/mechanical issues with the assigned truck to his or her supervisors immediately 
 Able to clearly communicate to customers on delivery sites and/or over mobile phones 
 
 Warehouse Responsibilities 
 
 Responsible for receiving, loading and unloading trailers, picking orders, restocking product and shipping with the use of a hand and mechanical trucks 
 Responsible for counter sales 
 Ensures customer orders are completed 
 Accountable for productivity and efficiencies of warehouse operations 
 Ensures all orders are picked and ready for shipment in a safe and timely manner; reports any exceptions to stakeholders 
 Maintains regular communication with customers/sales staff regarding service/delivery matters 
 Optimizes the use of warehouse resources to meet customer shipping requirements 
 Ensures efficient operations are maintained by optimizing availability of efficient and safe equipment, identifying process improvement opportunities, etc. 
 Reports any damage to equipment or shipments, ensuring photographs and reports are completed. 
 Maintains a clean, organized and safe warehouse environment 
 
 General Duties: 
 
 Successfully prioritize responsibilities 
 Follow all company policies and procedures 
 Follow all safety procedures of Crane Supply 
 Other duties as required/assigned including general housekeeping in truck and around shipping docks 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 Minimum of 2-3 years of driving experience in a Class 3 vehicle 
 Minimum 1 year warehouse distribution experience 
 Strong referrals from previous employers (to be verified as part of selection process) 
 Valid Class 3 license with a clean driver abstract 
 Valid Boom License an asset 
 Valid Counter Balance Forklift license an asset 
 High School Diploma 
 Bilingual French and English
