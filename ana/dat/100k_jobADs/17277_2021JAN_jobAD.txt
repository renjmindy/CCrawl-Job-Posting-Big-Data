 Commercial Finance Analyst 
 The Role 
 Gleeson Recruitment are partnering with an exciting business currently recruiting for a Commercial Finance Analyst within the finance team. The role assists the Commercial Finance Manager and their key stakeholders in analysing and reporting on the key business drivers, with emphasis on company and store performance and cost control. 
 Key Responsibilities 
 
 Be a key player in the preparation of the annual budget and rolling forecasts working alongside stakeholders from across the business 
 Production and communication of daily, weekly and monthly sales reports. 
 Business partner with a range of stakeholders to understand performance vs budget, promptly highlighting areas of concern, focus or potential improvement 
 Analysing margin drivers to provide greater insight in trading performance, including rebates 
 Supporting stores with queries and projects 
 Support Commercial Finance Manager in month end routines, maintaining strong internal controls 
 Pro-actively look for opportunities to improve reporting and analysis to better support stakeholders 
 Coach and develop 1 Direct report 
 Ad hoc finance analysis for new business projects and store queries, as required 
 
 **Skills & Experience ** 
 
 A highly motivated Qualified Accountant (ACA/ CIMA/ ACCA) with at least 1 year PQE 
 Advanced Excel skills with ability to handle large volumes of data. Some experience of Power BI would be beneficial. 
 Strong business partnering skills and analytical skills - able to provide calculated, commercial and operational support as well as challenge 
 Attention to detail and "Can do" attitude 
 Ability to communicate with stakeholders across the business via appropriate means 
 A strong team player who can work collaboratively as well as autonomously 
 Organised approach with the ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously and meet deadlines 
 Experience in a retail/ FMCG or multi-site operation is advantageous 
 
 Full driving license - some UK travel may be required 
 What's On Offer? 
 A growing brand with new branches exploding into action nationwide. 
 The opportunities for professional growth and development are immense and we actively support internal advancement through a fully developed and supported career path. 
 A huge range of benefits including a competitive salary, profit-based bonus scheme, share save scheme, childcare vouchers, thousands of retail discounts and offers, a company pension scheme, Cycle to Work, free Life Assurance and free Health Cash Plan
