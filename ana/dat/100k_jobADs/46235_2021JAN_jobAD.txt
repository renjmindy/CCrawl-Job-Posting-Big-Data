 Caregiver Jobs Australia 
 Posted on  February 7, 2013  by  caregiver 
 Joblane International Manpower Services Inc., one of the POEA accredited recruitment and placement agencies in the city of Manila, is looking for Nurses who are willing to work in  Caregiver Jobs Australia . Specified below are the qualifications for the said caregiver jobs Australia. 
 
 Male/Female 
 Registered Nurse 
 Must have at least 5 years experience as a nurse 
 Must have IELTS - Academic - with band score of 7 in all categories 
 
 Interested applicants for the said Caregiver Jobs Australia are required to send photocopies of their Resume, Employment Certificates, License, and IELTS to caregivers@joblane-international.net or apply online at www.joblane-international.net and attached your complete credentials. If you have any question about the said caregiver jobs Australia, you can refer them to Joblane International Manpower Services Inc. by calling their telephone numbers we have provided below.
