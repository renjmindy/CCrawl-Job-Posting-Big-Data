 Practicum Health Care Aide Instructor 
 Academy of Learning Career College 
 __Edmonton, AB 
 Apply Now 
 The  Academy of Learning Career College  is seeking a designated  LPN  or  RN  to fill its  Practicum Health Care Aide Instructor  position. The Instructor’s key role is to lead and contribute to the academic success of students and to the success of the College in achieving its mission, vision, and statement of purpose. The successful candidate will lead the block of the HCA Practicum program through hands on instruction. This position could become a full-time 8 hour/day position in early 2021. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Participate in instructor led orientation of new students online and/or by phone 
 Answer emails in real-time or to engage in instant messaging with students 
 Respond to student emails within 24 hours of receipt 
 Deliver instruction that meets module and program objectives, as set out in the AOLCC curriculum 
 Participate in the development and evaluation of curriculum and instructional materials 
 Prepare course outlines and teaching materials 
 Evaluate student progress; generate, administer and mark student coursework and exams 
 Monitor and discuss students’ progress at both an individual and group level 
 Assign and post grades 
 Hold consultations with weaker students to enhance learning 
 Address attendance issues or other problems as they arise 
 Administer reference and criminal checks as required 
 
 Skills / Abilities: 
 
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills 
 Presentation and group facilitation skills 
 Ability to motivate, engage and inspire students 
 
 Required Education / Experience: 
 
 Related diploma or degree from a recognized post-secondary institution 
 3+ years' experience as an LPN or RN 
 RN or LPN active License 
 Teaching experience is an asset 
 
 Part-time hours: 40 per week 
 Expected start date: 2020-12-07 
 Job Types: Part-time, Casual 
 Salary: $25.00 per hour
