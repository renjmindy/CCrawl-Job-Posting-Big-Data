 Career panel by Sinai CIG alumni 
 Posted by  Jamel Meslamani  on  2016-05-18 
 The Mount Sinai Consulting Interest Group is proud to announce the first career panel of Sinai CIG alumni. 
Leverage the experience of peers and learn about options and paths outside academia. 
We have an impressive list of invited panelists with extremely diverse experiences through academia and industry: 
 
 Adriana Arita   PhD   ,  Postdoctoral fellow 2012-2014 ; Life Sciences Consultant, Prescient Healthcare Group 
 Samara Bliss   BS   ,  MSSM MD class of 2017 ; Offering Manager, IBM Watson Health 
 Stephanie Duhoux   PhD   ,  Postdoctoral Fellow 2012-2014 ; Medical Science Liaison, Vaya Pharma Inc. 
 Ilana Seror   MPhil   ,  MSSM PhD class of 2010 ; Manager, Accenture, Pharma R&D management consulting 
 Sietse Jonkman   PhD   ,  Postdoctoral Fellow 2011-2016 ; Consultant, The Boston Consulting Group 
 Jose M. Perez-Sanchez   PhD   ,  Visiting Fellow 2013-2014 ; Lead Researcher and Quantitative Developer, Picofemto Biomedical Analytics 
 
 Friday, May 27, 2016 from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM 
Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai - 1468 Madison Avenue MSIT Learning Lab, Annenberg 11-46, New York, NY 10029 
 You can RSVP here: 
 http://www.eventbrite.com/e/career-panel-with-sinai-cig-alumni-tickets-25516359117
