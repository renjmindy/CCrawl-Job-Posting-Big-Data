 Work at home Job Leads for 4/12/2018 
 Furniture Customer Service Associates 
 Williams & Sonoma is hiring agents to work from home on a full-time basis to address customer questions and concerns regarding product and delivery information, process returns, issues replacements and credits and similar duties. You will need good computer, communication and data entry skills, a camera, a cell phone with headset and can work up to 50 hours per week. Pays $12 per hour with paid training. Nevada residents only. 
 Reservation Sales Associates 
 Omni Hotels & Resorts is hiring candidates to work from home taking inbound calls to assist guests with reservations, modifications, and cancellations. You must have good phone etiquette, experience working with basic computer programs, and can work one weekend day. Paid training with benefits. 
 Customer Sevice 
 GC Service is seeking home based agents to answer incoming/outgoing calls for their clients, educate callers on how to use their products and services, complete data entry and other duties. You will need customer service experience and can type quickly and accurately. You will need a land line phone with no features. Pays $9 per hour. Hiring in several states.

 Application Support Specialist 
 Mscripts is hiring US candidates to provide pharmacy application support to their users. You may configure and change requests for mscripts mobile pharmacy platform. You will need SaaS and mobile based technologies experience, problem solving skills and be fluent in Spanish. Part time. 
 PBX Operator 
 
 Tyme Global Technologies is looking for people to fill in shifts for various times who are friendly to interactive with hotel guests, enter tickets into hotel management systems, dispatch services for guests and other duties. You must be able to multitask, speak clearly and have good computer skills. Pays $10 per hour with annual pay raises. Full training and equipment are provided. 
 Customer Service Reps 
 Sykes is hiring full time customer service reps to answer questions, process transactions and resolve customer issues. You must be 1 year customer service experience, be empathetic, can speak on the phone and use a computer, have a Windows based computer with fast internet and 2 headsets. Land line phone needed. 
 Academic Writers 
 eNotes is currently looking for academic writers to produce content in a Q & A format. Work your own schedule. Pays $4 up to $37 per answer. 
 Customer Engagement Specialist 
 TeleNetwork is seeking qualified applicants in several states to provide technical and customer support. You must have excellent reading, written and verbal communication skills. Pays $10 per hour plus commission by advertising products and services. Paid training with benefits. 
 Search Engine Evaluator 
 Leapforce is currently seeking independent contractors to work from home evaluating and improving search engine results for one of the world's largest search engines. You must be internet savvy, have excellent web research skills, a broad range of interests and a smartphone. Position will last at least 6 months. 
 Claims Data Entry 
 Forrest T Jones & Company is hiring those with data entry and claims processing knowledge to process manual claims, check traces, etc. You will need 1 year claims processing/data entry experience, knowledge of PC and spreadsheet applications, and can type 7,000 kph. Will train onsite. Benefits provided.
