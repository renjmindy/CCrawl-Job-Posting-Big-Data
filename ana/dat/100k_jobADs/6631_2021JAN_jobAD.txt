 C/C++/Compiler expert (m/f/d) 
 Do you like to work with software development tools and in particular with C/C++ and compilers? 
 Your tasks 
 You develop and run tests for compilers and libraries. You test and qualify compilers and libraries which are used in the development of software of safety-critical systems (such as control units in the car). For this you test different compilers and libraries and examine the code coverage of the test suites. You will learn how to deal with safety standards like ISO26262. 
 We offer you 
 Validas AG is a young und continuously growing consulting company in the field of quality assurance for software-intensive embedded systems. In our work, we constantly use and develop latest state of the art technology. An attractive remuneration, customizable working hours and a well-equipped workplace in a spacious office in the heart of Munich awaits you! 
 You collaborate in the conception, adaptation and use of methods of software engineering, software validation and software testing. You work independently in demanding projects - always supported by an experienced and highly qualified team. 
 You have the following 
 
 Completed degree (Bachelor, Master or Diploma) or PhD in Computer Science, Mathematics or Engineering 
 Practical experience using different C and/or C++ compilers 
 Solid knowledge in C or C ++ 
 Experience in other programming languages or scripting languages (such as Python …) 
 Interest or experience in testing software and embedded systems 
 
 You have the following skills 
 
 Independent and methodical work 
 Quality awareness 
 Analytical thinking and curiosity to find out why something works (or doesn’t work) 
 Excellent communication and willingness to work in a team 
 Enjoy developing and presenting concepts 
 Very good written and spoken German and very good English
