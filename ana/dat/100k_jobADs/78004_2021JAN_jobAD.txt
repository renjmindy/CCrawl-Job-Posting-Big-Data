 OPPORTUNITY - Senior Architectural Technician 
Based in the West Midlands our client is seeking an experienced Senior Architectural CAD Technician to join their expanding team. What makes this opportunity quite rare is that it is working for a leading asset management and development organisation and thus present an opportunity for those wishing to come out of practice. 
 COMPANY PROFILE - Senior Architectural Technician: 
This private organisation was formed to provide high spec properties and exceeding client expectations while providing a complete service. They take huge pride in their work and continue to provide a leading service to their clients within the UK. 
 DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES - Senior Architectural Technician: 
 
 Produce detailed CAD drawings 
 Complete Measured Surveys 
 2D Modelling 
 3D Modelling 
 Travelling to the portfolio 
 Working closely with the wider organisation and design teams 
 Carrying out technical reviews of the design drawings 
 Supporting projects where required 
 Attending project meetings 
 
 PERSON PROFILE - Senior Architectural Technician: 
· Proven experience in similar role using AutoCAD 
· Experience of undertaking measured building surveys with availability to travel nationally 
· Confident, approachable manner 
· Degree qualified, or HNC/HND equivalent qualification 
· Strong knowledge of construction including building regulations and planning processes 
· Good organisation and time keeping skills 
· Ability to work alone and as part of a growing CAD team 
 PACKAGE - Senior Architectural Technician: 
Salary guide: £35,000 plus benefits
