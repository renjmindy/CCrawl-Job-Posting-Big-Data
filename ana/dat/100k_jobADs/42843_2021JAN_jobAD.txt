 Cost Controller 
 Job description 
 
 
 Join a global leader in the mining sector 
 
 
 Perth based role, working office hours 
 
 
 Ongoing contract 
 
 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking a Cost Controller to join Rio Tinto Sustaining Capital in Perth Central Park. The purpose of the role is facilitator of Purchase Requisitions / Purchase Orders in the Purchase to Pay Process, and timely processing of Invoices. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Assist with SAP data reconciliation in PRISM. 
 
 
 Ad-hoc assistance with PRISM. 
 
 
 General support to Cost Analyst. 
 
 
 Timely processing of Vendor invoices so they are paid within the payment terms. 
 
 
 Assist the Project Team with Vendor enquiries regarding invoices and purchase orders. 
 
 
 Provide correctly completed SAP Purchase Requisitions and supporting documentation. 
 
 
 Provide support for ad hoc Buyer email enquiries. 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Previous experience in Cost Control within the resource sector. 
 
 
 Experience in SAP purchasing transactions and reporting. 
 
 
 Basic to intermediate cost forecasting. 
 
 
 Intermediate Microsoft Excel. 
 
 
 Able to work as part of a team and independently.
