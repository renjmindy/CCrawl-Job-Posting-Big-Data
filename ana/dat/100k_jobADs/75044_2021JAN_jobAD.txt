 Accountant/Senior Analyst 
 Reporting to the Financial Controller for this mid-sized Health provider, I am looking for a hands on and qualified accountant to provide support for 8 business units. 
 Working in this small but friendly team, this is a varied accounting opportunity. 
 
 Prepare and Consolidate Management accounts 
 P&L, Balance sheet, KPI, Variance analysis and commentary 
 Balance sheet reconciliations inc accruals, prepayments, intercompany, deferred income etc. 
 Oversee accounts payable and receivable (Direct Reports) 
 Income recognition and reconciliation 
 Assist with annual budgets and reforecast 
 Payroll experience desirable 
 Treasury & cashflow 
 
 Sage 50 preferred 
 This is an office based 9-5 role (Covid-19 compliant). You will need to be a driver to access their remote site. 
 Free parking on site 
Pension, 25 days leave plus BH
