 Management Accountant 
 Management Accountant 
 
 Location 
 
 Stourbridge, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £26000 - £32000 per annum + study support if required 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Jo Payne 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 jo.payne@gleeson-rg.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 JMP100120_1578583367 
 
 Published: 
 
 about 1 year ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-02-08 
 I am looking after a great opportunity to join a growing business, in a newly-created role designed to offer support across all areas of finance. Will suit a CIMA/ACCA part-qual, with a passion for financial analysis. 
 This is a superb role for a talented Management Accountant, and a great opportunity to join an organisation offering a genuine career path. There is huge scope for development of your business partnering skills, with responsibility for the financial performance reporting for a number of sites across Europe. It would especially suit a CIMA/ACCA studier who is ready for the next step. 
 Areas of responsibility will be as follows: 
 
 Delivering value-add monthly management accounting information 
 Preparation of all month end reporting requirements 
 Acting as Finance Business Partner to a variety of non-finance stakeholders to support the budgeting & forecasting timetable 
 Preparation of cash flow 
 Variance analysis 
 Ownership of the P&L and balance sheet 
 Supervising & supporting the team across the Group 
 Complex reconciliations and other ad hoc tasks as required 
 
 The successful candidate will be fully supported on a path towards becoming a valued and highly-visible member of the senior finance team. 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 CIMA/ACCA part-qualified 
 Strong management accounting & analysis skills 
 High level of attention to detail 
 Excellent verbal, interpersonal, and written communication skills 
 A "hands-on" attitude, comfortable with completing month and year end tasks 
 
 Please click apply, or call Jo Payne at Gleeson Recruitment on 07813 608579
