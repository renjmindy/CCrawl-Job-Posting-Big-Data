 MARKHAM: OVERNIGHT CREW MEMBER 
 McDonald's Restaurants 
 __Markham, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Company Description 
 McDonald’s is the world’s number one food brand! 
 We are looking for great team members for our Markham location to satisfy the growing demand at our restaurants in this town. 
 Job Description 
 YOUR VIBE 
 You are a unique individual who likes to sleep in and prefers to work at night. Working overnight shifts gives you more flexibility with your daytime commitments and fits well your schedule. You are energetic and thrive in social situations, even at night. You get that service isn’t just a transaction—It’s about connection. 
 YOUR JOB 
 Offering exceptional customer service 
Greeting guests and taking orders 
Processing payments as a cashier 
Preparing food orders; be a ‘world-famous’ cook! 
Satisfying McCravings! 
 WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU? 
 Flexible work hours 
Opportunities for advancement 
‘World-class’ training program to support your success 
50% off food items at all McDonald’s restaurant locations 
Platinum Membership Program, offering you discounts at top national retailers 
Be a part of our exceptional ‘family’ 
Work for a company that gives back to your community through the Ronald McDonald House Charities 
Medical and dental benefits 
 Qualifications 
 Previous customer service experience an asset 
Candidates must be a minimum of 18 years old 
Able to work flexible shifts including evenings, nights and weekends 
Energetic and outgoing with strong work ethic 
 Additional Information 
 Full time staff MUST work a minimum of 35-40 hours and/or 5 shifts per week 
All overnight crew MUST be available to work 3 overnight shifts per week (overnight shift is 11:00pm – 7:00am)
