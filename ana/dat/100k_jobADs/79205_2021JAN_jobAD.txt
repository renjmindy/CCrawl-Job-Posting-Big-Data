 Program Assistant 
 Catholic Social Services 
 __Edmonton, AB 
 Apply Now 
 Catholic Social Services is currently recruiting for one Program Assistant - Casual (no guaranteed hours) in Edmonton, AB. 
 You will be joining an established Agency that is guided by faith to care for and bring hope to people in need with humility, compassion and respect. With more than 50 years of service delivery, Catholic Social Services is one of the largest multi-function social services agencies in Canada, with more than 1800 staff, and hundreds of volunteers delivering 130+ programs throughout Central Alberta. 
 Program Overview: 
You will be working in a Federal Halfway House whose primary focus is to assist the clients in making a successful transition to a responsible community life. As a Program Assistant, you are a team player who is dedicated to the supervision, support, assessment, and counselling of men or women on parole from federal institutions. 
 Supporting daily living, you have the ability to develop client relationships that are based on mutual respect. You have strong verbal communication skills and the ability to write clear, precise and detailed case reports. Using you’re your good decision- making abilities, you respond to difficult client behaviour in a non-reactive manner. 
 You have the ability to work independently as well as in a team environment. 
 Education and Qualifications: 
High school diploma or equivalent 
One year of experience working in the human service field 
Training and experience with corrections, addictions, the criminal justice system or adult offenders is preferred. 
 Police Information Check including vulnerable sector search and Intervention Record Check are conditions of employment and the financial responsibility of the candidate. Enhanced Security Check is a requirement for employment. 
 Compensation: 
The salary for this position is $18.35 - $20.64 per hour based on qualifications and experience. We offer flexibility and a supportive working environment 
 Shifts:  Overnight awake; available to work both week and weekend overnights. 
 We offer flexibility, a comprehensive benefits package and a supportive working environment. 
 Please apply online at www.cssalberta.ca/careers to competition number 20-286. 
 We thank all applicants. Only candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.
