 CASUAL - BILLING REPRESENTATIVE (UP TO 10 MONTHS) – LEDUC 
 JOB POSTING # C9-20 
 This is a casual full-time position and will last up to 10 months. The successful candidate is responsible for preparing accounts for: scheduled on-cycle billing; executing bill post process to create charges and statements; scheduling, coordinating and monitoring meter readings from the Field Collection System (FCS) into the Customer Information System (CIS); reviewing and closing CIS and Tariff Billing Code (TBC) Dragon service orders; processing account adjustments and corrections; administering the Retail Billing function; and performing other duties as assigned. 
 Qualifications include: 
Certificate in Business Administration or related discipline and 
Minimum 2 years related experience. 
 AltaGas Utilities employs individuals who are committed to safety and demonstrate a work ethic that includes honesty, loyalty, respect, reliability, accountability, and ethical behavior. 
 The compensation package for this position includes competitive wages and extensive benefits. 
 AltaGas Utilities Inc. is a company dedicated to the well-being of its employees. 
 Job Closing date:  until a suitable candidate is found. 
 Working Hours:  37.5 hours per week
