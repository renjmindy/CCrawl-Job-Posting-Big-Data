 Dump Truck Operator 
 Job description 
 Summary 
 Open Cut Coal Mine outside of Moura Ramping up Production. Looking for experienced and motivated Dump Truck Operators for an ongoing position. 
 About the Client 
 Our client is a globally diversified mining business who are leaders in innovation and technological advances and value their people about all else. Their portfolio spans across mining of all key resources and opportunities within this company are boundless. 
 Opportunities for up skilling and even permanency will be available to individuals who demonstrate a passion for upholding their key value 
 About the Role 
 As a key member of a high performing team, you will play an integral role in implementing the client's production requirements. This is a drive-in drive out (DIDO) role for an open cut coal mine in the Banana Shire situated 2 hours South West from Rockhampton and 7 hours North West from Brisbane. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 Operate mobile plant equipment, consisting of CAT 797 Dump Truck working under Shovel working on 7/7 Roster 
 Experience operating under the P&H; Electric Rope Shovel on the CAT Trucks is desired 
 Must have min CAT 793 Dump Truck Skill 
 Operate mobile plant equipment, consisting of KOM 830 Dump Truck working under Excavator working including PJ Day (5/4/4/5). 
 Must have min Komatsu 730 Dump Truck Skill 
 Skills/Experience 
 MUST Have SSE/Delegate Signed Skills List or Unsigned Skilled List Supported by RII 
 Experience operating under the P&H; Electric Rope Shovel on the CAT Trucks is desired 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 Current Coal Board Medical or willing to Obtain 
 Current Standard 11 or willing to obtain 
 Benefits 
 Camp and Meals Provided OR Rental Subsidy Being Paid Weekly for Moura Residents 
 Immediate Start for an Ongoing Role 
 Super Paid on All Hours 
 Monthly Production Bonus if Site hit Targets 
 Production (Trucks, Dragline, Dozers) â 6:45 â 7:15 (12.5 hour shifts)Â
