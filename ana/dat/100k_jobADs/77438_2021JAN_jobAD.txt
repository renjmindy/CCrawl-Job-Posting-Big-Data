 About Mixpanel

 About Mixpanel Engineering 
 
 
 The Engineering, Product and Design teams at Mixpanel together build Mixpanel’s product. The team consists of very talented and nice people who collaborate to solve problems. 
 
 
 We organize in Product Groups which are small groups (3 to 8 people) comprised of engineers, product managers and designers working on a problem or an investment area with complete autonomy over the development process. 
 
 
 We are customer focused and data driven - We like to iterate, learn and improve quickly using qualitative and quantitative data to continuously deliver customer value. 
 
 
 Our engineering team gets to work on really challenging and exciting engineering problems because of the nature of our product and our scale. 
 
 
 We use our own product on a daily basis! 
 
 
 Mixpanel serves over 26,000 companies from different industries around the world, including Samsung, Twitter, and BMW. Headquartered in San Francisco, Mixpanel has offices in New York, Seattle, Austin, London, Barcelona, Paris, Amsterdam and Singapore.

 About the role:(NUX) 
 First impressions are extremely important and that is even more true for products you pay for. Making those first experiences shine and helping those customers see the incredible value in Mixpanel analytics is the focus of the New User Experience team. With so much data and so many options for exploration, the new user experience can be overwhelming, the team’s goal is to improve that introduction and ultimately help Mixpanel retain those customers with greater success. The team will be 3 engineers working closely with product management and design to test out user experiences, iterate quickly, and use data-driven validation to get to the best solutions for our customers. 
 We’re looking for someone with: 
 A proven track record of launching and maintaining large-scale web products. Strong opinions about shipping bug-free software, but knowledge of when to compromise and move fast. Experience with JavaScript and knowledge of the current JavaScript landscape. 
 Why choose Mixpanel Engineering? 
 You will work with and learn from a diverse group of world-class talent. Many of our software engineers came from Google, Facebook, Amazon, Uber, Twitter, Dropbox, Palantir, LinkedIn, and Microsoft. It will be clear how the work you are doing will help us succeed. You will be excited about the work you do, the products you deliver, and the people you do it with.
