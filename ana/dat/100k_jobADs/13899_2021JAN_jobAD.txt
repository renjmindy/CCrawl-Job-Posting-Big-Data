 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently working with a well know manufacturing business based in Sutton Coldfield who are recruiting for a Management Accountant to join them on a permanent basis. The ideal candidate does not need to be ACCA,CIMA,ACA qualified but experience in a manufacturing environment with costing skills is a must. 
 Key Duties, Management Accountant, Sutton Coldfield 
 
 Produce cost analysis of the business, reviewing and (where appropriate) updating cost standards on a periodic basis. 
 Prepare & support the commercial organisation with profit margin analysis. 
 Work with colleagues to develop "variance to standard" reporting. 
 Work with the Group Financial Controller to deliver appropriate monthly & annual reporting of the organisation. 
 Work with divisional Management Accountant to review and, if necessary, amend the overhead recovery rates to establish a fair allocation between divisions, thus improving comparability 
 Ensure that inventory is appropriately controlled & valued, coordinating with Management Accountant as necessary. 
 Work with the commercial organisation & other colleagues to ensure stock are properly managed. 
 
 Key Skills & Experience, Management Accountant, Sutton Coldfield 
 
 Qualified Accountant or Accounting Experience in Manufacturing 
 Cost Experience 
 Stock Experience 
 Stakeholder Management skills
