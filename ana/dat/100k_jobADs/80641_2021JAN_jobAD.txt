 WINE LOVERS WANTED! 
 At the Wine Shop, we look forward to celebrating every occasion with our customers! Whether they're hosting a dinner party, enjoying a solo glass, celebrating a birthday, or maybe an engagement - our team is right around the corner to help them select the perfect match and provide fun wine know-how along the way. 
 Sound like your dream job? Keep reading - it gets better! 
 As a company we enjoy attracting and developing top talent who share in our passion and vision of pouring extraordinary into everyday life! We are looking for professional, passionate sales individuals who are focused on delivering a superior shopping experience to our customers.
