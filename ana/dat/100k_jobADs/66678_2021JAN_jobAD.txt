 Job Description 
 **Campaign Development Account Manager - Mon-Fri 
 Job Description for our Campaign Account Managers:** 
 
 The Campaign Development Manager supports the Account Managers, Sales Leaders, and Energy Clients on a day to day basis. The Campaign Development Manager develops campaign sales strategies, campaign meetings, campaign breakouts, and campaign specific outings for Energy Brokers in the Chicago area. 
 
 Campaign Execution 
 
 Supports and works as a resource in assisting field sales and key account management 
 Executes sales & marketing campaigns including: 
 Configuring campaign strategies 
 Pulls campaign eligible territories daily 
 Recommends client targeting strategies 
 Schedule and monitor recruitment strategies 
 Builds campaign specific reports 
 Communicates the progress of campaign and sales development 
 
 ** 
Reporting** 
 
 Builds and delivers campaign reports weekly 
 Develops and confidently presents results and insights from significant initiatives or overall trends across several Energy suppliers 
 Provides reports and data to account managers to accurately ensure proper Client representation 
 Communicates campaign results and analytical findings effectively to a variety of groups in the company
