 Are you an immediately available HR Administrator? If so then I have an exciting opportunity for an Interim HR Administrator to join a growing Business based in Milton Keynes who are currently going through a period of change. 
 The role will report to the Group Human Resources Director and providing a dedicated HR support function to Business Partners in delivering both high quality HR initiatives and services. 
 You will be responsible for the administration of a high volume of HR transactional activities across the business, spanning the employee life cycle. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Process job offers and employment contracts 
 General Administration 
 Support the recruitment process with documentation including onboarding and inductions 
 Support with running the payroll 
 Take minutes as a HR representative at investigatory, disciplinary and capability meetings 
 Raise PO's and track on SAP 
 Produce regular stats on HR metrics and generate monthly reports 
 Process any/all ad hoc HR queries in a timely manner and if required escalate to the relevant Business Partner/Department
