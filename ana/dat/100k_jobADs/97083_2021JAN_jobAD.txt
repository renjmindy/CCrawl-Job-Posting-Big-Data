 Quantity Surveyor 
An award winning national property company that specialises in New Build and Maintenance is seeking a highly experienced Quantity Surveyor to join their expanding team. The position would suit someone with experience, knowledge and passion to work with a variety of different clients and projects. 
 COMPANY PROFILE - Quantity Surveyor: 
This well-established property company was formed on the basis relationships, experience and reputation. They take huge pride in their work and continue to provide a leading service to their clients across the UK. 
 DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES - Quantity Surveyor: 
§ Produce and evaluate to ensure the cost of the supply of services, material, goods and works. 
§ Working on new build projects 
§ Working on maintenance and refurbishment projects 
§ Working within Hospitality, Residential, Leisure, Healthcare and Education sectors 
§ Closely working with Contractors, Clients and Suppliers 
§ Prepare tenders, bids and estimators 
§ Managing cashflow and individual project budgets 
§ Preparing monthly financial reports for both clients and internal management 
§ Attending internal/external meetings 
§ Prioritising payments, works and time where required 
 PERSON PROFILE - Quantity Surveyor: 
§ Qualified Quantity Surveyor 
§ Experience in working on a variety of construction projects 
§ Work in both Private and Public Sector 
§ Planned works experience 
§ Flexible to travel to fulfil responsibilities of the role. 
§ Working knowledge of construction contracts 
§ Self-motivated, punctual and analytical. 
§ Multi-tasker with excellent follow up skills. 
 PACKAGE - Quantity Surveyor: 
Salary guide: Competitive + Car Allowance/Company Car 
Please contact us for a confidential chat on this role as a Quantity Surveyor.
