 Admissions Agent/Counsellor 
 Evergreen College Mississauga 
 __Mississauga, ON 
 Apply Now 
 We are recruiting individuals who are passionate about helping candidates transition into new and meaningful careers. Our Education Agent/Counselors partner with our Admissions Team to identify and recruit students who require training or retraining in the fields of Business, Healthcare & Technology. In most cases, the Education Agent is promoting Ministry Registered Diploma programs and services. 
 Education Agent/Counselors are experienced net workers, are comfortable speaking one-to-one or in front of groups of people and are familiar with the post-secondary education system in Ontario. 
 Candidates are comfortable working independently and have proven success in a goal oriented environment. 
 Candidates are excellent communicators (English and one other language) and are comfortable with telephone, email and face to face correspondence. 
 Primary Responsibilities: 
 Primary responsibilities include networking with your community to generate inquiries (leads.) 
 Educating your recruits on career opportunities and Evergreen College diplomas. 
 Partnering with the Campus Admissions Team to enroll and support qualified candidates. 
 Identifying and attending events that will generate qualified prospects. 
 Other: 
You are able to work a variety of hours 
You are comfortable with a 100% commissions based opportunity 
Training and relevant resources will be provided
