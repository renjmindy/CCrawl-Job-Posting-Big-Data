 Latest POEA Jobs in Caregiver 
 Posted on  December 12, 2010  by  caregiver 
 For those who are looking for caregiver jobs abroad, here is the  latest POEA Jobs in Caregiver . For the start of December 2010, the POEA approved several job orders that will provide employment to caregivers who are looking for caregiver jobs abroad. These latest POEA jobs in caregiver are for the holy land of Israel and were approved by the POEA last December 3, 6, and 7 for three POEA accredited recruitment agencies. Specified below are the number of vacancies for each of the latest POEA jobs in caregiver. 
 JAO International Manpower Services  ------------------------------- 51 vacancies 
 International Progress Inc.  ------------------------------------------- 22 vacancies 
 Star World International Manpower & Placement Agency, Inc.  -- 30 vacancies 
 To apply for any of the Latest POEA Jobs in Caregiver, visit the office of the recruiting agency and submit your resume and application. For any questions regarding the said latest POEA jobs in caregiver, you may contact each recruiting agency through their landline numbers or email address provided below.
