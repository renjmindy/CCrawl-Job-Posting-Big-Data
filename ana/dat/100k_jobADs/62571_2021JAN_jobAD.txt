 Full-Time University Researcher, ecotoxicology and Hydrobiology 
 University of Eastern Finland, Joensuu -  North Karelia  , Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 University of Eastern Finland is one of the largest universities in Finland. Multidisciplinary University offers instruction in more than 100 major subject. University of Eastern Finland has about 15 000 students and employs approximately 2 800 people. 
 The University has campuses in Joensuu, Kuopio and Savonlinna. University of Eastern Finland invites applications for the following task: SCIENCE AND FORESTRY FACULTY 
 University Researcher, ecotoxicology and Hydrobiology (vacancy number 30445), Department of Biology, Joensuu campus university researchers conducted in ecotoxicology and hydro-biology field research as part of the Department's research profile (environmental change and human activities on the ecosystem) to support and development. University researcher seeking research funding, to give a lecture in its field and method of teaching (  English and Finnish)  , participates in ecotoxicology and hydro biology education in the field of design and development tasks, as well as to control the field studies and post-graduate studies. 
 Ecotoxicology and hydrobiology addition to job includes responsibility for water conservation, sustainable development and risk assessment related to teaching. Is also responsible for participation in the operation of the facility. University researcher to service in the elected must be suitable for a PhD, and he must have good teaching skills. University researchers to take a fixed period or until further notice (Administrative Regulations § 31). Language proficiency requirements for the task is provided by Government Decree 770/2009. 
 The university's teaching and research positions require that students master the language, Finnish or Swedish, which he is obliged to teaching. Position will be filled for the deadline 1.1.2013-31.12.2017. Fixed-term basis for a four-stage career model with a fixed-term research and teaching mission. 
 The salary will be determined by the university for teaching and research personnel in the salary system in accordance with the severity levels 6-7 (3 254.17 euro / Month). Task-specific part of the salary is paid in addition to a personal salary component which is not more than 46.3% of the job section. 
 The application The application deadline is 31.10.2012 at 15.00.
