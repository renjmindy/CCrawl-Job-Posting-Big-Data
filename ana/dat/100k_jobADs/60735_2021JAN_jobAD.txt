 Supervisor Financial Accounting 
 Ivaco Rolling Mills, a Heico Company, located in L’Orignal, Ontario is a world-class Wire Rod and Steel Billet producer. Conveniently located in close proximity to Montreal and Ottawa, allowing for an easy commute to two major cities. Our company consists of 538 employees and is always looking for talented, dynamic and hardworking individuals to join our team. Ivaco Rolling Mills offers a competitive salary, excellent benefits package as well as advancement opportunities. Invest in your future! 
 Role and Responsibilities: 
 · Ensures awareness of safe job procedures and provides adequate equipment for the job to be performed. 
 · Ensures timely, accurate and effective data information in financial reports/statements. 
 · Manages the section activities in a cost-effective manner. 
 · Acquaints new employees with the section, ensures they understand rules, policies and procedures. 
 · Organizes on the job training, cross-trains employees on related jobs. Provides coaching as required. 
 · Informs employees of performance expectation, monitors and evaluates employee’s performance. 
 · Applies provisions of the Collective Agreement, attempts to settle concerns, interpretation disputes at his/her own level. Documents violations to rules, policies and applies discipline as required. 
 · Ensures timely, accurate and effective data information in financial reports and statements. 
 · Assigns tasks, communicates and sets deadlines and priorities, initiates preventive and corrective actions to meet deadlines. 
 · Overseas month end closes all relevant journal entries 
 · Assists the Assistant Controller in preparation of various weekly accounting reports. 
 · Prepares various analysis for financial statements on an ongoing basis. 
 · Reconciles all balance sheets accounts on a monthly basis. 
 · Observes all safety rules.
