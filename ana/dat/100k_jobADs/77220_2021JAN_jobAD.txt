 Child & Youth Worker 
 Spark Lifecare 
 __Ottawa, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Love helping people? We're looking for passionate, hardworking, and self-directed people to join our team of caregivers. Spark Inc is an innovative and award-winning organization that matches clients to the best one to one care in the community. 
 CHILD & YOUTH WORKER 
Our clients are incredible youth seeking care in the Ottawa region and valley. After experiences such as trauma, complex medical diagnoses, mental illnesses, or issues in their natural development, they are looking for support with managing emotions and behaviours, developing healthy relationships, and sometimes accompaniment into the community. Our clients' interests range in everything from sports, to arts and crafts, to reading, to building things, and everything in between! You would be the ideal candidate to work with us if you are passionate about working with youth, are reliable, love working in a team, have a Child & Youth Worker degree or equivalent, and if you have a valid Non-Violent Crisis Intervention training or equivalent.

 Spark is looking to hire motivated, dedicated, and experienced Child and Youth Support Workers. In this role, you'll be working with children and youth in the community living with Autism Spectrum Disorder, varying mental illnesses, and behavioural challenges. 
 Care is tailored to each person, so each position is unique. Some clients need mainly in-home support. Others require support participating in community activities. Whatever the circumstances, we want people who will genuinely care about their well-being. 
 Why Choose Spark? 
 At Spark, we value community, work life balance and career development. To help foster your personal and professional growth, we partner with Spark* University, a non-profit focused on equipping, educating, and empowering caregivers. Spark U makes your job easier so you can focus more on you and your clients. 
 Whatever our clients' age, background, or circumstances, we help them choose the perfect fit from our handpicked team of passionate, skilled and committed caregivers. As a caregiver with Spark, you have a voice in the matching process. We believe that your input will foster the best long-term relationships with your clients. At Spark you'll have flexibility, autonomy and a team who truly cares about you.
