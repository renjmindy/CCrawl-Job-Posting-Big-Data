 My client is looking for a qualified, hands on Financial Controller who can help out during a busy period to cover for a short term period. 
 The company will be going into year end the moment you are due to start so require a solid all round FC who can be hands on and get stuck in to the number crunching. 
 You have an experienced team of staff to manage who will help pull most of the financial information together for you to consolidate and prepare for Audit. 
 
 Manage the preparation of the management accounting reporting pack 
 Ownership of the divisional budget and long term planning processes 
 Ownership of the quarterly divisional re-forecasts in order to compare current performance trends versus budget 
 Support the divisional leadership team with financial support in relation to strategic projects 
 Development of Customer Profitability analysis for the division 
 Ad-hoc projects as required
