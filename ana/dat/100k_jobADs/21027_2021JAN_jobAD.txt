 The Best & Worst Jobs in Canada in 2013, according to Canadian Business magazine. 
 by  Jan Moore  | Oct 15, 2013 |  Blog 
 I’m posting these because this may be of interest. If you are not already in one of these professions, neither list may appeal. It definitely looks like everyone needs to stay in school longer! 
 Top 10 Worst Jobs in Canada – for job prospects 
 
 Plastics processing machine operator 
 Printing machine operator 
 Foundry worker 
 Labourer, wood, paper & paper processing 
 Rubber processing machine operator 
 General office clerk 
 Harvesting labourer 
 Weaver or knitter (not craft artists) 
 Photographic & film processor 
 Pulp mill operator ** 
** 
 
 Top 10 Best Jobs in Canada – for job prospects 
 
 Aerospace engineer 
 Chemical engineer 
 Senior government manager 
 Real estate or financial manager 
 Lawyer 
 School principal or administrator 
 Electrical or telecommunications contractor 
 Petroleum engineer 
 Head nurse or health care manager 
 Oil & gas drilling supervisor
