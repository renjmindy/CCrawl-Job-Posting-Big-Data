 Nurse Aide Job Hiring 
 Posted on  November 8, 2012  by  caregiver 
 Teknika Skills and Trade Services Inc., a duly accredited recruitment agency in Quezon City, is currently accepting applicants for  Nurse Aide Job Hiring . This nurse aide job hiring is for government rehabilitation centres in the kingdom of Saudi Arabia which requires a total of 80 nurse aides. Provided below are the qualifications for the said nurse aide job hiring. 
 
 Female 
 22-40 years old 
 At least High School diploma 
 With or without experience 
 
 If you wish to apply for the said Nurse Aide Job Hiring, visit the office of Teknika Skills and Trade Services Inc., fill up their application form, and forward it together with your detailed and updated resume. If you have any question regarding the said nurse aide job hiring, please get in touch with Teknika Skills and Trade Services Inc. by calling their landline or mobile numbers we have provided below.
