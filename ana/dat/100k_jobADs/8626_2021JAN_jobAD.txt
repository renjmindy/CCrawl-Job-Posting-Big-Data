 Project Administrator - up to £30k - Cambridge - Permanent 
 Are you organised with the ability to manage multiple projects and engage with stakeholders at executive level? Are you looking to work for a dynamic, fast paced organisation? 
 Gleeson are working with a rapidly expanding organisation based in Cambridge to recruit a newly created position for a Project Administrator to support the Head of Department. This is an exciting position working in an executive team to deliver projects to deadline, research information and ensure compliance on an international basis. 
 This role will give you scope to work with the business on an international basis, liaising with the most senior stakeholders as part of the businesses change and transformation strategy. You will have sight on plans across the organisation and be a trusted member of the team. 
 You will be an individual capable of taking ownership of your workload, working towards multiple deadlines, and working on your own initiative to conduct research. You will be tenacious and able to identify key information for projects and make suggestions for improvements. 
 Responsibilities include: 
 
 Administration coordination and project support to the Team 
 Leading on the management of small, delegated projects 
 Supporting on corporate banking administration, group property and insurance schedules 
 Supporting project planning, maintaining information, progressing actions, and ensuring project timelines 
 Collecting and checking of data to produce reports to advise business areas 
 Collating and analysing business information for business partnering projects 
 Coordinate communication updates between key stakeholders across the assigned business areas or projects 
 Support the management and coordination of internal finance policy documentation, maintaining up to date compliance schedules and data documents 
 Working with all key internal stakeholders to draft and finalise project delivery plans 
 
 This is a position for a true professional, looking for a career working for one of the most exciting businesses. The ideal candidate will be: 
 
 A degree level qualification or equivalent is desirable (Finance, Legal or Business ideally) 
 Minimum of 3-5 years' work experience in the same or similar 
 Track record of effective grants management 
 Ideally end-to-end track record of successful project management 
 Demonstrable ability to build good relationships and effectively communicate (verbally and in writing) with a range of stakeholders 
 Track record of producing high calibre reports for a range of audiences 
 Experience in establishing professional and well organised communication with partner organisations, stakeholders, and community and business leaders 
 Commitment to working to, and facilitating, the highest standards 
 
 To apply for the Project Administrator position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
