 Full-Time Shoe Seller 
 HRV Services  -  Southern Ostrobothnia  , Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 HRV Services is working and business management personnel nationwide companies specializing in services for the network, which provides consulting and other professional services firms for a variety of human needs. The services cover the staff addition and subtraction, as well as training and consulting products, as well as staffing. The Ministry of Employment and Economic Development Administration HRV-services companies have worked to help and support staff in various situations since 1992. 
 We are looking for customer companies in Seinäjoki SELLER work delivered both seller and customer servant, so service minded, friendliness, reliability and sociability are important qualities. You are also hard-working, initiative-taking and reliable. Education and work experience more important to have the right attitude to work. English, Swedish and Russian language skills would be an advantage, but not essential. Work shifts are determined by open trade conditions and according to the season. 
 Shop is open on weekdays from 10 to 19, 11 to 16.30 Saturday, and sometimes also on Sunday at 12-16. Work begins once hired, but it is possible that a worker moves from a client company's own books later.
