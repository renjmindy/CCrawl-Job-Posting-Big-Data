 Operator Coal Mining Level 3 
 Job description 
 Dump Truck Operators 
 About the Role 
 As a key member of a high performing team, you will play an integral role in implementing the client's production requirements. This is a drive in drive out (DIDO) role for an open cut coal mine in the Bowen Basin. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 - Work 7/7 Roster - Straight Days / Nights 
 - 12 Hour Shifts 
 - Pre-start checks 
 - Maintain equipment 
 - Operate equipment safely 
 - Follow company policies and procedures 
 Skills/Experience 
 - Minimum 789 Experience 
 - MUST Have SSE/Delegate Signed Skills List OR RIIs 
 - Current Drivers Licence 
 - Current Coal Board Medical or willing to obtain 
 - Current Standard 11 or willing to obtain 
 A medical including drug and alcohol testing is required as part of the recruitment process. 
 Benefits 
 - Work/Life Balance - Even Time Roster 
 - Camp and Meals Provided 
 - Immediate Start 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors.
