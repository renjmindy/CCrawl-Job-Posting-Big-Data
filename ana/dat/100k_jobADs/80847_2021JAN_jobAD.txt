 Sweet Baby Jason! it's a Full-time job 
 Here's the deal: Flamingo is looking for a JR / INTERMEDIATE DESIGNER (acquisition and digital) to create paid ads, support digital projects and do an occasional off the charts fun project, in our New York, USA office. Once we can be back onsite, it would be ideal for you to be there with us, b/c we are wildly fun and our creative director bakes the best treats. 
 About 
 Do you like designing Paid Ads & designing for web? This could be for you! 
 Great taste level is the most important attribute of this role. 
 This person will need to be a design, typography, layout, and editing/animating superstar with a passion for social channels and compelling visual storytelling. Typography skills are a must, as are social and print experience and obsessive attention to detail. Being interested in wellness, pop culture and the good of womankind is crucial, as is the ability to be resourceful and think outside the box. Art Direction, animation/editing abilities are expected. 
 They should be a technically skilled designer able to do some still life photography and instagram story videos, light retouching, printed matter and typography work—efficiently and always with attention to detail. Furthermore, they should be constantly engaging with team members to learn how to maximize performance of their work and demonstrating those learnings where and when it’s applicable. 
 This person should have experience communicating with different audiences and be fluent in social media, paid ads, with specific experience on Instagram.

 Fine Print 
 What they will accomplish: 
 Work within the Flamingo Creative Team and partner cross-functionally on projects big and small, establishing trust along the way. 
 Understand and implement a cohesive look and feel across all channels. A deep understanding of visual and social media trends, interest in women’s wellness and the self care landscape as well as being connected to pop culture. 
 Majority of their work will be for Paid Channels & Flamingo's Website: 
 Translates key consumer insights into compelling visuals in an iterative but thoughtful manner. Working at a high volume with flexibility and desire to iterate and create new and unexpected concepts in parallel. Collaborate on generating a high volume of great work and beautiful pieces of still and moving content. 
 Own and produce assets for paid acquisition channels. Deeply understand and synthesize customer insights and data to help develop scalable ads 
 Collaborate with cross functional partners on consumer needs including paid ads for Facebook, Instagram, gmail etc. This includes still imagery, images with typography, slideshows and videos with animated text and CTAs. 
 Support the Design Director and Digital Designer to support new launches on shopflamingo.com & other pages to help the consumer journey. 
 Designing Emails for new customers, product launches & Campaigns. Key focus on the customer Journey from emails to PDP pages. Serve as a bridge between the integrity of the brand visually and satisfying the best consumer journey practices. 
 Being flexible and agile to iterate on Creative work is key to this position!
