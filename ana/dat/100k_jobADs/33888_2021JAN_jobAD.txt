 Dump Truck Operator 
 Job description 
 
 
 
 Join a global leader in the mining sector FIFO 2/1 roster to the Pilbara Competitive hourly rate About The Role WorkPac are seeking Dump Truck Operators to join a global leader in the mining sector on a FIFO 2/1 roster to the Pilbara. Fortescue has an exciting future ahead, establishing itself for future growth in a global market through innovation, product diversification and asset development. As a skilled Mining Operator, you will be responsible for operating mobile equipment at maximum efficiency, while maintaining safety, environmental and heritage standards to achieve required operational targets for the business. Duties & Responsibilities Meet daily operational targets. Maintain assets to operational capacity. Constantly strive to increase process efficiency. Demonstrate behaviour that aligns with company values. Comply with Zero Harm principles on a daily basis. Skills & Experience Demonstrated experience operating CAT 785, 789 and 793 Dump  Job title:
