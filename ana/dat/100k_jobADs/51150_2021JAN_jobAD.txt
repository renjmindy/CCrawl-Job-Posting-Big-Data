 Look At This! it's a Freelance job 
 Code and Theory is looking for a Freelance Freelance Associate Creative Director Team in New York, NY, USA. 
 About 
 At Code and Theory, Associate Creative Directors make and inspire work that is distinctive and effective. We are seeking an ACD Art/copy team to ideate and execute digitally-fluent campaigns for multiple pitches and clients, starting next week with the possibility to extend for the remainder of the year. 
 Your portfolio should present campaigns that are clearly grounded in strategy and manifest in unexpected ways; we are not looking for traditional broadcast work, but rather content for a diverse ecosystem that includes site, interactive, social, OOH and much more. 
 ESSENTIALS 
 - Strong portfolio of digital campaign work samples 
- Experience with airlines, B2B, and B2C preferred 
- Innate curiosity for the client's business and how to help them through the work 
- Mastery of Adobe CS, additional motion or other creative software programs a plus 
- Ability to work well in a team environment 
- Remote from anywhere in the US, but able to work primarily East Coast hours
