 Financial Analyst, Downstream 
 Tidewater Midstream and Infrastructure Ltd. is a publicly listed company dedicated to the acquisition and build out of strategic midstream assets such as gas plants, pipelines, storage, rail, downstream and export assets. 
 Tidewater values its people more than anything, and our commitment to aligning corporate performance to individual performance is unparalleled. We are a fast growing company and are consistently looking for great people to join our amazing team. If a culture of hard work, responsiveness, and results-driven is what you are seeking, we invite you to explore this opportunity at Tidewater. 
 Tidewater is seeking candidates for the full-time position of Financial Analyst, Downstream based in Calgary. The Financial Analyst will be responsible for developing, interpreting and implementing complex financial models including analysis of financial results and accounting concepts or techniques for financial planning and control. 
 Summary of Responsibilities: 
 
 Provide financial support to the business unit 
 Drive improvements in monthly, quarterly, and yearly financial analysis and reporting processes 
 Prepare Ad hoc economic analysis and management reports as needed 
 Prepares monthly lookbacks comparing accruals and budgets to actuals 
 Supports Finance and Commercial Operations as necessary 
 Prepare monthly revenue and operating expense accruals 
 Involved in billing and management of accounts receivable 
 Capital spending analysis 
 Journal entry review 
 Prepare monthly excise tax returns 
 Other tasks, as required 
 
 Education and Experience: 
 
 Degree in Accounting or Finance with 5+ years of relevant experience, specific Downstream experience would be considered an asset 
 Preference will be given to candidates who have completed their CPA designation 
 Experience with Qbyte would be an asset 
 Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite and other financial reporting software 
 Effective and highly collaborative team player with strong interpersonal and problem-solving skills 
 Ability to manage multiple tasks in a changing work environment 
 Knowledge of internal control processes 
 Attention to detail and time management skills 
 Solid oral and written communication skills 
 Ability to solve problems and manage workflow 
 Self-directed and ability to work in a dynamic team-oriented environment 
 
 Compensation: 
 We offer a competitive compensation package including an attractive base salary, short term and long term incentive plans. 
 Employee Benefits: 
 Tidewater offers a comprehensive benefit program including health, dental and medical coverage, paramedical services, a healthcare spending account and an Employee and Family Assistance Program
