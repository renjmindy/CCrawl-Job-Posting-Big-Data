 Daily Duties: 
You will be working as a part of the Crisis Assessment and Intensive Support Team to provide intensive support to young people, below 18 years of age, within the community who are experiencing mental health crisis. 
 You will also carry out emergency assessments for those who attend the A&E; departments also. 
 You will carry a caseload of service users and review them in their own homes, at school or further educational settings, children's wards or A&E; departments of local hospitals. 
 You will determine the most appropriate treatment plan and also triage any referrals and monitor risk in order to prevent service users from being admitted to hospital. 
 Person specification: 
The applicant must be DBS cleared and have previous experience in the field.
