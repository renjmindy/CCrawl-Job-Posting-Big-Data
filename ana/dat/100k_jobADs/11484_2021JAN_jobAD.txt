 Private Caregiver Jobs Abroad for Nurses 
 Posted on  October 14, 2010  by  caregiver 
 A POEA licensed recruitment agency located in Ermita, Manila is seeking Filipino nurses who are qualified  for  Private Caregiver Jobs Abroad for Nurses . These caregiver jobs hiring for nurses are for Private Caregivers for the middle east country of Qatar and were approved by the POEA last July 12. For the said caregiver jobs hiring for nurses, 25 qualified applicants will be selected for placement in private caregiver jobs abroad. 
 If you wish to be included in the list of possible candidates for the said private caregiver jobs abroad for nurses, forward your updated resume and application directly to the office of the recruiting agency. For further details or inquiries regarding the said private caregiver jobs hiring for nurses, you can contact the recruiting agency’s landline number indicated below. 
 SML Human Resources Inc. 
2nd Floor T.M. Kalaw Center 
667-A T.M. Kalaw Avenue, 
Ermita, Manila 
Telephone: +632 536 1108 
Fax: +632 536 3356 
Email: operations@smlhri.com 
Website: www.smlhri.com
