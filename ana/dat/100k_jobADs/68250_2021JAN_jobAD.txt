 Job Title: Production Planning Manager 
 Department: Supply Chain 
 Reports to: Supply Chain Manage 
 Job Overview 
 This role will be responsible for the day to day running of the production planning team and function, you will ensure that your team has the right levels of support, metrics and tools available to them to carry out their jobs effectively. You will also be the driving force behind planning processes to guarantee your team can scale to meet a complex multi site environment. 
 This role is fast paced and works across a large number of functions, we are looking for someone to bring experience and production planning knowledge that will support the growth of the business and the expansion into a multi site environment. 
 You will act as a point of contact for stakeholders outside of the production planning team and ensure good cross functional relationships are created and maintained between the teams, you will focus on delivering improvements to your teams processes and the wider supply chain. 
 You will continually challenge behaviours and processes to find smarter ways of working that contribute towards a noiseless planning operation. 
 What you’ll do (job tasks and responsibilities) 
 
 Oversee the day to day operations of the Production Planning Team 
 Ensure that production planning strategies are fit for purpose and scale with Gousto’s expanding multi site operation 
 Be the Subject Matter Expert for all production planning queries 
 Develop processes and use Continuous Improvement to increase efficiency 
 Work with key stakeholders to agree the best planning profile for all production lines 
 Implement production planning strategies that maximise line running time whilst working within operational constraints 
 Provide coaching and guidance to your direct team through regular 121’s and performance reviews 
 Design, develop and implement flexible solutions which help support the companies growth plans 
 
 Skills, knowledge and abilities required to be effective in this position 
 
 A minimum of 5 years experience in a Production Planning Management role 
 Ability to work in fast-paced evolving environment 
 Proven Management skills with the ability to drive a high performing team 
 Competency with Microsoft Excel 
 High level of computer literacy and ability to learn new software 
 Very high attention to detail 
 Highly analytical and enjoys problem solving 
 Experience using ERP, WMS systems 
 Experience in a food supply chain environment desirable but not essential 
 
 KPI’s 
 
 Shelf life to customer 
 OTIF 
 Waste % 
 Availability 
 Stock Cover 
 
 Our Culture 
 You’re a confident self-starter, able to juggle multiple projects in a dynamic, exciting and entrepreneurial environment. You’re initiative and a problem-solver with an eagle eye for detail. You’ll consistently go the extra mile to apply and achieve a high standard across everything you do. 
 Dream - Today’s good is tomorrow’s mediocre. We’re always looking for ways to improve Gousto, our team and ourselves. 
 Deliver - We understand where we can add the most value and obsess about making improvements every day. 
 Care - We can only achieve big things by working collaboratively. We respect different people’s skills, experience and points of view. We work as one team.
