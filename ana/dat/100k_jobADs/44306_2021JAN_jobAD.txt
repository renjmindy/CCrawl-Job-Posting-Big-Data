 Salary:

 Join MSKs  Software Engineering Team  as we meet the challenge of fighting cancer in the modern era. We will define a new model of care using software. You will join one of the work homes (Ecosystems, Clinical & Logistics Platform, Service Platform and Health Consumer Platform) in 2021. Your work assignment will adjust periodically depending on Train capacity and deliverables. 
 You are a highly skilled Mid-Senior Fullstack .Net Developer. You can design and develop from architecture, design to coding. You work well in a dynamic team environment working with the business, designers, architects, other developers and QA and DevOps engineers. 
 What you'll do: 
 
 Design and develop end user software applications. Youll also develop responsive frontend applications with a vision towards delivery on any channel. 
 Design and build backend integrations using various integration tools including .Net and IBM App Connect. You will choose the best tool for the solution. 
 Design and develop robust backend solutions that will not only be used by your immediate team, but across the Enterprise. Youll be adding to our API Portfolio (Microservices) that will enable our Toolmakers to transform the delivery of healthcare. 
 Collaborate in an agile team with Product Owners, Scrum Masters, System Architects, other Development Teams, QA Engineers, DevOps Engineers and Users. 
 
 Who you are: 
 
 Proficiency with server-side languages such as Python, C#, ASP.Net, and .Net Core. 
 Proficiency in one or more frontend technologies such as JavaScript, React.JS, TypeScript, Vue.JS, HTML, CSS 
 Experience with enterprise CMS (DXP) such as SiteCore, Adobe Experience Manager, Acquia, EpiServer, BloomReach, OpenText 
 Proficiency in Test Driven Development and ensuring high coverage of Unit Tests to enable an effective, production-safe automated CICD pipeline 
 Understands application integration, event driven architecture and building high volume services. 
 Familiarity with using Docker, Kubernetes or similar container technologies and setting up CI/CD pipelines is a plus. 
 Experience and familiarity with Cloud Architecture (AWS or Azure) is a plus.
