 We are looking for a Marketing Data Analyst to  join our team. You will work closely with the CRM Manager. The ideal candidate will possess exceptional creativity and artistic eye, a can-do attitude, proactivity and superior organizational skills. 
 Key Responsibilities 
 
 Providing analytics, reporting, and insights for major projects and campaigns, ongoing results/tracking of KPIs, and ad hoc requests. 
 Maintaining tracking, trending and forecasting of performance of all Marketing departments. 
 Analyzing effectiveness of various marketing tactics including leads, sales, retention and profitability trends. 
 Creating executive summaries and presentations for management review. 
 Contributing to the improvement of the current Marketing presentation template. 
 Automating reports and cross checks needed by the Department. 
 Contributing to the development of specifics for analytics dashboards (Looker), following the development and performing the necessary checks on data. 
 Contributing to the creation of a Customer Lifetime Value formula and a Churn Matrix. 
 Applying a continuous improvement mindset to all the analytics, reporting and tracking tools of the Marketing department. 
 Act as a point of contact for all data needs of the Marketing department. 
 Analyze existing marketing programs for profitability and revenue opportunity, and recommend improvements. 
 Use statistics and analytical methods to arrive at key insights and conclusions that help grow the business. 
 Work cross functionally to understand and account for operational impacts to data. 
 
 Who you are: 
 
 Proactive, driven, passionate and able to adapt according to situations. A real go getter. 
 Creative and with a willingness to try and test new things and constantly improve 
 Keen eye for detail 
 Able to manage time and work under tight deadlines. 
 
 Desired skills and experience: 
 
 Previous experience in a data analyst role. 
 Proficient MS Excel user with a strong analytical mind-set. 
 Interest for online Gaming. 
 Excellent written and verbal English communication skills. 
 Detail oriented and able to work within a high degree of accuracy. 
 Self-driven individual with a positive and hands-on attitude. 
 Team player with interpersonal skills. 
 Respect the importance of confidentiality. 
 
 Benefits: 
 
 Full-time employment contract. 
 Competitive salary and conditions corresponding to high demands of the position 
 Team building activities. 
 Everyday fully paid meal with beverage. 
 Encouragement of innovation and support in personal and professional growth. 
 
 Why join us? 
 We are a collaborative, forward-thinking, innovative team and work on cutting edge tech. We are passionate about what we do and the products that we create. We offer a competitive salary, unlimited training and amazing perks as well as a flexible superstar environment.
