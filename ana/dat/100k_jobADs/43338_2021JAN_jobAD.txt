 Logistics Officer 
 Job description 
 About the Role: 
 Workpac are seeking an experienced Logistics Officer for an exciting opportunity with our renewable energy client based in Regional NSW. 
 Reporting to the Logistics Manager, the successful applicant will support the logistics team to manage all stock and deliveries in and out of the warehouse on site. This particular project is a large scale, multi stage solar farm development. The position is a full time Monday to Friday role with an estimated 2 year contract available directly with our client. This is a local based role in the Armidale region and no accommodation or travel is provided. 
 Key Duties: 
 
 
 Manages stock control and reconciles with the data storage system. 
 
 
 Uses available software tools (Excel, MS Project) to ensure information is correctly stored and easily accessible and ensures databases are constantly updated. 
 
 
 Liaises with transport companies when necessary. 
 
 
 Adheres to all warehousing, handling, and shipping legislation requirements. 
 
 
 Assist Logistics Manager in day-to-day implementation of the Construction Logistics Plan. 
 
 
 Key Experience Required: 
 
 
 Previous experience working on a renewable energy project would be preferred 
 
 
 Previous logistics, supply chain or warehousing experience is essential 
 
 
 Experience dealing with suppliers ad purchase systems is preferred 
 
 
 Must hold a construction white card 
 
 
 Must hold a current valid drivers license 
 
 
 Must be able to pass a pre-employment medical and drug and alcohol test if required 
 
 
 Must hold full working rights in Australia 
 
 
 How to Apply: 
 Click Apply Now and our recruiter Julia Ryzner will be in touch. 
 Reference Number: 7312J2020309
