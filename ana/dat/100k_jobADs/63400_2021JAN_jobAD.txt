 Are you an experienced Assistant Management Accountant? Would you like a step up working for a reputable, professional and solution focused business?  If so, then please read on. 
 You will be joining a small, committed and hardworking finance team, reporting directly into the Finance Manager near Coventry. You will receive a salary of up to £28k per annum depending on experience alongside other great benefits. 
 Responsibilities include: 
 
 Assisting with preparation of monthly management accounts 
 Month end process 
 Updating and posting monthly journals 
 Prepayments and Accruals 
 Monthly Bank Reconciliations including investigating and resolving discrepancies 
 Updating reports and spreadsheets for management 
 Analysis and commentary 
 
 Is this role right for you? 
 This is a great opportunity for you to join as a Management Accountant. If you enjoy working in a team, often on your own initiative and have excellent excel skills this could be right for you. 
 Benefits: 
 
 Salary up to £28k per annum + Study Support 
 Pension 
 Free onsite parking 
 Open plan offices 
 Flexible working hours 
 
 If this sounds like you, submit your cv or call me on 012175299404.
