 Entry Level - Factory Hand - DAY SHIFT 
 Job description 
 Factory Hand/Labourer 
 About our business 
 Our client is established in the mining, engineering and capital solutions industry 
 About the role 
 
 
 Work can include and will not be limited to the following 
 
 
 Packing galvanised products 
 
 
 Shrink wrapping products ready for distribution 
 
 
 Process line tasks 
 
 
 Repetitive manual labouring - lifting over head 
 
 
 Flexible to work Saturdays as required 
 
 
 Benefits and perks 
 
 
 Immediate start 
 
 
 Overtime where required 
 
 
 Skills and experience 
 
 
 Previous experience in a similar environment 
 
 
 Overhead gantry crane experience advantageous 
 
 
 Heavy industrial and/or manufacturing experience 
 
 
 Physically fit 
 
 
 Basic power and hand tools experience 
 
 
 Strong adherence and understanding of OH & S in the workplace 
 
 
 Must have own transport - no public transport access 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
