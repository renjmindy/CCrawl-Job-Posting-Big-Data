 Caregiving Job Vacancies 
 Posted on  January 23, 2011  by  caregiver 
 A recently approved job order offers Filipino caregivers the chance of employment abroad through  Caregiving Job Vacancies  in Israel. This caregiving job vacancies in Israel was approved by the POEA last January 10, 2011 for a Quezon City based recruitment agency. For the said caregiver jobs abroad, 15 caregiving job vacancies are being offered to those who will qualify. 
 If you are interested to apply for the said Caregiving Job Vacancies, kindly submit your resume together with your application directly to the office of  JAO International Manpower Services. For more details regarding the said caregiving job vacancies, you may contact JAO International Manpower Services thru their telephone number specified below. 
 JAO International Manpower Services 
Unit D, 3rd Floor, Jacinto Building 
380 Scout Reyes corner Quezon Avenue 
Paligsahan, Quezon City 
Tel Nos.: 5103427 
Email address: admin@jaointernational.com 
Website: http://jaointernational.com
