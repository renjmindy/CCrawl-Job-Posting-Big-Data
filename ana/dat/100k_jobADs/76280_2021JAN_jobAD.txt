 Entrepreneurs Start Here is the leading student development program specializing in skills required to separate yourself with experience. We've been in the business since 1971, with hundreds of student leaders running summer window cleaning businesses Canada. Your parents probably know us as College Pro Window Cleaning, but our mission is to provide exceptional leadership and management training to student and young entrepreneurs through real-world experiences. Our values are to deliver what you promise, respect the individual, have pride in what you do, and to be open-minded to possibilities. 
 We are currently looking for motivated, strong, creative and intelligent candidates to be a part of our Crew Lead summer team and to lead the operations of their own piece of our business. 
 Job Description 
 Interview, select and lead a team of 2-3 crew members 
Manage the logistics of a window cleaning business operations in a specified region 
Maintain high levels of customer service excellence with our valued repeat customers 
Generate new customer leads through referrals and door-to-door canvassing 
Collect and submit customer payments 
Perform residential window cleaning, eavestrough cleaning and siding cleaning 
 Earnings 
 Earnings are based on a pay-for-performance model. The more you accomplish, the more you will earn.
