 Childcare Paralegal 
 Service Care Legal are currently recruiting on behalf of a Wiltshire based local authority who are seeking a Legal Support Assistant to join their Childcare legal team on a contract basis. 
 This is a fantastic opportunity for people starting off in their legal career or those looking to work within a local authority setting to advance their respective careers. 
 The main purposes of the role will be to: 
 , To provide a comprehensive legal support to the child care team and the legal department as a whole 
, Cases will include Care Proceedings, Adoption matters, Revocations and Discharge Proceedings and private law matters 
, Assisting the legal team to offer legal services to members and officers of the Council in terms of transactional work 
, Providing and preparing legal advice and legal documentation 
, The role will require the post holder to carryout functions as required by the fee earners within the litigation team and legal department 
 The ideal candidate would be someone who: 
 , Has at least 6 month worth of Paralegal or Legal Assistant experience, ideally within a Childcare Law capacity 
, Has a legal related qualification such as the LPC, LLM or LLB. 
 This would be advantageous but not essential 
, Strong organisational and time management skills 
, Someone who is able to work on sensitive cases and work well within a team 
 If this sounds like an opportunity of interest, please feel free to reach out to Lloyd Stanley on 01772 208969, or email Lloyd.stanley@servicecare.org.uk. 
 We also welcome referrals for this position, where a successful recommendation would be worth £250.
