 Gleeson RG are exclusively recruiting for a Financial Controller to join a growing Business with significant market share. This is a great role with significant autonomy with a very commercial overview from a finance perspective. 
 Role 
 
 Key Senior Finance leader 
 Management accounting and providing commercial and operational management information to support rapid expansion 
 Oversight of the day-to-day running of finance 
 Understanding financial performance, risks and financial management 
 Identify and implement process improvements to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of the monthly, quarterly and annual reporting process 
 Manage budgeting & forecasting process 
 
 Person 
 
 Fully Qualified (ACA, ACCA or CIMA) 
 Advanced excel skills
