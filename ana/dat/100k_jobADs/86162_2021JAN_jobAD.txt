 We are GetAgent.  A growing property tech company and the UK's leading estate agency comparison site. We're looking for an Office Manager who is flexible, adaptable and enjoys helping others. 
 After helping over 250,000 homeowners successfully sell their homes, in 2019 we doubled in size, moved out of a shared office space and into our shiny new office that we have all to ourselves. 
 We are evolving as a business and are developing some exciting new concepts. You'll be joining an energetic and inclusive office who have a clear sense of purpose and we have no plans of slowing down. Our focus is now building upon on our dynamic workspace ready for the new year. 
 What you 'll do 
 You'll work closely with the founding team and provide support where needed. You will act as a representative of the company, internally and externally and aim to build strong relationships. 
 _Your duties will include but are not limited to:  _ 
 Office Support 
 
 Payroll, reconciliation of accounts and VAT returns 
 Health & Safety, security, emergency procedures 
 Supplier relations 
 Ad Hoc support - always welcomed with a 'can do' attitude 
 
 Keep our house in order 
 
 General upkeep and facilities management 
 Maintaining and ordering stock 
 Ensuring our plants don't die, the dishwasher is loaded and run etc 
 Keeping everyone safe and ensure the adherence to procedures 
 
 Onboarding 
 
 Welcoming new starters and helping them adjust to their new environment 
 Setting up equipment i.e laptops, email, notepads etc 
 Signing of contracts 
 Showing them around the office 
 
 Help make GetAgent a great place to work 
 
 Organise parties and socials 
 Set up team building/bonding exercises and activities 
 Organise events like birthdays, lunches and other special events 
 
 What we need from you: 
 Intelligence  - You don't have to be Einstein, but we're looking for someone with the ability to successfully assess a situation and problem solve. 
 Communication  - Strong written and verbal English is imperative to succeed in this role; you'll be constantly liaising with the team, external entities and ensuring procedures are clearly stated. 
 Diligence  - We require someone who will go above and beyond whilst maintaining a sharp eye for detail. 
 Requirements 
 
 Previous Office Management experience is required. (Minimum 1yr) 
 Excellent numerical, communication and interpersonal skills 
 Bookkeeping and Xero experience is required 
 Proven team player with the ability to be self-motivated and work on own initiative 
 Be able to handle a fast-paced company, previous start-up experience would be great but is not essential. 
 The ability to thrive and voice your opinion in a growing environment. 
 You will have the ability to work smart and organise effectively. 
 You prioritise high impact work but never neglect the details. 
 
 What we offer 
 
 As you help grow the company so will your role and responsibilities. In time you'll have the opportunity to build and manage a team 
 An amazing culture - no matter which team you are on, you can grab a bite to eat with anyone in the company. Our office is fuelled by honesty, integrity, and of course, some laughter! 
 Competitive salary with regular salary reviews 
 We aim to promote from within 
 Weekly one-to-one with a mentor 
 25 days holiday (plus bank holidays!) 
 Private health insurance 
 Employer pension contributions (provided through Nest) 
 
 We will be conducting video interviews throughout December and would like the successful candidate to start in the new year, so get your CV over ASAP!
