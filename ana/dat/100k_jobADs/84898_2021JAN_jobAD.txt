 German Credit Controller - Bracknell 
 Our impressive, International business services client who operate their EMEA Shared Service Centre in Bracknell, are looking to recruit a German speaking Credit Controller / Accounts Receivable Controller to join their team in supporting and delivering to clients across EMEA. 
You will be responsible for a variety of different areas, around credit management, credit collection, cash application and account reconciliation and handling queries. 
 Duties as a German Credit Controller: 
 
 Collection of outstanding debt from across their German customer base 
 Customer risk assessment and credit analysis 
 Query resolution 
 Various reconciliations 
 Cash allocation, credit note and debit note processing to customer account 
 Reconcile disputes promptly and professionally across EMEA 
 Liaise with all necessary stakeholders (internal and external) to ensure customer accounts are fully reconciled 
 
 Profile: 
 
 Experience within Accounts Receivable or Credit Control, or Customer Services 
 Experience of chasing debtors across EMEA (preferably) 
 Experience of working with foreign currencies would be beneficial 
 Strong knowledge of Microsoft packages, Excel, Word etc 
 Excellent customer service skills across multiple locations 
 Excellent interpersonal skills 
 Industry or shared service experience 
 Fluency in written & oral English 
 Team Player 
 Accurate data entry skills. 
 Fluency in German 
 Experience with ERP systems- Oracle/SAP experience a plus 
 Ability to work and deliver collaboratively across a complex global matrix 
 
 This is a unique opportunity to join an award-winning business on a permanent basis, and our client can offer great training, on-boarding and long term development for your career. Superb modern offices and free parking are also on offer, and my client is based within easy reach of all main public transport routes. Please contact me for further information of this superb German Credit Controller opportunity based in Bracknell.
