 Back-End Developer in Nicosia 
 Full Time 
 
 __Post Date: January 12, 2021 
 __Apply Before: March 31, 2021 
 
 Job Description 
 On behalf of one of our clients specializing in Web Design and Software Development we are seeking to recruit a Back-End Developer to join their team in Nicosia. 
 Job Requirements 
 
 We’re looking for someone who has a BSc or MSc degree in Computer Science or a related field 
 You must have some front-end web development experience in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and Bootstrap 
 You must have experience when working with standard web application programming languages such as PHP, ASP, JSP, or Adobe Coldfusion. 
 Having experience in Relational Databases is a must 
 We’re looking for someone who has experience in Application Security 
 You must have a very good understanding of API Integrations 
 Being able to understand the business needs of a project and also know what to anticipate is something we’re looking for from all our developers 
 You will need a minimum of two years proven experience as a back-end developer 
 We’re looking for a back-end developer who has the ability to perform accurately, effectively and methodically under tight deadlines 
 If you have experience with the MetaTrader API, it’s definitely considered a plus 
 
 What our client offers 
 
 Competitive remuneration package 
 Private medical insurance 
 Discretionary bonus at the end of the year 
 
 How to Apply 
 If interested and you match all the requirements, please send your CV to  andriana.p@work-channel.com  , including the title of the position in the subject line 
 Please note  that due to the high volume of applications received, only successful candidates will be contacted
