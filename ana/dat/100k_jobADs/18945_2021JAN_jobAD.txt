 Job Description 
 Installation Supervisor, Waski Weld Oy Ab, Vaasa, Overseas We are looking for installation supervisors for mechanical installation supervision, as well as technical advice / oversee the work abroad project delivery. Project duration is about six months to several years. 
 We hope to previous experience of working abroad, as well as know-how from one of the following areas: "Wood treatment equipment and processes." Piping and tanks "Conveyors" Power Plant Technology We appreciate the cooperation and information technology skills, strong project skills, welding techniques, knowledge, experience in the engineering industry, and quality assurance techniques, and access control. 
 We require good English oral and written skills. 
 Calculate the benefit of other language skills. We offer challenging work, the opportunity to work for long periods abroad, as well as a competitive salary. Taking up a job does not require migration of Vaasa.
