 Repair Welder 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Ongoing opportunity 
 
 
 Positive team culture 
 
 
 27th of January start 
 
 
 About Our Client 
 Our Client is a one of the largest manufacturer and supplier of water pipeline systems with a history of infrastructure delivery for over 130 years and have supplied thousands of kilometres of pipeline through Australia's varied landscapes. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently recruiting for a Repair Welder for our Client in Naval Base. You will be working Monday-Friday. Pay rate of $36.27 base rate per hour plus Super. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Ability to work inside pipes to repair weld 
 
 
 Proven history in use of grinder 
 
 
 Ability to pass Instant DAS 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Good literacy and numeracy skills 
 
 
 Experience working with mild steel 
 
 
 Mig/Stick welding 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Own transport due to location 
 
 
 LF Ticket preferred 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities.
