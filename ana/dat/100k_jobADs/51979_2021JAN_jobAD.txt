 Financial Accountant, up to £47,500, Coventry, Corporate business 
 Gleeson Accountancy are delighted to be working in partnership with this well known, corporate, forward thinking business, to recruit a permanent qualified Financial Accountant based at their Head Office in Coventry. 
 Within your new role you will be a key member of the team, supporting the Financial Reporting Manager, ensuring the financial accounts, monthly reporting, financial planning and controls are completed correctly and efficiently. This will include stakeholder management, variance analysis and commentary, budgeting/forecasting, month end reporting and balance sheet control. 
 You will support the Reporting Manager in providing comprehensive financial accounts to the senior management team, ensuring appropriate financial control with a particular emphasis on timely, accurate and complete accounts with stringent deadlines along with input to the month-end accounting process. 
 In order to be successful, you will be a qualified ACA or ACCA accountant, ideally with a practice/audit background, and the ability to build strong relationships with all levels of the business. This role would suit a first or second time mover. 
 You will also have experience / ability of: 
 
 Group led reporting projects 
 IFRS 
 Advanced Excel 
 Working on your own initiative and perseverance to get the job done 
 Presentation skills 
 Well organised with determination to meet agreed deadlines 
 Ability to multi task and prioritise workload 
 Result and achievement oriented to a high standard 
 Good planning and communication skills 
 
 This business knows how to look after their staff. In return for this great role, you will receive a salary of up to £47,500 with excellent benefits, including flexible working, annual bonus, generous pension, free on-site parking, dedicated training, lifestyle benefits, as well as the opportunity for a fast track career progression. 
 For more information on this role, or to discuss your current situation, please contact Sophie Evans at Gleeson Accountancy, on 07812 079718 or 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
