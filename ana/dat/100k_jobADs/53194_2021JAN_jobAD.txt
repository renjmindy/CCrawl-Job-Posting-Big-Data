 Senior Manager - Data Management & IT Strategy 
 For an international technology client in Munich we are currently searching for a Senior IT Strategy & Data Manager. 
 This is your opportunity to join one of the biggest and mos innovative companies in Germany! 
 Do you have a strategic mindset and an excellent understanding of data & analytics? This is an excellent opportunity for you to take over a team focusing on using data to answer strategic questions and improve decision making. 
 Don't miss this unique opportunity to join a technology giant based in the heart of Munich! 
 Role Responsibilities 
 
 Disciplinary management of a team of 5-7 employees 
 Define the long term company-wide technology strategy 
 Analyse and visualise data for senior management and internal stakeholders 
 Collaborate with cross-functional development and operational teams 
 Challenge current structures and technical solutions 
 
 Role Requirements 
 
 3 - 5 years working experience in technology strategy 
 Extensive disciplinary management experience 
 Extensive understanding of the IT value chain, data management topics and newest technology trends 
 Excellent analytical capabilities 
 Consultancy background preferred 
 Complete English fluency, German would be hugely advantageous 
 
 If you would like to know more please send your CV to ltoplak@venquis.com or call 089 2109 3905 for more information. We look forward to your application!
