 Are you an experienced Learning and Development professional? If so, my client is currently looking for a Learning and Development Specialist to join their team in Wolverton, Milton Keynes. The business is currently going through a period of growth and this role will be a new addition to the HR function. 
 Working within a structured HR team, you will report into the Group HR Director, and will be responsible for Training Development and Delivery for the internal teams. This could include classroom-based learning, coaching and e-learning as well as evaluation of training delivery. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Gain understanding of business processes and systems by liaising with business colleagues and industry stakeholders as required. 
 Analyse training needs to produce a training needs analysis. Develop training plan and course curriculum based upon training needs. 
 Develop an appropriate mix of formal and informal development activities 
 Creation and preparation of training materials including course outlines, exercises, user guides, presentations and any other learning materials required. 
 Delivery of training and/or facilitation in formal courses, workshops and Model Office activities. 
 Engage and communicate with internal and external stakeholders 
 Create and deliver training presentations to promote system usage, share knowledge and motivate users. 
 Evaluate training delivery and application of training. 
 
 Beneficial Experience: 
 
 Expert knowledge of Microsoft Office. 
 Experience in the design and delivery of soft skills. 
 Knowledge and application of training theory, models and methods. 
 Knowledge of instructional design methodology - (analysis, design, development, implementation and evaluation). 
 Experience in delivering various training platforms (individual, classroom instruction, on-line training, blended learning etc.)
