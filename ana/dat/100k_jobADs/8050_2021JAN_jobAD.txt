 Gleeson Accountancy are delighted to be working in partnership with this well known business in Coventry, to recruit a part qualified Assistant Management Accountant. As an Assistant Management Accountant, you will provide financial support to various business functions through the proactive engagement with the wider business. You will be responsible for the preparation of financial information including cost centre reports, management accounts, forecasts and budgets to support business decisions. 
 As an Assistant Management Accountant, you will provide financial analysis and evaluation and contribute to the building of robust budgets and forecasts. You will undertake the month end management accounting process for the business functions you are responsible for, ensuring the data used is consistent and accurate, including the appropriateness of all inputs and outputs and completeness, consistency and accuracy of all data, ensuring accounting entries are appropriately classified in accordance with internal policies 
 To be considered for the role, you will ideally be a part qualified Accountant (full study package will be provided). You will have experience of reporting financial information with a good understanding of the Management Accounts process. Experience of SAP is desirable. 
** 
**In return, you will receive a salary of £26,000 - £30,750 plus a whole host of benefits, including 25 days holiday + statutory days with the option to buy/sell, a generous pension plan, as well as the opportunity to earn annual bonus (up to 5% based on company performance), free on site parking, full study support package and lifestyle benefits. Flexible benefits include cycle to work scheme, salary sacrifice car, insurance and healthcare packages. 
 For further information, please apply direct, or call Sophie Evans, Gleeson Recruitment, on 07812 079718. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
