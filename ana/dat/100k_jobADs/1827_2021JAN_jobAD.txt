 Interim School Business Manager - Birmingham - 3 Months - Day rate negotiable dependent on experience. 
 I have a great opportunity to join a fantastic school for a short contract to support them through a period of change. The Interim School Business Manager will take on full responsability for the finance function including ownership of all commercial actives within the school covering HR, estates, health and safety, catering, contracts, maintenance etc.. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Provision of Financial support to the leadership team 
 Drive commercial decision making 
 Financial Planning 
 Budgeting & Forecasting 
 Risk Management 
 Human Resources 
 Catering 
 Estates 
 Health and Safety 
 Project support where required 
 
 To be considered you will need to be a chartered accountant and have held a Finance Leadership and Business Support role within a school and be available at short notice. This role will require you to hit the ground running so I am ideally looking for an interim specialist with significant experience in the school setting. 
 This role is private and confidential, no additional information can be released until a CV has been received and positively vetted by the client and an NDA signed. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
