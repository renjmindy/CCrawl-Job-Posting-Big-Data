 Job Description 
 Ground Support Power Mechanic 
(GSE Shop) Individuals interested in this position should submit a resume on 
UPSJOBS.com. The resume will be forwarded to the GSE manager where it will be 
evaluated. Candidates need to have 3 to 
5 years of hands on experience with diesel & gas engines, hydraulics & 
electrical circuits, and pneumatic & air brake systems. Candidates must 
also have good troubleshooting and diagnostic skills on these types of 
components, circuits, and systems. 
 Job Description: Must possess 
own tools sufficient to complete general mechanic work. Will be required to locate and service 
vehicles and report on any assigned work. Preventative assignments will include 
oil changes, filter changes, minor general automotive repairs. Be able to lift a minimum of 70 pounds and 
work in various positions including under a vehicle. 
 Job Summary: Perform 
preventative maintenance on GSE equipment. 
Work includes, but not limited to, oil change, grease and lube, filter 
change, inspect and report general vehicle equipment condition. Minor repairs and tune ups. 
 Work Environment: Work to be 
performed in the GSE shop in an assigned work bay. Work to be completed prior to shift end and 
bay left vacant and clean.
