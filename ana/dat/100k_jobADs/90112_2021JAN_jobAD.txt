 Accounts Receivables Collection Agent-MON17488 
 Description 
 BOMBARDIER 
 At Bombardier, our employees work together to evolve mobility worldwide - one good idea at a time. If you have a good idea, we’ll provide the environment where it will thrive and grow into a great product or customer experience. Your ideas are our fuel. 
 In your role, you will: 
- 
Daily collection calls to customers on outstanding receivables 
 - 
Verify cash applications to accounts are accurate and in accordance with customer instructions 
 - 
Reconcile customer transactions and balances to maintain accurate account statements with minimal outstanding items for customer treatment 
 - 
Liaise with internal departments to support account maintenance such as disputed items, queries from customer 
 - 
Maintain accurate records and document customer communications 
 - 
Verify and maintain the contact information (names, telephone and email numbers) of all representatives responsible for account payment on an ongoing basis 
 - 
Make recommendations and assist in the quarterly calculation of the bad debt provision 
 - 
Perform Customer-specific and individual weekly and monthly governance reports 
 - 
Meet monthly collection targets 
 - 
Escalate status of delinquent accounts to management as required 
 - 
Review bill blocked orders and make appropriate decisions on holding or releasing orders using judgement and in general accordance with the credit policy 
 - 
Investigate and resolve customer inquiries 
 - 
Identify, document and follow-up on customer disputes 
 - 
Perform other related duties and department support as required 
 Qualifications 
 As our ideal candidate, 
 
 You have a minimum of 5 years of commercial collections experienc 
 You have excellent communication skills both oral and written in English (French is an asset) 
 
 - 
You are very strong in all Microsoft Office applications and are proficient in Excel 
 - 
You have a very strong knowledge of ERP systems, especially SAP 
 - 
You have strong analytical and problem-solving skills 
 - 
You have a professional demeanor and excellent interpersonal communication skills 
 - 
You interface comfortably with all levels of the organization (external and internal) 
 - 
As an active listener, you are customer service-focused and solutions-oriented with keen emotional intelligence 
 - 
Based on situation specifics, you have the ability to explain and remove roadblocks in the collections process and use sound judgement when making decisions 
 - 
You are detail oriented, have good organizational skills, and are able to perform under pressure 
 - 
You are able to work in a dynamic environment and adapt quickly 
 - 
You enjoy working in a team environment but are self-motivated and autonomous
