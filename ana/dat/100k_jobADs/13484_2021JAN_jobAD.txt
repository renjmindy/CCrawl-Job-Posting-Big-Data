 Consultant: 
 
 
 As the Executive Assistant to the Chief Financial Officer, your role will be varied, challenging and enjoyable; providing the opportunity to take ownership and tailor the position to best suit both the CFO and yourself while working in a rapidly growing organisation. 
 It is a corporate organisation, fast paced and professional environment that will suit an experienced Executive Assistant. 
 You will be based within their Birmingham offices. 
 As the EA to the CFO you will be responsible for the following duties: 
 
 Act as first point of contact for internal and external senior executives 
 Diary management, forward planning, email & communication management supporting CFO and 4 Heads of Departments. 
 Screen emails and telephone calls 
 Provide Senior Leadership Team with clear, pertinent business information to assist in the on-going management and decision-making of the business 
 Arrange & participate in meetings and events, booking venues, equipment and refreshments 
 Organisation of travel itinerary and logistics 
 Meeting support, including planning and pack/report preparation 
 Producing / editing presentations using PowerPoint 
 Manage all expense claims and reconciliation 
 Create financial and statistical tool and reports using Microsoft Excel & Microsoft PowerPoint 
 Invoice and budget management 
 
 To be considered for this role, you will have the following credentials: 
 
 Financially literate 
 Great interpersonal skills, with emotional intelligence 
 Solid communication skills, with excellent command of written and spoken English 
 Strong organisational skills, able to deal with an ever changing, fast paced environment 
 Previous senior / exec level EA experience within a large company 
 Extensive experience of working with Microsoft packages 
 
 Please send your CV to Jane Spearman or for a confidential discussion please call 0121 752 3822. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
