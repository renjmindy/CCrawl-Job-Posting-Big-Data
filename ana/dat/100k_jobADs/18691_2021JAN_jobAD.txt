 Role overview 
 As a developer with Sable you will be part of an inclusive team, in the heart of Berlin that is building the bank of the future from the ground up. You will own software delivery from end-to-end, including conception, development and right the way to deploying to our Kubernetes cluster on Google cloud. 
 About Sable 
 How often do you get to build something that is truly world-changing? Sable is building the first truly global bank, with customers and accounts in every country, defeating bureaucracy to build an institution that supports the future of work, travel and study 
 We're a  Y Combinator  backed company, and live with Bank Accounts and Credit Cards in the US, and working on scaling to 100,000 customers in the US, and soon globally. We ship fast, and write good code - Sable is a place where you can learn and grow as a developer.

 You have used a typed language previously 
 You can show us a portfolio of apps, websites of features you have built
