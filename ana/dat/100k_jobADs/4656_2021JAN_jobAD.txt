 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 8/6 : 7/7 roster 
 
 
 FIFO ex Perth, Busselton, Albany, and Geraldton 
 
 
 Ongoing position 
 
 
 Opportunities for Full Time employment with WorkPac in the future 
 
 
 About our Client 
 Rio Tinto operates the world's largest integrated portfolio of iron ore assets. These assets include a world-class, integrated network of 15 iron ore mines, four port facilities, a 1,700 kilometre rail network and related infrastructure. Rio Tinto are expanding operations in the Pilbara to epic proportions while introducing next generation technologies to deliver greater efficiency, lower production costs and improved health, safety and environmental performance. 
 About the Role 
 Supporting the drill and blast team at Rio Tinto's Brockman 2 mine site, you will be required to prime and load blast holes, mark up drill patterns, check drilling depths as well as various other activities associated with the safe and secure blasting operations on site. 
 Blast crew operators are required to be physically fit and have a tolerance for working outdoors in harsh conditions. 
 As a key member of a high performing team, you will play an integral role in implementing our client's production requirements. 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 12 months + blast crew experience 
 
 
 WA issued Dangerous Goods Security Card 
 
 
 Manual C Class Australian issued Drivers Licence (minimum) 
 
 
 HR drivers licence (highly desirable)
