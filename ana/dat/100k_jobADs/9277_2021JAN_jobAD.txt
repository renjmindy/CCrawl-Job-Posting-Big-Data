 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently supporting a successful and well known brand who are recruiting for a qualified accountant (ACA,ACCA,CIMA) to join them as a Finance Analyst 
 The organisation have coped extremely well with the pandemic and offer great benefits as well as a culture that prioritises good work life balance 
 key Duties, Finance Analyst, Lichfield £40,000-£45,000 
 
 Assist in the measurement, reporting and analysis of business performance 
 Assist in identifying and analysing key business revenue and costs drivers 
 Assist in reporting and analysing progress against strategic priorities / performance of key programmes including expansion and optimisation of operations 
 Assist in the production of robust revenue and capital budgets, quarterly forecasts and 4 year plans 
 Support and challenge the business in driving performance. 
 Review and interpret the business performance with operational management 
 Produce robust investment appraisals, draft board reports and appropriate sensitivity analyses in line with the capital and revenue expenditure processes. 
 Assist in the production of post investment reviews and development of appropriate reports to highlight key learnings, agreed actions, and emerging trends. 
 Provide financial support to ad hoc projects as directed. 
 Any other ad-hoc duties as requested by the Finance leadership team 
 
 Skills & Experience, Finance Analyst, Lichfield, £40,000-£45,000 
 
 Qualified Accountant (ACA,ACCA,CIMA) 
 Organised and able to deliver to deadlines 
 Ability to work under own initiative and demonstrate proactive approach to issue resolution 
 Experience of budgeting and forecasting 
 Ability to challenge current practice and processes with an eye to continuous improvement and delivery of a world class finance infrastructure 
 Commercial and financial acumen, and able to develop an understanding of the key drivers within the group 
 
 For more information on this superb finance analyst opportunity please do get in touch
