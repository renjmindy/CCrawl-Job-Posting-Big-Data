 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for December 
 Posted on  January 2, 2010  by  caregiver 
 Last December 2009, the POEA or Philippine Overseas Employment Administration approved several job orders that presented employment opportunities to Filipino caregivers seeking employment in caregiver jobs abroad. These POEA approved caregiver jobs were approved for the different Philippine based recruitment agencies and has a total job order balance of 165 caregiver jobs abroad. Among the countries seeking to employ qualified applicants for caregiver jobs abroad are Israel, Kuwait, and the United States. Listed below are the POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for the month of December 2009. The list includes the vacancy, the country of destination, the name of the recruiting agency, the date the job order was approved, and the number of caregiver jobs applicants to be recruited for each POEA approved caregiver jobs abroad.
