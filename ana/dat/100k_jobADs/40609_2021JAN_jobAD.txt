 Interim Commercial Financial Controller 
 
 Location 
 
 Telford, Shropshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Contract 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £300 - £350 per day

 Consultant: 
 
 
 A leading FMCG business based in Telford is looking for a Commercial Financial Controller on an interim basis. To act as part of the senior leadership team and be responsible for the sites finances, acting as crucial support to the general manager, in day to day decision making and long term strategic decisions. 
 If you are a qualified Finance Manager or Controller and enjoy a mix of financial control and commercial analysis this could be the perfect fit for you. 
 Day rate: £300- £350 a day 
 The role: 
 
 To ensure all group reporting requirements are met 
 Leading a team of 9 with 2 direct reports 
 Present monthly business review packs to the board including financials, key site issues and forecasts 
 Heavy involvement with cost control and capex forecasting and multi site transfers 
 Cost control- Analysing business spends in all areas and implement changes accordingly 
 Site budget preparation 
 
 The candidate: 
 
 You will be a formally qualified ACA/ACCA/CIMA accountant and have previous experience working for a FMCG corporation 
 Have a background in project driven businesses 
 Can support business change and drive process improvement 
 
 To apply for the Interim Commercial Financial Controller post, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
