 Registered General Nurse or Registered Mental Health Nurse 
 An outstanding job opportunity has arisen for a committed RGN and RMN to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the Southport area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This care home has qualified and experienced care staffs that are on hand 24 hours a day to provide a high standard of care and they are a highly trained and dedicated team who specialise in care of older people 
 As a Nurse your key duties include: 
 
 
     Ensure compliance with all legal, regulatory and best practice guidelines - identify, investigate and resolve risks proactively
 
 
 
     Participate knowledgably and professionally to all inspection visits, ensuring your team is ready at all times to do the same
 
 
 
     Champion appropriate independence and personal choice; developing, reviewing and updating care plans to meet our residents physical, social and psychological needs
 
 
 
     Ensure you follow a rigorous monitoring and recording practice; documenting efficiently, and thoroughly all matters relating to residents and employees
 
 
 
     Understand the key targets of your host home and support the Home Manager in their achievement by promoting and practicing high standards of nursing care and ensuring staff and shifts are supervised in a smooth manner
 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Able to work under pressure and prioritise workload
 
 
 
     The ability to lead a team, analyse problems and propose solutions or improvements
 
 
 
     Able to communicate effectively and to develop professional relationships
 
 
 
     Understands and effectively uses IT and communication systems required for the role
 
 
 
     Experience of participating in quality and clinical governance programmes
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent salary of £16.75 per hour. 
 We currently have vacancies for both Full Time and Part Time shifts available for RGN's to work through Night Shifts and Part Time shifts available for RMN's. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits: ** 
 
 Comprehensive induction and on-going training 
 Career development opportunities 
 Excellent work environment 
 Free parking on site 
 Free uniform 
 5.6 weeks of paid annual leave 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse either RGN or RMN with a valid NMC Pin
