 I am currently working with an manufacturing business based in Monmouthshire. They are looking for a Management Accountant to join them on a 4 month contract. The role is site based and therefore must be commutable from site. The successful candidate will be able to provide accurate and timely monthly management accounts, budgets, and forecasts to a high level of accuracy. They are only looking at candidates who are immediately available. 
 The ideal candidate will have: 
 
 Recognised accountancy qualification (CIMA, ACA, ACCA or QBE) 
 At least 3 years experience in a Management Accountant role 
 Has worked in a reporting and analytical capacity 
 Able to demonstrate an understanding of working within an FMCG environment 
 Food industry experience desirable but not essential 
 Negotiation and influencing skills 
 Strong excel skills 
 Costing experience desirable 
 
 They are looking for someone to start within the next 2 weeks. 2 stage interview process, video call followed by on site meeting. Please click apply if this is something of interest. Thanks
