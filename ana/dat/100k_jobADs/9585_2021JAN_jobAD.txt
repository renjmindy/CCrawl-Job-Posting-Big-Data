 Trillium Health Partners (THP) is one of the largest community-based acute care facilities in Canada. Comprised of the Credit Valley Hospital, the Mississauga Hospital and the Queensway Health Centre, Trillium Health Partners serves the growing and diverse populations of Mississauga, West Toronto and surrounding communities and is a teaching hospital affiliated with the University of Toronto. 
 Our Mission:  A New Kind of Health Care for a Healthier Community 
 When we set out to build our vision and future, we connected with our community - patients, families, visitors, physicians, staff and volunteers. The foundational goals of our new strategic plan - quality, access and sustainability - anchor everything we do. Our mission for a new kind of health care is built on an inter-connected system of care organized around patients - inside and outside the hospital. 
 At THP, we are relentless in providing high quality, compassionate care to our communities and take great pride in fostering an inclusive and accessible environment and we are all accountable for contributing to a healthy, safe and respectful environment for healing and promoting excellence in patient care though advancing patient and staff safety. If you are passionate about what you do, motivated to improve the health of the community, committed to excellence, quality and patient safety we would like you to join our Better Together team! 
 Key Qualifications: 
Graduate from approved Health Information Management Program 
Certification with the Canadian College of Health Information Management 
Maintain active membership with CHIMA 
Experience in a Health Information Management is essential 
5 years or more experience inpatient coding 
Proficient in MED2020, MEDITECH 
Proficient in ICD-10-CA/CCI and CIHI Coding Standards an asset 
Working knowledge of Microsoft applications 
Working knowledge of Chartmaxx, ORM/SIS, SCM, EIM, OBTV preferred 
Excellent decision making, problem solving and analytical skills 
Attention to detail, organized and ability to concentrate in a fast pace environment 
Ability to work independently remotely from home 
 Position Summary: 
Be part of a fantastic virtual team that contributes to the success of the hospital through the Health System Funding Reform 
Share Trillium Health Partners values of compassion, excellence and courage 
Code patient records in accordance with CIHI, MoHLTC, CCO and internal requirements 
Code according to ICD10CA and CCI standards 
Abstract all required patient information using the WinRecs abstract 
Achieve and maintain expected quality standards 
Accountable for the accuracy of coded information, performing regular quality checks on, and making corrections to the abstracted information 
Consistently achieving the production quota on a monthly basis 
Participate in departmental committees (in person if required) 
Attend monthly coding meetings (in person if required) 
Maintain ongoing communication with staff coders 
Maintain the confidentiality and privacy of all patient information 
Work on and accrue continuous professional educational credits as required by CHIMA 
 Internal candidates who believe they possess the necessary qualifications and experience for this position and who have been in their current position for at least six (6) months are encouraged to apply.
