 Hot Damn! it's a Full-time job 
 Hedley & Bennett is looking for a Creative Operations Manager in Los Angeles, CA, USA. 
 About 
 At Hedley & Bennett, our mission is to inspire and empower creativity and confidence in the kitchen. We make superfunctional, pro-grade aprons and kitchen gear for people who love to cook â€” right here in Downtown Los Angeles, CA! We're a growing group of dedicated and creative people and we're committed to relentless and constant improvement, fostering a community of amazing makers, going above and beyond to serve our customers, and uniting form and function in our products. We are seeking a Creative Operations Manager to join our growing Apron Squad! 
 The Creative Operations Manager will manage the creative process from an operational perspective. They'll manage the briefing process, budget, and workflow for creative projects, including assigning projects, tasks, and deliverables to designers, video producers, and copywriters, whether in house or freelance. The Creative Operations Manager will oversee all stages of production from briefing to final design and ensure all deliverables are received on budget and on time. As a part of the setting up projects for success, The Creative Operations Manager will also write copy for Hedley & Bennett channels, including: website, social media, blog, advertisements, and emails. 
 Experience: 
Our ideal Creative Operations Manager will have at least 3 years' experience in project management and writing copy. 
Previous experience working with a project management tool 
Experience in advertising or brand marketing is a plus. 
Experience working with data-driven startups is a plus. 
Knowledge of food, cooking, and/or restaurant world is a plus. 
Please submit a digital portfolio or website with your resume. Additional documents/files may be submitted to careers at hedleyb dot com.
