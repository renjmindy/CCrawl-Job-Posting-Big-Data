 Civil Leading Hand 
 
 
 Immediate Start - January 2021 
 
 
 New Road Project 
 
 
 Leading Civil Company 
 
 
 About the Role 
 An immediate start opportunity has become available for an experienced and highly motivated Civil Leading Hand for a Road Construction project based in Brisbane's Northside in early January 2021. The role will include managing a small crew in in the delivery of new pipe infrastructure and road infrastructure. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Assisting Project Supervisor in delegation and management of works 
 
 
 Ensuring safety standards are adhered to across staff and subcontractors 
 
 
 Ability to read and interpret plans and follow guidance from the onsite Supervisor 
 
 
 Ability to work independently with a proven track record of operating safely 
 
 
 Strong team player who supports our client's operations 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Proven experience and understanding of a construction site are essential 
 
 
 Must be physically fit for all Labour duties 
 
 
 Must have previous experience in drainage and road construction 
 
 
 Must have previous experience working as a leading hand 
 
 
 Holding operating tickets is preferred. 
 
 
 High standard for safety 
 
 
 Must be reliable and available for ongoing work 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 White Card
