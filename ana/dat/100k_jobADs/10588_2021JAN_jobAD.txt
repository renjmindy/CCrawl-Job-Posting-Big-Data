 Azimo improves the lives of millions of people around the world by building better ways to transfer money. It’s our mission to develop market leading technology that makes financial services affordable and available to everyone. 
 We are looking for an ambitious and experienced Product Designer with an option to work from a London office or remotely anywhere in the EU (regular travel to London required). 
 Reporting directly to the Head of Design, you'll be part of a cross functional team working closely with product managers, researchers, engineers and copywriters. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 You’ll identify problems, take complex issues, and create solutions that improve the user experience and impact key business metrics 
 You’ll develop a deep understanding of our customers, take a data driven approach to product design, and combine this with your strong design aesthetic to deliver truly impactful solutions 
 
 This is a hands on opportunity where you can make a big difference to the lives of millions of people around the world. 
 Requirements 
 
 4 years+ of product design experience 
 A portfolio featuring examples of your recent product design work 
 Experience with designing for iOS, Android and Web 
 The ability to communicate design goals and feedback clearly 
 The ability to understand the engineering constraints in your design process 
 Passion for good design and being able to understand what makes design good 
 Passion for problem solving and the ability to identify a problem based on qualitative, quantitative and behavioural data 
 Experience with user testing and a good understanding of human behavior 
 A keen eye for details with the ability to work with engineers to ship great products 
 The ability to hustle across an organisation to deliver the most value to our customers and the business 
 Prototyping and motion design experience will be beneficial 
 
 Benefits 
 
 Competitive salary, discretionary bonus and share options for top performers 
 Choice of new MacBook Pro 13” or 15” 
 26 paid holidays 
 Private Health Insurance 
 EAP support 
 Restaurant catered team lunch 
 Regular social activities, including company offsites 
 Work in a truly special team – we believe in what we do! 
 Regular travel to Poland and occasional travel to other European cities
