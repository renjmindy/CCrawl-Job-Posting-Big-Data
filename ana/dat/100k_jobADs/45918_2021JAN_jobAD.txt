 Care Giving Jobs Qatar 
 Posted on  August 31, 2011  by  caregiver 
 Jovineria International Manpower Services, a POEA accredited recruitment agency located in Ermita in Manila, is currently accepting applications for their POEA approved  Care Giving Jobs Qatar .  This care giving jobs Qatar had its Job Order approved by the POEA last August 15, 2011 allowing the recruiting agency to recruit 5 care givers who are qualified to the said care giving job vacancies. 
 To apply for the said Care Giving Jobs Qatar, proceed as soon as possible to the office of Jovineria International Manpower Services and submit your application together with your updated resume or you may send your resume via Email to manpower@jovineria.com. For further details or questions regarding the said care giving jobs Qatar, contact Jovineria International Manpower Services thru their landline numbers or email addresses listed below.
