 Digital Marketing Manager 
 A fantastic opportunity for a Digital Marketing Manager to join a growing high profile B2C organisation paying £40-45k based in North Birmingham. 
 What you'll do… 
 
 Digital campaign strategy, planning and delivery across all major channels including paid and earned media, SEO, social and web. 
 Work with 3rd parties, stakeholders and external digital agencies. 
 Raising brand awareness across the group 
 Manage the web development projects 
 Manage the CRM alongside client and customer data 
 Developing and maintaining digital touchpoints, usability and accessibility 
 Split testing and conversion rate optimisation 
 Reporting on and understanding metrics using a variety of analytics tools such as GA 
 Manage the digital budget 
 Implement marketing automation and targeted email workflows 
 
 What you'll have… 
 
 3+ years B2C digital campaign strategy & delivery experience including: 
 Social, Paid Search & SEO 
 Advanced Analytics & Insight 
 CRM & Web Management 
 Email Marketing 
 
 If you love working within a creative and inspiring environment, then this could be exactly the role that you are looking for. 
 To apply you must be able to demonstrate that you possess the right digital skills (as above), passion, integrity, trust and be an effective team player. 
 If you want to become part of an organisation that seeks and values your input, ideas and contribution, then we would love to hear from you. 
 Digital Marketing Manager. North Birmingham, £40-45k 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
