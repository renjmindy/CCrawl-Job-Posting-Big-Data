 Dishpatch partners with the country's best restaurants to help them produce and sell finish-at-home meal kits to every home in the UK.  We're building a new category for eating-at-home based around amazing food experiences. 
 Launched in July 2020 we're already working with some of the UK's leading restaurants (Angela Hartnett, Farang, Bubala, Andrew Edmunds) and have been featured in the Guardian, Telegraph, Forbes and the Evening Standard.  We're backed by early investors and operators of Farfetch, Trouva, Spotify and Songkick and growing fast. 
 We're looking for an entrepreneurially-minded Operations Manager to join our small but growing team.  You'll be responsible for building our Supply Chain function from scratch, and managing our Supply Chain operations.  Operations are an integral part of what we do and you'll play an important role in enabling growth. 
 The role is perfect for someone with e-commerce or FMCG supply chain experience or a Consulting background, looking for a fast-paced, hands-on startup role.  You'll be reporting directly into the CEO & Co-Founder (ex-BCG). 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Build our packaging Supply Chain function, from scratch 
 Identify, negotiate with and select our packaging suppliers 
 Forecast demand, place orders and ensure stock availability 
 Manage packaging costs 
 Develop KPIs and reporting 
 Building a repeatable and scalable process for all of the above 
 
 Requirements 
 
 3-5+ years of experience in Supply Chain or Ops role at an e-commerce or FMCG company 
 OR 3-5+ years of Top-Tier consulting experience 
 Structured thinker and problem solver with an ability to create scalable processes 
 Highly analytical and comfortable with numbers 
 Self-starting do-er, comfortable working in a fast-paced environment 
 Extremely organised with excellent attention to detail 
 Nice to have: experience and knowledge of packaging supply chain 
 
 Notes: 
 
 Equity options will be part of the offer 
 Our team is based in London, with flexible working (especially during current times)
