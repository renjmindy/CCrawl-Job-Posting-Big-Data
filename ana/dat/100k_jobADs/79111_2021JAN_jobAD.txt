 General Ledger / Financial Accountant 
 
 Location 
 
 Bracknell, Berkshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Temporary 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £12 - £16 per hour + 25 days hols + bens 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Fiona Price 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 fiona.price@gleeson-rg.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 FRP18368_1578484883 
 
 Published: 
 
 about 1 year ago 
 
 Duration: 
 
 3 months 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-02-07 
 
 Startdate: 
 
 ASAP 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 This is a very interesting, and superb opportunity to work for a large global organisation that is one of the world leaders within their industry. 
 Initially being hired on a 3 month basis, your General Ledger Specialist role will sit within the Shared Service Centre, who operate across 80 countries globally. 
 You will either by Qualified by experience, or Part Qualified, or indeed a stop-studier, but offering a wealth of experience within the General Ledger or Balance Sheet field. 
 The successful candidate within the General Ledger Specialist role will be responsible for: 
 
 Responsible for being the GL lead for sharing for best-practice and leading by example on all financial accounting duties 
 Active involvement across journal preparation, fixed assets, prepayments and accruals 
 Reviewing journal entries and actioning a list of monthly journal entries 
 Active involvement with balance sheet reconciliations, as well as explaining why balances exist in GL accounts and how they are calculated 
 Completing all month-end tasks 
 Providing analytical review and data analysis weekly and monthly 
 Preparing statutory consolidation packs 
 Ad-hoc projects 
 Daily use of SAP and Advanced Excel (full training will be given) 
 
 This is a unique role, based for our well-established client, based in Bracknell, who can offer plenty of free parking, on-site facilities and an opportunity to work for a high profile, highly respected business. 
 Ideally you will be available to start this role at short notice and be able to commit for 3 months with a view of long-term extensions. 
Please get in touch for further information. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
