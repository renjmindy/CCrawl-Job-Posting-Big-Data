 Our client is a global **** network, created to 'change the black narrative' by bringing the black professional global community together to solve problems through economic empowerment and connectivity. They are excellently positioned to bridge the gap between the black professional community and corporations. Today they have over 50,000 members on their network, launched a mobile and web platform, run offline and online events to up-skill the community, have a job board and have worked with 60+ corporate clients including Facebook and Accenture. They are currently looking for other passionate and determined team members to join them. 
 THE ROLE 
 The candidate will be a creative and engaging designer, with a proven track record in both video and graphics design, alongside experience in digital channels and social media platforms. The successful candidate will be using a variety of design applications and media, which will produce accurate, corporate, high-quality deliverables for our client. 
 Job Responsibilities: 
 
 Work on a range of design pieces, from social media posts to packaging design and full-scale branding projects 
 Select colors, fonts, photographs, layouts and other design elements to communicate creative concepts 
 Design visual concepts using graphic design tools including design software 
 Explore and conceptualize design solutions, work independently to execute work from scratch to delivery based on given creative briefs 
 Design and create campaigns for upcoming events and promotions 
 Provide and edit video content for social media and other avenues that will enhance the brand 
 Maintain up-to-date insight of trends and styles in graphics, motion and video design 
 Translate client needs and branding strategies into design strategies 
 Ensure compliance with required proposal deliverables 
 Create and manage a range of document and presentation templates 
 Work in a deadline driven environment without compromising artistic creativity 
 
 Key requirements 
 
 Excellent IT skills, and the ability to work under pressure to tight deadlines 
 3-5 years' proven experience in graphic design, video production & editing 
 An understanding of the latest trends and their role within a the BYP Network 
 Professional approach and the capacity to multitask 
 Excellent communication and presentation skills and the ability to explain complex technical terms in everyday language 
 Solid experience with digital technology and editing software packages 
 Demonstrable video editing ability with a strong portfolio 
 Creative mind and storytelling skills 
 
 Benefits 
 
 Flexible working pattern 
 The opportunity to work for a dynamic, innovative and expanding company 
 Full Induction 
 Team socials 
 
 Additional Application Instructions 
 Please include your CV and portfolio detailing your experience and skills.
