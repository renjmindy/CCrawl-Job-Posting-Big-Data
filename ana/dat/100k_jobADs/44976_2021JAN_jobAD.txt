 LO Reach Operator 
 Job description 
 Role Highlights 
 
 
 $31.00 per hour base + penalties 
 
 
 Monday - Friday with full-time hours 
 
 
 Warehouse located in Dandenong South 
 
 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are seeking a self-motivated and enthusiastic Warehouse Assistant/Storeman for our clients Headquarters and Warehouse in Dandenong South. This is a great opportunity to grow within their team in a busy and hands on environment. 
 This role is casual but for the right candidate there is the view for a permanent position. 
 You will be working Monday to Friday day shift with full-time hours and occasional weekend work. 
 The pay rate is $31.00 per hour base rate with penalties. 
 You will be required to assist the warehouse team in all aspects of receiving and delivering goods. You will be responsible for stock management and warehouse inventory. Heavy lifting and manual handling of stock is a day to day task and essential in this position. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Unloading and loading trucks 
 
 
 Showroom transition 
 
 
 Stock and inventory rotation 
 
 
 Forklift/Order Picker driving 
 
 
 Unloading containers 
 
 
 Occasional truck/van driving to deliver goods 
 
 
 Receiving & dispatching of goods 
 
 
 Customer service 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Forklift & LO Ticket (mandatory) 
 
 
 MR Drivers Licence (preferred) 
 
 
 Physically fit as heavy lifting involved 
 
 
 Prior experience in warehousing/forklift role 
 
 
 Ability to work independently and within a team 
 
 
 Experience with Warehouse Management System 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
