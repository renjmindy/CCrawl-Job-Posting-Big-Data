 Theatre Endoscopy Nurse 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Theatre Endoscopy Nurse to work in an exceptional hospital based in the Croydon, South London area. 
 As a Theatre Endoscopy Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This hospital offers services to the local community such as Outpatient Consultation, Day Case Surgery, Pharmaceutical Services and Diagnostics - Imaging and Endoscopy 
 As a Theatre Endoscopy Nurse your key duties include: 
 
 
     Support the Theatre Manager providing visible excellent patient care
 
 
 
     Work to communicate with Consultants building strong effective relations moving forward
 
 
 
     Assist the Unit in complying with all statutory and company quality standards
 
 
 
     Assist clinically based projects in the development of the theatre services and specialities
 
 
 
 
 An ideal Theatre Endoscopy Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     A strong track record in working well within a team
 
 
 
     Experience with JAG accreditation process required
 
 
 
     Experience and confidence working under their own initiative
 
 
 
     Comprehensive understanding of relevant legal and professional care/practice standards 
 
 
 
     Excellent communication and influencing skills
 
 
 
 
 **The successful Theatre Endoscopy Nurse will receive an excellent salary from £32,000 to £33,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     25 days Annual Leave + Bank Holidays
 
 
 
     Private Healthcare
 
 
 
     Contributory Pension Scheme
 
 
 
     Life Assurance
 
 
 
     On-going Training and Development
 
 
 
     Free off-site parking
 
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a valid NMC Pin
