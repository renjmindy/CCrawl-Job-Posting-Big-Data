 My client, a market leading retail business, is looking to recruit a Group SEO Manager. 
 My client, a well established business and instantly recognisable brand, is undergoing a sustained period of digital growth. This role will play a key role in the marketing department, not just leading to SEO function but also in working collaboratively with other channels. 
 The Group SEO Manager position will encompass: 
 
 Formulating and delivering the Group SEO strategy across planning, technical, content, offsite & promotion 
 Using search insight to help drive decision-making and innovation 
 Working collaboratively and cross functionally with Digital, Trading and external agencies 
 Leading the SEO team 
 
 The ideal candidate for this role will possess the following skills/expertise: 
 
 SEO thought leader with recent demonstrable track record delivering SEO success 
 Clear communication skills, strong man-management and stakeholder management skills 
 Passion for all things SEO, digital and ecommerce 
 
 Highly competitve package. 
 This role is West Midlands based - currently remote - significant flexibility will be considered but you must be able to attend the office every week.
