 WP Job Manager is a light and portable extension with regard to including job-board usefulness to your WordPress web-site. Being shortcode primarily based, it could work together with virtually any template and it is really very simple to operate. 
 What is included? 
Just about all core add-ons are contained: 
 Resume Manager 
Job Alerts 
Indeed Integration 
Job Tags 
WC Paid Listings 
Simple Paid Listings 
Application Deadline 
Bookmarks 
Apply with LinkedIn 
Apply with XING 
Job Applications 
Embeddable Job Widget 
CareerBuilder Integration
