 Management Accounting Manager - Birmingham - 45 - 50k 
 Gleeson are working with a retail organisation that operate from hundreds of sites across the UK and offer brands that are instantly recognisable. 
 The Management Accounting Manager aim is to deliver timely and accurate monthly management accounts, lead a small team and provide detailed analysis and support to the business. The role will report into the Head of Finance who is looking for an Accountant who has a wealth of month end, budgeting and forecasting experience, and can also delegate work to the junior members In the team and ensure they are being challenged and developed. 
 The business has gone through significant change recently which has resulted in a number of internal promotions due to individuals going "above and beyond". The Finance Director and Head of Finance see this role as a unique development opportunity for a strong Management Accountant to take the next step on the ladder, and are willing to provide the guidance and support needed to ensure it is a success. 
 The right person for the role will need to be outgoing and confident, with experience in business partnering, preparing month end and Qualified ACA, ACCA or CIMA. Managerial experience is essential and previous experience in a multisite environment would be an advantage. 
 The role is based on the outskirts of Central Birmingham with free parking and easily accessible from the motorway.
