 Job Description 
 Are you a registered Midwife? 
 Do you dream of living and working in New Zealand and enjoying a great work-life balance? 
 We are welcoming applications from suitably qualified and experienced Midwives to work in a variety of settings throughout New Zealand including Delivery Suites, Maternity Wards, and Community Services. 
 To apply, you will need: 
 
 Relevant Midwifery qualification 
 A minimum of 2 years’ experience as a registered Midwife 
 To be registered or eligible for registration with the Midwifery Council of New Zealand* 
 Fluency and comprehension in English to be able to communicate effectively (written and verbal) with patients, colleagues and other health professionals 
 Clinical experience in the planning, implementation, and evaluation of care for women and babies 
 
 *Guidance with your NZ Registration is available from our team.
