 Operator Loading Unit (Critical Equipment) Level 3 
 Job description 
 
 
 $53.79/ hour 
 
 
 BIBO - Mackay 
 
 
 6 Months with potential for ongoing 
 
 
 ABOUT OUR CLIENT 
 Our client is Australia's largest coal miner and exporter, the world's largest supplier to the sea borne coking coal market and largest coal producer and supplier of metallurgical coal. The client has several mine sites across the Bowen Basin consisting primarily of open cut mines 
 ABOUT THE ROLE 
 Seeking experienced Digger/Dozer Operators to work at a mine located near Nebo for a 6 month role with potential for ongoing work. 
 DUTIES & RESPONSBILBITIES 
 
 
 Operate Excavator/ Dozer Safely 
 
 
 Conduct Pre-Start Checks on Machinery 
 
 
 SKILLS & EXPERIENCE 
 
 
 Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Standard 11/ Equivalent 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 CAT D11 Dozer 
 
 
 1200 Tonne Digger 
 
 
 Dozer Push Experience 
 CULTURE & BENEFITS 
 
 
 4/5,5/4 Roster 
 
 
 Potential for ongoing work 
 
 
 Camp & Meals Supplied 
 
 
 Super Paid on all Rostered Hours 
 
 
 BIBO - Mackay
