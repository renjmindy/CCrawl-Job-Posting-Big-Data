 Job Description 
 Sales Associates act as a Halloween City representative to provide quality customer service. Each Sales Associate is to follow the basic policies and procedures that Halloween City has established in order to achieve our overall goals. All sales associates may be cross-trained as a cashier. 
 Job Responsibilities: 
 
 Acknowledges all customers through a greeting, smile and/or eye contact regardless of the task at hand 
 Maintains store zone assigned by manager on duty 
 Delivers exceptional customer service through personal contact with customers 
 Utilizes suggestive selling to help meet/exceed daily sales goals and enhance customer's shopping experience 
 Processes incoming freight as directed 
 Ensures the continued integrity of store merchandising including flow-o-grams, plan-o-grams and end caps 
 Maintains store appearance through assigned cleaning tasks and daily fronting/straightening/flexing of merchandise 
 Communicates inventory and replenishment needs to management team 
 Supports corporate sales initiatives such as 'Item of the Day' and contests 
 Complies with corporate Loss Prevention and Human Resources policies and procedures 
 Alerts manager on duty to possible shoplifters, store security and loss prevention concerns 
 Identifies and corrects store safety concerns immediately to prevent accidents 
 Completes assigned Halloween training program 
 Ensures the execution of company data integrity process 
 Cashier Responsibilities
 
 Operates the cash registers in accordance with company policies and procedures 
 Assumes responsibility for all transactions and paperwork associated with assigned register 
 Inspects packaged merchandise to ensure proper contents 
 Monitors flow of customer traffic, requesting additional cashiers or assistance when needed 
 Displays and maintains checkout merchandising 
 Supports corporate marketing initiatives at point of sale 
 Maintains an organized and clean appearance behind checkout area by stocking register supplies such as bags, register paper, etc. 
 Communicates needs for small bills and change to manager on duty with appropriate notice 
 Alerts manager on duty of any potential loss prevention concerns such as counterfeit money, quick change artists, etc. 
 Ensures that our customers complete their shopping experience with a positive image of Halloween City, by bagging their items appropriately and thanking them for shopping at Halloween City 
 
 
 
 This job description is not all-inclusive. Additional responsibilities will be determined by the management team, as dictated by store needs. 
 Job Requirements 
 Physical Requirements 
 
 Lift, push, and pull 50 lbs. 
 Frequent kneeling, bending and stooping 
 Climb ladders 
 Standing throughout entire shift
