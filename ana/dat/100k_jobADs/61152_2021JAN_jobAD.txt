 Description: 
 Our client has an immediate need for an .Net Developer; the successful candidate will be part of a team responsible for the design, development, testing and implementation of digital solutions and process control systems. 
 Requirements  : 
 
 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or Computer Engineering 
 Software Engineer / Developer with minimum of 1-2 years of experience developing in Java, C, C#, Visual Studio, R, and/or Python 
 Experience with both Linux and Windows environments 
 Development experience on PTC Thingworx IoT platform plus 
 Experience with Tableau a plus 
 Experience with SQL and Oracle a plus 
 Experience with SCADA and HMI programming a plus 
 Experience in a manufacturing environment desired 
 
 Additional Responsibilities: 
 
 Participate in, and / or lead high impact process control projects that provide financial benefits and result in improvements to safety, quality, and / or production yield 
 Work closely with the operations teams to understand requirements and identify improvement opportunities; translate into project or minor enhancement specifications, complete with work estimates and forecasted benefits 
 Design and maintain highly available and error resistant process control applications and systems 
 Play key role in the new design and maintenance of modern process control supervisory systems

 About ConsultUSA 
 Founded in 1997, ConsultUSA specializes in Project Management and Business Analysis focused IT positions within various industries. We also offer challenging technical engagements in Application Development, Data Warehouse/BI, QA, & Network Engineering. We are partnered with solutions company PMCentersUSA, a world-class consulting/training organization distinguished as a PMI® award winner and federal GSA-approved supplier.
