 About Our Client 
 Our client is privately owned Australia company and has become an industry leader in manufacturing and service of Electric Overhead travelling cranes in the Australian market. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking Dust Crew/Street Sweepers for a large mining company based in the Gulf of Carpentaria. You will be working a 7/7 Roster (rotating Day & night shift) FIFO Darwin only.

 Street Sweeping
