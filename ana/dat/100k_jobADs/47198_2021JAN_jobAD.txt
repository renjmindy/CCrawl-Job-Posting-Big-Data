 WHY DO WE SHOW EXPIRED LISTINGS? 
 Retirement Community Managers  - Resort Lifestyle Communities (RLC) is quickly becoming the preferred place to work in the senior housing industry. Our high standards of service, integrity and respect for our residents and employees define who we are. 
 As Community Mangers of RLC you will work and live in a beautiful, state-of-the-art retirement community and enjoy the support of a dedicated Home Office staff. If you have a passion for working with a senior population and providing exceptional customer service and understand the dedication required to successfully manage a retirement community and grow the business we would like to talk with you! 
 RLC develops, builds and manages independent living (age 55 and older) retirement communities. We currently have 12 communities in seven states and we are poised for significant growth. Our passion is providing a luxurious living environment for our residents while also serving delicious, well balanced meals, lively activities and exceptional customer service. We hire couples only to live on-site and serve as Resident Managers for the community. The scope of this position includes oversight and accountability for resident satisfaction, sales, food service, housekeeping, activities, maintenance, hiring, training and management of staff, and on-budget operation. Strong management and organizational skills are critical. 
 Benefits include health, dental, vision and life insurance, 401(k) Plan, paid time off (PTO), bonus and profit sharing opportunities.
