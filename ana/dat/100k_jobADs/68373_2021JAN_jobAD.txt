 Payroll Officer 
 We have an excellent opportunity for a sole Payroll Officer to join a fabulous company based in Birmingham to carry out their payroll for over 140 members of staff. 
 3 days a week, flexible on days £35,000 (Pro-rata) 
 Payroll Manager's Core Duties 
 
 Process and update payroll for over 140 staff members 
 Process all furlough payments and reconcile the monthly CJRS claim ensuring rebate before the end of that calendar month 
 Process and submit monthly payroll including statutory year end returns, P11Ds, P60's 
 Respond to payroll queries in a timely and professional manner 
 Process all RTI and Payroll tax forms 
 Process pension payments 
 
 Payroll Manager's Skills required 
 
 Experience carrying out pension and RTI processes 
 Furlough payment experience 
 At least 1 year's experience carrying out all aspects of payroll 
 Will need own transport as office is not located near any public transport routes 
 Any accountancy or payroll 
 Excellent communications skills
