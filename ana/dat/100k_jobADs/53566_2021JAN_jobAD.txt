 Director of Finance 
 Lannick 
 __Toronto, ON 
 Apply Now 
 About the Company 
 Our client is a national financial services organization. Built on a strong foundation and founded by local entrepreneurs with a proven track record, our client is poised for significant growth. 
 About the Opportunity 
 Lead and manage the Financial Planning & Analysis function, including developing forecast/plan (for product profitability, annual budget, optimization of operating expenses, etc.) and monitoring of these forecast/plans against the actuals. This will involve working closely with the senior management team across the firm 
Manage the treasury functions, including cash management for multiple entities (i.e. manage and monitor short-term as well as medium-term cash flow forecasts to actual), monitor capital requirements across entities and assist in the development and implementation of financial and tax strategies 
Manage securitization programmes, including facility renewals, monthly reporting, tranche planning as well as tranche closings. Proactively monitor securitization covenants and highlight emerging trends on a timely basis 
Act as Finance lead on business development projects and strategic. This will include: 
working with cross-functional teams to identify issues (owned by Finance) and recommend solutions for a successful implementation; 
assessing the economic outcome/impact of business opportunities and/or strategic initiatives by developing suitable financial models 
For public company reporting, act as point of contact for investor relations 
Work closely with Controller to review quarterly and annual financial statements and take the lead on drafting MD&A and press releases 
Work closely with external legal counsel to manage all aspects of public company related filings 
Prepare for and manage quarterly board and annual shareholders’ meetings 
Lead any capital raises or option grants/exercises 
Develop and manage key relationships with internal and external stakeholders 
Manage Cash Operations team and identify opportunities for process improvements or efficiencies to continuously improve on manual nature of team’s responsibilities and use of new features and services offered by the servicing platforms 
Lead in the development, implementation, monitoring and evaluation of effective and efficient internal controls and relevant policies for key processes 
Provide leadership/supervision to direct reports including assigning/reviewing work, performance assessments and providing clear expectations and meaningful Attract, retain and engage a high performing team with an expandable capacity to scale with the growth of the business. Act as a coach and facilitator to create and sustain a cooperative work environment to achieve set targets 
 About You 
 University Undergraduate degree with designation in Business, Commerce or equivalent; plus, a professional accounting designation (CPA) 
A minimum of 7+ years of relevant experience in Financial Services industry 
Track record of innovating and bringing structure and order to a complex process and/or issue 
Application of legal agreements including reviewing and contributing sections to these documents 
Investment/credit analysis; able to assess opportunity and risk, and add value to the deal structure and due diligence 
Proven analytical, financial modeling, accounting, and problem-solving skills 
Good knowledge of consumer finance products 
Excellent interpersonal, presentation and verbal/written communication skills 
Advanced MS Excel and database skills, including building complex multi-scenario financial models 
Thrives under continually changing needs, manage challenging situations by exhibiting grace under pressure and a clear ability to focus on results 
Quickly able to understand the fundamentals of what drives the business; can hit the ground running and identify opportunities for growth and increased synergies within the organization 
Effective problem-solving May be faced with situations where the answers are not clear. Must be creative and flexible in thinking through alternatives and able to contribute to effective business decisions and strategy 
Excellent organizational skills; can balance several projects concurrently; highly effective at time management and setting priorities 
A collaborator, able to build and develop relationships with key internal and external stakeholders 
Demonstrated commitment to high quality work; able to anticipate issues/problems and opportunities and provide pragmatic solutions 
High integrity and ethical standards combined with a focus on business controls 
A passionate desire to lead, drive and effect change; proven ability to lead and develop high performing teams 
 Salary Range 
 $120,000 - $130,000/year
