 Caregivers Job Abroad 
 Posted on  September 25, 2010  by  caregiver 
 A POEA accredited recruitment agency, is currently inviting applicants for its POEA approved  Caregivers Job Abroad . These caregivers job abroad is for the holy land of Israel and was approved for recruitment by the POEA last September 9, 2010 for a recruitment agency based in Makati City. With the said approval, the agency is allowed to recruit 25 applicants for placement in caregivers job abroad. 
 If you are interested to apply for the said caregivers job abroad, submit your updated resume and application directly to the office of the recruiting agency. For further details or inquiries regarding the said caregivers job abroad, you can dial the agency’s landline numbers indicated below.

Salcedo Village, Makati
