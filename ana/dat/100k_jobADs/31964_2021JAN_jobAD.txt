 Regional Finance Business Partner in Birmingham - 60 - 70k + Bonus 
 Are you a Commercially minded Accountant who enjoys partnering with internal and external partners, driving change and providing financial insight? 
 This role will see you working for a growing service provider and being the face of finance across the South West region. The Regional Finance Business Partner will be Birmingham based but will need to travel into Bristol (1/2 days a week) and will primarily focus on supporting the business with financial insight and detailed analysis, that will invariably support decision making and drive performance directly associated to the Operational teams. 
 This role would therefore be perfect for an individual who is looking to spend more time in the business, visiting different sites and pushing business performance though detailed analysis. An inquisitive mindset will be essential due to the need to improve processes and offer cost savings solutions. 
 The role is also offering flexible working with 2/3 days in the office or on the road with 2 days home based. 
 The organisation is well known for progressing talented individuals and are looking for an Accountant who is constantly looking to improve and is hungry to move into a leadership role in the future. 
 Some of the Regional Finance Business Partner in Birmingham responsibilities will include: 
 
 Business partner the Operational team, analysing and reporting on financial performance 
 Provide financial support, analysis and challenge to the business including financial performance reports and decision support analysis 
 To assist in the production of the company's management accounts to agreed deadlines 
 Investigate labour costs and provide recommendations on how to improve/reduce costs 
 Support Ops teams with Budgeting and Forecasting 
 To investigate and report on accounting issues identified during the preparation of the management accounts 
 To identify and implement improvements to processes and controls 
 To prepare and review the financial statements / reports for the relevant business area in a timely and accurate manner 
 To maintain the integrity of the businesses accounting transactions and ensure anomalies / variances are resolved with relevant parties.
