 Consultant: 
 
 
 I am currently working exclusively with a business with a nationwide footprint that are involved within high volume manufacturing,who are currently looking for an Finance Manager based in their Wolverhampton office. 
 This is a genuinely exciting opportunity for someone seeking the next step in their career. With an industry leading market share and a strong customer base, you will be responsible for internal and external stakeholder management, along with monitoring CAPEX and reconciling and monitoring stock within the division, alongside wider group operations, they company is looking for a strong personality to be the right-hand person of the Financial Controller. 
 The business itself has grown organically and through acquisitions, they are keen to add to their strong and friendly working environment with this role. 
 The position will be heavily focused on Business Partnering with other divisions of the group and working in a heavily commercial environment. The successful candidate will need strong technical ability along with solid relationship building skills. 
 This position will be focused around both the day-to-day financial operations, but also much larger group projects. You will ideally be ACCA, ACA or CIMA qualified and have the gravitas to implement change and nurture and develop relationships internally as well as externally. 
 For the successful Finance Manager, they are looking to pay between £40,000 to £50,000 DOE . 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
