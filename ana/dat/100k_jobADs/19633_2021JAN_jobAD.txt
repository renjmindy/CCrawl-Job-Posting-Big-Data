 Administration Executive (Part Time) at Megamac 
 **The Company  ** 
 Megamac is a leading, UK-based company specialising in all things Apple. We are an e-commerce business that sells consumer electronics and differentiate ourselves by focusing on user centric design and the latest technology. 
 We work from a vibrant co-working space with excellent transport links near Old Street in Central London. The office is COVID secure, has a gym and showers, bike storage and free refreshments. Typical working hours are 9am - 5pm. 
 Our culture is informal, hard-working and supportive. Our approach is fast paced, and we are constantly challenging, testing and adapting the way we do things. 
 We're proud of what we've built so far and are always looking for team players who can add real value to the business. 
 **Role Summary  ** 
 As part of our expansion Megamac is seeking a part-time Administration Executive to support with daily business administration and bookeeping. This is a multifaceted position that will give the right candidate exposure to different areas of the business, including Operations, Finance, Technology and Administration. The ideal person will have strong computer based skills and a positive, can-do attitude. 
 This is a rare opportunity to learn valuable business skills and practises, working directly with the company Founder, in a scaling, start-up environment. 
 We are paying the London living wage, which is currently £10.75/hr. 
 The role is offered on a flexible part-time basis (1 days minimum - 2 days maximum per week) and would be well suited to a Masters degree or part time student. 
 Roles & Responsibilities 
 The Administration Executive will support with various areas of the business, including: 
 
 Finance/Bookkeeping - Adding bills, invoices, reconciling payments, chasing invoices 
 Administration - Various adminstration tasks such as checking the goods our warehouse receives matches the supplier invoice, requesting and tracking credit notes and returns to our suppliers, etc 
 
 The Ideal Candidate 
 
 Recently graduated or current masters/undergraduate student 
 Knowlege of Xero, bookkeeping, reconciling 
 Strong organisational skills and excellent attention to detail 
 Excellent computer based skills 
 Fluent in English, with strong written and verbal communication skills 
 Analytical and comfortable with numbers and data 
 Able to prioritise and manage multiple tasks 
 Positive, can-do attitude 
 Pro-active, a fast learner and not afraid to try new things 
 Passionate about new ideas, with a keen interest in culture and technology 
 Interest in Apple and Apple products 
 Comfortable working at a fast pace 
 Adaptable and open to a start up working environment 
 
 Benefits 
 
 Amazing work space in Central London 
 Access to wellness programs and networking events 
 Free coffee, tea and snacks! 
 27" iMac Computer to work on 
 28 Days Holiday (pro-rated) 
 Workplace pension scheme 
 
 Published at 6 Jan 2021 - Expires on 29 Jan 2021
