 Consultant: 
 
 
 Our client a food manufacturer based near Ludlow, is looking for a Financial Controller to join them on a 12-18 month FTC. As the Financial Controller you will report into the Finance Director and also have 2 direct reports. 
 Key responsibilities: 
 
 To Business Partner the Management Team and Commercial team challenging where necessary their current processes, margin analysis and product costings 
 To produce the internal monthly management accounts and sales reports to include supporting the Group Finance Director with the preparation of the internal Board pack. 
 To effectively manage contracts (e.g facilities, lease vehicles) to ensure good value for money and sound business decisions. 
 To oversee the annual external audit and statutory accounts production 
 To mentor and coach other members of the team 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 Qualified ACA/ACCA/CIMA or equivalent 
 Strong analytical skills and effective decision making 
 Ideally knowledge of SAP 
 Ideally manufacturing industry background 
 
 If you are interested in this role please either contact Emily at Gleeson or click apply and I will be in touch
