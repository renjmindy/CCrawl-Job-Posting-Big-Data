 GRG Executive Search has been retained by a private equity backed Care Group to recruit a new Chief Financial Officer. Adopting a buy and build model with an ambitious grow and sell strategy our client is seeking to a hire a CFO with a strong track record of delivering on strategic growth agendas. Working alongside a highly successful senior management and NED team the successful CFO will play a pivotal role developing and driving the funding strategy of the business which will enable scale at pace. The CFO will work with the Executive Board to design overall business strategy including M&A activity and will lead accounting, forecasting, modelling, management information and the building/maintaining of a robust financial control framework. Management of external relationships with auditors, bankers, lawyers and investors as well as the management of key non-finance and other operational teams will also form a significant part of the role. The successful candidate will be an accomplished CFO with the ability to drive strategy alongside the leadership of finance, IT, legal and facilities. Previous exposure to an investor backed environment and prior successful exit experience would be a distinct advantage. Credibility, gravitas, emotional intelligence, integrity and passion are all key attributes the successful candidate will possess. For an initial conversation please contact for a confidential chat.
