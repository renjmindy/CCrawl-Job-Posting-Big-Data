 Product 
 Our product and e-commerce team innovates and pushes the industry forward every day at Workhuman. 
 
 Join one of Workhuman’s most unique departments. We are business-minded dreamers, designers, innovators. We are on a mission to change the world and make work more human by making an impact in the lives of employees every day through our Workhuman Cloud. 
 Our Product Team:  This team of visionaries is uniquely positioned to drive our suite of human applications, our fast-growing business, and the HR industry forward. Our team includes roles ranging from product managers and product owners to data analysts and UX designers. We shape the strategy and thought leadership of making work more human, act as the creative force behind our products’ amazing designs and user experience, and partner with development to take on the technical challenges of bringing that vision to reality. 
 We build a wide range of product experiences – recognition & rewards, HR applications performance management, e-commerce and merchandising, mobile, social and analytics are just some of our innovation areas. If you want to be a SaaS visionary while also rolling your sleeves up and diving into the creation, development and improvements of our product – join us on our Product team! 
 Our e-commerce Team:  This team engages and inspires our recognition audience through an incredibly crafted site experience. The redemption experience, vendor management, and merchandising process all come from this team. If you are an innovator who wants to help develop and drive business strategy, loves a challenge, and wants to join a growing department – look no further than our e-commerce team. 
 Whether you are just starting your career in the product space, or you’re a seasoned pro looking to take the next step – join us in our Framingham or Dublin offices and help us drive the HR industry forward. If you want to be part of a life-changing mission while working in a fast-paced and growing environment – come work with us.
