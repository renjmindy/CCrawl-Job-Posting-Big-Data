 About the Role 
 Our client is currently seeking experienced Digger/ Excavator Operators with Strong and Proven Production knowledge. Successful candidates must have operated up to 250 tonne diggers loading trucks and doing bench work. 
 Job/Site Information: 
 2:1 FIFO Roster 
 Thursday flying days 
 Mine site project 
 Day and night shift work

 ??? Current Australian Drivers Licence

 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors.
