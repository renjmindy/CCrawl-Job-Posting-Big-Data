 HR Waste Collection Driver 
 
 
 Monday to Friday Dayshift 
 
 
 $31.28per hour 
 
 
 Immediate Start 
 
 
 About the Role 
 We are currently looking for an experienced HR Waste Collection Driver. Our client is seeking an enthusiastic team member to join their team for an ongoing opportunity. 
 Duties and Requirements 
 
 
 Performing waste collection duties 
 
 
 Rear Loader Hard Waste Collection 
 
 
 Move bins 
 
 
 Cleaning truck as required 
 
 
 Comprehensive knowledge of safety requirements 
 
 
 Skills and tickets required 
 
 
 HR drivers licence essential 
 
 
 Proven experience operating rear lift 
 
 
 Willing to provide a driver history report for the past 5 years 
 
 
 White Card
