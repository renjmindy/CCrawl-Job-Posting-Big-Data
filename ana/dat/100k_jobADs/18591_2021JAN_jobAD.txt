 BIOC Controller 
 Job description 
 Mine Controller 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking an experienced and highly motivated Control Room Operator to support an energetic Brisbane based team. The successful candidate will be working a rostered shift with a possibility for an immediate start. 
 
 
 Brisbane based position 
 
 
 Payrate $50.00 
 
 
 12.5 hour shifts Day/Night & Weekends 
 
 
 About Our Client 
 Our client is a global leader in aluminium, with high-quality bauxite mines and alumina refineries that are operational in 36 countries around the world. The client has a strong commitment to deliver projects to the highest safety standards and considers new products and operations with an understanding of their customers' and communities' needs. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Recording of information from Mining operators 
 
 
 Monitoring fixed plant operations 
 
 
 Respond to Emergencies 
 
 
 Control and coordinate communication to relevant personnel 
 
 
 Tracking and recording of operational systems 
 
 
 Respond to fault findings and Emergencies 
 
 
 Skills and Experience: 
 
 
 Open Cut Mining experience within a similar role 
 
 
 Efficient data entry and administration skills 
 
 
 Previous Control Room Operator experience is highly advantageous 
 
 
 Ability to work in a fast-paced operational environment 
 
 
 Good interpersonal and Communication skills 
 
 
 Benefits of role: 
 
 
 Great Team environment 
 
 
 Opportunities for growth 
 
 
 Brisbane based role / CBD location 
 
 
 Rostered hours both Day / Night 
 
 
 Excellent pay rate 
 
 
 About WorkPac: 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels.
