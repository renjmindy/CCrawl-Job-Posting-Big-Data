 Full-Time Data Engineer 
 Adecco  -  Satakunta  , Finland 
 Job Description 
 Our international client AREVA is looking for Olkiluoto 3 site in Eurajoki an Data Engineer data collector for qualification database for temporary position for four months period. Work starts as soon as the right persons is found. 
 Criterions: 
 
 
 
 you have experience on technical details and mechanical components design (valves, pumps) and/or electrical /I&C design (sensors) 
 
 
 
 
 you have general knowledge in component and/or I&C design and of component supplier documentation 
 
 
 
 
 you have general office skills and MS Office is familiar to you 
 
 
 
 
 you have CADIS and WPE- system experience and some skills on documentation, Documentum - system knowledge is a benefit 
 
 
 
 
 you speak fluent English, German is a plus 
 
 
 
 Responsibilities: 
 A team of Engineering Data collectors will provide input from engineering for qualification and IT secure activities and collect the necessary data. The coal is the completion of the database. 
 Benefits: 
 We will offer good chance to get to know one of the worlds largest sites and it's way of working in international atmosphere. This is a good work experience for your CV.
