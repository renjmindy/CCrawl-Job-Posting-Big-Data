 Home Manager 
 An outstanding new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Home Manager to work in an exceptional care home based in the Eye, Diss area. 
 As the Home Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 The care home provides support for people with dementia and requires all types of care from nursing care, to residential and respite care 
 An ideal Home Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     In depth understanding of the legal requirements such as Health and Social Care Act/ CQC regulations/ KLOEs and rating characteristics
 
 
 
     Goes the extra mile for residents and staff
 
 
 
     An excellent team player during all the ups and downs
 
 
 
     Ability to deliver outstanding care for the residents
 
 
 
     At least two years' experience of managing a home of similar size
 
 
 
 **The successful Home Manager will receive an excellent salary up to £50,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 40 hours from 9am to 5pm.
