 Maintenance (Mechanical Systems Technician) 
 Apply for the position, Maintenance (Mechanical Systems Technician), with Barton Community College today or contact Mary Jane Felke for more information. Posted May 3, 2020. 
 [ Apply Online  __](To apply go to: jobs.bartonccc.edu Persons with hearing or speech impairment, please use the Kansas Relay Service at 1-800-766-3777 or dial 711. Position is open until filled. EEO/AA.)   (620)-792-9388  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 Barton Community College is seeking applications for a full time replacement maintenance position (Mechanical Systems Technician). Must be able to work flexible hours and be capable of lifting up to 100 lb. Applicants must be able to pass a motor vehicle report, pre-employment drug test, background check, and have an excellent driving record. Applicants must be able to pass pre-employment Physical Capacity Profile testing. Responsibilities: Maintenance & repair of College buildings & mechanical systems (Plumbing/HVAC/Electrical). Mechanical aptitude and experience; completion of a specialized course of study at a business or trade school, or 2 years equivalent experience. The hourly rate for this position is $16.04 to $18.32. Review of completed applications begins immediately. Position is open until filled.
