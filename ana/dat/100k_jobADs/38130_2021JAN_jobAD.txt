 Commercial Administrator, to support a busy sales team - Telford - £21-23k. 
 Our Client 
 We are working with an organisation that boasts an enviable reputation in its sector due to its product range and growth targets. We are now seeking a Commercial Administrator to join their vibrant sales team. 
 The Role 
 This role is a great opportunity to join a business that is experiencing an exciting trading period. It will be a busy and challenging time to work with them. The team operates with positivity and energy, and it is a well-organised set-up within lovely offices. 
 Reporting into both the Finance Director and the Commercial Sales Manager, the role will involve the following responsibilities: 
 
 Processing sales enquiries 
 Resolution of customer queries 
 Maintaining logistics and shipping documentation 
 Verification of customer contract information 
 Issuing quotes and sending out samples 
 Keeping track of supply issues and ensuring customers are kept informed of shipment dates 
 Processing of paperwork to ensure prompt deliveries and payments 
 Ad hoc support tasks for the sales team 
 
 The Commercial Administrator role is a permanent opportunity, and will offer variety and challenge to the successful candidate. 
 The Person 
 The successful candidate will ideally have a background in commercial sales admin, customer service and general office tasks associated with the smooth running of a business development team. You'll have a positive and enthusiastic personality, with a solid track-record working with Word and Excel, who is used to a pressurised office environment. 
 Commercial Administrator - Telford - permanent role paying up to £23k per annum.
