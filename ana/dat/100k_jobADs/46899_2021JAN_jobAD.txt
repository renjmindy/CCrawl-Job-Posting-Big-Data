 **  We are looking for skilled and professional Child and Youth Workers with mid to senior level CYW experience, to join our network of independent contractors. As an Independent Contractor at   wm +a   you work   according to your availability and in your area of interest/skill/expertise! ** 
 WORK AVAILABLE: Daytime / Weekdays 
 LOCATIONS: Georgina / York Region 
 REQUIRED: Diploma in CYW, DSW, BST or B.A. in related discipline 
 SETTINGS: School Support 
 @ wm+a  you have: 
 
 
 Flexibility of work: work options based on your availability 
 
 
 Commitment: 24 hour access to support when needed 
 
 
 Technical infrastructure: virtual service coordination; online scheduling and CRM system 
 
 
 Professional development: clinical training opportunities: ABA, OCD, ADHD, ODD, FASD and more! 
 
 
 Recognized credential: RBT (Registered Behaviour Technician) 40 hour online training available to active associates 
 
 
 Available mandatory trainings: CPI, SMG and UMAB 
 
 
 **Qualifications & Experience: ** 
 
 
 A minimum of 3 full years of experience with individuals diagnosed with ASD (autism), Mental Health Disorders or Dual Diagnosis 
 
 
 Must have Community college diploma or university degree in a relevant discipline(CYW, DSW, BA, BST) 
 
 
 Strong experience working with complex special needs population (with behavioural challenges) 
 
 
 Ability to work independently following a client BSP; critical thinking and sound decision making ability 
 
 
 Knowledge of the principles of Applied Behaviour Analysis/data collection is an asset 
 
 
 Vulnerable Sector Screening required; current VPC accepted within the last 6 months 
 
 
 Crisis training (CPI, UMAB, SMG) and Valid Standard First Aid and CPR Level C required 
 
 
 Who we are: 
 wm +a  provides specialized behaviour-based services to individuals with complex needs; Autism Spectrum Disorder, Dual Diagnosis, and Mental Health throughout Southern Ontario. We are looking for skilled, professional and value-focused  Child and Youth Workers  who in addition to their strong skills have a track record of demonstrated committed, a strong work ethic and value professional growth. 
 Responsibilities: 
 Providing individualized and/or crisis supports to children, youth and adults diagnosed with ASD (autism), Developmental Disability, Mental Health Disorder and/or Dual Diagnosis 
 Implementing client specific goals and ISP/BSP; teaching based on principals of ABA and other evidence-based practices 
 Work with client in their setting: residential, schools/day programs; hospitals and health care setting; family or independent living home; and the community at large 
 Collect, manage and analyze data/ daily documentation relevant to the clients’ specific programs and goals; participate/contribute to case coordination and multi-disciplinary service dialogue.
