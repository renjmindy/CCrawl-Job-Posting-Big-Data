 Labourer- Waste Management 
 Job description 
 Wagerup Location 
Ongoing opportunity for the right candidate 
40+ hours weekly 
 About the Role: 
WorkPac is currently seeking General Labourers for a Waste Management Yard in Bunbury. The role is initially 2-3 months with the opportunity for it to be ongoing for the right candidate. The role will be commencing in the next couple of weeks but must be available now for interviews and inductions if successful. The main duties involve sentry work, high pressure blasting and maintenance. The pay rate is $30,93 per hour + penalties. 
 Skills and Experience: 
General Labouring experience 
Driver's License (HR is highly desirable but not essential) 
Ability to pass a pre-employment medical and DAS 
Right to Work in Australia 
 About WorkPac: 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Looking after our employees is important to us. Through our myrewards platform, we offer benefits from retail rewards through to financial, health cover, insurance, superannuation services and more.
