Job Description & Requirements 
 Substitute Teachers 
 Elk Island Catholic Schools 
 All Communities 
 Elk Island Catholic Schools (EICS) offers you the opportunity to work in a faith-based, collaborative environment where everyone's skills, knowledge and attributes are respected. Come and join a great team! 
 We are accepting applications for teachers who will join our Substitute Teacher Roster for all of our communities (Sherwood Park, Fort Saskatchewan, Vegreville and Camrose). Preference is given to Catholic candidates. The successful candidates will be able to work in all our Division communities and possess a valid Alberta teaching certificate (or be eligible for). 
 New graduates are encouraged to apply. 
 We thank all applicants for their interest but only those being considered to advance in the selection process will be contacted.
