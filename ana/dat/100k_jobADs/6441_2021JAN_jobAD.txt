 Career Description 
 Surveyors use make exact measurements to determine property boundaries. They use surveys, source maps, previous survey data, photographs, computer records and other information to carry out surveys. They prepare maps of land surveyed including charts, drawings and survey notes and also document and analyze information. 
 What's next? 
 Get your  basic surveying certificate  from Western Piedmont Community College and read a  success story  from WPCC's program. 
 See a  video  about being a surveyor! 
 Connect with the  Western Piedmont Home Builder's Association .
