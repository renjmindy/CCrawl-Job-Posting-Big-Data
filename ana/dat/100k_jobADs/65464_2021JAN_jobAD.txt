 Sweet Baby Jason! it's a Full-time job 
 Savannah College of Art and Design is looking for a Senior Art Director  in Savannah, GA, USA. 
 About 
 Join SCAD as the senior art director for creative services. This creative and strategic thinker designs solutions for communications challenges including publications, ads, websites, signage, and more. The senior art director takes ownership of high-profile projects, provides art direction and leadership to graphic designers, and works with minimal supervision. 
 The ideal candidate has the ability to take direction, follow instructions, and be open to constructive feedback; maintains a professional and positive attitude; demonstrates good time management skills; has the ability to work independently as well as collaboratively; possesses knowledge of design, culture, and technology trends; maintains an eye for details and strong organizational skills; has strong typography skills and a working knowledge of design principles, production, and layout.
