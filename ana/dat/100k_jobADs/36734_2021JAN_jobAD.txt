 Career Opportunities 
 Clinical Therapist 
 (LPC, LMFT, LCSW) 
 
 Works Counseling Center, a self-pay group private practice in Nashville, is looking for part time and/or full-time psychotherapists to join our group practice. Our office location is located in the heart of Belle Meade. Requirements include being fully licensed in the State of Tennessee or licensed eligible, preferably with experience. Compensation is percentage based on income received, and paid out monthly. Therapist must have a niche(s) (children, couples, trauma, EMDR, family, etc.) and/or be willing to grow with a new niche if not already established. Works Counseling Center provides a steady stream of referrals for the therapist. Candidate must be independent, driven, collaborative, and enhance our fun, connected, and positive minded group of clinicians. Candidate will be employee status. If supervision is needed, it will be provided by a TN approved LPC supervisor. 
 We provide: 
 
 Steady stream of referrals 
 Competitive compensation 
 A receptionist who answers calls and schedules intake appointments 
 Monthly group case consultation 
 Bonuses every 6 months (marketing and blogging required to receive bonus) 
 Supportive team environment 
 Comfortable office space 
 
 Requirements are: 
 
 Current State of TN license as a LCSW, LMFT or LPC 
 Willing to work 15 hours per week or more 
 Prior private practice experience is a plus 
 
 To Apply:  Send Cover Letter and CV 
 *Required education: 
*Master's or Doctorate 
 *Recommended license or certification: 
*LPC-MHSP, LCSW, or LMFT 
 Job Types: Full-time, Part-time
