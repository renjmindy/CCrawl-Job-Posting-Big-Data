 Accounts Payable FT Temp-To-Hire 
   
   Workway, Inc.   
 __Website    __@workway 
 Workway is a professional staffing firm, working with organizations across the country to place exceptional candidates. We are looking for an ** ** AP Specialist ** ** responsible for the core invoice processing functions moving invoices from receipt to the posting of the invoice into the general ledger (SAP) through the E-Procurement platform (StarSource).   Your specific duties will include: 
 
 Works with the conventional processing of invoices and goods receipt verification using a three-way match, as well as the processing of credit memos 
 Processes customer service requests using the ServiceNow ticket management system, assists and supports buyers, and resolves vendor issues. 
 Determines the nature of inquiries and escalates or routes as needed to the disbursement desk, vendor master, or AP processors for invoice research 
 
 Specific qualifications for the  position include : 
 
 High School Diploma or equivalent required. 
 1 year minimum of Accounts Payable experience. 
 
 Let one of our recruiters assist you in finding and preparing for interviews with our clients. Whether you are interested in a direct hire, temp-to-hire, or temporary position, Workway can help you to put your best foot forward. Contact us today!
