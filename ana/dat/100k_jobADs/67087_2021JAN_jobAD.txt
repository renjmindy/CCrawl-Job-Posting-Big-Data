 Nanny Jobs Canada 
 Posted on  October 20, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This agency for nanny jobs in Canada began its operation in March 1980 to help find locally and internationally qualified nanny and caregiver jobs candidates that will provide professional services for families in need of household assistance. And as a result of 29 years of experience and continued direct contact with many overseas recruitment agencies, the said agency for nanny jobs Canada can assure their clients with the best possible service and nanny selection. 
 At present, the said agency for nanny jobs Canada offers full time and part time nanny jobs in Canada to qualified caregivers from various backgrounds and countries. The agency’s nanny and caregiver jobs candidates come from Europe, Asia, Israel, South America and the chosen candidates for the said nanny jobs Canada will be serving families and individuals from the lower mainland and beyond. 
 In order to qualify for nanny and caregiver jobs in Canada, all applicants must meet three requirements set by the CIC or Citizenship and Immigration Canada under the Live In Caregiver Program. These requirements are: 
 
 Six months of full-time training or 12 months of experience in paid employment in a field or occupation related to the job you are seeking as a live-in caregiver. 
 Successful completion of the equivalent of a Canadian high school education. 
 Ability to speak, read and understand either English or French.
