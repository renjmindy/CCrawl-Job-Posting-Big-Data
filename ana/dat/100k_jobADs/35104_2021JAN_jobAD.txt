 Senior Care Assistant 
 A fantastic new job opportunity has arisen for a motivated Senior Care Assistant to work in an exceptional care home based in the Northwich, Cheshire area. 
 As the Senior Care Assistant you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This care home provides family value-led residential and dementia care and support in a quiet residential area 
 As the Senior Care Assistant your key duties include: 
 
 
     Helping our residents with personal support and care
 
 
 
     Aiding their emotional well-being and social needs
 
 
 
     Administration and monitoring of medications to residents
 
 
 
     Serving meals/drinks and assisted feeding
 
 
 
     Interacting with our residents on a personal level
 
 
 
     Maintaining care plans
 
 
 
 An ideal Senior Care Assistant must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Experience in the care industry
 
 
 
     Have great communication skills both written and verbal
 
 
 
     Proven experience of leading a team in the care industry
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
 **The successful Senior Care Assistant will receive an excellent salary of £10.25 per hour. 
 This exciting position has Full Time and Part Time options available working 22, 33 or 44 hours a week on Night Shifts only. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     On-site parking
 
 
 
     Store discounts
 
 
 
     Flexible working hours
 
 
 
     Employee discount
 
 
 
     Wellness program
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must have an NVQ Level 3 in Health & Social Care or be willing to work towards this
