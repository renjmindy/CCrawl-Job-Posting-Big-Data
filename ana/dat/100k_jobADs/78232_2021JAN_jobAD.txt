 Production Sewing Worker at Worknet Staffing 
 Location:  Lafayette, LA 
 Production sewing worker needed ASAP for packaging company. Worker will use an industrial sewing machine to create product as well as doing packing. All workers, during downtime, act as floaters to help in assembly or other areas. Previous experience needed doing production sewing, producing multiples of the same or similar product. 
 Temp tp hire position 
$8/hr, plus production bonuses (which increases hourly rate based on work) 
Mon - Fri, 7am - 3:30pm; Sat (as needed), 5am - 11am/1pm. 
 Other requirements 
 
 Verifiable work experience in area needed 
 Willingness to complete and pass a pre-employment screen 
 Reliable transportation 
 Some flexibility in case of schedule changes. 
 
 Qualified candidates - please forward resumes to eugenias@worknet2k.com. Follow up with a call to our Lafayette office for confirmation of info sent and to discuss the position.
