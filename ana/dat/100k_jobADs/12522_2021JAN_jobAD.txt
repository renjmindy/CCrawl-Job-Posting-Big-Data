 Caregiver Jobs in Abroad 
 Posted on  November 23, 2010  by  caregiver 
 A POEA accredited recruitment agency is currently inviting caregiver jobs applicants for their POEA approved  Caregiver Jobs in Abroad . These caregiver jobs in abroad are for the holy land of Israel and was recently approved by the POEA last November 19, 2010 for a Makati based recruitment agency. For the said caregiver jobs in abroad, the agency will select 27 applicants who are best qualified for caregiver work abroad. 
 To apply for the said caregiver jobs in abroad, forward your updated resume and application directly to the office of Mariposa International Services Company Inc. For any questions regarding the said caregiver jobs in abroad, you may dial the agency’s landline numbers indicated below. 
 Mariposa International Services Co. Inc. 
M/F & 2F 61 Gil Puyat Avenue 
Makati City 
Tel Nos: 8437566 / 8432978 
Email Add: mariposamedina@vasia.com
