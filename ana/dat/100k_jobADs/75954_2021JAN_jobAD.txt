 Web Developer LOCALPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 We're on the hunt for a Web Developer to join us in the Steadfast Collective studio. You'll be joining a team of extremely talented creatives right here in Southampton. 
 Take a look at what the role entails and get in touch if you think you're a match! 
 You will be working on a range of projects from large-scale hosting apps to dashboards and everything in-between. We're looking for someone who can work independently, is keen to grow and wants to join a team that is looking to make a difference to the web. 
 Our ideal partner: 
 
 Is excited to build digital products that make the internet a better place. Creating value for the world. 
 Is happy to adapt to a fast moving client and internal products work schedule. 
 Has experience in building WordPress or CraftCMS themes & plugins. 
 Has Experience and interest in working with Laravel. 
 Has an understanding of GIT and modern workflow. 
 Has an understanding of Photoshop or Sketch. 
 
 Nice to haves: 
 
 Has designed and implemented API’s. 
 Has integrated with an external API such as Stripe or Algolia. 
 Has experience with Laravel Mix, and underlying technologies such as WebPack. 
 Has worked with queue drivers. 
 Has experience with a front-end JavaScript framework such as Vue, React or Angular. 
 
 Your time at Steadfast will be split 70% client projects / 30% internal products. 
 What we'll offer you: 
 
 Starting salary £22-26K (Full Time) 
 Flexible working hours 
 Choice of MacOS or Windows environment 
 Personal Development Plan 
 The chance to work on exciting and fast-growing applications 
 An opportunity to compete in our daily Mario Kart tournaments! 
 
 In short, we want to help you to grow to be the best version of you, providing opportunities for you to work on innovative projects!
