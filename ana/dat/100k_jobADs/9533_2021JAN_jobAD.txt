 Gleeson Recruitment Group have been appointed on an exclusive basis to appoint a number of temporary Credit Controllers to liaise with individuals to recover individual debt. 
 This role will be available on an on-going temporary basis and ideally we are looking for experienced individuals who can commit for the duration of the assignment. 
 You must be able to travel to Birmingham City Centre; this role will be based on site in the office in a safe; socially distanced office. 
 Responsibilities and duties include: 
 
 Collection of fees in accordance with invoice due dates 
 Liaise directly with fee payers, agents and guardians regarding student accounts 
 Follow up with individual students where required to assist in the process of collecting fees 
 Coordinate and maintain the record of status of action to chase all outstanding fees 
 Cashing of outstanding fees and to assist in the process of devising effective procedures for cash collection 
 Record payment adjustments to student accounts 
 Produce invoices for all new students to facilitate collection of fees in a timely manner 
 
 You must have at least 6 months credit control experience and be available to start straight away. 
 This role will be £10ph plus holiday pay 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
