 Posted on  November 29, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This is a great opportunity for qualified Filipinos to work in carer job UK. A job order was recently approved by the POEA allowing a recruitment agency to recruit qualified applicants for carer job UK. The said carer job UK was approved for recruitment by the POEA last November 13, 2009 for a recruitment agency based in Makati City. The job order balance or number of applicants to be recruited for the said carer job UK is 8 qualified applicants. 
 All interested parties who wish to apply for the said POEA approved Carer Job UK should proceed directly to the office of the recruiting agency to submit their application.
