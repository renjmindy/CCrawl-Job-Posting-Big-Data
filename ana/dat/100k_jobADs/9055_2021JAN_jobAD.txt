 Product Update: Get Ready for Our New Job Page Redesign 
 
  Shira Forman   
  / May 26, 2020   
 
 __ 
 __ 
 __ 
 __ 
 __ 
 At Workiz, we’re always looking for better ways to help you run your service business, and that’s why we’re excited to share with you our new and improved job page! 
 As your job page is one of the most essential pages in the Workiz platform, we wanted to make sure that it gives you the full picture in just one glance. Now you can have everything in one place, making it simple and easy for you to manage your business. 
 Let’s take a look at the changes! 
 1. Full Access from One Location 
 We have completely changed the experience. From this page you will be able to see all the relevant information about a job, whether its general information, notes, calls, and more! 
 2. Timeline: Get the Full Job Story 
 Our most important update is the  timeline feature  which displays the entire history of each job. From the first customer call until the final payment, you can check out the full story here. Instead of going over to your inbox and trying to find a conversation, you can now  see all your client communication  from the bar on the right-hand side. 
 3. Make Calls and Send Messages Right from the Job 
 Do you want to hear a client call? Message a client back? Now you can listen to calls, message clients, and add notes right from the job page! 
 4. New Drag-and-Drop Features 
 Workiz’s drag-and-drop tool helps you organize your line items and make simple changes without ever leaving the job page. 
 At Workiz, your time is valuable to us and we will continue making improvements to help you be more efficient and make sure you have the best possible experience with Workiz.  Have any comments, questions or suggestions for improvement?   We want your input! Reach out to our customer success team  here.
