 Consultant: 
 
 
 I have a fantastic opportunity for an Internal Communications and Stakeholder Executive to join a well-known organisation in Birmingham, who are well-known for their excellent working culture and who are going through extreme growth at the moment. They have some extremely exciting projects coming up both in the UK and internationally. This role will play a key part in communications within the organisation and with all external partners associated with the programme you will be focusing on. 
 This Internal Communications and Stakeholder Executive role will involve: 
 
 
 Identifying new business opportunities - including new markets, growth areas, trends, customers, products and services 
 
 
 Ensuring content and updates are shared with relevant stakeholders, delivery partners and internal audiences, helping to raise awareness internally 
 
 
 Helping to develop and implement internal communications that engages with delivery partners as well as the relevant internal departments 
 
 
 Supporting the development and delivery of a wider stakeholder engagement plan, to include outreach activity, ongoing mapping and tracking of activity 
 
 
 Capturing and monitoring feedback/insight to ensure the team's work is informed 
 
 
 Responsible for corporate and stakeholder digital engagement - to include corporate website, social media, newsletter and an intranet 
 
 
 Developing and maintain toolkit, to include presentations and marcomms assets 
 
 
 This Internal Communications and Stakeholder Executive position requires a candidate with the following skills: 
 
 
 Experience in using Photoshop or similar to produce engaging content for stakeholders and partners 
 
 
 Stakeholder relations at a regional and national level 
 
 
 Internal communications, including online outreach i.e. intranet management 
 
 
 Skilled in social media and website management 
 
 
 Delivery of corporate communications for senior stakeholder and partner audiences 
 
 
 Able to work in a fast-paced environment 
 
 
 Excellent communication skills and able to proofread to a high standard 
 
 
 Photoshop and design skills 
 
 
 To apply for the Internal Communications and Stakeholder Executive position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
