 Scrum Master - £65,000 per annum - Birmingham - Remote working 
 FTC/Fixed Term Contract 11 months. 
 Scrum Master needed to work along side Transformation Director for a business working on a large scale Data project .The client has traditionally been a Waterfall/Prince2 environment so they are keen for a ScrumMaster to come in and help change Persuasive and effective communicator with strong negotiating skills. 
 
 Possess a solid understanding and strong practical experience of agile delivery within Transformation projects. 
 Running stand up meetings 
 Evidence of leading continuous improvement initiatives. 
 Evidence of mentoring and coaching others to develop their skills and managing the performance of team members. 
 Experience of working with Azure & DevOps would be beneficial 
 Experience of facilitating workshops and discussions. 
 Ability to forge effective working relationships with stakeholders. 
 Considerable experience in a relevant technical environment. 
 Maintain effective and efficient delivery from your team during periods of remote working. 
 
 This role is a fixed term contract of 11 months but has the opportunity to be permanent at a later date. The role will be remote though the office location is Birmingham.
