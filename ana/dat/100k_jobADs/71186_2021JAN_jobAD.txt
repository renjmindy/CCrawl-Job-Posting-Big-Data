 Description 
 This job requires you to act as a hub and gather information about Singaporean tourists that have visited/are coming to Tokyo. There are only 10 easy questions to answer within the questionnaire! Please gather up to 10 different people (minimum 5) who will provide detailed answers. For each person answering all 10 questions, you will receive 1000yen. The way you recruit these people answering the questions are completely up to you. We only ask that the questions are sincerely and properly filled out. In the case of incomplete answers, we will not be able to pay you, so please tell your answerers this. On top of this, please identify each person answering the questionnaire in the basic information sheet within the document.
