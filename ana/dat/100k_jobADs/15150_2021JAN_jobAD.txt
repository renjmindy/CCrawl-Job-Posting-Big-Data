 About us: 
The YMCA cares about raising healthy and resilient kids, ensuring youth feel confident and supported, and helping people feel healthy and connected to others in the community. As the Lower Mainland's largest operational charity, our staff and volunteers provide vital community services that have a positive impact on some of the community's most pressing social issues. 
 We’ve been a part of the Lower Mainland for more than 130 years––a place where people of every age and background find the support they need to reach their potential. Serving 120,000 people annually, the YMCA of Greater Vancouver provides programs and services in health and fitness, aquatics, child care, camp, employment, education and training, child and family development, immigrant services, global initiatives, youth leadership development and health management. In all things, we align our activities with our values: 
 
 Do the right thing - We are caring, respectful, honest and responsible in all we do 
 Put people first - We believe in the strengths, perspectives and passion of people 
 Keep our promises -We do what we say we will do 
 Lead by example -We are courageous, speak with conviction, listen attentively and collaborate 
 
 About the Position: 
We are seeking a dynamic Manager, Finance to join our team. Reporting to the Vice President of Finance, the Finance Manager is a collaborative business partner and trusted advisor supporting an operations portfolio. The Finance Manager is responsible for the full range of accounting cycle duties and responsibilities, focused on the financial and operational accounting transactions for the YMCA of Greater Vancouver. 
 You are a leader with a strong background in accounting, financial management and budgets. You are an impact-driven professional with a curious mindset and the desire to continuously learn more. You are passionate about improving the way we work as we continue to grow and evolve. You will ensure the preparation of timely, accurate and complete accounting information and reports to meet both internal and external requirements. 
 Major Responsibilities: 
 
 Prepare and analyze meaningful financial information, statements, and reports, to provide current and accurate business information to YMCA operations portfolio 
 Support operating managers with annual budget preparation, ongoing accruals and adjustments, and monitoring internal statements and investigating significant variances 
 Problem solve with operating managers and provide guidance and advice on an ongoing basis 
 Create and distribute monthly and year-end financial statements in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles 
 Liaise with external auditors to provide them with the necessary information for annual audits of the YMCA operating charity and two foundations 
 Supervise 1-2 accounting staff and provide coaching and mentoring to all accounting staff 
 Make business process improvements to support ongoing growth of the YMCA 
 Preparation of year-end audit working papers 
 Ensure appropriate systems and controls are implemented and maintained 
 
 Skills & Qualifications: 
 
 Bachelor’s degree in related discipline 
 CPA professional accounting designation, an asset (or must be enrolled in CPA program and working towards designation) 
 Minimum 5 years progressive accounting experience 
 Leadership/supervisory experience 
 Advanced Excel skills (power pivot and analysis) Microsoft Power BI, an asset 
 Experience with Sage 300 and Microsoft Dynamics preferred 
 Excellent verbal and written communication skills 
 Successful candidates will be required to provide a current and satisfactory Criminal Records Check/Vulnerable Sector Search issued no later than six (6) months preceding start date
