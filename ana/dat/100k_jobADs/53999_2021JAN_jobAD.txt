 Part Time Bookkeeper 
 My well established and growing client located near Banbury are seeking an experienced part time bookkeeper with experience of using the system Javelin to join their organisation to work closely with the CEO and support him in the day to day accounting duties. 
This is a fast paced working environment that requires a true team working mentality and flexible working approach. You will report directly into the CEO and interact with suppliers, customers and heads of departments. You will produce all of the accounts to trial balance whilst undertaking a varied role. 
 Duties and Responsibilities: 
 
 Undertake all bookkeeping duties 
 Make bank payments and process bank receipts 
 Undertake Credit Control, Sales Ledger and Purchase Ledger duties 
 Complete VAT returns 
 Prepare information for the payroll bureau 
 Produce purchase orders 
 Ensure parts are correctly allocated 
 Ad-hoc project work specific to this industry sector 
 
 Experience Required: 
 
 Previous experience of working in a manufacturing environment is essential 
 You will be qualified by experience or hold an accounting qualification 
 Have demonstrable experience gained working in a similar role 
 The ability to pick things up quickly 
 Be able to turn your hand to any task required 
 Experience of the ERP system Javelin is essential alongside good excel skills 
 
 Hours:  25 hours a week - flexible on how those hours are made up 
 Full training and support will be provided and my client will offer true flexibility in regards to working hours as well as the option to work from home. You will enjoy working in a fast paced friendly and supportive working environment whilst being a valued member of the team. 
 For further information on the role of Part-Time Bookkeeper please contact me directly.
