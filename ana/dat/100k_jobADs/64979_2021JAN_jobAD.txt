 About Northeastern: 
Founded in 1898, Northeastern is a global research university and a world leader in experiential learning. The same commitment to connecting with the world drives our use-inspired research enterprise. The university offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate and graduate programs leading to degrees through the doctorate in nine colleges and schools. Our campuses in Charlotte, N.C., San Francisco, Seattle, and Toronto are regional platforms for undergraduate and graduate learning and collaborative research. Northeastern pursues advanced research in security and materials at the Innovation Campus in Burlington, Massachusetts, and in coastal sustainability at the Marine Science Center in Nahant, Massachusetts. 
 About the Opportunity: 
The Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering is looking to hire a Post-Doctoral Research Associate 
 Responsibilities: 
Perform basic or applied research of a limited scope, primarily using existing theories and methods. Assists the supervisor in the interpretation and publication of results and grants. Maintains the laboratory and may exercise functional supervision over supporting research staff. Primary responsibility is ensuring that the research is complete. 
 Qualifications: 
PhD in Electrical Engineering- Power Electronics 
 At least 10 years of experience in designing, fabricating, and testing medium power converters 
 Strong writing and oral communication skills 
 Publication in prestigious journals and conferences 
 Experience in PCB design, thermal management solutions, designing EMI filters, designing and testing high frequency SiC-based power converters.
