 Love's Brings Jobs, Truck Parking to 2 States 
 April 15, 2019  • by  WT Staff 
 Love's Travel Stops is now serving customers in Williams, Calif., and Toomsuba, Miss. The California location is brand new with 103 truck parking spaces, while the Mississippi locale reopened after closing in December for renovations and features 71 truck parking spaces. The two locations are bringing 60 new jobs to each state. 
 The more than 10,000-square-foot facilities are open 24/7 and both offer many amenities, including: 
 
 Love's Truck Tire Care center. 
 Eateries (Chester's, Godfather's Pizza, and IHOP express in Northern California and Arby's in Mississippi). 
 Eight diesel bays. 
 Seven showers. 
 Laundry facilities. 
 Fresh Kitchen Concept featuring salads, parfaits and Hot to Go tacos and sandwiches. 
 Bean to Cup Gourmet Coffee. 
 Brand-name snacks. 
 Mobile to Go Zone. 
 CAT scale.
