 Need Illustration - Instructions on how to mic a groom for Wedding 
 Published on the October 03, 2017 in Design & Multimedia 
 
 Project 
 Project Insights New 
 
 About this project 
 design-multimedia / illustrations 
 Less than USD 50 
 I am needing illustration and write instructions for each photo/image I have attached. Each image has its own description of instructions. Instruction for each image listed below. 
 Illustration need to be professional, clear and easily understood what the image is. For example in "Image 2" illustration should depict finger tapping on iPhone app with direction underneath or to the side of image. 
 These illustration and directions will be part of a larger manual. 
 Image Directions: 
 Pre-Wedding: 
 Image 1 - Power on iPhone 
 Image 2 - Open RODE App 
 Image 3 - Press the + at the bottom of the app to create a new record file 
 Image 4 - Start recording before placing on groom. 
 Groom/Officiant Clipart (Jacket) - Place lapel either on grooms/officiant tie or right lapel, place phone in inside pocket and place access cord in pocket. If groom has white tie or jacket, use white lapel. 
 Groom/Officiant Clipart (Vest or Just Shirt) If no jacket then feed down lapel wire down vest or shirt and clip lapel to right or left top of vest or center of short or tie. Place phone right or left pocket with access cord tucked in pocket. 
 After Ceremony: 
 Retrieve lapel and iphone from Groom and Officiant 
 Image 5 - Tap folder icon at top right of screen 
 Image 6 - Tap “File Share” tab. 
 Image 7 - Change file to AIFF 
 Image 8 - Tap “Create Audio File” and wait for file to create. Once file is done creating, turn phone off. 
 Remaining Images will be sent to hired freelancer.
