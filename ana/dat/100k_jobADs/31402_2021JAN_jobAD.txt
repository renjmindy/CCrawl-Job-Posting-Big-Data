 Financial Advisor (Commissions) - Leads Provided 
 A.L.I Financial & Investments Inc. 
 __Toronto, ON 
 Apply Now 
 We are seeking a financial advisor to focus on the insurance side of client portfolios. 
 Although the position is commissions and bonus based, our financial advisors can be provided with SALES LEADS by the branch management team to see clients and close sales! 
 _  COMPENSATION: _  Excellent Commissions Structure + Bonus 
 Uncapped Earnings :  **$100,000 + per year , renewal commissions for lifetime, plus bonus commissions. ****** **Not a Salary Role ****** 
 _  EXPECTATIONS: _ 
 1. To meet clients at the branch office or at your own setting to provide them with insurance solutions 
 2. To meet the professional standards of the financial industry and provide the right advice to clients while being aware of your own fiduciary responsibility 
 3. To service clients with their needs analysis, cash flow management and debt management 
 4. To understand the financial planning process and carry out business activities with a strong sense of relationship management 
 5. To have a holistic approach towards the financial planning process and provide Canadians with lifetime financial security 
 6. To practice business and financial transactions understanding the compliance laws and laws of ethics of the financial industry 
 7. To have a strong business acumen, understand the sales mind set and meet the quarterly and yearly sales targets 
 8. To continue to educate your own self with the industry's laws and regulations and complete the continuing education credits as a financial planner 
 _  WHO SHOULD APPLY: _ 
 Candidates With : 
 1. LLQP (highly recommended or intend to get one with the firm as soon as possible) 
 2. CLU, CHS, CFP (preferred) 
 3. College Diploma/University Degree or some previous work experience in the sales industry will be preferred. If selected for the role, we will put you on pace to complete your LLQP licensing requirements within 2 months, if you do not have the LLQP at the moment. 
 _  OTHER BENEFITS: _ 
 1. Top commissions structure in the industry plus bonus commissions. 
 2. Continued income for lifetime, depending on your sales growth 
 3. Business development program - the option of generating leads for your daily schedule 
 4. Enhanced training with sales concepts, sales cycle and product knowledge 
 5. Yearly bonus programs and yearly business travelling opportunities 
 6. Recognition programs and growth opportunities within the company 
 If you are seeking an exciting opportunity to challenge yourself with the sales role which is extremely rewarding, has top compensation and an opportunity to grow within the industry, send us your resume. 
 Candidate who speak English, Hindi, Punjabi, Urdu, Farsi, Arabic, Russian, French, Cantonese - All are most welcome to apply. 
 Job Type: Commission 
 Salary: $75,000.00 to $100,000.00 /year
