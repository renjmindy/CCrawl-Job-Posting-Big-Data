 [ 
 Hiring Full-Time Career Counselor 
 ](https://workforcesolutionspa.com/2018/07/25/hiring-full-time-career-counselor/) 
 __ 25th Jul 2018  __ Uncategorized 
 Workforce Solutions for North Central PA located in Kersey PA is seeking to fill a full- time Career Counselor in Jefferson County – Dubois area immediately. The position will be located in Brockway, PA. 
 Core duties include: coordination of career exploration, awareness and work-based learning opportunities for students, teachers and administrators, facilitate the development of education and industry/business partnerships in the region, and provide career counseling for middle and high school students. 
 Reliable transportation, ability to travel locally, and a valid driver’s license is required, along with Act 34 and 33 clearances. 
 It is preferred that the candidate have a bachelor’s degree in education, counseling, social services, human services or related field; or equivalent education/training and experience combined. In addition, the candidate must be self-motivated, able to work independently and have strong interpersonal skills. 
 **Interested applicants must send a resume and cover letter to Susan Snelick at ssnelick@ncwdb.org  with Resume in the Subject Line or mail to Workforce Solutions, attn. Susan Snelick, 425 Old Kersey Road, Kersey PA 15846 by Tuesday, August 14, 2018. **
