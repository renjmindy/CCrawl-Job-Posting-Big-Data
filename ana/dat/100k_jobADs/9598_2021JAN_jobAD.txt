 Mine Service Operators 
 Job description 
 
 
 $50.03/ hour 
 
 
 Accommodation & meals Supplied 
 
 
 BIBO OR DIDO 
 
 
 ABOUT OUR CLIENT 
 Our client is Australia's largest coal miner and exporter, the world's largest supplier to the sea borne coking coal market and largest coal producer and supplier of metallurgical coal. The client has several mine sites across the Bowen Basin consisting primarily of open cut mines 
 ABOUT THE ROLE 
 Seeking experienced Mine Service Operators to commence at a mine near Coppabella, you will be on a 7/7 Roster, Rotating Day and Night shift, DIDO or BIBO Mackay. 
 DUTIES & RESPONSBILBITIES 
 
 
 D11 Dozer Dozer push 
 
 
 Or Strong D10 Dozer push experience 
 
 
 SKILLS & EXPERIENCE 
 
 
 RII/ Training transcript signed by SSE 
 
 
 Minimum 6 months experience 
 
 
 Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Standard 11 or Equivalent 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 CULTURE & BENEFITS 
 
 
 7/7 Roster 
 
 
 BIBO OR DIDO 
 
 
 Camp & Meals Supplied 
 
 
 Super Paid on all Rostered Hours
