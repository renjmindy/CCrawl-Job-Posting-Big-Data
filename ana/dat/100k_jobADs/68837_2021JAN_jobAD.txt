 Do you have a way with words and a passion for writing engaging copy? My client, an exciting, market-leading business is looking to recruit an Online Content Executive. If this sounds of interest, then read on... 
 My client, based in Shropshire is looking to recruit an Online Content Executive, to join an established (and growing) marketing team. 
 The role will encompass: 
 
 Researching content/copy ideas, developing campaign ideas and writing engaging copy 
 Writing copy for all social channels 
 Writing copy for other (primarily online) channels from the website to email campaigns 
 Developing and delivering longer, in-depth articles including blog posts 
 Supporting the wider marketing team across the full marketing mix. 
 
 The ideal candidate will possess the following skills/expertise 
 
 Excellent copywriting skills 
 Demonstrable track record of writign across a range of channels 
 Ability to write both short, 'punchy' copy and longer, engaging articles. 
 Enthusiasm, desire to learn and high work ethic will also stand you in good stead. 
 
 Highly competitive package. 
 Interviews and onboarding to take place remotely if necessary. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
