 Are you an experienced Credit Controller? Are you looking to work for a credible industry leading business?  If yes, then please read on. 
 Redditch based and paying up to £25k per annum you will be joining a committed and hardworking finance team reporting directly into the team manager 
 
 Raising invoices in an accurate and timely manner 
 Ensuring cash is collected and ensure systems are updated with up to date information. 
 You will also be responsible for handling queries and ensuring effective resolution, whilst recognising potential problems and identifying areas for improvement 
 Bank Reconciliations, Bank Statements and Cash Allocation 
 Run regular aged debt reports and provide analysis. 
 
 Is this Credit Controller role in Redditch right for me? 
 This is a great opportunity for a Credit Controller who enjoys a customer focused position . It also offers the opportunity to get integrated within the business, become a real go to person and take ownership to develop strong working relationships with a varied client base. 
 You will receive the following benefits: 
 
 Salary up to £25k per annum 
 Pension 
 Benefits including free onsite parking 
 Open plan offices with onsite kitchen facilities 
 Flexible working hours 
 
 If you can picture yourself working for this developing business then apply today.
