 About the role 
 2021 is about growth for A Million Ads for both our people and product, as we experiment with new features and products.  You will be part of a cross-functional team of engineers, product specialists, UX designers, and QA;  focused on delivering high-value themes of work. 
 What you 'll do 
 
 Designing, implementing, maybe managing low-latency, high-availability, scalable services, and components. 
 Working on our user interface with digital audio and video producers and creatives to build personalised media. 
 Writing clean, efficient code and always improving the resilience of our system. 
 Working with the business more broadly, translating business requirements into an amazing product. 
 
 Who you are 
 
 You're likely to be working in a QA automation role right now. 
 You are passionate about automating tests in an agile environment. 
 You like some of the kit we like - think Javascript, Cypress, AWS, and Docker 
 You're good at analysing user stories, writing acceptance tests, and well structured test cases. 
 Have a desire to learn, gain more experience and look to improve 
 You have a curious mindset, you ask lots of questions, and have a need to understand how something works
