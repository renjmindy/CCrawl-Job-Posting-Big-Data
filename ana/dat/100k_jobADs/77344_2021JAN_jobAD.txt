 Gleeson are currently working with a superb manufacturing business based in Cannock who are recruiting for a Finance Manager to join them on a permanent basis. The successful candidate will ideally be a qualified accountant (ACA,ACCA,CIMA) with experience of working in a manufacturing business and have managed a small team. 
 Responsibilities: Finance Manager: Cannock: £40,000-£45,000 
 
 Prepare monthly management accounts for all areas of Operating and Capital expenditure 
 Reporting into the offsite Business Area Controller providing a monthly commentary on business performance 
 Support MD with budget management and periodic reforecasting 
 Develop, deliver and maintain robust accounting systems & processes to ensure the integrity of day to day accounting 
 People management of a small team of Credit Controller and Accounts Payable Assistant 
 Preparation of annual accounts including reconciliation and year end entries, working to tight deadlines and supporting external auditor 
 Managing monthly cash flow 
 Support senior management by identifying key issues and suggesting solutions 
 Identify system reports and improvements of controls including preparation of monthly KPI's 
 Producing and reconciling VAT statement 
 
 Qualifications:   Finance Manager: Cannock: £40,000-£45,000 
 
 CIMA/ACA/ACCA qualified with relevant manufacturing experience. Part qualified would be considered 
 Strong organisational skills and passion for meeting deadlines 
 IT literate 
 Have an analytical mind set 
 Strong communicator with the ability to present at a senior level 
 Advanced Excel skills and good systems knowledge (Navision preferable) 
 Team Management experience preferable but not essential 
 
 For more information on this excellent new role please do get in touch
