 Senior Fullstack Developer 
 Vimeo 
 Full time 
 Ness Ziona, Israel 
 Jan 7 
 Here at Vimeo Israel (formerly Magisto), we develop Vimeo's automatic video creation tools. These A.I. based tools enable any user to create a video that looks professionally edited, in a very quick, simple and cost effective way. Our tools make video creation accessible to anyone, allowing any person to express themselves with high quality videos. 
 As a Full Stack Web Developer you will develop Vimeo's web site for multiple platforms using all the latest and newest techniques; implement responsive designs, shape up UI, improve user interactions, flows and more. 
 What you’ll do: 
 
 Be part of a team of web developers working on the cutting edge, cloud based award winning video-creation platform. 
 Facilitate an agile development cycle. 
 Architect efficient and reusable front-end systems, and design the libraries and frameworks. 
 Collaborate with Product Designers, Product Managers, and other R&D members to plan, nurture, and implement an efficient and effective development process. 
 Translate UI/UX requirements into high-quality designs and implementation that grows with the needs of the business. 
 
 Skills and knowledge you should possess: 
 
 Technical Requirements: 
 Object Oriented JS 
 Deep understanding of web technologies 
 Strong CSS skills with a clean UI approach 
 React.js - must 
 Working with backend frameworks - must 
 React.js hooks approach - advantage 
 Webpack / AMD / require.js 
 Progressive web apps - advantage 
 Websockets - advantage 
 
 What should you bring to the team? 
 
 Your passion towards frontend concepts including reusability, good engineering and great user experience 
 5+ years of experience, perspective and deep understanding of the trends in the frontend world 
 Experience with leading development of significant features or projects 
 Experience in working with backend technologies 
 Extensive experience with modern FED workflows 
 Team player, Get-it-done attitude and quick learner 
 Excellent communication, collaboration and influencing skills 
 
 About us: 
 Vimeo is the world’s leading all-in-one video software solution. Our platform enables any professional, team, and organization to unlock the power of video to create, collaborate and communicate. We proudly serve our growing community of over 200 million users — from creatives to entrepreneurs to the world’s largest companies. 
 Vimeo is headquartered in New York City with offices around the world. At Vimeo, we believe our impact is greatest when our workforce of over 650 passionate, dedicated people, represents our diverse and global community. We’re proud to be an equal opportunity employer where diversity, equity and inclusion is championed in how we build our products, develop our leaders, and strengthen our culture.
