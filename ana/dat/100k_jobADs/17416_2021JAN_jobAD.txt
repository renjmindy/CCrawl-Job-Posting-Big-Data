 Career Description 
 Conductors coordinate activities of train crew on passenger or freight trains. Yardmasters review train schedules and switching orders and coordinate activities of workers engaged in railroad traffic operations, such as the makeup or breakup of trains and yard switching. They also signal engineers to begin train runs, stop trains, or change speed, using telecommunications equipment or hand signals.
