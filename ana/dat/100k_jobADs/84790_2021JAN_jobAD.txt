 JOB FUNCTIONS 
 [All jobs](/jobs?search=&location=&depart=subcat=All jobs) 
 The Bid team is responsible for understanding what Backbase clients need, developing an initial view of the solution, liaising with solution experts, then tailor the Backbase proposition to meet that need. We articulate this solution to the Backbase Sales/ Client/ Partner, and to close the deal. We handshake with delivery to ensure that the delivery team is completely aware of the proposed solution. The Bid manager will be an integral part of this unit. 
As part of the Backbase Strategy and Propositions team, you work closely with Sales, Solutions Engineering, Delivery and RnD Departments. This is a crucial role because you will be making sure our proposals result in a tailored integrated proposition, estimations, feasible project plans and alignment to delivery methodologies to meet our customer expectations. This position is a great chance to learn and grow within an amazing and recognized fintech company. 
 
 
 __You have excellent English language skills, written and spoken; 
 
 
 __You feel comfortable interacting with stakeholders within Backbase, and potential customers at various levels. Organizational and cultural sensitivity is something that comes naturally to you; 
 
 
 __You have at least 3-4 years experience in Solutions and Pre-sales world in the technology domain; 
 
 
 __Your experience in implementing Digital transformation projects is a must. You have an understanding of different SDLC methodologies (Agile, Waterfall); 
 
 
 __You have experience in implementing at least one program. Delivery experience in the digital domain of at least 5 years is a must; 
 
 
 __You are a presentation and a storytelling expert; 
 
 
 __You are creative and can visualize an end to end solution for Digital Transformation projects. You are a whiteboarding expert; 
 
 
 __You have experience in maintaining a strategic deal template library; 
 
 
 __You are able to verify that complex technical solutions & strategies are easily understandable, readable and compelling; 
 
 
 __As a subject matter expert, you use your gravitas to interact with key internal and external stakeholders and influence decision making; 
 
 
 __Your Digital Banking Business knowledge is a plus; 
 
 
 __Your APMP certification is a plus.

 THE JOB AT BACKBASE 
 You will define the Proposal (RFI and RFP) Strategy:  by collaborating closely with various sales representatives to ensure proper understanding of customer needs, priorities, competition analysis and define strategy of the proposal. Proposal Strategy frameworks creation and maintenance is also your responsibility. 
 RFI & RFP Response Management:  you will take the end to end responsibility of the RFP response and make a tailored and compelling proposition to win the opportunity in the Sales stages. 
 Estimations & Project Plans:  you will collaborate with the Customer Success team to ensure achievable plans and estimations are sent during the Sales process. 
 Collaboration:  you will work with all internal stakeholders, to ensure all proposal documents state Backbase qualifications and information accurately and favorably. You will also take on continuous improvement of estimations with delivery and RnD teams. 
 Knowledge Management:  you will contribute to continuous improvement and development of proposal management best practices. That includes creating and maintaining a knowledge repository of winning proposal documents & templates, estimation frameworks, and delivery methodologies.  apply now

 BACKBASE IN ATLANTA 
  __Atlanta 
 North America is one of our high-growth markets and home to our Atlanta product implementation hub. Five years ago we started with less than 6 employees in a small corner space. Now, we’ve grown into a close-knit team of over 100+ employees occupying two spacious and inviting office suites. This is Backbase North America’s Headquarters. 
 We’re situated in the most desirable ‘Inside the Perimeter’ location. Backbase Atlanta is based in the heart of Midtown Atlanta at 10 10th Street NW. If you are seeking breathtaking views of the Atlanta Skyline, look no further. We have easy access to restaurants, hotels, parks, theaters, neighborhood events, and more. This property is energy-star labeled with efficient and robust fiber optic options, redundant power, lobby wi-fi, and shared collaboration space. Take advantage of the onsite gym, a community lounge/game room with pool, ping pong, foosball, the conference center, communal lobby, and outdoor terrace…just to name a few. 
 #atl4life 
 CULTURE 
 Our Social Group, Basecamp, will keep you entertained with nonstop fun. They make sure that there’s something fun for everyone. Take a load off and join us for our monthly Happy Hour at one of the 33 restaurants within two blocks. There is lunchtime themed trivia and game nights with board games or your choice of Xbox/Playstation/Switch game competitions. 
 Wait, there’s more! 
 Theatre performances, summer parties, holiday parties, and baby showers for moms and dads. Plus we love to visit Top Golf, The Painted Duck Bowling and more. Of course, giving back is also important to us and we often host community service events as well. 
 insurance 
 Multiple options to choose from; you choose the plan that will work best for you and your family members. 
 lunch 
 Free daily healthy lunches, prepared by our in-house chef. Plus plenty fresh fruit, and snacks. 
 commute 
 With MARTA within a few feet away and a security access parking garage attached to our building with 835 covered parking spaces, you choose how you would like to get to the office and we will pay for either option. 
 social 
 Regular happy hours at the office with drinks, snacks and lots of laughter.
