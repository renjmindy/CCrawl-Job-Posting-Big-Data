 Warehouse Picker/Packer 
 Complete Shipping Solutions 
 __Edmonton, AB 
 Apply Now 
 Complete Shipping Solutions is a growing 3rd Party Logistics and Warehousing company looking to hire multiple people for the holiday season to pick, pack, and ship small parcel orders. 
 Shift hours can vary. 7 days a week. 
 This job posting is for seasonal positions, but high performance individuals will be considered for long term roles. 
 Our company prides itself on a customer service first attitude. We do everything possible to ensure the customer is happy and that our key performance indicators are as high as possible. 
 Individuals interested in this job will be in a high volume role picking and packing hundreds of orders per day. 
 The job entails the following: 
 Receiving product as it is sent to us by our customers. Counting all part numbers and quantities 100% accurately. 
 Putting away product and organizing the shelving as required to allow for maximum order picking efficiencies. 
 The ability to use handheld warehouse scanners and pick/pack orders in the warehouse management system. 
 Standing and Manual labor will be consistent during the work shift. 
 This is a entry level position with the opportunity to grow with our expanding company as new roles become available.
