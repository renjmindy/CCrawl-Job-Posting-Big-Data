 Job Description 
 **Zarky 's Fine Foods **is seeking high energy and self- motivated individuals to join our specialty Food Production team. We are always looking for talented staff to contribute to the growth and success of our unique offering of authentic foods. As a member of our team you will enjoy a family oriented environment that will provide meaningful work and opportunities to learn. Currently we are looking for a  Part Time Cashier Clerks  to perform the following: 
 -accurately serve customers on cash register 
 -demonstrate a pleasant demeanor when dealing with customers 
 -Must be responsible and able to handle fast paced environment 
 -Must demonstrate superior customer service skills have ability to learn cash within a grocery environment quickly 
 -Candidate must also be available throughout the day on weekdays, evenings and weekends 
 -Position will be approximately 15-20 hours per week with opportunity to transition into more hours if interested 
 -prior related experience on cash is an asset.
