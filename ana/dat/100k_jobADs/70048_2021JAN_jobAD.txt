 Company description 
 Mister Produce Ltd is a wholesale produce company servicing restaurants, hotels, schools, day care centers and caterers. 
 Job description 
 Applicants for this position would be responsible for picking orders, checking product and loading trucks. In addition general clean up would be involved. 
 This position starts at 8:00 pm in the evening until 3:30 or 4:00 am from Sunday to Friday (6 evenings per week). Candidates must be able to lift up to 50 pounds, be comfortable working in a cooler as well as being able to speak read and write in English. A good work ethic with excellent attendance is required. 
 As this is a night position, candidates who finish work at 3:30am or 4:00 must have a means to get home if they don’t have a vehicle. 
 Contract length: 6 months 
 Part-time hours: 30 per week 
 Job Types: Full-time, Part-time, Temporary 
 Salary: $14.25-$16.00 per hour
