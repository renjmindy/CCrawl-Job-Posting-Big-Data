 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 8/6 Roster - Day Shift 
 
 
 FIFO from Perth OR Residential to Karratha and Dampier 
 
 
 Casual ongoing role 
 
 
 Duties: 
 - Ordering and maintaining stock levels for all of site requirements 
 - High quantity stock takes 
 - Stock control using SAP 
 - Other duties as determined by the Workshop Supervisor 
 - Manual Labour 
 Skills and Experience: 
 - 2 years + experience working in a Mining Warehouse/ Tool Store 
 - HR Class manual driver license minimum 
 - High Risk Licence - Forklift 
 - SAP Experience 
 - Sound knowledge of Tools, MEM and Fixed Plant Equipment (Preferred)
