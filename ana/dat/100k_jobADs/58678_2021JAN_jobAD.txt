 Consultant: 
 
 
 The Finance Analyst will review processes and procedure and recommend process improvement in finance department. You must be advanced in excel with a proven record of creating reports or drawing out relevant financial data. 
 Client Details 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group are delighted to be partnered with an large reputable organisation based in Warwick. 
 Description 
 You will be responsible for; 
 
 All project related analysis 
 Supporting with current backlog of work 
 Understanding and creating financial reports 
 Communicating the financial accounts ( not producing but you must understand background) 
 Supporting the accounting team in building reports and producing data in order to provide the client with data required 
 Driving improvements and efficiency's( proposing alternatives to current processes) 
 
 Profile 
 The successful applicant will be; 
 
 Experienced in financial accounts 
 Passionate about technology 
 Confident in communicating with stakeholders 
 Extremely advanced with Excel - pivots, Vlookups and formulas 
 Part Qualified, Qualified or QBE 
 
 Job Offer 
 Competitive pro rata salary 
 Remote on boarding 
 Immediate start
