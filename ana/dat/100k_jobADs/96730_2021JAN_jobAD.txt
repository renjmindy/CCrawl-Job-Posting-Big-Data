 Pet Groomer 
 Purrdy Paws Pet Grooming Spa (Glebe and Stittsville) 
 __Ottawa, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Purrdy Paws Pet Grooming Spa voted Ottawa's favourite grooming salon 2019 And 2020 by Faces Magazine Awards. Also a Top Choice Awards 2020 winner is seeking: 
 Experienced Pet Groomer with minimum 1 year hands-on pet grooming experience. 
 Two grooming shop locations : one in the Glebe and one in Stittsville. 
 Must be able to work independently or as part of a team on all breeds of canines. 
 Knowledge of variety of breeds and trims both breed specific and pet and able to provide add on services such as nail grinding and teeth brushing. 
 Required to have excellent communication skills with clients and able to educate clients on how to maintain their pet's coat and health. 
 Strong standard of ethical grooming (humanity over vanity) 
 Must possess patience and a love of pets with a strong desire to grow within themselves and within the team. 
 Creative grooming, cat grooming and handstripping assets but can be taught to interested candidates. 
 No drama and a positive attitude is a must. 
 Please reply with resume and portfolio. (discretion assured) 
 Successful candidate will perform a paid grooming interview on one dog of managements choice. 
 Employed team members enjoy pet food/product at cost, medical/dental/injury benefits, flexible work schedule, bathing allowance after hours for their own pets, a receptionist to book/track/cashout their appointments, a bather for busier days, full tipout (very generous clients), opportunities for further training/growth, local events and trade shows and a positive and fun team and work environment.
