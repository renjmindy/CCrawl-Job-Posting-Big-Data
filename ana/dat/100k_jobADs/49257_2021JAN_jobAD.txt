 About Pine Park Health 
 Pine Park Health opens clinics in assisted living communities and uses technology to keep residents healthy. 
 About the role 
 Pine Park is hiring an intern for Marketing / Partnership positions. 
 As a Marketing / Partnership intern, you will partner with patients, assisted living communities, and Medicare Advantage plans. The purpose of the work is to expand our services to a new area. 
 Responsibilities and Duties: 
 You will interview new patients to understand what made them choose Pine Park Health. You will work to define user personas. You will document the patient's onboarding process. You will work to improve marketing materials - websites, brochures, etc. You will use tools like Airbale, Process Street, Notion, Coda, Zapier, Strikingly. 
 Qualifications: 
 Education level: Junior or Senior. Experience: Worked for a startup before Specific skills: Good writing and communication skills. Certifications: No Licenses: No Physical abilities: High energy
