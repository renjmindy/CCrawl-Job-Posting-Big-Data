 JOB POSTING: 
Fiberglass Laminator (Shelton, WA) 
Open Period: 1 December 2020 -- Until Filled 
Salary Range: $16-17 hour DOE with potential for additional bonuses and benefits 
Location: Shelton, WA 
Hours of Operation: Monday through Thursday: 6:30 AM - 5:15 PM 
 Pro Glass, Inc., is a well-established, high paced manufacturing company who successfully produces high-quality fiberglass products to utilities. 
 Hours: Monday -Thursday 10-hour shifts which, means a three-day weekend. We do have occasional needs for overtime which is paid at time and a half. 
 We are seeking highly energetic, full-time laborers to laminate fiberglass products. We have an emphasis on positive attitudes, teamwork, attention to detail and an ability to quickly learn. 
If you have additional Knowledge, Skills and Abilities (KSA’s) relating to the Fiberglass Laminator position or other labor experience please, emphasize on your application or resume.

 - Ability to lift up to 50 lbs. without assistance, 100 lbs. with assistance, and standing, moving, and bending for extended periods of time
