 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently supporting a private equity backed business based in Stafford who are recruiting for a qualified accountant to join them as a divisional Finance Manager. 
 Key Duties, Finance Manager, Stafford 
 
 Month End Close Process (including preparation of management accounts pack and balance sheet reconciliations) 
 Preparation for year end statutory audit 
 Management of Accounts Payable 
 Finance processes (including policies & procedures) 
 Management Information (including weekly/monthly KPI's) 
 Budgeting & Forecasting 
 Undertake additional project work as required 
 Assist in completion of Monthly Management Accounts, within prescribed deadlines 
 Support development of reporting weekly/monthly key performance indicators (KPIs) 
 Assist in preparation of annual Budget and longer term Plan/Forecast 
 
 Key Skills & Experience, Finance Manager, Stafford 
 
 Qualified Accountant (ACA, ACCA, CIMA) 
 Able to take on and manage all aspects of accounting function 
 Experience in a small to medium sized business with first hand experience of accounting systems 
 Ability to develop (finance) systems and processes 
 Must be a strong and effective leader & communicator. 
 Must be highly IT literate with advanced spreadsheet skills 
 
 This role offers scope for huge progression and exposure as well as all the other benefits associated with working in a PE backed business. Very realistic opportunity to be Financial Controller of a £30million T/O business within 2 years. 
 The successful candidate will receive an excellent basic salary, bonus, pension and other benefits, a highly flexible business that offers great work life balance whilst still giving you the opportunity to truly progress your career in finance. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
