 Sweet Baby Jason! it's a Full-time job 
 Saatchi & Saatchi NY  is looking for a Senior Art Director in New York, NY, USA. 
 About 
 We are looking for a Senior Art Director to join our highly awarded creative department. This person should be curious, competitive and ambitious. 
 They immerse themselves in everyday brands, culture and the creative industry. They may have already picked up some awards in their career so far and they are keen to make a name for themselves. 
 This person obsesses about the details. Someone who can think outside of their own comfort zone and be flexible in a fast paced environment. This person will be a conceptual 360 thinker across a variety of briefs. 
 A few more details about qualifications: 
 4-5 years of art direction experience for well-known brands. 
Has a strong POV but also has an open mind; is ready to listen and learn. 
Has strong technical design skills across a variety of programs. 
Understands the “best in class” use of each platform. You know what it means to be make something interesting, engaging and creative in the world today. 
Ability to concept innovative ideas and tactics that people will talk about. Always envisioning the potential outcomes of each idea; thinking through the earned media headlines, potential media responses, consumer reactions and tweets etc. 
Someone who always brings one extra idea that nobody saw coming, something bold, simple and perfect, the cherry on top. 
A versatile team player; able to work alone or in a team and able to take direction from creative directors. 
Participates in new business pitches 
Embodies our positive, diverse, ambitious and respectful culture. 
Someone who will love and champion our work.
