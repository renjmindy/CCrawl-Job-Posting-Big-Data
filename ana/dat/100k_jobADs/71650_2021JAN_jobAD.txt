 Grader Operator (Flat Rate) 
 Job description 
 We are currently seeking an experienced & ticketed Grader Operator for a major project near Longreach in Central Queensland township of Barcoo. 
 Ideally we are looking for someone local to Longreach or surrounding area or willing to FIFO on a 3 weeks on and 1 week off roster. 
 Must have a current Grader ticket & at least 2 years experience including operation of 140M Grader. 
 Other Civil Machinery tickets would be an advantage. 
 Construction white card is mandatory for this position.
