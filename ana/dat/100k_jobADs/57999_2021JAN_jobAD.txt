 Interim Head of Commercial Finance - West Midlands - 6 months - £500 to £600 per day LTD 
 My client operates in the FMCG/Retail space and has an immediate requirement for Interim Head of Commercial Finance to join their team for an initial period of 6 months. Reporting directly to the CFO you will partner all teams supporting trading activity adding commercial value through pricing, sales, and promotional activity to drive margin growth. The successful candidate will be a career Retail/FMCG specialist available on short notice. 
 Interim Head of Commercial Finance - Role in Brief 
 
 Support the commercial agenda 
 Work with the CFO to maximise financial support to the business 
 Support pricing, sales, and promotional activity through detailed industry analysis 
 Drive finance business partnering through commercial acumen 
 Add value through analysis and insight 
 Drive a continuous improvement environment 
 Coordinate management reporting activities 
 Review and maintain KPI'S to track overall skew performance 
 Analyse and influence business strategy 
 
 Interim Head of Commercial Finance - Required Experience 
 
 Qualified to ACA/CIMA/ACCA with appropriate post qualification experience in a relevant industry 
 Strong academic background and experience with fast-paced, large volume, dynamic operations 
 Significant experience of trade accounting 
 Excellent numeracy and analytical problem-solving capabilities 
 Evidence of impact through business partnering 
 Must drive a performance culture 
 Broad commercial understanding 
 Ability to communicate at all levels 
 Very capable relationship builder 
 A confident and positive persona and approach to business and colleagues 
 Willing and motivated to go the extra mile to deliver 
 
 To be considered for this role please submit your cv for review. As the role is private and confidential no details will be released until applicant's CV's have been reviewed and positively matched against the client requirements.
