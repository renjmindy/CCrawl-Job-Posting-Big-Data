 About the role 
 We are currently seeking experienced Civil Labourers for various sites across Townsville,QLD 
 Duties and Responsibilities: 
 General Labour duties 
 Site clean up 
 Road construction 
 Tasks will vary depending on site, candidate must be willing to adapt to change of duties if required. 
 Qualifications/skills: 
 Strong Civil construction background 
 Concrete experience- Desirable 
 Road Construction Experience -Desirable 
 White Card 
 Full Availability for an immediate start 
 Drivers licence
