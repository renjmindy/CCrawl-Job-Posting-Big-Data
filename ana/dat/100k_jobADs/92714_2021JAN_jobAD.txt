 PHYSIOTHERAPISTS 
 
 Working In Australia 
 Australia 
 Dec 07, 2020 
 
 Full time Healthcare 
 Job Description 
 Physiotherapy skills are in demand in Australia. 
 With access to world-class facilities and ongoing professional development opportunities, it’s a great time to advance your career in Australia. Work with highly skilled teams and enjoy work/life balance! 
 You will be enthusiastic, passionate and committed to providing a quality rehabilitation service to patients including assessments, management and reporting that reflects best practice intervention on a patients’ rehab journey. 
 Requirements: 
 
 Relevant tertiary qualification (BPHTY, BHSc or equivalent) 
 Overseas qualified physios must be eligible for registration with the Physiotherapy Board of Australia 
 Minimum of 2 years  ’ post-graduate physiotherapy practice experience 
 Fluency and comprehension in English to be able to communicate effectively (written and verbal) with patients, colleagues, employers and other health professionals **** 
 The ability to work both autonomously and collaboratively with other professionals 
 Ability to be adaptable, flexible and solution focused 
 
 Working In have 20 years’ experience in helping overseas professionals live and work in Australia.
