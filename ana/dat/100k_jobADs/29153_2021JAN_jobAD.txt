 Superjob: half of Russians manage to combine work with personal life 
 
 November 15, 2020 
 No Comments 
 
 Almost half of Russians (48%) believe that they have managed to achieve a balance between work and personal life, according to a survey by the Superjob job search service. Research results lead  RBK … 41% of the respondents adhere to the opposite point of view: 38% pay more attention to their career, 3% - to their personal life. 
 Young people are the best to maintain a work-life balance: 58% of respondents under the age of 24 said this. For comparison, among people 45 years old, 45% were able to achieve balance. 
 Priory depends on the level of salary, the survey showed. For example, among those who earn less than 29 thousand rubles. per month, 32% pay more attention to work. Among those surveyed with an income of 80 thousand rubles. 43% are more focused on their careers. 
 Which professions are better at balancing work and personal life: 
 
 lawyers - 58%; 
 accountants - 54%; 
 economists - 53%; 
 secretaries, account managers and call center operators - 51% each. 
 
 Who finds it harder to balance: 
 
 doctors - 39%; 
 marketing managers - 40%; 
 sales managers, PR managers and managers - 41% each. 
 
 _The study was conducted throughout Russia from October 18 to November 9. The survey involved 4.5 thousand people over 18 years old who have a permanent job - 200 representatives of 24 professional groups. _
