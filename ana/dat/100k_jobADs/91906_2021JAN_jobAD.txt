 Have you previously worked in distribution within a manufacturing environment? I have a Warehouse Team Leader role to work at a brand-new facility so a candidate who has worked for a "start-up" would preferable… 
 A leading distributor who supply to the automotive sector are looking to recruit a Warehouse Team Leader for their site near Coventry. My client is a rapidly growing business with significant career opportunities available for the right people, as they grow rapidly, they are acutely aware of the need for succession planning. 
 You will play a key role in ensuring the highest standards are achieved, you will provide supervision, support and leadership to a small team onsite. You will lead a team in ensuring that stock is received, stored, packed and transported to the company's high standards, dealing with any issues in the first instance and escalating only when it is necessary. You will communicate on performance issues and identify the training required, before personally coaching and developing your team to improve performance. 
 To succeed in the role of Warehouse Team Leader you will need to demonstrate warehousing and transport knowledge within a manufacturing environment. You will manage and develop your team in this fast-paced environment, motivating the team daily to reach operational KPIs. A candidate with FLT experience and who is comfortable ensuring all MHE is used correctly in accordance to H&S guidelines and legislation would be beneficial. 
 The successful candidate will receive a salary up to £24,000. The organisation is moving forward with pace and as a result, career progression opportunities exist for talented & hardworking people 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
