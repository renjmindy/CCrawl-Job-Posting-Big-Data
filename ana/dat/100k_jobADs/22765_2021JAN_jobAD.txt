 Live in Caregiver Job for Canada 
 Posted on  January 5, 2010  by  caregiver 
 A Canadian employer is hoping to recruit a Filipino caregiver who is qualified for  live in caregiver job . This live in caregiver job for Canada has a job order that was approved for recruitment by the POEA last December 21 for a recruitment agency located in Malate, Manila. The chosen applicant for the said live in caregiver job will be working right in the comfort of the employer’s home and will have the opportunity to reside permanently in Canada in the future. 
 To apply for the above mentioned live in caregiver job for Canada, all interested applicants must proceed directly to the office of the recruiting agency to submit their application. For further information on the qualifications and requirements for the said live in caregiver job, you can contact the recruiting agency in their contact infos stated below. 
 Luzern International Manpower Services Corp. 
1695 Unit 3 Town Homes, F. Benitez Street 
Malate, Manila 
 Tel No: 3030212 
Email Add: luzmanosa18@hotmail.com
