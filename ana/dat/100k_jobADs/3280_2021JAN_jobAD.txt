 UI/UX Artist 
 Hipster Whale Apply for this Job 
 Posted 
 January 4, 2021 
 Full Time 
 Anywhere 
 Remote 
 ** This position has been closed. 
** 
Hipster Whale is seeking a talented and experienced UI / UX Artist to join our team. This is a full time role. Ideally, you’ll be Melbourne based and already hold a visa allowing you to work in Australia. We’ll consider a remote position for the right candidate if you’re willing to be available for crossover with Australian business hours.  ‍ 
 ‍ 
 RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 Work with the team to define functionality and usability requirements 
 Mock-up wireframes & concepts to illustrate design solutions with the team 
 Create clear and appealing UI graphic assets 
 Test and iterate upon design implementation 
 Contribute to improving the overall game experience  ‍ 
 
 ‍ 
 REQUIREMENTS 
 
 Proven experience in a similar role, ideally in the games industry 
 A portfolio which showcases a healthy balance of form vs. function 
 A solid understanding of flow and UX 
 Strong graphic design skills 
 Experience working in a team environment, with strong communication skills 
 Able to incorporate constructive feedback and take/give direction well 
 Strong Adobe Suite skills 
 Working knowledge of Unity 
 
 ‍ 
 WORK CULTURE 
 We offer flexible working conditions, a lovely light-filled office right near Flinders Street Station (don’t worry, we also have blinds if darkness is more your thing), and training and development opportunities. Team happiness and fulfillment is essential for our business to be successful, and we know that means a respectful work/life balance. 
 We are an equal opportunity employer and encourage diverse applications. 
 If you can tick most of the requirements for a role but not all,  please do contact us . We are always interested to see candidates who could grow into an advertised role. Don't let the list of attributes scare you off if you feel a role is really you. 
 ‍ 
 HOW TO APPLY 
 Please email us at  jobs@hipsterwhale.com  with [Name – UI Artist] in the subject line. 
 The email should include: 
 
 A cover letter explaining your suitability for the role 
 Your location & visa status 
 Your CV containing a link to your portfolio 
 Link to a specific piece of your work that you would like to highlight as relevant to this role 
 
 We will do our best to respond individually to all applicants. We really look forward to seeing your work! 
 Applications close Tuesday, 12 January 2021. 
 ‍ 
 ‍ 
 ABOUT HIPSTER WHALE  ‍ 
 Hipster Whale is an entirely independent Melbourne based studio, founded in 2014. Hipster Whale’s first release,  Crossy Road   ,  was a viral success and has been downloaded hundreds of millions of times. Hipster Whale continues to make bright and funny games with broad appeal. 
 Hipster Whale released  PAC-MAN 256  with Bandai Namco in 2015,  Disney Crossy Road  in 2016 with Disney,  Piffle  in 2018 with Mighty Games, and most recently  Crossy Road Castle  in 2020.
