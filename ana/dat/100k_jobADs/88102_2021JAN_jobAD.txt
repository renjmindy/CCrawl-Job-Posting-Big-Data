 How it Works 
 Finding work online has never been easier, all you have to do is signup, create profile and you are ready to submit proposals for jobs. Clients automatically recieve proposal notification and select bidders based on proposals, freelaner's profile. 
 
 How do I Get Paid 
 As soon as your awarded a project, invoice is automatically created for the client to deposit funds. We provide an  escrow based  system where you money is held safely. Once project is completed you can withdraw funds to your bank account.
