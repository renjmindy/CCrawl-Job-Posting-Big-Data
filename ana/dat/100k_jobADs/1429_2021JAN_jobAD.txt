 We are currently looking to hire an Associate Digital Producer. 
 The Associate Digital Producer helps to oversee the full life cycle and efficient delivery of projects from discovery to deployment/release on assigned digital or integrated projects. The Digital Producer is also responsible for integrating with external development teams and other third-party vendors in order to ensure project delivery on time and budget, meeting client expectations. Excellent communication skills and problem-solving attitude are key. 
 Roles/Responsibilities: 
 
 Support the production teams in driving projects to completion within a given scope, budget and timeframe 
 Liaise with many stakeholders, team members, production vendors and media partners to assess project requirements and deliver production-ready files 
 Assist producers with daily project tasks including scheduling, generating estimates, creating status reports, creating asset trackers 
 Organize and recap internal meetings and ensure tasks involving the project team are complete from concept to delivery 
 Assist with preparing technical and bid specifications, estimates, gathering of needed assets and brief studio staff on project requirements 
 Experience with QA and testing of deliverables prior to deployment/delivery on multiple devices/operating systems 
 Assistance with submission of purchase orders, invoices, expense reports and tracking of billing to finance 
 Experience prepping, organizing and archiving project files and relevant project 
 Enforce & maintain digital asset management on agency server 
 Help the team vet production partners and vendors and be proactive about planning and organizing meetings and screenings 
 
 Requirements/Skills: 
 
 Bachelor’s degree, preferably in advertising or media 
 1-2 years previous experience in a production role, preferably in an agency setting or in-house production studio 
 Effective communicator—must be able to clearly and concisely communicate to agency staff, 3rd party vendors, and sometimes clients 
 Proficiency in project management software such as Work Front, Asana, Basecamp 
 Familiarity with managing/organizing and trafficking large amounts of content and media assets 
 Experience with bug tracking software such as Codebase or Jira 
 Experience in Doubleclick Studio and Sizmek a plus 
 Strong presentation and negotiation skills 
 Comfortable with strict deadlines and balancing changing priorities on an ongoing basis 
 Pharmaceutical advertising experience a plus 
 Great communication skills and problem-solving ability, work effectively under pressure with a solutions-based focus 
 Knowledge & understanding of digital design software such as Adobe CC and Sketch, familiarity with UX prototyping tools such as Invision, Microsoft Office + Google Suite 
 Understanding of formats and outputs possible for digital, print, video assets 
 Understanding of current web and related technologies (e.g. Agile development, responsive design, SSL, etc.) 
 Key attention to detail and an appreciation for design & technology.
