 Job Description 
 Vannetukku.fi is Finland's biggest wheels, tires and tire machinery shirts. Founded in 2006, vannetukku.fi part of a group, which employs 130 people and a turnover of EUR 35 million. 
 Vannetukku.fi has increased its sales every year by about 50%. Currently Wheel Tukku website has had more than 1.4 million visitors and Wheel Wholesale sells more than 100 000 per year of tire and rim. 
 We are the market leader in budget-class tires in Finland and we import, inter alia, Goodride tires, as well as Barzetta-special rim. We are looking for wholesaler task of tires and rims wholesale and retailer of deepening cooperation. You will adopt a previous sales experience. Also, knowledge of the tire, it is desirable, but not necessary. 
 A task which requires traveling to client at an average of three days a week. The heavy travel days of residency happy to central Finland. We offer top products and youthful atmosphere at increasing wholesale distribution of tires and rims.
