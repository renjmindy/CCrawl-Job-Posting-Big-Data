 Description 
 BOMBARDIER 
 At Bombardier, our employees work together to evolve mobility worldwide - one good idea at a time. If you have a good idea, we’ll provide the environment where it will thrive and grow into a great product or customer experience. Your ideas are our fuel. 
 In your role, you will: 
- 
Train and coach managers and their teams to build a culture of continuous improvement incorporating the principles of Lean 
 - 
Ensure the deployment and maintenance of the Achieving Excellence System, its processes and tools (5S, TPM, SMED, Kanban, Andon, Hoshin Kanri, etc.). 
 - 
Support managers in their annual strategic planning exercise and in their daily performance management 
 - 
Support managers in setting up their performance measurement system and dashboard (Indicators, targets, charts, etc.) 
 - 
Train and be an advisor to managers and employees in their problem-solving and help to process problems to resolved them quickly and effectively; 
 - 
Support the required change management; 
 - 
Identify wastage elimination opportunities and conduct Value Chain Improvement Workshops (in Kaizen mode) ensuring the tangible benefits sought by the corporate strategy; 
 - 
Manage improvement projects with multidisciplinary teams 
 Qualifications 
 As our ideal candidate, 
 - 
You have five (5) to eight (8) years of experience in the management of improvement or strategic projects (Kaizen mode, industrial engineering, etc.) and the deployment of a Lean culture in a manufacturing and office environment 
 - 
You have a good knowledge of a Strategic Planning process 
 - 
You have excellent problem-solving skills (Six Sigma, Kepner-Tregoe, etc.) 
 - 
 You have knowledge of performance improvement tools (example:  Lean, Daily Management System, Performance Indicators and Measurement System, etc.) 
 - 
You possess the capacity of organization and rigor in the accomplishment of the tasks entrusted. 
 - 
You have the ability to work in a context of transformation and resistance to change 
 Bombardier is an equal opportunity employer and encourages women, Aboriginal people, persons with disabilities and members of visible minorities to apply. 
 Whether your candidacy is moving on to the next step of the hiring process or not, we will keep you informed by email or by phone. Join us at careers.bombardier.com
