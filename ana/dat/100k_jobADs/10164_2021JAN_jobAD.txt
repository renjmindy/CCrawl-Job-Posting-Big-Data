 Home Care Jobs 
 Posted on  April 29, 2009  by  caregiver 
 Home care is an assortment of services for people of all ages that requires health or supportive care provided in the patient’s home by a home care provider. And to provide them with their required level of home care, a home health care company that offers 24 hour health and support services to their clients, is looking for individuals with the passion in caring for others. 
 The said home care company is operating 26 home care centres in different communities across Ontario all the way up to Alberta in Canada. Currently, the home care company is a proud partner in providing home care solutions to more than 80,000 home care clients and employs over 4,000 staff members on a full time and casual basis. The home care company also boasts of their highly skilled staff that provides all levels of home care services to a multitude of clients. And because of their dedication to their chosen career, the said home care company boasts of tremendous progress in the home care business over the past 30 years. Now, the said home care company is considered an important part of Canada’s health care system. 
 Over the past several years, as Canada’s population continues to grow, the demand for home care providers also increases and that’s the main reason why the said home care company is looking for qualified individuals to fill up the vacancy in home care jobs. So if you think you are qualified for the home care jobs vacancy and have the passion and commitment in caring for others, feel free to contact them by logging in to their website at www.homecarejobs.ca/aboutus/contactus.html or contact them through mail or personal visit in the specified address below. 
 ParaMed Home Health Care 
3000 Steeles Avenue East, Suite #700 
Markham, ON 
L3R 9W2
