 Manufacturing Technician - London, Ontario 
 High Park Company is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Tilray, a global leader in cannabis cultivation, processing, and distribution. Tilray serves patients in Canada and around the world with a diverse range of pharmaceutical-grade medical cannabis products as High Park Company focuses on creating distinctive products for adult Canadian consumers. 
 About High Park Company 
High Park Company was established to develop, sell, and distribute inspiring and iconic adult-use cannabis brands. 
 About Tilray 
Tilray is a global pioneer in cannabis research, cultivation, processing and distribution currently serving tens of thousands of patients in ten countries spanning five continents. 
 Looking to develop your career at the forefront of a rapidly expanding industry? 
 Reporting to the Manufacturing Manager, the Manufacturing Technician one will successfully learn Downstream Operations (Core Skills & Competencies) while working alongside experts within 12-18 months of continuous employment. 
 Role and Responsibilities: 
Manufacturing 
Whole Plant Breakdown 
Milling/Blending 
Packaging 
Finished Flower 
House Blends 
Extracts (Capsules, Droppers) 
Other 
Facilities & Equipment Cleaning 
Plant/Product Inventory 
General stocking of equipment and supplies 
 Qualifications and Education Requirements: 
Must have High School Diploma or equivalent. 
Basic computer skills considered an asset 
Positive outlook, willingness to learn and contribute to company success 
Maintain confidentiality and attain necessary skills/competencies 
Ability to take direction and follow all company policies/procedures 
Dependable and punctual 
Positive contribution and personal commitment/responsibility 
Must have access to transportation and the ability to travel to the Petrolia location as needed for support 
Must have the ability to obtain A/RPIC status as per Health Canada Access to Cannabis for Medical Purposes Regulations
