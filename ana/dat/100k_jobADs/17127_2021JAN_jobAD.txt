 CFAA Certified Fire Alarm Technicians 
 J.D.COLLINS FIRE PROTECTION 
 __Vaughan, ON 
 Apply Now 
 J.D.COLLINS FIRE PROTECTION  is an innovative, customer-oriented leader in the fire protection and life safety services sector. Celebrating its 27th year in business, J.D.Collins Fire Protection continues to expand and is looking for conscientious, customer-focused team players to be a part of the company. 
 We are seeking  JUNIOR, INTERMEDIATE & SENIOR CFAA CERTIFIED FIRE ALARM TECHNICIANS  to join our growing team. 
 RESPONSIBILITIES  include assisting with and performing various duties related to fire alarm systems and life safety equipment such as: 
 - servicing and testing 
 - inspections [monthly, bi-monthly, quarterly, annual] 
 - preparation of technical synopsis for follow up purposes 
 - performing small scale hose and extinguisher inspections 
 - performing small scale emergency lighting inspections 
 - on call rotation is required 
 QUALIFICATIONS  include: 
 - valid/current Ontario Drivers License with clean abstract 
 - CFAA certification with strong knowledge of CAN/ULC S536/04 & CAN/ULC S537-04 
 - experience performing fire alarm inspections, field repairs, troubleshooting, ground fault tracing 
 - strong working knowledge of major manufacturers' systems 
 - mobile platform/fall arrest certification an asset 
 - excellent written and verbal communication skills 
 - enthusiastic, detail oriented team player 
 COMPENSATION  : 
 - top wages - pay commensurate with experience 
 - company vehicle 
 - company uniform 
 - company cell phone 
 - benefits package 
 - employer matched RRSP package 
 Job Type: Full-time
