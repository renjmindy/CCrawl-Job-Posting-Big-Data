 Customer Success 
 Our Customer Success Team is comprised of true relationship builders who drive measurable customer results in a human way. 
 
 Where else do you have the opportunity to build partnerships and drive business results with some of the world’s largest companies? Right here at Workhuman. Our Customer Success Team is committed to helping our customers build and grow their recognition solutions by truly listening, understanding and partnering to deliver their business goals. 
 We focus on optimizing the customer experience and our Customer Success Team brings years of experience successfully delivering our suite of human applications that positively impact our customers employees globally. 
 Whether you are just starting your career in a client-facing environment, or you’re a seasoned pro looking to take the next step – join us in our Framingham or Dublin offices. Help us elevate company cultures worldwide. 
 If you’re driven to achieve results, are an outstanding communicator and relationship builder, thrive in a fast-paced and technical environment, and truly want to make people’s work lives better – we want to hear from you. We’re looking for the best in the business.
