 Your new company a leader in their field have an opportunity for an experienced Infrastructure Lead to join the team to grow and maintain the company's IT infrastructure you will also be tasked to deliver projects to take the infrastructure to the next level. 
 Your new role 
 As one of our Infrastructure Engineers you will be responsible for:- 
 
 
 Delivering a number of innovative Infrastructure projects 
 
 
 Provide expertise in day to day BAU 
 
 
 MUST HAVE- Drive the Azure/O365 Migrations and Implementations 
 
 
 Deliver changes using agreed frameworks and methodologies, ensuring a smooth transition into BAU 
 
 
 Assist with team training and ensuring documentation and support processes have been created/updated 
 
 
 Providing the service desk with a point of escalation for complex infrastructure related issues 
 
 
 What you'll need to succeed 
 The successful Infrastructure Engineer will possess a combination of the following skills:- 
 
 
 Knowledge of VMWare Server and Desktop Virtualisation Technologies 
 
 
 Experience with Microsoft Cloud Services like O365 And Azure 
 
 
 Proven track record leading and supporting complex IT Infrastructure projects 
 
 
 Knowledge of various infrastructure services including Microsoft Server, Active Directory, DNS, DHCP, GPO, PowerShell, Exchange 
 
 
 Telephony and networking experience (Meraki) 
 
 
 The role is paying £45,000 with option for some flexible home working and excellent company benefits.
