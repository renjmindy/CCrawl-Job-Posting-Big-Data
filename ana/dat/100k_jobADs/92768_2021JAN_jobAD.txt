 Position Overview 
 As a platform develops engineer you will be responsible to design, evolve and maintain developer and production platforms in Paidy. That includes CI/CD pipelines, test and production environments.

 Work with developers and platform engineers to continuously refine and improve our Change Management and Incident Management processes 
 Participate in Level 2 production support. 
 Effectively communicate status, issues, and risks in a precise and timely manner.
