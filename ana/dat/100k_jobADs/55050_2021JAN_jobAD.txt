 Mining Systems Technician 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights: 
 
 
 Ongoing Work 
 
 
 8:6 Roster 
 
 
 FIFO from Perth or Busselton 
 
 
 About our Client 
 Rio Tinto operates the worlds largest integrated portfolio of iron ore assets. These assets include a world-class, integrated network of 15 iron ore mines, four port facilities, a 1,700 kilometre rail network and related infrastructure. Rio Tinto are expanding operations in the Pilbara to epic proportions while introducing next generation technologies to deliver greater efficiency, lower production costs and improved health, safety and environmental performance. 
 About the Role 
 Reporting to the site-based Mining Systems Supervisor; you will provide direct support and be required to respond quickly when faults are identified to the sites mining system applications. 
 With the introduction of the Operations Centre, located in Perth, you will also act as the interface between site-based customers and the Operations Centre production systems to ensure ongoing user requirements are being delivered in a consistent and professional manner. 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Manual C Class Australian issued Drivers Licence (minimum) 
 
 
 Trade qualification in Auto Electrical, Radio Communication or Industrial IT 
 
 
 Extensive knowledge in Mining systems application such as Modular Mining (Dispatch, MineCare and Powerview) 
 
 
 Strong working knowledge of Komatsu Autonomous Haulage System 
 
 
 High Precision GPS 
 
 
 Demonstrated experience with Fleet Management Systems 
 
 
 Mobile radio network (WIFI) 
 
 
 Experience in Mobile radio network (LTE) and CCTV camera systems 
 
 
 Mine Production Reporting Solutions (Oracle an SQL based experience) 
 
 
 Experience in Pit Technology Solutions (i.e. Survey Technology, Wall Monitoring Solutions) 
 
 
 Experience with Autonomous Mining Systems 
 
 
 Open cabling license and endorsements 
 
 
 Proficient in the use of SAP
