 Principal/ Associate Structural Engineer 
 Principal/ Associate Structural Engineer 
 
 Location 
 
 Leicester, Leicestershire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Built Environment 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £50000.00 - £65000 per annum 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Rickie Barnfield 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 rickiebarnfield@workwithglee.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH21159_1596617077 
 
 Published: 
 
 6 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-08-24 
 
 Startdate: 
 
 ASAP 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Your new company,  are a forward thinking consultancy with multiple offices across the UK, they focus on civil, structural engineering as well as surveying to offer a unique selling point to their clients. the reason the role has become available is due to the structural director for the business retiring at the end of the year 
 Your new role,  you will take the lead of a small team based in Leicester to start with gaining more and more responsibility at the time goes on. working closely with the current director getting a good hand over of clients and key relationship for the business till he leaves. then when he leaves you will take charge of the structural division for the business and once you become director level you will be rewarded with equity in the business for your hard work. 
 In return,  you will receive a salary of up to £65000 depending on the level you go in to the business at with fantastic health benefits as well as personal ones with working from home as and when needed long after the pandemic is over. this is a truly fantastic opportunity for the right person so get in touch for more information.
