 Job Description 
 UPS is hiring individuals to work as  Full-Time/Seasonal/Temp Tractor Trailer Yard Shifters . This is not an over the road driving position but Tractor Trailer driving experience is required. This job requires continuous coupling and uncoupling of tractor trailer and shifting of equipment. Primary time will be spent moving and maneuvering tractor trailer equipment into position for the tractor trailer and package handling operations. May include other 'work as directed' activities.
