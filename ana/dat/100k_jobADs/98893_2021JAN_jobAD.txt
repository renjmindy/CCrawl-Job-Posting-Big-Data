 MMU Operator 
 Job description 
 
 
 $49.72-$54.78/ hour 
 
 
 Accommodation & meals Supplied 
 
 
 BIBO OR DIDO 
 
 
 ABOUT OUR CLIENT 
 Our client is Australia's largest coal miner and exporter, the world's largest supplier to the sea borne coking coal market and largest coal producer and supplier of metallurgical coal. The client has several mine sites across the Bowen Basin consisting primarily of open cut mines 
 ABOUT THE ROLE 
 Seeking experienced MMU Operators to commence in the Drill & Blast Department of a mine located near Moranbah. 6/6 roster (3 days, 3 nights, 6 off) BIBO - Mackay. 
 DUTIES & RESPONSBILBITIES 
 
 
 Operate MPU Truck 
 
 
 Open Cut Shot Firing Duties 
 
 
 General Blast Crew Duties 
 
 
 Support Shot Firing on bench during loading 
 
 
 Operate Wheel Loader/ Bobcat 
 
 
 SKILLS & EXPERIENCE 
 
 
 SSAN Clearance 
 
 
 Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Standard 11 or equivalent 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Previous Experience in Blast 
 
 
 CULTURE & BENEFITS 
 
 
 6/6 Roster 
 
 
 BIBO OR DIDO 
 
 
 Camp & Meals Supplied 
 
 
 Super Paid on all Rostered Hours 
 
 
 6 Monthly Retention Bonus
