 Here at mthree, we pair talented graduates with leading global businesses. We're always on the lookout for ambitious graduates to join the mthree Alumni Graduate Program, kick-starting your tech career within an entry-level Software Developer role and working onsite with a leading investment bank, located in Singapore. 
 Our 24-month graduate program begins with 6 weeks of paid training and career coaching, where you'll learn everything you need to hit the ground running as a Software Developer. 
 What you'll do: 
 If successful, you'll work with one of our clients as a software developer designing, building, testing and maintaining scalable and stable off the shelf application or custom-built technology solutions. 
 
 Participate and contribute in the entire implementation process for new applications and enhancements to existing applications 
 Provide input on the overall solution design 
 Build and test applications 
 Meet with business partners to understand business needs 
 Conduct root cause analysis and implement basic performance tuning 
 Responsible for ensuring the solution meets business needs 
 
 What you need: 
 
 B.S., Computer Science, MIS or within a STEM related subject 
 3.0 GPA or higher 
 Permanent right to work in Singapore 
 Development experience 
 Solid software engineering principles (data structures, OO, design patterns, multithreading) 
 Good trouble shooting and debugging skills 
 Understanding of COBOL, JAVA, JSP, Supertool, PL/SQL, UNIX shell scripting, etc. Amisys, Facets or other claims management system experience a plus. 
 Working knowledge of database concepts (SQL, stored procs, schema design) 
 Understanding of the formal software development lifecycle (SDLC) 
 Understanding of test-driven development 
 
 What you'll receive: 
 
 Fully paid, in-depth and interactive training with a diverse group of like-minded graduates, delivered by experts with working knowledge of the field. 
 A competitive graduate salary 
 Pay increases every 6 months 
 A flexible benefits package including gym membership 
 Ongoing training and pastoral care 
 Invaluable industry experience and the opportunity to start a career in a desirable and competitive industry 
 
 Important information on Diversity 
 We take great pride in celebrating the diversity of every individual who contributes to making mthree the company it is today and will be in the future. 
We value diversity both within mthree and with our partner companies, and we're proud to provide an environment where all our colleagues can flourish by promoting a strong culture of equality and inclusion. We never want people to change - only add to the amazing mix of people that work for mthree. 
 Should you require any reasonable adjustments during the recruitment process and beyond, then please email lexi.cameron@mthree.com to discuss the options available to you. All conversations will be confidential and will never directly impact your application with us. Please note that by emailing this address, it will not count as an application and only emails regarding reasonable adjustments will be responded to.
