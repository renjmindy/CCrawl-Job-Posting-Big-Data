 Job Seekers 
 Work with exciting companies in Australia’s most dynamic industries 
 For more than two decades WorkPac has helped thousands of Australians achieve rewarding long-term careers with some of the nation’s most reputable companies. 
 We understand that finding and securing a job or realising a career change is often a stressful process. We’re here to help make the process a little easier for you. 
 When you register with WorkPac you can enjoy 
 
 
 Direct access to Australia's best employers 
 
 
 Industry specialised recruitment consultants 
 
 
 Safety assured working environments 
 
 
 Your own personalised job seeker account with myworkpac (WorkPac's online job seeker website) - allowing you to easily search and apply for jobs online, update your details and resume, submit online timesheets and view your payslips at the click of a button 
 
 
 Competitive pay rates 
 
 
 Career and training advice 
 
 
 Check out our  FAQ section  and our  blog  for more job seeking tips
