 Sweet Baby Jason! it's a Freelance job 
 dripp is looking for a Freelance Copywriter: Brand Strategy and Tone of Voice in New York, USA. 
 About 
 Our brand makes forgotten home essentials unforgettable by obsessing over sustainability, design, and the cleanest materials. What started with a shower curtain liner has turned into a bigger DTC brand that will encompass an entire family of sustainable home products. We've taken a deliberate and thorough approach to our brand strategy and want to partner with the right copywriter to bring our brand voice to life. We're looking for a master of tone, a meticulous word crafter, and a strong collaborator. This is an opportunity to set the brand vocabulary for many years to come.
