 Job Title  : Finance Officer 
 P   rocess transactions and payroll, produce reports. 
 Salary:       £10,800 for 14 hrs p.w. (£27,000 p.a., pro rata) 
 Holidays:     30 days p.a. including bank holidays pro-rata 
 Location:  Newcastle               **  Period:**  Jan 2020 - Dec 2022 
 Main Tasks  **** 
 
 Process and record all financial transactions in line with IPC policies, using the QuickBooks Online system 
 Produce finance reports for management and for the funder, as required. 
 Process project payroll details, and record on QuickBooks. 
 Maintain confidentiality and data security in line with our policies and current legislation 
 Assist the project Manager to achieve the requirements of the funded project, as agreed from time to time. 
 
 Personal requirements  **** 
 
 Accounting experience: minimum 2 years’ relevant experience in similar role. 
 Appropriate qualification. 
 MS Office Skills ( Word and Excel)   [to be tested at interview] 
 Good written and verbal communication and organisation skills 
 Good attention to detail 
 Ability to stay calm under pressure 
 Methodical and thorough approach to work 
 
 
 Job Title  :  Data Administrator

 Salary:       up to £17,500 (dependent on experience) ~~~~ 
 Holidays:     30 days p.a. including bank holidays 
 Location:  Newcastle, with some travel to Teesside (driving is not necessary). 
 JOB Description  **** 
 Main Tasks  **** 
 
 Gather data from project workers each week as required.

 MS Office Skills ( Word and Excel)   [to be tested at interview]
