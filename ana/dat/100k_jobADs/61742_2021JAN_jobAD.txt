 Have you previously worked within automotive distribution? I have a Warehouse Manager role for an established client that I have assisted on previous recruitment drives, this a brand-new facility so a candidate who has managed a "start-up" would preferable… 
 A leading distributor who supply to the automotive sector are looking to recruit a Warehouse Manager for their site near Coventry. My client is a rapidly growing business with significant career opportunities available for the right people, as they grow rapidly, they are acutely aware of the need for succession planning. 
 You will play a key role in ensuring the highest standards are achieved, you will take ownership of your work and be given accountability to develop both the strategy for the site and your team. You will lead a team in ensuring that stock is received, stored, packed and transported to the company's high standards, dealing with any issues in the first instance and escalating only when it is necessary. You will communicate on performance issues and identify the training required, before personally coaching and developing your team to improve performance. 
 To succeed in the role of Warehouse Manager you will need to demonstrate good commercial knowledge in warehousing and distribution within an automotive environment. You will manage and develop your team in a fast-paced environment, demonstrating the ability to motivate others daily to reach operational KPIs. A candidate with experience of implementing/improving operational procedures and who is fully experienced within discipline, grievance and absence management procedures would be beneficial. 
 The successful candidate will receive a salary up to £45,000 plus benefits. The organisation is moving forward with pace and as a result, career progression opportunities exist for talented & hardworking people. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
