 Categories

 __ Solve Problems 
 __ Team Work 
 
 Career Description 
 Assist physical therapists to help people of all ages regain range of motion and control pain. Help patients with their ability to move and perform functional activities in their daily lives. PTAs work in a variety of settings including hospitals, private practices, outpatient clinics, home health, nursing homes, schools, sports facilities and more. 
 What's next? 
 Explore the  physical therapy assistant program  available at Caldwell Community College. The program is six semesters long and provides graduates with an Associate's Degree.
