 Community Manager/Lead 
 tinyBuild Apply for this Job 
 Posted 
 December 21, 2020 
 Full Time 
 Anywhere 
 Remote 
 tinyBuild  is looking for a full-time Community Manager. You will be moderating communities (Steam, Discord, Reddit, Twitch, etc.). You will be responsible for the community growth and development, as well as communications with influencers, monitoring and posting on social media, and promote feedback and collaboration between tinyBuild and our gaming community. 
 This is  a remote position .  ‍ 
 ‍ 
 Duties  ​ 
 
 Working closely with video game producers to execute marketing plans 
 Creating and managing social media communications for game projects 
 Represent the company and represent the assigned video games by engaging the public via social media 
 Ensure consistency in franchise tone of voice and personality across all social media platforms 
 Write engaging copy that is appropriate for the audience and platforms, primarily short form with occasional long form writing 
 
 ‍ 
 Qualifications 
 
 In-depth knowledge of all social media platforms including but not limited to: Discord, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, Youtube, Twitch 
 Passion for engaging the public and being front facing for the company 
 Strong written and verbal communication skills 
 In-depth understanding of Video Game Culture 
 Passion for video games and video game communities 
 Detail-oriented 
 1-year minimum experience in working in communications and engaging in Social Media 
 Bachelor’s Degree 
 
 ‍ 
 ‍  About tinyBuild  ‍ 
 Founded in 2013, tinyBuild is a game developer morphed into a video game publishing label. Known for games such as Hello Neighbor and Graveyard Keeper, the company has offices in Amsterdam, Netherlands; Riga, Latvia and headquartered in Bellevue, WA.
