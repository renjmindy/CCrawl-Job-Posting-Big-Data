 Gleeson recruitment group are currently partnering with a large business based in Tamworth to appoint a Group Finance Manager. This is an existing role that has been developed due to the company's expansion and success over recent years. My client operates in an ever changing industry that moves at a fast pace, they are performing extremely well even in the current climate. 
 The Group finance manager will ideally be suited to a practice trained candidate (ACA qualified) who has industry experience but first movers from the profession would also be considered for the role. 
 The Group Accounting Manager will be responsible for: 
 
 Assisting in the completion of the group's transition to IFRS 
 Preparation of IFRS accounting papers at each year end 
 Exercising tight control over the group's year end audit process ensure it runs efficiently and in line with agreed timetables 
 Developing the group's reporting software to efficiently streamline the monthly and annual reporting process in all territories 
 Consolidating all divisional monthly management accounts and challenging results where necessary 
 Reporting monthly consolidated results to external stakeholders 
 Selection and implementation of consolidation system 
 Acquisition accounting 
 Updating the group's accounting and tax policies and procedures and ensuring these are adhered to in all territories across the group 
 Group tax compliance and strategy, in conjunction with advisors 
 Special projects as required 
 
 The ideal candidate will: 
 
 Be technically proficient: Top 10 trained ACA 
 Be methodical with a keen eye for detail - the candidate should be able to demonstrate experience of running year end processes to a strict timetable and being able to follow though on commitments in a timely manner. 
 Thrive within a fast-paced and pressurised environment 
 Demonstrate strong interpersonal skills and the ability to influence 
 Beneficial but not necessary to have M&A experience
