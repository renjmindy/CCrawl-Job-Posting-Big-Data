 Sales Loader Operator 
 Job description 
 Role Highlights 
 
 
 Immediate start- $36 p/hr + penalties 
 
 
 Monday to Friday, 40 hour week 
 
 
 Dandenong South location- Short term role 
 
 
 About the Role 
WorkPac are seeking a Sales Loader Operator for a client located in Dandenong South. They will be required to load crushed rock product into trucks, ensuring the correct amount has been provided to the customer. The successful applicant will have prior experience within a similar role. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Operate Kawasaki Loaders within a yard to transport and load crushed rock product into trucks 
 
 
 Utilise the scale system in the Loader to ensure correct load is given 
 
 
 Complete appropriate pre start up checks on the equipment and ensure gear is operating optimally and safely 
 
 
 Follow a Monday to Friday roster, 8 hour days 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Previous experience as a Sales Loader Operator essential 
 
 
 Loader ticket 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Working rights in Australia 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities.
