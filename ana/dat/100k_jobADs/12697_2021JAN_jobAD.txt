 Keen to speak with immediately available, experienced ledger clerks, to join a small and welcoming finance team near Wolverhampton, as their Assistant Finance Manager. This role is reporting directly into the Finance Manager with responsibility for managing a transactional member of the team. You can expect a salary between £28,000 - £30,000 depending on experience. 
 As the Assistant Finance Manager you will: 
 
 Have responsibility for managing one direct report, ensuring their development and successful delivery of their transactional work i.e. purchase and sales ledger 
 Ensure closures of ledgers are met for month end and accounts are prepared to trial balance 
 Journal entries, prepayments, and accruals 
 Act as the main point of contact for payroll related queries and ensure data is present accurately and reported to HR 
 Reconcile and maintain balance sheet accounts and P&L analysis 
 Bank payments 
 Forecasting of invoicing and maintaining company bank accounts 
 Preparation for audits including schedules and analysis 
 Company credit cards 
 Overhead budgeting and forecasting 
 Manage tax queries and act as an advisory point 
 
 If you enjoy working in transactional finance, and want to join a successful business please get in touch or apply!
