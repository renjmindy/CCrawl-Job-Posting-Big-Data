 Boilermaker, Welder 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Ongoing work 
 
 
 Even time roster - 8:6 / 7:7 DS/NS 
 
 
 Competitive hourly rate 
 
 
 About Our Client 
 Our client are currently seeking experienced Boilermaker Welders for roles in the Pilbara. 
 About the Role 
 Our client are currently seeking experienced Boilermaker Welders for roles in the Pilbara. To be a suitable candidate you will have previous fixed plant experience working on crushers, claimers, stackers, conveyors. Previous mobile plant experience trays and buckets. You will ideally be coded welder to AS1554 Part 5 and have air arc experience. 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Australian Trade Qualified Boilermaker / Welder 
 
 
 C Class Manual Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Right to work in Australia 
 
 
 Working at heights 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors.
