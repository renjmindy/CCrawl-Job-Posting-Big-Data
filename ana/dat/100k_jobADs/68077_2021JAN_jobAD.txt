 My client, a market leading omni-channel retail business is looking to recruit a PPC Manager. 
 This role, a newly created position, will join a well established digital marketing team and will play a key role in the continued growth of their PPC acquisition channel. 
 The PPC Manager role will encompass: 
 
 Managing and implementing successful search engine paid campaigns using PPC and PLA activity to maximise the delivery of high quality site traffic 
 Optimising campaigns using a combination of automated strategies and data driven amendments to improve revenue and ROAS 
 Competitor research to develop detailed analysis of market share and help devise tactical bidding responses accordingly 
 Deliver insights using Google Analytics and detailed reports from a variety of data sources to build business cases 
 Ongoing reporting and optimisation 
 
 The ideal candidate for this role will possess the following skills/expertise: 
 
 Extensive PPC experience, managing budgets in excess of £1m 
 Demonstrable track record of delivering improved revenue performance and ROAS 
 Highly analytical and data-driven - with a commercial edge 
 Strong agency management skills 
 
 Highly competitive package.
