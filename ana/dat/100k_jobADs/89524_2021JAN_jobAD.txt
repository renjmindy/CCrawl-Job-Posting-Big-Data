 About FLOWER CO. 
 We sell weed on the internet. We're a vertically integrated legal cannabis company delivering next-day in Los Angeles, Long Beach, San Francisco, East Bay, Marin, Humboldt, Sacramento, and Vallejo. We're venture-backed and growing exponentially. 
 About the role 
 You'll be joining a product and growth team that has worked together successfully for years. The opportunity is to be one of the first hires on a fast-scaling team and have a major impact on the user experience. 
 Role & Responsibilities 
 
 Understand the lifecycle of customers and identify opportunities for growth 
 Manage customer communications 
 Communicate email campaign objectives, timelines and deliverables 
 Execute a product driven email and SMS strategy 
 Reports to the Head of Growth 
 
 The ideal candidate 
 
 3-5 years of experience in growth marketing roles 
 Experience segmenting users by behaivor using online and offline tools 
 Must be proficient in Excel/Google Sheets 
 Experience directly managing Customer dot io or similar tool is required 
 Experience clicking the send button on communication campaigns hitting hundreds of thousands of users is required 
 Professional or personal knowledge of weed brands and consumers 
 
 Technology 
 We're building an end-to-end software system for sourcing, fulfillment, delivery, and e-commerce in a highly regulated industry.
