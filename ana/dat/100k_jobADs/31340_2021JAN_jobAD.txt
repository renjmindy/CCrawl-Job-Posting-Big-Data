 Research and complete working paper for an Esports Award Ceremony - How to Organize an Esports Award Event 
 Published on the November 22, 2020 in Admin Support 
 
 Project 
 Project Insights New 
 
 About this project 
 admin-support / market-research-1 
 USD 100 - 250 
 We need to compile a working paper on 'organizing' an Esports award ceremony. Just like other awards, The Grammys, Golden Globe Award etc, we need a complete research paper to cover these key topics; 
 
 What are the esports award ceremonies around the 'world' - List them down 
 What 'types of awards' are presented in an Esports Award - List down with Genre 
 How to 'appoint' a panel judge for an esports award 
 Qualifications to be an Esports Award 'Judge' 
 Esports award 'guideline' & 'criteria' based on every type of awards listed in item 2) - Very important! 
 The 'judging' criteria and guideline for each listed award in item 2) 
- The merits & points of each judging criteria etc 
 Judging Form - Sample of a form 
 Percentage of Panel Judges Vote Vs Public Vote - how is it done 
 Best Practices of other esports awards around the world 
 Any other topics related, that can help especially the guidelines & criteria of awarding of each genre 
 Any other key topics needed to be in this research paper 
 
 Project overview 
 This project/research/working paper is to understand how to organize an Esports Award Ceremony. There many esports awards out there; check out his link - https://www.esportsawards.com/
