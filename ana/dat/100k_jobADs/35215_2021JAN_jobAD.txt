 Project Leader 
 Job description 
 About the Role: 
 WorkPac is currently seeking a Project Engineer based in Gladstone for an immediate start to lead Stormwater projects from engineering design through to execution. 
 This position would suit a Project Lead with TSS and pH experience with 5 years demonstrated expertise across managing brownfields projects. Please note the successful applicant will require practical and implementation exposure. 
 To be successful within this position you will have Civil or Mechanical Engineering qualification. You must be comfortable presenting and communicating and writing approval documentation, be an effective communicator with strong stakeholder engagement experience. You will need project controls process knowledge to manage the project effectively and be able to manage different streams of work in parallel. 
 Skills and Experience: 
 
 
 5 Years Experience in Brownfields Projects 
 
 
 Civil or Mechanical Engineering Qualification 
 
 
 TSS and pH experience 
 
 
 Batter stabilisation experience 
 
 
 Good interpersonal skills, stakeholder management, ability to progress multiple projects in parallel, manage risks, manage costs, manage external engineering service providers, review engineering deliverables 
 
 
 Current "C" Class Driver's License and Own Transport 
 
 
 Must be either an Australian Citizen or a holder of an Australian permanent residency visa to be eligible to apply for this position 
 
 
 Job Reference Number: BSINDJ2021106374 
 How to Apply: 
 Click on the Apply button or for more information please contact: 
 WorkPac Recruitment Co-Ordinator: Ilse Rall on (07) 3621 2915 quoting the above reference number.

 At WorkPac, we recognise we are strengthened by diversity. We are committed to providing a work environment in which everyone is included, treated fairly and with respect. We are an Equal Opportunity employer, and we encourage applications from women and Indigenous people.
