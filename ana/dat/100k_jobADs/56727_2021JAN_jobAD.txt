 Consultant: 
 
 
 For the big data competence center of a global financial services client we are currently searching for multiple In-house Advanced Business Analytics Consultants (m/f). 
 This is a key role in a rapidly growing area, and so the successful candidate can expect to play an interdisciplinary role, interfacing to multiple different business units. 
 The Role: 
 
 Manage strategic analytics projects across multiple functions 
 Identifying business areas that could benefit from data-centric solutions 
 Liaise with high level internal and external stakeholders 
 Conduct data analysis and develop analytics solutions 
 Perform internal training when required 
 Identify future trends in big data and analytics, based on academic research and exchange, to develop suggestions for improvements and new business opportunities 
 
 Role Requirements: 
 
 Completed degree in a related field (statistics, mathematics, information technology, physics or similar). A PhD would be advantageous 
 Solid theoretical and practical understanding of modern statistical modelling and machine learning techniques 
 Relevant work experience, ideally within analytics consulting 
 Knowledge of statistical programming languages (SAS, R, Python), as well as other programming skills in SQL, VB, etc. 
 Excellent communication skills in German and English
