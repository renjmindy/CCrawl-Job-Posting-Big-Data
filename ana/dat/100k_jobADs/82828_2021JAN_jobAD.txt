 I am currently working with a data driven business based in Birmingham who are on the lookout for a passionate, hands on Data Engineer to join their team. 
 The role sits as part of their data services team and will play a critical role across project work, along with driving their technology development and improvements. 
 You will support and develop process using Microsoft Stack and cloud technologies so a background in this is a must. 
 Requirements 
 
 Microsoft Azure Cloud, Data Warehousing 
 Microsoft Stack - SSIS, SQL, T-SQL 
 Desinging and Developing ETL data pipelines 
 Dimensional Modelling 
 Experience in an Agile/Devops environment 
 Power BI (Desirable)
