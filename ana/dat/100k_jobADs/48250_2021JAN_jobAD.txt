 A brand new role in a strategically focused business, which will be key in driving forward their approach to business information and analysis. 
 You will be responsible for providing analytical support ad insight to business cases and propositions, as well as support and maintain their business systems including reports and dashboards. 
 In order to be successful you will have the following - 
 
 Track record in delivering analysis of performance data and statistics 
 Analytics mindset, establishing reports and dashboards 
 Maintenance and support of CRM - Dynamics, Salesforce etc 
 Ability to influence senior stakeholders - you will be the central point of BI, providing evidence to drive decision making 
 Commercial mindset and ability to think strategically - maximising opportunities 
 Desire to contribute to a high performance culture
