 Children's Community Nurse 
 Service Care Solutions have a vacancy available for a Children's Community nurse to carry out specialist nursing tasks for sick and complex children in the community, either at home, nursery, school or college. 
 The role is based in Ealing and is full time, on a 3 month contract which could lead to a longer term booking. 
 You will need to be competent in line care, IV anti-biotics, gastrostomy, NGT's, and other specialist nursing tasks, including palliative care and complex care. 
 Knowledge and understanding of this patient group is essential. 
 You will need to be a car driver and have access to a car to deliver care in the community. 
 You must be NMC Registered, fully qualified and have previous experience of working within an NHS community setting.
