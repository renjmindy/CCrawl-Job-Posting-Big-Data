 As Interim Senior HR Business Partner, you will manage a team of HR Business Partners and partner with Central Functions. Based from Telford area, covering two sites. Salary £60-62k + Excellent Benefits. 12 Month FTC. 
 As Interim Senior HR Business Partner, you will help lead the People agenda, during a time of massive change and transformation. Partnering the SMT you will drive cultural change, change management, restructures, engagement and development. You will also manage a small team of HR Business Partners, and through the team ensure the delivery of the people agenda across the organisation. 
 CIPD Level 7, or equivalent as a minimum, you will have experience of working within global or matrix-structured organisations within Manufacturing/ Engineering businesses where continuous improvement/ six sigma is second nature to you as an experienced Senior HR Business Partner. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
