 Advisor / Sales Person, Paints 
 Rona 
 __Sainte-Thérèse, QC 
 Apply Now 
 The responsibilities of the Sales Advisor in the Painting department are : 
 
 Greet customers warmly and ensure a good customer experience; 
 Assess customer needs an provide expert advice on painting department products; 
 Present and demonstrate the product highlighting its positive features and suggest complementary products; 
 As per the customer’s request, mix paint, copy or make color corrections; 
 Support the marketing of products in its department such as: display of merchandise, visual presentation and labelling; 
 Keep up to date with new products and their functions; 
 Ensure and participate in the maintenance of his department; 
 Be aware of current promotions and flyers; 
 Develop a basic knowledge of all the departments in the store; 
 If required, operate a forklift, in accordance with the procedures and policies of the organization; 
 Comply with policies and procedures regarding loss prevention, breakage of goods and health and safety; 
 Perform other duties as requested by the supervisor. 
 
 Job Requirements 
 Do you like physical work, without being a renovation pro? Do you have a knack for first-class customer service? You have what it takes to be in our team! ASSET: experience in customer service, plus knowledge in our Painting department.
