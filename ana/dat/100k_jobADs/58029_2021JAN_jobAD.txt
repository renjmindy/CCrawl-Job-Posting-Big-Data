 Barclays is one of the world's largest and most respected financial institutions, with 329 years of success, quality and innovation behind us. We offer careers that provide endless opportunity – helping millions of individuals and businesses thrive, and creating financial and digital solutions that the world now takes for granted. 
 What will you be doing? 
 
 You will work with Enterprise Data Tools and Insights Team who design, build, and maintain the metadata and issue management applications used across the group 
 Connecting with leadership and fellow team members globally, delivering to meet specifications and timelines 
 Delivering across various programmes of work in a timely and quality manner 
 Presenting ideas and designs to colleagues and adapting the work based on the reviews and direct feedback given 
 Participating and providing feedback in code reviews 
 Developing innovative solutions that will enhance the client experience and provide a cost effective solutions 
 Developing skills and understanding of fellow team members through mentoring and code reviews 
 
 What we're looking for: 
 
 Degree in an engineering, mathematical or computer science discipline 
 Experience with application server development in the web and/or mobile domain 
 Middleware DB and Backend experience to be familiar with Express, GraphQL/Apollo, and NodeJS 
 Familiar with RDMS of MariaDB and/or Postgress with ORM Sequelize 
 
 Skills that will help you in the role: 
 
 Experience of operating in an Agile delivery environment 
 Test experience with some form of unit testing 
 Takes ownership of tasks and is able to monitor/report progress and deliver to completion 
 Able to demonstrate previous evidence of drive, energy, commitment and enthusiasm 
 
 Where will you be working? 
 We've been in Scotland for 250 years, and Scotland's significance to Barclays has never been as important as it is today. You can find the Aurora Building in Bothwell Street, a short walk from both Central and Queen Street Stations, and not far from Sauchiehall Street and Argyll Street, two of the city's main shopping hubs. We're committed to giving everyone the opportunity to thrive at Barclays, investing in our people, communities, and facilities – highlighted by our new state-of-the-art campus opening soon in the city centre.
