 Co-op Express Manager (Retail/Food)(Relocation required - Pangnirtung, NU) 
 Arctic Co-operatives Limited 
 __Pangnirtung, NU 
 Apply Now 
 Position:  Co-op Express (CStore) 
 Reporting To:  Co-op General Manager 
 Position Summary: 
 This position is responsible for managing the Co-op Express in a professional and profitable manner. 
 Duties and Responsibilities: 
 
 
 To train, provide direction, and evaluate staff. Advise the Co-op General Manager of areas necessitating discipline; 
 
 
 Ensure an adequate inventory level is maintained and coordinate ordering as required; 
 
 
 Ensure rules and regulations are followed by staff and customers; 
 
 
 To maintain housekeeping in Co-op Express to ensure it is kept clean and professional; 
 
 
 To prepare the menu and specials as required and cost out all meal items to ensure proper margin is being maintained; 
 
 
 Other duties and responsibilities as assigned by the General Manager. 
 
 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 
 5 – 7 years experience in the food industry and/or 
 
 
 Formal education and experience including a Cook Level 1 or Journeyman certificate; red seal certified; 
 
 
 Ability to train and motivate staff; 
 
 
 Retail experience in a small operation would be an asset; 
 
 
 Ability to work in a cross-cultural environment. 
 
 
 Job Types: Full-time, Contract, Permanent 
 Work remotely: 
 
 No
