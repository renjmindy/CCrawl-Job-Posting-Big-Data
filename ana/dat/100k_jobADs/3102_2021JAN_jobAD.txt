 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently partnering with a market leading business in Solihull in search of a Part Qualified Finance Analyst. This is a new accounting position that offers great progression opportunities as well as fantastic exposure to unique and exciting projects. 
 We are looking for an ambitious, commercially minded accountant or analyst to join the business to support across a variety of functions. The business is going from strength to strength and looking for someone to work within their current finance team. 
 The Role 
 As Finance Analyst you will report into the Finance Business Partner and be responsible for the following: 
 
 Build strong relationships to take a proactive approach to avoid budget overspends. 
 To provide insight to departments 
 Working with the team to develop KPI reporting 
 Assisting consolidation of group management packs for P&L, Balance Sheet and Cashflow 
 Accurate forecasting & tracking to ensure productivity 
 Ensure visibility & cost control around spend patterns across 
 Analyse implications of overspends 
 Reviewing overhead variances for divisions 
 Support the finance business partners 
 Liaise with teams to control productivity delivery 
 
 The Requirements 
 The ideal candidate for this role may have already acted as a finance analyst previously, or has worked in a similar role and looking for the next step. 
 
 Part-Qualified accountant - ACA/ACCA/CIMA. 
 Strong ability to communicate with stakeholders 
 Strong commercial acumen 
 Previous experience in a similar finance position 
 Ideally studied accountancy at University level. 
 
 What's on Offer? 
 
 Salary up to £35,000 + Study Support + benefits 
 Excellent career progression opportunities 
 Opportunity to work with a market-leading brand 
 A positive and down-to-earth culture 
 Flexible working arrangements
