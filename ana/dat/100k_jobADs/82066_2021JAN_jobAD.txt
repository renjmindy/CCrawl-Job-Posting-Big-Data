 Registered Home Manager 
 An outstanding job opportunity has arisen for a dedicated Registered Home Manager to work in an exceptional care home based in the Wolverhampton, West Midlands area. 
 As the Registered Home Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This care home is a purpose-built care home designed for older people requiring residential and nursing care. 
 The dedicated team ensure they meet the resident's individual care and social needs 
 An ideal Registered Home Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Previous older peoples care experience 
 
 
 
     Caring & compassionate with an excellent clinical track record
 
 
 
     Passionate about delivering outstanding care to older people
 
 
 
     Excellent organisation & leadership skills
 
 
 
     Effective communication skills
 
 
 
     Flexible, professional and committed to being the best
 
 
 
     Quality driven with a desire to continually improve yourself & our services
 
 
 
 
 **The successful Registered Home Manager will receive an excellent salary from £56,000 up to £58,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role and in return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Market competitive rates of pay
 
 
 
     Specialist dementia training and support from our award-winning, university-accredited 'Creative Minds' programme
 
 
 
     Range of salary sacrifice benefits including pension, car leases, gym membership, technology purchase schemes and more
 
 
 
     Online benefits portal with a range of offers and cashback rewards
 
 
 
 
 You will be considered for this position if you are qualified as a Nurse either RGN or RMN with a valid NMC Pin or have an NVQ Level 5 in Leadership & Management in Health & Social Care or equivalent
