 Adults Social Worker - Safeguarding 
 Adult Social Worker - Sandwell Council 
 We are currently recruiting for an Adult Services Social Worker to work in a Safeguarding team in Sandwell. 
 The safeguarding team is looking for a qualified, experienced (no less than 2 years Adult Social Work experience) and competent temporary social worker to support the team in responding to safeguarding referrals relating to our Sandwell residents. 
 The safeguarding team is a busy team and you must demonstrate a sound knowledge and understanding of relevant legislation and guidance, having applied safeguarding procedures in your practice. 
 The ideal candidate will have: 
, Social work degree, or equivalent 
, HCPC registered 
, Enhanced DBS 
, 3 years' experience as an Adult Social Worker 
, The ability to undertake person-centred assessments; developing care plans and panel reports 
, Full UK driving license and vehicle
