 Dynamics 365 Project Manager - Manufacturing £450 per day 
 A Coventry based manufacture is implementing Dynamics 365 as their software package and are currently looking for a Project Manager to work closely with the third party to help with the implementation. 
 The role will focus on internal stakeholders and working closely with board members so a successful transformation happens. The client is looking for someone who has been involved in a Dynamics 365 Implementation and has worked in Manufacturing. 
 Starting in January interviews will take place in December contract is likely to run till May 2021. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
