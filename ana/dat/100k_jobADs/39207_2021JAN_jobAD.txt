 Gleeson Recruitment Group are recruiting for a Financial Controller to join an established Business to be based in Leicester, with home working available at times. The role will look after the APAC region leading on all financial activities. 
 Role 
 
 Lead the Finance function 
 Lead regional team in APAC on financial and cost accounting, variance analysis and operational reporting 
 Maintain processes to ensure monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements are prepared 
 Preparing forecasts, budgets 
 Commercial partnering including business analysis, strategic oversight, identification of areas for improvement and management of key projects 
 Look after internal controls for region ensuring compliance with US and Asian accounting, tax and control requirements including SOX requirements. 
 Managing internal and external audits 
 
 Requirements 
 
 ACA OR ACCA OR CIMA Qualified 
 At least 5 years PQE 
 Must have Manufacturing experience 
 Experience in Multi-national 
 
 On Offer 
 Up to £90,000 + Car Allowance + bonus
