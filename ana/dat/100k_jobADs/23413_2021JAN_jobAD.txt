 Project Coordinator - Corner Brook Acute Care Hospital Project 
 Cahill 
 __Corner Brook, NL 
 Apply Now 
 The Cahill Group is one of the largest multi-disciplinary construction companies in Canada, with operations from 
Newfoundland to Alberta. We believe in taking care of our people with rewarding career opportunities, competitive 
compensation packages, and a dynamic working environment. 
 Project Coordinator 
 The Cahill Group is currently recruiting for a Mechanical Coordinator to support the Acute Care Hospital Project 
in Corner Brook 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Establish and build good relationships with peers and clients by responding to requests and 
 
 communicating information in a timely manner, exercising initiative and judgment to complete recurring 
 assignments 
 
 Complete quality & safety documentation, technical support for estimating, procurement, drafting, and 
 
 technical reviews & design builds 
 
 Assist with preparation of material takeoffs, estimates, budgets and fabrication schedules 
 Liaise with partners, consultants and clients as required 
 Participate in the preparation and review of Construction Plans to ensure that the proposed methods are 
 
 economical, practical and compatible with the field requirements. 
 
 Review and understand scope of work, project specifications, design instructions, and supplier 
 
 information 
 
 Analyze contract drawings in relation to construction method and sequence 
 Maintain construction documents, perform take-offs and estimates for change orders 
 Assist in various administrative and technical aspects of project management and will be responsible for 
 
 creating and administering FIWP’s, test packages and hand-over documentation 
 Qualifications 
 
 Minimum of 2 years’ experience in a related field 
 Bachelor of Engineering Degree or diploma in Engineering Technology, equivalent experience will also 
 
 be considered 
 
 Proficient with Microsoft Office 
 Excellent oral and written communication skills 
 
 Deadline for applications is October 10, 2019 at 5pm NST. 
 If you are interested in joining our growing team, please apply in confidence to employment@cahill.ca, quoting 
competition CBACH-PC-09-19. Cahill is an equal opportunity organization. While we thank all applicants for their 
interest, only those under consideration will be contacted.
