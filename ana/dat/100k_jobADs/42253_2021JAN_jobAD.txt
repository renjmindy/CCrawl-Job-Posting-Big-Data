 Qatar Nanny Jobs 
 Posted on  September 9, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This is an excellent opportunity for all Filipino nannies who are seeking employment in nanny jobs abroad. The Philippine Overseas Employment Administration or POEA approve last August 4, 2009, a job order for Qatar nanny jobs. The said job order for Qatar nanny jobs has a job order balance for 25 nanny and was approved for a recruitment agency based in Ermita, Manila 
 For more info on the said nanny jobs Qatar, you can E-mail the said agency at yelufa168@yahoo.com or contact the agency directly at the following contact infos: 
 168 Ye Lu Fa International Manpower and Promotion Services 
Rms. 303/305/309 3F Equitable PCI Bank Bldg. 
U.N. Avenue, Ermita 
Manila 
 Tel. Nos. : 5217233/ 2322886
