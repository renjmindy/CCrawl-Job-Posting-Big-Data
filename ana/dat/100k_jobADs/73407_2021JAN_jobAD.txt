 What next? 
 Call: (864) 643-0071 or stop by the SC Works Center to schedule an appointment. 
 
 
 
 
 Stop by the SC Works Center during our drop-in hours; see schedule below. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Appointments: 
 Job Seekers can call (864) 643-0071 to schedule an individual appointment with a counselor for questions or assistance. Appointments are available 8:30AM to 5:00PM Monday through Friday. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Cancellation and No-Show Policy for Scheduled Appointments: 
 If you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, please notify our office 24 hours in advance. This allows our staff to offer your appointment time to another job seeker who is in need of our services. 
 
 
 Drop-in Hours: 
 Job Seekers may drop-in for quick, 15 minute one-on-one counseling sessions from 8:30am to 5:00pm, Monday - Friday
