 _ 
US Business Services** is a marketing firm which THRIVES off of their ability to train Entry Level Sports-Minded Marketing and Sales Candidates into Market Manager Superstars._** 
 ** 
 US Business Services ** provides the opportunity for those looking to excel in the field of sales and marketing by utilizing a hands-on approach in management training.  US Business Services  focuses on developing and enhancing the competitive nature and willingness to lead within every potential candidate. This is a valuable opportunity for those who have experience in team leadership, sports marketing, advertising, sales, entrepreneurship and anybody with a competitive mindset.

We do not believe in seniority, we promote to management those who get the job done.
