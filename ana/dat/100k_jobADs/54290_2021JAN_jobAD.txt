 MIDWIVES 
 
 Working In Australia 
 Australia 
 Dec 07, 2020 
 
 Full time Healthcare 
 Job Description 
 Midwives are needed in Australia. 
 We are welcoming applications from suitably qualified and experienced Midwives to work in a variety of settings throughout Australia. 
 Skills and Attributes required 
 Relevant Midwifery qualification 
 
 Minimum of 2 years post-registration midwifery practice 
 Internationally qualified Midwives must have or be eligible for registration with the Nursing and Midwifery Board of Australia 
 Fluency and comprehension in English to be able to communicate effectively (written and verbal) with patients, colleagues and other health professionals 
 Clinical experience in the planning, implementation, and evaluation of care for women and babies 
 Strong commitment to the protection, promotion, and support of breastfeeding 
 The ability to make informed decisions that focus on providing quality maternity care for women 
 The ability to work both autonomously and collaboratively with other professionals 
 Ability to be adaptable, flexible and solution focused 
 
 Working In have 20 years’ experience in helping overseas professionals live and work in Australia.
