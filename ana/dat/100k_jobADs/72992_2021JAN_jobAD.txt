 Vivian Fernandes or more popularly known with the stage name Divine. He is an Indian rapper, singer, songwriter, and music composer. He was already a great name in the industry, but he rose to fame after the movie Gully Boy. It is said that the story of the film is based on his life. His “Kohinoor” track became famous within a night, and there is no one today who has never heard his name. So let us get to know more about Divine and his career. 
 Divine Net worth in rupees 2020: 
 Divine is an Indian rapper, singer, song composer, and lyricist. His primary source of income is the music and the brand endorsements that he takes up. With that, he also does concerts and various stage shows, which also garners him monetary funds. His net worth is approx. 15-16 crores. We are not sure how much he charges per song, but it might be between 1 to 2 crore rupees. Apart from that, he is also a part of many brand endorsements, and he also is apart of many stage shows. He has worked with many celebrities, and he has also made a notable contribution to Gully Boy. 
 Divine career and assets: 
 There is no doubt that Divine is one of the best rappers in the Indian Music Industry. He started off his career as an underground rapper in 2011. His song “yeh Mera Bombay” became extremely famous amongst all the Mumbaikars. This song was released in 2013, and it became a sensation. And this also got him the award of “Best Music Video of the year,” By the Rolling Stone in 2014. His primary inspiration is another sensational rapper Eminem, and this is clearly portrayed in the movie Gully Boy which is based on his life. After his song “yeh Mera Bombay,” he was given a contract by Sony Music India, which got him the breakthrough single “Mere Gully Mein,” which is still a viral song. And ever since then, Divine is known as the best rapper in the Indian music Industry.
