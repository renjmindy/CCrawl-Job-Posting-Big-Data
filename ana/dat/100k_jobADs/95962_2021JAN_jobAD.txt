 Job Title:  Merchandiser 
 Location:  Vegreville, AB 
 Start Date:  ASAP 
 Shifts:  10-12 hrs/wk, weekends and stat holidays required 
 Position Type:  Part-Time 
 Reports to:  Territory Sales Manager 
 Job Function: 
We are currently looking to hire a part time Merchandiser to join our Field Sales Support team in the Vegreville, AB area. Must be available mornings, afternoons, weekends, stat holidays, no benefits. $16.75/hour. Move product from back room to shelf at designated customers 
 Areas Of Responsibility:   A Merchandiser’s duties will include but are not limited to: 
Follow sign in/out procedure as per customer requirements 
Stock and rotate product on store shelves and displays 
Communicate with route operator on any product concerns 
Notify Territory Manager of any customer issues 
Properly stack and label returns for route operator 
 Physical Requirements: 
Ability to work with minimal supervision, self- motivated 
Must be organized and methodical 
Must be able to push/pull/lift up to 50 lbs with assistance 
Physically fit and able to perform frequent bending, twisting, kneeling, squatting 
Excellent communication skills 
Professionalism in customer service 
 Additional Requirements:   A Merchandiser will: 
Have a High School Diploma or equivalent GED 
Be responsible for their own transportation 
Possibly have split shifts including early mornings and late afternoons, weekends, stat holidays 
 To be considered for this role please apply with an updated resume by clicking “Apply Now” below. 
 We thank all interested applicants, but only those selected will be contacted. Please note all offers of employment at Bimbo Canada (Formerly Canada Bread Company Limited) are conditional upon the successful completion of a pre-hire background check. This may include anything up to and including a criminal history check, reference checks and/or credit check
