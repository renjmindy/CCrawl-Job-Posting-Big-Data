 My client, a high growth retail business based in the West Midlands, is looking to recruit an Amazon Channel Manager. 
 The role will lead on all Amazon activity - working across multiple global territories, and expanding into new markets as appropriate. 
 The ideal candidate for this role will possess the following skills/expertise: 
 
 Extensive amazon channel management experience, utilising FBA. 
 International experience 
 Track record in delivering profitable growth and entry into new markets. 
 Additional experience with Shopify would be advantageous. 
 
 This is a permanent role, based in the West Midlands - there will be some flexible working, so candidates with the appropriate skills skill-set from across the West Midlands are encouraged to apply. 
 Highly competitive package.
