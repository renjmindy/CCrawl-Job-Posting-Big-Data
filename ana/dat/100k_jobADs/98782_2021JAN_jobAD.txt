 Are you an experienced Quantity Surveyor looking for a new and exciting challenge? Do you want to work client side for a leading international retail brand? You have the opportunity go from working for a contractor to managing contractors on behalf of a leading retail client. 
 The opportunity 
Our client is a leading retail organisation within the UK and international retail sector. They have an in-house construction and development team that is responsible the coordination and delivery of new stores. As Quantity Surveyor you will join this team as the most senior Cost Manager / Quantity Surveyor with regular trips into their flagship office on Oxford Street. This role can be based from Northamptonshire, London or Nottinghamshire. The role will require travels to each of these locations from time to time. 
 The role 
As the first Quantity Surveyor hired by this in-house team you will have the opportunity to make this role your own through taking responsibility for cost sense checking all works carried out tight control of supply chain partners. Managing cost from stage 2 (White Box) stage onwards you will ensure tight control of costs and works undertaken by the supply chain. 
 On offer 
In return you will receive a basic salary of £60-70,000 with the opportunity progress in future. You will spend your time split between being site based, based from offices in Northampton, Nottinghamshire and regular trips to our clients stunning Grage A offices on Oxford Street.
