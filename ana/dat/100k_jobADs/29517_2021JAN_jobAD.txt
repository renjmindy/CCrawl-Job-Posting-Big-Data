 Mobile Crane Operator 
 Apply for the position, Mobile Crane Operator, with Hess Services, Inc. today or contact Joe Leroux for more information. Posted May 3, 2020. 
  7856214075  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 The Mobile Crane Operator is responsible for operating cranes to lift and haul loads and observes rigging to determine safety of loads. Duties include, driving equipment to and from worksites, inspecting rigging to ensure load safety, and documenting all related information as appropriate. Class A CDL and NCCCO or NCCER Crane Certification required. Applicants must be 18 years or older. High school diploma or equivalent preferred. Benefits include Competitive Wages, Paid Health/Dental Coverage, Retirement Plan, Uniforms and Paid Time Off. Pre-Employment Drug Testing is required. For questions, please feel free to contact the Human Resources Department at (785)621-4075. To apply may fill out an application online at www.hess-services.com, stop by our office located at 2670 E. 9th Street, Hays, KS 67601 or visit your local Workforce Center. EOE. ** This position is available in both our Hays, KS and Watford City, ND locations. **
