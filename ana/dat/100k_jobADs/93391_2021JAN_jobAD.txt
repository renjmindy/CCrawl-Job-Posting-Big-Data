 Dozer Operator 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 FIFO 3:1 Roster 
 
 
 Attractive Payrate 
 
 
 Temp to Perm after 3 months 
 
 
 About the Role 
 An ongoing opportunity exists for experienced Civil Dozer operators with MACA Mining to work at Western Turner. 
 Reporting to the Production Superintendent, you will form part of the production team as a mobile plant operator. 
 As a key member of a high performing team, you will play an integral role in implementing our client's production requirements. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Drop cuts 
 
 
 Pushing tip heads 
 
 
 Floor clean ups 
 
 
 Road maintenance 
 
 
 Ripping 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Demonstrated competency operating Caterpillar D10 dozers 
 
 
 Production dozer experience 
 
 
 Manual C Class Australian issued Drivers Licence (minimum) 
 
 
 National police clearance or willingness to obtain 
 
 
 Ability to pass pre-employment medicals and drug screens 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors.
