 Forklift Operator 
 Job description 
 
 
 $29.38/hr + Penalties 
 
 
 Monday - Friday Roster 
 
 
 Ongoing role 
 
 
 About the Client 
 Our client is a national group engaged in food processing. The Company has a strong commitment to Health and Safety, which enables to deliver a larger-scale of quality products all over the world. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking skilled and experienced Forklift Operator/Labourer. You will be working Monday - Friday Roster work for a local client near Bakers Creek. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Will be operating a forklift on site to load and unload trucks , 
 
 
 Lifiting heavy boxes of meat - picking and packing some orders 
 
 
 Reliable, committed, punctual, willing to learn and have a positive attitude 
 
 
 Experience in a meatworks facility is preferred 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Physically Fit - capable of lifting up to 15kg boxes 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Forklift licence 
 
 
 Willing to undergo Pre-Employment Medical (included Drug & alcohol) 
 
 
 Must be willing to undertake Q Fever immunisation 
 
 
 Right to work within Australia 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
