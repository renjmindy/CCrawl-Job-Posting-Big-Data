 Highly Rated Boots for General Construction Jobs 
 November 23, 2017 •  comp toe work boots  •  composite toe boots  •  safety toe boots  •  safety toe work boots  •  steel toe boots  •  steel toe work boots  •  work boots 
 Construction work requires the right tools and equipment. That includes having the right boots to take you through the day. The boots compiled here all have unique features to protect from the various types of jobs in construction work. Whether you are looking for the protection of a safety toe or need the boot to be waterproof, you’ll find one here that not only protects but delivers all day comfort. 
 Caterpillar Alaska P74124 Brown Leather Slip Resistant EH Rated Work Boot 
 The Caterpillar Alaska P74124 is comfortable and hard wearing for long days. The full grain leather construction has Goodyear welting. The cushioned footbed is designed to provide support all day long. The sole is slip resistant and EH rated for protection from electrical hazards. Soft toe. 
 Thorogood 824-6904 Black Leather Oil Resistant Cushioned Motorcycle Boot 
 This boot features a full brain oil tanned leather upper with Goodyear storm welt construction. While it was crafted for durability and hard use, this boot also provides long hours of comfort. The interior has a woven lining to keep feet dry. The boot’s collar and tongue are padded for additional protection and comfort. The polyurethane footbed delivered contoured cushioning and is removeable. There is a steel shank with a supple rubber midsole. The sole has special oil resistant rubber lugs for additional protection. 
 Chippewa Brown Leather Waterproof Composite Toe Slip Resistant Quake Work Boot 55211 
 The Chippewa Quake lace up work boot is tough. The coffee colored leather upper is constructed with Goodyear welt construction and a waterproof boot. An ASTM rated composite safety toe protects against crushing and force. A steel shank increases the boot’s stability. The midsole is constructed from rubber for flexibility. The sole is EH rated for protection from electrical hazards. 
 **** Rocky RKK0128 Hauler Brown Leather Composite Toe Waterproof Slip Resistant Work Boot 
 The Rocky RKK0128 work boot has many features to keep the wearer, not only safe, but also comfortable. The leather upper is crafted with Goodyear welt construction. The waterproof construction also includes an interior mesh lining to ensure that feet stay dry. It has the ratings ASTM F2413 and F2413 for toe protection and electrical hazards. A nylon shank provides flexibility. The footbed is constructed with memory foam to boost energy. It also delivery energy return by Rocky Rebound Comfort technology. The sole is slip and oil resistant. This is a boot that works as hard as you do. 
 Rocky RKYK086 Brown Leather Waterproof Steel Toe Metatarsal Meta Guard Work Boot 
 Tough jobs call for tough boots and Rocky delivers! These work boots features an ASTM rated F2413 metatarsal guard standard. It also features Rocky’s unique waterproof construction. It has heavily oiled leather with Goodyear welt construction and padding to protect the ankles. The interior has Rocky’s EnergyBed™ for comfort and has a TPU ladder shank for stability. The boot’s construction is reinforced with triple stitching. All of the stitching and laces are flame-resistant and the heel is high abrasion resistant. The sole also feature 3mm high heat lugs. 
 Look for boots designed to provide the protection you need. But get the comfort that you want.
