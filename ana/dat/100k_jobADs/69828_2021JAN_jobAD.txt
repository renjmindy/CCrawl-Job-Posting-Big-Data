 Diesel Fitter + Camp 
 Job description 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking a skilled and experienced Diesel Fitter, as well as a Leading Hand Diesel Fitters= to commence on a local town mine near Biloela, Queensland. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Previous mine site experience 
 
 
 Experience working on Dump Trucks, Water Trucks, Graders, Loaders and Axillary Equipment. 
 
 
 Leading Hand Position requires previous Leading Hand experience - but must be willing to be hands on working on Liebherr Excavators and Komatsu Dump Trucks. 
 
 
 Complete machinery inspections 
 
 
 Repair and Test Machinery 
 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 
 Previous as a Leading Hand in a Mining Environment 
 
 
 Current Standard 11 
 
 
 Current S1, S2, S3 & G2 
 
 
 Current Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Current Driver's Licence 
 
 
 Qualified Trade Certificate 
 
 
 Working at Heights & Confined Space qualifications are advantageous 
 
 
 Right to work within Australia 
 
 
 Benefits 
 
 
 Accommodation and Meals provided 
 
 
 Ongoing position 
 
 
 Competitive Rates 
 
 
 Super paid on all hours worked
