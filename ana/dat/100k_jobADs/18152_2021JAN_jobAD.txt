 German for the job 
 Welcome to our German courses. Our courses take place from 6 participants. 
 The maximum group size is 12. 
 German for Work B1/B2 - Registration 
21.01.21 - 01.04.21 (10 dates) 
Cost: 195 € 
Training units: 20 
Thursday 7.15 - 8.45 pm 
with Adelheid Kumpf 
 |   | 
 We train situations that are important in the working world at companies in Germany: Contact with colleagues, superiors and customers, communication by e-mail and on the phone, discussion and presentation, understanding intercultural differences. We work without a course book, but with current material (up-to-date information on the economic situation in Germany). 
Adelheid Kumpf gives feedback on grammar (especially articles and the passive voice) and phonetics. 
There is a course protocol in every course.
