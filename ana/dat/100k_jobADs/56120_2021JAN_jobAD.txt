 Personal Support Worker (PSW)-Long Term Care-(LTC) - Cambridge 
 Qualicare Waterloo 
 __Cambridge, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Attention:  All certified Personal Support Workers (PSW) interested in working in long-term care (LTC), we have a position for you, apply ASAP! 
 QCareKWC is looking for an experienced, skilled and reliable individuals to join our team as a certified Personal Support Worker to provide support to local facilities in the community. Opportunity is available in the Cambridge area. 
 QCareKWC is focused on creating a team of qualified individuals to enhance and advance the health of the community. We are open to Personal Support Workers (PSW) with a variety of backgrounds and training expertise. 
 Start date:  as soon as possible, 8-12 hour shifts, days, nights, afternoons. 
 We are searching for candidates that would be available for: 
 
 Full-time Days/Evenings in a facility environment 
 Consistent hours 
 Shifts throughout the week available 
 
 The QCareKWC Difference: 
 
 Competitive starting wages (based upon experience, education and responsibilities) 
 Continuing education and training programs 
 Mentorship opportunities 
 
 Personal Support Worker (PSW) Role: 
 
 Assistance with personal care, hygiene, and grooming 
 Assisting clients with mobility, ambulation, and transfers 
 Companionship 
 
 Personal Support Worker (PSW) Requirements: 
 
 Must have a Personal Support Worker certificate recognized in the province of Ontario 
 Valid work/study permit (if applicable) 
 Current CPR/First Aid Certification 
 Clean Vulnerable Sector Screening (within 3 months of hire) 
 Up-to-date Immunization Records (TB Test, MMR, TDAP, Varicella, Hep B) 
 Gentle Persuasive Approach (GPA) considered an asset 
 Non-Violence Crisis Intervention (NVCI) considered an asset 
 Excellent documentation skills 
 Ability to follow client care plan 
 Strong communication skills 
 Strong problem-solving skills 
 Excellent customer service skills 
 Reliable source of transportation required 
 
 While we thank all candidates who apply, only qualified candidates will be contacted for interviews.
