 Nurse Aide Job Hiring 
 Posted on  January 9, 2013  by  caregiver 
 A POEA accredited recruitment agency in Quezon City is now accepting applicants for their ongoing  Nurse Aide Job Hiring . The said nurse aide job hiring is for government rehabilitation centers in Saudi Arabia which has job vacancies for 80 applicants who have the following qualifications: 
 
 Female 
 22-40 years old 
 At least high school diploma 
 With or without experience 
 
 All those who wish to apply for the said Nurse Aide Job Hiring should proceed as soon as possible to the office of Teknika Skills and Trade Services Inc. and submit your resume. For additional information or questions about the said nurse aide job hiring, please get in touch with Teknika Skills and Trade Services Inc. by way of their telephone or mobile numbers specified below.
