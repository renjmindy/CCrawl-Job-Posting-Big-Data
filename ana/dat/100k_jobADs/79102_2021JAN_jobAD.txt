 This is a fantastic opportunity for a Digital Communications Manager to join a fantastic financial services organisation in Birmingham, who are well-known for their excellent work culture. The purpose of the role is to build the profile of the business across external and internal audiences. You will be the digital expert within the communications team, developing digital content that engages audiences and communicates corporate messages across the business. 
 This Digital Communications Manager role will involve: 
 
 Responsibility for the development of the company website 
 Ownership of the company intranet 
 Developing digital communication strategies 
 Create content for the website and social media channels 
 Managing digital assets, such as video and photography 
 Monitoring and evaluating the success of communication channels 
 
 This Digital Communications Manager position requires a candidate with the following skills: 
 
 A passion for digital communications 
 Understanding of social media and digital and the role it plays in communications 
 Proven experience of managing CMS platforms 
 Excellent and professional communication skills, both written and verbal 
 Outstanding stakeholder management skills 
 The ability to manage a number of projects at any one time
