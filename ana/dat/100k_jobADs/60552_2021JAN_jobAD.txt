 Bell Wireless Home Internet installation Technician 
 Seeking: 
 Energetic customer service oriented sub-contractor Technicians for the position of Field Installations and Repair. Multiple positions are available, with opportunities to work under various product lines. We are seeking to fill the position with a local candidate, living in Newmarket, Bradford, Alliston, Barrie or Surrounding areas.. We perform installations for an exciting and high demand product for a nation wide provider. 
 Responsibilities: 
 Drives to and from end user locations in a general area, 
 
 Operate equipment and a vehicle, tools and ladders in a safe and responsible manner 
 Relates with customers in a professional and polite manner\ 
 Provides an extremely high level of customer service 
 Comfortable with using handheld tools and working at heights 
 Ensures test equipment, laptop and tools are kept in excellent condition 
 Safety Conscious and able to adhere to a intensely structured safety program 
 
 Position Overview: 
 Drive to various customer home locations, while meeting customer appointment windows, and deliver exceptional customer experiences and high quality installations for Satellite TV, LTE Radio Internet. Troubleshooting and repair support for same product lines. Following strict installation and quality guidelines. 
 Requirements: 
 
 Prior experience in telecommunications and/or working in the field independently is an asset 
 Prior experience in a customer facing customer service role is a tremendous asset 
 A strong technical aptitude and general interest in the field 
 _  Must have a work vehicle that is capable of having a professional roof rack installed _ 
 Work tools and fiberglass ladders free from defects 
 Must be comfortable with computers, and able to handle various Windows based applications Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal 
 Must be legally authorized to work in Canada 
 Successfully complete a criminal background check 
 Evening and Weekend work is a must 
 Maintain a valid provincial driver’s license (Class 5) and provide a driver’s abstract quarterly 
 Must be willing to work outdoors in changing weather conditions 
 Must be technically inclined and have a desire to learn 
 Installation and repair experience in TV, Phone and Internet as well Fiber is an asset but not required 
 Self-motivated and highly organized to manage workloads with minimal direction 
 Wears appropriate safety equipment and follow outlined safety protocols and procedures 
 
 ** This position requires working in varying heights and spaces in accordance with provincial and federal guidelines** 
 Candidates from outside of Ontario, Canada will not be contacted or considered 
 Job Types: Full-time, Contract, Permanent 
 Salary: $60,000.00 to $90,000.00 /year 
 Licence: 
 
 G class (Preferred)
