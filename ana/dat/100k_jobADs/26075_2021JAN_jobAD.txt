 At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers' financial needs and help them succeed financially. We're looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you'll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience. 
 Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you. 
 Wells Fargo Technology is a team of more than 40,000 information technology and security professionals who help keep Wells Fargo at the forefront of America's diversified financial services companies. Employees execute an engineering-led IT strategy to deliver stable, secure, scalable and innovative services that provide Wells Fargo global customers 'round-the-clock' banking access through in-store, online, ATM, and other channels. Wells Fargo Technology plays a critical role in the company's customer and employee experience, business and risk management transformation, and growth agenda. 
 Hands-on senior full stack java developer who has experience in solving complex problems preferably in the commodities trade processing / capital markets domain. Acts in the highest level technical role as an individual contributor and/or team lead for the most complex computer applications and/or application initiatives. Utilizes a thorough understanding of available technology, tools, and existing designs. 
 
 Works with partner tech and functional teams to automate sourcing of trade & market data feeds, managing trade life cycle process, daily processing, reporting including reporting them to downstream systems. Works on the most complex problems where analysis of situations or data requires evaluation of intangible variance factors. 
 Designs and develops applications, tools, and processes that provide superior reliability and supportability. Plans, performs, and acts as the escalation point for the most complex platform designs, coding, and testing. Leads most complex multiple modeling, simulations, and analysis efforts. 
 Relevant Experience in writing unit tests, scenario driven tests, and/or test driven development is a must. Activities will include verifying program logic by overseeing the preparation of test data, automated testing, and debugging of programs. 
 Development activities include business driven enhancements, new development, maintenance and infrastructure type development (efficient storage of data, inter-process communication, etc.). Acts as expert technical resource to programming staff in the program development, testing, and implementation process. 
 Assures quality, security, and compliance requirements are met for supported area and oversees creation of or updates to and testing of the business continuation plan. 
 Works on Enterprise objectives including enabling safety and soundness through focusing on regulatory, governance, compliance, simplification, and data enabling. 
 Follows the firm mandated SDLC process to build, deploy, test, and successfully release code into production. Assists with any production issues as and when required for banking access through in-store, online, ATM, and other channels. 
 
 Required Qualifications 
 
 5+ years of JMS (Java Message Service) experience 
 5 + years of Java Web Services experience 
 10 + years of Core Java experience 
 5 + years of JSON (JavaScript Object Notation) development experience 
 5+ years of experience testing web-based applications developed in Java or .NET framework 
 7+ years of development and implementation experience with applications, databases, or a combination of both 
 3+ years of Openlink Endur experience 
 10+ years of software engineering experience 
 7+ years of experience in one or a combination of the following: securities, quants, artificial intelligence, or machine learning 
 
 Desired Qualifications 
 
 5+ years of capital markets experience 
 5+ years of C# experience 
 5+ years of C++ experience 
 A Masters degree or higher in computer science or finance 
 Basic knowledge of industry regulations related to building technological solutions 
 A professional certification in technology 
 
 Other Desired Qualifications 
 
 Pricing, risk, and greek calculations for financial derivatives 
 5+ years of Oracle Coherence development or implementation experience 
 5+ years of experience with server administration and job creation using Jenkins Continuous Integration server

 About Wells Fargo
