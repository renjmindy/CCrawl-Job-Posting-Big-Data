 Investment Banking Analyst 
 Fort Capital Partners 
 __Calgary, AB 
 Apply Now 
 Opportunity  : 2nd or 3rd Year Investment Banking Analyst (full-time) beginning – December 2020 
 Overview 
 Fort Capital Partners is seeking a highly motivated candidate to join its team as an Investment Banking Analyst in a full-time capacity in December 2020. The selected candidate will have the opportunity to work alongside experienced bankers who have spent the majority of their career’s at large investment banks, and seasoned executives in a unique, entrepreneurial environment. You will enjoy a dynamic and challenging work environment in which you will be tasked with creating client presentations, building financial models, and conducting research while executing a broad range of transactions, including M&A, financing, and capital markets advisory mandates. A great attitude, strong willingness to learn, and the ability to complete any task – big or small – will help to differentiate the successful candidate. 
 Primary Responsibilities 
 
 Creating and updating financial models, performing financial statement and capital markets related analysis as business needs dictate 
 Conducting industry research and communicating findings to deal team 
 Preparing client presentations (including compiling data and assembling presentation materials) 
 Maintaining information databases 
 Supporting transaction execution efforts, with increasing levels of responsibility and accountability over time 
 
 Requirements 
 
 Prior experience in Investment Banking, preferably with exposure to M&A deals and raising capital 
 Outstanding academic achievement with an undergraduate degree with academic coursework in finance, economics, or accounting 
 Strong attention to detail, and ability to work on multiple projects 
 Dedicated team player 
 Demonstrated critical thinking ability and problem-solving skills 
 Well-developed written and oral communication skills 
 
 Favorable But Not Required 
 
 Accounting or other corporate finance related professional experience 
 Completion of the Canadian Securities Course 
 Enrollment in the CPA, CFA or CBV designation programs 
 
 Application Instructions 
 Interested candidates should include their resume, cover letter and a copy of their most recent academic transcript. We thank you for taking the time to apply with Fort Capital Partners. We wish that we could personally respond to everyone that applies, however we only contact those individuals selected for the interviews.
