 Yardhand at Worknet Staffing 
 Location:  Lafayette, LA 
 Lafayette area business is currently seeking a yardhand for their facility. The person hired would be responsible for using hand tools and other equipment to break down and sort materials for processing and shipment. Work will be in an open yard setting and also in a covered area as well. Some other duties may be to unload, assist customers, operate a scale, sort items, and operate equipment for breaking down and bailing materials. Previous disassembly experience or other tools experience needed. 
 $10/hr with opportunity for overtime. 
Daytime hours: Mon - Fri, 7am - 4:30pm. Rotating Saturdays. 
Long term position 
 Other requirements: 
 
 Pre-employment screening 
 Reliable transportation to and from the job site 
 Previous experience in using hand tools and powered tools 
 Ability to work in the elements 
 
 Qualified applicants - please send resumes to eugenias@worknet2k.com.
