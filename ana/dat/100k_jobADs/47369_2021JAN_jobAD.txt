 Israel Jobs for Caregiver 
 Posted on  April 26, 2011  by  caregiver 
 A POEA accredited recruitment agency is currently in search of caregivers to fill vacancies in their POEA approved  Israel Jobs for Caregiver . The Job Order for the said Israel jobs for caregiver got the POEA’s approval last April 13, 2011. And with the said POEA approval the recruiting agency is allowed to recruit 29 applicants who will qualify for the said caregiver work abroad. 
 To apply for the said Israel Jobs for Caregiver, forward your resume and application directly to the office of JAO International Manpower Services. For more details or inquiries regarding the said Jobs for Caregiver, you may ask JAO International Manpower Services thru their landline numbers included in the recruiting agency’s info stated below. 
 JAO International Manpower Services 
Unit D, 3rd Floor, Jacinto Building 
380 Scout Reyes corner Quezon Avenue 
Paligsahan, Quezon City 
Tel Nos: 5103427 
Email address: admin@jaointernational.com 
Website: http://jaointernational.com
