 About this role 
 Our Technical Researcher is accountable for strategically applying their subject matter expertise on potential integration platforms to help developers be successful when developing the Hurree platform. Their core responsibilities are a combination of troubleshooting centred around APIs and integrations as well as being proactive when assisting with deliverables for the development team. 
 In this role, you'll get to: 
 
 Educate and support the development team on platforms being integrated with Hurree 
 Assist with API and integration development questions 
 Partner proactively with other teams such as Product, Engineering, Customer Success and more to improve the Hurree development experience 
 
 We are looking for people who: 
 
 Can identify and solve for the root cause of various technical issues by applying top-notch analytical skills and diving deep into technical assets 
 Can collaborate with other teams to tackle complex challenges 
 Possess versatile communication skills for working directly with customers and partners as well as when managing internal stakeholders 
 Have an aptitude for fast and agile technical learning 
 Have experience working with technical customers and demonstrating grit in getting to the bottom of complex issues 
 Are well-versed in web development fundamentals and basic programming concepts 
 Have the ability to self-teach difficult concepts and skills 
 Are adaptable to the changing needs of our developers, and the evolving responsibilities to address those needs 
 
 Benefits of working for Hurree: 
 
 Private healthcare 
 Pension 
 Opportunity for training 
 Flexible hours 
 35 days annual leave 
 Employee discounts via Perks Directory 
 Amazing colleagues to learn from and enjoy social events 
 
 Confidence can sometimes hold us back from applying for a job. But we'll let you in on a secret: there's no such thing as a 'perfect' candidate. Hurree is a place where everyone can grow. So however you identify and whatever background you bring with you, please apply if this is a role that would make you excited to come into work every day.
