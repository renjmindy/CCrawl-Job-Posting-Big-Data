 PHP Developer LOCALPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 Middleweight developer with good knowledge of PHP required for our busy studio. Salary dependent on experience. 
 You will have had experience working in a commercial environment with a good understanding of open source content management systems as well as knowledge of working with PHP, JavaScript, MySQL, GIT and SASS. 
 You will be equally comfortable working on commerce stores and non commerce sites with a passion for well structured code and keeping up to date with the latest technologies in development. 
 Additionally some Craft CMS/Magento/Vue.js knowledge would be advantageous but not essential. 
 We are a strategically led agency working with regional and global brands delivering digital expertise. You would work amongst a talented and very experienced dev team with close collaboration with the creative and client services teams. 
 If you would like to work on diverse, technically challenging and creative projects then drop us an email.
