 Consultant: 
 
 
 We are looking for Member Service Advisors to work in a dynamic, innovative and fast-paced call centre in Birmingham City Centre. 
 In this role, you will be working within my client's Member Services team, working collaboratively to manage customer queries, via inbound & outbound calls and email. You will have the opportunity to work within a fun and friendly atmosphere, whilst answering customer questions concisely and in a timely manner. 
 You must be target driven to achieve call volumes daily, so previous experience within a similar role is essential. 
 
 Excellent customer service skills 
 Excellent written and verbal communication - you will be on those phones! 
 Positive, can-do approach and attitude 
 Have a professional and personal demeanour 
 IT Literate, use of CRM systems and Microsoft Office Package (Excel, Word, etc) 
 Ability to learn quickly, and hit the ground running! 
 
 This is a temporary assignment starting from 3rd February for 6 Weeks. 
 You will be required to work 35 hours per week, Monday to Friday, between 9am - 5pm. 
 Don't delay and apply today! 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
