 The Access Control and Security Engineering team is responsible for ensuring proper security measures and risk management throughout the company. We manage a suite of applications, ranging from data classification, data security/protection, incident management, to general physical security such as access control in company facilities. Our team is seeking a self-motivated senior software engineer with an interest in designing solutions for complex development environments and implementing scalable architectures to improve overall security throughout all levels of management in the organization. 
 In this role, you will work closely with our business partners to identify critical problems and develop robust solutions with high resilience requirements. The business areas include but are not limited to our privileging management and checking system, employee badge system, visitor management system, crisis/incident management, and emergency mass notification system. If you love owning critical systems and are passionate about using the technology that's right for the job, please apply below! 
 We 'll trust you to: 
 
 Be the owner of the product - you will work closely with the business to build a strong product 
 Be a solid team player- you can expect to work with others in a highly, collaborative environment 
 Be innovative - you'll think outside of the box when coming up with technical solutions that deliver business value 
 Be passionate- you're passionate about our diverse set of users and products 
 Be proactive- you'll contribute ideas on everything from UI, product, tech stack and our development flow 
 
 You 'll need to have: 
 
 4+ years of software engineering experience in a strongly-typed object-oriented language (C#, Java, C++) 
 3+ years of experience with a relational SQL database. 
 Strong analytical and creative problem solving skills 
 BA, BS, MS, PhD in Computer Science, Engineering, or related technology field 
 
 We 'd love to see: 
 
 Professional experience with Python 
 Familiar with unit testing, CI/CD and Dev-Ops. 
 Database schema design, performance optimization skills and query tuning 
 Experience with web services, XSD/WSDL, SOAP, SOA and micro-services architecture
