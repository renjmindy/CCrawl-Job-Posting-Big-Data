 Commercial Accountant - Sales Finance 
 
 Location 
 
 Slough, Berkshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £50000 - £60000 per annum 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Rob Anderson 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 robanderson@workwithglee.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH20786_1590571561 
 
 Published: 
 
 8 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-06-26 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Commercial Accountant and business partner with the National Account Teams to develop and build strong relationships so that you are fully involved in their financials. 
 Represent finance within customer core teams, focused on driving growth & profitability 
Complete ad-hoc financial analysis on UK management projects to ensure robust financials behind decision making process 
Review pricing structure across all products within the customer to maximize profitability 
 ROLE 
 Work within the team to develop new tools & systems to improve the return on trade investment 
Review quarterly customer forecasting & annual plans 
Assess and improve the profitability of Category product launches 
Track and supervise the customer budget and spend with proven ability to challenge where returns on spend not achieved 
Perform key tasks within the commercial finance team, to ensure that reporting is both timely & accurate 
 Requirements 
 Preferably a qualified CIMA accountant with outstanding IT skills / report creation skills 
 Good academic background and strong commercial awareness and previous experience of business partnering in a cross functional environment with a preference for experience in the FMCG/Retail industry 
 
 Driven to take on additional responsibility in an organisation that provides a high level of autonomy with ambition to progress your career within commercial finance. 
 Proven track record of building relationships outside of the finance function with well-developed communication skills 
 Confident at the challenging the status quo and both influencing and challenging decisions at a senior management level 
 Multi-tasking - need to be organised, highly efficient and able push multiple projects forward at the same time 
 Entrepreneurial - help the commercial team develop new initiatives / spot and resolve problems 
 Advanced Powerbi or Tableau and Excel skills with experience of scenario modelling
