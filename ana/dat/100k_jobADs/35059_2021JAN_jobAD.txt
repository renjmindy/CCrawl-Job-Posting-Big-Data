 An exciting business based in the West Midlands requires a year end statutory accountant with strong IFRS experience to help out for a around four months. 
 My client is transitioning to IFRS so requires a self starter of a statutory accountant to help finalise the accounts. 
 
 Preparing the monthly profit and loss, and balance sheet reports. 
 Tax reporting and inventory processing. 
 Collecting and analysing data, which is then used in the preparation of weekly and monthly estimates. 
 Spreadsheet work 
 Looking through accounts and controls 
 Adhoc system work
