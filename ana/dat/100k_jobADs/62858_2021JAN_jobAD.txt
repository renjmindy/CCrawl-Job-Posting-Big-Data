 San Francisco Passes Pay Parity Ordinance Affecting Employee Onboarding 
 By  Montserrat Miller  on July 20, 2017 
 Posted in  Background Check 
 San Francisco's Board of Supervisor's  passed  a pay equity ordinance on July 11, 2017 which is now awaiting the Mayor’s signature. The “Parity in Pay” ordinance will become operative July 1, 2018.
