 Care Worker Job Hiring Japan 
 Posted on  November 18, 2020  by  caregiver 
 20 Care Worker - Japan 
 Qualifications: 
 
 Male or Female 
 Graduate of any 2 or 4 year medical course (BSN, PT, OT, Psychology, Medtech, Radtech, etc.) 
 Should have Caregiving or Healthcare NC II 
 No tattoo 
 No criminal record 
 Willing to undergo training for 6 months and stay in the dormitory provided 
 Ex-trainees are welcome to apply
