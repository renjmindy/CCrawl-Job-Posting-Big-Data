 Assistant Management Accountant - £30,000 - £35,000k 
 Our client is a large reputable organisation based in Leamington Spa. They are looking for an Assistant Management Accountant to join their team on a 6 month contract. 
 Assistant Management Accountant duties include: 
 
 Monthly balance sheet reconciliations 
 Payment processing 
 Cash flow forecasting 
 Invoice validation 
 Fixed asset management 
 Balance sheet reconciliation 
 Accruals and Prepayments 
 Journal entries 
 Annual accounts preparation 
 
 Assistant Management Accountant Experience 
 
 Have experience working in a similar type position - Essential 
 Be part ACCA/CIMA qualified or QBE - Preferred 
 Have experience using MS Excel - Pivot tables and VLook-Ups - 
 Be a strong communicator at all levels - Essential
