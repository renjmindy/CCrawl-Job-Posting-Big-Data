 Title Settlement Agent FT Temp-To-Hire 
   
   Workway, Inc.   
 __Website    __@workway 
 Currently we are seeking a highly motivated  Title Settlement Agent  for a national title company.  The Settlement Agent will prepare and process documents and coordinate loan settlement activities 
 Essential Responsibilities are listed below: 
 
 Audit each assigned file to ensure all required closing documents (payoff letters, clear title, taxes, etc.) are present and accurate 
 Prepare closing disclosures by accurately inputting all required information to ensure closing requirements are met 
 Prepare and distribute draft of closing disclosure to all appropriate parties to ensure conformance to all stated requirements prior to scheduling closing 
 Coordinate scheduling process by initiating contact with closer and following standard procedures to ensure closing conforms to all established requirements 
 Verify loan closings by following established guidelines set forth by lender to ensure loans are clearing on time and according to customer requirements 
 Provide back-up to various department functions when necessary to ensure continuation of department workflow 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 Minimum 2 title closing experience 
 Ability to work in a fast paced environment 
 Excellent communication skills both written and oral. 
 
 Let one of our recruiters assist you in finding and preparing for interviews with our clients. Whether you are interested in a direct hire, temp-to-hire, or temporary position, Workway can help you to put your best foot forward. Contact us today!
