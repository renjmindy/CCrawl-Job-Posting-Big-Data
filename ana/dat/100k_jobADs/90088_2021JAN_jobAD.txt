 Laboratory Assistant, Anandia 
 Aurora Cannabis 
 __Vancouver, BC 
 Apply Now 
 Reporting to the Manager, Sample Logistics, the Laboratory Assistant will be responsible for the following: 
 RESPONSIBILITIES: 
 
 Receive, inspect, and log-in testing samples into Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) according to established procedures; 
 Ensure that details in client requests are correct and entered in a timely and accurate manner; 
 Provide information internally to ensure there is clarity about expected sample types and volumes; 
 Sort and distribute samples from and back into inventory; 
 Receiving and managing laboratory and office inventory, including monitoring supplies and ordering; 
 Maintain a clean, safe and orderly work environment; 
 Handling and cleaning laboratory glassware and other equipment; 
 Assists other lab personnel in sample preparation, such as labeling and weighing samples; 
 May be cross-trained to perform basic laboratory technician duties, such as balance and pipette verification; and 
 Other duties as assigned. 
 
 QUALIFICATIONS & EXPERIENCE: 
 
 Completion of a tertiary education program or a high school degree with 5 years’ of work experience; 
 Excellent organizational skills, attention to detail and ability to troubleshoot; 
 Strong communication and interpersonal skills, including strong verbal and written communication skills in English; 
 Ability to work in a dynamic, fast-paced, team environment; 
 Experience and aptitude for data entry skills; 
 Personal computer skills, including strong typing ability and proficient use of Microsoft Office; 
 Experience with LIMS or other data entry system is an asset; 
 May be required to lift, move and carry up to 40 pounds; 
 Basic laboratory skills and experience an asset; 
 Multitasking capability; and 
 Familiarity with working in an ISO/IEC 17025, GMP or other regulated environment is an asset.
