 Senior Staff Nurse 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Senior Staff Nurse to work in an exceptional clinic based in the Bingley, West Yorkshire area. 
 You will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This clinic provides treatments such as knee cartilage surgery, knee arthroscopy, knee ligament surgery, cataract surgery and shoulder muscle repair 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     An excellent and high standard of clinical practice with excellent clinical outcomes
 
 
 
     Patients health and wellbeing across a complex pathway and changing caseload
 
 
 
     Communicates delivery and outcomes of care to patients and to other members of the team
 
 
 
     Develop excellent working relationships
 
 
 
     Maintain excellent standards with infection control
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent salary from £29,000 up to £31,000 per annum DOE. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Free Car Park
 
 
 
     Flexible working
 
 
 
     Training and Professional Development
 
 
 
     Holiday entitlement
 
 
 
     Pension scheme
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a valid NMC Pin
