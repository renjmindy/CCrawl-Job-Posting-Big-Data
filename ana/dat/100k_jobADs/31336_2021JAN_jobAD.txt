 Consultant: 
 
 
 Are you seeking a Team Leadership or Supervisory role is a fast paced Warehousing and Distribution type environment that works within the retail and E-Commerce environment and can offer unrivalled job security and career development potential. 
 This dynamic and exciting business is experiencing huge growth in opportunity and is currently running at over 200% of regular capacity and has a very strong long term growth strategy for the future which will offer excellent opportunity to those that are seeking career progression. 
 Due to this rise in business and future potential, they are now seeking a to make a number of additions to their leadership levels within this fast paced warehouse and distribution environment at both a Team Leader and Supervisor level. These are key roles within this large scale facility that runs a 24/6 working week across 3 shifts. There are fixed shift opportunities across all 3 shifts and so the business can be accommodating to different personal and family circumstances in line with the supervisory requirements of the roles. 
 The key tasks will be to manage the various teams across the areas of incoming goods, flow through the facility and out to dispatch on very tight and demanding delivery schedules. The Warehouse Supervisors and Team Leaders will be responsible for managing the assigned teams in line with both internal business process and requirements as well as the required social distancing and PPE requirements currently in place across all Key Facilities to ensure the health and safety of all staff members. 
 This is a busy but rewarding environment where career progression is available and encouraged and offers the opportunity to work within an environment that is experiencing growth unlike many other sectors and so offers great stability in uncertain times. 
 The ideal candidate will have a proven team leadership / warehouse / production supervisory background within a large scale and fast paced environment such as, 3PL, Warehousing, Distribution, Automotive or FMCG and will be able to lead an mentor a group of individuals within a challenging environment where pace and accuracy are key. 
 If this sounds like an opportunity that would work for you then please contact Matt Harris for more details asap as they are looking for people to start immediately.
