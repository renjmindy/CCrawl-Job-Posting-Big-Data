 Walsall, West Midlands

 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group have been exclusively retained on an assignment to recruit a Commercial Analyst for a successful and diverse manufacturing business based in Walsall. The Commercial Analyst is to report to the Finance Director and will sit within the finance structure therefore a qualified candidate (ACA, ACCA, CIMA) would be a preference but not essential. 
 The business is a private equity backed manufacturing business that has a diverse client base that offers stability to the business in the current climate, this role offers the opportunity to make a real commercial difference and the chance to make a huge impact. 
 Key responsibilities:Commercial Analyst, Walsall, £40,000-£50,000 
 
 Analyse financial information (including Gross Profit, inventory & variances) to provide insights to leadership. 
 Responsible for gathering financial and operational data and providing assessments, analysis of the data and profit/cash improvement activities. 
 Formulation/validation of pricing strategies. 
 Final verification of sales prices and margin (Ops are responsible for cost calculation). 
 Evaluate inventory levels and associated working capital requirements. 
 Supply Chain contract signoff. 
 
 Key Skills & Experience: Commercial Analyst, Walsall, £45,000-£50,000 
 
 Strong analytical skills. 
 Strong oral and written communication skills. 
 Report writing and presentation skills. 
 Experience of a private equity business. 
 Experience in a small to medium sized business. 
 Able to demonstrate relevant experience in a manufacturing business. 
 Experience as an Analyst. 
 A candidate should be ACA, ACCA, CIMA or qualified by experience. 
 
 For more information on this exciting new role please do get in touch
