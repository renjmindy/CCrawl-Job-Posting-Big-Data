 Environmental Service Aide - Kitchener/Waterloo 
 Qualicare Waterloo 
 __Kitchener-Waterloo, ON 
 Apply Now 
 QCareKWC is looking for professional and experienced individuals to join our team as an Environmental Service Aide. We provide a variety of services that focus on providing the best quality of life for clients in the community. 
 The successful candidate will be employed in residential and professional settings (e.g. home environment, office environment, etc). 
 As an employer, QCareKWC offers the following: 
• 
Competitive wages 
 • 
Flexible Schedules 
 • 
Benefits Program 
 Environmental Service Aide Role: 
• 
Conduct assessment of client's residential area, to determine hours needed to complete services 
 • 
Perform a variety of cleaning activities such as sweeping, mopping, dusting and polishing 
 • 
Ensure all rooms are cared for and inspected according to standards 
 • 
Protect equipment and make sure there are no inadequacies 
 • 
Notify superiors on any damages, deficits and disturbances 
 • 
Handle reasonable complaints/requests with professionalism and patience 
 • 
Maintain a friendly and professional relationship with clients. 
 Requirements: 
• 
Proven experience as a Cleaner or Housekeeper (minimum of 1 year) 
 • 
Ability to work with little supervision and maintain a high level of performance 
 • 
Customer-oriented and friendly 
 • 
Prioritization and time management skills 
 • 
Working quickly without compromising quality 
 • 
Recent Vulnerable Sector's Check 
 • 
Knowledge of English language 
 • 
Valid Driver's License and access to a vehicle
