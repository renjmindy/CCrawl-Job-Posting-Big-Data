 Zonal Manager- West 
 May 15 
 Posted by  Navin Rane 
 We are looking for a Zonal Manager- West for our client iStreet Network Limited which has a game changing eCommerce project- iStreet Bazaar. Please mail your applications to pooja@istreetnetwork.com  or call on 9821151716. 
 At iStreet Network Ltd we promise our customers end consumer reach to the remotest location in India. The Zonal Manager does just that in his/her allotted zone. We seek a leader who can roll up his /her sleeves and be flexible to do whatever it takes to meet the business objectives. 
 **** Job Profile- 
 
 Be a part of the growth strategy and build franchise in the assigned zone. 
 Meet targeted number of franchisees by directing and guiding Regional Managers under his/her zone. 
 Ensuring high service levels and Customer Satisfaction across all franchisees in the zone. 
 Ensuring franchisees are effectively trained and updated with latest information broadcasted by the Head Office. 
 Month on Month increase in Business Revenue through sales generated via franchisees. 
 Network Partner Retention. 
 Direct Responsibility for building and management of a team of Regional Managers and Sales Executives. 
 Recommends product lines by identifying new product opportunities, surveying consumer needs and trends in coordination with the franchisees. 
 Be a source of feedback from franchisees and end consumers for the Marketing Team to aid in developing the business strategy on the whole. 
 Inter Departmental Coordination and flexibility to execute various roles in order to meet Zonal Objectives. 
 
 **** Requirements- 
 
 Post Graduate Marketing or equivalent Degree 
 6-8 years of Retail/Branch/Channel Sales Experience

 Assistant Manager Operations-Leading eCommerce Company 
 Oct 22 
 Posted by  Navin Rane 
 We are looking for a Assistant Manager Operations- eCommerce for our client which is one of the largest eCommerce Company in India. It is an eCommerce enabler. The company is into online retail and creates an online channel for products to be sold.. Mail your applications to  consultant02@netsyscon.net  or call on 9819474458. 
 
 Report to-  Head Operations 
 Role Definition- 
 ****Manage a group of Trading Agents while coordinating with clients. Role is to list products on various E-Commerce sites by providing adequate information to the Buyer. 
 Knowledge, Skills and Abilities required- 
 Good Learner, Sincerity, Creativity, Accuracy and Speed, Leadership Quality, Strong Communication Skills 
 Job Description- 
 Lead a team of Trading Agents and Manage listings for Assigned Accounts. 
 List and Manage Products on various E-Commerce Sites by coordinating with various departments like Imaging Logistics and Customer Support. 
 Maintaining Stock Inventory at the Market Place. 
 Generating Invoices and verifying with products to be shipped 
 Updating shipping Status on E-Commerce Sites. 
 Timely Reporting 
 Be passionate about his/her work and walk an extra mile where required. 
 Requirements- 
 A career oriented go getter, flexible, hardworking with a never say die attitude. Should think out of the box and be eager to learn. 
 Minimum 2 years experience into a service oriented industry 
 Educational Qualifications Min Graduate. 
 Computer Savvy with good knowledge of the Internet 
 Microsoft Office 97-2003- Level Average 
 Internet Browsing skills-Level Excellent 
 Typing Speed- 25 words per min
