 Summary: full-time job until end of year in Barcelona (not remote work) in JavaScript and back-end in C# 
 Dear all, 
We are a German company of the Fin Tech sector. We work with most of the biggest Eurpoean banks like HSBC, Santander or Deutsche Bank. We are growing very fast! And we have opened a new office in Barcelona. 
We have already hired one full team of developers and we are looking for the second one to start a brand new project. In particular, we are planning to develop a web application that will use an AI algorithm to advice on best strategies to protect your investment against market fluctuations. 
 A brief summary of your tasks: 
- Write a new branch of products that will use AI to design hedging strategies 
- Analyze complex problems, propose solutions and discuss them with your colleagues 
- Work in a Scrum team responsible for the whole development cycle 
- Work in an international environment and collaborate with other teams in Germany and Switzerland 
- Have fun, learn and thrive professionally 
 We are looking for: 
- Full-stack software developer 
- Familiar with C# and .NET 
- Familiar with JavaScript 
- Strong analytical skills 
- Fluent in English 
- Team spirit 
- And last but not least... a good sense of humor! 
 It is a plus if you have: 
- Knowledge of Typescript and Angular or React 
- Knowledge of SQLServer and Entity Framework 
- Knowledge of Git 
 Are you interested? We look forward to hearing from you!
