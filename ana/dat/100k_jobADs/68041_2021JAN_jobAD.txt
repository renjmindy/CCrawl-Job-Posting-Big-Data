 Dump Truck Operator 
 Job description 
 
 
 Immediate Start 
 
 
 Lifestyle roster 
 
 
 $48.50/hour 
 
 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking experienced Dump Truck Operators to commence at a coal mine in the Moura region. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Operating Komatsu 830 Dump Trucks under Excavators 
 
 
 Pre-Start Checks 
 
 
 Meet production targets 
 
 
 Identify and report workplace hazards 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Komatsu 730 minimum experience 
 
 
 Signed Skills Lists 
 
 
 Min 12 months operating experience within a production environment 
 
 
 Current Standard 11 
 
 
 Current Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Current QLD Drivers License 
 
 
 Right to work in Australia 
 
 
 Culture & Benefits 
 
 
 Ongoing work 
 
 
 Weekly wages 
 
 
 Opportunity to work with a reputable company
