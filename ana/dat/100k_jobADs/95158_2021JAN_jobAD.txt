 Trainee MPU Operator 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Immediate start 
 
 
 $52.45 p/hr 
 
 
 High potential for permanent role after successfully completing probationary period 
 
 
 About our client 
Our Client is a leading manufacturer in explosives and is seeking a Trainee Explosives Operator for work in the Gunnedah Basin. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
Reporting to the Drill & Blast Supervisor, you will be required to: 
 
 
 Safely operate the MPU Truck in order to transport and deliver dangerous goods 
 
 
 Assist the Shot Crew where necessary 
 
 
 Maintain and organise the MPU Truck 
 
 
 Work as part of a team within a high risk work environment 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Unsupervised Handling Licence (UHL) (or willingness to obtain) 
 
 
 HR Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Must be able to supply a Police Check and Driving Record 
 
 
 Dangerous Goods Licence 
 
 
 Similar experience preferred but not essential 
 
 
 Ability to complete a Pre Employment Medical and D&A; Test 
 
 
 Please note: No camp facilities are offered, this is a residential role only. 
 About WorkPac
