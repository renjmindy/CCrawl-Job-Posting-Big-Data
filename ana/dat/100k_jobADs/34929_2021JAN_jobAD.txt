 Sample Reception & Handling Assistant 
 FTC 12 Months, with possibility to extend - Humber Bridge Hull 
 Our client, a leading provider of life science solutions, are now looking for a Sample Reception & Handling Assistant to join their team on site at Humber Bridge , Hull on a prestigious emergency project for a fixed term contract of 12 months that could well be extended further on a rolling basis. 
 The Benefits 
 - Salary up to 18 to 20k DOE 
 - Excellent benefits 
 The Role 
 
 Experience with Customer Service 
 Ability to build relationships quickly 
 Attention to detail in a fast-paced environment 
 Can do attitude with flexible approach to adapt to the department's needs 
 Reliable & Dedicated 
 Strong team player with a positive attitude 
 Excellent level of English (verbal and written) 
 Strong MS Office (particularly, Outlook, Word and Excel) 
 Excellent Administrative skills an advantage 
 Must be a driver and have transport due to location and shift patterns 
 
 This role is on a full-time contract basis for a fixed period of 12 months, with the possibility of further extensions. 
 There is a shift pattern as the emergency project is a 24/7 operation. 
 Shifts are as follows: 
 07.00 - 15.00 
 15.30 - 23.00 
 23.30 - 06.30 
 It is 37.5 hours a week. 
 1st shift is 37.5 hours , 2nd shift 35 hours and 3rd shift 32.5 hours , however, you are paid 37.5 hours every week. 
 This is an excellent opportunity to be part of a brand-new Team working on an exciting and new project. 
 If you feel you have the right experience and skills, please apply with your CV and a member of our team will get back to you.
