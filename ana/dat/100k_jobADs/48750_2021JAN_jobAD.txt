 Plant Operators 
 Enterra Feed Corporation 
 __Calgary, AB 
 Apply Now 
 Position: Plant Operators 
 Starting Date: As soon as possible 
 Reporting to: Supervisor 
 Location: Rocky View County, AB 
 At Enterra, our core purpose is to harness the power of insects to feed and care for the world. Since 2007 we have been pioneering large-scale, sustainable insect production in North America, producing the highest quality ingredients for our innovative animal feed and pet food customers. 
 We live by our core values of Quality, Integrity and Innovation. The team at this dynamic company is full of motivated and hardworking people that are committed to making the world a better place. 
 We have a new, 180,000 square-foot production facility in Rocky View County, Alberta – the first of its kind in the world. We are looking for committed and engaged Plant Operators in all areas of our facility to contribute to the safe and reliable operation of this unique and cutting-edge farm and a production facility. 
 WHAT ENTERRA OFFERS YOU 
 
 Competitive wages starting at $19.00/hour (plus $1.00/hour for shifts starting afternoon) 
 Comprehensive benefits package 
 Participation in company bonus programs 
 Positive company culture focused on open communication and transparency 
 
 AREA RESPONSIBILITIES/DUTIES: 
 All Areas: 
 
 Strict adherence to health and safety policies 
 Adherence to Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) when completing duties and responsibilities 
 Daily cleaning of equipment and areas to maintain high sanitation standards 
 Accurate reporting and data collection to maintain production records 
 Light preventative maintenance duties 
 
 A100 Feedstock Receiving and Preparation: 
 
 Receive and offload delivery trucks using forklifts; accurate data recording of truck deliveries 
 Use of skid-steer to prepare and process recycled food inputs (produce, grains, liquids) 
 Managing feedstock material flow in tanks and silos 
 Able to perform physical requirements of the position, which includes standing, bending, pulling, lifting and pushing. 
 Best suited for a hard worker who enjoys a fast-paced environment and doesn’t mind getting their hands dirty 
 
 A200 Larvae Rearing: 
 
 Feeding and rearing Black Soldier Fly (BSF) larvae using our proprietary technology, equipment, and procedures 
 Learning and understanding the health and biological requirements of BSF larvae 
 Heavy use of computer software programs to track and organize production units 
 Best suited for an effective multitasker who is well organized, has strong computer skills and has an interest in biological processes and technology 
 
 A300 Product Processing: 
 
 Efficiently operate and monitor industrial processing equipment to ensure the product meets company quality standards 
 Strict adherence to Standard Operating Procedures to ensure the quality of the product 
 Ensuring high sanitary and quality control standards are met 
 Use of forklift to organize and transport product within the work area 
 Able to perform physical requirements of the position, which includes long periods of standing, bending, pulling, lifting and pushing 
 Best suited for a hard worker who enjoys a fast-paced environment and is committed to meeting high-quality standards in the product. 
 
 A400 Hatchery and Breeding: 
 
 Learning and understanding the health and biological requirements of the adult BSF and eggs 
 Monitoring and ensuring biological requirements of the adult BSF are met 
 Careful attention to company protocols for predictable production flow 
 Use of forklift and pallet jack 
 Use of pressure washers, vacuums and other equipment to complete daily cleaning tasks 
 Best suited for a hard worker with high attention to detail who is interested in caring for the health of the adult flies and eggs 
 
 A500 Warehouse and Quality Control: 
 
 Bagging, storing and organization of finished product and inventory 
 Conducting quality control sampling and lab work (training provided as necessary) 
 Use of forklift to load finished products onto pallet racking and into delivery trucks 
 Ensuring high sanitary and quality control standards are met 
 Recording production data, shipping records, daily reports and other necessary data in adherence with company procedures 
 Best suited for a meticulous and detail-oriented individual who is comfortable both in the lab and on a forklift 
 
 A600 Incubation: 
 
 Learning and understanding the health and biological requirements of BSF larvae 
 Heavy use of a forklift to organize products in the work area 
 Use of pressure washers, vacuums and other equipment to complete daily cleaning tasks 
 Best suited for an adept forklift driver with strong organizational skills 
 
 QUALIFICATIONS: 
 
 Strong team player with good interpersonal skills and a strong work ethic 
 Fluent in English, with good verbal and written communication skills 
 Ability to work independently and in a team with minimal supervision 
 The basic use and knowledge of Microsoft Office (MS Outlook and MS Excel) 
 Ability to lift loads of up to 20 kg 
 Experience with industrial or farm equipment an asset 
 Ability to multitask and work in a fast-paced environment 
 Food manufacturing experience is an asset 
 Must hold a valid driver’s license 
 
 SCHEDULE: 
 
 The plant operates 6am to 12am, 7 days/week with morning, afternoon and evening shifts available depending on the area 
 Shifts are 8.5 hours, including two paid 15 min breaks and one unpaid 30 min break; 5 days/week – all schedules include shifts on Saturday, Sunday or both 
 Schedules are subject to change with advanced notice 
 
 For more information about Enterra, please visit www.enterrafeed.com.

 Selected candidates will be called and scheduled for an interview.
