 Over at American Writers & Artists Inc, they are hosting a FREE virtual event to teach you ways to make money from home. 
 Many working professionals, new moms, empty-nesters, folks who are recently retired or have been down-sized, and people who are just plain tired of the 9-to-5 feel exactly the same way. Just like you, they want to experience the benefits of owning their own home-based business. 
 And on February 19th, you can learn how you can take advantage of it for FREE during American Writers & Artists Inc.’s event of the year … Work-At-Home Career Summit. 
 
 During this completely virtual event, you’ll learn eight proven work-at-home opportunities in 2011 from work-at-home professionals who are successfully doing them, including how you can get started too.
