 Care Assistant Jobs Australia 
 Posted on  October 26, 2009  by  caregiver 
 As one of the leading providers of aged care in the Hunter and Central Coast Regions of Australia, this agency needs to maintain a substantial number of care assistants that will provide Retirement Living, Day Therapy, Residential Care, Community Care, Dementia Specific and Respite Care to more than 1,000 residents and clients. And to achieve their goals, they need to hire qualified applicants for care assistant jobs who are motivated, energetic, and dedicated persons, to join their Community care team. At present, they provide care to residents across 7 facilities and community care clients and offers casual, permanent, and part time positions. 
 To qualify, all applicants must possess the following: 
 
 Certificate in Aged Care Work (CHC30102) 
 Current First Aide Certificate 
 Previous experience in Residential Aged Care 
 Previous experience in documenting Residents' care and progress is a plus 
 Experience in dementia care is an advantage 
 Eligible to work in Australia 
 
 If you are interested in working in Care Assistant Jobs at one of their Residential Aged Care Facilities, please see the attached job description which can be found in their website at www.anglicancare.com.au or contact Anglican Care's Recruitment Officer, Jessica Meadows, on 4958 0093. 
 You may also address your applications to: Human Resources Manager, Anglican Care, Toronto Road, Booragul or jobs@anglicancare.com.au
