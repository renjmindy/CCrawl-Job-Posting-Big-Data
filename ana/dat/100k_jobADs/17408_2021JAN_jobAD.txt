 Location 
 
 Surrey, England 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £65000.00 - £85000 per annum + Bonus, Pension, Flexi bens

 Published:

 2020-02-12 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Based in Surrey, the position of Controller / Senior modeller for this Japanese IT / Electronics client requires a fully qualifed accountant to manage the areas of statutory obligations of tax and accounting within EMIEA. 
 Year 1 will be: 
 Leading a team of 4, you will be responsible for the implementation of new transactional in alignment with new cloud based reporting tool. 
 Business Partner with the existing finance & IT team to define detailed process 
 Manage the business process in documentation in a transparent and clear manner 
 Lead practical testing and training in finance and outsourcers 
 Year 1 - 3 
 Sustain financial operation in cooperation with management strategy 
 Manage the statutory requirement by advanced reporting of SAP 
 Support the regional branches in the EMIEA region with the finance team 
 Coordinate consolidation and Group Reporting to Japan 
 Required: 
 A fully qualified accountant with excellent communication skills and experience working closely with IT teams. 
 SAP/Hana 
 You will have experience working through a full life cycle of a implementation project 
 Ability to capture root cause of the key issues and provide solutions 
 Transparent and systemic way of work aligned with management 
 Demonstrate and implement continuous learning. 
 SAP Hana experience desirable.
