 Nanny Jobs Oman 
 Posted on  September 10, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This is a perfect opportunity for all the Filipino nanny who want to work in nanny jobs abroad. The POEA recently approved a job order for nanny jobs last September 4, 2009. The said nanny jobs are for the country of Oman and have a job order balance for 50 nanny jobs candidates. The said job order for nanny jobs Oman was approved for a recruitment agency based in Malate, Manila. 
 For more info on the said nanny jobs Oman, just visit the nanny jobs recruitment agency directly or contact them through the contact info stated below. This nanny jobs vacancy is on a first come first serve basis. 
 YOBHEL International Manpower Services Inc. 
1872 Taft Avenue cor. Remedios Street 
Malate, Manila 
Tel No/s: 3022035 / 3022036 
Email Address:  YOBHEL@I-NEXT.NET
