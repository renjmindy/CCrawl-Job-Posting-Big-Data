 Technical Writer REMOTEPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 Pixel & Tonic, the team behind Craft CMS, is looking for an experienced Technical Writer. The ideal candidate is someone who can communicate complex information clearly, concisely, and accurately. As a technical writer at Pixel & Tonic, your role will involve writing documentation, technical guides, and tutorials, as well as reading and writing example code. This position requires someone who is self-motivated, organized, and enjoys some level of content strategy, in addition to writing. Experience developing and using Craft CMS is preferred. 
 This position is an integral part of our plans to improve Craft CMS docs and educational resources. In addition to excellent written skills, we look for a candidate that has great verbal communication skills, is friendly, and has a good sense of humor. At Pixel & Tonic, we work hard to create software that developers love using, but we like to have fun while we do it! 
 In this role, you’ll have the opportunity to: 
 
 Make a meaningful impact on and help grow the Craft Community by providing documentation and educational resources 
 Help shape the future of documentation and written content at Pixel & Tonic 
 Travel internationally for conferences and team trips 
 Work remotely from anywhere in the world 
 Have access to all the tools and tech you need to work effectively 
 Earn a competitive salary with profit sharing and healthcare benefits 
 
 To succeed in this role you should have the following skills and experience: 
 
 Professional experience as a technical writer in the software and technology field 
 Strong writing skills with an emphasis on proper grammar, spelling, and composition 
 The ability to create from scratch as well as revise existing documentation, guides, and articles 
 Proven ability to translate technical and development processes into easily understood documentation, guides, and articles 
 Self-motivated and can work independently 
 Ability to communicate effectively with both technical and non-technical customers 
 Ability to analyze complex problems and present solutions 
 Web development experience 
 Experience with Craft CMS and Craft Commerce is preferred 
 
 How to Apply 
 If writing documentation and helping shape the future of Craft CMS sounds like your kind of challenge, we want to hear from you! Instead of your standard resume and cover letter, please send a short write-up addressing your experience, how it pertains to the skills listed above, a link to one or two writing samples, and a little bit about why you want to work for Pixel & Tonic, to  [email protected] , with “Technical Writer” in the subject line. 
 This can be a part-time or full-time salaried position.
