 Millwright Technician 
 Enterra Feed Corporation 
 __Calgary, AB 
 Apply Now 
 Position: Millwright with HVAC experience 
 Starting Date: As soon as possible 
 Reporting to: Maintenance Supervisor 
 Location: Rocky View County, AB 
 At Enterra, our core purpose is to harness the power of insects to feed and care for the world. Since 2007 we have been pioneering large-scale, sustainable insect production in North America, producing the highest quality ingredients for our innovative animal feed and pet food customers. 
 We live by our core values of Quality, Integrity and Innovation. The team at this dynamic company is full of motivated and hardworking people that are committed to making the world a better place. 
 We have a new, 180,000 square-foot production facility in Rocky View County, Alberta – the first of its kind in the world. We are looking for one experienced Millwright/Maintenance Technician with HVAC experience to contribute to the safe and reliable operation of this unique and cutting-edge farm and production facility. 
 WHAT ENTERRA OFFERS YOU 
 
 Competitive wages 
 Comprehensive benefits package 
 Positive company culture focused on open communication and transparency 
 
 AREA RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 
 Minimum 2 years of related experience; with HVAC knowledge 
 
 
 Analyze, diagnose and resolve plant problems 
 
 
 Foresee potential problems to prevent premature and unexpected breakdowns 
 
 
 Strong time management skills and organizational skills 
 
 
 Good mechanical aptitude 
 
 
 Able to read wiring diagrams and troubleshoot problems with electrical, air conditioning, exhaust fans and AHU units 
 
 
 Able to operate all necessary tools and equipment to perform various service projects 
 
 
 Must be able to work independently as well as in a team environment 
 
 
 Perform other duties as assigned 
 
 
 Familiar with BMS systems 
 
 
 Strict adherence to health and safety policies * Troubleshoot issues and work with the Maintenance Supervisor, Site Engineer, and other employees and vendors to resolve issues in a timely manner 
 
 Conduct regular preventative maintenance and repairs 
 Liaise with third-party contractors and suppliers to support maintenance requirements 
 Provide input into the continuous improvement planning cycle 
 Maintain accurate records on equipment inspections, preventative maintenance and inventory counts 
 Understand and respect the biological impacts of the process, including livestock health, nutrition, rearing and breeding 
 
 QUALIFICATIONS: 
 
 Multidisciplinary maintenance experience in an industrial environment, including mechanical, electrical, controls, HVAC, overhead cranes, and more 
 Forklift, aerial lift and fall protection certification or experience is an asset 
 Ability to lift loads of up to 20 kg 
 Ability to multitask and work in a fast-paced environment 
 Must hold a valid driver’s license 
 
 SCHEDULE: 
 
 The plant operates 6am to 12am, 7 days/week with the potential for morning, afternoon and/or evening shifts 
 Shifts are 8.5 hours, including two paid 15 min breaks and one unpaid 30 min break; 5 days/week 
 Schedules are subject to change with advanced notice
