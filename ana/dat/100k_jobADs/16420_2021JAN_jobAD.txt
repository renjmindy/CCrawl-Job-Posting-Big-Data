 Job description 
 Fitter 
 
 
 3-month role in Gunnedah 
 
 
 Monday - Friday (8 hour shifts) 
 
 
 $38/hr + penalties 
 
 
 About the Role 
 We are looking for an experienced Fitter with a broad range of skills in maintenance, repair, and installation of fixed plant-based equipment such as screw conveyors, hoppers & packaging plant machinery. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Scheduled & breakdown maintenance 
 
 
 Installing new plant equipment 
 
 
 Reading and interpreting drawings 
 
 
 Keep a safe work environment 
 
 
 Work unsupervised 
 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Trade Certification as a Fitter (Fitter & Turner, Machinist) 
 
 
 Ability to understand engineering drawings 
 
 
 High safety standard
