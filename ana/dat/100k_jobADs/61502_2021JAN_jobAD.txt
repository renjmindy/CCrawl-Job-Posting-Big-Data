 Care Giver Jobs Bahrain 
 Posted on  November 27, 2011  by  caregiver 
 A well-established multi-disciplined international manpower exporting company based in Quezon City is in need of caregivers for their POEA approved  Care Giver Jobs Bahrain . The recruitment of applicants for the said care giver jobs Bahrain was allowed by the POEA thru a Job Order that was approved last November 14, 2011 allowing ABCA International Corporation to select 3 caregivers for placement in the said caregiver work abroad. 
 To apply for the said Care Giver Jobs Bahrain, proceed directly to the office of ABCA International Corp. and forward your comprehensive and updated resume. If you have any question regarding the said care giver jobs Bahrain, don’t hesitate to contact ABCA International Corporation by way of their landline numbers we have provided below.
