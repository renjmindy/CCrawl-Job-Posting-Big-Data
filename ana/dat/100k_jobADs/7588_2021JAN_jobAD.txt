 PROJECT ACCOUNTANT - Located Near Marlow 
 Our Internationally successful fast paced rapidly growing organisation based near Marlow require an experienced part qualified or equivalent accountant to join their team. 
Reporting into the Financial Controller you will be responsible for the below duties and responsibilities. 
 CORE RESPONSIBILITIES: 
 
 Monthly Preparation of Revenue, CoS and Project WIP for review by Financial Manager 
 Create, update and reconcile projects; providing relevant documentation 
 Pro-actively raise and manage client invoicing and credit control, maintaining net 30 days debtors 
 Liaise with Project Managers regarding project cash flow and profitability margins 
 Weekly update of Project Finance reports and reconciliation with SalesForce 
 Assist with Monthly Financial Review with project team 
 Assist Finance Manager in timely closure of month-end and production of financial accounts 
 Assist Finance Manager with project analytics and reporting tools 
 Maintain audit trail and assist Finance Manager with all Audit requirements 
 Manage workload effectively with other Project Accountants to ensure high quality and timeliness of deliverables 
 Assist Financial Assistants at month end to ensure Finance Team achieves month end deadlines 
 Managing telephone and email queries from suppliers and internal colleagues 
 
 KEY REQUIREMENTS 
 
 Ideally part qualified, AAT, CIMA, ACCA or equivalent and with circa 2-3 years relevant experience 
 Must have transparent approach to delivering output which stands the test of scrutiny 
 Confident with financial principles and ability to check for completeness and accuracy of information presented 
 An eye for detail with good knowledge of Microsoft office applications and strong systems skills 
 Ability to review processes and systems with a view for continuous improvement 
 Analytical and inquisitive mind with ability to simplify financial data for non-financial colleagues 
 Ability to work to agreed deadlines and quality 
 Highly organised and able to manage conflicting priorities 
 Good inter-personal skills and is able to build effective relationships across the organisation 
 Flexible individual and team player with willingness to adapt to changing environment 
 
 You will enjoy working in a fast paced, dynamic and rapidly growing organisation where you can achieve excellent career development opportunities. 
 For further information on the role of Project Accountant please contact me directly.
