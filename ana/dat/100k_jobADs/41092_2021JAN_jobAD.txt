 Jobs and careers in Pfedelbach 
 Jobs at the Willi Elbe Group in Pfedelbach 
 JOBS AND TRAINING FOR PFEDELBACH 
 The Willi Elbe Group has a total of eight presences in Germany, Switzerland, Norway, Bulgaria, Mexico and China. The location in Pfedelbach, founded in 1966 due to a shortage of skilled workers at the company’s headquarters in Tamm, is our largest production plant in the world. 
 On a production area of ​​around 11,000 m², around 250 employees work in Pfedelbach. Jobs and training vacancies, especially in technical occupations, are regularly advertised. 
 In addition to state-of-the-art assembly technology for car steering spindles, the Willi Elbe Group in Pfedelbach has laser technology for aluminum welding as well as equipment for machining and Rilsan coating. 
 Living and working in the Pfedelbach region 
 Pfedelbach consists of six districts, but has only around 9,000 inhabitants. The municipality belongs to the region of Heilbronn-Franconia and is located in the south of the Hohenlohe district, in the federal state of Baden-Württemberg. The Upper German-Rhaetian Limes – a Roman wall and a UNESCO World Heritage site – runs through Pfedelbach. 
 Heilbronn is around 30 km away, Stuttgart 70 km. But in the direct vicinity of Pfedelbach there are also many appealing sights as well as attractive jobs. Here is a small excerpt ...
