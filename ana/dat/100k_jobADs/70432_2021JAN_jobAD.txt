 Due to sickness cover, my client needs an immediately available Interim ER Advisor to start asap. £17.95PH - £18.40PH. 
 Working as part of a small team you will provide Employee Relations advice and guidance remotely. As an experienced ER professional you will have strong working knowledge of the ACAS Code of Conduct. You will also support around Welfare and Long term sick cases. 
 As an experienced Interim ER Advisor, you will be immediately available. You will work remotely initially, but should guidelines change you will be required to work in the Lichfield office.
