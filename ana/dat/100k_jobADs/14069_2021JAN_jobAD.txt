 Sweet Baby Jason! it's a Full-time job 
 ntwrk is looking for a Motion Graphics Designer in Los Angeles, CA, USA. 
 About 
 At NTWRK, we’re looking for a Motion Graphics Designer to deliver the NTWRK audience an elevated content experience through engaging on-screen graphics. 
 What You'll Do 
Support the Content group with all on-screen graphics needs such as intro cards and lower thirds 
Develop templatized show graphics packages that can be shared with various internal teams and external creative partners 
Translate static branding treatments into motion graphics 
Create visual treatments and animations for various social media franchises 
Work closely with various creative partners to execute and deliver marketing assets that support NTWRK’s daily product drop 
Partner with internal and external motion graphics designers to create promotional videos for key initiatives and virtual festivals 
Present new concepts and executions to the creative leader 
Develop and implement creative treatments that contribute to, and are in line with, NTWRK’s brand position and identity
