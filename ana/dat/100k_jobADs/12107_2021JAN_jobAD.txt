 Administrative Support 
 BAYVIEW TRUCKS & EQUIPMENT LTEE - Moncton 
 __New Brunswick 
 Apply Now 
 Responsible for providing Administrative support to all departments of the Moncton Truck dealership. The Administrative Assistant works well in a team environment, demonstrating exceptional organizational skills and a positive attitude 
 Duties include but are not limited to: 
 
 Assist the Truck Sales Administrator to manage, process and file all Truck Sale deals. ie; Set up of stock truck information in the business system, update records routinely with information from Kenworth and sales department. Take Vehicle registration changes to Service New Brunswick 
 Maintain daily update of current inventory and location and assist with flooring audits as required. 
 Follow up on Open LPO’s to close or VOID 
 Maintain Customer Master list 
 Balancing cash reports, preparing deposits, 
 organizing and filing documents. 
 Preparing weekly time recaps for Parts and Service hourly employees. 
 Ensuring adequate inventory of office supplies, coffee, water and cleaning supplies for staff. 
 Assisting in the Service department with answering phone calls and greeting customers; always providing excellent customer care and support. 
 
 Job Type: Contract 
 Salary: $15.00 to $16.00 /hour 
 Experience: 
 
 office administration: 3 years (Required) 
 
 Location: 
 
 New Brunswick (Required)
