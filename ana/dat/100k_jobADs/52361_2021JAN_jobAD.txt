 WHO WE ARE 
 The BYP Network was created to 'change the black narrative' by bringing the black professional global community together to solve our own problems through economic empowerment and connectivity. Following recent events, BYP is more relevant than ever and is excellently positioned to bridge the gap between the black professional community and corporations. Today we have over 50,000 members on our network, launched a mobile and web platform, run offline and online events to up-skill the community, have a job board and have worked with 60+ corporate clients including Facebook, Accenture, AirBnb, Nationwide, BT etc. Our vision is to be the go-to platform for black professionals and grow the network to 500,000 members globally in the next 12 months. We are looking for other passionate and determined team members to help us get there! 
 THE ROLE 
 We are looking for a Copywriter and Content Creator to write and publish innovative, impactful and industry related content, for our online audience. You will be responsible for researching and developing, engaging brand messaging across various social media and web-based platforms. We are looking for strong writers who are self-motivated, organized and possess strong self-editing skills. The ability to effectively collaborate with internal teams is crucial for success. 
 DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 Create clear, compelling copy for our website, events, newsletter 
 Use the best digital practices from UX, SEO and other digital disciplines to maximise reach of the content 
 Produce daily blogs 
 Support the BYP content strategy with high quality research and ideas for content pieces 
 Re-work and re-purpose existing content that's been created to maximise discoverability and reach for new partners and community members we're on boarding 
 Create case studies to highlight our customers and company's success 
 
 KEY REQUIREMENTS 
 
 2+ years of proven content and copywriting experience 
 Excellent writing, editing and proofreading skills 
 Ability to work under pressure and efficiently 
 Good knowledge of digital content creation and promotion strategies 
 Team spirited - you'll be working with both our community and corporate teams. 
 Excellent time management and organisational skills 
 Self-motivated to work independently and deliver high quality work without close supervision 
 
 WHAT WE OFFER 
 
 Competitive salary 
 Work from home flexibility 
 Training and support 
 Experience in a high growth, high profile company 
 Access to a global network 
 Socials with like-minded individuals

 Please include a cover letter detailing your experience and motivations.
