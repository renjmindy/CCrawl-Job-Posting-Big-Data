 Content Writers for Seo Articles 
 Published: Last month  Bids: 11 
 We are looking for Content Writer to produce at least 10 English articles for each of the following industries: 
 - Pet Products 
- Accounting Software (for a brand which is popular within South East Asia market) 
 The articles will be used in a website intended for SEO purpose. 1,000-1,500 words are needed for each of the article. 
 You should have the following skills: 
 - Basic understanding on how to write SEO-friendly contents 
- Familiar with using WordPress editor (Gutenberg) to produce contents 
- Comfortable with researching on given topics / keywords 
 If you are selected for this job, the preferred workflow will be as follows: 
 - You will be given the following: 
 
 a primary keyword, and some secondary keywords 
 One or two sample articles for those keywords for your reference 
- You are required to draft out the content structure of the article that you will be writing based on the given keywords and your own research. You should propose the title of the article, and also the sub-topics within that article 
- We will review your proposal and if your proposal is approved, you can proceed to write the article contents. 
- You will be given login to a staging WordPress site where you can write and save the articles. 
 
 Our expectations on the articles quality: 
 - The articles must pass plagiarism checker with uniqueness score above 90% 
- Besides text, the content should consist of images, tables, embed videos and other content types whenever it is relevant. 
- We do not expect perfect grammar but the contents should be easy to comprehend by human. Newbie content writers are welcomed to apply. 
 Please answer the attached screening questions so we can better know your writing skills. 
 Include a single word (in all capital) that best describes you (e.g. passion, humble, hardwork and etc) in your application cover letter so we can filter out those who apply without finish reading this job description.
