 Gleeson Recruitment Group are delighted to be working in partnership with a growing privately owned Manufacturing Business based in Worcester who are looking for a Finance Director to look after Group Finance strategy. This will be a number 1 role in a face paced and hands on role for a market leader. 
 Role 
 
 Leading the groups finance strategy 
 Coordinating the production of the Group's consolidated results 
 Ensuring management accounts for all business areas are produced on a monthly basis within agreed timescales 
 Maintaining and developing the Group's financial control environment ensuring compliance with external and internal audit requirements 
 Managing the finance function 
 Representing Finance on the Group's Senior Management Team (SMT) and contributing to Group wide initiatives 
 
 Requirements 
 
 Fully qualified (ACCA, CIMA OR ACA) with 10 years PQE minimum 
 Manufacturing Experience 
 Microsoft Dynamics experience 
 Leader of Finance in a Group structure is desirable 
 
 Salary & Benefits 
 Up to £90k depending on experience 
 Package will include a Car Allowance, substantial bonus dependent on company performance and enhanced pension. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
