 Position Overview: 
The Senior Release and Change Management Analyst is responsible for CIBC Mellon’s Change and Release management framework and associated processes and procedures in support of continuously delivering project releases from the planning phase to deployment and handoff to operational support across multiple service providers. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Accountable to provide support to Tier 1 applications specific release management resources in various business areas and departments, as well as to service providers and vendors, by coordinating and providing release management consultation in a timely and efficient manner 
 Leads communication and manage expectations from project teams and stakeholders during the planning and implementation of new project releases 
 Liaises directly with internal Information Technology employees and vendors, participates in release/change vendor meetings Conduct release readiness reviews for project implementations/stakeholders for top tier applications and vendors 
 Assessing, analyzing and approving all technology changes impacting CIBC Mellon, across multiple service providers 
 Responsible for all communication of changes and change impact to CIBC Mellon stakeholders as required 
 Responsible to provide KPI metrics for measuring and reporting on Change & Release activity while identifying opportunities for improvement. KPI metrics includes: 
 
 o Tracking release changes, percentage of success of release changes, use of testing 
environments, and release calendars across all system applications, service providers and 
vendors 
 
 Ability to define critical analytics to measure success, drive continuous improvement, and make key data driven decisions 
 Formalizes and oversees a release plan that covers integration testing, user acceptance testing, documentation and associated operational procedures, such as system management instrumentation, support procedures, run book procedures, service catalog, configuration management database (CMDB) and rollback planning 
 Peer mentor analysts in the release and change management processes and provide guidance according to the department mandate 
 Contribute to process improvements within release and change management activities 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or equivalent discipline 
 4-6 years of in-depth experience in release management of a technology platform 
 understanding of ITIL Release & Change Management disciplines and Project Management methodologies and lifecycle 
 Working knowledge of systems development lifecycle 
 Ability to influence results through decision-making and influencing key stakeholders 
 Self-starter, very detail oriented and extremely efficient and effective working with and obtaining support from cross-functional teams 
 Experience in Asset Servicing systems would be an asset 
 
 Working Conditions Unique to Job (i.e. Standard Office Environment) 
 
 Normal office hours, with some requirement for after hours or weekend to support major initiatives 
 Fast-paced work environment, with multiple competing high priority initiatives
