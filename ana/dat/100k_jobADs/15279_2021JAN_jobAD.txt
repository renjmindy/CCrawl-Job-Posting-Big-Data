 Maintenance Site Administrator 
 Job description 
 Site Administrator 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Ongoing role 
 
 
 10 hour shifts 
 
 
 On site systems training provided 
 
 
 Mine site based role 
 
 
 About the Role 
 The successful candidate will be an integral part of our clients administration team in providing quality administrative support in the day to day running of their operations, with the primary focus to deliver an efficient, effective and professional Administration service based in the Gunnedah basin, the roster will initially be Wednesday to Friday then moving to Monday to Friday. 
 Duties 
 
 
 General Administration duties 
 
 
 Organising inductions & on-boarding 
 
 
 Accommodation bookings 
 
 
 PPE & Parts Ordering 
 
 
 Raising purchase orders 
 
 
 Creating quotes 
 
 
 Use of spreadsheets 
 
 
 Assisting with creating rosters 
 
 
 Data entry 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Advanced skills and proficient in Microsoft office Suite (Excel, word) 
 
 
 Demonstrated ability to multi-task and manage multiple priorities with excellent time management skills 
 
 
 Ability to work well under pressure and in a fast paced environment 
 
 
 Energetic and highly motivated 
 
 
 Team player with strong interpersonal skills 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors.
