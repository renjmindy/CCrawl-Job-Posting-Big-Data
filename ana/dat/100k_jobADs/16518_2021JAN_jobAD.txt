 Agent Representative - Sales - Insurance - Call Center 
 US Energy Co. 
 Job Description 
 Entry level positions in: customer service, sales, and sales management, individuals who want to excel within a company. We also offer paid training for selected candidates. 
 We are expanding at a tremendous rate! We are currently hiring for entry level and experienced customer service and sale representatives who we can bring onto our already prestigious firm. Our company is looking for ATHLETES and SPORTS-MINDED individuals who are going to take us to the next level during this unprecedented time of expansion! 
 ** 
What’s Our Competitive Edge?** 
 1. We aren't the new guys on the block in Consulting, Sales & Customer Quality! We are local, established and already connected to the community. 
 2. Our proven consulting system filters through our target market and identifies the long-term, low-maintenance and high-profit customers. 
 3. We have generous benefits and a great company culture. 
 ** 
Because our demand is so great and we only promote from within, we need career-minded individuals who can develop a working knowledge of our systems and have the ambition to learn, teach, develop and lead others. The agent will need to have a great student mentality and be able to succeed at two things simultaneously; working with and taking care of customers, as well as learning about leadership, management and our systems.** 
 ** 
Paid training included.** All positions are entry level, (regardless of experience) and promotions are based upon initiative and merit.  A 4 year college degree is preferred but not mandatory. 
 Job Requirements 
 JOB REQUIREMENTS FOR AGENTS: 
 • Strong verbal, interpersonal and listening skills 
 • Effective organizational and proactive problem solving skills 
 • Confidence in working in an environment of complexity and ambiguity 
 • Expertise in time management 
 • Highly motivated self-starter 
 • Ability to work as a team member in a dynamic, fast-paced environment as well as independently to deliver results 
 • Demonstrated leadership in community and/or professional organizations 
 • Ability to travel overnight are required. 
 • Bachelor’s Degree is preferred – Most Commonly Considered Majors: Public Relations, Communications, Economics, Marketing, Management, and Business & Entrepreneurship 
 ** 
 We Are Looking For COMPETITIVE, STRONG, SPORTS-MINDED Individuals To Take Our Company To The Next Level. Our firm maintains the policy of training our employees from scratch in the following areas.** 
 • Entry Level Management 
 • Campaign Development 
 • Event Coordination 
 • Public Relations 
 • Sales Associates 
 • Customer Service
