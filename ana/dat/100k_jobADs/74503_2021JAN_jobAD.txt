 Are you an experienced Assistant Accountant? Can you start work immediately? 
 I am looking to speak to immediately available Assistant Accountants to join my client based in Lichfield for a period of 6 months. You will be supporting the Management Accountants with balance sheet reconciliations, bank reconciliations and preparations of monthly management packs. 
 You should be AAT qualified and have started your ACCA/ CIMA. You can expect a salary between £20,000 - £25,000 depending on experience. 
 Assistant Accountant duties 
 
 Monthly management accounts preparation 
 Assist with the production of profit and loss 
 Maintain sales invoices reports 
 Accruals and prepayments 
 Bank reconciliations 
 Balance sheet reconciliations 
 Journal postings 
 
 To apply for the Assistant Accountant position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
