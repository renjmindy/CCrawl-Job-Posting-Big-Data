 Clinical/Forensic Psychologist 
 A fantastic new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Clinical/Forensic Psychologist to work in an exceptional service based in the Mansfield, Nottinghamshire area. 
 As a Psychologist you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This service provides rehabilitation for men with a Mental Illness and/or Personality Disorder. 
 The aim of the service is to equip the service user with the skills needed for community living and increased independence 
 As the Clinical/Forensic Psychologist your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     Supporting the clinical team and area of service in developing new initiatives and ways of working
 
 
 
     Working with internal and external agencies to enhance treatment pathways for service users
 
 
 
     Supervising and line managing qualified and in-training staff members
 
 
 
     Working effectively as a team member, and to take the lead on psychological assessment and interventions within the service area
 
 
 
 An ideal Clinical/Forensic Psychologist must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Experience working in a similar setting.
 
 
 
     CBT skills
 
 
 
     Leadership experience
 
 
 
 **The successful Clinical/Forensic Psychologist will receive an excellent salary of £40,000 pro rata. 
 This exciting position is a Part Time role working hours in between 16 - 24 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     The equivalent of 33 days annual leave - plus your birthday off!
 
 
 
     Free meals and parking
 
 
 
     Wellbeing support and activities
 
 
 
     Career development and training
 
 
 
     Pension contribution
 
 
 
     Life Assurance
 
 
 
     Enhanced Maternity Package
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be BPS recognised Psychology degree with HCPC registration
