 Consultant: 
 
 
 A well-known, market leading service provider based in Leeds are looking for a Finance Systems Data Analyst to join their team to support a business critical project. 
 You will be responsible for not only providing support to month end reporting and delivery of data migrations, but you will also act as a data reporting SME performing data extraction and analysis across multiple platforms. 
 Along with the ability to troubleshoot reporting issues, you will also maintain documentation on data models an provide support across the systems. 
 What do we need from you? 
 
 Advanced SQL experience with the ability to analyse large data sets and run SQL queries 
 End to end data analysis and extraction 
 Knowledge of SAP and background in Finance/Understanding of Accountancy principles 
 Ability to build data transformation models 
 Understanding of ETL processes and ability to forecast and report 
 Passionate about data and determination to resolve issues 
 
 Please apply here with your CV or contact Keeley for further information.
