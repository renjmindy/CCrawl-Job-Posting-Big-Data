 Live in Caregiver Job 
 Posted on  July 4, 2011  by  caregiver 
 Families from the holy land of Israel are in need of the services of live in caregivers who are willing and qualified to work in  Live in Caregiver Job . These live in caregiver job was approved for recruitment by the POEA last June 17, 2011 for a licensed recruitment agency located in Salcedo Village in Makati City. And to fill the vacancies for the said live in caregiver job, the recruiting agency needs to find 30 caregivers who are interested and qualified for the said caregiver work abroad. 
 If you are interested in applying for the said Live in Caregiver Job, proceed directly to the office of Uniwide Technical Services Inc. and submit the following: 
 
 Two (2) sets (clear copies) of your biodata stating your objective and the job or position you desire 
 Two (2) pieces 2×2 I.D. picture, white background, with collar and no makeup 
 NBI Clearance 
 Photocopy of valid Passport 
 Photocopy of Birth Certificate 
 Photocopy of Marriage Certificate 
 
 If you have any question regarding the said live in caregiver job, contact Uniwide Technical Services Inc. thru their landline number or email address that we have provided below. 
 Uniwide Technical Services, Inc. 
Unit 2-E Classica 1 Condominium 
112 H.V. de la Costa St., Salcedo Village 
Makati City 
Phone: +63-2-817-3627 
Fax: +63-2-812-0112 
Email: recruit@uniwide.com.ph 
Website: www.uniwide.com.ph
