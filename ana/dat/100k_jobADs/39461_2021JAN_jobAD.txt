 Production Operator 
 Job description 
 Manual handling and ongoing exposure to hazardous chemicals 
 Our Client is currently looking for an experienced store person for ongoing work within their western suburbs warehouse. 
 The Role 
 The purpose of this role is to maintain all stock in the warehouse and pick orders using safe manual handling procedures in an effective and timely manner. 
 Key Responsibilities 
 Undertake all duties in a safe, responsible and courteous manner 
Plan, pick and cross check orders 
Ensure warehouse is neat, clean and in a 'ready to pick' state at all times 
Assist in all site stock takes 
Key Competencies, Skills and Qualifications 
 Must have current and valid forklift licence 
Understand company OH&S; 
Good communication skills 
A sound understanding of stock control 
Possess a high level of accuracy with attention to detail 
Good time and organisational skills 
Able to work in an active and fast paced environment 
Flexible and adaptable 
Have a basic understanding of the end to end distribution process
