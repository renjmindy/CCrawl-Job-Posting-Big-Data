 Home Manager 
 An outstanding new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Home Manager to work in a brand new nursing home which will be opening in Spring 2021 based in the Liverpool area. 
 As the Home Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This nursing home will offer nursing, dementia, residential care, mental healthcare and specialist learning disability services to its residents 
 An ideal Home Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     In depth understanding of the regulatory requirements such as Health and Social Care Act, CQC regulations, KLOEs and rating characteristics
 
 
 
     Passionate about providing high quality person-centred care and be dynamic and driven with clear ambition to develop
 
 
 
     Ability to deliver outstanding care for our residents
 
 
 
     Dementia experience is essential for this position
 
 
 
     Leadership skills
 
 
 
     Commissioning a new luxury care home would be valuable experience
 
 
 
     Great organisational skills
 
 
 
 **The successful Home Manager will receive an amazing salary up to £60,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role for 40 hours a week from 9am to 5pm. 
 In return for your hard work and expertise you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Annual performance related pay
 
 
 
     Comprehensive induction programme
 
 
 
     Career development opportunities from a fast-growing group
 
 
 
     Generous holiday allowance
 
 
 
     Private medical cover
 
 
 
     Generous pension contribution
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse on RGN or RMN with a valid NMC Pin
