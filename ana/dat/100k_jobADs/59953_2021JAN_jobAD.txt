 EASTERN AIRWAYS RECRUITS CABIN CREW WITH AND WITHOUT EXPERIENCE 
 The airline based in UK, Eastern Airways has opened the application pre-screening portal. The company is recruiting flight attendants for Cardiff base. You don’t need previous cabin crew experience to join them. Once your file passed the first recruitment phase, you will be placed on waiting pool for a future assessment day. 
 Requirements: 
 
 18 years of age 
 Able to work independently as a single cabin crew member 
 Previous experience in a customer service role 
 Immaculate appearance with weight in proportion to height 
 Own transport, hold a full clean driving license and able to report to your base within 90 minutes 
 Have internet access 
 Have or be eligible for the right to live and work in the UK. 
 
 Download the pre-screen  [Application Form](https://www.easternairways.com/_assets/media/editor/Careers/Pre-screen Cabin Crew application form 2020.docx)  , complete it and electronically email to this address:  HR.recruitment@easternairways.com . The email subject should be:  Web Application . Good luck! 
 If you want to prepare for the future cabin crew interviews, please contact us by email: contact@nicoletagherman.ro. Our trainer will give you all the details about our Online 1:1 Preparation Courses held daily.
