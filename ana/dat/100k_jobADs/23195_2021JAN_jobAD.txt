 Senior Full-Stack Developer (Python) 
 Location : 
 Kyiv / Kharkiv 
 We are ready to offer : 
 
 Competitive salary based on your experience level

 Opportunity to work and communicate directly with the customers 
 Your health support via gym compensation and vacation policy 
 Free English classes twice a week (if working from the office) 
 Partly or fully compensation of specialized conferences, meet ups etc. 
 Modern spacious office in the heart of the city or remote job 
 
 About the work : 
 In this role you will be working on the core products, developing software spanning cloud servers, web applications and edge devices as a part of a dynamic team of software and algorithm developers. 
 About the project : 
 It enables autonomous operation of heavy machinery using machine vision technology (perception). The company has certificated CE products for cranes and excavators. 
 Technological stack: Python, PostgreSQL, Angular, Redis, Elastic Search, Docker, AWS, Linux. 
 Requirements : 
 
 5+ years of professional software development experience 
 Knowledge and experience with cloud environment and services (AWS - an advantage) 
 Backend oriented - proficiency in server-side languages, technologies and methodologies, in Linux environment 
 Experience with CI/ CD pipelines, hands-on Docker and docker-compose 
 Experience with building APIs and services using REST, HTTP requests, etc. 
 Excellent written and verbal communication and interpersonal skills 
 
 As an advantage : 
 
 Server-side languages: Python, Node.js etc. 
 Experience with distributed SW architecture and design: micro-services, multi-processing/ multi threading, etc. 
 Familiarity with Frameworks such as Angular / React / Vue etc. 
 Experience with Python web frameworks (Django, Flask, Falcon) – strong advantage 
 Experience with SQL and databases (PostgreSQL – an advantage)
