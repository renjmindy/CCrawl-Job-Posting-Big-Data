 Consultant: 
 
 
 My client is looking for a Fiannce Ststems Analyst to join their team on a 9 month FTC. The role is to help with the delivery of their Aderant finance system to help improve the service they provide. The role is homebased but there may be travel to the midlands on rare occasions. 
 Key duties: 
 
 Assist with the design and configuration of the Aderant finance system along with the finance team. 
 Supporting the UAT and go live assistance of the Aderant solution and working with key stakeholders to test, improve and ultimately deliver the final solution. 
 Reporting issues and working alongside the finance system team and Aderant to provide solutions 
 Completing data conversions, modifications and customisation's for the system implementation 
 
 The successful candidate must have the following: 
 
 Recent experience in the implementation of Aderant is essential. 
 A proven ability to work efficiently and effectively under strict deadlines, including experience in prioritising competing workloads and managing expectations 
 Drive, self-motivation and enthusiasm 
 Commitment to delivering excellent levels of service to internal and external clients at all times 
 
 They are looking for candidates who are available to start with them in the next 2 months. Please apply if this is something you think would be suitable for you.
