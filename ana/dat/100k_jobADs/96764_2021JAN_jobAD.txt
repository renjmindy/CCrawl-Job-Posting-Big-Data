  CAREEREALISM TV: Creating a Strong Career Identity  
 Work It Daily 
 January 18, 2009 
 On this week's episode, nationally syndicated career strategist,  J.T. O'Donnell , shares why you need to pay attention to creating a strong career identity. If you aren't developing a good personal brand, then you are missing out on the best job opportunities. She answers viewers questions that included: 
 
 
 How do I reach out to companies I want to work for when A) I don't know anyone there, and B) they aren't hiring. 
 
 
 What should I say in the e-mail when I send my resume to a company? 
 
 
 How do I get hiring managers to pay attention to me? 
 
 
 Watch today to learn how you can build a brand that will get you the results you are looking for.
