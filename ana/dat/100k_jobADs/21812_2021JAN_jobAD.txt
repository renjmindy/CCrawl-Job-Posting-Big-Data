 Immediate position available for an Accounts Payable Clerk. 
 The ideal candidate should have strong organizational and communication skills, the ability to multitask and needs to work well independently, as well as within a team setting.

 -Preparing and maintaining proper records for all accounts payable

 1-2 years of accounting and/or bookkeeping experience is preferred.
