 Registered Mental Health Nurse 
 Band 5 Registered Mental Health Nurse (RMN) Southampton 
 I am currently recruiting for an experienced Registered Mental Health Nurse to support patients with a variety of issues including Personality Disorder, Eating Disorder, Learning Disabilities, Rehab, Drugs & Alcohol. 
 As a Registered Mental Health Nurse, you will be based in a reputable NHS hospital within the modern city of Southampton and will play a pivotal role in the nursing care of patients. 
 The Limited Company rates of pay are up to £28.00 per hour (Uplifts for Nights and Weekends) 
 Requirements for a Registered Mental Health Nurse are as follows: 
- 6 Months Post graduate experience in Mental Health Nursing position 
- Enhanced DBS Certificate 
- NMC Registration 
- Mandatory Training Certificates 
- Vaccinations (TB, Hepatitis B, Varicella, MMR) 
 Service Care Solutions are specialist providers of staffing to the NHS for both nursing and healthcare assistant across the UK. 
 The benefits of Service Care Solutions for a Registered Mental Health Nurse: 
- £150 Sign up bonus for qualified workers 
- Dedicated and experienced one-to-one consultant support 
- FREE enhanced DBS check 
- FREE occupational health checks and immunisations 
- £250 Training Allowance 
- £250 Referral Bonus 
- Daily Payroll Runs 
- Nationwide provider of mental health staff to over 40 different NHS trusts 
 This position is available immediately, so for more information please contact myself or forward your CV to Jake.Nuttall@servicecare.org.uk
