 Family Support Worker 
 To join an established team within Norfolk Council based in Norwich. 
 This role is to manage and be accountable for a complex caseload of vulnerable 
children, young people and families to provide targeted support and 
challenge as part of an integrated approach to prevent family problems from 
escalating. 
Candidate Profile 
The successful candidate for this position will have experience of working as a Family Support Worker. 
 Has a willingness to learn new skills on the job and be able to work within a fast paced team environment.
