 Digital Marketing Officer 
 A fantastic opportunity for a  Digital Marketing Officer  to join the marketing team for a B2B professional services organisation based in Solihull paying up to £32-£36k with upto 15% pension! 
 What you'll do… 
 
 Execute B2B digital marketing campaign strategy across a range of digital channels to generate required leads, build brand awareness, encourage cross sell and improve customer retention. 
 Using knowledge and experience, recommend, test and implement latest digital tools and strategies to continually improve execution of digital marketing campaigns and optimise channels. 
 Develop and implement innovative ideas for new digital marketing approaches to achieve Marketing Plans 
 Demonstrate the ability to cut through the "noise" to make a company message resonate 
 Reporting and analysis of results to demonstrate ROI 
 Act as the area digital marketing expert and go-to resource 
 
 What you'll need… 
 
 Broad B2B strategic digital marketing experience, innovation and creativity. 
 Experience of delivering segmented targeted campaigns 
 Knowledge of managing performance marketing channels; social media, paid media, SEO, data analysis. 
 Minimum 3 years experience of working with B2B digital tools including social channels, marketing automation platforms and interactive marketing tools 
 Experience of Pardot, Hootsuite, Google analytics and Salesforce desirable 
 Demonstrable ability to deliver industry leading key metrics 
 Excellent written and oral communication, copy writing and attention to detail, ability to multitask and work with different teams 
 Hands on with the ability to work autonomously and implement at pace 
 Mindset to test new channels in a controlled way and react quickly to continually improve performance 
 
 What you'll get... 
 
 A competitive salary -(£32-36k) 
 Work from home days 
 Free onsite parking 
 25 days holiday plus 8 bank holidays (plus one extra day at Christmas) 
 Private Pension up to 15%! 
 Private Health Care 
 Corporate discounts scheme 
 Annual performance based bonus (usually 2-3% of salary) 
 Great modern working environment 
 
 Digital Marketing. £32-36k. Solihull, Birmingham, West Midlands.
