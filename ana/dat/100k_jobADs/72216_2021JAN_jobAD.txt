 Company Description 
 BlueOptima provides industry-leading objective metrics in software development enabling organisations to deliver better software, faster and at a lower cost. Our technology empowers 12 of the Fortune50 companies, 4 of the 10 largest banks in the world and 6 of the S&P50 companies. 
 Founded in 2006, BlueOptima is a profitable, high-growth independent software company with 160+ employees across 6 locations: HQ in London and 5 additional offices in India, Mexico and the US. 
 Job Description 
 We are looking for a HO Insights Consultancy based in Phoenix, Arizona to lead our Data Science team and drive customer engagements. The successful candidate will be driven, with expertise in senior stakeholder persuasion along with project and team management experience and a passion for data. You and your team will closely work with customers to identify key drivers of value, position the relevant data insights offerings and flawlessly deliver them.  Provided expert consultancy and build senior relationships to help drive the value of engagements and successfully position the Data Science team, along with responsibility for all projects. 
 Key Responsibilities 
 
 Understand and articulate customers' needs in terms of data insights 
 Build relationships to sell BlueOptima's Data Science services across new and existing clients 
 Manage and develop a team of Data Analytics Consultants 
 Reach and surpass own KPIs such as team utilization, revenue and customer satisfaction 
 Guide customers and own team on key technical decisions related to Data Science 
 Train Sales and Customer Success teams on Data Science value proposition positioning 
 
 Qualifications 
 
 Master's degree or equivalent, preferably in a technology related discipline 
 3+ years experience leading and growing teams 
 4+ years as customer-facing project manager or management consultant (MBB preferred) 
 Strong communication and presentation skills, including C-level engagement 
 Experience with Data Analysis & Data Visualisation tools (i.e. Tableau, Qlikview, SAS BI) 
 Understanding of the SDLC and statistical analysis desired 
 Python/R programming and SQL/database administration experience is a plus 
 
 Additional Information 
 Why work for us? 
 
 Profitable product with strong value proposition yielding 100% year-on-year revenue growth 
 Inclusive, collaborative and meritocratic working culture with 22 nationalities represented 
 Organic company headcount growth, resulting in career opportunities 
 Annual company meet-ups, of which last one took place in Cancun, Mexico 
 Competitive salary: $95k - $105k annually (OTE $135k - $145k) 
 
 All your information will be kept confidential according to EEO guidelines 
 Interview Process 
 Our recruitment process comprises of four stages to practically test candidates' abilities: 
 
 Send us your CV - should you be shortlisted, a member of our Talent Acquisition team will be in contact with you to arrange an initial chat. 
 Should your skill set and attitude match the requirements of the role, we will arrange a second interview with the Hiring Manager of the role. 
 The next step involves a work sample, which we allow you a maximum of 7 days to complete 
 Providing the work sample is to a good standard, we will invite you to our office for the final interview, where you will meet your potential new manager.
