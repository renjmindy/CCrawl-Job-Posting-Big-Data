 Excavator Operator 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 2&1 Roster 
 
 
 FIFO from Perth 
 
 
 Opportunities for full time employment in 3 months 
 
 
 About the Role 
 An ongoing opportunity exists for experienced production excavator operators at Fortescue Metals Group Cloudbreak mine site. 
 Reporting to the Production Superintendent, you will form part of the production team as a Production Excavator Operator, loading autonomous dump trucks. 
 As a key member of a high performing team, you will play an integral role in implementing our client's production requirements. 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Experience operating Terex RH120, 170 and 340 excavators or equivalent 
 
 
 Caterpillar D10, 11 dozer experience would be essential 
 
 
 Autonomous site experience highly regarded 
 
 
 12 months + mining production experience 
 
 
 Current National Police Clearance 
 
 
 Manual C Class Australian issued Drivers Licence (minimum)
