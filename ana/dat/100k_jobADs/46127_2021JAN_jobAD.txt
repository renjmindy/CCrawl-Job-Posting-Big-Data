 LIMS Administrator - Deeside 
 LIMS Administrator 
 FTC 12 Months, with possibility to extend 
 Deeside 
 Our client, a leading provider of life science solutions, are now looking for LIMS Administrator to join their team on site with an emergency COVID 19 project for a fixed term contract of 6 months that could well be extended further on a rolling basis. 
 As a LIMS Administrator you will need to be well versed in data collection and processing software as well as experience in processing laboratory extracted data, hence you will know the process/format and will be able to hit the ground running with in this emergency project. 
 The Benefits 
 - Competitive Salary 
 - Excellent benefits 
 The Role 
 
 Support the Covid lab teams with administration duties 
 Receipting submissions through laboratory information management system (LIMS) 
 Adhere to SOP's, work instructions and Quality Management system (QMS) at all times ensuring good laboratory practice (GLP) is followed 
 Report any non-conformances using local and company quality systems 
 Take a proactive approach to Continuous Improvement within all aspects of the day to day role 
 Take a proactive responsibility for the Health and Safety of yourself and your colleagues 
 Communicate effectively to team members to ensure a smooth flow of work through the laboratory process. 
 Work in accordance with values of customer focus, competence and team spirit, quality and integrity 
 Ability to build relationships quickly 
 Attention to detail in a fast-paced environment 
 Have a Can-do attitude with flexible approach to adapt to the department's needs 
 Excellent level of English (verbal and written) 
 Strong MS Office (particularly, Outlook, Word and Excel) 
 Experience in a hospital, clinical or scientific environment, use of LIMS would be an advantage 
 Excellent Administrative skills 
 Must have own transport and can drive 
 
 This role is working on a shift pattern, earlies and lates, on a full-time contract basis for a fixed period of 12 months, with the possibility of further extensions. 
 This is an excellent opportunity to be part of a brand-new Team working on an exciting and new project. 
 If you feel you have the right experience and skills, please apply with your CV and a member of our team will get back to you
