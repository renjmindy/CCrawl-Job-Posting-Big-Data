 Hyperion (HFM) Systems Accountant 
 Hyperion (HFM) Systems Accountant 
 
 Location 
 
 Leeds, West Yorkshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Contract 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £45000 - £55000 per annum 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Arshi Thind 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 ArshiThind@workwithglee.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH18417_1578512907 
 
 Published: 
 
 about 1 year ago 
 
 Duration: 
 
 12 months 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-02-07 
 
 Startdate: 
 
 ASAP 
 Overview: 
 
 To be a key player in the finance systems team, providing operational support for the HFM environment 
 To ensure that the system and processes are compliant and up to date with all relevant reporting standards, practices and audit requirements. 
 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Ensure that all changes, data mapping and translation between the ERP and other financial systems using Hyperion FDMEE is processed correctly; 
 Test internal processes and procedures for the HFM toolset, ensuring that all policies, procedures and guidance documentation are up to date; 
 Provision security class access and manage existing security classes/groups for HFM and Hyperion Financial Reporting (HFR) 
 Ensure compliance with audit and security reviews; 
 Provide training and insight to business users; 
 Consult with functional groups across Finance teams to understand and clearly articulate business requirements and ensure that the HFM application and services are aligned with business needs; 
 Ensure data integrity and consistency across multiple reports; 
 Provide HFM support during critical reporting periods such as Year-End finance reporting 
 
 Qualifications and experience: 
 
 Qualified Accountant (preferred), or part-qualified/ qualified by experience; 
 5+ years hands-on experience with HFM, FDMEE application administration, including creating and maintaining Hyperion Business Rules; 
 In-depth accounting knowledge, with hands-on experience of managing systems administration and minor development; emphasis on HFM, Smart view, and Essbase; 
 Experience of acquiring and transforming data from ERP Finance systems - ideally SAP ECC.
