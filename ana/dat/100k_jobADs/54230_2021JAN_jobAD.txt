 Email to Case Maintenance: Creating Bulk Delete Jobs 
 __ 132 views  __ August 16, 2018 __ March 2, 2020 __ 0 
 Maintenance involves deleting  event log  records created by Email to Case Automation add-on on a regular basis. This can be done by creating  Bulk Delete  jobs from the  Settings ➤ Data Management section of CRM . 
 Bulk Deleting Emails 
 Sample Example: Creating Bulk Deletion job which runs every 7-day and deletes the event log records created by Email to Case Automation add-on: 
 Go-to Settings ➤ Data Management  and click “  New  ”: 
 Step 1  : Start Bulk Deletion Wizard, click ‘Next’ 
 Step2  : Define Search Criteria, click ‘Next’ 
 Step 3  : Select Bulk Deletion Options: 
 Step 4  : Submit the Bulk deletion job
