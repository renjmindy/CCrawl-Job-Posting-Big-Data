 Legal Clerk 
 Service Care Legal are currently recruiting on behalf of a local authority in Lincolnshire, who are seeking a Legal Clerk to join their Childcare Litigation team. 
 This is a fantastic opportunity for recent law graduates, experienced Clerks or those legal professionals interested in public sector work. 
 Please find below further details about the role: 
 Please note this this position is currently working from home, and this is unlikely to change for the duration of the contract. 
 ROLE: Legal Clerk 
LOCATION: Lincolnshire (remote working) 
CONTRACT: Initial 3 months, ongoing. 
 37 hours per week. 
RATE: Up to £11.62 per hour LTD (umbrella) 
 The Role 
 , To provide administrative and clerical support to the Legal Services team, particularly in the Childcare Litigation team 
, To maintain and update case management system and update department case lists 
, To enter all legal post in the log and issue appropriate acknowledgement letters 
, To undertake duties (where able to do so) such as scanning, printing, photocopying 
 The Person 
 , At least 6 months of previous Legal sector experience is highly desirable 
, It is essential to have strong administrative skills and a strong communicator 
, Comfortable in undertaking extra adhoc duties when required 
, To have strong IT literacy with systems including MS Word, Excel and teams 
 If this sounds like an opportunity of interest, please feel free to reach out to Lloyd Stanley on 01772 208969, or email Lloyd.stanley@servicecare.org.uk. 
 We also welcome referrals for this position, where a successful recommendation would be worth £250.
