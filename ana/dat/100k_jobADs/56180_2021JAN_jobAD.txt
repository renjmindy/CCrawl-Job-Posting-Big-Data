 Full-Time Marketing Communications Manager 
 
 Anywhere 
 
 Job Description 
 We are modernizing our marketing team and seeking an enthusiastic and innovative Marketing Communications Manager to join us in a new role in Lahti. Job description In this role you will have a chance to work with an internationally known and attractive brand. You will lead a first class team of 3 professionals to execute the Group level brand and external communications strategy globally. You will report to the Marketing Director. 
 Your main tasks will be: 
 
 Brand management, development and related training 
 Integrated marketing communications activities 
 MarCom partner network management 
 New product commercialization process management together with product managers 
 Media relations 
 Management of, and also active participation in article, white paper, issues (e.g. blogs) production 
 Measurement and analyzing brand performance and success of our communications activities, as well as competitor and main markets 
 
 To succeed in this position, we expect you to have: 
 
 Master's Degree in organizational communications, marketing, or related Experience (pref. 10+ years) in MarCom management within international B2B and/or industrial goods and services 
 Experience and interest in web communication, including social media, Inbound marketing etc., and their utilization in business development 
 Effective project management skills 
 Fluent oral and written communication  skills both in Finnish and English  , other languages appreciated 
 Good briefing skills, and an ability to manage effectively large entities 
 Ability to conceptual thinking and seeing the big picture 
 Comfort in working under pressure within tight deadlines 
 Good interpersonal and teamwork skills 
 
 What we offer: 
 
 Broad and exciting role in which you will have a chance to develop Kemppi's MarCom activities in the global market and support the business in today's marketing communication 
 Chance to work with an internationally known and attractive brand and with modern and versatile welding management solutions consisting of equipment, software and services 
 An opportunity to develop your professional skills at a successful and growing company of 16 subsidiaries worldwide 
 Additional training needed for the job
