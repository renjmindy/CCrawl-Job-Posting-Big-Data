 Matthew Schultz's Music Career - From Dark To Light 
 Posted on  February 4, 2013  by  WUIS - Your Source for News 
 Area artist and musician  Matthew Schultz  has seen and been in his fair share of art and music scenes. In this interview, he looks back on the past couple decades of music that he's been a part of. From the industrial rock super-group Pigface, to the ambient stylings of his latest musical project, Schultz recently spoke with WUIS'  Rachel Otwell  about taking his music from dark, to light: 
 https://wuisnews.files.wordpress.com/2013/01/schultz.mp3
