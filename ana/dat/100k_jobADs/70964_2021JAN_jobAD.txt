 Now Brewing – Associate Brand Manager, partnerships! #tobeapartner 
 From the beginning, Starbucks set out to be a different kind of company. One that not only celebrated coffee and the rich tradition, but that also brought a feeling of connection. We are known for developing extraordinary leaders who share this passion and are guided by their service to others. 
 “We are building an enduring company, with mission and values grounded in the human experience” 
– Kevin Johnson, Starbucks Coffee Company 
 We are seeking candidates with: 
 
 Passion for partnership development and marketing, with thorough business acumen 
 Strategic thinking capabilities to support the development of innovative campaigns 
 Previous experience implementing, managing and optimizing partnerships 
 
 As an associate brand manager, partnerships, you will be responsible for: 
 
 Project management and assisting with implementation of large-scale strategic partnerships, requiring management of cross-functional, cross-border and external teams 
 Workback schedule creation, monitoring and risk management 
 Development of, implementation and execution of promotional marketing campaigns, collaborating with necessary internal teams (marketing, legal, finance, etc.) 
 As the face of the organization, ability to build strong business relationships with external parties 
 Must be comfortable presenting plans, results and learnings internally and externally (where relevant) 
 Forecast, measure, and report the results of ongoing project executions, including co-branded promotional offers, and overall long-term business results 
 Co-lead presentation development for leadership shareouts 
 Manage annual budget development, ongoing financial forecasts and tracking 
 
 Summary of Experience 
 
 Progressive experience in partnership development, marketing or other relevant environment - 3-5 years (project management experience - 2+ years) 
 Experience managing external partner relationships – 2+ years 
 University degree in marketing, business or related field 
 Experience working within a global organization and localizing for the Canadian market preferred 
 Experience working for or with a loyalty program preferred 
 Experience working with external agencies preferred 
 
 Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities 
 
 Solid business background and financial acumen, able to develop business cases with strong customer-centric focus backed up by data 
 Project management skills; organized, extremely high attention to detail, able to manage multiple priorities and meet deadlines 
 Ability to communicate and collaborate with a diverse set of stakeholders 
 Ability to solve complex problems and take new perspective on existing solutions 
 Proven ability to respond to and prioritize changing demands effectively 
 Ability to function effectively with ambiguity 
 Occasional local travel required for offsite meetings will be required
