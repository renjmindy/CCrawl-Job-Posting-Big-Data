 Job Title:  Storefront Team Member 
 When you become a Team Member at Tim Hortons you truly join a team. Perhaps it will be your first job, maybe you are working while you study, or you are building a career within Tim Hortons. No matter what your career goal, we will be here to support and encourage you along the way. Your path is just beginning! 
 Restaurant Location:  1160 Hanwell Road, Fredericton NB, E3C 1A4 
 Availability:  Must be Available for Day Shifts 6 AM- 4 PM, must be available Monday-Sunday, 32-40 hrs/week 
 Job Status:  Full Time, Permanent Not suitable for Students 
 Number of Vacancies:  1 
 Position Description: 
As a team member, your top priority is guest satisfaction. Whether you are the very first person our guests encounter or producing quality finished bakery items that our guests come to enjoy and love you will have the opportunity to deliver and create exceptional guest experiences. Your energy and passion for guest service are what make you a top team member in this fast-paced environment while your ability to multi-task and communicate with your fellow team members will contribute to your success. 
 Our Team Members are the smiling faces serving our guests at our drive thru's, front counters, and sandwich stations. As part of this position you will be trained in each of these areas. You will be deeply immersed in training to learn about our operations standards, food safety, health and safety, cleaning and sanitizing, and guest service. Part of this position will include maintaining our restaurant's cleanliness. As part of the position you will be required to sweep, mop, clean restrooms, change garbage's, clean the parking lot, clean the dining area, and clean behind the counter. 
 Apply to this opportunity if you: 
Demonstrate a passion for guest service and creating exceptional guest experiences 
Enjoy working with a successful team in a safe environment 
Have a strong work ethic and are committed to your team 
Have the ability to work under minimal supervison, with a small team
