 Jobs and careers in Rippershausen 
 Jobs at the Willi Elbe Group in Rippershausen 
 JOBS AND TRAINING IN RIPPERSHAUSEN 
 The Willi Elbe Group now has eight locations worldwide. The company is represented in Germany, Switzerland, Norway, Bulgaria, Mexico and China. The factory in Rippershausen, Thuringia has been in existence since 1992 - and celebrated its 25th anniversary in 2017 accordingly. 
 Around 170 employees work in Rippershausen, on a production area of ​​around 9,000 m². Jobs and training courses - especially in technical professions - are offered regularly. 
 From machining, hydroforming, laser welding and plastic injection molding technology all the way to a linked assembly line for mass production: State-of-the-art equipment guarantees premium-level production. 
 Living and working in the Rippershausen region 
 The community of Rippershausen is located in a scenic area in the district Schmalkalden-Meiningen, in the federal state of Thuringia. Consisting of two districts, Solz and Melkers, it has only about 900 inhabitants. 
 The county seat, Meiningen, is only about 5 km away from Rippershausen. To the north, Erfurt is around 95 km by car, and to the south, Würzburg is around 130 km away. But in the vicinity of Rippershausen, there are not only attractive jobs but also a lot to see. Here is a small excerpt ... 
 Meininger Staatstheater 
 Goetz Cave Meiningen Bertholdsburg Meiningen 
 Ski jumping facility in Kanzlersgrund 
 Vehicle Museum in Suhl 
 After all, having a good work/life balance is essential for you to be happy with your job in Rippershausen! And you can deliver full performance as well. Both are important. 
 Benefits we offer 
 - Jobs at a rapidly-growing plant of an internationally successful automotive supplier 
 - Good development and advancement opportunities 
 - Performance-related wages 
 - Living and working in an especially scenic and culturally attractive region around Heilbronn 
 - Future-proof jobs
