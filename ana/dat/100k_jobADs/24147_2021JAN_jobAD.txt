 Treasury & Banking Manager - SAP/Hana (Ops) 
 
 Location 
 
 Surrey, England 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £70000.00 - £85000 per annum + Bonus, Pension, Flexi bens 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Rob Anderson 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 robanderson@workwithglee.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH18205_1578907764 
 
 Published: 
 
 about 1 year ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-02-05 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 This Surrey based electronics / tech client require a qualified accountant with a background in banking / treasury to lead the change of corporate banking operations aligned with the implementation project of a SAP based cloud system. 
 Responsible for managing the area on centralised corporate banking and control collection / payment throughout EMIEA. 
 Responsibilities: 
 Take the lead in establishing new treasury and banking operation in practice and alignment to the new cloud based SAP project 
 Lead the practical testing with finance and outsourcers 
 Establish centralised corporate banking by defining process of collection and payment 
 Year 0- 3 
 Sustain corporate banking integration and automatic process by function of post cloud SAP based implementation 
 Manage the banking and automatic process by function 
 Manage centrally receivable and payable in EMIEA 
 Credit management including credit insurance 
 Manage bank fees, foreign currency transaction 
 Supervise indirect procurement 
 Requirements: 
 Qualified accountant 
 SAP/HANA skills essential 
 High communication skill and enthusiastic power to enable objective achievement 
 Deep knowledge of banking and experience of improvement on corporate banking 
 Ability to capture root cause of the issue and propose solutions 
 Transparent and systemic way of work aligned with management
