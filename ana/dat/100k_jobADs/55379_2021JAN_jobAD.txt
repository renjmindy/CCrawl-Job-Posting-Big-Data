 Plant Management Accountant - 50 - 60k + Bonus - Birmingham 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group are supporting a US listed manufacturer business in Birmingham to recruit a Plant Management Accountant who will support the Financial Controller to ensure that all group financial requirements are met, and provide support relative to cost accounting. The role will also manage and maintain inventory processes and controls and complete general accounting functions, operational improvements and other accounting projects. 
 The Finance team is 7 strong and the team enjoy a relaxed environment and a culture that is fast paced, but with plenty of support available. To make the transition of this role successful the current incumbent (who is retiring) is happy to stay on to provide an in depth hand over. The role has two direct reports who will support with management accounts production, and will need continuous development and motivation. 
 Responsibilities include 
 
 Management of cost and Inventory accounting; reporting including KPIs and costing dashboard 
 Assistance with the forecasting and budgeting of inventory levels, costs of goods sold and overheads 
 Ensure compliance with internal controls related to inventory and cost accounting 
 Month-end close process, including preparation of journal entries, reconciliation and analysis related to cost of goods sold, as well as inventory valuation, balances, adjustments and reserves 
 Preparation of standards (annual review and costs update) 
 Management of metal hedging 
 Stock take management 
 Manage ad-hoc group queries 
 
 Right person for Plant Management Accountant 
 The right person for this role will be ACA, ACCA or CIMA Qualified with a wealth of experience gained within a hands on manufacturing environment. Managerial experience will also be a benefit combined with the ability to build relationships across the business. This is an excellent opportunity for an Accountant who enjoys getting behind the numbers and enjoys driving change.
