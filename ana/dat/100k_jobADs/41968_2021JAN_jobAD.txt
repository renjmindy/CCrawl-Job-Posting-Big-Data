 Water Treatment System Installer 
 Nelson Water Inc. 
 __Ottawa, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Nelson Water Inc., located in Ottawa, with over 35 years of experience in the water treatment industry is expanding and looking for a Water Treatment System Installer. 
 The successful candidate will be responsible for installing water filtration equipment such as softeners, filters, ultra-violet systems, reverse-osmosis systems and complex chemical injection systems. This is a technical hands-on position that requires skills with power tools, hand tools and related equipment. Preference will be given to those with experience in plumbing, water system maintenance and equipment installations. 
 Possession of any of the following qualifications or certifications would be considered an asset: 
 Licensed well technician. 
 Licensed Plumber or apprentice. 
 Appliance repair. 
 MOE Certification as “Trained Person”, “Operation in Training” or Limited Sub-system Operator”.
