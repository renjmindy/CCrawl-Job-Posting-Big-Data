 My client, a Midlands based business, is looking to recruit an Ecommerce Director. 
 A market-leader in their field, my client has a strong online presence and exciting future growth plans. 
 The Ecommerce Director will report directly into the CEO and lead the overall ecommerce strategy. You will be directly responsible for the online trading performance of the business; in addition you will strive for constant, sustainable growth. The role will encompass: 
 
 Direct responsibility for driving sales and profits for the site 
 Full ownership of the e-commerce trading strategy 
 Broader ownership of digital marketing and acquisition strategy 
 
 The ideal candidate for this Ecommerce Director role will possess an impressive online trading track record; you will be able to demonstrate sustainable online growth. At your core, you are a commercially focused ecommerce professional, passionate about driving profitable sales growth. You will have worked in a retail, onmi-channel environment. Broader digital marketing knowledge is also key. 
 Highly competitive package. Due to the anticipated high-volume of applicants I will endeavour to respond as promptly as possible, responding first to those with the most relevant skills and expertise. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
