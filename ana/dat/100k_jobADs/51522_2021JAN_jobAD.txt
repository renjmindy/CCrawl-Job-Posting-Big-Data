 9-12 Minute Video Edits 05/14/2018 
 Published on the May 14, 2018 in Design & Multimedia 
 
 Project 
 Project Insights New 
 
 About this project 
 design-multimedia / make-or-edit-a-video 
 USD 50 - 100 
 Please see the following video to understand the style of video I am looking to create:  https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ym4uVS-5tSA 
 I will provide you with a voice over and YOU must find images, audio and video. I can provide you with access to video and audio from  Videoblocks.com  and  Audioblocks.com. 
I will also require some brief subtitles and title pages. 
The Videos will not exceed 12 minutes in length, most will be about 10 minutes long. Each video takes about 3-4 hours to create. 
 Requirements: 
- Must Speak English 
- Proficient with Adobe Premiere pro 
- Good work ethic, the better the work the more I will pay 
 If this interests you I can send you a sample script and voice over for you to make a 1 minute sample video, If I am happy with it I will have you work for one week at the rate below. 
 The pay will start at $20 USD per video to start. After the first 3 videos I will move to $22 USD. I will work to increase pay as more videos are made.

Subcategory  Make or edit a video
