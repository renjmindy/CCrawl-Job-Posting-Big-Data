 You might remember the serial 'Kasauti Zindagi Ki' coming on Star Plus, in which there was a main character around whom the entire serial revolved and its name was Prerna. Actress Shweta Tiwari played the role of Prerna in that serial. Shweta Tiwari has made the audience crazy on the basis of her acting, apart from this serial, Shweta has also spread her flames in some films, today we are going to tell you a few things about Shweta Tiwari. 
 Before appearing on the TV screen, Shweta has also done jobs when she started working when she was just 12 years old. It will probably be a little difficult for you to do this but at that time they used to get only 500 rupees as salary. His job was in a travel agency, while doing the job, he thought of trying his hand at acting. 
 Shweta is originally a resident of Uttar Pradesh, she was born in Pratapgarh, she left her home and came to Mumbai to fulfill her dream of acting. Shweta Tiwari is counted among the most fit actresses of TV these days, apart from this Shweta is also very active on social media. 
 When Shweta was only 18, they got married for the first time, but their marriage could not last long after which the two separated. After getting divorced from the first husband, Shweta also married second but their marriage also did not last long and Shweta separated. 
 Actress Shweta Tiwari also has two children, a girl and a boy. Both of Shweta's children live with her, the girl's name is Palak who is 18 years old while the son is younger and her name is Reyansh. From a young age, Shweta has taken many responsibilities and today she is the result of that she has made her identity as a successful person. 
 Even though Shweta is 39 years old today, but she does not let any shortage in fitness matters, no matter how busy her routine is, she does not miss the opportunity to keep herself fit. She definitely goes to the gym at least 3 times and sometimes if she is unable to go to the gym, she exercises for 1 hour at home, besides, she loves cardio, swimming, weightlifting and yoga. Despite Shweta being so old today, she does not seem to see that she is 39 years old.
