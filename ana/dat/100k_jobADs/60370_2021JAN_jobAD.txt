 Can You Believe It?! it's a Full-time job 
 Savannah College of Art and Design is looking for a Media Designer  in Remote. 
 About 
 SCAD seeks a Media Designer to design and develop online courses for eLearning. 
In this position, you will conceptualize, design and develop online courses and custom content for eLearning. You will have the primary responsibility for the branded design and programmed development and interactives in online courses. 
In this hybrid design and development role, the ideal candidate is a unicorn—skilled in visual design and front-end coding and approaches the role with the spirit of a designer as author. The candidate should have a strong competency with web authoring languages HTML, CSS, and Java Script as well as visual design and digital development software such as Illustrator, Photoshop, Dreamweaver, Sketch, and other related programs. The ability to communicate clearly, manage projects to successful completion, collaborate with an integrated learning team and collegiate groups are all essential. 
Requirements: 
Strong front-end coding experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript 
Visual design and branding experience 
Bachelor’s Degree 
 Preferences: 
API integration capabilities 
Back-end coding knowledge including PHP and related languages
