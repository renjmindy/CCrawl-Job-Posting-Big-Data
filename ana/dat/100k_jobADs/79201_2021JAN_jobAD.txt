 Caregivers Jobs Abroad 
 Posted on  September 17, 2010  by  caregiver 
 Provident Overseas Placement Agency, one of the POEA accredited recruitment agencies is looking for applicants who are qualified for  Caregivers Jobs Abroad . These caregivers jobs abroad are for the holy land of Israel and have a job order that was approved for recruitment by the POEA last September 13. Accompanying the said job order is a job order balance of 30 applicants to be chosen for placement in caregiver jobs abroad. 
 If you wish to apply for the said caregivers jobs abroad, submit your resume and application as soon as possible, to the office of the recruiting agency in Makati City. For more details or if you want to make an inquiry regarding the said caregivers jobs abroad, you can refer to the agency’s landline numbers or email address indicated below. 
 Provident Overseas Placement Agency 
Unit B1 DNS Building 
Filmore cor. Arellano Avenue 
Makati City 
Tel Nos: 232-9948 / 551-7741 
Email Address: provident@hotmail.com
