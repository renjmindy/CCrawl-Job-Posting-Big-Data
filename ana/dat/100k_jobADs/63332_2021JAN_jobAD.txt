 Employment Opportunities in Logistics 
 According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor and Statistics, employment of packers and packagers will increase by more than 13% through 2018. Additionally, warehouse laborers and material movers will increase by more than 3%. In total, the supply chain and logistics industry is an emerging and in-demand career path and the Kansas City region has a growing need to fill employment. 
 Several national and international distribution companies have located in and around Logistics Park Kansas City (LPKC) and are looking for talented workers. Some of the positions with immediate availability include: 
 
 Material Handlers 
 Lift Truck Operators 
 Maintenance Technicians 
 Tractor-Trailer Truck Drivers 
 Logistics Service Agents 
 Front-Line Supervisors 
 Distribution and Operations Management
