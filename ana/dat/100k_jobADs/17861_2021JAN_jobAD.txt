 Solar Jobs 
 Looking after our people and our planet 
 Build your career with Australia's top employers at the click of a button! 
 The clean energy boom is creating construction job opportunities around the country and WorkPac is delivering renewable energy projects all over Australia. With direct access to fantastic employment opportunities with industry leaders, you can have your pick of temporary or permanent solar jobs to build a professionally and financially rewarding long term career. 
 WorkPac has provided teams to high-profile projects including Sundrop Farm and the Moree Solar Project. Join WorkPac today to secure your place in Australia's renewable energy industry. 
 A career in solar takes care of your family and your planet.
