 My client, a market leading manufacturing business, is looking for an Interim Financial Controller to join their busy Finance Team on a 12 month FTC (maternity cover). 
 The Role 
 As the Financial Controller, you will be responsible for the development and preparation of periodic accounting and financial statements and reporting the financial objectives specifically Revenue,EBITDA, margins, Operating Cash Flow (OCF), and control of Working Capital requirements. The aim of which is to support the business leaders in decision making and improvement initiatives to achieve organisational success. 
 On a day to day basis you will provide: 
 - Financial reports and updates with detailed analysis and variance reports 
- Prepare financial statements to feed into the preparation of forecasts and budgets 
- Manage financial accounting procedures to identify discrepancies in line with company best practice and legislative requirements 
 The ideal candidate will be either ACA, ACCA, CIMA qualified or equivalent. They are also looking for an individual who has experience of working within a finance department, ideally within manufacturing and has a clear understanding of the role of a Financial Controller.
