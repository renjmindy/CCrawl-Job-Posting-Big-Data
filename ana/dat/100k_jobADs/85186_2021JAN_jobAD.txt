 by  Mickaela  | Jan 15, 2021 | | 
 
 Full Time 
 Lincoln, NE 
 Posted 1 week ago 
 
 POSITION TITLE:    Production 
 REPORTS TO:    Supervisor/Plant Manager 
 PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:   Responsible for performing production work. Starts, stops, operates and cleans machines. Assembles and inspects finished products to ensure high quality. 
 ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS: 
 
 Starts up and stops equipment. 
 Cleans production area; sweeps and remove debris. 
 Loads and unloads product from machines for processing. 
 Ensures conformance to specifications. 
 Labels product for identification. 
 
 QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCE: 
 
 Understands and follows basic verbal instructions. 
 Maintains the production pace of other team members. 
 Must be authorized to work in the United States on a full-time basis. 
 
 PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS: 
 
 Requires the ability to walk and stand for long periods of time. Must be able to stoop, kneel, bend, crouch, crawl, climb ladders or equipment.
