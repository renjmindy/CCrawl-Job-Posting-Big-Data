 Consultant General Adult Psychiatrist 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Consultant General Adult Psychiatrist to work in an amazing hospital based in the Charlwood, Surrey area. 
 As the Consultant General Adult Psychiatrist you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This is a large purpose-built medium-secure service with good facilities. 
 Working alongside a multidisciplinary team, you will provide care and support for adult men 18+ with a forensic background and enduring mental health illnesses such as personality disorder, substance misuse issues and behaviours that challenge 
 As the Consultant General Adult Psychiatrist your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     Having RC responsibilities for around 10 patients
 
 
 
     Leading a MDT
 
 
 
     Coordinating and chairing ICRs, CPAs, professionals meetings
 
 
 
     Presenting evidence at MHRTs and Managers hearings
 
 
 
     Entering notes onto electronic notes system and keeping dashboards up to date
 
 
 
     Covering the other consultant whilst they are away
 
 
 
     Keeping up to date with mandatory training and attending relevant hospital wide meetings
 
 
 
 An ideal Consultant General Adult Psychiatrist must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Excellent communication skills - both verbally and written
 
 
 
     Ability to build effective relationships with service users colleagues and other professionals
 
 
 
     Good IT skills are essential
 
 
 
 **The successful Consultant General Adult Psychiatrist will receive an excellent salary up to £70,000 per annum DOE. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role and in return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:**
