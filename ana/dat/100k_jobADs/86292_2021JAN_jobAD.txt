 Are you a Buyer based in the Worcester area with experience of purchasing within a fast paced manufacturing environment? Can you read and understand engineering drawings? Do you have strong MRP/ERP experience? If so, please read on. 
 As Buyer you will have responsibility for procuring components, mechanical and electrical parts to support manufacturing. You will raise purchase orders, use the MRP system extensively and also have responsibility for management of stock. You will develop and maintain supplier relationships and negotiate lead times and reductions with suppliers. 
 In order to carry out the Buyer role, you will need the following skills and experience: 
 
 Experience of managing stock 
 Strong communication skills 
 Previous experience of buying within a manufacturing environment 
 Strong MRP/ERP systems experience 
 
 In return, you will receive a salary of circa £30,000 dependent upon experience plus benefits 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
