 Will Selva: Sports Career 
 
Will Selva in the NFLN offices. 
 
Will Selva hosting in the NFLN studios. 
 
Will Selva standing in front of the NFLN logo. 
 
Will Selva at the NFLN news desk. 
 WIll Selva has spent his sports broadcasting career working for top networks like CNN, NFLN, and Fox Sports West. Will started out his career working for KRON/BayTV where he provided the latest news coverage of local and high school sports. Besides providing this coverage, Will Selva also hosted on "Daybreak" which was a morning show preceded the "Today" show time slot. On "Daybreak," Will Selva was able to meet and interview notable celebrities like Charlton Heston, Johnnie Cochran, Shock G, and Willie Brown. 
 After working for KRON/BayTv, Will began anchoring for KXTV where he visited China in order to cover a game between the Houston Rockets and the Sacramento Kings. Since then, Will Selva has hosted or anchored for CNN, NFLN, Fox Sports 1, and Fox Sports West. In 2005, Will started at CNN where he co-hosted with Robin Meade on "Morning Express with Robin Meade." Also while at CNN, Will covered Super Bowls 40 and 41.
