 Commercial Finance Business Partner 
 
 Location 
 
 Essex, England 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance

 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £70000 - £80000.00 per annum + Benefits 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Rob Anderson

 robanderson@workwithglee.com

 Published: 
 
 11 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-03-20 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Our client is a specialist pharmaceutical client based on the Cambs / Essex border. 
 Reporting into the Financial Director, the Commercial Business Partner will be an experienced fully qualified accountant with at track record supporting commercial strategy. 
 You will have existing experience within the Pharma / Medical / Devices sector 
 Responsible for provision of comprehensive commercial product analysis and reporting. Operating as the link between HQ and the local FP&A team you will be adept as developing internal and external relationships to support the short and long term strategic direction 
 Act as Finance business partner for assigned areas 
 Assist in evaluation of investment/commercial opportunities and resource allocation decisions 
 Assist in the development of system tools to provide clear performance measures for business. Ensure accurate Daily Customer and Product Sales reporting *comparison to budge, LE and PY, reporting of margin by product and customer, reporting of customer performance v BDI. 
 Drive real accountability for return on investment and sales performance as appropriate 
 Monitoring financial performance 
 Co-ordinate and review month-end process for defined functions. To include: Preparation of 'TM' on WD2 (expected NS and EBITDA), Preparation of 'Landing' on WD16, Monthly close:- Hospital rebate reconciliation, supplier rebate reconciliation (UK Suppliers), Price and Volume variance analysis, Preparation and issue of monthly management pack 
 Monitor results against forecast/budget and provide accurate, timely financial management information and commentary 
 Control Processes 
 Ensure existence of budgetary control via the AED and Purchase to pay processes; challenge spend where appropriate 
 Preparation of Bi-annual forecasts, loading of forecasts and budget into system, preparation of annual budget from gross sales to gross margin 
 Ensure the close monitoring of rebate accruals so that reported sales are materially accurate 
 Ensure accuracy of Purchase to Pay transactions provide adequate integrity for reporting purposes 
 I am looking for a qualified accountant with a commercial bias and outstanding influentilal communication skills. Pharma / Medical experience essential. Essex / Cambridgeshire base.
