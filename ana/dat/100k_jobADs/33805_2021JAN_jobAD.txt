 Our growth in 2020 has been phenomenal - despite the pandemic, people are still looking for love and we're helping thousands of people around the world get married every month. We're looking for someone starting out in digital marketing to help us take our user acquisition to the next level. 
 We're looking for 
 
 We are currently looking for an enthusiastic, forward-thinking and data-driven digital performance marketing manager to join us and help us grow the business further. 
 You will need to be hands-on and operative from day one, as well as having strong analytical skills. 
 We've spent the past year shaping the basics of our digital marketing effort, you'll help the Digital Performance Marketing Lead build processes to 10x everything. 
 You'll work closely with and learn from a team of Product Marketers, in-house Creatives, and Data Scientists. 
 Along with the Digital Performance Marketing Lead, you'll be managing a multi-million dollar user acquisition budget. You'll have ownership over a set of countries within our global UA strategy. 
 
 Execution 
 
 Manage the day-to-day of campaigns across various platforms: Google, Facebook, Snap, Apple, TikTok and more. 
 Work alongside in house creatives to spec high performance ad assets 
 Optimise campaigns with data scientists in order to improve performance continuously. 
 
 Requirements 
 
 1+ years experience in a performance manager role (agency or client-side), ideally with a focus on mobile acquisition 
 A basic understanding of the technical side of app user acquistion or a desire to learn, good analytical skills and always look into improving processes (e.g. automation of reporting, marketing activity etc) 
 Working knowledge of Facebook Ads Manager and Google Ads (UAC campaigns a plus). Bonus points for Snap, TikTok and Apple. 
 Good project management and communication skills. You will need to work with multiple stakeholders, both internal and external 
 Eager to learn, you'll bring passion and expertise to the exciting future we are building. 
 
 Strategy 
 
 Implement user acquisition strategies including (but not limited to) UAC, paid social, display, video and programmatic. 
 Build clear presentations in order to communicate the acquisition strategy for a wide range of stakeholders, both upwards and downwards. 
 Work with Digital Performance Marketing Lead to create and maintain a 'Best Practices' knowledge bank that can be applied to new market launches, new campaigns etc. 
 Track organic reach of all aspects of the business and add value in terms of boosting this 
 
 Tracking & Reporting 
 
 Coordinate the implementation of tracking and ensure its integrity with engineering teams. 
 Work closely with the data science team in order to build accurate and well organised reporting 
 
 Why muzmatch? 
 
 Work at an exciting and high-growth tech startup that's thriving during these uncertain times 
 Learning Fridays - company-wide, one day a month is dedicated to your self learning and development 
 We're well-funded and have the budget to run exciting projects 
 Work with talented marketing and technology professionals who can help you learn great skills for your data science career 
 Regular company socials - including hikes, games nights, go karting, and dinners (post covid!) 
 Fully remote at the moment 
 We'll kit you up with a 15'' Macbook Pro Touchbar and home office furniture if required 
 Monthly catchups - we're all here to look out for each other during these strange times 
 Our team is incredibly diverse with people from all walks of life
