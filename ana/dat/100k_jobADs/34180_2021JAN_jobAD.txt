 I am working with a construction firm in Stourbridge who are looking for an immediately available Accounts Payable Clerk to join their team on a temporary basis basis, which will see you through to Christmas time and potentially into the new year. This role is reporting directly into the Finance Manager. 
 Please note this is an on site position which has all the correct measures in place in line with Covid-19. 
 Working in a small, committed and hardworking finance team based in Stourbridge, you will be an experienced accounts payable assistant, with strong Excel skills and have had previous experience with the system SAGE 50. You can expect an hourly rate between £11.00- £11.50 ph. 
 Responsibilities include: 
 
 Purchase Ledger and Invoice receipting 
 Investigating and resolving discrepancies 
 Effective communication with suppliers 
 Bank reconciliations 
 Petty Cash reconciliation 
 Company Credit Card reconciliation 
 Spreadsheet Maintenance 
 
 Is this role right for you? 
 This is a great opportunity for you to join a tight team as an accounts payable assistant where your energy and enthusiasm will genuinely be rewarded. If you enjoy working in a team, love all things finance and want to develop your accountancy skills - this could be the one for you. Click apply to be considered.
