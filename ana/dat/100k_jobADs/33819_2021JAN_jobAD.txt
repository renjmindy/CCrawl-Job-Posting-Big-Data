 Caregiver Jobs Jordan 
 Posted on  February 25, 2011  by  caregiver 
 A Makati based recruitment agency with office branches in Bacolod City, Pangasinan, and Iloilo City is in search for caregivers who are qualified for  Caregiver Jobs Jordan . These caregiver jobs Jordan were approved for recruitment by the POEA last February 23, 2011 and has a Job Order balance of 100 caregiver jobs. To qualify for the said caregiver jobs abroad, applicants must be: 
 
 Female - 23 to 35 years old 
 Registered Nurse / Midwife / Caregiver 
 Experienced in Disabled Centers with residential care for a minimum of 1 year 
 Physically Fit 
 
 If you are interested to apply for the said Caregiver Jobs Jordan, proceed directly to any of the offices of Concorde International Human Resource Corp. and submit 2 sets of the following Requirements: 
 
 CV 
 College Diploma 
 TOR/RLE 
 License 
 Board Rating 
 Board Certificate 
 High School Diploma & Form 137 
 Whole body picture (2) 
 2×2 picture (2) 
 passport 
 certificates of employment 
 
 If you have any question regarding the said Caregiver Jobs Jordan, you may contact Concorde International Human Resource Corp. thru their telephone numbers specified below.
