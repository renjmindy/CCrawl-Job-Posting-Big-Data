 Administrator 
 Job description 
 About the Role: 
 Workpac are seeking an experienced Administrator for our mining supply client based in Mackay. 
 Our client is a leading equipment supplier to the mining industry, and they are in need of additional administration support in their maintenance department. This role will be supporting the Maintenance Superintendent was day to day administration tasks. This is a fast paced role and requires a strong administrator with either site experience or mechanical knowledge. This is a Monday to Friday full time role. This is an initial 2 to 3 month contract with the possibility to go permanent for the right candidate. 
 Key Duties and Experience Required: 
 
 
 Assist with day to day administration of the maintenance department 
 
 
 Complete tasks including ordering parts, data entry, processing timesheets, completing PPE orders and liaising departments via phone and email 
 
 
 Must be able to work in a fast paced environment, multi tasking on jobs that come through 
 
 
 Hold previous experience in an administration role ideally in the mining industry 
 
 
 Previous on site mining admin experience is ideal 
 
 
 Strong knowledge of mining equipment and parts in preferred 
 
 
 How to Apply: 
 Click Apply Now and our recruiter Julia Ryzner will be in touch.
