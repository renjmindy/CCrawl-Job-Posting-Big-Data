 30 Jun  2014 
 Full-Time Senior Java Developer 
 Comptel -  HELSINKI  , FI 
 Job Description 
 We are searching for an experienced Senior Java Developer who is goal-orientated, self-motivated and a strong team player to join our R&D organization. 
 You take great pride in your work, and you are willing to take responsibility and deliver quality focused products. You enjoy working in an international, dynamic and agile environment. You have the skills and ability to visualize how things should work, and how to translate this into a functioning solution. 
 We offer you a chance to join a team of R&D professionals, where all work together to achieve the best results and have a genuine chance to contribute to the final product. We believe in each other's skills and value each other's work. If these are values that you are passionate about, then you might just be the person we are looking for. 
 Requirements - Good oral and written communication skills in English - Experience in software design patterns - Extensive experience in Java programming - Good knowledge of UNIX and Linux operating systems - Good knowledge of some of the following: - Knowledge of Online Charging Systems (OCS) and Policy Control and Charging Functions (PCRF), 3GPP specifications - Understanding of rating and charging problematics in operators business - Diameter protocol - Knowledge of networks and network elements (LTE, GGSN, DPI, PCEF) - Knowledge of database clusters, high availability - Experiences of high throughput/low latency systems - Knowledge of Subscriber Profile Repositories (SPR) - Online/offline mediation In addition to sound technical skills and passion for software development we value people who are fun to work with, enjoy working in multinational team and who do not mind to lend a helping hand for people in need. We develop and maintain the competence and motivation of our experts by providing regular trainings and supporting 70/20/10 learning model in everyday work with the help of our experts.
