 Part Time Financial Controller

 asap 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 A great opportunity has come up in a exciting and innovative business in Birmingham City Centre. My client is looking for a Financial Controller to join then on a part time basis (2 or 3 days per week). As well as working in fantastic modern offices in the heart of the City Centre, they offer flexible working. This is perfect for parents who need be available for school drop offs/pick-ups or just individuals who want to avoid the rush hour traffic at all costs! 
 The role: 
 The role offers great flexibility too and is split across two key areas, finance and operations: 
 Business Finance 
 
 Managing all finances for the business whilst being respectful for all company compliance 
 Responsible for forecasting budgets, setting targets, performance, solvency & cashflow 
 Cashflow management, budget reviews, VAT accrual, management accounts 
 Identify trends, problems and solutions 
 Paying invoices 
 
 Business operations 
 
 Overseeing and implementing heath and safety within the workplace 
 Dealing with onsite HR issues 
 Basic technical issues (including IT) 
 GDPR queries/issues 
 Maximising our assets 
 
 Skills, Requirements, Qualifications 
 
 ACA/ACCA/CIMA/QBE 
 Self-motivated 
 Work efficiently & effectively 
 
 Ideally the successful applicant will be able to start asap but the client is happy to wait for the right person. If this sounds up your street please apply. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
