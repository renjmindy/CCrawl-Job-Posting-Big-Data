 Inside Alabama’s Auto Jobs Boom: Cheap Wages, Little Training, Crushed Limbs 
 Posted on  March 24, 2017  by  Herbert Dupree 
 
 Having grown up in Alabama prior to the auto plants arrival, I can see how being 3rd world status would attract certain industries to the area. If a corporation can cut corners and get away with eliminating protections, then they will do it and not admit to it. 
 
 The South’s manufacturing renaissance comes with a heavy price.
