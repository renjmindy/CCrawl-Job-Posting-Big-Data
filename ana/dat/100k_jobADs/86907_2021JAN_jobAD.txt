 Support Worker 
 Spark Lifecare 
 __Ottawa, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Love helping people? We're looking for passionate, hardworking, and self-directed people to join our team of caregivers. Spark Inc is an innovative and award-winning organization that matches clients to the best one to one care in the community. 
 SUPPORT WORKER 
 Our clients are lovely individuals seeking care in the Ottawa region and valley. After experiences of trauma such as car accidents, complex medical diagnoses, or facing issues with their natural development, they are looking for support in their daily life, including organization, meal prep, transportation, emotional support and more. Our clients have a wide range of interests, from cars and mechanics, to word games, to good conversation, to pets, and everything in between! You would be the ideal candidate to work with us if you are passionate about helping people, are reliable, love working in a team, and have experience supporting individuals! 
 HOURS: 
Flexible schedule options 
 RATE OF PAY: 
Starting at $20/hour 
 Requirements 
 Cover letter outlining why you would be a great match for Spark. Tell us who you are! 
Valid First Aid and CPR Standard level C certification 
Smartphone with a data plan 
Criminal Record & Vulnerable Sector Check (within the last 12 months) 
 Assets 
 Experience working in a multi-disciplinary team 
Driver's License “G” class with access to a vehicle 
 Spark is looking to hire motivated, dedicated, and experienced Support Workers. In this role, you'll be supporting individuals in the community requiring direct care with personal hygiene, meal preparation, organization, and light housekeeping. 
 Care is tailored to each person, so each position is unique. Some clients need mainly in-home support. Others require support participating in community activities. Whatever the circumstances, we want people who will genuinely care about their wellbeing. 
 Why Choose Spark? 
 At Spark, we value community, work life balance and career development. To help foster your personal and professional growth, we partner with Spark University, a non-profit focused on equipping, educating, and empowering caregivers. Spark U makes your job easier so you can focus more on you and your clients. 
 Whatever our clients' age, background, or circumstances, we help them choose the perfect fit from our handpicked team of passionate, skilled and committed caregivers. As a caregiver with Spark, you have a voice in the matching process. We believe that your input will foster the best long-term relationships with your clients. At Spark, you'll have flexibility, autonomy and a team who truly cares about you. 
 Whatever your age, ability, orientation, ethnicity, race, culture, religion or gender identity - you are welcome. Our community is stronger and more vibrant when it is more diverse.
