 The Hitman of the Indian cricket team is none other than Rohit Sharma. Everyone today is aware that Rohit Sharma is one of India’s best cricket players. After starting his career in 2007, he has grown so much as a sportsman and has successfully created his image as an exceptional player. Apart from cricket, he is also a part of big brand endorsements, and his income keeps on seeing a hike because of the same. So let us get to know more about the player and his lifestyle. 
 Rohit Sharma Net worth 2020: 
 According to the sources, Rohit Sharma is one of the most valued players in cricket. He is also known as the Hitman because of the skill and talent this man has shown in his game. As of 2020, his net worth is estimated to be 22 million, which is approx. 172 crore rupees and more. By 2021, his net worth is estimated to rise up to 200 crores. Rohit Sharma is the brand ambassador for famous sports brands like Adidas, LaLiga, and a few finance companies as well, and he charges approx. 20-30 lakhs from one brand. He used to belong to a middle-class family, but now he owns apartment worth rupees 40 crores. Rohit’s Net worth grows up to 15% every year. 
 Rohit Sharma career: 
 Rohit Sharma was born in Nagpur, Maharashtra, and started his career as a bowler. His coaches could recognize his talent and skills and advised his parents to give him proper coaching by admitting him to a good institute. He got a chance to play for the Indian cricket team in the year 2007, in the world cup. After that, there has been no looking back for Rohit Sharma. Today he is counted as one of the best players in the Indian team. He holds many records on his name, like scoring 3 double centuries in one day international. He also has the record of achieving the highest half-century in T20. There are many more records to be added here because seeing his career graph from the time he began has always seen an upward hill. And we do wish him all the luck for all the future matches. 
 Rohit Sharma family and relationships: 
 Rohit Sharma’s parents are Gurunath Sharma and Purnima Sharma. He has one sibling Vishal Sharma. He was born in Nagpur, Maharashtra, he hailed from a middle-class family. Sharma is married to Ritika Sajdeh, who he met in 2008 in a function. She works as a sports manager, and she has always had a keen interest in sports. The couple got married and have a daughter named Samaira Sharma, she was born on the 31st of December 2018.
