 Staff Nurse RGN - Pre Assessment 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Staff Nurse RGN - Pre Assessment to work in an exceptional hospital based in the Croydon, South London area. 
 You will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This hospital offers services to the local community such as Outpatient Consultation, Day Case Surgery, Pharmaceutical Services and Diagnostics - Imaging and Endoscopy 
 An ideal Staff Nurse RGN - Pre-Assessment must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Experience within an Outpatients and Ward/Ambulatory Care Unit
 
 
 
     Experience pre-assessing patients for surgical procedures via face to face, telephone or online
 
 
 
     Current registration with the NMC
 
 
 
     Previous Pre-Assessment Nursing Experience
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
 **The successful Staff Nurse RGN - Pre Assessment Nurse will receive an excellent salary from £33,000 to £36,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     25 days Annual Leave + Bank Holidays
 
 
 
     Private Healthcare
 
 
 
     Contributory Pension Scheme
 
 
 
     Life Assurance
 
 
 
     On-going Training and Development
 
 
 
     Free off-site parking
 
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a valid NMC Pin
