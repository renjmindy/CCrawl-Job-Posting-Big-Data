 Shift Patter: Days 4 on 4 off 
 Gousto are on a mission to become the UK's favourite way to eat dinner! 
 Our DREAM? To be the most-loved way to eat dinner, and for every meal to leave the planet better off. Gousto is changing how people shop, cook and eat food at home. It’s an incredibly exciting time to join our team! 
 Are you our next Materials Planner? 
 You’re pretty busy. Juggling multiple projects alongside the day-to-day. Armed with initiative, and a problem-solver with an eagle eye for detail. You sometimes need to go the extra mile, but you know from experience that's how things work in a busy Supply Chain department. 
 You enjoy collaborating with different stakeholders, alongside monitoring supply chain activities. When there's any availability issues to be resolved, you step right in - ensuring we deliver on time in full every time to our Gousto customers. 
 **  Your day-to-day ** 
 
 Processing forecasts from data team 
 Running reports from the ERP system 
 Placing purchases with suppliers 
 Sending forecasts to suppliers 
 Checking goods have been accurately received onto WMS / ERP system 
 Requesting stock counts where and when required 
 Ensuring that correct system procedures are adhered to 
 Investigating and rectifying stock issues 
 Creating arrival documents 
 Suggesting continuous process improvements 
 Ensuring stock levels of raw materials are sufficient to meet orders on the pick lines whilst also keeping wastage levels to a minimum 
 Producing OTIF reports to manage suppliers 
 
 Requirements 
 
 Confident using Microsoft Excel 
 High level of computer literacy and ability to learn new software 
 Experience with stock management or supply chain in FMCG sector 
 Experience using ERP, MRP, WMS systems 
 Flexible working pattern to support a 7 day function
