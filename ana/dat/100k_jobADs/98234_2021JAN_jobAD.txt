 Assistant Accountant - Temp to Perm - Bracknell 
 This is a very unique, interesting, and superb opportunity to work for a large global Business Services industry that is one of the world leaders within their industry, where you will join the Financial Accounting team as an Assistant Accountant. 
 Initially being hired on a 3 month basis, your Assistant Accountant role will sit within the Shared Service Centre, who operate across over 60 countries globally. 
 You will ideally be someone who is keen to learn, and previously have some transactional finance experience - gained from within the Financial Accounting space. Training will be given, but previous and recent experience would be preferable as this is a very fast paced role and fast-paced business, where no two days are the same! The team that you will be working with are also the selling point here - as you will be working with AAT and ACCA and CIMA studiers, as well as some Qualified Accountants also. You may well be a studier or someone keen to progress your accounting knowledge. 
 The successful candidate within the Assistant Accountant role will be responsible for: 
 
 Active involvement with journal preparation, fixed assets, prepayments and accruals 
 Reviewing journal entries and actioning a list of monthly journal entries 
 Active involvement with balance sheet reconciliations and bank recs 
 Completing all month-end tasks 
 Providing analytical review and data analysis weekly and monthly 
 Ad-hoc projects 
 Daily use of SAP and Intermediate Excel (full training will be given) 
 
 This is a unique Assistant Accountant - Temp to Perm role, working for our well-established client, based in Bracknell, who can offer plenty of free parking, on-site facilities and an opportunity to work for a high profile, highly respected business. 
 Ideally you will be available to start this role at short notice and be able to commit for 3 months with our Bracknell client, with a view of long-term permanent extensions. 
Please get in touch for further information of this superb Assistant Accountant - Temp to Perm position. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
