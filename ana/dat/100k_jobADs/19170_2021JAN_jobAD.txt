 Are you an experienced Indirect Procurement Manager? Are you ambitious with a view to being in a senior procurement role in the future? If so please read on as this role could be perfect for you. 
 Based in Walsall, this Procurement Manager role be responsible for end to end procurement including tendering, selection, negotiation and engaging with stakeholders. You will identify opportunities to make improvements and deliver value for money, carry out market analysis and attend supplier meetings. You will forge strong relationships with both internal stakeholders and suppliers. 
 In order to carry out this role, you will need the following skills and experience: 
 
 Previous experience within an Indirect procurement role 
 Excellent stakeholder engagement skills 
 Experience of running tenders, negotiating and managing contracts 
 Strong relationship building skills 
 Working towards or CIPS qualified 
 Sourcing experience 
 
 In return you will receive a salary of circa £40,000 + car + benefits. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
