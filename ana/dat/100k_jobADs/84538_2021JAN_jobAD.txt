 Deputy Manager 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Deputy Manager to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the Ryde, Isle of Wight area. 
 As the Deputy Manager you will be working one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This nursing home pride themselves in providing the best possible care in a friendly and homely atmosphere. 
 The home has been carefully equipped throughout to meet the special needs of all residents and to provide a safe and comfortable environment 
 As the Deputy Manager your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     Maintain the general day-to-day running of the Home
 
 
 
     Working alongside the Administration Manager and Housekeeper
 
 
 
     Provide continuity of care, laid down by the procedures operating within the Home
 
 
 
     Be available to offer support and advice to all other members of staff, residents and relatives
 
 
 
     Work as a committed member of the Management Team within the Home and support the Home Manager specifically taking the lead responsibility for Dementia, GSF, and staff training as well as auditing the Home (care plans, medical needs, etc.)
 
 
 
 An ideal Deputy Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Specialist Nursing Practices
 
 
 
     Knowledge of Care Quality Commission (CQC) Guidelines
 
 
 
     Gold Standard Framework for end of life Care
 
 
 
     Knowledge of Dementia Strategy and Dementia mapping is desired but not essential
 
 
 
     It is essential that the post holder shows strong professional leadership
 
 
 
 **The successful Deputy Manager will receive an excellent salary up to £46,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role with Supernumerary plus on the floor.
