 Unity Developer 
 Glitchers Apply for this Job 
 Posted 
 November 30, 2020 
 Full Time 
 United Kingdom 
 Remote 
 Glitchers are a highly motivated, talented team that punch well above their weight in terms of quality and output. They've been making games that not only entertain but go beyond entertainment since 2013. Collaborations with forward thinking brands as well as creating original IP are all part of the day job. 
 We're delighted to offer an exceptional role for a  Unity Developer . You will be part of a diverse, welcoming team whose aim is to build incredibly high quality, considerate games at Glitchers. 
 As a Unity Developer you would be responsible for implementing game functionality, designs & concepts as part of a collaborative team. You must be able to write and maintain high quality, clean and reliable code. You should be comfortable reusing/extending existing libraries where it makes sense and communicating your thinking clearly on daily standups. 
 This role covers maintaining existing projects and working on new projects in addition to working with both internal and external teams. You will be expected to give time estimates for your tasks when planning and communicate any issues clearly and concisely. 
 This role will see you take on responsibilities for a project to ensure features are completed and the standard of quality is maintained. 
 Important: Please, no recruiters. Contractors will also be considered, please include day rate and salary expectations in your application.

 Requirements 
 
 4+ years of experience 
 Firm understanding of Unity/C# 
 Experience with Unity UI, Rendering (Built-in, URP), Animator, VFX. 
 Skilled with GIT version control + GIT LFS 
 Able to write and maintain entire systems 
 Experience in handling multiple projects, while working to meet live deadlines 
 Remote is OK, but candidates must be in the UK 
 English speaking, good communication skills 
 Availability for standups at 9:15am GMT daily
