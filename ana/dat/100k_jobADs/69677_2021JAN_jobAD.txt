 POEA Jobs Caregiver in Israel 
 Posted on  December 8, 2009  by  caregiver 
 Once again, employers from the holy land of Israel are in need of qualified and dedicated Filipinos to fill various jobs caregiver vacancies. The job order for the said jobs caregiver in Israel was approved by the POEA or Philippine Overseas Employment Administration last December 2, 2009 for a recruitment agency based in Salcedo Village in Makati. The job order balance or number of applicants to be recruited for this caregiver jobs abroad is 27 jobs caregiver applicants. 
 All Filipino caregivers, who wish to apply for the said jobs caregiver vacancy in Israel, must visit the office of the recruiting agency to submit an application. The recruiting agency’s office address and contact infos are provided below in case you want to know some details pertaining the above mentioned POEA Jobs Caregiver in Israel. 
 ** 
CPM Manpower Services, Inc.** 
8F Act Tower Condominium 
G. Puyat Avenue cor. H.V. Dela Costa Street 
Salcedo Village, Makati 
 Tel Nos: 8181464 / 8181592 / 8181593 / 8173314 / 8178419
