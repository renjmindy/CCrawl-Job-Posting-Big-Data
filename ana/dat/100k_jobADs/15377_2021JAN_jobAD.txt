 Kitchen Designer 
 Global Alliance Home Improvement Products Inc. 
 __Wasaga Beach, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Global Alliance is a growing outlet for home improvement products, serving the communities of southern Ontario. Our stores offer a wide variety of materials, including floors and trims, paint, light fixtures, kitchens and baths. Though we operate out of three locations - Toronto, Glencairn (Barrie) and Wasaga Beach - the organization works closely together with a strong family culture. 
 We are excited to continue expanding our team, and are currently looking for a Kitchen Designer to join our Wasaga Beach store. The focus of this position will be to help roll out our new line of kitchens and cabinets, helping with the designs and layouts using our 2020 Kitchen Software. Designers will lead the conversations with our clients and come up with solutions based on their budget, layout and needs. This immersive role will also have exposure to all faucets of our business, with responsibilities ranging from merchandising, selling, accounting and administrative tasks. 
 Our organization works in a fast paced environment, where things are always changing. To succeed in this position you must be able to multitask, change tasks rapidly and deal with unpredictable obstacles, all while maintaining a polite and professional demeanor with our clientele. Furthermore, the culture of our organization also offers the opportunity for your opinions to sway our business decisions, as each employee's input is highly valued.
