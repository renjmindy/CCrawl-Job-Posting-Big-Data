 Theatre Practitioner 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for committed Theatre Practitioner to work in an exceptional treatment centre based in the Banbury, Oxfordshire area. 
 As the Theatre Practitioner you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 The treatment centre is one of the largest providers of private healthcare in the country. 
 The purpose built unit has been designed to combine an exceptional standard of patient accommodation with the technical equipment and facilities that modern medicine demands 
 An ideal Theatre Practitioner must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Current surgical/anaesthetic experience within a hospital environment
 
 
 
     Comprehensive understanding of relevant clinical standards in a surgical or anaesthetic environment
 
 
 
     The ability to work in other Clinical departments as required
 
 
 
     Excellent communication skills - written and oral
 
 
 
     Excellent customer care skills
 
 
 
     A good standard of computer literacy
 
 
 
     The ability to work within a team
 
 
 
     The ability to time-manage effectively
 
 
 
 
 **The successful Theatre Practitioner will receive an excellent salary from £28,000 to £34,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits: ** 
 
 
     Basic pro rata entitlement 33 days Annual Leave which increases with service up to possible 39 days
