 I am currently working with a leading leisure business in their search for a Head of Project Delivery, This business has grown strength on strength consistently over the last few years and are continuing to do so as they have established themselves as on of the best in their industry at what they do. 
 You will have the opportunity to join a company with a great culture and work ethic in a senior project management position where you will lead a team of Project Managers in a role that is also hands on. This business is always looking at ways to better themselves and grow further in a highly competitive leisure industry and stay ahead of the curve therefore property is a fundamental part of this business. 
 The successful Head of Project Delivery will be able to show a genuine passion for the industry and their role and be a natural leader. In addition, you will be able to demonstrate a stable career history managing contractors and CAPEX to ensure maximum value for your organisation. Projects will range in size and you will have performed a similar role for either a contractor or client in either hospitality, retail or leisure sectors. 
 On offer is a highly competitive salary of up to £70,000 with the potential to go higher for the right person and a very attractive benefits package which includes a bonus scheme. 
 This is a fantastic opportunity to play a key role in this business's property team and further your career in a progressive client-side business. If you have worked in a similar role or are looking for the next step up, then click the "apply now" button and your CV will be sent directly to Zac Doherty. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however, please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
