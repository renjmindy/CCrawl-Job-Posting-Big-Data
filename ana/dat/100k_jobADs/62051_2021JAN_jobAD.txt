 Key Account Manager - North West Saskatchewan 
 Territory covers North West Saskatchewan. Specific location will be negotiable. 
 Reporting to the Regional Sales Manager, as the Key Account Manager you will act as a business partner to our commercial customers in your territory by maintaining long term relationships to maximize sales opportunities. You will also be identifying new opportunities in selling directly to growers. 
 Duties include the following: 
Manage a portfolio of partner accounts to achieve long-term success; 
Develop mutual performance objectives, sales targets and critical milestones with your channel partner; 
Coordinate with Farmers Edge Operations and Client Success teams, communicating their involvement required with the channel partner to meet the partners' expectations; 
Manage the relationship with existing channel partners by providing training and guidance to ensure partners are successful in acre acquisition; 
Establish and maintain effective working team relationships with all support departments at partner locations; 
Ensure continual assessment and clarification of partner needs on an ongoing basis; 
Communicate concerns regarding channel partner agreement compliance your Regional Sales Manager; 
Develop a territory sales and marketing plan for partner specific strategies, tactics and execution; 
Identify new opportunities for direct to grower sales execution; 
Set and track sales targets, aligned with Farmers Edge objectives; 
Monitor sales metrics (e.g. quarterly sales results and monthly forecasts); 
Monitor customer, market, and competitor activity weekly to build and adapt appropriate sales strategy relevant to the geography; 
Demonstrate and lead presentations of Farmers Edge product and services; 
Other duties as assigned. 
 Qualifications: 
Bachelor's degree in business, agribusiness, or related field; 
5+ years of sales experience, preferably in the agriculture industry managing business to business accounts; 
Excellent interpersonal skills with the ability to build strong relationships with business partners; 
Developed negotiation skills with a proven sales record; 
Motivated, driven and passionate about sales; 
Able to work independently with a commitment to your Regional Sales team objectives; 
Travel required within region. 
 What can Farmers Edge offer you? 
 Competitive Compensation with an attractive bonus program; 
Cost share health benefits, 80% employer paid; 
Employer matched retirement package; 
Company truck, cell phone, and laptop; 
Work perks program providing discounts in travel, retail, food, and more! 
Opportunities for advancement; 
Employee referral program.
