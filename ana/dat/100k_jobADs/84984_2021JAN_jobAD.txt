 Registered General Nurse 
 A fantastic new job opportunity has arisen for a committed RGN to work in an exceptional nursing home based in Haslemere, Surrey area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This is a purpose-built nursing home that provides a range of services. 
 From nursing care to dedicated support for those living with dementia. 
 The highly experienced team are committed to enabling residents to live the life they choose 
 As a Nurse your key duties include: 
 
 
     Assessment of residents' needs, production and implementation of Care plans and accurate documentation on residents' records
 
 
 
     The organisation of the Home and attitude of the staff should reflect the need for residents to achieve and maintain maximum independence
 
 
 
     To maintain a quiet and calm atmosphere by encouragement and example whilst caring for the residents
 
 
 
     Helping unqualified staff with in-service training and to keep abreast of current post-registration training
 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Experience in the same or similar role
 
 
 
     Good communication skills
 
 
 
     Leadership skills
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent salary from £19.48 to £21.00 per hour. 
 We currently have vacancies for both days or night shifts available. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Refer a friend bonus
 
 
 
     Employee Benefit Package - great savings, discounts and rewards, including discounts at major supermarkets, High Street Stores, Cinemas and many more
 
 
 
     Employee Assistance Programme
 
 
 
     Free onsite parking
 
 
 
     Friendly team and very supportive Manager
 
 
 
     Full training and Induction programme for all staff
 
 
 
     Working with Buddy and Mentor experience
 
 
 
     Fully funded apprenticeship programme available with opportunity to develop your career
 
 
 
     Great opportunities for promotion
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a valid NMC Pin
