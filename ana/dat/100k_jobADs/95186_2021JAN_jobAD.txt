 Jobs can be defined in many ways. We often think about jobs as being permanent. An accountant is an accountant anywhere right? Wrong! Leaders and organizations define jobs and can do a better job of defining those jobs to help promote sustainability. Listen in to learn more about how changing the way jobs are designed can help create healthier careers!
