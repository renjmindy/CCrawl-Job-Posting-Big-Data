 My client, a well established consumer (B2C) business, is looking to recruit a Head of Product Management. 
 The Head of Product Management will report directly into the Marketing and Product Director and will play a key role in end-to-end product management. The role will involve managing, leading and inspiring a team of Product Managers. This position will work across the entire business and will work closely with stakeholder across: Marketing & Digital, R&D, Design, Sales and Supply Chain. 
 This full lifecycle Head of Product Management role will encompass: 
 
 Leading the creation of Product and Category strategies through to deliverables and product roadmaps 
 Managing and updating the department annual calendar 
 Leading the Product Management team 
 Creating channel and distribution plans and timescales in line with sales targets 
 Influence and support marketing campaigns 
 New product development and launch. 
 
 To be considered candidates must have the following expertise: 
 
 Extensive product management experience within consumer goods 
 Team leadership experience 
 Strategic, end-to-end product management experience. 
 
 This role is based in the West Midlands. 
 Highly competitive package.
