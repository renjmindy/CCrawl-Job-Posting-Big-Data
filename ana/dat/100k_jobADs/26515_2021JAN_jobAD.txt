 Company 
 Geomiq is a technology company manufacturing the future - We accelerate the speed of how technology products are designed, developed, produced and brought to market. We are working with some of the worlds leading businesses with a goal to re-define how parts are designed, manufactured and used. Our platform will enable products to hit the market 5X faster. 
 We have helped customers from BMW, Rolls Royce, Shadow Robot and Medical companies to be first to market, develop unimaginable technology and win global contracts. 
 So far we have built a team with the leading minds in software, engineering and computer vision. We are seeking a Junior Sales Executive to join our rapidly growing business. 
 Product 
 The product itself is an online platform that; analyses the design and manufacturability of a product, provides cost optimization recommendations, provides only the most competitive quotes and allows ordering of custom manufactured parts through an easy to use interface. Our main customers are Product Designers, Design Engineers and Purchasing Departments who often get recommended by peers through word of mouth. 
 Check our the platform here:  www.geomiq.com  and view a video of the platform in action here:  https://vimeo.com/371601469 
 **Who are we looking for? 
 ** We are looking for a Sales Savvy individual to go to market on our supply side and onboard the worlds best manufacturers wherever they are in the world! 
 We are growing fast and looking for a number of talented, and highly-motivated inidviduals to join our Sales team. There is a lot of scope for responsibility and the individual should be comfortable with taking ownership of [and managing] multiple demands required in a startup. 
 Responsible for designing and driving a comprehensive demand generation strategy, lead gen all the way through to a Qualified Opportunity. Targeting agreed segments or territories you will be a driving force behind Geomiq's success. 
 Experience; 
 
 B2B or B2C Sales experience 
 Tele-Sales experience 
 F2F Sales experience 
 Commercial knowledge and understanding of outbound sales 
 Sales brokering 
 Upsell through account management 
 Effectively using CRM systems
