 My client, a well established trade business, looking to recruit a Campaign Manager, with a strong Trade background. 
 This key position will play a pivotal role in engaging, acquiring and retaining trade customers. 
 The Campaign Manager will be responsible for: 
 
 Devising, developing and delivering multi-channel marketing campaigns to a range of different trade customers 
 Develop the annual calender - whilst also being agile and responsive to quick-fire campaigns. 
 Identify and segment different trade customers and develop tailored campaigns accordingly. 
 Monitor and measure all campaign activity 
 
 The successful candidate for this position will possess the following skills/expertise: 
 
 An background in trade marketing and a demonstrable track record in this area 
 Multi-channel campaign management experience. 
 Commercially minded and results focused. 
 
 Highly competitive package
