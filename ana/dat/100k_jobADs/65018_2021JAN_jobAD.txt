 KansasWorks Statewide Virtual Job Fair - January 26 - 28 
 1/26/2021 8:00 AM to 1/28/2021 5:00 PM 
 Click here for more information. 
 Statewide Virtual Job Fair with multiple employers from our local area participating as well as many more statewide.
