 Caregiver Jobs Abroad UK 
 Posted on  March 18, 2011  by  caregiver 
 Calling all nurses, midwives, and caregivers, the POEA recently approved a Job Order that allows a Makati based recruitment agency to recruit applicants who are qualified for  Caregiver Jobs Abroad UK . These caregiver jobs abroad UK are for the position of  Senior Carers  and was approved for recruitment by the POEA last March 8, 2011. For the said caregiver jobs abroad UK, a total of 4 applicants will be chosen for placement in caregiver work abroad. 
 INITIAL REQUIREMENTS: 
 
 Resume with detailed job description 
 Employment/training certificates 
 Diploma & transcript of records 
 Board certificates/ratings/PRC_ID 
 NBI clearance 
 Passport 
 6 pcs. 2 x 2 colored photos (w/collar) 
 2 whole-body photos (4R / in business attire or uniform with white background) 
 
 To apply for the said Caregiver Jobs Abroad UK, proceed directly to the office of Bison Management Corp. and submit your resume together with all the initial requirements stated above. If you have any question related to the said Caregiver Jobs Abroad UK, you may contact Bison Management Corp. thru their telephone numbers or email address specified below.
