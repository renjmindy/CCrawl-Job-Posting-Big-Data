 Entrepreneurship seems to be one of the best options to create a new source of a closely-knit global team for a better and wider global perspective. New start-up ventures are opening up with diverse services and products being offered with an innovative mindset. Entrepreneurs hire professional lawyers to implement the knowledge of business in coordination with laws and regulations. This indirectly creates more work opportunities for students who are aspiring to pursue a career in law. 
 A degree in law encompasses a wider range of subject areas paving a way for professionals to get settled in diverse fields. Organsations worldwide hire law graduates to sort their legal documentation and keep their business in synchronization with legal procedures of a particular nation. Amazon, Uber, Health-cart, and other multinational firms hire law graduates to settle their legal obligations for  operational success . 
 Nowadays, educational institutions are coming up with integrated degree courses to let students understand the dynamics of different subject areas to cater to a specialized stream. These specialized streams help students in gaining knowledge of both subjects so that they can connect between different zones of study by highlighting unifying concepts and cutting across focal lines. It’s a great way to gain skills in two different fields. 
 You can improve your knowledge and employability by achieving a qualifying law degree by developing knowledge of criminal behavior and its origins. You’ll acquire a solid grounding in both legal and criminological principles including practical  professional skills , subject-specific abilities, intellectual skills, and general transferable skills. 
 It is a highly specialized, rewarding, and revered field of study. There lies a goal at the core of the justice system, to keep the individuals, families, and communities safe. You can advocate for victims of crime, assist in solving crimes, counsel, rehabilitate criminals, and help to prevent crime in your community. 
 
 Career opportunities with a degree in law: 
 
 Public defenders:   Public defenders are court-appointed attorneys, paid by public funds, and work for citizens in need. Individuals who cannot afford to hire a prosecutor to defend their cases in court are assigned public prosecutors by the judge to represent their case in the court of law. 
 Solicitor:   Solicitors are responsible for giving legal advice by researching cases and legislation to draft legal documents by liaising with clients and other professionals. Solicitors are trustworthy and responsible individuals necessitating confidentiality by integrity in a non-prejudicial manner. 
 Professor:   A career as a professor or lecturer is also an interesting option for many graduates. Students can also opt for higher education programs like Masters or Ph.D. to increase their knowledge and eventually take part in a research paper at some prestigious institutions across the globe. 
 Criminal lawyers:   The experts in this domain analyze cases to conduct their research for determining a probable outcome to devise effective strategies for defending their clients in court. They defend and represent the case of a client accused in some criminal case by presenting evidence to the jury and prepare legal documents, including legal appeals and briefs. 
 
 LLB focuses on criminal law, contract law, constitutional/administrative law, equity and trusts, land law, tort law, European law and so many other aspects of restoring law and order situations.
