 Band 6 CAMHS inpatient Nurse. Chalkhill. 
 CAMHS Mental Health Nurse, Hayward's-Heath, Band 6. 
 Vacancy for a Band 6 CAMHS nurse to work in the on the 136 Unit for CAHMS at Chalkhill, based at Hayward's-heath. 
 Chalkhill is a hospital that treats children and young people (aged 12-18) experiencing emotional difficulties, mental health problems and eating disorders. 
 A senior Mental Health Nurse is needed to provide clinical nursing support to patients and their families and carers to help to educate them and the patient about their mental health problems. 
 The candidate will have an ability to liaise with and work in collaboration with other relevant agencies according to the needs of the young person. 
 Candidates must be a qualified RMN. 
 Experience working with children/young persons is required and confidence/competence in risk assessing is essential. 
 The role is full-time, working Monday-Sunday, Long Days, on a long term NHS contract. 
 The hourly rate is: 
Monday - Friday £26.50 p/h 
Saturdays £29 p/h 
Sundays £31 p/h 
 If you are interested in applying, please contact Oliver Parkinson on 01772208963 or send your CV to oliver.parkinson@servicecare.org.uk 
 Please also bear in mind our £250 referral fee bonus for any nurse you refer who we place in to work on a 3 month contract. 
 We also offer a £150 sign up bonus for any new nurses that register with our agency. 
 This will be paid in your first pay packet.
