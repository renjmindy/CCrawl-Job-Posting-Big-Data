 Nanny and Caregiver Jobs Abroad 
 Posted on  October 28, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This is an excellent opportunity for nannies and caregivers in Hong Kong who are looking for opportunities to work in Canada. An agency for nanny and caregiver jobs abroad located in the Greater Toronto area is constantly looking for qualified nanny and caregiver jobs abroad candidates who can provide quality and experienced help. These placements for nanny and caregiver jobs abroad can be live-in, live-out, full time or part time and on a permanent basis. 
 This agency for nanny and caregiver jobs abroad is a Canadian employment agency licensed to operate in Hong Kong by the Hong Kong Labor Board. And to scout out the best nanny and caregiver jobs abroad candidates for families in Canada, they continuously travel to Hong Kong to look for nanny and caregiver jobs abroad candidates who are warm, competent, experienced, and emotionally mature for any of their positions and can provide in home care for the children of the families they work with in Canada. 
 If you are located in the GTA please feel free to give them a call at 416 633-0017 and they would be happy to set up a time to personally interview you. If you are located in Hong Kong and interested for the said nanny and caregiver jobs abroad, please email or call and they will advise you of the date of their next trip where they will be able to take your application and interview you. If you are located outside of either Canada or Hong Kong, please feel free to email or phone and they can further discuss what would be most beneficial to you. The goal of this agency for nanny and caregiver jobs abroad is to match the requirements of both the family and the caregiver as closely as possible to create a rewarding placement.
