 About Our Client 
 WorkPac are seeking experienced Trades Assistant to commence at Jilalan to assist within a maintenance crew. Ideal candidates will have experience using power tool within a railway environment. Candidates will form part of a crew in the Wagon Workshop. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking an experienced Trade Assistant to commence at a role for a local client in Mackay. You will be working on a ongoing project in Mackay with successful candidates starting as soon as possible. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 A "hands on" attitude 
 
 
 The ability to work independently and safely in a fast paced environment. 
 
 
 Also work well with others in a team environment. 
 
 
 Be able to solve problems on the go 
 
 
 Able to us a variety of powertools 
 
 
 Skills and Experience: 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 Experience with Oxy and Grinders 
 
 
 Previous experience as a Trade Assistant 
 
 
 Current Driver's Licence 
 
 
 Right to work within Australia 
 
 
 Benefits of role: 
 
 
 Great team environment 
 
 
 Ongoing role 
 
 
 Local position
