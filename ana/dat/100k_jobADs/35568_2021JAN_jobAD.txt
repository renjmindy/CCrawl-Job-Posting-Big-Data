 General, Urology and Gynaecology Sister 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a dedicated General, Urology and Gynaecology Sister to work in an exceptional hospital based in the South West of London area. 
 You will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This special service is one of the leading private hospitals with an excellent reputation for delivering high quality healthcare. 
 The hospital also provides private hospital treatments including full diagnostic and endoscopy procedures, orthopaedic surgery including spinal treatment, varicose vein treatment, hernia surgery, vasectomy and cosmetic surgery 
 An ideal General, Urology and Gynaecology Sister must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     A warm, considerate and empathetic character
 
 
 
     The ability to make decisions and use your initiative
 
 
 
     Strong communication skills
 
 
 
     Passion to deliver excellent care in a rewarding and what can be emotionally challenging environment
 
 
 
     A flexible and positive attitude
 
 
 
 ***          ** You must be registered in the UK with your professional body and able to demonstrate current Theatre experience 
 **The successful Sister will receive an excellent salary around £40,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Access to genuine opportunities to grow, develop and specialise in your career
 
 
 
     Contributory pension scheme
 
 
 
     25 days' annual leave plus the opportunity to buy/sell more
 
 
 
     Hourly rate enhancements for work in the evenings, weekends and bank holidays
 
 
 
     Private Healthcare and Life Assurance
 
 
 
     Free offsite parking and a subsidised staff restaurant
 
 
 
     Free uniform
 
 
 
     Access to our employee Discount Programme
 
 
 
     Wellbeing centre and access to 24/7 employee assistance line for free advice
 
 
 
     Free DBS checking
 
 
 
     Long service, Employee recognition and appreciation awards
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be trained in scrubbing for cases, and have at least 5 years post registration experience
