 Gadget Discovery Club is changing the way people discover innovative, new tech. We are a fast growing Direct To Consumer business with an exciting mission to help people discover innovative gadgets that upgrade their entertainment and lifestyle. 
 Ranked in the top 100 fastest growing UK start-ups, we're now looking for a Paid Social Manager to join us on our mission and help us with our ambitious growth plans. You should have a growth mindset, a passion for digital marketing and a desire to build a world-class paid social team. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Drive growth of our social acquisition channels including Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads & Snapchat Ads 
 Conceptualise cutting-edge video ad campaigns & write amazing copy that attracts our target audiences 
 Manage video producers and graphic designers to produce on-brand creatives 
 Monitor KPI's daily & optimise ad spend to deliver monthly conversion & ROAS targets 
 Produce a monthly performance report & analysis for our CMO 
 
 Competencies 
 
 Passionate about Facebook media buying & customer acquisition 
 A good understanding of lookalikes, custom audiences, campaign budget optimisation & attribution models 
 Process driven and methodical approach to growth 
 Growth minded, has commercial-nous and the ability to prioritise well 
 A creative flair to produce and implement new ideas 
 
 Requirements 
 
 1 to 3 years media buying experience within a performance-based business 
 Experience in running paid ads on Facebook/ Instagram 
 Great commercial & analytical skills 
 Good understanding of brand building and attribution 
 
 Benefits 
 
 Work in a Startups 100 business, with lots of opportunity to learn, progress & succeed 
 Be part of a like-minded team that's passionate about digital marketing & brand building 
 Free smoothies, food & drink for breakfast & snacks during the day (when we're back in the office) 
 Earn a performance based commission on top of your salary 
 Enjoy our fun monthly (covid-friendly) socials! 
 
 If you're ready to join us on this exciting journey to help people discover innovative gadgets that upgrade their entertainment and lifestyle, we'd love to hear from you. Just submit your application and we'll follow up with you on the next steps!
