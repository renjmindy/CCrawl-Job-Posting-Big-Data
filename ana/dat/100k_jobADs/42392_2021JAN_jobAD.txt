 CAREERS EVENTS THIS WEEK 
 
 Wednesday 1st November 2017 3-4pm   REID Lecture Theatre 
 S   altire Foundation Launch GSA Event:  PAID PLACEMENT OPPORTUNITY 
 The Saltire Foundation offer penultimate year students (3rd years students) an opportunity to undertake a paid placement during the summer months before final year study. As a Saltire Scholar you will gain a highly rewarding placement that will develop your professional practice and CV as well as entry into the Saltire Scholar networks. 
 
 Find Out about the Saltire Scholarship Programme:  https://www.entrepreneurialscotland.com/saltire-scholar-internship-programme 
 Read about GSA Saltire Scholar Caitlin Berry’s 2017 summer internship:  Caitlin Berry Glasgow School of Art 
 Find out about Previous GSA Saltire Scholars on CANVAS:  https://canvas.gsa.ac.uk/courses/256/pages/gsa-saltire-scholars 
 
 REID LECTURE Theatre building 2 GSA campus map:  http://www.gsa.ac.uk/media/1415972/29239_gsa_campus_map_online_aw.pdf 
 GRADUATE OPPORTUNITIES 
 If you are a final year student and looking for ‘graduate employment opportunities, don’t forget that Graduate Schemes, Training and Opportunities launch in September and will be advertised throughout your final year. Make sure you keep up-to-date with the latest opportunities and apply within the deadlines.
