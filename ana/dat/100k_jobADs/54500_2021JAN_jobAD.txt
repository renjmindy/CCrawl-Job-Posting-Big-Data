 Sr. Manager, International Business Development 
 Roots 
 __York, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Head Office, 1400 Castlefield Ave, York, Ontario, Canada Req #1152 
Wednesday, November 18, 2020 
 Roots is more than just an/the iconic Canadian retailer, we are a group of passionate employees who act with integrity, trust each other and do what is right. We work in a space where people can grow and develop, with a team of people who own results and are dedicated to seeing Roots win. We seek to build longstanding relationships with partners who share our commitment to maintaining high ethical standards of operation. 
 We are currently seeking a Manager, International Business Development who can be nice, will seek inspiration from our roots, can commit to achieving results the right way and loves to think big to join us on our journey. 
 The Manager, International Business Development is a key member of our International Business Development team, reporting to the VP, International Business Development. This role will be responsible to deliver sustainable revenue growth in targeted international markets through wholesale partnerships, online marketplaces, multi-brand stores and department stores. This role is for a detail-oriented, analytical thinker who has demonstrated knowledge of international market sales channels, platforms, market regulations, supply chain and business cultures. 
 The Manager, International Business Development for Roots will be responsible for and evaluated on the following: 
 Develop and execute market entry strategies including marketing, operations, and logistics 
Establish strong and long-term relationships with international strategic partners/brands/governments 
Build and execute strategic international business development plans and drive mutually beneficial programs with strategic partners 
Collaborate with internal stakeholders and functions for development, alignment and implementation of new channel support, including internal process improvements. 
Identify specific market requirements and manage the compliance process for the wholesale offering (i.e. CIQ, Chinese Guobiao) in collaboration with Sourcing/Compliance Teams, suppliers and business partners 
Collect and analyze reporting and data regarding customer preferences and buying habits to identify market potential and factors influencing product demand creating actionable business insights to support revenue growth 
Conduct analysis of sales performance and product demand to develop and track critical KPI’s related to sales, pricing, margins and expenses for business optimization 
Prepare business reviews to the senior management team regarding project progress and challenges 
 Top candidates for the Manager, International Business Development role will have the following: 
 A minimum 5-year proven sales track record in high-profile, multi brand stores and top international market platforms 
Passion for Roots product 
Fluency in English and Mandarin 
An extensive network in the international wholesale, retail and online platforms 
Experience developing fashion brands globally 
Strong analytical and quantitative skills – forecasting and budgeting 
Strong proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and PowerPoint, with advanced proficiency in Excel 
Experience with financial reporting and analysis 
Excellent organizational skills with emphasis on priorities and goal setting
