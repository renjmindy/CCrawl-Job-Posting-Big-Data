 Cornershop Grocery Shopper and Delivery Driver (Car Required) 
 Cornershop 
 __Toronto, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Cornershop  is an on-demand grocery delivery app that delivers an hour of free time to your door along with your groceries. We are delivering millions of avocados to happy customers throughout Latin America, Canada and the US. 
 We are currently seeking Shoppers in  downtown Toronto  to pick and deliver groceries to households in the area. 
 What we offer: 
 - A transparent payment model. You can easily access a breakdown of your earnings. 
 - The opportunity to earn extra income through bonuses and peak-hour incentives 
 - Weekly pay! 
 - Flexible work - work when you want! No need to schedule shifts. 
 - Competitive rates - Earn up to $17-20/hour! Shoppers are paid a base amount per order, per item picked, per km, and more! 
 - We welcome applicants from different backgrounds. No previous experience necessary. 
 Requirements: 
 - A valid Ontario license (G2 or higher) and a clean driving record 
 - Access to a reliable car (1998 or newer model) 
 - Eligible to work in Canada

 - Access to a smartphone and at-least 2GB of data
