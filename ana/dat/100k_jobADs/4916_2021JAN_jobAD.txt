 Consultant: 
 
 
 I am currently working with a FTSE 250 business based in Birmingham, as they have an exciting opportunity for a PMO Analyst to join a high performing function. 
 Supporting the PMO Manager, you will have varied tasks including analysing data and project deliverables, maintaining portfolio risks, along with assist in the implementation of processes and methods of best practice. 
 This is a great opportunity to have oversight across critical projects, along with liaise and support senior stakeholders across the business. 
 In order to be successful you will have the following 
 
 Previous experience in a PMO analyst position 
 Ability to interrogate data and provide project analytics and reports 
 Familiar with an Agile environment and processes 
 Supported implementation of processes and created user friends docs and reports 
 Knowledge of 3PM methodologies and project/risk governance 
 
 Please apply here with your CV.
