 Journeyman Ford Technician 
 Island Ford 
 __Duncan, BC 
 Apply Now 
 Island Ford Superstore is located in the warmland of Vancouver Island. The Cowichan Valley offers temperate climates not only compared to the rest of Canada but even compared to other cities on Vancouver Island. Between Nanaimo and Victoria, you can enjoy a work-life balance, scenic back roads, and small-town living without being too far away from a city center. When you’re not out enjoying the majestic island, enjoy where you work. At Island Ford, we are guided by three main principles: Passionate, Committed, and Empowered. Joining our team means you are joining a group of people who are passionate about what they do every day. We are also committed to delivering the best possible customer service. And finally, feel empowered working for a company that believes in you. Locally owned and operated, we take pride in investing in our employees to help them reach their dreams, and with the backing of a larger auto group advancement opportunities across the country are available for the right individuals. Currently, we are looking for a Ford Certified Red Seal Mechanic to join the team. 
 About the role: 
 Diagnose the problem, follow the steps to fix it, become a superhero in the customer’s eyes. As a Journeyman technician, you have seen it all, and either know how to fix the problem or what steps to figure out and fix the problem. When you fix it right the first time, and sooner than expected, that’s what makes you a superhero in our customers’ eyes. If you are ready to get your hands dirty in a Ford dealership that sees everything from Ford Fiestas and Fusion Hybrids to Roush Mustangs and F-150’s, we want to hear from you. 
 Processes you’ll be Committed to following 
 
 Interacting with all customers and coworkers with the utmost respect 
 Following all company policies and procedures including the code of conduct 
 Adhering to all policies, procedures, and guidelines established by Ford Motor Company 
 Efficiently and effectively diagnosing and repairing vehicles 
 Taking steps to maintain Island Ford’s status as the top-rated Ford Dealer in BC 
 Following processes and procedures to develop excellence, daily 
 Delivering results above or consistent with company standards 
 Advise customers on work performed and any next steps they should take in language they understand 
 Maintaining a safe work area 
 *This list is not comprehensive
