 Caregiving Job Hiring Bahrain 
 Posted on  September 4, 2012  by  caregiver 
 Last August 30, 2012 the POEA approved a Job Order allowing Allskills Manpower Services Inc. to recruit caregivers who qualifies for  Caregiving Job Hiring Bahrain . For the said caregiving job hiring Bahrain, Allskills Manpower Services Inc. needs to recruit 10 caregivers who qualify for the said caregiver work abroad. 
 To apply for the said Caregiving Job Hiring Bahrain, proceed as soon as possible to the office of Allskills Manpower Services Inc., fill up their application form, and submit it together with your comprehensive and updated resume. For more details or inquiries about the said caregiving job hiring Bahrain, you can contact Allskills Manpower Services Inc. at 4981523 to 24. 
 Allskills Manpower Services, Inc. 
Ground Floor Abad Santos Building 
1398 General Luna cor. Apacible Streets 
Paco, Manila 
Tel Nos: +632 4981523 to 24 
Fax Nos: +632 5237439 
Web Add: www.allskills.com.ph
