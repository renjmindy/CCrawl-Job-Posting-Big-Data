 Covid-19 Testing Multi-skilled Operative 
 A local authority in Halton are looking for covid 19 support operatives to assist with mass testing across the borough. 
 The main purpose of the role is to reduce the incidence of institutional outbreaks and spread of COVID-19 the Government has introduced mass testing in Halton. 
 We are recruiting individuals to work as part of a team of Multi-skilled Operatives supporting COVID 19 testing. 
 You will complete a wide range of general daily tasks such as queue management, cleaning and deep cleaning, registration, preparing and processing tests, providing guidance for self-testing, disposal of medical waste including PPE and ensuring that the site is set up efficiently for all service users for the day. 
 You will need to have the flexibility to change tasks as and when required and rotate into the different roles, ensuring to always provide general support and deliver service excellence for our service users. 
 You must be flexible to work on a shift pattern between 9am and 6pm including weekends and available to start immediately.
