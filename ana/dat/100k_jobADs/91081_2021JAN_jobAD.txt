 Canada Nanny Jobs 
 Posted on  October 8, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This agency for Canada nanny jobs is a Vancouver based company with over 10 years of industry experience. Their reputation as a nanny jobs placement agency continues to excel as they successfully match nanny jobs applicants and clients with similar interests and personalities. Their database of nanny jobs candidates has many international nannies which consists mostly of European, Filipino, South American, Spanish, French speaking, Canadian nannies and many others. And they have the experience and qualifications to help you with your nanny jobs search. Our agency is also able to assist anyone looking to visit or immigrate to Canada. At the said Canada nanny jobs agency, they look for nannies that are competent and emotionally mature who loves children and enjoy family life. 
 To qualify as a candidate for the said nanny jobs agency, all applicants must demonstrate a natural ability to working with people and be able to provide a safe and stable environment for the children in their care. Nanny jobs applicants must also recognize the individuality of children and have the ability to provide a creative environment which fosters trust, self-esteem, independence and learning. The nanny jobs agency also recommend that all nannies make a minimum one year commitment to their families. To be represented by the said nanny jobs agency, the applicant must have: 
 
 A minimum of 1 year of full time experience as a nanny, au-pair, a nurse or preschool teacher (montessori). 
 First Aid and CPR (willing to take it, if you don't have one) 
 Excellent English or French communication 
 Excellent verifiable child care references 
 Love for children and family life 
 Childcare Education is a bonus 
 Criminal/ Police clearance 
 Drivers license is a benefit 
 
 If chosen as a candidate for employment, this nanny jobs agency will offer you support through the process, answer questions about the process, and help you with processing your documents. Once you are registered online, the said nanny jobs agency will contact you to provide them with additional documents. In case you are looking to immigrate to Canada and looking for assistance they can recommend you to a certified Immigration Consultant who is a member of CSIC. Among the nanny jobs that the agency offers includes: 
 
 Full-time live in nanny and caregiver positions all over Canada 
 Full-time live out nanny positions in Vancouver, Calgary, Edmonton, Ottawa, Toronto 
 Part-time live out nanny positions
