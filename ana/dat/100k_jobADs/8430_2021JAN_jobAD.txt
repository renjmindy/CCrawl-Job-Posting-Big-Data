 Location 
St Laurent, QC 
 Position 
IT Support Level 1, full-time permanent 
 About the Company 
 If you are looking for a stable company with a modern culture, then this is the right place for you. Our client is looking for a IT Support Technician that would like to grow within the company. The position is a combination of remote and in-person support to clients. 
 The Right candidate for this company: 
Team player 
Passionate 
Flexible 
Organized 
Excellent communication skills 
Can work independently 
Wants to constantly learn 
Has ambition to grow 
Able to manage multiple tasks in an environment of rapid change and shifting priorities 
 The responsibilities will include: 
Serving as the first point of contact for clients seeking technical assistance over the phone, email or in person 
Gathering and analyzing information about the user’s issue and determining the best way to resolve their problem 
Performing remote troubleshooting through diagnostic techniques and pertinent questions 
Providing basic support and troubleshooting, such as password resets, printer configurations, break/fix instructions 
Replacing computers or printers as required 
Walking users through the problem-solving process, either face to face or over the telephone 
Perform ticket routing and escalation to Level 2 and Level 3 support. 
Providing accurate information on IT products or services 
Responding within reasonable time limits to service calls 
Prioritizing and managing many open cases at one time 
 Requirements: 
College Degree DEC or higher in Computer Science or a related discipline 
A+ Certification is a must 
1 year experience in IT Support Level 1 
Bilingual (English and French, speaking) 
Has own vehicle, able to go offer support to clients at their offices 
 Compensation and Benefits Details: 
Our client offers a competitive compensation packaged between $40,000-$45,000 based on experience, plus vacation and health benefits
