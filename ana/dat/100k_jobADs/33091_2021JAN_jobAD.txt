 Post Office Manager 
 Shoppers Drug Mart 
 __Mississauga, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Location: 
5602 Tenth Line W, Unit 101, Mississauga, Ontario, L5M 5S5 
 Join a locally owned and operated store to help deliver health, beauty and convenience services in your community, and keep the customer at the centre of everything you do. 
 Why this role is important? 
 SUMMARY: 
Responsible for organizing and delivering full service retail postal services (courier items, postal boxes, money transfers, call-in parcels) to Shoppers Drug Mart customers, maximizing department revenue and customer satisfaction. 
 DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES: 
Human Resources 
 Ensures Post Office staff demonstrate superior customer service behavior 
Interviews and hires postal clerks 
Conducts disciplinary procedures up to and including termination, with the approval of the Front Store Manager and Associate 
Trains employees and provides on-going coaching and feedback regarding postal sales 
Completes and conducts performance reviews for postal outlet staff 
Recommends performance pay increases to the FSM and Associate 
Demonstrates CORE Values and Blue Culture 
 Customer Service/Selling 
 Ensures sales maximized through developing effective sales techniques 
Answers customer inquiries regarding products, return policies, etc. 
Resolves customer complaints according to established guidelines 
Keep up to date on postal regulations, price increases and new services 
Promotes and encourages use of the SERVE model 
Ensures customer authorization has been received before initiating service 
Instructs clients about postal services 
Maintains positive relationships with customers 
Ensures the confidentiality of customer information 
 Operations 
 Completes all paperwork connected with post office sales 
Works with Front Store Manager to maintain balanced postal inventory and work within budget 
Responsible for regular maintenance and cleanliness of postal equipment and area 
Have a working knowledge of mint and coin products 
Have knowledge of all fees related to customs and Collect on Delivery (COD’s) 
 Financial 
 Assists in achieving financial objectives for retail postal outlet 
Maintains control of postal product inventory, purchasing stock, and managing cash 
 Marketing and Merchandising 
 Creates and maintains proper displays to reflect seasonal and promotional programs of both Shoppers Drug Mart and Canada Post 
Ensure signage in the department reflects current programs, policies, and procedures 
Co-ordinates and implements Canada Post promotions 
 General 
 Complies with all store policies and procedures 
Ensures loss prevention standards and procedures followed in the department and all reports printed and reviewed daily (ie: no sale report, void report, items not on file) 
 Complies with all health and safety requirements as follows:  Provides H&S policy direction and planning; reviews information and delegates responsibility; provides support to H&S policy; holds staff reporting to them accountable 
Presents clean image to the customers in accordance with Uniform Policy and Dress Code Guidelines (eg. clean appearance, approved uniform, name badge) 
Perform other duties, including Front Store and Cash duties as required 
 QUALIFICATIONS 
Planning, Judgement and Decision Making 
 Works independently and manages priorities in a timely manner 
Independent thinking and decision making when dealing with issues or problems that necessitate analytical skills to gather information, assess potential risks and devise appropriate solutions 
 Experience 
 Problem solving skills 
Bondable 
Customer service skills 
Effective verbal and written communication skills 
Commitment to Customer Service 
Ability to utilize postal equipment 
 WORKING CONDITIONS 
 Ability to work flexible shifts which may include nights and weekends 
 PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS 
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds 
Ability to climb ladder

 Why work in a Shoppers Drug Mart store? 
 Each store has an Associate-Owner, so you will work for a local business owner while having the support of a national brand. Benefit from a purchase discount program, flexible and varied schedules, competitive pay and online learning through SDMU. 
 Take ownership of your work and find more ways to care about your patients, co-workers, customers and community. 
 Associate Owners in the Shoppers Drug Mart network recognize Canada's diversity as an opportunity to better serve their communities, and strive to reflect the nation’s evolving diversity in the products they sell, the people they hire, and the culture they create in their stores. Accommodation is available upon request for applicants and colleagues with disabilities. 
 In addition, we believe that compliance with laws is about doing the right thing. Upholding the law is part of our Code of Conduct – it reinforces what our customers and stakeholders expect of us.
