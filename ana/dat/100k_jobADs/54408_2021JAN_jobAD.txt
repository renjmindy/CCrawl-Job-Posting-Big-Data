 Caregivers Jobs Israel 
 Posted on  January 23, 2011  by  caregiver 
 Two job orders were recently approved by the POEA for a Malate based recruitment. The said job orders are for  Caregivers Jobs Israel  and were approved for recruitment last January 12 and 13. With the said job order approval, the recruiting agency is allowed to recruit 30 and 15 applicants respectively who will qualify for the said caregivers jobs abroad. 
 If you are interested in applying for the said Caregivers Jobs Israel, forward your resume directly to the office of Hopewell Overseas Manpower Network Inc. For more details or inquiries regarding the said caregivers jobs Israel, contact Hopewell Overseas Manpower Network Inc. thru their telephone number provided below. 
 Hopewell Overseas Manpower Network Inc. 
2nd Floor, Lopez & Cabana Building 
2104 Taft Avenue, Malate 
Manila 
Tel No: 3034046 
Telefax: 5217010 
Email Add: homni2004@yahoo.com
