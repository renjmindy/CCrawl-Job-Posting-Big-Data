 Accounts Assistant / Assistant Management Accountant 
 
 Location 
 
 Solihull, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £25000.00 - £28000.00 per annum 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Laura Parkes 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 laura.parkes@gleeson-rg.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH22430_1609778300 
 
 Published: 
 
 18 days ago 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 Laura Parkes 
 Do you want to work for an innovative, fast paced, growing business? An award-winning business based in Solihull are looking to expand their small finance team in response to growth. This is a permanent position paying up to £28,000 per annum. 
 We're looking for a sparky, vibrant and dynamic individual able to hit the ground running. You will be the type of individual that is happy to process invoices, prepare monthly management accounts and help with ad hoc duties. 
 This is a truly exciting position where although you will have routine tasks, each day can be varied. Therefore you will have a flexible and adaptable working style. You need to be diligent with a great attention to detail whilst working in a small finance team reporting directly into the Financial Controller. 
 As the business goes from strength to strength you will need to be resilient and hard working to handle growing demands and not be afraid to share ideas to make improvements and efficiencies. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Processing sales invoices, receipts, and payments 
 Processing employee expenses 
 Preparing and posting journals 
 Preparation of Quarterly VAT Returns 
 Balance Sheet Reconciliation 
 Preparation of monthly management accounts 
 Updating company records 
 Liaising with internal and external stakeholders 
 
 This position is ideal for someone with previous experience in a finance department, perhaps AAT Qualified or ACCA/CIMA part qualified. 
 To apply for the Accounts Assistant position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
