 THE JOB AT BACKBASE 
 Backbase's customers are mostly banks. Our platform is considered to be the leader of engaging digital banking platforms that helps businesses to become digital-first banks. 
 In this role you will deliver industry-leading training to Backbase employees and customers who are typically junior/senior developers, QA's, Systems Engineers, and Solutions Architects. To ensure clarity and comprehension, you will create training materials for best-practice implementation and usability of the Backbase products. Additionally, you are responsible for liaising with stakeholders to ensure the training materials are up-to-date with the latest features and functionalities. 
 As a technical trainer, you are an excellent developer with good communication and presentation skills. Your success will be measured by swiftness, accuracy, clarity and the ability to explain complicated things simply. 
 Nowadays, we mostly deliver training sessions online through either live stream or pre-recorded videos. In the post-Covid world, we will be delivering classroom training in different locations worldwide.  apply now
