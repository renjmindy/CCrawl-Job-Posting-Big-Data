 Kuwait Caregivers Jobs 
 Posted on  July 4, 2011  by  caregiver 
 The oil rich country of Kuwait is currently in search of caregivers who are qualified for  Kuwait Caregivers Jobs . The recruitment of qualified applicants for the said Kuwait caregivers jobs was made possible thru the POEA’s approval of a Job Order last June 21, 2011. The said Job Order has corresponding vacancies for 30 caregivers which will be selected by a recruitment agency with offices in Malate and in Vigan in Ilocos Sur. And to included in the list of candidates for the said Kuwait caregivers jobs, applicants must be: 
 
 Between 23 to 35 years old 
 Hardworking 
 With good moral character and working attitude 
 
 And to apply for the said Kuwait Caregivers Jobs, you may visit bestoneinternational.com and fill up their Online Application. Or, you may proceed directly to any of the office of Best One International Services and Consultancy Inc. and personally handover your application together with the following requirements: 
 
 Bio-data 
 Diploma (college / high school) 
 Passport and NBI 
 Employment Certificate 
 
 For further details or inquiries regarding the said Kuwait caregivers jobs, you may contact Best One International Services and Consultancy Inc. thru their telephone numbers or email address specified below.
