 Piano Teacher 
 Sweet Music Lessons 
 __Uxbridge, ON 
 Apply Now 
 We are looking for a part-time piano teacher to start in January 2021 with our Uxbridge music school. 
 Sweet Music Lessons offers private, weekly lessons in Uxbridge, Ontario in students' homes. 
 We are looking for a teacher with the following characteristics: 
 - Must be able to teach piano at a high level (other instruments such as voice, guitar and ukulele are assets) 
 - Must be familiar with RCM 
 - Responds quickly to emails 
 - Reliable & organized 
 - Enjoys working with children 
 - Has own transportation and willing to travel to students' homes 
 - Patient 
 - Able to teach technique and repertoire in a fun, engaging manner 
 - Able to personalize lessons to each student's preference 
 We offer a flexible working environment - you set your own schedule with the students. 
 Teachers will be asked to undergo a police background check upon a successful interview. 
 Please email us with your cover letter and resume. Thank you. 
 Job Types: Part-time, Contract 
 Salary: $39.00 per hour 
 Benefits: 
 
 Flexible schedule 
 
 COVID-19 considerations: 
- Hands must be washed / sanitized upon arrival at the home 
- Teachers and students must wear masks or face shields at all times 
- Teacher and student must remain at least 6' apart at all times 
- Singing lessons will be online only for the time being

 Teaching: 1 year (Required)
