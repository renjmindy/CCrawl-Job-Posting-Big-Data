 Are you interested in empowering people to achieve life-changing goals? 
 We're building a deep-tech ed-tech company and we're looking for someone with business commercial and operational experience. 
 We're here to help people around the world learn a language in a new and exciting way. 
 We are also backed by antler (antler.co) and looking to begin in the new year. 
 Who are you? 
 
 A partner, first; someone who wants to own the problem, not just execute on goals. 
 You care deeply about our mission - which relates to education and specifically, language acquisition, but also about driving business and technological impact through improving user experience and research. 
 You take a thoughtful approach to decision making, and at least on the technical side, you know when to move fast, and when to slow down and do things right. 
 You are serious about data, experimentation, and data-driven decision making; You have an impact-driven approach, with the ability to translate commercial business needs into deliverables. 
 You want to be working and lead an early-stage start-up and you understand what it means. 
 You believe the best product is one that helps users accomplish their goals. 
 
 Your Qualifications 
 
 You have experience building commercial and pricing models. 
 Sales; You've got the confidence and the skills to identify customers and go after them and negotiate contracts. 
 Team building: You're not only passionate but you have got what it takes to build a successful, strongly aligned team. 
 You are comfortable zooming in and out. This means being strategic or getting into the weeds when you need to. 
 You understand what is required to commercialize products and have proven success doing so. 
 B2B experience is a bonus. 
 Building financial models is a bonus. 
 Hiring experience is a bonus. 
 Start-up experience is a bonus. 
 
 What we can offer 
 
 Equal and fair equity. 
 Salary upon seed funding (scheduled for Q1). 
 Remote work, access to WeWork. 
 An opportunity to shape a product and build fun and empowering company culture.
