 Daily Duties 
The focus of your role will be to support the qualified staff with the primary delivery of care to the services patients. 
 
 
 You will be working within a hospital an acute adult's psychiatric wards, a CAHMS ward (male and female) and eating disorders providing support towards mental health recovery. 
 All the service users will have varying degrees of mental health severities which will require an individual and tailored approach in their care. 
 You will be assisting the specialist mental health nurse with the day to day care of the service users. 
 Therefore, it is crucial you are a team player, outgoing and strong at building relationships.
