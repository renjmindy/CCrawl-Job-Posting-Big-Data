 Substitute Opportunities in New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Illinois, and Massachusetts

 __Substitute Opportunities 
 __All Insight Partner School Districts 
 __Posted 2 years ago 
 About Insight: 
 Insight is one of the fastest growing education staffing companies in the country. Our company staffs substitute teachers, paraprofessionals, clerical staff, non-instructional aides, and more in over 60 school districts. If you are looking for a fast-paced job that fits your schedule, visit  www.workwithinsight.com/apply  now to start your online application. 
 Job Description: 
 Daily Substitute Teachers, Paraprofessionals, Non-Instructional Assistants, and more are needed to work in Insight's partner school districts in New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Illinois, and Massachusetts. 
 Substitute positions offer a great way to get a foot in the door at the school district of your choosing while making some extra cash! Insight also allows you to transfer easily between districts in order to find your best fit. 
 Take advantage of a flexible work schedule and gain valuable hands-on experience in the classroom before earning a degree. Help students learn subject matter and skills that will contribute to their development as mature, able, and responsible men and women in the absence of the regular teacher. Make important connections with school administrators and educators, who may write reference letters or sit on hiring boards in the future. 
 Job Requirements & Qualifications: 
 Requirements and qualifications vary by position and school district. 
 How to Apply: 
 Please visit  www.workwithinsight.com/apply  to start your online application. Once you submit our online application, a recruiter will reach out to you in 48 business hours to guide you through the next steps of the hiring process. Please contact our office at (856) 406-6015 or email  support@workwithinsight.com  for more information. 
 Insight is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
