 The service station network requires you, as the retailer, to operate the service station as an independent agent. This method offers competitive business opportunities for entrepreneurial minded individuals to grow.

 You will have the following qualities: 
Understand, share and uphold our commitment to safety 
Focus on operational excellence 
Execute against BG Fuels’ Policies and Standards every day 
Deliver on BG Fuels’ business plans and actively participate in initiatives, programs and promotions 
Support and grow the loyalty program 
Be aware of local market activity and industry trends 
Exceptional customer service orientation 
Engage directly with customers to foster loyalty through superior service 
Hire, train and coach your staff to provide an exceptional customer experience 
Community engaged individuals 
Actively engage in the local community, to build and strengthen the connection to our customers
