 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently working on an exciting opportunity for a Senior Executive PA to join a brand new Chief Executive Officer in Telford area. 
 You will be working closely with the CEO and be responsible for complex diary management, preparation of board packs, taking minutes where required and liaising with internal and external stakeholders on a highly confidential basis, so the ability to act with discretion and handle confidential information is essential. 
 You'll need to be flexible and travel alongside CEO in attendance to other sites across the business within the Shropshire & Birmingham areas, ability to use your own initiative and make effective decisions. 
 You will be communicating and collaboratively working with other Personal Assistants across the business, to manage a quality service. 
 If you believe you have the skills and experience to provide efficient support at Executive level, then call Jane Spearman for a confidential discussion today on 0121 752 3822. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
