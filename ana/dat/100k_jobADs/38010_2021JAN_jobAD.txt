 Job Description 
 Are you an experienced perioperative nurse? 
 Do you dream of living and working in New Zealand and enjoying a great work-life balance? 
 We are welcoming applications from suitably qualified registered nurses with perioperative experience. Have you worked in DOSA/Pre-Assessment, Theatre and/or PACU? 
 We have opportunities throughout New Zealand and would love to hear from you! 
 
 ****To apply, you will need: 
 
 
 A nursing qualification comparable to a Bachelor of Nursing degree in New Zealand 
 Current registration with an overseas regulatory authority 
 A minimum of 2 years’ nursing experience 
 To be able to communicate effectively (written and verbal) with patients, colleagues, and other health professionals. 
 
 NB. To practice as a registered nurse in New Zealand, registration with the Nursing Council of New Zealand is required. Guidance with your NZ Registration is available from our team. 
 **APPLY NOW AND START YOUR JOURNEY TO NZ! 
** 
 How to apply: 
Please click on the apply button and complete the short application form. To speed up your application, please upload a copy of your CV. 
 Working In NZ Health are international healthcare recruitment and immigration specialists. We are partnered with a large healthcare network throughout New Zealand. As the preferred agency for overseas applicants, we guarantee your application will be considered and prioritised.  ** _ 
 **** 
Our expert teams in NZ and the UK provide a fully supported process to help secure you a job and visa plus guidance on relocation.
