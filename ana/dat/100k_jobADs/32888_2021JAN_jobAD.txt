 Mobility Sales and Service Advisor 
 If you are looking for a role that gives you a sense of achievement, were you can make a positive impact on someone's quality of life, then get in touch. 
 This Wednesbury based organisation have recently celebrated their 15th year anniversary and has a strong reputation within the mobility field. The sales team is expanding and are looking for energetic, adaptable, like-minded individuals to accommodate their growth. We are looking for target-driven individuals who have the self-discipline to work alone in addition to the drive to hit team targets. 
 Confident conversational skills are a must as this role will include dealing with customer enquiries, both written and verbal. We are looking for an individual who has a personalised approach to customer service, with the interpersonal skills needed to quickly build a rapport with people from all different backgrounds. 
 You will need the pragmatism and adaptability to provide a tailored service. The nature of this role requires you to hold a full UK driving license as you will be expected to arrange demonstrations of the vehicles and attend events. 
 We are looking for a people person that is passionate about customer service. If you would like to discuss this opportunity further, please contact me for a confidential conversation 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
