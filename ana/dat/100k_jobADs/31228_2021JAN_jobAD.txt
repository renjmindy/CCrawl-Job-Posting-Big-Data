 Full-Time Infectious Disease Nurse 
 The City of Espoo -  Espoo  , Etelä-Suomi, Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 Health services including a reception function, oral health, substance abuse and mental health services, student health care, and environmental health. 
 We will work with an important public health interest. We renew our operations to meet the challenges of the future. We attach importance to customer-oriented services, the availability and promotion of local people's health, as well as employee satisfaction and well-being. 
 Job description: Infectious Disease Nurse is an infectious disease physician as an independent work in pairs, as well as an expert in Espoo, social and health care outpatient representative of regional and national networks. Duties include communicable diseases and control measures necessary to perform an infectious disease doctor. Acting as a social and health care business unit of infectious disease expert. 
 Analyses would be an epidemic, as well as statistics and data compilation and transmission. Infection control-related cooperation projects and working groups. Infectious Disease Work-related training and development functions. 
 Eligibility Requirements: 
 A licensed nurse or a licensed nurse. We appreciate your understanding of infectious diseases and their prevention skills. Good knowledge of microbiology. Communicable diseases-related legislation and national guidelines, as well as knowledge about how they are applied in practice. 
 Good written and oral presentation skills. The ability to take into account situations of interaction of different cultures and backgrounds from the interaction challenges. Flexibility and good resistance to pressure.  English and Swedish language skills, as well as other language skills.
