 Consultant: 
 
 
 A business based near Worcester are looking for a Finance Manager to join them on a permanent basis to ensure the smooth running of the day to day financial function of two small businesses. 
 Flexible working will be given but you will need to be able to hit the ground running controlling all transactions for the business and completing month end accounts. 
 You will be working in a pivotal role for the business reporting into an off site Finance Manager. You will be stepping into a role where you will be supported but have the ability to work on your own initiative as part of a small and steady team. 
 This is a position of responsibility where your strong attention to detail and reliability to work closely to deadlines will be appreciated. 
 Duties include: 
 
 
 Monthly management Accounts 
 
 
 Profit & Loss 
 
 
 Asset Register 
 
 
 Processing Accruals and Prepayments 
 
 
 Petty Cash 
 
 
 Purchase Ledger 
 
 
 Sales Ledger 
 
 
 Bank Reconciliations 
 
 
 About you: 
 
 Confident communicating with internal and external stakeholders. 
 Excellent IT skills including Excel 
 Attention to Detail

We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however, please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application.
