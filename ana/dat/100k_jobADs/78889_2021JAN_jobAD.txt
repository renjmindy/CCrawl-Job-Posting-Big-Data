 Instructional Designer 
 Robertson College 
 __Calgary, AB 
 Apply Now 
 ECG Education Canada Group - Robertson College  is looking for an  Instructional Designer  to design course content for a Brightspace learning environment for various programs, courses and training initiatives. This role involves working with the Teaching and Learning team to lead the design and implementation of e-learning experiences at The College. 
 This is a  part-time contract  position with flexible working hours and location. The initial  term is three months with the possibility of extension . Compensation will be based on education and experience. 
 Responsibilities 
 · Collaborate with a team to refine Robertson’s course design approach. 
 · Contribute to strategic discussions around Robertson’s LMS strategy. 
 · Apply evidence-based instructional design theories, practice and methods in course creation. 
 · Design course content that engages students in meaningful, interactive, and transferrable learning. 
 · Create support material and media (audio, video, simulations, role plays, games etc.). 
 · Develop evaluation criteria and assessment instruments that align with instructional goals and outcomes. 
 · Stay abreast of current developments and issues in adult education and instructional technology; incorporate new methods, techniques and information into e-learning design. 
 · Conduct instructional research and analysis of learners and contexts. 
 · Complete day-to-day LMS administrative responsibilities, including but not limited to, uploading of course content and providing LMS-related technical support to instructors upon course commencement. 
 · Maintain project documentation and course folders. 
 · Maintain and improve upon existing online course content. 
 Requirements 
 · Degree or diploma in instructional design, educational technology; experience in developing curriculum, or training programs will also be considered. 
 · A minimum of five years of professional instructional design experience. 
 · Proven knowledge and experience with course development software and Learning Management Systems (Brightspace, D2L preferred). 
 · Demonstrated knowledge of learning theories and instructional design models. 
 · Highly self-motivated and self-directed; objective-oriented with a high-level of attention to detail. 
 · Sharp analytical and problem-solving skills. 
 · Demonstrated ability to work independently and as part of a team. 
 · Ability to communicate effectively through writing, especially in writing effective copy, instructional text, and audio scripts/video scripts. 
 · A combination of education and experience may be considered. 
 · Excellent verbal and written communication skills. 
 Please apply by sending your  resume  and  cover letter . This posting will close on  November 25, 2020.
