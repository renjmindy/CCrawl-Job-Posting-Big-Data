 About our Client 
 Rio Tinto operates the world's largest integrated portfolio of iron ore assets. These assets include a world-class, integrated network of 15 iron ore mines, four port facilities, a 1,700 kilometre rail network and related infrastructure. Rio Tinto are expanding operations in the Pilbara to epic proportions while introducing next generation technologies to deliver greater efficiency, lower production costs and improved health, safety and environmental performance. 
 About the Role 
 An ongoing opportunityÂ exists for skilled and experienced Light Vehicle FitterÂ who will contribute to the successful operations of the Nammuldi mine site located in the Pilbara region of Western Australia. 
 Reporting to the Light Maintenance Equipment Supervisor, you will repair and conduct mechanical and electrical preventative maintenance and repairs on 4WDs, ancillary equipment and road trains. 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Proven experience in the repairs and maintenance of 4WD light vehicles, road trains and ancillary mining equipment 
 
 
 Exceptional diagnostic and troubleshooting/interpretive skills in both the mechanical and electrical components of vehicles 
 
 
 Relevant Australian recognised trade certification 
 
 
 Air-conditioning accreditation (AAC02 - Automotive Air Conditioning Licence) 
 
 
 Manual C Class Australian issued Drivers Licence (minimum) 
 
 
 HR Drivers Licence (preferred)
