 Job description 
 Labourer - Expression on Interest 
 
 
 $29.40 per hour, plus penalties + travel allowance 
 
 
 Adelaide, Southern suburbs and Northern suburbs 
 
 
 Roles to commence early 2021 
 
 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are seeking Expression of Interest for Civil labourers within the Adelaide on street upgrades, Sub divisions and general civil sites. There will be 6 months work on a multitude of sites ranging from Northern suburbs, central CBD, southern suburbs and Adelaide hills. 
 Duties & responsibilities 
 
 
 Experienced civil labourers 
 
 
 Setting levels, 
 
 
 Spotting plant, 
 
 
 Using wacker plates and leg rammers 
 
 
 Assisting the pipe layers to lay pipe 
 
 
 General site labouring 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Dogman ticket and MR truck licence an advantage, but not essential 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Demonstrated previous experience working on a construction site 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
