 ABOUT CLIENT 
 Our client is a global leader in the commercial explosives industry. 
 ABOUT THE ROLE 
 Seeking experienced Shot Firer to commence at a mine located near Blackwater. The position is ongoing working a 7/7 roster, 12 hour shifts. 
 DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 
 Priming Blast Holes 
 
 
 Loading explosives into blast holes 
 
 
 Stemming of blast holes 
 
 
 Other duties as required 
 
 
 SKILLS & RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 
 Shot Firer Licence 
 
 
 Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Standard 11 
 
 
 Manual Drivers Licence 
 
 
 SSAN or Qld Security Card 
 
 
 Desirable - MPU Experience 
 
 
 2-3 Years Experience Minimum 
 
 
 CULTURE & BENEFITS 
 
 
 Camp & Meals Supplied 
 
 
 7/7 Roster, 12 hour shifts
