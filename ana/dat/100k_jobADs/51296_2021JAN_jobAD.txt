 Clinical Lead 
 An amazing job opportunity has arisen for a committed Clinical Lead to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the Norwich area. 
 As the Clinical Lead you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This nursing home offers highly skilled and passionate support staff who provide high standards of tailored care designed to meet the unique needs of each person 
 An ideal Clinical Lead must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     You will be already working in a Senior role in a similar environment
 
 
 
     You will be passionate about Dementia care, caring for the elderly, and always strive to provide great quality care, making every day better than the last; promoting our core values: maintaining independence and dignity to our service users
 
 
 
     You'll be up to date with current evidence-based practice and have a working knowledge of CQC Standards and Regulations and will work using your initiative making effective decisions to improve care quality
 
 
 
     You will be a team player, demonstrating outstanding communication skills and will be able to demonstrate effective teamwork as part of a multi-disciplined team as a Staff Nurse
 
 
 
 **The successful Clinical Lead will receive an excellent salary up to £20.00 per hour and the annual salary is up to £41,600 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role for 40 hours a week working through out Days. 
 Supernumerary may include some clinical hours to fit the needs of the business.
