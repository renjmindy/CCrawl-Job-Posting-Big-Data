 Live in Caregiver Job Bahrain 
 Posted on  February 9, 2011  by  caregiver 
 For those who are looking for Live in Caregiver Jobs, the POEA recently approved a job order that offers employment opportunities to those who are qualified for  Live in Caregiver Job . This live in caregiver job is for the country of Bahrain whose Job Order was approved by the POEA last February 3, 2011 for a recruitment agency located in Ermita, Manila and a branch office in Cebu City. For the said live in caregiver job Bahrain, only 5 applicants will be chosen for employment in caregiver work abroad. 
 To apply for the said Live in Caregiver Job Bahrain, you may personally submit your resume and application to the office of Seven Ocean International Manpower Corp. in Ermita, Manila or in their branch office in Cebu City. Or, you may visit their website and fill up their Online Application. For any question regarding the said Live in Caregiver Job Bahrain, you may contact Seven Ocean International Manpower Corp. thru their telephone numbers provided below. 
 Seven Ocean International Manpower Corporation 
Unit-908 Pearl of the Orient 
1240 Roxas Blvd, Ermita 
Manila 
Tel Nos: +632 400-6788 / +632 521-2241 
Website: http://sevenoceanmanpower.com 
 BRANCH OFFICE 
 3/F 104 V. Gullas Street 
Cebu City
