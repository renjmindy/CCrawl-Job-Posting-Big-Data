 Mechanical Fitter 
 Job description 
 Role Highlights 
 
 
 $50.00 per hour flat rate 
 
 
 Work between the workshop & HV mine sites 
 
 
 Ongoing role with a min. 4 days per week 
 
 
 About the Role 
 Our Client is seeking 2x Mechanical Fitters with previous Fixed Plant/CHPP experience to work between their workshop in Beresfield and local Hunter Valley mine sites. 
 The role is an ongoing/long term position and would be working a min. 4 days per week with an opportunity for more days in the new year. 
 The days will be a min. 8 hours in the workshop with most days on site averaging between 10-12 hours. 
 The rate is $50.00 per hour flat rate. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Provide maintenance and repairs to mining/mechanical equipment within a CHPP environment 
 
 
 Must be reliable and committed to the role 
 
 
 Experience in screens, air filtration, pumps and conveyors 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 MUST have previous CHPP/Fixed Plant experience 
 
 
 MUST have Confined Space & Working at Heights 
 
 
 Current MDG25, Order 43 Medical and Site Inductions are a BONUS 
 
 
 Cert III in Mechanical Engineering or equivalent 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Ability to Pass and Pre Employment Medical and D&A; 
 
 
 Have valid working rights in Australia 
 
 
 Current resume reflecting your experience 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
