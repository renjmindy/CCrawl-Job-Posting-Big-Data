 Financial Planning Analyst 
 Berkshire 
 Permanent 
 £50,000 - £55,000 a year + bonus 
 The Opportunity 
 A rare opportunity to join a multi-award winning and market leading international PE backed business services client based in Slough. 
 The position has arisen due to an internal restructure and business expansion. 
 I am looking for outstanding finance talent with an impressive educational and academic track record of achievement. 
 Ideally you will have secured your full accounting qualification in the last 1-3 years preferably from either a Top 10 firm or within a corporate environment. 
 This role is pivotal in supporting the leadership team with consistent, accurate and consistent data analytics within an FP&A function support multiple territories. 
 You will build, produce and report on key commercial information including sales trend lines, pricing, profitability etc to enable the further growth of the business. 
 This role provides a mix of traditional FP&A, data analytic and business support. 
 
 Financial Planning and Analysis 
 Data integrity 
 Improvement on speed and accuracy of data 
 Dissemination of international sales information 
 Development of strategic data analysis and reporting skills within an acquisitive international business. 
 You will be a Qualified Accountant (ACA, ACCA, CIMA) FP&A analysts with stable and progressive track record 
 
 Requirements: 
 You will be passionate about producing insightful and comprehensive analysis to produce robust reporting around data accuracy, data reporting, trend analysis . 
 You will be a team player looking to develop your FP&A and analytic skills 
 Excellent planning and time management skills with clear and concise communication skills. 
 Successful Applicant 
 As a qualified or qualified by experience accountant, you will have experience of demonstrating practical skills from a larger business or Practice background. 
 Applications are also sought from Practice trained accountants possibly with corporate secondment experience looking for their first commercial role. 
 You will be ideally looking to progress to the next stage within your early career. You will be looking to join a forward thinking organisation that is on an upward trajectory both locally and internationally. 
 This may suit a qualified (ACA, ACCA or CIMA) - newly qualified or qualified by experience candidate. 
 What's on Offer 
 On offer to the successful candidate, is an excellent business culture within the FP&A team, a mentor that will help progress your career to the next stage. You will be given an excellent benefits package along with a competitive basic salary. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
