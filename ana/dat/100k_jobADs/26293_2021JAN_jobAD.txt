 Dump Truck Operator 
 Job description 
 
 
 
 Job Highlights $47.68/ hour Ongoing Position Meals and Accommodation Provided About the Role WorkPac are currently seeking a skilled and experienced Dump Truck Operator to commence in a role near Glenden. The successful candidate will be passed out (RII or SIGNED Skills List) on Electric or Mechanical trucks and be able to pass a Pre-Employment Medical. The roster is 7/7, straight Days/Nights. Duties & Responsibilities Ability to pass a Drug & Alcohol Test Ability to pass a Pre-Employment Medical Operation of minimum of CAT 789 Haul Truck or KOM 785 Skills & Experience Experienced Mechanical and Electric Truck (MT 4400 or similar) Current Standard 11 or obtain Current Coal Board Medical or obtain Current Drivers Licence Right to work within Australia About WorkPac WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors.
