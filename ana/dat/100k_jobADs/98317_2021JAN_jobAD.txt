 I have a fantastic opportunity for a Brand Manager to join a well-known organisation in Birmingham, who are well-known for their excellent working culture and who are going through extreme growth at the moment. They have some extremely exciting projects coming up both in the UK and internationally. This role will be perfect for someone who is an expert in branding, who has worked on a number of high-profile branding campaigns. 
 This Brand Manager role will involve: 
 
 Planning and managing the development and implementation for the brand strategy 
 Working with the brand leads for other associated organisations, ensuring all branded collateral falls in line with brand guidelines 
 Developing creative concepts for the organisation 
 Developing the central brand toolkit 
 Producing marketing collateral which tells the brand story for the organisation and coming up with ideas and suggestions which bring the brand to life 
 Managing and helping external designers, event specialists and photographers 
 
 This Brand Manager position requires a candidate with the following skills: 
 
 Experienced in the development and delivery of brand strategies within both a B2B and B2C environment 
 Experience of working in a delivery partner environment, with a number of key external stakeholders 
 Experience of working on international branding campaigns 
 Excellent and professional communication skills, both written and verbal 
 Outstanding stakeholder management skills 
 The ability to manage a number of projects at any one time 
 
 To apply for the Brand Manager position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
