 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently working with an industry recognised top employer who are recruiting for a Group Financial Accountant and is based in Nottingham. This position reports to the Group Financial Controller, and forms part of the financial reporting team. 
 This really is a fantastic opportunity for a first or second time mover to join a listed business in a great group function. With massive exposure to senior stakeholders this role offers demonstrable and defined routes of progression within 18Months 
 Key Duties, Group Financial Accountant, Nottingham 
 
 Monthly reporting 
 Assist the Director of Financial Reporting in ensuring the accuracy and integrity of the group consolidation 
 Preparation of the monthly consolidated cash flow reports for the Group 
 Responsible for actioning all direct journals in the Consolidation and Reporting system 
 Responsible for monitoring all of the Group's intercompany accounts, both P&L and balance sheet 
 Working with the Group Treasurer, ensure accuracy and transparency of data for bank covenant calculations 
 Management of a range of holding companies, including monthly reporting, budgeting, forecasting, and completion of UK GAAP statutory accounts where necessary 
 Provision of monthly reporting to the Group CFO and other internal management as requested 
 Build effective relationships with finance teams across the group 
 Provide advice and support on technical accounting issues to divisional finance teams and senior management 
 
 Skills and Experiences, Group Financial Accountant, Nottingham 
 
 ACA qualified with big four or mid-tier audit background 
 Technically strong, with in depth UKGaap knowledge 
 Ability to deliver accurate results to tight deadlines with good attention to detail 
 Experience with general accounting, financial statement preparation, consolidations, monthly close processes, and cash flow reporting (Desirable) 
 Familiarity with multi-currency, group consolidations (Desirable) 
 Highly systems literate 
 Strong interpersonal and communications skills required 
 Willingness to improve processes and drive efficiency's 
 Highly motivated, self-starter and results-oriented 
 
 This role is really an excellent opportunity for a first time mover from practice to join an excellent industry role and progress there career quickly
