 Our Tech Stack üì≤ 
 
 TypeScript with React and React Native on the frontend 
 Go on the backend (experience not required for this role)

 What are we looking for üîç 
 We're hiring a capable and motivated React Native developer to help continue to build a reliable and beautiful app for WalkUp. 
 WalkUp is seating over 600,000 people a week and grew 800% last year. We've raised over $1m in Seed Funding and are growing our software engineering team. 
 You'll join 4 other developers, working closely with all sides of the system. We all work together to solve the problems that we face everyday. 
 We're passionate about clean, testable, self-documenting code, we use React Native exclusively for our Android and iOS, and target the most recent SDKs and technology so we can use the latest and greatest device features available to us. We use Firebase as a backend and Kubernetes to allow us to iterate new features very quickly. 
 What you will work on: 
 
 As part of the early team of WalkUp you will have a huge impact on the direction of the company and the features that we decide to build. You will mostly be working on our iOS and Android apps however we want someone that has the ability to jump between different areas to help other parts of the team. 
 
 You should apply if: 
 
 You want to build a product that you and everyone you know uses all the time 
 You love React Native 
 You've shipped and iterated on at least one app in the App Store 
 You have worked with custom build process using fastlane (i.e. not expo) 
 You love turning excellent design into beautiful and easy to use products 
 You're comfortable working in a team that deals with ambiguity and challenges every day 
 Have an understanding of the principles of computer science 
 You have worked with Redux in a production level app 
 You want to create smooth products with cool animations 
 
 Bonus points for (but not essential) 
 
 Have worked with Firebase before 
 You have worked with Redux Saga 
 Built an API or backend in Node.js 
 Worked with NoSQL 
 Worked with animations at an advanced level
