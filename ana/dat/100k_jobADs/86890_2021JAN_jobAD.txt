 About US: 
 Peartree Group Property Management is an award wining dynamic Property Management company who looks to create amazing communities within the properties they manage while providing cost effective Property Management services to their owners. We are a small, but focused group who are not afraid to think outside the box and get the work done. 
 At a glance: 
 Our goal is to find an amazing like-minded sales professional to help us grow our management portfolio and find amazing tenants for the new owners. 
 If you enjoy meeting new people, networking, providing a great service to a grateful customer but are currently limited to selling retail to customers who walk in off the street then Peartree may be what you need. We offer a more exciting and dynamic sales experience where as a full-time sales person you will be instrumental in building our business and brand to new clients. We are looking for an organized, digitally savvy communicator with a competitive spirit, eager, comfortable with being creative, and trying new things. In this role, you will help shape the sales experience for customers and tenants alike. Real-estate experience is an asset. 
 Benefits are clear: 
 The individual will work independently doing what they love. A chance to help build the sales system and team.
