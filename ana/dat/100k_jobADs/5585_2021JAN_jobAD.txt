 About Our Client Our client is a large Electrical Business partnered with WorkPac, they are currently seeking an experienced Local Electricians for a project located in Mugon commencing of the 4th of January 2021. Murgon is based in the South Burnett Region. About the Role WorkPac are currently seeking experienced Commercial and Construction based Electricians. The successful candidates will be required to carry out a scope of work beginning with the Underground installations and associated Civil / Inground works. You will also be able to conduct all relevant Electrical work across a commercial project. The initial works will commence early January 2021. The ideal candidate will be available and local to the region for that time frame. There is potential for an ongoing opportunity for the right candidates.
