 Are you a driven and creative marketing executive? Do you have outstanding design skills? Are you fascinated by social media and digital marketing? Most importantly… do you love Gelato as much as we do? Then we may be made for each other! We are a passionate and rapidly expanding Italian Gelato brand based in central London, looking for an enthusiastic marketer who values aesthetics, branding and understands our consumer. 
 Position summary 
 The marketing executive works within the marketing team and reports directly to the Marketing Manager. We are looking for an exceptional all round marketer, with a particular interest in content creation and graphic design.  The candidate is expected to take ownership of development for existing and emerging marketing channels, as well as structuring and implementing future marketing activities. 
 You need to be able to run campaigns and projects independently. You will be able to make recommendations with regards to how our brand should be developed and what to engage with, to maximise project ROI and successfully support the sales team with marketing campaigns that will directly contribute to the overall sales target. 
 The candidate must also have a genuine interest and knowledge in Italian culture and cuisine. Additionally, this position requires a highly proactive individual with a deep interest in brand management and how to grow and develop a business from an overarching brand mission. 
 Key Responsibilities 
 Social Media/Digital Marketing 
 
 Plan, post and streamline social media accounts (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram) and maintain a monthly social media calendar 
 Interact with our followers on all social platforms by replying to their comments and engaging with influencers/potential consumers 
 Track key social media analytics and understand how to improve our future content 
 Developing digital marketing strategies with an aim to increase engagement through campaigns and brand message. 
 Using consumer Insights to report on trends and deliver valuable reports across the business 
 
 Content Creation 
 
 Create graphics for social media content using Adobe Illustrator/InDesign/Photoshop (support given) 
 Create content using photography/videography skills 
 Create new or edit existing marketing collateral using Adobe Illustrator/InDesign/Photoshop 
 Write monthly newsletters following our template through MailChimp 
 Make necessary content changes to our website (Wordpress) 
 Ensuring uniform content and tone of voice for text, graphics and other elements within the communities 
 
 Events 
 
 Plan and forecasting the year's events that fit Remeo's brand values 
 Organise and manage all aspects of events, including with liaising with external agencies 
 
 Brand development 
 
 Planning, developing and executing critical marketing campaigns to grow the brand across all markets 
 Clear ownership of ongoing brand performance, research and external market trends, and analyse data from multiple sources to build key brand, retail, shopper and consumer insights 
 Responsible for media & communications strategy; delivering measurable activations 
 Manage and forecast annual marketing budget 
 
 Must have skills 
 
 1-2 years of experience in the FMCG industry 
 Proficient photography skills, with the ability to create social media content on a weekly basis 
 Proven track record of delivering impactful marketing campaigns from start to finish 
 Experience in organising and executing premium events 
 Ability to work autonomously whilst being an organised and structured individual that can prioritise tasks 
 Excellent organisation skills with the ability to manage multiple projects at once 
 Excellent communication skills and can work collaboratively with a team 
 Good knowledge of Adobe products (e.g. Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign) 
 Excellent knowledge of Microsoft Office Products (e.g. Word, PowerPoint, Excel) 
 Able to remain calm under pressure and a robust/resilient attitude towards challenges 
 An interest in design and all things aesthetic 
 Basic knowledge of WordPress with ability to make basic changes
