 Caregivers Jobs Israel 
 Posted on  August 19, 2010  by  caregiver 
 Through a POEA approved job order Filipino caregivers are being offered employment opportunities in  Caregivers Jobs Israel . The said job order was approved by the POEA last August 12 for RISE Manpower Services, a POEA accredited recruitment agency based in Malate, Manila. Accompanying the said job order is a job order balance for 30 caregivers to be selected for placement in caregiver work abroad. 
 If you are interested for the said caregiver job ads proceed directly to the office of the recruiting agency and submit your resume and application. For additional info or inquiries regarding the said caregivers jobs Israel you can refer to the recruiting agency’s landline numbers or email address indicated below.
