 Deputy Manager 
 An amazing job opportunity has arisen for dedicated Deputy Manager to work in an exceptional care home based in the Wolverhampton, West Midlands area. 
 As the Deputy Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This special care home is a purpose-built care home designed for older people requiring residential and nursing care. 
 The dedicated team ensure they meet the resident's individual care and social needs 
 An ideal Deputy Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Strong Management and Leadership skills
 
 
 
     Caring & compassionate with an excellent clinical track record
 
 
 
     Passionate about delivering outstanding care to older people
 
 
 
     Excellent organisation skills
 
 
 
     Effective communication skills
 
 
 
     Flexible, professional and committed to being the best
 
 
 
     Quality driven with a desire to continually improve yourself & our services
 
 
 
 
 **The successful Deputy Manager will receive an excellent salary of £20.00 per hour and the annual salary for this position is £41,600 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 40 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Market competitive rates of pay
 
 
 
     Specialist dementia training and support from our award-winning, university-accredited 'Creative Minds' programme
 
 
 
     Full library of interactive learning resources from the Aged Care Channel in every home for personal & professional development
 
 
 
     Range of salary sacrifice benefits including childcare vouchers, pension, gym membership, technology purchase schemes and more
 
 
 
     Online benefits portal with a range of offers and cashback rewards
