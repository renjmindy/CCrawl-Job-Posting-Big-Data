 Warehouse Officer 
 Job description 
 About Our Client 
The Supply Officer is responsible for supporting the daily supply chain activity requirements for the Mine. This role requires the Supply Officer to play an integral part in the implementation, delivery and sustainability of our client's commercial operations activities whilst maintaining Zero Harm. 
 Through adopting and embracing Company policies and procedures, the Supply Officer will support and provide guidance in conducting effective and efficient warehouse operations in inventory management. This high level of customer service provided to dependencies will lead to optimising the our clients business performance model and will minimise operational downtime. 
 About the Role 
 
 
 Conduct role functions in accordance with technical instructions, policies and procedures. 
 
 
 Conduct related Logistic Information System report activities in accordance with BAU policies. 
 
 
 Process issues and receipts in accordance with BAU policies. 
 
 
 Conduct storage, stocktaking, inventory and disposals management activities in accordance with BAU policies. 
 
 
 Provide a high level of customer service to dependencies. 
 
 
 Conduct liaison with suppliers. 
 
 
 Comply with occupational health and safety systems. 
 
 
 Comply with workplace equity and conflict policies. 
 
 
 Participate in on the job and continuous training. 
 
 
 Skills and Qualifications 
 
 
 Current Standard 11 
 
 
 Current Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Supply Officer experience at least 2 years (Will accept experience in spare parts transaction orientated roles) 
 
 
 Must be computer literate, have a good work ethic and show initiative 
 
 
 Culture and Benefits 
 
 
 Monday to Friday Roster 
 
 
 3 month contract 
 
 
 $44.43 Flat Rate 
 
 
 Camp Supplied (if not local)
