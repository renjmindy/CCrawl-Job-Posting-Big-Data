 Part-Time Hour security trainers 
 Hyria Training Ltd  -  Päijänne Tavastia  , Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 Hyria training to implement Hyvinkää-Riihimäki in serving the needs of students in vocational training and employment development auxiliaries. We want to be the most respected pioneer in vocational education and training and a strong regional actor. Welcome to our team. Train the security at various levels: basic security, Security Guards and Security Officer, Specialist in the field as well as different skills and training card (security guard job criteria, security guard course, stewards, safety at work, hot work, and the social and health safety). We train our security, both young and adult students. We are looking for a part-time salaried instructors, who have the qualifications and expertise in at least one or preferably more of the following: the use of force, hot work, security badges, law enforcement or security guard training techniques section. The work is mainly-time (8-16 range), and is ranging from various units (principally in Riihimäki, Malmi in Helsinki and Lahti, Finland). We particularly appreciate the strong diversification of the security of practical work experience and teaching experience. Eligibility requirements are determined by the task of Regulation (A14.12.1998/986), the. Qualified applicants in the absence of the task can be suspended to choose the candidate who does not meet the eligibility requirements. Remuneration is determined by professional adult education centers in the collective agreement. Prior to the commencement of work selected must be approved certificate of health and children working in the criminal history of the Act (Act 504/2002) with a criminal record. The task is to use the trial period. Primary office in addition to the employment area may be all Hyrian offices. ,
