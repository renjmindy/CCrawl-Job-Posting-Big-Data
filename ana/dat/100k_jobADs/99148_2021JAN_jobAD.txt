 Caregiver Job Hiring 
 Posted on  March 22, 2011  by  caregiver 
 An employer from the holy land of Israel is currently in need of Filipino caregivers who are qualified for  Caregiver Job Hiring . These caregiver job hiring was approved by the POEA last March 2, 2011 for a recruitment agency based in Makati City. And with the said POEA approval, the agency is allowed to recruit 30 caregiver job applicants for placement in caregiver work abroad. 
 To apply for the said Caregiver Job Hiring, you may submit your resume directly to the office of Provident Overseas Placement Agency or you may visit their website and fill up their Online Application. If you have any question regarding the said Caregiver Job Hiring, you may ask Provident Overseas Placement Agency directly thru their telephone numbers or email address provided below. 
 Provident Overseas Placement Agency 
Unit B1 DNS Building 
5059 Filmore cor. Arrellano Sts., Palanan 
Makati City 
Tel Nos: 8329498 / 5513409 
Fax No: 5517741 
Email Add: 
popa@provident.com.ph 
provident_01@hotmail.com 
Website: www.provident.com.ph
