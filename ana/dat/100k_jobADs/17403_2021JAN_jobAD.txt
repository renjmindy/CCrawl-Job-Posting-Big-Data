 Want to work on one of the most exciting and innovative fields of technology in the recent years? Want to shape the future of Conversational AI and build an impactful business around it? We're looking for talented and motivated people to join our team and push frontiers! 
 This is a very exciting opportunity to be the first salesperson on the ground, to contribute to shape a sales development strategy with the founders and to give it your personal touch, based on your expertise, experience, and success stories. 
 To apply, you will include along with your resume, a short paragraph describing your unique fit for this role and why you would be qualified to be the first salesperson on the ground in a deep tech start-up. Looking forward to reading your masterpiece - no need for an essay, feel free to keep it short and sweet! 
 
 What you will do at Wluper 
 
 Identify new opportunities within highly targeted enterprise clients 
 Establish meaningful relationships with potential clients and partners 
 Develop a network of strong relationships with key industry influencers, customers, and partners 
 Lead compelling sales presentations with all levels of a customer organisations 
 Shape and refine our sales process, by creating and maintaining sales funnel and pipeline 
 Forecast and manage sales pipeline and deals to meet revenue targets and company goals 
 Supporting the leadership team on creating a sales playbook ready for future hires 
 Implement and use a CRM to track and report on sales activity metrics 
 Deliver feedback to the product and engineering team to support roadmap development 
 Attend conferences, meetings and industry events 
 
 Please keep in mind that we are describing the background we imagine would best fit the role. However, even if you don't meet all the requirements, yet you are passionate about this opportunity, we would love to get to know you. 
 
 What you will bring to the Wluper team 
 
 BSc, MSc, or higher in Business Administration, or any other relevant field 
 5+ years of relevant sales experience, opening new business opportunities in a B2B SaaS/tech company, ideally close to the AI/ML field 
 Hunger to do whatever it takes to help set up and sustain new sales and business development efforts for our startup 
 Excellent written/verbal presentation, communication, and relationship-building skills 
 Ability to quickly make complex products and concepts easy to understand for everyone 
 Desire to work in a small, early-stage company and a fast-paced environment 
 Flexibility, open-mindedness, and hustle 
 Ability to create change, not just follow a process 
 
 
 Nice to haves for this role 
 
 Previous experience in planning sales strategies and scaling processes in an AI startup 
 Knowledge/background working with or understanding of deep learning/machine learning/AI tools, ideally even within the voice/NLP space 
 Marketing acumen and/or experience coupled with a strong interest in building a top-class sales pipeline 
 
 
 What we offer at Wluper 
 
 Equity/stock options 
 Flexible hours and a working from home policy 
 26 days paid holiday per year, plus company and bank holidays 
 A MacBook Pro, and other tech that supports you doing your work 
 Participation at training workshops, 1-on-1 sessions, conferences, and events 
 Regular (remote) team events as well as regular (remote) social events 
 Healthy snacks and breakfast when in the office 
 
 We're currently all working remotely as a temporary measure against the pandemic. You will be able to work remotely too, but please note you still need to be authorised to work in the UK for any role within our company. 
 We at Wluper are a diverse, motivated, and fast-moving team of researchers and engineers, with extensive experience in many areas. We celebrate diversity which is why we are always looking to grow our team with talented people, regardless of race, colour, religion, nationality, gender, disability, or age  **  . **
