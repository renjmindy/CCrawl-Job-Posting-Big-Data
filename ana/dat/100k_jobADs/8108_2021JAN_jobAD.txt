 Vacancy Description: 
Are you passionate about social media, marketing and graphic design? Draper and Dash have a 
fantastic opportunity for an exceptional and creative Graphic Designer to join our team. 
A key aspect of the role will be focused on creating executing, eye catching and inspirational 
marketing campaigns, tactical plans, contribution to the overall strategy, promotions and events. 
You will be responsible for managing and delivering the business' social media, user growth and 
user retention for its UK, US and Australian user base. If you think you are a highly motivated, 
technical minded and dynamic individual with a flair for creativity and growth- let us know! 
Are you are looking for an exciting and rewarding new challenge along with the opportunity to 
self-develop? If you think you can bring creative and innovative concepts to what do, we 
encourage you to ping us some details of your portfolio. 
 Primary Tasks and Responsibilities: 
 
 Daily update of Draper and Dash's social media accounts 
 Managing weekly mailers to clients 
 Designing information packs on D&D's solutions 
 Helping to manage a marketing campaign for a product launch 
 Social media following expansion 
 Online user acquisition 
 Creating marketing campaigns 
 And more 
 
 Essential Requirements: 
- Excellent command of English 
 
 1-2 years' experience within graphic design and marketing 
 Confident user of the Adobe Creative Suite 
 Basic web/HTML knowledge (Especially Wordpress) 
 Video editing skills 
 Excellent English skills as you may need to write marketing content 
 Excellent research skills 
 Excellent people skills 
 Excellent IT skills 
 Create high visual impact 
 Creative flair and design - Originality 
 Excellent time management and organisational skills 
 Accuracy and attention to detail 
 Ability to manage time, meet deadlines and work within a budget 
 Candidate must have an iPhone 
 
 About Draper and Dash 
Draper & Dash Healthcare (D&D) is a London based Venture Capitalist (VC) backed healthcare AI 
and Machine learning predictive data and analytics company. We leverage data from the public sector 
and private healthcare companies both nationally and globally. We have been providing organisations 
with solutions that improve quality, safety, outcomes, efficiencies and opportunities. 
 Over the years we have worked with over 70 healthcare providers in the UK, US and Australia, 
helping them to drive cost improvements, form new strategic partnerships, improving health outcomes 
and more.
