 Venquis is the UK's leading business change and transformation recruitment consultancy, specializing in senior appointments across financial services/ insurance and Industry + commerce. 
 We are looking for a talented individual to join us here at Venquis! 
 If you want to be trained by a leading learning and development team, to become famous with in a specific industry sector and you are an ambitious, self-motivated individual, we could be exactly what you're looking for. 
 Role 
 - Initially candidate Resourcing and trained up to a 360 Recruitment Consultant 
 - Managing complete Recruitment life cycle 
 - Candidate facing 
 - You will become famous for your niche vertical market 
 - Networking, attending events and marketing yourself and Venquis to businesses and candidates within your market 
 - Active and passive candidate attraction 
 - Screening and interviewing experienced candidates 
 Requirements 
 - Graduate or grad calibre 
 - Sales experience, ideally B2B 
 - Excellent telephone manner 
 - Entrepreneurial nature 
 - High achiever 
 - Ambitious/ driven with a strong will to succeed 
 Salary & Benefits 
 - Starting basic salary 25k + 25% commission 
 - Private pension - Private healthcare (Vitality/Pru) 
 -25 days holiday 
 - 1 free Voluntary day per year + Sabbatical (1 week for every year employed) 
 - Subsidised gym membership 
 - Fit for work (free classes including boxing, yoga & advise from nutritionist) 
 - Annual xmas party & multiple other social events 
 - Dress down Friday's 
 - Annual company trip for the entire business 
 - Top billers club (Vegas 2018) 
 - Billers lunch clubs 
 Job Type: Full-time
