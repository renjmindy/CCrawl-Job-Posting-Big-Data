 Serviceman 
 Job description 
 Serviceperson with Air Con Ticket 
 Job Highlights 
 12:9 Roster 
 Casual Ongoing Work 
 FIFO from Perth or DIDO from Port Hedland 
 About the Role 
 Our client is currently seeking an experienced Serviceperson for an ongoing position based in the Pilbara. The candidate will be required to work do preventative maintenance and servicing to a fleet of trucks, light vehicles and buses. Transport of tools by freight to site is available weekly. The is a 12:9 roster. 
 Primary responsibilities will be: 
 Service and preventative maintenance of; 
 6 Man/Mercedes 4WD Buses 
 4 Man/Isuzu MR and HR 4WD Trucks 
 Approx 15 Light vehicles including Toyota Hilux, Prado, 70 Series. 
 Fleet Management of above vehicles, including; 
 Detailed monthly preventative checks, 
 Ordering of parts for service and maintenance of critical spares. 
 Fault finding and emergency repair to Air Condition units on all vehicles. 
 Emergency fault finding and repair to Generators. 
 Requirements 
 Must have HR drivers licence 
 Must have strong experience servicing all of the above. 
 Have up to 3 years recent experience 
 Must have your Air conditioning ticket 
 Be able to pass a Pre Employment medical 
 Must be reliable and have a good attitude 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors.  At WorkPac, we recognise we are strengthened by diversity. We are committed to providing a work environment in which everyone is included, treated fairly and with respect. We are an Equal Opportunity employer and we encourage applications from women and Indigenous people.
