 POEA Jobs for Caregiver 
 Posted on  November 10, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This POEA jobs for caregiver opportunity is being offered to qualified Filipino caregivers who are seeking employment in jobs for caregiver abroad. The Philippine Overseas Employment Administration or POEA recently approved this job order allowing a Pasay City based recruitment agency to recruit qualified candidates in jobs for caregiver. The said jobs for caregiver are for the country of Israel and the job order for the said jobs for caregiver was approved by the POEA last October 22, 2009. The said job order has a corresponding job order balance of 28 available caregiver jobs abroad. 
 To apply for the said caregiver jobs abroad, all interested parties should proceed directly to the recruiting agency to file their application and bring their most recent resume to the agency’s office address stated below. For further info, you can contact the recruiting agency for the said jobs for caregiver through their telephone number indicated below. 
 World Wiser International Inc. 
2nd Floor, DM Building 
2004 FB Harrison 
Pasay City 
 Tel No: 8243784
