 Cash and Banking Clerk 
Monday- Friday (part time hrs) 
£20,000- £25,000 - 6 month FTC 
 Have you got extensive experience processing online payments? Are you looking to join a global business with a prosperous future? 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group is currently working alongside a global company based in modern offices situated in Birmingham. They are now looking for a Banking & Payments Assistant to join their accounting team on a 6 month FTC. 
 The Role 
 You will be responsible within the team to ensure compliance, allocation of receipts, making payments, bank reconciliations and producing reports. 
 You will be expected to ensure information is accurately and efficiently processed in line with company best practice. 
 Key responsibilities include: 
 
 Allocating Receipts 
 Making BACS and CHAPS Payments 
 Online banking 
 Opening and Closing Bank Accounts 
 Verifying Cheque Payments 
 Bank Reconciliations and resolving discrepancies 
 Handling queries 
 Reviewing banking information 
 Producing reports 
 Supporting Accounts Payable 
 
 (Full job description available) 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 Experience working in a cashier / banking environment 
 High volume, fast paced environment 
 Strong IT skills including Excel 
 Confident communicating with internal and external stakeholders.
