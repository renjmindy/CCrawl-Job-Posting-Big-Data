 Caregiver Job Abroad Singapore 
 Posted on  January 1, 2013  by  caregiver 
 This is good news for caregiver job applicants who are in the provinces of Pangasinan, Cebu, and South Cotabato. A POEA accredited recruitment agency with offices in the said provinces is currently accepting caregiver job applicants who are qualified for  Caregiver Job Abroad Singapore . The said caregiver job abroad Singapore has 50 vacancies to filled by applicants who have the following qualifications: 
 
 Female 
 25-40 years old 
 At least vocational diploma/short course certificate 
 At least 2 years experience 
 With TESDA Certificate is an advantage 
 
 If you are qualified and interested in applying for the said Caregiver Job Abroad Singapore, please visit the office of D A Rodrigo International Inc. and submit your comprehensive resume together with the following: 
 
 High School Diploma 
 Valid Passport 
 
 For more details or inquiries about the said caregiver job abroad Singapore, please get in touch with D A Rodrigo International Inc. by means of their telephone or mobile numbers we have provided below.
