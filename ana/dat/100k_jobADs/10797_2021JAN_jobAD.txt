 The Seminole Tribe of Florida is looking for a Payroll Supervisor. A Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting, Bookkeeping, Finance, Human Resources, or a related field is required. A minimum of four (4) years of previous experience working with centralized automated payroll processing systems or similarly related financial accounting field to include some leadership or supervisory-level payroll processing experience is required; or any equivalent combination of relevant education, training and experience will be considered. American Payroll Association (APA) certification is preferred. Prior experience in Kronos and Lawson System is highly preferred. For more information, please contact Joshua Lohn, Recruitment Manager, at 954 966-6300, ext. 11138.
