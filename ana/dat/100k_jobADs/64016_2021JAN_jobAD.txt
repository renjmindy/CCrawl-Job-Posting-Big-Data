 Are you passionate about content and social media? Do you have hands on experience organising and promoting digital content and Social Media across a range of platforms? 
 If so, an opportunity for a talented Senior Content Executive has arisen to work on a Permanent basis based in the centre of Birmingham (with flexibility). Offering a competitive salary of up to 28,000. You will lead the social and content strategy as well as lead a small digital team of 2. 
 
 What You'll do… 
 
 Work across multiple Social Media platforms - owning projects from conception through to delivery. 
 Deliver exciting and engaging content marketing. 
 Write and produce impeccable copy and engaging online and offline content to help drive organic growth and user experience. 
 Lead a Digital Marketing Executive and PPC Executive 
 
 
 What You'll Need… 
 
 Highly focused background within Social Media and Content 
 Experience of producing content to deliver outstanding results and achieving targets. 
 Skilled in analytical tools to improve campaign outcomes. 
 Experience of leading small teams would be advantageous.
