 Retail Sales Associate - Hays 
 Apply for the position, Retail Sales Associate - Hays, with Nex-Tech Wireless, LLC today or contact Lisa Pierce for more information. Posted Jun 23, 2020. 
  Apply Online  __    785-621-3612  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 Looking for  Something Different ?  At Nex-Tech Wireless, we’re always working to bring the latest technology to the communities we serve.  Join our hometown team as a  Retail Sales Associate  and have the opportunity to earn over $16.00 per hour including commissions! 
 We are seeking customer-oriented candidates to: 
 
 Promote and sell Nex-Tech Wireless products and services 
 Build and maintain relationships with new and existing customers 
 Educate customers on wireless products, services, and promotions 
 Build relationships through community engagement 
 Resolve product and service issues and determine customer needs and best solutions 
 Conduct follow-up calls to ensure continued customer satisfaction 
 
 Required Skills and Qualifications: 
 
 One year of customer service, telephone sales, or equivalent work experience 
 High School Diploma or equivalent 
 Customer-focused attitude 
 Ability to organize and handle multiple tasks 
 Strong communication and problem-solving skills 
 Availability to work evenings, weekends, and some holidays 
 
 We proudly offer: 
 
 Competitive pay and opportunity for monthly commission 
 A fun work environment 
 401(k) Retirement Plan with Company Match 
 Health, Dental, and Vision Insurance 
 Life, Dependent Life, and AD&D Insurance 
 Short and Long-term Disability Insurance 
 Generous Paid Time Off 
 Employee Discounts 
 
 How to apply:  Submit an online application at  https://www.nex-techwireless.com/job-application  and send a current resume and cover letter to  [email protected] .  Nex-Tech Wireless is an EEO employer.
