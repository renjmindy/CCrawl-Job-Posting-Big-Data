 Calgary - Business Development Specialist 
 **  BIS Training Solutions ** is a fast-paced, high growth organization providing leading software for the safety industry. We are expanding our team and seeking an experienced, full-time, business development specialist for the Calgary region, who has a proven track record of success in generating sales results and managing key client accounts. 
 The successful applicant will be responsible for profitably growing our client base in Southern Alberta and abroad, through the development, execution, and follow through of effective sales strategies that foster development of positive long-term client relationships. 
 The ideal candidate for this position will: 
 
 Have excellent English written and oral communication skills 
 Possess a natural ability to generate leads and secure new accounts 
 Excel at making presentations to mid and senior level executives 
 Love meeting new people and have a natural ability to quickly develop rapport with strangers 
 Thrive on developing and fostering long-term positive client partnerships 
 Be extremely outgoing, charismatic, positive, enthusiastic, self-motivated, and professional 
 Be able to travel to attend tradeshows, make sales presentations, and provide client care 
 Work effectively remotely from home or client locations 
 Quickly and easily overcome client objections and rejections 
 Be an exceptional listener with the ability to quickly and accurately determine client needs and identify effective solutions for their resolution 
 Be extremely tech savvy and comfortable with constantly changing technology 
 
 The successful candidate will be responsible for: 
 
 Developing a comprehensive knowledge of BIS products and programs 
 Effectively prospecting and qualifying potential clients to support long-term business growth 
 Generating, managing, and converting high-potential sales leads procured through a variety of mediums, including but not limited to tradeshows, social media, website submissions, mail campaigns, networking, SEO campaigns, and sales calls 
 Effectively onboarding new clients and providing exceptional ongoing care to existing ones 
 Working with the management and business development teams to develop and execute successful sales and marketing initiatives in line with corporate values 
 Tracking and reporting on business development efforts and revenue growth 
 Monitoring industry trends to identify and develop new sales and marketing propositions 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 Minimum five years’ proven business development experience 
 Strong computer skills including above average working knowledge of the Microsoft Office suite 
 
 Job Type: Full-time 
 Experience: 
 
 Sales: 5 years (Preferred)
