 Quality Assurance Auditor 
 Aurora Cannabis 
 __British Columbia 
 Apply Now 
 Whistler Medical Marijuana Corp. a wholly owned subsidiary of Aurora Cannabis Inc. is currently recruiting for a Quality Assurance Auditor to join our team for Whistler and Pemberton facilities in BC. The incumbent will report directly to the VP Product Development and Quality Assurance and will be responsible for the following: 
 Responsibilities: 
Assist in Developing and writing SOPs for all areas of the facility ensuring compliance with Cannabis Regulations and GPP/GMP 
Assist with conducting investigations regarding deviations or complaints, including gathering of data, and identification of root cause 
Provide guidance to manufacturing with respect to GPP, GDP, and GMP 
Build and maintain good working relationships with manufacturing to promote openness and compliance 
Verify fulfilled orders, including label verification 
Verify weight of bottled products, including oil 
Create master labels, and verify actual labels against them 
Perform required line clearances during manufacturing operations 
Verify electronic inventory records of dried cannabis, packaged product and live plants 
Perform on floor auditing of records including sanitation, log books, and PBRs 
Perform batch record review for completed PBRs from grow, oil production and bottling 
Perform QA duties for materials management such as release label creation, application, and inventory follow up 
Participate in Self Inspection Process 
Assist QAA and QA Person when necessary 
 Requirements: 
Degree in related science field 
Minimum 3 years’ experience in a related role 
Experience working in a regulated environment, pharmaceuticals, and/or NHP manufacturing considered an asset 
Working knowledge of GMP and GPP as they relate to the Cannabis Regulations 
Ability to understand, follow and mentor production staff regarding SOP compliance 
Excellent writing and math skills 
Strong attention to detail 
Strong organizational skills 
Ability to conduct critical analysis of situations in order to determine root cause of deviations 
Ability to pass HC background check 
Ability to multitask and adapt to an evolving work environment 
 We would like to thank all applicants for their interest but only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
