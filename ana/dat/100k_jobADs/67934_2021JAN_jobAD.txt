 Do you want the rare opportunity to lead an exciting new Project in Yorkshire through from cradle-to-grave? Do you want to work client-side in an in-house development team? 
 If your answer is "Yes, I do" then please read on. 
 Gleeson Built Environment have been retained to appoint an exceptional Construction Project Manager to lead a new commercial and hospitality development scheme in Yorkshire. Working for the client you will join their in-house development team during the embryonic stage of the project to set up and plan this approximately £40mln scheme and see it through to successful completion in 2022. 
 Our client is a well know organisation in the hospitality space who have built an excellent reputation and are market leader in their field. They are strengthening their real estate development team as they move into a new phase of growth, embarking on new building projects in various locations across the country. 
 This project is situated in South Yorkshire and will see a greenfield location prepared and converted into a site that will include a hotel, retail units and significant infrastructure works. Our client is therefore seeking a high calibre Construction Project Manager from the Yorkshire area who can take full responsibility for the delivery of this ground-breaking scheme. 
 As the successful Construction Project Manager you will manage contractors, stakeholders and brand partners throughout the lifespan of the project. You will possess significant building and infrastructure experience having managed large groundworks and infrastructure packages in the past. You will have been responsible for large-scale construction projects from an early stage and have seen them through to operation having worked for a developer, contractor or consultant previously. 
 On offer to the successful candidate is a highly competitive basic salary and benefits package on a project with significant lifespan. You will join an exciting team with huge plans for the future. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
