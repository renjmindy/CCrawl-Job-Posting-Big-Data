 Supervising Social Worker - Islington Council 
 Supervising Social Worker - Islington Council 
Service Care Solutions are currently recruiting for a Supervising Social Worker to join Islington council on a locum basis. 
 Please see below for details. 
 PAY - £28-£32ltd per hour d.o.e. 
HOURS - full time 
 HOW TO APPLY - please send your up to date cv to Jennifer.lynch@servicecare.org.uk or alternatively call 01772 208964 and ask for Jennifer for more information. 
 WHAT YOU NEED 
, Satisfactory Enhanced DBS 
, Active SW England Registration 
, Recognised Social Work qualification 
, Post qualifying experience in a similar field 
, To build effective relationships with the child, young person, family or carers to decide the best way to keep them safe and improve outcomes to ensure child protection is always prioritised 
, Provide support using best evidence direct work, support identified needs, and support children and young people in placements, those being adopted and moving to independence 
, Work with a wide range of professionals, helping them to understand the child and young persons needs and ensuring that these are met in different contexts 
, To provide supervision to carers, individually, in families, facilitating groups and training within the fostering and permanence roles 
, Communicate clearly and sensitively with children and families/carers 
, Produce written case notes and reports which are well argued, focused and jargon-free with sound analysis and rationale for actions 
, Be able to develop a holistic view of family circumstances 
, To have awareness of what abuse indicators are and use this to collate multi-agency information and where necessary confidently lead investigations to protect children 
 Benefits of working with Service Care Solutions 
, A specialist consultant specific to you with a wide range of social work knowledge 
, Excellent and competitive rates of pay 
, An extensive range of exclusive Social Work vacancies available throughout the UK 
, Expert payroll service processed twice per week 
, Frequent job updates sent to you exclusively to meet your requirements 
, £250 bonus offered for referring a friend to any job role 
, Loyalty Bonus Scheme
