 Registered Care Home Manager 
 An exciting job opportunity has arisen for a committed Registered Care Home Manager to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the New Eltham, London area. 
 As Registered Care Home Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This care home is designed for older people requiring dementia nursing and general nursing care. 
 The home offers a supportive environment with comfortable lounge areas. 
 The dedicated team ensure we meet the resident's individual care and social needs 
 
 An ideal Registered Care Home Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Caring & compassionate with an excellent clinical track record
 
 
 
     Passionate about delivering outstanding care to older people
 
 
 
     Excellent organisation & leadership skills
 
 
 
     Effective communication skills
 
 
 
     Flexible, professional and committed to being the best
 
 
 
     Quality driven with a desire to continually improve yourself & our services
 
 
 
 
 The successful  **Registered Care Home Manager will receive an excellent salary up to £60,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role and in return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Market competitive rates of pay
 
 
 
     Specialist dementia training and support from our award-winning, university-accredited 'Creative Minds' programme
 
 
 
     Full library of interactive learning resources from the Aged Care Channel in every home for personal & professional development
 
 
 
     Range of salary sacrifice benefits including childcare vouchers, pension, car leases, gym membership, technology purchase schemes and more
 
 
 
     Online benefits portal with a range of offers and cashback rewards
 
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must have experience of managing a large home
