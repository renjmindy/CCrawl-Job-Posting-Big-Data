 B.J. Parker 
 "Personal coaching and image building techniques to help you identify who you are and what you really want." 
 
 Location:  Chicago, Illinois 
 
 How will clients benefit with you as their coach? 
 
 
 Clients will have a personal cheerleader 
 
 
 Clients will receive an objective ear and open mind 
 
 
 Clients will experience someone who will challenge their faulty objections
 What results have clients reported after working with you? 
 
 
 More clarity on objectives 
 
 
 How to set measurable goals 
 
 
 Less stress 
 
 
 Quantifiable accomplishments 
 
 
 Stronger sense of self - personally and professionally
 What do clients say about working with you? 
 
 
 Intuitive 
 
 
 Professional 
 
 
 Pit Bull 
 
 
 Fun 
 
 
 Honest
 What are 5 strengths you have as a coach? 
 
 
 A plethora of personal experiences 
 
 
 Ability to separate fact from fiction 
 
 
 Coaching expertise with African Americans 
 
 
 Great listener 
 
 
 Varied professional talents and competencies
 What is your coaching philosophy? 
 
 
 
 Everyone benefits when we exhibit our authentic, best selves. Often our talents and competencies become buried in faulty belief systems. God made us to be magnificent examples of human excellence. As a coach, my commitment is to help clients shape appropriate perceptions and remove biases and objections to achieving their goals. 
 
 What types of people do you typically coach? 
 
 
 Small business owners 
 
 
 Professionals in career & life transition
 What is your professional background? 
 
 
 Image and Life Skills Coaching, Media Training, Public Relations Consulting, Human Resource Development, Workplace Training and Development, Public Speaking 
 
 
 Corporate clients have included: Metropolitan Pier & Expostion Authority, McClendon Report, Dwebz Corporation, SBC Ameritech, City Colleges of Chicago, Littler Mendelsohn, Covad Communications, Lee Hecht Harrison, Vernon Williams Architects
