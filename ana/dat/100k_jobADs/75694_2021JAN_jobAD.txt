 3d Content Quality Assurance - Ongoing 
 Published on the November 11, 2020 in Design & Multimedia 
 
 Project 
 Project Insights New 
 
 About this project 
 design-multimedia / 3d-models 
 USD 50 - 100 
 We are looking for a person or a team to quality check 3D content made for us. 
 You would be required to point out and clearly provide feedback on what needs to be changed on 3D content to bring it within expectations from reference photos. 
 You will need an ARKit and ARCore mobile device. 
 It would be about 3hrs per day for 6 weeks. 
 To receive the full brief, you will need to confirm you have the necessary devices and also provide a portfolio showing your attention to detail.
