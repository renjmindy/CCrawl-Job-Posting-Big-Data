 Digital Content and Marketing Campaign Officer (Temp) 
 Are you passionate about videography? Do you have a polished show-reel portfolio? Would you like a career move that allows you to put your creative skills at the heart of your daily responsibilities? Are you immediately available? 
 If so, an opportunity for a talented Digital Content and Marketing Campaign Officer has arisen to work on a temporary contract at a top Education institution in the Midlands. 
 What You'll do… 
 
 Produce highly engaging multimedia user content for a range of target audiences. Including video, illustrations, photography, and audio for college brands. 
 Responsible for pre-production, filming, editing, audio mixing, and mastering to a high standard. 
 Write and produce impeccable copy and engaging online and offline content, Design, and creation of standout info-graphics. 
 Proofreading, writing and editing content, developing user journeys, participating in events, and producing offline content. 
 
 What You'll Need… 
 
 A very clear understanding of what video content is and how to tell internal and external brand stories. 
 Expert user of Adobe Creative Suite software, especially After Effects, Premiere, and other relevant software such as Photoshop, InDesign, and illustrator. 
 Hands-on experience with UX Design. 
 Demonstrable experience of creating and editing video, audio, blog, illustrations, and photography. 
 Skilled in analytical tools to improve campaign outcomes. 
 Degree level qualification in a relevant discipline
