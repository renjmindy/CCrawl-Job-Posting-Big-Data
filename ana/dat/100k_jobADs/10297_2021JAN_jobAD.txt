 How to Make a Successful Career Change Workshop 
 
 Description 
 Additional information 
 
 The  How to Make a Successful Career Change Workshop  is a live, online, real-time, instructor-led workshop that you can attend from the comfort of your home or office. This workshop welcomes and encourages participants to relax from their day to day routine, reflect about what matters most and renew critical aspects of their careers. 
 This workshop is an opportunity to stop and spend time in a peaceful atmosphere, reflecting upon and exploring the important factors governing our everyday experience. 
 ****Who is it for? **** 
 
 Anyone who wants to decide exactly what they want and crystalize answers to the following questions: What do I want to accomplish? Why? What do I want to experience? Who do I want to become? 
 
 ****Essential Information **** 
 
 
 
 Duration: 
 90 Minutes 
 
 
 
 
 Location: 
 live, online instructor led workshop 
 
 
 Time: 
 will be agreed on a case by case basis (once payment has been received) 
 
 
 Includes: 
 Guided reflective writing exercises, topic related mini-resources toolkit. 
 
 
 Maximum number people: 
 One-on-one Workshop 
 
 
 Workshop delivered by: 
 Julieta Timane 
 
 
 Prerequisites: 
 Willingness to change
