 This PE backed organisation has experienced significant growth over the past few years, organically and through acquisition. The business is poised for further growth in 2020. The sector is transforming and embracing technology as it expands into new markets. 
 For this multisite business, I am seeking a qualified Commercial Finance manager to look after four direct reports withing the Commercial team and partner within the local area, giving you exposure to director level stakeholders on a regular basis. This is a high-profile role which will enable you to develop unexplored territory. 
 The role is seeking a robust and effective business partner who is looking to lead a team and drive operational and commercial change. 
 Areas of responsibility include: 
 
 Responsibility for local area management packs, budgets and forecasts for the UK business 
 Building highly effective internal stakeholder relationships 
 Support the FD with acquisitions and mentoring junior team members. 
 
 This is an opportunity to work within a fast growth PE environment, there is enormous scope for career development. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
