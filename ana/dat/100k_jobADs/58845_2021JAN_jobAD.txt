 About One Step Forward: 
 Our educational program seeks talented and experienced tutors in subject areas:  Physics and Chemistry for Grade 12 students . We help both struggling and gifted students reach their full potential by supplementing the instruction they receive in class and guiding them toward study practices and homework help that can help our students achieve their academic goals. 
 At One Step Forward, tutors work with individual students one-on-one, providing in-home and online tutoring options. We pride ourselves on teamwork as our students, tutors, and staff are treated as family while working towards the same goal! Our OSF Team is looking to hire individuals who can not only teach the required material, but to also relate with the students of today! 
 Examples of Tutor Responsibilities: 
 
 
 Meet student(s) at tutoring location (in-home, library, online) 
 
 
 Go over homework, assignment, and/or exam prep in the applicable subject area 
 
 
 Be welcoming, friendly, and outgoing! 
 
 
 **Required Skills & Qualifications: ** 
 
 
 Must have excellent work-communication and time management skills 
 
 
 Must be organized, reliable, and knowledgeable within their respective area of teaching 
 
 
 Able to tutor students in-home and online 
 
 
 Able to create customized educational material catered to the needs of each student 
 
 
 Experience with using Google Classroom & Zoom for online tutoring sessions. 
 
 
 Able to relocate within designated work area 
 
 
 Able to commit to a full school year of tutoring
