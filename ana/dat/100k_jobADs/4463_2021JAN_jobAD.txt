 Project Manager - Financial Services - FCA Regulation project 
 Interim for 6 months 
 Homes based 
 Outside of IR35 - competitive daily rate 
 My client is FTSE 250 financial services business based in the centre of Birmingham. 
 Following the introduction of compulsory regulation in their sector, the business i looking for season project manager to come on board and support the team on all project activities to make sure they are fully FCA compliant in accordance with new regulations. 
 Day to day you will be working with the business to scope out what needs to done and to then design, build and deliver the change programme to include establishing appointed intermediaries in the market place that are also fully FCA compliant. 
 In order to be successful in this role you will have a proven track record in the successful project delivery of financial services product regulatory requirements whether it be insurance products or other fiance related products that have strict FCA regulation. You will ideally be a Prince 2 certified project manager as well and be used to proving all the governance and structure to ensure successful project delivery. 
 Please don't hesitate to apply for the role with your up to date CV. I will endeavour to respond to all relevant applications within 72 hours. Interviews will be held next week via video conference.
