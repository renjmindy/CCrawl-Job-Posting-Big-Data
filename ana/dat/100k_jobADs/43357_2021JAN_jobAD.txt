 Our client is a Metal Works factory producing very high quality internal, external parts and components for luxury super yachts and other bespoke internal and external finishes. They are looking to recruit a Technical production worker! 
 Our client is a Metal Works factory producing very high quality internal, external parts and components for luxury super yachts and other bespoke internal and external finishes. They are looking to recruit a Technical production worker at their factory in Montfoort, near Utrecht, The Netherlands. 
 **What are you going to do?   ** 
 
 
 
 You are responsible for use of all manual hand tools; 
 Operating an CNC machine: cutting metal materials to precise size; 
 Polishing and grinding the materials; 
 Sorting and labelling material 
 Make your own work schedule; 
 Warehouse management; 
 Cleaning workshop. 
 
 The must haves! 
 
 
 
 Must possess a good understanding of general machining practises 
 Must have technical experience with handling power tools, cutting metals; 
 Must have a flexible attitude towards work; 
 Must speak and understand English 
 Must have a good sense of humor! 
 
 We will provide training so the CNC machinery will be your new best friend ??.
