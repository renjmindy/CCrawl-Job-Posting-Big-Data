 Purchase Ledger Clerk - Birmingham - paying up to £23k pro-rata - 25 hours per week 
 Are you an experienced purchase ledger clerk looking for one of those rare part time opportunities? 
 I am working with a well-established and growing business based near Birmingham. This is a great opportunity to join a business that is experiencing high levels of commercial activity. It will be a busy role for a candidate looking to use their Purchase Ledger knowledge within an inclusive, stable team. Commutable from Wolverhampton, Walsall, Sutton Coldfield and Birmingham. 
 Specifically, the role will involve the following tasks: 
 
 Batching, matching and coding invoices 
 Resolution of supplier queries 
 Supplier statement recs 
 Checking GRNIs 
 Posting to correct codes 
 Verification of invoices and credit notes 
 Assisting with the preparation of information for the Finance Manager and other individuals across the organisation 
 Ad hoc admin and other support activities for month end 
 
 The successful individual will be a valued member of this vibrant finance function. 
 The Person 
 The successful person will have good experience in a purchase ledger environment, with a positive and enthusiastic personality, and a strong willingness to offer help and support to a busy team of people. This role will suit someone with a desire to use their purchase ledger knowledge to improve efficiency of processes and procedures, who is used to working in a very high-volume environment. Strong knowledge of Word & Excel, and experience with a large ERP, would be highly advantageous. 
 The Benefits 
 
 Part time hours 20 - 25 hours a week 
 Free on site parking 
 Growing Stable Business
