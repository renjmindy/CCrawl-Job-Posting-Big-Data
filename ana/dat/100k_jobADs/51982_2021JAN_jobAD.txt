 Hot Damn! it's a Full-time job 
 ntwrk is looking for an Ecommerce Photographer in Los Angeles, CA, USA. 
 About 
 At NTWRK, we’re looking for a Ecommerce Photographer to support each NTWRK product launch with ecommerce photography that highlights key details and showcases quality. 
 What You'll Do 
Photograph all products in adherence to categorical style guides 
Develop standardized workflows to improve speed and efficiency 
Work with Creative Manager to catalog all inbound samples 
Demonstrate an exceptionally high level of attention to detail that upholds NTWRK’s creative standards 
Prepare every product by cleaning, steaming, and removing any visible imperfections 
Apply an in-depth knowledge of studio lighting and staging techniques 
Process all imagery using templatized color profiles. Color correct imagery as needed in reference to physical items 
Provide detailed notes and direction to in-house Photo Retoucher as needed 
Demonstrate an ability to work quickly within a fast-paced environment
