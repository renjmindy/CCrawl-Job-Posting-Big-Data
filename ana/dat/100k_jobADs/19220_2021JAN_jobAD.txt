 Frendo Marketing Intern 
 Frendo App is a digital health solution for endometriosis sufferers and those with suspected symptoms. Endometriosis is a chronic illness that affects 1in9 people worldwide. A diagnosis takes on average 8 years, therefore digital diagnostic techniques and tele-health are more important than ever to reduce the delay in diagnosis. 
 Frendo's screening feature is the first step in a sufferer's diagnosis journey, aiming to break the cycle early, giving them a place to assess their symptoms and directing them on to a medical professional more quickly. Through our social network, Frendo App connects users with a community of others and allows them to track and manage their symptoms, offering them personalised insights to improve the management of their illness. 
 Working with our Founder and Head of Growth, you will execute the social media and digital marketing initiatives. You will get ongoing coaching to develop your skills whilst launching the brand to new markets. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 
 Social/Digital media community posting in line with Growth strategy 
 
 
 Produce content and creatives in line with marketing strategy and brand 
 
 
 Execute email campaigns 
 
 
 Drive new user acquisition & app downloads via digital marketing 
 
 
 Input into the development of marketing tools to build growth stack i.e. Google Analytics, Segment, Mailchimp 
 
 
 Participate in brainstorming sessions to test new growth hacking and user acquisition methods. 
 
 
 **  Qualifications & Qualities 
 ** 
 
 
 You are a motivated, ambitious individual and like to be part of an energetic and growing team 
 
 
 You have an entrepreneurial spirit 
 
 
 You like what we do https://www.frendo.co.uk/ and you're eager to be part of our journey 
 
 
 You love thinking outside the box, you're a hard worker 
 
 
 You have working knowledge of Canva, Later, Microsoft Office Suites (Coaching can be provided for other platforms) 
 
 
 You are creative, full of ideas and you are able to put yourself in an audience's shoes 
 
 
 You have strong written and communication skills 
 
 
 About the role 
 
 
 Opportunity to lead to a permanent role in the future 
 
 
 Part-time/Full -time internship with a flexible schedule 
 
 
 This is an opportunity to be part of the growth of a unique MedTech startup and own specific KPIs that then you will be able to detail in your resume 
 
 
 An entrepreneurial working environment with remote work, flexible hours 
 
 
 Based in London or remote 
 
 
 Start date of role: December 2020 
 
 
 To apply please submit your resume to dearbhail@frendo.co.uk and we will be in touch if we believe that you are a good Frendo fit.
