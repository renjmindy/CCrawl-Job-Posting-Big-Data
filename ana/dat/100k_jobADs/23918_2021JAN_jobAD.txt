 Are you an experienced Fundraising & Events Manager looking for a new challenge? Do you have exceptional communication skills, the ability to negotiate and manage a team of Events fundraisers? Are you able to multitask and effectively organise large scale events? If so, we have an opportunity for you. 
 A growing and ambitious Charitable Trust is looking to recruit a talented and driven Fundraising & Events Manager into the central Birmingham team. Offering a competitive salary in the region of 30k and the chance to make a real difference in the lives of others. 
 Working as part of a dynamic fundraising team, this role will be responsible for developing a voluntary income from individuals and organisations through fundraising events, products and partnerships nationally. 
 
 What you'll do… 
 
 Full management of events and challenges. 
 Project management and promotion of fundraising campaigns. 
 Recruiting, organising and inspiring volunteers to help with events. 
 Develop and lead on pitches for new corporate supporters 
 Develop and implement the organisation's fundraising strategy, for sign off by the Head of Charity, to deliver sustainable income growth. 
 Develop and implement innovative fundraising initiatives to secure new income streams. 
 Develop external relationships with potential corporate supporters and donors. 
 
 What you'll need… 
 
 Extensive experience in large scale events. 
 Fundraising experience. 
 Good management skills demonstrating the ability to manage a team. 
 Excellent sales and negotiation abilities and exceptional communication skills. 
 The ability to build and maintain professional relationships. 
 Creative thinking with the ability to solve problems. 
 Excellent project management skills. 
 Open to work occasional weekends and Evenings. 
 
 What you'll get… 
 
 Salary in the region of 30k. 
 Central City location. 
 The chance to work for a good cause and make a real difference to people's lives.
