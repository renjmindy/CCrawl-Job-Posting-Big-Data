 Full-Time Guide 
 Fiskars Hembygdsförening -  FISKARS  , FI 
 Job Description 
 Fiskars Museum searching for the summer, two manual guides duties include customer service at checkout museum, museum shop care, guidance in the museum district and the museum premises comfort and control. 
 Guides are also involved in other tasks such as the Museum of digitization, time travel and workshops. The tour guides require customer service spirit and interest in the history of the transmission of the ironworks. Course of their duties are required to  have Swedish, Finnish and English skills. Other language skills would be an advantage. 
 We appreciate the museum field of study and experience in similar tasks. Working time: 7h 15 min / day (weekdays), 6.5 hours / day (weekends) five days a week (up to 36h 15 min / week). Weekends included in the working agenda of the regular working hours. Salary: private museums in the collective agreement, the two guides are hired for a period 1.6-31.8.2014.
