 Sales Expert 
 Rona 
 __London, ON 
 Apply Now 
 The Sales Expert is responsible for ensuring that every customer has an exceptional experience by suggesting the right products and services necessary to complete their projects. A passion for service and a willingness to develop product knowledge are key components of this role. 
 RESPONSIBILITIES: 
 
 Ensure that every customer experience exceeds the customer’s expectation 
 Proactively provide RONA customers with expertise and assistance 
 Quote projects, follow up on leads and close sales 
 Use computer software to develop deck, fence and garage packages 
 Promote add on sales, product benefits and services such as installation, financing, & deliveries 
 Inform customers of current promotions and financing options (RONA Advantage Card/Air Miles) 
 
 Job Requirements 
 
 Customer service focused with the ability to master and continuously apply the 8 Service Standards 
 Motivated and able to meet and exceed sales targets through ensuring each customer’s needs are satisfied 
 Desire to assist colleagues including through coaching and mentoring Team Members in their development 
 Strong listening and communication skills 
 Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to remain calm and positive in stressful situations 
 Mature, responsible with sound decision making skills 
 Proficient with computers, able to learn new software and processes 
 4-5 years’ relevant previous experience required 
 Construction or home renovation experience an asset 
 
 Work Conditions 
 To support the Contractor's desk.
