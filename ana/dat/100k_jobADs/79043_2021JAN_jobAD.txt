 Member Service Representative Direct Hire 
   
   Workway, Inc.   
 __Website    __@workway 
 As a Member Services Representative, you will focus on new accounts as you provide efficient and courteous customer service, cross-sell products and services and make referrals to other departments when appropriate in order to generate new business.  Your specific duties in this role will include: 
 
 Opening and maintaining accounts offered 
 Preparing and maintaining account paperwork, including signature cards, check orders, transfers and service charges 
 Providing complete account information to clients in a timely manner 
 Counseling customers on various types of investments and how to best reach their financial objectives 
 Accepting and processing deposits and withdrawal on commercial, personal and savings accounts 
 Providing coverage for tellers, as needed 
 
 **Job Requirements  ** 
 As a Member Services Representative, you must be highly organized and detail-oriented with strong analytical and creative problem-solving skills. It is also important that you display excellent verbal and written communication and interpersonal skills and the ability to interact with customers and coworkers in a friendly and professional manner.  Specific qualifications for the position include: 
 
 High school diploma or GED 
 2 years’ experience in a New Accounts position working directly with clients in a bank or credit union 
 Knowledge of New Accounts documentation with different types of personal and business accounts 
 Strong knowledge of bank products and services 
 Ability to use a PC and alpha-numeric keyboard
