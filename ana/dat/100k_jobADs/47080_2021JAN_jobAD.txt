 Senior Product Manager to join an agile and growing team of problem solvers. 
 Role overview 
 About CAIS 
 CAIS is the leading alternative investment platform for financial advisors who seek improved access to and education about alternative investment funds and products. 
 CAIS provides financial advisors with a broad selection of alternative investment strategies including, hedge funds, private equity, private credit, real estate and structured notes, allowing them to capitalize on opportunities and/or withstand ever-changing markets. Additionally, CAIS provides an industry-leading learning system, CAIS IQ, that helps advisors learn faster, remember longer and improve client outcomes. 
 Primary Objectives 
 
 Develop product strategy, goals, roadmap, and requirements for a cross-functional team of engineers, product designers, and analysts working closely to take ideas from conception to launch 
 Use an effective combination of research, data, and product intuition to make timely decisions 
 Deeply understand the "why" behind everything we do and instil that understanding across all levels of the organization 
 Serve as a communication hub to facilitate collaboration and alignment throughout all departments 
 Collaborate with other product managers and designers to ensure the proper delivery of an optimized experience for our clients in all aspects of our product 
 
 Qualifications 
 
 At least 5 years of experience building and shipping technology products at scale 
 A natural, deep curiosity that drives you to learn, investigate, and constantly ask why 
 Curious and data-driven, with an iterative mindset; Can make decisions based on data and intuitions 
 Sense of ownership and a "whatever it takes" attitude towards getting stuff done 
 Comfort with ambiguity, and an instinct for moving quickly 
 Boundless creativity when it comes to generating ideas, but an understanding that execution is what ultimately matters
