 Work at home Jobs you can Start Today 
 Published by  aw1219  on April 20, 2017April 20, 2017 
 We may earn money or products from some of the companies mentioned in this post. 
 Are you anxiously looking to  work from home  without having to wait for a few days, a week, or even longer? The good news is that you don't have to wait. There are several companies that have tasks and gigs that need to be completed every single day. This means that directly after you sign up or apply online, you can begin working. Some companies may even pay you the very  same day  that you start working from home. 
 
 Please keep in mind that you may be required to complete a series of skills or assessment testing or have to take part in some kind of online training before you being working. Make sure that you set aside some time to complete these necessary steps so that you will have a pleasant experience and can complete your work with very few errors or mistakes. This will help you to earn more money in the long run. Try to apply to any job or gigs that you have a desire or skills for that are included in this post below. Remember to visit the  weekly job section  and  Friday freebie pages  on this website for even more jobs that you can start the same day. 
 
 Speechpad 
 
 This is a high-quality transcription company that boasts a quick turn around time, high accuracy, and excellent customer service. Our support staff is available 24 hours a day to assist customers and transcribers at any time. Additionally, we are constantly reviewing our tools available to offer transcribers their best experience. Opportunities for growth within the company are available with a little time and hard work. 
 Best Mark 
 This is another company that needs mystery shoppers to complete various assignments at businesses and establishments near their location as well as phone shops from home. 
 Course Hero 
 Help high school and university students get real answers to their questions as a tutor. Course Hero is looking for Tutor's who are strong in their particular field, whether it's biology or zoology, students are seeking a wide variety of tutors. As a tutor, you can earn an average of $3 for each question answered on Course Hero. This comes out to an hourly wage of between $12-$20/hour. Course Hero tutors earn on average $300/week answering questions when and where they please. Top tutors will make $500 a week! 
 Clickworker 
 Complete freelance gigs and tasks from your smartphone in data categorization, transcription, translation, data collection, writing and so much more. Weekly pay and open worldwide. 
 Content Gather 
 Work from home writing articles or submit previously written articles for Content Gather and get paid within 24 hours. 
 Humanatic 
 You can start working the same day or shortly thereafter as a call reviewer. You will simply listen to recorded calls and sort them into various categories based on how the call was answered or not answered. You can download their app to complete calls from your smartphone if you like. Open to teens and to everyone no matter where you reside. 
 VIPKid 
 VIPKID needs thousands of work-from-home teacher’s to teach English online to kids in China. You can decide when and for how long you want to work from home – and don’t worry, users do not need to know any foreign language or have formal teaching experience. Teachers can earn as much as $22/hour working on the VIPKID platform. 
 The Forum Wheel 
 This is more of a fun gig rather than a work at home job because you will earn money to post on various forums that you are interested in. Daily pay as well. 
 Amazon Mturk 
 You can start working on Hits called human intelligence tasks from Mturk whenever you want including rating articles for Hubpages, transcribing audio files, completing data entry tasks, and more. Please note that you can only work on individual tasks at a time. 
 Literably 
 Work from home transcribing audio files from adults but mostly children who are attempting to read prepared texts. There is no experience required and you can set your own schedule. Pays over $10 per hour with weekly pay. 
 Babbletype 
 Proofreaders, transcribers, and translators can work from home for this company who knows how to spell and use the internet to research terms. Pays weekly via PayPal for all completed work. You will need a computer, good headphones and an internet connection. 
 Usertesting 
 Make $10 as you visit a website or app and speak your thoughts out loud. You can expect to spend up to 20 minutes of your time for each test. Pays within 7 days or less. 
 Whatusersdo 
 Help companies improve their website on tablets, desktop, and mobile devices. You make $5 per test when you give your feedback. 
 UpWork 
 Work on various freelance projects on multiple categories. You can get hired quickly and receive payment via direct deposit, ACH, PayPal, Wire Transfer, Local funds transfer, Payoneer, and Skrill. 
 Cambly 
 Get paid to tutor and chat with students from around the world. No experience is necessary. You can start taking calls in minutes. You can log in and work whenever you want. Pays $10.20 per hour every Monday via PayPal.
