 Senior Quantity Surveyor 
I am working on behalf of a well-established award winning main contractor who is seeking a Senior Quantity Surveyor to work on a major Highways Schemes in Anglia to be based in Bedforshire area. 
 The company is a tier one contractor and has a strong reputation as a great employer. 
 They have a full order book of work across the Infrastructure Industry. 
 Working within the Commercial team you will be part of an energetic and self-motivated environment. 
 Senior Quantity Surveyor Requirements: 
 
 
 Experience working as a quantity surveyor within a main contractor environment is essential. 
 
 
 Project experience within the civils or utilities industry, ideally on highways or earthwork projects. 
 
 
 Previous experience managing or mentoring more junior members of the team 
 
 
 Desire to be part of a team with a positive work ethic in a good office environment. 
 
 
 Strong NEC Contract knowledge 
 
 
 You need to be ambitious and motivated for a next move in your career
