 Job Title:  JobSeeker Recruitment Manager 
 Location:  Austin, Texas strongly preferred 
 Office:  Combination of In-Person and Virtual 
 Organizational Overview 
 WorkMonger is an online job matching service that aims to transform the way social sector organizations and jobseekers find each other, beginning with non-teaching roles in the US education sector. Our goal is to enable better, faster, cheaper hires than alternative hiring solutions and to become the dominant hiring tool and service in the social sector. 
 Position Overview 
 WorkMonger is seeking an entrepreneurial, strategic, highly-organized, and collaborative individual to manage the planning and implementation of a targeted JobSeeker recruitment strategy. This is a full-time, exempt position, reporting directly to the Founder & CEO. 
 Minimum Requirements 
 
 Bachelor’s degree 
 Strong written and oral communication skills 
 Passion for, and experience with, relationship-building 
 Professional experience in the education sector 
 Professional experience in talent recruitment 
 
 Additional Qualifications 
 
 The ability to manage a diverse workload, competing priorities and deadline sensitive projects 
 A commitment to setting and meeting long-term & short-term goals 
 Demonstrated experience in planning, organizing and prioritizing using measurable outcomes 
 Strong sales orientation with an interest in building and maintaining relationships and managing across diverse stakeholder groups 
 Ability to contribute to the development and support of a positive team culture 
 A deep commitment to shared success 
 A high level of professionalism, customer service orientation and attention to detail 
 A desire to innovate and support an entrepreneurial organization 
 Ability to adapt, think critically, and work in a constantly-evolving environment 
 Technical expertise in Microsoft Office Suite, cloud-based systems and database management 
 
 Personal Characteristics: 
 
 A deep passion and commitment to making a positive impact in the world 
 A belief that every individual is uniquely gifted and has something of value to contribute to society 
 An excitement at the opportunity to build WorkMonger from the ground up 
 Hard working, enthusiastic team player with a “can do” attitude and a willingness to roll up their sleeves and do whatever it takes to support organization priorities 
 Independent, organized self-starter and problem-solver who takes the initiative to identify and assist in solving challenges both within and beyond areas of direct responsibility 
 Ability to maintain a professional demeanor, positive attitude and the highest ethical standards at all times, especially during high-pressure periods 
 Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment with ambiguity and shifts in scope of duties 
 Willingness to occasionally travel 
 
 Responsibilities: 
 JobSeeker Recruitment & Talent Pipeline Development: 
 
 Develop and execute on a recruitment strategy to bring a diverse group of talented candidates into WorkMonger’s JobSeeker database in collaboration with the WorkMonger team 
 Identify and optimize new, innovative talent pipelines and partnerships 
 Work closely with those involved in Employer and Job recruitment to ensure that the JobSeeker pipeline and Employer/Job pipeline are aligned 
 Represent WorkMonger at conferences, recruiting events, networking opportunities, etc in order to generate excitement for WorkMonger among prospective JobSeekers 
 Cultivate leads to ensure “lead to JobSeeker” conversion in alignment with WorkMonger goals 
 Focus recruitment efforts on strategies that will produce the greatest number of quality, aligned applicants for the fewest dollars spent 
 
 __ JobSeeker Engagement: 
 
 Engage JobSeekers throughout the matching process to ensure JobSeeker retention while working towards an eventual hire 
 Develop and execute a strategy, system and schedule of touch points with JobSeekers, including mass emails, newsletters, online content, etc. 
 Solicit feedback from JobSeekers to ensure we are providing the highest-quality service possible at each and every step of the process 
 Support WorkMonger’s profile process to ensure it is streamlined, provides for the most enjoyable profile completion process, and maximizes JobSeeker sign-ups 
 
 Additional Responsibilities: 
 WorkMonger is a start-up organization that is, consequently, in a time of constant change. As such, it is highly likely that the JobSeeker Recruitment Manager's responsibilities will grow, shift, or evolve frequently. In addition, due to WorkMonger’s small team, there will be times when miscellaneous work needs to be accomplished and is assigned to the JobSeeker Recruitment Manager despite falling outside of his/her core duties. WorkMonger employees are encouraged to be flexible and responsive to all changes in scope of duties. 
 Organizational Strategy 
 
 Serve as a thought partner in all parts of WorkMonger’s strategy 
 
 Compensation 
 
 Full-time, salaried position 
 As a start-up, WorkMonger does not yet offer benefits. We will support you in the process of securing your own.
