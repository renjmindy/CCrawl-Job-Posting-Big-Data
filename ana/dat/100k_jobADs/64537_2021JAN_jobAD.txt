 Director of Institutional Research 
 Apply for the position, Director of Institutional Research, with Barton Community College today or contact Mary Jane Felke for more information. Posted Jan 14, 2021. 
 [ Apply Online  __](To apply go to: jobs.bartonccc.edu  Persons with hearing or speech impairment please use the Kansas Relay Service at 1-800-766-3777 or dial 711.EEO/AA.)   (620)-792-9388  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 Barton Community College  is seeking applications for Director of Institutional Research.  This position has primary responsibility of providing leadership and motivation to accomplish the College’s overall planning efforts through the implementation of the College’s strategic goals, objectives and actions. Also, to provide the technological institutional research infrastructure to support the Learning, Organizational Culture, Knowledge Management and Planning and Decision Making systems of the college.   Two to five years of related director-level experience; experience or training in the collection, analysis, and presentation of research data, with excellent applied research skills.   A Bachelor’s degree is required; a Master’s degree is preferred. Review of completed applications begins immediately.
