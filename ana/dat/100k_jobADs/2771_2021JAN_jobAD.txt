 English teachers needed in Vietnam 
 
 
 
 | 
 English teachers needed in Vietnam 
 Public Language Centre 
 | 
 Viet Nam 
 Hanoi 
 | 
 1300-1600  USD  /  Month 
 
 
 
 Apply 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Apply 
 Your position will be assigned based on experience and expertise. Teachers are provided with texts, pacing guides, flashcards, and other resources to help them teach their students. You will teach kids between 6 and 10 years old. 
 Full-time teaching positions are 5 days per week. The 3 weekday (Monday-Friday) shifts will be scheduled from 5:30pm-9:30pm while both weekend shifts will be scheduled between the hours of 8:00am-6:00pm, with guaranteed hours of 70 hours per month. During June and July (Summer School), classes are scheduled during day-time hours Monday-Friday as well. 
 Roles and Responsibilities: 
  Teaching the provided syllabus using ESL methods 
  Creating and administering engaging speaking activities 
  Administering mid-term and final tests 
  Participating in monthly training workshops and meetings 
  Attending parent-teacher meetings annually 
  Regular communication with our office 
  Participating in retention activities (Halloween, Christmas etc.) 
 Required/Desired Qualifications: 
  A CELTA, TESOL, Trinity or equivalent TEFL Teaching Certificate* 
  Experience teaching IELTS 
  A BA/BSc degree in an academic subject 
  A clean Police Clearance Check 
  Must be a native English speaker or equivalent with evidence of high proficiency 
  Verifiable references** 
  Prefer in-classroom ESL experience 
 
 Online only TEFL certifications will not be accepted. You must have a TEFL with teaching practicum as part of the course 
 
 ** For non-native speakers, references must cover at least 3 years ESL teaching outside of Vietnam 
 Salary & Benefits: 
  70 guaranteed hours of teaching per month (after probation) 
  Base gross salaries range from 30-33 million VND minimum. Most teachers teach a few more hours, earning 33-37 million VND 
  End-of-contract bonus 
  Visa-Run flight reimbursement for Local Hires of up to 4,200,000 VND (payable after completing Probation) and Initial Entry flight for Overseas Hires up to 12,600,000 VND (payable on contract completion) 
  Airport pickup and 10 days and initial hotel accommodation for Overseas Hires of approx. 4,200,000 VND 
  Severance payment - 1⁄2 month per year of service 
  Paid Annual Leave and Sick Leave (up to 19 days) 
  Vietnamese National Holidays, Christmas Day and International New Years Day (approx 11 days per calendar year) 
  Issuance of Vietnamese State Medical card 
  Full In-Patient, Partial Out-Patient, Evacuation and Repatriation Medical Insurance for which the employer pays 1⁄2 of the annual premium 
  Visa and Work Permit sponsorship fully provided and paid for by the employer (except for document authentication) 
  Direct deposit for payroll on the same day each month 
  Assistance in setting up a local bank account with an international bank 
  Welfare team support, with 24/7 emergency assistance 
  Ongoing training in a group setting 
  Intensive induction program 
  Professional development opportunities
