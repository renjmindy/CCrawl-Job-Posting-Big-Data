 Quality Assurance Coordinator 
 Aurora Cannabis 
 __Leduc, AB 
 Apply Now 
 Aurora Cannabis Inc. is a world-renowned integrated cannabis company with an industry-leading reputation for continuously elevating and setting the global cannabis industry standard. 
 Through our wholly owned subsidiaries, strategic investments, and global partnerships, Aurora provides a wide range of premium quality cannabis and hemp products and services, develops innovative technologies, promotes cannabis consumer health and wellness, and delivers an exceptional customer experience across all its brands. 
 Aurora’s operations span multiple continents and focuses on both the medical and recreational cannabis production and sales, patient education and clinic counselling services, home hydroponic cultivation, extraction technologies and delivery systems, and hemp-based food health products. 
 We operate around the globe pursuing new and emerging cannabis markets where possible through our owned network of import, export and wholesale distributors, our e-commerce and mobile applications, brick and mortar retail stores, as well as grocery store retailers, and pharmacies. 
 QUALITY ASSURANCE COORDINATOR 
 Aurora Cannabis Inc. is currently recruiting for a Quality Assurance Coordinator to join our Air Facility, located in Leduc, AB. The incumbent will report directly to the Manager, Quality Assurance – Edibles (West), and will be responsible for the following: 
 KEY RESPONSIBILITIES: 
Represent QA as an ambassador for regulatory compliance with respect to GMP, Cannabis Regulations and company SOPs 
 Complete production batch record review and all required follow-up 
 Complete documentation review for manufacturing records, logbooks, and all required follow-up 
 Assist with investigation of non-conformances leading to identification of the root cause 
 Assigning and implementing effective CAPA to promote continuous improvement 
 Assist with the Production Planning to ensure batches and runs allocated for shipping have been released and all associated documentation has been reviewed and closed out 
 Work closely with Production and Quality Assurance Auditors to ensure facility compliance is maintained 
 Assist in the development of training programs and perform training sessions for personnel as required 
 Assist with the implementation and continuous improvement of quality procedures 
 Assist in drafting and writing SOPs that comply with Cannabis Regulations and GMP 
 Assist with the tracking of metrics around compliance and efficiency and recommend measures to improve productivity and product quality 
 Participate in both internal and external audits as required 
 Assist QA Associate, QA Supervisors and QA Manager as required 
 KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES: 
Understanding of Quality Management Systems including batch record review, batch release, document control, change control, deviations, investigations and CAPA 
 Fluency in MS Office is required 
 Competency of technical writing (SOPs, Annual Reports, Investigation Summary) 
 Ability to work independently, with a strong attention to detail 
 Ability to multitask and adapt to an evolving environment with changing priorities 
 Effective verbal and written communication in English 
 Experience using ERP systems is an asset. 
 EXPERIENCE AND EDUCATION: 
Bachelor of Science or diploma in a science-related field 
 1-2 years’ experience in the Quality Assurance field 
 Experience working in a regulated environment, pharmaceuticals, or natural health product manufacturing environment is preferred 
 We would like to thank all applicants for their interest but only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
