 We are a dynamic digital business based in Central London with all the benefits of an agile and fast-growing start-up with internal funding to provide stability. 
 We are now looking for a Lead Software Developer to join our team to help us with the rapid growth we are experiencing.  Leading our team of software developers, currently 4 full time, 4 outsourced. Plans are in place for expanding this team. This role reports directly into our CTO. 
 fu3e. is made up of all sorts of people - from data scientists and real estate business consultants, to rugby players, cyclists, climbers, software engineers, designers and many more. 
 If you are looking for a new challenge, we'd love to hear from you. 
 Key responsibilities. 
 Your daily tasks will include ensuring that yourself and the team carry out: 
 
 Creation of new system functionality 
 Amendments to existing features and bug fixes, utilizing the accompanying notes or design documents 
 Research and development of proof of concepts 
 Structure code in a modern fashion, utilising OO concepts and structured documentation 
 Writing thorough automated tests for your code 
 Develop and maintain the code base of our project management system, fu3e 
 Maintain the architecture & security of fu3e 
 Peer-review and testing codebase from other software developers 
 Produce database exports for special reports 
 Provide estimations for new features and bug fixes 
 Assist in the recruitment and on-boarding of software developers and QA personnel
