 Contracts Coordinator 
 Job description 
 - Monday to Friday 
- Immediate Start 
- Bowen based, in the Whitsundays QLD 
 About the Role 
We are currently looking for an experienced Contracts Coordinator for a 12 month contract, for a Rail Company - opportunity for contract extensions or permanent roles may apply for the right candidate! 
 Desired Experience & Competencies 
- Minimum 4 years' experience in a Contracts Coordinator 
- Contract management ensuring that value is realised, and deliverables are provided on 
schedule. 
- Ensuring existing and new contracts are executed to negotiated contract T's & C's 
- Strategic planning and sourcing processes through to execution to deliver fit for purpose 
- goods and services that represent value for money within timeframe and budget. 
- Strong oral and written communication; and interpersonal skills 
- Excellent organisational and time management skills 
- Proven ability to work effectively independently or as a team member 
- Highly motivated with ability to use initiative 
- Ability to maintain confidentiality of tasks and business information 
 Key Deliverables 
- Strong technical experience in developing and executing commercial sourcing strategies, 
preferable within a rail operations and maintenance business. 
- Minimum of 2-5 years' experience in relevant commercial or procurement discipline 
- Tertiary qualifications in business, commerce, economics, or related discipline 
- Sound understanding of risk management practices and commercial law 
- Strong stakeholder engagement, interpersonal and facilitation skills 
 Culture & Benefits 
- Opportunity to work with a reputable company 
- Ongoing work for the right Candidate 
- Weekly wages 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
