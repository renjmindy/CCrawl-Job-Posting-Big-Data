 Family Therapist 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Family Therapist to work in an exceptional hospital based in the Hatfield, Hertfordshire area. 
 As the Family Therapist you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This hospital specialises in treating children and young people who are diagnosed with an eating disorder and require inpatient/residential, day-care or outpatient care 
 As the Family Therapist your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     To provide family therapy as an autonomous professional at a highly specialist level employing evidence-based approaches
 
 
 
     Undertake a specialist psychological and systemic assessment of children and families
 
 
 
     To create and deliver, participate in and sustain highly specialised therapeutic interventions with groups of children/families and professionals
 
 
 
     The provision of highly specialist one-to-one clinical consultations to other clinical staff on the use of family therapy and systemic psychotherapy techniques
 
 
 
     Work alongside other team members in the provision of individual and group-based programmes
 
 
 
     Initiate and participate in evaluating systemic approaches so that the effects go beyond individual cases and improve the effective running of that service for the individuals, children, young people and/or their families
 
 
 
 **The successful Family Therapist will receive an excellent salary from £42,000 to £48,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     The equivalent of 33 days annual leave - plus your birthday off!
 
 
 
     Free meals and parking
 
 
 
     Wellbeing support and activities
 
 
 
     Career development and training
 
 
 
     Pension contribution
 
 
 
     Life Assurance
 
 
 
     Enhanced Maternity Package
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be UKCP registered and hold an AFT membership
