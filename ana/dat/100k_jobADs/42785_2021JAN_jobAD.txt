 Kuwait Job Caregiver 
 Posted on  December 19, 2012  by  caregiver 
 A POEA accredited recruitment agency with offices in Manila, Kuwait, and Singapore, is now accepting qualified caregiver job applicants for placement in  Kuwait Job Caregiver . The said Kuwait job caregiver has 60 vacancies to be filled by applicants who have the following qualifications: 
 
 Female 
 25-35 years old 
 At least Bachelor's/College Degree 
 Minimum of 5 years experience as a Caregiver/Volunteer Nurse 
 Working experience in a Home Care environment 
 Experience in GCC Countries is a definite advantage 
 With pleasing personality 
 Able to understand and communicate in English 
 
 All qualified and interested applicants for the said Kuwait Job Caregiver may apply in person to A & C International Resources Inc. by submitting 2 copies of the requirements listed below in a long white folder. Or email your resume to apply@anc-ph.com. 
 
 Resume 
 Employment Certificates 
 Training Certificates 
 Diploma 
 Passport 
 NBI
