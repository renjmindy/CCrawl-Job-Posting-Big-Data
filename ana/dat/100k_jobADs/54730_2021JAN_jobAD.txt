 Clinical Lead Nurse 
 Nurse Seekers are currently proud to be recruiting a Clinical Lead nurse to work for a well respected nursing care provider based in the Wallsend area. 
 My clients nursing and residential care home is perfectly located near the city of Newcastle, with great access to the local shops and amenities with excellent public transport links. 
 The home offers the highest standard of care across all areas and caters for Elderly residents, Dementia as well as Younger Adults. 
 The home and the group recognises the importance of delivering person-centred care, delivered by well-trained and supported staff with the aim of bringing out the best in everybody. 
 They are now looking to expand their dedicated team with an enthusiastic, experienced and ambitious Clinical Lead Nurse. 
 The successful candidate will ideally be an RGN or RMN Qualified Nurse with experience in a care home setting, experience in in a senior role is preferred but not essential. 
 You will need to possess excellent communication skills and a commitment to delivering a high standard of care to the residents. 
 You must have a genuine passion to offer care to the elderly and have a positive outlook and can-do attitude. 
 You will be responsible for attending to the physical and emotional care needs of residents, promoting a holistic, person-centred approach. 
 Directly reporting to the Home Manager, as the Lead Nurse, you will be accountable for your team, making sure that they are providing the highest standard of care and nursing expected of a Nurse across all aspects of the role. 
 In return for your hard work my client is offering an excellent rate of pay with great staff benefits. 
 A long with this you will receive on going, in-house training. 
 Fantastic career opportunities will be given to you to expand and develop your own career within a forward thinking, modern healthcare provider.
