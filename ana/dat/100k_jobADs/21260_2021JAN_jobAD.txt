 Amica is a friendship app for women travelling solo. Our mission is to empower women to feel secure during their solo travels while creating meaningful connections with like-minded travellers. 
 We believe that Amica will transform the way that women travel. We are excited to welcome a passionate Head of Product into the early-stages of our journey. Our app is currently in BETA and we are looking for someone with a strategic eye for product development to launch, grow and scale the Amica App. 
 Remote working for the foreseeable future; no preferred location of applicant. 
 Role: 
 
 Translating business requirements to technical specifications. 
 Leading on establishing the product vision, product roadmap and launch plan. 
 Actively involved in defining success and product OKRs. 
 Ensuring development and deployment are completed to time and budget. 
 Oversee UI/UX to maintain a high-quality product. 
 Gathering and actioning technical requirements for new features and third-party integrations. 
 Linking business development and monetisation strategies into the product. 
 Growing in a culture of constant improvement and evolution. 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 Expertise in leading App design and development projects. 
 Experience in overseeing UI/UX for mobile applications. 
 Knowledge of front-end and back-end development, with the ability to work effectively with a software development team. 
 Experience in working on affiliate programmes. 
 Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills. 
 Ability to independently lead projects, while enjoying collaboration in a team environment. 
 Strong attention to detail and desire to constantly improve and optimise product. 
 Very strong communication skills; ability to convey complex concepts to variety of audiences. 
 Excited about being an integral part of a product that will enable and encourage women to travel solo. 
 
 Compensation: 
 This role is currently offered as an equity position. Once investment is secured (Feb-March 2021), the role will be offered as a full-time equity + salary position. 
 If this sounds like the role for you, please do apply! We can't wait to hear from you.
