 Master Class on Job Hunting Strategy 
 For STEM Majors 
 The job market is getting more and more competitive, especially for STEM majors and specialized roles. Each open role gets hundreds of CVs from candidates from some of the best schools in the world, so how do you stand out? 
 This masterclass partnered with  HKSTP , gave tips and insights from  Sho Dewan , lead  career coach  and founder of Workhap. Sho helped students deep dive into exactly what the recruiters and hiring managers are looking for in their ideal candidate. 
 In this webinar, students learn how to: 
 
 STAND OUT in a competitive job market 
 Identify their target companies and roles 
 Think like a Hiring manager 
 Leverage LinkedIn to attract recruiters
