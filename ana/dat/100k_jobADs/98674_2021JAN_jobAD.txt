 An exciting opportunity has arisen for an experienced Interim HR Business Partner to work in this large Manufacturing organisation, for a 12 month FTC. Salary £50-52k + Car Allowance. Based Staffordshire. 
 As Interim HR Business Partner, you will partner with specific Manufacturing Business Units, and the SMT within those areas. This will include developing strong Union relations, managing change, employee relations, engagement, development and succession planning. 
 CIPD qualified or equivalent, to be successful for the role you will have extensive experience of working in large-scale, or matrix structured Manufacturing or Logistics businesses, within a HR Business Partnering structure. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
