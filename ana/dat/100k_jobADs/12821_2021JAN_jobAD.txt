 Management Accountant 
 
 Location 
 
 Lichfield, Staffordshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £35000.00 - £40000.00 per annum + Benefits including flexible hours

 Tom Soten

 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently working with a national brand retailer who are recruiting for a qualified accountant (ACA,ACCA,CIMA) to join there group finance function as a Management Accountant. 
 This organisation is a household name and has performed exceptionally well during the Covid pandemic and so offers a stable and secure role for the successful individual. The role also offers a good deal of progression and the role has become available due to an internal promotion 
 Key Duties, Management Accountant, Lichfield, £40,000 
 
 Month end timetable and close management 
 Reconciliation of sub-ledgers to general ledger 
 Reconciliation of general ledger balance sheet accounts, including bank reconciliations and accurate status of balances 
 Process journals 
 Claim forms and overriders 
 Review of trial balance 
 Review of financial results 
 Close general ledger 
 Preparation and review of management accounts 
 Executive reporting 
 External reporting 
 
 Key Skills & Experiences, Management Accountant, Lichfield, £40,000 
 
 Qualified Accountant (ACA,ACCA,CIMA) 
 Ability to work collaboratively with cross functional colleagues and colleagues across Financial Control and the rest of the business 
 Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written 
 Excellent attention to detail both in the preparation and reviewing of data 
 Must have prepared complex management accounts using a variety of both system and manual processes 
 Able to challenge information and processes effectively 
 
 This is a fantastic opportunity for candidate to join a stable business based in Lichfield that offers fantastic progression opportunities as well as good work life balance 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
