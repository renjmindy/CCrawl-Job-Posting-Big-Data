 Chemistry Tutor 
 Sylvan Learning Centre 
 __Calgary, AB 
 Apply Now 
 Interested in helping students achieve academic success? If so, consider a tutor position with Sylvan Learning Centre in Northwest Calgary. Sylvan is recruiting individuals who can tutor high school chemistry. Tutors work with students in a 2:1 setting in centre and 1:1 for online sessions, providing individualized instruction to each student. Tutoring is currently being offered to students 4-8 pm weekdays and daytime hours on weekends. 
 Successful applicants should have a degree with a major or minor in chemistry or be in the last year of the degree program. The person who best qualifies has a positive attitude, works well with students of all ages, and is an effective motivator.
