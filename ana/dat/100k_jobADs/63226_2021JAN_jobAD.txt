 Jo-Anne Harrison

 joharrison@workwithglee.com

 Published: 
 
 4 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-11-04 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 My client, a private equity backed business with an exciting growth plan requires a Management Accountant to join their established finance team at their offices in Newbury. 
 Reporting into the Financial Controller you will take on a broad and varied role that will suit a part-qualified ACCA/CIMA studier who has gained strong accounting experience in their career to date. You should also possess the ability to identify process improvements where appropriate. 
 This will be an office-based role (although working from home is currently supported). You will enjoy a hands-on role where you will be responsible for: 
 
 Month-end close & Reporting 
 Payroll 
 VAT returns, reconciliations and submissions 
 Fixed Asset Register Management 
 Intercompany and Revenue Recognition 
 Support in forecasting and annual budget process 
 Support in the annual year-end audit 
 Recommend, develop and implement process and systems improvements 
 Ad-hoc project work 
 
 Experience Required: 
 
 Minimum of 2 years of management accounting experience gained with extensive experience gained in the profit & loss and the balance sheet. 
 You will possess strong systems skills including intermediate to advanced excel 
 You will be part-qualified within the ACCA/CIMA qualification with at least 2-3 years of accounting experience. 
 You will be a self-motivated and hard-working individual with great attention to detail 
 You will be a true team player with a personable nature 
 
 You will enjoy working in a truly supportive and team orientated organisation who supports its staff and ensures they are able to develop and progress in line with the needs of the business. You will receive a comprehensive package which also includes study support. 
 For further information regarding this opportunity please don't hesitate to contact me. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
