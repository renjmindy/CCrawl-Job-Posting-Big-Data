 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for April 2010 
 Posted on  May 14, 2010  by  caregiver 
 For the month of April 2010, the POEA approved several job orders that will provide employment opportunities to Filipinos who will qualify for caregiver jobs abroad. The said POEA approved caregiver jobs are for the different POEA accredited recruitment agencies and carries a total of 101 caregiver jobs vacancies for the country of Israel, 50 caregiver jobs vacancies for the U.S., and 5 vacancies for the beautiful Middle East country of U.A.E. Specified below is the list of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for April 2010 which includes the name of the recruiting agencies and the number of applicants to be selected for each POEA approved caregiver jobs. 
 Israel --- Star World Int'l Manpower & Placement Agency, Inc. -- 4/6/2010 -- 11 
Israel --- JAO International Manpower Services ----------------- 4/8/2010 -- 27 
Israel --- Mariposa International Services Co. Inc. --------------- 4/8/2010 -- 16 
Israel --- International Progress, Inc. ---------------------------- 4/12/2010 -- 21 
Israel --- Sunace International Management Services Inc. ------- 4/16/2010 -- 15 
U.S ----- Oriental Placement Center Inc. ------------------------- 4/20/2010 -- 50 
U.A.E. -- Sulyap Inc. --------------------------------------------- 4/20/2010 -- 5 
Israel --- CPM Manpower Services, Inc. -------------------------- 4/26/2010 -- 11 
 To apply for any of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for April, submit your application directly to the office of the recruiting agency. For more info on the requirements and qualifications, you can contact the recruiting agency’s landline numbers or email address which will be provided upon request.
