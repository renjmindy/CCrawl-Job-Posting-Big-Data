 YourTour 
 
 Website:  virtual-visits.co.uk 
 
 YourTour is an innovative,  well-funded  and ambitious company dedicated to digitally replicating the spatial and emotional engagement of an in-person experience. We work closely with universities in the UK to develop cutting-edge technologies and bring these to market. Our products currently range from a suite of desktop, mobile and web applications that allow our users to create, share and consume 360° video 'virtual visits'. 
 Our team of engineers work on deep, ground-breaking innovations, part-funded by the UK Government in the form of Innovate UK grants. 
 Our customers range from heritage to property to education. You'll have the opportunity to work at the edge of research and innovation, whilst at the same time delivering production-ready products to our customers, including Oculus. 
 
 Opportunity: incredible opportunity to push your limits, develop significant experience by owning and delivering mission critical features within our product range. The role is not limited to just software development, you will also be involved in product, strategy and design. 
 
 
 Salary: £36-£51k depending on experience, plus share options 
 
 
 Experience: 3-5 years as professional software developer (we expect you can jump in and contribute on our web-apps immediately) 
 
 
 Contract: 6 months Full Time Contract with possibility for extension, UK residents only due to the nature of our funding and the role is remote. 
 
 What we are looking for 
 You are a talented and motivated frontend developer who thrives on developing innovative and easy to use web applications. You are independent, proactive, and not afraid to try new technologies or to seek out new solutions. You are committed to delivering excellent user experience and great quality products. 
 What you'll do 
 The role is primarily frontend-focused but you will also contribute to other parts of the product life cycle such as backend development, dev ops. 
 
 Design and build a modern, scalable, distributed cloud-based application using cutting-edge technologies 
 Work on the full life cycle, turning an initial brief into production-ready code 
 Make technology decisions and contribute to product strategy 
 Write clean, performant, maintainable and well-tested software that has few regressions 
 Address issues related to performance, scalability, and reliability 
 Ensure the codebase meets cross-browser, accessibility and high-speed requirements 
 The first project will involve in refactoring and improving our web 360 video tour player and to add support for VR in our Player. 
 
 Essential 
 
 Proven experience working extensively with JavaScript 
 Good understanding of web fundamentals, HTML and CSS 
 Experience applying development best practices and patterns in relation to security, scalability and performance 
 Good understanding of frontend technologies such as Angular/Vue/React 
 Experienced in interfacing with API backend 
 Experience with task runners and package managers (such as npm, yarn, webpack and Grunt) 
 Experience with unit testing 
 Experience with version control systems (Git) 
 A very keen eye for detail 
 Can demonstrate having developed / created complex web-app(s) 
 Eligible to work in the UK and registered as a UK tax payer 
 
 Our Stack 
 
 Frontend: JavaScript, HTML & CSS [open to you bringing a framework into our stack such as React, Vue or AngularJS] 
 Backend: .NET 
 DevOps: Azure 
 
 How to apply 
 Email CV and covering letter  including links to some of the work you have done (essential that you can jump in and start contributing with minimal training)
