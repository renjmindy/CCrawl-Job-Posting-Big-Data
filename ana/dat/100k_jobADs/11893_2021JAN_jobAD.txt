 Why work at WORKNET? 
 Utilize your passion. Provide compassionate care. Work with like-minded, collaborative colleagues. Strengthen your knowledge through education. Career growth. Give back to your community. Benefits and work/life balance that provide an exceptional employee experience. That’s our  why.  See below for available opportunities and apply today to join our team. 
 Medical providers – Pennsylvania & New Jersey 
 WORKNET Occupational Medicine is always scouting for skilled professionals to join our team including: full-time and/or part-time physicians, nurse practitioners, and physician’s assistants in the southeastern and central Pennsylvania regions as well as southern New Jersey. We emphasize quality medical care and provide the highest level service to our patients and their employers. Office hours are Monday through Friday, nights or weekends. 
 We offer competitive salary and benefits for full-time employees including medical, dental and vision plans, flexible spending plan, medical malpractice, life and disability insurances, 401(k) with employer match, paid vacation, time and reimbursement for CME. Experience in primary care or emergency medicine a plus for all positions. Occupational medicine experience preferred. Please send CV to:  [email protected]
