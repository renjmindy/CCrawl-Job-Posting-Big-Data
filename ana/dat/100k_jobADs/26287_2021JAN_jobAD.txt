 Sweet Baby Jason! it's a Freelance job 
 revelation is looking for a Freelance Video Producer in New York, NY, USA. 
 About 
 Revelation is seeking a proactive, creative, and passionate Freelance Video Producer to work on dynamic social-first content. The Freelancer Video Producer will be the central point person in managing every stage of the video production process. Working closely with the Content Manager and Social Media Manager, the ideal candidate will be successful in developing content that is on brand, innovative, and elevates the business and marketing goals of our clients in mainly beauty and lifestyle categories. 
 Duties include: 
-Developing overall concepts, strategy, key messaging, and direction for short and long-form video content 
-Managing talent and logistics for remote and in-studio content creation 
-Creating scripts, shot lists, video briefs, and copy for omni-channel videos 
-Provide clear, actionable, and timely feedback to in-house and freelance video editors 
-Overseeing production timelines to ensure all deadlines are met 
-Collaborating with social and editorial teams for creative ideation and asset optimization/distribution 
-Analyzing video and social data to inform strategy to support an “always optimizing” approach 
-Pulling clear and relevant references to share with talent/clients 
-Researching industry trends on all social media platforms and forming actionable plans accordingly 
-Sourcing royalty free audio tracks to enhance video quality and platform optimization 
 Requirements: 
-Ability to multi-task and meet tight deadlines 
-Deep understanding of social media platforms, particularly TikTok, YouTube and Instagram 
-Video editing or graphic design skills a plus 
-Clear communication and direction when working with talent 
-Ability to respond to feedback well and implement into future tasks 
-Must be a team player, and be flexible when meeting needs of clients 
-Availability to work at least 75 hours per month 
 To be considered please include: 
-Resume 
-Cover letter 
-Link to website/portfolio or samples (PDF also accepted)
