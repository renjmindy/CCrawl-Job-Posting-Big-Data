 Description 
 Looking for a CDL Class A Driver for our Mobile Mini delivery division in LaCrosse, KS.  Some of these loads will cross state lines of NE, OK, TX Panhandle but are primarily based in western/southwestern/northwest Kansas. 
 **  Flexible -Part Time Position - This is not a daily position but is flexible to work with other part time positions or for a retired individual not wanting to commit to a 40 hour week. Would be most suitable for someone living within 30- 60 miles of LaCrosse, KS. 
** 
 
 Driver will be expected to drive a semi with trailer weighing greater than 26,000lbs 
 Driver can expect to be home nightly and weekends . 
 Must have current CDL license with medical card. 
 Must have clean MVR and be able to pass drug testing.
