 Interim Finance Director - London - £600/£800 Per day LTD - 6 months 
 My London based recruitment client with an International footprint is looking for an Interim Finance Director to lead their business through a period of significant change. Ultimately the successful candidate will support with the return to business as usual and subsequent permanent back-fill. As a candidate you will need experience of the recruitment sector, have led teams through change, have experience of an owner managed or private equity backed environment as well as decent exposure to M&A, ideally in an international setting. 
 Interim Finance Director - Role in Brief: 
 
 Responsibility for company wide budgeting and forecasting 
 Strategy planning 
 Interpret MI and add commentary for the board 
 Monitor cash flow, accounts, and other financial transactions 
 Take a leading role in M&A activity 
 Team management (Finance, operations, payroll, invoicing, data etc..) 
 Team development and hiring responsibility 
 Prepare monthly and annual reports 
 Minimise financial risk and exposure 
 Liaise with auditors 
 Create and maintain relationships with the banks 
 Update and implement financial policies and procedures 
 Maintain a policy manual for the finance department 
 Ensure that all financial practices are in line in statutory regulations and legislation 
 
 Interim Finance Director - The Candidate: 
 
 ACA, ACCA, CIMA qualified or equivalent 
 7+ years post qualification experience 
 Experience of a growth environment (SME) 
 Experience of leading through change 
 M&A 
 Raising funds 
 leadership skills 
 Exceptional communication skills, both written and verbal 
 Excellent attention to detail 
 System skills: Sage, Excel and QuickBooks 
 
 The role of Interim Finance Director is highly confidential no additional information can be released until the client has positively vetted your CV and a non-disclosure has been filed.
