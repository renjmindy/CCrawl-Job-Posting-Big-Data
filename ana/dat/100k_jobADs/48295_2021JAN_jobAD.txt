 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently supporting a long standing and growing business based in Birmingham City centre to recruit for a Management Accountant. 
 The purpose of the role is to provide accurate and timely management and financial information to senior stakeholders. You will have accountability for providing key financial indicators on the businesses UK performance. 
 This Management Accountant role is a full time permanent role. Interviews will be initially held over video interview and work from home is currently in place. The salary on offer is up to £36k per annum depending on experience. 
 Management Accountant duties include: (Full Job Description available) 
 
 Management reporting - preparing mid-monthly forecasts, variance analysis and reporting to assist drive profitability. 
 Month end preparation, review, and submission 
 Preparation budgets 
 Balance Sheet Reconciliation 
 Preparation of journals, accruals, and prepayments 
 Preparation of Sales Forecasts 
 VAT Returns 
 Develop Financial and operations processes 
 Drive improvement of forecasting and month end processes 
 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
