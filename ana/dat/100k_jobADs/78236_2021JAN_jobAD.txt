 JOB SEARCH AFTER A JOB LOSS – VIDEO 
 The Job Search After A Job Loss Video  utilizes The Ten Steps To A Job Search format to assist viewers in organizing good job search efforts, perfecting networking skills, honing interviewing tips and techniques and instructing in the creation of a professional resume.

 Description 
 
 Description 
 The importance of a Job seekers’ efforts in locating that perfect job is covered in this video. The job seeker needs to feel confident and accomplished when they present themselves to a potential employer. They need to go to that interview equipped with every tool possible. 
 Viewers are assisted in every aspect of job searching.  The Job Search After A Job Loss  utilizes the Ten Steps To A Job Search format consisting of various types of suggestions offered to help one keep track of job search activities. Also discussed in this video is a short presentation on interviewing as well as resume writing.
