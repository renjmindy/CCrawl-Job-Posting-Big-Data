 We are re-imagining the future of asset heavy industries such as construction, government, and manufacturing by building products that solve organisations' most complex challenges. 
 Launched in 2019, DMRC is working with well-known clients in the UK to deliver truly transformational solutions. At DMRC, we identify opportunities at the intersection of technology, innovation, and design, but do not stop there. We develop our own software solutions to solve the most critical problems our clients face and make them accessible to the wider market. 
 About you: 
 We are now looking for a Rockstar Architect/Lead Developer to lead our product portfolio and work with our team and development partners to continue developing and supporting our products as we scale them. 
 You must be an entrepreneurial, self-starter that wants to build products that make a difference. You will work alongside our technology consultants to continue the development of our apps, building new features, optimising performance, and developing new solutions that we plan to take to market   (hands on development is a must) . 
 What we 're looking for: 
 Technical skills 
 
 Demonstratable experience developing, optimising enterprise grade applications. 
 Architecture:   Modular Monolith Architecture, Microservice, Domain Driven Design Architecture, multi-tenancy 
 Front End:   JavaScript, React, Angular JS 
 Back End:   ASP.NET Zero/ASP.NET Core, ABO.io, Node.js, Microservices 
 
 Soft skills 
 
 Good English skills with the ability to communicate clearly with teams and clients 
 You work with minimal supervision and thrive in a flexible environment 
 Strong communication skills and ability work collaboratively in a team to find solutions to problems 
 You proactively raise concerns and opinions in a constructive manner that helps the team to work better 
 You think out-of-the box to innovate and find new solutions 
 You are honest and transparent in everything you do 
 A results-oriented, high energy, and focused approach to your work 
 You always communicate professionally with clients and colleagues 
 You continuously meet milestones and deadlines 
 You find ways to add value in everything you do 
 
 What you will get from us 
 
 Autonomy to design and develop cutting edge products 
 A collaborative environment to experiment with new technologies 
 Career progression and opportunities to develop your skills 
 Exposure to a wide variety of projects 
 An output focused work environment and flexible hours 
 No bureaucracy or politics 
 No legacy systems
