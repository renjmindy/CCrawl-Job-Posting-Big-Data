 **Company description 
** EQUITY-X is an equity valuation software provider based in Level39. EQUITY X LTD. won the first prize at Startup Grind Europe 2017, executed a data agreement with S&P Global in 2018 to unlock its potential to grow globally and announced the providership with BT Radianz for global secure distribution in capital markets last September. EQUITY X LTD. is gaining traction and actively looking for a web designer to create the company websites from scratch. 
 **Job description 
** EQUITY-X requires a web designer to design mobile and desktop websites and work with developers to ensure the design quality in the websites so that we can shape the contents to attract finance professionals as clients and obtain quality leads from the company website. We need a custom layout to be designed as well as the creation of info graphics and imagery to support the website branding and overall experience. The ideal candidate should have extensive experience in responsive design and the ability to define the architecture of information of the website. It will be a major advantage if the candidate has experience in HTML, CSS and any content management system. 
 Term: 
- 1 month (or 2 months) 
 Specific duties: 
- Drawing up detailed website specifications; 
- Defining and designing the information architecture; 
- Synthesising website goals and designing content layouts; 
- Suggesting ideal contents which are suitable for B-to-B business for financial institutions; 
- Creating website layouts and organising information for easy consumption; 
- Taking the lead on the user experience of the website; 
- Conceptualizing original web design ideas that bring simplicity and user-friendliness to complex subject matter, and optimizing landing pages; 
- Reusing branding materials as well as product look; 
- Creating mockups, prototypes and user flows; 
- Mocking up page layouts to validate text size, colours and layouts; 
- Digital retouching and image editing; 
- Designing the website's visual imagery and ensuring it is in line with branding policies; 
- Designing infographics to support information and ideas on the website; 
- Designing a fully responsive website solution; 
- Ensuring legibility and quality is carried across all viewports; 
- Meeting relevant legal requirements such as freedom of information and privacy; 
- Working closely with a front-end developer to help shape the user interface interactions; 
- Working closely with a front-end developer to convert design into functional and creative CSS; 
- Testing the website and identify any issues related to website designs; 
- Handing the completed website design and its digital assets to us; and 
- Working remotely (temporarily due to COVID-19) and reporting to CEO on a daily basis. 
 **Key requirements: 
** - 3+ years of experience in web design 
- Team player 
- Passion and enthusiasm for elegant, aesthetic, and dynamic computer graphics 
- Knowledge of HTML and CSS 
- Proficiency in designing interfaces using design tools such as InVision and Zeplin 
- Portfolio which demostrates your ability to create beautiful web designs 
- Experience designing the mobile and desktop websites for FinTechs and/or financial institutions 
- Experience working with web developers 
- Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design or related 
 **Nice to have: 
** - Internationally recognized awards 
- Critical thinking and problem-solving skills 
- Good time-management skills 
- Good interpersonal and communication skills 
- Web development experience (writing HTML, CSS and JavaScript) 
- Experience with content management systems 
- Search engine optimisation experience 
- Proven track record of web design with great conversion rates
