 We look for humble, self-starter engineers with a strong eye for detail. 
 Role overview 
 We have a varied stack and our core application is built in Clojure. We are building microservices on top of this and we work with the latest technologies such as Postgres, Docker and AWS to name a few. Our tech team organises itself in an organic and efficient way to work, whilst each member takes a lot of responsibility for the end to end delivery of a core service/feature. 
 Responsibilities   : 
 
 Building a low latency, high availability and scalable global platform 
 Creation of new Microservices/Event Driven Architecture across the business 
 Globally scalable systems for capturing and tracking in all things related to property management. 
 Proactively engaging in code review, pair programming sessions and continuously contribute to well documented and styled code.. 
 
 Requirements   : 
 
 You have at least 1 year production experience building products in Clojure and any two of Ruby, Python, JavaScript, Java, Scala, Go, C/C++ or C# 
 You thrive in a TDD/BDD agile culture 
 You love an environment focused on good Engineering practice, code reviews and regular pairing 
 You are always happy to collaborate with others, guiding and mentoring where needed 
 You have worked on full cycle product development and you are not afraid of deployment 
 You are an avid problem solver and have experience in systems design and architecture 
 You are well experienced in communicating efficiently and effectively as part of full remote teams. 
 
 Conditions  : 
 
 Flexible working hours, but minimum of 35 per week. 
 Evolving role with growth 
 Perks and bonuses and potential to try new technologies 
 Tickets to conferences
