 Do you have a background in supporting SQL based reporting tools? Would you like to be a part of a fast developing area, integrated into a business that has experienced year on year growth? 
 I am working with a UK based PLC in the Birmingham area that are on the lookout for a Junior Systems Administrator to join their Finance team. 
 You will be responsible for maintaining and developing systems that contain accurate data across the business, liaising with third parties and work across small change projects. 
 In order to be successful you will have the following - 
 
 Background in access database and system administration 
 Data management skills with understanding of SQL 
 Passion and interest in technology and tools (such as Power BI etc) 
 Can do attitude with good analytical and problem solving ability 
 
 Please apply here with your CV.
