 Forensic ADHOC RMN 
 We are currently recruiting for a ADHOC Registered Mental Health Nurse to work within Forensic/Secure settings within the Private sector in Woking. 
 The positions are Band 5/ Band 6, with uplifted rates of pay for nights and weekends. 
 This is an excellent opportunity for anyone looking for full time shifts around family, education, other work or as an opportunity for flexible part-time employment. 
 The Limited company hourly rates of pay are as follows: 
Days: £23 ltd per hour 
Nights- £24 ltd per hour 
Weekends- £25 ltd per hour 
 You will be working within forensic wards in the private sector. 
 Aiding the recovery of patient's monitoring their well being and assisting with on-going treatment. 
 You may be required to move/escort patients and provide personal care for some higher dependency patients. 
 Essential: 
-Recent experience working as a Registered Nurse in the mental health sector 
-Proof of Nursing Qualifications and NMC 
-Must be able to engage with patients/clients 
-Flexible 
-NMC Pin in date 
 Desirable: 
 -Experience of working within Secure Units 
 The benefits of Service Care Solutions: 
 
 
 Twice weekly payroll runs 
 
 
 £250 training allowance 
 
 
 Excellent pay rates 
 
 
 up to £250 referral bonuses
