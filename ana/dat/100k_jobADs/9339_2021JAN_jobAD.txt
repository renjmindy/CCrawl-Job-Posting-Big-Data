 Job Description 
 |   At Matrix Providers - You come first. Always. 
 Your success   is the focus   of our entire business. 
 **We are hiring for a qualified civilian  **  Neurodiagnostic Technologist /   Electroencephalographic Technologist (EEG Tech) to join our team of talented professionals who provide health care services to our veterans and their families at Joint Base Lewis-McChord in Tacoma, Washington. 
 Benefits: 
 • Status: Full-Time 
 • Schedule: Monday - Friday 7:30am - 4:30pm 
 • Competitive financial package with a comprehensive insurance package including health, dental, vision, and life coverage 
 • Accrued Paid Time Off (PTO) & Paid Holidays 
 • 401(k) Plan 
 The Neurodiagnostic Technologist / EEG Technologist must have the following qualifications: 
 • Degree: Certificate 
 **• Education: Complete certification in Neurodiagnostic / **  Electroencephalographic T   echnology 
 • Certifications: Must possess and maintain current course completion documentation for basic life support (BLS) through an American Heart Association approved training program and BLS shall be renewed every two years 
 • Board Certification: Certified by the American Board of Registration of Electroencephalographic and Evoked Potential Technologists (ABRET) 
 **• Experience: One year of experience as an **  Neurodiagnostic Technologist / EEG Technologist 
 **•  **  Licensure- Current, active from any state 
 • Due to contractual obligations with the DoD, Health Care Workers performing under this contract must either be a U.S. citizen, or as defined by the Department of Homeland Security, U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services, either a noncitizen national of the United State, lawful permanent resident of the United States, or alien authorized to work in the United States 
 You will love being part of the Matrix family of Providers whose focus since day one has been selflessly giving back to our hard-working and devoted military servicemen and women. Matrix Providers has always been a proud veteran-owned company and military family supporter and is a staunch ally of our active-duty and veteran service providers. 
 Matrix Providers is an equal opportunity employer. Qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability or protected veteran status.
