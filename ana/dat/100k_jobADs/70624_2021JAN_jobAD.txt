 Role - Financial Reporting Analyst 
 Location - Wolverhampton 
 Salary - £34000-£39,000 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group are supporting a high profile business with the appointment of a permanent Financial & Reporting Analyst based in Wolverhampton 
 The Opportunity 
 This role presents the opportunity to join an amazing business which is renowned for diversity, inclusion and innovation. 
 The position of Financial & Reporting Analyst will report to an exceptional Finance Manager that has a proven track record of developing and advancing the careers of aspiring finance professionals. Furthermore, it will also have direct access to the finance leadership team including the Head of Finance & Chief Financial Officer providing fantastic visibility & exposure to the entire organisation. 
 The role could be described as a hybrid role which will support the core financial reporting & month-end process, whilst also being involved in producing a several high value & business critical internal commercial reports to support the Directorate team and Board. 
 On Offer 
 Core Financial Reporting & Month-End Duties: 
 
 Balance sheet reconciliations 
 External audit preparation 
 Internal audit preparation 
 Accruals, journals and prepayments 
 Bank reconciliations 
 Reviewing bank payments 
 
 Commercial Reporting Duties: 
 
 Preparation of monthly reporting packs - P&L, cost centre analysis, revenue & margin analysis 
 Variance analysis 
 Budgeting 
 Forecasting 
 Payroll analysis - overtime, remuneration, gender pay gap etc. 
 
 To be considered for this role, you must have the following experience: 
 
 Part qualified or finalist level 
 Strong financial reporting and analytical skills 
 Payroll analysis experience would be highly advantageous 
 Intermediate to advanced user of Excel 
 Strong verbal and written communication skills 
 
 Please do not hesitate to contact Tom Flavell for a private and confidential discussion regarding this opportunity. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
