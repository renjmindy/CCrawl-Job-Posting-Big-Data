 A UK based PLC in Birmingham, who are going through an exciting technology overhaul to improve their BI function and platform are looking for a Business Intelligence Architect to join their team. 
 Hoping to change the way they consume and improve real time data, which will in turn will aid better business critical decisions, the Business Intelligence Architect will be responsible for the architectural design, development and management of data across the structure, along with maintaining the data warehouse. 
 The successful candidate will have the following: 
 
 Experience in Microsoft BI Stack such as SSIS, SSRS and Power BI 
 Track record of providing architectural designs to create business solutions including ETL services 
 Background in working in an Agile environment, adopting devops structure 
 Experience of working with Microsoft Azure 
 Liaise with project stakeholders to define requirements and solutions for the BI capability 
 Willingness to put customers first and exceed their expectations 
 An open mind towards change 
 
 Please apply here with your CV or contact Keeley Goodall for further information.
