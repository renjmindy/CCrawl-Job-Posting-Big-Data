 Unity & Graphics Engineer 
 Rara Apply for this Job 
 Posted 
 November 23, 2020 
 Contract 
 Anywhere 
 Remote 
 We're looking for an experienced Unity and Graphics Engineer to join our small and mighty engineering team for a 4 month contract with the opportunity to convert to full time after.  ‍ 
 ‍ 
 Who we are?  ‍ 
 We're Rara - a well funded startup with a mission to make game creation accessible to everyone. Game creation is hard. We don't think it should be. We are creating a new kind of game engine that lets anyone build, share and play games entirely through their phone. 
 Video games will be the most important artistic medium of our time. We are on a mission to empower a new generation of creators to express themselves through games. 
 You can read  our values   here   ‍ . 
 ‍ 
 Responsibilities  ‍ 
 
 Design and implement core game systems from start to finish 
 Wear many hats as a skilled technical generalist who will contribute across areas 
 Pitch ideas, experiment rapidly, fail quickly and learn fast 
 Write clean, performant, well-documented code 
 Rapid prototyping of ideas 
 
 Requirements 
 
 5+ years of experience with Unity 
 Deep knowledge of Unity and C# 
 Built and shipped games in the past 
 Experience with VFX and graphics 
 Comfortable with git and version control 
 Strong written and verbal communication skills 
 Passionate about usability and user experience 
 Driven, passionate, self-starting, creative problem solver 
 
 Bonus points! 
 
 Experience with mobile focused development 
 Experience with multiplayer and networking 
 Experience with building social features 
 Ready to join asap  ‍ 
 
 ‍ 
 Why should you apply?  ‍ 
 We are well-funded and backed by top notch investors from the tech and gaming industry. 
 You’ll work alongside amazing team members who have worked on category defining games like  Subnautica ,  Terraria ,  Universim ,  Rise of Industry  and more. 
 As the 5th team member, you'll be a core part of the founding team and get significant say over the future and direction of the product. 
 You can work from anywhere. We are remote-first, our team is currently distributed across 4 continents. 
 All full-time members get equity in the company.
