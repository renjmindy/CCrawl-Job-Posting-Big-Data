 Job description 
 
 
 ONGOING ROLE 
 
 
 GERALDTON LOCATION 
 
 
 COMMENCING ON 29/01/2020 
 
 
 About the Role: 
 WorkPac is currently seeking a General Hand for a Waste Company in Geraldton. The role will consist of operating on a sort line, general yard maintenance, and operating a forklift moving bails of cardboard around the station. The pay rate is $30.43 plus penalties an hour working Monday - Friday 6am- 2pm. This role will be commencing on 29/01/2020. 
 Skills and Experience: 
 
 
 Forklift Ticket 
 
 
 General Labouring experience 
 
 
 Right to Work in Australia 
 
 
 Must be able to pass a pre-employment medical and D&A; 
 
 
 About WorkPac: 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Looking after our employees is important to us. Through our myrewards platform, we offer benefits from retail rewards through to financial, health cover, insurance, superannuation services and more.
