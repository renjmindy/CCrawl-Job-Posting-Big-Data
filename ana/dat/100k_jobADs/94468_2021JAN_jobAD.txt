 My tech-driven client - through growth and success are expanding their Data team and looking to recruit a Support Analyst. 
 Focused on driving improvement across the business, you will be responsible for analysing data, designing and developing data pipelines and data modelling. 
 This a great opportunity due to the hybrid nature of the position - not only will you be providing visualisations and dashboards, but you will also be supporting database/warehouse and support issues across the application and data platforms. 
 In order to be successful in this role you will have the following 
 
 Expert use in SQL - writing queries 
 Supporting and developing ETL data pipelines 
 Data analysis experience and visualisation - Power BI 
 SSRS, SSIS, T-SQL 
 Understanding/Background in front end applications - Python/React JS
