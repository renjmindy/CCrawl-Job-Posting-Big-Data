 WHO WE ARE:  At Horseshoe Resort, we are a team that loves to serve people, priding ourselves on the service that our colleagues deliver to our guests. Horseshoe Resort is always looking for talented individuals to join our growing team of hospitality professionals and recreation enthusiasts. If you are outgoing, friendly and love adventure and the outdoors, you may be the person we are looking for! 
 Position Summary 
 The Snowmaker is responsible for efficient operation of snowmaking machinery and equipment. 
 Duties and Responsibilities: 
Complete all snowmaking operations and other tasks as assigned 
Equipment operation 
Some mechanical maintenance and welding 
Placing protective padding around snowmaking hydrants, lift towers, etc. 
 Qualifications: 
Previous snowmaking experience is an asset 
Must be 18 years or older 
Valid driver’s license 
Snow mobile operation an asset 
Must be able to work rotating shifts, evenings, overnights and weekends 
Physically able to lift and carry up to 50lbs 
Able to work in inclement weather conditions
