 ServiceNow Engineer - £250 per day - OUTSIDE IR35 - Birmingham 
 Birmingham based client are migrating a legacy system to ServiceNow system and requires someone for a short term contract to make sure this runs smoothly. This a hands on role plus dealing with a to a 3rd party vendor so wouldn't suit a consultant or a release manager. 
 You will be required to work independently as the main point of contact with ServiceNow in the business. This would suit someone who has been involved in working with ServiceNow on a migration or an implementation project. 
 The project is for 3 months but might extend depending on the scale of the project good contract for a business doing a number of exciting projects within IT. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
