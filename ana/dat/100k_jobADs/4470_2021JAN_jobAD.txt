 Donations Collector, Langford and Colwood area 
 Diabetes Canada 
 __Langford, BC 
 Apply Now 
 Picking up and delivering clothing and small household items from Diabetes Canada donation bins that are placed out in the community. 
 Who are you? 
 You might be a handyman, labourer, contractor, sports club, small business owner, student, semi retired. 
 Duties include: 
 Empty donation bins on a regular basis (daily if required) 
Bag loose items as required (bags supplied) 
Ensure cleanliness around the donation boxes 
Complete drop box log sheets 
Transport goods to storage facility 
Meet Diabetes Canada Driver to load goods on truck 
Skills and Requirements: 
 Only applicants who own or have access to a pick-up truck, truck or van will be considered 
Must have a valid Driver’s license 
Able to lift up to 50 lbs 
Must have or be willing to get an HST number 
Hours and Compensation 
 Self employed contractor position so hours are flexible. 
Compensation varies depending on volume of donations collected and delivered 
 Expected start date: 2020-10-28 
 Job Types: Full-time, Part-time, Freelance 
 Salary: Up to $39,596.00 per year 
 Work remotely:
