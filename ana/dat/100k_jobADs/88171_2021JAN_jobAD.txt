 Experienced Operators 
 Job description 
 Single & Multi Skilled Operators 
 
 
 Work 7/7 Roster Straight Days / Nights 
 
 
 12 Hour Shifts 
 
 
 DIDO Near Moura ( Biloela) 
 
 
 About the Client 
 Our Client has a 80-year mining history, they deliver the full suite of mine services. We challenge ideas, create solutions and manage performance to help our clients achieve best-practice resource recovery, increase productivity and optimise cash flow. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac currently have a number of opportunities for experienced Dump Truck or Multi Skilled Operators to commence work immediately. This work could start as early as next week. You will form part of and contribute to the Mine Operations Department of a Bowen Basin Mine 
 An absolute commitment to working safely, complying with all site safety standards and ensuring a safe and environmentally healthy workplace is essential. It is imperative that you possess the ability and aptitude to maintain high standards of safety, equipment serviceability, housekeeping and compliance with mine working procedures. 
 You will need to have at least 12-18 months experience operating Dump trucks , or Dump Trucks and hold 2 other operating skills and /or Excavators. They hold the following equipment on site : 
 
 
 Excavators: Hitachi 5500, Leibherr R994 
 
 
 Dozers: KOMATSU 375/ CAT D10, D11 
 
 
 Graders: CAT 16 
 
 
 Loaders: CAT 992 
 
 
 Trucks: CAT 789 
 
 
 Skills/Experience 
 
 
 Dump Truck Operators - Min CAT 789ll, must have at least 18-24 months operating experience 
 
 
 Excavator Operators (300T plus experience) - Hitachi 5500, Leibherr R994 Skills Required 
 
 
 Multi Skilled Operators Truck Plus Two Other Skills Including; Grader (CAT 16), Dozer (Komatsu 375, Dozer CAT D10, D11), Loader 
 
 
 Trainer Assessors - Require Cert 4 TAE and Operators Skills 
 
 
 MUST Have SSE/Delegate Signed Skills List 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Current Coal Board Medical or willing to Obtain 
 
 
 Current Standard 11 
 
 
 If Successful you will be required to attend a pre employment medical including drug and alcohol testing 
 Benefits 
 
 
 Work/Life Balance - Even Time Roster 
 
 
 Camp and Meals Provided 
 
 
 BIBO Camp to Site 
 
 
 Immediate Start
