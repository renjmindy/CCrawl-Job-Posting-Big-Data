 Sweet Baby Jason! it's a Full-time job 
 You're probably not one to take a job just to make your friends jealous, but in case you are, Book of the Month is hiring a Creative Operations Project Manager in New York, NY, USA. 
 About 
 We are looking for a Project Manager to to make our creative and marketing pipeline fly! Key attributes include a love of detailed project planning, coordinating cross-functional tasks, and delivering time against tight deadlines. 
 The role: 
 Track and manage creative projects through concepting, approvals, and distribution of final assets to ensure accurate and timely execution of projects. 
Serve as lead contact point for marketing and creative projects -- attend team meetings, set expectations, provide updates, and solve problems. 
Help manage creative resources play air traffic controller to increase efficiency and quality of the team's output. 
Bring a positive, solution-oriented mindset to tackling problems and help unify and motivate colleagues. 
Optimize and improve our processes over time. 
Source and manage external creative vendor relationships (designers, agencies, photographers, producers). 
 Key requirements: 
 4+ years of project management in the creative space 
Strong organizational and problem solving skills 
Understanding of how creative teams work 
Ability to work on multiple projects, fast timelines and with different personalities 
Master of problem solving and being proactive 
Content production, understanding of adobe suite a plus
