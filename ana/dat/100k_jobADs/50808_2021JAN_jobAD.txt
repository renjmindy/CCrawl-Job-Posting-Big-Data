 Job Offers 
 During its over 25 years as a real estate com­pany, VIB Vermögen AG can look back on con­tinuous corporate growth. Since our stock market listing, we have achieved a firmly es­ta­blished dimension within a pan-European comparison. 
 With a clear focus on high yield commercial properties, we are constantly seeking individuals who aim to work within a dynamic growth environment and who wish to capitalise on career opportunities. As a reliable and forward-looking company, we can offer you very varied professional opportunities in return. 
 If you are interested in working together with us on the sustainable growth of our company, then we would very much like to meet you for a discussion. 
 Please apply to us for the following job op­por­tu­ni­ty. If you are generally interested in wor­king for us, and are currently unable to find a job offer that matches your ideas, then we would also be very pleased to consider your un­soli­cited application 
 Please fill in the  Data Compliance  and together with you application send it to  personalvib-agde .
