 Bottled Water Driver/Bottled Plant Operator 
 Nelson Water Inc. 
 __Ottawa, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Nelson Water Inc., a leader in the water treatment and bottled water delivery business, has an immediate opening for a multi-functional Licensed DZ Driver dispatched from our Warehouse at our facility located in Carp. This is a normal Monday to Friday, days only job with weekends and holidays off. 
 Main duties are to deliver Water Bottles / Coolers to customers on a pre-determined scheduled route in the Ottawa and surrounding area. Assist and provide support to the Warehouse staff when required. Training will be provided. 
 Job Type: Full time- year-round- Monday to Friday-No seasonal layoff. Daytime only-no evenings, no weekends or nights. 
 Salary: Starting at $22.00 per hour plus bonus and commission. 
 Benefits: Health insurance. Vacation pay. Holiday pay. Extended health, medical and dental plan Dental insurance. 
 Requirements: Be physically fit and capable of repeatedly lifting 50 lb. objects. Have a clean driving record with a DZ Driver’s License and be capable of driving both automatic and standard transmission vehicles. Possess a forklift operator’s certificate or equivalent experience.
