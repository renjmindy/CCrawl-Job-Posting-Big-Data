 Welder - Flux Core 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Day Shift 
 
 
 Due to start January 11th 
 
 
 Long-term opportunity for the right people 
 
 
 About the Role 
 Our client is a multi-disciplinary heavy engineering constructor to the oil and gas, metals and minerals, infrastructure and defence sectors. WorkPac are currently seeking Structural and Heavy plate Fluxcore Welders for our client in Henderson to start Monday 11th of January 
 
 
 Dayshift Monday to Friday 6am to 4.30pm & 6 Hours on a Saturday morning 
 
 
 Structural Steel & heavy plate work 
 
 
 Skills and Attributes 
 
 
 Be able to complete a 3G & 4G Weld Test 
 
 
 Committed to a Safe Working Environment 
 
 
 12 months experience in a similar environment 
 
 
 Results orientated 
 
 
 Ability to engage and build relationships with people across the business 
 
 
 Promote teamwork and collaboration 
 
 
 Ability to be flexible and deal with daily challenges 
 
 
 Learning orientation 
 
 
 Experience working in a workshop on heavy plate and heavy pipe 
 
 
 Essential Requirements 
 
 
 Trade Certificate 
 
 
 Overhead crane experience advantageous 
 
 
 C Class Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Right to Work in Australia 
 
 
 Must be able to pass full medical & DAS 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
