 Hot Damn! it's a Full-time job 
 Therabody is looking for a Video Editor  in Los Angeles, CA, USA. 
 About 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 Independently edit all aspects of social media creative, including intro’s, featurettes, social-forward promos, educational/training material, user generated content, mashups and after effects. 
Short-form quick cut fast paced social and mobile first direct response acquisition ads that drive website traffic and lead to sales. 
Create video iterations to test on social media platforms to analyze and increase performance. 
Audit current social accounts as well as new accounts to assess creative needs. 
Evaluate and interpret video performance metrics. 
Research digital ad trends and apply that knowledge to creative decisions. 
Ability to present concise outlines or storyboards to support your thinking, where applicable 
A firm grasp of digital camera formats, frame rates, codecs, compression techniques and file-based workflows 
Motions graphics and animation skills 
Keen eye for detail in sound, pacing and story of edits 
The ability to juggle multiple projects and deadlines 
Proficient online editor: color correction, music supervision and post-production sound mixing 
Knowledge of video camera operation, lighting, and sound recording is a plus 
 The Right Person Would Have: 
 Bachelor’s degree preferred 
4+ years of experience editing creative content for social media platforms. 
4+ years of experience editing in Adobe Premiere Pro. 
Master in Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects 
Proficient in full Adobe Creative Suite 
Experience in RED CINE-X PRO, a plus 
 Portfolio Required with Application 
 Benefits: 
 We offer medical, dental and vision plans, 401k 
All you can eat snacks and beverages 
Open workspace 
Fun, collaborative environment!
