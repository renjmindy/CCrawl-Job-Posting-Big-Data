 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs Israel 
 Posted on  February 11, 2013  by  caregiver 
 Three Job Orders were recently approved by the POEA allowing three licensed recruitment and placement agencies to recruit caregivers for placement in caregiver jobs Israel. These  POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs Israel  has a total of 60 caregiver job vacancies whose Job Orders were approved by the POEA last February 1, 5, and 6, 2013. Listed below are the names of the recruitment agencies and the actual number caregiver job vacancies for each agency. 
 CPM Manpower Services, Inc. -------------------------- 15 
Hopewell Overseas Manpower Network Inc. ------------ 15 
HRD Employment Consultants & Multi-Services, Inc. --- 30 
 Those who are interested in applying for any of the said POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs Israel may submit their resume and other credentials personally to the office of any of the recruiting agencies.
