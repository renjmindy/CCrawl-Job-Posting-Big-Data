 Varun Dhawan started his career in the field of entertainment in 2014. His debut movie got him the most praises, and the film is none other than “Student of the Year.” After that, he has landed many successful films, and his career graph is quite impressive as well. He made his debut with Alia Bhatt and Siddharth Malhotra. Varun Dhawan is born in Mumbai, and his father, David Dhawan, is a very famous Bollywood director. Let us look at his career graph, net worth, and more details about Varun Dhawan. 
 Varun Dhawan Net worth 2020: 
 According to the sources, Varun Dhawan is one of the highest paying actors in Bollywood. His career graph is spectacular, and has his movies have been doing quite desirable in the box office. His Net worth as of 2020 is 29 million, which is 216 crore INR. Per the film, Varun charges between 5- 7 crore rupees. Varun’s Net worth in the year 2021 is estimated to increase up to 35 million dollars. Apart from that, Varun makes a fair amount of money from brand endorsements, stage shows, and events. He is known for his ads about new apps, cars, and so much more where brands pay him millions of rupees. He is also seen as the youth icon by many, and is fan base is undoubtedly massive. 
 Varun Dhawan career: 
 Varun Dhawan made his debut with Karan Johar’s “Student of the year.” He made his debut with Alia Bhatt and Siddharth Malhotra. He has played many roles in his career, and he is also known as the modern Govinda. The reason behind this name is one; he is a massive fan of Govinda, and two, he has acted in many movies like him. He has starred in many successful films like ABCD2, Street Dancer 3D, Kalanak, Badrinath ki Dulhania, Judwaa 2, and many more. He has been recently spotted outside many production houses for meetings. Therefore there are many movies that the fans can expect from Varun in the coming year.
