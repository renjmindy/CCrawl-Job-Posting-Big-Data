 Ron Van Dyke - We Really Need More Inside Jobs - 12 June 2013 
 Posted on  June 12, 2013  by  lucas2012infos  | Comments Off on Ron Van Dyke - We Really Need More Inside Jobs - 12 June 2013 
 Uploaded on 12 June 2013 by  Ron Van Dyke  **  ( Lucas: I am sorry for you Ron this sobbing pity me video I have compassion for. Still expecting things and still searching for things outside of you to come and save you. Where is empowerment to Ron - still waiting!! Find the answer inside . Still picking on others for your inability to see your keep going in circles.  ) _ 
_** 
 I know I cannot speak for everyone; yet I sense that what I need most in my life is to connect more strongly with the Kingdom of Heaven within me. I believe that is what most of us need. I'm not suggesting that we ignore what's going on in the world or deny the reality of the conspiracies to defraud humanity of its birthright. On the contrary, we need to use that awareness to propel us to dig deeper and fly higher, discovering within us a Kingdom that cannot be shaken by whatever goes on around us. Like many, I tend to be blown about by events that either seem like potential answers to prayer or the opposite: more of the same insanity. Yes, I want stability in my world; however, I really need that strength inside of me, not dependent on anything else. I've experienced that in the past; yet as events are unfolding now, I get really anxious, wondering when and how the vision of a world that works for everyone will finally manifest. I need more inside jobs.
