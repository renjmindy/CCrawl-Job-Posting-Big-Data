 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson RG are recruiting for a Financial Controller to join an established US owned Business based in Birmingham. You will be responsible for Reporting and Control requirements in a specific region. 
 Role 
 
 Delivering monthly financial detailed review of reporting for the region 
 Analysis of P&L and Balance Sheet, review of the commentary from the markets, preparation of the commentary of main variances versus the latest forecast 
 Detailed review of budget and quarterly re forecast reporting for region 
 Liaising with parent company in both UK and US on various matters including tax 
 Preparation of the decks used in quarterly re forecast/budget presentations 
 Acting as the main HQ point of contact for financial reporting matters of several operating companies 
 Managing regional Sarbanes Oxley compliance requirements 
 Monitoring of Capex 
 Adhoc reporting/analysis for senior management 
 Manage Finance Team 
 
 Person 
 
 Fully Qualified (ACA, ACCA or CIMA) 
 Background in Audit 
 Advanced excel skills 
 Strong accounting experience specifically, statutory accounting, cost accounting and analytical review, system & process review, exposure to compliance and SOX auditing 
 Experience in group accounts or consolidations 
 
 On Offer 
 Up to £75,000 + package 
 Flexible working 
 Comprehensive bonus
