 AImuno  is a Latvia-based biotech start-up focused on developing an integrative biomarker to distinguish between potential responders and non-responders to checkpoint inhibitor immunotherapy among Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC) stages III and IV patients. 
 We at Aimuno are a team of experts in the fields of AI and bioinformatics with a solid background in developing IT solutions for personalized diagnostics of cancer patients.  AImuno project in immune-oncology was currently selected by Roche and became a part of its Innovation Incubator supported by Roche Latvia and Roche Canada. 
 
 We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist with the following duties and skills: 
 
 Duties: 
 
 Building machine learning models using statistical and machine learning methods (neural networks) in the field of medical data analysis 
 Development of models for classifying objects based on images and additional information ( multi-input and mixed data model ) 
 Analysis of requirements for computer vision systems 
 
 Required: 
 
 experience with version control systems (Git) 
 knowledge of Python (including numpy, pandas, jupyter) 
 knowledge of modern neural network concepts for image recognition (TensorFlow, Keras, PyTorch) 
 Knowledge of algorithms and methods of machine learning, deep learning, convolutional neural networks 
 Knowledge of basic image processing algorithms (edge ​​detection, Object detection, Optical flow, etc.) 
 Knowledge and practical experience in developing machine learning models. 
 Knowledge of technical English 
 Experience in computer vision 
 
 Desirable: 
 
 Experience with Amazon Web Services 
 Experience with medical images and / or other biological data 
 Knowledge of OpenCV 
 Knowledge of basic data structures and classical algorithms
