 Be a Data Scientist that works with real Big Data 
 Role overview 
 Are you a Data Scientist that wants to work with real Big Data on real Big Data problems? We are a Consultancy that does just that, whilst looking to develop some of most up to date Machine Learning solutions. We work with some of the most innovative industries and we want someone to help take them further. 
 Your Role: 
 This role is very similar to the other Data Scientist position shared, however the main difference is the amount of responsibility that you have and that you hold more commercial experience of the skills required 
 To advise our customers of the benefits for using big data and data science solutions to revolutionize their business 
 Work with large amounts of data to maintain, experiment and find new approaches 
 Be a Data Mining expert, find connections in largest data mountain, recommend classifications or forecasting 
 Cultivate relationships with all teams and other stakeholders, as well being able to demonstrate clear visualizations of the data 
 Your solution will enable our customers to handle Big Data efficiently 
 Redefine technical barriers using your own experience 
 Your Strengths: 
 A degree in Computer Science, Mathematics/Statistics or similar field.  PhD would be highly beneficial. 
 You must be strong programmer and statistician, be comfortable with large number crunching 
 Programming skills, particularly Python or Java. Other languages welcome, but must be able/willing to develop other languages 
 Experience working Hadoop, Spark, MapReduce, AWS or other with distributed systems, streaming data processing or cloud architectures 
 Extensive commercial background in Machine Learning, ideally Time-Series and Image Analysis.  NLP would be beneficial not necessary. 
 Proven record of designing and implementing real-time multivariate models 
 English language is required.  German language is highly beneficial 
 What We Want: 
 An individual that is motivated and has a lot of potential to develop their own Data Science skills and test themselves. Someone who wants to work on Big Data problems and work with large numbers to find solutions. 
 NorCom Information Technology 
 NorCom is a full-chain supplier for big data solutions. We have a team of highly qualified consultants and data scientists to develop needs-based big data and machine learning solutions according to our customers' business strategy. For Data Science, our main focus is the Automotive industry, as you know their is an area that can be vastly improved, developed by the use of Machine Learning/Deep Learning. We have our own Data Lab in Munich where we build and implement our own solutions, so you will rarely have to travel offsite. And if you do, our customers are all located locally, so you should never even be away for a full day. We also like to develop within our organisation and regularly offer training on some of the most recent techniques. This ensures that we can not only, continue offering our customers the best most up to date solutions, but that our team benefits on building their skills.
