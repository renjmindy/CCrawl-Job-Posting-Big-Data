 We are currently seeking a motivated and enthusiastic Social Marketing Assistant with an excellent understanding of Instagram. You will be joining us at an incredibly exciting time for the company and will be working alongside the Chief Marketing Officer and Co-Founder to support our marketing activities. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Sourcing graphics and imagery for online content 
 Proposing new ideas and concepts for social media content 
 Creating dynamic written, graphic, and video content 
 Developing and maintaining campaign and project schedules 
 Running social media channels to enhance audience engagement - copywriting and scheduling articles and posts 
 Monitoring and reporting on competitor activity 
 
 Key skills: 
 
 Strong multitasker who can adapt to changes quickly. 
 Self-starter that can work on their own initiative and isn't afraid to suggest and develop creative ideas 
 You pride yourself on your astute attention to detail 
 Social media management experience 
 Proficient using multi-social posting programs such as Hootsuite 
 Experience in using the Canva content creation platform 
 General knowledge of Search Engine Optimisation and internet ranking for web content 
 Excellent written and verbal communication skills 
 Experience in video editing / Adobe After Effects would be advantageous

 Why Join Wrangleapp.io 
 ---------------------------------------
