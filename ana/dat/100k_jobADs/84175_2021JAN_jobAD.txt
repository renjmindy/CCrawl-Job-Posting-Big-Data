 Steelfixer/Concreter 
 Job description 
 Job highlights 
 
 
 2/1 Roster 
 
 
 Potential for ongoing work 
 
 
 Attractive hourly rate 
 
 
 About the Role 
 This role will predominantly involve Concreting/Steel fixing work across Yandi and Nammuldi mine site. The scope of working includes fuel bay modifications which will include removing and relocating shelters, lube containers and installation of new offices. Trenching works and concreting, shed installation and lubrication rack installation. 12 hour days. 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 2+ years experience in large scale Steel fixing/concreting 
 
 
 RIO inductions (desirable) 
 
 
 Ability to pass a pre-employment medical 
 
 
 Good attitude 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
