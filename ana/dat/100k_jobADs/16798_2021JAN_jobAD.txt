 Spanish Teacher | Egg Harbor Township High School 
 Egg Harbor Township School District 
 __Long-Term Substitute Teaching Jobs 
 __Atlantic County, NJ 
 __Posted 1 year ago 
 About Insight: 
 Insight is one of the fastest growing education staffing companies in the country. Our company staffs substitute teachers, paraprofessionals, clerical staff, non-instructional aides, and more in over 60 school districts. If you are looking for a fast-paced job that fits your schedule, visit  www.workwithinsight.com/apply  now to start your online application. 
 Job Description: 
 Insight is looking for a Long-Term Substitute Spanish Teacher to work in the Egg Harbor Township School District. This assignment will be from March 2 - April 30, 2020. Pay for this position will be $135/day. 
 Job Requirements: 
 
 Ensure students receive the required instruction according to the school’s curriculum 
 Teach prepared lessons and prepare own instruction materials 
 Maintain student records regarding grades and absences 
 Assign and grade homework and class assignments 
 Consult with administrators on their responsibilities during the primary teacher’s absence 
 
 Job Qualifications: 
 
 CE/CEAS/Standard in Spanish 
 Candidates must already have or must be willing to undergo a criminal history check (fingerprinting) through the Department of Education. 
 Excellent organizational and communication skills 
 Ability to interpret prepared lessons and successfully instruct students using these materials 
 Able to successfully manage the classroom and determine when disciplinary action is necessary 
 Can observe and evaluate students on both individual assignments and over the course of the instruction 
 Communicates effectively with teachers, administrators, and parents to ensure student success 
 
 Insight is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
