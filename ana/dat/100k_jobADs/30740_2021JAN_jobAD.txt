 Exciting organisation based in Solihull are looking to recruit an interim Payroll Manager for 6 months. The client is looking to pay circa £250-300 a day. 
 The duties of the Payroll Manager will include: 
 - Process improvement 
 -To understand and comply with the requirements of the Sarbanes Oxley (SOX) 
 - Processing monthly payrolls 
 - Setting up new starters and personal details 
 - Process incoming time sheets & expenses 
 - Print relevant payroll reports 
 - Resolve any payroll queries 
 - Ad hoc tasks 
 - Advising employees on PAYE matters. 
 - Processing payments by BACS 
 - Reconciliations of Payrolls. 
 - Producing and analysing reports. 
 Key Skills: 
 - The Payroll Manager must have at least 3- 5 years experience within payroll 
- The ideal candidate will have experience of being in a similar position of a Payroll Manager and dealing with complex payroll queries, good payroll knowledge and strong administration skills. 
- This is a great opportunity to join a leading organisation based in Solihull, please apply or get contact if you think you are suitable for this role.
