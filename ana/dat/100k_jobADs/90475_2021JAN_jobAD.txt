 SC Works COVID-19 Information and Resources 
 The SC Works Centers in Anderson, Clemson, Easley and Seneca offer "easy to use" information, services, training and other tools you need to find your next job and move ahead in your career. Whether you are in transition, in school, laid off from your job, unemployed or have the skills and education to do more than what your current position calls for, we can help you get a solid footing for your next position or career or ultimately connect you to a job. 
 Our services help you find out what your skills are, provide assistance with a job search, and develop a plan to get you back to work.

 ACCESS POINTS 
 SC Works WorkLink partners with several community agencies to provide access points for job search services. As part of the SHARE Network, these Access Points offer access to a computer and many of the career services you would find at the comprehensive and satellite SC Works Workforce Centers in Anderson, Oconee and Pickens Counties 
 Access Points are available at the following locations: 
 Adult Education 
Anderson Adult Education – Districts 1 & 2 
Anderson County (Districts 3, 4 and 5)
