 This is a  business project lead  role in  Trading & Order Management  responsible for supporting the team head and product owner in driving the  development and   implementation of a core banking capability to book trades non-stop  in the Bank's core banking platform. 
With a clear focus on  order management, trading, execution, booking and post trade  processes the role holder will support the Sponsor and product owner to make key management, operational, commercial and financial decisions to drive and deliver the overall business strategy. 
Support  key transformational project  and overall digital initiatives impacting trading & order management team in partnership with the technology team. Ensure that overall project delivery is effectively managed within defined and agreed service level to drive and support business needs  . 
The candidate is required to have knowledge and experience in financial products, order management, trading, execution, trade booking processes and systems. 
 Responsibilities 
The  business project lead  is responsible for a new project to  enable T24 non-stop booking for cash equities, payments and FX cash products  by leading business requirements gathering, requirements analysis, designing workflows and business processes in collaboration with dealing desk, front office RM/MA, operations, finance etc. to ensure quality and timely delivery of the project. 
The role holder is expected to lead working group sessions for requirements analysis and prioritization, partner with IT and project manager to define the agenda of project steering committee and seek endorsements and decisions. 
 
 Work closely with end users to identify user needs for requirements gathering and elicitation. 
 Drive detailed business requirements and their documentation according to Banks's standard project management guidelines. 
 Collaborate with Technology BA, technology solution team and software engineers to make sure requirements are well understood. 
 Partner with external vendors (as required) to jointly deliver with Technology and project team the roadmap and objectives of the T24 NON-STOP project within scope, timeline and budget. 
 Lead UAT planning and coordinate UAT between all user groups to seek sign-off. 
 Prepare rollout plan; follows through with operations, front office, COO office and compliance teams on feature rollout & obtain regulatory approvals as required. 
 Prepare user training material, support user training, and conduct 1-1 trainings if required to key user council members. 
 
 Qualifications 
 
 Experience in  managing complex, cross-functional business and technology related projects  in banking and financial services. 
 Minimum of  8-10 years of experience in any trading system  (eg. FINIQ, BloomBerg, ULLINK, TradingScreen, FIS, Murex etc.)  and core banking platform Temenos T24 and TAP . Previous business implementation and/or business support experience for equities and equity options (Exchange traded and OTC). 
 Knowledge and experience of  core banking products, processes, and architecture  with focus on securities and derivatives modules / capabilities and post trade processing. 
 The candidate ought to have relevant  experience in financial products, trading processes and systems from private banks, wealth management  or management consulting in driving transformational and/or platform projects. 
 A driven self-starter who is delivery focused and possesses strong communication, influencing and presentation skills 
 Robust stakeholder management skills and good interpersonal skills. Ability to work confidently and collaboratively with cross-functional stakeholders of varying seniority. 
 Well organized with a natural inclination for conceptual thinking, planning strategy and solutioning; able to multi-task and meet multiple deadlines 
 Possess excellent problem-solving skills; a bility to work independently, is pragmatic and enjoys high energy levels 
 Thrive in a fast-paced environment and be able to adapt to complex demands in a diverse culture 
 Degree in Business Economics, Finance, Engineering, Computer Science 
 Possession of MBA degree, relevant certifications (eg. Agile, Scrum, Project Management, Design Thinking, User Experience) or qualification is advantageous
