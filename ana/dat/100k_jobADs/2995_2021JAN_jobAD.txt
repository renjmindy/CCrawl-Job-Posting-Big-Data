 Payment Services Representative I (2019-0412) 
 SaskEnergy 
 __Regina, SK 
 Apply Now 
 SaskEnergy is seeking a new member to add to our Finance team. The Payment Services Representative I performs duties associated with recording financial transactions to customer accounts in the Customer Service Information System (CIS Solution) and Miscellaneous Accounts Receivable system. This includes investigating and finding solutions, as well as communicating and liaising with internal departments and customers to ensure accuracy of recording payments and reporting. 
 Qualified applicants will possess: 
Grade 12 or equivalent. 
Intro to Financial Accounting (BUS 285 or equivalent). 
One (1) year experience in customer information database applications. 
One (1) year experience in computer applications such as word processing spreadsheets. Proficiency in Microsoft Excel is an asset. 
 Application Instructions 
 Please apply online with your cover letter and resume outlining your qualifications. 
Applications must be received online by 4:00 pm on November 7, 2019. 
For more information on this position, please contact Human Resources at 306-777-9091.
