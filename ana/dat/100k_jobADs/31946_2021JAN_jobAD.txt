 The Department: Security 
 In the emerging industry of digital assets, there is nothing more important than trust (which is why Geminiâ€™s very first hires were Security experts). The Gemini Security team forms the backbone of all that we do and is as diverse as the number of challenges we tackle in the crypto space.  The Role: Senior Security Engineer 
 Security has always been the foundation of Gemini and protecting our customers is at the core of our mission to empower the individual through crypto; trust is our product. The Security Engineering team at Gemini develops and manages security tools and platforms, assists users to design and assess the security internal systems, builds automation to simplify manual or complex processes, and researches business related topics including smart contracts and applied cryptography. In addition, this role will also work closely with our Security Operations, Product Security, and SRE teams. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Design, deploy, and maintain security solutions supporting security and engineering 
 Develop tools and automation that integrate security into systems and process, enabling our employees to do their jobs better and more securely

 Improve the monitoring and alerting capabilities, portability, and efficiency of deployed architectures, by creating and maintaining effective and repeatable Infrastructure as Code 
 Design, document, and maintain secure system architectures for new and existing capabilities 
 
 Minimum Qualifications: 
 
 Proficiency in a common scripting language 
 Strong written and verbal communication skills; attentive to details 
 Strong understanding of web application security and networking fundamentals 
 Experience hardening Linux based operating systems and familiarity with Linux internals 
 Able to troubleshoot and debug issues, and demonstrate a methodical approach to root cause analysis 
 
 Preferred Qualifications: 
 
 Ability to read and write code written in  Python ,  Scala ,  Java ,  C/C++ &  Golang 
 Experience working with cryptographic hardware (PKCS#11, FIPS 201-1 PIV, smartcards, etc) 
 Familiarity with configuration management tools ( Ansible ,  Chef ,  Puppet , etc)
