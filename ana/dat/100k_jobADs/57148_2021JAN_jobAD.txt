 Level Artist 
 Cut to Bits Apply for this Job 
 Posted 
 December 15, 2020 
 Full Time 
 Montreal, QC 
 Remote 
 Studio Cut to Bits is looking for a 3D modeler and Level Artist to be the principal artist on our unannounced game for an original IP on the Unity engine. The game is a light fantasy title so we are looking for someone that can create models and hand painted textures to match our style. 
 Our ideal candidate is someone who has enough experience and motivation to work independently as well as being a good collaborator. As a part of a small core team we also want someone who enjoys indie games and can bring their own ideas to the table. 
 ‍ 
 ‍  Responsibilities 
 
 Create in game 3D models 
 Create Textures 
 Build levels and create striking visual compositions 
 Optimization and bug fixing  ‍  ‍ 
 
 ‍ 
 ‍  Qualifications 
 
 Experience in 3D modeling 
 Experience in texturing 
 Experience with working in a game engine 
 Shipped a minimum of one commercial game  ‍ 
 
 ‍ 
 Nice to have 
 
 Proficiency in Blender 
 Proficiency in Substance painter 
 Proficiency in working with the Unity engine 
 
 ‍ 
 The position is full time with benefits. It will start as a remote position but will become on site when it is safe for us to return to our studio in the future. 
 ‍ 
 ‍  How to Apply 
 Send your CV to  info@cuttobits.com  with the subject "Applying for Level Artist". 
 ‍  ‍ 
 ‍  About Cut to Bits 
 Cut to Bits is a small indie studio started by AAA veterans based in Montreal. We've worked on critically acclaimed and successful franchises such as Assassin’s Creed, Bioshock, Far Cry, Grand Theft Auto, Rainbow Six, and Spider-man. We're looking to create fun innovative games that will appeal to a wide audience.
