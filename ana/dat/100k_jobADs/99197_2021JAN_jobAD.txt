 Care Assistant 
 We are working with a fantastic care home who are looking for healthcare assistants to build positive relationships with vulnerable adults, enhance their independence, improve their quality of life and help them become participating members of their community. 
 We are looking for compassionate, motivated and keen people to provide professional care in Amersham. 
 Full time and part time positions available. 
 You will need to: 
 
 
 Be committed, caring and compassionate and keen to provide professional high quality care. 
 
 
 Be reliable and trustworthy 
 
 
 Be able to work on your own initiative. 
 
 
 Previous experience is preferred but not essential. 
 
 
 Working alternate weekends and some evenings will be a requirement 
 
 
 Full training is provided.
