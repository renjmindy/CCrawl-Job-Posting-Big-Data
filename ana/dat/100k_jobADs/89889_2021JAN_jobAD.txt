 Mushoku Tensei Jobless Reincarnation Release Date:  Another Isekai anime announcement and this might be the biggest of them all. Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation is an anime adaptation of a Japanese Light Novel series of the same name. The Light Novel is written by Rifujin na Magonote. The actual thing is Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation is considered as the best of all the Isekai stuff out there. 
 So, now that it is getting an anime fans are losing their mind and really can't wait for the anime to drop out. We just hope that the anime is long enough and not rushed. So, at least 4 cours or more, nothing less than that.

 Mushoku Tensei Jobless Reincarnation 
 The story revolves around a 34-year-old man who is a sore loser and thinks that his whole life is a waste and he hasn't done anything meaningful for his whole life. One day, he saw a speeding truck heading towards a group of kids. He managed to save the kids and after that, he realizes that he has been reincarnated in a world of sorcery and sword. He resolves to become successful in his new life and he started his life under his new name Rudeus Greyrat. He becomes highly skilled at magic due to his genetic factors. He will face many challenges in his new life and he will have to clear all the challenges.

 Mushoku Tensei Jobless Reincarnation Release Date 
 The anime adaptation of the series was announced on March 15, 2019. And on October 18, 2019, it was announced that the series will premiere in late 2020. But a new preview revealed that due to the COVID outbreak throughout the world the release date has been shifted to 2021. The particular date is yet to be announced, but we will update you as soon as we get the information. 
 Mushoku Tensei Jobless Reincarnation Cast And Staff 
 Cast:- 
 
 Rudeus Greyant Voiced By Yumi Uchiyama (Re: ZERO) 
 Roxy Migurdia Voiced By Konomi Kohara ( Kaguya Sama: Love Is War) 
 Sylphiette Voiced By Ai Kayano ( Konosuba) 
 Eris Boreas Greyant Voiced By Ai Kakuma (Demon Slayer) 
 
 More cast members will be revealed soon and we will update it here as soon as we get the information about it. 
 Staff:- 
 The series is being directed by Manabu Okamoto. It will be animated by Studio Bind. Character Design will be handled by Kazutaka Sugiyama and Yoshiaki Fujisawa will handle the music composition department. And Egg Firm will produce the series. 
 Mushoku Tensei Jobless Reincarnation Preview And Spoilers 
 The first trailer of the anime was released a year ago revealing that the series will release in 2020. And a few days ago, the second trailer was released which revealed that the series has been postponed to 2021. 
 https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SPaUMTlkJQc 
 Both of the trailers received amazing reactions from the fans. Fans loved the animation and were really excited for the anime. 
 Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation is not just another Isekai anime, It has been at the top spot since 2012, even after SAO is in full throttle mode. So, it's getting an adaptation that is really a very big deal. Now, we think that the first season of the anime might cover till volume 6 of the manga. The anime might also include a really epic fight that every mags fan loved.
