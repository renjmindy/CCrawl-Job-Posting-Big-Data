 Jobs Caregiving 
 Posted on  August 12, 2010  by  caregiver 
 Israel, the leading employer of Filipino caregivers is again inviting qualified applicants for its  Jobs Caregiving . These jobs caregiving have a job order that was approved for recruitment by the POEA last August 3 for World Wiser International Inc., a POEA accredited recruitment agency based in FB Harrison in Pasay City. For the said jobs caregiving a total of 24 applicants will be chosen for placement in caregiver work abroad. 
 If you think you are up to the task and interested to apply for the said jobs caregiving, submit your resume and application directly to the office of the recruiting agency. For further details or inquiries regarding the said jobs caregiving, you can dial the agency’s landline number provided below. 
 World Wiser International Inc. 
2nd Floor DM Building 
2004 FB Harrison 
Pasay City 
Telefax: 8243784
