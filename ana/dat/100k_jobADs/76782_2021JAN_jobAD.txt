 Coach and Bus Technician - 310T REQ 
 Joe Foley Bus Lines 
 __Madoc, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Joe Foley Bus Lines Ltd., is looking for a 310T Truck & Coach Technician for our shop located in Madoc, ON to work on our fleet of Coaches, School Buses and Assorted Other Vehicles. 
 This is a part time position, primarily providing assistance to our early evening school bus runs. 
 We are looking for a highly motivated individual who takes pride in their work to join our team. Must be able to follow shop standards and procedures, work as a member of a team, maintain a clean, professional appearance, and maintain a neat and tidy work-space. 
 Requirements: 
 
 310T Truck and Coach Technician Certification 
 Experience with JPRO Diagnostics an asset 
 3 years experience required 
 Valid G-class driver's license with clear abstract; B licence would be an asset. 
 Must be able to pass a Criminal Record and Vulnerable Sector Check 
 Demonstrated significant experience on a variety of vehicles and engines 
 Problem solving and analytical skills 
 
 Role and Responsibilities 
 
 Perform repair and maintenance on school buses 
 Inspect, test, and diagnose equipment to identify malfunctions 
 Use a variety of tools and specialty equipment to diagnose problems with vehicles 
 Use a variety of hand tools, power tools, and specialty equipment to repair vehicles 
 Knowledge and experience of medium or heavy duty truck repair and maintenance 
 Knowledge of vehicle systems to ensure they are in proper operating condition 
 Test drive vehicles to diagnose malfunctions or to ensure they are working properly 
 Assist drivers with malfunctioning equipment 
 Keep vehicles in proper repair to pass MTO & DOT and other standard inspections 
 
 Remuneration will be based on experience. 
 Immediate start available 
 Application deadline: 2020-09-30 
 Job Types: Part-time, Temporary, Casual 
 Benefits: 
 
 Casual dress 
 Flexible schedule 
 On-site parking 
 
 Schedule: 
 
 Monday to Friday 
 
 COVID-19 considerations: 
Individual work bays, masks, sanitizer, gloves and face shields provided, laundered coveralls. 
 Education: 
 
 AEC / DEP or Skilled Trade Certificate (Required) 
 
 Language: 
 
 English (Required) 
 
 Work remotely: 
 
 No
