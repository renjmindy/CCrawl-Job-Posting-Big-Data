 Immediate starts 
 
 
 Long-term opportunity for the right people 
 
 
 About our Client 
 Our client is a multi-disciplinary heavy engineering company that contributes to the oil and gas, metals and minerals, infrastructure and defence sectors 
 About the Role 
 We are seeking Boilermakers for Day and Night shifts with structural steel and heavy plate experience for our Client in Henderson. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Committed to a Safe Working Environment 
 
 
 Monday to Friday, 6 Hours Saturday 
 
 
 Ability to be flexible and deal with daily challenges 
 
 
 Proven history of workshop in last 12 months 
 
 
 Experience with heavy Structural and Plate work highly regarded 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Trade Certificate 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 12 months experience in a similar environment 
 
 
 Proven experience in structural steel and heavy plate work 
 
 
 Overhead crane experience 
 
 
 C Class Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Working at Heights, EWP and Confined Space highly regarded 
 
 
 Right to Work in Australia 
 
 
 Must be able to pass full medical and DAS 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels.
