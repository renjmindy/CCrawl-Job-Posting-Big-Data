 My client, a Consumer business based in Worcestershire, is looking to recruit a Digital Marketing Executive. 
 This role will join a well established digital and marketing team, in their Droitwich based head office. 
 The Digital Marketing Executive role will focus on either: 
 
 PPC - hands-on paid search management with a significant monthly budget 
 
 Or 
 
 Content Management - working across content production, customer journey, conversion and analytics. 
 
 Based on the requirements above, the ideal candidate will possess either a strong PPC background or extensive CMS experieince. If you do not possess strong PPC skills then you will be both analytical and creative in your outlook. 
 Highly competitive package. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
