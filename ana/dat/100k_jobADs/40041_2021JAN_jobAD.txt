 Senior Full Stack Developer (RoR/React.js) 
 Location : 
 Kyiv / Ukraine (Remote) 
 We are ready to offer : 
 
 Modern Ruby (2.6.3), modern Rails (6.0.3.2), constantly upgrading and using modern language and framework features 
 Custom DDD-alike architecture on top of Rails (operations, view models, decorators) 
 RSpec-based BDD (with a lot of custom matchers and add-ons), close to 100% test coverage 
 Strict style agreements, validated by Rubocop 
 Tough yet pragmatic code quality control, enforced by code reviews 
 Lots of non-trivial algorithmic tasks with large and fuzzy texts processing 
 Lots of non-trivial third-party APIs integrations 
 Clear vision for upcoming “big” system features and path towards them 
 Working at the product company where our people are at the heart of what we do 
 Opportunity to work at a start-up environment and international team 
 Competitive salary 
 15 working days paid vacation, paid sick leaves 
 Health insurance 
 Free drinks and fruits (daily) 
 Happy hours (pizza, beer and board games) 
 Corporate leisure activities 
 Sponsored training/certification opportunities 
 
 About the project : 
 The product is an artificial intelligence platform that uses contextual data, specific acoustic models and current events to provide each client with an adapted model that is constantly improving over time. 
 Technical stack: Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Sidekiq, CoffeeScript, Docker, AWS (EC2, ECS, S3, AutoScaleGroup), Heroku. 
 Requirements : 
 
 3-5+ years of experience in software development 
 Experience with Ruby on Rails is a must 
 Has experience in HTML5, CSS, JS 
 Experience in writing complex database queries 
 Great interpersonal skills and team player 
 Fast learner, motivated and independent 
 Passion for making a great product and taking it to the highest level of quality 
 
 As an advantage : 
 
 Good English (Reading/Writing) 
 Bachelor’s Degree in computer science or related field 
 Prior startup experience 
 
 Expectations : 
 As a Ruby on Rails developer, you will be responsible for developing the web platform which enables to revolutionize the transcription and translation industry. 
 You will work in a dynamic, agile environment with highly talented team members delivering content that is implemented in production environments very rapidly.
