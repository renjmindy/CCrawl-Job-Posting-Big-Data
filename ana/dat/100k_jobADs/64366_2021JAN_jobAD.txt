 Are you fed up of working nights or continental shifts? Would you like to step up to a new challenge within a leading FMCG business? 
 My client based in Telford are looking for an experienced Multi Skilled Engineer to join their maintenance department. Work in both a team and on your own initiative you will attend reactive maintenance across the whole site followed by planning future PPM schedules. Given the nature of work it is an extremely hands on position working within a light to medium environment. 
 The successful Multi Skilled Engineer will have a similar background to the follow: 
 
 NVQ / City & Guilds qualified to Level 3 
 Apprentice trained within either Electrical or Mechanical (Ideally Electrically biased) 
 Have previously worked within an FMCG or Food environment. 
 Have the ability to work at a fast pace and be able to make quick decisions for the better of the business 
 
 In return my client offers a fantastic shift pattern consisting of double days (6am to 2pm / 2pm to 10pm) along with a very competitive salary up to £32k with an OTE of £45k.
