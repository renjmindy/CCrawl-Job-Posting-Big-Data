 Job Search Jump-Starter Workshop 
 
 If you're looking for advice and guidance on the best way of kicking off an effective job search, this 'Job Search Jump-Starter' workshop is for you. 
 You will learn: 
 ​ 
 ​​ 
 
 The ways the job search process has changed since you last looked for a job, and what this means for you. 
 How to effectively prepare your job search to reduce stress and overwhelm, and set yourself up for success. 
 Ways to boost your job search confidence. 
 How to update your CV and cover letter for the latest do's and dont's. 
 How to use and optimise LinkedIn to support your job search. 
 Strategies for tapping into the hidden job market, including working your network. 
 The job search resources available to you, including key resources for finding a job in Berlin.​ 
 
 ​​ 
Perfect for anyone who is returning to work after a career break or wanting to change careers, you will come away with a bundle of handouts, including CV and cover letter templates, and all your burning job search questions answered - the best way of 'minding the gap' on your CV, when and how to ask for flexibility during the application process, how to answer those tricky interview questions about your home and family life, and many more. 
 The cost of the 2-hour workshop includes a one-on-one 45-minute Skype or phone call giving you expert feedback on your choice of CV, cover letter or LinkedIn profile.  Individual follow-up sessions will be arranged at your convenience after the workshop. 
 There will be a maximum of 8 participants - a small enough group for everyone to get individual support. ​ 
 Date:   14 February 2017 
 Time:  10.30-12.30am 
 Location:   Easy Busy , Berlin 
 Price:  65 EUR for Early Bird tickets for bookings made before 31 January. From then the price goes up to 80 EUR before bookings close on 7 February.
