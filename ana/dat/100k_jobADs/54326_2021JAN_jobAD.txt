 Temporary Restaurant Duty Manager 
 Äkäskero Nature Resort Ltd -  Lapland  , Finland

 Job Description 
 Äkaskero is a small hotel / restaurant / Management Company in 20 km from the center of Äkaslompolo Pallas-Yllastunturi national park. 
 We need our restaurant duty manager for the winter season. All of our customers come from Central Europe husky, so the English or German language skills are essential. Our restaurant has a weekly menu of all the customers eat in the evening prior to the menu by. Housing Opportunity 2 person cottage hotel in the yard. Public transport is not available, so a car for mobile recreation is needed. Lumikenkailymaastot aivat next to the hotel and the nearby terrain ski trail run in February. Hotel Äkaskero
