 Role overview 
 As a Backend Engineer in the Team Order Management, you will build the retail order engine that processes contracts of hundreds of buyers with over 2000 suppliers. Your systems will ensure that 300000+ products are available at the right time and place for our customers. 
 WHERE YOUR EXPERTISE IS NEEDED 
 
 Meet business needs while maintaining scalable architecture and keeping dependable consistency of business objects 
 Own the whole development cycle - from architecture design to implementation and testing, as well maintenance of our products 
 Establish rock-solid operation of systems and continuously improve the setup, to ensure that merchandise worth a billion Euros a year can be ordered 
 Collaborate with other technology and business teams to ensure that our solutions are well integrated in the larger context of Zalando 
 
 WHAT WE‚ÄôRE LOOKING FOR 
 
 5+ years of experience in dedicated Java (+Spring Boot) projects and active usage of SQL in your recent assignments while writing well-structured and tested code in the spirit of Clean Code that easily scales to enterprise level

 Willingness to support the Frontend development (ReactJS) is a plus

 Experience in RESTful API design, system architecture as well as building microservice-based systems, preferably in the cloud and in building event-driven systems
