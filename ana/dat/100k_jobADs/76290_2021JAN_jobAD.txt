 Warehouse Assistant 
 Bangkok Meat Balls Food Co., Ltd 
 __Scarborough, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Who are we? 
 Since 1998, Bangkok Meat Balls Food is a family operated business that has been supplying the Greater Toronto Area and surrounding cities in Ontario with leading quality Asian style meatballs. We are proud to have grown to become one of the most competitive importers and distributors in the GTA. We are focused but not limited to Thai, Vietnamese, Chinese, and other Southeast Asian food products. 
 We are seeking a Warehouse Assistant to support our growing business 
 We are seeking a Warehouse Assistant who is professional, honest, reliable, analytical, motivated, accurate and committed to customer satisfaction. The ideal candidate would have graduated from  Supply Chain Management.  The position involves picking, shipping, receiving, inventory control, warehouse and equipment maintenance. 
 Work Schedule 
 Monday to Friday 
 9:00 am – 5:00 pm 
 Must be able to work overtime and weekends when needed 
 Responsibilities and duties 
 · Assist the inventory controller with daily tasks 
 · Communicate with the office to ensure accuracy with sales orders and purchase orders 
 · Enter inventory movement, completed picks, received purchase orders, credit returns into the inventory system 
 · Pick orders with accuracy 
 · Restock bins 
 · Maintain an organized inventory system, track and follow an organized approach for the products and investigate any inventory discrepancies when needed 
 · Assist drivers and customers to load cargo vans and trucks with equipment 
 · Quality Control on incoming and outgoing shipments 
 · Keep the warehouse clean and organized daily 
 · Maintain material handling equipment; forklift batteries 
 · Ensure health and safety guidelines are adhered to 
 · Perform other warehouse functions as assigned 
 The successful candidate shall possess the following: 
 · Minimum 1 year of experience in warehouse activities 
 · Valid counterbalance forklift certification 
 · Knowledge of inventory control and best practices 
 · Knowledge of warehouse safety procedures 
 ·  Studied Supply Chain Management 
 · Must have safety shoes and be able to lift and carry up to 60 lbs unassisted 
 · Strong attention to detail 
 · Excellent problem-solving and decision-making skills 
 · Solid attendance, punctuality, and work history 
 · Good written and verbal communication skills 
 · Strong sense of time, organization, and urgency 
 · Proactive and positive attitude 
 · Must be able to work in a team as well as individually to accomplish company goals 
 Recommendations 
 · Knowledge of Fishbowl Inventory System is an asset but not necessary
