 Business Systems Manager - Tewkesbury - £35,000 - £45,000 
 ERP, Sage, systems, IT manager, Infrastructure, Microsoft, SQL, support, EDI, Data, SAP. 
 I am recruiting for a Business Systems Manager to join a global business based in Tewkesbury. This role is a newly created position to come on board and take full ownership of IT and systems and managing the new ERP implementation of Sage X3. This role requires someone who has a good understating of ERP systems and someone who is able to lead projects effectively. 
 The ideal candidate will have worked on and ERP implementation previously, have good all round IT knowledge and have ambitions to grow a division as the business continues to grow. This really is an exciting opportunity to be the start of an established global IT division. 
 Required experience: 
 
 Strong understating of ERP systems (Sage X3 would be an advantage). 
 IT infrastructure systems knowledge. 
 Background within IT support. 
 Comfortable in a business facing role. 
 Management of 3rd parties. 
 Knowledge of SQL databases desirable 
 
 If you have the required experience and would like to find out more about this position, please apply here.
