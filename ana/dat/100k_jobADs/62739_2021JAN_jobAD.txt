 YOU GOT THE JOB, NOW WHAT? 
 Category:  Job Retention Skills 
 You’ve Got The Job, Now What? Workbook assists individuals who have just been assigned to their new jobs and what they can expect with regard to rules on a new job setting, along with what employers will expect from them, as good employees. 
 YOU GOT THE JOB, NOW WHAT? quantity

 Description 
 
 You’ve Got The Job, Now What?  covers the top five guidelines to work maturity. This covers employers’ expectations, basic hygiene, appearance, dress and how to be responsible on the job. Twenty tips of being a successful employee are outlined and described. 
 This workbook takes the reader through their inventory of what they bring to work. It also addresses the various types of attitudes encountered while on the job. In addition, tips are given on how an employee should act while on the job and how to be great at work as well as a section on working with a purpose.
