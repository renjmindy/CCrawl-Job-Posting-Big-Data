 Full time mid-weight to senior developer LOCALPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 We are looking for a mid-weight to senior developer to join us. You will be working with an energetic creative team to build digital projects for our clients. We are an integrated brand consultancy, continually looking at ways to push the potential of digital from a functional and emotional perspective to support the larger brand narratives we create. 
 The job is to realise and build digital experiences, working closely with the team and our clients. We primarily use CraftCMS and work with front-end Javascript frameworks such as Vue to create fluid user experiences and maintainable code. Our work often extends beyond the browser and we continually encourage ways to bring code into the design process. 
 Key Responsibilities 
 
 Working with the creative team to build digital brand experiences 
 Problem solving with clients and the team to turn ideas into reality and produce the best technical outcome 
 Ability to develop responsive websites and progressive mobile apps using best practices 
 Build reusable code and libraries for future use 
 Advise on best practice across a variety of applications from external facing websites to internal web-apps that help organisations 
 Optimise applications for performance and scalability 
 Developing and mentoring team members 
 
 Skills And Experience

 Understanding of build tools such as Gulp and Webpack

 Good use of Node.js and client-side frameworks such as VueJS*, React and Angular
