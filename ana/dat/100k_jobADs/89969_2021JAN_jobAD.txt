 ABOUT THE ROLE 
 Seeking experienced Mechanical Fitters to commence at mine site in Queensland Central Highlands Region. 
 Duties and Responsibilities: 
 
 
 Provide maintenance mining/mechanical equipment 
 
 
 Ability to troubleshoot faults 
 
 
 Assessing and prioritising breakdown service requirements 
 
 
 Work collaboratively with maintenance team including planners/supervisors 
 
 
 Identifying, evaluating, and reporting design and process issues 
 
 
 Compliance and commitment to upholding all site safety procedures 
 
 
 SKILLS & RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 
 Trade Qualified Mechanical Fitter 
 
 
 Right to Work 
 
 
 Demonstrated mining experience - Skills List 
 
 
 Current Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Current Standard 11 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 ABOUT WORKPAC
