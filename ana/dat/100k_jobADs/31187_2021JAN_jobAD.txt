 Consultant: 
 
 
 I am looking to speak to super-efficient, well organised Executive Assistants, who would be interested in a 9-month FTC based in the centre of Birmingham. 
 You will be providing a high level of secretarial and administrative support to the CEO and acting as a supervisor for the office. Ideally you will have a previous Executive Assistant/ Personal Assistant background and are available to start August 2020, for the full duration of the contract. 
 Depending on experience you can expect a salary of up to £31,000 per annum. 
 You will be welcomed into a fun, friendly and vibrant office environment and receive an excellent benefits employee package. 
 Video interviews will commence July 1st. 
 As the Executive Assistant you will be responsible for: 
 
 Provide secretarial support to the CEO 
 Diary management, booking meeting and arranging travel 
 Preparation for board meetings, organsing packs 
 Attending meetings with CEO 
 Accurately recording minutes from meetings 
 Occasional management and oversight of business projects 
 Maintaining and monitoring business budgets and expenses 
 
 For more information along with the full job specification please don't hesitate to get in touch.
