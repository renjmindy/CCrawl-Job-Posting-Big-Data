 As an experienced HR Business Partner my client - an entrepreneurial, growing business is looking for an individual who can support the business change and transformation. Based in Stoke. Salary £50k-55k + Benefits. 
 Reporting into the HR Manager, as HR Business Partner you will work closely with the Managers across the business, coaching and developing their capabilities to manage more effectively. You will also focus around Employer Branding, performance management, workforce planning, and recruitment. 
 With a strong coaching style, you will bring with you existing HR skills to help develop the HR function, and you will be able to demonstrate where you have implemented changes, and know "what good HR looks like". 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
