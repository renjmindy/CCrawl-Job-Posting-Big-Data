 Cookie from PHP (programming language), PHP data identifier. Contains only a reference to the current session. No information is stored in the user's browser and this cookie can only be used by the current website. This cookie is mainly used in forms to improve the user experience. Data entered in forms is stored for a short time, for example, if there is an input error by the user and the user receives an error message. Otherwise all data would have to be re-entered.

 Career 
 Launch Your Future with Us 
 Start your career at Wieland Fudickar and become a part of our team. 
A secure job includes interesting advanced training opportunities and friendly coworkers. 
 We also offer entry-level employees the opportunity to complete a vocational training program in wholesale and foreign commerce. 
We are a vocational training company certified by the South Westphalian Chamber of Industry and Commerce in Hagen. Acquire a recognized degree by completing an interesing, hands-on program.
