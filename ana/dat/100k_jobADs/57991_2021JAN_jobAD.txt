 Call Center Representative FT Temp-To-Hire 
   
   Workway, Inc.   
 __Website    __@workway 
 As a Call Center Rep, you will be handling outbound and inbound calls to gather or clarify information.  Your specific duties in this role will include: 
 
 Accurately documenting all information pertaining to accounts 
 Meeting or exceeding all performance goals 
 Preparing and/or processing documents (reviewing them for accuracy and completeness; updating information and/or evaluating against policy; comparing elements for consistency or logical relationship, etc.) 
 Receiving researching and responding to incoming questions; providing information, explaining policies and procedures, and/or facilitating a resolution 
 
 Job Requirements 
 As a Call Center Representative, you must be motivated and reliable, with strong analytic and problem-solving skills. It is also important that you display excellent communication, interpersonal, and customer service skills and the ability to articulate and explain information clearly.  Specific qualifications for the position include: 
 
 High school diploma or equivalent 
 1+ years of Data Entry and Customer Service experience is preferred 
 Great communication 
 Strong Telephone Preference
