 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson Finance are delighted to be working on an exclusive basis with this modern, forward thinking SME business in Leamington Spa, to recruit a permanent Accounts Assistant. Reporting into the Company Accountant, you will be responsible for an all round transactional finance role. 
 Key duties include purchase ledger invoice processing, bank reconciliations and payments, sales ledger, reconciling supplier statements, dealing with supplier and customer queries, data cleansing, journal entries and general finance administration. 
 In order to be considered, you will ideally be available immediately or on short notice, and have proven experience in a hands on finance department. You will be careful and diligent, good humoured and personable. 
 In return, you will receive a salary of up to £25,000 dependant on experience, plus a benefits package including amazing on-site facilities, free parking, training and development, flexible working, pension, employee discount, plus other lifestyle benefits. 
 For more information on this fantastic opportunity, please apply direct, or call Sophie Evans, Gleeson Recruitment, on 07812 079718. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
