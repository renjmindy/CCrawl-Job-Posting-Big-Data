 Full-Time Software Engineers/Senior Software Engineers 
 Blancco  -  Joensuu  , North Karelia, Finland 
 Job Description 
 Blancco is searching for multiple  Software Engineers/Senior Software Engineers  to join the company's development department in Joensuu, Finland. As a (Senior) Software Engineer your tasks will contain different levels of implementation and design, within WebUIs, network communication protocols, database communications, performance optimizations, system data gathering, data conversion and/or Linux drives, depending on your skill set and experience. Blancco is a pioneer and market leader in certified data erasure. Blancco software is the proven data erasure solution for millions of users around the globe and is the industry's most certified erasure software. The company serves users across a wide range of industries, including banking, finance, government and defense, as well as IT Asset Disposal professionals. Blancco operates from an extensive network of international offices and partners across Europe, North America, Asia, and Australasia. Over 90% of sales are generated outside of Finland. 
 As a (Senior) Software Engineer you should have experience in the following: 
 
 C, C++, Java, Python, JavaScript, CoffeeScript, HTML5, CSS, XML or Scala Web development and database knowhow, is considered as a plus (must for Java developers) 
 Unit testing 
 Spring and Hibernate, is considered a plus for Java developers 
 Linux development or Windows development, low level development is considered a plus 
 Designing of complex system architectures, is considered a plus (must for Senior) 
 Quality Assurance processes and methodologies, is considered a plus (must for Senior) 
 Design patterns, is considered a plus (must for Senior) 
 Working in multinational teams 
 Individual problem solving 
 
 The requirements for this role include: 
 
 2+ years of experience in software development (5+ for Senior) 
 Willingness to commit to a long term career, with no immediate need for relocation 
 Ability and willingness for constant learning 
 Good social skills 
 A good sense of humour 
 Good written and verbal communication skills in English
