 Job Description 
 Grand County Library District is looking for a part time library lover! This clerk position is an entry level professional library customer service that is fun and fast paced. The ideal candidate would be willing to work in multiple library facilities, have excellent communication skills, is dependable, and willing to learn our collections and services.
