 Long-Term Substitute School Counselor- Hamilton NJ 
 Hamilton Township School District 
 __Long-Term Substitute Teaching Jobs 
 __Mercer County, NJ 
 __Posted 8 months ago 
 About Insight: 
 Insight is one of the fastest growing education staffing companies in the country. Our company staffs substitute teachers, paraprofessionals, clerical staff, non-instructional aides, and more in over 60 school districts. If you are looking for a fast-paced job that fits your schedule, visit www.workwithinsight.com/apply now to start your online application. 
 Job Description: 
 Insight is looking for a Long-Term Substitute School Counselor to work in the Hamilton Township School District. Candidates should be accessible with excellent communication skills and a profound interest in helping students to attain their academic and career goals. This assignment will be from September 1, 2020– January 18, 2021. Pay for this position will be $135/day for Days 1-20, and $150/day for Days 21+. 
 Job Requirements: 
 
 Provide counseling and group guidance to help students cope effectively personal, social, academic, career, and family concerns. 
 Prepares various forms of correspondence. 
 Obtain and evaluate student data, including test results, personal histories, school records, teacher’s reports, parental information, etc. 
 Provide classroom guidance focusing on post-high school planning, career exploration, family and/or peer relationships and decision-making 
 Address issues that impact students at risk of failing to attain their full academic potential 
 Evaluate and prepare student transcripts. 
 Implement programs dealing with decision-making, conflict resolution, self-esteem, coping strategies, etc. 
 Serve as the school-based resource for dealing with drop-out prevention, substance abuse, pregnancy, homelessness, and crisis prevention/intervention. 
 Performs related duties as assigned. 
 
 Job Qualifications: 
 
 CE/CEAS/Standard School Counselor Certification 
 Candidates must already have or must be willing to undergo a criminal history check (fingerprinting) through the Department of Education.
