 We are looking for a talented and ambitious new Product Owner to manage the CRM development team. 
 You will manage a multidisciplinary team of designers, engineers and testers, reporting to senior leadership to deliver an ambitious programme of work bringing our next generation open source charity CRM to fruition. 
 Your role will be to manage the end to end product development lifecycle by preparing specifications, backlog grooming and managing agile sprints. 
 As a CRM expert you will be able to shape the overall vision for the product, working with the leadership team to identify and prioritise the roadmap and feature set. 
 We're a very process driven team who always seek to do things "the right way" and we're looking for a person who enjoys the same principles. 
 In short, we're a team of self-confessed geeks who love tinkering and tweaking until something is just right, and we're looking for someone with the same "can do" attitude to join our growing team. 
 Key responsibilities: 
 
 Work closely with Product Management and Senior Management to ideate the product roadmap 
 Take ownership of delivering to a critical timeline of milestones 
 Prepare technical specifications and elaboration of user stories 
 Plan and prioritise product feature backlog 
 Provide an active role in mitigating any issues that might impact successful team completion of release/sprint goals 
 
 What we are looking for: 
 
 3+ years experience delivering digital products as a Product Owner 
 3+ years experience working with and ideally developing CRM / ERP products 
 A strong focus and experience working with agile scrum methodologies 
 
 What we value in people: 
 
 Great attention to detail 
 Creative thinker with a vision 
 A person who likes to immerse themselves in product ecosystems to really understand client needs 
 Brings a Can do / Make it happen attitude 
 You have the ability to grasp concepts quickly 
 You have excellent organisational and time management skills 
 Someone who loves technical challenges 
 
 About the company: 
 Our mission is to enable digital transformation in organisations that deliver social good. We work with Charities across their digital stack helping organisations to rebuild their processes using flexible open-source tools. We help organisations reimagine the way they work so they can focus on impact. 
 We're a bootstrapped startup that has grown to over 40 in the team based solely on word of mouth from the work we've been producing. 
 Uniquely, we base all our solutions on open source products, helping our work to have the greatest possible impact.

 If you think this is the role for you, please visit the careers page on our website to send your CV through to us with a short covering statement explaining what caught your interest and why you think you'll fit in.
