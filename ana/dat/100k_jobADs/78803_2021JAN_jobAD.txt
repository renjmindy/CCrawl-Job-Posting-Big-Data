 Operator Coal Mining Level 3 
 Job description 
 Mining Operators 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Ranging from $44.90 - $47.68 per hour on 7/7 Roster 
 
 
 Accommodation & Meals Supplied 
 
 
 6 Monthly Incentive Bonus 
 
 
 About Our Client 
 Our client is Australia's largest coal miner and exporter, the world's largest supplier to the sea borne coking coal market and largest coal producer and supplier of metallurgical coal. The client has several mine sites across the Bowen Basin consisting primarily of open cut mines. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking a skilled and experienced Haul Truck / Excavator and Service Operators to commence at a mine near Nebo. You will be working a 7/7 Roster, Day and Night Shift. Our Client is seeking Operators who have operated on site for a minimum of 6 months on a mine site. There is also a BIBO service from Mackay. BIBO or DIDO or Local. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Operate a range of machine including but not limited to Hitachi 5600 - Similar models / Size equipment, CAT 793 - Similar models / Size equipment 
 
 
 Complete Pre-start checks on machinery. 
 
 
 12.5 Hour Shifts. 
 
 
 Follow Company as well as Site Procedures & Policies to a safe standard. 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 RII/ Training Transcript 
 
 
 Minimum of 6 months mine experience operating on a minimum of a CAT 793 
 
 
 Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 BMA Core or Standard 11 
 
 
 Right to work within Australia 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
