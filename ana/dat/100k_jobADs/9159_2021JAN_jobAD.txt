 Manual Machinist-Aviation 
 MTU Maintenance Canada 
 __Delta, BC 
 Apply Now 
 Reporting to the Team Leader, the incumbent will perform one or more of the following processes in the repair of engine rotables or aircraft components: machine grinding, machine milling, machine turning, hand polishing, CNC operation and programming, general inspection. 
 Core Competencies 
 
 Must possess a provincial machinist trade qualification. 
 Must have completed a recognized apprenticeship with the Company, or have a minimum of 4 years equivalent experience by other means acceptable to Quality Assurance for the implementation of technical work on engines/modules. 
 Must be able to interpret drawings and documentation. 
 Must have knowledge of machine shop equipment. 
 Experience with machining and processes of repairing aircraft engine components would be an asset. 
 Experience with CNC milling and grinding machines would be an asset. 
 
 Key Responsibilities 
 
 Use machining processes to repair engine rotables or aircraft components. 
 Follow direction of shop leadership. 
 Comply with company policy and procedure manuals. 
 Recommend additions or changes to work documents. 
 Advise Leads of tool shortages. 
 Ensure machines are cleaned and maintained. 
 Make suggestions to Team Leaders for process improvements. 
 Keep shop licenses current. 
 Provide on-the-job training as needed.
