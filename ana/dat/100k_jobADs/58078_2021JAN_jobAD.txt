 Long Term Maintenance Planner 
 Job description 
 
 
 
 WorkPac are currently seeking an experienced Maintenance Planner to join Dampier Salts Asset Management Team as a Long-Term Planner. The role joins a Perth based team comprising of a second Planner and Planning Specialist along with a Maintenance System Specialist and an offshore team. The team support three Dampier Salt operations with general master data improvements, maintenance tactics and strategy implementations/reviews, ZBB budgeting, work management support, shutdown alignments and major component replacement across all fixed plant and mobile asset types. This role will be primarily Perth based with site travel required 3 days every second week.
