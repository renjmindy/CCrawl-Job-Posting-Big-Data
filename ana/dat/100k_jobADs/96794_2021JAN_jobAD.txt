 Sunday Work at Home Jobs 
 Published by  aw1219  on November 22, 2020 
 We may earn money or products from some of the companies mentioned in this post. 
 Some of you may be sitting at home this Sunday afternoon  browsing the web , drinking your favorite cup of coffee or tea, or catching up on the latest news. To give you a jump start on your work at home journey, I decided to provide you with a list of new  work at home jobs  that you can apply to today. 
 Work at home tip:  Remember, the earlier you apply, the faster you may get a reply to work from home. Many of the jobs are filled on a first-come first-serve basis. Applying today, could help you to work from home this week. 
 Be sure to come back tomorrow by visiting the  weekly job section  and on Friday at the  Friday freebie page  for even more work at home jobs from companies hiring people to work in customer service, some  non phone , global work at home job opportunities, as virtual assistants, and so much more. Join our Facebook groups  Workersonboard Community  and  Real Work from Home Jobs  for additional job leads. You can also subscribe to my  YouTube channel  for additional work at home update and job leads. 
 Customer Service  
 Unum is now hiring reps to respond to customer inquiries with timely and accurate information. This job will require you to have solid verbal skills, be computer proficient, and be customer service oriented. Pays $15.86 per hour working full-time hours with benefits. 
 Call Center Rep - Will supply computer and ethernet  
 Kelly Services is hiring reps to answer multiple calls for a variety of clients, update customer database, respond to inquiries, process requests, and other related duties. You must be able to multitask and have your own internet with a cell phone. Pays $12 per hour. Full-time. 
 Customer Experience Specialist 
 Chegg is now hiring candidates to diagnose student's needs via phone, chat, or email to resolve issues, monitor users on the site, and work on projects. You must be passionate about helping students succeed, have good verbal/listening skills, can pick up new technology quickly, and can work a flexible schedule. 
 Remote Weekend Customer Service Associate 
 Rent the Runway is seeking agents to work Saturdays and Sundays developing lasting relationships with customers, assist customers to find styles that meet their individual needs, and other duties. Requires 2 years customer service experience, be a great communicator, are empathetic, and can problem-solve. Training provided. 
 COVID-19 Contact Tracers - Equipment provided! 
 
 Kindred Healthcare is now hiring anyone located anywhere in the United States to work from home as a contact tracers on a part-time basis. 
 Member Service Rep 
 Virgin Pulse is hiring reps with call center experience to provide support, information, and quality programs to the employees of client companies. You will also help with tech isues related to the website. Requires 1 year customer service experience, can type 30 wpm with accuracy, have strong computer skills, and can learn quickly. Full-time with weekends off. 
 Search Quality Raters - Referral bonus! 
 Sykes is seeking people who are internet savvy to evaluate and rate search engine results. Requires strong computer and analytical skills, and have your own PC, internet, and activated smartphone with wifi capability. Training provided. You will work 15 to 20 hours a week. Set your own schedule. Pays $10 per hour plus $250 for every person you refer. 
 Offsite Invoice Payment Specialist 
 Cass Info is seeking individuals to accurately enter data to pay utility invoices and prepare customized rports. You must type 9,000 kph, be self-motivated, and have a high level of concentration. Hourly pay. 
 Freelance Work - Cash bonuses 
 Clickworker is looking for people to work from home to complete non-phone tasks on your smartphone or computer categorizing data, searching the web, translating, trascribing, etc. Pays weekly and open worldwide. Cash bonuses up to $50 this month! 
 Subject Tutors 
 
 Course Hero is seeking people to work from home helping high school and college students get answers to questions on various subjects. You can work your own schedule. Pays up to $20 per hour. Must reside in the US, UK, CA, AU, or NZ. 
 Nielsen Panel 
 Get paid for your internet usage plus Nielsen gives away $10,000 each nmonth to their members. Join today and start earning as soon you download. 
 Ibotta 
 Get $10 to download this free app plus earn free cash back when you upload your receipts. Get free dinner for a limited time with 100% cashback. 
 Paid Viewpoint 
 
 Get paid cash instantly to take mini polls from your computer. Takes less than 2 minutes of your time. 
 PrizeRebel 
 Get free PayPal cash or gift cards to complete short tasks, watch videos, find offers, answer questions, and more. Bi-weekly contests to earn more cash. Must be 13. Open worldwide.
