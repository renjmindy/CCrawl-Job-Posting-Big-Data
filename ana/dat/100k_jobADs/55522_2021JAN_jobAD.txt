 A global retailer is currently looking or an Omni-Channel Programme Manager to join the team and work on a huge platform implementation programme. We are looking for someone with good omni-channel experience who has worked across both digital and ecommerce platforms in an agile environment. 
 The Omni-Channel Programme Manager defines, manages and delivers one or more customer-sponsored, cross-functional Programmes to agreed time, cost, quality, risk and benefits specifications. 
 The Omni-Channel Programme Manager's prime responsibility is to ensure that the Programme delivers the business benefit through alignment to the business objectives. 
 The Omni-Channel Programme Manager is one of the primary points of contact between IT and the Business and will lead cross-functional delivery teams to achieve business benefits. 
 TASKS & RESPONSIBILITIES: 
 
 Maintains close working relationships with stakeholders and suppliers in order to better understand conflicting business requirements where they arise and be part of the team to prioritise and control the outputs to the overall benefit of the business. 
 Develops and maintains relationships across IT and wider business community 
 Establishes and manages an effective stakeholder management and communications strategy 
 Develops and maintains relationships at appropriate level 
 Develops and maintains relationships at senior level with external suppliers, in support of Project Managers 
 Develops and maintains relationships with the Programme's functional team members 
 Acts as an ambassador for IT within the broader business communities 
 Leads, manages and develops the functional Programme team to meet the requirements of the business and performance standards 
 Defines the strategic direction for the Programme 
 Encourages a culture of continuous improvement and best practice to achieve operational effectiveness across the Programme 
 Sets clear objectives and deliverables to ensure that Programme's functional team perform against defined requirements 
 Motivates and develops the team through delegating tasks according to team strengths and interests 
 Manages change and transition effectively to minimise the disruption to project delivery and people 
 Works with the appropriate stakeholders to define and agree the vision, requirements, scope, objectives and desired benefits of the Programme 
 Defines and executes Programme and Project governance to ensure the Programme and associated Projects are aligned coherently with each other and comply with current governance standards 
 Plans, monitors and controls the overall output of the Programme and Projects within his/her area to ensure that these deliver the required products, to the required standard of quality and within the specified constraints of time and cost 
 Monitors and controls the overall output of the Programme and projects within his/her area to ensure that these deliver the business benefits defined in the business case 
 Resolves issues and initiates corrective actions 
 Reports to the appropriate governance bodies as required with the Programme on status, plans, costs, dependencies, issues and risks 
 Deploys best practice across projects and aligns with current standards and initiatives 
 Manages closure of the Programme and projects back into business as usual 
 
 ROLE REQUIREMENTS: 
 
 A professional business planning/management, programme planning qualification or equivalent 
 Omni-Channel project/programme management experience 
 Web application, digital, ecommerce experience 
 Agile methodology 
 Professional Qualification (degree or significant industry experience) 
 Significant project and programme management experience 
 Proven operational or line management experience in a senior position 
 Understanding and experience of more than one project management methodology 
 Excellent inter-personal and communications skills 
 Extensive network and supplier base 
 Good facilitation skills - able to run workshops and external project meetings 
 Substantial experience in influencing at Board level 
 Ability to quickly assimilate knowledge from outside own area of expertise 
 A solid understanding of Programme Accounting including budget analysis and forecasting 
 
 KEY SKILLS 
 
 Customer focused approach 
 Commercially aware with ability to influence at all levels 
 Ability to deliver projects on time and to budget 
 Good problem resolution skills 
 Ability to deliver quality work according to prescribed standards 
 Work in a structured and analytical manner 
 Ability to communicate complex technical issues to a non-technical audience 
 Able to work as part of a team or on own initiative
