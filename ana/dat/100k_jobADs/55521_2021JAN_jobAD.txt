 Qatar Caregiver Job 
 Posted on  February 7, 2013  by  caregiver 
 Herbert’s International Inc., a POEA accredited recruitment agency located in Ermita in Manila, is currently accepting applicants for  Qatar Caregiver Job . This Qatar caregiver job has job vacancies for 5 applicants who will be selected based on the following qualifications: 
 
 Female 
 With at least 2 years experience 
 Good personality 
 With good communication skills 
 Can finish 2 years contract 
 
 Those who are qualified and interested in applying for the said Qatar Caregiver Job should submit the following requirements as soon as possible to the office of Herbert’s International Inc. 
 
 Resume 
 Certificates 
 2 pcs. 2×2 pictures 
 1 copy whole body picture
