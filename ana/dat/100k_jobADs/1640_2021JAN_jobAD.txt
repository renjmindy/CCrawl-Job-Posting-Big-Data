 Consultant: 
 
 
 Looking for something dynamic, forward-thinking? Passionate about Digital Marketing? Would you like to work on international campaigns? 
 We are looking for someone with a strong commercial focus and a huge passion for marketing who will bring bags of initiative. 
 Situated in the centre of Birmingham, our client is a working on a large-scale international project, with huge potential. 
 Key duties and responsibilities: 
 
 Creating, planning and launching multi-channel digital marketing campaigns 
 Measuring and reporting the performance of all digital marketing campaigns 
 Working across a number of digital channels, such as email marketing, social media, display advertising and SEO 
 Driving traffic to the company website and driving leads 
 Creating and implementing a social media strategy for the business 
 
 Minimum requirements: 
 
 Substantial experience within digital marketing within a B2B environment 
 Hands on experience of all digital channels, including email marketing, SEO, PPC, social media etc 
 Degree in marketing or equivalent (CIM, IDM). 
 The ability to keep calm under pressure and work to deadlines. 
 Strong people management skills, verbal and written communication and to be able to work effectively as part of a team. 
 Be creative and have an innovative approach. 
 
 Please note, this role is a fixed term contract until March 2023 
 To apply for the Digital Marketing Manager position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
