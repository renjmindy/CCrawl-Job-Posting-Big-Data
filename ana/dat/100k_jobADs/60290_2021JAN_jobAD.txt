 Dual studies at the State Academy of Studies Riesa 
 The VKTA is a practical partner of the University of Cooperative Education Riesa in training in laboratory and process engineering. 
 We regularly offer students an apprenticeship for practical training within the framework of the practice-oriented dual study course Bachelor of Science in Radiation Technology or Environmental Technology on 1 October. 
 The following requirements are expected: 
 
 the general higher education entrance qualification (Abitur) or 
 the technical college entrance qualification or 
 the subject-related higher education entrance qualification (scientific orientation) 
 Readiness and health suitability to work in radiation protection areas
