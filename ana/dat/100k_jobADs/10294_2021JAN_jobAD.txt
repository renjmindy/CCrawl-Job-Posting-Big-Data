 Civil Labourer 
 Job description 
 WorkPac Townsville is currently recruiting for multiple Civil Labourers for a number of Earthwork and Pipe Trenching Projects in Townsville. 
 To apply you MUST have: 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 Confined Space Certificate 
 
 
 Be physically fit & capable of working in extreme heat conditions and lead stream environments 
 
 
 Be reliable, proactive and hard working 
 
 
 Able to pass a Pre Employment Medical and Drug and Alcohol Screening 
 
 
 Apply Now! 
 Camila Lazcano 
 Phone - 07 4744 6905 
 WorkPac is Australia's largest, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Looking after our employees is important to us.
