 Head of Compliance 
 
 Location 
 
 Kidderminster, Worcestershire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £75000.00 - £85000.00 per annum + car, pension, PHI, bonus 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Jamie Carter

 jamiecarter@workwithglee.com

 Published: 
 
 11 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-03-17 
 A leading manufacturing and distribution company based in Kidderminster, is searching for an ACA Qualified Accountant who has gone down the Risk and Compliance career path since qualification. This position will pay a salary in the region of £75k - £85k + car and bonus. 
 This is an opportunity to work for a fast growth, entrepreneurial PLC, who have experienced substantial growth through acquisition in recent years. As a result, they have created a position for a Head of Risk and Compliance to come in to the Business and build relationships with the various different Businesses they have acquired, in order to create a risk and compliance framework to implement throughout the organisation. 
 We are searching for a well educated, high achieving and ambitious individual, who is ACA qualified with substantial post qualification experience gained in risk and compliance positions. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
