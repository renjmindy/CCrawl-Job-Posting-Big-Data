 FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT AFTER A JOB LOSS – VIDEO 
 The Financial Management After A Job Loss Video is designed to help individuals better manage their financial situation. The trainer offers help with tips and techniques of shopping, spending and saving.

 Description 
 
 Description 
 People preparing for a transition in their livelihood are wise to manage their financial resources carefully.  The Financial Management After A Job Loss Video  offers practical training in the basics of budgeting, debt management, financial projections, and ways to conserve resources. There are many questions facing people who must deal with financial uncertainty. “How will I make it?” “Can I afford to go back to school?” “Can we manage what we have?” These questions are answered in this video presentation. 
 Viewers are encouraged to take an active role in keeping track of, and managing their finances. Financial goals and planning for the future are also discussed. They are encouraged to bring their own concerns and financial questions to light for the purpose of discussing with their own families.
