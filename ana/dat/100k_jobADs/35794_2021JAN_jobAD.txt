 Jobs for Caregivers 
 Posted on  October 23, 2012  by  caregiver 
 Two POEA accredited caregiver agencies are currently accepting applicants for their POEA approved  Jobs for Caregivers . The said caregiver agencies are located in Makati and Quezon City whose Job Orders were approved by the POEA last October 10, 12, and 18, 2012 allowing the said caregiver agencies to recruit a total of 54 caregiver job applicants for placement in Israel jobs for caregiver. Listed below are the names of the said caregiver agencies and the number of available jobs for caregivers for each agency. 
 CPM Manpower Services, Inc. ----------- 30 jobs for caregivers 
JAO International Manpower Services --- 24 jobs for caregivers 
 All those who wish to apply for any of the said Jobs for Caregivers should proceed as soon as possible to the office of any of the caregiver agencies listed above to submit their comprehensive resume.
