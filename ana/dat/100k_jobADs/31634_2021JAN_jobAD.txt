 Senior Counsel - Toronto, ON or Seattle, WA 
 About Tilray 
Tilray is a global leader in cannabis research, cultivation, processing and distribution. We aspire to lead, legitimize and define the future of our industry by building the world’s most trusted cannabis and hemp company. We are committed to setting the gold standard of care and excellence in our industry. 
 Looking to develop your career at the forefront of a rapidly expanding industry? 
 Tilray’s values guide each employee’s work every day. Those values are: 
Act with Integrity – we rely on honest, transparent and trustworthy behavior and communication 
Be Steadfast – we have an unwavering obligation to our patients, consumers, providers, colleagues and community to be sincere, accountable and responsive. 
Care – to achieve abundance in our crops and our lives, we must act with passion, focus and dedication 
Collaborate – we believe that none of us is as smart as all of us and together we succeed 
Pursue Excellence – we believe in our ability to spark innovation, achieve greatness, and overcome any risk, challenge or failure met along the way. 
 Reporting to the General Counsel, the Senior Counsel will provide broad support to Tilray’s cannabis and hemp business. You will be working alongside and in support of our highly experienced business team and legal professionals. The legal department is a great environment to learn and develop as an attorney. 
Role and Responsibilities 
A leader in the legal function focused on supporting the growth of the business across all categories which includes: 
Identifying and managing key legal/regulatory risks and opportunities in the marketing, distribution, import/export, and registration of medical and adult-use cannabis products, CBD and hemp products 
Supporting the negotiation and documentation of commercial transactions, M&A, and partnerships, weighing multiple legal and non-legal inputs 
Working closely with business units to ensure that all commercial activities are conducted in a strategic and compliant manner 
Staying up to date on regulatory framework and evolution to be able to counsel the business appropriately 
Providing guidance on regulatory interpretation and training on topics of interest and related to compliance 
Providing guidance and review of marketing, packaging, advertising, and product claims, and support the development of new and innovative product offerings 
Drafting, negotiating and providing legal counsel on a variety of commercial matters 
Dealing with complex, significant matters that cut across legal and related areas weighing multiple inputs 
Serving as an in-house generalist and attending to a wide variety of legal and quasi-legal matters on an as-needed basis; and 
Undertake special projects as required to support new business initiatives. 
Working with broader legal team to ensure efficient and effective delivery of legal services in-house, including by: 
Maintaining template collections and intake systems, and training the business on their use; 
Implementing technology solutions to streamline legal administrative tasks 
Collaborating with and providing support to other members of the legal team; 
Managing work of outside counsel and efficient and cost-effective support; and 
Supporting the continuous improvement of legal processes to promote efficiency 
Contribute positively to the culture of the legal team and the company as a whole 
Due to the dynamic nature of the industry, the scope of your job may evolve and change by business demands and regulatory/ legal framework 
 Qualifications and Education Requirements 
Must be licensed to practice law in the United States or any Canadian province 
Minimum 8-10 years of experience working in a top tier law firm and/or in-house legal department 
Leadership experience preferred 
Proven negotiation, drafting and analytical skills for various types of complex commercial contracts and transactions 
Prior experience providing legal counsel directly to business clients 
Must be relationship-oriented with excellent business judgment, strategic thinking, and project management skills, and prepared to work in a cross-functional, fast-paced team environment 
Must possess excellent verbal and written communication skills, including ability to brief and communicate complex issues in a succinct manner 
 Who You Are 
Excited to work in a fast paced and intellectually challenging environment 
Possess ability to handle competing priorities and quickly acclimate to unique regulatory environments 
Have a strong work ethic and be able to work well under pressure, independently and with minimal supervision 
Have flexibility to travel on an as-needed basis 
Ability to manage multiple projects at once, to synthesize information from multiple sources, and to follow through and meet deadlines 
Resourceful and creative in your approach to problem solving 
Focused on pragmatic business advice and solutions 
Professional and collaborative with the ability to build and maintain trusted relationships with stakeholders 
Positive “can-do” attitude 
Excited to be supporting work across the global in a fast growing and evolving industry
