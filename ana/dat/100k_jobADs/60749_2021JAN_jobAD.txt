 Full-Time PHP Developer 
 Authority Movers  - Posted by  Jim Peters  -  _ _ Anywhere 
 Job Description 
 The  Authority Movers  company invites a PHP developer who loves modern technology, who is easy to climb and has a good sense of humor, who is ready to learn and master the technology stack quickly, both independently and with the support of colleagues. We are looking for a person who burns with development, technologies, tools so much that he writes code, reads profile articles and books, solves problems because he likes it very much, not by force. If you recognize yourself in this description, we will be happy to see you as part of our team. 
 PREFFERED SKILLS 
 
 PHP 5/7 knowledge 
 Okay, YII2; 
 knowledge and experience in applying design patterns 
 layout skills (HTML, CSS, SASS, LESS, bootstrap) 
 SQL knowledge at developer level (Mysql, MongoDB) 
 git experience 
 Linux experience, Nginx 
 
 RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 corporate project development and support 
 case analysis and evaluation 
 code maintenance 
 Development of new modules and support of existing internal projects 
 
 In response, we offer the following conditions: 
 
 remoted work 
 career progression 
 corporate parties, office parties and a lot of positive things. 
 competitive salary, biannual indexation based on Performance Review results 
 continuous development and exchange of experience within the team, training and certification at company expense
