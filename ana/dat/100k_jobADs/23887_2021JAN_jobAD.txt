 Job Seeker Resources 
 The Learning & Career Center has a workforce specialist on-site at LPKC three days per week (Monday, Wednesday and Friday) to provide job seekers and employers with services and support including: 
 
 Free online job postings 
 Job seeker screening 
 Information sessions and job fairs 
 Assessments 
 Subsidized work-based learning 
 Labor market information 
 Salary comparison analysis 
 Job description development 
 
 Find information on topics such as resume writing, interviewing preparation, career advising, hiring events, job search tools and much more at  workforcepartnership.com .
