 Technology 
 Our technology team is responsible for developing our award-winning SaaS human applications as part of Workhuman Cloud, and innovating across our internal infrastructure. 
 
 Our powerhouse team of tech-savvy experts drives our business forward with the highest level of development, support, and a continuous improvement strategy. 
 Join one of our teams and work with us on exciting technologies and large-scale projects. 
 Our Global IT Infrastructure Team:  This team looks after our cloud and corporate IT infrastructures. 
 Our Cloud Infrastructure Team:  This team takes care of our production infrastructure. We are focused on maximizing the latest cloud, DevOps and infrastructure technology, ensuring our cloud infrastructure is secure, scalable, and available 24/7. 
 Our Corporate IT Infrastructure Team:  This is our internal operations backbone, supporting our internal teams by providing top-level support and service, and staying on the forefront of security and technological advancements. 
 Our Systems Engineering Team  : This team ensures our back-office systems and infrastructure are available and optimized 24/7. We’re building the very best and constantly evolving. 
 Our Architecture Team:  This team works on our architecture roadmap items, ensuring they are applicable across systems and feature teams. 
 Our Engineering Team:  Our scrum teams consist of software development (Java) test and automation engineers, product owners, UX designers, and software architects. Product management provides strategic direction, supported by respective members of the executive team. 
 Our Framingham and Dublin offices remain on the forefront of innovation. If you want to have purpose and meaning with your work and thrive in a fast-paced and growing environment – come work with us.
