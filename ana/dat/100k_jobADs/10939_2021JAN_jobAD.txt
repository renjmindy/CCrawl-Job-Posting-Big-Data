I have written articles about a lot of these opportunities in the past for those who strictly want to work from home without speaking to customers or anyone else. My most recent post was entitled, " Top 60 Non Phone Work at home Jobs " that include some global opportunities.

This post will provide you with additional companies that you can apply to that offer hourly pay, a set rate, will send you a check in the mail and so much more.
