 Caregiver Job Israel 
 Posted on  November 23, 2009  by  caregiver 
 The country of Israel is one of the major destinations of Filipino caregiver job applicants who are seeking employment in caregiver jobs abroad. And to help Filipino caregiver job applicants realize their dream, the POEA approved two job orders allowing a Philippine based recruitment agency to recruit qualified applicants for Caregiver Job Israel. The said caregiver job Israel was approved for recruitment by the POEA last November 12 and 18, 2009 for a recruitment agency based in Parañaque City and has a corresponding job order balance of 13 and 16 caregiver job vacancy respectively. 
 To apply for the said caregiver job Israel, all interested applicants are advised to proceed directly to the office of the recruiting agency. For more details on the said caregiver job Israel, you can contact the recruiting agency directly on their contact infos stated below. 
 Star World Int 'l Manpower & Placement Agency, Inc. 
231 Quirino Avenue, Tambo 
Parañaque City 
Tel. Nos: 8524575 / 8546100 / 8548042
