 Would You Like to Teach Crochet? 
 Posted on  December 6, 2012  by   Morris and Sons Team 
 0 
 
 Do you love to crochet? Are you a passionate teacher? 
 We are currently looking for an experienced crochet teacher to teach classes at our Melbourne store, starting January 2013. If this sounds like a job for you please enquire via email on  info@morrisandsons.com.au  or by phone on 02 9299 8588. 
 Happy Crocheting!
