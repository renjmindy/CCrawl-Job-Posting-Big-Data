 High - Materials Controller 
 Job description 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities. 
 About the Role 
 Support the Project Lead, to test and roll out SAP modules, Remote Logistics Management (RLM) and Extended Warehouse Management (EWM) 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 Your duties and responsibilities includes but not limited to:- 
 
 
 Coach and advise team members ensuring effective RLM and EWM process and system use. 
 
 
 Support the Line Managers with embedding processes during Hypercare/Warranty phase by: 
 
 
 Triaging 1SAP process issues from 1SAP system issues - ensuring only valid system issues are logged 
 
 
 Monitoring and escalating 1SAP service tickets as required 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 To be successful in this role you will have the following: - 
 
 
 Strong understanding of inbound logistics, inventory, and warehouse management business processes. 
 
 
 Experience in warehouse management and logistics 
 
 
 Experience in SAP Remote Logistics Management (RLM) and Extended Warehouse Management (EWM) 
 
 
 Previous experience in SAP implementations favorable 
 
 
 Demonstrated ability to coach and enable team members. 
 
 
 Ability to clearly and effectively communicate at all levels of the organisation. 
 
 
 Demonstrated analytical and problem-solving ability. 
 
 
 Demonstrated ability to establish and maintain credible and constructive relationships. 
 
 
 Demonstrated ability to engage or motivate others without the use of formal authority. 
 
 
 Apply Now 
 For more information please contact Juanique Liebenberg on 03 9647 5148 for a confidential conversation.
