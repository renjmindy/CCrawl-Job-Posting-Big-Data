 Senior Nurse 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Senior Nurse to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the South West London area. 
 As the Senior Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This special nursing home offers residential care, nursing care and specialist dementia care in a warm, homely environment. 
 The highly trained and dedicated staff team get to know each resident and their family individually offering personalised care and support 
 As the Senior Nurse your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     To lead the care teams to ensure high standards of care are provided to the service users
 
 
 
     You will ensure that the residents' medical, physical and emotional requirements are met
 
 
 
     Mentoring and motivating colleagues to champion residents' safety and dignity will be part of your role
 
 
 
     As well as helping to maintain accurate records, you will also undertake ongoing training of the new staff
 
 
 
     You will act as a person in charge, standing in for the Manager where required where your duties will be to: lead, motivate and mentor the care team to ensure the smooth running of the home;
 
 
 
     Distribution of medication, dressings, catheterisations, tube feeding and management of medical conditions
 
 
 
     Liaise with health and social care professionals for the improvement of the home and the care of the residents
 
 
 
     Promoting the service users independence, choice and dignity by delivering the best standard of care
 
 
 
 
 An ideal Senior Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Provide both residential and nursing care to older people with nursing
 
 
 
     Experience with dementia would suit
 
 
 
     Able to show empathy and warmth
 
 
 
     A bubbly and outgoing personality
 
 
 
     Able to write care plans, wound care, administering medication, attending MDT meetings and other nursing skills
 
 
 
     Assist in assessments
 
 
 
     Delegate duties to junior staff
 
 
 
 
 **The successful Senior Nurse will receive an excellent salary of £19.47 per hour and the annual salary for this position is £37,966.50 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role for 37.5 hours a week working throughout Day Shifts only.
