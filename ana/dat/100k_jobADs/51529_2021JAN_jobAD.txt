 Technician Trainer 
 Job description 
 
 
 $40.54/ hour 
 
 
 6 Month Contract with possible extension 
 
 
 Day Shift Only 
 
 
 ABOUT OUR CLIENT 
 Our client is Australia's largest coal miner and exporter, the world's largest supplier to the sea borne coking coal market and largest coal producer and supplier of metallurgical coal. The client has several mine sites across the Bowen Basin consisting primarily of open cut mines 
 ABOUT THE ROLE 
 Seeking experienced Technician Trainer to work in the Site Systems Department of a mine located near Blackwater. 
 DUTIES & RESPONSBILBITIES 
 
 
 Administering and ensuring the integrity of the sites training records 
 
 
 Reporting of Site Compliance with regards to legislation and organisational capability 
 
 
 Assist with verification and Data entry into Training systems 
 
 
 Ensure all training documentation is verified and complaint 
 
 
 Proactive continuous improvement of data processing and administrative training processes 
 
 
 Customer service 
 
 
 SKILLS & EXPERIENCE 
 The successful candidate will have knowledge and relevant experience in training within a coal mining environment, with the ability to work with large volumes of data efficiently, both paper based and electronic. Will have relevant experience as an administrator, high levels of initiative, impeccable attention to detail, positive attitude, and excellent communication skills with the ability to manage high level of interaction with all levels of the business. Demonstrated success in MS Office and Learning Management Systems will ensure success in this role. 
 CULTURE & BENEFITS 
 
 
 Monday - Friday 
 
 
 Super Paid on all Rostered Hours 
 
 
 Day Shift Only 
 
 
 MUST have accommodation in the Blackwater area (no camp included with the role) 
 
 
 ABOUT WORKPAC 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors.
