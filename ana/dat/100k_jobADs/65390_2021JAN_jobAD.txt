 Job description 
 We'll be opening soon in Duncan, and are now accepting applications from independent health and wellness professionals looking to join the Purely Wellness group! 
 We're open to members from a wide range of disciplines, including physicians, therapists, RMTs, chiropractors, counsellors, acupuncturists, nutritionists, naturopaths, and more. 
 There are several rooms and options available for practitioners to share our brand new facility, along with resources to help manage and grow your business. 
 Practitioner rooms will be ready in December, and available to use free of charge by all founding members until January while we ramp-up operations. 
 Please get in touch here or at www.purelywellness.com to tell us about yourself, discuss your needs, or to book a tour of our space while still under construction.
