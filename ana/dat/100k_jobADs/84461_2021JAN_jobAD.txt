 Get Details On Two Workamping Jobs! 
 Posted by 120632 on 02/21/2020 
 Get All the Details On These Workamping Opportunities! 
 Jobinars (free online webinars) are a fun and informative way to connect with Employers who want to tell you all about their Workamping programs! 
 These Employers will show you photos and take you through the details of what they have to offer to Workampers. You'll leave having a much better feeling of if this job is right for you or not. 
 If you can be on the webinar live, please join in!  You can type in any questions that you'd like to ask the Employer who is presenting. 
 And if you can’t join us live, please register anyway and we will send you an email after the webinar takes place with a link to watch the recording at your convenience. 
 
 Upcoming Online Jobinars 
From Two Long-Time Workamper Employers 
 Yellowstone General Stores - Wednesday, March 4 at 2pm CT 
 [Webinar was cancelled by Delaware North due to their presenter being sick] 
 Live the Dream in Yellowstone and join us for an informational Job-Fair-Style Event! Learn everything you need to know about Workamping with Yellowstone General Stores next summer and how we can help make living in Yellowstone a reality for you. Join our Jobinar to work with Delaware North in Yellowstone. Don’t miss this opportunity to participate in this event! It’s your first step to a whole new way of experiencing Yellowstone. 
 Join this enthusiastic staff and make life-long friends while enjoying first-hand the amazing wonders of living in Yellowstone National Park. We will do our best to work with your available dates and find the best Yellowstone General Store for your Yellowstone interests. We offer full hookup RV sites and a few with comfort stations. 
 
 The Unbeetable Experience - Wednesday, March 11 at 11am CT 
 Register by clicking here 
 Learn everything you need to know about Workamping for Express Employment Professionals recruiting for the Sugar Beet Harvest. Presented by the Express Employment Professionals Training Team, you'll get to see and hear the details of this "unbeetable" opportunity! 
 Earn up to $2,500 in 2 weeks! (Harvest length varies based on mother nature.) Eastern North Dakota & western Minnesota; western North Dakota & eastern Montana. Camping fees paid! Work at the receiving stations - not in the fields. 
 
 Michigan Beet Harvest - Wednesday, March 18 at 11am CT 
 Register by clicking here 
 Learn everything you need to know about Workamping for Express Employment Professionals at the MICHIGAN Sugar Beet Harvest! Presented live by the Express Employment Professionals Training Team, you'll get to see and hear the details of their NEW opportunity! 
 Earn up to $1,050 per week. Camping fees paid! NOT working in the fields. No experience necessary. Weekly overtime! Positions include Piler Operators & Special Equipment Operators. 
 We look forward to seeing you online with us!
