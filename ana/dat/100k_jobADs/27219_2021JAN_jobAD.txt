 Marketing Coordinator 
 Annapurna Interactive Apply for this Job 
 Posted 
 October 30, 2020 
 Full Time 
 Los Angeles 
 Remote 
 We're looking for a marketing coordinator to assist with projects from pre-production through post-launch. You will work with our internal team, various development teams, and external platform partners to assist with campaign, roadmap, and sales planning, asset production and coordination, and other tasks related to marketing, social media, content creation, CRM, merchandising, partnerships, etc. 
 The ideal candidate should at least have two years of full-time experience in a marketing or production role or related field, preferably in the video game industry. Experience with creative suites (Adobe, etc.) is essential. Familiarity with platform-specific development portals is also a plus. 
 ‍ 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Liaise and organize assets for campaigns, sales promotions, brand efforts: social media, email, web, video, storefronts, digital advertising. 
 Suggest timelines, track progress, and flag potential roadblocks for marketing needs related to each of our game projects. 
 Create assets as requested or edit existing assets provided by development teams to fulfill existing needs or incoming requests. 
 Support our online and retail merchandising efforts through self-sourced means as well as external/licensing partners. 
 Assist with the execution of public-facing initiatives, such as conventions, PR events, livestreams, and other opportunities as they come up. 
 Work with our team to contribute ideas, plans, feedback related to marketing and project-specific strategy. 
 
 ‍ 
 Requirements 
 
 Proven experience in a marketing or production related role, preferably in the video game industry. 
 Project + time management, communication, organizational, and interpersonal skills are a must. 
 Understanding of digital marketing assets, specifications, and requirements for various platforms. 
 Experience working within a small team. 
 Experience working with external partners and a strong ability to maintain those relationships. 
 Knowledgeable in social media, digital office suite products, and creative suite products related to asset creation. 
 
 ‍ 
 All roles are remote for the immediate future, with the goal of being based in Los Angeles once it’s safe to return to studio work. 
 
 ‍

 Please email us at  hiring@annapurnainteractive.com  with a copy of your resume attached and the job title in within the subject line.

 ‍  About Annapurna Interactive 
 Established in 2016, Annapurna Interactive works with independent game creators from around the world, helping them create and release personal experiences for everyone. The company is a division of  Annapurna Pictures . 
 Some notable games released by the company include  WHAT REMAINS OF EDITH FINCH  (2017 BAFTA Best Game),  FLORENCE  (2018 BAFTA Mobile Game), and  OUTER WILDS  (2019 BAFTA Best Game).
