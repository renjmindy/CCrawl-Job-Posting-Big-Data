 Our client is a tier one hedge fund looking for C++ Developers across market-making, delta-one and arbitrage. Their focus for 2020 and onwards is to make Asia Pacific their leading region globally, and they are piling resources into building the best quant / tech and trading team in APAC. 
 Opportunity: 
 
 As a candidate you will work as part of an established, experienced development team to build best in class technology platforms integrated with real time trading paradigms 
 You will propose new designs and modify existing ones to continuously improve performance, functionality, and stability of the system. 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 Strong programming skills in C++ development experience on a UNIX platform. 
 Professional experience with multi-threaded application design and development. 
 Experience with key languages, e.g. Python, R, Perl, shell scripting. 
 Knowledge of different UNIX IPC mechanisms (sockets, shared memory, semaphores, etc.). 
 Professional experience with distributed systems. 
 Ability to analyze and solve complex business and technical problems with robust, resilient and scalable technical solutions. 
 An understanding of financial markets and instrument types. 
 Solid communication and interpersonal skills applicable to a dynamic trading environment. 
 
 Incentives: 
 
 The opportunity to be part of a tier one hedge fund that is determined to heavily invest in the best-in-class technology. 
 Greenfield buildout opportunity. 
 
 If you are interested in this position, or would like to explore other opportunities within banking and finance technology, please send your detailed resume to anitha.suresh@bahpartners.com or call +852 5525 2105 for a confidential discussion. 
 BAH Partners is the leading supplier of the best finance technology talent for most of Hong Kong's top-tier financial institutions.
