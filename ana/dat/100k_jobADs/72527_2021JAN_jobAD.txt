 Outpatient Department Manager 
 An outstanding new job opportunity has arisen for a dedicated Outpatient Department Manager to work in an exceptional hospital based in the Stafford, Staffordshire area.

 An ideal Outpatient Department Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Significant exposure in a senior role to external stakeholders such as Consultants, Physicians, Allied Health professionals, patients and suppliers
 
 
 
     Independent sector experience would be desirable
 
 
 
     Advanced Life Support is an advantage
 
 
 
     Immediate Life Support
