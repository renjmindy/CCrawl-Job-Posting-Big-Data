 Administrator 
 Job description 
 
 
 WORKING TUESDAY TO SATURDAY 
 
 
 3 MONTH CONTRACT 
 
 
 35.00 P/H + SUPER 
 
 
 WorkPac is currently seeking a Transport Administrator to join one of their departments located in Newman. Working as part of a team you will be involved in everything from high volume data entry, general administration, liaising with clients, inputting dispatch orders, filing run sheets, update transport request registers with all new and completed details, enter all trailer movements. If you like a fast paced working environment we would like to hear from you. 
 Skills & Experience Required 
 
 
 Transport knowledge desirable 
 
 
 Proficient in the use of SAP 
 
 
 Right to work in Australia
