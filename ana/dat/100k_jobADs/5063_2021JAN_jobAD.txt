 Education Jobs in New Zealand 
 Are you looking for New Zealand employment opportunities in education?  Working In has the latest education sector jobs available for overseas candidates.  If you are an educator who is qualified to work in New Zealand, your next role could be with one of the country's leading employers. 
 New Zealand's education system is admired internationally, attracting over 130,000 students every year from overseas.  Whether you are a tertiary professor or a qualified primary school teacher, there are many opportunities throughout the country for qualified overseas candidates to get a great job in the education sector.  Advance your teaching career by finding a job in New Zealand today!

 Search Education and Training Vacancies 
 Each New Zealand school year begins in February, and most teaching vacancies occur between September and January.  However, you are likely to find vacancies all year round. 
 To search for current training and education jobs in New Zealand, use the Working In search tool.  You can filter education sector jobs by region, city, company name, experience level and salary range to find a role suited to your individual requirements and lifestyle.  International candidates who have the right to work in New Zealand are welcome to fill education and training job vacancies. 
 Education jobs in New Zealand for International Teachers 
 To get a full-time teaching job in New Zealand, you must be registered and hold a current practising certificate.  Working In has a variety of job opportunities in New Zealand for qualified international teachers. 
 In the higher education sector, there is a particularly strong demand for qualified tertiary lecturers - a job that currently appears on the Government's skills shortage list.  Thus, now is a good time for overseas workers to apply for these roles.  Browse our available vacancies today to find an education job that perfectly matches your skills and experience.
