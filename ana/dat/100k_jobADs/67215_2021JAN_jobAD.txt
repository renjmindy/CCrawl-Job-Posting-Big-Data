 Jobs Caregiver Canada 
 Posted on  March 10, 2009  by  caregiver 
 A caregiver employment agency based in Quebec City, Canada and managed by three business executives is seeking qualified caregivers to provide their expertise and personal service to their increasing number of clientele. 
 They specialize in: 
 
 Newborn care - nannies and nurses 
 Children of all ages 
 Live in caregivers 
 Live out caregivers 
 
 For overseas workers who wish to come to Canada as Live In Caregiver, and need an employer, please download and fill up their application form which can be found at the URL below. Once it is filled in, please fax or mail it to their offices. 
 www.mlhhomecare.com/?m=employment.php
