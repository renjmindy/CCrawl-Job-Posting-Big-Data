 [All jobs](/jobs?search=&location=&depart=subcat=All jobs)  Marketing 
 You passionately own the Product Marketing responsibility for North America where you deeply understand how to execute complex enterprise software product launches simply, beautifully and successfully with your hands-on experience. Next to being hands-on, you run and motivate a cross-functional team and successfully collaborate with multiple customer-facing as well as internal teams. You will be reporting to the SVP Strategy. 
The Product Marketing team is the critical facilitator of product knowledge within Backbase, ensuring product launches are executed successfully and product information is always up to date and ready for use by our customer-facing teams. 
The team owns go-to-market strategies and helps to define compelling features/benefits backed by competitive insights, customer centricity, and market trends. Responsibilities include being the marketing business partner for a product portfolio, generating and amplifying customer insights, and facilitating modern GTM tactics and strategies that make our customer-facing teams successful. 
 
 
 __5+ years of experience in B2B product- or performance marketing required. Banking, payments, tech or SaaS experience is a requirement. However, a can-do mentality is of more importance than many years of experience; 
 
 
 __Street smart and digital savvy. Proven to be able to do more with less. Do-it-yourself mentality. A true team player, hands-on and self-motivated, able to drive progress and hit deadlines; 
 
 
 __Creative and design-minded. You have a passion for design and showcase this actively by optimising your deliverables for show-time. This means you polish your deliverables until they’re an absolute WOW. You’re detail-oriented and easily push deliverables from good from great; 
 
 
 __Solid experience with both technical and commercial product communications 
 
 
 __Mastered in IT, Business or Marketing; 
 
 
 __Strategic thinker, able to balance mid-term vision with short-term practical steps, tactics and goals; 
 
 
 __Ability to grasp complex, technical subjects and make them easy to understand; 
 
 
 __Excellent English speaking, writing, and presentation skills, as well as the ability to persuade, inspire, and motivate others; 
 
 
 __Strong project management skills and the ability to act decisively. Comfortable working across time zones and multiple cultures; 
 
 
 __You have the courage to learn and to share your learning process with us.

 THE JOB AT BACKBASE 
 You will closely collaborate with Product Management, Sales and Marketing in all the go-to-markets that you and your team prepare. Day-to-day, you’ll sit with one foot in marketing and another in product, working closely with both teams to share insights and turn them into beautiful and impactful deliverables. 
 In everything you do, you have a strong design angle and eye for quality, along with the ability to grasp and translate technical capabilities into highly relevant and exciting solutions for our customers. You love distilling stories to their simplest form and communicating that in creative ways that others wouldn’t. 
Key Responsibilities: 
 
 
 __GTM Strategies - Collaborate with Strategy, Sales and Product Management to align impactful go-to-market approaches, leveraging market insights and research to make our product positioning highly differentiating 
 
 
 __Product Collateral - Collaborate with Strategy, Sales and Product Management to deeply understand our propositions and translate them into impactful communication content for internal and external usage 
 
 
 __Content Creation - Write and create compelling product collateral, white-papers, brochures and data sheets, ROI calculators, blog posts, case studies and other special purpose content and video 
 
 
 __Thought Leadership - You drive Thought Leadership in your region by being the spokesperson for Backbase in the market, ensuring our point of view is clearly positioned across the Analyst landscape, as well as on any outing of Backbase 
 
 
 __Product Demos - Support the creation of superior product demos with relevant demo journeys to be used by our customer-facing teams, partners and prospects 
 
 
 __Project/Program Management - Drive and coordinate regional launch calendars, ensuring alignment with the sales execution and product roadmap. Hold regional status meetings and meet with individual teams to provide status updates and to close any feedback loops

 BACKBASE IN BOISE 
  __Boise 
 Backbase Boise is home to our #1 growing sales and marketing teams. They focus on finding and nurturing business opportunities in the North American market. Teamwork, friendly competition, and challenging the norm are everyday activities here. 
 And we have quite the view to keep us inspired. Located on the 17th floor of the US Bank Plaza, one of the tallest buildings in the state of Idaho, our office is right in the heart of Downtown Boise. Steps away from many popular restaurants, microbreweries, and shops, you’ll find us well-linked to all regions of the Boise MSA.
