 |  POSITION SUMMARY 
This position requires a detailed understanding of embedded control system electronics and hardware interfacing. You will be part of a team that works on feature enhancements & anomaly resolutions, and test firmware algorithms to manage and interact with hardware. This role will require proven ability to work with electronics hardware personnel and analytical skills to provide issue resolution. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Contributes for the coding and construction of C and C++ based embedded systems 
 Contributes to requirements specification and documentation for feature development. 
 Reports, analyzes, debugs, and corrects anomalies. Supports quality improvements. 
 Works effectively both independently and in a team environment to deliver solutions in a fast-paced environment.
