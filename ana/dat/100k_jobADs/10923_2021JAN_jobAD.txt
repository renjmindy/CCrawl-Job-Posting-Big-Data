 Data Engineering Lead 
 
 Location 
 
 Staffordshire, England 
 
 Sector: 
 
 IT

 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £55000 - £65000 per annum + + car, bonus and pension

 Keeley Goodall

 Consultant: 
 
 
 An exciting opportunity has arose for a Data Engineering Lead to join a customer-centric organisation based in the Staffordshire area, on a business critical migration. 
 Not only will you be a part of a fast-growing Data and Analytics team, you will be pivotal in supporting the delivery of strategy across the area, as well as responsible for the transition to a platform solution. 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 Experience and knowledge of implementing Microsoft Azure products (Data Lake, Factory, Databricks) 
 Track record and responsibility of the migration of a Data Warehouse to Microsoft Azure 
 Expert in SQL and Power BI 
 Understanding and experience of designing data warehouse solutions and providing support to the architecture 
 Implementation of ETL solutions and programming abilities (Python preferable) 
 Previously managed and mentored a small team 
 Ability to communicate effectively and work collaboratively to drive best practice. 
 
 This is a great opportunity for someone who is keen to work for an organisation that values and understands the importance of Data and Analytics. There is a competitive salary on offer, along with additional benefits such as a car allowance, bonus and training opportunities. 
 Please contact Keeley for further information or apply here with your CV. 
 Please note that in the current market we are experiencing a large number of applications. If you have not heard back on your application within 10 days, please consider it unsuccessful. 
 I will endeavour to get back to everyone in due course, but this may take longer than normal. Thank you for your patience and understanding. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
