 An award-winning leisure organisation in the Midlands are looking to recruit a talented Marketing Manager into their business to lead and develop their marketing campaigns for the brand. 
 Reporting into the Head of Marketing this role will be focused on delivering multi-channel marketing, advertising and promotional activity for the brand with responsibilities including: 
 
 Delivering and managing seasonal marketing plans 
 Managing relationships with trade, affiliates and online partners 
 Create CRM plans to engage and develop target audiences 
 Managing and delivering marketing plans on time and on budget 
 Managing external agency relationships 
 Managing press and media enquiries 
 Working alongside the wider marketing team to develop the brand 
 Competitor analysis 
 Management reporting on activity, ROI and creating future recommendations 
 
 To be considered for this role you'll ideally have gained your experience within a leisure or retail environment and have the following skills: 
 
 Broad marketing experience including - trade marketing, ATL advertising, digital, social media, print collateral and PR 
 Experience of managing external agencies 
 Line management experience and be currently working at a Marketing Manager level
