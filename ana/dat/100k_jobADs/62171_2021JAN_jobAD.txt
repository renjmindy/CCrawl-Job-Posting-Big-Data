 WHY DO WE SHOW EXPIRED LISTINGS? 
 STILL HIRING DURING COVID-19    Safe Harbor Boys Home seeks mission-focused "called to serve" couples with varied skills and talents to mentor, supervise, "parent" and direct our residents. Live on campus couples provide direct care for a small group of residents (12), age 14 to 17, to foster their character, spiritual, emotional growth, along with vocational skill sets. Program provides live in quarters aboard one of our program vessels (power or sailboat) OR you may bring your own vessel or RV. 
 Electric connections & other services available for RV's.   Seeking couples who have practical life experience,  boating, sailing  ,  general maintenance  experience, vocational skills - gardening, off grid living, carpentry, mechanical, sewing, canvas work, boat repair, marina work, etc..are assets.  See our website for more information.   Houseparent shifts rotate with two weeks on and one week off.   We require marriage according to Biblical standards and our Statement of Faith.  Salary for couples start at $48,000.  Health Insurance, a 403(b) retirement program, paid time off, dining on campus, and other benefits available.  We have some restrictions on pets.
