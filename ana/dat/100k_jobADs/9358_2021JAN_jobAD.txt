 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for July 
 Posted on  August 6, 2010  by  caregiver 
 For the month of July, the POEA approved four job orders allowing four POEA accredited recruitment agencies to recruit qualified caregiver jobs applicants for its  POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for July . These POEA approved caregiver jobs for July are for the countries of Kuwait, Israel, and the United Kingdom and carries a total of 188 caregiver job vacancies abroad. Specified below is the list of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for the month of July. The list includes the name of the recruiting agency, the specific job vacancy, and the number of applicants to be selected for each POEA approved caregiver jobs for July. 
 Best One Int 'l Services & Consultancy Inc.  --- Caregiver ----- 50 vacancies for Kuwait 
 Mariposa Int 'l Services Co. Inc.  --------------- Caregiver ----- 23 vacancies for Israel 
 Pearl of Panay Worldwide Manp. Services  --- Senior Carer --- 100 vacancies for UK 
 CPM Manpower Services, Inc.  ------------------ Caregiver ----- 15 vacancies for Israel 
 To apply for any of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for July, visit the office of the recruiting agency and submit your updated resume and application. For any inquiries regarding the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for July, you can contact the recruiting agency directly through their landline numbers or email address which will be provided upon request.
