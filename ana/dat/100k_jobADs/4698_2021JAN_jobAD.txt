 ABOUT US 
 The Steam Generating Replacement Team (SGRT) is a Joint Venture of Aecon, AECOM and Framatome. SGRT is responsible for the replacement of the Steam Generators in the Bruce Power Unit 6 Nuclear Power Station in Kincardine, Ontario. 
 The project, which is part of Bruce Power’s Life Extension Program, will allow Bruce Power’s CANDU units to continue to operate safely through to 2064 and is a part of the Major Component Replacement (MCR) program. 
 Work is scheduled to begin in 2019 with installation of the crane pad, then in 2020 perform insulation removal and metrology of the existing steam generators and ultimately replacement in 2021. 
 PURPOSE OF THE POSITION 
 The Quality Specialist reports to the Project Quality Manager and will be responsible for the development of quality associated documentation and processes which comply with customer contract, technical specifications, Code and Standard requirements and local jurisdictional requirements. The Quality Specialist will work closely with Project Management and will be responsible to assist with the development of Comprehensive Work Packages (CWP’s) which includes development of Construction, Inspection & Test Plans (CITP’s). 
 This development work will be performed in accordance with an Integrated Quality Management Plan (IQMP) and a Project Execution Plan. The Quality Specialist shall identify and report quality problems and problems related to the effective implementation of the IQMP. 
 ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES 
 The Quality Specialist is responsible to identify verification, examination, testing requirements. The Quality Specialist shall have direct access to a level of management necessary to ensure that appropriate quality assurance and quality control requirements are implemented. The Quality Specialist is responsible: 
 
 
 To support Project Site Team on matters pertaining to quality; 
 
 
 To report conditions affecting quality to the Project Quality Manager; 
 
 
 Developing the Inspection, Examination & Testing steps and strategically inserting them in the CITP; 
 
 
 Working collaboratively with the FE, Project Coordinator & Trade Supervisor to complete the CITP or CITP Addendum 
 
 
 Working collaboratively with Project Coordinators to complete the CWP; 
 
 
 Route CITP’s or CITP Addendum’s for AI/ANI and the customer’s review & acceptance, where applicable; 
 
 
 Preparation of History Docket / History File Packages; 
 
 
 Assist with the review of purchase requisitions; 
 
 
 Assist with the disposition of NCR’s; 
 
 
 Assist with implementation of actions associated with documented Corrective Action Reports
