 Look At This! it's a Full-time job 
 Josie Maran is looking for an Art Director, Packaging & Print in Los Angeles, CA, USA. 
 About 
 This Senior Art Director role requires an eye and focus for print and packaging. You must have packaging experience with specific focus on beauty/skincare is a must. In this role you will work across retail, online and packaging for our business. You will oversee and focus on all print projects working collaboratively and cross functionally with marketing and social teams as well as with the existing design team and any freelance resources. The Senior Art Director will work collaboratively with the other Senior Art Director who will be focused on Digital/Social within the group. The two Art Directors will work collaboratively as the senior most leaders within the creative group under the Creative Director where they will drive design and concept for the entire creative experience: campaigns for social, digital, packaging and out of home. 
 YOU MUST HAVE A DEEP UNDERSTANDING OF PACKAGING 
• You have a knack for creative thinking and problem-solving 
• You value team collaboration but are highly generative on your own 
• You have the ability to balance strong creative with challenging timelines 
• You thrive on positive energy 
 WHAT WE NEED YOUR TALENTS, SKILLS, AND PASSION FOR 
• Concept & execute creative innovative beauty packaging as well as print projects that include retail execution, fixtures, promotional kits, and advertising 
• Own the strategy and application of creative across all projects: applying strategic and creative intent in packaging, print, and in-store 
• Directing and leading the brand voice globally–across all JMC channels 
• A keen understanding of beauty packaging from industry trends to flawless production 
• Ensure all work released from the Design Studio meets Creative standards, both conceptually and in execution and holds true to the brand DNA 
• Creating and managing a streamlined process and workflow between key stakeholders from marketing, dot com, and creative teams 
• Highly generative collaborations with external creative agency part
