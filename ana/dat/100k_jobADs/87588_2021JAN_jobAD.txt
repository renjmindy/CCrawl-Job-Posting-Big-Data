 Who are We? 
 A leading provider of enterprise cloud applications, Workday delivers financial management, human capital management, planning, and analytics applications designed for the world’s largest organisations. 
 Workday at a glance: 
 
 Workday has more than 7,900 customers with more than 70% live on Workday applications and more than 600 customers headquartered in Europe 
 Workday achieved an industry-leading 97% customer satisfaction rating in its latest customer satisfaction survey 
 Workday has more than 12,400 employees globally, with more than 2,375 based in Europe 
 Our customer community represents more than 45 million workers 
 
 Be Part Of The Workday Analytics Story. 
 We’re revolutionising business intelligence and analytics. Our Czech team is working with today’s most relevant technologies and tools, including artificial intelligence and advanced analytics, to build high-performance, responsive user interfaces for data visualisation. Right from Churchill square in Prague, Workday continues to build the future of business intelligence and machine learning for over 7,900 customers, including Amazon, Airbnb, Atlassian, Adobe, and Airbus—and that’s just the ones starting with 'A'. 
 Enjoy the market-leading benefits and stability of a growing global company alongside an agile, local office culture. This is your chance to be the foundation of our future teams while working with some of the smartest people in the industry who are pioneering the tech behind our analytics platforms. 
 Come be a part of the Workday Analytics story. 
 Career Opportunities. 
 At Workday in Prague, you will have the opportunity to work with some of the smartest people in the industry who are pioneering the tech behind our future analytics platforms and also learn from a global team of the industry’s most talented engineers. 
 Join the Workday Business Intel team. 
Workday is an emerging leader in analytics and modern business Intelligence. Our team of experts in data processing, Hadoop, and Spark solve complex problems with broad impact for our customers. Join our team of engineers and product managers who are pushing technology to the limits. Apply for a Workday analytics engineering job today. 
 Join the Workday machine learning team. 
At Workday, machine learning engineers have the unique opportunity to make our product more contextual, clever, and personalized than ever before. If you’re passionate about developing intelligent applications that millions use every day, we’d love to have you on our team. 
 Game Changing Technology. 
 The Workday Prague team is working with ground-breaking (AI/ML/Analytics-based) technology building high performance, responsive user interfaces for data visualization that will revolutionise intelligence for our 7,900 customers. 
 There are no side projects here — at Workday in Prague you will be working on greenfield projects building and delivering services which are core and central to Workday as a whole. By joining our team, you will have the unique opportunity to make an immediate impact on a product with 45+ million users. 
 At Workday, we have one version of software, one customer community, and one codeline. Together, these things empower us all in ways that weren’t possible in the legacy software industry. The benefits that we provide to our customers cannot be matched, which is proven by our 97% customer satisfaction rating.
