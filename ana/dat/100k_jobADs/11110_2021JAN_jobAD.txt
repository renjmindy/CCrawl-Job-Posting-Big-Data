 Carpenter 
 Job description 
 Carpenter 
 
 
 3 Month role 
 
 
 Based in Paramatta 
 
 
 $45.00 per hour + penalties 
 
 
 About the Company 
Our client manufactures a comprehensive range of quality aluminium windows, doors and balustrade products. Many of the products are unique in the national market, delivering Australia wide. Specialising in innovative products and maintenance-free systems like powder coated reveals, architraves, single opening door frames, cyclonic applications, DIY and many other brilliant concepts. 
 About the Role 
We are currently looking for Experienced Carpenter for a large manufacturing aluminium company. You must be experienced with metal and glass balustrades. 
 Duties & responsibilities 
 
 
 Marking out from grid lines off plans / surveyors marks / drawings. 
 
 
 Concrete hammer drilling , Injection of fixing studs 
 
 
 Installing Aluminium posts on jack nuts and hex nuts ( trimming required once correct RL is worked out ) 
 
 
 Installing set width Top , Mid and Bottom rails using tek screws 
 
 
 Installing Perforated mesh in to Opening using tek screws. 
 
 
 Accuracy and quality is paramount. 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Trade certificate in Carpenter 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 Right to Work in Australia 
 
 
 Have a current Drivers Licence
