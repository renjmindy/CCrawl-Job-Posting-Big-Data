 Shape the future of technology at Synqrinus 
 Role overview 
 Synqrinus is hiring a full-time Director, Technology to lead the software department for 2021. The role is tasked with making executive technology decisions on behalf of the company, including managing a technology budget and making investments to align the company with their vision for its technological needs. You should be a strategic thinker, an effective communicator and an expert in technological development.  The role is hands-on and you will be expected to be a proficient software engineer. 
 You will work directly under the founder and Chief Innovation Officer and will act as both a technology and business expert,  making decisions that will impact the current and future operations of the company.  Technology is at the core of what differentiates Synqrinus, and this leadership role is pivotal for the company. 
 This is a fully remote position during COVID and option for remote after COVID. 
 A few of the best perks: 
 
 Working with a team of really talented and passionate people who care about helping each other do their best work 
 Our work environment is dynamic, flexible and suited to strong problem solvers who like a challenge they can see all the way through to the end user 
 An opportunity to gain exposure to all aspects of the business and contribute in a meaningful way 
 
 Role: Director, Technology 
 Core Responsibilities: 
 
 Leads engineering team in a hands-on capacity 
 Manages engineering team utilizing their competencies 
 Perform annual performance reviews and career development plans 
 Sets vision for internal systems and processes and how they will be used in the company 
 Leads design and architecture for new features and systems 
 Shares knowledge and acts like a leader within the software team and broader company 
 Creates timelines for the development and deployment of all technological services 
 Maintains a user-focused outlook and aiding in the delivering of software projects to market / the 
 team 
 Manages technology budgets and time frames 
 Staying on top of technology trends and developments 
 
 Relevant Characteristics / Skills: 
 
 5+ years experience working as a software engineer 
 2+ years experience in a management role 
 3+ years experience working on mid or larger scale distributed systems 
 3+ years experience with modern web development techniques and best practices 
 Experience or strong interest in working with a functional language. Knowing Clojure will be a 
 preferred bonus 
 Ability to delegate effectively and efficiently 
 Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills 
 Extensive technological knowledge with an eye for the future
