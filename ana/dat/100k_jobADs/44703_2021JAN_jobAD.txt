 Registered General Nurse 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for a committed RGN to work in an exceptional care home based in the Mid Glamorgan, Wales area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading healthcare providers 
 This care home is a purpose-built care home designed for older people with mental health needs requiring nursing care. 
 The home is also designed to support those with mental health needs over the age of 40 years 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Good Nursing Experience
 
 
 
     Caring & compassionate with an excellent clinical track record
 
 
 
     Passionate about delivering outstanding care to older people
 
 
 
     Excellent communication, organisation and leadership skills
 
 
 
     Quality driven with a desire to continually improve yourself and our services
 
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent salary of £16.00 per hour. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working through Night Shifts. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Specialist dementia training and support from our award-winning, university-accredited 'Creative Minds' programme
 
 
 
     Range of salary sacrifice benefits including childcare vouchers, pension, car leases, gym membership, technology purchase schemes and more
 
 
 
     Online benefits portal with a range of offers and cashback rewards
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a valid NMC Pin
