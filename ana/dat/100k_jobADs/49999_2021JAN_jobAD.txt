 Are you an experienced HR Business Partner looking for a new opportunity in the North Staffordshire area? 
 Do you have the gravitas and pace to join a commercial business who are going from strength to strength with a huge change and transformation agenda. If so, then this could be the role for you. 
 Reporting into the Head of HR you will work in partnership with senior stakeholders in Operations, providing professional and credible HR business support. You will develop and implement the People Plans across the business supporting cultural and organisational change. 
 As the HR Business Partner you will: 
 
 Work in partnership with the leadership teams in a full generalist role 
 Lead on all ER casework 
 Develop and build strong relationships across the senior management teams 
 Coach and challenge business leaders 
 Use HR Analytics to identify areas for improvement 
 Lead on the talent agenda, succession plans and talent development 
 Be comfortable working operationally 
 
 To apply for the HR Business Partner role: 
 
 Proven experience of working as HR Business Partner in a commercial environment, ideally in a customer facing or call centre environment 
 Extensive HR Generalist experience to include talent management, succession planning, and Employee Engagement 
 Experience of driving cultural change as well as driving capability and performance within the business 
 Strong senior level influencing and stakeholder management skills 
 MCIPD Qualified
