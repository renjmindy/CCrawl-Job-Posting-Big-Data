 Job Description 
 CUSTOMER SERVICE REPRESENTATIVE 
 MUST HAVE A RELIABLE FORM OF TRANSPORTATION 
 *** PLEASE NOTE THIS IS AN ENTRY LEVEL POSITION WITH SALARY RANGE FROM $40,000-$60,000* 
 COLUMBIA, MARYLAND** 
 
 Calling all Customer Service Managers, Marketing Assistants, Sales Graduates, Marketing Majors, and Others with an Interest in Learning Marketing in a fun, innovative environment ... 
 
 US Business Services, in Columbia, Maryland, is looking for enthusiastic and ambitious ENTRY LEVEL Customer Service Representatives to join our growing marketing team. 
 ** 
Entry Level Customer Service Representatives must be:** 
 
 Enthusiastic 
 Ambitious 
 Creative 
 Proactive 
 
 This exciting role will best suit someone with a positive outlook and great customer service skills. 
 
 Company Incentives for Entry Level Customer Service Representative: 
 
 Continuous support and advice 
 Fun, professional, results-driven environment 
 Clients & career confidence spanning a variety of industries 
 Personal and business advancement opportunities 
 
 
 About US Business Services: 
 We are Columbia's fastest growing consulting company. We specialize in delivering engaging sales and marketing presentations for our clients to increase their market share and maintain brand recognition. 
 
 
 Job Requirements 
 What's next for our Entry Level Customer Service Representative ? 
 Our expansion plan currently involves us developing our client portfolio with multiple campaigns scheduled to launch across the US in 2020 and into 2021. As such, we are looking for a Customer Service Representative who wishes to be part of a innovative, fast paced company, spear heading its development across the region and providing high quality client services. 
 
 ***** NO EXPERIENCE NOR DEGREE REQUIRED ***** 
 
 Graduates, career changers, and career starters are encouraged to apply; however, the following degrees/experiences are the best matches: communications, business administration, management, advertising, marketing, public relations, political science, psychology, military, sports management, and athletic experience, Graduates, students and anyone with the following backgrounds are also welcome to apply: sales assistant, customer service assistant, marketing assistant, sales representative, marketing representative, retail assistant, customer service training, call center inbound, retail manager, call center outbound, catering, hospitality, front of house, bar person, customer service manager, direct sales and any other customer service or sales role. 
 
 Apply now for immediate consideration! 
 To ensure a response within 48 hours, please be sure to include your correct contact information. Also, please be sure to monitor your cell phone for missed calls and voicemail messages and monitor your email inbox (including your junk/spam folder) for replies from us!
