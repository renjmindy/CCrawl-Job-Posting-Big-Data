 Roller Operator / Labourer 
 Job description 
 About the Role 
 Our client is currently looking for an experienced and skilled roller operator within rural Queensland. This medium sized Civil Construction company is looking to build an additional crew due to additional new and upcoming projects in the area. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Roller operations within a road works environment 
 
 
 Ability to read and interpret plans and follow guidance from the onsite foreman 
 
 
 Ability to work independently with a proven track record of operating safely 
 
 
 Strong team player who supports our client's operations 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Previous experience in civil construction including road works 
 
 
 Able to complete labouring work also 
 
 
 RII for Roller Operations 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 White/Blue Construction card
