 Kangana Ranaut is known as the Queen of Bollywood for several reasons, which includes her talent as an actress. She is one of Bollywood’s highest paying actors and has proved her worth with each film she has done. She made a strong debut with the movie Gangster and is known for her explosive interviews and opinions. Everyone today is aware of how the actress is bold and never backs down in supporting the truth and raising her views. This is why she is also loved by the millennials, and her fan base is enormous. Let us get to know more about the actress Kangan Ranaut. 
 Kangana Ranaut Net worth in rupees 2020: 
 According to the sources, Kangana Ranaut is one of the highest-paid actresses in the Bollywood industry. She started her career with the movie Gangster and made her mark with films like Queen, Manikarnika, Panga, Rangoon, and many more. Her total net worth as of 2020 is 13 million, which approx—93 crore rupees INR. Apart from movies, Kangana also earns from her brand endorsements and deals. For each brand endorsement, she charges between 5 to 7 crore. In the last few years, it is noted that her net worth has grown up to 37% and will definitely see more hike in the coming future. 
 Kangana Ranaut career and assets in rupees: 
 Kangana Ranaut was born on the 23rd of March in Bhambla. Before she ventured into the entertainment industry, she wanted to become a doctor. But her fondness for the acting grew, and she trained under Arvind Gaur. She made her debut with “Gangster” which has won her best debut female award. There is no doubt that her acting skills are fabulous. She has also won many awards for her movies and abilities, including 3 national honours and Filmfare awards. Apart from that, she has also received a Padmashri award. Kangana has a 3-floor building on Pali hill in 2017, which she has built as her office cum studio where she spent 48 crores. And she also has a bungalow which is approximated to 10 crores. Apart from this, she also owns a few expensive cars. 
 Kangana Ranaut family and relationships: 
 Kangana Ranaut’s parents are Amardeep Ranaut, is a businessperson and her mother, Asha Ranaut, is a schoolteacher. She also has a sister Rangoli Chandel who works as her manager and stays with her in Mumbai. She was born in a small village in Himachal Pradesh, in a Rajput Family. Not much is known about the actress’s personal life, after her breakup with the Bollywood's heartthrob Hrithik Roshan. After that, she has not been rumoured with anyone, and neither has she made the news with any for her relationships. She recently made headlines because of her quarrel with the political party Shiv Sena. And this is all that we have on the actress Kangana Ranaut.
