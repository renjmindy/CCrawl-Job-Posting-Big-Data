 Our client is a global **** network, created to 'change the black narrative' by bringing the black professional global community together to solve problems through economic empowerment and connectivity. They are excellently positioned to bridge the gap between the black professional community and corporations. Today they have over 50,000 members, launched a mobile and web platform, run offline and online events to up-skill the community, have a job board and have worked with 60+ corporate clients. They are currently looking for other passionate and determined team members to join them. 
 THE ROLE 
 We are looking for an enthusiastic Global Ambassador Program Manager to be the face and lead the Ambassador function. As a successful candidate, you will be representing the brand in various capacities. To excel in this role, you will have excellent communication, networking skills and the ability to build a database of networks and well as training and developing a team. Previous experience as a Global Brand Ambassador or a similar role is strongly desired. 
 DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 Develop, deliver and where required present the Global Ambassador strategy 
 Lead, grow and manage the Global Ambassador program 
 Recruit and manage Global Ambassadors, Country Ambassadors and Ambassadors 
 Provide training and resources to equip the Ambassador Team to increase global visibility of the Network 
 Help coordinate events for the Ambassadors 
 Carry out webinar training sessions & monthly meetings with the Ambassadors 
 Devise innovative growth strategies for the Ambassador program 
 Represent the brand positively in a multitude of professional and social settings 
 Reporting and tracking progress and outcomes of Ambassador program 
 Liaise with various internal departments to increase brand awareness worldwide 
 Create & manage a database of networks and corporations 
 Create packages and proposals to send to organizations and event hosts 
 Providing feedback and insight on new products and services 
 
 SKILLS/EXPERIENCE: 
 
 Proven work experience as a Global Ambassador Manager or Associate Ambassador Manager 
 Well organised with excellent project management skills 
 Experience in identifying target audiences and devising effective campaigns 
 Strong analytical skills partnered with a creative mind 
 Proficient in Microsoft Office, including Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint 
 Can work in an organized way under pressure, prioritise workload and meet tight deadlines 
 Good confident communicator with excellent manners and positive attitude 
 Start to finish attitude, willing to learn and achieving results 
 Able to proactively manage and organise work with minimal supervision 
 Excellent team player, willing to regularly share knowledge and expertise 
 Be enthusiastic, ambitious, honest and hardworking 
 
 BENEFITS 
 
 Competitive salary 
 Work from home flexibility 
 Laptop 
 Training and support 
 A professional reference 
 Access to a global network 
 Socials with like-minded individuals
