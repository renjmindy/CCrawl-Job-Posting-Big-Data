 MacPhee Ford is looking for a fully licensed Red Seal technician with Ford Diesel course completion. Our truck shop is growing and we need a Ford Diesel Technician to assist our customers to keep their trucks on the road. 
 MacPhee Ford is looking for a Diesel Technician with excellent diagnostic skills and strong communication skills to work with our customers and advisors in their fleet services. 
 The successful candidate will have a good knowledge the Ford Diesel Powertrains, the associated repair procedures, warranty procedures and maintenance requirements of the Ford Super Duty products. 
 Job duties include: 
 
 Diagnostic proficiency 
 Attention to detail 
 Complete repairs in a timely manner 
 Post-repair road tests 
 Maintain tools, equipment and the shop 
 Follow warranty policies and procedures 
 Ensure clear, accurate written documentation of all repairs
