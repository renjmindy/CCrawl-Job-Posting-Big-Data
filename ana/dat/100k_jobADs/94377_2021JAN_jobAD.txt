 Nanny Jobs 
 Posted on  April 30, 2009  by  caregiver 
 As a nanny jobs employment agency, they know that having an accomplished career while taking care of the family needs is a very big task. Thatâ€™s why a nanny jobs employment agency based in Montreal, Canada is working hard to alleviate those hard working moms from the stress brought about by the day to day activities of a working mother. To accomplish this task, the said nanny jobs employment agency is always looking to recruit qualified female nannies from different countries to fill the various nanny jobs vacancy. To qualify for the various nanny jobs vacancy, the nanny jobs candidate should have extensive experience in childcare and household management. The nanny jobs candidate should also be fluent in the English language because most of their clients are English speaking families with young children who needs the assistance of a qualified nanny jobs applicant. 
 The said nanny jobs positions are available in the Montreal district of Quebec, Canada and a minimum salary of $8.50 / hour plus paid vacations is being offered for the qualified nanny jobs applicants. The chosen foreign applicants for the nanny jobs will also be given the opportunity to become permanent residents of Canada after two years of continuous employment in nanny jobs. 
 To start your application process for the nanny jobs vacancy, you must send them your application papers together with your photos. When an arrangement have been reached between the nanny jobs applicant and an employer for the nanny jobs, the nanny jobs agency will assist you with all the requirements needed for the hiring process. 
 For more info, you may log on to their website at www.serenta.ca/ or E-mail them at nannies@serenta.ca
