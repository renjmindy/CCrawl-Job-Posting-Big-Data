 Project Coordinator 
 Job description 
 About the Company 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking a Project Coordinator to assist with delivering Yandi's 2021 capital portfolio. FIFO from Perth, 8:6 roster. The successful candidate will be primarily responsible for ensuring the project's contractors are supervised on site with the required level to ensure safe execution of scope. This includes reviewing JHA's, permits and providing strong frontline leadership, along with liaising with project managers to manage varying project hygiene related tasks tender, scheduling, mobilisation, execution, forecasting and budgeting. 
 Duties include: 
 
 
 Provides timely and relevant updates to appropriate stakeholders and decision makers. 
 
 
 Supports appropriate project governance (including risk and issue management and resolution) 
 
 
 Supports compliance with standardised project processes, systems, and tools across projects. 
 
 
 Coordinates engagement and performance of project vendors as required. 
 
 
 Collaborates with stakeholders to define, estimate, and deliver on requirements. 
 
 
 Assists in driving improvements within the project. 
 
 
 General project support 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Similar experience as a Project Coordinator 
 
 
 Demonstrated problem solving abilities 
 
 
 Knowledge on Rio's safety policies and procedures 
 
 
 SAP and MS Office proficiency 
 
 
 Strong communication skills 
 
 
 WA C Class Drivers Licence
