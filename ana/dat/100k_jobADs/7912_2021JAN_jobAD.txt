 Market Job Description

 About the Role 
 ** 
You will work as full stack developer in design, development and testing of the PEGA application in an enterprise environment. Must have Design and Development and testing experience using PEGA 7.3 and higher version. 
 ** 
 Responsibilities 
 ** 
 
 
 Design, Development and testing using PEGA with a preferred certification in PEGA according to role. 
 
 
 Design the code, help lead with task updates and assignments, help develop automated test cases in PEGA test suite technically. 
 
 
 Perform the development activities as demanded by the project 
 
 
 Follow SDLC processes - Agile, TDD, BDD 
 
 
 Essential  ** 
 Qualifications 
 ** 
 
 
 University degree in related discipline. 
 
 
 6+ years' experience in PEGA application design , development, support and testing 
 
 
 PEGA CSA and CSSA 
 
 
 ** 
 Desired Qualifications 
 ** 
 
 
 Communicate and work harmoniously with all the stakeholders 
 
 
 Analyze, Build and test the requirements 
 
 
 Experience working with Database like Oracle 
 
 
 PEGA CSLA
