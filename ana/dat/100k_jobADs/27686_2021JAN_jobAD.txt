 Career 
 **The following positions are available at The Nail Artelier: 
 
 Intern 
 Junior Nailist 
 Senior Nailist 
 
 1. Intern 
 Our internship programme is designed to take trained but inexperienced nailists on a challenging learning journey to transform them into skilled professionals and nail artists. Under the guidance of our senior nail artists, you will learn a wide variety of nail art techniques and refine your basic techniques.** 
 As you will spend more time learning than providing services to customers, you will be provided with a basic allowance and expected to fulfil a six month bond to The Nail Artelier as a Junior Nailist upon completion of the internship programme. The internship programme will last between 1 month to 3 months, depending on your learning ability. 
 To apply for an internship at The Nail Artelier, you must have completed a comprehensive nail course (covering natural nail care and acrylic and gel extensions) with certification from a reputable nail school. 
 No prior working experience is needed, but diligence, a positive attitude towards learning and a passion for nail art are a must. Interns must commit to working at least 3 days a week. 
 ** 2. Junior Nailist 
 Our junior nailists are trained and experienced nail artists who are able to execute a wide variety of nail art. Under the guidance of our senior nail artists, you will learn complex nail art techniques and hone your skills in acrylic/gel extensions.** 
 To work as a junior nailist at The Nail Artelier, you must have completed a comprehensive nail course (covering natural nail care and acrylic and gel extensions) with certification from a reputable nail school. 
 In addition, you must also possess at least  one  year of relevant working experience,  or  have a good portfolio of nail designs. 
 Diligence, the ability to work well as a team, a positive attitude towards learning and a passion for nail art are a must. 
 ** 3. Senior Nailist 
 Our senior nailists are trained and experienced nail artists who are able to execute a wide variety of quality nail art and gel/acrylic extensions. Under the guidance of our Directors, you will further hone your skills and develop the ability to provide customisation services.** 
 To work as a senior nailist at The Nail Artelier, you must have completed a comprehensive nail course (covering natural nail care and acrylic and gel extensions) with certification from a reputable nail school. 
 In addition, you must also possess at least  two  years of relevant working experience,  or  have an impressive portfolio of nail designs and quality gel/acrylic extensions. 
 Diligence, patience in mentoring others, the ability to work well as a team, a positive attitude towards learning and a passion for nail art are a must. 
 **To apply for a position, please call us at 62988028 or email us at thenailartelier@gmail.com to arrange for an interview.
