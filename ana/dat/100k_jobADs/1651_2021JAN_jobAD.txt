 Accounts Administrator - Birmingham Central - £20k to £25k 
 Our Client 
 I am working with an amazing business that is experiencing high-levels of growth, and they seek someone to join their team on a permanent basis. Operating out of the most stunning of premises, they will offer this person a chance to get involved in a wide-variety of functions, including payroll, accounts payable and general operational support. 
 The Role 
 The successful person will have a solid grounding in accounts, with a desire to let their imagination run wild to support this flourishing business. Specifically, the role will involve the following tasks: 
 
 Processing payments and allocating receipts 
 Expense management 
 Generating reports for senior managers 
 Dealing with supplier queries 
 Keeping accurate employee records and handling sensitive documentation in an appropriate manner 
 Banking 
 Other general "book-keeping" tasks 
 
 This is a role that will suit a positive, organised individual with a flair for record keeping, who can help with the growth of an entrepreneurial organisation.
