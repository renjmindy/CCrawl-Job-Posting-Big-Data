 Atom Learning, one of the UK's fastest growing education technology startups, is hiring a content manager. 
 Atom Learning is an online learning platform that is making education fairer. We want to provide all children whatever their background and wherever they are in the world with an affordable, high-quality education. To achieve our goal, we are combining exceptional teacher-made content with machine learning to keep students on their optimal learning paths. 
 Atom Learning is growing rapidly with hundreds of schools in the UK and abroad using the platform and thousands of independent subscribers. We have partnered with one of the largest global publishing houses with distribution into thousands of schools in the UK and abroad; we are in talks with national governments to develop their digital learning strategies; and we're just getting started! 
 We are ranked one of the UK's top 100 startups by Startups100, are a finalist for the AI Award in the Lloyds Bank Business Awards, won the Best AI-based Solution for Education in the AI Breakthrough Awards, were a finalist in the Education Investor awards 2018 and Amazon Growing Business Awards 2018 as Young Company of the Year. 
 We are a growing team of 50 with offices in Soho (currently working remotely); we have big ambitions and anticipate being a team of at least 100 in a year's time. 
 The role: 
 Atom Learning is hiring a Content Manager to join the team at our offices in Soho and lead the content production of our expansion into the USA. 
 You will be an integral member of our curriculum team working on a new and exciting project. You will lead the creation of exceptional content for Atom Learning's expansion into the American Education market. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 You will be responsible for creating exceptional curriculum guides, linked to the SSAT and ISEE syllabus for English, maths and reasoning. 
 You will lead a team of freelance content writers to quality assure exceptional content. 
 You will design instructive videos and help sheets to support learning. You will manage multiple projects to deadlines. 
 You will edit and revise existing curriculum content. 
 
 Ideal Candidate: 
 
 Teaching or tuition experience for the American Curriculum. 
 Extreme attention to detail and excellent written American English. 
 Strong organisational skills to manage multiple tasks concurrently. 
 Excellent communication skills to liaise with external and internal colleagues. 
 Flexibility, and a willingness to learn and jump in to help where needed. 
 Loves start-ups and understands the challenges and demands of a fast-moving, ever-changing business. 
 A love of education and an interest in pedagogy. 
 
 What we offer: 
 
 28 days holiday + bank holidays. 
 Fully remote/flexible work (currently working fully remote). 
 Competitive pay. 
 Full auto-enrolment workplace pension. 
 High degree of autonomy and exposure to the running of all business areas of a successful start up. 
 The opportunity to become an integral member of a fast growing technology company. 
 Frequent team dinners and drinks. 
 An office on Dean Street, Soho.
