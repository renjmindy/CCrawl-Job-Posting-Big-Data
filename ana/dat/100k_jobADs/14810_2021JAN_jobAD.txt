 Underground Operator 
 Job description 
 
 
 Permanent positions 
 
 
 Performance based bonuses 
 
 
 Various Rosters available 
 
 
 Work and live in the Illawarra Region with a reputable Mining Company 
 About the Role 
 We are seeking suitably experienced Underground Operators to join our team. You will be an integral crew member with strong leader skills who can drive performance through production processes and instil a safety-oriented culture. 
 Rosters worked are Weekday (Day, Afternoon and Night Shift) and Weekend (Day and Night Shift), preferences will be considered however not guaranteed. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Safely and effectively operate and/or maintain machinery to a standard and quality required to meet target outputs. 
 
 
 Comply with the requirements of the Site Safety Management System, including completing Take 2s and Job Task Analysis as required etc. 
 
 
 Present for shift fit for work in accordance with the site Drug and Alcohol Policy 
 
 
 Maintain required qualifications and/or skills as identified by the Deputy or as per the Training Needs Analysis, ensuring all qualifications are kept up to date. 
 
 
 Comply with the Mine Operating System within your area of responsibility. 
 
 
 Work with the maintenance and mining personnel including the Deputy and Undermanager, to actively participate in tasks to ensure that production and other targets are achieved during the shift 
 
 
 Uphold and implement high level of housekeeping 
 
 
 Record all necessary information such as malfunction breakdown, incidents, inspections etc. in the appropriate location. 
 
 
 Ensure timely and accurate reporting of all events and hazards and participate in workplace investigations or observations as required. 
 
 
 Work with physical assets, plant and equipment as defined by the Operations standards and operating procedures. 
 
 
 Report all hazards and incidents and take corrective actions immediately where possible. 
 
 
 Benefits and perks 
 
 
 Permanent positions 
 
 
 Work and live in the Illawarra Region 
 
 
 Work with a reputable and leading Mining Company 
 
 
 Employee Assistance benefits Program 
 
 
 Performance based bonuses 
 
 
 Skills, experience, and qualifications 
 
 
 Current Order43 medical or willingness to obtain 
 
 
 Current CABA and NSW Mines rescue Generic Induction certification or willingness to obtain 
 
 
 Must be willing to undergo and pass a drug and alcohol test and functionality assessment 
 
 
 Minimum 1 year experience working underground as an Operator 
 
 
 Ability to effectively communicate and influence across various levels of the business 
 
 
 First Aid Certificate 
 
 
 An extensive understanding of the work practices in the Underground Coal Mining Industry 
 
 
 Documented Underground Coal Mining competencies / appointments from previous mines 
 
 
 Valid Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Ability to work with other team members
