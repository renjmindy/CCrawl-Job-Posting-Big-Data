 Are you an expert in content creation? Are you looking to join a business that is going through rapid growth and a digital transition? Are you an on-site  SEO  specialist looking to be a part of a well experienced team? 
 If so, I have a fantastic perm opportunity for a Content Executive to join a fantastic B2C service focused business, based on the outskirts of Birmingham. Offering a salary of 25-30k, you will be handed responsibility to manage and oversee content across an impressive number of micro-sites. 
 What you'll do… 
 
 Be the editor and controller for the online content for a group of regional branches. 
 create and maintain engaging web copy and content for an allocated group of micro-sites. 
 Building relations with operations, taking supplied information from regional branch locations and turning it into enticing and engaging online copy that resonates with the target audience. 
 Maintain a clear and consistent tone of voice in line with the brand values. 
 
 What you'll need… 
 
 Excellent copywriting skills. 
 Technical skills with experience of using CMS. 
 An advanced understanding of  SEO . 
 Strong organisation skills with the ability to prioritise a busy workload. Be able to simultaneously manage multiple projects with short deadlines. 
 Excellent attention to detail that ensures high quality standards of execution. 
 Keen to continually develop digital knowledge. 
 
 What you'll get… 
 
 Competitive salary of 25-30k. 
 Attractive benefits package.
