 Gleeson Recruitment Group are working exclusively with our client based in Birmingham City Centre to recruit an Assistant Finance Manager for a 4 month contract. 
 The ideal candidate will have experience of dealing with:- 
 
 Cash flows 
 Review all invoices created and approve 
 Chase any outstanding Contract invoices 
 Time sheets received for contractors 
 Partner invoices received for contractor 
 Calculate Contractor GP 
 Calculate contract commission 
 Monthly payroll 
 Pay all supplier invoices 
 Request increased limit for customers when necessary 
 Draw down money when required 
 Quarterly - calculate consultants commission 
 EC sales 
 UK and Dubai VAT return 
 Pay PAYE 
 
 This role will be starting in August in order to handover before maternity starts. Starting salary of £35-£42k and will be for a four month period. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
