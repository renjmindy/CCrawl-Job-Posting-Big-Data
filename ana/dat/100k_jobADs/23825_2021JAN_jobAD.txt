 PA to Board of Directors 
 
 Location 
 
 Wolverhampton, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Executive Support 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £25000 - £30000 per annum

 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group is seeking a Personal Assistant to support a Board of Directors within the health care sector for a client based in the Wolverhampton area. 
 Duties: 
 
 The PA will be up for the challenge of working in a fast-paced environment and comfortable with managing a range of complex priorities. 
 Managing the Director's email & diary, ensuring actions and meetings are appropriately prioritised 
 Planning and co-ordination of global travel including supporting the development of business meetings and making all travel arrangements 
 Acting as the first point of contact for enquiries from a range of internal & external customers and stakeholders 
 Planning & coordinating Management Team meetings, team events and annual conferences Undertaking a variety of administrative activities including preparation of reports and presentations compiling briefings for meetings; overseeing the team's budgets and expenditure 
 
 The ideal PA will have the following skill sets: 
 
 Previous experience in a Personal Assistant role 
 High level of attention to detail and accuracy 
 Ability to operate with minimal supervision and deal with a heavy workload and wide range of tasks, using initiative and judgement to resolve or redirect more complex issues 
 Proven experience of providing administrative support 
 Confident communication skills including ability to communicate with senior business executives & key decision makers in the private and public sectors 
 Experience of handling sensitive and confidential information 
 Self-motivated and ability to prioritise well and deliver to tight deadlines in a high-pressured environment 
 Excellent knowledge of Microsoft Office 
 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
