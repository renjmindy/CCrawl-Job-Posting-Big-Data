 Key Account Credit Controller 
 
 Location 
 
 Birmingham, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance

 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £22000 - £24000 per annum

 Ryan Doolan

 Published:

 2020-03-05 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Are you an experienced Credit Controller? Is your experience within B2B ? Do you have experience in chasing blue chip businesses and managing a ledger of key accounts? 
 We are recruiting exclusively for a Credit Controller in Birmingham paying up to £24k per annum. You will be joining a committed and hardworking credit control team of 7 and reporting directly into a supportive Credit Manager. 
 
 Management of up to 100 live accounts made up for key accounts and large, blue chip businesses. 
 Managing a ledger value of up to £10m. 
 Bank reconciliation 
 Run regular aged debt reports and providing analysis. 
 Resolving any queries in a professional manner 
 
 Is this role right for me? 
 If you have prior credit control experience and are looking for a new challenge within a brilliant team then yes! 
 If you would be interested in working for this business then apply today. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
