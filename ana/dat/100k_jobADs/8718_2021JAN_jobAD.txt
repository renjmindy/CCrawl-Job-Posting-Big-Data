 Freelance Photographer 
 WordSouth is a creative services firm. We work with electric utilities and telecommunications companies that serve rural and small-town America. Their mission becomes our mission as our hard work and big ideas generate results and earn their trust in us as a true communications partner. WordSouth is seeking talented contract freelance photographers to capture engaging photos in a variety of settings that will align with our clients’ marketing goals. 
 Job Type  : Contract 
 Contract Duration  : Per-assignment basis 
 Work Location  : Remote 
 Ideal candidates have the following qualifications  : 
 
 Proven work experience as a photographer 
 Ability to capture high-quality photos in a variety of settings 
 Exceptional attention to detail 
 Ability to follow strict deadlines 
 Highly proficient in photo editing software 
 Portfolio of previous work 
 Excellent communication skills 
 
 Responsibilities  : 
 
 Capture engaging photos that fulfill assignment requirements 
 Work directly with subjects to schedule photoshoots 
 Edit photos to produce high-quality images ready for print in publications and promotional materials 
 Respond to communication in a timely manner (email, phone calls, etc.) 
 Adhere to all deadlines and detailed instructions for each assignment 
 
 Requirements  : 
 
 Camera equipment necessary to capture professional photographs 
 Computer to upload photos 
 Access to a broadband connection 
 Transportation 
 
 Compensation  : Negotiated per project 
 To apply, please send resume and link to portfolio to  hello@wordsouth.com .
