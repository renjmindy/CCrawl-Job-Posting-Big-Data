 Business Analyst Process Mapping/Business Process Analyst 
 Birmingham/Remote working 
 £45,000 - £50,000 per annum 11 month fixed Term contract 
 Working within a major Data Transformation project a Business Analyst with strong Process Mapping and strong Data knowledge is required for a 11 month fixed term contract with potential of going perm afterwards. 
 
 Evaluate existing operational process's, identifying efficiency and consistency opportunities to support the transformation programme 
 Facilitate process orientated workshops to negotiate effective fit for purpose outcomes 
 To lead and support implementations of process improvements 
 Support the operational maturity of process definition, design, implementation and continuation of measure and analysis 
 Work with transformation stakeholders and SME's to align operational processes supporting internal data, management information, key performance indicators and software application requirements 
 To adhere to relevant methodologies and standards that meet the needs of a fast-paced business. 
 Extensive process design management engaging at senior leadership communities 
 Excellent facilitation and consultancy abilities, combined with the ability to engender a clear vision within the minds of the customer 
 Broad based business applications background 
 Proven ability to develop and design documents including training manuals, process outlines, flowcharts and implementation procedures 
 Previous demonstration of building and monitoring internal controls and driving continuous improvement initiatives
