 Caregiving Jobs 
 Posted on  August 2, 2010  by  caregiver 
 A POEA accredited recruitment agency is currently looking for qualified Filipino  Caregiving Jobs  applicants for its principal in Israel. The said caregiving jobs have a job order that was approved by the POEA last July 28 for CPM Manpower Services Inc. For the said caregiving jobs, the recruiting agency is tasked to select 15 applicants for placement in caregiver work abroad. 
 If you are interested to apply for the said caregiving jobs, submit your updated resume and application directly to the office of the recruiting agency. For further details or inquiries you can dial the agency’s landline numbers specified below. 
 CPM Manpower Services, Inc. 
8F Act Tower Condominium 
G. Puyat Avenue cor. H.V. Dela Costa Street 
Salcedo Village, Makati 
Tel Nos: 8181464 / 8181592 / 8181593 / 8173314 / 8178419
