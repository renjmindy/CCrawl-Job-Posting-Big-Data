 About Motorway 
 Motorway is reinventing how cars are bought and sold online. Our mission is to bring the car industry into the future through ground-breaking products and best in class technology. Founded in 2017 we are growing rapidly and power thousands of vehicle transactions every month. With just a few taps, private car sellers can compare live offers for their car online, select their best deal and sell it within 24 hours. To make this possible, we work with over 1,000 professional car buyers and dealerships nationwide, offering them instant access to private car sellers through an automated technology platform, with over 1m sale enquiries in the last 12 months. 
 About the role 
 At Motorway we are looking for an IT Support Engineer with fantastic customer service skills and a passion for IT & Technology. We're a values driven, results focussed organisation and we're looking for someone to bring 
 The successful candidate would be working alongside the IT Manager responsible for the support of our 100+ staff based across multiple office locations. The responsibilities will include onboarding new members of the Motorway team, configuring workstations, resolving support desk tickets, working closely with our customer facing teams  and maintaining our business critical IT systems. You'll also need to be confident in researching and implementing change, recognising trends in IT support and keep our internal knowledge base up to date. 
 This role will involve occasional travel to our office in London. 
 What we 're looking for 
 
 You will have;
 
 Extensive experience with Mac management platforms such as JAMF or Casper 
 Experience providing support in business environments with Windows 10, Mac OS, Android & iOS. 
 Previous experience working with and supporting Google G Suite 
 An understanding of AV systems - TVs, Google Hangouts etc. 
 An understanding of networking hardware &  Networking - Firewalls, Switches, Networking etc. 
 Previous experience supporting cloud based customer support and telephony platforms 
 The ability and willingness to learn new systems quickly. 
 
 
 You should have fantastic customer service skills and be able to remain calm under pressure. 
 You should have demonstrable experience working within a Service Desk environment in a small to medium sized organisation 
 We're looking for someone with great confidence, a highly approachable nature, and the ability to instill trust. 
 You should be extremely organised and able to manage multiple tasks at any given time. We need someone who enjoys variety and is willing to get stuck in. 
 You will need to be a proactive person who uses their initiative to do what needs to be done without being asked 
 You should be a quick and creative problem solver who delivers well thought through solutions 
 
 What we offer 
 
 A competitive salary 
 Enhanced Maternity/Paternity leave 
 Pension Scheme 
 Discounted Calm membership 
 Cycle to work scheme 
 The chance to join a rapidly growing company that cares first and foremost about its people 
 The chance to work with an experienced and passionate team with a big vision 
 A fun working environment with regular social events
