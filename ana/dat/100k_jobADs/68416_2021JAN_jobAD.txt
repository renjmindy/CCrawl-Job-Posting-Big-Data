 Main Responsibilities of the role: 
 
 End to end Supply chain responsibility. From Purchase order to Pay. 
 12 month forward demand plan of inventory, working in conjunction with sales and marketing. 
 Forecasts created across multiple product categories and serving different channels across the business. 
 Ensure accurate inventory data, and ensure we are always maintaining the right level of stock by managing the flow of purchase orders from our suppliers. 
 Increase the partnership with retailers (monthly meetings/calls to get accurate forecast) and improve the service level. 
 Loss analysis of slow moving lines, providing recommendations to the business on optimal SKU line up. 
 Develop and maintain relevant supply chain and logistics KPIs. 
 Escalate any risks or opportunities to the Leadership team. 
 
 About you 
 
 Outstanding data and reconciliation skills. Ability to analyse past performance, and monitor and feedback the reliability of product availability with relevant stakeholders. 
 Passion for Inventory Management and Demand planning 
 Advanced Excel Skills 
 Forecasting tools knowledge is desirable 
 Ideally an FMCG background. 
 
 ABOUT HOWND 
 ETHICAL PRODUCTS FOR HAPPY HOUNDS 
 We're a multi-award-winning ethical lifestyle brand for dogs. We create 100% plant-based health, wellness and natural hygiene products for dogs under one happy woof (oops, roof). We live and breathe the core ethos of our brand. We will never put profits above the welfare of any living creature. We will continue to create products that help keep dogs happy, healthy for life while encouraging others to live a planet-kind, cruelty-free lifestyle. We believe the world would be a better place if more people embraced the power of plants. Our products are kind to our precious planet and the animals that inhabit it. That's why dogs and their humans love HOWND. We are convinced that dogs can thrive without compromising the welfare of other animals. Since day one, HOWND pet care has been certified by Cruelty-Free International and is accredited by The Vegetarian (Vegan) Society, PETA Cruelty-Free and The Ethical Company Organisation.
