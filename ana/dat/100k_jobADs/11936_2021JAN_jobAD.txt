 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for January 2011 
 Posted on  February 11, 2011  by  caregiver 
 For the month of January 2011, the POEA approved several Job Orders that offers employment opportunities to caregivers who are looking for caregiver jobs abroad. These  POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for January 2011  are for the countries of Israel, Canada, and Qatar. Specified below are the names of the recruiting agencies for the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for January 2011 and the number of vacancies for each POEA approved caregiver work abroad. 
 ISRAEL  ----- CPM Manpower Services, Inc. -------------------------- 50 caregiver jobs

 CANADA  --- Luzern International Manpower Services Corp. --------- 18 caregiver jobs 
 ISRAEL  ---- Hopewell Overseas Manpower Network, Inc. ------------ 45 caregiver jobs 
 ISRAEL  ---- NYD International Placement Agency Inc. --------------- 13 caregiver jobs 
 QATAR  ---- September Star Incorporated --------------------------- 150 caregiver jobs 
 If you wish to apply for any of the said POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for January 2011, proceed directly to the office of the recruiting agency and submit your resume together with your application. If you have any question you wish to be answered regarding the said POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for January 2011, you may contact the recruiting agency thru their telephone numbers which will be provided upon request.
