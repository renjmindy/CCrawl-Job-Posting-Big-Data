 About Notable Labs 
 Notable Labs is a mission-driven precision medicine startup building a translational drug discovery platform to identify treatment options for blood cancer patients. 
 About the role 
 Notable Labs is looking for a product development scientist to develop the first clinical application of our ex vivo drug sensitivity testing platform. You’re a research biologist who’s excited about applying your laboratory skills towards a clinical diagnostic product. As a member of the Product Development team at Notable, you will execute assay development and validation projects which translate technology developed by our Research & Development team into CLIA tests focused on hematologic malignancies. The team will rely on your laboratory experience in executing product specification and validation studies. 
 Technology 
 We use robotics, custom software, and machine learning to identify personalized drug combinations and simulate clinical trials.
