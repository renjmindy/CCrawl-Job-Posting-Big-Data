 Outreach Account Manager 
 Authority Linking is the first venture out of the London based ventures builders, 10Fold Ventures. 10Fold creates and invests in B2B SaaS products that solves problems for rapidly scaling businesses. 
 Authority Linking is an SEO platform that helps agencies become more efficient with their outreach. We take care of the outreach and content allowing agencies to build up their clients backlink portfolio and help them start growing their organic traffic. 
 The Role 
 As an Account Manager,  you will be responsible for onboarding quality publishing partners onto the platform and ensuring we deliver a top user experience throughout. You will also work with our agency partners to ensure we are delivering quality content. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 Helping to develop and optimise our outreach strategy 
 Researching and creating media/outreach lists 
 Building out our outreach materials and sequences 
 Working alongside our agencies to help them deliver quality campaigns 
 Building relationships with publishers and helping them through the onboarding process 
 Creating great and engaging content for our publishers 
 Performance reporting (weekly/monthly) 
 Backlink analysis and competitor backlink analysis 
 Skills and Experience: 
 Bachelor's degree (preferably in Marketing, Media or Journalism) 
 Someone who is happy to get stuck in and learn too 
 Basic Understanding of SEO fundamentals 
 Great communication skills 
 Super organised
