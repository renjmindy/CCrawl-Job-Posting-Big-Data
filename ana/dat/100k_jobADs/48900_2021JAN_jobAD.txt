 Financial Planning & Analysis Manager 
 
 Location 
 
 Wolverhampton, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £65000.00 - £75000.00 per annum + car allowance, pension, bonus 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Jamie Carter

 jamiecarter@workwithglee.com

 Published: 
 
 about 1 year ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-02-21 
 A rapidly growing and acquisitive PE Backed Consumable Products Business based in Wolverhampton, are seeking a talented qualified accountant to join their business on a permanent basis as Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) Manager. The FP&A Manager would be paid a salary in the range of £65,000 to £75,000 + car allowance + pension, PHI and bonus. 
 This is a newly created position brought about by a significant increase in new customers, strategic acquisitions and continued success in a competitive market place. The FP&A Manager would report directly to the Finance Director and as a key member of the Executive Management team, will manage the FP&A function for the business, providing support and advice to ensure that business decisions are made with the benefit of clear and timely financial information and advice. This role will take more of a macro focus at time, looking at financial strategy with the Finance Director and wider Board members, helping to match the ambitions of the investors for this well placed and already successful business. This is a Business with a high number of customers with recurring revenue, also dealing with big data issues, so these themes need to be embraced in delivering this role successfully. 
 Required Experience 
 As a qualified accountant (ACA, ACCA, ACMA) you will demonstrate effective and efficient budgeting experience with the ability to plan and forecast strategic changes. You must also have financial modelling experience and the ability and personality to act as a Finance Business Partner. A proven leader, you will have the flexibility to adapt to changing conditions and be able to effectively communicate at all levels (including Boradroon level)throughout the business with commitment to quality and service. 
 To apply for the FP&A Manager position in Wolverhampton, please click on the "apply now" button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter (Jamie Carter). We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
