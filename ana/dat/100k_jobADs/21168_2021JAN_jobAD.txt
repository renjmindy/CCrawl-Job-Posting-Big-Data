 Are you immediately available? Do you have extensive experience within a Credit Control position in the manufacturing industry? 
 I am seeking a Credit Controller for a well-known manufacturing organisation, based in Wednesbury for a period of 12 months. You can expect a salary between £25,000 - £30,000 depending on experience. 
 Credit Controller duties: 
 
 Dealing with a high volume of outgoing and inbound calls from customers/ clients 
 Chasing customers via telephone, email, and letter 
 Escalating issues to senior management 
 Placing accounts on/ off hold in at appropriate times 
 Account reconciliation 
 Query resolution 
 Production of statements 
 Review aged debt to minimise outstanding balances 
 Cash allocation 
 Increasing credit limits 
 Checking legal indicators and preparing accounts for litigation 
 
 Experience/ skillset required: 
 
 Have worked within a busy credit control environment at a senior level 
 Previous account reconciliation experience 
 Dealt with a high volume of inbound/ outbound calls 
 Can start on short notice
