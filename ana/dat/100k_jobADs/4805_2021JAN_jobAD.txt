 Position Description: 
 Reporting to the Director of Care, In-Charge RN and Team Lead RPN, you will be responsible for performing basic resident care, personal care, as identified in the resident Care Plan. You will be responsible for documenting, observing, and reporting changes in resident conditions to appropriate team members, and practicing infection control and safety measures in accordance with Oneida Long Term Care policies. Successful candidates will assist assigned residents with activities of daily living and maintaining a safe and clean environment for residents and other duties as assigned. 
 Requirements: 
 
 Personal Support Worker (PSW) Certification from an educational facility approved by the Ministry of Training, Colleges and Universities 
 Proof of a 600-hour program which includes clinical and practicum 
 Current BCLS 
 Demonstrated excellent customer service, organizational and problem solving skills 
 Demonstrated initiative and the ability to function both independently and as part of a multidisciplinary team 
 Demonstrated aptitude to foster caring and trusting relationships with patients and families to support resident and family centered care 
 Demonstrates values consistent with Oneida Long Term Care's Code of Conduct and is committed to fostering a healthy and positive work environment in accordance with Health and Safety policy and legislation
