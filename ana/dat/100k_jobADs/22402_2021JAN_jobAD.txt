 Excavator Operator - 2020 
 Job description 
 Excavator Operator 
- Local Work In Townsville 
- 12 months of work for the right person 
- Work To Commence early-mid January 
 About the Role 
We are currently seeking experienced Excavator Operators with Marine Construction experience for upcoming project work based in Townsville. 
 Skills and Experience 
- Must have over 3 years experience operating an Excavator; Komatsu 45, 60 and 85T machine using GPS (grabs and buckets) to construct a 3 layered rock bund. 
- Experience in Marine or Coastal Construction is highly regarded 
- Ability to work autonomously and in a fast pace environment 
- Ability to work 12-hour shifts / 6 days a week 
- Must have reliable transport and a QLD Drivers License 
- QLD Construction White / Blue Card 
- Ability to pass a Pre-Employment Medical / Dug & Alcohol 
- Passport or Birth Certificate 
- 2 contactable work referees to confirm experience 
- Must be reliable and have a strong commitment to safety 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australias leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the countrys leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels.
