 Adult Social Care Lawyer 
 Service Care Legal are currently working on behalf of a local authority who are seeking an Adult Social Care Lawyer to join their busy Legal Department. 
 This role would require someone to hit the ground running. 
 ROLE: Adult Social Care Lawyer 
LOCATION: Oxfordshire 
RATE: 35 to 45 per hour 
CONTRACT: 3 months ongoing, 37 hours per week 
 The Role 
 , To manager a full caseload of Adult Social Care matters through to completion 
, Cases will include, but not limited to, Mental Capacity Act, Care Act, Mental Health Act and Safeguarding. 
, You will also be expected to deal with Court of Protection applications and provide Care Act advice 
, To provide other council members with up to date legislative advice 
 The Person 
 , A qualified Solicitor, Legal Executive or Barrister with at least 2 years' PQE with Social Care Law 
, Able to manage a full caseload of complex Adult Social Care cases through to completion 
, Previous experience of working within a local authority is essential for this position 
 If this sounds like an opportunity of interest, please feel free to reach out to Lloyd Stanley on 01772 208969, or email Lloyd.stanley@servicecare.org.uk. 
 We also welcome referrals for this position, where a successful recommendation would be worth £250.
