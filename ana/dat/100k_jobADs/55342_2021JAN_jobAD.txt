 We're looking for a Bioprocess Engineer to join our awesome team. 
 As an essential member of our R &D team, you will design and execute experiments to help develop the best adipose tissue culture system in the world. Working alongside brilliant tissue culture scientists and computational biologists, you will spend your time bringing our bioprocess from the bench to the bioreactor. 
 At every step, you 'll play a huge role in shaping the future of a young and dynamic startup. 
 We make real animal fat. Without the animals. 
 Based in London, Hoxton Farms is cultivating a revolution. We combine cell biology and mathematical modelling to make a better kind of fat. Starting from just a few cells, we grow purified animal fat in bioreactors to produce a delicious, cruelty-free and sustainable ingredient. 
 Fat is magic. It's the secret to delicious food. Plant oils aren't a good replacement: the world needs a clean source of animal fat to create meat alternatives that look, cook and taste like the real thing. 
 -- 
 You should have: 
 
 
 
 A PhD (or equivalent experience) in upstream bioprocess engineering, biomanufacturing, biochemical engineering or a related discipline 
 
 
 
 Experience with bench-scale and/or pilot-scale mammalian stem cell bioreactors 
 
 
 
 Enthusiasm, curiosity and drive 
 
 
 
 A talent for team work and collaboration 
 Ideally you'll have experience in some, but not necessarily all, of the following areas: 
 
 
 
 Suspension bioreactor implementation and operation 
 
 
 
 Microcarrier or aggregate culture experience 
 
 
 
 Bioprocess optimisation 
 
 
 
 GMP manufacturing 
 
 
 
 Tissue culture automation 
 
 
 
 Computational bioprocess modelling
