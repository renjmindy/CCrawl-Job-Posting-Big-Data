 Home Based Business Ideas That You Can Start Simultaneously With Your Job 
 __ June 22, 2019December 16, 2019  __ Worziyan Mavolo

 It takes courage and a lot of dedication to start your own business venture. It’s like thinking about things from the perspective of an owner and not an employee. These days many people want to take a break from the daily grind and look for home-based profitable online business ideas so that they can earn more and also relax. 
 No matter how much rewarding your current job is, you would want to look for  small profitable business ideas  list that can help you decide. Luckily, it is quite easy to start a business from scratch and that too without spending a lot of your fortune. Starting your own business is definitely riskier than working regularly at a 9 to 5 job. However, if you do the perfect research about the business you want to start, then you can easily hustle your way to the top. What’s great is that technology is here to help us. It has become quite simple to set up a business online. 
 I guess, it is the time to discuss some home-based  profitable online business ideas . Let’s check them out. 
 1. Creating chatbots 
 I don’t need to stress on the fact that artificial intelligence and machine learning are going to be the two big things in the future. Yes, they still are but many companies are yet to adopt the concepts. If you are someone who has an interest in chatbots that we now see on many famous companies’ websites, then you can also help a business in creating one. It helps the company in interacting with the customers and also understand their behaviour. 
 2. Blogging 
 Let’s name some really big publications. Buzzfeed, Vox Media, and others - what they have in common? Well, they are nothing but blogs that have now become huge media publication houses in the world. You can also start one easily at your home. All you need is a website where you can start writing. You will need to figure out the ways to drive traffic and the best part is that you can write about your favorite field. 
 3. Graphic/Web Designing 
 Are you a creative person who likes to paint their own imagination in any form? If yes, then you can opt for these two different businesses. There are several companies in the world that only provide services for these two and are making huge profits. You can opt for both of them too. 
 4. Social Media Consultant 
 The power of social media and love for it have been witnessed by all of us in the last decade. Most people love social media and spend a lot of time on different platforms. This, in turn, results in becoming an expert of social media. So, this talent can be utilized by providing services to different companies by becoming a social media consultant and these days almost every company needs them. The best part is that you can do it from your home. 
 5. Tester/Reviewer 
 Most of the companies look for people who can test or give reviews of their product before it is launched in the market. This has two benefits - you don’t need to purchase the product as it comes to your free of cost and also understanding the making process of companies. 
 You can opt for these  small profitable business ideas  list and choose what suits you the best.
