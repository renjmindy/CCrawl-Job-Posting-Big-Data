 Retail and Wholesale Trade Manager 
 Pacific Fresh Food Market 
 __Pickering, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Pacific Fresh Food Market is currently hiring a Supermarket Retail and Wholesale Trade Manager for its Pickering location. 
 One of the premier Chinese supermarkets in the Pickering area, Pacific Fresh Food Market offers customers over 10,000 different food and grocery items to choose from, all available in a bright, clean, and well-organized space. Always fresh-in-stock items include fresh fruit and produce, meats, and sea foods. We also offer frozen and packaged foods, snacks, and drinks, while bakery goods and ready-to-eat foods are always prepared fresh daily. 
 We’re currently looking for a Retail and Wholesale Trade Manager to join our growing team to oversee our Pickering store’s product inventory, which mostly consist of Chinese and Asian ethnic grocery goods. 
 Store location:  1300 Kingston Rd, Pickering, ON L1V 3M9 
 Offered salary:  $64,000/year, for 35 hours/week 
 Job duties: 
Evaluate Pacific Fresh Food Market’s current grocery inventory and make suggestions for continuous improvement based on market research and understanding of the purchasing habits of Chinese and Asian consumers 
Forecast future inventory needs based on past sales and inventory depletion figures 
Establish new sourcing networks and strengthen existing supplier relationships for grocery goods desirable to our customers 
Maintain strong business relationships with suppliers and vendors 
Assist with formulating budget for the company’s annual sales and inventory cost 
Assist with developing and implementing marketing and promotional activities including weekly flyer specials, keeping in mind product inventory life cycles 
Help establish Pacific Fresh Food Market’s export wholesale business of Canadian goods to China 
 Job requirements: 
College diploma 
At least 5 years of management experience in the food product or grocery industries 
At least 4 years of purchasing experience in the food product or grocery industries with particular expertise in Asian products 
Fluency in Mandarin is required to communicate with suppliers and vendors 
Can use MS Windows, Microsoft office suite (Word, Excel, Outlook), and inventory tracking software 
 Please send your resume to Tracy Zhuang at pacificfoodhr@gmail.com to apply.
