 Hot Damn! it's a Internship job 
 Look Studios is looking for an Intern Graphic Design in New York, NY, USA. 
 About 
 Looking for interns in New York AND London 
 COMPANY 
Look Studios is a new expansive creative studio with a focus on imaginative visual storytelling. With editorial backgrounds in digital and print for publications such as The New York Times, The New Yorker and Vogue, we bring a journalistic sensibility to a broad range of international clients across disciplines and borders. Specializing in content creation, we merge digital narratives with the real world. The goal of 
Look Studios is to create impactful and immersive experiences that break down barriers and leave a lasting impression. Above all else, we invite you to Look at the world anew. We work with clients including American Vogue, Nike, Chanel, Cartier, Louis Vuitton, MAC, Glossier and Hermès. 
 POSITION OVERVIEW 
As the Studio Coordinator, you will be involved in every aspect of the studio’s logistics alongside the Creative Operations Manager. We are looking for a self-starter with strong organizational and communication skills and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment. 
 WHAT YOU'LL DO: 
Assist Art Director in graphic design of layouts, mockups, decks, editorials 
Support Art Director in executing graphic explorations for various projects 
Manage ongoing conceptual visual research 
Research new creative talent including photographers, directors, editors, and illustrators 
Help with research and archive organization 
Support creative team on various projects 
 REQUIREMENTS: 
Ability to multitask in a fast-paced environment 
Strong eye for design / photography 
Creative problem solving and self sufficiency 
Time management and high level organization skills 
Excellent communicator—in person, over the phone, via email, etc. 
Experience with Photoshop, Premiere, Adobe Suite and Google Suite 
 BONUS SKILLS: 
Experience using Adobe After Effects and other motion graphics software 
Photography 
Production 
Videography 
Video Editing
