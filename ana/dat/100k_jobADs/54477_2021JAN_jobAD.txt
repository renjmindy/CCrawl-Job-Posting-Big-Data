 Dump Truck Operators 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 2:1 Roster 
 FIFO ex Adelaide 
 Great pay rates 
 High Profile Civil Construction Project 
 About the role 
 A Dump Truck Operator is required to form part of the onsite civil project team for a major civil construction project based in Roxby Downs. As a key member of a high performing team, you will play an integral role in delivering the client's civil project outcomes in a safe manner. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Operating Dump Truck. 
 
 
 Proven track record of operating safely 
 
 
 Strong team player who supports our client's operations 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Operation of CAT 745C, 777F and 740B 
 
 
 Verification of Competencies to validate previous operating experience essential 
 
 
 Previous experience working on large civil or mining projects is essential. 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 Australian HR Driver's Licence 
 
 
 Right to Work in Australia
