 Electrician 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Western Brisbane based 
 
 
 Immediate start 
 
 
 Competitive rate of pay 
 
 
 About the Client 
 Our client is a Leading Electrical Contractor with projects across Commercial, Construction and Industrial areas. 
 About the Role 
 You will be a versatile Electrician looking to work alongside a positive and hard working team. 
 Ideally you will be familiar with large scale commercial or warehouse construction projects with the ability to work to deadlines and managed work schedules. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Installing conduits and pipe and all aspects of lighting and power 
 
 
 Cable hauling/pulling 
 
 
 Working on in ground installations 
 
 
 Installation of Cable Tray / Brackets 
 
 
 Skills & Experience/Qualifications 
 
 
 Electrical Licence 
 
 
 LVR/CPR 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 EWP Over 11M 
 
 
 Manual handling certificate 
 
 
 Culture & Benefits 
 
 
 Market Rate 
 
 
 Consistent work 
 
 
 Safety and Team driven environment 
 
 
 Why work for WorkPac? 
 WorkPac is Australia's lading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
