 Remote Mortgage Loan Processor Direct Hire 
   
   Workway, Inc.   
 __Website    __@workway 
 As a Loan Processor, you will prepare and process mortgage loan files from application through approval. This will involve obtains all necessary documentation as well as ensuring compliance with the bank, regulatory, and investor guidelines as well as customer service standards. Your specific duties as a Loan Processor will include: 
 
 Preparing loan files submission to underwriting 
 Reviewing and verifying each file to ensure that all information is correct and current 
 Ordering credit reports, title commitments, surveys, and appraisals from the appropriate parties and reviewing them for acceptability prior to submitting for approval 
 Acting as a liaison and maintaining communication between customers and all parties involved in the transaction 
 Submitting files to underwriting for approval and sending completed loan packages to appropriate underwriter for final approval and validation 
 Preparing approved loans for closing and coordinating closings with lenders, borrowers, and title companies 
 Requesting additional information from title companies, builders, realtors, and others by phone, fax, or letter as required 
 Preparing and mailing Adverse Action Notices and updating the Loan Origination System (LOS). 
 
 **Job Requirements  ** 
 As a Loan Processor, you must have strong analytical, problem-solving, and organizational skills with the ability to thrive in a fast-paced, high volume, and deadline-oriented environment. Specific qualifications for the Loan Processor position include: 
 
 High school diploma or GED; one-year certificate from college or technical school a plus 
 1+ years of recent processing experience in the mortgage industry. 
 Proficiency in conventional, FHA and VA loans a plus 
 Basic understanding of loan processing and closing policies, procedures, documents, underwriting requirements, governmental and agency requirements, terminology and automated processing systems 
 Basic understanding of credit policies and approval processes 
 Computer proficiency, particularly with Microsoft Office Suite 
 Experience with mortgage software, such as Calyx Point, Encompass, Data Track or Empower
