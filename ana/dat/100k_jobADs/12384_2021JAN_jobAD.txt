 Immigrant Services Association of Nova Scotia 
 __Halifax, NS 
 Apply Now 
 English as an Additional Language (EAL) Instructors 
 ISANS is seeking EAL Instructors to teach adult newcomers the language they need to integrate into 
 the community and the labour market. We are looking for full-time, part-time and /or evening 
 Instructors. Reporting to the Supervising Team Lead and the Manager, Language Services, the EAL 
 Instructors will be responsible for: 
Developing and delivering the Community Language Program to adult immigrants using the 
 Community Language Curriculum Guidelines 
 Conducting ongoing needs assessments and language assessments using Canadian Language 
 Benchmarks 
 Conducting client surveys and program evaluations twice a year 
 Preparing and designing appropriate and relevant materials for teaching purposes 
 Keeping up to date records of lesson plans and teaching activities 
 Liaising with other instructors to offer and gain support 
 Keeping up to date on EAL and settlement issues 
 Keeping up to date records of client attendance and other information on database and by other 
 means as required and directed 
 The ideal candidates for these positions will have the following: 
 Education: 
Bachelors Degree or equivalent combination of education and experience will be accepted 
 Recognized TESL qualification 
 Completion of the CLB Bootcamp online training required (http://learning.language.ca/) 
 Experience: 
2 years experience in a similar position teaching adults 
 Experience working in a cross-cultural environment; delivering direct service to immigrants 
 preferred 
 Experience working with Canadian Language Benchmarks 
 Experience in EAL curriculum development 
 Knowledge: 
Understanding and knowledge of adult education principles 
 Skills: 
Strong written and verbal English communication skills 
 Ability to work independently and as part of a team 
 Strong time management and organization skills 
 Proficient with MS Office applications 
 Additional languages an asset 
 Terms of Employment 
 12-month Fixed-term contracts, renewable pending funding confirmation 
 Full-time, part-time and/or evening positions available 
 Depending on position, candidates may be required to have a reliable car and valid driver’s 
 license 
 Commencement Date:  ASAP 
 Closing Date:  ongoing recruitment 
 To apply:  E-mail your cover letter & resume merged in 1 document to careers@isans.ca, stating in 
 subject line the position you are applying for. 
 We wish to thank all applicants for their interest and effort in applying for this position. 
 However, only candidates selected for interviews will be contacted.
