 About Northeastern: 
Founded in 1898, Northeastern is a global research university and the recognized leader in experience-driven lifelong learning. Our world-renowned experiential approach empowers our students, faculty, alumni, and partners to create impact far beyond the confines of discipline, degree, and campus. 
 Our locationsin Boston; Charlotte, North Carolina; London; Portland, Maine; San Francisco; Seattle; Silicon Valley; Toronto; Vancouver; and the Massachusetts communities of Burlington and Nahantare nodes in our growing global university system. Through this network, we expand opportunities for flexible, student-centered learning and collaborative, solutions-focused research. 
 Northeastern's comprehensive array of undergraduate and graduate programs in a variety of on-campus and online formatslead to degrees through the doctorate in nine colleges and schools. Among these, we offer more than 195 multi-discipline majors and degrees designed to prepare students for purposeful lives and careers. 
 About the Opportunity: 
The Roux Institute at Northeastern University is currently seeking a motivated scientific software engineer to research and develop sophisticated analysis tools at the cutting edge of bioinformatics and computational systems biology. One collaborative research project, between the University of Maine (Dr. Benjamin King) and Northeastern University (Dr. Mingyang Lu) aims to devise and optimize an AI-based network modeling software application and develop a related web server. 
 Responsibilities: 
The candidates will participate in a multidisciplinary environment and have opportunities to interact with researchers from Northeastern University, the Roux Institute in Portland, Maine, and University of Maine. 
 Qualifications: 
Applicants must have a strong background in software engineering and software development, with expertise in a popular programming language, such as C++, R and/or Python, and the Unix operating system. Candidates need to have experience in web server development. Applicants must have a commitment to problem solving and good communication skills. A B.S./B.A. degree in Computer Science or a related field is required. A graduate degree in Computer Science or related field and 5+ years of experience is preferred.
