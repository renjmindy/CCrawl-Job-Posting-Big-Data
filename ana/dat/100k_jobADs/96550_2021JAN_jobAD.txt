 Are you an experienced Recruitment Manager looking for a new opportunity in the Cambridgeshire area? Have you worked within the Digital/IT/Technology space? Are you commercially focused, resilient with the gravitas to work in an extremely fast paced environment? 
 If this sounds like you then I am currently looking to recruit a Recruitment Manager based in Cambridge for a Global business. 
 This is a superb opportunity to join an innovative and cutting-edge business who have gone from strength to strength with ambitious growth plans and genuine passion for performance in all of their business areas and in their people. 
 With a Digital/IT/Technology background, you will work in partnership with the business and work with Senior Stakeholders to develop and build the recruitment team and the resourcing strategy. 
 Essential Requirements: 
 
 Previous In-house recruitment experience in the Digital/IT/Technology sector 
 Proven experience of managing and developing a recruitment team 
 Experience of developing a resourcing strategy 
 Experience of talent management including workforce planning, development, retention, and succession planning 
 Experience of direct sourcing tools (such as LinkedIn, talent pooling, Executive Search) 
 Experience of recruiting across EMEA and APAC would be ideal but not essential 
 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
