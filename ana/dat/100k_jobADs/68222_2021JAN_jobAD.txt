 Clinical Services Manager 
 A fantastic new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Clinical Services Manager to work in an amazing care home based in the Leigh, Greater Manchester area. 
 As the Clinical Services Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading healthcare providers 
 This care home provides long and short-term care for people with a range of different care needs, including nursing, residential and palliative care, along with care for older people with disabilities and dementia nursing care 
 As the Clinical Services Manager your key responsiblities include: 
 
 
     To ensure appropriate minimum standards are maintained by monitoring and that appropriate professional support is provided to senior staff /team leaders
 
 
 
     Appropriate reviews of care plans /risk assessments and medication administration
 
 
 
     To ensure all assessment tools are in place and are fully utilised
 
 
 
     Ensure all residents have a complete care assessment prior to admission to the home, taking account of their physical, social and psychological needs
 
 
 
     To ensure as a minimum an annual re assessment of each resident's needs
 
 
 
     Deploy staff efficiently in relation to care duties to be covered by setting up and controlling the duty rota
 
 
 
     Check and monitor care staff hours and authorise time sheets in absence of Home Manager
 
 
 
     To establish good personal relationships with residents and their families
 
 
 
     Work with the Home Manager to ensure all induction and mandatory training is completed
 
 
 
     To identify other specific training needs
 
 
 
     To work with the Home Manager on achievement of action plans from external regulators or internal support functions
 
 
 
 
 An ideal Clinical Services Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position 
 
 
     Ability to communicate at all levels
 
 
 
     Have previous Management experience in a similar setting
 
 
 
     Ability to work on own initiative unsupervised
 
 
 
     Work to deadlines
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
 **The successful Clinical Services Manager will receive an excellent salary of £42,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role for 40 hours a week working through Day Shifts. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Competitive Salary
 
 
 
     Generous Holiday Entitlement
 
 
 
     Training & Progression
 
 
 
     On-site parking
 
 
 
     Flexible working hours
