 Digital Marketing Specialist 
 North America Vocational Education Group 
 __Vancouver, BC 
 Apply Now 
 WEDO Education, a subsidiary of North America Vocational Education Group (NAVEG), is seeking a driven, self-motivated, creative, marketing enthusiast who will take on the role of our Digital Marketing Specialist. This role will be involved in all levels of WEDO’s digital marketing strategy including, paid, owned and earned media. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Work closely with the marketing team to develop social media campaigns that help to achieve corporate marketing goals 
 Plan, coordinate, shoot, produce and edit video stories or videos. 
 Generate, edit, publish and share engaging content daily (original text, photos, videos and news) 
 Develop weekly reports that will be submitted to the marketing manager 
 Stay up-to-date with current technologies and trends in social media, design tools and applications 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 Bachelor degree in Marketing, Communications or related field 
 2+ years' experience in social media management 
 Excellent knowledge of Tiktok, Youtube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, WeChat, Instagram, Google+ and other social media best practices 
 Intermediate-level experience in photography and image editing 
 Excellent copywriting skills 
 
 Job Types: Full-time, Permanent 
 Salary: From $18.00 per hour 
 Schedule: 
 
 8 hour shift 
 
 Language: 
 
 Mandarin (Required) 
 
 Work remotely: 
 
 No
