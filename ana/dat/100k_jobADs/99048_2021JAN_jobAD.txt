 Math Teacher Jobs in New Zealand 
 Are you a qualified math teacher looking for work in New Zealand? There are plenty of exciting job opportunities throughout the country for teachers. The chances of getting a math teacher job are good, due to a shortage of skilled workers in this discipline. There is particularly high demand for math jobs in the Auckland region. There is also a shortage of qualified teachers in rural areas of the country. 
 Working In has the latest math teacher vacancies available. Our vacancies include primary school teaching jobs & secondary school teaching jobs. Browse our job openings and find your ideal teaching role in New Zealand.

 High School Math Teacher Jobs 
 New Zealand has a short supply of qualified math teachers in secondary schools. There's an increasing number of students entering high school and not enough teachers. Many high schools throughout the country are actively recruiting experienced math teachers. If you have relevant experience, apply for one of our math teacher jobs. Your next teaching role could be with one of New Zealand's leading schools! 
 Primary School Math Teacher Jobs 
 Are you looking for primary math teacher jobs in New Zealand? There is a regional shortage of primary school teachers in Auckland. The demand is especially high for teachers of mathematics, along with the sciences. Working In has a range of primary math teacher jobs in New Zealand. Browse our full range of vacancies to find a role suitable for your level of experience.
