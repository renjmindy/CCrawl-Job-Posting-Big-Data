 Screener (Temporary Full-time) - Evening Position 
 Calling all Frontline Heroes who are ready to make a difference. 
 We are painfully aware of the impact that COVID-19 has had on our society. Unfortunately, long-term care and retirement communities have felt this impact even more so, given we take care of the most vulnerable population. 
 We are looking for that special someone who is ready to join our team and support the safety of our Home. If you are passionate about making a difference, have a strong moral compass, are committed to teamwork and not working anywhere else, this is the right opportunity for you! 
 **We are passionate about providing compassionate care for families ' loved ones, where the residents of our home come before anything else. Our values at Berkshire Care Centre follow a ‘People’ focused Philosophy Participation Excellence Openness Performance Longevity Efficient Use of Resources. This philosophy can be seen in every aspect and level of our home. ** 
 As a Screener, you will be part of our Nursing team, and you will report directly to the Director of Care. These positions will be hired for Temporary Full-time hours during the pandemic. 
 The Screener will follow a two-week rotating schedule. Schedule will be 8 shifts and include weekends. A schedule will be provided and flexibility is required.
