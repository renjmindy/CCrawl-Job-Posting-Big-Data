 Senior Nurse 
 Our client is one of UK's leading healthcare providers and they are currently seeking for a motivated Senior Nurse to work in an exceptional care home based in the Croxteth, Liverpool area 
 This is a medium sized home in Liverpool which provides Palliative care, Parkinson's nursing, Dementia nursing and convalescence. 
 The home is  split across five buildings, care for short or long term, nursing and residential dementia care, nursing, respite, convalescence and palliative care 
 As the Senior Nurse your key duties include: 
 
 
     Be responsible for leading a team of Nursing and Care staff to ensure excellent standards of care
 
 
 
     Stand in upon absence of the Unit Manager
 
 
 
     Plan rotas/recruit/train and mentor staff
 
 
 
     Build strong relationships with local regulator/providers and Social Services
 
 
 
     Comply to NMC guidelines with administration of medicine
 
 
 
 An ideal Senior Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     You must have previous experience in a nursing management role
 
 
 
     You must have a proven track record in delivering good care results
 
 
 
     You must have a good understanding of CQC/CI and other regulatory bodies (DoLs/MCA)
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
 **The successful Senior Nurse will receive an excellent salary of £19.00 per hour. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working throughout Day Shifts. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Free DBS
 
 
 
     A fantastic working environment
 
 
 
     Competitive salary
 
 
 
     Flexible Pay
 
 
 
     Robust training programme and ongoing career development
 
 
 
     A comprehensive bonus scheme including: gift vouchers, refer a friend, a pension, attendance reward and much more!
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse either RGN or RMN with a valid NMC Pin
