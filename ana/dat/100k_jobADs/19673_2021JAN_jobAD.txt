 Senior Charge Nurse 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for a dedicated Senior Charge Nurse to work in an exceptional care home based in the Paisley, Scotland area. 
 As the Senior Charge Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This is an amazing home which provides palliative care, Parkinson's nursing, Dementia nursing and convalescence to very high standards to their patients 
 
 As the Senior Charge Nurse your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     Be responsible for leading a team of Nursing and Care staff to ensure excellent standards of care
 
 
 
     Plan rotas/recruit/train and mentor staff
 
 
 
     Build strong relationships with local regulator/providers and Social Services
 
 
 
     Comply to NMC guidelines with administration of medicine
 
 
 
 
 An ideal Senior Charge Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     You must have previous experience in a nursing management role
 
 
 
     You must have a proven track record in delivering good care results
 
 
 
     You must have a good understanding of CQC/CI and other regulatory bodies (DoLs/MCA)
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
 
 **The successful Senior Charge Nurse will receive an excellent salary of  £38,634.96 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role for 42 hours a week, working through out Night Shifts. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Paid Breaks - At selected Homes
 
 
 
     Free Meals On Long Days
 
 
 
     Free DBS
 
 
 
     Ongoing Career Progression
 
 
 
     Pension Scheme
 
 
 
     Assistance With Revalidation
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse either RGN or RMN with a current active NMC Pin
