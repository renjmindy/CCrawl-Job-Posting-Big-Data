 A national commercial fleet business with an outstanding reputation and heritage are looking to recruit a talented Senior Communications Manager into their growing media relations team. 
 Covering a wide geographical area from the South East to the Midlands, this role will be responsible for the following: 
 
 Defining and implementing a ground up communications plan to support the transformation and development of the business 
 Create and deliver a communications change management plan which engages all stakeholders in the project 
 Work alongside the wider marketing and business teams to deliver regular and efficient customer communications 
 Define the brand architecture and lead brand reputation across all formats 
 Develop and execute a media and social media strategy 
 Develop external marketing and communications services to support the communications strategy 
 Work with HR to develop the employer brand proposition and define communications streams to role out. 
 
 The level of position requires experience in the following: 
 
 Experience in leading internal and external communications strategy 
 Change management experience 
 Excellent media relations experience 
 Demonstrable skills in delivering internal communications across a broad employee base
