 Intake and Assessment Social Worker 
 Cardiff Council currently have a vacancy available for a social worker to join their Children's Services. 
 The role will be a full time position with an immediate start available and will be for an initial period of 3 months with a view to this being extended on a rolling basis! 
 The successful candidate will be required to support Children's Services Single Point of Contact which is a front door/intake team. 
 The role will respond to new referrals, undertake assessments and carry out Section 47 investigations across the Cardiff area. 
 The role will be accountable to a team manager, and work as part of a wider dynamic team of experienced staff. 
 The ideal candidate will have: 
, Social work degree, or equivalent 
, Social Work England or Social Care Wales registered 
, Experience of working with children and families 
, Full UK driving licence
