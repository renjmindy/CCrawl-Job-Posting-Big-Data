 Caregiving Jobs 
 Posted on  July 4, 2011  by  caregiver 
 Two Makati based recruitment agencies that are duly licensed and accredited by the POEA is currently in need of caregivers for their available  Caregiving Jobs . The said caregiving jobs is in the holy land of Israel and was approved for recruitment by the POEA last June 3 and 17 2011. For its vacancies, the recruiting agencies are allowed to recruit a total of 43 caregivers who will qualify for the said caregiver work abroad. Specified below are the available caregiving jobs for each of the recruiting agency. 
 Star World International Manpower & Placement Agency, Inc.  --- 20 caregivers 
 CPM Manpower Services, Inc.  ----------------------------------------- 23 caregivers 
 If you wish to submit an application for the said Caregiving Jobs, visit the office of any of the recruiting agency and submit your application together with your resume. For additional information or questions regarding the said caregiving jobs, you may refer to the agency’s telephone numbers or email addresses we have provided below.
