 Head of Development / Development Manager - Hands on. 
 £60,000 per annum - plus bonus 
 An exciting opportunity for a Head of Development / Development Manager for a growing technology lead business based in Lichfield is needed. The role will be working in development team managing a team of between 10-15 developers but also being hands on developing and mentoring junior members of the team. 
 The business is looking to double in size over the next 2-3 years so recruitment and increasing the development function will be required moving forward. 
 The development function uses the .NET Framework so as the role will still be hands on C# & .NET experience is essential. The business is currently working with Agile and Scrum methodologies though someone with a strong background helping implement and improve these areas would be beneficial. 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 Experience of managing/heading up a development team 
 Creating and defining the development strategy moving forward 
 C#, .NET, SQL, 
 Agile/Scrum - Scrum Master certification would be beneficial 
 Mentoring junior members of the team. 
 
 Really exciting business that, that is in a market strong position and looking to take its development team to the next level.
