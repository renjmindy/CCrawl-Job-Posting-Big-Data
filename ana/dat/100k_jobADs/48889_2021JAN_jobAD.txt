 Full-Time Project Developer / Project Manager 
 PROKON Wind Energy UK Ltd -  Vaasa  , Ostrobothnia, Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 Project Developer / Project Manager Central and Southern Finland. Wind power. Prokon Wind Energy UK Ltd is a fast growing company in the field of wind energy. We are a part of the German group of companies Prokon Unternehmensgruppe GmbH, which also acts as a bio-energy, bio-diesel and wood processing industry. The Group currently has a total of 1,000 employees in Germany, Poland and Finland. Operations in Finland began in January 2012. Our office is located in Vaasa Yrittajankatu 13 For further company information, see pages www.prokon.net We are looking for the Vaasa office project developers in wind power projects. The project developer, looking for potential areas of wind power parks. Central and Southern Finland is the main area of work. 
 An important part of your job is to guide the project and the related licensing processes. You contact partners, local authorities, public authorities, energy companies and landowners. You gather information from stakeholders and respond to its success. The work includes traveling domestically, so a driving license is essential. You are a social and negotiation skills as well as you can easily create a new contacts. You are also systematic and enjoy independent study. Require the applicant to complete the management of the Finnish language, as well as a  good knowledge of English . Swedish and German language skills and experience in land use and the related planning would be an advantage. Experience and knowledge, as well as renewable energy projects through a project will be useful, but is not a requirement. We appreciate you have already accumulated contact networks with partners or stakeholders. Your education may be, for example, economic or technical resources in the field. After a few years of work experience are considered in your favor.
