 Core information 
 
 Role title: Project Engineer 
 Remuneration: £40,000 - £60,000 
 Start date: Flexible 
 Report to: Senior Engineer 
 Location: London 
 Hours: Full time 
 Contract: Permanent 
 
 Position 
 Our team is looking to hire a 4+ years experienced project engineering lead to work in our team on the project management of several projects including design, automation, and installation. 
 Our Company 
 ALP Technologies is a profitable renewable engineering energy company based in London and Berlin. We have won numerous awards on research and development projects for technologies to benefit lower income countries covering several different innovative fields and technologies. Our company is designing, prototyping, and testing a state-of- the-art li-ion battery storage system that is the smartest and lowest cost in the world for 4 billion people to access renewable energy. Our battery system called BRIC is the world's most affordable smart battery system designed for low income countries capable to be used by recycled battery cells and operated in a circular economic way. 
 Qualifications 
 Mechanical/electronic/mechatronic engineering background. Keen interest in renewables, energy sector, electrical design.
