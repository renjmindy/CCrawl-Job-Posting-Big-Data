 Are you an experienced Interim HRIS Analyst, seeking their next challenging interim opportunity? Based in Shrewsbury, up to £50k for a 6 month contract. 
 As Interim HRIS Analyst, up to £50k, you will work closely with the Head of Resourcing and be instrumental in reviewing, developing and implementing processes. You will put in place a HR dashboard, producing monthly reports around Resourcing and vacancy data. 
 As an experienced Interim HRIS Analyst you will be experienced in understanding process mapping, pulling together HR KPI's on a monthly basis , and producing robust HR reporting.
