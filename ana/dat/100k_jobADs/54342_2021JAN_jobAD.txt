 Job Title:  Part-Time Packer/Inspector 
 Job Duration:  On-going temporary 
 Pay Rate:  $11+ BOE 
 Hours:  Flexible, you tell us when you are available to work 
 Location:  Huntley, IL 
 Job Description: 
 
 Removes completed thermoformed sheets from the thermoforming machines 
 Visually inspects each thermoformed sheet. Removes and sets aside all non-conforming products as per inspection control plan and/or visual samples per operator/ Q.A. instructions 
 Reports all non-conforming parts to the operator 
 Assembles cardboard cartons 
 Removes individual parts from the thermoformed sheets and stack them per job specifications 
 Loads individual parts per job specifications into cartons and seals each carton 
 Maintains a safe, clean and organized work area 
 
 Requirements   : 
 
 Ability to continuously lift, reach, stand and walk throughout the day 
 Ability to lift up to 50 pounds 
 Excellent attention to detail 
 Good written and verbal communications skills 
 Good manual dexterity and agility 
 6 months pervious production line experience preferred 
 Ability to pass a drug screen and background check
