 Your Opportunity: 
 Description: 
Reporting to the Health Care Manager at Fort Saskatchewan Correctional Centre, this position functions as part of an interdisciplinary team within a Correctional setting with adult patients who present for primary care (routine screening, disease management) and urgent care (injury, illnesses) services. The Nurse Practitioner (NP) is a regulated member of the health care team who utilizes the nursing processes, critical thinking, problem solving and decision making skills to provide care to populations across the life span. The NP provides expert clinical care in managing the health needs of a variety of populations and requires further knowledge and decision making skills in assessment, diagnosis and clinical management. The primary responsibility of the NP is to provide comprehensive patient care as a central member of the health care team. The NP provides leadership and actively participates in program development and evaluation, research and clinical teaching activities. The NP participates in interdisciplinary collaboration to determine and regularly review local guidelines for drug prescription, distribution, monitoring, and record keeping ensuring consistency with provincial prescribing and distributing guidelines.
