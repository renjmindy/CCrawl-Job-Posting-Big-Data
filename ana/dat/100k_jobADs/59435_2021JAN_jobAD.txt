 Job Description 
 Doctor of Veterinary Medicine 
 Responsibilities: 
 · Practice in accordance to state practice act and principles of veterinary medical ethics 
 · Leverage evidence-based medicine and continue pursuit of education to deliver quality patient care 
 · Devote yourself to the culture of preventive care 
 · Adopt and positively impact new veterinary standards and guidelines 
 · Deliver medical and surgical treatments with formulary approved pharmaceuticals, supplies, and equipment, in accordance with Clovis Pet Hospital Medical Quality Standards 
 · Partner with clients as an advocate for the pet to plan follow-up visits, provide at home-care instructions, and education to ensure the lifelong health of their pets 
 · Maintain relevant, comprehensive medical records with the support of practice systems. 
 · Foster an effective veterinary support team by communicating medical standards, ethical practices, and your veterinary knowledge and experience 
 Requirements: 
 · State Veterinary Board License in good standing 
 Additional Licensing: 
 · A State Controlled Substance (SCS) License. If you do not already hold an SCS, Clovis Pet Hospital encourages you to obtain this license after your start date. 
 We understand that your real passion for pet well-being is why you chose this profession. At Clovis Pet Hospital, we believe it is just as important to support the well-being of those who devote their lives to this cause. Through our extensive network and support structure, Clovis Pet Hospital offers a wide array of benefits and programs, empowering you to focus on yourself and your family, as you care for pets and clients. 
 · Competitive salary

 · Medical and dental benefits 
 · $1,500 yearly for continuing education
