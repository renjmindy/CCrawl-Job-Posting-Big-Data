 Terminal Manager - relocate to Brandon, MB 
 Westcan Bulk Transport 
 __Canada 
 Apply Now 
 At Paul’s Hauling, we dedicate ourselves to delivering complete, customer-driven transportation solutions. Our team of industry professionals has built a reputation for safety, on-time delivery, and unparalleled customer service. 
 Our vision will revolutionize transportation and logistics within the liquid bulk industry through its national scale, advanced technology, and an uncompromising commitment to be the employers of choice. 
 Reporting to the Director of Operations, the Terminal Manager is responsible for all aspects of the day-to-day safety and operation of the Brandon Terminal. You will be expected to deliver on your own objectives while supporting the Terminal in all operational activities. 
 About the position: 
 
 Working closely with dispatch team to ensure a superior level of service is provided to all internal and external customers 
 Providing support and direction to the Safety Department to ensure our safety culture is maintained and processes and policies followed 
 Facilitating the effective utilization of people and equipment 
 Preparing annual budgets, analyze monthly financial statements, and monitor terminal profitability 
 Analyzing data to predict staffing requirements, liaise with Human Resources to ensure recruitment needs are fulfilled 
 Responsible for the overall supervision, hiring, retention, training, and development of all staff within the Terminal 
 Ensuring customer requests are responded to in a timely manner, including preparation of required information for meetings and planning sessions 
 Conducting site visits for customer service reviews and service level validation – resolve differences as required 
 Realizing effective communication of available resources to other regional dispatch centers and developing a strong working relationship to ensure effective execution of customer service requirements 
 Ensuring compliance with Federal Regulations and company Policies and Procedures 
 Participating in employee relations, such as dispute resolution, discipline, and communications in conjunction with Human Resources, and create and maintain a positive work environment 
 Working closely with Shop Supervisor to ensure that all equipment that requires repair/maintenance is made available and aligned with maintenance budget 
 Administering other duties as required 
 
 What you bring: 
 
 Post-secondary education and or training in a related field or considerable experience to supplement 
 5 years of diverse experience with supervisory responsibilities in the transportation industry required, with exposure to a variety of commodities 
 Experience with Windows-based programs (Word, Excel, and Outlook), TMW experience is an asset 
 Knowledge of heavy-duty truck maintenance and repairs an asset 
 Required to be on-call as needed 
 Exceptional verbal and written communication skills 
 Well organized, detail-oriented, enthusiastic, and confident with a roll up your sleeves attitude towards work 
 A self-starter with a highly energetic results-oriented work style 
 Ability to maintain a sense of humor in challenging situations 
 Excellent problem solving and analytical skills 
 Capable of handling confidential information with discretion and professionalism 
 Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment, requiring initiative and ambition, able to work independently 
 A solid business presence complimented by personal drive, resourcefulness, maturity, integrity, honesty, and sound business judgment 
 
 What’s in it for you: 
 
 Very competitive compensation including immediate company-paid benefits package for you and your family 
 We offer a wide range of other employee programs and incentives including a pension program, annual bonus opportunities, and scholarships opportunities for your children 
 We are a fun team and maintain a family-like atmosphere 
 We listen to your ideas and give you an opportunity to make changes 
 We have an uncompromising commitment to safety and lead by example every day through actively caring about one another
