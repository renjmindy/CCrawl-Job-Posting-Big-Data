 Job Description 
 Clearfire is searching for a full time front-end web developer to join our team. This position will be tasked with frontend markup (HTML, CSS, Javascript), Craft and other CMS implementation, and backend development. We're looking for a versatile developer who can work within a variety of platforms and languages. 
 Our projects vary in size and complexity, and often include features and functionality that go beyond traditional brochure sites. We integrate our clientâ€™s sites with a variety of systems and APIs, which involve custom CMS plugin development or more complex web applications. 
 This is a remote position, and we're looking for a full-time team member with daytime availability. 
 About Clearfire 
 Clearfire is a small team of creative professionals. We operate with our clients as partners, digital strategists and an extended production team. 
 We've been at this a long time, and draw from a sizable vault of experience when tasked with solving problems through design and technology. Our clients range from small web startups to Fortune 100 companies, each with their own unique challenges. 
 Requirements 
 You should be able to say yes to  **  most **  of the following: 
 
 Do you have experience with Craft CMS or other CMS platforms? 
 Are you experienced in front-end web development via HTML, CSS and Javascript? 
 Do you have experience in using CSS pre-processors (SASS, LESS)? 
 Can you to take provided designs (Photoshop, Sketch, Figma), extract assets, and create front-end markup? 
 Do you have an eye for detail? 
 Are you competent or familiar with version control (Git)? 
 Do you have experience creating front-end markup using web standards, responsive design and cross-browser compatibility? 
 Have you worked remotely before? 
 
 Nice to haves: 
 
 Experience with frontend CSS and JS frameworks like Tailwind, Vue.js, Alpine.js, etc. 
 Experience with other CMS or eCommerce platforms like Statamic, Wordpress, ExpressionEngine and Shopify. 
 Experience with PHP and frameworks like Laravel.
