 Design Lead 
 JPC Architects - Seattle, WA 
 Posted: ** 1/15/2021 ** 
 
  Apply Now  
  Save Job  
  __ Print     __ Email     __ Share  
 
 **** JPC Architects is looking for a Design Lead to join our firm. 
 JPC Architects is the leading corporate interiors and architecture firm in the Pacific Northwest. Here at JPC, we think outside the box and challenge norms to create attractive, innovative, cohesive spaces that exceed our clients’ expectations. Our collective team of architects, interior designers, and technical staff is committed to excellent client service and superior design. 
 The  Design Lead  position is action-oriented, forward-looking, and broad in scope. It requires a strong sense of urgency, initiative and competitive drive to get things done by influencing others, delegating, developing contacts and building relationships. There are a wide variety of activities that allow for individual expression and freedom to take action independently. Having a positive impact on others in order to achieve results is also important. The position requires social poise, warmth and friendliness, as well as the ability to stimulate and motivate others in order to achieve the results necessary for success. Frequently, there will be a need for flexibility, risk taking, and innovation. 
 Position Overview: 
 
 
 Firm-wide Design Resource - enthusiastically share your passion for design and your expert knowledge with JPC 
 
 
 Lead presentations to large, high-profile clients 
 
 
 Establish and maintain strong client relationships 
 
 
 Establish creative concepts that meet clients’ vision and support project scope and budget 
 
 
 A design mentor across the firm, guiding team members in their professional growth 
 
 
 Actively participate with external industry groups on behalf of JPC 
 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 
 Bachelor’s or Master’s Degree in Architecture 
 
 
 Architectural license 
 
 
 12+ years of Interior Architecture experience 
 
 
 Proven track record of establishing and maintaining clients 
 
 
 Experience in all phases of design with a primary focus on concept development and design direction 
 
 
 Demonstrated ability to mentor junior staff 
 
 
 Ability to collaborate effectively with a diverse team of designers, clients, contractors, consultants and other key stakeholders 
 
 
 We have celebrated over 30 years in business, growing to a staff of 70 talented team members. At JPC, you can expect to find a dynamic, fun, and creative environment. Our culture fosters collaboration and the pursuit of fresh ideas. 
 We work hard and have fun doing it. Not only will you find us creating amazing designs for our clients, you’ll also find us celebrating staff birthdays and anniversaries, giving back to our community, working out together at our onsite gym, enjoying happy hours (all virtually for now, of course!), and coming together as a team to reach our goals. 
 To find out what it’s like to work at JPC, check us out on . 
 JPC offers competitive compensation and excellent benefits including flexible work schedules, Paid Time Off, medical, dental, vision, 401K, and more. 
 What’s next?  Send us your resume and portfolio, describing how insanely talented you are, and we’ll set up a time to connect.
