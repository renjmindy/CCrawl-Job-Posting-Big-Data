 Remote Autocad Designer at Essentia, Inc. 
 
 We update this page regularly once we find more remote AutoCAD drafting jobs opportunity in this location. 
 Key  __ Responsibilities 
 
 Create design drawings and splice schematics in AutoCAD for aerial and underground fiber optic projects on deadline and at a high level of quality 
 Perform quality assurance on drawing packages created by other CAD designers 
 Revise drawings per redlines 
 Learn and apply standards of drafting and engineering practices 
 Develop templates used to create drawing packages. 
 Provide technical support to design engineers and construction management 
 Assist supervisor with various administrative duties, such as engineering records, permitting, or obtaining existing utility or right of way records. 
 Maintain project drawing files, drawing library, and associated records and documentation per company policies. 
 Identify and troubleshoot problems whenever needed to move projects forward. 
 
 Experience & Qualifications 
 
 At least 2 years AutoCAD experience in the telecommunications, including aerial and underground fiber optic networks. 
 Proficient with Microsoft Office (Outlook, Excel, Word), and Google Earth as well as editing in ArcGIS tools. 
 
 · Able to perform work and duties independently as well as in a team environment 
 
 Outstanding communication skills (both written and verbal) and customer service skills 
 Detail orientation and organization skills 
 Basic mathematical skills, e.g. averages, percentages.
