 Packaging Assemblers - Day Shift 
 Job description 
 Packaging Assemblers 
Our client, a Specialty Packaging company based in Brompton, are seeking experienced Packaging workers to join their team ASAP. 
 Start ASAP 
Ability to work fast and meet KPI's 
 About the Role 
The successful candidates will be experienced packaging operators who are physically fit and thrive on working in a team environment. These candidates will be assembling cardboard materials and packing on to pallets ready for delivery. The work is quite repetitive so candidates must be able to work autonomously. 
 Skills/ Experience 
Available Monday to Friday on DAY/AFTERNOON/NIGHT shift 
Be available five days per week 
Able to do repetitive physical tasks for 8+ hours a day 
Quick learner and fast worker 
A strong work ethic, and able to use initiative 
Must have a strong knowledge of OHS procedures
