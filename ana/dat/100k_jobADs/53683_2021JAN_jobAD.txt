 CAREER 
 
 
 
 
 Working at WIR elektronik stands for teamwork with enthusiasm for new innovative products in a modern and technically well-equipped working environment. 
 We have complex tasks and work areas from production over administration to our own development department. Trusting and respectful interaction at all levels is an essential component of our daily work; not only on the paper. Because WIR knows: Only with a dedicated team of satisfied employees, products will be created that will make our customers’ life easier. 
 WIR is always on the outlook for unconventional thinkers who take responsibility and work together to shape the future of WIR elektronik GmbH. Our employees get the freedom to continue their personal and professional development in order to continue growing in the future.
