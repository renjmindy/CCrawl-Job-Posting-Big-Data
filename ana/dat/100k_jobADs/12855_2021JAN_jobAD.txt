 Speech Language Therapist 
 Apply for the position, Speech Language Therapist, with Developmental Services of Northwest Kansas today or contact Christina Peterson for more information. Posted Dec 10, 2020. 
  Apply Online  __  [ (785) 625-5678  __](tel:(785) 625-5678)   [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 One full time position available, can be located in Stockton or Russell. 
 Assist in the planning, organizing and the implementation of the early intervention services to infants and toddlers with developmental disabilities within the counties served by Kid-Link. Assess, evaluate and provide assistance in the development and implementation of the Individualized Family Service Plan (IFSP) in meeting goals that are driven by the family outcomes desired.  Provide services to the child/family utilizing the Primary Service Provider model to build capacity of the parents/caregivers to support the child. The treatment of the children and their families with dignity and respect is a prime responsibility. 
 Education - Master's degree in Speech-Language Pathology, Communications Disorders and Certification by the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association. 
 Experience - Experience with individuals with developmental disabilities preferred. 
 Benefits available include Health/Dental/Rx coverage through BCBS, Vision through Vision Care Direct, Aflac Policies, FSA/HSA, company paid life insurance, and retirement through KPERS.
