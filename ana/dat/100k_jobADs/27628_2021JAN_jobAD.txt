 Are you an experienced Management Accountant? Would you like to work for a reputable, professional and solution focused business? If so, then please read on. 
 You will be joining a small, committed, and hardworking finance team, reporting directly into the Finance Director near Birmingham City Centre. 
 As a part-qualified management accountant, you will work to ensure timely reporting and accurate month end accounts. To be considered, you will be part qualified (ACCA or CIMA), ideally this role will suit someone who has previously worked in an SME environment. 
 Responsibilities include: 
 
 Delivering value-add monthly management accounting information 
 Preparation of all month end reporting requirements 
 Preparing final journals for accruals and prepayment 
 Acting as Finance Business Partner to a variety of non-finance stakeholders to support the budgeting & forecasting timetable 
 Preparation of cash flow 
 Variance analysis 
 Ownership of the P&L and balance sheet 
 Complex reconciliations 
 Transactional invoice processing and other ad hoc tasks as required 
 
 Is this role right for you? 
 This is a great opportunity for you to join as a Management Accountant. If you enjoy working in a team, often on your own initiative and have excellent excel skills. The successful candidate will be fully supported on a path towards becoming a valued and highly visible member of the finance team. 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 CIMA/ACCA part-qualified 
 Strong management accounting & analysis skills 
 High level of attention to detail 
 Excellent verbal, interpersonal, and written communication skills 
 A "hands-on" attitude, comfortable with completing month and year end tasks 
 
 Benefits: 
 
 Salary up to £40k per annum 
 Plus an attractive benefits package, including flexible working.

We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application.
