 IQ Inc.  is currently seeking an experienced  Software QA Test Engineer  who has a desire for achieving excellence in customer satisfaction, process and product quality, and reliability. In this role, you will work closely with your team to complete ongoing testing efforts and scheduled tasks in a Linux or UNIX Environment. Excellent communications skills are a must! 
 IQ Inc.  is located in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. We partner with innovative industries throughout the United States to create and enhance life-changing technologies. At IQ we are passionate about the growth and development of our people, our clients, and our community. 
 In this role you will: 
 
 Create test plans/protocols/procedures/cases for each release. Assure all test documentation is complete prior to the start of the test cycle. 
 Execute tests according to defined test plans, procedures, and cases. 
 Identify and log defects in the defect tracking tool. 
 Assist with the classification, management and resolution of all software defects. 
 Participate in program, design, and document reviews (including requirements reviews). 
 Assists the Functional Manager in the establishment/modification of department processes and follows/documents all work associated with the assigned responsibilities fully and accurately. 
 Follow established Quality Assurance processes and standards for the formal delivery of work artifacts. 
 Complete specific work as assigned with minimal assistance. Assignments are varied in nature and limited in scope/complexity. 
 
 
 Some "must-haves" include: 
 
 3+ years of professional experience in software testing 
 working knowledge and understanding of Quality Assurance practices 
 fundamental knowledge of Linux or UNIX operating system 
 familiarity with basic networking concepts 
 proven ability to be a collaborative team member in a testing effort for complex and medium size projects 
 Microsoft Windows operating system education/experience 
 Microsoft Office, EXCEL and WORD education/experience 
 
 It would be great if you also had: 
 
 working experience with scripting languages like Python, Perl, BASH 
 knowledge and use of quality management methods and tools. 
 
 We also need someone who has a   positive attitude  ,  **analytical thinker, motivated by working as part of a terrific team, and is great with people.  ** We are interested in every qualified candidate who is eligible to work in the United States. However, we are not able to sponsor visas. 
 **  We have some of the best employees who want to change the world through technology and community involvement. Why not join us... **
