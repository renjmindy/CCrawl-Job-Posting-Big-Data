 Finance Analyst 
 Architect Films Inc. 
 __Toronto, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Job Description 
 
 Analyst responsible for Architect’s corporate accounting. Reporting to the Executive in Charge of Finance. 
 Responsible for managing accounts payable, payroll w/Ceridian, PPE, accounts receivable and revenue. 
 Complete period-end procedures within a public company’s control and IFRS accounting framework. 
 Assist the Executive in Charge of Finance and Production Accountants for any related corporate audits, production audits, and tax credit audits. 
 Ownership of production company accounting, once production has been completed. 
 Track and comply with HST and Payroll tax filings. Assist with corporate tax filings. 
 
 Opportunities 
 
 Develop, under an industry-trained CPA, quarterly and annual financial reporting skills within a publicly traded company. 
 Gain comprehensive knowledge on the film industry funding environment. 
 Increase in responsibility within a fast-growing company. 
 
 Requirements 
 
 3-5 years of accounting experience. 
 Experience with large enterprise resource planning systems; Netsuite experience would be preferred. 
 Organization skills are critical to the role and if missing any key requirements, a willingness to learn. 
 Television or Film industry experience an asset, including knowledge of Ontario & Federal tax credits, as well as any additional funding opportunities. 
 Corporate Tax experience an asset. 
 
 Job Type: Full-time 
 Experience: 
 
 accounting: 3 years (Preferred)
