 We currently have a full-time opportunity of a Financial Analyst in our Administrative Office in SW Calgary. 
 Find meaningful work with seniors 
 Enjoy working on a high-performance team while making a difference in the lives of independent seniors. 
 Choose an opportunity to support our vibrant communities across Calgary 
 Benefit from flexible hours, healthcare coverage and temporary incentives. 
 Reporting to the Chief Development Officer and working within the Silvera Capital Development Department, the Financial Analyst duties: 
 Key responsibilities include: 
Partners with Project Managers to ensure data accuracy. Prepare analysis of performance for each project and perform variance analysis, including but not limited to forecasts, invoicing, and budgets. Perform weekly and monthly analysis of program performance and trends, and prepare various financial reports thereon (including bench-marking of operational metrics) 
Collecting and analyzing financial data including budgets, income statement forecasts etc. 
Support internal processes in terms of delivery and financials, including procuring, budgeting, costing, resource allocations, time entry and corrections, and accounts payable. Assist with bank reporting requirements 
Developing recommendations Change to supportsccostvteviews etc to reduce costs and improve company's financial performance 
Ensuring that company's goals and financial standing are taken into account 
Collecting and processing data to prepare sophisticated reports on financial status and risks 
Building financial models, and performing bench-marking and process analysis 
Analyzing company's actual results in comparison with forecasts to track and determine its financial status 
Organizing business studies on past, future and comparative performance and developing forecast models. Researching and identifying trends 
Reconciling transactions by comparing and correcting data 
Consulting with managers to help with long term and strategic decision making 
 Qualifications required are: 
Degree or Certificate in Accounting, Finance or Commerce preferred 
3+ years' experience of cost accounting for multiple projects. Relevant prior experience in the construction industry and with YARDI systems would be an asset 
Profound experience of working with statistical analysis and statistical packages using various tools such as spreadsheets, databases, MS Office and financial software applications 
In-depth knowledge of financial forecasting and diagnosis, corporate finance and information analysis 
Excellent presentation, reporting and communication skills. High attention to detail, organized and thorough 
Strong knowledge of current financial subjects, accounting, tax laws, money market and business environments
