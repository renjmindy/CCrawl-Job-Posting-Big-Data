 Level 7 
 Job description 
 Have experience or be eager to train and work in various areas of our meat processing plant 
Be comfortable working in a production environment, boning room or slaughter floor as well as handling raw meat and animal bi products. 
Learn and perform labouring duties such as trimming, packing, weighing, labelling, machine operating, manual handling and cleaning 
Comfortable with heavy lifting and being on your feet for 9 hour 
Comfortable working in an environment with meat 
Reliable, committed, punctual, willing to learn and have a positive attitude 
Experience in a meatworks facility is preferred 
 Mon-Fri (5 days per week) 
Start times between 5:00am-6:30am 
8 hours per day 
Have reliable transport and drivers licence 
Willing to undergo pre-employment medical (including a drug and alcohol test) and ONLY FOR GOING PERM 
Must be willing to undertake Q Fever immunisation. The cost will be charged back to the client 
Must be physically fit
