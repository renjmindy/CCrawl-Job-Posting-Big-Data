 Job description 
 Construction Labourer 
 
 
 Flat rate 
 
 
 Local work 
 
 
 Immediate start 
 
 
 About the Role 
The successful candidate will be working on a construction project in the Dubbo Area. 
 Previous experience in a construction environment would be advantageous. You will be well versed in working as part of a dedicated team to get projects completed on time, whilst strictly adhering to WHS. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 General labouring duties 
 
 
 Assist tradespeople on site 
 
 
 Assist with pipelaying 
 
 
 Reading of laser 
 
 
 Assist with construction of roads and footpaths 
 
 
 Follow instruction from supervisor 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 General Construction Induction card 
 
 
 Previous labouring experience 
 
 
 Demonstrated ability to work safely and effectively while unsupervised and also as a project team member 
 
 
 A commitment to Safety, Environmental and Cultural Awareness 
 
 
 Ability to pass a drug and alcohol test 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels. 
 Apply online today with a current resume including copies of current qualifications and two contactable work referees. 
 At WorkPac, we recognise we are strengthened by diversity. We are committed to providing a work environment in which everyone is included, treated fairly and with respect. We are an Equal Opportunity employer and we encourage applications from women and Indigenous people.
