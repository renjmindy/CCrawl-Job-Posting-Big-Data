 Deputy Manager 
 A fantastic job opportunity has arisen for a committed Deputy Manager to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the New Eltham, London area. 
 As the Deputy Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This care home is designed for older people requiring dementia nursing and general nursing care. 
 The home offers a supportive environment with comfortable lounge areas. 
 The dedicated team ensure we meet the resident's individual care and social needs 
 
 As the Deputy Manager your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     To support the Home Manager by being responsible for key areas of service provision and in their absence to ensure that high standards of care are achieved in line with regulatory requirements and that the service remains viable
 
 
 
     To provide leadership and advice to colleagues in addition to undertaking line management responsibilities and Deputise for the Home Manager in their absence
 
 
 
     Co-ordinate and lead on the quality and management of care and treatment of residents living within the home, providing support for residents when required
 
 
 
     Participate fully with the multidisciplinary team and undertake direct patient care as required
 
 
 
     Manage and direct the delivery of clinical services through line management of the Clinical Lead within the home, ensuring it complies with statutory regulations, current legislation and meets quality standards
 
 
 
 An ideal Deputy Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Strong Management and Leadership skills
 
 
 
     Caring & compassionate with an excellent clinical track record
 
 
 
     Passionate about delivering outstanding care to older people
 
 
 
     Excellent organisation skills
 
 
 
     Effective communication skills
 
 
 
     Flexible, professional and committed to being the best
 
 
 
     Quality driven with a desire to continually improve yourself & our services
 
 
 
 
 **The successful Deputy Manager will receive an excellent salary of £22.50 per hour and the annual salary for this position is £46,800 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 40 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Range of salary sacrifice benefits including pensions options, technology purchase schemes, cycle to work scheme and more
 
 
 
     Online benefits portal with a range of offers and cashback rewards
 
 
 
     28 days annual leave 
 
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a current active NMC Pin
