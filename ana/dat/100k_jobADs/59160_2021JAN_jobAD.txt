 Care Giver Jobs Abroad 
 Posted on  December 9, 2010  by  caregiver 
 Three POEA accredited recruitment agencies are currently accepting applicants for their POEA approved  Care Giver Jobs Abroad . These care giver jobs abroad are for the Holy Land of Israel and were approved for recruitment by the POEA last November 26 and 30. For the said care giver jobs abroad, a total of 112 care giver jobs vacancies are being offered to those who are looking for caregive work abroad. Specified below is the breakdown of the vacancies for the said POEA approved care giver jobs abroad. 
 Star World International Manpower & Placement Agency, Inc.  --– 54 vacancies 
 Uniwide Technical Services Inc.  --------------------------------------- 28 vacancies 
 HRD Employment Consultant and Multi-Services, Inc.  --------–----- 30 vacancies 
 If you are interested in applying for any of the said POEA approved Care Giver Jobs Abroad, simply visit the office of the recruiting agency and submit your resume together with your application.
