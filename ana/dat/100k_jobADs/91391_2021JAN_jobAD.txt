 PROJECT MANAGER   DIGITAL   MARKETING   BRANDING   CONTRACT   LONDON   ASAP 
 My client is part of a leading firm in its field and are urgently looking for an experienced Project Manager to work with marketing teams within the business on a large Digital Marketing Programme to lead a number of different digital marketing initiatives and to bring what they have already to a whole new level. 
 We're looking for someone who comes from a Marketing and/or Change background and has strong experience leading large digital / marketing / branding type projects end to end in large organisations. 
 The ideal candidate MUST have all of the following: 
 -  An experienced Project Manager 
 - Strong experience of leading marketing/branding type projects end to end 
 - Worked in large organisations and with global teams 
 - Strong communication across all levels of the business up to C-Level execs 
 - Understand Project methodology such as Agile/Waterfall 
 - Insurance / Financial Services sector experience is beneficial but not essential 
 If interested, please apply ASAP or send your latest CV to  jkaur@venquis.com  summarising relevant experience on your CV.
