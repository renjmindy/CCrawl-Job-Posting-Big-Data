 Registered General Nurse 
 An excellent job opportunity has arisen for a committed RGN to work in an exceptional care home based in the Halewood, Liverpool. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This special care home provides the highest quality of care to all their residents. 
 They pledge to raise standards across the board, working with the CQC to ensure that when next inspected, the home receives a better rating. 
 Their friendly and approachable management teams are always on the go 
 As a Nurse your day to day duties include: 
 
 Leading, supervising and training the care team 
 General Nursing duties-Wound care/general observation/blood sugar level testing/liaising with other healthcare professionals to give the best care. 
 Being responsible for ordering, storing and administering medication 
 Keeping precise, up to date records 
 Maintaining the Client's dignity and respect at all times 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these knowledge and skills as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 Excellent communication skills 
 Ability to deliver care, without direct supervision 
 Competent in Medication Management 
 Ability to follow the Nursing process-Assessment/Diagnosis/Planning/Implementation/Evaluation 
 Maintaining the Client's dignity and respect at all times 
 
 **The successful Nurse will be offered an excellent salary of £18.00 per hour. 
 We currently have vacancies for both Full Time or Part Time positions available for Nights only. 
 In return for your hard work and dedication you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 Paid Breaks - At selected homes 
 Free meals on long days 
 Free DBS 
 Ongoing career progression 
 Pension scheme 
 Assistance with Revalidation 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a current active NMC Pin
