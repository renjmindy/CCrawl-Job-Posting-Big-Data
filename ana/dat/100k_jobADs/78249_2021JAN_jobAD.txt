 Civil Labourer 
 Job description 
 About the vacancies 
 WorkPac are seeking experienced Civil & Construction Labourers for work assignments across various sites in Brisbane. These positions range from general labouring to skilled labouring opportunities that have the opportunity of ranging from short term to long term roles. Although we are seeking people with an extensive background in either the Construction or Civil industry, we are also encouraging people who are new to these industries to also apply for the opportunity to further their career as a Labourer. The successful candidates will have the opportunity to work Monday - Friday on dayshifts with the potential to work nightshifts when required and you will be exposed to a variety of different tasks across multiple sites. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Setting up & cleaning sites 
 
 
 Trade Assisting 
 
 
 Spotting machinery 
 
 
 Pipe laying 
 
 
 Pit Building 
 
 
 Grade checking 
 
 
 Trenching & back-filling 
 
 
 Utilising hand & power tools 
 
 
 Lifting & carrying materials 
 
 
 Loading & unloading vehicles 
 
 
 Working in a team environment 
 
 
 Following strict safety standards 
 
 
 Skills & Qualifications 
 
 
 MUST have Construction White/Blue Card 
 
 
 Background across the Civil or Construction industry highly desirable 
 
 
 Ability to follow directions and work apart of a team 
 
 
 Australian drivers license 
 
 
 Availability for an immediate start 
 
 
 Reliable and strong work ethic 
 
 
 Culture & Benefits 
 
 
 Full time hours 
 
 
 Ongoing work for the right candidate 
 
 
 Wages paid weekly 
 
 
 Apply Now! 
 WorkPac is Australia's largest, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Looking after our employees is important to us.
