 Freelance Writer 
 WordSouth is a creative services firm. We work with electric utilities and telecommunications companies that serve rural and small-town America. Their mission becomes our mission as our hard work and big ideas generate results and earn their trust in us as a true communications partner. WordSouth is seeking talented contract freelance writers to write articles about a variety of topics that will align with our clients’ storytelling and marketing goals. 
 Job Type  : Contract 
 Contract Duration  : Per-assignment basis 
 Work Location  : Remote 
 Ideal candidates have the following qualifications  : 
 
 Proven work experience as a journalist, reporter, copywriter, or similar position 
 Ability to write about a wide variety of topics 
 Excellent communication and writing skills 
 Exceptional attention to detail 
 Ability to follow strict deadlines and fact-check information 
 Highly proficient at basic computer skills (word processor, web search, etc.) 
 Portfolio of previous work 
 Strong research skills 
 
 Responsibilities  : 
 
 Write quality articles acceptable for publication 
 Interview key persons to gather information for story 
 Research and organize key facts 
 Respond to communication in a timely manner (email, phone calls, etc.) 
 Adhere to all deadlines and detailed instructions for each assignment 
 
 Compensation  : Negotiated per project 
 To apply, please send resume and link to portfolio to  hello@wordsouth.com .
