 Clinical Team Leader 
 A fantastic new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Clinical Team Leader to work in an exceptional hospital based in the Darlington, Country Durham area. 
 As the Clinical Team Leader you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This hospital provides specialist inpatient care for adults with mental health needs, who present with complex needs and behaviours that challenge, in a safe and supportive environment 
 As the Clinical Team Leader your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     You need to be able to demonstrate quality in delivery of nursing care and the clinical environment
 
 
 
     Ensure that MHA and MCA standards are upheld and work to uphold NMC's code of practice whilst ensuring all relevant policies are in place, reviewed and updated as and when necessary (e.g.  
 
 
 
 ward guidelines/patient information) in liaison with the Ward Manager and MDT 
 
 
     Provide support for the Ward Manager in delivering Clinical Leadership for the nursing team at all times
 
 
 
     You will participate in pre-admission assessments and the hospital clinical governance agenda and monitor standards of care through regular audit and take action where standards are not met
 
 
 
     Ensure clinical records are well maintained and systems are in place for staff to communicate effectively on clinical issues ensuring awareness of legal implications and ensure all ward management responsibilities are met and all reports are up to date
 
 
 
     Responsible for ensuring that the ward team possesses the skills, knowledge and motivation to deliver the highest quality of care to service users with complex mental health and self harming behaviour, and their families
 
 
 
 An ideal Clinical Team Leader must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Excellent clinical, team and communication skills
 
 
 
     Able to demonstrate quality in delivery of nursing care and the clinical environment
 
 
 
     Be forward-thinking Nurses, who will lead and coach staff by example
 
 
 
     Have noticeable leadership skills along with a strong drive to develop other members of the team
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
 **The successful Clinical Team Leader will receive an excellent salary from £34,471.06 to £46,260.24 per annum + including enhancements. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:**
