 As Regional Property Manager you have the opportunity to join a people focused organisation that is passionate about their brand. Our client is looking for the right experience but more importantly the right culture fit. If you are a Regional Property Manager and feel this could be you then read on; 
 We are working with an exciting organisation that own a large portfolio of properties in the UK within the hospitality sector. They are truly excited and passionate about their brand and place their people and customers first in everything they do. 
 They now seek a Regional Property Manager to manage capital expenditure of maintenance and fit-out works across their property base in the Southern area of the UK. The region covers the Midlands down to the South Coast and across to East Anglia. Therefore, the ideal candidate will reside within this region however our client may consider those outside of this area. 
 The successful Regional property Manager will be able to show a genuine passion for the industry and their role and be a people person. In addition, they will be able to demonstrate a stable career history managing contractors and CAPEX to ensure maximum value for their organisation. Projects will range in size and you will have performed a similar role for either a contractor or client in either hospitality, retail or leisure sectors. 
 On offer the successful Regional Property Manager is a competitive base salary of up to £54,000 company car or car allowance and an amazing benefits package. 
 To apply for this Regional Property Manager opportunity please click the "apply now" button and your CV will be sent directly to the Zac Doherty. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however, please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
