 Caregiver Job Work 
 Posted on  September 20, 2013  by  caregiver 
 A leading recruitment agency for caregivers who wish to work in Israel is again looking for qualified caregivers for their POEA approved  Caregiver Job Work . The said caregiver job work had its Job Order approved by the POEA last September 6 and 16, 2013 allowing the said recruitment agency to recruit a total of 60 caregivers for placement in caregiver work abroad. 
 To apply for the said Caregiver Job Work, please visit the office of CPM Manpower Services Inc. and personally submit your detailed and updated resume. If you have any question you wish to ask regarding the said caregiver job work, you may contact CPM Manpower Services Inc. by calling any of their telephone numbers listed below.
