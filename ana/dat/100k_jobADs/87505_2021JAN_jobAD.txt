 Job Description 
 Front Desk Agent- 
 A Front Desk Agent is responsible for assisting guests with a continuous effort to deliver outstanding guest service. As a Front Desk Agent, you will assist in the following; 
 -Check in/Check out procedures 
 -Answering telephone calls 
 -Assist guests & potential guests with inquiries and reservations, including walk-in guests, telephone calls and emails. 
 -Obtain payments with acute accuracy 
 We go above and beyond for our guests. Their time away from the everyday should be anything but ordinary and positively extraordinary. 
 Position Requirements; 
 -Must have customer service experience 
 -Must be a people person. If you love humans, this is the place for you! 
 -Must enjoy being part of a fun, hard-working, & loyal team 
 -Must provide an updated resume with 3 professional and 3 personal references 
 -Must be able to pass a background check 
 -Must possess the ability to adapt in a fast-paced, fun and BEAUTIFUL environment 
 -Must be willing to assist with housekeeping duties a few days a week throughout the winter months and during training. 
 Our guests are what make our world go-round, but we couldn’t do it without our amazing staff. We have staff members that have been a part of our team for up to 10 years! If you’re looking for a place to invest your skills that offers room to grow and learn, then we want to invest in you! Hourly rate starts at $13 but depends on experience. Housing may be available to the right candidate. Please apply within; 
 Western Riviera Lakeside Lodging & Events
