 CAREERS 
 HorrorAddicts.net  is a fan-based, fan-run horror podcast, blog, and publishing site. We do not pay those who work for us, but we do offer free training and other benefits that come with being part of an ever-evolving team of horror enthusiasts. Those who have been on staff have gone on to run their own blogs, have their work published, and even produce movies. Whether you are an aspiring writer or entertainer looking to get your feet wet in the industry, an established professional in the horror biz, or just a fan like us, we welcome you. Our goal is to inspire, promote, and geek-out with Horror Addicts around the globe. If that is your goal too, come join the fun. 
 OPEN POSITIONS 
 **************************************** 
 WRITER 
 HorrorAddicts.net is always looking for writers. If you have knowledge or even an interest in reviewing, reporting, or digging up news on horror topics, you are right for the job. A healthy amount of fan boy/girl geekiness is an added plus. 
 Basic skills:  A general knowledge of Facebook and email is expected. A good knowledge of writing, grammar, English, and the area you are writing in is expected. Added plus if you know how to use WordPress, but that can all be taught if needed. 
 POST ENDS:  Always open. 
 To Apply  : Send answers to the following questions to[ horroraddicts@gmail.com](mailto:%20 horroraddicts@gmail.com) with the subject line: WRITER. 
 
 What is your name? 
 What is your main interest in writing? Are you a novelist, short story writer, journalist? 
 What is your experience with horror in general? Do you watch movies? Read books? Visit websites? What are some of your favorites? 
 What is your knowledge of HorrorAddicts.net 
 Why would you like to join the HorrorAddicts.net Staff? 
 
 **************************************** 
 Behind the Scenes Help 
 HorrorAddicts.net is looking for people to complete clerical tasks and help us make our horror addicts happy. 
 Training:  This is an entry-level position with possibility of advancement if the applicant wishes. You will help with the basic running of the site under the supervision of David Watson. 
 Basic skills:  A general knowledge of Facebook and email is expected. 
Added plus if you know how to use Google Calendar, Google Drive, and WordPress, but these can all be taught during training. 
 POST ENDS:  Always open. 
 To Apply  : Send answers to the following questions to[ horroraddicts@gmail.com](mailto:%20 horroraddicts@gmail.com) with the subject line: Behind the Scenes 
 
 
 What is your name? 
 
 
 Do you have a career or skills that would help in your job at HorrorAddicts.net? 
 
 
 What is your experience with horror in general? Do you watch movies? Read books? Visit websites? What are some of your favorites? 
 
 
 What is your knowledge of HorrorAddicts.net 
 
 
 Why would you like to join the HorrorAddicts.net Staff? 
 
 
 What are you looking to get out of working for HorrorAddicts.net? Like are you looking to work with a certain division to learn more about podcasting, publishing, or blogging? 
 
 
 Do you work for any other publishing house or company that would make working for us a conflict of interest?
