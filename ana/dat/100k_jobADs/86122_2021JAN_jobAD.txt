 Control Systems / Instrumentation Electrician 
 Job description 
 Job Highlight 
 8/6 Day Shift : 7/7 Roster Night Shift 
Role open to Diverse Applicants 
FIFO from Perth to Karratha 
 About the Role 
 This role is a great opportunity for an enthusiastic individual with a strong commitment to safety to be part of a collaborative team. The successful candidate will have Dual trade qualifications; in Instrumentation and as an Electrical Tradesperson. 
 Based at Rio Tinto's Karratha Rail Site and Reporting to the Crew Leader, you will be: 
 Diagnosing faults and proactively managing breakdowns of equipment or process failures; Working with electronic control systems including microprocessors, sensors, and final control elements used in automated processes; 
 Perform complex troubleshooting, fault rectification duties, repairs, modifications and preventative maintenance 
 Developing and following planned maintenance activities, Completing regular visual inspections and test on plant & equipment. 
 Skills and Experience 
 - Instrumentation Certification is a must 
- High Level of Fixed Plant & Automation experience 
- PLC Control systems Experience is a must 
 - Experience in Gas, Hydrocarbon, Coal Processing highly regarded 
- WA Electrical Licence 
 About our Client 
 Rio Tinto operates the worlds largest integrated portfolio of iron ore assets. These assets include a world-class, integrated network of 15 iron ore mines, four port facilities, a 1,700 kilometre rail network and related infrastructure. Rio Tinto are expanding operations in the Pilbara to epic proportions while introducing next generation technologies to deliver greater efficiency, lower production costs and improved health, safety and environmental performance. 
 About WorkPac
