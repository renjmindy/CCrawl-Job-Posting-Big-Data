 Home Manager 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Home Manager to work in an exceptional care home based in the Erdington, Birmingham area. 
 As the Home Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This nursing home is a medium size home housing adult over the age of 65 those who are living with Alzheimer's and other forms of dementia 
 An ideal Home Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Knowledge of CQC standards & Regulations
 
 
 
     Strong background working within a care environment
 
 
 
     Proven Management experience with positive outcomes
 
 
 
     Ability to self-motivate, work on your own initiative as well as being a team player
 
 
 
     Good leadership qualities
 
 
 
     Able to demonstrate experience with leading an improvement plan
 
 
 
     Effective communication skills both written and orally
 
 
 
     Familiarity with use of a computer for word processing & email
 
 
 
     A passion for providing quality care and support
 
 
 
     A flexible approach towards work
 
 
 
     Understanding of how the care business works
 
 
 
     Able to demonstrate some business acumen
 
 
 
     Able to deal with a crisis effectively
 
 
 
 **The successful Home Manager will receive an excellent salary of £28,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 40 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Pension Scheme
 
 
 
     Ongoing Career Progression
 
 
 
     28 days holiday
 
 
 Excellent work environment 
 
 
 
     Free car park
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must have an NVQ Level 5 in Health & Social Care or equivalent
