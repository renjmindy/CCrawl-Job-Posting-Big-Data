 Caregiver Jobs Hiring for Israel 
 Posted on  April 14, 2010  by  caregiver 
 Employers from the Holy Land of Israel are once again in need of Filipino caregivers for  Caregiver Jobs Hiring for Israel . And to facilitate the process, a Parañaque based recruitment agency is tasked to search for qualified applicants for the said caregiver jobs hiring for Israel. The said caregiver jobs hiring for Israel have a job order that was approved by the POEA last April 6 and carries a job order balance of 14 caregiver jobs hiring applicants to be selected for caregiver work abroad. 
 If you think you are qualified and interested to apply for the said caregiver jobs hiring for Israel, submit your application and necessary credentials to the office of the recruiting agency. For inquiries regarding the said caregiver jobs hiring for Israel, you can dial their landline numbers indicated below. 
 ** 
Star World Int 'l Manpower & Placement Agency, Inc.** 
231 Quirino Avenue, Tambo 
Parañaque City 
Tel. Nos: 8524575 / 8546100 / 8548042
