 Digital Marketing Manager 
 
 Location 
 
 Liverpool, Merseyside 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Marketing and Digital 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £35000.00 - £40000.00 per annum 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Ben Williams 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 ben.williams@gleeson-rg.com

 Published: 
 
 6 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-08-22 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Looking for something dynamic, forward-thinking? Passionate about Digital Marketing? Possess a creative flair? You're in the right place! 
 We are looking for someone with a strong commercial focus and a huge passion for marketing who will bring bags of initiative. 
 Situated in the centre of Liverpool, our client is a thriving, well-established and award-winning business, who have recently launched a new offering within the group. 
 Key duties and responsibilities: 
 
 Creating, planning and launching multi-channel digital marketing campaigns 
 Managing paid media campaigns across Facebook, YouTube and Instagram 
 Project managing the build of a new company website 
 CRO and UX across the company website 
 Management of a specialist SEO and PPC agency 
 Creating and implementing a social media strategy for the business 
 
 Minimum requirements: 
 
 Substantial experience within digital marketing within a B2C environment 
 Experience of project managing the build of a new website 
 Hands on experience of all digital channels, including email marketing, SEO, PPC, social media etc 
 Degree in marketing or equivalent (CIM, IDM). 
 The ability to keep calm under pressure and work to deadlines. 
 Strong people management skills, verbal and written communication and to be able to work effectively as part of a team. 
 Be creative and have an innovative approach. 
 
 To apply for the Digital Marketing Manager position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
