 Boilermaker 
 Job description 
 
 
 3 MONTH CONTRACT 
 
 
 WELSHPOOL LOCATION 
 
 
 IMMEDIATE START 
 
 
 JOB TITLE 
 About the Role 
Reporting to the Production Supervisor, the purpose of this position is to complete the assembly of new Komatsu OEM unit model lines and when required, site-based commission of units to ensure that they comply with our optimum quality standard on a large range of machines including utility, construction and mining equipment. 
 Skills & Experience Required:- 
 
 
 Relevant Boilermaker Trade Certificate with a minimum of one (1) year of trade experience 
 
 
 Current Forklift License (LF), Elevated Work Platform (WP) - preferred or the ability to obtain 
 
 
 MR Driver's licence - essential 
 
 
 Prior experience and exposure to working on all types of heavy earthmoving, construction and mining size equipment - essential 
 
 
 Broad knowledge of Komatsu Equipment 
 
 
 Previous experience in the mining industry or related heavy industry - essential 
 
 
 Demonstrated ability to interpret schematics, mechanical and engineering drawings 
 
 
 Demonstrated ability to self-manage and take responsibility 
 
 
 Demonstrated problem solving ability 
 
 
 Hold/Ability to obtain a current First Aid certificate desirable 
 
 
 Ability to demonstrate a basic level of computer literacy 
 
 
 Ability to effectively communicate and work with internal departments seamlessly on a daily basis whilst demonstrating an understanding of our customers requirements. 
 
 
 Ability to demonstrate excellent troubleshooting skills. 
 
 
 Ability to complete a full Pre-Employment Medical Check as well as random onsite drug testing. 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australias leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the countrys leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities 
 At WorkPac, we recognise we are strengthened by diversity. We are committed to providing a work environment in which everyone is included, treated fairly and with respect. We are an Equal Opportunity employer and we encourage applications from women and Indigenous people.
