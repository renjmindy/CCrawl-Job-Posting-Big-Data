 Administrative Assistant 
 Apply for the position, Administrative Assistant, with Northwest Kansas Area Agency on Aging today or contact HR for more information. Posted Jan 13, 2021. 
  785-628-8204  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 The Northwest Kansas Area Agency on Aging is accepting resumes for a part-time Administrative Assistant.  We are looking for a responsible individual to perform a variety of administrative and clerical tasks. Duties of the Administrative Assistant include providing support to our managers and employees, assisting in daily office needs and managing our company’s general administrative activities. Administrative Assistant responsibilities include making travel and meeting arrangements, preparing reports and maintaining appropriate filing systems, billing and collecting client fees, and daily data entry. The ideal candidate should have excellent oral and written communication skills, exhibit precise attention to detail, and be able to work in an organized fashion. If you have previous experience as an administrative assistant and familiarity within our industry, we’d like to meet you. Ultimately, a successful Administrative Assistant should ensure the efficient and smooth day-to-day operation of our office. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Answer and direct phone calls 
 Organize and schedule appointments 
 Assist with coordination of meetings and take detailed minutes 
 Write and distribute email, correspondence memos, letters, faxes and forms 
 Assist in the preparation of regularly scheduled reports 
 Develop and maintain a filing system 
 Order and stock office supplies 
 Ensure office supplies and reception areas are in a neat and organized manner 
 Complete intakes for service requests and make referrals 
 Record agency mail in a database 
 Assist with general accounting duties 
 Maintain contact lists 
 Submit and reconcile reports 
 Greet and provide general support to visitors 
 Other projects and duties as assigned 
 
 Qualifications and Skills 
 
 Accounting background - Bachelor's degree is preferred 
 One year of experience in the geriatric/social service field is preferred 
 Ability to pass a background check 
 Experience as an administrative assistant 
 Knowledge of office management systems and procedures 
 Working knowledge of office equipment, like printers, mail, and fax machines 
 Proficiency in MS Office (MS Excel and MS Word in particular) 
 Excellent time management skills and the ability to prioritize work 
 Attention to detail and problem solving skills 
 Excellent written and verbal communication skills 
 Strong organizational skills with the ability to multi-task 
 Skills to work in a cooperative and coordinating way with other agency staff 
 Ability to communicate and interact with older people and individuals with disabilities 
 Ability to travel as required 
 
 Benefits 
 
 Work schedule is Monday – Friday, 6-7 hours/day.  Successful applicant will receive holiday, vacation and sick time, and will be eligible for KPERS retirement benefits.
