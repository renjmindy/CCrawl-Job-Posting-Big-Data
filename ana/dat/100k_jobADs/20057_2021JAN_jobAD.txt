 Spanish Credit Controller 
 
 Location 
 
 Wolverhampton, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance

 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £22000 - £23000 per annum + Excellent Benefits

 melanie.poulter@gleeson-rg.com

 Published:

 Gleeson Recruitment Group are looking to appoint an experienced Credit Controller; with fluent Spanish language skills to start as soon as possible on a permanent contract for our client based in Wolverhampton. 
 This role will offer flexibility of working hours for the right candidate 
 Duties will involve: 
 
 Liaise with clients ensuring a professional relationship 
 Chase outstanding debts via telephone and email correspondence 
 Check customer's credit ratings with the bank as well as with nominated credit companies 
 Collate and prepare reports 
 Maintain effective records to ensure dealings with customers are properly recorded 
 Liaise with internal departments to follow up on any outstanding issues 
 Source and provide customer information to resolve payment queries 
 
 Immediate start for the right candidate; salary up to £25,000 for the right candidate. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
