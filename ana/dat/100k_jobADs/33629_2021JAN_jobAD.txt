 I have a fantastic opportunity for a Marketing Manager to join a fantastic B2C focused business in Birmingham, who are well-known for their excellent working culture and who are going through extreme growth at the moment. This role is ideally suited to someone with a strong multi-site retail focused background. 
 This role offers significant flexibility - you are only required to be in the Birmingham office once a week! 
 This Marketing Manager role will involve: 
 
 The management of a variety of Marketing specialists and channels, including brand, events, creative, and with a real emphasis on digital marketing 
 The management of a number of digital, marketing and creative agencies 
 Ownership and creation of a clear content strategy for the business 
 Development of a multi-channel digital strategy, including the development on multiple websites, SEO and PPC 
 Brand management - being a brand guardian for the organisation, ensuring all content is within brand guidelines 
 
 This Marketing Manager position requires a candidate with the following skills: 
 
 Proven commercial experience of working within a multi-site retail organisation 
 Proven experience of developing and implementing digital marketing strategies 
 Significant experience of managing and building a team of marketing, creative and digital specialists 
 Excellent and professional communication skills, both written and verbal 
 Outstanding stakeholder management skills 
 The ability to manage a number of projects at any one time 
 
 To apply for the Marketing Manager position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
