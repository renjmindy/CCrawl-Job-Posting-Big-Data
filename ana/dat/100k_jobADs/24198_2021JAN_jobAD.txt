 Registered Mental Health Nurse 
 Band 5 Registered Mental Health Nurse (RMN) 
 Are you a Mental Health Nurse RMN looking for a change? Or are you looking for Full time hours in Weston Super Mare? As long as you're passionate about Older Persons Mental Health and delivering high quality care then we want to hear from you. 
 I have a role available at an NHS Mental Health Facility based within Weston Super Mare 
 To be successful in this role, you should be a fully qualified Mental Health Nurse (RMN), very confident and have experience in this field. 
 It's essential that you are supportive, both to patients and colleagues, and can remain calm under pressure, using strong communication skills at all times. 
 The Limited Company rates of pay are up to £30.00 per hour (Uplifts for Nights and Weekends) This would be paid through an Umbrella Company 
 Requirements: 
- 6 Months Post graduate experience in Mental Health Nursing position 
- Enhanced DBS Certificate 
- NMC Registration 
- Mandatory Training Certificates 
- PMVA / MAPA Training (Preferred but not essential, training can be provided) 
- Vaccination History (Hepatitis B, MMR, TB and Varicella / Chicken Pox)

- £150 Sign up bonus for qualified workers 
- Dedicated and experienced one-to-one consultant support 
- FREE enhanced DBS check 
- FREE occupational health checks and immunisations 
- £250 Training Allowance 
- £250 Referral Bonus 
- Daily Payroll Runs 
- Nationwide provider of mental health staff to over 40 different NHS trusts
