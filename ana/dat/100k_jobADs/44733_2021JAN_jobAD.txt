 Caregiver Job Canada 
 Posted on  September 18, 2012  by  caregiver 
 Luzern International Manpower Services Corp. recently had their Job Order approved allowing them to recruit caregivers for placement in  Caregiver Job Canada . The said caregiver job Canada has vacancies for 5 caregivers which was verified and approved for recruitment by the POEA last September 13, 2012. 
 For complete information regarding the said caregiver job Canada, please contact Luzern International Manpower Services Corp. by dialing their landline numbers we have indicated below.
