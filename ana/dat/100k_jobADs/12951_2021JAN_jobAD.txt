 Lab Technician 
 Job description 
 Role Highlights 
 
 
 Part-time hours, 4 hour days, 3-5 days per week 
 
 
 No experience required 
 
 
 6 week assignment 
 
 
 About the Role 
WorkPac are currently looking for a Lab Technician for a role at Woodlawn Water Treatment Plant based at Tarago NSW. The successful candidate will be required to have good numeracy and literacy skills as they will need record data. 
 Mon to Fri 4 hour days although can be spaced over 3 days if most practical for the candidate. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Testing water quality (ammonia, nitrates etc) 
 
 
 Using an oven, drying out solids 
 
 
 Recording data such as weights, estimations and results etc 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Background within a sampling role would be advantageous although not essential 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Ability to pass a Pre Employment D&A; 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities.
