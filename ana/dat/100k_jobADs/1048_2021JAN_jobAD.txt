 Company Description 
 _Here at Idlewild Spirits Distillery, we pride ourselves on creating high quality and intriguing new-age spirits & killer cocktails that explore all facets. _ Nestled at 8,787 feet in the Rocky Mountains, our distillery pub offers a casual-fine dining experience in the town center of Winter Park, Colorado. Made with Colorado grains and fresh snowmelt of the Rocky Mountains, we create a variety of craft spirits: whiskey, bourbon, gin, rum, vodka & small batch liqueurs. Our kitchen is from-scratch so we are always looking for talent who enjoys creative dishes and collaboration.
