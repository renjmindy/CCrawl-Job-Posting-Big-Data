 Full-Time Project Manager 
 Joint Authority of Kainuu, HR Services -  Kainuu  , Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 Joint Authority of Kainuu 01/01/2013 searches the opening of Kainuu union project manager in the period 01.01.2013 - 31.12.2014. The project manager is responsible for the EU's Karelia ENPI-funded project. 
 The project will be built polttoaineteholta 1MW bio-energy power plant in Kostomuksha. The construction of the plant's electricity output of about 300kW. Kostomuksha future bio-energy plant is scheduled for completion by end-2014. The project includes two bio-energy forum organized in Kajaani and Kostomuksha, and the operation of a plant related to the energy industry training. The project involves Finnish and Russian companies. 
 Project is managed by the beginning of 2013 will begin operations Kainuu Regional Council and the partner is Kostomuksha administration. The project is implemented in close cooperation with the participating firms and Kostomuksha administration. We require an appropriate university degree, experience in project activities, as well as  proficiency in English . Russian language skills would be an advantage. We value teamwork, skill and experience in project management, as well as an interest in cross-border cooperation.
