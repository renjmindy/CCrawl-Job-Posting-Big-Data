 Career Advice and Coaching 
 The question of personal career planning can frequently arise at different times during the course of your working life.  And professional support can help you clarify  important questions such as: What do I want to achieve; what is important to me; which brands or products do I really find most exciting; what sort of things do I really enjoy; how can I secure that particular job role? And likewise help you to ponder questions like: Am I still with the right employer; can I reach  my career goals here  ; or can I secure the position I want here? 
 Career consultancy includes professional  advice and coaching, which focuses on your career  and the realisation of concrete measures which help  you achieve professional success . We consider your ambitions and potential, help you review your past professional career, and evaluate the  viable prospects available in the current job marke  t. 
 CAREER ADVICE involves developing a strategy for your own professional success. This intensive coaching essentially includes: 
 
 a personal career plan 
 the consideration of different life/ professional phases 
 reviewing your past professional career 
 ambitions and potential 
 an evaluation of viable opportunities in the current job market 
 the definition of your strengths to facilitate reorientation 
 
 The goal is to develop and execute a very personal and positive career strategy. It can lead to a Newplacement consultation.
