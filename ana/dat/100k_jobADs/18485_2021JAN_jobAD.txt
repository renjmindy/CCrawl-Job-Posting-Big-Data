 Work at home Job Openings for 2/9/2018 
 Remote Services Technician 
 Support is looking for remote workers for an entry level role to answer inbound calls and provide basic troubleshooting for high speed internet and wireless gateways. Requires excellent communication skills, type 30 to 40 wpm, have 1 year related experience and can follow instructions. Pays $9.50 per hour during training. 
 Appointment Setters 
 Brighten Communications is looking for callers with previous cold calling or telemarketing experience to make calls representing professional organizations. You will need a good working phone with unlimited long distance service. Pays $12 per hour to start working part time. Weekly pay. 
 Support Tech 
 TeleNetwork is looking for agents who are familiar with printer, mobile technology troubleshooting, software, hardware support, etc. to work from home. You must be able to research problems and find solutions quickly with excellent reading and verbal skills. Pays $10 per hour with paid training and benefits. Regular attendance and punctuality necessary. Full time. 
 Evaluators 
 Berlitz is seeking people to deliver and report results for language tests, score tests, enter test results and other related duties. You will need strong communication skills, knowledge of basic software and have a degree in a relevant field of study. 
 Remote Stylists 
 Dia & Co is seeking remote part-time stylists to put together curated fashion boxes for their clients. You will also offer styling tips. You must be obsessed with fashion, have 2 years in retail or customer service, and can work 10 to 25 hours per week. Must live in Dallas Texas or Indianapolis Indiana. 
 
 Transcription 
 Transcription Express is seeking skilled reliable transcriptionists who can work Monday through Friday transcribing 4 to 5 hours a day. You must type 60 wpm with 90% accuracy, be proficient in spelling and live in the US. Pays for each page produced. 
 Grub Hub Customer Service 
 NexRep is in need of agents to help customers and restaurants using their GrubHub app get assistance. You must be able to work on the weekends and evenings, have data entry skills and can work 20 hours a week. Hourly pay. 
 Sales & Service Rep 
 Convergys has openings for reps in 35 states to answer calls for their clients regarding billing inquiries, troubleshooting, cancelling services, etc. You must have a PC that is less than 6 years old, a land line phone and be internet savvy. 
 Customer Service Rep 
 Progressive Insurance is hiring people to work from home answering questions about billing, explaining policy coverage, helping customers with insurance needs, processing renewals, etc. You will need 1 year work or educational experience, computer skills and can multitask. Company will provide you with office equipment and training. Pays $15 to $19 per hour! Select states. 
 Virtual Assistant 
 Time Etc. is looking for virtual assistants who have previous experience working from home and can work evenings and weekends to assist clients with personal tasks like general administration, travel arrangements, making phone calls, etc. Pays $11 per hour. 
 Drivers 
 
 Door Dash is looking for people to deliver food and other items to customers. No car needed. You can make good money working your own schedule. Weekly pay. 
 Fronto 
 Get paid cash or gift cards when you unlock your phone or tablet. 
 Feature Points 
 Get free Bitcoins, PayPal cash, gift cards and more when you download free apps. You must keep them installed for 2 minutes to get credit. Available on the App store or Google Play. 
 PrizeRebel 
 Get free prizes, gift cards and cash to complete daily tasks, watch videos, give your opinion and more. Must be 13 to join. Open worldwide.
