 Support Worker 
 Support Worker (SW) Wingrave 
 MacIntyre School is a mixed Independent school for children with extreme special needs and severe learning difficulties. 
 It is situated in the village of Wingrave near Aylesbury, Buckinghamshire. 
 The service users are aged 10 -19 years of age and severe and profound learning, ASD and challenging behaviour. 
 In addition, some children may suffer with epilepsy and minor physical impairments. 
 As a support worker you will provide high quality and compassionate care to the service users and be responsible for their general well-being. 
 The Limited Company rates of pay are up to £13.00 per hour (Uplifts for Nights and Weekends) Personal Limited Companies can be used for this role 
 Requirements: 
- 6 - 12 Months experience within Learning Disabilities or similar 
- NVQ Level 3 or be working towards it 
- Enhanced DBS Certificate (Update Service Preferred) 
- Online Mandatory Training 
- Vaccination History (Hepatitis B) 
 The Benefits of Service Care Solutions include: 
 - £150 sign-up bonus for qualified workers 
- Dedicated and experienced one-to-one consultant support 
- Competitive rates of pay for temporary and fixed contract work 
- FREE enhanced DBS check 
- FREE occupational health checks and immunisations 
- Approved supplier to over 400 organisations nationwide 
- Loyalty reward scheme 
 If this role is something that you are interested in applying for please forward your CV to Jake.Nuttall@servicecare.org.uk
