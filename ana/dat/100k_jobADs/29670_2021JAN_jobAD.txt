 Darra Based Role 
 
 
 About The Role 
 Our client is a world class designer and manufacturer of building products with state of art equipment. Working with WorkPac, our Client is seeking a Manufacturing Manager for their Cabinetry and Kitchen Design sector based in Darra. You will be skilled in rolling out Production with experienced in cut & edge cabinet manufacturing, particularly in assessing and advising on machinery requirements and factory layouts. 
 This is a rare opportunity to step into a newly created role where you will have the opportunity to work with Senior Leadership to set up and create an efficient production environment and culture. The ideal Candidate will have a Carpentry, Shop Fitting or Cabinet Making Trade background having worked from all aspects of the trade in to a Managerial role. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Manage daily operations 
 
 
 Assure products are manufactured to specifications, quality and safety standards 
 
 
 Develop and maintain production processes 
 
 
 Communicate with maintenance personnel to assure best practice 
 
 
 Achieve strong coordination within production area 
 
 
 Work closely with General Manager to achieve company goals 
 
 
 Manage Human resource activities of production employees 
 
 
 Skills and Experience: 
 
 
 Trade Qualification as a Cabinet Maker or Carpenter 
 
 
 Prior demonstrated success in a Production Management position especially related to cut & edge cabinet manufacturing, particularly in assessing and advising on machinery requirements and factory layouts 
 
 
 Exceptional communication and leadership skills 
 
 
 Strong focus of improvement and development 
 
 
 Quality control experience 
 
 
 Strong leadership skills 
 
 
 Sound computer skills 
 
 
 Benefits of role:
