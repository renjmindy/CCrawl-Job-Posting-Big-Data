 Are you a talented engineer and fancy using software to solve complex engineering problems? Are you an engineer who is passionate about design but looking for something more from your career? If this is you we have a unique opportunity that will give the opportunity to diversify into new areas and do something a bit different with your skills. 
 Our client is a world leading supplier of state-of-the-art engineering products and solutions to the global construction industry. They provide innovative software solutions for the design and manufacture of products for building projects around the world. 
 This market leading business is looking for a talented Engineering Product Owner who is able to interpret engineering documents and designs whilst engaging with customers and engineers across Europe and Africa. You will interpret engineering requirements and communicate a clear vision to members of the team. There is an expectation that the successful candidate will be passionate about ensuring our client can provide the best possible software tools to engineers across the region. 
 In this role the Engineering Product Owner will have the opportunity to work across a diverse and interesting multi-national region with most meetings via video conferencing and phone calls. You will play an integral part in the development of our clients software and engineering solutions to the market and be customer facing. You will be able to take your experience as an engineer into a new role and diversify your experience. 
 To be successful in your application as Engineering Product Owner you will be a clear communicator, be collaborative and flexible. You will be a civil, structural or software engineer and be passionate about using software to solve problems. In return you will join a progressive, supportive and flexible business who are extremely stable even in such uncertain economic times. There is a great package and career on offer for the right candidate along with the opportunity to travel abroad from time-to-time, so, get in touch if you would like to know more. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
