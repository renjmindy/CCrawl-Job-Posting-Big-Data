 Financial Analyst (FP&A)-MON17457 
 Description 
 BOMBARDIER 
 At Bombardier, our employees work together to evolve mobility worldwide - one good idea at a time. If you have a good idea, we’ll provide the environment where it will thrive and grow into a great product or customer experience. Your ideas are our fuel. 
 In your role, you will: 
 
 Contribute to the overall process for the development of the five-year strategic plan, the annual budget and periodic forecast updates; 
 Support the consolidation, analysis and assessment of the P&L and Inventories by monitoring the actual financial performance against the annual budget, five-year strategic plan and periodic forecasts; 
 Perform scenario analysis of business initiatives relative to the annual budget and five-year plan; 
 Ensure governance and monitoring of the financial performance action plan; 
 Support change management by driving key business initiatives in the business unit with the goal of continuous improvement. 
 Prepare financial reporting presentations (Five-year plan, Annual budget, Quarterly Operations Review); 
 
 Qualifications 
 As our ideal candidate, 
 
 You hold a bachelor’s degree or the equivalent; 
 You hold an Accounting Designation (CPA); 
 You have 6-10 years of experience in Financial planning & analysis or relevant experience; 
 You have good knowledge of accounting norms; 
 You possess planning and organizational skills necessary to coordinate workload around multiple assignments; 
 You have strong analytical and judgment skills necessary to evaluate alternatives and formulate recommendations in support of management decision-making; 
 You have a very good knowledge and high technical skills of Microsoft Excel; 
 You are bilingual (English and French) both written and oral 
 
 Bombardier is an equal opportunity employer and encourages women, Aboriginal people, persons with disabilities and members of visible minorities to apply.
