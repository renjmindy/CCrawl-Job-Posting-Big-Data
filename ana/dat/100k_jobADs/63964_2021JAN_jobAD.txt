 Craft CMS Developer (Essex, UK or UK remote) REMOTEPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 Enovate is seeking to hire an enthusiastic and ambitious PHP Web Developer ideally with previous agency experience to work on an a range of existing and new website and web application projects utilising Craft CMS and Laravel. 
 We don't want to limit applications so applicants can either be based in our office in Chelmsford, Essex or work remotely from anywhere in the UK or a mixture of both. We're also open to a part-time arrangement and already have other staff who only work 4 days per week. 
 This is a permanent role with an initial 3 month probationary period. 
 Responsibilities 
 The successful applicant will be comfortable with developing websites and web applications from scratch. The role will include developing, extending and maintaining custom plugins for Craft CMS and contributing to Laravel projects so experience with Laravel and Craft/Yii would be very beneficial. Requirements gathering, scoping and time estimates are also essential aspects of the role. Along with an ability to communicate your technical vision to both non-technical team members and clients. 
 You have: 
 
 Excellent PHP programming skills including OOP, HTTP principles and PHP optimisation 
 Strong JavaScript skills and experience with frameworks such as Vue.js and jQuery 
 Excellent knowledge of MySQL database design and development 
 Knowledge of Git version control 
 Familiarity with Linux shell 
 Acute attention to detail and will stop at nothing to achieve perfection 
 Ability to work autonomously and manage your workload and deadlines effectively 
 A relentless drive for efficiency, performance and speed throughout all your work 
 A readiness to embrace and use new approaches and to keep up-to-date with industry advances 
 Superb written and verbal communication skills 
 
 Not essential but it would be good if you have: 
 
 Experience with Craft CMS 
 Experience with Laravel 
 A relevant university degree 
 Experience with Nginx 
 Experience with cloud infrastructure such as AWS 
 Project management experience 
 Knowledge of SEO 
 
 We have: 
 
 A great salary, commensurate with experience 
 24 days holiday 
 A relaxed working atmosphere 
 Interesting and varied projects to get your teeth into 
 Monthly training time to tackle a task/project/goal of your choosing 
 Company pension 
 Modern, air conditioned office 
 Christmas and summer parties 
 Paid for outings to industry events/conferences
