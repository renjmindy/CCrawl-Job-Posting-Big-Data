 Job Seeker Services 
 
 If you are out of work and looking for a job or are employed and want a better job, help is available at WorkSource. 
 
 Whether you need a resume update or training to give you the skills needed for today’s jobs, we have free programs to help you get a job. We may be able to pay for training so you can advance in your career. And we can connect you to other resources for some extra support as you look for work or go through training or school. 
 
 At WorkSource, you can 
 
 
 Search for a job or post your resume at  www.WorkSourceWA.com 
 
 
 Get one-on-one assistance 
 
 
 Attend workshops on resumes, interviewing, social media networking and more 
 
 
 Learn about free training to help you advance in your career or learn a new industry 
 
 
 Assess your interests and abilities to target jobs you’d be good at and enjoy doing 
 
 
 Identify industries that are growing and find out where the jobs are now and will be in the future 
 
 
 Use computers, internet, fax and copiers (will be available when it is safe to resume in-person services) 
 
 
 Obtain referrals to programs for assistance with housing, transportation costs, childcare, food, clothing and more 
 
 
 Explore paid internships and on-the-job training 
 
 
 
 Construction, healthcare, manufacturing, technology & transportation are growing industries!

 Attention Kelso and Longview Residents 
 Are you working and still struggling to make ends meet? Are you looking for work? If you live in South Kelso or The Highlands in Longview, you may qualify for free job training and employment services that will help you get a good-paying job to support your family. 
 We can: 
 
 Help you find your next career 
 Connect you to training + work experience 
 Provide resume + interview assistance 
 And more! 
 
 Services include childcare, transportation, work gear and other resources so you can increase your income without losing the benefits that keep your family healthy. 
 Contact us at 360.577.2250 to learn more.
