 OUR DIVERSITY IS OUR STRENGTH 
 At Workshop/APD, a diverse collective of architects, interior designers and product designers leverages its vast and highly varied experiences to solve complex design challenges, resulting in spaces that are exceptionally beautiful and accessible. In a hands-on, highly collaborative environment, we work seamlessly to realize complex projects with passion and skill, working across luxury residential, high-end development, and hospitality typologies. Committed to constantly honing and elevating our craft, we provide the tools, education, and mentorship needed to grow as individuals and a team, and understand that architecture and design are a responsibility, with lasting effects on our clients and the greater community. We believe in an inclusive culture of equity, respect and openness, and above all, joy in the beauty we bring to the world.
