 __   What You Can Expect to Learn from this Course 
 Four significant pillars influence your chances of getting hired in the sports world: 
 
 Gaining the RIGHT Experience 
 Building and Maintaining Your Network 
 Mastering Your Resume, Cover Letter and Personal Brand 
 Interviewing Skills for Jobs and Internships 
 
 In this course, we will explore Building and Maintaining Your Network, going deep into the strategies and techniques that will make a difference for you in your sports career. 
 There will be a combination of written content, videos, downloadable worksheets and podcast episodes to help you learn the subject matter. You will have assignments, to keep you on track, and quizzes after most sections to further enforce what you have learned.
