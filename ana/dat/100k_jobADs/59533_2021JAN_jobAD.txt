 Exciting opportunity to join a pe backed tech business in the financial services sector. The role is responsible for the financial operations of the business including leadership of the team and a number of projects. This is a critical phase for this organisation and there are a number of deliverables including systems upgrade, process improvement and cashflow forecasting. 
 The successful candidate will be a qualified accountant with experience of working within an sme environment. We also require an individual with systems experience, specifically upgrade. Finally, this role would suit an energetic, passionate leader who can reach out to finance and non-finance individuals and deliver results quickly.
