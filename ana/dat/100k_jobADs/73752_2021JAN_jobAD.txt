 My client, a high-growth healthcare business, is looking to recruit a Head of Sales. This is a key role in the leadership structure of my client's organisation. 
 The role will encompass: 
 
 Sales strategy development 
 Team management including sales development 
 Commercial analysis and reporting 
 Product development support 
 
 In order to be considered for this role, you must have worked within a sales role in the healthcare/medical/pharma sector - i.e. sold to healthcare buyers. 
 You will have a demonstrable track record of selling into the health sector. In addition, you will have managed a small and agile team. You must be have a track record of building and delivering a sales strategy. 
 Please note, this role is a remote position - permanently. You will manage a remote team. 
 Highly competitive package.
