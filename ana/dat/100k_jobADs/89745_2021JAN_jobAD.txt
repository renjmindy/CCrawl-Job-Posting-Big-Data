 Journeyman Electrician 
 Superior Elevator ULC 
 __Thunder Bay, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Superior Elevator ULC is a joint venture between Cargill Limited and Parrish and Heimbecker (P&H). We are looking for a skilled Electrician who has experience with industrial equipment maintenance. We operate a busy, clean grain handling facility and consider safety our top priority, so we are looking for a professional whose values reflect our own. 
 Responsibilities and Duties: 
 
 Perform predictive maintenance and corrective maintenance on facility assets 
 Complete scheduled preventative maintenance inspections on critical equipment 
 Installation of new equipment 
 Participate in meetings, training activities, and the plant safety program 
 Read and interpret blueprints, schematics and detailed repair instructions related to plant equipment and machinery 
 
 Qualifications and Skills: 
 
 Possession of Inter-provincial Journeyman Electrician Certificate 
 Must be willing to work overtime and shift work, including weekends 
 Must be willing to work from heights and in confined spaces 
 Basic knowledge of industrial safety with an absolute commitment to safety 
 Strong problem solving/troubleshooting skills, good written and verbal communication skills 
 Strong work ethic and organizational skills 
 Knowledge of soft starts, variable frequency drives and experience with PLC’s would be an asset 
 
 Benefits: 
 
 Competitive compensation 
 Excellent pension and benefit package 
 
 Job Type: Full-time 
 Experience: 
 
 Journeyman Electrician: 1 year (Preferred) 
 
 Education: 
 
 AEC / DEP or Skilled Trade Certificate (Preferred)
