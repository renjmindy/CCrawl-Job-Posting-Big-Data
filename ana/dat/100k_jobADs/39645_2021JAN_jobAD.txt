 Dye House Machine Operator 
 Categories 
 
 __ Solve Problems 
 
 Career Description 
 Load correct number of carriers with correct yarn into dye machine, using overhead hoist. Verify formula against batch ticket. Weigh chemicals into specific buckets and correctly label if not already prepared. Retrieve appropriate dye from the store area. Verify dye by comparing to lot number. Manually mix chemicals and dye according to specifications. Follow all safety rules and wear proper Personal Protective Equipment (PPE.) Add dye to machine according to procedure and prompt from machine. Monitor machine for alarms or noticeable mechanical issues such as reversal valve or temperature errors. Take sample of dye lot and leave machine on sample function until supervisor approves. Complete process once approved by supervisor. Unload carriers from dye machine using overhead hoist and place paperwork on yarn. Ensure correct procedures are followed and documentation is accurate. Maintain a safe, clean, and organized work area.
