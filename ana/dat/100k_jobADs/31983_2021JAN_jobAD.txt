 **Financial Controller - Hampshire - FTSE 250 - £85/£90,000 + Car & Package ** 
 My FTSE 250 client is looking for an outstanding Financial Controller to join their team. The role is available due to a new contract being won and so they have a pressing requirement. The successful candidate is likely to come from an outsourcing or contract led business that has been through a significant period of change. This role will suit a highly motivated candidate who is looking to progress to become a Finance Director within the next couple of years and can travel to London as required. The main purpose of this role is responsibility for the financial control on a large, high profile contract. 
 Key responsibilities - Financial Controller: 
 
 Management of the contract finance team 
 Management and production of monthly Management Accounts and relevant variance analysis in line with the reporting deadline 
 Monthly reporting 
 Monthly forecasting 
 Provide a project accounting function with key emphasis on cost management, benefits tracking and value analysis 
 Management and maintenance of programme P&L and balance sheet 
 Business planning 
 Ensure that invoices are raised in a timely manner to meet contractual obligations 
 Ensure all WIP is recorded, calculated correctly and managed so as to minimise risk 
 Maintaining and developing a strong control environment 
 Risk analysis on the P&L 
 To identify and manage financial risk and issues and to provide solutions 
 Ensure adherence to financial contractual obligations 
 Ensuring Group policies and procedures are followed 
 
 Required - Financial Controller 
 
 Qualified (ACA/ACCA/CIMA) 
 Excellent academics 
 A background in a large contract led business 
 Excellent oral and written communication 
 Experience of business transformation or a fast-paced change environment 
 Ability to manage own workload and work autonomously 
 The ability to clearly delver financial information to different stakeholders 
 
 My client delivers outstanding development opportunities for highly motivated, commercially aware candidates who are looking to drive their careers forward. They are a market leader and a strong, recognisable business brand with an enviable market position. 
 As this role is private and confidential no additional information will be released until a cv has been received and positively vetted.
