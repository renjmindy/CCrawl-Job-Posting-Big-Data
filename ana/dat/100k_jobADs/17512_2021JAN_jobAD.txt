 Financial Planning Analyst / BI Analyst / Commercial Accountant 
 Twyford 
 Permanent 
 £54,000 a year + bonus 
 The Opportunity 
 Due to an internal restructure, I am looking for outstanding talent with a track record of achievement and development. 
 Ideally you will have secured in the last (1-3 years) your full accounting qualification and preferably have big Top 4 trained with either commercial secondment experience or a from an international business. 
 This role is pivotal in providing support to the international teams as they expand across multiple territories. The requirement is for the provision of commercial information on sales trend lines, pricing, profitabilitity etc. This a mix of producing excellent accurate reporting and working across the business to drive strategic support. 
 
 
 Big data analysis and dissemination of international sales information 
 
 
 Development of strategic data analysis and reporting skills within an acquisitive international business. 
 
 
 You will be a Qualified Accountant (ACA, ACCA, CIMA) FP&A analysts with stable and progressive track record 
 
 
 About Our Client 
 Our client is an international B2B / B2C client with market leading product, service and manufacturing experience. Due to a global restructure, they are looking to develop the quality of financial data and sales analysis to drive future revenue. 
 You will be passionate about producing insightful and comprehensive analysis to produce robust reporting around data accuracy, data reporting, trend analysis . 
 You will be able to manipulate large data sets and have a methodical, pragmatic and questioning mind with the ability to communicate the results efficiently. 
 Excellent planning and time management skills with clear and concise communication skills. 
 Job Description 
 As the FP&A your core responsibilities will be to analyse the sales performance of specific regions and utilise your experience to improve the quality of the analysis that is being presented. This FP&A Analyst will focus on improving the data accuracy and reporting within the BI system. 
 Successful Applicant 
 As a qualified or qualified by experience accountant, you will have experience of demonstrating practical skills from a larger business background. 
 Applications are also sought from Practice trained accountants with secondment experience from a commercial environment. You will ideally be someone that is looking to progress to the next stage within your career. You will be looking to join a forward thinking organisation that is on an upward trajectory both locally and internationally. 
 This may suit a qualified (ACA, ACCA or CIMA) - newly qualified or qualified by experience candidate. 
 You will have a detailed understanding of how an FP&A department works and be able to adapt quickly to different processes. 
 What's on Offer 
 On offer to the successful candidate, is an excellent business culture within the FP&A team, a mentor that will help progress your career to the next stage. You will be given an excellent benefits package along with a competitive basic salary. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
