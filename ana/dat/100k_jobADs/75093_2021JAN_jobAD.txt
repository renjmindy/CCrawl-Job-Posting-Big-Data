 About FLOWER CO. 
 We sell weed on the internet. We're a vertically integrated legal cannabis company delivering next-day in Los Angeles, Long Beach, San Francisco, East Bay, Marin, Humboldt, Sacramento, and Vallejo. We're venture-backed and growing exponentially. 
 About the role 
 Skills:  Data Modeling, Data Analytics 
 FLOWER CO. is looking for an energetic data-driven analyst with significant google sheet and excel expertise and a passion for building tools that solve problems / free up team time to become our first Product Operations Analyst. You will get amazing exposure to all of the workings of a successful cannabis startup from tech and cannabis industry veterans. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 We are moving faster than software can keep up, so your job is to help pick up the slack for what the product doesn't handle yet by building and operating excel / google sheet tools so we can MVP as many things as possible 
 Analyze customer data to inform next sets of MVP tests 
 Lead special projects and initiatives 
 Provide backup support when software changes require excel data manipulation for compliance information QA 
 Create models with and on behalf of the CEO + leadership team 
 
 Requirements 
 
 2-4+ years experience at one or combo of top-tier strategy consulting, private equity (operator side), or in an operations role at a fast-growing startup 
 Ability to juggle many projects at once 
 Skills with quickly manipulating data (including Excel and Google Sheets, others tools a plus) and drawing insights (bottom line up front plz) 
 Amazing people skills and the ability to understand people's needs who don't have experience manipulating data (for tool building and data gathering) 
 Distinguished performance at a top university 
 
 About the CEO: Ted Lichtenberger is a serial cannabis founder, former McKinsey consultant, and Y Combinator alumnus. He co-founded Old Pal as well as FLOWER CO. in 2018, in addition to a supply chain company West of West, with 6 CA Cannabis licenses. More info:  https://www.linkedin.com/in/tedlichtenberger/ 
 Technology 
 We're building an end-to-end software system for sourcing, fulfillment, delivery, and e-commerce in a highly regulated industry.
