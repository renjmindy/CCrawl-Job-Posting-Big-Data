 Job description 
 Multi Skilled Operator 
 
 
 Immediate Start 
 
 
 Temporary to Permanent Opportunity 
 
 
 Growing Civil Company 
 
 
 About the Role 
 A temporary to permanent opportunity has become available for an experienced and skilled Operator with capabilities across Excavator Final Trim, Roller and Skid Steer Operations. Working on projects across Brisbane and South East Queensland, this medium Civil Construction company is building an additional crew due to additional project wins in the area. Offering an immediate start, the core business includes carpark construction, storm water, retaining walls and demolition. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Highly skilled operation of Excavator (13-14t) Final Trim, Rollers and Skid-steer 
 
 
 Ability to read and interpret plans and follow guidance from the onsite foreman 
 
 
 Ability to work independently with a proven track record of operating safely 
 
 
 Strong team player who supports our client's operations 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Previous experience in civil construction including carpark construction, storm water, retaining walls and demolition (as required) 
 
 
 Machine operating tickets for Excavator, Skid Steer and Roller 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 White/ Blue Construction Induction Card 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
