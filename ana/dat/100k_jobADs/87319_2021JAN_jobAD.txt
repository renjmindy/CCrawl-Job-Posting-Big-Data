 Another Creative Inc is looking for a Designer in Los Angeles, CA, USA. 
 About 
 
 Our motto is  be curious. And indispensable. 
 The ideal personality for this role is  collaborative, supportive, curious. 
 You need to be well-equipped to handle  at times, high churn production design work while balanced with longer lead, strategy driven projects. 
 You 'd be working on  design assets across digital (social, web, email), print, branding and website design. 
 The best way to describe our culture is  we want to enjoy each other's company and make great work together! These people are who we spend 80% of our weeks with, let make it count.
