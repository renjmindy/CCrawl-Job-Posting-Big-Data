 Who are we kidding? We need all 3 - the Jobs, the Economy and the Environment. 
 Date:  May 3, 2018 Author:  Jelinek Real Estate   0 Comments 
 Who are we kidding? We are all "people". And like people around the globe, we need food, water, homes, health and well, a life! We need all three - the Jobs, the Economy and the Environment - that the energy industry in all of its forms - provides. There's a lot of mouths to feed and an immense amount of energy needed across the country and around the world to power up civilization. Mankind needs to plan for the long game with jobs and strong economies and simultaneously protect the environment the best we can.
