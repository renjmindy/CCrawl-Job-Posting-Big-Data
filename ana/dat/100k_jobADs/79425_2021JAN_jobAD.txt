 Talented, enthusiastic back-end developer LOCALPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 Working with the technical director on back-end and CMS development for our projects. You will be involved in every stage of your projects, from planning out and building sites through testing/QA, deployment, delivery and post-launch support. You will typically be working on 2-3 projects at a time and work closely with our front-end developers integrating their template code into content-managed sites and dynamic web applications. 
 You'll also be working with the team in supporting our catalogue of existing sites and providing ongoing support to our project managers and our clients. 
 You will be enthusiastic about web development and excited about new tools, techniques and technologies but know when this distracts from Getting Things Done. You must be self-motivated, organised and focused in your work but happy collaborating with others 
 Necessary skills and requirements 
 
 A passion for web design 
 Build dynamic sites with PHP 
 Work with relational databases, ideally MySQL 
 Experience integrating front-end templates into Content Management Systems 
 Using version control systems, notably Git 
 
 Nice to haves 
 
 Working with Craft CMS or ExpressionEngine 
 Configuring and working with LAMP/Nginx server hosting and DNS 
 Experience developing custom PHP with Yii or similar framework (eg Laravel) 
 Building and working with eCommerce websites, payment gateways, etc 
 Working/integrating with third-party APIs 
 Familiarity with front-end technologies, especially JavaScript/AJAX 
 
 The role will be based in our Bath office. No full-time remote working, sorry. 
 Further details on our website at  https://ournameismud.co.uk/journal/back-end-developer-position
