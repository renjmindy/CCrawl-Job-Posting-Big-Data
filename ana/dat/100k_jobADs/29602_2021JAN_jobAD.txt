 About Iron Ox 
 Iron Ox is building robotic greenhouses to make fresh produce accessible to everyone! 
 Interested in helping feed people using advanced robotics and machine learning? Come join us. We're a team of ex-Google[x] and Willow Garage engineers developing a fully automated robotic farm to make the highest quality produce accessible to everyone. 
 About the role 
 About Us 
 Iron Ox is reimagining the modern farm, utilizing robotics and AI to grow fresh, consistent, and responsibly farmed produce for everyone. 
 Our experienced team of growers, plant scientists, engineers, and innovators are passionate about deeply understanding and developing this new wave of technology to feed an ever-growing population. Our mission at Iron Ox is dedicated to addressing some of the most significant issues in food security, now and in the future. We’re focused on sustainable, scalable food production for a changing climate and an ever-growing population. 
 Who We’re Looking for 
 Iron Ox is looking for a Senior Product Analyst with excellent product sense, analytical expertise, and interpersonal skills to own the models and analysis for our indoor hydroponic greenhouse farms and produce portfolio. You’ll enable the organization to make data-driven decisions by providing product insights and analysis to help drive business initiatives. 
 As a member of the product team, you will collaborate across the organization with plant science, engineering, sales, finance, and operations teams to conduct in-depth quantitative analyses that guide strategic decisions. You’ll spend your time building models and tools to gain and provide insight into the business. Your insights will help the product team advocate for our internal and external customers. 
 What You'll Be Doing 
 
 Business & financial modeling of greenhouse facilities, sales, and our product portfolio 
 Build and own models, research, and analysis for greenhouse throughput, operations, automation, and facility design 
 Market research analysis of product seasonality, customer trends, sales, and business opportunities 
 Work with product management to understand business and stakeholder requirements to scope and drive high impact projects 
 Contribute to our team and company data culture by sharing an understanding of our internal and external products and by being a data analysis champion 
 Help departments design and analyze experiments that provide insight and a deeper understanding of our products 
 Collaborate with stakeholders to build models that provide insight into project roadmap, product selection, and overall business strategy 
 Define and monitor key metrics that help drive business goals 
 
 What You'll Bring 
 
 Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent 
 5+ years of experience as a Product or Financial Analyst, preferably at a startup, management consulting, or in finance 
 Experience modeling unit economics, cost of goods, gross & operating margin 
 Experience building tools for internal stakeholders 
 Expertise building data visualization tools to convey actionable insights to product managers and business leaders 
 Passion for problem-solving, critical thinking, and data analysis 
 Strong communication, listening, and interpersonal skills 
 Resourceful and willing to roll up your sleeves 
 
 Nice-to-Haves 
 
 Experience with agriculture, produce, and farming 
 Experience with robotics and factory automation 
 At least 1 year of experience in tech working at a startup

 Competitive compensation and meaningful equity package
