 Can You Believe It?! it's a Full-time job 
 Get your Mother off your back with a job as a Head of Graphic Design at Underlining in San Francisco, CA, USA. We're a very fast paced & motivated team. 
 About 
 Underlining® is a cosmetics company with multiple D2C brands: TatBrow® (Eyebrows), Nailboo® (Nails), HIDE™ (Concealer & Foundation). We're a small team with immense experience building online businesses. 
 We're looking for a talented Graphic Designer who can own all design related tasks from start to finish for our cosmetics lines (branding, packaging, emails, web UI/UX/Design, creative photo editing, etc.).. Someone who is motivated, eager to learn more, can think creatively but also put a marketers hat on and design to convert. 
 We've grown to almost 1,000,000 customers worldwide. You'll be working with some of the best D2C marketers in the industry. 
 A plus if you have experience in these areas: 
 
 Front End Dev / Shopify Liquid 
 Motion Graphics 
 3D
