 Overview 
 Percona is at an extraordinary period of time and we are looking to add an enterprise level sales representative (ESM) to our outstanding team. We are specifically seeking someone with an established network within the United Kingdom. 
 This role is made for someone that has a successful track record in new business development within large enterprise accounts. We believe in value, having a process, owning a sales methodology and overall creating a great positive work environment that achieves very high results.

When safety permits, you will travel at least once a month to customer meetings, industry meet-ups and related conferences (more if you like or if needed for prospective client needs).  About Percona 
 Percona is a respected thought leader in the open source community. We provide services and software for MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL and MariaDB to open source users globally.
