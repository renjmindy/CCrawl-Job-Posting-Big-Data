 Junior Developer REMOTECONTRACT 
 Job Description 
 Mod Creative Inc. is seeking a junior level freelance developer to work within a small, creative and collaborative team. Mod Creative Inc. is a digital agency that specializes in connecting people and businesses through mobile and interactive media solutions, harnessing the power of data. We are helping brands and organizations to engage people across the digital landscape through apps, kiosks, and technology solutions. 
 Requirements: 
 
 
 Proficiency in building User Interfaces with: 
 
 
 Twig (alternately LARAVEL, BLADE, PUG) 
 
 
 Vue (alternately ES 6, ES 2016) 
 
 
 Tailwind (alternately FLEXBOX, CSS GRID). 
 
 
 
 
 Understanding of Craft CMS and its ecosystem is a huge bonus. 
 
 
 Located in the Continental United States 
 
 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 
 Engage in collaborative work with Mod Creative team members. 
 
 
 Ensure that projects are completed in a timely and accurate manner. 
 
 
 Communicate effectively with peers, team leads, and customers. 
 
 
 20 hrs + per week working on projects remotely within a team of diverse talent. 
 
 
 Job Type: Freelance Contractor 
 Hourly Rate: $40 per hour 
 Location: Remote
