 About Protrans: 
Protrans Personnel Services Inc. is a full-service recruitment company specializing in supporting customers with their contingent workforce needs in transportation, warehousing and distribution, manufacturing and administrative placements. For twenty-five years our commitment to our customers and employees has earned us the reputation of being a premium staffing company providing our customers flexible resources to meet their goals while matching quality employees with rewarding career options. 
 Protrans Personnel Services is headquartered in Dieppe, New Brunswick with branch offices in Mississauga, Toronto, Cambridge, Stratford, Tillsonburg as well as Fort Edward, New York State.
