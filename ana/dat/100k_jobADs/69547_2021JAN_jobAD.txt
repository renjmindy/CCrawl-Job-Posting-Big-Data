 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs Hiring Canada 
 Posted on  April 23, 2010  by  caregiver 
 The POEA or Philippine Overseas Employment Administration recently approved a job order that allows a POEA accredited recruitment agency to recruit qualified applicants for  Caregiver Jobs Hiring Canada . The said caregiver jobs hiring Canada was authorized last April 15 for a recruitment agency located in Malate, Manila. For the said POEA approved caregiver jobs hiring Canada, 2 caregiver jobs hiring applicants will be chosen for placement in caregiver work abroad. 
 If you have the qualifications and wishes to apply for the said POEA approved caregiver jobs hiring Canada, proceed as soon as possible to the office of the recruiting agency and submit your application. For inquiries on the said POEA approved caregiver jobs hiring Canada, you can contact ** Luzern International Manpower Services Corp. thru  **landline numbers specified below. 
 Luzern International Manpower Services Corp. 
1965 Unit 3 Town Homes, F. Benitez Street 
Malate, Manila 
Tel No: 
(632) 303-0212 
(632) 303-0329 
Fax No: (632) 303-0211 
Email Add: luzernjobs@eastern.com.ph
