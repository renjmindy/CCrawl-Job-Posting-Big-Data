 My client, a well established online retail business with a portfolio of sites, is looking to recruit a On-Site Search Manager. 
 The On-Site Search Manager will join a highly effective digital team. 
 The role will encompass: 
 
 Analysing on-site search to increase conversion, demand, click through rate and bounce rate 
 Making decisions on upcoming site search enhancements 
 Leading third party vendor communication on the implementation of strategies to drive improvements for browsing 
 Working with the CRO team to create new A/B tests and act as the business lead in the ideation of testing strategies 
 Developing and defining recommendations for site enhancements to improve the customer experience through content presentation and navigation 
 
 The ideal candidate for this On-Site Search Manager position will possess the following skills/expertise: 
 
 Experience in a similar on-site or product search capacity 
 A good understanding of CRO, testing and customer journeys 
 Excellent Analytical and Reporting skills 
 
 Highly competitive package. 
 Whilst this role is based in Birmingham, significant flexibility will be considered.
