 We are looking for apprentice carpenters (2+ years of experience) to assist lead carpenter in multi-family remodels and soft-story retrofits. Starting ASAP. 
 JOB DUTIES: 
 
 Renovation process from demo to clean up 
 Fixture installation and installing cabinets 
 Window and door installation 
 Baseboards and crown molding 
 Framing 
 Occasional materials pickup and transfer 
 Cover the driving area of greater Contra Costa County 
 
 MUST HAVES: 
 
 2+ years of carpentry experience 
 Follows directions 
 Ability to measure and cut with precision 
 Experience framing floors, walls, ceilings, roofs 
 Experience operating circular saw, reciprocating saw, chop saw, nail gun 
 Reliable. You show up on time every day and work hard 
 Tools (Levels, tape measure, drill, nail bar, screwdrivers, framing hammer, square, etc) 
 PPE: hard hat, steel toe boots, safety glasses 
 Valid drivers license 
 Truck or Van (with mileage compensation) 
 
 JOB DETAILS: 
 
 Rate: $25 p/h 
 Full time/40 hours 
 Paid weekly 
 Typical work week M-F 8:00AM - 4:30PM

 References, drivers & background check will be required
