 Job Resources 
 If you’re interested in Workamping, you’ve chosen the right place to start your journey! Our primary focus is helping fellow RV enthusiasts connect with employers across the country. 
 Whether you're looking for full-time work, a part-time job or volunteer opportunities, Workamper News is here to help you find the right avenue for living and working on the road. 
 Below are several resources for learning about specific jobs and opportunities across the U.S. and Canada. Of course, there are even more job-finding tools available to Workamper News Gold, Platinum & Diamond members, such as employer research tools and an online resume builder. Join today and jump-start your Workamping lifestyle! 
 How to Find a Workamping Job 
Read over this list of helpful hints to prepare for your job search. 
 Sample Job Listings 
Not sure what kind of opportunities exist in the Workamping world? Browse through some of our sample listings to get a feel for what—and who—employers are seeking. 
 Sample Work Agreement 
Before accepting an opportunity with an employer, take a look at a sample agreement. 
 Volunteer 
Make a difference by Workamping on a volunteer basis. Find out more about how it works and where volunteers are needed. 
 Jobinars 
Like webinars, which connect participants and presenters virtually, Jobinars provide employers with the ability to present their opportunities as they would at a job fair. 
 Featured Employers 
These employers have opted to share more about their Workamping opportunities via a website page with information, photos, and even audio and video recordings. 
 Daily Hotline 
Receive new "help wanted" job listings right to your email inbox!  Intro & Gold members receive a delayed Hotline of jobs previously posted. Platinum & Diamond members receive the Hotline email with new opportunities submitted by employers each day. You'll see immediate openings and opportunities for future seasons. Hotline jobs are also organized on a website page where you can organize by state, date posted, and do keyword searches. 
 Workamper News Magazine 
Published bi-monthly, our magazine is  the  catalog for finding Workamping opportunities. Each issue is filled with job listings organized by state, and accompanied by articles about Workampers and the RV lifestyle. Platinum & Diamond ** ** Members can receive a hard copy in the mail and/or view it online too (we know it can be hard to get mail as RVers!).  Intro & Gold members can only access the previously published issue online.
