 A large Lloyd's of London Insurance Company based in London are looking to hire an IT Business Analyst to work on a large multi-year actuarial and pricing programme. 
 Key requirements: 
 
 
 Experience of London/Lloyd's Insurance Market or General Insurance Company market 
 
 
 Experience working on large scale and global projects/programmes. 
 
 
 Exposure in implementing application solutions in the following business areas: 
Actuarial and Price Rating, Underwriting, Accounting and Finance 
Delegated Underwriting Authority, Bordereaux Management and Exposure Management 
WTW Toolset: Radar Base, Radar Dashboard, Radar Live, Emblem 
 
 
 Exposure in implementing application solutions in the following business areas: 
 
 
 Business Intelligence, Data Analytics, Data Quality 
 
 
 Data warehousing 
 
 
 Communication and stakeholder engagement skills will be important as working with all functions from Actuarial, Underwriting, Operations and IT. 
 
 
 Strong written and verbal communication skills. 
 
 
 Experience working closely with software engineers within a technology function. 
 
 
 Experience working in Agile and Lean Processes 
 
 
 As for now, all employees are working remotely. Prior to COVID the company offered very flexible working and will continue to do so when things return to normal.
