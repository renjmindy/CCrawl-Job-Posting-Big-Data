 Are you an experienced PMO Manager with a track record in setting up and embedding PMO governance and frameworks? Have you worked across high profile projects and looking for a new opportunity? 
 This Midlands based, industry leader are going through a huge change agenda and require a PMO Manager to join their business. Having oversight of all areas of the business, you will influence and educate key stakeholders, along with improve project delivery and reporting. 
 Reporting to Exec level, this is a brand new role with opportunity to shape and introduce a PMO function to help aid business decisions and deliver effectively. 
 What's required? 
 
 Experience of embedding a PMO capability with responsibility across processes and resources - running with this to a BAU stage 
 Worked across various project activities - such as IT/Marketing/Analysis 
 Outlined and ran governance of delivery frameworks 
 Track record of liaising and influencing key stakeholders - whether this be through holding project meetings/regular reporting 
 Experience of planning, managing project activities and identifying opportunities for best practice.
