 JOB TITLE: Financial Controller 
REPORTS TO: Finance Director 
SALARY: £70,000 - £80,000 including bonus 
 LOCATION: Essex / some UK travel 
 Company; My client is a £40m division of an AIM listed nationwide support services business. 
 Recent growth has been driven organically and through acquisition and this role reporting into the Financial Director is required to provide improvement to existing processes, controls and governance. 
 This is a superb opportunity for an ambitious, fully qualified candidate to manage a small team (3), lead on business critical projects as well as ensuring robust financial controls. 
 The successful candidate will be fully qualified accountant with PQE experience gained ideally from a listed company. 
 The Role 
 The Financial Controller will act as the "right hand" to the Finance Director and will assist in ensuring the finance department is aligned to the needs of the business. 
 The Financial Controller is specifically responsible for the day-to-day management of the financial operations & control function of the department. This role is highly visible within the organisation and a great opportunity for an individual looking for a challenging, rewarding and varied position. The Financial Controller will immediately be required to improve processes and document controls and policies, ensuring the business is ready for its growth trajectory. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 
 To take ownership for all aspects in day to day management of the operational finance team, including management accounts, accounts receivable and accounts payable 
 
 
 Develop and document business processes and accounting policies to strengthen and maintain and internal controls 
 
 
 Evaluate and manage risk - highlighting any concerns to senior management 
 
 
 Oversee monthly management accounts process including production of key business KPI's and reporting to head office, with accuracy to an extremely high level 
 
 
 Preparation of the monthly board packs for the Directors (including commentary against variances, KPI's, insights and trend analysis) and full balance sheet reconciliations 
 
 
 Manage all audit deliverables through to production of statutory signed accounts 
 
 
 Oversee and improve customer invoicing processes, including self bill routines and analysis 
 
 
 Assist with annual budgeting process 
 
 
 Monitor capital expenditure against budget 
 
 
 Provide leadership, coaching and motivation to an established but inexperienced finance team, including regular appraisals, objective settings and improvement plans 
 
 
 Produce ad-hoc reporting as and when required 
 
 
 Oversee VAT return process 
 
 
 Oversee Intrastat / EC Sales submissions 
 
 
 Ensure that financial systems are fit for purpose and implement continuous upgrades / development plans so they keep pace with the Company's growth 
 
 
 Integrate acquisitions 
 
 
 Keep up to date with accounting standards to ensure compliance 
 
 
 Deputise for the Finance Director when required 
 
 
 **Qualifications & Experience: ** 
 
 Qualified Accountant (ACA, ACCA) with minimum 5 years PQE 
 Demonstrable experience of dealing with large volumes of data 
 Experience managing audits and producing statutory financial statements 
 Must have previously led a team of 8+ staff 
 Strong communication, negotiation and influencing abilities (written & verbal) 
 Advanced Excel skills and accounts system administration 
 Excellent organisation and planning skills with the ability to work to strict deadlines whilst maintaining the quality and accuracy of the work 
 Must have a hands-on approach 
 Provide examples of managing risk and changing policies/procedures to do so 
 New system implementation would be highly advantageous 
 
 Competencies and Behaviours 
 
 Motivated to create an environment of continuous improvement 
 Strong analytical mind with attention to detail 
 Takes ownership of tasks and can work autonomously 
 Encourages teamwork and can delegate tasks appropriately 
 Compliance and governance oriented 
 Robust complaint handling & resolution skills 
 Customer & service focused
