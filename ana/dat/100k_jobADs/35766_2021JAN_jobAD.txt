 View Job Applicants in Search 
 Written by  Joe Visconti 
Updated over a week ago 
 When you want to compare your applicants in an apples-to-apples way, you can use the "View Applicants in Search" function. 
 Navigate to your "My Jobs" page and select the job you want to see. 
 On the page where you can view your applicants, select "View Applicants in Search" from the upper right corner: 
 
 You can then use the Advanced Search filters to see who has what particular skills, experience, ability to work on-site and more:
