 Management Accountant 
 
 Location 
 
 Shropshire, England 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Temporary 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £225 - £275 per day 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Rebecca Prince

 rebeccaprince@workwithglee.com

 Published: 
 
 about 1 month ago 
 
 Startdate: 
 
 Jan 2021 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 Rebecca Prince 
 My client is a leading business in its industry and they require an interim management accountant to cover for around 3-6 months whilst they recruit the right permanent person. 
 
 Supporting the Director of Finance in budget development, budgetary control, forecasting, financial modelling, and the development, design and implementation of systems and controls, financial policies and procedures; 
 Working closely with the department heads to develop the annual operating budgets, forecasts and the periodical reforecasts; 
 Working closely with the departments to ensure that all income and costs are captured 
 Preparing and updating the cash flow forecasts and monitoring results; 
 Month end reporting
