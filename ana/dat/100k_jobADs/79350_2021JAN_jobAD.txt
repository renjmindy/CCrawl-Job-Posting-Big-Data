 Boilermaker 
 Job description 
 WorkPac are currently seeking Boilermakers (Structural Steel) for a variety of workshop based positions across South Australia. Based in the Southern Suburbs of Adelaide, these roles offer an immediate start, attractive rates of pay and ongoing work. 
The work you will perform will vary greatly including Pipe, Plate & Structural. 
 Skills and Experience 
To be successful, you will require: 
 Relevant recent experience. 
Ability to read and interpret drawings. 
Strong commitment to OH&S.; 
Ability to pass a fabrication test.
