 Weekend Merchandiser (Midland) 
 PepsiCo 
 __Midland, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Want to work with a billion dollar company that’s fast paced and fun? 
 Pepsico Foods Canada is your choice employer if you’re hungry for: 
Pay to help fund your tuition 
Supplemental income for your family or extracurrillars 
Experience to build and refine critical skills 
Opportunity for future career growth 
PepsiCo Foods Canada (Frito Lay/Quaker Canada) is now one of Canada’s largest and fastest growing food companies. We’re looking for energetic, independent and motivated individuals to join our team as Weekend Merchandisers in the Orillia area. 
 At PepsiCo Foods Canada, you will have the opportunity to learn what it takes to develop and sustain a growing snack food business and stretch your current skills by merchandising our products to a set group of grocery stores. You will be provided with a robust onboarding/training program to set you up for success. 
 As a Merchandiser you will be responsible for: 
Merchandizing of our entire line-up to various retail partners 
Executing merchandising programs at store level 
Direct communication with our retail partners (store managers/site owners) 
Providing exceptional customer service 
Qualifications/Requirements 
 Qualifications / Requirements : 
So if you have… 
 Excellent customer service skills 
A desire to be physically active 
Interest in working on your own with minimal supervision 
A competitive mindset and the drive to be the best 
English and/or French oral, written and reading skills 
Valid G or G2 drivers license and access to a reliable vehicle 
Wage - $16/hour
