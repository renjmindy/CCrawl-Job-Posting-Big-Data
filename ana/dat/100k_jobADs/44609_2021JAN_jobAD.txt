 Are you passionate about Digital Events? Do you have hands on experience organising and promoting webinars, Live chat events and Live Q & A's on a variety of Social Media platforms? 
 If so, an opportunity for a talented Digital Content Executive has arisen to work on a Permanent basis based in the centre of Birmingham. Offering a competitive salary of up to 25k. You will be a part of the Communications team with a heavy focus on Digital Events. 
 
 What You'll do… 
 
 Work across multiple Social Media platforms to host and promote Digital Events. Owning projects from conception through to delivery. 
 Experience in building Email Campaigns from creation to automating. 
 Write and produce impeccable copy and engaging online and offline content to help drive organic growth and user experience. 
 Proofreading, writing, and editing content, developing user journeys, participating in events, and producing online content. 
 
 
 What You'll Need… 
 
 Direct exposure to Digital Events. 
 Highly focused background within Social Media. 
 Experience of producing content to deliver outstanding results and achieving targets. 
 Skilled in analytical tools to improve campaign outcomes. 
 Good problem solver with a passion for using the latest technologies. 
 The ability to utilise Organic Channels for lead gen in a B2C environment. 
 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
