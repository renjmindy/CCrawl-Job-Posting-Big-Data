 Accounts Receivable Manager based in Solihull 
 Your new company 
Your new company are a leader in their field based in Solihull and are looking for an Accounts Receivable Manager to join their team on a long term temporary basis. 
 Your new role 
Your new role will include but not be limited to; 
 
 Managing of the daily cash receipts 
 Review full aged debt report on a regular basis and identify and rectify any issues 
 Review any new account forms/credit changes 
 Responsibility for all direct to consumer and major accounts, understand pricing and payment variances and resolving any issues 
 Supervisory of the AR Clerks to ensure queries, credit notes and manual invoices are processed efficiently and bad debts are kept to a minimum 
 Preparation of half and full year credit note provision 
 Ensure all monthly statements are created, reviewed and distributed to customers , including debt chasing letters. 
 Responsibility for online export credit monitoring and insurance declarations. 
 Ensure the AR team are updating statement/invoice email addresses and monitoring any that are missing 
 Any other ad-hoc queries as requested by other departments. 
 
 What you'll need to succeed 
You will need previous experience in a similar role, be available at short notice as this role is an urgent requirement. You will have supervisory experience and be able to hit the ground running. We are looking for a positive can-do attitude and great communication skills. You will also be able to demonstrate excellent Excel skills
