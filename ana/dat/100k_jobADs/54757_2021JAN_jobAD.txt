 Saudi Job in Caregiver 
 Posted on  September 25, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This POEA approved Saudi job in caregiver are for Filipinos who want to have the chance to work abroad and practice their profession. It has a POEA job order that was approved last August 3 and 4 and has a job order balance of 5 and 8 available Saudi job in caregiver. The job order for the said Saudi job in caregiver was approved by the POEA for a recruitment agency based in Malate, Manila. 
 To apply for the said Saudi job in caregiver, all interested applicants should visit the recruitment agency in their office address indicated below. For inquiries on the said Saudi job in caregiver, you can contact the recruiting agency on their telephone and Email address specified below. 
 Saudi Placement Company 
Ground & 2nd Floor 1920 L. Guinto Street 
Malate, Manila 
 Tel No: 5241060 
Email:  saudiplacementco@yahoo.com
