 Company Description 
 Hildyard & Clifford LLP is a non-reserved legal services provider. We provide high-quality legal advice and tailored legal documentation ​to businesses, social enterprises, and individuals. 
 Job Description 
 As a Business Development Contractor, you will be responsible for generating new leads and new business for Hildyard & Clifford LLP. This is an exciting opportunity for a dynamic and ambitious individual to enhance their professional experience and grow our business. 
 Core Responsibilities 
 
 Refer prospective clients to us; 
 Generate new business by attending professional networking events; 
 Generate new clients and new business by developing and implementing additional business development ideas and strategies. 
 
 Person Specification 
 
 You will have the ability to work effectively both independently and as part of a team; 
 You will be a good self-motivator and capable of working flexibly and remotely; 
 You will be serious and diligent, and will communicate professionally and courteously with other members of the team; 
 You will be fluent in the English language; 
 You will have excellent oral and written communication skills - especially when interacting with clients - and will be an excellent networker; 
 No legal background is required for this business development role; 
 This role is offered on a commission-basis, and is outside IR35.
