 A client of Gleesons based in Burton-on-Trent is looking for a Project/Management Accountant who is immediately available to join them on a 3 month contract. This is initially a project role, they are looking for someone who can re-enter any errors made on their stock system therefore are looking for Strong SAGE experience and advanced excel skills. They are looking for someone to start on Monday 5th October. Please apply if you are immediately available and commutable from Burton-on-Trent. 
 The ideal candidate will have: 
 
 Recognised accountancy qualification (CIMA, ACA, ACCA) or QBE 
 At least 3 years experience in a Management Accountant role 
 Recent SAGE 200 experience essential 
 Advanced excel skills
