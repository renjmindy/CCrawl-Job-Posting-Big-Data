 Tech Lead - Retail Platform (Go) - Design & build mission-critical applications 
 Role overview 
 As a Lead Engineer, you will help your team build mission-critical scalable applications that improve and automate processes across departments in Zalando Outlets. You will help your colleagues to grow and guide your team to work autonomously on inspiring challenges. 
 WHERE YOUR EXPERTISE IS NEEDED 
 
 Design and build applications that power processes across all departments (including our warehouse) using leading-edge technologies 
 Ensure a state-of-the-art product development methodology providing a great developer experience for the internal users of your systems 
 Lead a small and agile team of 2 other engineers 
 Coach your colleagues to help them grow their technical as well as their soft skills 
 
 YOU ARE SKILLED 
 
 You have strong architecture knowledge in designing APIs and Microservices, databases design, software design and cloud technologies: 5+ years of experience in developing backend GoLang, (Postgre)SQL databases and AWS. Previous experience working with Kubernetes and Docker is a plus 
 Passionate about the agile way of working. As a Lead Engineer, you lead and coach your employees as a servant leader 
 Constantly strive to improve the environment you are working in, the architecture you are building and the skills of your colleagues and yourself 
 Take ownership of the products you build from design to operations 
 
 PERKS AT WORK 
 
 Culture of trust, empowerment and constructive feedback,  open source  commitment, meetups, game nights, 70+ internal technical and fun guilds, knowledge sharing through tech talks, internal tech academy and  blogs , product demos, parties & events 
 Competitive salary, employee share shop, 40% Zalando shopping discount, discounts from external partners, centrally located offices, public transport discounts, municipality services, great IT equipment, flexible working times, additional holidays and volunteering time off, free beverages and fruits, diverse sports and health offerings 
 Extensive onboarding, mentoring and personal development opportunities and an international team of experts 
 Relocation assistance for internationals, PME family service, and parent & child rooms* (*available only in select locations) 
 
 We celebrate diversity and are committed to building teams that represent a variety of backgrounds, perspectives and skills. All employment is decided on the basis of qualifications, merit and business need. 
 ABOUT ZALANDO OUTLETS 
 Zalando Outlets are the Zalando retail stores in Germany. We offer many different articles of the Zalando assortment and welcome customers to visit us offline in our unique stores. Our team stands for the perfect shopping experience - because shopping offline should be as fun as shopping online!
