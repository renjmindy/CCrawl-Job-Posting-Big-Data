 Prayer Request - Old Job or Employment, Severe Back Pain 
 by Carlo Durante 
(Revere, MA, USA) 
 Hi, I am writing to request prayer to either receive my old teaching position that I loved or be given a career where I would thrive. I am almost bankrupt. I would love to go back to my old job where I was happy. In order for this to happen, the current teacher needs to get a promotion as a Vice-Principal or Principal. A lot of people are talking within the school system that she'll get asked to work in administration as she desires. This would allow me to get my old job back. I am also suffering from a lot of back pain too. I hope I don't need another back operation. Please help. Thanks! 
Carlo Durante
