 **About Lifted  ** 
 We are a VC backed tech startup on a mission to lift lives by transforming care at home. Driven by our values and our industry-leading technology, we are revolutionising home care; with better supported Carers and total transparency for families powered by our data-driven care platform. 
 We're inspired by personal experience and we're growing very quickly. We're really proud that in just 18 months, we've delivered over 40,000 hours of care, gained exclusively 5 star reviews on TrustPilot; a glowing Care Quality Commission report; and an award for the best new care company in the country. 
 **Who we are looking for  ** 
 We are looking for an incredible Business Development Lead to help us lift more lives. You will be responsible for quickly building a network of clinical partners referring families to Lifted. You will communicate the quality and innovation of our service, always putting our social mission front and center to partners. You will be proactive; hands-on; full of energy and ideas and able to deliver to ambitious targets. Working with our Head of Growth, you will be a vital part of our success. We are building the future of care at home and your work could not be more important. 
 **The role  ** 
 
 Find and develop a network of partners referring families to Lifted 
 Think creatively about how to approach new partners, build relationships and keep them engaged to refer families to Lifted 
 Build a network of digital affiliates 
 Prioritise partnerships most likely to deliver results 
 Work with product team, to ensure the value of our product is communicated to partners 
 Hit ambitious growth targets 
 
 About you 
 
 Strong record of building revenue generating partnerships 
 Willingness to get out and about (virtually as well as in person) to promote Lifted and our mission 
 Track record of building revenue through partnerships rapidly 
 Self-starter with huge energy and ability to execute 
 Experience in high growth environment 
 Passion for transforming care and affinity with our mission 
 
 About you 
 
 Performance based bonus 
 Options in Lifted 
 Work on something that matters - we are building the future of care and we are changing lives every day 
 Be part of a team that loves what we are bundling and is driven to improve lives 
 Huge potential to help us scale in the UK and beyond. We have doubled our team in the last 6 months.
