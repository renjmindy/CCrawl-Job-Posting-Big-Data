 Would you be keen to work for a leading, technology driven organisation in the heart of Birmingham? Do you have strong SQL Development experience, along with full Microsoft BI Stack? 
 The organisation is currently going through a large migration and require to support on a business critical project, you will be the bridge between commercial and technical and be a part of a fast paced team. 
 The successful candidate will have the following - 
 
 Understanding of Microsoft full BI Stack - SSIS, SQL, SSAS, Power BI 
 Background of working on ETL Migrations 
 Familiarity with data extraction, cleansing processes and developing data warehouse 
 Core MS SQL Server administrative skills 
 Ability to translate business requirements
