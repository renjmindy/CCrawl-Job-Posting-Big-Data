 Diesel Fitter / Light Commercial Vehicle Mechanic 
 Job description 
 WorkPac are currently seeking experienced Diesel Fitter or LV Mechanic for an ongoing role working locally in Townsville for an equipment maintenance provider. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Machinery Breakdowns and General Fleet Maintenance on Defence Force Equipment, Trucks and Mobile Plant Equipment 
 
 
 Rebuilds of Defence Force Equipment 
 
 
 Mandatory Requirements 
 
 
 Trade Certificate 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Ability to pass a Low Risk Medical, Drug & Alcohol 
 
 
 About Workpac
