 Hot Damn! it's a Full-time job 
 Fern is looking for an Associate Creative Director in Atlanta, GA, USA. 
 About 
 Fern is on the hunt for an Associate Creative Director to join the team in Atlanta and/or Remotely. As an ACD, you will inspire and drive our talented team and set an ambitious benchmark of quality across all visual avenues. You are comfortable conceptualizing ideas, have a heightened aesthetic sense of style & design, and are suited for leading both large and small teams of artists. 
 During this time, Fern is working remotely, and this position is a global hunt for a top creative person dedicated to producing outstanding work. The Candidate will need to work on an East Coast schedule. 
 Skills & Qualifications: 
10+ years of experience in the Design & Animation industry 
3+ years of client presentation skills and team management 
Deep understanding of animation pipelines & workflows. 
Excellent typography and graphic design skills 
Advanced knowledge in applications for design, animation & stop motion is a plus 
Ability to work in a collaborative team environment 
Engagement with industry, cultural trends, and developments 
Excellent communication, chill vibes, a sense of humor 
Self-motivated, attention to details 
Capable of managing multiple projects and tight deadlines 
Drive to produce incredible work that surpasses industry norms 
Ability to create & direct projects from beginning to end 
Be a good person 
 Studio benefits. 
10 PTO days annually & 10+ Paid Federal Holidays (more added in 2021). 
Work From Anywhere or studio in Atlanta.
