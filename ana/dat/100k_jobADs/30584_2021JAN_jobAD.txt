 Full-Time Professor, Department of Forest Economics and Policy 
 The University of Eastern Finland -  North Karelia  , Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 The University of Eastern Finland (UEF) is one of the largest universities in Finland. The UEF is a multidisciplinary university, which offers teaching in more than 100 major subjects. The university has approximately 15,000 students and some 2,800 members of staff, and the university's campuses are located in Joensuu, Kuopio and Savonlinna. 
 The University of Eastern Finland is inviting applications for the following position: FACULTY OF SCIENCE AND FORESTRY Professor of Forest Economics and Policy (post no 30549), School of Forest Sciences, Joensuu Campus The languages of instruction and supervision at the School of Forestry  are Finnish and English . For the position in question, command of the  English language  is sufficient. The position will be filled with an open-ended contract. 
 The salary of the post is placed on levels 8-10 in the salary system for teaching and research staff (4 543,23 - 5 796,43 euros/month + personal performance component). 
 The application period closes on 8 November at 3 pm (Finnish time). The application should be delivered to the registry office of the Joensuu or Kuopio Campus: University of Eastern Finland Kuopio Campus Registry P.O. Box 1627 FI-70211 Kuopio FINLAND or University of Eastern Finland Joensuu Campus Registry P.O. Box 111 FI-80101 Joensuu FINLAND A probationary period will be applied to new members of the university staff (general regulations on the employment relationship at the University of Eastern Finland, 30 Nov. 2009)
