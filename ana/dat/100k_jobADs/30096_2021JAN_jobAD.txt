 We are a fast-growing YouTube specialist influencer marketing agency and we are looking for a full-time Campaign Producer to join the team! 
 Main duties 
 
 Sourcing and reaching out to appropriate creators tailored to each campaign 
 Communicating with clients and content creators 
 Work with the Creative Strategist to ensure the campaign is running smoothly 
 Manage production and campaign administration e.g. booking accommodation, travel, Invoices, contracts etc. 
 Track and monitor campaign costs and results 
 Run content approvals by giving feedback to YouTube creators, ensuring they meet campaign requirements and confirm content publication dates 
 
 Essential Experience 
 * Deep knowledge of YouTube creators and social media platforms
* Must be highly organised and attentive
* Interpersonal skills - ability to empathise with creators, especially during stressful times
* Strong email etiquette
* Experience in a fast paced and dynamic work environment  
 
 Preferred Experience 
   * Second language preferred
  * Previous entry-level industry experience in a digital marketing agency environment with a focus on campaign and content strategy.
  * Confidence to work independently and on fast-paced campaigns
  * Ability to use Keynote and spreadsheets 
  * Some editing experience
  * Willingness to learn in a fast-paced start-up environment
  * Must be a team player
  * Excited to attend industry events and develop your skills
 
 If you're organised, love YouTube and speak more than one language - please apply!
