 Project Manager (Electrical) 
 Job description 
 About the Company 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking a Project Manager to join Rioâs Utilities team, based at the Operational Centre at Perth Airport. 5:2 roster, ongoing contract. The successful candidate will be responsible for leading and delivering a 220kV Power Supply Project that aims to construct business critical electrical infrastructure at Dampier. 
 Duties include: 
 Working within a project team including engineers, consultants, project engineers and site teams to deliver project within budget and schedule 
 Overseeing all aspects of project delivery by managing budget, scope and schedule while maintaining a high level of stakeholder engagement throughout lifecycle 
 Project monthly reporting 
 Contractor performance and compliance to the contract 
 Managing project risk 
 Project HSE culture and compliance 
 Operational readiness, including shutdown readiness 
 Skills and Experience 
 Relevant tertiary qualification in Electrical Engineering, Project Management, or related discipline 
 15+ yearsâ experience as a Project Manager within Electrical 
 Experience in Power Systems Design and Engineering 
 Experience managing high-value complex projects 
 Leadership skills 
 Strong understanding of relevant Australia Standards 
 Proficiency in SAP, MS Office, and MS Project 
 Strong stakeholder engagement 
 Mining background 
 Strong communication skills, both verbal and written 
 WA C Class Drivers Licence
