 Rail Maintenance: Operator Maintainer 
 Job description 
 Job Highlight 
 8/6: 7/7 Roster or 5/2 Roster available 
FIFO from Perth or Tom Price Residential 
 Casual on-going position 
 About the Role 
 This role is a great opportunity for an enthusiastic individual with a strong commitment to safety to be part of a collaborative team. Reporting to the Crew Leader, you will be: 
 - Operating Rail Maintenance machinery such as Excavator, Loader so experience with this machine is highly regarded 
 - Associated tasks to do with track maintenance 
 - Vehicle loading, unloading, handling equipment & machinery 
 - Reporting faults to supervision as identified 
 - Safely executing breakdown repairs and recovery of various rolling stock assets including but not limited to; turnouts, IRJs, frogs and sleepers and derailment scenarios 
 - Maintain a high level of safety awareness and comply with all RTIO Company Policies and procedures 
 - Actively contribute to continuous improvement projects increasing safety whilst reducing risk & cost 
 Mandatory Requirements: 
- WA C Class Drivers Licence (HR Preferred) 
- Experience working in a hot environment 
 Preferred: 
- Rail Experience - Certificate II or III in Rail Infrastructure 
- Excavator, Loader competencies 
- Rio Tinto Iron Ore inductions or previous experience 
- Sound technical and computing abilities 
- High Risk Work Licence Dogging, Vehicle Loading Crane, Mobile Crane 
 - Experience working with rail specific Hand Tools 
 - Experience with Rolling Stock recovery highly regarded 
 About our Client 
 Rio Tinto operates the worlds largest integrated portfolio of iron ore assets. These assets include a world-class, integrated network of 15 iron ore mines, four port facilities, a 1,700 kilometre rail network and related infrastructure. Rio Tinto are expanding operations in the Pilbara to epic proportions while introducing next generation technologies to deliver greater efficiency, lower production costs and improved health, safety and environmental performance.
