 Interim P2P Implementation Lead - Staffordshire - 15 Months - Circa £75,000 per annum 
 My blue-chip client has a requirement for an Interim P2P / S2C Implementation Lead to join their business on an fixed term contract for circa 15 months. The P2P / S2C Implementation Lead will be the overall lead for the project, accountable for achieving key milestones of the implementation of both purchase to pay and source to contract modules. Reporting to senior finance, you will work closely with the project team, including Project Managers and Leads across different functions in the business, along with a small, dedicated team which you will take management responsibility for. 
 Key responsibilities: 
 
 Overall Implementation Lead for the project and delivery of key milestones 
 Lead the project team and play a leadership role in the wider project team 
 Engagement with key stakeholders 
 Ensuring the new system is successful, in terms of technology, processes and cultural change 
 Oversee the project, manage requirements, influence final design and delivery of the solution 
 Set and manage expectations on delivery time-lines, holding colleagues and suppliers to account 
 Lead on engagement with key stakeholders including leadership team 
 Highlight any risks identified, as well as leading on resolving, managing or mitigating these 
 Key point of contact for escalation of project or supplier issues 
 Oversee user acceptance testing, assessment of readiness for go live etc 
 Ensure system implemented meets the agreed requirements and scope, including processes, capability and control and governance requirements 
 Day to day management and coaching of project team 
 
 The Candidate: 
 
 Prince2 qualified 
 Either a chartered accountancy qualification or CIPS procurement qualification 
 Significant experience working with and leading end to end P2P / S2C implementation 
 Strong understanding of both P2P and S2C related processes 
 Demonstrable track record of delivery 
 Ability to communicate at all levels 
 Highly organised
