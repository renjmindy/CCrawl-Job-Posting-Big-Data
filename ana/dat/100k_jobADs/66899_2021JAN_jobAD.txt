 Tyre Fitter 
 Job description 
 Tyre Fitter 
 Role Highlights 
 
 
 Salary package of $75, 000 to $90,000 + super 
 
 
 Full time, secure role 
 
 
 Mine site based role - Gunnedah region 
 
 
 About the Role 
WorkPac are seeking a skilled Tyre Fitter for a permanent role with our Client based on a Gunnedah mine site. The successful candidate will be required to work a 4 panel, rotating roster - 12 hour shifts. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Work with light passenger vehicles and light, medium & heavy trucks fitting industrial tyres, underground mining tyres and light and heavy earthmover tyres. 
 
 
 Comply with mine site rules and procedures while providing a high quality service 
 
 
 Work independently and autonomously, at times, as well as part of a team 
 
 
 Follow a 4 panel roster, rotating day and night shifts (6/4, 4/4/, 4/6, 4/4) 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Relevant Trade Qualification 
 
 
 Experience with trucks and earthmoving machinery 
 
 
 Drivers Licence (HR desirable) 
 
 
 LF Ticket (or willing to obtain) 
 
 
 Must be able to pass a Pre Employment Medical and Drug Screening 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities.
