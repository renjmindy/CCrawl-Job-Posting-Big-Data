 IGA Valemount

 JOIN US BECAUSE YOU LOVE GOOD FOOD, WORK HERE BECAUSE YOU LOVE A GREAT OPPORTUNITY 
 For more than half a century, British Columbians have chosen IGA for a friendly face and good food. We’re a hub of community life and today, a place with fresh and revitalized energy, ready to be the grocery store of choice for shoppers. We are simple goodness. 
 IGA Valemount   (located in 1195 5th avenue)  is currently looking for a passionate foodie to join our team as a  Bookkeeper/Cashier.  With a passion for food, you are a person who loves working with people and creating a positive experience for others. 
 Job Overview 
 As a Bookkeeper, you are responsible for carrying out the full range of duties as outlined below and instructed by the Department Leader in a professional, responsible, accurate and timely manner. You will deliver exceptional customer service by fostering customer loyalty, fulfilling customer needs, and actively contributing to an environment of employee and customer engagement. 
 
 Accurately verify paper currency, change, credit card receipts and checks, and totals against print outs 
 Efficiently count and verify funds in a safe and prepare for pick-up by an armoured carrier 
 Ensure all products received at store level are accurately recorded, entered into the system and verified by Store Leader 
 Submit invoices weekly to H.O to ensure timely vendor payment 
 Receive and accurately break down funds delivered by armoured carrier 
 Efficiently and accurately prepare statistical reports as needed 
 Accurately reconcile refunds/voids on a daily basis 
 Proactively assist in customer service area during break relief and busy periods 
 Effectively multitask and apply sound organizational skills ensuring deadlines are met 
 Proactively answer phone calls directed to you and reply to all email correspondence in a timely manner 
 Maintain a clean and organized work area 
 
 Qualifications 
 
 2 years' bookkeeping experience 
 Cash handling and customer service experience 
 Previous administrative experience an asset 
 Exceptional verbal and written communication skills 
 Detail-oriented and highly organized 
 Excellent computer skills; familiarity with Outlook, Word and Excel
