 Self Storage Resident Property Manager - Apple Valley, CA

 The position: 
 The Resident Property Manager is key to our success, and must be professional, friendly and knowledgeable. You will be responsible for the daily operation of the storage facility. You will spend your workday split between office tasks, patrolling the property and indoor/outdoor maintenance. We would like someone who is dedicated and looking for a long-term position. 
 PLEASE NOTE  This is a Resident Property Manager position, meaning the employee is required to live onsite. The compensation package includes onsite housing and paid utilities (water, gas and electric). 
 Essential Duties and Responsibilities: 
 
 Assist customers with their storage needs 
 Convey features and benefits of our storage facility to all customers 
 Maintain an exceptionally clean facility, including the outside maintenance 
 Patrol facility on a continual daily basis, both inside and outside 
 General office: administrative work, data entry, answer phones, update customer files 
 Execute all documents associated with reservations 
 Process payments 
 Organize and maintain supplies 
 Other duties as assigned 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 Excellent sales and customer service skills 
 Management skills 
 Ability to multitask 
 Moderate computer skills: Microsoft Office, storage internet-based software a plus 
 Must be able to lift 25lbs 
 Must be able to stand and walk around: Property is 12 acres 
 Must be able to work full time/weekends/on-call 
 
 Experience: 
 
 Customer service: 3 years (Required) 
 Management: 3 years (Required) 
 Storage Facility: 1 year (Preferred) 
 
 *** Applicants will be required to pass a detailed background check. References will also be requested***
