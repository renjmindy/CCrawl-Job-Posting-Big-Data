 This is a great opportunity for motivated English-speaking Javascript developers in Munich! 
 Having been successfully on the market for 10 years now, my client is a well established Software as a Service provider in central Munich. 
 As the team is growing, we are now looking for several experienced Fullstack or Frontend Javascript Developers who are looking for a challenging and interesting role in an international team with start-up atmosphere. 
 What you will be doing: 
 
 You will develop and improve the company's frontend and backend based on modern Javascript frameworks 
 You will develop as well as design innovative UI patterns 
 Working cloesly with one of the company's founders, you will deliver a high quality SaaS product to your customers 
 
 Your profile: 
 
 You have a university degree in Computer Science or a related field 
 You have relevant professional experience in the development of web applications with  JavaScript 
 You have worked with  HTML5  ,  CSS  ,  Angular  or other SPA,  Node.js  and  NoSQL  databases, e.g. MongoDB 
 You have worked with  AWS  in at least 3 services 
 You speak English fluently 
 Your homebase is in Munich 
 
 What's in for you: 
 
 You work in a diverse international team and hand in hand with the company founders 
 You will get lots of training opportunities 
 You will use the latest technologies inculding ML or Serverless technologies 
 You will have lots of team events throughout the year 
 
 
 I'm looking forward to your CV!  jrichter@venquis.com
