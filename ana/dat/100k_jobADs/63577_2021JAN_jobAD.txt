 Office/Accounting Clerk - Western Tire & Glass 
 The Western Group of Companies is looking for an experienced Office/Accounting Clerk. This person will be responsible for all aspects of books and accounting records for our growing construction business and will bring a positive impact to our organization and help our company develop, grow and reach its full potential. 
 The responsibilities of the office/accounting clerk will include: 
 Completing full-cycle bookkeeping including payroll and government remittances 
 AP responsibilities:  entering invoices, checking statements, reconciliation of company credit cards, petty cash, Vendor management 
 AR Responsibilities:  Progress billing 
Organization of filing system (electronic and hard copy) 
Maintenance of financial schedules and sub-legers 
Preparation of weekly and monthly project reports for operations and management 
Completing work in process reconciliations for various projects. 
Development of internal policies and practices for operations improvement 
Assisting the owner/operator as needed 
Other duties as required 
 The successful individual will have a proven track record in bookkeeping, account reconciliation and project budget tracking. Key characteristics we are looking for include process-oriented thinking with strong analytical skills and an eye for detail. The applicant also must have exceptional communication and organizational skills, as well as the ability to work independently. 
 Job Type:  Temporary; Full-time
