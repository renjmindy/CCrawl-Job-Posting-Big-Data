 Springpod was born from our desire to transform the way young people engage with employers and universities. We empower students to take control of their career path by connecting them with leading employers and education providers. From work experience placements to apprenticeships and beyond, we are determined to help students discover their future career through innovative and modern engagement platforms- before they leave education. 
 We're looking for enthusiastic, hungry and target driven Partnership Managers to join the Springpod team and help drive Springpod's awareness amongst the Enterprise Employer Sector. You will report into our Head of Partnerships and the role will consist of generating new business and managing existing relationships to drive up-sells and renewals. 
 We support some of the most exciting organisations in the World. These include Airbus, NHS, Fidelity, The Royal Air Force, Cancer Research UK, HSBC, JLL, Nestle and many more. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Research accounts, identify key players, generate interest and develop accounts to create opportunities 
 Strategically develop business within the enterprise employer sector 
 Demonstrate superb account management skills, increasing account value and ensuring exceptional client service 
 Updating our CRM, managing your own pipeline and reporting back on active deals 
 
 Requirements 
 
 2-3 years proven sales experience and a track record of success in B2B sales, taking leads from discovery to close 
 Confidence in selling an innovative technology solution, taking both online demos and face-to-face meetings 
 Experience selling marketing technology or media 
 Experience with deal sizes of £25k+ and sales cycles taking 1 - 3 months 
 Experience using HubSpot CRM or Salesforce 
 
 Bonus: Experience in the student recruitment sector and have sold to employers, colleges or universities (not essential). 
 Benefits 
 
 Fast-paced start-up environment with a lot of responsibility and self-management 
 Competitive salary and commission structure (OTE - 50K+) 
 Very sociable team with monthly team incentives 
 The ability to work remotely 
 Training & CPD budget
