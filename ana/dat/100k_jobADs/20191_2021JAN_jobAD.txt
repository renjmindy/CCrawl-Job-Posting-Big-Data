 Customer Service Representative (Joseph Brant Hospital) 
 Precise Parklink 
 __Canada 
 Apply Now 
 Company Description: 
As Canada's parking industry leader for over 30 years, our expertise in providing full-service parking solutions has evolved into urban mobility intelligence. We now innovate and support solutions required for all modes of transportation including: public transit, parking and electric car charging stations. 
 Job Summary: 
Precise ParkLink’s Client Relations team is looking for an onsite Customer Service Representative (CSR) to be responsible for the day-to-day parking operations at Joseph Brant Hospital in Burlington, Ontario. The CSR will ensure that the policies and procedures are met for the client and the company. 
 Location: 
Joseph Brant Hospital, Parking Office, 1245 Lakeshore Rd, Burlington, ON L7S 0A2 
 Employment:  Casual, when required 
 Compensation:  Hourly 
 Mandatory Attributes: 
Advanced organizational skills 
 Advanced communication skills both verbal and written 
 Advanced attention to detail 
 Demonstrate initiative and willingness to self-educate 
 Obtain a positive and professional attitude under high pressure or changed circumstances 
 Advanced active problem-solving skills 
 Self-managed while accurately following instructions 
 Job Responsibilities: 
Maintain on-site parking equipment and perform 1st level equipment repair; 
 Perform Daily Site Inspections; 
 Provide parking instruction/guidance/assistance to patients, visitors, and staff as needed; 
 Ensure patients, visitors, and staff receive the correct pass and/or are registered into the system properly; 
 Process cash, credit and debit transactions as well as daily deposits and refunds; 
 Act upon all comments/complaints in a prompt and friendly manner; 
 Demonstrate patience in all customer contact situations by providing a pleasant and professional tone and manner; 
 Create and keep up to date reports as assigned by head office and the hospital; 
 Demonstrate good judgement when responding to customer issues to ensure good customer service; 
 Perform audits/investigations into parking passes or other incidents as required; 
 Report to the Site Supervisor 
 On-call for after-hours and during the weekends 
 Hiring Contact: 
Submit resume via email to Khalid Iqbal, Client Relations Manager, kiqbal@precisebi.com.
