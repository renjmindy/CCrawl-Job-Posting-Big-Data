 Home Cleaner 
 Seniors for Seniors 
 __Gloucester, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Introduction 
 Seniors for Seniors is a non-medical home care agency proudly serving seniors for over 30 years. Part-time casual employment is available for retired individuals to provide companionship to seniors. 
 If you are a mature adult and reside in Gloucester, Ontario, and are seeking the opportunity to provide home cleaning service and enjoy helping fellow seniors, please email us your resume. 
 Duties and Responsibilities: 
Adheres to the safe work/risk management policies and practices of Seniors for Seniors. 
Works within the Ethical Practice Framework of Seniors for Seniors. 
Understands the range of services provided by each division of the Spectrum family and how it contributes to our model of Total Client Care. 
Continually assess, anticipate, identify and respond to new and changing needs of clients and communicates available Total Client Care resources to clients when appropriate. 
Listens, observes, and communicates any concerns/changes in the client’s physical and/or emotional condition immediately to the Branch Manager and Client Care Coordinator 
Vacuuming/dusting floors and internal surfaces 
Cleaning bathrooms or showers 
Mopping floors 
Cleaning/dusting furniture and appliance as per client 's request 
 Skills and Qualifications: 
Warm, compassionate individuals. 
Flexible and able to converse with a wide range of individuals. 
Experience caring for the elderly. 
Several years of life experience 
Ability to speak other languages an asset. 
Prior cleaning experience is an asset 
Must be prepared to obtain a copy of their police vulnerable check. 
 Closing Information 
 We thank all applicants; however, only those individuals selected for interviews will be contacted. 
 Please note that successful applicants will require a current TB testing and current Police Reference Check suitable for working with the vulnerable sector prior to commencing employment. 
 Seniors for Seniors, a division of Spectrum Health Care is an equal opportunity employer and we will accommodate any needs under the Canadian Charter of Rights and Freedom, Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act and the Ontario Human Rights Code. 
 Should any applicant require accommodation through the application processes, please contact the Human Resources Department at 416-964-0322 for assistance.
