 Part-time role with a West Bromwich-based business, which will suit someone with strong cashiering & reconciliation experience. 
 This is an ideal opportunity for someone looking for five mornings a week. 
 Ideally, I would like to speak to candidates who fulfil the following criteria: 
 - experience with high volumes of cash processing 
 - allocating incoming payments 
 - statement reconciliations 
 - a friendly, collaborative attitude, with a willingness to help and support other areas during busier periods 
 - solid skills in Word and Excel 
 - strong attention to detail 
 This is a delightful business, with a great team of people in a lovely environment. 
 If you would like to discuss your suitability for this role, please drop me a line on 07813608579
