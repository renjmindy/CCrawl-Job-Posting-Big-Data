 Accelerometer and gyroscope 
 Published on the May 01, 2017 in IT & Programming 
 
 Project 
 Project Insights New 
 
 About this project 
 it-programming / mobile-development 
 Less than USD 50 
 The project is about how social media and digital platforms degrade the individual self and society. 
To represent this I have made several objects, one of them is a whip to attach to your smartphone device. 
The idea is that you hit yourself with this device and upload how hard and how many times you have fustigated yourself. 
 I need the values of the accelerometer and gyroscope so the phone  "calculates" how many times and how hard you have hit yourself with the whip. After, I need these values so the user can upload it on facebook and social media sites. 
 This data uploaded on social media sites wants to raise the question of what kind of material are we sharing on the net.
