 Registered General Nurse or Registered Mental Health Nurse 
 An amazing job opportunity has arisen for a committed RGN or RMN to work in an exceptional nursing home service based in the Bedford area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This specialist care home provides a specialist dementia nursing service. 
 They provide each person with a comprehensive care package tailored to meet their individual needs, a named nurse and a key worker. 
 The dedicated team ensure that they meet the resident's individual care and social needs 
 As a Nurse your day to day duties include: 
 
 
     Ensure the highest possible standards of care, assessing residents' needs and wishes, and developing services to enhance their quality of life
 
 
 
     Champion appropriate independence and personal choice; developing, reviewing and updating care plans to meet our residents physical, social and psychological needs
 
 
 
     Oversee all aspects of medicine management on your shift - ensuring medicines are appropriately received, stored and administered, in accordance with company policies and current legislation
 
 
 
     Build the positive reputation of the Business; liaise professionally with visitors and other external stakeholders
 
 
 
     Ensure all elements of resident experience are positive, from admission to discharge
 
 
 
     Participate knowledgably and professionally to all inspection visits, ensuring your team is ready at all times to do the same
 
 
 
     Ensure compliance with all legal, regulatory and best practice guidelines - identify, investigate and resolve risks proactively
 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Previous older peoples care experience is desirable but not essential
 
 
 
     Caring & compassionate with an excellent clinical track record
 
 
 
     Passionate about delivering outstanding care to older people
 
 
 
     Excellent organisation & leadership skills
 
 
 
     Effective communication skills
 
 
 
     Flexible, professional and committed to being the best
 
 
 
     Quality driven with a desire to continually improve yourself & our services
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent salary of £17.50 per hour and the annual salary for this position is £34,125 per annum. 
 We currently have vacancies for both days and night shifts available.
