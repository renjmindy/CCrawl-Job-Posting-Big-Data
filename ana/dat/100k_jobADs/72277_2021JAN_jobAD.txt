 Clinical Unit Manager 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Clinical Unit Manager to work in an exceptional care home based in the Dennistoun, Glasgow area. 
 As Unit Manager you will be working for one of the UK's leading healthcare providers 
 The team of professionals and well-trained care assistants provide 24-hour care to ensure all residents comfort and well-being. 
 They like residents to get to know one another in a safe and supportive environment. 
 Their aim is to make every resident feel completely at home and their 'person centred' approach emphasises respect and dignity 
 As the Clinical Unit Manager your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     Be responsible for leading a team of Nursing and Care staff to ensure excellent standards of care
 
 
 
     Plan rotas/recruit/train and mentor staff
 
 
 
     Build strong relationships with local regulator/providers and Social Services
 
 
 
     Comply to NMC guidelines with administration of medicine
 
 
 
 An ideal Clinical Unit Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position : 
 
 
     You must have previous experience in a management role
 
 
 
     You must have a proven track record in delivering good care results
 
 
 
     You must have a good understanding of CQC/CI and other regulatory bodies (DoLs/MCA)
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
 **The successful Clinical Unit Manager will receive an excellent annual salary of £38,634.96 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role for 42 hours a week working through Day Shifts. 
 In return for your hard work and expertise you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Free DBS
 
 
 
     A fantastic working environment
 
 
 
     Competitive salary
 
 
 
     Flexible Pay
 
 
 
     Robust training programme and ongoing career development
 
 
 
     A comprehensive bonus scheme including: gift vouchers, refer a friend, a pension, attendance reward and much more!
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse either RGN or RMN with a valid NMC Pin
