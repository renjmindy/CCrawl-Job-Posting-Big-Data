 Property Tax Processor FT Temp-To-Hire 
   
   Workway, Inc.   
 __Website    __@workway 
 Workway is a professional staffing firm, working with organizations across the country to place exceptional candidates. We are looking for a  Property Tax Processor: 
 
 Procures tax amount data from taxing agency jurisdictions. 
 Tax amount data procurement method consists of telephone, e-mail, and fax communication to taxing agency jurisdictions. 
 Interprets tax amount data and statuses per property taxes received from taxing agency jurisdictions and effectively identify tax status for payment processing. 
 Follows agency guidelines and special processing instructions. 
 Builds Tax Lines as needed utilizing the TED table. 
 Balances all disbursements prior to the end of the day and submit all payment back daily. 
 
 Specific qualifications for the position include: 
 
 High School Diploma 
 Previous experience with Property Taxes is required 
 Strong Excel skills required 
 
 Let one of our recruiters assist you in finding and preparing for interviews with our clients. Whether you are interested in direct hire, temp-to-hire, or temporary position, Workway can help you to put your best foot forward. Contact us today!
