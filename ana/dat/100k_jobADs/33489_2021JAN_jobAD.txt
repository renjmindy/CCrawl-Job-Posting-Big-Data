 Can You Believe It?! it's a Freelance job 
 Dave.com is looking for a Freelance Portrait Photographer - Los Angeles  in Los Angeles, CA, USA. 
 About 
 Dave is seeking a freelance portrait photographer to capture ~20 portraits of our community in their homes / environments around the LA metro area across a 2-3 day shoot. Photographer to provide gear and is responsible for shooting and finishing a select number of portraits for use across our communications channels. See inspiration photos (not our work) below for look / feel / locations. 
 We will conduct this shoot with a minimal footprint and small crew for COVID safety. The aim is to shoot ~5-7 people per day in their spaces, with no / minimal setup or staging. We are going for authentic docu-style portraits not elaborate setups requiring large lighting or art direction. Photographer to provide all gear and source an assistant as needed. All equipment should be mobile ready with portable / battery power. 
 Please include examples of work or link to your portfolio with examples of lifestyle portraiture.
