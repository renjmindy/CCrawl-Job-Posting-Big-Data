 Clean Room Supervisor 
 About Our Client 
 Our client is a medium sized Food Processing company partnered with WorkPac, they are currently seeking an experienced Clean Room Supervisor to commence immediately in a permanent position. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are seeking an experienced Clean Room Supervisor to commence immediately in Gilston. This is a permanent opportunity working afternoon shifts, the successful candidate will have an extensive background working in the Food Processing industry and will have previous experience in a Supervisor or Management role. You will also have experience conducting basic hand on duties such as preparing various foods, packaging and all round Warehousing tasks where required. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Picking salad meals 
 
 
 Loading/unloading 
 
 
 stacking bags & crates 
 
 
 Cleaning 
 
 
 Monitoring staff 
 
 
 Quality assurance 
 
 
 Implementing safety standards 
 
 
 Direct communication with management 
 
 
 Meeting strict safety standards 
 
 
 Working both individually and apart of a team 
 
 
 Skills & Qualifications 
 
 
 Minimum 2 years recent experience in a similar role 
 
 
 Strong background in the food processing industry 
 
 
 Supervisor or management background required 
 
 
 Industry qualifications advantageous 
 
 
 Current resume with 2x work related referees 
 
 
 Hold a current Australian drivers license 
 
 
 Availability for an immediate start 
 
 
 reliable and strong work ethics 
 
 
 Culture & Benefits 
 
 
 Full time hours 
 
 
 Permanent position
