 About us: 
 Emotech is based in London, UK, and found in 2014. We are dedicated to exploring a multimodality system that blends various machine learning algorithms, allowing for bespoke solutions to AI needs for software and hardware. Emotech was selected by TechCrunch as one of the Top 14 European Startups and then Top 30 Business Cases in China by Financial Times. In 2019, Emotech introduced the first multimodality AI English teaching assistant solution launched in Huawei HDC and awarded the first London Business Award. Emotech has been in collaboration with renowned universities since 2015 starting from UCL, Imperial College, the University of Edinburgh to the University of Cambridge, University of Oxford, CMU. 
 About you: We are looking for a bright and enthusiastic team assistant who is passionate about working at a start-up company, results-based performance and keen to learn a lot. 
 Location: N1 Islington 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Processing all supplier invoices and expense reimbursement claims 
 Helping with the recruitment process including sending tasks and scheduling appropriate meeting times 
 Booking rooms, arranges meetings, and planning refreshments 
 Assists in planning and arranging events, including organising catering 
 Maintains stock lists and orders office supplies as needed 
 Prepare purchase orders as requested Monitor progress of incidents 
 Maintains accurate records for employee holiday requests 
 Helping to prepare marketing materials such as public-facing PowerPoint presentations and word documents 
 
 Must Haves: 
 
 Excellent communication skills and the ability to work well in a team 
 Relevant experience of providing secretarial and administrative support to a team gained in a complex environment 
 Establishing effective office systems and procedures 
 Working in a pressurised environment with conflicting priorities and without supervision 
 Strong time management and prioritisation skills to work efficiently 
 Ability to use a variety of design software- including Adobe Premiere, Photoshop, Illustrator 
 Passionate about technology products 
 Right to work in the UK 
 
 Additional: Fluent in Mandarin 
 We are working remotely due to COVID 19. If you are interested in this position and meet the above criteria please get in touch. We are looking forward to hearing from you!
