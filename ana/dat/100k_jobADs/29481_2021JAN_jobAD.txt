 Fruit Picker/Farm Worker - various locations in North West Tasmania (34 views) 
   
  WTC  
  Casual  
 Davenport, Tasmania 
 04/01/2021 
 Share: 
 
 We are currently looking for enthusiastic and motivated people to join our farm teams. 
 Roles: 
 
 Picking – strawberries, raspberries, blackberries and blueberries 
 Roles available December until May 
 No experience required – full training will be provided 
 
 Work pattern: 
 
 Typically 5 or 6 days a week, Monday to Sunday 
 Hours per day can be up to 10 hours depending on the stage of the crop 
 
 Availability of work: 
 
 Work will commence from mid-October on-wards 
 The timing of crop peaks can vary between the different berry types however the busy periods are typically between December through to April 
 Following the harvest / picking season, farms have a wide range of jobs including general farm work, irrigation and maintenance work. 
 At the end of the harvest / picking season, there may be opportunities for work in other regions 
 
 Pay rate: 
 
 Picking is paid under Piece Rate 
 An average competent Picker has the opportunity to earn more than the base rate in the Horticulture Award 
 All employees will view and sign a Piece Rate Agreement outlining the minimum rates they will be paid 
 
 Pay arrangements: 
 
 Weekly pay every Thursday by Bank transfer with emailed payslips 
 Superannuation is paid at a rate of 9.5% on top of all wages 
 
 Benefits: 
 
 
 Higher than the Horticultural Award wage rate (base hourly pay rate currently 2.5% above the horticulture award base rate) 
 
 
 Enterprise Agreement including double Piece Rate pay for work on Public Holidays and longer paid breaks than required by the Horticulture Award 
 
 
 Ongoing work available through to April/May in Tasmania 
 
 
 sites are classed as regional for Working Holiday 2nd and 3rd year visas 
 
 
 Full safety induction 
 
 
 Work health and safety managed as a priority on site 
 
 
 Full COVID protocols in place to protect all workers - registered COVID Safe Business TAS
