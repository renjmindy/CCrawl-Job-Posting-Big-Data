 We are looking for an experienced   Robotics/Controls Engineer   to join our growing development team. We are building a product on the cutting edge of robotics. You will have the opportunity to tackle multidisciplinary issues in a dynamic environment. You will be working in a highly collaborative, diverse and supportive team. 
 What will you be doing? 
 
 You will be a fundamental part of our controls and robotics chapter, while working in cross-disciplinary teams 
 You will contribute to the robot controller, the trajectory planner, and the overall motion performance 
 You will be in close contact with the backend and with frontend stacks, investigating and solving state-of-art robotics challenges 
 You will be instrumental in improving our robot, building applications, configuring and testing robots 
 
 What will you bring to the team? 
 
 Master's degree in robotics, mechanical engineering, control systems or similar 
 Solid background in controls and robotics systems, kinematics, dynamics, motion planning, closed-loop control systems 
 Python and/or Matlab/Simulink programming 
 Ability to develop and implement complex algorithms, learn and apply new languages, tools and frameworks 
 Experience working in a fast-paced, results oriented commercial environment (min 1 year) 
 Experience with Mac and/or Linux development environment 
 Superb analytical and problem-solving skills 
 Excellent collaboration and communication skills 
 Great organizational and time management skills 
 Desirable: C++ programming skills 
 Desirable: Previous experience with startups would be great, but not mandatory
