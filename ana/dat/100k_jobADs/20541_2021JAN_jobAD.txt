 Are you an experienced Fundraising Manager looking for a new challenge? Do you have exceptional communication skills and good stake holder management? If so, we have an opportunity for you. 
 A growing and ambitious Charitable Trust is looking to recruit a talented and driven Fundraising Manager. Offering a competitive salary of £35-38k, remote working and the chance to make a real difference in the lives of others. 
 Working as part of a dynamic fundraising team, this role will be responsible for growing the individual giving fundraising pillar and will support the Head of Fundraising across Corporates, Trusts and Events. 
 What you'll do… 
 
 Act as an ambassador for the Charity with internal and external stakeholders. 
 Support the fundraising team to maintain a  social  networking presence for community and corporate events. 
 Write engaging tweets, posts, and web copy to promote the Charity. 
 Play a proactive role in raising the profile of the Charity. 
 Contribute to the development of new fundraising activities. 
 Deliver & implement audience communication plans that encourage donor retention. 
 Undertake talks and presentations which promote the work of the Trust and create enthusiasm for participation in fundraising activities. 
 Keep the CRM system up to date, tracking all donors, communication and income generation. 
 
 What you'll need… 
 
 Previous fundraising experience. 
 Highly motivated and numbers driven. 
 Excellent sales and negotiation abilities and exceptional communication skills. 
 Experience in successfully delivering campaigns across multiple channels. 
 The ability to build and maintain professional relationships. 
 Creative thinking with the ability to solve problems. 
 CRM management experience. 
 Strong social & digital experience. 
 Comfortable with remote working with occasional travel to central hubs. 
 
 What you'll get… 
 
 Salary of £35-38k. 
 Competitive Benefits package 
 Remote working. 
 The chance to work for a good cause and make a real difference to people's lives.
