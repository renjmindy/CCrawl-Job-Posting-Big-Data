 About Mixpanel Engineering 
 
 
 The Engineering, Product and Design teams at Mixpanel together build Mixpanel’s product. The team consists of very talented and nice people who collaborate to solve problems. 
 
 
 We organize in Product Groups which are small groups (3 to 8 people) comprised of engineers, product managers and designers working on a problem or an investment area with complete autonomy over the development process. 
 
 
 We are customer focused and data driven - We like to iterate, learn and improve quickly using qualitative and quantitative data to continuously deliver customer value. 
 
 
 Our engineering team gets to work on really challenging and exciting engineering problems because of the nature of our product and our scale. 
 
 
 We use our own product on a daily basis!

 About the role: 
As a designer working with the Data Trust team, you will help technically-minded users get their data into Mixpanel and maintain its quality over time. Your focus will be reimagining the developer experience as well as improving our existing data management and debugging tools. This is critical to customer success because product analytics isn't very useful without accurate, reliable data. Recent projects from this team include: 
 
 Lexicon Schemas API — sync an internal data dictionary or tracking plan with Mixpanel 
 Lexicon Import — import data definitions from Segment, mParticle, Iteratively, or Avo 
 Service Accounts — authenticate requests to APIs with this special type of user 
 
 What You’ll Work On: 
 
 Designing and building public APIs and documentation infrastructure 
 Improving the quality and understanding of data via our data governance tools 
 Creating tools to help customers identify, debug, and fix incorrect data 
 Ensuring Mixpanel works flawlessly with other best-of-bread tools 
 
 We’re Looking For Someone Who: 
 
 Has the ability to design user interfaces, user flows, interactions and prototypes that bring clarity to complex problems 
 Possesses a high level of comfort with the technical aspects of an Enterprise tool and/or API product 
 Understands modern engineering tools and practices 
 Leverages quantitative as well as qualitative data to audit and analyze their work 
 Works closely with designers, product managers and engineers to see a project from concept through implementation and beyond 
 Connects every design decision to business and user goals 
 Presents to teammates and stakeholders
