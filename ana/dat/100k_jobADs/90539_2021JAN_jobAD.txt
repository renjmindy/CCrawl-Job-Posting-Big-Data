 Caregivers Jobs 
 Posted on  November 19, 2011  by  caregiver 
 A POEA duly licensed and accredited recruitment agency located in Makati City is now accepting applicants for their available  Caregivers Jobs . This caregivers jobs is in the holy land of Israel and had its Job Order approved by the POEA last November 10, 2011 with vacancies for 30 caregivers. 
 If you are interested in applying for the said Caregivers Jobs, proceed directly to the office of CPM Manpower Services Inc. and personally submit your application together with your comprehensive resume. For any questions regarding the said caregivers jobs, please contact CPM Manpower Services Inc. by way of their telephone numbers we have provided below.
