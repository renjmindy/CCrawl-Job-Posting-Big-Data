 Senior Sales/Underwriting Associate 
 Apply for the position, Senior Sales/Underwriting Associate, with Smoky Hills PBS today or contact Kelli King for more information. Posted Jun 26, 2020. 
  Apply Online  __    785-483-6990  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 Are you an outgoing, self-motivated individual?  Smoky Hills PBS is looking for a Senior Sales/Underwriting Associate to add to our team.  The primary duty of the Senior Sales/Underwriting Associate will be to cultivate and solicit support from businesses and agencies for our programming, local and national productions. 
 Responsibilities: 
 -Work as a team with station management to maximize sales/underwriting for Smoky Hills PBS 
 -Identify corporate leaders’ interests, set up meetings, discuss and sell underwriting for Smoky Hills PBS’s programs and events 
 -Build and maintain strong relationships with current clients while developing new business 
 -Prepare contracts, discuss terms and obtain signatures of clients 
 -Achieve rigorous financial goals 
 -Work with Smoky Hills PBS staff to ensure proper placement of underwriter’s logos and spots 
 Experience/Qualifications: 
 -Prior sales experience (media sales preferred) 
 -Bachelor’s degree preferred but not required 
 -Computer software knowledge required 
 -Exceptional interpersonal skills 
 -Excellent verbal and written communication skills 
 -Team player who is detail-oriented 
 -Exceptional proofreading and research skills 
 -Valid driver’s license required as there will be travel in central and western Kansas 
 Please email cover letter and resume to  [email protected] .
