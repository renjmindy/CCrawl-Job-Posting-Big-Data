 Junior Project Manager 
 We are looking for a Junior Project Manager with strong administrative skills and a passion for building great products. 
 A successful candidate will need to be organised, proactive and motivated to get things done. Have a can do attitude and to be able to overcome any blockers that come their way. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Assist the PMO team with analysing scope of new projects or feature requests, highlighting risks and assumptions, assigning responsibilities and ownership, defining project objectives and success factors, devising a project delivery plan 
 Ensure projects are delivered to a high quality standard 
 Ensure all relevant issues are communicated appropriately and send regular status updates to project stakeholders 
 Ensure project documentation and project boards are up to date 
 Coordinate project staff during all phases of the project 
 Provide support to team members, internal colleagues and clients 
 Prioritise and meet deadlines 
 
 Required: 
 
 Strong administrative and communication skills 
 A technical background 
 Past experience with working in a software development environment and using issue tracking tools (eg. Jira, Github Issues) 
 
 Nice to have: 
 
 IT related degree 
 Programming experience 
 Project management experience
