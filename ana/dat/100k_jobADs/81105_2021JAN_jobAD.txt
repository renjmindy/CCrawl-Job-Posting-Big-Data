 Karelia University of Applied Sciences Student Union POKA searching for a  tutor and lobbying Secretary  for a fixed term contract (maternity leave substitute). Fixed-term employment lasts for 01/08/2014 - 30/06/2015. Fixed-term employment can not continue any longer. 
 Tutor and lobbying the secretary duties include organizing tutor Karelia University of Applied Sciences peer, member services, public information and student implementation of legal protection. We are looking for a collaborative and customer spiritual person who wants to work in the young students' interests in the organization of the works. Mission provides excellent opportunities and the ability to continually develop themselves and to act independently in different situations. Applicants are required to good oral and written Finnish and English-language proficiency, as well as a wide range of computer skills. Job management requires co-operation and communication skills, initiative, ability to work within the limits of the time, as well as self-directed organization and prioritization skills.
