 Metal Shop Foreman 
 Mirmil Products 
 __Trenton, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Mirmil Products is looking to add a Metal Shop Foreman to their team. This individual will be responsible for overlooking the day-to-day activities of the metal shop. 
 Responsibilities include (but are not limited to): 
 
 Acting as coach, leader and supervisor – assigning work, proactive communication with shop and engineering, and setting individual targets for employees 
 Hands on assistance with fabrication and assembly of multiple architectural metal configurations 
 Coordinating new job start-ups and scheduling projections 
 To have the ability to work with light and heavy steel and be mechanically inclined 
 Planning the sequence of tasks to fabricate weldments and use measuring instruments 
 Assembling and fitting metal sections and plates together to form complete assemblies or sub-assemblies 
 Monitoring the quality of fabrication 
 Communicating with other employees and supervisors 
 Being hands on and pitching in to help when needed 
 Carrying out hold points, sign-offs and other quality initiatives 
 Providing design and estimating feedback 
 Helping provide standard procedures and give technical instruction 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 Must have previous Architectural Metal fabrication and foreman experience 
 Must be able to analyze and interpret engineering drawings 
 Must have excellent team leadership and training skills 
 Must have excellent welding abilities 
 Must be able to use multiple machines (shear, bend, grinders, cranes, etc..) 
 Passionate about cleanliness, organization and doing it right the first time 
 Fluent in English with excellent communication skills (written and verbal) 
 
 Experience: 
 
 Previous experience as foreman will be an asset (3-5 years) 
 
 Wage will be based on experience. Only those selected for an interview will be contacted. Thank you to all who apply. 
 Job Type: Permanent 
 Job Types: Full-time, Permanent

 Casual dress

 Day shift
