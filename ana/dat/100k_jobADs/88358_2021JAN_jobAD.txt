 Anaesthetics Nurse 
 A new job opportunity has arisen for a Anaesthetics Nurse to work in an exceptional hospital based in the Chelmsford, Essex area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This hospital provides a wide range of services including Surgery, Medicine, Oncology, Plastic and Cosmetic surgery, and Paediatrics. They are continually introducing new technology to ensure they provide the best experience for patients and support for their hard working teams and have just opened 2 additional purpose built theatres 
 An ideal Anaesthetics   Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Your registration should not have any restrictions or conditions
 
 
 
     Experience in paediatrics desirable
 
 
 
     ALS/EPALS certificate desirable
 
 
 
     Excellent written and verbal communication skills
 
 
 
     Flexibility and adaptability to meet the changing needs of the business
 
 
 
 The successful Anaesthetics  **Nurse will receive an excellent salary of £36,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     25 days annual leave plus the option to buy more
 
 
 
     Contributory Pension Scheme
 
 
 
     Access to genuine opportunities to grow, develop and specialise in your career
 
 
 
     Private Healthcare
 
 
 
     Life Assurance
 
 
 
     Free parking and subsidised staff restaurant
 
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be an RGN, ODP or a Scrub & SFA and must be valid NMC or HCPC registration
