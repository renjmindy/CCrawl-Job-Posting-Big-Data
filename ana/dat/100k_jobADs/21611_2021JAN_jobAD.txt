 Lovely opportunity for an Assistant Accountant who enjoys the mainstream elements of a finance team. Working in a delightful environment, my client will welcome into their team an individual with strong experience in fixed assets, month end tasks and reconciliations. 
 If you are not interested in studying, but you want to be a valued member of the team, this could be the place for you. 
 Responsibilities for this role will include: 
 - month end support 
 - balance sheet reconciliations 
 - maintenance of the fixed asset register, including disposals & additions 
 - capital expenditure reporting 
 - other ad hoc tasks to support the Finance Manager 
 Based in Wolverhampton, this role is to start immediately, and will pay up to £25k. It will suit someone with a steady and logical approach to their work, who has advanced skills in Excel. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
