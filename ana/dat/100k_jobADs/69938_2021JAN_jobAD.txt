 Clinical Unit Manager 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Clinical Unit Manager to work in an exceptional care home service based in the Wakefield area. 
 As a Unit Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 The residents at this special care home has an individual care plan designed to give them the best support possible. 
 The highly trained team ensure residents have as much independence as possible, while getting the care they require 
 As the Clinical Unit Manager your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     Be responsible for leading a team of Nursing and Care staff to ensure excellent standards of care
 
 
 
     Plan rotas/recruit/train and mentor staff
 
 
 
     Build strong relationships with local regulator/providers and Social Services
 
 
 
     Comply to NMC guidelines with administration of medicine
 
 
 
 
 An ideal Clinical Unit Manager must have these skills as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     You must have previous experience in a nursing management role
 
 
 
     You must have a proven track record in delivering good care results
 
 
 
     You must have a good understanding of CQC/CI and other regulatory bodies (DoLs/MCA)
 
 
 
 
 **The successful Clinical Unit Manager will receive an amazing salary of £18.00 per hour and the annual salary for this position is £39,312 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role, for 42 hours a week working on Day Shifts only including 6 supernumerary hours. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Paid Breaks -
 
 
 
     Free Meals On Long Days
 
 
 
     Onsite Parking
 
 
 
     Free DBS
 
 
 
     Ongoing Career Progression
 
 
 
     Pension Scheme
 
 
 
     Assistance With Revalidation
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse either RGN or RMN with a current active NMC Pin
