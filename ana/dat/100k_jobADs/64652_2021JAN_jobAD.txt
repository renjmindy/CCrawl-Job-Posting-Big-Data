 Radiology Nurse 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Radiology Nurse to work in an exceptional hospital based in the Chelmsford, Essex area. 
 As a Radiology Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This hospital provides a wide range of services including Surgery, Medicine, Oncology, Plastic and Cosmetic surgery, and Paediatrics. They are continually introducing new technology to ensure they provide the best experience for patients and support for their hard working teams and have just opened 2 additional purpose built theatres 
 As a Radiology Nurse your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     Providing high quality patient focused care, whilst assisting the consultant radiologists undertaking interventional procedures, such as drainages and biopsies under CT, fluoroscopy or ultrasound guidance for both outpatients and inpatient
 
 
 
     Scrubbing or circulating in the dedicated angiograpgy suite and interventional fluoroscopy rooms during invasive cases such as peripheral angioplasty, IVC filter insertion, insertion of nephrostomies/ urethral stents and embolisation particularly uterine artery embolisations, CT guided radio frequency ablation
 
 
 
     Administering IV sedation and the monitoring of patients during procedures
 
 
 
     Responsible for any patients/ visitors to the department who become unwell and for initiating emergency care for them as appropriate
 
 
 
     To provide support to the radiographers, radiology department assistants, nuclear medicine technologists
 
 
 
     To be involved in the teaching of nursing and radiography students
 
 
 
 **The successful Radiology Nurse will receive an excellent competitive salary. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Competitive salary
 
 
 
     25 days annual leave plus the option to buy more
 
 
 
     Contributory Pension Scheme
 
 
 
     Access to genuine opportunities to grow, develop and specialise in your career
 
 
 
     Private Healthcare
 
 
 
     Life Assurance
 
 
 
     Free parking and subsidised staff restaurant
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Registered Nurse with a valid NMC Pin
