 Concreter 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 3 weeks work 
 
 
 2nd - 20th December 
 
 
 FIFO Perth - Kalgoorlie 
 
 
 About the Role 
 Our client is currently seeking an experienced concreter who can hand finish for an immediate start. You will have work from the 2nd - 20th December working 13 day fortnight, 11 hour days and be staying in camp accommodation, $55 per hour + $5 site allowance. 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Experience forming up concrete footpaths and crossovers/driveways 
 
 
 Experience laying concrete footpaths and crossovers/driveways 
 
 
 Experience to screed and finish footpaths and crossovers/driveways 
 
 
 C Class licence 
 
 
 National Police Clearance within 6 months (or happy to apply for one) 
 
 
 Ability to pass drug & alcohol test 
 
 
 Right to work in Australia 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
