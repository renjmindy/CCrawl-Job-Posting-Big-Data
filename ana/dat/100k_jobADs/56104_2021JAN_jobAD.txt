 About us 
 After being founded by the entrepreneur and IT-Specialist Florian Frey and the former CEO of Scout24 Holding Dr. Martin Enderle, allmyhomes ( www.allmyhomes.com ) rose to the No. 1 platform for the marketing of new building estates in Germany within three years, increasing to a size of 160+ employees from over 30 different nationalities. 
 Following the entry of TPA Real Estate Brands founder Lutz Grimm, we continue to revolutionize the real estate industry through digitalization and the supply of targeted data for room concepts of the future. In addition to the continuous improvement of our product, Europeanization is one of our next steps in our growth. 
 For our Product Development department, we are currently looking for a Product Manager (m/f/d) in a permanent position in our office in Berlin-Ostkreuz. 
 Your tasks 
 
 Continuous development of our platform in collaboration with cross-functional teams (4-7  people) and close interaction with other business representatives 
 Develop feature ideas that address user problems in the real estate business 
 Make decisions based on business objectives, user feedback & analytics 
 You understand user needs as well as business requirements to translate them into product requirements 
 Research of industrial trends  to support the product vision and strategy development 
 
 Your profile 
 
 Solid experience in product management field incl. all aspects of a successful product development cycle 
 Business and technical understanding 
 Experience working in scrum/agile methodology 
 Passion for product development (web and mobile products) in a fast growing environment 
 Self-driven, entrepreneurial mindset, confident appearance as well as a structured and independent working style 
 Very good English skills and a good command in German desirable 
 
 Our offer for you 
 
 20 years experience  in the real estate- and advertising industry 
 A  dynamic  startup atmosphere with  room for change 
 An  international  team (ca. 160+ employees, ca. 30 nationalities) 
 A comprehensive onboarding, regular  feedbacks  and  performance reviews 
 Individual development opportunities and  further education 
 Good ideas for a  sustainable  life and work 
 A centrally located office with numerous connections  (Berlin-Ostkreuz) 
 Visa application support  for non-EU citizens 
 
 If you are interested, you can quickly and easily send your application by clicking the "Apply now" button to Lisa Hermann.
