 Can You Believe It?! it's a Freelance job 
 You're probably not one to take a job just to make your friends jealous, but in case you are, Byte New York is hiring a Freelance Sr. Lead Designer (Art Director)  in New York, NY, USA. 
 About 
 (This is a freelance position with possibility to hire on permanent, full-time basis in the future) 
 KEY RESPONSIBILITIES 
Oversee all assigned design projects, from conception to delivery 
Provide guidance and mentorship to design, animation and video production 
Help determine the appropriate daily/weekly resourcing for projects 
Maintain brand consistency throughout all our marketing projects 
Design original pieces and provide art direction 
Generate ideas to portray concepts and advertise products/services 
Review junior-senior designers’ work to ensure high quality 
Being strategic with creative and utilizing appropriate team members 
Build a productive and collaborative relationship with other teams within the business - Those being Account planning, Creative planning & Media, CRM & Analytics. 
Stay up-to-date with industry developments and tools 
Problem solving daily challenges in a fast paced environment 
 SKILLS AND PREVIOUS EXPERIENCE REQUIRED 
7 years’ proven work experience as a Senior Designer, Graphic Designer or similar role 
Portfolio of completed design projects and campaigns 
Strong presentation skills 
Experience with team management 
Experience working on digital / social media projects 
Experience delivering clear and constructive feedback 
Extensive knowledge and experience with the Adobe Suite 
Strong aesthetic skills with the ability to combine various colors, fonts and layouts 
Attention to visual details 
Ability to meet short deadlines and collaborate with a team on how to prioritize appropriately 
Experience in production (working with crews and editors)
