 Marine Project Advisor (Electrical) 
 Job description 
 
 
 Join a global leader in the mining sector 
 
 
 Initial 12 month contract with potential extension 
 
 
 FIFO 8/6 to the Pilbara 
 
 
 About The Role 
 WorkPac are seeking a Marine Project Advisor with an electrical trade background to join Rio Tinto's Supply Chain Services team within Sustaining Capital on a FIFO 8/6 roster to the Pilbara. The successful candidates will provide site contractor management, construction expertise and implementation skills to deliver projects safely in a sustainable, timely and cost-effective way. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Ensure that all site construction activities comply with Rio Tinto standards and policies for HSE. 
 
 
 Provide safety leadership and coaching through daily field leadership. 
 
 
 Assist the engineers for quality assurance of project deliverables. 
 
 
 Provide advice on the implementation of construction management plans. 
 
 
 Complete accurate and timely reporting documentation. 
 
 
 Build and maintain collaborative stakeholder and end user engagement on site. 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Marine experience in construction or maintenance of marine infrastructure. 
 
 
 Contractor management and construction supervision experience in the resource sector. 
 
 
 Trade qualified Electrician with A Grade Electrical Licence. 
 
 
 Current WA C Class Driver's license minimum. 
 
 
 Strong decision-making capability along with ability to work autonomously. 
 
 
 Excellent interpersonal, written and oral communication skills. 
 
 
 Ability to lead, influence and motivate. 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
