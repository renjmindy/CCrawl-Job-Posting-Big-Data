 Description 
 
 Description 
 The Interviewing Gets The Job Presentation  : During & After The Interview is the one of two presentations developed to assist the job seeker for a successful job interview experience This second part of the presentations covers everything the job seeker will need to know during and after the interview. This will cover all aspects of the interview itself. The interview process will be discussed in detail, to include such things as questions that employers can ask job seekers and even questions that the job seeker may want to ask an employer. How to close the interview will be covered and also things that the job seeker can do after the interview to help a job seeker have a better chance of a successful interview and possible placement.
