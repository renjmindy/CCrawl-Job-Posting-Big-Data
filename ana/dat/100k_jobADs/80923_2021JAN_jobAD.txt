 Early Career Clinical Pharmacologist Abstract Competition 
 4 Sep 2020 Prizes awards ,  opportunities pharmacologenius 
 If you are a medical student, internal medicine and core medical trainee (IMT/CMT) or a pre-registration pharmacist / pharmacists up to 2-years post qualification, and you are employed or studying in UK or Ireland, you can participate to  The Early Career Clinical Pharmacologist Abstract Competition . 
 Abstracts should be related to the following categories: 
• Research projects linked to clinical pharmacology 
• Audit or quality improvement project on the use of medicines 
• Education abstracts on safe prescribing 
• Interesting case reports related to clinical pharmacology 
 Authors of the top 3 abstracts from each discipline will be invited to present to  Pharmacology 2020  and the final winner will be awarded £250. 
 Deadline: 18 September 2020, 23:59 GMT.
