 A client of Gleesons, based in Burton-on-Trent are looking for an Interim Management Accountant to join them on a short stint on a 2-3 month contract. The successful candidate will Provide accurate and timely monthly management accounts, budgets, and forecasts to a high level of accuracy. They are only looking at candidates commutable from Burton on Trent and available to start within next 2 weeks, there will be a mix of remote working on on-site working. 
 The ideal candidate will have: 
 
 Recognised accountancy qualification (CIMA, ACA, ACCA) 
 At least 3 years experience in a Management Accountant role 
 Negotiation and influencing skills 
 SAGE 200 experience highly desirable 
 Strong excel skills
