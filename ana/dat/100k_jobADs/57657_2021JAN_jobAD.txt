 Careers@Workpac 
 Are you ready to be part of something great? We don’t do ordinary. We do extraordinary. 
 Talented people are critical to the success of our business, clients and candidates and our selection process reflects this. We search for the best people to complement and champion our culture and the type of work we do. Our talented teams are developing solutions that impact millions and we’re looking for people to join us on our journey. 
 At WorkPac Health and Social Care, you are part of something great, where you are constantly challenged, inspired and supported. 
 Our Culture 
 We get a kick out of what we do, and it shows! 
 WorkPac Health and Social Care people are entrepreneurial in spirit and understand that together as a team we are stronger, bolder and brighter. 
 We’re dedicated to creating an environment driven by our aspirations, where people can feel comfortable and supported, but are challenged to be the best they can possibly be. Where passion and intelligence are the norm, people have the freedom to think outside the box to embrace challenges and solve problems. Most importantly, we strive to deliver our people an environment where they really enjoy coming to work. 
 Our cultural values keep us agile and inspired, working together to explore new opportunities, realise possibilities and dream big.​
