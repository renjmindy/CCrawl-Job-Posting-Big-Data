 Scheduler 
 Job description 
 
 
 5:2 / 4:3 roster 
 
 
 Residential to Karratha or FIFO from Perth 
 
 
 12 Month Contract 
 
 
 The successful candidate would need to have excellent organisational skills and ideally have SAP knowledge, the role is coordination between Execution, planning & scheduling to ensure the execution teams are set up for success. This role is primarily office based in 6 mile. 
 Skill and Experience 
 Self-managed and proactive attitude 
 Budgeting and organisational skills 
 Actively monitor resourcing needs and respond to resourcing changes 
 Able to input data in multiple systems including but not limited to Word, Excel, SAP, and HOS. 
 Effectively operate under emergency and/or demanding situations 
 Establish and maintain effective staff relationships 
 Follow all RTIO policies and procedures 
 Excellent written and verbal communication skills 
 Read and interpret track schematics - desirable 
 Essential to be able to work in a team and influence people 
 Rio Tinto operates the worlds largest integrated portfolio of iron ore assets. These assets include a world-class, integrated network of 15 iron ore mines, four port facilities, a 1,700 kilometre rail network and related infrastructure. Rio Tinto are expanding operations in the Pilbara to epic proportions while introducing next generation technologies to deliver greater efficiency, lower production costs and improved health, safety and environmental performance.
