 Caregiver Jobs Hiring in Israel 
 Posted on  September 2, 2010  by  caregiver 
 A Makati based recruitment agency is currently inviting Filipino caregivers to apply for its POEA approved  Caregiver Jobs Hiring in Israel . These caregiver jobs hiring in Israel have job orders that were approved for recruitment last August 9 and 25 for Sunace International Management Services, Inc. For the said job orders a total of 59 applicants will be selected for placement in caregiver work abroad. 
 If you have the training or experience and interested to apply for the said caregiver jobs hiring in Israel, visit the office of the recruiting agency and forward your resume and application. For more details or inquiries you can refer to the recruiting agency’s landline numbers or email address indicated below. 
 Sunace International Management Services Inc. 
Unit 201, 2F Midland Mansion 
Arnaiz Avenue, Legaspi Village 
Makati 
Tel. Nos: 8184246 / 8184647 
E-Mail: sunace_services@yahoo.com
