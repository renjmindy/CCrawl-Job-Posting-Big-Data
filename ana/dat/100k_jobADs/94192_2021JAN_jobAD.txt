 | Position Summary: 
 Perform a variety of electrical engineering inspection duties as determined by assignment. Validate that construction of each project is in compliance with the contract documents. Ensure adherence to Hospitals and department policies and procedures. No patient care assignment. 
Accountability: 
 
 
 ARCHITECTURE - Serve as a consultant on mechanical, mechanical, and architectural coordination and design matters, including code compliance, special needs accessibility and fire safety considerations 
 
 
 ENGINEER - Serve as consultant and designer of mechanical and electrical matters, including code regulations, special needs accessibility, and fire safety considerations. Proficient with mechanical or electrical calculations for sizing system component 
 
 
 COMPLIANCE - Ensure project operations are in compliance with design specifications and University, state, and federal policies and regulations 
 
 
 COMPLIANCE - Participate in compliance meetings as requested 
 
 
 CONSTRUCTION - Observe each phase of construction of a project; inspect as appropriate 
 
 
 CONSTRUCTION - Oversee all aspects of the day-to-day management of construction projects; monitor and coordinate work performed by architectural, engineering, and construction firms, as well as institutional, state, and local entities 
 
 
 CONSTRUCTION - Provide construction administration or construction oversight, monitoring site safety procedures and quality of work, resolving and reporting any issues immediately; ensure contractor compliance with contract 
 
 
 CONTRACT - Maintain a detailed knowledge of each project contract documents 
 
 
 OBSERVATIONS - Record observations daily noting acceptable and unacceptable work; report unacceptable work to the designated representative immediately 
 
 
 REPORTS - Research and prepare various reports pertaining to operations, equipment, policies, procedures, and/or other issues as appropriate 
 
 
 DEVELOPMENT - Enhance professional growth and development through participation in educational programs, reading current literature, attending in-services, meetings and workshops 
 
 
 REPORTS - Prepare periodic reports for superior or others as needed/assigned 
 
 
 Education Requirements: 
 
 Bachelor's Degree in related discipline 
 
 Experience Requirements: 
 
 5 years directly related experience 
 
 Licensure/Certification Requirements: 
 
 Registered Professional Engineer (PE), Electrical 
 
 Experience Requirements - Preferred: 
 
 7 years directly related experience 
 
 Physical Demands Requirements: 
 
 Medium Work: Exerting 20 to 50 pounds of force occasionally, and/or 10 to 25 pounds of force frequently, and/or greater than negligible up to 10 pounds of force constantly to move objects. Physical Demand requirements are in excess of those for Light Work. 
 
 Working Conditions Requirements: 
 
 
 Med Haz: Dirt, dust, fumes, odors, bad weather, noise 
 
 
 May be required to work on ladders or scaffolding
