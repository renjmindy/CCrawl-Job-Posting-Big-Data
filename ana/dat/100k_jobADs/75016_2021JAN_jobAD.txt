 We are looking for a Lead QA Tester to join our super talented Unity development team in Brighton. In this role, you will be involved in a range of diverse projects, varying from architectural applications, VR/AR, mobile gaming, and educational games whilst leading and executing all aspects of QA and testing in relation to Unity development. 
 You will work directly with the development team and other testers to implement efficient QA processes. The QA Lead will be able to analyse development roadmaps, schedules, and sprint goals in order to determine the best test plan and determine what to test in relation to development prioritization. As well as applying hands-on QA testing, the QA lead will support and manage the needs of other QA testers in the team. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Run test cycles on a per sprint and build release basis 
 Establish and evolve formal QA processes, ensuring that the team is using industry-accepted best practices 
 Oversee administrative aspects of software testing including establishing metrics and reports 
 Develop and execute test cases, plans and procedures 
 Diagnose defects and track them from discovery to resolution 
 Partner with engineering teams to stay informed of new technologies and new features being introduced and to ensure that system components are designed for testability 
 Develop deeper technical understanding of our development processes and products in development 
 Respond to all requests from team members in a timely and professional manner 
 Support other QA team members 
 
 The ideal candidate will have: 
 
 3+ years' experience in the software quality field including prior hands-on quality engineering experience 
 Ability to develop and execute test cases, plans and procedures to support various development methodologies 
 Familiarity with the software development life cycle and test cycles; Sprint deliveries, Function and feature testing, Systems and backend services, Deployments and differences therein, User accounts, Stress & scale, Smoke & sanity checks 
 Understanding of different format testing procedures, and deployment 
 Understanding of existing QA best practices and methodologies to design, implement and automate processes 
 Understanding of Jira and available tools for issue management 
 Understanding of Unity in relation to latest code retrieval and running 
 Experience coordinating with external QA resources and supporting QA resources within the team 
 Detailed and effective written communication skills for documenting the features tested and bugs found 
 Highly organised, detail oriented, extremely responsive and able to prioritize a large number of tasks 
 Self-motivated, works well independently and with others 
 
 ** **  What West Pier can offer you: 
 
 Flexible working hours 
 A competitive salary which is reviewed on an annual basis 
 Company pension scheme provided by NEST 
 Ongoing training with career development 
 A friendly, creative and focused studio atmosphere 
 Regular in-house events and social outings, such as board games, poker nights, cheese board Friday's, escape rooms and much more 
 Weekly fruit delivery with healthy snacks and locally roasted coffee 
 Free eye tests + standard glasses for display work 
 Flu vaccinations are offered on a yearly basis 
 A West Pier t-shirt is provided on the first day 
 Enviable location in Brighton city centre, two minutes from the beach and five minutes from Brighton rail station
