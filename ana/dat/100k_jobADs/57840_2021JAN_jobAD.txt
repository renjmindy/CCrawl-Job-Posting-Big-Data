 Are you a Buyer that has worked within manufacturing? Are you familiar with consignment stock, kanban and inventory control? Are you immediately available? If so, this temporary role could be perfect for you. 
 Based in Cannock, you will be responsible for keeping the order book up to date, calculate kanban and safety stock figures and expedite stock. You will be confident in communicating with suppliers, engineers and look for continuous improvement opportunities. 
 In order to carry out this role, you will need the following skills and experience: 
 
 Previous experience in a buying role 
 Familiar with using Microsoft Navision 
 Strong communication skills 
 A hardworking and resilient attitude 
 
 In return the successful Buyer will receive an hourly rate of between £11 - 13 per hour including holiday pay. You will need to be immediately available as this is a temporary role for 3 months initially. 
 If you meet the above criteria, please do not hesitate to apply. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
