 Deputy Manager 
 An amazing job opportunity has arisen for a committed Deputy Manager to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the Tooting, London area. 
 As the Deputy Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This special nursing home provides a home where older people can be helped to live as independently as possible. 
 The home provides care for people living with dementia. 
 The aim is to offer residents a positive and supportive environment 
 As the Deputy Manager your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     Responsible for all aspects of running the home in the absence of the Care Home Manager, accountable for the standard of care delivered within their home
 
 
 
     Leading the clinical team on a shift by shift basis
 
 
 
     Directing support staff to ensure the delivery of quality care
 
 
 
     Working closely with the care & nursing team, relatives and other agencies to ensure person-centered care plans are in place that put the individual at the centre of all interactions, ensuring dignity and respect are maintained at all times
 
 
 
     Carry out duties in accordance with NMC guidelines, leading the care & nursing team on shift and providing clinical leadership, practice development, feedback and reflection to Registered Nurses and Care Assistants as well as new and developing members of staff
 
 
 
 An ideal Deputy Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Have a minimum of 1 years managerial experience of leading a care team in the care of older people, or related social care setting
 
 
 
     Have evidence of continuing professional development
 
 
 
     Have a working knowledge of the regulatory framework relating to Care Homes
 
 
 
     Possess good communication and interpersonal skills, both verbal and written
 
 
 
     Demonstrate the ability to lead, support and motivate a dedicated care team
 
 
 
     Be able to take responsibility for the safeguarding of older people
 
 
 
     To be committed to the ongoing training and development of your team
 
 
 
 
 **The successful Deputy Manager will receive an excellent salary of £22.85 per hour and the annual salary for this position is £42,775.20 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role for 36 hours a week working predominantly Days, however some Nights may be required, also you are required to work Weekends on rota basis. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     6.6 weeks holiday including Bank Holidays, increasing to 7 weeks with five years of service
 
 
 
     Training and continuing personal development
 
 
 
     Free uniform
 
 
 
     Free initial DBS Check
 
 
 
     Free staff meals when on duty
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as Nurse either RGN or RMN with a valid NMC Pin
