 Look At This! it's a Full-time job 
 HOLY SHIT! Underlining is hiring a Head of Advertisement Video Editing to work on new video ads for the internets most popular beauty products in their San Francisco, CA, USA office. 
 About 
 Underlining® is a cosmetics company with multiple D2C brands: TatBrow® (Eyebrows), Nailboo® (Nails), HIDE™ (Concealer & Foundation). We're a small team with immense experience building online businesses. 
 We're looking for an experienced video editor who can create conversion oriented advertisements for social media. IMPORTANT: we are after conversion content rather than beautiful content. We're looking for someone who understands how to sell using video. How to leverage human emotion to drive viewers into buyers. 
 As well as contributing ideas to edits, coming up with new video ad edit concepts & being able to execute start to finish. 
 Here are some examples:

 We've grown to almost 1,000,000 customers worldwide. You'll be working with some of the best D2C marketers in the industry.
