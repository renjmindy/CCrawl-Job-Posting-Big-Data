 Location 
 
 Nottingham, Nottinghamshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance

 Temporary

 £325 - £425 per day 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Arshi Thind

 4 months 
 
 Expiry date:

 Startdate:

 Preparation of the Group annual and interim financial statements, including co-ordination of divisional submissions and ownership of the Group consolidation. 
 Assist with the preparation of technical accounting papers for the Audit Committee 
 Key point of contact for the Group's auditors 
 Preparation of other technical accounting guidance as required 
 Preparation of the monthly Board pack, including Group management accounts and monthly consolidation 
 Co-ordination of tax compliance for the Group with divisions and external tax advisors 
 Daily cash reporting to the Group CFO 
 Weekly cash flow forecasting (from divisional submissions) for reporting to the banks. 
 Submitting to the banks required monthly information, producing covenant compliance calculations and requesting facility utilisation 
 
 The successful candidate will be ACA/ACCA qualified, and available to commence in the role at short notice.
