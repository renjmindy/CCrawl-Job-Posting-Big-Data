 First Time Move Accountant

 Accounting and Finance

 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £30000 - £40000 per annum + Bonus 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Sophie Evans

 sophieevans@workwithglee.com

 Published:

 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-11-11 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson finance are delighted to be working in partnership with this SME business in South Warwickshire, to recruit an Accountant. Reporting into the Senior Financial Controller, this is a 360 role, managing the full finance process, up to Management Accounts, producing weekly, monthly, quarterly and yearly reports, cash flow, preparation of accounts for audit, assist the wider business with year-end work, and compile VAT returns and EC Sales Lists. 
 Besides working closely with your colleagues in the Finance team, you will also find yourself liaising with all other departments on a regular basis, as well as attending frequent team, company and customer meetings. 
 You will be AAT qualified, part or fully qualified, and we looking for a first move from practice. You will be personable, well presented and have an excellent working knowledge of accounting software and Microsoft office (particularly Excel) are essential. 
 In return, you will receive a salary from £30,000 - £40,000 dependant on experience, bonus up to 10%, flexible working, and the opportunity to work within a strong and diverse team. Company culture is taken seriously, you will become part of a modern, forward-thinking and inclusive organisation, capable of offering a stimulating environment in which to accelerate your career. 
 If this sounds like you, please call Sophie Evans on 07812 079718 to discuss further or apply direct. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
