 Renard set to resign from Morocco job 
 July 7, 2019January 10, 2020   Benjamin Hajji 
 The charismatic manager is expected to announce his departure from the Moroccan national team next week. 
 According to the reliable website,  11Lions.nl  , Hervé Renard will step down from his job as head coach of the Moroccan national team, after three and a half years. The news certainly don't come as a surprise, after the surprise exit to Benin in the Africa Cup of Nations round of 16. Renard has already hinted about possibly leaving on numerous occasions, explaining how people have tried to sabotage him and refusing to comment on his future.
