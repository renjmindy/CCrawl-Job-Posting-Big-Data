 Senior Finance Business Partner - Warwick 55 - 60k + Bonus 
 Gleeson are supporting an International organisation to recruit a Senior Finance Business Partner who can provide robust challenge and support to business leaders to maximise potential opportunities and minimise risks. 
 The role will report directly into a Finance Director, who has high expectations and will always demand the very best, but also understands the need to develop individuals and will ensure the Senior Finance Business Partner is provided the skillset to take on their role in the future. 
 The role will act as a Business Partner to the commercial team and will provide decision support through detailed analysis, and interpreting management information in order to influence key decisions. Ideally you will have experience of partnering at a senior level, and poses the ability to provide easily digestible analysis and challenge where necessary. The role will be seen as crucial to the delivery of continuous improvement to ensure better sourcing decisions and productivity improvements, and take the lead in forecasting and budgeting. 
 The role will suit an Accountant who is ACAACA, ACCAACCA or CIMACIMA Qualified with experience of working in a fast paced environment and the hunger to drive change, whilst working autonomously.
