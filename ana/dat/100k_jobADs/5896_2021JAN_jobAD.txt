 Health Benefits 
 Click here to view our excellent health benefit options! 
 We know our Employees are the reason our residents "Love Where They Live" and so we show our appreciation for them by offering a multitude of benefits and perks! 
 We are proud to be able to offer such a wide variety…AND even our Part Time employees enjoy many of these perks too! 
 ALL employees enjoy the following: 
 
 FREE soup/salad daily in Oasis Cafe 
 Retirement plan with company match 
 FREE Flu Shot annually 
 FREE hams/turkeys at Thanksgiving & Christmas 
 Company provided uniforms (if required to wear a uniform) 
 Access to Exercise Room and Pool (limited hours) 
 Employee Assistance Program (EAP) – FREE counselling services 
 Tuition discounts at Rasmussen College, Hodges University and Nova Southeastern University 
 Scholarship program for continuing education 
 FREE Medical Clinic visits – 6 per fiscal year 
 FREE Annual Employee Health Fair 
 Company paid Employee Appreciation events including breakfasts, cookouts, luncheons, and annual holiday party 
 Various discounts to area attractions, gyms, movie theaters & MORE! 
 
 Part Time employees working at least 20hrs/week enjoy all the above PLUS: 
 
 Tuition Reimbursement Assistance 
 Optional AFLAC insurance plans 
 Generous Paid Time Off plan 
 Annual Service Award recognition 
 Participation in the resident Employee Appreciation Fund 
 
 Full Time employees working 30hrs/week or more enjoy all of the above PLUS: 
 
 Robust insurance package: Medical, Dental, Vision, as well as Short and Long Term Disability plans 
 FREE Life Insurance policy
