 Consultant: 
 
 
 Are you a part-qualified accountant looking for an analytical position with future career development? 
 Based in South Birmingham, this role is a permanent full-time position for a part-qualified accountant who is currently working their way through their ACCA or CIMA. 
 You will be reporting into a fully qualified accountant and take a proactive approach management accounting and finance analysis, for branches across the UK. You will have already started your ACCA or CIMA and can be motivated and supported with the rest of your studies by the business. 
 The ideal candidate will have a solid foundation in accounting skills, supported by a natural flair in communication and ability to analyse date. 
 You can expect a salary between £20,000 - £25,000 PA + study package. 
 The Position 
 
 Daily, weekly, and monthly reports 
 Provide analysis 
 Support the forecasting and budgeting 
 Posting of month end journals, accruals, and prepayments 
 Liaising stakeholders 
 Assisting on business projects and queries 
 
 The Candidate 
 
 A part qualified ACCA/ CIMA accountant with at least 2 years Management Accounting experience 
 Experience of preparing and presenting information to management at board meetings 
 Excellent organisation and time management skills, and to be able to work with multiple entities 
 An excellent team player 
 
 Benefits 
 The following benefits package is available for the Finance Analyst role: 
 
 Study package for ACCA/CIMA 
 Salary between £20,000- £25,000 depending on experience 
 Pension 
 Fun environment to work in, excellent office space with mapped out career progression with flexibility on where you see your future career going. 
 
 Process: 
 Interview process will be 2 stage via video interview.
