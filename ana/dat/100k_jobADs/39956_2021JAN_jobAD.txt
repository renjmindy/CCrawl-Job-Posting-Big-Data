 We are a technology start-up in stealth-mode that is led by an experienced founding team with previous IPO, exit and leadership experiences at companies like Amazon, Delivery Hero and Rocket Internet. We are backed by the world's leading venture capitalists and are seeking smart, hands-on and energetic talent to add to our team. 
 We are passionate about disrupting the future of global convenience retail, through on-demand delivery, and are laser-focused on creating a product that is geared towards delivering a lovable experience to our customers. 
 We're currently recruiting our first fully-fledged team and you'll have the opportunity to join us early as a key team member with clear paths for growth. 
 As a New Location Launch Associate, you'll have the unique opportunity to help us launch many of our new locations and warehouses as we grow. 
 Your responsibilities will include: 
 
 Alongside our Head of New Location Launches, you will work within the New Locations team and external contractors to ensure our locations are fitted out correctly 
 You will execute all tasks necessary to launch a new location ranging from managing the project timeline to building up shelves 
 You will be in the field launching our new warehouse 
 You will be on site for the first week after launch to ensure that our culture is reflected 
 You will communicate with many different external stakeholders and across many different internal departments e.g. suppliers, warehouse staff, partners 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 You have some prior experience in a role in a similar environment (food retail is a big plus, but not essential) 
 Strong organization skills - time management, resource planning 
 Effective communicator - able to disseminate ideas in a clear, concise manner to the relevant stakeholders through both verbal and written communication 
 Passion for technology, start-ups and on-demand services 
 Attention to detail while while not sacrificing speed and scalability 
 Hands-on mentality, willing to roll your sleeves up 
 Entrepreneurial mindset, comfortable in highly ambiguous environment and able to independently take responsibility from day one with ability to work with little direction
