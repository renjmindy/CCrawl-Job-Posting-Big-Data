 Excellent opportunity for an experienced and qualified Tax Manager to join a large corporate business based in Coventry - a well-known brand - as their Tax Manager. This is a broad in-house tax role covering VAT and Corporation tax, and other tax projects. 
 The Tax Manager role will be responsible for: 
 
 Managing the company VAT returns (VAT accounting, reporting, & compliance) 
 Responsible for the Corporation Tax (compliance & reporting) 
 Improving the tax and treasury function: maintaining, updating and improving the Group policies and procedures 
 Managing the relationship with the External Tax auditors 
 Tax reporting to the Directors and SMT 
 Transfer Pricing 
 iXBRL submissions 
 Tax risk Management 
 Tax Strategy 
 Intrastat & Income tax returns 
 Other associated projects with regards to tax and treasury 
 
 The successful Tax Manager will be a qualified (CTA) tax advisor, and have ideally 3+ years post qualification experience. This can be straight from within a Professional Services firm, or from within Industry. The successful Tax Manager will have a strong academic background, and have outstanding attention to detail, work to tight deadlines, and a commercial mindset. 
 The following benefits package is available to the successful Tax Manager: 
 Base salary: £60-70,000 (DOE) 
 Car/ Car allowance 
 Up to 20% bonus 
 Contributory pension, healthcare, and other benefits inline with a large corporate business. 
 To apply for the Tax Manager position in Coventry, Warwickshire, or for further information, please apply with your CV at your earliest convenience, or call Philippa Templeton at Gleeson Recruitment Group on 07960 086 368, or email Thank you 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
