 Customer Service Administrative Assistant/Receptionist 
 JOB SUMMARY: 
Reporting to the Community Manager and/or the Assistant Community Manager, the Administrative Assistant is responsible for the day-to-day management of the main reception office including the scheduling, operation and coordination of supplies and services, telecommunications and technology needed to support the company’s business operations. The Administrative Assistant performs a variety of clerical and support functions as may be required by the company. 
 QUALIFICATIONS AND SKILLS: 
High school graduation and successful completion of Office Administration or similar certification 
 Ability to type a minimum of 55 wpm 
 Working knowledge and experience using Microsoft Office; Excel including formulas, Word including formatting, Powerpoint and Outlook 
 Experience in operating and administering an electronic payroll and scheduling program 
 Ability to multi-task, manage time effectively and prioritize work 
 Effective customer service, interpersonal and conflict resolution skills 
 Organizational skills 
 Experience in Home and Community Care an asset 
 Able to anticipate needs and take initiative to complete work in the absence of direct supervision and constant direction
