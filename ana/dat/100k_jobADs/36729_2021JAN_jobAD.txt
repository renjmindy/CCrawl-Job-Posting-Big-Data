 Role overview 
 As a Senior Software Engineer you will help your team build mission-critical applications that improve and automate processes. You will also help your colleagues to grow technically and otherwise.

 In the Platform Experience Model (PXM) team, we are building solutions to continuously provide transparent feedback on performance metrics related to their articles on the Zalando site to our numerous merchants as well as steer partners to attain stellar performance metrics. 
 WHERE YOUR EXPERTISE IS NEEDED
