 Job Description 
 ACCOUNT EXECUTIVE 
Our goal is to connect our clients with great businesses, and we’ve fundamentally changed the way businesses make buying decisions. By taking word of mouth and a transparency to the local market. We believe in helping businesses make smarter and more informed decisions about where to spend their money. 
 As an  Account Executive at Capital Energy Group  , you will help businesses and consumers save money on their energy needs, and add to a healthy bottom line with a consultative approach. But it doesn’t stop there -  Capital Energy Group  provides a one year track for your professional growth, which means you’ll always know where you stand. We outline what you need to be doing every day, month, and quarter to reach the next level. Increased compensation, title, and responsibilities will keep your career moving in the right direction. If you’re looking for a career (not a job), a company that's invested in your personal development, a solid compensation package, and great training, we know just the place. 
 ** 
Task-At-Hand for an Account Executive:** 
 
 Manage your own leads: utilize resources to cultivate new leads and develop/maintain your pipeline 
 Make a high volume of daily sales calls to businesses 
 Drive the full sales cycle to attain new business: make introductory presentation, assess needs through qualifying, work with teams and resources, and close the deal 
 Achieve and consistently exceed monthly sales goals 
 
 Job Requirements 
 What’s Required for Account Executive: 
 
 Resilience and an ability to overcome objections 
 Positive attitude and a drive to win 
 Coachability, openness to feedback, and dedication to consistent self-improvement 
 Demonstrated sales aptitude, with relentlessly high standards (you’re never satisfied with mediocrity) 
 A genuine curiosity about people, and the innate ability to inspire passion in others 
 Bachelor’s Degree or 1 year of relevant experience 
 
 What an Account Executive will Get: 
 
 Competitive, uncapped compensation (First year earnings avg 60k) 
 Uncapped Growth potential- NO GLASS CEILING! 
 Inspirational work environment 
 Personal Mentors to ensure growth and development 
 Monthly team activities
