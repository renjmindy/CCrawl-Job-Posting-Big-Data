 What you’ll be doing... 
 ** 
 We are looking for a passionate, full stack Lead Engineer for our team in San Jose. You will work with a group of engineers to help build high performance, scalable, resilient web applications that are leveraged by teams throughout BlueJeans by Verizon. 
 
 Utilizeexcellent oral and written communication skills. 
 Designing and building large scalable web services. 
 Working with RESTful APIs and common RESTful design patterns. 
 Communicate and work well within a geographically distributed team. 
 
 ** 
 
 What we’re looking for... 
 ** 
 You'll need to have: 
 
 Six or moreyears of product development experience. 
 Bachelor's degree or four or more years ofwork experience. 
 Six or more years of relevant work experience. 
 Experience building scalable web services with RESTful APIs. 
 Experience working in ES5/HTML5/CSS3. 
 Experience withJava and using frameworks such as Spring. 
 Experience in JavaScript based Front-End’s suc React/Backbone/Angular/Ember . 
 
 Even better if you have: 
 
 Bachelors/Master’s degree in Computer Science or related field. 
 Experience in real-time communication products. 
 Understanding of database technologies as well as API cache design and caching technologies. 
 Experience in scaling large high transaction volume web services. 
 Experience in cloud native technologies. 
 Contributions to open source projects.
