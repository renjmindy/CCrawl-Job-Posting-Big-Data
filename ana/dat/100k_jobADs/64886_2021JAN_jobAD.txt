 Career Description 
 An industrial electronics technician maintains and repairs the electronic equipment used in manufacturing facilities, such as PC-based controllers, air conditioning and heating systems, boilers, and conveyors. They may be responsible for installing, inspecting, and improving the efficiency in equipment as well. Required Education - Completion of an apprenticeship or an associate's degree program in industrial electrical technology.
