 Job Duties: 
 1.Meet with class for assigned hours and deliver relevant instruction in lecture and lab settings. 
2.Collaborate with local industry, internal and external partners to determine training needs, and participate in developing and delivering training that meets those needs. 
3.Provide instruction for courses in advanced manufacturing, customized training for business and industry, and training support to other workforce programs as available. 
4.Communicate course expectations to students in a consistent and understandable manner, e.g., by developing course outlines and establishing learning outcomes consistent with college policies. 
5.Maintain accurate course records. 
6.Prepare, review, and update course outlines. 
7.Ensure course content is meeting industry standards. 
8.Assist in maintenance of safe and orderly instructional spaces as needed, including advanced manufacturing lab equipment. Training conducted at Durham Technical Community College and at employer Sites. 
 Minimum Qualifications: 
 Bachelor's Degree in Advanced Manufacturing, Electrical Engineering, or relevant field.
