 Property Expansion Intern 
 About the job 
 You will join a well-funded start-up and get the help to execute Zapp's expansion delivering on our aim to expand across London and the rest of the UK, working directly with the founders, Head of Property Expansion and senior leadership team. 
 What to Expect 
 Zapp is building a network of Satellite Warehouses across London and beyond while leaning on a rider network to deliver customers 'orders. As one of Zapp's first employees you will experience the exciting entrepreneurial roller coaster first hand. 
 Key Responsibilities 
 As Property Intern you will report to the Head of Property Expansion and help scale Zapp's footprint fast. You will: 
 
 Assist with Zapp's expansion into new areas 
 Source suitable new properties to serve as Zapp's Satellite 
 Warehouses, either directly, with external acquisitions consultants, or through working with commercial real estate partners 
 Manage the process of signing new leases, with the support of external teams 
 You will constantly look to refine and increase the efficiency at which Zapp can launch into new locations 
 
 Requirements 
 You are an ambitious individual, believe in Zapp's mission and get excited taking on huge challenges. You are: 
 
 Experience in sourcing and negotiating property acquisitions for a rapidly expanding business. 
 Experience working to tight deadlines with many work-streams, both internal and external 
 Experience in fast paced environments. 
 Comfortable with ambiguity, short deadlines and changing 
 Experienced in building relationships 
 Well organised with  administrational experience.
