 Caregivers Job Abroad 
 Posted on  May 18, 2013  by  caregiver 
 Success International Placement Inc., a Makati based recruitment and placement agency duly licensed by DOLE and the POEA, is currently accepting applications for  Caregivers Job Abroad . The said caregivers job abroad are in Singapore where 50 caregivers job vacancies must be filled with candidates who have these qualifications: 
 
 Female 
 23 to 42 years old 
 At least vocational diploma/short course certificate 
 With at least 2 years experienced in the same field 
 
 If you are qualified and interested to apply for the said Caregivers Job Abroad, proceed ASAP to the office of Success International Placement Inc. and submit your detailed and updated resume. If you have any question regarding the said caregivers job abroad, please call the telephone number listed below. 
 Note  : For manpower pooling only
