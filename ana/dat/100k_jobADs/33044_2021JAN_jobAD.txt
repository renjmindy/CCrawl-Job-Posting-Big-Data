 Craft Commerce Developer REMOTEPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 Pixel & Tonic, the team behind Craft CMS, is looking for an experienced Commerce Developer. The ideal candidate is experienced with Craft Commerce and Craft CMS, can overlap at least 4-5 hours a day with AWST business hours, and has a background in ecommerce development. 
 At Pixel & Tonic we work hard to create tools and software that developers love using and are looking for a developer that enjoys tough development challenges and working both independently and collaboratively. All members of the Pixel & Tonic team participate in customer support and help to shape the future of our products. 
 In this role, you'll have the opportunity to: 
 
 Make a meaningful impact on thousands of customers and developers by continuing to improve Craft Commerce 
 Work on the future of content-powered ecommerce 
 Travel internationally for conferences and team trips 
 Work remotely from anywhere in the world 
 Have access to all the tools and tech you need to work effectively 
 Earn a competitive salary with profit sharing and healthcare benefits 
 
 To succeed in this role you should have the following skills and experience: 
 
 Experience with Craft CMS and Craft Commerce 
 Experience with modern PHP development 
 Experience with the Yii PHP framework. 
 Excellent written and verbal communication skills 
 Ability to communicate effectively with both technical and non-technical customers 
 Ability to analyze complex problems and present solutions 
 Are self-motivated and can work independently 
 Experience with Vue.js a plus 
 Experience with GraphQL and REST API development a plus 
 
 How to Apply 
 If helping shape the future of Craft Commerce sounds like your kind of challenge, we want to hear from you! Instead of your standard resume and cover letter, please send a short write-up addressing your experience, how it pertains to the skills listed above, and why you want to work for Pixel & Tonic, to  [email protected] , with “Craft Commerce Developer” in the subject line. 
 This is a full-time salaried position.
