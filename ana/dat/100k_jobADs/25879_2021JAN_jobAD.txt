 Caregiver Job Vacancies Kuwait 
 Posted on  July 20, 2010  by  caregiver 
 The POEA recently approved a job order allowing a recruitment agency to select qualified applicants to fill Caregiver Job Vacancies Kuwait. The job order for the said caregiver jobs vacancies was approved last July 9 for Best One International Services & Consultancy Inc., a POEA accredited recruitment agency based in Malate, Manila. For the said caregiver job vacancies, a total of 50 applicants will be selected for placement in caregiver work abroad. 
 If you think you are qualified and interested to apply for the said caregiver job vacancies Kuwait visit the office of Best One International Services & Consultancy, Inc. and submit your resume and application. For any inquiries regarding the said caregiver job vacancies Kuwait you can contact the agency’s landline numbers or email address indicated below. 
 Best One International Services & Consultancy, Inc. 
1810-B San Marcelino St., Malate 
Manila 
Tel. No: 3532429 
Fax No: 5212780 
Email Address: bestone@recruitment.com
