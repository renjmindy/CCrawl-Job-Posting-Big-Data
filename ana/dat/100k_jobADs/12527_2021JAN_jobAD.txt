 Sherri Thomas 
 
 Location:  Chandler, Arizona 
 
 How will clients benefit with you as their coach? 
My clients get hired, promoted and some take their career in a whole new direction. 
 What results have clients reported after working with you? 
Career advancement, promotions, changing job roles, industries and professional goals 
 What do clients say about working with you? 
See my client testimonials at:  http://www.careercoaching360.com/testimonials/ 
 What is your coaching philosophy? 
 
 As a PMP certified project manager, career coach and strategist, I'm passionate about everyone being in a meaningful and inspiring career. Professionals who know how to showcase their strengths, talents and accomplishments are the ones getting hired, promoted, and experiencing successful careers. As a personal career coach, I help clients transition quickly and easily into a new career. 
 
 What types of people do you typically coach? 
 
 Project Managers 
 People who are ready to transition into a new career 
 Professionals who need help finding a new job 
 Professionals in Project Management who want to change job roles, industries or create a dual career path 
 
 What is your professional background? 
Sherri Thomas is a Career Strategist, international speaker and best-selling author of "The Bounce Back" and "Career Smart - 5 Steps to a Powerful Personal Brand" which is currently on AMAZON's TOP 10 LIST for personal branding books. 
 She is a thought leader in career development, and regularly sought after by the media for career strategies including the Wall St Journal, TIME Magazine, Smart Money magazine, the New York Daily News and NBC TV Phoenix Channel 12. 
 My bio can be viewed at:  http://www.careercoaching360.com/about/
