 Part time teaching assistant (TA) for Advanced Placement (AP) subjects 
 North America Vocational Education Group (Wedo Education) 
 __Vancouver, BC 
 Apply Now 
 Job Description: 
 WEDO Education, a subsidiary of North America Vocational Education Group (NAVEG), offers among other courses, auxiliary education support in Advanced Placement classes. 
 **Duties & Responsibilities: ** 
 
 Attend regular course meetings and regularly communicate with the instructor about content, supplementary materials, student performance etc.; 
 Hold and monitor an online discussion/ Q&A forum through Google Classroom; 
 Conduct regular tutorial and review sessions as required; 
 Help students individually or in small groups with assignments / test questions and provide academic support 
 Serve as a liaison between instructors, academic team, parents, and students 
 Mark assignments, tests, manage course grades, and provide feedback to students / parents on performance 
 Oversee students’ learning progress and provide sufficient feedback to the instructor to improve the educational product as needed 
 PT Position, two days a week, may include weekends and non-traditional hours 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 Bachelors degree in relevant subjects preferred however we will entertain candidates at the senior undergraduate level ( >Y3) 
 Related work experience with senior high school students; 
 Familiarity with the AP format is an asset; 
 Educational background in any of economics, calculus, chemistry, psychology required. Greater breadth of coverage is an asset; 
 Excellent verbal and written communication skills; 
 Bilingual in English and Chinese; 
 Must possess a patient, compassionate, enthusiastic and positive attitude; 
 A passion for teaching is required. 
 
 Job Type: Part-time
