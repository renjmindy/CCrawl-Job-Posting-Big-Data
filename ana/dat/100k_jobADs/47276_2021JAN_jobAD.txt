 WHO WE ARE 
 The BYP Network was created to 'change the black narrative' by bringing the black professional global community together to solve our own problems through economic empowerment and connectivity. Following recent events, BYP is more relevant than ever and is excellently positioned to bridge the gap between the black professional community and corporations. Today we have over 50,000 members on our network, launched a mobile and web platform, run offline and online events to up-skill the community, have a job board and have worked with 60+ corporate clients including Facebook, Accenture, AirBnb, Nationwide, BT etc. Our vision is to be the go-to platform for black professionals and grow the network to 500,000 members globally in the next 18 months. We are looking for other passionate and determined team members to help us get there! 
 THE ROLE 
 We are looking for a creative SEO Specialist to optimize our content on our website based on analytics and keyword research. You will be responsible for managing all SEO activities such as content strategy, link building and keyword strategy to increase rankings on all major search networks. 
 DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 Optimise content copy and landing pages for SEO ranking 
 Perform ongoing keyword delivery, expansion and optimisation 
 Research and implement SEO recommendations 
 Develop and implement link building strategy with our Corporate and Community partnerships 
 Work with tech team to ensure SEO best practices are properly implemented across our sites and product 
 Work with Corporate Team to drive SEO in content programming 
 Excellent understanding of the principles and the ability run an effective SEO program that enhances the engagement, traffic and relevance of our digital content 
 
 KEY REQUIREMENTS 
 
 3-5 years' experience in carrying out SEO Audits 
 Proven experience in performance marketing, conversion and online customer acquisition 
 Experience in content marketing & conversion rate optimisation 
 Expertise in Google Analytics and other website analytics tools 
 Team spirited & great communicator - you'll be working with both our community and corporate teams 
 Excellent time management and organisational skills
