 Are you an experienced Interim HR & Payroll Shared Services Manager looking for an opportunity to join an organisation at a time of change? Salary £55-59k, based City Centre Birmingham. 
 Reporting into the HR Director, as HR & Payroll Shared Services Manager you will lead the HR Admin and Payroll teams through a time of restructure, implementation of a new HRIS and a full-scale review of processes across the organisation. 
 As an experienced Interim HR & Payroll Shared Services Manager you will be able to demonstrate a track record of leading teams, have developed strong stakeholder management relations as well as developing strong HR and Payroll processes. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
