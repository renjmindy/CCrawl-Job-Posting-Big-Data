 A Financial Services Client of Gleesons is looking for a Senior Project Financial Analyst to join their team on a 12 month FTC. The role has come about as their is a demand for an extra pair of hands within the transformation team due to on going projects. The successful individual will be involved in various projects during their time with the business and need to be able to hit the ground running, so previous experience of project based work is essential. 
 To be considered for the role you must possess the following: 
 
 Qualified Accountant (ACCA, ACA, CIMA or equivalent). They may also look at candidates who are due to sit final exams within the next 12 months 
 Strong projects background 
 Able to use own initiative (the role will be 5 days working from home initially - they are hoping to get back into the office in the new year) 
 Microsoft Dynamics experience (desirable) 
 Financial Services experience (desirable) 
 
 They are looking for the successful individual to start week commencing 5th October 2020. If you are interested please click apply! 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
