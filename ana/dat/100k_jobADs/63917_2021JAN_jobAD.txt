 Ski Instructor - Smithers, BC 
 Hudson Bay Mountain Resort 
 __Canada 
 Apply Now 
 SKI INSTRUCTOR, FULL TIME 
 Reports to:  Inside Operations Manager & Rental Shop Supervisor 
 Hudson Bay Mountain Resort is a community focused ski resort located 20km west of Smithers in beautiful Northern British Columbia. In operation since 1969 Hudson Bay Mountain Resort offers over 300 acres of skiable terrain and over 500 meters of lift accessed vertical, offering a wide variety of terrain, for beginners, novice and advanced skiers and snowboarders. 
 Hudson Bay Mountain is looking for qualified, certified individuals to join our Snow School team for the 2019/2020 season. 
 Instructors must teach safe and fun lessons to a variety of ages and ability levels. They must teach and adhere to the Alpine Responsibility code at all times, maintain a professional attitude and represent HBMR’s values while carrying out their duties. Beyond teaching, instructors are also expected to help in the Rental Shop and Repair Shop, assembling rental equipment for guests before and after lessons, and doing small repairs to rental equipment. They will also be expected to shovel snow as a part of morning duties and help Ski Patrol with sweep at the end of the day. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 
 Arrive with staff van (or other transportation) before mountain operations start, in order to prepare for the day i.e. Shovel, arrange pre-sets, clean rental shop, participate in morning meeting etc. 
 
 
 Attend “check ins”, ready to teach, at Snow School 15 minutes prior to each lesson time 
 
 
 Teach for the full allotted lesson time 
 
 
 Wear proper assigned uniform and nametag at all times 
 
 
 Assist with Kids Kamp, which may include planning of the Kamp, riding the bus with participants, supervising lunch, and participating in arts & crafts 
 
 
 Ensure all guests are aware of the Alpine Responsibility Code and are following it at all times 
 
 
 Report any and all accidents/injuries during lessons to the Inside Operations Manager and Base 1 immediately 
 
 
 Ensure rental forms are filled out properly completely before starting rental process 
 
 
 Ensure Progress Cards are filled out completely and properly at the end of each school group lesson 
 
 
 Assist guests with proper boot fitting and adjustment 
 
 
 Ensure accurate ski/board selection, binding adjustment, pole measurements and helmet sizing 
 
 
 Ensure knowledge of ski/board tune-ups and adjustments 
 
 
 Maintains a good relationship with guests, while being courteous and professional 
 
 
 Instructs guests in the correct use of rental equipment 
 
 
 Notifies Inside Operations Manager and Rental Shop Supervisor if equipment is requiring repair or replacement 
 
 
 Maintains the Snow School Staff room in a tidy and organized manner – washes dishes after they are used 
 
 
 Maintains the Rental Shop and Repair Shop in clean & organized order 
 
 
 Carries out related duties as assigned 
 
 
 Fosters an atmosphere of safety and co-operation, and promotes positive teamwork in the Rental Shop as well as in all other HBMR locations 
 
 
 Qualifications   : 
 
 
 At minimum, a Level 1 instructor's certification as a ski instructor through the Canadian Ski Instructors Alliance (CSIA) 
 Previous experience in ski instruction is preferred; a condition of employment is a criminal background check. 
 Job Type: Full-time, Seasonal 
 Job Types: Full-time, Contract 
 Salary: $14.00 to $16.00 /hour 
 Experience: 
 
 Snow School: 1 year (Preferred) 
 
 Licence: 
 
 CSIA Level 1 (Required) 
 
 Language: 
 
 English (Preferred)
