 A UK based PLC in Birmingham who are going through an exciting change to improve their processes, are on the lookout for a Business Intelligence Analyst to join them on a Fixed Term Contract until the end of Dec 2020. 
 Hoping to improve the way they consume real time data, the Business Intelligence Analyst will be responsible for the creation of Power BI reports, work as part of a scrum environment and encourage the uptake of self-service reporting. 
 In order to be successful in the role you will have: 
 
 Advanced experience in creating Power BI Report and analysis 
 Ability to create metrics and data sets using DAX 
 Background of working in an agile environment and understanding of the Azure data platform 
 Track record of testing proposed BI releases 
 
 Please apply here with your CV. 
 Interviews will be taking place over video as soon as. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
