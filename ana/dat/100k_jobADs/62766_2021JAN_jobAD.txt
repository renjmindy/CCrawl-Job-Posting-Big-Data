 Careers advice - Munich Employment Agency 
 
 January 14, 2021 
 No Comments 
 
 Career advisor and advisor for rehabilitation and participation at the Munich Employment Agency 
 
 Who are we addressing? 
 
 Our offer is aimed at all pupils 
 
 
 Our range of services for you 
 
 Orientation about professional opportunities and questions of career choice 
 personal career advice in one-on-one interviews 
 Clarification of individual skills and suitability for certain professions 
 Development of career alternatives 
 Advice on funding opportunities 
 
 
 Contact 
 
 I am there for you in all questions of career choice. 
 Office hours (brief information): 
by arrangement 
 Detailed consultation appointments: 
by appointment either through your teacher or by email or service hotline (free of charge) 
 Postal address: 
SFZ Munich South 
Boschetsrieder Str. 35 
81379 Munich
