 Care Assistant Jobs Canada 
 Posted on  June 16, 2011  by  caregiver 
 A leading POEA accredited recruitment agency that is committed to finding only the best-qualified candidates for their clients' job requirements is in search of Care Assistants who are qualified for  Care Assistant Jobs Canada . This care assistant jobs Canada was approved for recruitment by the POEA last June 10, 2011 for the said recruitment agency whose office is located in Malate, Manila. 
 If you are interested in applying for the said Care Assistant Jobs Canada, you may proceed directly to the office of Industrial Personnel and Management Services Inc. (IPAMS) and submit your resume or you may visit their website and fill up their Online Application. If you have questions regarding the said care assistant jobs Canada, you may ask Industrial Personnel and Management Services Inc. by way of their telephone numbers or email address listed below.
