 Customer Service Representative 
 Apply for the position, Customer Service Representative, with Cross Manufacturing, Inc. today or contact Keshia for more information. Posted Jan 1, 2021. 
  Apply Online  __    913-433-7777  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 Overview: 
 With over 70 years experience, Cross Manufacturing, Inc. exists to glorify God by helping feed the world physically and spiritually.  We design, manufacture, and market hydraulic components to meet needs worldwide.  Our Core Values are to do the right thing, live within our means, and be dependable.  Cross is seeking a  Customer Service Representative  at our Lewis or Hays location. 
 
 Job Responsibilities: 
 The Customer Service Representative processes customer orders promptly and accurately.  This position communicates frequently and professionally with customers to ensure consistent customer satisfaction.  This position also coordinates with internal team members to ensure customer needs are met. 
 Requirements: 
 A successful candidate has excellent communication skills, is detail-minded, and works well as part of a team. 
 Desired Qualifications: 
 ·  Prior customer service experience preferred 
 ·  Strong computer skills, including Microsoft Office 
 ·  Farm, agriculture, hydraulic or equivalent related knowledge is a plus 
 
 Compensation and Benefits: 
 In return for these requirements and responsibilities, Cross offers competitive compensation and an excellent benefits package. 
 
 Application Procedure: 
 Cross Manufacturing, Inc. utilizes the Culture Index Survey, a wordlist which measures employee and applicant work-related temperaments and behaviors. We are planning on using this information to better understand, recruit, manage, and motivate our employees at all levels of the organization. Please complete this 5-7 minute survey in order to be considered -  https://www.cultureindex.com/c/3C8072 
 Send cover letter and resume to  [email protected] . Please reference the listing by job title. 
 
 Post-offer drug testing is required. 
 Cross is an equal opportunity employer.
