 Registered Nurse (RGN, RMN or RNLD) 
 A new job opportunity has arisen for a committed RGN, RMN or RNLD to work in an exceptional care home based in the Woodlands, Doncaster area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This care home provides luxury care accommodation, supported by a service that supports an independent lifestyle. 
 The team is experienced in residential and nursing provision, while also being trained to specialise in dementia care 
 As a Nurse your key duties include: 
 
 
     Provide personal care and support with reference to individual care plans
 
 
 
     To provide care through the night, checking residents at regular intervals according to their care plan
 
 
 
     To respond promptly to calls for assistance while respecting
 
 
 
     To engage in conversations to help maintain residents' sense of identity; upholding dignity at all times
 
 
 
     Assessing and planning nursing care requirements
 
 
 
     Monitoring and administering medications
 
 
 
     Taking residents samples, pulses, temperatures and blood pressures
 
 
 
     Writing records
 
 
 
     Supervising junior staff as well as managing the unit
 
 
 
     Providing emotional support to residents and relatives
 
 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Team Player
 
 
 
     Ability to communicate effectively at all levels
 
 
 
     Ability to work on own initiative
 
 
 
     Must be trained to administrate medication
 
 
 
     Must have Dementia Experience
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent salary of £17.00 per hour and the annual salary for this position is £29,172 per annum. 
 We currently have vacancies for both days and night shifts available. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Holiday entitlement
 
 
 
     Uniform provided
 
 
 
     Free car park
 
 
 
     Career progression
 
 
 
     Pension scheme
 
 
 
     Flexible working opportunities
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse either RGN, RMN or RNLD with a valid NMC Pin
