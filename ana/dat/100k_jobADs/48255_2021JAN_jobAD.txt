 Brezaa is smart intelligence interests focused relationship app on IPhone and Android from London, which aims to make meeting people less awkward and more fun, through true or false personalised icebreakers and interests matching with smart intelligence algorithms. As we are more than just a picture, we have received great five star reviews and building a great team. 
 Where we 're at: 
 
 Current Team 16-20, teams of IOS, backend / front end, UI.US design, marketing, admin, motion deigns 
 App available on IOS and Android with almost 10k downloads 
 Social Media, Web & App teams 
 Investment from founder to date

 Media Coverage

 Investor pitch deck ready
