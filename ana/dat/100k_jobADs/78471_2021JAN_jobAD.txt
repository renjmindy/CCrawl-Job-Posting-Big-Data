 Aurora Cannabis 
 __Edmonton, AB

 LOGISTICS DATA SPECIALIST 
 Aurora is seeking a Logistics Data Specialist. With operations excellence and continuous improvement as our end goal, this role will work within our Logistics - Medical Fulfillment team. This position can be located in the Greater Toronto Area or Edmonton area. Work from home considerations are available during the COVID-19 pandemic. 
 MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES: 
Collect, prepare and publish various types of data for reporting, using several platforms per a defined schedule 
Develop and maintain BI reporting, dashboards and visual formats 
Develop policies and procedures for the collection, analysis and storage of data 
Provide in-depth data collection and analysis as required by the business 
Network with business subject matter experts to fully understand the business mechanics that generate the data 
Identify/understand relevant datasets/tools/platforms to support the best practice use for analytics 
Own and continuously improve launched analytical tools/models 
Facilitate Stakeholder Management expectations and agreed objectives 
Liaise with 3rd party operations and corporate areas regarding data inputs/outputs 
 QUALIFICATIONS: 
Minimum 3 years’ experience in Business Intelligence/Analytics, data gathering and reporting 
Experience in Inventory Management, Order Fulfillment 
Ability to respond to dynamic and rapidly changing environment 
Comfortable working independently with minimum supervision 
Experience at responding to complex/ stressful situations and resolving issues positively 
Able to follow and uphold standard operating procedures precisely and consistently 
Strong documentation skills and attention to detail 
Computers and technology oriented 
Proficient with Microsoft Office (Outlook, Excel) 
Excellent communication skills (English both written and verbal)
