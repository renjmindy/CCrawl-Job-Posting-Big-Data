 Installation Worker / Rigger

 Rigger 
 
 
 Immediate Start 
 
 
 On-going Work 
 
 
 Weekly Pay 
 
 
 Competitive Pay Rate with Allowances 
 
 
 About Our Client 
 Our client is involved the installation of technologically advanced racking systems for large logistical distribution centres for world-renowned multi-national brands and chains all over the world. 
 About The Role 
 Our client is an international client working on the multi-million dollar project in Redbank Plains â Distribution Centre. They will be installing the racking systems and inhouse steel construction (mezzanine floors). 
 There will be a site supervisor on the floor that will provide training to the workers. We are looking for a solid crew that can remain on the project from start to finish for 12 months and onwards as so required. There are career opportunities with this client as they have potential for further work here in Australia including the next distribution centre in Sydney. 
 They are willing to train their workers and are also open to offering rare opportunities to travel with them to the next project where all expenses will be paid for and also permanent opportunities with this world renowned global company. 
 Skills & Requirements 
 
 
 Own transport and Licence 
 
 
 Working at heights ticket (you will at times be working 20m in the air) - there is a height allowance for 15m and above 
 
 
 EWP Over 11 metres (high risk licence) 
 
 
 Construction White/Blue Card 
 
 
 High risk working environment experience 
 
 
 Good attitude and willingness to work 
 
 
 Desirable Skills but NOT Mandatory â will be highly regarded 
 
 
 Racking system background or similar experience 
 
 
 Warehouse experience with installing
