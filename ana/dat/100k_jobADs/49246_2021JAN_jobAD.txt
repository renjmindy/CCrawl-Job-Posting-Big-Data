 Factory Hand 
 Job description 
 We are currently looking for 2 Factory Hands for MBL, Wingfield and the details are as follows: 
 About the role: 
Comfortable to operate production lines that process raw poultry, pig, goat, beef, etc offal 
Fast-paced, multi-shift rendering plant, with possible overtime and Saturdays work 
Operating equipment and machinery 
Monitor raw materials delivery 
Fill in paperwork 
Other relevant duties as required 
 The right person for the role: 
Ability to work with raw material that produces strong smells. 
Experience in the meat or poultry manufacturing industry. 
Focus on the safe working environment 
A forklift license is mandatory 
A boiler ticket would be an advantage 
 Allowances: Shift Allow if worked on afternoon and night shift 15% of the hourly rate, Dirt Money, etc. 
 Working hours: Rotating shift every fortnightly - day, afternoon, and night, 5 days work - Mon to Fri, 2 days off - Sat & Sun.
