 Regal 
 
 Workway Regal is an organization founded to create permanent relationships between businesses and candidates. 
 Workway Regal was created specifically to provided Direct Hire and Executive Search services within the areas of Administration, Human Resources, Legal, Marketing and Operations across a variety of industries. One of our key competitive advantages revolves around the long-term relationships we’ve developed with companies, many of which have been in place for more than a decade. The deep knowledge we gain by knowing our clients so well allows us to provide much stronger matches between candidates and companies. These matches create working relationships that generate much more beneficial relationships between businesses and individuals. 
 With over 20 years of experience, our Executive Recruitment Team takes the time to gain a thorough understanding of your company and its values, making us a natural extension of your business. We personally meet with every individual we recruit, which assures you receive only the highest quality candidates that also fit your company’s culture. 
 You can expect our recruiting process to: 
 
 Reduce the length of time your position stays open 
 Cut financial costs associated with the recruiting process 
 Improve the quality of the candidates you interview by providing custom testing and background checks 
 Lead to longer working relationships between your company and the candidate 
 Make your company a much stronger competitor within its industry
