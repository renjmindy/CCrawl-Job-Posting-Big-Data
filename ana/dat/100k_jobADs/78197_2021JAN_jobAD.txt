 Career Cruising 
 There's no magic formula for choosing a college or deciding on a future career, but exploring your options in Career Cruising is a great place to begin. Career Cruising is a complete college and career planning program that is FREE for all Sterling Jr. & Sr. High School students. 
 
 Explore colleges and careers 
 Take career assessments 
 FREE SAT/ACT test prep 
 Search and apply for scholarships 
 Create a resume, record community service hours, leadership positions, awards received, and more! 
 
 Students, if you forget your username and password, be sure to to see Mrs. Wohler and she can remind you what it is. Parents also have access to Career Cruising. If you would like access to the Parent Portal, please contact Mrs. Wohler at wohlert@usd376.com.
