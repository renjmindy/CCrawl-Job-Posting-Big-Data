 We are currently seeking a positive, energetic, skilled graduate/individual with either strong graphic design skills to join our exemplary team. You will be joining us at an incredibly exciting time for the company and will be working alongside the Chief Marketing Officer to support our marketing activities. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Creating ideas, concepts and high-resolution designs 
 Producing branded character illustrations 
 Creating Icons (illustrator / SVG's) 
 Developing branding identity and company branding guidelines 
 Design company graphics and social media assets such as infographics and content images 
 Maintain internal style guides, branding guides, image repositories and design systems 
 Design advertising material such as PPC banner ads 
 Design for print including merchandise, flyers, brochures and other marketing materials 
 Creating visual materials for events. 
 
 Skills & Qualities 
 
 Advanced use of Photoshop and Illustrator 
 Ability to come up with creative concepts and designs for new website looks, branding and graphic assets 
 Creative and artistic, with a keen visual eye and exciting ideas 
 Excellent interpersonal skills with ability to communicate effectively at all levels 
 Ability to comprehend and uphold brand style guidelines, producing 
 work of a suitable style 
 Ability to solve an array of challenges through visual design 
 Must be able to mediate creative problems to arrive at the best solution 
 Manage time effectively, ability to multi-task and meet deadlines 
 Self starter with the ability to work independently and collaborate with a team 
 Professional demeanor with high degree of comfort speaking with internal clients

 Why Join Wrangleapp.io 
 --------------------------------------- 
 - You will be part of a company that will disrupt an entire industry 
 - We provide a fun and entrepreneurial working environment within an exciting high-growth startup business, where you can use your talent to make a real difference. 
 - This is a rare opportunity to build a truly global product from the ground up, and join early a startup that will soon be a growing business of exceptional engineers working on the latest technologies. 
 - You will gain first-hand experience in building content / assets for an AI-powered mobile application and delivering it to the end users. 
 - You will work side-by-side with the CMO who will offer crucial mentor ship. 
 - After funding, we will be offering a very competitive remuneration package, flexible hours, generous holiday allowance, regular social events and many other perks we continue adding as the company grows.
