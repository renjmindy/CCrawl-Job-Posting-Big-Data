 Gleeson Finance are working in partnership with this prestigious business in Warwick, to recruit a Finance Analyst. Reporting into the Senior Manager, you will support all aspects of commercial reporting for the department. We are looking for a part qualified, adaptable and a highly motivated self-starter who is capable and comfortable when working in a cross-functional project environment. 
 You will take ownership of the budgets and forecasts, monthly internal resource planning, review and analysis, business planning, cost centre control, preparation of monthly management information and accounts, lead the preparation and presentation of financials to senior management, lead and coordinate cross-functional teams to resolve key financial risks or issues and challenge operating interfaces to help achieve the most cost-efficient results. 
 In order to be considered, you will be an ambitious individual, educated to degree level and be studying towards a professional accountancy qualification, with strong Excel and previous experience within project and commercial accounting. 
 In return, you will receive a salary of £38,000 plus outstanding benefits, including private medical cover, 26 days annual leave + statutory bank holidays, 12:30 Friday finish, bonus based on individual performance, free parking, generous pension scheme and employee discounts, including lifestyle benefits. 
 If you are interested in hearing more, please call Sophie Evans, Gleeson Recruitment, on 07812 079718, or apply direct. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
