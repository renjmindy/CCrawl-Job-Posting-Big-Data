 About Greenleaf Lawson Architects 
 Greenleaf Lawson Architects is a full service Architectural Firm, located in Mandeville, Louisiana. Specializing in commercial work, and working exclusively in 3-Dimensional Software. Greenleaf Lawson Architects is currently licensed in Louisiana, Mississippi, Texas, Alabama and Florida. As a firm, Greenleaf Lawson Architects prides ourselves on innovation, collaboration and integration. Our services include full use of Building Information Modeling (BIM) throughout the design, production and construction phases of all projects. This tool is the backbone of our services. TECHNOLOGY IS OUR TOOL, BUT "PEOPLE-CENTRIC" DESIGN IS OUR SERVICE. TECHNOLOGY CANNOT REPLACE THE CREATIVE, INSPIRED SOLUTIONS WE PROVIDE UNIQUELY TO EACH CLIENT.  Our Purpose: We build a collaborative, multi-faceted team that balances strong client-centered relationships with leading edge technology to design inspired solutions from concept to completion. Our Values: IMPACT:  We strive to have a positive and lasting impact on our clients, users of our designs, employees and the community.  EXCEED EXPECTATIONS:  We work in partnership with our clients to identify their unique needs and create a finished product that will delight them exceeding their expectations.  INNOVATION:  We continuously strive to remain on the forefront of our profession with our ideas, strategies, solutions, tools and way of thinking.  DEDICATION:  We are committed to seeing our creations from concept to completion, taking ownership of our work and delivering our clients the highest quality, most cost-efficient product. FRESH:  We explore all possibilities to give our clients creative, new and impactful designs that will reflect the culture of the client and become an attractive beacon in the community.   COLLABORATION:   We believe that the creative process is stronger as a team, and the end result yields the most success when approached collaboratively.
