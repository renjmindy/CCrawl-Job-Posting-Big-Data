 Are you a commercial expert seeking a new role as Senior Quantity Surveyor in the Oxfordshire area? Does managing cost on large and complex residential schemes come naturally to you? 
 We are working with a leading high-end residential developer to identify a Senior Quantity Surveyor to manage a low-rise residential scheme in the South Oxfordshire area. This exciting and unique scheme will continue for at least 5 years. 
 This is a great opportunity for a bright, committed and personable Senior Quantity Surveyor to join a growing business who are part of the most successful group in their arena. Managing the commercial function and specific packages on a large and long-term development you will facilitate delivery and cost effective completion of the scheme through to the sale of the properties. 
 As the successful Senior Quantity Surveyor you will be an excellent communicator, a team player and be great at managing relationships. You will possess business awareness and be able to demonstrate successfully undertaking your role in the past with a stable track record. 
 On offer to the successful candidate is an excellent salary, car allowance and bonus scheme. To find out more please contact stephenbrandsma@workwithglee to set up a call. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
