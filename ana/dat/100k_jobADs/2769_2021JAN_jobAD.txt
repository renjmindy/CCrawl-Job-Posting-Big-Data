 Consultant: 
 
 
 My client is looking to grow their Business Support Functions and looking for a Commercial Support Associate to join their expanding team! You will be reporting in to UK Head of Customer Services and working closely across all functions within their Head Office, as well as field-based representatives. 
 You will be responsible in managing the business processes and taking accountability to amend and elevate the efficiency of existing processes. 
 The ideal candidate will have the following attributes: 
 
 Strong Business Administration - you will be collating and producing reports in line with company KPI's and supporting the wider team with ad hoc complex tasks utilising the Microsoft Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) 
 Strong written and verbal skills - the ability to communicate effectively with both internal and external stakeholders is essential 
 Work proactively in a fast-paced environment, remaining calm when attending to customer's requests 
 Flexible and personable - ability to get the job done and be versatile, supporting in all areas of the business as and when required. 
 
 For more information, please contact Jane Spearman directly on 0121 752 3822 and send a copy of your CV today. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
