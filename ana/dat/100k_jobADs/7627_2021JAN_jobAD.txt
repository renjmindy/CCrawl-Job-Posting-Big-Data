 We are a technology start-up in stealth-mode that is led by an experienced founding team with previous IPO, exit and leadership experiences at companies like Amazon, Delivery Hero and Rocket Internet. We are backed by the world's leading venture capitalists and are seeking smart, hands-on and energetic talent to add to our team. 
 We are passionate about disrupting the future of global convenience retail, through on-demand delivery, and are laser-focused on creating a product that is geared towards delivering a lovable experience to our customers. 
 We're currently recruiting our first fully-fledged team and you'll have the opportunity to join us early as a key team member with clear paths for growth. As our Admin & Office Manager you'll have the unique opportunity to work alongside an experienced C-Suite and work in a fast-paced, early-stage start-up environment. You will work directly with our Founder & CEO. 
 Your responsibilities will include: 
 
 
 Make sure office is always equipped with all the needs of the team 
 
 
 Help executives to arrange their daily schedule, setting up meetings, travel, speaking engagements and ensuring they follow their agenda 
 
 
 Manage information for internal and external communication including memos, emails, presentations, reports 
 
 
 Supervise the performance of clerical and administrative staff 
 
 
 Receive and direct phone calls and distribute correspondence 
 
 
 Perform office management operations by keeping up with office supply inventory 
 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 
 3+ years' experience of working on an Executive Assistant or Senior Executive Assistant position 
 
 
 Profound experience with office management systems and procedures, as well as major office gadgets and applications 
 
 
 Outstanding MS Office knowledge and English proficiency 
 
 
 Strong organisational and time management skills, 
 
 
 Strong verbal and written communications skills 
 
 
 Confidentiality and discretion 
 
 
 High School degree; PA diploma or certification will be a bonus 
 
 
 Hands-on mentality, willing to roll your sleeves up 
 
 
 Entrepreneurial mindset, comfortable in highly ambiguous environment and able to independently take responsibility from day one with ability to work with little direction 
 
 
 Available to start in-person in London as as soon as possible
