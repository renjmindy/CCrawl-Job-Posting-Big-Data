 Are you an experienced Commercial / Sales Manager / Key Account Manager within the automotive sector seeking an opportunity to join a true global organisation who supplies some of the key vehicle components and systems to a large number of global OEM clients? 
 Are you able to lead commercial accounts and projects within a challenging customer schedule and liaise both internally with stakeholders across the EMEA region as well as with customer engineering and manufacturing teams at various sites? 
 This role will require both drive and tenacity as well as commercial acumen and attention to detail where both the business and its customers demand a right first time approach. As such, you will be expected to deliver against both business commercial targets as well as customer satisfaction and the role will give both challenge and future opportunity within this dynamic and large organisation where career development can extend across the UK as well as globally for the right person. 
 If you wish to be considered for this role, you must have that automotive commercial / sales / account management experience coupled with working knowledge of JLR systems, commercials and processes in order to deliver against this challenging role. You will be able to work as part of a strong team and at the same time have the drive and self motivation to take ownership of your individual accounts and projects. 
 To learn more about this exciting and challenging opportunity with unparalleled career potential, please get in touch with Matt Harris for a confidential discussion. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
