 Formwork (Carpenter) 
 Job description 
 
 
 
 About the Role Our Client who is a specialist in Concrete and Formwork across commercial projects is currently seeking Formworkers/Carpenters for various Northside based projects. This role will involve various duties such as construction of footings, boxing out for cross over drains and associated concrete works. Immediate availability is key What is on offer - Competitive pay rate including penalties On going work Safety conscious workplaces with high quality standards To be considered for the role you must - Be a Trade Qualified Formwork Carpenter Hold White Card, Drivers Licence and reliable transport Have an up to date work history with two recent references About the Company WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors.
