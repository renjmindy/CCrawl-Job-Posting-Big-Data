 Do you want to take your career to the next step with an industry leader that also genuinely cares about their employees? My client is seeking talented, experienced and driven Java Developers to join their industry leading and award winning organisation.  As a Java Developer you will join a rapidly expanding Software Engineering Team. Your focus will be on to effectively and efficiently design and develop software applications to the high quality across various project types, from digital security solutions to software that is out of this world. You will be given the opportunity to develop many new technical skills and must be ambitious and keen to grow your skill set as well have the ability to work in a fast paced environment.

 Experience with high volume data processing systems 
 Hands-on experience with messaging platforms (Ultramessaging (LBM), Solace, JMS)

 Hands-on experience with Unix/Linux
