 Registered General Nurse 
 An amazing job opportunity has arisen for a motivated RGN to work in an exceptional care home based in the Clydebank, Scotland area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This special care home has a team full of professionals that provide palliative care, Parkinson's nursing, Dementia nursing and convalescence to very high standards to their patients. 
 Each house provides a safe and secure environment where residents can feel completely at home. 
 The team of nurses and carer assistants take time to get to know every resident; tailoring the care they provide to suit each individual 
 As a Nurse your day to day duties will include: 
 
 
     Leading, supervising and training the care team
 
 
 
     General Nursing duties-Wound care/general observation /blood sugar level observation /liaising with other healthcare professionals to give the best care.
 
 
 
     Being responsible for ordering, storing and administering medication
 
 
 
     Keeping precise, up to date records
 
 
 
     Maintaining the client dignity and respect at all times
 
 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have the following skills as they are beneficial to this position: 
 
 Able to work under pressure and prioritise workload 
 The ability to lead a team, analyse problems and propose solutions or improvements 
 Able to communicate effectively and to develop professional relationships 
 Understands and effectively uses IT and communication systems required for the role 
 Experience of participating in quality and clinical governance programmes 
 Able to show a can-do attitude always 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will be offered an excellent salary of £15.90 per hour and the annual salary is £39,686.40 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role for 48 hours a week working throughout Night Shifts. 
 In return for your commitment and dedication you will be offered the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Free NMC pin renewal
 
 
 
     Competitive pay rates
 
 
 
     Free PVG check and uniform
 
 
 
     Refer a friend
 
 
 
     Paid breaks
 
 
 
     Skilled training with a qualified Nurse and fantastic opportunities for career development.
 
 
 
     Robust Continuing Professional Development
