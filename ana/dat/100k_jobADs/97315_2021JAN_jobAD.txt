 Description 
 Hi! I run a startup in Tokyo which tries to create media about introducing Japanese culture in English. 
 We need writer who can consistantly work with us. 
 For selection, would you like to write sample article so that we can discuss further? 
*Please be aware that sample article won't be compensated! 
 【Who we are looking for】 
- Native or fluent in English 
- Living/Has lived/Visited for more than year in Japan 
- (Ideally) can read Japanese to collect information about Japan online 
 【Compensation】 
6 articles(excluding sample) $30 total 
 【Amount of work】 
More than 400 word with at least 7 photo 
Specification will be provided with guideline which is attached in this post. 
 *Please note that If article is not following the guideline, we cannot compensate for your work.

 ▼People who would fit this job 
・Interested in Japanese culture 
・Creative individuals who write regularly 
・Earn living by using writing skill 
・Interested in building a network with people living in Japan 
 *You don't necesarry have to live in Japan to do this job. 
 ▼Theme of article 
Food/Sight Seeing/Hotel/Culture 
 ▼Reference article 
・Top 10 recommended Onsen (Hot springs) with great nature in Japan 
・12 boutiques fashionistas from all over the world is visiting 
・7 Sushi-go-round places with English menu in Ueno 
・7 Otaku shops Japanese anime Otaku recommends

Please write one sample article. PLEASE FOLLOW THE GUIDELINE ATTACHED or you will lose the right to apply for this job.
