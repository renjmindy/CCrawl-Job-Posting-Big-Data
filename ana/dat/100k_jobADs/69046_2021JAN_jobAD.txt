 Egg Harbor Township School District

 About Insight: 
 Insight is one of the fastest growing education staffing companies in the country. Our company staffs substitute teachers, paraprofessionals, clerical staff, non-instructional aides, and more in over 60 school districts. If you are looking for a fast-paced job that fits your schedule, visit  www.workwithinsight.com/apply  now to start your online application. 
 Job Description: 
 Insight is looking for a Long-Term Substitute Special Education Teacher to work in the Egg Harbor Township School District. The special education teacher is responsible for providing oversight to Special Education students while their primary teacher is absent. This assignment will be from March 30 - June 30, 2020. Pay for this position will be $135/day. 
 Job Requirements: 
 
 Works with Special Education students that experience emotional, learning, and/or physical disabilities 
 Knowledge of the developmental levels of students in the classroom and the different ways these students learn 
 Ability to develop well-structured lessons with challenging, measurable objectives and appropriate student engagement strategies, pacing, sequence, activities, materials, resources, technologies, and grouping 
 Ability to organize and analyze results from a variety of assessments to determine progress toward intended outcomes and uses findings to adjust practice and identify and/or implement appropriate differentiated interventions and enhancements for all students 
 Maintains accurate, complete, and correct records as required by law, district policy, and administrative regulations 
 
 Job Qualifications: 
 
 CE/CEAS/Standard in Special Education 
 Candidates must already have or must be willing to undergo a criminal history check (fingerprinting) through the Department of Education.
