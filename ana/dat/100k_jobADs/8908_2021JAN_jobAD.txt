 CREDIT CONTROLLER - Birmingham Business Park - TEMP TO PERM - 23-25Kpa 
 Exciting opportunity for experienced Credit Controller for a Temp to Perm role in a successful business based near the Birmingham Business Park. We are looking for an experienced, enthusiastic and energetic candidate who can take ownership of the Ledger and improve/increase collections. 
 You will have full responsibility for the credit function including: 
 
 
 Pro actively collecting cash from live accounts 
 
 
 Cash allocation 
 
 
 Raising Credit Note approvals 
 
 
 Bad debt reporting 
 
 
 Taking ownership and responsibility for accounts 
 
 
 Daily sales ledger control and reconciliation. 
 
 
 Sales statements. 
 
 
 All aspects of credit control, releasing orders, chasing outstanding accounts 
 
 
 Investigate and resolve queries in a timely manner 
 
 
 To maintain customer accounts and credit limits in line with credit control policy. 
 
 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
