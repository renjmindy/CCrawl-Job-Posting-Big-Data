 Job Description 
 UPS' Automotive Department's mission is to maintain a safe, and reliable fleet to provide excellent customer service. UPS maintains a world-class maintenance and repair program, recognized throughout the automotive industry. UPS is widely recognized as an employer of choice for technicians and body repair professionals. 
 UPS operates an internal body shop that provides collision repair and complete paint and detailing for the UPS fleet. The body shop also completes special projects related to UPS vehicles used for display or public relations. 
 Responsibilities and Duties 
 
 ****Performs collision repair, welding and fabrication in both steel and aluminum 
 Repairs doors, latches, and other trim 
 Sands, preps and paints delivery vehicles 
 Details and applies decals to finished paint jobs 
 
 Knowledge and Skills 
 
 Collision repair 
 Panel replacement 
 Welding and cutting 
 Paint preparation 
 Completes paint and detail process 
 Skilled in welding aluminum and fabrication - Preferred
