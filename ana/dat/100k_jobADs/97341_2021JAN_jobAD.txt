 Caregivers Jobs Hiring 
 Posted on  December 4, 2010  by  caregiver 
 A recruitment agency that’s been deploying Filipino workers worldwide since 2000 is currently looking for caregivers who are qualified for  Caregivers Jobs Hiring . These caregivers jobs hiring is for the holy land of Israel and was approved for recruitment by the POEA last November 30, 2010 for a POEA accredited recruitment agency based in Quezon City. For the said caregivers jobs hiring, 27 applicants will be given the opportunity to work in caregivers jobs abroad. 
 If you think you have the qualifications for the said Caregivers Jobs Hiring, submit your updated resume together with your application directly to JAO International Manpower Services. For more details or inquiries on the said caregivers jobs hiring, you may contact JAO International through their landline numbers provided below. 
 JAO International Manpower Services 
Unit E, 3rd Floor, Jacinto Building 
380 Scout Reyes corner Quezon Avenue 
Paligsahan, Quezon City 
Tel Nos.: (632) 774-0995 / (632) 332 4234 
Email address: admin@jaointernational.com 
Website: http://jaointernational.com
