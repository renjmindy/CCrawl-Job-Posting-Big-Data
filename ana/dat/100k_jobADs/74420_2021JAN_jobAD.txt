 Electrical Trades Assistant 
 Job description 
 Trades Assistant 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Yeppoon Based Project 
 
 
 New project, potential to move on to the next stage 
 
 
 Competitive rate of pay and ASAP start 
 
 
 About the Client 
 Our client is a Leading Electrical Contractor with projects across Commercial, Construction and Industrial areas. 
 About the Role 
 You will be a versatile Trades Assistant looking to work alongside a positive and hard working Electrical team. Upcoming vacancies for a large commercial construction project in Yeppoon commencing late November until April 2021. You will be assisting in cable running, lighting and power installation, testing and working within a small Electrical Crew. 
 Ideally you will be familiar with large scale commercial construction projects and have experience working with Electricians. You will need a strong eye for detail and the ability to work to deadlines and managed work schedules. 
 This is for Local Candidates only. No accommodation provided. 
 Skills & Experience/Qualifications 
 
 
 EWP, High Risk ??? highly desirable 
 
 
 White/Blue card 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Commercial Construction experience ideal with cable haul experience. Minimum, construction experience and must be local to Yeppoon 
 
 
 Available to start Immediately 
 
 
 Culture & Benefits 
 
 
 Competitive pay rate 
 
 
 Potential to move to the next stage of the project 
 
 
 Safety and Team driven environment 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
