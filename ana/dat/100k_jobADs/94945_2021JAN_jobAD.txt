 Gas Engineer 
 We have an excellent opportunity for an experienced Gas Engineer to join our team in Stockton on Tees. 
 We have an established department covering repairs and maintenance to domestic property. 
 We are looking for an experienced engineer to cover installations, servicing and maintenance of gas boilers, fires, central heating and other domestic aspects throughout the Tees Valley region. 
 This is a fantastic opportunity to secure sociable, full time hours Monday to Friday. 
 You will be working a standard 37 hour week with potential to take overtime. 
 An immediate start is also available. 
 You will need; 
 , Domestic Gas Qualifications (CCN1, HTR1, etc) 
, Gas Safe Registered 
, Full UK Driving Licence 
, Previous Experience (3+ years) 
 Please contact Lewis on 01772 208 967 for more information. 
 To apply for this role please email your CV to lewis.jackson@servicecare.org.uk
