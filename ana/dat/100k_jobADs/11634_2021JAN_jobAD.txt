 Caregiver Jobs Abroad 
 Posted on  October 30, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This caregiver jobs abroad opportunity is being offered to qualified Filipino caregivers who are seeking employment in caregiver jobs abroad. The Philippine Overseas Employment Administration or POEA recently approved a job order allowing a Quezon City based recruitment agency to recruit qualified candidates for caregiver jobs abroad. This caregiver jobs abroad is for the country of Israel and the job order was approved by the POEA last October 22, 2009. The said job order has a corresponding job order balance of 30 available caregiver jobs abroad. 
 To apply for the said caregiver jobs abroad, all interested applicants should proceed directly to the recruiting agency and bring their most recent resume to the agency’s office address stated below. For further info, you can contact the recruiting agency for the said caregiver jobs abroad through their telephone number and Email address indicated below.
