 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs Bahrain 
 Posted on  March 1, 2012  by  caregiver 
 Mari Human Resources Inc., a POEA accredited recruitment agency located in Ermita in Manila, is looking for caregivers for placement in their  POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs Bahrain . These POEA approved caregiver jobs Bahrain was approved for recruitment last February 21, 2012 with corresponding vacancies for 5 caregivers who will qualify for the said caregiver work abroad. 
 If you wish to apply for the said POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs Bahrain, you may submit your resume personally to the office of Mari Human Resources Inc. or you may submit them via email to marihumanresources@hotmail.com. If you have questions regarding the said POEA approved caregiver jobs Bahrain, you can ask them directly to Mari Human Resources Inc. by way their landline number specified below.
