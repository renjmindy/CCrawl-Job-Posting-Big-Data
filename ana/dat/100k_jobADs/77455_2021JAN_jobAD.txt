 Human Resources Manager 
 Dreaming Rainbow 
 __Stony Plain, AB 
 Apply Now 
 Supports Through Dreaming Rainbow (STDR)  has existed since 2014 as a non-profit organization that supports and creates opportunities of empowerment for individual, children and young adults, with disabilities across  Stony Plain and St. Alberta, Alberta . 
 We believe and deliver Person-centered, Trauma-informed, Strength-based, Trust-based intervention as our best practices when supporting individuals who experience barriers to opportunity and access. 
 Human Resources Manager Description: 
As an addition to the Agency’s Workforce Development and Management (WDM) Department, this full-time role will support the recruitment, on-boarding and scheduling process of every STDR Personnel. 
 Hours of Work: 20-25 hours/week. 
 The Human Resources Manager will be expected to have, and be able to: 
 Maintain Employee Relations and   Life cycle 
 
 
 Communicate professionally and respectfully with Individuals and their families we support, STDR Employees, and community representatives; 
 
 
 Utilize recruitment, screening and assessment tools to conduct pre-employment screening: schedule interviews via cold calls, reference checks; 
 
 
 Schedule and coordinate employment contractual sign-ups; 
 
 
 Manage filing, data entry/tracking via Social Solutions (ETO Software/Apricot) and maintaining Employee, Program and Agency files; 
 
 
 Support in maintaining staff schedules and bridge gaps in day-to-day challenges; 
 
 
 Collaborate with WDM Personnel and Director to develop recruitment strategies: seek partnerships with recruiters and other platforms to recruit new hires; 
 
 
 Support WDM Department processes: investigations, performance enrichment and progressive disciplinary actions, performance evaluations and exit interviews. 
 
 
 Ensure Agency compliance with Provincial and Federal Legislation 
 
 Strong awareness and application of Employment Standards, Worker’s Compensation Board (WCB), Occupational Health and Safety (OH&S), and Alberta Human Rights Code; 
 Ability to work independently as well as in a team environment; 
 Maintain Confidentiality and Privacy of Individuals, STDR Personnel and sensitive matters; 
 Adhere to policies, procedures and commit to values and philosophies of STDR; 
 Report to Workforce Development and Management (WDM) Management and Executive Management. 
 
 Provide Organizational Support 
 
 Develops recruitment and retention strategies; 
 Schedule and support in the preparation for meetings and appointments, interviews and training; 
 Maintain Employee and Agency Databases; 
 
 Participate in ongoing learning to improve our agency’s best practices; 
 
 Identifying opportunities of improvement in policy and procedure, system and overall practice; 
 Support the maintenance and organization of the main office; 
 Adherence and maintenance of Health and Safety of the main office; 
 Support the management of Agency Social Media pages. 
 
 Position Requirements and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Administrative Tasks 
 
 
 Recruitment and On-boarding Management 
 
 
 Organizational Duties and Support 
 
 
 Social Media Management 
 
 
 Qualities: flexible, able to work independently and within a team, ability to work in a fast-paced environment, open to change and eager to learn 
 
 
 The Human Resource Manager will be offered: 
 
 Opportunities to grow within the agency; 
 A diverse team of Employees from various backgrounds and experiences; 
 Various In-house Agency training opportunities: 
 SIVA (trauma-informed); Abuse Prevention; Equity, Diversity and Inclusion; WHIMS; FOIP; Policy and Procedures; Documentation 
 Health Benefits and Self-care days upon 6 months of employment and more! 
 
 Qualifications 
 
 Over 2 years of Human Resources experience; 
 Human Resources Post-secondary Diploma or Degree is preferred; 
 Human Services and Social Sciences, Community and Disability Support Services; 
 Strong knowledge of Microsoft Office; ETO Software/Social Solutions (Apricot) would be an asset; 
 Bilingual (English and French) is ideal; 
 Clear Criminal Record and Vulnerable Sector Intervention Check; 
 Class 5 Driver Licence and willing to obtain 2-million-dollar insurance; 
 First Aid & CPR is preferred. 
 
 Ideal Experience 
 
 2-5 years or more of Administrative and Human Resource (HR) experience is required; 
 Strong understanding of Employment Standards, Occupational Health and Safety (OH&S) standards as well as PIPA and FOIP; 
 Strong communication skills, both verbal and written is required; 
 Knowledge of working in the disability field and with high-risk behaviours is preferred; 
 Experience scheduling and working with Social Solutions and Scheduling is an asset.
