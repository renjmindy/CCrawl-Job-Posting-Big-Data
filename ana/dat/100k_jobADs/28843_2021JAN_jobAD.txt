 Spanish, Portuguese, German, Italian phone call translation services 
 Published on the November 12, 2019 in Writing & Translation 
 
 Project 
 Project Insights New 
 
 About this project 
 writing-translation / translation 
 USD 50 - 100 
 We wish to hire freelancers who speak English and at least 2 or more of the below languages: 
Spanish, Portuguese, German, Italian, French. 
The work is simple: we will provide you with phone conversations of average duration of 3-6 mins. You will have to translate the conversation in English. 
Should be a simple task as all the phone calls will have almost similar conversation. We expect the volume to be 50 to 100 calls each month depending on the languages covered by freelancers. 
Each phone call translation will be paid flat USD 2 and should be delivered in 24 hours.
