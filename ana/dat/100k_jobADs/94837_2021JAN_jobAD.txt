 Administrator 
 Job description 
 
 
 Start mid December 
 
 
 Potential for full time work 
 
 
 Residential to Newman 
 
 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking an experienced Administrator. 
 This role will initially be a relief position covering aspects of reception, administration, pay roll and mobilisation duties. 
 The relief work will be available from mid-December running through to March 2021 with the possibility to then obtain a full time payroll position. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 The successful candidate will be responsible for, however not limited to the below duties: 
 
 
 General administration 
 
 
 Payroll 
 
 
 Basic flee scheduling 
 
 
 Mobilisation duties 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Previous experience in a similar role 
 
 
 WA C Class Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Ability to pass a Drug and Alcohol Screen 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels.
