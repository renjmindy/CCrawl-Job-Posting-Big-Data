 CNC Machinist - 5 axis Mill-Turn 
 Van der Graaf Inc. 
 __Brampton, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Van der Graaf Inc. is seeking exceptional talent to join our team! We are looking for an experienced CNC Mill Machinist with at least 2 - 3 years of 5-Axis mill-turn experience. 
 Job Summary 
 
 Set up and operate 3, 4, and/or 5 Axis CNC Mill machines with an emphasis on 5 Axis machining. 
 Completes required work consistent with customer’s blueprint and specifications. 
 Inspects work for conformance to specification. 
 Completes all paperwork accurately and keeps work area clean. 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 Minimum of 2 - 3 years successful experience setting up and operating 5-Axis CNC Mill Machines 
 Extensive knowledge of Mill tooling. 
 Knowledge of Mazak and Fanuc control 
 Ability to read and understand blueprints. 
 Proficiency with various materials including steel and stainless steel. 
 Strong problem-solving skills and good mathematical skills. 
 Strong understanding of GD&T. 
 
 Job Type: Full-time
