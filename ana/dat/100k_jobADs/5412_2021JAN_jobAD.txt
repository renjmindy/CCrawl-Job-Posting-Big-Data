 Are you looking to develop a career in procurement? Do you have strong analytical skills and a proactive, self-starting attitude? If yes, I have a Procurement Analyst role that could be perfect for you. 
 Based just outside of Birmingham city centre this Procurement Analyst role will have responsibility for proactively lead tender preparation & process, encompassing identify business requirements of supplier, prepare tender documentation & timings, initial sourcing of quotes/pricing & contract management. You will also run reports on PSL spend and analysis and challenge on suppliers used outside of the PSL . You will also report and analyse supplier rebates and support SLA performance management and reporting. 
 In order to carry out this role, you will need the following skills and experience: 
 
 Excellent attention to detail 
 Analytical thinker with excellent problem solving ability 
 Intermediate to Advanced excel skills 
 Self motivated and an excellent communicator 
 
 This is a newly created role where you will have the opportunity to take on more responsibility and mould the role into your own. You will receive a salary of £20,000 to £25,000 plus CIPS study support and opportunity for progression. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
