 Caregiver Job Hiring 
 Posted on  May 24, 2012  by  caregiver 
 JAO International Manpower Services, thru the POEA approval of its Job Order, is now accepting caregiver job applicants for placement in their POEA approved  Caregiver Job Hiring . The said caregiver job hiring is for employers in the holy land of Israel and was made possible thru the approval of its Job Order last May 17, 2012 with openings for 7 applicants who will qualify for the said caregiver work abroad. 
 Applicants who wish to apply for the said Caregiver Job Hiring are advised to proceed as soon as possible to the office of JAO International Manpower Services and personally submit their comprehensive resume. If you have any question regarding the said caregiver job hiring, please refer them to JAO International Manpower Services by calling their telephone numbers provided below.
