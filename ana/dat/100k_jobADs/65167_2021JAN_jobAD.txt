 Group Financial Accountant: £45,000, Walsall 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently recruiting a fantastic opportunity for a Financial Accountant to join a Group Finance function. Reporting to the Group Financial Controller you'll be responsible for reporting and managing consolidated group financial performance 
 Duties & Accountabilities: Group Financial Accountant: Walsall 
 
 Production of timely, accurate and reliable monthly Board reports setting out relevant financial information and insight. 
 Setting timelines and formats for business FDs and their finance teams to provide information to ensure group reporting is delivered on time 
 Involvement in the production of statutory accounts for multiple legal entities 
 Production of accurate, reliable, and timely monthly P&L's, balance sheets and cash flow statements 
 Production of Group reports that inform business leaders & Exec 
 Working alongside the broader Group Finance team to ensure financial performance is appropriately communicated to the board of external stakeholders 
 Analysing and checking data to ensure it is accurate and reliable and spotting issues with underlying data to be investigated and resolved 
 Producing ad hoc reporting and analysis as required 
 Recommending and implementing improvements in Group's financial controlling and reporting environment 
 
 About You: Group Financial Accountant: Walsall 
 You'll be an ACA or ACCA qualified accountant who ideally trained within a top 10 accountancy firm. You will have relevant IFRS experience and strong technical accounting knowledge. Demonstrating credibility, you'll have the ability to build relationships and communicate with confidence and challenge constructively. Organised, with great attention to detail you'll be comfortable working independently while making a team contribution. This truly is a great opportunity for a first time mover to join a top rated group function in a role that offers not only good exposure but also progression opportunities. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
