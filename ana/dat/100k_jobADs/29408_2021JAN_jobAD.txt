 Food Services Supervisor 
  Rady Children's Hospital   |  San Diego, CA 
 Posted Date 11/20/2020 
 Description 
 JOB SUMMARY: 
 The Supervisor, Food Services provides leadership and overall direction for RCHSD retail and catering food services operations and programs. Oversee retail, catering, production operations and plans, administers and manages all unit activities related to retail and catering operations, cash management, staffing levels and reporting processes complying with RCHSD and regulatory agency standards. 
 Assists in the successful operation and customer service of the Food Services Department within a hospital setting. Helps ensure client/customer service and satisfaction with efficient cost effective management meeting and exceeding stated expectations. Assists in the responsibility for all foodservice-related activities and be primarily involved in patient meal assembly and delivery; including quality improvement, sanitation, infection control and all facility-related activities. 
 MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS: 
 Bachelor's Degree 
 5 years of experience 
 ServSafe Certificate 
 PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS: 
 Master's Degree 
 8 years of experience 
 Dietary Manager Certificate 
 OTHER: 
 Registered Dietitian a plus
