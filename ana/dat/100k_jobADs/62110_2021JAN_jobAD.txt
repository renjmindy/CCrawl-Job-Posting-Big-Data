 New work at home Job Listings 
 Published by  aw1219  on October 17, 2017 
 We may earn money or products from some of the companies mentioned in this post. 
 Today, I have posted a list of companies hiring people to work out of their home offices that you can apply to today. There are additional work at home job leads listed on the weekly job section or  Friday freebie pages . Please make sure that you apply to any job(s) that you may be interested in as soon as possible before they get filled. 
 If you do not have any work experience, please be aware that there are a lot of employers that will provide you with online training as long as you have the skills and access to a computer or internet connection. Don't forget to check back again for additional work at home jobs at  Workersonboard.com . 
 Work at home Job Leads 
 Customer Support Rep 
 Kraken is seeking remote workers to respond to clients via email and live chat. You must be detail oriented, enjoy helping others, hard working and can create excel formulas or execute simple scripts. 
 Voice Over Artist 
 Filmless is looking for individuals to help them record professional voice overs for their clients from their home studio. You must have 5 years experience and be able to perform under tight deadlines. 
 Customer Service 
 Sykes is hiring reps to answer questions, process transactions, and resolve their customers issues. Agents must have 1 year customer service experience, be empathetic, and can speak on the phone and use a computer at the same time. Benefits provided. 
 Customer Support/Sales 
 Lock Depot, Inc. is seeking someone to handle all customer service needs and requests including but not limited to processing orders received by mail, phone, fax, email and chat. You will also enter customer orders, research through manufacturers websites and other related duties. You must have a newer computer and USB headset. Pays $12 to $15 per hour. 
 Transcription 
 
 Net Transcripts is seeking individuals who can transcribe audio content of conferences, seminars, meetings, etc. You must be able to type 80+ wpm with excellent grammar and proofreading skills. Fast internet is strongly recommended. You must also be able to start working immediately. 
 Infomercial Sales 
 Nex Rep is seeking remote agents to answer calls for Cooper Chef and take orders. There is no high-pressure sales tactics involved. You will read from a prepared script to give product information and special promotions. You must have good telephone and closing skills. You will work 10 hours a week earning up to $20 per hour. 
 Ibotta Care Agent 
 Ibotta,(cash back app company) is seeking applicants who live in the Colorado area to analyze and respond to email inquiries from Ibotta users. You will also moderate receipts, work on special projects and be their advocate. You must own or have knowledge of Apple or Android devices, be friendly and have strong problem-solving skills. Pays $12 per hour. 
 Seasonal Customer Service Reps 
 MAI has immediate openings for customer service reps to provide assistance on a wide variety of products. You must have effective communication and PC skills, good attendance and can work 20 hours a week. Pays $10 per hour. 
 Home Agents 
 
 Teleperformance is seeking professionals to frontline technical and customer support for iOS devices and carrier related issues. You will also be responsible for all customer inquires, questions and issues. This position is available immediately. Pays $13 per hour with benefits. 
 Inbound Tech Support 
 Kelly Connect is seeking remote workers who have good customer service skills, enjoys technology, can handle a lot of tasks and is comfortable interacting online via chats, instant messaging and more to work from home. Pay starts at $12 to $14 per hour during training. 
 Survey Savvy 
 Get paid cash via check in the mail to complete online surveys and participate in new market research studies. You will get paid additional cash monthly to download their app. Must be at least 14. 
 Feature Points 
 
 Get free cash or gift cards to download free apps. You must keep them installed for at least 2 minutes. 
 Lazy Rewards 
 Tired of paying tons of money for mobile data? With Lazy Rewards you can easily recharge your phone for FREE.
