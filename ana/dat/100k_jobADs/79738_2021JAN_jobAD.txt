 Cloud MSP Engineer 
 Onix Networking Corp 
 __Toronto, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Summary:  The Managed Services Team helps customers transform and evolve their business using cloud computing systems. As a Cloud MSP Engineer on the Managed Services team in this rapidly evolving business, you will work with established customers to ensure the successful operation of their Cloud services 
 Scope/Level of Decision Making:  This is an exempt position operating under limited decision-making and supervision. Position performs a variety of assigned activities, referring more complex issues to the manager. 
 Primary Responsibilities: 
Research, diagnose, troubleshoot, and identify solutions to resolve client issues 
Document findings and produce walkthrough documentation where applicable 
Review and respond to remote monitoring and management (RMM) system alerts and notifications 
Escalate critical technical issues and support tickets when appropriate 
Remotely support, manage, maintain, and troubleshoot items like networks, hardware, software, and other related peripherals 
Upgrade OS and 3rd party software packages including security and system patching 
Maintain and monitor system backups and Disaster Recovery (DR) environments and procedures 
Utilize the following (and future) toolsets, applications, and cloud platforms: 
G Suite 
Kaseya VSA 
FreshService 
FreshDesk 
PagerDuty 
Google Cloud Platform (GCP) 
AWS (Amazon Web Services) 
 Required Skills and Experience: 
Previous experience in a managed services environment or similar fieldwork preferred 
Ability to proactively monitor and maintain production environments with a focus on IT environmental health 
Excellent documentation skills, including the ability to track, log and document customer interactions and follow-up procedures and to document findings and create walkthrough documentation 
Excellent time management skills and ability to work independently in a remote or home-office environment 
Understanding and familiarity with remote troubleshooting tools and methods 
Minimum of 3 years experience in a technical support/network-admin role supporting: 
Windows Servers (2008, 2008R2, 2012, 2012R2, 2016 and 2019) 
Linux (Ubuntu, CentOS, Red Hat, SUSE, Debian) 
Windows Active Directory, Domain Controllers and Group Policy. 
Microsoft Server Applications such as Exchange, SQL, Sharepoint, IIS etc. 
DNS, DHCP, FTP, SNMP, SMTP, IP, SSH, Telnet, NTP, SMB, WINS, HTTP and HTTPS protocols and services 
Understanding of VMware and Hyper-V and other virtualized environments 
Knowledge of programming/scripting languages; Python, JavaScript, PHP etc. 
Task automation and configuration management utilities such as, Windows Powershell and Linux bash commands 
Experience with 3rd party software installation and support 
Working knowledge and remediation of Antivirus and Anti-Malware software and infections 
Variety of Enterprise Firewalls, routers, switches and VPN appliances such as Checkpoint, PaloAlto, Cisco or SonicWall 
Variety of Backup and DR services such as Actifio, Veritas, Commvault 
Strong network and infrastructure architecture, troubleshooting and support 
Excellent time management, communication, and customer service skills 
Must demonstrate a security-minded focus and execution of all related MSP tasks and responsibilities. 
Ability and willingness to be on-call 
 Preferred Skills and Experience: 
Google Certification - Professional Cloud Security Engineer, Professional Cloud Network Engineer 
2+ years working with support teams and using ticketing systems 
 Education:  Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Data Science or a related discipline preferred, but not required 
 Travel Expectation:  15% Domestic. 5% International 
 Salary Range:  $50,000 - $80,000 USD
