 About this project 
 it-programming / web-design 
 Over USD 3,000 
 Heepsy 
We´re Heepsy, a leading high growth Influencer Marketing platform based in Bilbao (Spain). We help more than 1,000 clients build influencer marketing campaigns globally. At the core of our technology lies an influencer search and analytics engine capable of identifying and categorizing millions of social media accounts using complex infrastructure and algorithms. 
 We´re looking for 
We´re seeking motivated and talented individuals to help us improve our product and launch new exciting features that our clients demand. 
 In particular, you will lead the frontend development in an already launched and successful product developed with highest development standards and practices along with a team of highly talented individuals comprising product management, ux, back-end, data engineering, ai and quality assurance. 
 You should apply if 
- You care about software excellence 
- You understand we move fast 
- You can demonstrate a good understanding of web technologies 
- You enjoy challenges across the entire stack 
- You are pragmatic in your approach to writing software (e.g. You take context into consideration, and don’t follow rules blindly) 
- You enjoy working with others 
- You embrace agile team culture and put new ideas and expertise on the table 
- Embrace both the uncertainty and the advantages of a startup working environment 
 Requirements 
- 5 years of proven experience in frontend 
- 3 years of proven experience in modern JavaScript frontend frameworks (knowledge in React will be preferred) 
- Knowledge of JavaScript development tool belts ( git, yarn or npm, babel, webpack, etc.) 
- Experience in Unit & Integration testing and experience with JavaScript testing frameworks such as Jest (or equivalents) 
- Capable of sharing examples of apps you´ve worked in 
 It helps if you have 
- Bachelor degree in computer science or equivalent 
- Knowledge of the whole development stack 
- Experience in a start-up 
- Experience in working as part of an Agile product team 
- Fluent in either English or Spanish 
 We offer 
- Eur 25-35k a year 
- Opportunities for training and development 
- Company-sponsored courses, conference passes & technical books 
- Remote friendly environment, work from anywhere 
- Flexible working schedule 
- Onboard a growing project in a crucial stage of development 
- Work with top talent 
- Exceptional good work atmosphere 
- A friendly, humble and talented group with 10+ different nationalities 
- English, French & Spanish courses
