 Human Resources Intern TFT (NOHFC) 
 St Joseph's Care Group 
 __Thunder Bay, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Bargaining Unit:  NON-UNION 
 Status:  Full Time TEMPORARY 
 Department:  Human Resources 
 Site:  St. Joseph's Hospital 
 Shifts:  Days 
 The Human Resources Intern will work closely with the Coordinator Recruitment & Retention, focusing on a variety of activities that will allow the Intern to apply skills acquired through their recent education in a professional setting and gain a better understanding of the role of human resources professionals in a large health care organization. 
 Qualifications: 
THE IDEAL CANDIDATE 
 In addition to being a recent graduate from a business/commerce degree or diploma program with specialization in human resources and/or marketing, the HR Intern will need to be customer and client focused, have a positive attitude, be collaborative, and be creative and innovative. 
 Northern Ontario Heritage Fund requirements: 
University or college graduate who has graduated within the last three years from an accredited college or university. The candidate must be a graduate of a post-secondary degree or diploma program. The position will be a first full-time employment in the candidate's field of study.
