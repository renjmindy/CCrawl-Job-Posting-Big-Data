 Gleeson Recruitment Group are working in partnership with a leading professional services organisation to recruit for an experienced temporary Credit Controller to join their busy accountancy team based in Redditch. 
 Client Details 
 This is a leading global organisation with a prestigious portfolio of key account clients... 
 Reporting into the Credit Control Manager your duties will include: 
 
 Ensure that customer invoices are paid in a timely manner and debtor days are minimised 
 Accounts Reconciliations 
 Meet individual KPI targets as set by the department 
 Maintain own personal portfolio of accounts 
 Build and maintain internal and external relationships 
 Daily/monthly reporting and account reconciliation as required 
 Escalate problem accounts to senior management 
 
 The main requirements include: 
 
 Previous credit control experience is essential 
 Exposure to dealing with large ledgers over a million 
 Strong team player 
 First class communication skills 
 Organised with strong attention to detail 
 
 If you have the required skill set and available on a short notice period, please apply! 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
