 Gleeson Finance are working in partnership with this well-known corporate business in Warwickshire to recruit a Payroll Administrator. Reporting into the Payroll Team Leader, we are looking for a passionate and committed Administrator to support the team to ensure that people get paid accurately and on time. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Provide a professional and responsive payroll service 
 Ensure all work is produced and validated in a timely manner 
 Processing new starter paperwork 
 Following leaver processes to ensure final payments and paperwork is complete 
 Collating and processing SSP 
 Resolving any discrepancies in data and calculations 
 General administrative duties in the department 
 Preparing and processing weekly payroll 
 
 In order to be considered, you will have excellent attention to detail, a good level of Excel knowledge (VLookUp, Pivot tables), and experience of the payroll process, and be a driven and enthusiastic individual. 
 In return, you will receive a salary of £23,000 plus a competitive benefits package including life insurance, pension, flexible working, free on-site parking and lifestyle benefits. 
 If you want to know more, please call Sophie Evans, Gleeson Recruitment, on 07812 079718, or apply direct. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
