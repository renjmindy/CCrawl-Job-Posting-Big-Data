 Daily Duties: 
Based in central Norwich, the ward is a 16 bedded unit for both males and females experiencing acute mental health problems. 
 These service users are deemed to be experiencing acute mental illness and their risks cannot be managed safely in the community. 
 They ward will provide assessment and treatment services for acute mental disorders and rehabilitation including: 
, Schizophrenia 
, Depression 
, Acute Anxiety 
, Personality Disorder 
, Bipolar Disorder 
 You will be working on the Acute Adult ward, to identify the most appropriate treatment and recovery plans and support service users through their journey to return back to the community. 
 The ward benefits from the dedicated input of an Acute Psychiatric Consultant and a full multi-disciplinary team (Occupational Therapists, Complimentary Therapists, Pharmacist and Psychologist). 
 Working alongside the nursing team they have support workers and administrators and receive regular input from the home treatment team. 
 Working as part of the multi-disciplinary team, team working skills are essential. 
 Person specification: 
The applicant must be DBS cleared and have previous experience in the field.
