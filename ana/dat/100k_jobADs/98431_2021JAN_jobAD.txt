 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently partnering with an exclusive Treasury Manager position to join a role as Treasury Manager, the treasury manager is based in Walsall. . This role offers fantastic support & development as well as the ability to build a career 
 
 Responsibilities: Treasury manager, £50,000-£60,000, Walsall 
 
 Manage all cash management functions including cash position, transfers, account funding. Ensure adequate liquidity for working capital needs. 
 Manage and forecast Cash and Funding activities. 
 Responsible for Holding company bank accounts. 
 Month end treasury duties. 
 Manage inter-company cash, including inter-company lending, dividends, and capital injections. 
 Implement a hedging strategy and policy across the Division 
 Monitor the performance of the key financial controls across the group, ensure these are adhered to and managed effectively, and identify/follow up exceptions as appropriate. 
 Support the Financial Controller in creating, maintaining and enforcing policies, procedures and governance to meet compliance requirements 
 Drive continuous improvement in financial reporting processes aimed at increasing the level of standardisation, efficiency and accuracy of the financial reporting. 
 Liaise with external auditors to ensure an efficient audit process, as well as proper and timely resolution of the audit issues identified. 
 Work as part of the Divisional Finance team to ensure a co-ordinated approach. 
 Provide monthly and ad-hoc analysis to support the Senior Management Team in their roles. 
 
 Qualifications and Experience:   Treasury manager, £50,000-£60,000, Walsall 
 
 ACA/CIMA/ACCA qualification or treasury equivalent; 
 Treasury experience, 2+ years post qualification; 
 Experience in implementing foreign exchange hedging strategy and policies; 
 Experience in analysing, understanding and improving internal financial controls; 
 Excellent knowledge and practice of enhancing internal processes; 
 Engaging communicator demonstrating influencing skills and an ability to adapt approaches to differing audiences; 
 Highly results-orientated with demonstrable success, achieving and exceeding challenging targets both personally and through others, providing explanations and proposed resolutions to problems rather than purely identifying problems 
 Well organised, with the ability to multi-task, prioritise and manage competing demands; 
 Continuously involved in pursuing high quality standards, embracing changes, recognising improvement opportunities and learning from experience; 
 Strategic thinker, creative and flexible, with ability to present concepts in a clear and simple way; 
 Proficient IT skills including MS Word, MS Excel and MS PowerPoint, and comfortable with numerical reasoning.
