 Live In Caregiver Jobs in Cyprus 
 Posted on  November 5, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This is good news to all Filipino caregivers who are looking for live in caregiver jobs abroad. Last November 4, 2009, the POEA or Philippine Overseas Employment Administration approved a job order allowing a Philippine based recruitment agency to recruit qualified Filipino applicants for Live In Caregiver Jobs for the country of Cyprus. The said live in caregiver jobs has a corresponding job order balance of 100 live in caregiver jobs. 
 To apply for the said POEA approved live in caregiver jobs, all interested applicants should submit their application and comprehensive resume to the office of the recruiting agency. The agency’s office address and contact infos are all available upon request to anyone who is interested in applying for the said POEA approved live in caregiver jobs in Cyprus.
