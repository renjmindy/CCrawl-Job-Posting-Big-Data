 Are you immediately available? Do you enjoy reconciling accounts? 
 I am looking for a Reconciliations Clerk to join my client based in Wolverhampton for a period of 6 months, to assist with complex reconciliations over a busy period. You can expect an hourly rate between £10 - £11ph. 
 Reconciliation Clerk duties: 
 
 Reconciliation of assigned accounts (daily), ensuring all banking has been accurately recorded 
 Multi- currency reconciliations 
 Processing journals for the cash team to reconcile and ensure posts align with purchase ledger and sales ledger 
 Produce weekly banking reports showing performance of all business units 
 
 To be considered for this Reconciliations Clerk opportunity you will have: 
 
 Previous bank reconciliation experience 
 High attention to detail 
 No notice/ able to start immediately 
 
 To apply for the Reconciliation Clerk position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
