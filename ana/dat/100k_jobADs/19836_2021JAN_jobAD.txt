 | 
 Alan De Back 
 Location:  Washington, DC Area 
 How will clients benefit with you as their coach? 
Clients find that I do an excellent job of carefully listening to their career issues, and then helping guide them towards their own plans of action. 
 What results have clients reported after working with you? 
The biggest result that clients report is success in their job search. They also report that they are more aware of their strengths, and how those strengths relate to their career progression. 
 What do clients say about working with you? 
Comments I’ve been give include that I’m an excellent listener, knowledgeable, and results oriented. 
 What are 5 strengths you have as a coach? 
 
 Listening skills 
 Compassion 
 Perceptive 
 Knowledgeable of Federal government 
 Knowledgeable of local business environment 
 
 What is your coaching philosophy? 
Totally client centered – helping clients determine their own plans of action and solutions. 
 What types of people do you typically coach? 
I have coached a wide variety of individuals, but typically college-educated professionals. 
 What is your professional background? 
My professional experience includes non-profit, government, corporate, and small start-up. My background has provided me the ability to understand a wide variety of work environments.
