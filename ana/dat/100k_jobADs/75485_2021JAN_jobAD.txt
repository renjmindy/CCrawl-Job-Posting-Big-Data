 Look At This! it's a Freelance job 
 Here's the deal: Red Bull Media House is looking for a Freelance Mid-Sr. Product Designer, UX/UI to design across Mobile, TV, Web, AR/VR, in our Los Angeles, CA, USA office. Be a team contributor as much as you are a self-starter. Not afraid of the unknown? Able to work on simultaneous projects at a time? Come help shape and grow our current design systems while having immediate impact on global products. 
 About 
 
 Three words to describe this job are  Versatile, Fast-Paced, Multi-platform 
 This is a great opportunity because  you can have a global impact on a product across a variety of platforms. Your input and voice important and valued. 
 The ideal personality for this role is  a self-starter, contributor and collaborator with a love for teamwork the expertise to go from concept to prototype to completion. 
 You need to be well-equipped to handle  juggling simultaneous initiatives and responsibilities while enhancing current design systems. 
 You 'd be working on  Mobile apps, TV apps, AR/VR experiences, some Web related topics, Design Systems 
 The best way to describe our culture is  open-minded and experimental 
 You 'll definitely hate this job if  you need structure and constant hand holding.

 Fine Print 
 • Comfortable working outside of a traditional design role on projects small or large (from concepts to presentations to all mobile platforms) 
• Systems thinking and previous experience with design system teams a plus. 
• Technical Design - ability to code or skim the front-end (a plus, but not a requirement) 
• Strong communication skills, both written and verbal. 
• A passion and enthusiasm about quality and attention to detail. 
• Advanced knowledge in Sketch (Figma a plus) 
• Designing user interfaces across a variety of devices and operating systems (iOS and Android) mobile, tablets, desktop (Wearables and connected TV a plus) 
• Creating and conceptualizing wireframes and interactive prototypes 
• Comfortable with creating presentations (Powerpoint, Keynote, or equivalent) 
• Ability to communicate, explain, present your design to the team and others
