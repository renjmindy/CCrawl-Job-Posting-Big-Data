 27-May-2018 "Win Any Job Interview Smartly! - Interview Skills Session @ Essa Lab, Gulshan 
 An interview skills session was organized at Essa lab on 27th May 2018 
 The clip of this session can be seen  here . 
 If you want to organize this session in your organization, educational institute, college, school, NGO, call me at  0335-  6924700 
 We learned 
How to be presentable in a job interview 
How to give your first best impression on the interviewing person or panel 
How normal interview differs from the panel interview and vice versa. 
What are the common misconceptions about a job interview? 
What are the common mistakes a person make and how to avoid it? 
How to be confident in a job interview 
What are the commonly asked questions and how to answer them? 
Mock Interview in the end
