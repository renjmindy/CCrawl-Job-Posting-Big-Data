 An exciting, newly created role working for a retail business based in Staffordshire. My client is looking to recruit a Digital Content, Social and Loyalty Executive. 
 The Digital Content, Social and Loyalty Executive will report directly into the Head of Marketing and play a key role in the development and deployment of marketing campaigns, with a particular focus on web content, social media and email marketing. 
 The role will encompass: 
 
 Support the wider marketing team in the development annual marketing plan 
 Management of all social media activity, from idea generation to content creation, deployment, monitoring and reporting 
 Support the overall CRM plan and lead on all email marketing activity. 
 Develop, working closely with the design team, digital content for a range on online platforms 
 Social and web performance reporting 
 
 The ideal candidate for this role will possess the following skills/expertise: 
 
 A positive, customer-centric approach to marketing 
 A track record of delivering effective social media campaigns - from a strategic and tactical perspective 
 Extensive email marketing experience 
 Demonstrable experience of managing end-to-end marketing campaigns across acquisition, engagement and retention 
 A background within retail would be preferable 
 
 Highly competitive package - role based in Lichfield.
