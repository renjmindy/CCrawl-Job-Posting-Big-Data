 As a building surveyor you have a great opportunity to work for a business who are passionate about their brand and what they represent, My client is looking for an experienced Building Surveyor who has worked in a client side role before either in retail, hospitality or leisure if you feel this could be of interest to you then read on; 
 We are working with an exciting business who own a large portfolio of properties across the UK in an industry that is performing very well, this business has a great company culture as well as being one of if not the best at what they do in a highly competitive Leisure industry! 
 This business is highly acquisitive and are looking to grow further over the next year therefore they seek to find a building surveyor who will get involved in the Acquisitions Process to make sure the buildings are to standard and "are fit for purpose" so the project works can be agreed. You will play a key part in the fit out and refurbishment process as well therefore CAPEX experience is ideal you will also ideally hold a MRICS status although this is not essential. 
 On offer to the successful candidate is a highly competitive salary of up to £60,000 with the potential to go further for right individual as well as an attractive benefits scheme. 
 To apply for this Building Surveyor opportunity please click the "apply now" button and your CV will be sent directly to the Zac Doherty. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however, please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
