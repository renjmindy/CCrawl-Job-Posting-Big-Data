 About this role 
 Hurree is seeking out a Sales Manager with the ability to close deals to join our team. They are responsible for successfully selling the Hurree value proposition to leads. 
 In this role, you'll get to: 
 
 Close new business consistently at or above quota level 
 Become an expert in marketing platforms 
 Serve as a brand enthusiast for Hurree's platform 
 Follow up on highly qualified leads 
 Work collaboratively with marketing, business development and product development departments to execute sales 
 Bring your thinking, strategies and ideas to advance our company's values, unique culture and vision for the future 
 
 We are looking for people who have: 
 
 Experience within a sales team that produces and exceeds their goals consistently 
 Experience in a startup environment 
 2 to 3 years full cycle selling experience 
 A sharp focus on their goals 
 The ability to advance the vision of Hurree 
 Emotional intelligence 
 A powerful work ethic 
 Superior communication skills 
 Exceptional closing skills 
 
 Benefits of working for Hurree: 
 
 Private healthcare 
 Pension 
 Opportunity for training 
 Flexible hours 
 35 days annual leave 
 Employee discounts via Perks Directory 
 Amazing colleagues to learn from and enjoy social events 
 
 Confidence can sometimes hold us back from applying for a job. But we'll let you in on a secret: there's no such thing as a 'perfect' candidate. Hurree is a place where everyone can grow. So however you identify and whatever background you bring with you, please apply if this is a role that would make you excited to come into work every day.
