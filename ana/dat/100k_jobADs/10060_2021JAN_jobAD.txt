 Operations Site Manager 
 Manchester 
 Our client, a leading provider of life science solutions, are now looking for an Operational Site Manager to manage their teams in Manchester with their work with Covid-19 testing and clinical trials for a fixed term contract of 6 months that could well be extended further on a rolling basis. 
 The Benefits 
 - Salary competitive DOE 
 - Excellent benefits 
 This is an exceptional opportunity to help impact the pandemic and play a part of the resolution of this global crisis. 
 The Role 
 Operational Logistic Supply chain, Warehouse Managers with experience in managing or supervising teams from supply chain, production and logistics management will be considered. 
 The post holder will be responsible for all site operational matters and help to achieve all SLA's and KPI's for the client's Covid-19 testing services to support the UK response to the pandemic.
