 Friday Work at home Job Leads

 There are a lot of work at home opportunities for remote workers right now. In fact, companies like Nationwide, Hilton and so many other employers are looking to hire candidates to start working from their home office and recruiting right now. If you are in need of a work at home job, please apply to the latest work at home job leads today. Don't forget to visit the  Friday freebie pag e,  weekly job section  and join our official  Facebook group at Workersonboard Community  for additional work at home job leads and encouragement.  Virtual Customer Service  Nationwide has immediate openings through Conduent for reps to support their customers and provide solutions to their technical issues. They are hiring in several states and are looking for people who love technology, can type 35 wpm, and have 6 months customer service experience. Pays $12 per hour with benefits.  Photo Researcher and Editor  Ranker is seeking those with 2 years photo researching and editing experience to review images for quality and subject matter.  Virtual Tech Support  Conduent has immediate openings for home agents with a technical aptitude to support customers with technical issues in a timely manner. Requires 6 months experience dealing with the public. Pays $12 to start.  Virtual Customer Care Professional  American Express is hiring remote workers to consult with card members addressing customer inquiries, providing solutions, reinforcing benefits and other duties. Must be tech savvy, can multitask, and have a land line phone. Company will provide monthly telephone and internet fees.  Freelance Fact Checker  Ranker is hiring individuals to check stories for factual accuracy. Requires 2 years fact checking experience.  Tech Support Specialist  Mixmax is hiring reps to provide email and chat tech support, answer product questions, troubleshoot bugs and provide product feedback. Requires 1-3 years experience. Hiring in select states only.  Daily Freelance Writer  Ranker is seeking writers to research and write 3 articles a day 5 day a week for an entertainment site. Requires previous experience and advanced research skills.  Data Entry  SESAC is seeking a data entry specialist to ensure that licensing data is entered into the database accurately. Must have good communication and be proficient with Microsoft Office suite. Part time.  Call Center Rep  Randstand is seeking care experts to help customers answer questions about their appliances via inbound calls. Must have 1 year experience, type 35 wpm and intermediate computer skills. Pays $11 per hour. part time.  Mturk  Work from home for Amazon completing short tasks from home in transcription, data entry, editing, etc.  Make Money  Be an Avon Rep and get paid to sell their products and earn a commission. Comes with a website and starter kit.  Free Gifts, Prizes and Cash  NCP will reward you for scanning your weekly purchases. Earn points towards gifts and take part in the weekly, monthly and quarterly sweepstakes.  Product Report Card  Earn cash and rewards by sharing your opinions.  Pinch Me  Get free samples delivered to your home. No credit card or shipping fee ever required.  Free Gift Cards  Join Swagbucks to get free gift cards from your favorite stores. Open worldwide.
