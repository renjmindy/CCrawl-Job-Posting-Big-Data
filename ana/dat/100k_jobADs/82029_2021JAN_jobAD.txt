 Are you an experienced HR Business Partner looking for an opportunity to help shape and develop the HR offering for this growing SME. The role is based in Walsall. Salary £45-48k. 
 As the HR Business Partner for this client group, you will review and update policies, develop attraction strategies, coaching and developing of Managers and their capability, organisational review and design and succession planning. 
 CIPD level 5, or equivalent, you will be a self-starter with a strong generalist background, you will be happy to influence and drive through change around the HR agenda. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
