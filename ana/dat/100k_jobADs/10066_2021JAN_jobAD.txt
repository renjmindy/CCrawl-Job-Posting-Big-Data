 Investors Relations Officer

 Description 
 Job Responsibilities 
 
 Acting as a point of contact between the bank and stakeholders/  investment community 
 Manage strategic investor relation plans pertaining to evolving company issues 
 Develop, coordinate, and participates in presentations to investor relations client base; brokers, shareholders, research analysts and investment groups 
 Strategically source and maintain relationship with research analysts and brokers 
 Manage the development and distribution of external communication materials to investors 
 Work with the Company Secretary to develop and execute common and all-encompassing investor relation strategy 
 Work with Financial control, Brand & Marketing Communications and Legal in the preparation, review and distribution of corporate press releases and any related filings 
 Research and provide appropriate written updates for internal reporting 
 Perform other duties as may be assigned by supervisor

 As per performance management

 Requirements 
 Required Competence: 
 
 Demonstrated ability to understand, analyse, and interpret financial data of organizations. 
 Ability to manage and anchor sessions with prospective and existing investors 
 Exposure to regulatory frameworks of listed companies (local and international). 
 Excellent communication and presentation skills. 
 Project and People management experience 
 
 Education: 
 A first degree from a reputable University, MBA or Professional Qualification will be an added advantage 
 Work Experience: 
 4-5 years' experience in relevant role or analyst role in a financial services organisation.
