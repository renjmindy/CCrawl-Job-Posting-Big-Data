 Description 
 My client has mandated us to search for a Systems Engineer (Sales) based in South Africa to add to their growing, global sales organization. 
 Description 
 My client helps businesses fight cybercrime, protect data and reduce security risks. With cloud and managed security services, integrated technologies and a team of security experts, ethical hackers and researchers they work for clients all over the globe in around 100 countries. 
 They now seek a highly motivated and technically skilled Systems Engineer (Sales) toi be based in Johannesburg but can be home based in South Africa with travel. 
 The successful candidate will be the primary technical resource throughout the sales process. 
 Core duties include propose, design, and present technical solutions. 
 This position will allow you to use your technical knowledge to represent their products and services. 
 The Systems Engineer will report to the manager of Systems Engineers for EMEA, and will work with Account Managers and Solutions Architects to close business and ensure customer satisfaction. 
 This is a great career opportunity for an individual interested in working at one of the area's hottest companies.
