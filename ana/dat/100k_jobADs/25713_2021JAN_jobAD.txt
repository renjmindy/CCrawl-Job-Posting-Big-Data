 Lawyer m / f / d | Job in Berlin 
 
 November 28, 2020 
 No Comments 
 
 The Ostdeutsche Sparkassenverband (OSV) - service provider for the savings banks in the federal states of Brandenburg, Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania, Saxony and Saxony-Anhalt - is looking for a legal team member as soon as possible 
 Your tasks: 
 
 Advice and support for the savings banks and the association in banking law 
 Legal advice on product development, especially digital products 
 You participate in specialist committees and working groups of the Sparkassen-Finanzgruppe. 
 You will also design legal training events for the savings banks and carry them out at the locations in Berlin and Potsdam or, if you wish, directly at the savings banks on site. 
 
 Our expectations: 
 
 two state law exams, at least one "satisfactory" 
 like professional experience in banking and / or IT law 
 Strong teamwork, a communicative working style, very high level of initiative, resilience, flexibility, creativity as well as independence and presentation security 
 IT affinity 
 
 Your perspectives: 
 
 a professionally and communicatively challenging job 
 very good development opportunities 
 a job for an employer that has been recognized for its commitment to employees and has received the “Work and Family Audit” certificate several times 
 a modern, well-equipped workplace in the fundamental questions / legal team and - after familiarization - a very employee-friendly home office practice 
 a remuneration appropriate to the position according to the TVöD-S, as a component of the retirement provision an additional pension for the public service and the other usual social benefits 
 the opportunity to expand your experience, skills and professional responsibility 
 
 If you want to accept this interesting challenge, please apply by January 3rd, 2021. 
 The head of the legal team, Mr. Andreas Heffner (030-2069 1740), will be happy to provide you with further information in the department of fundamental questions. 
 Applications from people with disabilities will be given preference if they are suitable.
