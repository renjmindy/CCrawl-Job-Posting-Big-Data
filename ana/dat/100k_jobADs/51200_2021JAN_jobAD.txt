 Babysitter Jobs in Qatar 
 Posted on  September 14, 2009  by  caregiver 
 The Middle East country of Qatar is currently in need of applicants for various babysitter jobs in Qatar. The POEA jobs order for the said babysitter jobs in Qatar was approved last August 19, 2009 and has a corresponding job order balance for 50 candidates for babysitter jobs in Qatar. The said job order was approved for a recruitment agency based in Ermita, Manila. 
 All Filipinos who are interested for the said babysitter jobs in Qatar may visit the recruitment agency directly involved, in their office address below. You may also contact the recruitment agency by phone for more info. 
 Future Careers Recruitment Agency Inc. 
R 201,203,204,602 Jalandoni Building 
1444 R. Salas, Ermita 
Manila 
 Tel Nos: 4501265 / 5244538
