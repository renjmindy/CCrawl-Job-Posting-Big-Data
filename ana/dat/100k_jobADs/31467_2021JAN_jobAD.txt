 Can You Believe It?! it's a Freelance job 
 You're probably not one to take a job just to make your friends jealous, but in case you are, MTV is hiring a Freelance Motion designer in New York, USA. 
 About 
 MTV Brand Creative is looking for a freelance motion designer with strong design and animation skills to join 
our team. 
 This is a project-based assignment, starting sometime in December, with a possible 3-month commitment and option to renew. 
 Qualifications: 
 · Knowledge of After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign is a must 
· Experience with Premiere Pro and C4D is a plus 
· Minimum of five years professional design/animation experience 
· Must be eligible to work in the United States 
 Additional Qualifications 
 · strong eye for typography and composition 
· be comfortable taking direction from Art directors and creative directors 
· self-motivated and self-sufficient 
· detail oriented and able to work under tight deadlines 
· ability to articulate creative concepts 
· eager to learn and grow within our team
