 Painter & Decorator 
 We have an excellent opportunity for an experienced Painter in Nottingham to join a repairs and maintenance team working on domestic properties including social housing. 
 We have an established department covering all aspects of housing repairs, as a Painter within this team you will be responsible for repairs and maintenance to kitchens and bathrooms. 
 This is a fantastic opportunity to secure sociable, full time hours Monday to Friday. 
 You will be working a standard 40 hour week with potential to take overtime. 
 Specialist tools will be supplied but own basic hand tools will be required. 
 You will need; 
 , NVQ or City and Guilds in Painting and Decorating 
, Full UK Driving Licence 
, Own Vehicle 
, Previous Experience (3+ years) 
, Valid CSCS Card
