 A fast growing and highly regarded services business based in Warwickshire are looking to recruit a talented Senior PR Executive into their marketing and communications team. 
 The role will be responsible for: 
 
 Developing and executing external and internal communications plans 
 Create PR pans and campaigns 
 Work alongside the press office, answering media queries and managing reputation management 
 Develop and manage social media channels 
 
 The level of position requires experience within: 
 
 PR and communications (either agency side or in-house) 
 Excellent stakeholder management skills 
 Social media management 
 
 An excellent salary, working environment and career development opportunities are on offer. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
