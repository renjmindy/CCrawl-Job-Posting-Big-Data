 Registered General Nurse 
 Our client is one of UK's leading healthcare providers and they are currently seeking for a motivated RGN to work in an exceptional care home based in the Croxteth, Liverpool area 
 This is a medium sized home in Liverpool which provides Palliative care, Parkinson's nursing, Dementia nursing and convalescence. 
 The home is  split across five buildings, care for short or long term, nursing and residential dementia care, nursing, respite, convalescence and palliative care 
 As a Nurse your day to day duties include: 
 
 
     Ensure the highest possible standards of care, assessing residents' needs and wishes, and developing services to enhance their quality of life
 
 
 
     Champion appropriate independence and personal choice; developing, reviewing and updating care plans to meet our residents physical, social and psychological needs
 
 
 
     Oversee all aspects of medicine management on your shift - ensuring medicines are appropriately received, stored and administered, in accordance with company policies and current legislation
 
 
 
     Build the positive reputation of the Business; liaise professionally with visitors and other external stakeholders
 
 
 
     Participate knowledgably and professionally to all inspection visits, ensuring your team is ready at all times to do the same
 
 
 
     Ensure compliance with all legal, regulatory and best practice guidelines - identify, investigate and resolve risks proactively
 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Excellent communication skills
 
 
 
     Ability to deliver care, without direct supervision
 
 
 
     Competent in Medication Management
 
 
 
     Ability to follow the Nursing process-Assessment/Diagnosis/Planning/Implementation/Evaluation
 
 
 
     Maintaining the Client's dignity and respect at all times
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will be offered an excellent salary of £17.50 per hour. 
 You will be required to work Night Shifts only. 
 In return for your hard work and dedication you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Paid Breaks - At selected Homes
 
 
 
     Free Meals On Long Days
 
 
 
     Free DBS
 
 
 
     Ongoing Career Progression
 
 
 
     Pension Scheme
 
 
 
     Assistance With Revalidation
