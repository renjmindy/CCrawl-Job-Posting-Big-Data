 About Turing Labs Inc. 
 Do you want to build enterprise-grade software that uses data, ML, and solves industry-specific business problems? Read on. 
 From soap and shampoo to snacks and beverages, consumer products fill our homes and affect our everyday lives. But it is very hard to build a product; takes years. Not anymore, Turing, enterprise software, uses data and ML to bring speed and operational efficiency to the legacy processes. 
 Simply put, Turing is becoming the gold standard when it comes to configuration optimal consumer products from millions of combinations. Unlike generic ML, Turing is a domain-specific solution, resulting in exponential growth. 
 
 In Covid, our software use has skyrocketed as employees want to develop new products while staying at home. 
 
 Our customers say Turing is the fastest way to develop new products.  Turing has proven to be the most accurate ML engine outperforming DIY data science software and tech giants. 
 Founders have decades of domain experience + expertise in top 1% ML algorithms. 
 Many of the world’s leading brands use Turing to innovate new products. We’re growing quickly; new customers go on the waitlist. We’re remote-friendly, YC backed+other notable SV investors, and well-funded. 
 We small but a team of warriors. Average employee experience is 10+years - so you are joining a team of highly-seasoned professionals. Our engineers have built large-scale enterprise-grade software for private and government sectors including NSA and homeland security. 
 See our coverage at TechCrunch, WSJ, Business Insider, and Forbes 
 About the role 
 Skills:  Node.js, PostgreSQL, TypeScript, Machine Learning, Java 
 Turing's workflow and ML are transforming the 120-year old process of developing consumer products. These products make part of your daily lives. We are positioned to dominate this space but a lot needs to be built. 
 Why join Turing? 
 Team of warriors:  We have not figured out everything and that’s fine. You will work in an environment where we collaborate, push each other, and challenge the status quo. 
 Be the creator:  You get an opportunity to create the DNA of the product, team, and culture 
 Leadership:  You work closely with founders to lead and execute journey from Seed to Series X 
 Your role: 
 Do you dream of building and scaling complex engineering software while closely working with founders and customers? Read on: 
 Lead by example:  Translate user needs, co-create product vision, translate into engineering needs, and execute it 
 Make strategic decisions:  You would make strategic decisions about the culture, product, technology stack, and features 
 Solve complex problems:  You will be solving complex engineering problems, particularly backend, while working with smartest ML data scientists 
 Build enterprise-grade software:  Billion dollar consumer brands will use what you built. You will be building enterprise-ready secure software; easy to use but complex behind the scene. 
 What we expect: 
 
 You have an entrepreneur soul and adaptable to a fast-growing environment with a high tolerance for ambiguity 
 You are champion traversing all parts of the software stack and build with a focus on the user. You write clean, maintainable, scalable code and develop, test, and iterate quickly. 
 You will assess business and product requirements and propose viable solutions that account for scale and performance. 
 You will establish foundational engineering & coding standards 
 
 
 Note: This role holds a tremendous opportunity to help shape the future of our company and product while also benefitting from generous equity. Depending on the performance, the role could transition into that of CTO.
