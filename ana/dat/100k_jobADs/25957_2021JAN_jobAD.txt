 Worksource Montgomery  |  Career Support  |  Where to Find us 
 WorkSource Montgomery is a proud partner of the American Job Center Network, designed to provide a full range of assistance to job seekers under one roof. Authorized by the  Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act of 2014 , these locations, also known as  One-Stop Centers , offer training referrals, career counseling, job listings, and similar employment-related services. 
 WorkSource Montgomery operates with a coalition of providers: Maryland Department of Labor, Licensing and Regulation, Division of Rehabilitative Services, and local non-profits and agencies to provide comprehensive, quality services. Visit our job centers in Wheaton and Germantown, and our  Helping Individuals Reach Employment (H.I.R.E.)  satellite location in East County to gain the tools and skills you need to succeed in your job search.
