 Job Posting: Saskatchewan Indian Gaming Authority - Learning & Development Consultant 
 Posted on  October 7, 2009  by  Richard Schwier  under  Job Postings 
 General Information: 
Organization Saskatchewan Indian Gaming Authority 
Position Type Full-Time 
Position Name Learning & Development Consultant 
Location City/Town Saskatoon 
Province or State Saskatchewan 
Country CANADA 
Target Audience Business - Human Resources Mgmt; Business - Management; Education 
Target Levels Alumni - Bachelor's Degree 
Compensation: Pay Range $45861.00 to $57327.00 
Currency Canadian Dollars 
Requirements and Duties: 
Qualifications and Requirements -Successful completion of a bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Commerce, Education or other related discipline. 
-Several years of directly related experience and training may be considered. 
-Experience in the design, development and implementation of training programs. 
-Experience in conducting needs assessments. 
-Experience in designing instruments to assess and determine return on training investments. 
-Experience in evaluating internal/external learning programs and facilitators. 
-Experience with leadership development and training will be an asset. 
Duties and Responsibilities Reporting directly to the Director Learning & Development, the Learning & Development Consultant will be responsible for designing, delivering, and organizing training workshops, programs and initiatives within SIGA. Responsibilities include: Scheduling, organizing and delivering designated training modules organizational wide; Coordinating training schedules; Conducting pilot sessions for all new training initiatives; Creating lesson plans, agendas, learning materials, job aids and evaluation questionnaires; Ensuring that lesson plans meet the objective of the training initiative; Tracking workshop attendance numbers; Conducting learning needs analysis with individual employees; Identifying learning gaps, and creating personal learning plans that work towards employee development goals; evaluating return on training investment. Working with outside vendors to secure venues, resources and promotional items required for training sessions; Working with SIGA's other trainers to ensure consistent training methods throughout the organization; Remaining up-to-date on industry best practices for facilitation and employee development needs and trends; Developing and contributing to maintenance of a procedural manual for the Learning and Development Consultant position; Other duties as assigned by Director Learning & Development.
