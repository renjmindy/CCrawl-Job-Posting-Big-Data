 Caregiving Jobs Israel 
 Posted on  May 27, 2011  by  caregiver 
 Calling all caregiver job applicants who are looking for Caregiving Jobs Israel. A Job Order was approved by the POEA last May 19, 2011 which allows a Quezon City based recruitment agency to recruit caregivers who are qualified for  Caregiving Jobs Israel . This opportunity of employment in caregiving jobs Israel have a total vacancy for 30 caregivers who will qualify for the said caregiver work abroad. 
 If you are interested to be included in the list of possible candidates for the said Caregiving Jobs Israel, visit the office of International Progress, Inc., fill up an application, and submit it together with your resume. If you have any question regarding the qualifications, requirements, or placement fees for the said caregiving jobs Israel, you may contact International Progress, Inc. by way of their telephone numbers or email addresses listed below. 
 International Progress, Inc. 
Unit 1, Landsdale Arcade Complex 
86 Timog Ave. Corner Mother Ignacia Avenue 
Paligsahan, Quezon City 
Telephone No: (632) 332-65-74; (632) 351-62-04 
Fax: (632) 412-06-12 
E-Mail: 
ipiees@ipi.com.ph 
jobs@ipi.com.ph 
ipi_jobs@ipi.com.ph 
Website: http://www.ipi.com.ph/v1/
