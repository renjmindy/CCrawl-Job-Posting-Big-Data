 Part-time Human Resources Advisor, 30 hours per week 
 Up to -£29,000 pro rata per annum 
 Gleeson are currently working with a Birmingham based business who are looking for a Part time HR Advisor to join their team. 
This is an exciting time to join the organisation as they develop and embed their People Strategy, transform how they deliver their HR service, and complete the implementation of their first integrated payroll and HR system. 
 In a full generalist role, duties will include: 
 
 Leading on all ER advice and guidance and leading on all casework 
 Pro-actively coaching managers through people issues 
 Policy and process development 
 Training and development 
 Leading and developing wellbeing initiatives 
 Assisting with HR projects 
 
 Ideally level 5 CIPD Qualified, you will come from a generalist HR background where you have been able to influence and act as a trusted HR Advisor to the business. Confident on all aspects of HR you must be able to demonstrate that you have lead on HR casework. 
 This role is working 30 hours a week. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
