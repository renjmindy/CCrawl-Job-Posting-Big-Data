 Junior High Science Teacher 
 Apply for the position, Junior High Science Teacher, with TMP-Marian Jr/Sr High School today or contact Tom Meagher for more information. Posted Dec 2, 2020. 
  7856256577  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 Thomas More Prep-Marian Jr/Sr High School (3A co-ed Catholic school, enrollment 264 students, JH portion is 90 students) is seeking a full-time junior high science teacher to inspire students to become proficient in the scientific method, analyzing data, experimentation, and creative thinking and problem solving as well as other skills necessary for success in college and career. 
 A successful candidate will have a minimum of a Bachelor’s degree in Science Education, Biology or related science field and be certified (or able to obtain certification) in Kansas. A Master’s degree is preferred and will command a higher salary, as will teaching experience. 
 Successful candidates will have a strong ability to teach skills and content, while also organizing projects and experiences that are both cross curricular and college/career preparatory. Teachers at TMP-Marian are expected to develop caring relationships with students that allow them to demand greatness. TMP-Marian believes giving students multiple pathways to success will help all levels of students persevere in high school and in the future. 
 Experience and/or interest in a wide variety of technological tools and STREAM is a plus. TMP-Marian uses Google Classroom with students having 1:1 access to Chromebooks. Other technology tools like 3D printers, drones, and CNC machines are available. Students will learn 21st Century communication skills in a variety of platforms. 
 TMP-Marian’s uniform dress code and college preparatory focus provide a strong academic learning environment. If you are a serious educator ready for the opportunities that a Catholic school can offer, please consider applying with a cover letter, resume, and three references to Principal Chad Meitner at  [email protected] .
