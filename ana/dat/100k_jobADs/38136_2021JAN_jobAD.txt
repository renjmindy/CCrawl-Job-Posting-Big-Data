 Admissions Counselor 
 Apply for the position, Admissions Counselor, with NCK Tech College today or contact Liz Fixsen for more information. Posted Jan 4, 2021. 
  Apply Online  __    7857389060  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 **Opening for Admissions Counselor:   **North Central Kansas Technical College has an  opening for an Admissions Counselor on the Hays Campus.  This is a 12-month position, responsible for facilitating all aspects of the recruitment of students. 
 Qualifications include: 
 
 Excellent communication skills (verbal and written) 
 Strong computer skills 
 Ability to lift, set-up and transport recruiting displays, promotional items and College simulators 
 Ability to travel extensively throughout the school year 
 Associate’s degree required; bachelor’s degree preferred 
 
 Duties and Responsibilities include: 
 
 Coordination of admissions effort with multi-member Admissions team; 
 Extensive travel in primary and secondary recruiting areas during peak times with some overnight travel required; 
 Attend career planning conferences, trade shows and fairs; 
 Actively develop and maintain relationships with high school counselors and technical education instructors; 
 Coordinate High School visits; 
 Maintain accurate and timely information in computer database system; 
 Coordinate all follow-up efforts in conjunction with the admissions effort; 
 Host campus visits; 
 Actively participate in College-wide recruitment and orientation events; 
 Develop and maintain travel calendar of admissions activities; 
 Maintain regular communication with supervisor regarding admissions efforts; 
 Perform other duties as required by the Dean of Student Services. 
 
 Benefits include: 
 
 Competitive pay; 
 Benefits including medical, vision, dental, health and dependent care savings account; KPERS 401K retirement plan, & KPERS 457 plan; 
 Excellent vacation and sick leave plan; 
 Yearly clothing allowance; and 
 College provided cell phone and laptop 
 
 Applicants shall include a cover letter, resume, unofficial transcripts, and contact information for three professional references. To apply online, go to https://ncktc.edu/job-opportunities/. Questions should be directed to Liz Fixsen, Search Liaison, at  [email protected]  or call 785-738-9060.  Position open until filled.  www.ncktc.edu
