 Gleeson Recruitment Group is actively seeking an experienced Accounts Assistant to join a reputable business based in Stratford Upon Avon. You will be working in a medium sized finance team reporting directly into the Finance Manager. 
 You can expect a salary between £23,000 - £25,000 depending on experience + excellent company benefits. 
 Due to the lovely rural location of the office your own form of transportation is needed. 
 This is a great time to join a successful and growing business with exciting prospects! 
 The role: 
 
 Purchase ledger invoice and payment processing 
 Supporting Sales Ledger 
 Reconciliation of client accounts 
 Preparation and input of nominal journals 
 Instrstat reports 
 Journals, accruals, and prepayments 
 Stock reconciliation 
 Assisting with yearend audit 
 Assisting with internal audit requirements and visiting clients 
 Processing expenses 
 Occasional sales ledger duties 
 Management reports 
 
 Ideal candidate: 
 
 Present a strong purchase ledger background 
 Ability and desire to gain new skills and exposure into other aspects of finance 
 Enthusiastic 
 Must be proficient with Excel, Word and Outlook 
 Desire to progress within the business 
 
 To apply for the Accounts Assistant position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
