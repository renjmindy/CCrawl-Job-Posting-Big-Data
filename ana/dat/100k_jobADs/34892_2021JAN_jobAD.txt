 This position is preferably located in Carrboro, NC, however we are open to other remote locations. 
 The Rundown: 
 As a member of the Retail Experience Team, this position will assist with leading retail education and training strategies. This role will focus on store-level retail trainers and the overall development of our future brand leaders! Our Retail Experience Managers play a key role in running the day to day operations of our business and the Retail Trainer will have the opportunity to mentor and develop our Retail Experience Managers. This individual will lead our Retail Experience Manager training from the overall strategy through to the execution. Another key role this position will support as needed is our Leadership Development Program trainees. 
 Our Retail Trainer will drive brand-wide sales metrics by focusing on education for our store-level Retail Experience Managers, ultimately crafting a consistent customer and staff experience across the brand. The Retail Trainer will curate educational content and communicate new resources on a monthly basis. This role will have the opportunity to partner with other departments to support all aspects of sales and service training. 
 If you enjoy speaking in front of a crowd, coordinating and executing summits/workshops, and educating and training others, this role is for you! 
 Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

 Qualifiers 
 
 Minimum of two years of experience as a retail trainer, retail manager, or similar role 
 Ability to travel 
 Experience with Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and retail POS systems 
 Excellent verbal and written communication and presentation, and facilitation skills 
 Hardworking, positive, and self-motivated 
 Collaborative and customer-centric 
 
 Other requirements: 
 
 Position requires occasional walking and standing with light (1-10 lbs.) carrying, lifting, pushing, or pulling. The position is mostly sedentary and requires the employee to have close visual acuity to perform job requirements. The position requires frequent talking, hearing, and repetitive motion with the hands, wrists, and/or fingers. This position is located within an office environment and is not substantially exposed to adverse environmental conditions.
