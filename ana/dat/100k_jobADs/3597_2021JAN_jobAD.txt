 German-speaking Assistant Accountant - £24-28k per annum 
 The Business 
 Our client is a well-established business based near Dudley. Due to recent growth in their major European markets, we are now seeking a German-speaking Assistant Accountant to join their finance team. 
 The Role 
 The individual we seek will support the current team across a variety of financial reporting activities. The role will ultimately be focused on the following areas: 
 
 All aspects of sales & purchase ledger 
 Supporting the budgeting & forecasting process 
 Liaising with the German entity to resolve complex queries and invoicing issues 
 Complex bank & balance sheet reconciliations 
 Assisting with the preparation of information for monthly management accounts 
 Performing all end of month duties 
 Working alongside the Financial Controller to instigate process improvements 
 
 This is a critical role within a very pressurised yet supportive team, and will suit someone keen to find a challenge alongside an opportunity improve their accountancy skill base. 
 The Candidate 
 The person we seek for this role will have strong attention to detail, with a broad spectrum of finance skills. If you wish to study AAT/ACCA/CIMA, full financial and pastoral support will be available. 
 We need a fluent German-speaker for this role.
