 Receptionist FT Temp-To-Hire 
   
   Workway, Inc.   
 __Website    __@workway 
 As a  Receptionist  , you will be the first point of contact for the company and will be responsible for establishing a warm, welcoming, and professional atmosphere for clients, customers, and all other visitors.  Your specific duties in this role will include: 
 
 Answering very busy phone lines and greeting customers with warmth, efficiency, and professionalism 
 Greeting visitors and issuing passes when required 
 Maintaining front reception area and conference rooms 
 Managing incoming and outgoing mail and vendor deliveries 
 Assisting departments or employees with word processing, filing, copying, scanning, faxing, etc. 
 
 Job Requirements 
 
 High school diploma or GED 
 1-3 years of administrative experience 
 Strong phone etiquette and diplomacy 
 Proficiency with computers and technology 
 
 Let one of our recruiters assist you in finding and preparing for interviews with our clients. Whether you are interested in a direct hire, temp-to-hire, or temporary position, Workway can help you to put your best foot forward. Contact us today! 
 To receive state and federal compliance posters, e-mail hr@workway.com  or call 972.514.1515.
