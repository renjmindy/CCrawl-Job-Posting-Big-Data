 Associate Sound Designer 
 Hexany Audio Apply for this Job 
 Posted 
 November 29, 2020 
 Full Time 
 Bell Gardens, CA 
 Remote 
 As an  Associate Sound Designer  at Hexany, your role will be highly creative and collaborative. Though the focus will be designing sounds and creating content, you will likely handle additional tasks such as mixing/engineering sessions and implementation in Wwise or similar tools. We’re currently working on a variety of awesome unannounced projects and place a strong value on work-life balance; your weekends and evenings will be your own!  ‍ 
 ‍ 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Designing awesome sounds for all types of games and interactive experiences 
 Mixing in stereo and surround formats 
 Field recording source material 
 Implementing sounds in Wwise and other audio middleware 
 Occasionally recording, editing, and processing dialogue  ‍ 
 
 ‍ 
 ‍  Qualifications: 
 
 A passion for video games 
 Minimum 1 year experience working in video game sound design 
 Proficiency with Reaper, Pro Tools, or a similar DAW 
 Experience working with Wwise or similar audio middleware 
 Extreme attention to detail and great organizational skills 
 A team-player mentality and the ability to take direction and constructive criticism from project leads  ‍ 
 
 ‍ 
 Pluses: 
 
 Experience integrating assets in Unity + Unreal 
 Experience working in VR 
 
 ‍ 
 This is a full-time salaried position in-house. Contractors need not apply. Also note this is NOT a music composition position. 
 Due to COVID-19 we are presently permitting work-from-home, but due to the need to provide our team with specialized computers and equipment, relocation to the Los Angeles area is required prior to start. Unfortunately we do not have any flexibility in this regard and cannot offer exceptions to this policy. 
 ‍ 
 How To Apply 
 Applications must be submitted through the Google Form below using the “Apply Now” button. Applications sent via email will not be considered.
