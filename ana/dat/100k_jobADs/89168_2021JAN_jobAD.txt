 Are you an experienced, CIPS qualified Procurement and Contracts? Are you a motivated self-starter with the ability to work in a stand alone role? This opportunity could be the one for you! 
 Based in central Birmingham this organisation is seeking a Procurement Consultant. The role will involve UK travel when required. You will have responsibility for providing procurement support and advice, reviewing and updating ITT documents and publishing and implementing these documents. You will also ensure that you are maximising contract effectiveness. 
 In order to carry out this role you will need the following skills and experience; 
 
 CIPS qualified 
 Experience within a senior procurement role 
 Strong experience writing ITT documents and managing tenders 
 Excellent stakeholder engagement skills 
 Strong communication skills 
 
 In return, you will receive a salary of £50,000 to £70,000 dependent upon experience, plus benefits. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
