 Japan Caregivers Job 
 Posted on  January 30, 2014  by  caregiver 
 JCJ Management Services and International Promotions, a JITCO accredited sending organization based in Makati City, is currently accepting applicants for  Japan Caregivers Job . The said caregiver work abroad is being offered to 50 caregivers job applicants who will be selected based on the following qualifications: 
 
 Male or Female 
 At least 22 years old 
 Must have at least high school diploma 
 Graduate of caregiver course (6 mos.) 
 TESDA Certificate (NC II) 
 With or without experience 
 Knows how to speak Japanese language (if ex-Japan) 
 Willing to learn Japanese language (first timer) 
 Employment Certificate (local or abroad) 
 With valid Passport 
 Fresh graduates are welcome to apply 
 
 If you are qualified and interested to apply for the said Japan Caregivers Job, please visit the office of JCJ Management Services and International Promotions and submit your updated resume together with photocopies of your credentials. For more information in connection with the said Japan caregivers job, please get in touch with JCJ Management Services and International Promotions by way of their telephone numbers we have specified below.
