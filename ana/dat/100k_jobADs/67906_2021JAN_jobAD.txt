 Site Engineer - Civil 
 Job description 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently recruiting for a Civil Site Engineer for a role which will run for most of 2021. The road construction project is in the APY Lands in remote South Australia on a 3:1 FIFO roster. Accommodation is camp accommodation on site which is a self-sufficient camp on the project site. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 You will be responsible for: 
 
 
 Maintain quality assurance records to meet DIT specifications 
 
 
 Coordinate quality assurance testing and survey records 
 
 
 Implementation of company quality and safety systems 
 
 
 General correspondence with the client regarding day to day operations 
 
 
 General assistance with project management duties (with support from the Project Manager) 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 To be successful in this role you will have the following: - 
 
 
 Degree or Diploma in Civil Engineering 
 
 
 Proven experience in the Civil Industry in road construction 
 
 
 Ability to work closely with Clients, Subcontractors and Suppliers 
 
 
 Proven communication & organizational skills 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Industry Induction White Card 
 
 
 Apply Now 
 For more information please contact Juanique Liebenberg on or 03 9647 5148 for a confidential conversation. 
 Job order reference no. 7004J202185776 
 At WorkPac, we recognise we are strengthened by diversity. We are committed to providing a work environment in which everyone is included, treated fairly and with respect. We are an Equal Opportunity employer and we encourage applications from women and Indigenous people.
