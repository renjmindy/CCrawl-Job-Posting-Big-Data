 If you are currently looking for a  work at home job , there are a few companies hiring that I found online to share with you. Some of these companies and positions hire in the United States and also Canada. I have included the details, pay, and any other information that may be helpful in your work at home journey. 
 Remember, you can find even more work at home companies hiring right now by visiting the following pages.

 **Work at home Jobs - Weekend Edition ** 
 Aha! 
 This company is currently hiring writers and customer service professionals to work from home anywhere in the United States and Canada with previous experience. They offer a generous salary along with equity, benefits, and a profit-sharing program. 
 IXL Learning 
 Hiring English Content Editors and Math Video Creators to work from home to create short instructional videos for 6th, 7th, or 8th-grade math that is up to 5 minutes in length. You must have experience teaching and tutoring math. Pays $300 per completed video. 
 Exam Works 
 
 Has work at home positions in various categories including Client Coordinators to work from home open to any location in the US. This position will require you to handle inquiries from clients in a prompt manner. Requires 1-year clerical experience and can type 40 wpm with computer proficiency. 
 Hello Innovation 
 This company hires Happiness Heros and Customer/Tech Support Specialists to work from home to assist online shoppers to resolve customer inquiries, place new orders, provide product guidance, and advice. Offer flexible scheduling, paid holidays, bonuses, and other perks. 
 Teach English 
 Education First is looking for people to work from home whenever you want to teach English to kids online. You will earn $20 per hour and more working during peak times. Must live in the US or UK. 
 Customer Happiness 
 Farmstead is now hiring because of a great demand for grocery delivery to assist customers and drivers via phone, email, and text. You will also manage orders, delivery status, and driver information. You must have a desire to help others with good communication skills. Full and part-time openings with a starting pay of $16 per hour. 
 Customer Experience Happiness Ambassador 
 Bark is seeking those with strong internet connections to work remotely on a temporary basis to respond to their customers, resolve customer accounts, and other duties. You must love dogs, be empathetic with excellent writing, typing, and communication skills. Part-time that includes weekends and evenings. 
 Social Media Editors 
 
 Minute Media is hiring social media editors to manage the posting and engagement of their social media accounts. Requires 1-3 years experience, love of social media, good writing skills, and knowledge of the sports community. 
 Subject Tutors 
 Course Hero is seeking a wide variety of people to connect with high school and university students who are strong in their particular field, whether it's biology or zoology. Tutors earn an average of $3 for each question answered on Course Hero or an hourly rate between $12-$20/hour. Work whenever you want. 
 PeekYou 
 Work from home as a blogger/social media specialist managing their social media engagement accounts across various social networks and portals. Your primary responsibilities will include managing all of PeekYou's social media portals and pages (such as Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, etc.) while actively responding to and helping our various users through these channels. Requires experience and knowledge of social media. 
 These jobs came from our FB Group (More posted today also) 
 Caroo 
 
 Caroo formerly knowns as Snack Nation is hiring Care Consultants to work from home to help customers order remote care packages, answer questions, and other duties. Requires 1-2 years related experience, a good listener, a compelling writer, and be self-motivated. 
 99 Social 
 Work from home as a Virtual Assistant writing short social media posts, curating articles and photos for their clients. No copywriting experience required but you must be fluent in English, have good research skills, can submit quality content every week, and can work Monday through Friday. Offers a flexible work schedule.
