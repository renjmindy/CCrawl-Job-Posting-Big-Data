 Bank Reconciliations Clerk Shirley - working remotely. 
 An impressive and well established company based in Shirley(Solihull) are looking to recruit a hardworking and conscientious Bank Reconciliations Clerk on a short term temporary basis. 
 Please note this role will be working remotely,. 
 Main duties of the role: 
 
 This will be to clear unreconciled bank items via a data download on excel. 
 They will need to match debits and credits and DD's against invoices within the system (again on Excel) 
 
 What you'll need to succeed 
 As well as excellent communication and interpersonal skills, a can-do attitude is required, with the ability to effectively manage suppliers in order to maintain open and effective communications with suppliers at all times.
