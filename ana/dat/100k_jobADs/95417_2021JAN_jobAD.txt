 Job Description 
 We are seeking a Federal Government Contracts Attorney to become a part of our team! Trial experience in federal contracting a must. 
 Responsibilities: 
 Small Denver law office seeks federal government contract attorney for work or an Of Counsel position. Must have of government contract law experience with the Federal Acquisition Regulation, appeals and litigation, small business programs and agency regulations, Contract Disputes Act claims and Requests for Equitable Adjustment.
