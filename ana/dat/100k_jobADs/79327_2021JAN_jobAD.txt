 Description 
 Cashier—Part-Time, Casual 
 Scarborough Health Network—Scarborough, ON 
 Aramark Canada at Scarborough Health Network is seeking a part-time cashier to join our Food Services team. The Part-Time Cashier is responsible for accurately processing retail merchandise transactions, maintaining the balance of the cash drawer, and providing customer service to guests of the location. 
 Shift Schedules:  6:30 am to 2:00 pm, Monday to Friday. Preference will be given to applicants that are flexible/available to work during the weekdays and weekends. 
 Assigned duties include: 
Process customer payments by operating a register and handling cash and credit card transactions. 
Greet and assists customers while anticipating their needs. 
Count, organize, and balance cash drawer; fill out the cashier slip; and make deposits. 
Adheres to cash handling policies and procedures. 
Ensures security of company assets. 
Maintains excellent customer service and a positive attitude towards guests, customers, clients, co-workers, etc. 
Managing the servery which includes but is not limited to coffee/tea/bakery station, replenishment of confectionary products, and replenishing products in the Express cooler 
Making sure there is sufficient supply of coffee bags as well as filling the bakery station when baked goods are brought out. 
Provide the manager with information on what is needed for the weekly order from our suppliers. 
Maintaining cleanliness of the work station. 
Other duties and tasks as assigned by manager. 
Qualifications 
Previous customer service experience preferred. 
Previous cash handling experience preferred. 
Basic math and counting skills required. 
Demonstrates interpersonal and communication skills, both written and verbal. 
Must be able to work independently with limited supervision. 
Requires occasional lifting, carrying, pushing, pulling of up to 25 lbs. 
Must be available to work flexible hours including evening and weekends. 
 The finalist candidate for this position will need to complete and successfully pass a Criminal Background Check and a Vulnerable Sector Screening (if the operations we service require this). If hired for this position, your continued employment with us would be conditional upon Aramark being satisfied that you have successfully cleared both these pre-conditions.
