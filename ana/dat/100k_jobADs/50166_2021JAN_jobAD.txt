 Hydroponics Grower - Relocate to Leamington (Windsor Area) 
 We are the largest greenhouse cucumber producer in North America and are seeking an experienced Grower to join their team. Reporting to the Master Grower, your diploma/degree in agriculture, coupled with years of hands-on greenhouse operations experience, leadership and diplomacy abilities, as well as advanced know-how of plant nutrition, at all crop growth stages, are required, to step into this role with confidence. 
 What’s in it for you: 
 
 
 Work for a leader in the produce industry - our facility is new, modern, and has the latest technology available 
 
 
 Leamington offers a small town lifestyle, while also being within 50KM of Windsor, Chatham-Kent - housing marketing is affordable 
 
 
 Pay is competitive 
 
 
 Work with an awesome team of tenured staff and industry professionals 
 
 
 What we’re looking for: 
 
 
 Greenhouse experience is preferred 
 
 
 Knowledge of irrigation systems/strategies for optimal greenhouse environment 
 
 
 Ability to work under pressure and to strict deadlines to ensure crop work is completed on time 
 
 
 Ability to work in a multilingual environment and be a team player 
 
 
 Good computer skills within Microsoft Office an Asset 
 
 
 What you’ll be doing: 
 
 
 Working on irrigation systems/strategies for optimal greenhouse environment 
 
 
 Assess and interpret water samples/sheets
