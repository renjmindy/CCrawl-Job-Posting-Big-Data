 Registered Nurse (RGN, RMN or RNLD) 
 An outstanding job opportunity has arisen for a dedicated RGN, RMN or RNLD to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the Telford, Shropshire area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This nursing home offers luxury accommodation and the highest standards of quality residential nursing, palliative and specialist care to residents including those with Alzheimer's and other dementia-related illnesses 
 
 As a Nurse your key duties include: 
 
 Providing leadership and support for support staff and act as a champion for residents' safety and dignity 
 Maintaining skills and knowledge and ensure that care is delivered according to latest guidelines and best practice evidence available 
 Ensuring the ongoing safety of service users, residents, staff and visitors through systematic assessment, monitoring and reviewing processes 
 Ensuring that clear and accurate records and communication systems relating to clinical and care delivery are maintained and effectively used 
 Acting as a role model for good clinical and care governance practice 
 Taking charge of the unit regularly and, in the absence of the management team, accepting responsibility for the smooth and effective running of the unit 
 Helping to maintain the security of the building and its environment 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Provide both residential and nursing care to older people.
 
 
 
     Able to show empathy and warmth
 
 
 
     A bubbly and outgoing personality
 
 
 
     Dementia experience
 
 
 
     Able to write care plans
 
 
 
     Assist in assessments
 
 
 
     Having knowledge of mental health would be advantage
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent flexible salary. 
 ****This exciting position is a Full Time role for 38.5 hours a week working throughout Night Shifts. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 Excellent Induction

 Paid NMC membership fee 
 Workplace Pension 
 A range of holiday, retail and leisure discounts 
 Nurse Mentor and Refer a Friend bonus scheme 
 Many opportunities for promotion
