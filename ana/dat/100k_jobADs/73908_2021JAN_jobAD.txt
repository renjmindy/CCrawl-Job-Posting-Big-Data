 Web/CMS Developer (Craft) LOCALPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 We're looking for a Craft CMS developer in our Boston office. We recently redeveloped our Craft 3 multisite which includes  https://www.datto.com   https://www.backupify.com  and  https://www.openmesh.com  and we are seeking a team player who loves Craft and likes solving problems. 
 A look inside the job: 
 
 Develop, test, and deliver clean, secure, production-ready PHP code 
 Analyze existing code and make dynamic refactoring changes as required 
 Be a critical thinker and creative problem solver, with acute attention to detail 
 Be a confident developer and a motivated self-starter with a drive to learn and absorb new technologies 
 Be a team player, coordinating with our Boston and Norwalk-based web team to craft innovative solutions 
 Be able to effectively communicate ideas and strategies with project managers and other members of the web team 
 
 About You: 
 
 A minimum of 3 years experience in PHP development 
 Strong understanding of the MVC pattern / OOP 
 Strong familiarity with Craft CMS or other PHP based CMS development 
 Strong knowledge of PHP frameworks such as Yii, Laravel, Symfony, or Zend 
 MySQL experience, with proven ability to write efficient SQL queries 
 Solid understanding of web application security principles and practices 
 Efficiency using the command line, as well as CLI tools and utilities 
 Demonstrable fundamental knowledge of front-end technologies including JavaScript, HTML, CSS, AJAX, etc. 
 Strong knowledge of git 
 Proven understanding of task runners/pre-processors such as grunt, gulp, or webpackh 
 
 Oh, and we have a great time, we make cool stuff (4 storey LED wall with updates through Craft and integrated with Slack? Yeah...), have unlimited paid vacation (yep), great health care, stock options, yadda yadda yadda. Come work with us! 
 Unfortunately we can't accept remote applications, but we do have relocation assistance for the right candidate.
