 About Our Client 
 Our client is a locally owned Engineering and Fabrication company with experience in workshop fabrication and repairs, on site repairs and maintenance. Their clients range from small individual consumers to large commercial and industrial businesses. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking a skilled and experienced Mechanical Fitter with welding/boiler making experience to commence at a workshop in Hemmant. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Experience in fault finding 
 
 
 Assure safe working practices 
 
 
 Experience working on mechanical equipment 
 
 
 Welding skills, tig, mig, stick 
 
 
 Complete machinery inspections 
 
 
 Repair and test machinery 
 
 
 Skills & Qualifications 
 
 
 Trade Qualification 
 
 
 Recent experience with fabrication and fitting structural steel 
 
 
 Experience working within a workshop environment 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Full PPE 
 
 
 Current Resume with minimum 2 x work reference's 
 
 
 Availability for an immediate start 
 
 
 Reliable and strong work ethics 
 
 
 Culture & Benefits 
 
 
 Full time hours 
 
 
 Ongoing work for the right candidate 
 
 
 Wages paid weekly
