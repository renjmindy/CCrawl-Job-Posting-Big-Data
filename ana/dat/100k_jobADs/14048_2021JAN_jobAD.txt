 Role overview 
 We at Platform team  provides the services, tools, and knowhow to make the Business units operationally independent. The domains we cover are Android/iOS core development and CI/CD, Data Platform providing analytics as a service, and back end core development and Infrastructure. 
 As an iOS Engineer  in the Platform Infrastructure team, your main objective will be to help the developers in the Business Units to deliver their features as fast, secure and efficient as possible. You will provide building pipelines, dashboards, and ways to quickly find issues in the app builds. You will also own and maintain shared libraries used by the app as well as parts of the code with a focus on core functionality such as network protocols, event logging, and feature flags. 
 What we expect from you: 
 
 5+ years experience in programming using Swift or Objective C 
 Deep understanding and hands-on experience in iOS, development tools, application life cycle, asynchronous server communication, and UX optimisation 
 Hands-on experience with different app architectures and design patterns 
 Experience with unit testing 
 Solid proficiency in Core Data 
 Good understanding of networking protocols 
 Ability to plan and prioritise your own work items 
 Ability to interact with iOS team members and other stakeholders 
 Fluent in English, in speaking and writing 
 
 What will you work on? 
 
 Being responsible for developing and maintaining shared libraries 
 Maintaining and building tools for release management (Release dashboards, CI/CD pipelines, etc) 
 Being responsible for source code management using Bitbucket 
 Helping other teams to build Analytics and Logging pipelines, Networking layers, Feature flags, and application frameworks Being proactive, and aware of iOS platform changes and helping feature teams to be up to date 
 Drive technical improvement by applying software craftsmanship in everyday work as well as by identifying and leading larger refactoring/improvement initiatives. 
 Drive cross-team technical initiatives. 
 Working together with Release team and Android platform development Being an active member of the Tech Forum
