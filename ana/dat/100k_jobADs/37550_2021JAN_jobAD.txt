In both groups, there will be leader of the group in addition to about 10 people, and each group is responsible for separately about 3 000 University staff and students can use the client information technology support and maintenance services. 
 Information Technology Specialist acts as the supervisor of local support group also organizes group tasks and to keep in touch with your customers.
