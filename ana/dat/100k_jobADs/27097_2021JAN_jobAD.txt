 Diesel Fitter 
 Job description 
 
 
 
 3-month role 
 high potential for ongoing work $35/hour + overtime penalties + superannuation Role based at Gepps Cross About Our Client Our client is a large workshop who works on Maintenance / Overhauls / Servicing of trucks trailers and utes. About the Role Our client is currently looking for a Diesel fitter to join their team in Gepps Cross. Reporting to the Workshop Supervisor, the Heavy Vehicle Maintenance Technician will perform maintenance and repair work at the workshop and breakdown work as required throughout Metro Adelaide. Duties & Responsibilities Working on light and heavy vehicles Fault Finding / Repairs / Diagnostics Maintenance / Overhauls / Servicing - Ability to manage and coordinated Maintenance plan of all fleet. Trouble Shooting, Good diagnostic skills Breakdowns Able to maintain records Demonstrated commitment to equipment reliability and quality outcomes Skills and Experience A current HR licence min Relevant trades qualifications Prev  Job title:
