 Sarkari Jobs Quest - A new Journey Begins for Freshers in India 
 Posted by SJ December 4, 2020December 13, 2020 Posted in Uncategorized 
 The Indiapulse team continues to expand and excel in the field of education and career. In a recent event, it has launched another website  Sarkari Jobs  to cater to the growing needs of freshers and professionals. The team, through its jobs portal, is committed to bring accurate, authentic and timely updates for  all government jobs in India  as well as recruitment notifications from some top multi national organizations in India. 
 While the focus is on career opportunities available for Freshers, Sarkari or Government jobs are available for experienced professionals as well. The focus remain on FRESHERS and candidates with no or very little experience in their field. 
 Features & Services: 
 Main focus of Sarkarijobs.com, as mentioned, remains providing fastest possible alerts for all government jobs notifications. Other services include updates for  Admit Cards ,  Sarkari Result ,  Employment News  and Current Affairs.  Sarkari Naukri  is also part of the  India Pulse  and Recruitment Alerts portals providing video notifications of all recruitment done by government and major multinational companies in India. At the time of writing this blog, the team is also evaluating how a discussion and community forum can be beneficial for candidates with varied interests.
