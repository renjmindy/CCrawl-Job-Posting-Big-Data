 ABOUT US 
 At Nleru Ventures, OUR WHY and DRIVE is INVESTING IN NIGERIAN FOUNDERS AND NIGERIAN BUSINESSES. We invest in Nigerian founders and Nigerian businesses globally. We identify the best early-stage growth-driven startups with the potential to become billion dollar companies. We work closely with the startup, providing them pre-seed funding, professional services and advisory services. 
 ABOUT THE ROLE 
 We are looking for a university student or graduate with less than 1 year investment analysis experience with keen interest to gain useful work experience as an Investment Analyst. 
 KEY RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 Perform all elements of company analysis, sector analysis, economic analysis and idea generation 
 Perform ongoing analysis and monitoring of active portfolio investment 
 Share key insights and key recommendations with the team 
 Attend meetings with management teams of current and potential investments and other value chain stakeholders of current and potential investments 
 Develop specific market knowledge in sustainable sectors and impact focussed sector 
 Write investment cases, investment blogs, market commentary and other content as required 
 Collaborate with key stakeholders in our value chain 
 
 Essential Qualifications / Experience 
 
 Able to commit to working every Saturday from 9 am - 5 pm UK time for 3 - 6 months. 
 Strong desire to gain valuable work experience as an Investment Analyst 
 Willingness to gain in-depth knowledge and understanding of the Nigerian financial market dynamics and performance drivers 
 Proficient/willingness to construct, maintain and interpret financial models 
 Proficient in Excel 
 Proficient/willingness to learn Python for investment modelling and analysis 
 Proficient in conducting research 
 Doing or completed a quantitative related bachelor's degree 
 
 Essential Skills / Competencies 
 
 Candidate has a strong work ethic, can work independently and proactive 
 Candidate is flexible, commercial orientated and works well in a team 
 Proficient in Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Word, and Microsoft PowerPoint 
 Fluent in English with strong writing and oral presentation skills.
