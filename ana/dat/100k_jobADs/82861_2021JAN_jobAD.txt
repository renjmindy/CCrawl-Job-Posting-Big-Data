 ABOUT OUR CLIENT 
 Our client is one of the world's largest globally diversified natural resource companies. Their operations comprise around 150 mining and metallurgical sites and oil production assets. 
 ABOUT THE ROLE 
 Seeking experienced Trainer Assessor with Simulator experience for a mine near Glenden. Prior experience on simulators is required. 
 DUTIES & RESPONSBILBITIES 
 
 
 Conduct Pre-Start Checks on Machinery 
 
 
 Train Operators on equipment 
 
 
 Operate Equipment Safely 
 
 
 SKILLS & EXPERIENCE 
 
 
 Trainer Assessor Ticket 
 
 
 CAT 789, Terex 4440 Experience/ Sign Offs 
 
 
 Experience with simulator training 
 
 
 Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Standard 11 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 CULTURE & BENEFITS 
 
 
 Monday - Friday 
 
 
 Ongoing Position 
 
 
 Close to Mackay 
 
 
 Camp & Meals Supplied 
 
 
 Super Paid on all rostered hours
