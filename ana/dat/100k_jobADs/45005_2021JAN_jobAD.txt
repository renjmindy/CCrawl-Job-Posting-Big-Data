 About Wells Fargo 
 Wells Fargo & Company (NYSE: WFC) is a leading global financial services company headquartered in San Francisco (United States). Wells Fargo has offices in over 30 countries and territories. Our business outside of the U.S. mostly focuses on providing banking services for large corporate, government and financial institution clients. We have worldwide expertise and services to help our customers improve earnings, manage risk, and develop opportunities in the global marketplace. Our global reach offers many opportunities for you to develop a career with Wells Fargo. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you will feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience. We are looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you. 
 
 Market Job Description 
 ** 
 About Wells Fargo India 
 ** 
  Wells Fargo India  enables global talent capabilities for Wells Fargo Bank NA., by supporting business lines and staff functions across Technology, Operations, Risk, Audit, Process Excellence, Automation and Product, Analytics and Modeling. We are operating in Hyderabad, Bengaluru and Chennai locations 
 Department Overview: 
 Wholesale Technologies supports various applications for the Wholesale line of businesses. The teams are spread across various locations including EGS. Team member will be working from any of the EGS India work locations. The technology team has various applications based on Java Stack, Microsoft, DWH, Etc.,. 
 ** 
 About the Role 
 ** 
 Profitability, Pricing and Analytics group is seeking a software engineer who performs automation framework development activities ranging from analysis through deployment in a highly complex business analytics environment implemented using various Data tools - Informatica ETL, EXADATA, TERADATA and OBIEE 12c. 
Highly skilled application developer to work in AGILE methodology as part of self-sufficient scrum team with core Java based application development, building data driven automation framework and CI CD with strong experience in RDBMS, UFT and Selenium tool. Responsible for analysis, development and interaction with global team. 
 ** 
 Responsibilities 
 ** 
 
 
 Design, develop and execute software solutions to address business issues 
 
 
 Provide architectural blueprints and technical leadership to our IT team 
 
 
 Evaluate and recommend tools, technologies and processes to ensure the highest quality product platform 
 
 
 Collaborate with peer organizations, quality assurance and end users to produce cutting-edge software solutions 
 
 
 Interpret business requirements to articulate the business needs to be addressed 
 
 
 Able to direct the development process from conception to completion, and oversee the efforts needed 
 
 
 Troubleshoot code level problems quickly and efficiently 
 
 
 Document development process in order to develop procedures for application use 
 
 
 ** 
 Essential Qualifications 
 :** 
 
 
 At least 8+ years of hands-on IT experience in designing and developing enterprise applications using Oracle 11g/12c SQL, PLSQL and Unix Shell Scripting 
 
 
 Demonstrate strong hands-on experience in engineering Oracle based enterprise solutions. 
 
 
 Experience in OFSAA Infrastructure components (Rule Engine, DQ, Business Process, Measures) is advantage. 
 
 
 Experience creating Data Model using ERWIN or similar tool. 
 
 
 Experience designing inbound and outbound interfaces. 
 
 
 Must have strong knowledge on Data Warehousing (DWH) concepts, ETL concepts and data analysis capabilities. 
 
 
 Demonstrate understanding of Data archiving, Data reconciliation, Error handling mechanism, standards and best practices. 
 
 
 Must have hands-on experience in Linux/UNIX shell scripting. 
 
 
 Work with onsite partners and business analysts to understand and develop database/DW related requirements. 
 
 
 Exposure to Job Scheduling tools like Autosys. 
 Non-Technical: 
 
 
 Good problem solving and analytical skills 
 
 
 Significantly contribute to design and analysis sessions to ensure sound team decision-making 
 
 
 Actively involved in system and user acceptance testing activities, including issue resolution. 
 
 
 Complete technical documentation to ensure system is fully documented. 
 
 
 Excellent verbal and written communication skills. 
 
 
 Share best practices and lessons learned from past implementation efforts. 
 
 
 The flexibility to work outside of normal working hours to meet deadlines. 
 
 
 ** 
 Desired Qualifications: 
 ** 
 
 
 Experience in Banking Domain 
 
 
 Exposure to Agile Development Methodology 
 
 
 Exposure to OBIEE, TERADATA. Informatica
