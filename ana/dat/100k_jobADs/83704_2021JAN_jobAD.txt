 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group is actively looking to appoint an Accounts Administrator on an on a Permanent basis for a business based in Coleshill. You will be reporting directly into the team manager and working alongside a small and friendly team. 
 This role is to start as soon as possible and will be paying up to £28,000 for the right candidate. 
 Responsibilities include: 
 (Full job description is available) 
 
 Purchase Ledger 
 Sales Ledger 
 Bank Reconciliation 
 Review aged debtors / Creditors 
 Building internal stakeholder relationships 
 Monitor Transactions 
 Work towards KPIs 
 
 Is this role right for you? 
 This is a great opportunity for you to join as an Accounts Administrator. If you enjoy working in a team, often on your own initiative and have excellent excel skills this could be right for you. 
 We are looking for someone who is deadline driven and ambitious with study support for AAT for the right person. 
 Benefits: 
 
 Salary up to £28k per annum + Study Support (AAT) 
 Pension 
 Free onsite parking 
 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
