 Are you a Warehouse Shift Manager looking for a new challenge in 2020? I'm recruiting for a Shift Manager who can drive productivity and shift KPIs for a dynamic 3PL logistics business based at their site near Minworth… 
 I have this exciting opportunity to join a leading 3PL distribution business, they are looking to recruit a Warehouse Shift Manager as they go through an exciting period implementing new contracts into their site based near Minworth (Birmingham). The successful candidate will earn a competitive salary c£34,000 with additional benefits… 
 The new Shift Manager will have an autonomous role reporting directly to a Head of Operations, and as such you will manage your team with a focus on productivity and driving performance. You will manage your team accordingly and as part of the role you will train and mentor them where necessary. 
 The site is a dynamic fast-moving retail distribution environment where the key responsibilities will be to engage with your team to drive shift productivity. There is definite career progression for the right candidate as the company have a proven record of promoting from within, they have worked very hard on producing a great culture onsite where everyone is pulling in the same direction. A candidate with experience within e-fulfilment could transition nicely into this role although good people managers from fast moving warehouse environments will also be considered, as with most retail distribution environments understanding fluctuating volumes will be paramount. 
 The company is in an exciting growth phase over the next 12 months which will mean new opportunities moving forwards at all levels, with any retail distribution environment you may be required to work the occasional weekend shift to meet company needs. 
 Based near Minworth, Birmingham you will be paid a competitive salary c£34,000, the role will suit a self-starter who is team driven and can communicate at all levels. 
 For more information please apply to the role or contact Phil Birch on 07970 211517.
