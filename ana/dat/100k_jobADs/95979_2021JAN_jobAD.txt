 Customer Service representative 
 Are you an looking for a new Customer Service role, working Monday to Friday 9-5? 
 We are looking for a passionate and driven Individual to work for a global market leading company who employees over 140,000 people worldwide. 
 This position is for a 7-month contract starting asap. 
 This is an exciting opportunity to gain exposure within account management as you shall be managing a portfolio of customers, increasing sales by being able to build rapport and identifying 
 Key duties: 
 
 Proactively manage a portfolio of clients to increase sales revenue. 
 Maximse sales opportunities for target products in support of campaigns 
 Work with the sales teams to refine target customers and contact data to improve campaign efficiency 
 Pro-actively manage Customers accounts and conduct customer satisfaction surveys 
 Manage orders, communicate order changes and ensure service levels are kept to a high standard 
 Take responsibility for all customer queries relating to your agreed target customers and proactively manage until conclusion 
 Be an ambassador for the company - the face/ voice of to all Internally and externally. 
 
 Key skills: 
 
 Proven Customer service within an Account management role 
 Excellent communication skills, ability to converse on all levels 
 Organised, proactive, and has good time management skills 
 Flexible and adaptable to changes 
 Strong IT skills, specifically in Excel- exposure to SAP would be advantageous 
 
 Should you wish to discuss this position further then please contact me directly
