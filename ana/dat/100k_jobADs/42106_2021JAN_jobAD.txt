 Your opportunity to join the team behind the UK's leading virtual reality experience and help build a scalable, lean and tech-enabled finance function. Over the next 18 months, OTHERWORLD is expanding from 1 to 4+ sites and growing from 30 to 150 people. We are building out a finance function to support this growth. 
 You will be the second finance hire and will work closely with the Head of Finance to develop and introduce new systems, processes and controls that will become part of the DNA of OTHERWORLD for years to come. 
 It is a broad role with the scope for long-term progression for the right performer. You will support various key stakeholders across the business. From supporting the Head of People with payroll benefits, to providing fixed cost reports to the the Head of Operations and site General Managers. We are looking for someone ambitious, who is keen to develop a career in finance and preferably has experience of working in a start-up or hospitality business. You will be interested in challenging standard procedures, and inquisitive about exploring more efficient solutions, even to the most routine processes. 
 As Finance Associate, you'll be based in our East London office, working directly with and reporting to the Head of Finance. 
 You will: 
 
 Prepare monthly balance sheet reconciliations 
 Track and reconcile bank statements 
 Work closely with site General Managers on fixed cost reports 
 Process monthly payroll and keep organised records 
 Own the Accounts Payable function and reconcile supplier statements 
 Provide administrative support during budget preparation 
 Perform ad-hoc finance analysis and reporting when required 
 Research new systems, processes and technology products that will help support an efficient finance function 
 
 Requirements 
 
 1-3 years experience in a finance role 
 Good knowledge of accounting and bookkeeping procedures 
 Proficiency in creating spreadsheets and using financial functions, ideally familiar with Google Workspace and Google Sheets 
 Experience with Xero or equivalent accounting systems 
 Character and excitement to work in a start-up environment 
 Excellent analytical ability and attention to detail 
 Strong passion for hospitality, gaming and technology 
 Curiosity to challenge the norms 
 
 Benefits 
 
 This role is available on a full-time basis 
 Salary of £26-34k depending on experience and aptitude 
 Potential for performance-related bonus 
 Share options depending on length of service 
 Discounts on VR, food and drink in our venues 
 Support for study days and exam fees depending on level of qualification
