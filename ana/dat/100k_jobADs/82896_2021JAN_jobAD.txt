 Junior Accountant in Limassol 
 Full Time 
 
 __Post Date: January 8, 2021 
 __Apply Before: March 31, 2021 
 
 Job Description 
 On behalf of one of our clients a regulated Forex broker, we are seeking to recruit a Junior Accountant to join their team in Limassol. 
 Job Responsibilities 
 
 Bookkeeping of invoices 
 Petty cash management 
 Bank statement monthly reconciliation 
 Supplier invoices check 
 Carry out any further duties assigned by the line manager 
 Contribute to a positive and dynamic working environment 
 Constant communication with the billing department in regards to the existing methods of withdrawals, problems associated with payment systems, transfer of funds, provision of the necessary documents and so on 
 Communication with the IT department in the matter of servers’ payments, monitoring and adding invoices, solving problems with payments 
 Collecting documentation on request from the accounting department or auditors 
 Reconciliation of client funds (excel spreadsheet) 
 Perform daily reconciliations per payment provider 
 Provide feedback of information regarding payment providers reconciliations to other departments and/or to management whenever needed 
 
 Job Requirements 
 
 University Degree in Accounting/Finance/Management or similar field will be considered an advantage 
 Fluency in the English language 
 Excellent communication skills 
 Great attention to detail 
 Able to work in a fast paced environment and multi-task 
 Ability to structure and analyze large amounts of data 
 Attention to detail, ability to independently make decisions and work under pressure 
 Ability to work with MS Word, MS Excel and generally computer literate 
 Strong team spirit, organizational and time management skills 
 
 How to Apply 
 If interested and you match all the requirements, please send your CV to  andriana.p@work-channel.com  , including the title of the position in the subject line 
 Please note  that due to the high volume of applications received, only successful candidates will be contacted
