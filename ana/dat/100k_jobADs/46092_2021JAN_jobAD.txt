 Nursing Aide Job Abroad Saudi 
 Posted on  October 2, 2012  by  caregiver 
 Teknika Skills and Trade Services Inc. recently had their Job Order approved allowing them to recruit qualified applicants for  Nursing Aide Job Abroad Saudi . The said nursing aide job abroad Saudi had its Job Order approved last September 10, 2012 with job vacancies for 80 applicants who are: 
 
 Female 
 22-40 years old 
 At least high school graduates 
 With or without experience 
 
 If you are interested in applying for the said Nursing Aide Job Abroad Saudi, proceed as soon as possible to the office of Teknika Skills and Trade Services Inc. and personally submit your comprehensive resume. For any inquiries regarding the said nursing aide job abroad Saudi, you can contact Teknika Skills and Trade Services Inc. by way of their telephone or mobile numbers listed below.
