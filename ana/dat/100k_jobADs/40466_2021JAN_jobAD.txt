 Role Overview - Registered Nurse Jobs 
 Our Registered Nurses work across a variety of clients supporting their immediate and planned needs within metro, regional, rural and remote facilities. Our dedicated RN team work towards achieving shared goals providing service that is nationally recognised. 
 Registered Nurses are the backbone of our business and provide exceptional service throughout our WorkPac network, including private and public hospitals, aged care, primary care and rehabilitation facilities, correctional facilities, mining and allied health across the country. Our Field Team Members are highly motivated and dedicated Registered Nurses, who are committed to providing an outstanding customer experience at every interaction! 
 About You 
 If you love a fast-paced and ever-changing environment where you could work close to home or experience travel opportunities if you choose, then WorkPac Health and Social Care have the variety you are looking for. We are always seeking energetic and enthusiastic personalities, with demonstrated experience, who have the ability to work well as part of a broader team and autonomously. 
 Key Experience and Qualifications Required 
 
 
 Current AHPRA practising registration and ideally 3+ years registered nursing experience 
 
 
 Current National police clearance 
 
 
 Exceptional customer service skills 
 
 
 Demonstrated time management and facility operational skills 
 
 
 Experience within high activity workplaces within multi-disciplinary teams 
 
 
 Critical thinking and collaborative approach to coordination of care 
 
 
 ICU, Midwifery, NICU/PICU and Emergency experience is highly sought 
 
 
 Ventilator competency is highly desirable
