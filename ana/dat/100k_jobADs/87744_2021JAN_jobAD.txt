 Finance Analyst 
 Finance Analyst 
 
 Location 
 
 Coventry, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £24000.00 - £30000.00 per annum + + Study Support 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Laura Parkes 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 laura.parkes@gleeson-rg.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH18050_1580407443 
 
 Published: 
 
 12 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-02-29 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 A professional services business based in Coventry are looking for a Finance Analyst to join their business reporting into the senior management team. You will be stepping into a role with clear career progression routes, supportive management and excellent training. 
 Progression is a real prospect in this business, not a false promise, and high achievers will be pushed into senior roles and provided with additional responsibilities, which will include line management and project lead opportunities. 
 You will be provided all the support needed to get up to speed in an extremely commercial environment. It is a perfect opportunity for an accountant who is part way or almost through their CIMA or ACCA and wanting to develop on your already strong finance experience as you aim to qualify in CIMA or ACCA. 
 Ideally, you'll have previously been working in an assistant management accounting or finance analyst role and looking to further develop your skill set. 
 
 Budget and Business Plan preparation 
 Cost Centre Management (Control, Monthly Accounting and Forecasting review & analysis). 
 Working with Business managers and providing accurate and timely information and reporting 
 Providing financial analysis and evaluation 
 Contributing to the building of robust budgets and forecasts. 
 Assisting in the undertaking of the month end process 
 Ensuring the data used is consistent and accurate 
 Assisting in compiling and producing financial performance reports, 
 Ensuring reporting is relevant, timely and accurate and providing commentaries and analysis which adequately explain any data variances 
 Supporting Management Accountants in maintaining and monitoring the financial control framework. 
 
 About you: 
 
 CIMA/ACCA part-qualified 
 Confident communicating with internal and external stakeholders. 
 Excellent IT skills including Excel 
 Ambitious with a desire to succeed 
 
 To apply for the Finance Analyst position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
