 Creating An Award-Winning Workplace Where Parents Are Supported 
 Workiva was recognized on the 2020 Best Workplaces for Parents list by Great Place to Work. In honor of the award, members of the It Takes Village Employee Resource Group share how Workiva has supported them – especially during a challenging year.  17 People. Multiple Locations. One Team Culture. 
 Last year in a pre-pandemic world, the Presentations product team at Workiva set a goal to establish a single, unified team culture across our locations. They wanted everyone to have timely access to all information and feel connected to the team identity. When Workiva moved to 100% remote work at the beginning of 2020, the team was prepared to implement the following tactics to keep everyone informed, connected and moving forward on goals and projects. 
 November 19, 2020
