 $17/hr - Construction Laborer 
 by  Mickaela  | Jan 15, 2021 | | 
 
 Full Time 
 Lincoln, NE 
 Posted 1 week ago 
 
 Direct placement opportunity with a great company available!! 
 This position is a labor position and includes but is not limited to framing, spanning of wire, installing conduit, assist on the ground with setting poles, loading and unloading material, operating a bobcat, tamping, and additional duties. Previous experience in construction is preferred but not a requirement. 
 Must have good work history with verifiable references. 
 Hours may range from 40-60 per week, Monday thru Friday. Saturdays may be required certain times of the year. Weeknight travel may be required. 
 Minimum Qualifications: 
 • High School equivalency 
 • Able to clear high security background check 
 • No loss of valid driver's license for 2 years 
 • Able to lift / push / pull 100 pounds (physical required) 
 • Stand kneel / bend on uneven surfaces for 13 hours per day 
 • Work 100% outdoors

 • Dental Insurance 
 • Vacation Pay 
 • Holiday Pay 
 • Matching Retirement Plan 
 • Tool Purchase Program 
 • Company Provided Uniforms 
 • Generous Per Diem 
 • Reimbursable Apprenticeship Programs
