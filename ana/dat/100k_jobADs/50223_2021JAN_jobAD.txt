 Management Training 
 Our business is growing rapidly with increasing demand for our services from our clients.  STC Direct Philly  is looking to expand new markets in the next year. Along the way, Account Managers will benefit from an intensive Management Training Program in all aspects of business management, sales and marketing, and team leadership.
