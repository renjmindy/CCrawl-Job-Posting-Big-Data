 Inn Steakhouse Host 
 Horseshoe Resort 
 __Barrie, ON 
 Apply Now 
 JOB PURPOSE: 
To greet all guests in a courteous, helpful, and friendly manner 
To help other staff when needed 
 DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES: 
To greet and welcome guests to the restaurant in a courteous and prompt manner 
Seat guests with menu’s 
To wipe down and reset tables promptly once the guests have left 
Assist service staff as needed 
To answer the phone and take orders 
Take out garbage and recycling at end of shift 
Inform kitchen of large groups / special circumstances 
Empty dish cart when full 
Other duties as assigned 
 HEALTH & SAFETY: 
Follow the general rules as well as all Health & Safety procedures as outlined in the Horseshoe Valley Resort Health & Safety Policy. 
Work with Health & Safety Committee to fully comply with their requests 
Work in a safe manner as outlined in the Horseshoe Valley Resorts Health & Safety responsibilities for workers 
Inform Manager of any unsafe working conditions observed 
 GENERAL RULES: 
Perform all duties and responsibilities in a timely and efficient manner in accordance with the established policies and procedures 
Always treat the guest and all resort employees with respect, courtesy, efficiency, and friendliness 
Always work as a team 
Always conduct yourself in a professional manner 
 QUALIFICATIONS & REQUIREMENTS: 
Ability to cope with pressure 
Must be able to lift up to 40 lbs
