 Mobile Plant Operator 
 Job description 
 
 
 $47.68/ hour 
 
 
 Work 12.5 and get paid up to 13 hours 
 
 
 Ongoing Position 
 
 
 ABOUT OUR CLIENT 
 Our client is one of the world's largest globally diversified natural resource companies. Their operations comprise around 150 mining and metallurgical sites and oil production assets. 
 ABOUT THE ROLE 
 Seeking experienced Dump Truck Operators or Multi Skilled Operators to commence at a mine located near Glenden. 
 DUTIES & RESPONSBILBITIES 
 
 
 Conduct Pre-Start Checks on Machinery 
 
 
 Operate Equipment Safely 
 
 
 SKILLS & EXPERIENCE 
 Experience operating the following equipment; 
 
 - Trucks; Terex 4400, CAT 785, 789, 793 
 
 - Dozer; CAT D10, D11 
 - Grader; CAT 16, 24 
 - Loader; CAT 988, 992 
 
 
 Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Standard 11 or Equivalent 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 
 CULTURE & BENEFITS 
 
 
 7/7 Roster 
 
 
 Upskilling Available 
 
 
 Camp & Meals Supplied 
 
 
 Super Paid on all rostered hours 
 
 
 ABOUT WORKPAC 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leaving companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
