 Mobile Plant Operator 
 Job description 
 MECHANICAL and ELECTRIC TRUCK OPERATORS 
 
 7/7 Roster 
 Immediate Start 
 Meals and accommodation supplied 
 
 WorkPac currently have a number of opportunities for experienced Electric and Mechanical Truck Operators to commence work immediately. This work could start as early as next week. You will form part of and contribute to the Mine Operations Department of a Bowen Basin Mine near Glenden. 
 An absolute commitment to working safely, complying with all site safety standards and ensuring a safe and environmentally healthy workplace is essential. It is imperative that you possess the ability and aptitude to maintain high standards of safety, equipment serviceability, housekeeping and compliance with mine working procedures. 
 To be successful in these roles you will have the following: 
 1. Experienced Mechanical and Electric Truck (MT 4400 or similar) 
2. Current Standard 11 or obtain 
3. Current Coal Board Medical or obtain 
4. Current Driver's Licence 
5. A willingness to work rotating rosters 
6. Ability to contribute and work positively in a team environment 
7. Sound Work History 
 Candidates please note that a medical including drug and alcohol testing is required as part of the recruitment process.
