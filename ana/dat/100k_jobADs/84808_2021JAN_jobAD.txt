 Are you ready for a new entrepreneurial challenge and keen to join an extremely ambitious Climate Tech startup? Then you are at the right place: we at Sequest (www.sequest.io) are building a solution that will unlock global-scale climate action. We are looking for a co-founder who is passionate about sustainability, keen to get stuck in from the beginning and own the product development. 
 What we are looking for: 
 
 
  Experience in building a hybrid hardware/software product before. Experience in an early-stage startup or launched a product from scratch.
 
 
 
  Hands-on mentality; budget and time mean you will need to roll up your sleeves.
 
 
 
  Strong interest in sustainability.
 
 
 
 What you can expect: 
 
 
  As a Co-Founder and Chief Product Officer, you will have a lot of responsibility
 
 
 
  IT infrastructure in place and MVP close to completion
 
 
 
  You will make an impact from day 1 with a role tailored to your skillset
 
 
 
  You will benefit from appropriate founder equity levels.
 
 
 
 Please share your CV (hello@sequest.io) and add any other relevant information you wish to draw to our attention in support of your application. This position is flexible. Get in touch if this sounds like an interesting opportunity and you want more information!
