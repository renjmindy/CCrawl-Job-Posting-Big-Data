 The Latest Work at Home Job Openings 
 Published by  aw1219  on November 13, 2017 
 We may earn money or products from some of the companies mentioned in this post. 
 Looking to find out who is hiring remote workers to work from home right now? If so, you have come to the right place because I have included a few work at home job leads that you can read about and apply to online, if you are interested. If you are new to this website or not a regular visitor, please make sure that you subscribe to this blog via email on the right hand section of the sidebar so that you can get the latest work at home job postings delivered to your email inboxes. 
 Feel free to browse this entire website and make a special visit to the the  weekly job section  and  Friday freebie pages  for additional work at home job leads posted last week. some of the openings may not be filled as of yet so I encourage you to apply. If you need more information about any of the jobs that I have posted, please contact the company directly so that you can get all of your questions answered. Please be sure to share this post if you know anyone who has the goal or desire to work from home. 
 Work at home Job Leads for 11/13/2017 
 Customer Service 
 Quill, a Staples company is looking for reps to partner with their customers to determine office product and related business needs. You will need 1 year customer service experience, be a good listener, have Microsoft Office skills and can train onsite for 3 weeks. This is a full time permanent work at home position. 
 Customer Service Reps 
 Enterprise Holdings is hiring reps to answer general questions, research reservations, address complaints and other related duties. You must have 2 years experience, be willing to work weekends and can handle difficult conversations. Hourly pay. Hiring in several states. 
 Premier Support Technician 
 Asurion is hiring remote workers who live in the Nashville area to support products across multiple applications including hardware, software, apps, etc. Requires 6 months customer service experience. Competitive pay with benefits. 
 Cable Support 
 Sutherland Global is seeking agents to take inbound calls from customers to assist with questions about products, services, answer programming questions or troubleshoot issues. You will need basic keyboarding skills, good verbal and problem solving skills and can multitask. Paid training. 
 Storage Sales 
 
 Uhaul is seeking agents to answer incoming calls regarding storage rental inquiries, accept tenant payments and make reservations for storage rooms, Uhaul trucks, trailers, towing devices, etc. You must enjoy helping people, have a good understanding of US geography and want to work from home part-time. 
 Hotel PBX Operator 
 Tyme Global Technologies is hiring those who can speak clearly to provide excellent services to hotel and their guests. You must have excellent communication and good computer skills. Pays $9.50 per hour. If interested, please email your resume to  jobs@tymeglobal.com  with a 2 minute audio recording of your voice explaining why this role interests you and about yourself. 
 Starry Expert 
 Starry is looking for someone to be responsible for responding to customer emails, phone calls and live chats. You must have excellent customer service skills, have 1 year customer service or troubleshooting customer service experience, and strong organizational skills. You must be based in New York or Massachusetts. 
 Video Captioners 
 Rev.com is looking for people to watch a wide variety of video content and add text captions. You can work whenever you want. Pays $0.40 to $0.75 per video minute. Weekly pay via PayPal. 
 Online Teachers 
 VIP Kid is currently looking for online teachers to teach young students in China via video-conferencing. Teachers will earn on average between $14-$22 per hour. 
 Inbound Call Reps 
 
 Great Virtual Works is looking independent business owners who are internet savvy and can learn quickly to take inbound calls. You will generate revenue every 30 minutes. Certification will be complete in 10 days. Weekly pay. 
 Quick Rewards 
 Get paid cash within 24 hours to watch videos, play games, completing offers and more. 
 Inbox Dollars 
 Get paid to watch TV, videos, search the web, find deals and so much more with this website. You can also use their mobile app. Checks will be sent in the mail once you reach $30. 
 Cash 4 Books 
 
 Sell your used books and textbooks for real cash. Cash will come fast. Free shipping. 
 Cash Crate 
 Start making money right away by taking surveys, playing games, completing offers and more. Must be at least 13.
