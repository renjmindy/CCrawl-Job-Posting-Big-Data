 Dozer/Grader Operator 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 FIFO 3:1 Roster 
 
 
 Day Shift Only 
 
 
 6 month project 
 
 
 About the Role 
 An opportunity exists for experienced dozer operators to work in Port Hedland. The role will include but not limited to Bulk Earthworks, rail, less than 100m road haulage, blending, piling, stacker footers, concreting to support the new holding facility for Iron Ore to be shipped out of Anderson point. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Bulk Earthworks 
 
 
 Road Haulage 
 
 
 Blending 
 
 
 Piling 
 
 
 Stacker footers 
 
 
 Concreting 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Demonstrated competency operating Caterpillar D8- D10 dozers 
 
 
 Production dozer experience 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 Manual C Class Australian issued Drivers Licence (minimum) 
 
 
 National police clearance or willingness to obtain 
 
 
 Ability to pass pre-employment medicals and drug screens
