 Career Services 
 Whether you’re a student looking for employment opportunities or an employer seeking talented workers, the Learning & Career Center (LCC) offers career service and job seeker resources. The LCC's workforce specialist is on-site three days per week (Monday, Wednesday and Friday) to make sure employees and job seekers have access to the tools necessary for success.
