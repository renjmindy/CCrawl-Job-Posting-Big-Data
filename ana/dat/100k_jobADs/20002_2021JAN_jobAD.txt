 Consultant: 
 
 
 Do you have a marketing related degree accompanied with a few years of industry experience? Are you looking to progress into a varied full marketing mix role? Would you like to join a team of well experienced marketers? 
 
 If so, a fantastic opportunity has arisen for a 'marketing coordinator' to join a well established and progressive company working in cutting edge technology within the world of manufacturing. Based in the Telford area our Client is offering a competitive salary of 23-25k for an enthusiastic and forward-thinking marketer. A great chance is on offer to join a talented team to take the company forwards using popular social media channels, events and marketing campaigns. 
 
 What you'll be doing… 
 
 Plan and create innovative marketing briefs. 
 Campaign analysis and reporting. 
 Communicate with other business functions to ensure all stakeholders are aware of new initiatives and proposed marketing activities. 
 Lead on marketing initiatives and multi-channel campaigns. 
 Generate on and offline content, e-marketing, social media and business development materials. 
 
 What you'll need… 
 
 Relevant degree in Marketing, Communication, Business or related area 
 Successful marketing or related experience 
 Credible experience in a wide variety of digital marketing channels. 
 Experience in content writing and copy proofing. 
 Proven track record in social media marketing. 
 Experience with all MS applications (including Excel, Word and Power Point) 
 Full clean UK driving Licence. 
 
 What you'll get… 
 
 
 Competitive salary of 23-25k. 
 Growing company offering progression opportunities. 
 Pension contributions. 
 25 days holiday. 
 5% annual bonus based on revenue. 
 On-site parking.
