 Location 
 
 Oxfordshire, England

 Permanent

 Consultant: 
 
 
 GRG Exec Search are pleased to be partnering with a transport business based in the Home Counties region for a newly created Sales & Commercial Director role. 
 This is a superb opportunity to join an innovative and cutting edge business who have gone from strength to strength with ambitious growth plans. Joining the business at an exciting time of change and transformation you will ensure that profitable business growth is accelerated and that commercial success is achieved. 
 Part of the Senior Leadership team team, reporting directly to the CEO, you will be responsible for driving a high performing team where you will be responsible for future sales, business growth, bid submissions and identifying profitable commercial opportunities. 
 What we are looking for: 
 
 Proven track record in a similar leadership and commercial role - ideally within the Transport/Aerospace industry 
 Experience of driving profit, reducing cost and identifying profitable commercial opportunities 
 A demonstrable track record of driving progressive, commercial relationships with a variety of internal and external stakeholders 
 Experience of managing and negotiating on all bid submissions 
 Evidence of building, managing and leading, high-performance teams with a strong track record of performance management 
 Credible, effective communicator with interpersonal skills to engage with and win over others 
 Analytical & data focussed but being able to articulate and communicate in the right way 
 Creative and solutions orientated, being able to discuss options 
 Commercially astute and able to drive business performance 
 
 My client is looking for a combination of strong leadership, commercial awareness and a relentless drive for both incremental profit and customer satisfaction.
