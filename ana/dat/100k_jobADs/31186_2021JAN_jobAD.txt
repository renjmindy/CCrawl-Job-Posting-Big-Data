 Experienced Metal Fabricator 
 Mirmil Products

 Apply Now 
 Mirmil Products is an architectural millwork company located in Trenton. We produce custom commerical millwork and metal pieces for customers in Canada, the U.S and overseas. 
 Some of our jobs include metal work. Our metal work is extremely intricate and our jobs are unique and different. Our commercial jobs include casinos, hotels, nightclubs, and post secondary institutions. 
 Mirmil Products is looking to add an experienced metal fabricator to our team. 
 The right candidate must have: 
 
 The ability to read architectural drawings 
 The ability to fit and weld various grades of steels (e.g., aluminum, stainless) 
 The ability to operate a shear, press brake, saws, and other various tools 
 The ability to perform manufacturing of metals to tight tolerances 
 
 Wage will be based on experience. Only those who are selected for an interview will be contacted. Thank you to all who apply.
