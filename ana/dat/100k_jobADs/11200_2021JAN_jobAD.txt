SUMMARY 
 The Leasing Coordinator will provide administrative support to the Manager- Leasing & Tenant Relations in the area of lease documentation preparation, tracking and recording, receipt and distribution of mails and correspondences, lease documentation scanning and file maintenance. Where required, the Leasing Coordinator abstracts lease documentation and responds to simple lease interpretation requests and/or forwards such requests to relevant lease administration team members 
 KEY DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES 
 Act as point of contact with Property Managers, clients, tenants, leasing inquiries for new leasing, amendments, lease renewals, terminations & assignments with copies of supporting information 
Coordinate the production of all reports including third-party leasing plans, tenant correspondence log, monthly transaction activity reports; distribute reports to external & internal stakeholders. Prepare ad hoc reports, as required 
Assist Leasing/Tenant Relations Mgr. in the preparation of listing agreements & marketing packages for surplus space & other properties 
Coordinate & monitor 3rd party KPIs (3rd party lease index) 
Process temporary occupancy requests 
Manage all transaction/lease files to ensure their accuracy, completeness & security 
Preparation of lease documentation, based on client supplied templates and client accepted business terms 
Assist the Leasing Director & Leasing/Tenant Relations Mgr. to ensure that the delivery of services meets or exceeds the requirements as set out in the contract & complies with internal BGIS policies & standard operating procedures 
Meet with tenants 
 Knowledge & Skills 
 College Diploma focusing on Real Estate Management and Administration 
More than 3 years experience of commercial leasing experience with a commercial real estate brokerage firm (preferred) or corporate real estate environment 
Administrative and organizational skills. Attention to detail. 
Computer skills in spreadsheet, database, and word processing applications 
Familiarity with commercial leases. 
Strong customer focus and a total commitment to customer satisfaction 
Strong team player 
Strong time management skills 
Bilingual (French and English- written and spoken) 
 Licenses and/or Professional Accreditation 
 None required
