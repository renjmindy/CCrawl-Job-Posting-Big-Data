 About JobAdder 
    
 
 JobAdder is a dynamic and innovative Software as a Service (SaaS) organisation with over 130 employees across 5 countries and customers worldwide. 
 We are a leading recruitment management platform that assists companies and recruitment agencies to create a competitive advantage within their recruitment and hiring processes. 
 With Seek as our parent company we are fortunate to have access to a broad range of multinational resources whilst still maintaining our unique identity and the ability to make quick decisions. 
All of our people actively contribute to our culture by lifting each other up, approaching problems with a positive mindset, sharing success, celebrating diversity and making software people want to use. 
 Our plans haven't changed through COVID19 and we will be scaling rapidly in the next 12-18 months. We cannot wait to help play a role in rebuilding a variety of industries with our clients and partners.

 The Opportunity and Responsibilities: 
 We are seeking an experienced Data Analyst who can hit the ground running and work on the following tasks: 
 
 Data migrations from clients legacy applications that involve restoring, mapping, updating and transferring into the target database 
 Preparing reports for stakeholders and project team 
 Creating visualisations of data 
 Provide analysis to various internal departments across the business 
 You will be part of a team of Project Managers, Business Analysts and Datas Analysts 
 Generating information and insights from data sets and identifying trends and patterns 
 Developing strategies to optimise the quality of data delivery 
 Devising new data collection and analysis processes 
 Assessing quality of data and removing or cleaning data 
 
 Experience Required: 
 If the following describes you, we would love to chat: 
 
 Ability to work on multiple projects at the same time - Detail orientated 
 Comfortable with autonomy, ambiguity and variety 
 Have an appetite for a new work environment where you love coming to work everyday 
 Excellent analytical skills - the ability to identify trends, patterns and insights from data. 
 Presentation skills - ability to write and speak clearly to easily communicate complex ideas in a way that is easy to understand 
 Problem solving skills 
 You gain satisfaction from making a significant impact on client's business 
 Understanding of databases such as SQL Server, Oracle and SAP 
 Understanding of ETL framework and ETL tools including Alteryx and Microsoft SSIS
