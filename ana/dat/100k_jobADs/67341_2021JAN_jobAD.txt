 Financial Accountant - new position for a growing business based in Telford. 
 This is a great opportunity to work with a rapidly growing organisation in a role that will look after the day-to-day running of a small finance team. 
 The Role 
 Main responsibilities will include: 
 
 Overseeing all day to day finance operations 
 Balance reconciliations 
 Resolving queries and other outstanding issues for month & year end requirements 
 Preparation of financial accounts 
 VAT and EC sales reports 
 Payroll calculations 
 Overseeing the ledgers 
 Managing the P&L 
 Provision of information for budgeting & forecasting purposes 
 Assisting the Financial Controller with ad hoc tasks as required 
 
 This is a permanent opportunity that will offer a salary ranging from £30-35k per annum plus benefits, depending on experience. 
 The Candidate 
 The ideal candidate will have a proven track record as an experienced accountant within manufacturing. 
 Formal qualifications are not a requirement however - personality fit and a positive attitude are key attributes for success in this role. 
 Please click apply, or call Jo Payne on 07813 608579 for more details.
