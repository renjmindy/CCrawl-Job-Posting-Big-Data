 Caregiver and Housekeeper Jobs Cyprus 
 Posted on  May 18, 2010  by  caregiver 
 Employers from the European Union member country of Cyprus are currently looking for Filipinos who are qualified for  Caregiver and Housekeeper Jobs Cyprus . To facilitate the recruitment process, the POEA approved a job order last May 13 that allows a Malate based recruitment agency to select applicants who are qualified for Caregiver and Housekeeper jobs Cyprus. For the said caregiver and housekeeper jobs Cyprus, a total of 99 applicants will be chosen for placement in caregiver and housekeeper jobs abroad. 
 If you think you have the qualifications and interested to apply for the said caregiver and housekeeper jobs Cyprus, visit the office of the recruiting agency and submit your application and credentials. For further details and inquiries on the said caregiver and housekeeper jobs Cyprus, you can contact the recruiting agency through their landline number indicated below. 
 Unlimited Manpower Resources Inc. 
3rd Floor, Room 302, East 21 Manila Bldg. 
1724 A. Mabini St., Malate 
Manila 
Tel No: 5671411 
Mobile No: 0919-2862861
