 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for August 
 Posted on  September 6, 2010  by  caregiver 
 For the month of August, the POEA approved 9 job orders that will provide employment opportunities to Filipino caregivers who are seeking employment in Caregiver Jobs Abroad. These  POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for August  are for the different POEA accredited recruitment agencies and for the countries of Israel, Cyprus, and Bahrain. Specified below is the list of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for August which includes the country of destination, the name of the recruiting agency, and the number or vacancies for each POEA approved caregiver jobs. 
 ISRAEL ----  World Wiser International Inc.  ------------------------ 22 vacancies 
ISRAEL ----  CPM Manpower Services, Inc.  -------------------------- 20 vacancies 
BAHRAIN --  Saosan Manpower Services  ---------------------------- 5 vacancies 
ISRAEL ----  Sunace International Management Services Inc.  --- 29 vacancies 
ISRAEL ----  Jedi Placement Agency Inc.  ---------------------------- 9 vacancies 
ISRAEL ----  RISE Manpower Services  ------------------------------- 28 vacancies 
CYPRUS ---  OFW Recruitment Agency  ------------------------------- 20 vacancies 
ISRAEL ----  Sunace International Management Services Inc.  --- 30 vacancies 
ISRAEL ----  Star World Int’l Manp. & Placement Agency, Inc.  --- 17 vacancies 
 To apply for any of the above stated POEA approved caregiver jobs for August, simply submit your resume and application directly to the office of the recruiting agency. For further info or inquiries on any of the above stated POEA approved caregiver jobs, you can refer to the recruiting agency’s landline numbers or email address which will be provided upon request.
