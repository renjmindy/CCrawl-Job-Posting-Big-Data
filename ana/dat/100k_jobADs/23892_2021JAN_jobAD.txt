 Language school in Oaxaca, Mexico is looking for English Teachers to join their team ASAP. 
 Oaxaca is a city in central Mexico. It’s known for its colonial buildings, many of which are made from green volcanic stone. 
 Teaching English classes of 8-12 students of varying English proficiency (including exam preparation courses) and ages (predominantly young learners). 
 You can utilize our text books & work books. Add your own twist to the lesson plans to make sure your students are learning as much as they are having fun. 
 Responsibilities: 
 – Teach during the week and on Saturdays. 
 – Plan and prepare lessons before each class. 
 – Manage the classroom and administer student discipline as needed. 
 – Facilitate testing during the course, including marking tests and grading assignments. 
 – Collaborate with the Academic Coordinator. 
 Requirements: 
 – 1+ year of experience teaching. 
 – Enthusiastic, energetic and creative personality with genuine interest and love of teaching and children. 
 – Responsible, approachable and friendly. 
 – Highly organized, detail-oriented and punctual. 
 We offer shared apartments and depending on the type and size of the room it ranges 3000-4500 pesos per month.
