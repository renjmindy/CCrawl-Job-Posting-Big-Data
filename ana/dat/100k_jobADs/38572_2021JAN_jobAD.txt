 Friday Work at home Jobs & Freebies 
 Published by  aw1219  on March 2, 2018 
 We may earn money or products from some of the companies mentioned in this post. 
 Do you want to work from home in customer service, as a virtual assistant, teaching students online or even as an online stylist? If so, you will find plenty of legitimate opportunities from companies that are hiring remote workers to complete various tasks. In addition to the traditional work at home jobs, you can also select to work some freelance side gigs in your spare time for extra cash. 
 Make sure to visit the  weekly job section ,  Friday freebie pages  and even check out my  YouTube channel  for additional job leads posted throughout the week that are yet to be filled. You can also join our  Facebook Community  where members have been sharing call backs from companies, interviews, even hires as well as payment proofs. 
 Before you apply, please visit my  Amazon Shop  for all of your work at home essentials so that your home office will be fully equipped so that you can begin working from home as soon as possible. 
 Friday Work at home Jobs & Freebies for 3/2/2018 
 Newsletter 
 There are more work at home jobs in my newsletter that you can check out and apply to online. 
 Customer Service 
 Sykes is hiring full time customer service reps to answer customer questions, process transactions and more. You will need 1 year customer service experience, be able to speak on the phone and use a computer, be empathetic and have a land line phone. Benefits provided with hourly pay. 
 Tech Support Customer Service Agents 
 Transcom is hiring tech savvy agents to work from home full time providing support to one of their clients. You will need excellent verbal skills, experience browsing the web, can type 40 wpm and live in the US or Canada. Pays $11 per hour. 
 Furniture Service Associates 
 Williams & Sonoma is now hiring candidates to work 20-29 hours a week from home addressing customer questions and concerns regarding delivery and product information, place orders for customers, process returns and other related duties. You must have strong data entry skills and a  desktop computer or laptop with a home or cell phone with a headset . Pays $12 per hour with paid training. 
 Seasonal Customer Service Rep 
 
 Swim Outlet is hiring reps to respond to their customer's need via phone, email and live chat. You will research information, manage and resolve complaints, process orders and more. You will need a B.S. degree and 1 year customer service or call center experience, strong verbal skills, can type 40 wpm and live in either California, Oregon, Ohio, Washington, or Connecticut for training. 
 Dashers 
 Door Dash is looking for dashers to deliver food and other items to local merchants and customers. You can work your own schedule and set your own hours. No car needed. Weekly or daily pay. 
 Telephone Reps 
 Next Wave @ Home is seeking people to make outbound educational outreach calls and conduct interviews with citizens across the country on various topics. There is no selling or fundraising involved. You must have a pleasant phone demeanor, good verbal skills, a  PC or Mac computer , internet and access to a printer. Pays $12 to $16 per hour. 
 Customer Service/Inbound Sales Reps 
 VIP Desk Connect is hiring people to work from home as Sales Brand Ambassadors to handle inbound calls for their clients to find service professionals for maintenance, repair and improvement projects. This position requires 2 years customer service experience, the ability to work weekends, can mutlitask and type 35 wpm. Paid training. Select states only. 
 Data Entry 
 Apex Systems is seeking an hourly data entry temp for one of the largest online retailers. You will need previous data entry, great typing and Microsoft Office skills. Possible remote position for the right candidate. 
 
 Customer Experience Advocate 
 Whistle is seeking individuals to work from home 40 hours a week assisting their customers via email and phone with tech support needs, resolving customer issues, and other related duties. You must be familiar with mobile phones, Bluetooth, have previous experience and good verbal skills. Pays $13 per hour plus bonus and commission. Must live in California. 
 Data Collector 
 Cati Network is seeking people to gather responses that poll the American public on political, commercial and social matters. There is no selling involved. You will need a  PC, headset  and can choose your own schedule. Pays $8.50 to $10 per hour. Part time. 
 Qriket 
 Get paid cash to play live games or spin the wheel with this free app. Get 10 bonus spins when you join. 
 Ibotta 
 
 Get cash back to upload your receipts plus $10 when you join. Instant pay with this free app! 
 Perk 
 Play fun trivia games, watch TV, shop online, scratch off and more for free gift cards, cash and other rewards.
