 Senior Analyst, Commodity Risk Management 
 Inter Pipeline 
 __Calgary, AB 
 Apply Now 
 We are a major energy infrastructure business that has experienced tremendous growth over the past decade. This growth has provided our employees with stability, positive challenges, new career paths and opportunities to work in a dynamic team environment. As one of Alberta’s Top 75 Employers we are proud to say no matter where your role at Inter Pipeline may lie, you are provided ample opportunities for valued contribution and innovation, with a strong emphasis on employee growth. 
 Located out of our Calgary office, the individual will report to the Manager, Risk Analytics and become a contributing member of the commodity risk management team. The Senior Analyst, Commodity Risk Management will support the daily management of Inter Pipeline’s commodity risk management activities. This position will also assist with the development and ongoing operation of a dedicated Middle Office function and ETRM system to support Inter Pipeline’s marketing and hedging activities. 
 Qualifications: 
A financial professional with a degree in finance, economics, or mathematics 
 Designation would be preferred:  CFA/FRM/ERP 
 Experience: 
Minimum of 5 years related experience 
Highly analytical, detail oriented, strong experience with report development for marketing activities 
Experienced presenting to and responding to inquiries 
 Skills : 
Excellent communication and teaching skills (verbal and written) and strong interpersonal and presentation skills 
Adaptable, innovative, self-motivated and a well-organized team player 
Experience with ETRM systems 
Advanced user of Excel, & PowerPoint. PowerBI and VBA skills for excel preferred 
 Key Activities and Responsibilities: 
Develop deep understanding of Inter Pipeline’s commodity exposures, future strategies and prevailing market fundamentals 
Assist with formation of a dedicated Middle Office function to support marketing and hedging activities 
Design, control and deliver insightful reporting and analytics that assist decisions via Business Intelligence tools 
Daily reporting responsibilities for exposures and MtM 
Ongoing responsibilities to forecast commodity pricing across commodity exposures and forward delivery periods 
Support ETRM system implementation, testing, and ongoing operations 
Become a Subject Matter Expert on the ETRM platform 
Develop materials to assist monthly Risk Management Committee activities relating to commodity price forecasting and risk reporting 
Familiarity with industry standard risk tools and methodologies, such as Value at Risk, valuation models, stress testing, back testing 
Ongoing daily administration which may include risk reporting, deal confirmations, training, configuration, data upload, control oversight and user permissions 
Middle Office compliance with Inter Pipeline’s applicable Policies and Procedures 
 Hours: 
Full Time, Monday – Friday 
 As this position is identified as security sensitive, employment of a final candidate will be conditional upon completion of employment verification and criminal background check assessments with satisfactory results.
