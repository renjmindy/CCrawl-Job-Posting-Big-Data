 Production Worker 
 Job description 
 About the vacancies 
 We are currently accepting applications for people who have a strong background within the Warehousing industry and are available for work across various sites in Brisbane. These positions will range from short term to long term opportunities and have both day and afternoon shifts available. 
 The person we are looking for will have a proactive, responsible attitude towards working safely and will have a positive, team orientated approach to work. You will be confident working throughout the Warehousing Industry in a fast pace environment and the ability to carry out all duties to the standard required. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Picking/Packing 
 
 
 Loading/unloading trucks 
 
 
 Palletising 
 
 
 Inventory control 
 
 
 Manufacturing 
 
 
 RF scanning 
 
 
 Manual labouring 
 
 
 Working apart of a team 
 
 
 Skills & Qualifications 
 
 
 Previous experience within the Warehousing industry highly desirable 
 
 
 Forklift ticket advantageous 
 
 
 Drivers License and reliable transport 
 
 
 Current resume with 2x work related references 
 
 
 Available for an immediate start 
 
 
 Culture & Benefits 
 
 
 Full time hours 
 
 
 Ongoing work for the right candidate 
 
 
 Wages paid weekly
