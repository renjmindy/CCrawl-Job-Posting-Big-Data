 Kitchen Cabinet Installer 
 Mobel Cabinetry 
 __Hamilton, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Mobel Cabinetry  is an Ontario based kitchen manufacturing company that specializes in kitchen cabinetry, vanity production. We've grown dramatically over the years and are looking for cabinet and vanity installers. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Read and understand drawings 
 Install cabinet and millwork components according to drawing specifications. 
 Dedication to quality craftsmanship. 
 Operate and use a variety of hand tools and equipment. 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 Minimum 2 years’ experience in cabinet production/ custom woodworking 
 Knowledge of crown moulding 
 CSA Safety Approved footwear 
 Have reliable transportation to and from work 
 
 Salary: 
 Starting at $25 per hour. 
 You will not be bound by Mobel Cabinetry for full time work and hence will be working on project based job. The earnings will go up to 5% of total project cost depending on amount of work.
