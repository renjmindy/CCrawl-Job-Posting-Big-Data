 Digital Marketing Assistant 
 
 Location 
 
 Droitwich, Worcestershire

 Marketing and Digital

 Permanent

 £22000.00 - £25000.00 per annum

 Consultant: 
 
 
 A brilliant opportunity to join a fact-paced marketing department within a leading Consumer Business based in Droitwich, due to recent growth and expansion, this is a brand new role for the business. You will be responsible for supporting the ongoing delivery of engaging digital marketing campaigns, and will be involved in a variety of digital projects spanning content creation, website management and product launches. This is pivotal role within a busy marketing department and will be ideal for someone who is very hands on and has passion for all things digital! 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Working with the digital team to aid the execution of the digital marketing strategy across a range of channels, including paid search, content creation and website management. 
 Manage the CMS system and day to day website management including updating key information, uploading product images, ensuring product data is accurate, and web copy. 
 Research market trends, customer behaviour, competitor activity and suggest future improvements. 
 Continuously looking to optimise the website and improve the customer journey. 
 Reporting on campaign results, website traffic and digital engagement. 
 
 The ideal candidate will: 
 
 Have a qualification in marketing or experience working in a similar digital marketing role. 
 Experience using a CMS system - Magento would be advantageous 
 Experience using reporting and insight tools - Google Analytics would be desirable. 
 Excellent attention to detail, and the ability to work in a fast paced environment. 
 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
