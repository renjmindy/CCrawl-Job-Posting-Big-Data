 Automotive Spray Painter 
 Job description 
 
 
 $35.00 per hour + penalties 
 
 
 Laverton Location 
 
 
 Monday to Friday 
 
 
 About the Role 
WorkPac are currently seeking experienced Automotive Spray Painters for an agricultural machinery company based in Laverton. The successful applicant will be required to work Monday to Friday on an ongoing basis & will be an experienced spray painter who has worked in the automotive industry before and experienced in colour matching working with agricultural machinery and trucks . 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 - Spray painting and doing patchwork for trucks and agricultural machinery. 
 
 
 - Colour Matching 
 
 
 - Preparation work - e.g., sanding, masking etc. 
 
 
 - General housekeeping 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 - Proven experience in spray painting 
 
 
 - Trade Qualification Automotive Spray Painting 
 
 
 - Own reliable vehicle 
 
 
 - Right to work in Australia 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
