 Senior IT Systems/Network Cloud Engineer 
 Full Time 
 
 __Post Date: December 15, 2020 
 __Apply Before: February 28, 2021 
 
 Job Description 
 Work Channel Recruitment is working closely with a digital services company and they are looking to recruit a Senior IT Systems/Network Cloud Engineer to join their team. 
 Job Responsibilities 
 
 Build and setup new development tools and infrastructure, including among others Continuous Integration, Continuous Delivery and Continuous Deployment pipelines 
 Implement and manage cloud infrastructures using “infrastructure as code” practices and tools 
 Manage deployments and operations on Kubernetes clusters 
 Optimize and automate development and release processes for maximum speed and scalability 
 Analyze software deployment and release requirements and provide recommendations. 
 Standardize and harmonize deployment and infrastructure management policies 
 Ensure the availability of managed systems 
 Ensure that safety and security of managed systems 
 Investigate and resolve technical issues 
 Design procedures for system troubleshooting and maintenance 
 Direct collaboration with development to ensure that development follows established processes and works as intended (proper configuration and release management) 
 Creates, updates, and maintains documentation, diagrams and other detailed instructions. 
 Create tutorials to help end-users use a variety of applications 
 Planning out projects and being involved in project management decisions. 
 Participating in continuing education and training to remain current on best practices 
 
 Job Requirements 
 
 BSc in Computer Science, Engineering or relevant field 
 Proven working experience as IT Systems/Network Cloud Engineer or similar role - working in DevOps teams and prior experience with highly scalable systems (cloud) 
 Experience with user acceptance testing and environment troubleshooting 
 Hands-on experience with RESTful services and Microservices Architectures 
 Knowledge of cloud technologies e.g. Google Cloud/ Amazon AWS 
 Strong experience with continuous integration, automation, orchestration, container management and build tools such as GitLab, Jenkins, Ant, Maven, Gradle, Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, Vagrant, etc. 
 Experience with monitoring tools such as Graphite, Grafana and Prometheus or other 
 Experience creating infrastructure as code, including applications/servers’ blueprints, load balancers, networks, and other deployments 
 Expertise in security management in containers and clusters 
 In-depth knowledge of GNU / Linux (Ubuntu/Debian/Centos) and Windows systems 
 Experience with infrastructure scripting solutions such as Shell, PowerShell or Python 
 Ability to quickly grasp complex technical concepts and simplify them 
 Problem solving skills 
 Good time-management skills 
 Team spirit; strong communication skills to collaborate with various stakeholders 
 Fluency in English (written and spoken) 
 
 What our client offers 
 
 Full-time permanent employment contract 
 Competitive net salary and conditions corresponding to the demands of the position 
 13th salary 
 22 annual leave days 
 Private medical insurance (after probation period - 3 months) 
 Flexible working hours – 7 hours per day with 1-hour break 
 Work from home policy 
 Encouragement of innovation and support in personal and professional growth 
 Opportunity to deal with International customers 
 Team building activities 
 
 How to Apply 
 If interested and you match all the requirements, please send your CV to andriana.p@work-channel.com, including the title of the position in the subject line 
 Please note  that due to the high volume of applications received, only successful candidates will be contacted
