 A growing, Innovative, multi-sited business based in Cannock are recruiting a Finance Manager on a temporary basis for a duration of 6- 9 months. Salary for this role will be circa £35k - £38K (£16-£20ph) depending on experience. 
 This is a superb opportunity for a talented Finance Manager or experienced Management Accountant, to join a business where they will take on full responsibility of UK/ Ireland financials. 
 Interviews will take place at the start of February with a view to starting within 1 x week. 
 Please note occasional travel will be required (expenses paid). 
 Finance Manager Responsibilities include: 
 
 Delivering value-add monthly management accounting information 
 Management of fixed asset registers 
 Preparation of all month end reporting requirements 
 Preparation of cash flow 
 Variance analysis 
 Intrastat submissions 
 Ownership of the P&L and balance sheet 
 Supervising & supporting the team across the Group 
 Complex reconciliations and other ad hoc tasks as required 
 
 (Full Detailed Job Description available) 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 CIMA/ACCA part-qualified/Qualified/QBE 
 Strong management accounting & financial control skills 
 Manufacturing sector experience 
 Strong Excel skills 
 High level of attention to detail 
 Excellent verbal, interpersonal, and written communication skills 
 A "hands-on" attitude, comfortable with completing month and year end tasks 
 
 To apply for the Finance Manager position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
