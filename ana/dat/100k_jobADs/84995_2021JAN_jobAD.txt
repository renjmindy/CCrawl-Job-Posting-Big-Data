 The Company 
 inflo.Ai is a London-based software company building proprietary AI technology to make SEO content creation and blogging simple, fast and affordable for overstretched in-house marketing teams, SMEs and marketing agencies. 
 inflo.Ai's goal is to become the world's preferred provider of content marketing technology to grow online. 
 The role 
 inflo.Ai is looking for a Sales Development Representative. This position is critical in ensuring the company continues on a pathway to Series A planned for late 2021. inflo.Ai is now adding new members to its core business development team and we're looking for an overachiever focused on booking new sales opportunities to work alongside the company's Co-Founder & Head of Sales. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Skilfully engage and qualify prospective clients 
 Engage with prospects to ultimately book demos to grow sales pipeline 
 Maintain CRM, documentation and admin to highest quality 
 Skilfully chase prospective clients 
 Maintain detailed admin, documentation and analysis of leads in HubSpot 
 Engage with prospects (Marketing Managers, Business & Agency Owners) and articulate the product in an effective manner 
 Conduct discovery with prospects to evaluate pain points 
 Deliver relevant marketing content to prospects 
 
 Key Skills & Experience 
 
 Educated to Degree level 
 A passion for sales 
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills 
 Self-motivated, proactive, flexible - a can-do, agile attitude necessary to thrive in a scale-up environment 
 Good time-management 
 Proven track record for articulating a product/service and building rapport with prospects 
 Strong communication skills, including written, verbal, presentation and on social media 
 Ability to pinpoint customer pain points and accurately assess the transactional value for both the company and customer 
 
 Benefits 
 
 Competitive salary with equity options and annual bonus 
 Strong meritocratic culture where everyone's opinion is valued and considered 
 Pension scheme 
 25 days of holidays, with 7 bank holidays 
 Flexible working with an office in South London 
 Learning & Development - dedicated time to learning each quarter
