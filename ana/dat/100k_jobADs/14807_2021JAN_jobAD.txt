 Caregiver Jobs Kuwait 
 Posted on  September 15, 2009  by  caregiver 
 The oil rich country of Kuwait is in search of qualified applicants for caregiver jobs Kuwait. And for the said  caregiver work abroad , the POEA has approved a job order that would allow a recruitment agency to recruit applicants for the said POEA jobs for caregiver in Kuwait. The said POEA jobs order has a corresponding job order balance of 43 caregivers to be employed in various caregiver jobs Kuwait. 
 The job order for the said caregiver jobs Kuwait was approved for recruitment for a recruitment agency based in Malate, Manila. To apply for the said caregiver jobs Kuwait, all interested applicants are advised to visit the recruitment agency in their office address below or contact the recruitment agency by telephone or E-mail address that are specified below. 
 Al Gizza International Manpower Services 
939 J. Nakpil Street, Malate 
Manila 
 Tel Nos: 5247515 / 4501057 
E-mail:  ykholoul@yahoo.com
