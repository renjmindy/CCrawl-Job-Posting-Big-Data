 PROJECT MANAGER - RE-BRANDING - MARKETING - REMOTE WORKING - EUROPE TRAVEL - CONTRACT  ** 
 ** 
 Looking for a Project Manager with extensive and recent experience of managing marketing and re-branding type projects end to end as part of an acquisition.  Please Note:  This role is predominantly remote working and you will have the opportunity to travel to offices in Europe as and when needed. 
 Details of the contract: 
 Role:  Project Manager 
 Day rate:  £450 - £550 per day 
 Contract:  6 months initial (opportunity to go permanent thereafter) 
 Location:  Remote working with some travel to offices in UK, Netherlands, Belgium, and France as and when required 
 Start date:  September 2020 
 Role  : 
 Manage the design, build and implementation of a new group brand within the company. The first 6 months of this position will be a contract role working on audit / investory of all current countries, workshop on new brand approach and values, roll out of new collateral, websites, logo, signage and corporate identity manual whilst working with an outside agency. Following the 6 months, there will be an opportunity to go permanent thereafter. 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 An experienced Project Manager / Marketing Manager 
 Must have led similar corporate marketing branding/rebranding type projects end to end 
 Understand Project methodology such as Agile/Waterfall 
 Have worked in a small - medium sized firms 
 Strong communication skills across all levels of the business up to C-Level execs 
 Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) experience is nice to have 
 Be able to travel to offices in UK, Netherlands, Belgium and France as and when needed. 
 Available to start in September 
 
 How to Apply: 
 If this is you, please apply as soon as possible or send your latest CV to  jkaur@venquis.com  for a fast track review and if suitable, I will be in touch within 48 hours.
