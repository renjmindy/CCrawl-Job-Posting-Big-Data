 WSC is a not-for-profit agency with a mission to improve the quality of life for persons with disabilities through training and employment. Although the majority of our employees have significant disabilities, WSC has a career for just about anyone. Additionally, WSC is a full-service career incubator. Our process includes vocational evaluation to help assess skill sets and interests, job hunting assistance, application assistance, interview assistance, and job placement both inside and outside WSC.
