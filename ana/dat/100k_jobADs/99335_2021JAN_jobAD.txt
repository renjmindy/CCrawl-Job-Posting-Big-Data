 My client, a fast growing specialist manufacturer requires an interim Financial Controller to manage the day to day finance operations. 
 The role will have responsibility for managing the team and preparing the financial statements. 
 The successful candidate will have a background in project accounting, have experience of using Sage (100 or 200) and be a hands on accountant. 
 This is a super opportunity to join a rapidly growing sme with a strong sense of purpose.
