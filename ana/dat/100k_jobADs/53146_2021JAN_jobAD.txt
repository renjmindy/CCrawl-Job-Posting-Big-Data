 Part-Time Transport Nurse 
 Haanpää International -  HELSINKI  , FI 
 Job Description 
 Haanpaa Group is a leading manufacturer of liquid chemicals, total logistics services company in the Nordic countries, the Baltic region and Russia as well as in the Nordic countries and continental Europe between logistics. Our offices are located in The Netherlands, Norway, Sweden, Finland, Russia and Estonia. 
 Your responsibilities include tank container transport, orders management, transport planning and monitoring. You are in daily contact with customers, both domestic and foreign partners and stakeholders. In addition, you take care of the customer billing and invoice verification. We hope you will experience the tank container transport and logistic happy to training. IT skills are up to date.
