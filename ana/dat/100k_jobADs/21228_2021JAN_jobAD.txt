 Senior Social Worker - Children with Disabilities Team - Dudley - £29.93/hr LTD 
 An exciting vacancy has become available in Dudley for a Senior Social Worker with experience to join their CWD team. 
 You will ideally have experience of working with disabled children and their families and should have an understanding of legislation that underpins this work. 
 It will be essential for you to have experience of child protection, court work and children in care. 
 A Children with Disabilities Social Worker will: 
Work directly with disabled children Cover and support duty Carry out single assessments Undertake child protection work, children in care work and high level child in need Work with a 'children first' approach Incorporate social inclusion principles, anti-oppressive practice and person centred ways 
Essential Requirements: 
HCPC registration 
Eligibility to work in the UK 
Social work degree 
Post-qualified experience in children's services
