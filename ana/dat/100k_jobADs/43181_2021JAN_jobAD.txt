 About the role 
 The Director of Finance will be a strategic decision maker with the senior leadership team and run all financial operations as New Story continues to grow its impact scope and depth. This is a tremendous opportunity for a finance leader to maximize and strengthen the internal capacity of a well-respected, high-impact organization. New Story’s projected revenue for 2020 is 10-12M with growth plans in 2021 of 20M. 
 The Director of Finance will utilize and manage an outsourced Acuity.co financial team (Financial Manager, Controller, and Bookkeeper) to be successful. While we are looking for a leader to bring in new knowledge, processes, and strategy the below is a list of responsibilities. 
 MONTHLY 
 
 Review monthly financial reports and close for accuracy, areas of saving/optimization, and opportunities for better financial forecasting, management, and efficiency 
 Manage and meet with our Acuity.co Financial Manager, Controller, and Bookkeeper 
 Ensure program and business models are developed in sync 
 Review and approve invoices and larger expenses 
 
 QUARTERLY 
 
 Manage treasury and forecast cash flow 
 Communicate financial trends and analyses to Executive team 
 Create financial plans by using historical information and projected organizational plans 
 Work closely with senior leaders to understand relationship between planning and resources required 
 Analyze cost-effectiveness and impact-effectiveness of program, operational, or financial scenarios 
 Update and implement all necessary business policies and accounting practices; improve the finance department’s overall policy and procedure manual. 
 
 ANNUALLY 
 
 Prepare annual budget and long-term financial model 
 Build out and oversee internal controls and financial process 
 Own end to end management and review of New Story’s annual IRS required audit and 990 process and and liaise between accountants, auditors, audit committee, and Executive team 
 
 YOU MUST HAVE 
 
 At least seven to 10 years of overall professional experience; ideally six-plus years of broad financial management experience, ideally in the nonprofit sector. 
 Experience managing annual budgets greater than 5M 
 The ideal candidate has experience of final responsibility for the quality and content of all financial data, reporting and audit coordination for either a division or significant program area 
 A successful track record in setting priorities; keen analytic, organization and problem solving skills which support and enable sound decision making 
 Excellent communication and relationship building skills with an ability to prioritize, negotiate, and work with a variety of internal and external stakeholders 
 Personal qualities of integrity, credibility, and dedication 
 Flexible and a self-starter 
 Proven effectiveness leading and managing professionals in finance and accounting 
 
 Technology 
 Our stack is Ruby on Rails, Rspec, React, React Native, Postgres, Heroku, and AWS.
