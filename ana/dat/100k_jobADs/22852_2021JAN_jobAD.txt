 Receiving Worker / Reach Truck Operator at Worknet Staffing 
 Location:  Carencro, LA 
 North Lafayette area distribution warehouse is seeking a receiving worker to unload trucks, check in items, review and ensure orders are correct, some inventory, and stocking. Work will involve the use of a sitdown forklift and reach truck. Electric palletjack experience is a plus. This position will require the worker to work in dry areas, walk-in coolers and walk-in freezers. Previous experience needed. 
 $12/hr 
Full time, Mon - Fri, 4am - until 
Temp to perm 
Full benefits after permanent hire, including bonuses 
 Other requirements: 
 
 Previous verifiable work experience in area needed 
 Work references 
 Pre-employment screening 
 Reliable transportation 
 Flexibility in availability for changing schedule
