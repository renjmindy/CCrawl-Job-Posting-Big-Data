 As Growth Marketing Manager you lead our inbound marketing strategy, drive our global digital demand generation programs and demonstrate its success with full funnel reporting. 
 Banking is changing rapidly. With Big Tech and Neobanks taking customer expectations to new heights, the industry is quickly transforming. At Backbase we are powering this digital revolution. As an Amsterdam-based global software company we have made it our mission to be the preferred banking app for 10% of the world’s smartphone users. We are here to make a positive impact on people’s daily lives by making finance easier, and to turn banking into valuable experiences. That’s why we help banks across the globe become digital-first, create people-centric solutions and enable them to deliver superior digital experiences to their clients. 
 With your data driven approach you create insights and track the revenue contribution and attribution of our global and local B2B online marketing campaigns. You will think creatively and analytically and work collaboratively across the organization. You continuously run experiments to maximize lead generation and customer engagement. To be successful in this role, you must be able to thrive in a fast-paced environment with a “roll up your sleeves” attitude and entrepreneurial spirit. 
 Being part of an international marketing team, you will work closely together with the field marketing managers, campaign marketeers, digital marketing specialist, copywriters and visual designers to create impactful online campaigns. You are based in our Amsterdam HQ and report directly to the Director of Growth and Marketing. 
 We're looking for an analytical and creative individual to experiment, iterate quickly and optimize continuously; 
 
 
 __5+ years of developing and executing multi-channel marketing strategies and demand gen programs, of which the majority in B2B; 
 
 
 __Proficiency in Google Adwords, Analytics, Data Studio, Linkedin Campaign Manager and demonstrated experience in B2B full funnel measurability; 
 
 
 __Ability to thrive in a demanding, fast paced, high growth environment under tight deadlines; 
 
 
 __Solid understanding and experience of what user lifecycle is and how to optimize each of its different stages; 
 
 
 __Great communicator who thrives in a collaborative environment; 
 
 
 __Data-driven personality with willingness to take calculated risks; 
 
 
 __Experience working with data scientists, data visualization tools, attribution modeling, and data feeds; 
 
 
 __Hands-on, proactive approach and established testing philosophy; 
 
 
 __Experience in modern digital Account Based Marketing practices.

 BACKBASE IN AMSTERDAM 
  __Amsterdam 
 Where it all began! Two guys, our founder Jouk and his partner, set out on a mission “to make the internet more beautiful”. Fast-forward 16 years, our offices in Amsterdam are home to over 480 people. The Amsterdam HQ is the largest of our offices and boasts an international mix of talented people. Working in software engineering, sales, marketing, academy or business support, we have a great team that’s driving our growth. 
 Located in Amsterdam-Oost (East), our EMEA HQ offices are a stone’s throw away from the beautiful Tropenmuseum, the Amsterdam Royal Zoo and famous windmill brewery Brouwerij ‘t IJ. Famous for its breathtaking architecture, canals, and over 881,000 bikes, Amsterdam is a city that’s easy to fall in love with.
