 DELIVERY TRUCK DRIVER 
 Kent is locally owned and operated, and we continue to grow in your community. Our mission is to be the best source of home improvement products through superior service, quality products and everyday great prices. 
 Kent offers delivery service for our customers through our fleet of light duty trucks and vans as well as commercial flat beds and boom trucks. Our drivers need to ensure the highest standards of safety and customer service. 
 This opening is for a driver for commercial trucks (3 ton with air brakes; or 5 ton box, curtain side truck or hitch hiker) and requires a class 3 license, or a class 5 license with an Airbrake Endorsement. 
 Responsibilities: 
Works safely 
Completes reports and paperwork as required related to shipping orders, return of product, vehicle inspections and driving logs 
Communicates as required with customers and dispatch 
Assists in loading vehicle and is responsible for load securement 
 Requirements: 
Safety mindset 
High attention to detail 
Able to work outside in variable temperatures and weather conditions 
Able to do physical work 
 We Offer: 
A safe work environment 
Incentive programs 
Employee discount 
Full time benefits after probation period including health, drug and dental coverage; disability insurance and RRSP 
Employee and Family Assistance Plan 
Opportunities for growth and career advancement 
Training
