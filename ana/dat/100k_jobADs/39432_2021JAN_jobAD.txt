 **E-commerce Fulfilment Assistant  ** 
 Background 
 PLANK Hardware is a fresh new hardware brand selling accent hardware pieces via multiple online channels. It was founded in 2018 by Tom Revill, an entrepreneur and experienced e-commerce consultant, with the mission of enabling people to be creative in their homes and add their own finishing touches to their renovations. Tom's co-founder Annie Aveyard joined the business in 2019 - Annie has a long standing finance career and most recently acted as Head of Finance at a scaling e-commerce furniture retailer. 
 The brand has big plans for 2021, driving growth by focussing on range expansion, developing the B2B proposition and rolling out new B2C retail channels. The business is already profitable and is looking to achieve sustainable, profitable growth. 
 The Role 
 This role is a full time fulfilment position supporting our Operations Manager. You would be working in a small team with one other Fulfilment Assistant. It is a physical and practical role working in our warehouse in Leyton, North East London. 
 Requirements 
 
 Picking and packing orders ready for courier collections 
 Assisting with managing the warehouse and keeping it ship shape 
 Checking stock quality and reporting/resolving any issues 
 Processing returns 
 Unpacking inbound  deliveries and restocking the warehouse 
 Running regular stock takes and calibrating the Inventory Management System 
 
 Desired 
 
 Self starter with proven practical skills 
 Highly organised and diligent 
 Good time management 
 Fulfilment experience desirable but not required 
 
 Benefits 
 
 Company pension scheme 
 Private health plan 
 20% Friends & Family discount 
 
 Location 
 
 Leyton, North East London
