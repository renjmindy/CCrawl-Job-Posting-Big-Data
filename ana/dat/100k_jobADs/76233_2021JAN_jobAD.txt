 Senior Architect - Architecture 
Based in the West Midlands our client is seeking a highly experienced Senior Architect to join their expanding team. The position would suit someone with experience, knowledge and passion to work within a creative architectural practice. 
 COMPANY PROFILE - Senior Architect: 
This award-winning multi-disciplined Architectural Practice was firm formed on the basis exceeding client expectations and providing a complete service. They take huge pride in their work and continue to provide a leading service to their clients within the UK and across the Globe. 
 DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES - Senior Architect: 
§ Understand design process from briefing, planning to design 
§ Strategy planning 
§ Master-Planning 
§ Client and site visits 
§ Lead and manage internal and external project teams 
§ Working closely with the wider organisation and design teams 
§ Carrying out technical reviews of the design drawings 
§ Supporting projects where required 
§ Attending project meetings 
 PERSON PROFILE - Senior Architect: 
§ Experience in Building services 
§ High level of experience in Revit Software 
§ High level of experience in a variety of CAD packages 
§ Experience in working with Projects from £20M+ 
§ Working knowledge of JCT contracts essential 
§ Experience of working on High Density Residential, Commercial, Education, Rail and Mixed-use Developments. 
§ Self-motivated, Creative and Analytical. 
§ Multi-tasker with excellent follow up skills. 
 PACKAGE - Senior Architect: 
Salary guide: Competitive 
Please contact us for a confidential chat on this role as a Senior Architect.
