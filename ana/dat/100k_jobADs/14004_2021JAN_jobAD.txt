 Job Title:  Head of Marketing 
 Reporting to  : Co-Founders, Chief Executive Officer and Head of Education 
 Responsible for  : Marketing 
 Remuneration:  Mix of Equity and/or Cash 
 Studeo is an exciting new business that intends to change the way that we think about elite education and also what we expect from it. Attracting the best teaching talent, it is setting out to widen the reach of top-level education through the medium of a premium online tutorial service. 
 Our goal is to democratise elite education and become the go-to platform for online tutoring. Our proposition ambitions to significantly improve students' grades, help students enter the best universities and make education excellence accessible regardless of family background. 
 Studeo is looking for an ambitious, experienced and passionate Head of Marketing to join our team to help us revolutionise education. She/he will be responsible for leading our marketing strategy and its execution. We have received first round of funding from investors and on track to launch our MVP in April 2021 and expect to raise a Seed investor round in parallel. 
 What you will be doing 
 
 Own and drive the strategy for the full marketing plan (B2C, B2B, B2G), 
 Lead the execution of the marketing plan including launch campaign, advertisement, SEOs, landing page optimisation, and paid remarketing 
 Drive customer acquisition strategies 
 Set users testing KPIs and track their performance 
 Test various channels, track their efficiency and allocate future marketing budgets accordingly 
 Track customers' journey and drop off points 
 Build a marketing team 
 
 **Your Qualification  ** 
 
 Minimum 4+ Years marketing experience (including digital marketing) 
 Experience in marketing strategy (including digital marketing) 
 Experience in start-ups preferential 
 Education sector experience would be a plus 
 Experience in marketing to 15-18-year-old age range would be ideal 
 French-speaking would be a plus 
 GDPR understanding 
 
 Your Attributes 
 
 The ideal candidate will have a self-starter and pro-active mindset 
 You thrive in changing environments, and you have a problem-solving approach 
 Creative thinker 
 Result based mindset 
 Ability to lead projects independently 
 Excellent attention to detail 
 
 Key Benefits of working at  Studeo 
 
 Strategic role, rapidly evolving into a leadership position 
 Senior role with daily interactions with Co-Founders 
 Fully remote and flexible 
 Large marketing budget planned (significant marketing spend once Seed funding within next 4 months) 
 Ability to build and lead a marketing team 
 Results based mindset 
 Mix of equity and/or cash
