 BECOME A STUDENT WORKER ! 
 Come work with us to build valuable practical experience for your future career. We offer the chance to work on ongoing infrastructure projects with flexible working hours. 
 
 Here’s what we offer: 
 
 temporary part-time position up to 20 hours per week 
 fair pay 
 an attractive office environment in the heart of Berlin 
 opportunities for career growth at a dynamic company 
 exciting projects 
 flexible working hours 
 
 
 What we’re looking for: 
 
 enrolment in a European university 
 students in their final years of study for Bachelor’s or Master’s degrees 
 good knowledge of German (at least B1) 
 for planners: advanced knowledge of GIS systems 
 initiative and reliability
