 Career 
 What began in 1921 with the manufacturing of X-ray and welding protection gear in Leipzig, Germany, turned into a globally successful company with an outstanding reputation in “X-Ray Protection and Medical Suspension Systems”. The family-owned company currently has more than 170 employees. Since 2006 MAVIG VivaScope develops and distributes confocal laser scanning microscopes for medical research and cosmetic applications. VivaScope products are now used in more than 800 clinics, medical practices, institutes and labs worldwide.
