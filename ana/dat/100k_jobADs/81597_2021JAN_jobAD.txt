 Position Description 
 CGI is hiring an IBM FTM Digital Payments Developer to join our financial services team in Pittsburgh, PA. 
 At CGI, you get to build solutions as a full time consultant with a world-class IT company. You will be involved in a mission critical project working with smart, dedicated people like yourself, and making an impact with both internal and client stakeholders. Take your talents to a company that inspires personal and professional growth and values your ideas. 
 Your future duties and responsibilities 
 • Develop payments processing applications using IBM FTM 
• Build Finite state machines using Rational software architect 
• Build and deploy IIB message flows 
• Creating service framework tasks using Java 
• Build Rest services and integrations with several interfaces 
• Map and transform the input and output format types 
• Unit test application and support QA testing 
• Responsible for deployments in several environments 
 Required qualifications to be successful in this role 
 General: 
• Strong interpersonal and communication skills 
• Experience with Agile Methodology 
 Must Have Technical Skills: 
• 6+ years of experience as an expert in IBM Financial Transaction Manager (FTM) product 
• 6+ years of experience with IIB 
• Experience in other IBM products such as WTX and MQ. 
• 5+ years of java development experience 
• Experience working on large BI projects/programs 
• Payment Processing Systems in large banks 
• Payments messaging standards such as NACHA, ISO 20022, SWIFT 
• SOA design principles 
• Modeling using RSA 
• Mapping for the different types of Payment standards 
• Experience with application development on WebSphere Message Broker or a similar ESB 
• IBM WebSphere Transformation Extender 
• Developing Restful web services 
 Good to know: 
• Experience in WTX mappings 
 Education: BS in Computer Science, Software Engineering, related IT field or equivalent combination of experience and education 
 We offer a competitive compensation package where you will be rewarded based on your performance and recognized for the value you bring to our business. In addition, our benefits package includes multiple medical, dental, & vision options, both a Shared Purchase Plan (SPP) for CGI stocks and a 401(k) plan, as well as numerous other life style benefits. CGI also provides sick leave and vacation pay.
