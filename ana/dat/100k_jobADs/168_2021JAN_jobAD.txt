 RESOURCES and SUPPORT for those affected by COVID-19 
 **Niagara 's WorkSourceOne **has the resources and expertise to help you find your next great position! 
 If you feel you have a pretty good start on your job search but just need access to the tools... 
 At each of our two One Stop career center locations you will find on-site resource rooms open Monday - Friday equipped with computers (w/Internet access as well as resume/cover letter templates and other tools) phones, fax machines, & copiers so you can hit the ground running. **** Or maybe you would like some assistance in preparing as effective and quick a job-search as possible?  If so, we are here to help! 
 [We provide:](/Portals/0/docs/Resources/Center Resource Room list of services 2018.pdf?ver=2019-01-18-112910-447) 
 
 Opportunities to meet directly with Businesses through events like weekly Business Recruitments, Open Recruitment sessions, and even Job Fairs 
 On-the-Job Training incentives which reimburse your new employer up to 75% of your starting salary to help you nail down the job offer for a position where you may have a skills gap 
 The area's Hottest Jobs updated frequently throughout the week right here on this website's ** HotJobs page ! ** 
 Regular informative Workshops delivered by Center employment professionals as well as Hiring Managers from local companies -  Upcoming Workshops 
 A weekly networking group called POD (Professional Opportunity Developers) for mid-to-upper management level individuals seeking re-employment 
 Resume preparation assistance and review services 
 Computer and Internet access with Assistive Technologies available including; headphones, adaptive computer (screen magnification, Read & Write Gold, JAWS, Dragon Naturally Speaking) high contrast keyboard with larger print characters, slimblade trackball, scanner and desktop microphone 
 Career Counseling for those struggling with their job search, or thinking of changing careers but not really sure where to begin 
 Access to current job openings 
 Men's & Women's Clothes Closet for interview preparation and career wear 
 Message Service for those who may not have a reliable phone service for employment-related contacts from businesses
