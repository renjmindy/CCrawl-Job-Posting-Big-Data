 My client, a market leading retail business, is looking to recruit an SEO Manager with a strong technical understanding. 
 The SEO Manager will join a highly skilled digital and development team, within a business undergoing a sustained period of digital growth. 
 The role will work on both digital campaigns and digital developments/digital products/micro-sites. You will ensure campaigns and new site builds are optimised effectively. 
 The SEO Manager will possess a strong technical understanding. You will also demonstrate strong communication and collaborative skills. Experience of working closely with developers would be advantageous. 
 Highly competitive package. West Midlands based - currently remote; significant future flexibility will be considered.
