 Caregivers Jobs Bahrain 
 Posted on  July 5, 2011  by  caregiver 
 An employer from the oil rich country of Bahrain is in need of caregivers who are qualified for  Caregivers Jobs Bahrain . This caregivers jobs Bahrain have a Job Order that was approved by the POEA last June 28, 2011 for a POEA accredited recruitment agency located in Malate, Manila. And to fill the needed manpower, the recruiting agency needs to find 5 caregivers for placement in the said caregiver work abroad. 
 If you wish to be included in the list of possible candidates for the said Caregivers Jobs Bahrain, proceed as soon as possible to the office of Saosan Manpower Services and forward your resume and application. For more details or if you have any question regarding the said caregivers jobs Bahrain, you may contact Saosan Manpower Services by way of their telephone number listed below. 
 Saosan Manpower Services 
1845 San Marcelino Street 
Malate, Manila 
Tel No: 3534790
