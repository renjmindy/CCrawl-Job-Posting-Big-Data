 Gleeson Recruitment Group are exclusively recruiting for a Commercial Financial Controller to join an established Business who have gone through a positive growth journey in the last year. You will be a key Business Partner to non Finance with a very operational remit. Based in North Birmingham but must be flexible to travel. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Manage FP&A function 
 Provide financial and commercial guidance to non finance teams to enable them to achieve their objectives. 
 Undertaking strategic analysis and assisting with strategic planning 
 Producing long-term business plans 
 Undertaking research into pricing and factors affecting performance 
 Carrying out business modelling and risk assessments 
 
 Background 
 
 Fully Qualified (ACA, ACCA or CIMA) essential 
 Been in a senior operational Finance role 
 Solid commercial and business awareness 
 Excellent communication- Internal & External stakeholders 
 An analytical approach to work 
 
 On Offer 
 Up to £85k + Car + Bonus 
 Travel required
