 POEA Approved Caregiver Job Hiring July 2011 
 Posted on  August 10, 2011  by  caregiver 
 For the month of July 2011, the POEA approved 6 Job Orders allowing 5 POEA accredited recruitment agencies to recruit caregivers for caregiver job hiring. The said  POEA Approved Caregiver Job Hiring July 2011  are for the countries of Israel, Kuwait, and Qatar and have vacancies for 71 caregivers. Listed below are the names of the recruiting agencies, the country of destination, and the number of caregivers needed for each of the said caregiver work abroad. 
 ISRAEL 
CPM Manpower Services, Inc. ------------------ 18 Caregivers 
Mariposa International Services Co. Inc. ------- 10 Male Caregivers 
Mariposa International Services Co. Inc. ------- 18 Female Caregivers 
 KUWAIT 
Best One Int'l Services and Consultancy Inc. --- 10 Female Caregivers 
A.D.M. International Manpower Services Co. --- 10 Caregivers 
 QATAR 
Placewell International Services Corp. ---------- 5 Caregivers 
 If you are interested in submitting your application to any of the said POEA Approved Caregiver Job Hiring July 2011, proceed directly to the office of your chosen recruiting agency. If you have any question regarding your chosen POEA approved caregiver job hiring, feel free to contact the recruiting agency by way of their landline numbers we have provided below.
