 Job description 
 Sprinkler Fitter 
 
 
 Are you looking for a lifestyle change? 
 
 
 Are you looking to make a role your own? 
 
 
 Are you seeking opportunities to develop your career? 
 
 
 About the Role 
 Our Client, a Leading Fire and Security Company are seeking a Sprinkler Fitter, based at Tarong. 
 The successful candidate would need to live in the South Burnett area or be willing to relocate. You would work across high profile Industrial sites and facilities across the Tarong region as well as some South Burnett sites. 
 The role will be responsible for servicing, installing and commissioning fire protection systems, and delivering excellent results and customer experience. 
 This is Permanent Full time role with Immediate commencement with our Client. If you value onsite relationships and are excited by working with multiple important stakeholders, this is the role for you. 
 In this role you will have the opportunity for Career Development, independence and the opportunity to completely own your role. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Installing, diagnosing, servicing, repairing and carrying out minor works on fire protection systems and equipment to our Client's standards of quality and delivery 
 
 
 Maintaining excellence whilst exceeding customer service expectations at every available opportunity 
 
 
 Potential for Breakdown or On Call related work 
 
 
 Complete and provide evidence of maintenance performed on equipment in an accurate and legible manner, using systems including; i-Service hand held technology 
 
 
 Stock and inventory management 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Minimum 5 Years experience as a Qualified Sprinkler Fitter 
 
 
 Extensive all round experience within Fire and Suppression systems 
 
 
 A sound knowledge of the applicable Australian Standards relevant to Fixed Systems fire protection equipment 
 
 
 Trade Cert / QBCC Certification 
 
 
 Strong Customer service 
 
 
 Drivers License 
 
 
 Culture and Benefits 
 
 
 Great remuneration 
 
 
 High profile client list with a strong book of work 
 
 
 Immediate start in a role you can truly call your own
