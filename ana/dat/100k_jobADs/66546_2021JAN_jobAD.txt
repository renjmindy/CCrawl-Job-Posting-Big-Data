 Interim Group Finance Director - Midlands with travel - 500/600 per day LTD 
 My client is looking for an Interim Group Finance Director to join their business. You will have demonstrable experience of preparing a group of businesses for sale through to successful exit. You will be required initially to support the CEO in tidying up the group function before moving into value add commercial activity. The role is likely to be on average 3 days per week but you would need to be flexible to scale up as required. The role will be Midlands based with an open attitude required to UK travel. The client is likely to add additional businesses to their portfolio and this role will need to support all (potential) M&A activity. 
 You will be a fully qualified chartered accountant with at least 5 years post qualification experience. You will have a strong grounding in the engineering sector ideally within an owner managed group of businesses. You will need to be technically capable with strong excel skills as well as demonstrable commercial acumen with regards preparing a business for sale and a typical M&A skill set. 
 This role is highly private and confidential, absolutely no additional information will be released until a CV has been received and positively vetted by the client. The full brief can only then be discussed upon completion of an NDA following client approval. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
