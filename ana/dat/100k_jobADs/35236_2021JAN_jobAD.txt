 Israel Job Caregiver 
 Posted on  October 5, 2012  by  caregiver 
 A licensed recruitment agency, with local offices in Manila and Iloilo and foreign offices in the countries of Korea and Hong Kong, is now accepting applicants for  Israel Job Caregiver . The said Israel job caregiver is for male and female applicants who have the following qualifications: 
 
 23-45 years old 
 At least Vocational Diploma/Short Course Certificate 
 3 years experience in hospital or private (local or abroad) 
 Can speak and write English language 
 
 If you are qualified and interested in applying for the said Israel Job Caregiver, report personally to any of the offices of Dahlzhen International Services Inc. and submit the following requirements: 
 
 Passport (valid for 3 years) 
 NBI Clearance (Travel Abroad) 
 TOR/Diploma 
 Birth Certificate (NSO-Security Paper) 
 Must take Hebrew lessons upon arrival 
 Need to take one week Caregiver Training Course including TESDA and OWWA 
 Marriage Certificate (NSO Security Paper) for married applicants 
 CENOMAR (Certificate of No Marriage issued by the NSO) for single applicants 
 
 If you have any question regarding the said Israel job caregiver, please contact Dahlzhen International Services Inc. by calling their telephone numbers we have specified below.
