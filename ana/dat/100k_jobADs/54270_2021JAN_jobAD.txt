 About C.T. Male Associates 
 For over a century, C.T. Male Associates Engineering, Surveying, Architecture, Landscape Architecture & Geology, D.P.C. has forged long-term relationships with a diversified mix of public and private clients in the municipal, state, federal, commercial and industrial markets – allowing us to develop a project portfolio as diverse as our client base. Our core values are the same today as they were in 1910: Experience, Expertise and Excellence. At C.T. Male Associates, we pride ourselves on being client-driven, solution-oriented and project focused and are committed to delivering projects that succeed in achieving our clients’ exact goals and objectives. Our diverse staff includes talented engineers, architects, surveyors, and many more, allowing us to offer the most innovative solutions and comprehensive service packages in today’s competitive market. At C.T. Male Associates, we strive to remain on the cutting edge of technical and regulatory changes. Our unique ability to integrate our technical disciplines makes us a strong choice to take a project from conception to completion. Headquartered in Latham, NY, C.T. Male Associates also has offices located throughout New York in areas such as Glens Falls, Syracuse, Johnstown and Poughkeepsie. EEO/D/V/F/M
