 Job Description 
 **  Are you ready for a career that taps into your passion to help others, to improve their quality of life, and to be a professional caregiver, focused on safety and service to your clients **  ? 
 Ellensburg Home Care Services is currently seeking energetic, caring people to join our professional home care provider team. Home Care Providers needed in the Ellensburg and Upper County Areas. 
 A Home Care Provider works independently to implement a prescribed personal care plan that assists local seniors and disabled individuals to live independently, in their own homes. 
 You must be at least 18 years of age (verification required) with, a valid Washington driver's license, with an acceptable driving record and proof of insurance required. Our professional caregivers must have excellent work habits, honesty, and compassion. You will be working in another person’s home and that requires trustworthiness and integrity. With responsibility comes reward. 
 
 Effective 01/01/2021 starting pay is $16.60 with additional raises as you accumulate hours of service 
 Work hours offer flexibility and variety 
 Health insurance is very affordable and available when you work 80 hours or more per month 
 Your union has an excellent retirement plan 
 Training time is paid 
 Mileage reimbursement for use of your personal vehicle for work 
 
 This work can be strenuous and will involve a full range of physical activities. 
 $2.30 ADDT’L TEMPORARY HAZARD PAY until March 31, 2021, for every hour worked. 
 Our employees’ health and safety is of the upmost importance to us, as such we supply all personal protective equipment (PPE’s). 
 We do not accept resumes in lieu of our application. Applicants may apply directly at  https://seniorliferesources.clearcareonline.com/apply/ . 
 Home Care Services is a program of Senior Life Resources, an SE WA ALTC COG and Yakama Nation contractor.  Senior Life Resources is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
