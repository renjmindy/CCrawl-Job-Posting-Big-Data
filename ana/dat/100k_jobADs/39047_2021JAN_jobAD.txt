 Consultant: 
 
 
 Our client is a multi-disciplined Birmingham based consultancy who are looking for a Fire Risk Assessor to join them in an initial 6 month contract. 
 As Fire Risk Assessor you will undertake Fire Risk Assessments on existing tall buildings in and around Birmingham. You can undertake this role as Fire Risk Assessor outside of IR35 through either your own Limited Company or we can introduce our Umbrella Company PSL. 
 The Ideal Candidate: 
- Relevant Memberships/ Qualification e.g. Institution of Fire Engineers, FRACS 
- 5 years' experience in a similar role as Fire Risk Assessor 
- Able to conduct Fire Risk Assessments and advise clients on fire safety and fire risk. 
 Rate:  Negotiable 
 Start Date:  Immediately 
 Duration:  6 months 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
