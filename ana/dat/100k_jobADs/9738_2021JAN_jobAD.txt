 Brand New Work at Home Jobs (Weekend Edition) 
 Published by  aw1219  on October 10, 2020 
 We may earn money or products from some of the companies mentioned in this post. 
 I found some really interesting  work at home jobs  that I wanted to share with you today. This is in addition to the work at home jobs that I posted yesterday on the  Friday freebie page  and throughout the week on the  weekly job section . Be sure to apply to any jobs that you are interested in and share them with your family and friends. 
 A lot of companies are recruiting home agents to complete various tasks from your home office. This means that you can be selective but you do not want to delay applying to any job offers that you desire because they may only have a few openings and could get filled quickly. Additional work at home jobs are posted on the following sites: 
 Workersonboard Community 
 Real Work from Home Jobs 
 Please feel free to join so that you can get access to more free work at home job listings. 
   
 Customer Retention 
 Lumen is now hiring reps to resolve residential customer issues, offer new products and services as well as build a good customer rapport. You must be computer literate, be punctual, strong listening skills, and have a distraction-free workspace at home. Hourly pay with benefits. The company will supply all equipment. 
 Call Center Agent 
 Parallon has openings for those with superb customer service skills to work part-time assisting other call center agents to generate revenue for hospitals. No prior experience required. Pays $13 per hour with benefits. 
 Travel Agent - Computer equipment provided! 
 Teleperformance is recruiting agents to answer calls from card members who want to redeem their rewards points to book trips. You will be responsible for some data entry and for booking leisure domestic and international reservations. Requires previous work from home call center experience, travel experience, be proficient with computers, and strong judgment skills. Paid training with benefits. Hiring for all shifts. 
 Customer Support 
 
 Sywbol is looking for people to assist their customers with technical problems that use their products and services. You will also update their databases and assist with training junior customer support reps. Requires patience, previous customer service experience, and excellent communication skills. 
 Customer Support I 
 VSP is seeking reps to respond to customer inquiries regarding prescriptions, plans, products, services, etc. Requires 1-2 years of customer service experience, excellent verbal skills, working knowledge of MS Office, and can work any shift. 
   
 Universal Call Center Rep - Company will provide laptop and headset 
 Nieman Marcus is hiring reps to render sound underwriting decisions on new credit card applications, review existing credit line requests, and general customer service inquiries. Must be a great listener, have 2 years of experience, and exceptional technical skills. 
 Virtual Tutors - Global opportunity! 
 Course Hero is looking for people to work their own hours helping high school and college students get answers to their course-specific questions. Earn $12 to $20 per hour. 
 Customer Support 
 Rev.com is now hiring reps to assist their customers and freelancers via phone, chat, and email processing incoming orders, and other duties. Requires 1-year sales or customer support experience, be organized, tech-savvy, be patient, and can work afternoons and evenings. 
 
 Inbound Support - Benefits provided 
 GoDaddy is hiring reps to work from home troubleshooting GoDaddy products, providing consultation to customers, and other duties. Requires 6 months of customer service experience, good research skills, knowledge of internet technology, and can multitask. 
 Community Support Associate - Long-term 
 ModSquad is now seeking experts with an enthusiastic attitude to answer tech support questions via email. You must be internet savvy with great people skills, excellent communication, some experience with live streaming, and can work long-term. Competitive salary with benefits. 
 NCP - Super Easy! 
 Get free cash, gift cards, prizes like a Smart TV, cool household items, electronic devices, toys, and so much more plus all members have a chance to win $20,000 in cash for scanning barcodes from your purchases. 
 Nielsen Computer Panel 
 
 Get free rewards and cash for your internet usage plus win $10,000 with their monthly giveaway! 
 PrizeRebel 
 Get free PayPal, Visa, or free gift cards fast to take polls, watch cool videos, complete offers, and so much more. Must be 13. Open worldwide.
