 Accounting and Finance

 Permanent

 Contact: 
 
 Jo Payne

 Published:

 Fantastic opportunity for a part-qualified accountant to join a world-class team. Full study support on offer. 
 My client is a global leader in its field. We now seek a talented part-qual to continue their path to qualification, based at their UK Head Office in Wolverhampton. 
 Responsibilities for this role will include: 
 - all aspects of month end 
 - accruals & prepayments 
 - balance sheet ownership, including reconciliations, interrogation of items, coding, etc 
 - supporting the FP&A process 
 - accruals & prepayments 
 - taking a key role in the statutory financial reporting & consolidation process in UK & US GAAP 
 - intercompany re-charges 
 This role offers a rare opportunity for you to enhance a wide-range of accounting skills, and will pay up to £28k pus full study support. There will be a structured development plan to full qualification for the successful candidate. 
 Please call me on 07813 608579
