 Deputy Clinic Manager 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Deputy Clinic Manager to work in an exceptional dialysis unit setting based in the Havant area. 
 As the Deputy Clinic Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This service offers an alternative to traditional large dialysis chains and product companies, working closely with the NHS to deliver the highest level of care in the most comfortable environment 
 As the Deputy Clinic Manager your key responsiblities include: 
 
 
     To manage the dialysis clinic in the absence of the manager, encompassing effective leadership of staff and ensuring high standards of patient care
 
 
 
     To develop and promote good working relationships between the company and the NHS
 
 
 
     To provide specialised nursing knowledge and skills to enable junior members of the nursing team to develop their skills and knowledge
 
 
 
     Ensure all technical equipment are fully maintained and operational and water quality conforms to Renal Association Standards
 
 
 
     Discuss health concerns with patient; review patient concerns with the multi-disciplinary team as necessary
 
 
 
     Act in professional advisory role to staff providing supervision and guidance, monitoring and evaluating their performance on a continuing basis
 
 
 
     Undertake necessary performance measures such as audits, reviews and patient/staff surveys to ensure compliance with internal, NMC and healthcare best practice guidelines
 
 
 
     Assess, plan, implement and evaluate patient care programmes
 
 
 
     Act as a named staff nurse for a group of assigned patients ensuring 'great care' is provided at all times
 
 
 
     Proactively participate in quality and service improvement programmes
 
 
 
 An ideal Deputy Clinic Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Post basic qualification in renal nursing such as diploma, ENB Course 136 or equivalent.
 
 
 
     ENB998 or equivalent (teaching/assessing)
 
 
 
     At least two years' experience in Haemodialysis working at a Senior level
 
 
 
     Experience of managing and leading a team of nurses
 
 
 
     Awareness and Understanding of NMC code of professional conduct
 
 
 
     Be able to work autonomously and within a multi-disciplinary team
 
 
 
     Have a positive and dynamic approach to providing safe and effective delivery of care
 
 
 
     Excellent communication skills with fluency in English
 
 
 
 **The successful Deputy Clinic Manager will receive an excellent salary from £31,005.00 to £32,727.50 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     27 days annual leave (plus Bank Holidays) increasing with service
 
 
 
     Life Assurance, Discounted Fitness Membership, Stakeholder Company Pension
 
 
 
     Relocation allowance
