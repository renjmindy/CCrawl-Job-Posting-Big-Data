 Are you a part qualified Management Accountant? Would you like to work for a reputable, professional and solution focused business?  If so, then please read on. 
 You will be joining an innovative, growing and committed finance team, reporting directly into the Senior Manager near Coventry. You will receive a salary up to £30k depending on experience alongside other great benefits including study support, career progression and flexible working hours. 
 Responsibilities include: 
 
 Producing of monthly management accounts 
 Developing reporting 
 Prepayments and Accruals 
 Building relationships across the business 
 Preparation of variance analysis 
 Assist prepare financial statements and year end audit 
 Monthly Cost Review 
 Preparing annual budget and forecasts 
 Bank and Balance sheet reconciliations 
 Updating reports and spreadsheets for management 
 System and process improvement 
 
 Is a Management Accountant role right for you? 
 This is a great opportunity for you to join as a Management Accountant, If you enjoy working in a team, often on your own initiative and have excellent excel skills this could be right for you. Ideally you will be part qualified in CIMA or ACCA. 
 Benefits: 
 
 Study Support if required 
 Pension 
 Free onsiteonsite parking 
 Open plan offices 
 Flexible working hours
