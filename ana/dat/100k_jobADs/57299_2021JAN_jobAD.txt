 EnerSys - Bellingham, WA

 Job Description 
 EnerSys is the global leader in stored energy solutions for industrial applications. We have over thirty manufacturing and assembly plants worldwide servicing over 10,000 customers in more than 100 countries. Worldwide headquarters are located in Reading, PA, USA with regional headquarters in Europe and Asia. We complement our extensive line of Motive Power and Energy Systems with a full range of integrated services and systems. With sales and service locations throughout the world, and over 100 years of battery experience, EnerSys is the power/full solution for stored DC power products.We continue to grow in our industry by ensuring all employees possess the following core competencies: customer focused, team player, engaged, ethical, strong communication skills, and works safely. 
 This position will be focusing primarily on our Reserve Power applications, which include telecommunications, IT, uninterruptible power supplies (UPS), utilities and switchgear, security, emergency lighting and general electronics. Some of the reserve power brands include PowerSafe,DataSafe, and Genesis. Our extensive product range serves a variety of reserve power applications and consistently delivers the most effective, powerful and reliable batteries available. 
 Job Purpose 
 With limited supervision, this position will be responsible for professional customer support of Alpha software platforms as well as the creation and maintenance of related knowledge base articles and customer-facing training materials. This is a temporary position. 
 Essential Duties and Responsibilities 
 Develop an extensive working knowledge of the software platforms to be supported, both through self-study and in coordination with development staff 
 Provide end-user application support via in-application chat, trouble ticketing systems, e-mail and phone 
 Troubleshoot issues reported by end-users and provide solutions 
 Facilitate problem-solving between end-users and Alpha development staff 
 Participate with Alpha development staff in testing of and familiarization with new product releases 
 Create and publish easy to understand knowledge base articles 
 Design, create and publish training materials (text, graphics, video) for end-user use 
 Coordinate, produce and publish user-facing web-based training and seminars 
 Provide both ad hoc and scripted live training based upon available materials as directed 
 Qualifications 
 Two year Degree in Engineering or related field 
 2 to 4 years of experience in software support /development of documentation and training materials 
 Superb verbal and written language skills 
 Advanced knowledge of mainstream browsers (e.g.: Chrome, Edge, Safari) and the use of web-based applications 
 Experience diagnosing problems with software products 
 Analytical and troubleshooting skills 
 Ability to effectively organize and maintain written training and knowledge base materials 
 Ability to effectively communicate technical information in non-technical terms 
 Ability to communicate professionally by phone and in writing 
 Familiarity with relational database concepts and tools 
 General Job Requirements 
 This position will work in an office setting, expect minimal physical demands.
