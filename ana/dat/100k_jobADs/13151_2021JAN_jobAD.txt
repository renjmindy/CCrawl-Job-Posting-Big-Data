 Boilermaker 
 Job description 
 Boilermaker 
 
 
 12 Week Position 
 
 
 Accommodation and Meals provided 
 
 
 Lifestyle Roster 5:4/4:5/5:5  - Day and Night Shift 
 
 
 About Our Client 
 Our client operations are located in two of the largest and most productive metallurgical coal basins in the world, the Bowen Basin in Queensland, Australia and the Central Appalachian region of the US, servicing customers throughout the Asia-Pacific, Americas and Europe. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac has opportunities for skilled and experienced Boilermaker who will form part of and contribute to a Mine in the a Bowen Basin Mine. Must have Bucket Rigging Experience. You will be working a Lifestyle Roster 5:4/4:5/5:5 with successful candidates starting as soon as possible. 
 An absolute commitment to working safely, complying with all site safety standards and ensuring a safe and environmentally healthy workplace is essential. It is imperative that you possess the ability and aptitude to maintain high standards of safety, equipment serviceability, housekeeping and compliance with mine working procedures. 
 Duties & Responsibilities: 
 
 Fabrication and maintenance of buckets 
 
 Pre-Requisites for this position include: 
 
 
 Trade Qualified Boilermaker 
 
 
 Bucket Rigging Experience 
 
 
 Minimum 6 months mine experience 
 
 
 Current Standard 11 Induction 
 
 
 Current Coal Board Medical - conducted within 2 years 
 
 
 Current Driver's Licence 
 
 
 A willingness to work rotating rosters 
 
 
 Ability to contribute and work positively in a team environment 
 
 
 Sound Work History 
 
 
 Current Driver's Licence and Right to work within Australia 
 
 
 Benefits of role: 
 
 
 Accommodation and Meals provided 
 
 
 Lifestyle Roster
