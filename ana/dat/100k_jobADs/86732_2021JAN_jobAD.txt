 About Ochsner Health 
 Ochsner Health System is Louisiana’s largest non-profit, academic, healthcare system. Driven by a mission to Serve, Heal, Lead, Educate and Innovate, coordinated clinical and hospital patient care is provided across the region by Ochsner's 40 owned, managed and affiliated hospitals and more than 100 health centers and urgent care centers. Ochsner is the only Louisiana hospital recognized by U.S. News & World Report as a “Best Hospital” across three specialty categories caring for patients from all 50 states and more than 60 countries worldwide each year. Ochsner employs nearly 25,000 employees and over 1,300 physicians in over 90 medical specialties and subspecialties, and conducts more than 700 clinical research studies. Ochsner Health System is proud to be a tobacco-free environment. For more information, please visit ochsner.org and follow us on Twitter and Facebook.    Ochsner Health System is Proud to be an Employer of Choice.    Voted "Best Place to Work” by CityBusiness Magazine  Ranked #11 on the 2018 Indeed list of Best Hospitals To Work  Named to the Becker’s Healthcare 2018 list of “150 Top Places to Work in Healthcare”  Recognized by Zippia as one of the “20 Best Companies to Work for in New Orleans, LA”  Named to the Becker’s Healthcare 2018 list of “100 Great Hospitals in America”  Named as a 2018 Top Workplace by Times-Picayune  Ochsner’s CEO Warner Thomas recognized as a 2018 Top CEO on Glassdoor  Named to the Becker’s Healthcare 2018 list of "52 Great Health Systems to Know"  Ranked #73 on the Forbes 2018 list of "Best Employers for New Graduates"
