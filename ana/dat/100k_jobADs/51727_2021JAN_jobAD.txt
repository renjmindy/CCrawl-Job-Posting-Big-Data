 How to track source of candidates for job boards that are not listed by default on ATLAS   Print  
 Modified on: Wed, 8 Jul, 2020 at 1:00 PM 
 
 The first step is to contact support in order to add the new candidate source tag in our system: 
 EX: (Company name) 
 Once you received a confirmation that the new source name is added in our database, you may start updating job links in order to track the candidate source. 
 The process is simple, all you need is to add the following at the end of every job link: 
 ?source=(New source)
