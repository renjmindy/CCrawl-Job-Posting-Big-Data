 Start your career with Bank of Singapore as an  SAS   Engineer  in the IT Engineering team! ! 
 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 
 Provide technical and functional leadership to various areas including projects, enhancements and L3 support. 
 
 
 Work as a solution manager interacting with the architects, project managers to provide inputs, and effort estimates to the development of the various project artifacts namely logical design, functional design, technical design, project initiation document. 
 
 
 To carry out development as well as supervise developer(s) or engaged development services, System Integrator or vendor to deliver required software application change enhancements (CRs), bug fixes, and change for risk remediation. 
 
 
 To deliver prototypes (when relevant) based on high level business and functional requirements. 
 
 
 Ensure all development have been unit tested in compliance to design and functional specifications and provide support to downstream testing such as SIT and UAT. 
 
 
 Provide 3rd Level Application Support for analysis and issue resolution; assist team lead in technical problem root cause analysis and see to delivering solution to eliminate or fix root cause in production problems. 
 
 
 Maintain a working knowledge of business processes and solution design. 
 
 
 Provide and demonstrate technical leadership to junior team members . 
 
 
 Qualifications 
 
 
 Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or equivalent 
 
 
 Minimum 8 years of experience in SAS development/support 
 
 
 Proficiency in use of SAS Data Integration (DI)/ SAS Visual Investigator (VI) 
 
 
 Proficiency in use of SAS Visual Analytics (VA) /SAS Visual Text Analytics (TA) 
 
 
 Support data acquisition, validation, cleaning/preparation, and management of incoming data sources to facilitate analysis 
 
 
 Experience in development/deployment/operationalization of fraud and security intelligence solution on SAS Viya 
 
 
 Knowledge of financial industry preferred 
 
 
 Ready to learn new tools 
 
 
 Resources are able to interact with client technical team independently with clarity 
 
 
 Ability to explore new fields and share accumulated knowledge
