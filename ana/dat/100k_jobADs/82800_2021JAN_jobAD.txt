 Internships 
 We provide paid internship opportunities during summer, fall, and winter with the possibility of full-time or part-time employment after your program. 
 We work with a variety of schools, such as:  Concordia University  ,  École Polytechnique  ,  McGill University  ,  École de technologie supérieure (ETS)  of Quebec University, and others. 
 If you're a school administrator looking for or information, please contact:  internship-inquiries@workjam.com
