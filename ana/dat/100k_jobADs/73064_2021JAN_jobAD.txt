 Working at XELIS 
 A future with definite benefits! 
 XELIS is globally engaged for numerous well-known customers, and is also one of the leading companies in the high-end lightweight thermoplastic composites sector at home and abroad. 
 Work in a place, where motivation and performance are perfectly aligned - our aim is to create an environment where our employees feel comfortable, and where they are challenged as well as supported in their development according to their capabilities. We therefore place a key value on an open corporate culture, and the supporting of the personal potential development of each individual. Because we know: Motivation is the key to the success of the company. We would be more than happy to welcome you as a contributing factor to this success, and therefore encourage you to apply for one of our open positions.
