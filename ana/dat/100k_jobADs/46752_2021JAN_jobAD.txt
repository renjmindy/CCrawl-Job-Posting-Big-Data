 Senior Developer LOCALPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 Pennebaker is a strategic brand agency that uses digital and analog tools to help brands reach their full potential. 
 We’re looking for a Senior Front-end Developer to join us in building beautiful websites and web applications. The candidate should be an excellent communicator that collaborates well with our staff of developers, designers, and UX professionals. 
 The Responsibilities 
 
 Able to write front-end and back-end code, and implement and configuring content management systems 
 Help estimate hours needed to implement responsive designs 
 Help solve the team’s toughest technical challenges 
 Mentor Developers I-III and manage the growth of the team 
 Write code for selected web-based projects 
 Help manage and optimize DevOps processes as the team grows 
 Contribute to post-project retrospectives 
 Work with other departments to ensure the complexity, resources, and scalability of all development efforts are fully understood 
 
 Skills 
 
 Ability to “speak Client” during meetings and to interpret client’s goals into action items 
 Basic knowledge of analytics packages and how they work, as well as how to implement them 
 Working knowledge of best UX/UI practices 
 Excellent communication skills 
 Strong attention to detail 
 Highly collaborative 
 
 Technical Requirements 
 
 Advanced knowledge of terminal usage (can shell into Ubuntu servers with ease): MySQL, Bash Scripting 
 Advanced knowledge of HTML/CSS/JS front-end techniques: HTML5, SCSS, VueJS, Twig 
 Working knowledge of server-side languages and techniques: PHP, Node.js 
 Working knowledge of git-related GUI tools (SourceTree, Github) 
 Basic knowledge of analytics packages and implementing event-specific analytics triggers 
 Knowledge of advanced web security practices is a plus 
 Advanced knowledge of Craft CMS a major plus 
 
 Experience 
 6-8 years 
 Benefits 
 
 Health, vision, and dental insurance 
 401(k) with an employer match 
 Paid vacation and sick time 
 
 Pennebaker is a strategic branding & marketing communications firm in Houston, Texas. For 35 years, we’ve been helping companies reach their full potential. Today, our team includes 27 full-time employees and a tested group of talented strategic partners. Passionate about our work, we take our clients’ success personally. We hire smart, creative people to tackle the difficult challenges our clients face. We’re excited about adding another team player to our growing firm.
