 Gleeson Recruitment are looking to appoint an experienced and committed Payroll Specialist. This is an exciting opportunity to support the transition of Payroll responsibilities from an outsourced service provider to an in-house operation, with a focus on establishing a high performing, innovative, and successful Payroll team for the future. This role will offer the chance to showcase your skills, while developing strong relationship with key stakeholders. This role reports to the UK Payroll Manager. 
 Key responsibilities: 
 
 You will support the transition of payroll responsibilities from an outsourced service provider to an in-house solution 
 Maintain payroll information by developing systems to direct the collection, calculation, and processing of data. (e.g. salaries, benefits, PAYE, third party deductions) 
 You will update payroll records by reviewing and approving changes to collective agreements (T&C's), salary bands, allowances, benefits, and other entitlements 
 You will direct the HR team through orienting workloads in line with planned payroll deadlines, monitoring performance and providing guidance/training where necessary 
 Prepare final payroll reports, net comparisons, employee counts, new starters & leaver checks, BACS initiation, ready for sign-off by Payroll Manager 
 Report on KPI's/SLA's to show operational performance and make suggestions to improve service delivery 
 Support compliance with HMRC/Government legal requirements by studying existing and new legislation; assessing the impact on operations and making suggestions to support continued compliance 
 Maintain payroll guidelines by writing and updating policies and procedures 
 
 Who we are seeking 
 
 Experience as a Payroll Specialist (or similar role), with detailed knowledge and understanding of the end to end payroll cycle 
 Knowledge and understanding of statutory returns, e.g. RTI, P11D's, PSA's 
 Understanding of current legislation and future changes 
 Ability to develop internal & external relationships 
 Excellent analytical skills, with a keen attention to detail 
 
 Desirable 
 
 Knowledge / competence in using SAP based systems 
 Experience of working in a fast paced environment 
 
 This is an exciting time to join a fast paced business - Salary of £40-£46k - Plus excellent benefits
