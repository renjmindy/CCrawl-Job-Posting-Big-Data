 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group are supporting a multi-site engineering organisation in Herefordshire who are looking for a Group Accountant to join their team, with an aim of supporting the Group FC. 
 The role will sit within a team who are responsible for Group Statutory and Management Accounts, Analysis and Business Partnering with senior management and will offer an opportunity to develop the skillset needed to develop into an FC in the future. Due to the nature of the role, it will offer a wealth of hands on accounting exposure, whilst also providing exposure to strict reporting deadlines and interaction with Senior Stakeholders across the region. The role is looking for a real career climber. 
 The ideal candidate will have group reporting and consolidation experience and preferably be ACA qualified. 
 Group Accountant Package: 
 
 £50,000 - £60,000
