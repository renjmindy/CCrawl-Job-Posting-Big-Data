 Job Description 
 FastROI Ltd is the joint type, growing and evolving, electronic and mobile commerce expert, software and services. 
 Our products and services suitable for business and public sector needs. We deliver our solutions to electronic data collection, storage, and transfer data between different information systems. 
 If the following technology areas are our areas of expertise, please contact: Java, Spring, Hibernate, Jasper reports, PostgreSQL / SQL interfaces, as well as the communication between the servers. You are responsible for software design, documentation, programming, and internal testing. Work in close cooperation with the project manager and other ohjelmisto-/asiakasprojekteihin participating team members. We appreciate the self-motivation, social skills, and high-quality performance. 
 As a growing organization, FastROI Ltd offers a wide range of jobs and a competitive salary. You have the opportunity to work in a creative environment. Customer-specific projects to get involved with our customers to develop the business and services. We invest in our employees' well-being and education.
