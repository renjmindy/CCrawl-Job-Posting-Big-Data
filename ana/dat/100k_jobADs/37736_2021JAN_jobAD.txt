 Job description 
 Water Cart Operator 
 
 
 5:2 roster 
 
 
 12 months contract 
 
 
 Accomodation and Meals provided. 
 
 
 About Our Client 
 Our client is a family owned and operated business with extensive experience in the plumbing industry. The Company has a strong commitment to Health and Safety, which enables to deliver larger-scale multi-disciplinary projects involving roads and mine site projects. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking a skilled and experienced Water Cart Driver to commence at a project near Mt. Coolon. You will be working a 5:2 Roster with successful candidates starting as soon as possible. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Operate HC Water Cart 
 
 
 Operate trucks safe and competent 
 
 
 Assure workplace hazards are reported and identified 
 
 
 Meet production target requirements 
 
 
 Pre-start checks on machinery 
 
 
 Skills and Experience: 
 
 
 HR/MC licence required 
 
 
 Previous Roller experience in road construction 
 
 
 Current Driver's Licence 
 
 
 Right to work within Australia 
 
 
 Current Driver's Licence and Right to work within Australia 
 
 
 Benefits of role: 
 
 
 5:2 roster 
 
 
 12 months contract 
 
 
 Accomodation and Meals provided.
