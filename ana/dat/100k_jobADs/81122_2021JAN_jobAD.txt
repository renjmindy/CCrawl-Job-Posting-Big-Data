 Caregiver Jobs Placement for Israel 
 Posted on  September 6, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This is a perfect opportunity for all the Filipino caregiver who want to apply for caregiver jobs placement for Israel. The POEA recently approved a job order last August 13, a job order balance for 26 caregivers for caregiver jobs placement. The said caregiver jobs placement is for the country of Israel and the said job order for caregiver jobs placement was approved for a recruitment agency based in Parañaque. 
 For more info on the said caregiver jobs placement for Israel, just visit the said caregiver jobs placement agency directly or contact them through their landline numbers stated below. 
 Star World International Manpower & Placement Agency Inc. 
231 Quirino Avenue, Tambo 
Parañaque 
Tel. Nos: 8524575 / 8546100 / 8548042
