 My client based in Edgbaston are looking for a part time Accounts Administrator to join their team on an ongoing temp basis. Reporting to the Office Manager you will be joining a small team and will need to be hands-on and self motivated, ensuring that all relevant work is completed in a timely and accurate manner. 
 Duties: 
 
 Working as part of a busy team of 4 covering all aspects of finance and administration for the company 
 Daily post dues 
 Take inbound calls 
 Data processing 
 General administration including; typing, filing and scanning 
 
 Person: 
 
 Confident on Microsoft Excel and other Microsoft Office systems 
 Comfortable working in a busy and fast paced environment. 
 Professional, self motivated and proactive in your approach; 
 Focused on the delivery of a first class service; 
 Robust in your approach, with the ability to win the trust and confidence of others; and a team player who is approachable and able to work alongside others.
