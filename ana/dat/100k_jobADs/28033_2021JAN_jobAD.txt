 Consultant: 
 
 
 You will be a proactive and organised individual used to working in a KPI driven environment working towards strict deadlines whist maintaining accuracy and quality of work. Working in a large team you will have excellent communication skills to work with both your direct team as well as external stakeholders. This is a permanent full-time position based in Birmingham. 
 Duties include: 
 
 You will focus on ensuring invoices are produced and processed to deadline 
 Ensuring compliance with business guidelines 
 Monitor unbilled invoices and ensure that queries are resolved in timeframes to reduce unbilled activity 
 Complete reporting on any issues or unresolved queries 
 Liaise with stakeholders 
 
 
 Person Specification: 
 
 Excellent communication skills. 
 Ability to take ownership 
 Proactive
