 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson RG are working with a contract driven business based in Wolverhampton who are looking for an Interim Financial Accountant to join them on a 3 month contract. The Financial Accountants position will be to support the Finance Manager through their impending year end. 
 Responsibilities include: 
 
 Production of statutory accounts and management accounts 
 Management and preparation of all Balance Sheet reconciliations and Audit files 
 Liaising with Group Finance and Auditors to ensure the successful delivery of accounts 
 
 Experience required: 
 
 Qualified Accountant (ACA, ACCA or CiMA) or equivalent 
 Ability to produce high quality work within agreed deadlines 
 Ability to manage expectations of Senior Managers, Directors and external stakeholders 
 Communicates in a clear and practical manner, both verbally and in writing 
 
 If you think you are suitable for this role please apply.
