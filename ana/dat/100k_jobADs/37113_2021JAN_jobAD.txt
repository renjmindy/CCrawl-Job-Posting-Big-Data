 My client, a successful and well established business based in the West Midlands, is looking to recruit a Franchise Marketing Manager. 
 My client has undergone a period of sustained growth and has identified further growth opportunities across their franchise network. 
 The Franchise Marketing Manager position will encompass: 
 
 Planning and delivering the lead generation strategy 
 Developing and delivering marketing campaigns for franchisee recruitment 
 Delivering multi-channel campaigns across channels to include: CRM-email, Press, PR, Social, Web and Content 
 
 The successful candidate for this role must have the following: 
 
 B2B marketing experience 
 Food retail experience 
 Multi-channel marketing experience with a focus on lead generation 
 
 This role is Midlands based, but with some flexibility; candidates with the above 'must-haves' from further afield are encouraged to apply.
