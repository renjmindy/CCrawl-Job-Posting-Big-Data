 Excavator Operator 
 Job description 
 Job highlights 
 - 12.5 hour days 
 - 2 x 1 roster 
 - Day shift and night shift available 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac is currently seeking experienced Excavator Operators for a 2 x1 roster at Koolyanobbing Mine site. Successful candidates will have had experience on the 2600 and 3600 Excavators within a production environment. 
 Skills & Experience 
 - Current Australian drivers licence (HR) 
 - 12+ months experience on 2600 & 3600 Excavators 
 - Construction awareness certificate 
 - Right to work in Australia 
 - Ability to pass a pre-employment medical or D&A; screening 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
