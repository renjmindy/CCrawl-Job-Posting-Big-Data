 Application Support (financial systems) - Birmingham - £30,000 - £35,000 
 Software, financial systems, Sage, cognos, support, ITIL, service owner, business applications, ERP, finance, SaaS, cloud, Architecture. 
 We are currently recruiting for an Application Support Specialist to join a growing IT division based in central Birmingham. The role will be managing support of a range of finance applications along with relationship management of 3rd party suppliers. It will be a technical hands-on support position dealing with end user queries and becoming the subject matter expert. 
 The ideal candidate will have a background within Application Support of any financial packages. Sage experience would be desirable but not essential. 
 Required experience: 
 
 Software support / application support experience. 
 Experience working closely with finance teams and systems. 
 Relationship management of 3rd party suppliers. 
 Excellent communications skills with end users, stakeholders and 3rd parties. 
 Preparation of training materials. 
 Managements of upgrades and installations. 
 ITIL certified (desirable). 
 Working knowledge of the following systems would be highly desirable:
 
 Sage X3 ERP 
 Cognos reporting 
 Eskar AP Solution
