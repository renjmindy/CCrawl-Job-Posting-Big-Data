 Child Care Jobs Australia 
 Posted on  March 20, 2009  by  caregiver 
 A Maidstone based specialist recruitment agency that offers services to both employers and employees in the social care sector is seeking qualified and unqualified social care staff to fill up their vacancy in the voluntary, statutory, and private sector. The chosen candidates may choose from temporary or permanent work assignments for child care jobs Australia. 
 The agency is offering a wide range of attractive and career building opportunities to those who are qualified by training and experience. If interested, you may view their current child care jobs vacancies by visiting  www.kentscp.com/en/Home
