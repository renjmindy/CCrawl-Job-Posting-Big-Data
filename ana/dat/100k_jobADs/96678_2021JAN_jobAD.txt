 Position - Payroll Specialist 
 Salary - £32,000 - £36,000 
 Location - Leicester 
 Must have iTrent systems experience to be considered for the role 
 An exciting opportunity has arisen for an enthusiastic, hardworking payroll Specialist to join a dynamic team in Liecester. The role is offered as full time and permanent and would suit a highly motivated quick learner looking to take the next step in their career and progress in the business 
 This is a fantastic opportunity to join a Growing business who are keen to progress the successful candidate. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Processing payroll from start to finish on a weekly and monthly basis in an accurate and timely manner and in accordance with company and statutory policies 
 Pensions administration 
 Dealing directly with any payroll related queries 
 Calculating holiday pay 
 Calculate any statutory pay - SSP, SMP, SPP 
 Produce and process P11D, P45 and P60 forms for employees 
 Processing starters and leavers 
 
 Key Competencies 
 
 An experienced payroll Specialist 
 Highly accurate 
 High level of customer service 
 High organisation skills
