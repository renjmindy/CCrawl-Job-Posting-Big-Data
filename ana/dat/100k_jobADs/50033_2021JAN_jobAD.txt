 Job Description 
 If you are an Eden Associate or Elder Care Specialist (or desire to become one), your skills are needed at our organization. We are hiring RN care partners. we´re looking for a floor nurse (RN) to work Evening Shift (8-hr shifts) e/o weekend. We are a unique long-term care community focusing on the well-being of elders and staff. 
 Consistent assignments, caring for the same residents all the time is a goal in our approach to care. Our Director of Nursing is progressive and has an engaging leadership style making input from the staff expected. We make decisions using learning circles every day. Come join one of our neighborhood teams and love where you work 
 Depending on your employment status or classification, you may have access to the following: 
•Onsite Wellness Center with gym and warm-water pool 
•Employee Discounts in Bistro and Wellness Center 
•Education Reimbursement Program 
•Access to a full range of benefits 
•Paid time off 
•An opportunity to do the best work of your life 
 What you’ll do: 
 - Explains procedures and treatments to patient to gain cooperation, understanding, and allay apprehension 
- Administers prescribed medications and treatments in accordance with approved nursing standards 
- Maintains awareness of comfort and safety needs of patient 
- Observes patient, records significant conditions and reactions, and notifies the Physician of patient’s condition and reaction to drugs, treatments, and significant incidents; may notify supervisor as needed 
- Performs nursing assessments to detect deviations from normal and assess condition of patient 
- Responds to life-saving situations based upon nursing standards, policies, procedures, and protocol 
- Documents interventions, patient behaviors and physical assessments for assigned patients 
- Initiates a patient/family-education plan according to the individualized needs of the patient/family, as prescribed by Physician 
- Participates in quality improvement activities 
 What you have: 
 - Registered Nurse (RN) license in good standing in Colorado 
- At least two years' nursing experience in sub-acute or long-term care (leadership experience is a plus) 
- Flexibility, initiative, determination, professionalism, and a good heart 
 What you'll get: 
 - Access to our state-of-the-art wellness center! 
- Employee Discounts in Bistro 
- Education Reimbursement Program 
- Access to premium benefits (Aetna Medical and Dental, VSP, Life, LTD, pet insurance and more!) - FT status 
- Access to 401k with company match to help you remain financially healthy - any status 
- An opportunity to do the best work of your life
