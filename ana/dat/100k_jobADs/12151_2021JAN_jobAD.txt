 DUTIES 
The Health Care Aide collaborates with an interdisciplinary team to ensure resident/client care is delivered in a way that acknowledges resident choice, dignity, privacy, independence, spirituality and individuality inclusive of a home-like environment: 
 
 Recognizes resident/clients as unique individuals with unique cultural, spiritual and psychosocial needs 
 Advocate for our resident/clients and observe and collaborate with the team to report any changes in the residents/clients condition as well as any residents/clients needs or concerns 
 Engage in activities of daily living and respond to residents/clients needs for assistance with bathing, dressing, meal service, grooming, and portering, while respecting the residents/clients choice 
 Provides quality service according to the care plan and the GSS/GSC Standards of Care including development and updating of care guides/plans 
 Promotes team work by helping others as a peer coach through the sharing of knowledge and experience to enhance residents/clients care 
 
 QUALIFICATIONS 
 The ideal candidate will bring to this opportunity: 
 
 Recognized Health Care Aide Certificate or equivalent 
 Grade 12 or GED 
 First Aide 
 Alberta: Registration with the Alberta Health Care Aide Directory 
 Alberta: Food Safe Basic or equivalent 
 BC: Registration with the BC Care Aide and Community Health Worker Registry 
 BC: Food Safe 1 (post July 29, 2013) or equivalent 
 Able to communicate information in English both verbally and in writing 
 Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to deal courteously and positively with residents/clients, resident’s family, visitors and team members 
 Demonstrates flexibility by changing routine to accommodate residents/clients needs 
 
 REQUIREMENT 
 All new employees are required to submit: 
 
 a current and clear Criminal Record Check and if applicable, a Vulnerable Sector Check that has been obtained no more than 90 calendar days prior to the hire date; 
 a tuberculosis screening that has been completed no more than 12 months prior to the hire date.
