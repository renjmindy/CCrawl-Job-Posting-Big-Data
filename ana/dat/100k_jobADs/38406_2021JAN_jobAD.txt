 My client, a well established retail business with a strong digital presence, is looking to recruit an SEO Manager. 
 This role, a permanent position, will join a well established digital team to lead on their SEO activity. A digitally led business, this role will help drive organic traffic as part of the overall acquisition strategy. 
 The SEO Manager role will encompass: 
 
 Implement, deliver, and improve an SEO strategy, including campaign work 
 Regular technical audits and keyword research to improve traffic and visibility 
 Develop link building/outreach campaigns 
 Make recommendations to the content team to drive search results 
 Improve on-page/off-page and technical SEO 
 Build out an SEO team. 
 
 The ideal candidate for this SEO Manager will have proven experience in improving SEO performance and delivering successful SEO strategies. In addition: 
 
 Hands-on SEO strategic implementation 
 Strong technical software skills: Screaming Frog, Google Analytics, etc 
 Able to create reports to demonstrate performance 
 
 Highly competitive package.
