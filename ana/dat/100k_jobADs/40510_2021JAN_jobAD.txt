 Once Again, Real Jobs Offered by Working Solutions Make the News 
 __03/14/2019 
 Dallas, Texas  – Working Solutions, a leader in on-demand contact center outsourcing, is among the companies highlighted on KHOU 11’s “Don’t Waste Your Money” broadcast:  The best work-from-home jobs of 2019. 
 About Working Solutions 
 With 20+ years of success, Working Solutions is a recognized leader in onshore, on-demand contact center solutions. Its remote workforce includes sales, customer care and technical experts—with 110,000+ registered agents in the United States and Canada. They deliver fast-flex business process outsourcing (BPO) services for clients and their customers across diverse industries, such as consumer services, healthcare, retail, travel and hospitality.
