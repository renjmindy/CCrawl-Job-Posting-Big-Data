 Career 
 Specialists and junior executives 
 
 Scope for development at work, with your family and for you personally. 
 Our experts are the factors of success behind our products and complete solutions. Their expertise and often decades of experience ensure our global growth and our company’s innovative strength. Reason enough to perfectly adapt our framework conditions, working time models and communication channels to our employees’ needs and to inspire them in the long run. 
 Individual development opportunities 
 Regular development talks, advanced training by the Wirthwein Academy and external courses help you to develop your strengths further and grow into new assignments and projects. 
 More flexibility and family 
 Learn about our versatile working time models and how flexibly we deal with your individual life situations and requests. 
 Become acquainted with Wirthwein 
 A good personal support during your familiarization phase is a decisive factor and the reason why you will feel at home after only a few weeks. 
 Team performance 
 We are experts who complement their expertise and jointly master challenges. We in the team motivate ourselves to new top performances in a productive and familiar working atmosphere. 
 Your personal fulfillment 
 You will work there where you can play to your strengths. In the process, we will challenge you in an international environment, assign you responsibility and count on your ideas and initiatives. 
 Innovative strength 
 We are not followers. We are industry leaders. Digitalization, lean management and innovative technologies do not only enhance the process efficiency of our plastic solutions but also motivate and inspire our staff. 
 We are in search of personalities who match with us. 
 Our family-run company does not just fill vacancies. We are in search of team players with a highly developed social competence and the will to develop further every day anew. 
 
 You like to assume responsibility and are ready for new challenges 
 You like to work as a team, in small international project teams and with much appreciation and respect for your colleagues 
 You have a technical affinity and feel inspired by new technologies 
 You want to get better every day 
 You are not in search of a step stone but of a long-term perspective 
 
 Your career start with Wirthwein.
