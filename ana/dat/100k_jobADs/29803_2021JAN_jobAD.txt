 From Rietberg to the world 
 In a globalized world, we can make a valuable contribution to our international customers with our products and services. Our employees get in contact with new cultures and habits, daily. The combination of high quality products and efficient production processes – made in Germany – secures jobs and enables the sustainable development of the production location Germany. 
 With about 60 sales partners in 44 countries, our focus is not only on the national, but also on the international markets. Our subsidiaries and partners worldwide support us with our task. 
 In a global work environment you want to collaborate with national and international customers? As a global group of companies, we are a reliable partner for our customers - in Germany and around the world. 
 
 WP Kemper  is part of the WP BAKERYGROUP - the world's largest provider of complete baking processes.
