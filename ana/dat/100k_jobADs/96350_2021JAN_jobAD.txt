 IT Infrastructure Lead 
 Annual Salary:  £43-48,000 
 Location:  Birmingham 
 Position:  Permanent 
 
 
 Would you consider yourself a subject matter expert in IT Infrastructure? Confident in being accountable for the Infrastructure of a global business? Looking for a new challenge to progress into an Infrastructure Manager? 
 Well, we have an opportunity that may be right up your street. My client is looking for an experienced Infrastructure Engineer/Lead to take ownership of their infrastructure and be the most senior level of escalation for the IT department. 
 Reporting to the General IT Manger you will be responsible for maintaining the company's business systems including; servers, virtualisation, storage, networks and security. 
 WHAT TECHNICAL EXPERIENCE YOU WILL NEED: 
 
 
 VMware vSphere. 
 EMC VNX SAN (RecoverPoint). 
 Windows Server/Active Directory (2003 - 2012). 
 SQL server (2005 - 2012. 
 SSL VPN (SonicWALL). 
 SonicWALL Firewalls. 
 LAN/WAN Technologies. 
 HP Servers (ProLiant). 
 Office 365/Exchange Online. 
 VoIP Systems. 
 HP ProCurve (desirable). 
 Cisco Meraki Wireless Systems (desirable). 
 Symantec Backup Exec/Endpoint Protection (desirable). 
 
 You will need to be proficient in most the above systems and platforms but are not expected to an expert in them all. 
 If you are interested in what you have read so far and would like to find out more, please apply and wait to be contacted OR if you are confident that this is for you call me right away on 0121 752 3818. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
