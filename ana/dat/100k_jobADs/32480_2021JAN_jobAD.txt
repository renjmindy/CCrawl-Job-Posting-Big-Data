 Career 
 Your Future with CONTRACT 
 As a company, CONTRACT is open to new ideas, developments and suggestions. Mostly it is people who bring in exactly that. We are looking for contacts that enrich us in this sense, people who are looking for an exchange and who are interested in creating something together that will enable them, their colleagues and their customers to grow. In the ideal case, developments in our company can be linked to your personal life stages. Whether this phase is designed as an internship, as a cooperation or as a permanent position, we will find out together. Come to us. We look forward to discussing this with you in a personal conversation. 
 You could see our open positions on our german website. 
 If you are interested in a cooperation with us we are happy if you contact us via phone or mail.
