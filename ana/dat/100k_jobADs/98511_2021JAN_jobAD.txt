 Most WorkSource offices are closed for in-person services due to COVID-19 but you can still get help with your work search by  contacting your local office  or by using LiveChat feature on this web page.  Job Description 
 **Commercial Cleaners 
** 
**Full and Part-Time Positions 
 $13.00-13.50** 
 Action Cleaning Services is looking for   Janitors   to clean local commercial buildings. No experience is necessary and all training will be provided. 
 **Shifts are currently available: 
** 
 **Full-Time:  **  Monday-Friday 5:00 pm -1:00 am 
 Part-Time: 
 
 Thursday 9pm to 12:30 amand Friday 5:30 pm to 9:30 (some flexibility) 
 Monday through Friday: 3 to 5 hours daily between 8am and 1pm 
 Float Schedule 
 
 ** 
 Some of the duties required include:** 
 
 Sweeping, mopping, and vacuuming floors 
 Cleaning glass and mirrored surfaces 
 Dusting, including high dusting, fixtures and furniture 
 Disinfecting sinks, toilets and walls 
 Restocking toilet paper, garbage bags and paper towels 
 Emptying trash and separating recycling 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 Must be 21 years of age or older and legally able to work in the U.S. 
 Pass a criminal background check 
 Able to lift and carry up to 25 pounds 
 Demonstrated ability to use a smartphone and send/receive professional emails 
 Able to stand, reach, kneel, lift and walk continuously throughout shift 
 Successfully pass a comprehensive background check 
 Able to speak, read, and write effectively in English 
 Have a clean driving record (2 or fewer violations in the last 3 years) 
 Pass a drug test including marijuana 
 
 Action Cleaning Service is an equal opportunity employer.
