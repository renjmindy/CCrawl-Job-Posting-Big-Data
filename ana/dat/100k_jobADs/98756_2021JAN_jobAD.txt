 Contracts And Sourcing Analyst 
 Job description 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia창s leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country창s leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities. 
 About the Role 
 This role will play a key part in delivering strategic sourcing services and repairs to meet business unit and internal engineering requirements. This is initially a 6 month contract with the possibility of extension. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 You will be responsible for: 
 
 
 Sources and contracts of tactical and strategic goods, services and repairs to meet business unit and internal engineering requirements, including local and capital procurement projects. 
 
 
 Establishes and administers group and Site-specific goods and services contracts through understanding and determining end user requirements, and collaborating with Site-based stakeholders at multiple levels. 
 
 
 Maintains ongoing and recommends a reliable supplier base through building strong relationships with suppliers. 
 
 
 Identifies, reports and implements opportunities for improvement to systems and processes with guidance from line manager. 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 To be successful in this role you will have the following: - 
 
 
 Relevant qualification in Contract management, Supply Chain or higher will be highly regarded 
 
 
 Previous experience in contract management and strategic sourcing in engineering & mining essential. 
 
 
 Demonstrated ability to foster strong relationships with suppliers 
 
 
 Superior communication skills in English
