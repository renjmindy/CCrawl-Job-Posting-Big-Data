 Worksource Montgomery  |  Career Support  |  Who We Serve  |  Immigrants 
 We want to help you apply the knowledge, skills and training you've gained throughout your career to emerging and lucrative fields here in the United States. WorkSource Montgomery partners with Upwardly Global and Montgomery County’s Gilchrist Immigrant Resource Center to provide assistance to our immigrant population looking for training and opportunities in the Montgomery County. 
 Upwardly Global 
 Upwardly Global helps work-authorized immigrants, refugees, asylees, and Special Immigrant Visa holders (SIVs) restart their professional careers in the United States. Visit  upwardlyglobal.org  to learn more. 
 To see our referral form please  click here! 
 The Gilchrist Center 
 The Gilchrist Center is a gateway for County immigrants: they can obtain information and referrals to programs and services offered by government agencies and community organizations, strengthen their life skills through English and basic computer classes, and increase their knowledge to prepare for civic life and/or U.S. citizenship. The Gilchrist Immigrant Resource Center is a community where residents can contribute their talents to help others, and it is a testament to Montgomery County’s commitment to building one of the world’s most welcoming communities. 
 Visit  montgomerycountymd.gov/gilchrist/  to learn more. 
 The Literacy Council 
 The Literacy Council of Montgomery County (LCMC) sustains and grows the social and economic equity of adults in our region through education and workforce training. LCMC teaches adults to read, write, and speak English in a vibrant multicultural community where English proficiency is a tool for success. LCMC offers no cost  English as a Second Language classes . Learn more on their website:  literacycouncilmcmd.org
