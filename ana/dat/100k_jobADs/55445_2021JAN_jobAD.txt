 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs 
 Posted on  September 7, 2009  by  caregiver 
 When applying for caregiver jobs abroad being advertised by Philippine recruitment agencies, it is important that the caregiver jobs applicants first check with the POEA if there is an existing Job Order approved by the POEA for the said caregiver jobs. Second, it is also important that all caregiver jobs applicants must make it a point to check with the POEA if the agency you are going to apply to is licensed by the POEA to recruit applicants for abroad. 
 At present, there are more than 700 caregiver jobs approved for recruitment by the POEA for the year 2009 and this is for the different recruitment agencies in the Philippines. The said POEA approved caregiver jobs is for the countries of Israel, Qatar, Kuwait, United Arab Emirates, Bahrain, US, Italy, Cyprus, New Zealand, Oman, Saudi Arabia, and Hong Kong. 
 To know the details about the said POEA approved caregiver jobs, click  HERE . 
 To know the status and contact infos of each recruitment agency listed with the POEA, click  HERE . 
 Before applying for any caregiver jobs, be sure to verify with the recruitment agency involved if the Job Order is still active or not.
