 Are you a payroll expert looking to join an exciting and developing business? Do you enjoy a payroll position focused on employee support, solving queries and building relationships? If so, then read on. 
 As a payroll officer you will be working in a busy team based near Coventry. 
 Responsibilities include: 
 
 Responsible for the collation of payroll information, together with query handling and ensuring the smooth running of the payroll 
 Calculate end to end weekly and monthly payroll 
 Process SSP/SPP/SMP payments 
 Resolve HMRC /DWP queries 
 Produce weekly / monthly reporting including holidays, absence 
 Act as the first point of contact for company car and health care and salary sacrifice scheme 
 
 Is this role right for you? 
 This is a great opportunity where you will take ownership of your own payroll area running end to end processing as part of a wider team. 
 Benefits: 
 
 25 Days holiday + Bank Holidays 
 Pension 
 Free onsite parking 
 Open plan offices 
 Flexible working hours
