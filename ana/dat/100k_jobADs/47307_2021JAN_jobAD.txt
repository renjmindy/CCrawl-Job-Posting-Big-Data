 Be the key player to run our infrastructure and devops. Work closely with our VP of Eng and Head of Security. 
 Role overview 
 We want you to be part of our success story. Our growth has opened an opportunity for an ambitious Dev Ops Team Lead. If you value autonomy and accept the responsibility that comes with it, are keen on experimenting and learning from failure, do not accept low effort and strive to constantly improve yourself and your team, you will be a great fit. 
 What you will do: 
 
 Own our infrastructure from, CI/CD to trace monitoring and log management 
 Solve scalability and resilience problems using gitops, infrastructure as code, microservices, container orchestration, distributed databases, and message queues 
 Maintain the security of Passbase systems 
 Build and mentor a great team 
 Help shape and execute our processes 
 Design the overall architecture alongside other experienced professionals 
 Learn and grow with us

 Grafana / Prometheus, ELK, psql, Redis 
 AWS (EC2, S3, RDS, ElastiCache)
