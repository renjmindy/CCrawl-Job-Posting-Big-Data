 I am working with a growing construction firm in Tamworth who are looking for an immediately available Accounts Payable Clerk to join their team on a permanent basis. This role is reporting directly into the Finance Manager. 
 Working in a small, committed and hardworking finance team based in Tamworth, you will be experienced in accounts payable assistant and looking to move into a role where you will manage your ledger and also get involved in a variety of other tasks. You will receive a salary of up to £24k depending on experience alongside other great benefits. 
 Responsibilities include: 
 
 Purchase Ledger and Invoice receipting 
 Investigating and resolving discrepancies 
 Effective communication with suppliers 
 Bank reconciliations 
 CIS payments 
 Managing the sub contract ledger 
 Petty Cash reconciliation 
 Company Credit Card reconciliation 
 Spreadsheet Maintenance 
 
 Is this role right for you? 
 This is a great opportunity for you to join a tight team as an accounts payable assistant where your energy and enthusiasm will genuinely be rewarded. If you enjoy working in a team, love all things finance and want to develop your accountancy skills - this could be the one for you. Click apply to be considered.
