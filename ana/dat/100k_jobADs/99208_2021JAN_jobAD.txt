 POEA Approved Jobs Caregiver for March 2011 
 Posted on  April 14, 2011  by  caregiver 
 For the month of March 2011, the POEA approved several Job Orders that will provided employment opportunities to Caregivers who will qualify for Caregiver Work Abroad. These  POEA Approved Jobs Caregiver for March 2011  are for the countries of Israel, Qatar, and the UAE and has a total of 250 jobs caregiver. Specified below is the list of the said POEA approved jobs caregiver for March 2011. The list includes the country of destination, the names of the recruiting agencies, and the number of vacancies for each of the POEA approved jobs caregiver for March 2011. 
 ISRAEL 
Provident Overseas Placement Agency --- 26 caregivers 
RISE Manpower Services ----------------- 25 caregivers 
CPM Manpower Services, Inc. ------------ 30 caregivers 
CPM Manpower Services, Inc. ------------ 15 live in caregivers 
 UAE 
Mi Amore International Services Inc. ----- 5 caregivers 
 QATAR 
September Star Incorporated ------------ 150 caregivers 
 If you are interested to apply for any of the said POEA Approved Jobs Caregiver for March 2011, proceed directly to the office of the recruiting agency and submit your resume and application.
