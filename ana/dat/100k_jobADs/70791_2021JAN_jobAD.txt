 Online Merchandising Executive 
 
 Location 
 
 Leicester, Leicestershire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Marketing and Digital 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £25000.00 - £30000.00 per annum 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Darrah O'Sullivan 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 DarrahO'Sullivan@workwithglee.com

 BBBH21681_1610114757 
 
 Published: 
 
 10 days ago 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 Darrah O'Sullivan 
 A brilliant opportunity to join a fact-paced ecommerce department within a leading B2C Business, due to continued growth and success this is a newly created role for the business. As the Ecomm Merchandiser you will be responsible for assisting in the delivery of the trading strategy with a key focus on defining the ecommerce product portfolio, optimising products for the web, and the deployment of content. 
 The Role: 
 
 Continuously look to improve the customer experience and user journey - to drive trading performance and increase sales. 
 Day to day management of the CMS system, ensuring the site is maintained and all product information is accurate and up to date. 
 Manage the promotional activity on the web inline with the annual trading calender, ensuring maximum ROI. 
 Responsible for maximising sales opportunities, with a view to commercialise every customer visit to site. 
 Work with the insight team to analyse sale performance in depth - and findings are used to optimise future trading strategies. 
 Work with the UX team, to deliver test landing pages, split A/B testing with a view to improve bounce back rates etc.. 
 Management of the product portfolio and on-site product placement, ensuring consistency is used across search terms and filters. 
 
 The Candidate: 
 
 Will be commercially driven, and understand the importance of driving website traffic. 
 Will be highly organised and be able to hit tight deadlines 
 Will be result driven and have a strong analytical mindset - utilising data to optimise product performance. 
 Have excellent attention to detail. 
 Will have the ability to communicate with key stakeholders.
