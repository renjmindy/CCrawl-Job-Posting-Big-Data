 Administrative Assistant 
 Apply for the position, Administrative Assistant, with Ellis County today or contact Janet Schmidt for more information. Posted Jan 13, 2021. 
  Apply Online  __    785-621-1736  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 Ellis County Clerk’s office is accepting applications for a three-quarter time position (30 hours) of an Administrative Assistant. This employee is responsible for answering the telephone, assisting the public, accounts payable, working with elections, payroll backup, passport agent, scanning and other related duties as deemed necessary or as required. High school diploma or GED required and one year of experience in secretarial or office administrative position are required. You may apply at the link below or applications can be obtained from the Clerk's Office, as well as a list of job duties and requirements, 718 Main Street, Hays KS or by calling (785)628-9410. Applications will be received in the Ellis County Clerk’s office until February 3rd at 5:00pm. Ellis County reserves the right to accept or reject any or all applications. 
Ellis County is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
