 Everything you need to know about Dancing On Ice star Sonny Jay, from career to relationship status

 Dancing On Ice  is returning to screens super soon, with the series  set to start on Sunday 17 January  – hurray! 
 And joining the lineup will be Capital Radio DJ Sonny Jay, who will be skating against other famous faces including TOWIE star Billie Faiers, Emmerdale actor Joe-Warren Plant and singer Jason Donovan. 
 The 27 year old announced the news of his Capital FM breakfast show , which he hosts alongside I'm A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! star  Roman Kemp .

 He joked: "I'll be happy to leave this competition knowing that I can skate backwards with a beer in my hand. Anything else is a bonus!" 
 Roman, also 27, told his pal: "I'm so happy for you mate," as the announcement was made live on air. 
 But aside from his radio presenter duties, who is Sonny Jay? Here’s what you need to know… 
 Who is Sonny Jay? 
 Sonny shot to fame on Britain's Got Talent in 2012, reaching the final with his boyband Loveable Rogues. 
 The band were signed to Simon Cowell's Syco Music record label after finishing in fourth place on the show, releasing a top 10 hit, What A Night. 
 They split shortly after their headline tour in 2014, with Sonny since turning his attention to presenting.
