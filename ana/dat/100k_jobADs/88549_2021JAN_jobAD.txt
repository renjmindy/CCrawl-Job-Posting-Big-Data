 Senior Project Leader 
 Job description 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac is currently seeking a Project Leader for our client located in Gladstone for a 3 month project with the possibility of extension. The Project Leader role will be related to the planning and delivery of projects from initiation to completion, and focused on achieving objectives through managing resources, budgets, changes, and risks. The successful applicant will have a strong background in large scale structural projects within a mineral processing environment. 
 About our Client 
 Our client is a leading global mining group that focuses on finding, mining and processing the Earth's mineral resources. Active in the alumina sector for more than 110 years operating large-scale, high-quality bauxite mines and alumina refineries and holds the world's most modern and competitive aluminium smelters portfolio. 
 Accountabilities of the role: 
 
 
 Development and implementation of projects in accordance with the approved plan and scope. 
 
 
 Safe delivery of projects by incorporation risk assessment processes in each project phase development. 
 
 
 Application of appropriate project governance and safety mitigation processes whilst delivering the projects through all phases. 
 
 
 Analyses project resourcing levels and seek approval to meet project scope, quality, budget and timelines. 
 
 
 Ensure compliance with standardised project processes, systems and tools across projects. 
 
 
 Coordinates engagement and performance of project vendors as required. 
 
 
 Coordinates project resources to complete the assigned scope. 
 
 
 Collaborates with key stakeholders to drive a culture of collaboration, participation and improvement. 
 
 
 Support the team day-to-day activities in team environment. 
 
 
 Education & Qualifications 
 
 Formal Engineering qualification structural / process or related disciplines. 
 
 About WorkPac: 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels. 
 If you would like to apply for the role, please follow the link or for a confidential discussion please contact Ilse Rall 07 3621 2915
