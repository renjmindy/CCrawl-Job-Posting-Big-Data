 Bill Campbell 
 "Become aware of the power of conscious positive choice and learn how to use it everyday." 
 
 Location:  Chicago, Illinois 
 
 How will clients benefit with you as their coach? 
 
 
 Work with a coach who is their supportive partner in designing their success and creates an environment which values their humanity 
 
 
 Recognize that balance between career and personal life enhance both 
 
 
 Gain greater clarity and confidence
 What results have clients reported after working with you? 
 
 
 Personal and professional dreams realized 
 
 
 New focus and direction in daily life 
 
 
 Enhanced awareness of dynamic personal power 
 
 
 Happiness
 What do clients say about working with you? 
 
 
 "His ability as my coach is reflected in the change that has taken place in my life over the past two years. Bill has given me encouragement and has assisted me in determining what goals I wished to accomplish, how to accomplish them and enjoy the process of growth as they are manifested."  --Educator 
 
 
 "Since I met Bill, I have gone from a person who was frightened to attend a weekend writing workshop by herself, to a woman who is the host of her own radio show."  -- Broadcast Personality 
 
 
 "What I appreciated about working with Bill is that he had a keen ability to zero in on the essence of what I needed to focus on."  -- Publisher 
 
 
 "Through Bill's guidance I learned what my priorities were and were not, what my values were and were not, what I did and did not need in my life at this particular time. Through my coaching relationship with Bill I attained some personal goals that until then seemed to be out of reach. I gained a greater sense of confidence and was able to move forward in my life."  -- Corporate Executive 
 What are 5 strengths you have as a coach? 
 
 
 Excellent Listener 
 
 
 Incisive Questioner 
 
 
 Authentic Human Being 
 
 
 Sensitive and wise advocate 
 
 
 Great Coach
 What is your coaching philosophy? 
 
 
 
 I work with people who have decided to make positive choice a way of life. They are connected, committed and intentional about accepting the responsibility for their own success. They are Positive On Purpose™. 
 
 What types of people do you typically coach? 
 
 
 Executives, managers and CEO's 
 
 
 Entrepreneurs 
 
 
 Medical and Mental Health Professionals 
 
 
 Educators 
 
 
 Artists
 What is your professional background? 
 
 
 President of CampbellQuest Ltd. a consulting, coaching and communications firm. Campbell is a veteran television journalist and dynamic speaker. 
 
 
 Prior to broadcasting, Campbell was director of Communications for the Chicago Urban League and served as staff assistant to the administrative assistant to the Mayor of the City of Chicago. 
 
 
 On the boards of trustees/directors of several Chicago area institutions including: the University of Chicago School of Social Service Administration, the National Conference for Community and Justice (NCCJ), Elmhurst College, the Chicago Department of Cultural Affairs Advisory Board and the Greater Chicago Food Depository. 
 
 
 B.A. from Carleton College (Northfield, Minnesota) and was a William Benton Fellow at the University of Chicago.
