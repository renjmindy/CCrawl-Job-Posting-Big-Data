 About the Role 
 We are growing rapidly and looking for an experienced Deep Learning Engineer to join our Deep Learning Team, with a focus on NLP. You'll be leading the efforts on things like: 
 
 Improving Deep Learning Models for automatically punctuating and casing text 
 Researching and building Deep Learning models for various NLP tasks, such as NER, Summarization, Sentiment Analysis, and various types of text classification tasks 
 Coordinating and preparing datasets for training new models 
 
 We're a small, creative, and democratic team interested in pushing the state of the art forward. 
 Qualifications 
 
 3-5+ years of experience with Python 
 3+ years of experience training various NLP and NLU models with modern model architectures 
 2+ years of experience with Deep Learning frameworks like PyTorch and TensorFlow 
 2+ years of experience training distributed deep learning models on GPUs 
 
 Technology 
 All of our Deep Learning models are trained in PyTorch, on extremely large in-house datasets, across dozens of v100s in our dedicated training clusters. 
 For our production stack, we deploy everything into a modern Python micro-service architecture in AWS (Flask, Tornado, Docker, etc).
