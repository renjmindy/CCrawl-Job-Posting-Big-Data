 RN and LPN PRN/On call 
 Apply for the position, RN and LPN PRN/On call, with KVC Hospitals today or contact Peter Barndollar for more information. Posted Dec 3, 2020. 
  Apply Online  __    913-890-7475  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 KVC Hospitals in Hays, KS offers psychiatric residential treatment services for children and adolescents in a safe, secure and child-friendly environment. We are seeking Registered Nurses and Licensed Practical Nurses to attend to medical health needs of the children in our care, and work as a collaborative member of the treatment team. 
 Base pay for RN starts at $26 per hour. 
 Base pay for LPN starts at $19 per hour. 
 Licensure/Certification: Licensed as a Registered Nurse or Licensed Practical Nurse in the state of Kansas. 
 Experience: Three to six months related experience 
 KVC provides a competitive compensation and benefits package including medical, dental, vision and life insurance plans; paid time off; and a 403(b) retirement plan. 
 Pre-employment drug screen, physical and background check required.  Must be 21 years old.
