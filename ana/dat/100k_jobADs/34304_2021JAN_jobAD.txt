 Craft/Web Developer FULL TIME - Australia LOCALPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 Our client is looking for a Lead web developer to continue the development and roll-out of new web platforms based on Craft CMS. 
 The role is part of a small team located in their new office in a heritage building in the heart of Fremantle. The role has flexibility, responsibility and autonomy with an attractive salary package. 
 You will be enthusiastic about web development (especially Craft!) and excited about new tools, techniques and technologies but know when this distracts from Getting Things Done. You must be self-motivated, organised and focused in your work but happy collaborating with others. 
 We offer an environment where you will be encouraged to learn and grow with the company. We encourage you to keep learning and will support relevant endeavours to do so.
