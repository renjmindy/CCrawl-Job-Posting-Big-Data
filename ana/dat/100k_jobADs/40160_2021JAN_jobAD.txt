 Fundraising Assistant Internship 
 Heritage Skills Development Centre (HSDC) 
 __Scarborough, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Job Description 
 HSDC is looking for a Fundraising Assistant to help our agency raise the necessary funds needed to enable us meet our program goals and objectives 
 Duties/Responsibilities: 
 
 The individual will be responsible for designing, and implementing long-term fund raising plans and strategies. 
 The person will also be responsible for our community fundraising campaigns, assist in developing fundraising letters; research potential donors; submit and prepare proposals. 
 Also, he/she will participate in soliciting funds from individuals, corporations and foundations and identify other opportunities that will help to increase the organization’s revenue. In addition, the individual will be a fundraising resource person, write introductory donor letters and follow-up donor’s correspondence. 
 The Fundraising Assistant will participate in developing the organization’s fundraising strategy including establishing major and planned donors, managing individuals and corporate donations, event sponsorship, major gifts, etc. 
 
 Skills: 
 
 Excellent writing skills and ability to produce and/or edit documents 
 Excellent organizational skills and the ability to meet deadlines 
 Detail oriented, analytical and problem solving skills 
 Demonstrated ability to work and deal with diverse population 
 Superior telephone manner and strong interpersonal skills 
 High level of sound and independent judgment, reasoning and discretion 
 Great team work skills, to work collaboratively with volunteers and staff 
 Strategic, analytical and evaluation skills and positive work attitude is essential 
 Understanding of various methods of fundraising campaigns and strategies 
 Ability to use basic computer programs such as Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel  a  nd Microsoft PowerPoint 
 Excellent time management, multi-tasking skills and the ability to prioritize tasks with minimal supervision 
 PLEASE NOTE THIS IS AN UNPAID INTERNSHIP THAT THE APPLICANT CAN USE FOR SCHOOL/COLLEGE CREDITS 
 
 Job Types: Full-time, Part-time, Internship, Volunteer
