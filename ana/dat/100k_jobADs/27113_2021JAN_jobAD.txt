 Caregivers Job Abroad 
 Posted on  February 11, 2012  by  caregiver 
 Three Job Orders were recently approved by the allowing three licensed recruitment to accept applications for  Caregivers Job Abroad . The said caregivers job abroad is in the holy land of Israel and its Job Orders were approved by the POEA last February 1, 2, and 3, 2012. Provided below are the names of the recruiting agencies for the said caregivers job abroad and the number of available caregivers job for each agency. 
 CPM Manpower Services, Inc. -------------------------- 30 vacancies 
God's Will International Placement, Inc. ---------------- 30 vacancies 
HRD Employment Consultants & Multi-Services, Inc. --- 21 vacancies 
 To apply for any of the said Caregivers Job Abroad, visit the office of any of the recruitment agency listed above and submit your detailed resume. For details regarding the qualifications, requirements, or placement fees for the said caregivers job abroad, just dial the recruitment agencies landline numbers we have provided below.
