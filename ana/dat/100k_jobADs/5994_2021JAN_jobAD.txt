 Description 
 BOMBARDIER 
 At Bombardier, our employees work together to evolve mobility worldwide - one good idea at a time. If you have a good idea, we’ll provide the environment where it will thrive and grow into a great product or customer experience. Your ideas are our fuel. 
 In your role, you will: 
 
 Lead high severity security incidents from discovery to closure and inform stakeholders about data breaches. 
 Gather, analyze and maintain forensics to support investigative, risk and mitigation efforts. 
 Develop comprehensive incident reports and investigation summaries. Execute lessons learnt with incident responds teams and propose improvements on procedures and security controls. 
 Develop, mature and streamline incident response playbooks. 
 Ensure that incident response requirements are captured and delivered by the security program. 
 
 Qualifications 
 As our ideal candidate, 
 
 You have a bachelor's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Information Science or relevant field of study 
 You have 7 years hands-on experience responding to cyber-attacks and significant experience in the following areas: Network security monitoring, host-based live response & analysis, event log-based detection & analyse, 
 You have advanced knowledge of common attack methods and their detection techniques. 
 You have advanced knowledge of network traffic analysis, related tools 
 You have experience with malware analysis concepts and ability to perform basic static and dynamic analysis 
 You have highly Skilled with log analysis tools, correlation rules, and managing dashboards
