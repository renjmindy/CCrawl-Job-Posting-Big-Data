 About Our Client 
 Our client is is an experienced national freight company at Coppabella moving comprehensive range of commodities, including mining, agricultural, industrial and retail products for a diverse range of customers across Australia. The Company has a strong commitment to quality and safety, which enables to distribute a larger-scale of multi-disciplinary projects all over Australia. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking an experienced Admin Assistant for a DIDO position at Coppabella. You will be working a Monday to Thursday with successful candidates starting as soon as possible. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Answering and directing phone calls to relevant staff 
 
 
 Being a point of contact for a range of staff and external stakeholders 
 
 
 Schedule appointments and maintain calendars 
 
 
 Data Entry 
 
 
 Collate and distribute mail 
 
 
 Prepare communications, such as memos, emails, invoices, reports and other correspondence 
 
 
 Write and edit documents from letters to reports and instructional documents 
 
 
 Create and maintain filing systems, both electronic and physical 
 
 
 Skills and Experience: 
 
 
 Good written and verbal communication skills 
 
 
 Sound organisation and time management skills 
 
 
 Attention to detail 
 
 
 Be able to problem-solve and work independently 
 
 
 Computer Skills are essential 
 
 
 Current Driver's Licence and Right to work within Australia 
 
 
 Benefits of role: 
 
 
 Immediate Start 
 
 
 Competitive Rates 
 
 
 Meals and Accommodation supplied
