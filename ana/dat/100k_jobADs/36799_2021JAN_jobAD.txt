 Project Lead - Mining Technology 
 Job description 
 
 
 Join a global leader in the mining sector 
 
 
 Opportunity to think beyond and extend your knowledge 
 
 
 Work in a successful, well-established and high performing team 
 
 
 About The Role 
 WorkPac are seeking a Project Lead to join the high performing Mining Technology team within the RTIO Sustaining Capital division. The successful applicant will be responsible for the delivery of autonomous mining technology deployments and upgrades across Pilbara sites. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Engage and work collaboratively with others on autonomous mining technology deployments and upgrade projects. 
 
 
 Managing performance, scheduling, logistics and safety of contractors. 
 
 
 Providing onsite supervision of project deliverables when required. 
 
 
 Assist with the drafting and reviewing of project documentation. 
 
 
 Manage cost control, purchasing and budgeting. 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Mining experience with mechanical or technology exposure. 
 
 
 Strong Project Management skills and experience with exposure to autonomous equipment. 
 
 
 University degree in engineering, electrical, mechanical or technology. 
 
 
 Experience in commissioning or maintenance of mobile equipment. 
 
 
 Mining production and team supervision experience. 
 
 
 Clear communication skills, both written and verbal. 
 
 
 A strong understanding of the Microsoft suite of programs. 
 
 
 Sound stakeholder management and networking skills. 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
