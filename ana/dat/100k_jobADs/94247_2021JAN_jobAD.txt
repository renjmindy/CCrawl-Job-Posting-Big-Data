 Are you an experienced Events fundraiser looking for a new challenge? Do you have exceptional communication skills and the ability to negotiate? If so, we have an opportunity for you. 
 A growing and ambitious Charitable Trust is looking to recruit a talented and driven Events Fundraiser into the central Birmingham team. Offering a competitive salary of £22,500 and the chance to make a real difference in the lives of others. 
 Working as part of a dynamic fundraising team, this role will be responsible for developing a voluntary income from individuals and organisations through fundraising events, products and partnerships nationally. 
 What you'll do… 
 
 Support the delivery of the Charitable Trust's fundraising plans and targets 
 Support the fundraising team to maintain a social networking presence for community and corporate events. 
 Write engaging tweets, posts, web copy and take photographs to promote the Trust. 
 Play a proactive role in raising the profile of the Trust within the local community and nationally. 
 Contribute to the development of new fundraising activities. 
 Undertake talks and presentations which promote the work of the Trust and create enthusiasm for participation in fundraising activities. 
 
 What you'll need… 
 
 Previous fundraising and/or events experience. 
 Excellent sales and negotiation abilities and exceptional communication skills. 
 IT and social media skills. 
 The ability to build and maintain professional relationships. 
 Creative thinking with the ability to solve problems. 
 Excellent administrative and project management skills. 
 Open to work occasional weekends and Evenings. 
 
 What you'll get… 
 
 Salary of £22,500. 
 Central City location. 
 The chance to work for a good cause and make a real difference to people's lives.
