 Company Overview 
 Wilson Medical Center 
 Wilson Medical Center is committed to providing excellent care to our community. We serve residents in a six-county service area, including Wilson, Wayne, Nash, Johnston, Greene and Edgecombe counties. Services include interventional cardiology, state-of-the-art cancer care, including clinical trials, women’s and children’s services, and onsite rehabilitative and nursing care. Wilson Medical Center offers the community a full range of services and specialties through our five hospital-based physician practices. Dr. Nadine Skinner and Physician Assistant Becky Buckner offer primary care services for all ages. Dr. Sayeed Hossain of Pulmonary Associates of Wilson, Dr. Ken Johnson of Wilson ENT & Sinus Center, Dr. Louis Antignano of Wilson Gastroenterology and Dr. Ben Thomas of Wilson Neurology all offer the community specialized care in Wilson.
