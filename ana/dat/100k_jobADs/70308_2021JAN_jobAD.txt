 Full-Time Personal Assistant Spanish and English 
 Honkalampi-säätiö, Kuopio -  Kuopio  , Northern Savonia, Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 Wanted two Personal Assistants for part time jobs to work in a small family in central Kuopio. 
 A good work opportunity for a super nice and trustworthy person. Assisting for both child and an adult. Previous work experience not necessary, attitude count the most. Work training given. Though own initiative is necessary. Work suit for naturally helpful person who is willing to do his/her best. Nonsmokers only. 
 Job does not include lifting of persons, personal hygiene or child upbringing. Work requires physical strength, energy and basic skills of life, such as housekeeping and excellent communication skills.  Language requirements: English in practice OR Native Spanish + English in practice.
