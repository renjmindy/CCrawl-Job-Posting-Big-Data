 About this project 
 sales-marketing / televentas 
 USD 100 - 250 
 GB Advisors is seeking for Venezuelans software sellers (Remote) to attend its clients in all America. It’s essential that you speak english fluidly (INGLÉS con fluidez) we offer an excellent salary usd. 
 You must have experience in sales of IT products and knowledge in technology 
- Advanced English (Mandatory) 
- Experiences in sales and post sales. 
- Systems Engineer, computing TSU or related (Desirable). 
- Desirable knowledge of the use of a CRM. 
- Ability to communicate clearly over the phone. 
- Proactive and skilled individual solving problems. 
- Have a computer for work from home. 
- High speed internet available. 
- Headset with microphone and headphones for the computer. 
 Identifying and securing potential opportunities through a relationship-based approach Orchestrating a complex sales processes involving sales, marketing, IT and executive leadership. Maintain and retain long term client relationships; participating in retention and up-sell opportunities.
