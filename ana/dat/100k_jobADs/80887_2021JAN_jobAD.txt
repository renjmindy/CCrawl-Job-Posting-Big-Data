 Job Description 
 Grand Kids Learning Center is seeking applicants to join our team! We are a non-profit, quality rated, early learning center serving families in two locations in Fraser, CO. 
 Our mission is to provide high quality early learning for children, ages 8 weeks through 6 years, that encourages growth and exploration through cognitive, language, physical, and social/emotional development in a safe and nurturing environment. 
 We are seeking qualified applicants to teach in our toddler room. Applicants must be kind, loving, honest, self-directed, responsible, flexible, dependable, a team player and have strong communication skills. Early childhood education and experience is preferred. 
 Teacher responsibilities include but are not limited to: 
 
 
 Building positive lasting relationships with children and parents 
 
 
 Overseeing the well-being and safety of all children in your care 
 
 
 Planning or helping plan the curriculum 
 
 
 Regular parent communication 
 
 
 Facilitating activities with the ability to modify plans, if needed 
 
 
 Our full job description is available by request. 
 Benefits include: paid time off, profit sharing, paid college level courses and training in ECE, retirement plan and health stipend. 
 Please email your resume with a brief description of your experience with children or call 970-509-5002, ext. 1 for more information about the position.
