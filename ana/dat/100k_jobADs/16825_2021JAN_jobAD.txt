 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for February 2011 
 Posted on  March 5, 2011  by  caregiver 
 For the month of February 2011, the POEA approved several Job Orders that offers employment opportunities to those who will qualify for Caregiver Jobs Abroad. The said  POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for February 2011  are for the countries of Kuwait, Bahrain, UAE, Jordan, and Israel. Listed below is the list of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for February 2011. The list includes the country of destination, the specific caregiver job vacancy, the name of the recruiting agency, and the number of vacancies for each POEA approved caregiver work abroad.
