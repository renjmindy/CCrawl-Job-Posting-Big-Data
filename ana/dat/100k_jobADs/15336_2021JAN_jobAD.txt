 Our new project needs a dedicated 3D Environment Artist to help shape our world and refine our artistic style. We are looking to build a team of astute, creative, and knowledgeable people who care about making games for the right reasons. 
 We are currently working remotely and hoping to find an ideal candidate who is ready to join our growing team and become an integral member of uvula.
