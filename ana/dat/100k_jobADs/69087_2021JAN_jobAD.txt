 Bloomberg Index is a rapidly growing business in Bloomberg. We provide proprietary indices for investors to measure, analyze, and invest in global financial markets. Our Indices are the gold standard in Fixed Income Markets. Clients benchmark in excess of five trillion dollars of assets against the Bloomberg Indices. With this level of global impact, we ensure our product can be relied on every single day and meets the highest level of quality. 
 Our global team is responsible for the software platform that enables large scale and high-quality daily production of the Bloomberg Indices. Our system acquires millions of data points from various financial markets, analyzes, processes, aggregates, distributes, and publishes the Index data. 
 We use the wealth of financial data within the Bloomberg terminal to help clients construct new index strategies, examine and back-test index membership, performance, and characteristics, and monitor index levels. 
 We 'll Trust you to: 
 
 Take ownership of the full development cycle, from understanding the business needs to coding, testing, deployment, and maintenance. 
 Embrace working together as a team - mentoring and learning from their existing teammates to break knowledge silos and ensure that our team is producing reliable software, and our system is well documented and well understood to our clients. 
 Solve problems in the Big Data and distributed computing domain. 
 Build fast, reliable, flexible and high-performance software. 
 Be quick, resourceful, resilient, and an excellent collaborator. 
 Learn to balance the best of technical and product knowledge to craft unsurpassed product solutions for our customers 
 Work in a highly collaborative environment where teamwork is the key to success 
 
 You 'll need to have: 
 
 2+ years of programming experience demonstrating the ability to design systems and debug issues 
 BA/BS, MS, or Ph.D. in CS or related field (or) relevant work experience 
 2+ years of experience with object-oriented design, data structures, algorithms, databases and all phases of software development 
 Willingness to work with C++ as your primary development language. 
 Self-motivated with pragmatic problem-solving skills 
 An interest in the financial domain 
 Ability to work in a fast-paced environment 
 
 We 'd love to see 
 
 Experience in designing and maintaining distributed systems or mission-critical software. 
 Experience with Test Driven Development, CI/CD tools like Jenkins 
 Familiarity with distributed technologies like Apache Solr, Apache Cassandra or Apache Spark. 
 Experience or interest in building financial application in Multi-asset Index and Portfolio Analytics Domain
