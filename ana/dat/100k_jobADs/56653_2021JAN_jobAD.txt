 My client, a market leader in their field, is looking to recruit an Adobe Campaign Developer. 
 The purpose of this Adobe Campaign Developer role to support delivering marketing solutions utilising the Adobe stack of Analytics, Campaign and Target. You will work closely with the insight team to define, scope and build customer-centric solutions utilising Adobe Campaign. 
 The role is required to work alongside the marketing agency for support, and other external technical parties. 
 You possess extensive Adobe Campaign experience and solid experience of data analysis & design to build efficient high-performance solutions. 
 Highly competitive package. 
 Currently remote - the role will require 2-3 days per week in the Bristol office in the future.
