 Our Internationally successful fast paced rapidly growing organisation, who are a key leader of their industry, and based near Marlow require an Office and HR Administrator to join their team. This is a unique, broad and hands-on Administration role, where no two days will be the same, and you will be reporting to, and supporting the HR Manager with a variety of duties and responsibilities. 
 This is a superb hands-on role where previous experience of working in a varied office support based role, and/or possibly an HR role will be advantageous. This role would suit someone with a passion to launch their HR career, where you will be learning from a highly experienced HR professional. 
 The key areas that the successful Office and HR Administrator will be able to offer: 
 - Excellent communication skills - both verbal and written 
 - Highly confident and proactive 
 - Able to manage various personnel at all levels 
 - Highly organised and structured 
 - Able to multi-task with ease 
 - Be able to prioritise 
 - Offer a high attention to detail 
 - Be Passionate and driven - results and solution orientated 
 - Able to take on for more responsibility 
 - A strong eye for detail 
 - Be able to offer a keen interest in HR 
 Supporting the HR Manager and the Recruitment Manager, your duties as the Office and HR Administrator will include: 
 - First point of contact for all HR admin queries 
 - Supporting the Recruitment Manager with all employment contracts and administration 
 - Drafting of all employee offer letters 
 - Utilising the HR Systems daily 
 - Gathering and collation of HR data and metrics - monthly, quarterly and annual 
 - Benefits administration support 
 - HR project administration assistance 
 - Outgoing and incoming reference request administration 
 - Covering general telephone enquiries & office reception including signing in visitors 
 - Ensure meeting rooms & office are prepared for external visitors 
 - Purchase Orders - raising and management 
 - Fleet Car management duties 
 - Supporting on company communications including managing company newsletter 
 - Supporting the HR Manager with arranging company corporate events 
 - Other ad hoc office management duties 
 This is a really unique Office and HR Administrator role, based near Marlow, where no two days are the same. Please do get in touch for more details. Our client can offer a great benefits package, very modern offices, and free parking. Please contact me for additional information.
