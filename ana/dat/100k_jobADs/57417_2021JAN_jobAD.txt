 Israel Caregiver Jobs for Filipinos 
 Posted on  June 9, 2010  by  caregiver 
 Israeli employers are currently seeking to employ Filipino caregivers who are qualified for  Israel Caregiver Jobs  **** for Filipinos . The said Israel caregiver jobs for Filipinos have a job order that was approved by the POEA last June 3 for Star World International Manpower & Placement Agency Inc., a POEA accredited recruitment agency. For the said Israel caregiver jobs for Filipinos, a total of 20 applicants will be selected for placement in Caregiver Work Abroad. 
 If you are interested to apply for the said caregiver jobs vacancy, submit your comprehensive resume and application directly to the office of the recruiting agency. For additional info or inquiries, you can contact the agency in their landline numbers provided below. 
 ** 
Star World Int 'l Manpower & Placement Agency, Inc.** 
231 Quirino Avenue, Tambo 
Parañaque City 
Tel. Nos: 8524575 / 8546100 / 8548042
