 Job description 
 Administrator 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Local role based in Gunnedah (must live within 15mins of Gunnedah to apply) 
 
 
 $32/hr + penalties 
 
 
 Monday to Friday: full time hours 
 
 
 Maternity leave role 
 
 
 About the Role 
 The successful candidate will be an integral part of our clients administration team in providing quality administrative support in the day to day running of their operations, with the primary focus to deliver an efficient, effective and professional Administration service. 
 Duties 
 
 
 Data entry 
 
 
 Invoicing 
 
 
 Raising purchase orders 
 
 
 Branch Reception Duties 
 
 
 Service department filing duties 
 
 
 Answer branch phone 
 
 
 Assist with the Coordination of Timesheets 
 
 
 Continuous Improvement / Participation in QA Audits 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Proficient in Microsoft office Suite (Excel, word 
 
 
 Demonstrated ability to multi-task and manage multiple priorities with excellent time management skills 
 
 
 Ability to work well under pressure and in a fast-paced environment 
 
 
 Energetic and highly motivated 
 
 
 Team player with strong interpersonal skills 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australias leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the countrys leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
