 Israel Caregiver Job 
 Posted on  November 29, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This POEA approved caregiver job are for caregivers who have the necessary skills and experience for the said position. It was approved last November 26, 2009 for a recruitment agency based in Makati City and has a corresponding job order balance of 30 caregiver job. The chosen candidates for the said caregiver job will be placed in the Middle East country of Israel. 
 To apply, all qualified and interested parties should proceed to the office of the recruiting agency to submit their application for Israel caregiver job. For a more specific info on the qualifications and requirements for the said caregiver job vacancy, you can contact the recruiting agency in their landline numbers and Email address stated below.

Unit 201, 2F Midland Mansion 
Arnaiz Avenue, Legaspi Village 
Makati 
 Tel. Nos: 8184246 / 8184647 
E-Mail:  sunace_services@yahoo.com
