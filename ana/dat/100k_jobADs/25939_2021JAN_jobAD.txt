 I am working with a growing SME situated in Sutton Coldfield; they are looking for an immediately available, dynamic Accounts Assistant to join their team on a temp to perm basis; this role is reporting directly into the Financial Controller. 
 Please note this is an on site position which has all the correct measures in place in line with Covid-19. 
 Working in a small, committed and hardworking finance team, you will be an experienced Accounts Assistant, with strong Excel skills. Depending on experience you can expect a salary between £22,000 - £25,000 PA. 
 Responsibilities include: 
 
 Purchase Ledger and Invoice receipting 
 Investigating and resolving discrepancies 
 Effective communication with suppliers 
 Bank reconciliations 
 Petty Cash reconciliation 
 Monthly/ quarterly VAT returns 
 CIS submissions 
 Company Credit Card reconciliation 
 Spreadsheet Maintenance 
 
 Is this role right for you? 
 This is a great opportunity for you to join a tight team as an Accounts Assistant where your energy and enthusiasm will genuinely be rewarded. If you enjoy working in a team, love all things finance and want to develop your accountancy skills - this could be the one for you. Click apply to be considered.
