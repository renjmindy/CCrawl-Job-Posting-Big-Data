 Unity Gameplay Programmer 
 DragonBear Studios Apply for this Job 
 Posted 
 December 21, 2020 
 Contract 
 Melbourne, AU 
 Remote 
 We are seeking a full-time  Gameplay Programmer/ Generalist Programmer  (12 Month Contract) to join our small, playful, and diverse team on-site in CBD Melbourne, Australia. Flexible work hours, and partial remote work can be negotiated. 
 Diverse and underrepresented candidates are strongly encouraged to apply. 
 This is a newly created role designed to support the Lead Programmer and the Technical Director.  ‍ 
 ‍ 
 ‍  About You:  The ideal candidate has excellent communication skills, loves coding, problem solving, and is overall a joy to work with.  ‍ 
 ‍ 
 We are seeking candidates who have: 
 
 ‍  ‍  Positive attitude and strong sense of initiative 
 Strong written and verbal communication skills 
 Proficiency with C# / Unity3D 
 Strong problem solving skills 
 At least one shipped commercial title, or equivalent experience. More shipped titles are a bonus. 
 Ability to design solutions to meet studio and project needs 
 Ability to work collaboratively with designers, producers and QA 
 
 Here’s some tasks that we are currently working on that will be distributed among the programming team - if you have an interest in any of them then we’ll work that in: 
 
 UI programming, AI, Networked multiplayer, Gameplay Events, Shaders, Asset and Data management, and more 
 
 ‍ 
 Bonus points  (but not required): 
 
 Unit testing 
 Experience launching on both PC and consoles 
 Game design and/or level design skills, and the ability to articulate these concepts 
 
 ‍ 
 About DragonBear Studios:  ‍ 
 We're a Melbourne based collective of cross disciplinary creatives, and our team is comprised of over 50% women, culturally diverse, queer, and Indigenous storytellers bringing our meaningful tales to you through play. 
 Our studio culture is playful, supportive and kind, and full of diverse and highly creative game makers. Crunch ice-cream, not people. 
 We are currently developing our adorably cute debut game, Innchanted. 
 ‍ 
 About Innchanted 
 Innchanted  is a co-op adventure where you discover magical potions, serve otherworldly guests, and pacify monsters. Gather your party and journey on a quest to overcome the evil Wizard – your landlord!
