 Consultant: 
 
 
 I am currently working with an emerging, technology driven business in the Birmingham area, who are looking for Business Analysts to join their tech team. Not only will you be working in a core function of the business, you will also be supporting global teams, identifying business needs and translating business requirements to both technical and non-technical stakeholders. 
 The successful candidate will help design the future operating model, identify improvements and efficiencies of processes, whilst working with the latest technologies. Having exposure to all areas of the business, you will lead the documentation and analysis of processes and be a part of the projects from cradle to grave. 
 What is required? 
 
 Track record of providing as-is and to-be analysis and supported the implementation of changes to processes 
 Experience of working in process automation, machine learning/artificial intelligence or demand analysis 
 Understanding of Finance concepts, with good analytical and mathematical capability 
 Ability to analyse and identify solutions through the use of automation/predictive analytics 
 Previously supported and designed operating models with a track record of producing documentation and reporting 
 Experience in data analysis, process mapping and the use of SQL and Microsoft stack 
 Worked in a Financial Services/Functional environment in a similar role 
 
 What's in it for you? 
 
 Opportunity to work for a global organisation with exposure to all areas of the organisation 
 Be a part of a strategic direction, focusing on business-critical projects 
 Work with the latest technologies and have the opportunity to input and provide solutions for better practice 
 Competitive salary + benefits 
 
 Please contact Keeley for further information or apply here with your CV. 
 Please note that in the current market we are experiencing a large number of applications. If you have not heard back on your application within 10 days, please consider it unsuccessful. 
 I will endeavour to get back to everyone in due course, but this may take longer than normal. Thank you for your patience and understanding. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
