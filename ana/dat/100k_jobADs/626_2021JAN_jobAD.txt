 RGN Ward Nurse 
 An amazing job opportunity has arisen for a committed Ward Nurse to work in an exceptional hospital based in the South West of London area. 
 As a Ward Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This special service is one of the leading private hospitals with an excellent reputation for delivering high quality healthcare. 
 The hospital also provides private hospital treatments including full diagnostic and endoscopy procedures, orthopaedic surgery including spinal treatment, varicose vein treatment, hernia surgery, vasectomy and cosmetic surgery 
 As a Ward Nurse your key duties include: 
 
 
     Assessment planning
 
 
 
     Implementing and evaluating care
 
 
 
     Wound care
 
 
 
     Drug administration
 
 
 
     Problem solving
 
 
 
     Managing your own workload
 
 
 
 
 An ideal Ward Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Current medical and/or surgical experience within a hospital environment
 
 
 
     Comprehensive understanding of relevant clinical standards
 
 
 
     The ability to work in other Clinical departments as required
 
 
 
     Excellent communication skills - written and oral
 
 
 
     Excellent customer care skills
 
 
 
     Passion to deliver excellent care in a rewarding and what can be emotionally challenging environment
 
 
 
     The ability to work within a team
 
 
 
     The ability to time-manage
 
 
 
     A commitment to support individuals and assist as necessary with activities of daily living including personal care/therapy, social, emotional, and recreational needs and aspirations
 
 
 
 **The successful Ward Nurse will receive an excellent salary up to £32,000 per annum + enhancements. 
 We currently have vacancies for both Full Time or Part Time opportunities available. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Contributory pension scheme
 
 
 
     Access to genuine opportunities to grow, develop and specialise in your career
 
 
 
     25 days' annual leave plus the opportunity to buy/sell more
 
 
 
     Flexible shift options
 
 
 
     Private Healthcare and Life Assurance
 
 
 
     Free offsite parking and a subsidised staff restaurant
 
 
 
     Free uniform
 
 
 
     Access to our employee Discount Programme
 
 
 
     Wellbeing centre and access to 24/7 employee assistance line for free advice
 
 
 
     Free DBS checking
 
 
 
     Long service, Employee recognition and appreciation awards
 
 
 
     Hospital bonus / employee incentive scheme
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a current active NMC Pin
