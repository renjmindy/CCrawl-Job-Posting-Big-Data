 Job description 
 Experienced Boilermaker : 
 
 
 Blackwater location 
 
 
 Ongoing position 
 
 
 Lifestyle roster 5:4, 4:5, 5:5 
 
 
 WorkPac is currently seeking an experience Boilermaker role for an open cut coal mine located 30kms north of Blackwater. 
 Skills and experience: 
 
 
 Proven experience on a coal mine site as a Boilermaker 
 
 
 Experience with Bucket rigging 
 
 
 Current Standard 11 
 
 
 Current Driver's Licence 
 
 
 Current Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Sound work history and references 
 
 
 As part of the recruitment process you will be required to obtain a Drug and Alcohol Screen 
 
 
 Benefits of role: 
 
 
 Camp and meals provided for non locals 
 
 
 ongoing position 
 
 
 Competitive hourly rate 
 
 
 About WorkPac: 
 WorkPac is Australias leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the countrys leaving companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
