 Qualicare Homecare of Kitchener-Waterloo (and surrounding areas) is looking for a compassionate, caring and reliable individual to join our family as a Homecare Nurse (RPN/RN). Qualicare is now hiring for Overnight support in Kitchener and surrounding areas.

 Consistent hours

 Tracheostomy and Ventilation Care training/experience 
 Conduct assessments and implement a plan of care

 Graduate of accredited Registered/Licensed Practical Nurse, Registered Nurse program

 Mobile phone
