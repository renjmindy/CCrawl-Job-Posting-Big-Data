 Retail Finance Business Partner 
 
 Location 
 
 Tamworth, Staffordshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £55000.00 - £65000.00 per annum + car + bonus 
 
 Contact: 
 
 John Taylor 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 john.taylor@gleeson-rg.com

 Published: 
 
 9 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-06-03 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson are currently working with a market leading FMCG / Retail business to help recruit a Retail Finance Business Partner. Our client's brand and reputation has stood strong for decades and as a business they offer fantastic development, career progression and ambition. This is a truly progressive organisation and as a result they are looking to appoint an experienced finance professional (CIMA, ACCA or ACA) to join the commercial finance team. 
 Role and responsibilities: 
 Act as a business partner to operations to drive profitable performance via insightful analysis 
 Provide analysis by product, customer spend and category 
 Revenue and margin analysis at store level offering advice for improvements 
 Provide regular budgets and forecasts to the board 
 Work closely with Marketing to ensure ROI of campaigns is maximised 
 Work closely with operations to complete the budget planning process providing influence and challenge 
 You must be a qualified accountant (CIMA, ACCA or ACA) and have worked in a retail environment. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
