 15 Work at home Jobs that Pay over $15 Per Hour 
 Published by  aw1219  on November 7, 2019 
 We may earn money or products from some of the companies mentioned in this post. 
 Working from home is much easier than a few years ago because there is an abundance of real companies hiring remote workers. But what is hard is trying to find a job that pays above minimum wage. Although this pay rate may vary from state to state, I have come up with a list of companies that pays at least  $15 per hour  working full or part-time. 
 Right now is a great time to look for and apply to work from home jobs because there are a lot of companies who have an urgent need for people to help them with their workload due to peak seasons, for the upcoming holidays, etc. Please feel free to go to the  weekly job section  and  Friday freebie pages  for additional and current work at home companies hiring. You can also join our Facebook groups for more work at home job leads, news, free resources, insight and tips below.

 Homebasedmommie 
 Pleio 
 This company recruits and contracts with individuals to work from home as Good Start mentors to help people who have been prescribed medication to get into a good routine of taking them through your encouragement. Although it is not an hourly paying job, you will end up making between $17 to $21 per hour and some Good Starters make even more! They also give great incentives in the form of bonuses and free gift cards. 
 American Express 
 This company hires remote workers to assist customers with travel reservations. You will research, plan, and execute customized travel experiences via domestic and international air, car, and/or hotel travel arrangements. This company will reimburse you for your phone and internet fees. The hourly pay is close to $19 per hour! 
 Startups.com 
 Work from home as a virtual assistant helping clients with personal and business calendar management, research, managing and marketing social media, emails, expense reporting, and other related duties. This company pays between $13 to $18 per hour depending on the client mix. 
 Education First 
 This company is currently looking for people who reside in the US and UK to work from home whenever you want teaching young students how to learn English remotely. You can start within 2 weeks earning up to $20 per hour plus more teaching during peak times. 
 Kelly Connect 
 Work from home for Apple through Kelly Connect providing support to their users who experience trouble or need assistance with username and password problems, installing and uninstalling apps, email issues, etc. After 90 days you will receive a $1 extra an hour raise plus a $500 bonus if you meet their hour requirement. Agents can earn up to $17 per hour with this company. 
 
 Liftopia 
 This company will pay you $15 per hour to help individuals from all over the world to get on the slopes by assisting them with purchases, responding to issues with existing orders. This is a 40 hour a week work from home job. 
 Aspire Lifestyles 
 This company needs consultants to work from home fulfilling Concierge requests from premium card members and also deliver excellent customer service. This is a full-time work at home job with no selling required. Pays $15.50 per hour up to $45,000 a year! 
 Progressive Insurance 
 No doubt you have seen this company advertise their car insurance with a spokeswoman that goes by the name of Flo. You can work from home for this company by supporting existing customers to manage their policies. Not only will you earn up to 15% bonuses for working weekends and performance bonus rewards but you will get medical, dental, and vision benefits that pay from $16.50 to $18.90 per hour. 
 VIPKID 
 VIPKID needs thousands of work-from-home teacher’s to teach English online to kids in China. This is an amazing work-from-home opportunity – and don’t worry, users do not need to know any foreign language or have formal teaching experience. Teachers can earn as much as $22 per hour working on the VIPKID platform. 
 Scribe 
 Work from home as a Ghost or co-writer working your own schedule. You will earn $60 to $70 per hour as a freelance writer! 
 Aira 
 This company needs people to help those who are visually impaired to accomplish tasks that may include reading books to their children, cooking, shop online and more. This is a very rewarding work at home job that pays $15 per hour. 
 Course Hero 
 
 Course Hero is seeking people to get paid for helping high school and college students with course-specific questions. Pays $3 per question answered or $12 to $20 per hour. Some tutors make $500 a week! 
 Writer/Copywriter/Editor 
 Coalition Technologies is seeking people with excellent English writing skills to write and edit original content for their client’s websites. You will need previous experience. Pays $20 to $30 for 500-word page articles twice a month. 
 ShoutVox 
 Work from home creatin engaging content on a variety of topics from technology to pop culture. Requires at least 1-year experience creating content on the web. Pays $18 to $30 per hour. 
 Peckham 
 Work from home as a technical customer service rep helping customers with general maintenance tasks, resolve complex IT problems, hardware and software issues. This company will provide you with a computer, monitor, and headset plus pay you an hourly wage of $16.97 per hour with health and benefits coverage. 
 Door Dash 
 Although this is not a work at home job, working for this company delivering food and other items to their customers allows you to work your own schedule without a car and makeup to $15 per hour and up. 
 
 UberEats 
 Deliver food when you want and make good money bringing people the things they love. With tips, you can earn up to $15 per hour depending upon how many deliveries you make. Deliver with your car, bike, or scooter. 
 Postmates 
 Deliver for Postmates and take 100% home of what you earn. Earn easy money on your own time. You will need a bike, car, motorcycle, or scooter.
