 Remote Title Processor/Settlement Agent Direct Hire 
   
   Workway, Inc.   
 __Website    __@workway 
 We are looking for a  Remote Settlement Agent/Escrow Processor  for a national residential title company. 
 Your specific duties will include: 
 
 Clear title and prepare Closing Disclosures, closing statements and balance with lenders 
 Schedule closings with the Notary, lender, borrower, broker, etc. 
 Order title and tax updates, save to file and ensure they are back prior to closing, etc. 
 Review files to ensure they are clear to close, review clearance notes, etc. 
 Prepare Affidavits, Shipping labels, KV docs to accompany lender packages. 
 Keep track of settlement calendar and follow up for missing closing packages with clients. 
 Respond to client inquiries in a timely fashion 
 
 Specific qualifications for the position include  : 
 
 5+ years of Title processing experience, required 
 Experience working residential real estate title files in New Jersey - Required 
 Experience with SoftPro Select - Preferred 
 Bachelor's degree - Preferred 
 Ability to work with minimal supervision, in a high production environment 
 
 Let one of our recruiters assist you in finding and preparing for interviews with our clients. Whether you are interested in a direct hire, temp-to-hire, or temporary position, Workway can help you to put your best foot forward. Contact us today! 
 To receive state and federal compliance posters, e-mail hr@workway.com or call 972.514.1515.
