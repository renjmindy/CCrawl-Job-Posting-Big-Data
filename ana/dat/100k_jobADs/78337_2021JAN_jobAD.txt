 Role overview 
 What will I be doing? 
 
 As a full-stack engineer (m/f/d) you will work on advancing our entire platform (API, Command Line Tools, IDE plugins and much more) on a strong focus on Ruby but also with other technologies such as Go, Elasticsearch etc. 
 You use best practices in your code as well as mentoring others to do the same 
 You think creatively and work outside the box ‚Äì we love sharing ideas 
 You work pragmatically and keep a high level of productivity 
 You share your knowledge in regular Brown Bag sessions 
 You follow and improve our agile processes with weekly sprints and daily deployment 
 You take part in pair programming and code-reviews to optimize our code quality 
 
 What skills do I need? 
 
 Experienced in building web applications in Ruby on Rails 
 Skilled at writing clear, concise and performant code with unit and integration tests 
 Familiar with git and GitHub 
 Ability to collaborate with others (also cross-functionally) to solve problems creatively and effectively 
 Excellent verbal and written communication skills in English (bonus points for German) 
 Motivation to help shape our team and product and take responsibility 
 
 What does Phrase offer? 
 
 We follow the holacracy approach, allowing for a strong involvement of each individual, efficient communication and high personal responsibility 
 We organize regular team-wide hackathons and invite the developer community for tech events (usergroups, meetups etc.) 
 We let you choose your hardware 
 We prefer output over attending long meetings 
 We work together as a team with a long-term perspective 
 We want to get things shipped and have ambitious goals but after a productive day we all enjoy having time for life‚Äôs other enjoyments 
 We provide a budget for attending conferences and cooperate with Urban Sports Club to offer special rates for team members
