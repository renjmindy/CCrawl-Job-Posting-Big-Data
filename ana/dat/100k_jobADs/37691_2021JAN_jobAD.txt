 Birmingham, West Midlands

 Permanent

 £35000 - £45000 per annum + bonus, 25 days holiday

 Gleeson Recruitment Group is currently working exclusively alongside a business in Birmingham City Centre as they look to recruit an experienced Accounts Payable Manager professional to look after a new team. 
 This role is paying circa £40k although it may be possible to go slightly higher depending on experience plus bonus, 25 days holiday and a superb central location. 
 The Role 
 You will be managing a new team of experienced AP professionals and for the first six months, will be tasked with reviewing current processes and implementing improvements where required. 
 Principle Accountabilities are as follows: 
 
 Manage the daily activities, training and development of the team. 
 Review and improve systems and processes where required. 
 Ensure the team are achieving and exceeding KPI's. 
 Month End Reporting. 
 Attending management meetings and presenting to Senior Stakeholders. 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 Competent in all areas of the Accounts Payable process 
 Experience of managing an Accounts Payable team (SSC experience would be beneficial). 
 Effective stakeholder management skills. 
 Strong IT skills. 
 Lots of energy with the ability to motivate others 
 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
