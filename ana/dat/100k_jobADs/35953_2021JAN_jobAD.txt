 Nanny Jobs in New Zealand 
 Posted on  March 17, 2009  by  caregiver 
 A nanny agency that’s been in operation since 2002 and with offices based in Auckland, London, and Surrey is seeking qualified, active, and enthusiastic nannies for their various nanny jobs vacancies. They offer permanent and temporary, daily or live in, job opportunities for nannies, mother’s help, and baby sitters. 
 They have a number of existing nanny jobs opportunities for those who want to work overseas but applicants need to commit to work for at least one year and need to be eligible for the appropriate visa. 
 Interested applicants may visit  www.kiwioznannies.co.nz/  for more info or to view all nanny jobs opportunities. Or, if you want to register as a nanny jobs applicant please E-mail your CV with a cover note or outline of your situation to  contact@kiwioznannies.co.nz
