 Registered General Nurse 
 An exciting job opportunity has arisen for a committed RGN to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the Birkdale, Southport area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading healthcare providers 
 This nursing home that has been converted from a large three storey house. 
 The home can accommodate people with a variety of nursing needs 
 As a Nurse your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     Ensure the promotion of high standards of nursing care
 
 
 
     By ensuring all staff are supervised appropriately and the shift runs in a smooth and efficient manner
 
 
 
     You will perform all general nursing duties, following CQC and NMC guidelines, and report to the home manager
 
 
 
     Update care plans and administer safe medication
 
 
 
     You will provide a high standard of care and work within a team as well as on your own initiative
 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     You must have excellent communication and written skills
 
 
 
     Previous experience working within a nursing home or elderly care setting
 
 
 
     Self-motivated, resilient and well-organised
 
 
 
     You will be a team player but able to work on your own initiative
 
 
 
     You will be dependable
 
 
 
     Able to work under pressure and flexible to meet the needs of the residents and the home
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent salary of £16.75 per hour. 
 This exciting position is Part Time role, working 12 hours a day. 
 You will work 1 shift in one week and then 2 shifts in week 2. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 Comprehensive induction and on-going training 
 Career development opportunities 
 Excellent work environment 
 Free parking on site 
 Free uniform 
 5.6 weeks of paid annual leave 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a current active NMC Pin
