 OPENTEXT - THE INFORMATION COMPANY 
 As the Information Company, our mission at OpenText is to create software solutions and deliver services that redefine the future of digital. Be part of a winning team that leads the way in Enterprise Information Management. 
 The opportunity: 
The Technical Writer will play a critical part in creating content for the Customer Operations department, which provides technical support and a variety of services for our global customers. Our ideal candidate is an experienced professional with demonstrated creative writing/presentation creation skills. Someone who can take complex technical ideas and repackage them into simple yet elegant business executive ready concepts. 
 You are great at: 
 
 Write and revise copy for blog posts, email campaigns, social media, ads (digital and print), presentations, videos, and brochures. 
 Convert complex technical information into simple and informative content for customers, partners, and the business. 
 Collaborate with the business and creative lead to take concepts and turn them into graphic content for PowerPoint slides. 
 Become well versed in the technical language of our industry and distill it for a business audience. 
 Work cooperatively in a team environment and exhibit strong intra-team and inter-team communication 
 
 What it takes: 
 
 Minimum 2 years of professional experience in a technical marketing or publishing environment 
 Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (PowerPoint, Excel, Word) 
 Proficient with Adobe Creative Cloud 
 Deadline driven, organized with a keen understanding of how people view and understand content.
