 Software Plugin Developer (Freeform) REMOTEPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 The software side of Solspace is expanding! 
 We're looking for a full-stack developer who would be responsible for ongoing Craft plugin development, maintenance and providing some assistance with support requests related to the Solspace  Freeform  and  Calendar  plugins. Below are the most important skill requirements: 
 
 PHP  - Yii2 framework, event-driven architecture 
 MySQL  - intermediate knowledge 
 Twig 
 Craft CMS  - experience with plugin development is a bonus 
 Freeform  plugin - experience with Freeform and/or Calendar is a bonus 
 ES6+  - the latest in javascript language 
 React.js  - intermediate knowledge preferred, no large scale application architecture experience necessary, just the overall understanding of how React works.  If no experience, a willingness to learn React. 
 Node.js  - basic experience with task runners Gulp, Grunt or any of the others, for deployment scripts 
 SCSS  - or any other CSS pre-processors 
 Webpack  - for bundling react apps 
 REST API 
 Basic knowledge of how integrations with other products work and look 
 
 In addition to that, we're looking for someone that is enthusiastic about the Craft community, takes pride in their work, and helping others! Solspace is a fun and rewarding place to work, and we enjoy listening to the feedback of our customers and continually pushing the boundaries of what our software can do! 
 This is a full time permanent remote position, however, we are open to applicants that wish to work anywhere between half and full time. 
 How to Apply 
 To apply, please send your resume to  [email protected]  along with links to any software/plugins you have developed or samples of your work (if available).
