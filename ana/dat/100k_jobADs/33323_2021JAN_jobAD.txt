 Graphic Designer 
 We are a technology start-up in stealth-mode that is led by an experienced founding team with previous IPO, exit and leadership experiences at companies like Amazon, Delivery Hero and Rocket Internet. We are backed by the world's leading venture capitalists and are seeking smart, hands-on and energetic talent to add to our team. 
 We are passionate about disrupting the future of global convenience retail, through on-demand delivery, and are laser-focused on creating a product that is geared towards delivering a lovable experience to our customers. 
 As our Graphic Designer you'll have the unique opportunity to work alongside an experienced C-Suite and work in a fast-paced, early-stage start-up environment. You will be instrumental in all things design and visual, with a focus on our branding, merchandising, online ads, and marketing material. 
 Your responsibilities will include: 
 
 Building reusable visual design methods and toolkits 
 Create a brand guideline based on our visual identity using best practices. 
 Design content for prospect- and customer-facing materials including promotional campaigns, product marketing, user adoption campaigns, sales enablement, etc. including for web, email, digital advertising, video, documents, infographics and more. 
 Working closely with our Growth, Marketing, Commercial and Real Estate teams to ensure their requirements are met consistently. 
 Understand the brand and constantly improve our brand design elements 
 
 Background: 
 
 A strong ability to listen to internal stakeholders' key requirements, translate this into visual conception (develop designs), take on board feedback and adapt and refine as appropriate. 
 Experience designing all kinds of content including for social media, digital advertising, emails, ebooks, whitepapers, fact sheets, sales decks, etc. 
 Knowledge of core design and visual communication principles (typography, layout, colour theory, white space, usability, etc.) 
 Experience using design software and processes (Adobe suite, HTML and CSS, Flash, print production, etc.) 
 Creative and innovative 
 Excellent attention to detail working with a high level of accuracy
