 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson RG are exclusively working with an established SME Business based in Carlisle for a Financial Controller. 
 Key remit will to co-ordinate all management reporting and partner with the SMT in providing financial direction. 
 Role 
 
 Preparation of budgets, financial reports and management accounts 
 Ensure monthly accounts are prepared within agreed time-frames 
 Develop and maintain financial control systems and procedures to establish 
 Manage forecasts and evaluating the projected financial performance 
 Prepare profit and cash flow forecasts 
 Work with commercial team on pricing 
 Provide clear recommendations on the balance of profitability and customer service 
 Manage Finance team 
 
 Requirements 
 
 Fully Qualified (ACCA, ACA or CIMA) 
 Strong management skills and ability to mentor 
 Ability to challenge and influence senior management and team 
 
 On Offer 
 Up to £75,000 + Package 
 Salary on offer will be dependent on experience and fit for the role.
