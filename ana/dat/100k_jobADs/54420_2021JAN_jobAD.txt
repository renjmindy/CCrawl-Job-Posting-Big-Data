 Registered General Nurse 
 An amazing job opportunity has arisen for a committed RGN to work in an exceptional care home based in the Barrhead, Glasgow. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This special care home is made up of two houses. 
 The team professionals offer care and support for the elderly in dementia care. 
 It's vitally important for the residents to feel at home, they have created a warm, friendly environment with an individually appropriate balance of care and independence. 
 The home encourages residents to lead as full and active a life as possible 
 As a Nurse your day to day duties include: 
 
 Leading, supervising and training the care team 
 General Nursing duties-Wound care/general observation /blood sugar level observation /liaising with other healthcare professionals to give the best care. 
 Being responsible for ordering, storing and administering medication 
 Keeping precise, up to date records 
 Maintaining the Client's dignity and respect at all times 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Able to work under pressure and prioritise workload
 
 
 
     The ability to lead a team, analyse problems and propose solutions or improvements
 
 
 
     Able to communicate effectively and to develop professional relationships
 
 
 
     Understands and effectively uses IT and communication systems required for the role
 
 
 
     Experience of participating in quality and clinical governance programmes
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent salary of £16.65 per hour. 
 We currently have vacancies available for Night Shifts only.
