 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently supporting a well know FMCG organisation based in Birmingham who are recruiting for a qualified Financial Accountant. The role has become available due to an internal move and offers the successful candidate an excellent opportunity for progression. This role would ideally suit a qualified accountant with experience working in a similar Financial Accountant role. 
 Financial Accountant 
 Key Responsibilities include: 
 
 Stock reporting and reconciliation, liaising with the stock team. 
 Responsibility for coordinating balance sheet reconciliations from the team and feeding back any queries. 
 Posting of monthly balance sheet entries. 
 Coordinating the ATSE Intercompany procedures, posting recharges and liaising with counterparts across the group. 
 Monthly balance sheet reconciliations. 
 Assisting with treasury and reporting treasury movements to group monthly. 
 Monthly balance sheet reporting to group. 
 Balance sheet forecasting. 
 Assisting in the annual audit and statutory accounts preparation. 
 Assist the Financial Accountant Manager when necessary. 
 Adhoc accounting requirements as necessary 
 
 For more information on this excellent Financial Accountant position based in Birmingham please do get in touch
