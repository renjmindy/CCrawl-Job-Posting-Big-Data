 About bugzy 
 bugzy is a new online tool, designed to help both brands and developers alike in identifying, highlighting, recording and tracking bugs, tweaks and fixes, across mobile, tablet and desktop devices for web-based projects and platforms etc. 
 Although similar solutions currently exist, it's how the data will be managed and distributed which will makes bugzy a cool and universal tool.

 Technical Experience/Requirements 
 
 Basic development capabilities e.g. 1-2 yrs experience 
 Proficient in React, CSS and JavaScript, although TypeScript would be advantageous 
 Experience in tech related start-ups, working through the varies dev stages e.g. concept, MVP, Beta etc. 
 
 About you 
 
 UK based (preferably London or home counties) 
 Creative thinker and not afraid to put your ideas forward 
 Passion for working with start-ups and delivering solutions that help others 
 Highly motivated and driven to succeed 
 Organised and able to work under pressure 
 Excellent communication skills 
 
 Our team 
 We have a vast amount of experience across design, marketing/advertising, tech, finance and start-ups, with a number of successful exits between us. 
 Remuneration 
 During the initial concept and MVP stage, the successful applicant will be remunerated in equity i.e. shares in the company. Once the MVP stage is live and we've onboarded test users, a permanent and competitive paid contract will be offered, including benefits such as pension, healthcare, travel loan etc. 
 How to apply 
 Please send us a short covering letter, along with a copy of your CV, as well as URLs for past/present projects to demonstrate your work.
