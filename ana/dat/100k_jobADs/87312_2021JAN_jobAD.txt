 Pacific Science & Engineering (PSE) is seeking a highly qualified professional to join our full-time technical staff as a  human factors engineer specializing in system engineering . 
 Successful candidate will serve as a human factors engineer on teams of professionals conducting system research, analysis, and design to address challenging problems of people using complex, computer-based and automated systems. 
 Candidates should be capable of working flexibly in small multi-disciplinary teams on several concurrent projects, with minimal supervision. Excellent verbal, written, and technical communication skills are necessary. Some travel is necessary. U.S. Citizenship is necessary for this position in order to comply with government contracts requiring successful completion of a security clearance for access to classified information. 
 
 Desirable education, knowledge, skills, and demonstrated experience include: 
 
 Advanced degree or equivalent work experience in human factors engineering, systems engineering, software engineering, or similar 
 System engineering processes 
 Operational concept development, requirements analysis, and architectural design of software-based, human-integrated systems 
 Writing systems and/or software engineering documents 
 Creating model-based systems engineering (MBSE) products 
 Working with users in operational environments 
 Interface design and prototyping 
 Systems Modeling Language (SysML), Unified Modeling Language (UML), and/or Service-Oriented Architecture Modeling Language (SOAML)State-of-the-art web development technologies (e.g., NodeJS, Angular 2+) 
 Department of the Defense Architecture Framework (DoDAF) 
 ISO 9241-11:2018 and ISO 9241-210:2019 
 Human factors standards and methods MS Office and analysis software 
 Test and evaluation 
 Usability testing and analysis 
 Three or more years of applied industry experience highly valued 
 
 Responsibilities of the position are: 
 
 Collaborate on multidisciplinary teams performing human factors research, design, and development 
 Develop and analyze operational concepts, evaluate technical feasibility of completed graphical user interface (GUI) designs, and design front-end architectures to accompany completed GUI designs 
 Develop creative and evidence-based solutions to complex, applied problems 
 Assume responsibilities and contribute at all levels in project teams

 About Pacific Science & Engineering 
 PSE is a San Diego based small business, specializing in human factors research, development, and design services, for clients worldwide. We take a scientific approach to improving human performance in complex work domains, including military, national security, medical, and commercial information systems. PSE provides a flexible work environment, competitive benefits, and compensation based on accomplishment and experience.
