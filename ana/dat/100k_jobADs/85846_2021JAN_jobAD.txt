 Python DevOps Engineer for Job in Sweden 
 Written by  Elena Zakharian Posted on August 26, 2018August 26, 2018 Leave a comment 
 Do you have a big passion for test automation, CI/CD frameworks, deployment pipelines and/or configuration management? Are you looking for adventure? On behalf of our client in Gothenburg, Lingonberry Talent Acquisition is looking for talented Python developers with experience in test automation who want to relocate to Sweden and be part of a world-class international team of developers. Our client is a software consultancy in the automotive sector and develops mobile applications and Infotainment apps based on Android. 
 Located on the Swedish west coast, Gothenburg is the second largest city in Sweden. The population in the metropolitan area is around one million. Besides being a center for the tech industry, Gothenburg offers a rich cultural life and a booming entertainment sector, with plenty of bars, restaurants, microbreweries and craft shops, sports events, concerts and much more. Our clients pay competitive salaries on the Swedish market and will help you acquire a working visa and accommodation in Gothenburg. Check out this video showing a few of the highlights from life in Gothenburg:  https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QkCgd9YfZ34 
 Your mission objectives, should you choose to accept: 
 
 You will develop, improve and maintain the build and test automation framework, and enable our client to get better and better at continuous integration and continuous delivery 
 You will work in a team together with other skilled developers and achieve success together 
 
 Required Skills  (we think you have some or most of these from each bullet): 
 
 Python 
 Linux environment 
 Shell script 
 Gradle 
 Git 
 Bitbucket or Stash 
 Artifactory 
 Jira 
 Eclipse 
 
 Delicious Extra Skills: 
 
 Jenkins 
 Gerrit 
 Chef or Puppet 
 Android Sim Toolkit Application (STK) 
 Java and/or C++ 
 Cyber security knowledge 
 
 We offer you: 
 
 Opportunity to relocate to Sweden and become a part of a world-class international team 
 Competitive salary of 3000 - 4000 euro per month 
 Help with acquiring work visa and accommodation in Sweden 
 Traveling to international conferences, parties, participating in hackathons and opportunities to grow 
 Private healthcare insurance 
 Fitness account 
 Five weeks paid vacation 
 Extra pay for any overtime 
 
 It is very important that you feel motivated to relocate to Sweden. Does it sound like your cup of tea? Contact  jobs@lingonberrytalent.se
