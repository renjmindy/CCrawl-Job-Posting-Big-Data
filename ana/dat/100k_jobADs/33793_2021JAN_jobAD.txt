 Are you looking for a role that offers an enjoyable challenge: 
 Assistant Accountant role on an interim contract for a small business based in Solihull.. They have lots of parking on site. If this role sounds of interest, please apply! 
 Client Details 
 My client is a small business based in Solihull. They are looking for an Assistant Accountant to join there small Finance team on an interim basis. You will report into the head of Finance and be responsible for the transactional accounts and journals and accruals and process improvement. 
 Description 
 Assistant Accountant responsibilities: 
 
 
 Processing of Accounts Receivable invoices. 
 
 
 Resolve billing queries. 
 
 
 Management of receivables. 
 
 
 Collection, coding and getting approvals for all Accounts Payable invoices. 
 
 
 Contact and follow up with vendors when needed. 
 
 
 Preparation of correcting Journal Entries, accruals and account analysis. 
 
 
 Being responsible for all cash controls, including cash and credit card receipts for on-site sales. 
 
 
 Profile 
 The successful Assistant Accountant must: 
 
 Must have excellent interpersonal and communication skills 
 Ability to manage diversity in job responsibilities with precision and accuracy in work 
 Must be hands on and be able to multi-task 
 Good working knowledge of Excel 
 Have experience in the above job duties
