 Theatre Nurse/ODP - Scrub 
 A fantastic new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Theatre Nurse/ODP - Scrub to work in an exceptional clinic based in the Bingley, West Yorkshire area. 
 You will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This clinic provides treatments such as knee cartilage surgery, knee arthroscopy, knee ligament surgery, cataract surgery and shoulder muscle repair 
 An ideal Theatre Nurse/ODP - Scrub must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Sound written and verbal communication skills
 
 
 
     Proven ability to work effectively in a team environment and independently as required
 
 
 
     Demonstrated customer service skills
 
 
 
     Demonstrated computer skills
 
 
 
     Ability to plan equipment and consumable requirements
 
 
 
 **The successful Theatre Nurse/ODP - Scrub will receive an excellent salary from £33,000 to £35,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Competitive Salary plus enhancements where eligible
 
 
 
     Contributory pension scheme
 
 
 
     Access to genuine opportunities to grow, develop and specialise in your career
 
 
 
     25 days' annual leave plus the opportunity to buy/sell more
 
 
 
     Flexible shift options
 
 
 
     Private Healthcare and Life Assurance
 
 
 
     Free onsite parking
 
 
 
     Access to our employee Discount Programme
 
 
 
     Wellbeing centre and access to 24/7 employee assistance line for free advice
 
 
 
     Free Uniform
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN or an ODP with a valid NMC or HCPC Pin
