 Seminar and Workshop Lecturer 
 Job Description 
 Wordvice, an international English editing company, is seeking academic professionals with experience in areas of research writing and publishing to lead lectures at seminars and workshops. We are currently seeking candidates living in countries in which Wordvice operates: South Korea, Japan, China, Taiwan, and Turkey. Lectures will travel to various institutions around these countries for various events. 
 Lectures will be given on a variety of academic topics related to research writing, manuscript preparation, the journal submission and publishing process, and more. 
 Example of seminar/webinar topics might include: “How to Follow-up on a Submission to a Journal,” “How to Avoid Plagiarism in Your Research Paper,” “Common Mistakes Made by Beginning Research Writers,“ “Writing the Methods Section,” etc. 
 ** Sample instructional videos from our YouTube channel for reference : ** 
 
 ”Academic Webinar: How to Write an Effective Abstract” 
 “Improving Research Writing for Publication” 
 “How to Write a Journal Submissions Cover Letter” 
 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Travel to universities and institutions around the country and give lectures on academic topics 
 Prepare necessary lecture content and materials (presentation outlines, scripts, PPTs, etc.) 
 Answer question from the audience about issues related to the lecture topic 
 Provide feedback and instruction (in workshops) 
 
 Qualifications 
 ** Basic Qualifications ** 
 
 
 Experience writing research papers in the social or natural sciences 
 
 
 Experience with the journal submission and/or publication process 
 
 
 Previous public speaking/lecturing experience 
 
 
 Proficiency in English 
 
 
 Education: Master’s degree or higher
** Preferred Qualifications ** 
 
 
 Extensive lecture and workshop experience 
 
 
 Experience in research or journal publication 
 
 
 Proficiency in local language of the country 
 
 
 Ideal candidates will have one or more of the following positions/experiences : 
 
 university professor/assistant professor 
 researcher 
 graduate and doctoral student 
 journal editor 
 other academic experts with related experience lecturing/writing about research writing or the journal submissions process 
 
 
 
 Conditions 
 
 Location :  Universities and institutions around the given country 
 Frequency :  Contingent on scheduled events (~1-4 lectures per month) 
 Duration :  Ongoing 
 Payment :  Please indicate expected pay per lecture in application

 A list of which kinds of topics (academic writing/journal submissions/journal publishing) you would be interested in presenting 
 Your expected pay per lecture
