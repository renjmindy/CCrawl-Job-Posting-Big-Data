 Are you an experienced Accounts Receivable team leader looking for a new position where you have strong team leader skills in a position where you can ensure workload is planned effectively and deadlines are met. 
 You will be working with the team to meet service level agreements, ensure unallocated cash targets are met and customer satisfaction is at its highest. 
 Duties include: 
 
 Leading and motivating a small team 
 Conducting Team 121's and ensuring KPIs are achieved 
 Resolving customer queries 
 Allocating workload amongst the team 
 Ensuring accurate transaction processing 
 Processing cash received into company bank accounts 
 Posting and allocating cash 
 Balance sheet reconciliation 
 Variance analysis 
 Raising Credit Notes 
 Monitoring all compliance 
 Reporting on activities in accounts receivable 
 Driving down unallocated cash targets 
 Liaising with internal and external stakeholders 
 
 The ideal person will be a strong communicator, with a confident and determined mindset to drive through change and improvement. 
 If you're interested in working for a motivated, challenging and forward thinking business this may be the role for you. 
 To apply for the Accounts Receivable Team Leader position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
