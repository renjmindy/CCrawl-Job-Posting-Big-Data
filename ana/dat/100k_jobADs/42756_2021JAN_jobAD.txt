 Registered General Nurse 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a committed RGN or RMN to work in an exceptional care home based in the Swanage, Dorset area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This care home offers nursing and residential care for those over the age of 60. 
 The team also cares for dementia sufferers and those who need respite care, palliative care. 
 With fully trained nurses available 24 hours a day, help is never far away at the home 
 As a Nurse your key duties include: 
 
 
     Ensure compliance with all legal, regulatory and best practice guidelines - identify, investigate and resolve risks proactively
 
 
 
     Participate knowledgably and professionally to all inspection visits, ensuring your team is ready at all times to do the same
 
 
 
     Champion appropriate independence and personal choice; developing, reviewing and updating care plans to meet our residents physical, social and psychological needs
 
 
 
     Ensure you follow a rigorous monitoring and recording practice; documenting efficiently, and thoroughly all matters relating to residents and employees
 
 
 
     Understand the key targets of your host home and support the Home Manager in their achievement by promoting and practicing high standards of nursing care and ensuring staff and shifts are supervised in a smooth manner
 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Be passionate about delivering person centred care
 
 
 
     Up to date clinical practices
 
 
 
     Evidence of continual professional development
 
 
 
     Provide excellent clinical support and supervision to your Team to ensure the safe
 
 
 
     Smooth and efficient running of the service
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent salary from £17.50 to £18.50 per hour. 
 We currently have vacancies for both Full Time or Part Time positions available for Day Shifts only. 
 In return for your hard work and expertise you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Career Progression
 
 
 
     Free Uniform
 
 
 
     Car Park
 
 
 
     Pension
 
 
 
     DBS
 
 
 
     Holiday Entitlement
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse either RGN or RMN with a live NMC Pin
