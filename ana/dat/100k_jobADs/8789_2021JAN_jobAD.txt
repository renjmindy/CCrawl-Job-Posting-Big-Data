 **Our Company 
** 
We are The Smart Container Company. An early-stage B2B SaaS startup disrupting the draught beer industry. Our product, KegTracker™, is an IoT device that instantly turns kegs and casks into SMART containers. Our technology creates transparency in the supply chain, removes bottlenecks, reduces waste, and enables a more sustainable and circular economy. 
 Originally founded in New York City, The Smart Container Company is now headquartered in the United Kingdom. We are a diverse and talented team that supports a collaborative culture and delivers value through the application of technology, strategy, and process improvement. 
 **Our Product and Service 
** 
KegTracker™ is a disruptive, innovative, first-of-its-kind, patent pending, non-intrusive IoT device for asset tracking with condition monitoring of kegs. Our technology instantly connects existing containers to the internet and tracks location, volume, temperature, and movement for each asset. 
 Leveraging data science and information collected from the containers, we empower breweries, distributors and retailers with real-time reports and insights that help them 1) Make More 2) Save More and 3) Save The Planet 
 Job Description 
 This is an opportunity to work with a young startup and bring to market IoT technologies that have revolutionized the legacy draft beer industry. You will be expected to operate at an exceptional level of performance, deliver high quality deliverables, and engage with product development, business development, and marketing teams. 
 Duties 
 
 Supporting the development of our sustainability and market intelligence services 
 Interpreting research studies and creating analysis for strategic decision making 
 Working directly with the marketing function to create sustainability content 
 Influencing and gaining commitment at all levels of the organization 
 Representing the voice of the beer and IoT industries 
 
 Experience Desired 
 
 Interest in beer, food & beverage, logistics, or technology industries 
 Background in Sustainability 
 Knowledge of sustainability efforts, trends and KPIs in the consumer goods industry 
 Project management experience preferred 
 
 Education 
 
 Bachelor's Degree in Sustainability, Marketing or Operations Management 
 MBA preferred
