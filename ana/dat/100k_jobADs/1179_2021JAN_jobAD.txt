 Registered General Nurse 
 An amazing job opportunity has arisen for a motivated RGN to work in an exceptional care home based in the Netherton, Dudley area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading healthcare providers 
 This beautiful care home provides palliative care, Parkinson's nursing, convalescence and Dementia nursing 
 As a Nurse your day to day duties include: 
 
 
     Leading, supervising and training the care team
 
 
 
     General Nursing duties-Maintaining acceptable levels of personal hygiene, clean beds and proper nutrition for the Residents.
 
 
 
     Being responsible for ordering, storing and administering medication
 
 
 
     Observations/ Monitoring of blood sugar levels and wound care
 
 
 
     Keeping precise, up to date records and communicating with Drs and the Home Manager
 
 
 
     Maintaining the Residents' dignity and respect at all times
 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 Able to work under pressure and prioritise workload 
 The ability to lead a team, analyse problems and propose solutions or improvements 
 Able to communicate effectively and to develop professional relationships 
 Understands and effectively uses IT and communication systems required for the role 
 Experience of participating in quality and clinical governance programmes 
 Able to show a can-do attitude always 
 
 **The successful Nurse will be offered an excellent salary of £16.50 - £17.50 per hour. 
 We currently have vacancies for both days or night shifts available. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Paid Breaks - At selected Homes
 
 
 
     Free Meals On Long Days
 
 
 
     Free DBS
 
 
 
     Ongoing Career Progression
 
 
 
     Pension Scheme
 
 
 
     Assistance With Revalidation
