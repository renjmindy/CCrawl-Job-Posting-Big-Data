 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Mid January start 
 
 
 3 Swings Work 
 
 
 Lake Way Location 
 
 
 About Our Client 
 Our Client is a Western Australian company that was established in 1969 in Western Australia and has since developed into a trusted supplier of fabricated steel products to the industry. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are recruiting Innershield Welders for 3:1 Roster, (3 swings) based in Lake Way to commence mid January. 
 
 
 12 hour Day shifts 
 
 
 RDO â Half day on second Sunday of each swing 
 
 
 $51.00 Flat Rate plus Super 
 
 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Must have own Adflow and any additional welding PPE 
 
 
 Able to pass Innershild Weld Test 
 
 
 Ability to pass Pre-employment Medical and DAS 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Trade qualification 
 
 
 Must have High Risk WP Ticket 
 
 
 Working at Heights (renewed within the last 2 years for validity)
