 I am currently working with a manufacturing business in Worcester who are looking for an Interim Management Accountant on a 3 month contract. My client is looking for an individual who can hit the ground running and get stuck into all the management accounts. They are looking for someone who is commutable from their site near the City Centre of Worcester. 
 Duties will be: 
 
 
 Responsible for posting & reconciling of all payroll 
 
 
 The monitoring of all accrual & prepayments. 
 
 
 All balance sheet reconciliations relevant to your area of responsibility. 
 
 
 Budgeting and forecasting 
 
 
 Financial analysis 
 
 
 Assist the Senior Finance Manager to complete the statutory accounts for the UK entities 
 
 
 Ad hoc reporting 
 
 
 The candidate will either be ACCA/ACA/CIMA or equivalent. Please apply if this sounds suitable for you.
