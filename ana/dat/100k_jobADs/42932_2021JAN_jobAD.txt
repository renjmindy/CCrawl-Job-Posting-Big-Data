 Full-Time Gold Seller - Buyer 
 Goldbuyers / Guld & Metallhandel Finland Oy  -  Uusimaa  , Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 We are looking for sellers / buyers in Helsinki, for our shop. You are by nature forward-looking, ambitious, creative, organized, open to the new, empathetic. You keep the change, solve problems with your mind, you are able to quick decisions and you know how to plan your time well and efficiently. You understand that the job brings with it great responsibility, and if necessary, you have flexible working hours. 
 In addition, you speak English too. 
 For a good basic education and knowledge increases, so does your responsibility. You continue to receive additional training. 
 The salary base salary + commission. 
 Trading under the collective agreement. Are you interested in taking part from the very beginning, and to increase the Finnish chain? We offer excellent opportunities for the right kind of people. The sooner you grasp the opportunity, the better chance you have to make rapid progress in a growing organization. 
 Gold Buyers is one the fastest growing companies and currently we are opening new customer points in different parts of Finnish. We offer our customers an easy way to change your old and broken gold, silver and platinum items immediately liquidate the nearest shopping center. Selling unnecessary gold us you will also help the environment. In this way, we can work together to reduce the environmental impact of extraction of gold caused by environmental stress.
