 First Annual Women and Minorities Career Fair 
 Posted on  September 20, 2013  by  rdmink 
 Tuesday, September 24th  from  9am-2pm. 
 This is the first annual nontraditional trades career fair, located at  Spokane Community College  Apprenticeship Training Center, 2110 N. Farcher Way, Spokane. The event features hands-on workshops and is geared toward women and minorities. There is no cost to attend. For more information, call 279-1211. 
 Check out  The Pacific Northwest Inlander  for more upcoming Spokane-area events.
