 Kuwait Caregivers Jobs 
 Posted on  November 24, 2012  by  caregiver 
 Three Job Orders has just been approved by the POEA allowing three of Manilas premiere recruitment agencies to recruit caregivers for placement in Kuwait Caregivers Jobs. These Kuwait caregivers jobs has total vacancies for 97 caregivers whose Job Orders were all approved by the POEA last November 15, 2012. Provided below are the names of the recruiting agencies and the actual number of Kuwait caregivers jobs for each agency. 
 A & C International Resources Inc. ----------------- 20 caregivers jobs 
Spring Resources Management & Promotion Inc. --- 47 caregivers jobs 
FORS International Inc. ----------------------------- 30 caregivers jobs 
 All those who wish to apply for the said Kuwait Caregivers Jobs are advised to submit their comprehensive resume personally to the office of any of the recruiting agency listed above.
