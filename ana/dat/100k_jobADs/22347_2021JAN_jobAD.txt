 My Career 
 
 This Career information will act as a catalyst for awareness and consideration on the importance of career planning and management which is a subject most people deprioritise resulting in a lack of strategic direction and, in most cases, lower income. By watching the My Career Videos, people secure access to insights from experienced career advisors and seasoned, successful managers. Through these insights, it is possible to increase knowledge levels around career planning and therefore develop a more informed plan.
