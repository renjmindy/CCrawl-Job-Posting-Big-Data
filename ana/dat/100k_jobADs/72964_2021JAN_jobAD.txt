 Technician for in vivo electrophysiology (with a possibility of doing a PhD) 
 POSITION CLOSED 
 Technician (with a possibility of doing a PhD): 
 The  Neuroinformatics Laboratory  at the  Nencki Institute of Experimental Biology , invites applications for a  technician position . 
 Available from – February 2018 
 Duration – approx. 3 years 
 Description 
The Laboratory of Neuroinformatics is seeking to recruit a technician interested in neuroscience to assist and learn in vivo electrophysiology experimental techniques. The position is part of an NCN funded project, there is a possibility that the position will be transferred into a PhD position. Experimental work will be carried out in both anesthetized and freely moving rats and full training will be given within the laboratory. The position is available for approx. 3 years and the candidate should ideally have the following: 
 
 An interest to learn in vivo electrophysiology 
 Some previous work with rodents (ideally) 
 Some experience with signal processing (ideally) 
 Good fluency in English 
 
 The position is full-time, with an expected start date February 2018. Salary will depend on experience. 
 Required Documents 
Please send your CV and a letter of motivation to Dr M J Hunt  mhunt@nencki.gov.pl .
