 Care Giver Job Abroad 
 Posted on  March 9, 2011  by  caregiver 
 A POEA accredited recruitment agency is currently inviting care giver job applicants for their POEA approved  Care Giver Job Abroad . This care giver job abroad is for the country of UAE and was approved for recruitment by the POEA last March 3, 2011 for a recruitment agency located in Ermita, Manila. And with the said job order approval, the agency is allowed to recruit 5 applicants who are qualified for the said care giver job abroad. 
 If you are interested to apply for the said Care Giver Job Abroad, kindly submit your resume together with your application to the office of Mi Amore International Services Inc. If you have any question regarding the said Care Giver Job Abroad, you may contact Mi Amore International Services Inc. thru their telephone numbers provided below. 
 Mi Amore International Services Inc. 
S301, 302, 303, & 304 AP Building 
1563 F. Agoncillo & P. Gil Street, Ermita 
Manila 
Tel Nos: 3025517 / 3025465 
Email Add: miamore@cavite.i-next.net
