 Store Manager 
 Job description 
 Role Highlights 
 
 
 Permanent role direct with our client 
 
 
 Monday to Friday position- ASAP start 
 
 
 Company ute and fuel card 
 
 
 About our Client 
Our client is well established in the Newcastle area in the furniture retail industry looking to add an experienced Store Manager to the team, this role will be based in Muswellbrook. 
 About the Role 
 
 
 Generate sales and meet sales targets in store 
 
 
 Inventory control and management 
 
 
 Order and receipt stock 
 
 
 Complete run sheets for orders and deliveries 
 
 
 Administration tasks including in store POS 
 
 
 Staff rostering and team management 
 
 
 Benefits and Perks 
 
 
 Immediate start for the right person 
 
 
 Use of company ute and fuel card 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Must have experience in merchandising 
 
 
 Must have previous retail sales experience 
 
 
 Management experience to lead a team 
 
 
 Administration experience ie Point of sale system 
 
 
 Proven sales records to achieve sales targets 
 
 
 Be able to lead by example 
 
 
 Training will be provided to ensure your understanding of the product range in store 
 
 
 Monday to Friday hours - 9am to 5pm* 
 
 
 Must be able to work every second weekend* and/or public holidays (shared roster) 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
