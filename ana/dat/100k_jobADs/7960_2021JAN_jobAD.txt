 Accounts Assistant - Dudley - £21-24k. This is a great opportunity to join a vibrant & growing business, where you will be able to support using your varied skill base. 
 We are working with an organisation that is consistently growing and developing within its sector. We are now seeking an additional Accounts Assistant to join their finance team to support this growth plan. 
 The Role 
 The Accounts Assistant role is ideally suited to someone with a broad skill base. The team operates with positivity and energy, and it is a well-organised set-up within lovely offices. The role will involve the following tasks: 
 
 Multiple bank reconciliations 
 Supplier statement recs 
 Posting payments & receipts to correct codes 
 Closure of the ledgers at month end 
 Dealing with supplier & customer queries 
 Credit Control 
 Ad hoc support tasks for month & year end 
 
 This is a permanent opportunity, suited to someone who enjoys a challenge within a stable environment. 
 The Person 
 I am seeking someone with a desire to bring their varied skills in ledgers, credit control and reconciliations. You'll have strong attention to detail, with a methodical and logical approach to your workload. 
 Accounts Assistant - Dudley - permanent role paying up to £24k per annum.
