 Guest Services: Front Desk/Housekeeping - Maintenance 
 We are looking for awesome Guest Services Front Desk/Housekeeping and Maintenance team, to work 30-40 hours each per week who are passionate about ensuring our campers have the best time, every time. 
 Located just 11 miles north of Nashville, spend your summer experiencing "Music City" and the Nashville area, from tours to museums, to music venues and the Grand Ole Opry. Positions are available October 1, 2018 - March 30, 2020 (possibly longer). We pay for all hours worked - most positions are 30-40 hours per week. Work campers must have RV and live on-site. 
 **Guest Services/Front Desk  **- This position is responsible for, but not limited to, completing registrations, reservations and store sales while following procedures. Careful and accurate money handling, processing cash and credit card transactions while utilizing the computer, credit card terminal, and other front desk equipment. Handling camper issues with respect by listening and acknowledging their concern. Knowledge of KampSight (K2) is a plus. Housekeeping i.e. cleaning restrooms and cabins. 
 **Maintenance  **- Skilled maintenance and electrical knowledge is a plus. Providing exceptional guest services is a must, as this position also requires working as an ambassador escorting guests to their site, filling propane, delivering firewood and ice. Other duties include park maintenance, grass cutting, weed eating, help with pool maintenance and other duties as assigned. Projects are to be completed properly, safely and timely. 
 We would love to have the right team join us around the campfire. We're looking forward to hearing from you. 
 Compensation: 
 Couples are paid for all hours worked, the hourly rate is dependent upon experience. Benefits also include the following: free full hook-up site (30/50amp), (water/electric/sewer & cable), two days off a week with your partner, uniform shirts, and employee discounts on laundry, propane and store merchandise.
