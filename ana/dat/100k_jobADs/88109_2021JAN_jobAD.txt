 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently recruiting for an experienced Security Programme Manager to join an fantastic Birmingham based business who have seen a rapid amount of growth over the last 12 months. This role will be suitable for someone who has experience within the delivery of security programmes from a technical and business change aspect. This role will be an initial 3 month contract however extensions are always likely. 
 What are we looking for? 
 
 Past experience at Senior Project or Programme level would be ideal 
 Work will focus on the business change aspect of a major security programme
 
 Working with key stakeholders and third party suppliers to develop policies, process and procedures 
 Support planning and stakeholder management to engage correct audience to review and contribute to process and procedure development 
 Manage workshops across four European sites to understand business impacts, quantifying and documenting these 
 
 
 Familiarity with, ISO27k or other structured security standard would be advantageous 
 Must have experience of implementing security programmes of work including the business change aspects (not just technical) 
 Standard project / programme management activities - cover for other PMs, planning, reporting, risk management, budget management, managing stakeholders & third party suppliers 
 
 Location: Currently home working due to COVID-19; UK head office is Birmingham, and affected sites are located in Germany, Italy & Spain. Some attendance at UK head office may be required for this role and some travel within Europe as required - when safe to do so. 
 Day rate £525 a day - Outside of IR35 - Immediate Start - 3 Months + Extensions 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
