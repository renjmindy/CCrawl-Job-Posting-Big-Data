 Are you an experienced HR Business Partner looking for a new opportunity in the London area? 
 With multi-site experience, do you have the gravitas and pace to join a commercial business who are going from strength to strength with ambitious growth plans. 
 Working as part of a regional HR team, with a head office in the Midlands, you will work in partnership with senior stakeholders across the business in a multi-site role providing professional and credible HR business support. 
 As the HR Business Partner you will: 
 
 Work in partnership with the leadership teams in a full generalist role 
 Develop and build strong relationships across the senior management teams 
 Coach and challenge business leaders 
 Lead on the talent agenda, succession plans and talent development 
 Be comfortable working operationally 
 
 To apply for the HR Business Partner role: 
 
 Proven experience of working as HR Business Partner in a commercial environment, ideally in a fast paced multi-site role 
 Extensive HR Generalist experience to include talent management, succession planning, and Employee Engagement 
 Experience of driving cultural change as well as driving capability and performance within the business 
 Strong senior level influencing and stakeholder management skills 
 CIPD Qualified 
 
 The role is home-based, covering sites across North London, Herts, Cambridgshire, Suffolk and Surrey. Salary up to £57k, with 45p per mile expenses. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
