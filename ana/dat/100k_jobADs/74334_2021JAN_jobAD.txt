 General Farm Hand (Base Rate) 
 Job description 
 General Farm Hand / Labourer 
 General Farm Hand is required to clean crocodile pens, feeding and general farm maintenance. May also include work in hatchery and other duties as required. Must be physically fit & able to perform manual tasks. Farm South of Cairns so must be local to Cairns or Gordonvale area. 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Own transport and license 
 
 
 Must be physically fit. 
 
 
 Solid OH&S; awareness 
 
 
 General labouring duties 
 
 
 Why work for WorkPac? 
 WorkPac is Australia's lading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
