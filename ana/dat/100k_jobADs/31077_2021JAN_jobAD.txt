 Executive Assistant to Chief Sales Officer 
 Power seeks a well rounded, self motivated individual to work as the executive assistant to our Chief Sales Officer. The desired candidate will have the ability to multi-task, prioritize and work in a fast-paced, ever-changing environment. 
 Responsibilities include but are not limited to: 
 
 Work on Keynote presentations and Numbers spreadsheets organizing and analyzing data 
 Assist with marketing functions such as social media and sales manuals 
 Meeting preparation – developing agendas, presentations and taking meeting minutes 
 Follow up with Regional Vice Presidents to ensure completion of previously agreed action items 
 Preparing confidential correspondence 
 Organize and manage calendar 
 Close collaboration with the Executive Team 
 Conducting research in support of CSO’s projects and strategic planning 
 Coordinate content for weekly conference calls 
 Coordinating travel arrangements – transportation and accommodations 
 Managing household tasks and personal projects 
 
 Qualifications 
 
 Highly organized and detail oriented 
 Strong verbal and written communication skills 
 Proficient in Apple and PC software 
 Proactive, motivated, and driven 
 Professional, ethical team player 
 Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to work effectively with people at all levels 
 
 Requirements 
 
 
 Positive attitude, with a passion for learning. 
 
 
 1-2 years of experience in an administrative role 
 
 
 Excellent computer skills - We use Apple Mac Computers 
 
 
 Highly organized and able to prioritize under pressure 
 
 
 Strong written & verbal communication skills 
 
 
 Proven ability to deal with highly confidential and sensitive information 
 
 
 Ability to task switch in a fast paced environment and anticipate last minute changes 
 
 
 Attention to detail is mandatory 
 
 
 Resourceful Team player who is also effective independently 
 
 
 Proactive approaches to problem-solving and strong decision-making capability 
 
 
 Flexibility with last minute changes 
 
 
 Location  : Chester, PA (Alongside the Chester Waterfront, at the base of the Commodore Barry Bridge, next to the Philadelphia Union Soccer Stadium).
