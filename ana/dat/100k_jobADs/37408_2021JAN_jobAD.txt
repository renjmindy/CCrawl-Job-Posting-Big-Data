 Podcast #8: Critical thinking and your career with Fiona Fennell 
 gradireland editor Ruairi Kavanagh talks to Fiona Fennell, founder of the STEM consultancy  Think4Purpose  about graduate careers in STEM and the value of thinking differently in terms of your career. Think4Purpose helps STEM professionals integrate what is known with how to think collectively for the purpose of learning why.
