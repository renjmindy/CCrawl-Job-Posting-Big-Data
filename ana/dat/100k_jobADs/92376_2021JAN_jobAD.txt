 Temporary Greenhouse Workers 
 Auranmaan henkilöstöpalvelu -  Anywhere 
 Job Description 
 We are looking for greenhouse workers to work with flowers and vegetables. Applicant must be motivated and able to work in hectic environment. Driving license and an own car is a plus. We can organize accommodation. 
 Work is suitable for both, men and women. Salary is based on collective agreement. Length of the work depends on applicants skills. We can organize year-round work for good workers.
