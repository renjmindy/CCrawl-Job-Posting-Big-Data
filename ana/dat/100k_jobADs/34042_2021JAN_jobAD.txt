 How would you like to join a leading Midlands multi-discipline business as Head of Procurement? Are you a hands-on leader with experience of managing a procurement function? Then, please read on - this role could be perfect for you. 
 As Head of Procurement, you will be reporting into a Head of Finance that truly values procurement and work for a business that is committed to driving change. You will ensure that this organisation procures all goods and services at the best price and build long term relationships with suppliers. You will build the procurement team to ensure they deliver value and increased support to the organisation, lead on key initiatives and implement new and improved systems to support procurement processes. A key part of the role will be to oversee a Source to Pay project along with managing a spend of circa £100M 
 You will have responsibility for 2 direct reports and as a team, you will work towards reducing suppliers, reviewing their performance, ensuring that payment terms are agreed and effectively implemented, and driving collaborative procurement. 
 In order to carry out this role effectively, you will need the following skills and experience: 
 
 Previous experience of running a procurement function 
 Ideally MCIPS qualified 
 Strong negotiation skills 
 Excellent people management skills 
 The ability to challenge current practices and processes 
 A collaborative approach 
 Excellent communication skills - both verbal and written 
 
 In return, you will receive a salary of circa £65,000 to £75,000 dependent upon experience, car allowance and bonus scheme + other benefits. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
