 Heymarket 
 
 Overview 
 Heymarket helps businesses message with their customers over SMS, Apple Business Chat, Facebook Messenger, and more. Heymarket is used by over 1,200 businesses including Dignity Health, Blue Bottle, and Uhaul. Heymarket was founded by the team behind Manymoon, the #1 app in the Google Apps Marketplace and acquired by Salesforce. 
 We are seeking our first sales person drive mid-market lead generation. This is an excellent opportunity for a self-starter that is looking to build a sales process with a company that has already reached product/market fit. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Build and manage a strong sales pipeline while exceeding quarterly sales targets 
 Work with the CEO to develop a plan for continued pipeline growth 
 Provide accurate forecasting through Salesforce 
 Manage full sale cycle from lead generation, qualification, and working with Customer Success to conduct demos, and closing 
 Utilize prospecting tools such as ZoomInfo and Outreach to identify and engage with key decision makers 
 Own and iterate on the sales playbook 
 
 Requirements 
 
 2+ years of startup SaaS sales experience 
 4-year undergraduate degree 
 Experience with targeted prospecting, qualifying leads, and product demos 
 Experience with Salesforce, Outreach, ZoomInfo, and LinkedIn Sales Navigator 
 Ability to adapt easily to new situations and eager to learn/contribute 
 Excellent verbal and written communication skills 
 
 Heymarket is remote, but has an office in the SoMa neighborhood of San Francisco. Benefits include medical, dental, and vision insurance. To learn more about the company, please visit our website at  heymarket.com .
