 Job Description 
 Phillips Medisize offers healthcare companies comprehensive product design and manufacturing services. We operate in a customer-oriented and manufacture mm. a variety of drug delivery devices, medical and laboratory equipment and supplies, medical kits, as well as medical and diagnostic equipment. 
 Phillips is Medisize production both in the U.S. and in Europe, where the factories are located in Kontiolahti in the Netherlands, Ireland, Switzerland and the Czech Republic. Size of the company's turnover in 2012 was about USD 500 million and a staff of over 2000, of which 830 in Europe The current corporate structure was created in the summer of 2011, when Medisize Corporation subsidiaries were transferred to the American Phillips Plastics Corporation ownership. 
 We are growing rapidly and we are looking for Joensuu our assembly production support team Tuotantoteknikkoa responsibilities will include assembly equipment maintenance, production equipment, as well as participation in the development of new projects to support. Suitable for such a college or polytechnic-level technical training (eg technician), but you have at least 3 years of experience in assembly technology, pneumatics and automation. Experience in the plastics industry and knowledge of GMP requirements, as well as understanding of the CE requirements would be an advantage. You are cooperative, spontaneous and not afraid to take on new challenges. 
 You'll be able to work both orally and in writing in the  English language and you have good IT skills  (ERP knowledge would be an advantage). Applicants are required in addition to accountability, independent problem-solving skills, good team work and reporting skills, readiness to shift work.
