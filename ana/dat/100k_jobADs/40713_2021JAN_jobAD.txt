 Labourer-Demolition 
 Job description 
 Role Highlights 
 
 
 Monday to Friday role, immediate start 
 
 
 $32.25 per hour 
 
 
 Short term role (1-2 weeks) 
 
 
 About the Role 
WorkPac are seeking Labourers for asbestos removal in Umina. The successful candidate will have a non-friable asbestos removal ticket and preferably a background in demolition. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Assist with the safe removal of asbestos 
 
 
 Demolition labouring 
 
 
 Work independently and autonomously, at times, as well as part of a team 
 
 
 Follow a Monday to Friday roster 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Be physically fit 
 
 
 Previous experience within demolition (ideal) 
 
 
 Non-Friable Asbestos Ticket 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities.
