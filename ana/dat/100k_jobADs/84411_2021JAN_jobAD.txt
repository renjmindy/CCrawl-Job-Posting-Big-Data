 Doing the Math on a WK Job in Jan/Feb Issue 
 Posted by 120632 on 12/28/2020 
 Read the Jan/Feb 2021 issue of Workamper News Magazine Online Now! 
 Featured Article: Fundamental FAQs About Workamping 
 When people are just starting out in the Workamping lifestyle or they’re exploring the opportunity, they often have lots of questions. Questions are good! Asking questions is part of the process and nobody likes surprises. Whenever something is unclear, it’s always best to ask questions. People call the Workamper News office every day asking questions. However, when you’re new to Workamping, you might not even know what questions to ask. Here are some frequently asked questions (FAQs) that we routinely answer.
