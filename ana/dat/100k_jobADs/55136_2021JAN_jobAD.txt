 Home Manager 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Home Manager to work in an amazing nursing home based in the Exeter, Devon area. 
 As the Home Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This nursing home Provides nursing care, dementia care, palliative care and respite care to its residents 
 As the Home Manager your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     You will be responsible for managing the day to day running the home
 
 
 
     Leading a team of Care professionals and with a passion and commitment for delivering the highest standards of care to residents
 
 
 
     You will form strong relationships with staff, residents and their families and your presence in the home will be pivotal to its success
 
 
 
 An ideal Home Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Previous experience in a residential and/or nursing setting
 
 
 
     Strong operational management experience
 
 
 
     Give the highest quality of personalised care to our residents in line with their individual care plans
 
 
 
     Work together as a team to enrich our residents' lives
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitdue always
 
 
 
 **The successful Home Manager will receive an excellent salary of £50,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 40 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     25 days annual leave (rising to a maximum of 30 days) plus bank holidays
 
 
 
     Life assurance
 
 
 
     CQC performance bonus
 
 
 
     Excellent training and development opportunities
 
 
 
     Loyalty Award available
 
 
 
     Refer a friend scheme with payment of up to £750 available
 
 
 
     Contributory pension from company
 
 
 
     Access to a free 24/7 support service providing support including legal, financial, emotional and medical advice
 
 
 
     Discounts on shopping, holidays, cinema, dining, days out and much more via 'My Rewards'
 
 
 
     Criminal Records Checks are funded by the company
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse either RGN or RMN with a valid NMC Pin
