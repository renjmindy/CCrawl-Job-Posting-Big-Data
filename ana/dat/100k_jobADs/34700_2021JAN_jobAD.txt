 COVID Administrative Assistant - Temporary Full-time - Occupational Health and Safety (202756) 
 Sunnybrook Health Sciences Centre 
 __Toronto, ON 
 Apply Now 
 We currently have a Temporary Full-time opportunity for a COVID Administrative Assistant in the Occupational Health and Safety department at our Bayvew Campus. 
 Purpose of the Role: 
Provide administrative support to daily operations of the Occupational Health & Safety Clinic (OHS) Clinic and staff COVID-19 testing. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
Ensure staff COVID-19 paperwork/requisitions are accurate 
Perform screening and checking in of employees for a COVID-19 swab in a professional and courteous manner 
Screen employees for basic symptoms (list to be given) 
Complete COVID paperwork for data entry 
Answer phone calls– screen and redirect staff if required 
Check in employees and place them on the signup sheet 
Offer alternatives to visitors or staff seeking services not available from OHS 
Process department requests and monitor 
Provide administrative support to Management 
Monitors service quality, and identify opportunities for improvement 
 Qualifications/Skills: 
College diploma or undergraduate degree 
Must be organized in approaching work tasks and able to prioritize effectively to deal with competing demands 
Must have excellent working knowledge of Microsoft Office (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint) 
Must be proficient in written and oral communication skills to support our requirement to read, edit and compose correspondence 
Must be very adept at exercising tact and discretion when dealing with highly sensitive and highly confidential issues 
Must be able to demonstrate flexibility as well as a comfort level with taking initiative on a wide range of issues 
Must be able to work independently with limited direction 
Good problem-solving skills and an ability to work as a team member 
Must demonstrate the ability to appropriately respond to changing circumstances 
 Qualified candidates are invited to submit their cover letter and resume (in one document) quoting 202756 to:
