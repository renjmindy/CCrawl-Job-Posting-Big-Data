 Customer Service Position 
 We are in search of qualified customer service candidates to help lead our company to the next level. We are an industry leader in the hotel deep cleaning service. Our crews travel throughout the country working at different hotels cleaning carpets, a/c, tile furniture and drapes. The customer service representative would be the liaison between the crew, the hotel and the corporate staff. This position requires travel nationwide with a 3-4 month commitment. Travel expenses, lodging and meals are covered. This is a salaried light labor position. Must be 21 yrs old have a valid DL pass a background and drug screen. 
 Ideal candidates have previous customer service experience, are willing to pitch in when shorthanded, and posses exceptional interpersonal communication skills. Coaching and developing experience a strong plus. 
 Technician Position 
 Do you enjoy travel? Are you a hard worker? Can you be on the road for 3-4 months at a time? If so then Fibercare wants you!!! 
 We are a leader in the hotel deep cleaning service industry. Our technicians travel throughout the country with a crew of 2-3 deep cleaning carpets, drapes, furniture, tile, and A/C units in hotels. This is a very physically demanding job and requires you to be able to bend, stoop kneel and squat throughout the day and lift 50-75 lbs every 15-20 minutes, No experience required. 2 weeks paid training $12.50/HR. Benefits include: Health, Dental, 401K, vacation pay, and road warrior bonus. Plus, you get to see the country!! Lodging is provided and meals allowance offered. Must be 21 years old, have a valid DL and pass a background and drug screen. If you think you would be a valuable part of our family please apply online or call for more information.
