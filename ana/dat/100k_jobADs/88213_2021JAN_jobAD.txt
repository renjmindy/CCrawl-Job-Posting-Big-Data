 Frontend Developer at Steadfast REMOTEPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 What kind of  developer  are we looking for? 
 A front-end developer with 3+ years experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. As a custom shop, most projects require a variety of front-end technologies and skillsets. 
 But, perhaps even more importantly, we’re a small and tight-knit team that values more than just a list of qualifications. If you care deeply about the quality of your work, enjoy cutting up with a great group of people, and are always looking for opportunities to grow and improve, we’d love to have you on board. 
 At this time, we are only accepting US-based applicants. 
 What are the day-to-day  responsibilities ? 
 
 Develop responsive, cross-browser compatible websites 
 Develop responsive email campaigns 
 Abide by, evolve, and enforce code standards to help maintain a clean, consistent code base 
 Consult with dev lead and design team to create and manage technical requirements 
 Manage time and workload effectively; understand when and how to reach out for guidance 
 Write relevant and thorough comments in code and in commits 
 Launch and maintain existing websites 
 Stay on top of industry trends and new web technologies 
 
 What are the  skill requirements ? 
 
 Deep expertise and knowledge in HTML5+, CSS3+, Vanilla JavaScript, and jQuery 
 Ability to write semantic, accessible, and search-engine friendly code 
 Cross browser, responsive, and mobile development skills 
 Ability to work successfully in a team, independently, and take initiative on tasks 
 Experience with Craft CMS or other CMS platform integration 
 Basic knowledge of and ability to write PHP 
 Basic understanding of server config/DNS for website management 
 Knowledge of email development quirks and workarounds 
 Basic understanding of UI/UX 
 
 A few nice-to-haves... 
 
 Experience with modern frameworks such as Vue or React 
 Advanced PHP knowledge 
 Experience with Git and version control 
 Experience with NPM, Yarn, Babel, Webpack, etc.
