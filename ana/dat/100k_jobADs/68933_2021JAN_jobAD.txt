 Consultant: 
 
 
 I am currently working with an emerging, technology driven business in the Birmingham area, who are looking for a Project Manager to join their tech team. Not only will you be working in a core function of the business, you will also be supporting global teams, as well as supporting customer implementation projects. 
 The successful candidate will play a key part in supporting business critical projects, driving improvements and have scope across various size projects - all within a rapidly growing, leading software business. 
 What is required? 
 
 Previous role in the Project Management space, with the ability to manage various size projects 
 Agile experience 
 Prince2, APM qualification 
 Track record of budget, resource management, as well as RAID logs and project planning 
 Senior stakeholder experience - internal and external 
 Worked in a Financial Services/Functional environment in a similar role 
 
 What's in it for you? 
 
 Opportunity to work for a global organisation with exposure to all areas of the organisation 
 Be a part of a strategic direction, focusing on business-critical projects 
 Work with the latest technologies and have the opportunity to input and provide solutions for better practice 
 Competitive salary + benefits 
 
 Please contact Keeley for further information or apply here with your CV.
