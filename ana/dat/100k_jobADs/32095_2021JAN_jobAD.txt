 Job offers in Ibiza! 
 15/02/201415/02/2014  by  Ibiza Link , posted in  casting a ibiza ,  Casting Ibiza 2014 ,  empleos en ibiza ,  ibiza ,  ibiza 2014 ,  ibiza by night ,  job in ibiza ,  job offers in ibiza summer 2014 ,  job opportunities ,  job opportunities ibiza ,  job opportunities in ibiza ,  jobs in ibiza ,  jobs in ibiza summer 2014 ,  lavorare a ibiza ,  lavorare ad ibiza estate 2014 ,  lavoro a ibiza ,  ofertas de trabajo en ibiza ,  offerte lavoro estate 2014 ,  offerte lavoro ibiza estate 2014 ,  trabajar en ibiza ,  trabajo verano 2014 ibiza ,  trabajos en ibiza ,  work in ibiza ,  working in ibiza 
 We follow to give news about job offers in Ibiza, trying to help our friends that are searching a job in the island. But.. don't forget to read the disclaimer!! 
 Today we talk about two different offers: 
 a) Cotton beach club Ibiza shared last 29th of january this offer: "Cotton Beach Club Ibiza is seeking bartenders, waiters, beach boys with experience, charisma and healthy lifestyle. We need people from May-October, please contact merete@cottonbeachclub.com with CV and photos". 
 For further infos this is the link on fb to the Cotton's fanpage: https://www.facebook.com/pages/Cotton-Beach-Club-Ibiza/417639155007470?fref=ts 
 b) we found this offer at Madbar Ibiza, posted the 7th of February. For further infos this is the link to the fb fanpage: 
 https://www.facebook.com/madbaribiza?fref=ts 
 
 Good luck and don't forget to read the disclaimer! 
 DISCLAIMER 
 Ibiza by night isn’t a job agency and isn’t involved directly or indirectly in any casting. 
 Ibiza by night doesn’t receive any economical advantage or profit from castings. 
 Ibiza by night   doesn’t assume any responsability for the job opportunities published on these pages. 
 The job opportunities are offered directly from the employers. 
 For further informations contact directly ONLY the organizers of castings at mentioned mail addresses and, please, don’t send us CV or more information requests because of we can’t help you.
