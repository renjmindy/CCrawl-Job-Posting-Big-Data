 Business Analyst-Supply Chain Systems 
 Roots 
 __York, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Head Office, 1400 Castlefield Ave, York, Ontario, Canada Req #1126 
Tuesday, November 24, 2020 
 Roots is more than just an/the iconic Canadian retailer, we are a group of passionate employees who act with integrity, trust each other and do what is right. We work in a space where people can grow and develop, with a team of people who own results and are dedicated to seeing Roots win. We seek to build longstanding relationships with partners who share our commitment to maintaining high ethical standards of operation. 
 We are currently seeking an IT Business Analyst – Supply Chain Systems who can be nice, will seek inspiration from our roots, can commit to achieving results the right way and loves to think big to join us on our journey. The IT Business Analyst – Supply Chain Systems will report to the Manager, Supply Chain Systems and be responsible to provide process and system support to our business users. 
 The IT Business Analyst – Supply Chain Systems for Roots will be responsible for and evaluated on the following: 
 Participating in the investigation and analysis of specific business requirements and user needs 
Taking part in the delivery of suitable solutions according to agreed specifications that add business value to products and services within budget and time constraints 
Working through the implementation of upgraded or new software or hardware 
Executing and supporting various levels of application testing 
Completing application quality assurance for system modification/software upgrade 
Updating and implementing operational procedures through projects or support to ensure smooth operation and acceptable performance 
Leading the training of the DC operations personnel through projects or support 
 Top candidates for the IT Business Analyst – Supply Chain Systems role will have the following: 
 Post-secondary Education, preferably in business or applied computer science 
3 – 5 years’ of experience in developing and maintaining medium to large size scaled business systems 
Proficiency in HighJump One WMS or other WMS (Warehouse Management System) 
Familiarity with supporting various interfaces from and to WMS 
Familiarity with supporting WCS (Warehouse Control System) 
Knowledge of warehouse/supply chain functions and processes 
Knowledge of EDI (Electronic Data Interchange) configuration and solutions. Experience with setup, mapping, testing, onboarding new vendors, troubleshooting, etc.) 
Ability to find innovative solutions to meet identified business needs 
Exposure to supporting and troubleshooting multiple integrated solutions 
Excellent computer related and developmental skills, with the ability to learn new software 
Advanced technical skills and documentation skills 
Basic to intermediate SQL query-writing skills 
 Founded in 1973, Roots Canada is a publicly held Canadian lifestyle brand that sells apparel, leather bags, small leather goods, footwear and active athletic wear with over 100 retail stores in North America and Asia.
