 Hot Damn! it's a Full-time job 
 GLOW | AGENCY is looking for an Art Director in Manhattan, NY, USA. 
 About 
 GLOW is a strategic, creative agency that takes pride in calling the biggest brands, networks, and platforms a partner. We work across the world of entertainment and technology, crafting amazing experiences for our clients, their audiences, and fans. We have a passion for building communities and delivering breakthrough creativity. 
 Role 
As an Art Director at GLOW you’ll conceive and develop a design vision and creative concepts that are forward-thinking, inspired by and driving trends, and show a deep appreciation for design as a craft. You’ll drive and work hands-on to develop captivating design that elevates composition, form, typography, motion, and interactivity. You’ll be a tastemaker for our team and our clients, inspiring with your talents. 
 You should possess a strong understanding of digital platforms and design for cutting-edge experiences across web, products, and social. You’re attentive to details, and well-versed in both creating and applying visual design systems, layouts, typography, and can speak to a range of design inspiration. 
 Responsibilities 
° Direct, define, and design creative executions 
° Lead and contribute to a range of projects from fast-paced daily deliverables to large tent-pole campaigns and strategies 
° Inspire, mentor, and direct a team of designers, producers, and writers 
° Craft presentations and campaigns that shatter expectations 
° Define and evolve standards for design and creative production 
° Own and communicate a creative POV that sets a high bar 
 Credentials 
° 4+ years of design and art direction experience, some within an agency or in-house, developing breakthrough design and brand campaigns 
° Passion for working with client leaders and presenting creative in an engaging way 
° Ability to collaborate with multiple teams internally and externally to drive great design 
° Strong knowledge of and capacity to design for Motion
