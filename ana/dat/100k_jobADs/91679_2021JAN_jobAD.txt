 Full-Time Sales Representative 
 
 Uusimaa  , Finland 
 
 Job Description 
 We are a leading Finnish company specializing in the cleaning and protection of textile-covered surfaces. Among our customers are: cinemas, hotels and conference rooms. 
 Now we are seeking a sales representative for the Swedish market, you will be responsible for the Swedish market - sales and customer contacts. You are a hard-working, young, mildly workaholic and socially competent sales representative and also has knowledge and experience in B2B sales. 
 As your advantage is seen training, experience or studies in the area. Your workplace will be in Sweden and working area is the whole of Sweden. The working language is Swedish but proficiency in Finnish and English are in your favor.
