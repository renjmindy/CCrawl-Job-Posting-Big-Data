 Team Accounting Manager 
 Team Accounting Manager 
 
 Location 
 
 Reading, Berkshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £54000.00 - £62000 per annum + Bonus 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Rob Anderson 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 robanderson@workwithglee.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH20806_1591191448 
 
 Published: 
 
 8 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-07-03 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Our client is dedicated to delivering exceptional global consultancy services by developing relationships across multiple disciplines. 
 With impressive and continued growth, the business is expanding its Thames Valley operations and is recruiting a number of new roles which will develop your broad accounting and technical skillset. 
 Job Specification 
 
 Responsible for delivering a co-ordinated service to a portfolio of clients 
 Managing service delivery on accounting/financial matters (i.e. outsourced CFO/FC) 
 Detailed review of all accounting output 
 Ownership of accounting output 
 Management of resource requirements within the team through business cycle 
 Managing the workflow of the team on a day to day basis 
 Maintaining a high standard of service and good client relations 
 Co-ordinating service delivery with other disciplines 
 Managing the effectiveness of accounting technical resources 
 Managing team billing 
 Team debt management 
 You will also have responsibility for Business Planning, People Development, Risk Management and Business Development 
 
 Skills Required 
 
 Qualified Accountant, preferably ACA or ACCA or CIMA 
 Multi discipline awareness 
 Business risk aware 
 Client service orientated 
 Strong people and team management skills, especially to develop people by motivating and coaching 
 Capable of managing a small team 
 Good written and oral communication 
 Good presentation skills 
 Willingness to build relationships and to influence positively at all levels 
 Able to use own initiative and identify issues 
 Openness to involve management in resolution of issues 
 Active role in disseminating information, both technical and client related, to other team members to increase awareness 
 
 What you will get in return: 
 
 A genuinely unique opportunity to be part of an expanding global business and to grow with the business 
 Exposure to all aspects of the business 
 Working directly with an external client portfolio, maintaining client relationships 
 The ability to apply all technical knowledge to practical scenarios 
 Opportunity to manage a team and people management 
 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
