 2019 Screen Technician - Level 1 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Ongoing Shut Downs 
 
 
 Competitive Rates 
 
 
 Progressive & Supportive Safety Focused Company 
 
 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are seeking Trades Assistants with the reliable, respectful and "Can Do Attitude" to support one of our Leading Process Engineering Clients. 
 If successful employees will be tasked with using power and hand tools to assist in the removal and replacement of worn fixed plant screen panels in screen houses. 
 Shuts starting on the 4th February at Roy Hill, BHP and FMG mine sites. 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Working at heights (within 2 years) 
 
 
 Confined space (within 2 years) 
 
 
 Gas Testing (within 2 years highly desirable) 
 
 
 Trade Assistant Experience 
 
 
 BHP, Roy Hill and FMG Inducted (highly desirable) 
 
 
 Right to Work in Australia 
 
 
 Ability to pass a Medical 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels.
