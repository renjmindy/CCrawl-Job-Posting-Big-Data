 Temporary Field partner for a market survey 
 MarketingPRO -  HELSINKI  , FI 
 Job Description 
 MarketingPRO, a market intelligence company globally active, is looking for a professional freelance co-operation for survey projects in Helsinki area related to construction and handling industry (price detection from official dealers). 
 The project will imply a total commitment of approximately 8-12 full working days that candidate will organize within a time frame of 4-6 weeks. We foresee initial 5-full-working days start-up on field (mandatory) with the clear aim of tutoring by introducing the collaborators to our business philosophy and the survey activities. 
 People usually collaborating with us are people able to manage autonomously their time: professors, PhD, journalist, freelancers, sales-agent, students, etc. Here few details and conditions of the upcoming project: START-UP: end of june 2014; TIMING: 4-6 weeks; CAR: desirable; LANGUAGES: Finnish (fluent), English (advanced).
