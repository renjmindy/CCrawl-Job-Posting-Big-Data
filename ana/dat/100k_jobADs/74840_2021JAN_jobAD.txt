 Welder - Flux Core 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Day shift / Night shift positions available 
 
 
 Henderson workshop 
 
 
 Commence 11th Jan 2021 
 
 
 About the Role 
 Our client is currently seeking a workshop welder for ongoing work in their Henderson workshop. You must be competent in passing a 3G & 4G weld test prior to commencement. Experience on plate and heavy pipe is a must. 
 Working weeks are Monday to Saturday (half day Saturday) 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Australian Trade Certificate 
 
 
 Ability to pass 3G & 4G weld test 
 
 
 Previous workshop experience on plate and heavy pipe. 
 
 
 Must be able to work 6 days a week - Mon to Friday, half day Saturday (50 hour weeks) 
 
 
 Ability to pass Pre Employment Medical and Drug Alcohol Screen 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
