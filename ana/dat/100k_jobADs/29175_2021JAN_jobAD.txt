 Job Highlights 
 
 
 $47.68/hour Flat Rate 
 
 
 7/7 Roster 
 
 
 Ongoing Position! 
 
 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking skilled and experienced Dump Truck Operators to commence at a Mine near Dysart. The successful candidate will be on a 7/7 Roster (Straight Days, Straight Nights). This client has CAT 793Ã¢s and CAT 797 on site, the successful candidate would require to be passed out on a minimum of a CAT 789. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Operation of CAT 793 or CAT 797 
 
 
 Completion of Risk Assessments 
 
 
 Complete Pre-start checks on machinery 
 
 
 12 Hour Shifts. 
 
 
 Follow Company as well as Site Procedures & Policies to a safe standard. 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 RII/ Training Transcript - CAT 789, 793 or/and 797 
 
 
 Minimum of 6 months mine experience 
 
 
 Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 BMA Core or Standard 11 
 
 
 Right to work within Australia 
 
 
 About WorkPac
