 Job Seekers 
 We'll look after you... While you look after others 
 As a leading nursing agency to our nation’s premier health facilities, WorkPac Health and Social Care is constantly seeking ways to support registered nurses in their career as they support our communities. 
 To meet the increasing demand for quality healthcare delivery, we partner with hospitals, aged care, health and corrections facilities to provide qualified and experienced registered nurses. 
 Through our in-house purpose built recruitment system, registered nurses gain access to the top nursing jobs around the country. 
 As one of Australia’s fastest growing nursing agencies, we are seeking the best agency nurses in town.

 Shift flexibility that works around you 
 
 
 Excellent pay rates 
 
 
 Personal recruitment consultant dedicated to you 
 
 
 RIPEN work across Queensland 
 
 
 Ongoing training & development 
 
 
 Continued professional development 
 
 
 Great social environment
