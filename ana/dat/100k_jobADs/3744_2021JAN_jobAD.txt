 Orthopaedic Team Leader 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Orthopaedic Team Leader to work in an exceptional clinic based in the Bingley, West Yorkshire area. 
 You will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This clinic provides treatments such as knee cartilage surgery, knee arthroscopy, knee ligament surgery, cataract surgery and shoulder muscle repair 
 An ideal Orthopaedic Team Leader must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Excellent orthopaedic scrub experience
 
 
 
     Leadership experience
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
     Promote best practice and contribute towards quality improvements within the theatre department to ensure excellent and safe patient outcomes
 
 
 
     Experience managing loan equipment and stock management
 
 
 
 **The successful Orthopaedic Team Leader will receive an excellent salary from £36,000 to £40,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Competitive Salary plus enhancements where eligible
 
 
 
     Contributory pension scheme
 
 
 
     Access to genuine opportunities to grow, develop and specialise in your career
 
 
 
     25 days Annual Leave + Bank Holidays (pro rata)
 
 
 
     Private Healthcare and Life Assurance
 
 
 
     Free onsite parking
 
 
 
     Access to our employee Discount Programme
 
 
 
     Wellbeing centre and access to 24/7 employee assistance line for free advice
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN or ODP with a valid NMC/HCPC Pin
