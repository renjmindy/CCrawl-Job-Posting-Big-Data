 Gleeson Recruitment Group are delighted to be working with an exciting and fast growing national business as they seek to appoint a Head of Payroll in Warwick. This newly created job role is a pivotal hire for my client and will not only lead the businesses payroll function but become a key part of the senior leadership team. 
 This interesting and dynamic company has multiple sites across the UK and has experienced sustained growth year on year ever since. Despite the challenges brought on to everyone by Covid-19, this business is still trading well, projecting growth within 2020 and is a real success story in what has been a tough year for most. 
 The Head of Payroll will be a fast paced and varied role which will incorporate the day to day management of an expanding payroll team, but will also be tasked with the delivery of a number key, added value process improvement and systems projects. In addition to developing a well-run, high performing and efficient Payroll team, the successful applicant will be tasked with the following: 
 - Integration and development of monthly reporting and M.I 
- Ensuring the payroll function is fit for purpose to manage significant further growth 
 This is a unique opportunity to come available on the Warwickshire market and will be a role that requires a strong character who possesses a mix of hands-on management experience and a strategic and commercial outlook. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
