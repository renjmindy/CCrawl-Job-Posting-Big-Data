 Water Cart Operator 
 
 
 7/7 roster 
 
 
 Ongoing role 
 
 
 Accomodation and Meals provided. 
 
 
 About Our Client 
 Our is a major maintenance contractor and provider in the mining, oil and gas industries. The Company has a strong commitment to Health and Safety, which enables to deliver larger-scale multi-disciplinary projects involving roads and mine site projects. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking a skilled and experienced Water Cart Driver to commence at a project near Dysart. You will be working a 7/7 Roster with successful candidates starting as soon as possible. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Operate HR Water Cart 
 
 
 Operate trucks safe and competent 
 
 
 Assure workplace hazards are reported and identified 
 
 
 Meet production target requirements 
 
 
 Pre-start checks on machinery 
 
 
 Skills and Experience: 
 HR licence required 
 
 
 Previous experience in mining and road construction 
 
 
 Current Driver's Licence 
 
 
 Right to work within Australia 
 
 
 Current Driver's Licence and Right to work within Australia 
 
 
 Benefits of role: 
 
 
 7/7 roster 
 
 
 Ongoing role 
 
 
 Accomodation and Meals provided.
