 Are you an IT Procurement Manager? Are you immediately available and seeking a new interim contract? If so please read on. 
 Based in Birmingham this role will be a mix of home and office working. You will have responsibility for supporting procurement for a cyber security programme of work. You will identify all programme RFPs, reduce risk and deliver value for money with fit for purpose contracts. 
 In order to carry out this role, you will have the following skills and experience: 
 
 Experience of IT procurement at a global level 
 CIPS qualified 
 Ability to operate under pressure and meet tight deadlines 
 Ability to hit the ground running 
 
 This role is for approximately 3 months with a rate of circa £400 per day. If you meet the above criteria, please do not hesitate to apply. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
