 Job opportunity at Raw house ibiza! 
 06/09/2014  by  Ibiza Link , posted in  ibiza ,  ibiza 2014 ,  ibiza by night ,  job in ibiza ,  job opportunities ,  job opportunities ibiza ,  job opportunities in ibiza ,  job opportunity in Ibiza ,  jobs ibiza 
 Many friends are writing us to know if there are job opportunities in winter in Ibiza. 
 It's not easy, but Ibiza by night will update you if there will be interesting job offers. 
 And, in the group "Job center, Looking for work or workers" we found this opportunity.. we share for you. 
 If your dream is to remain also in winter in Ibiza.. good luck! 
 This is the text of the announcement: 
 " RAW HOUSE IBIZA 
 Our coming pop diner is looking promising, the table is filling up quickly. Raw House Ibiza is Looking for Service Help … 
 We would love to welcome one fresh and open team member with experience in hospitality and an interest in Raw Food. 
 ∞ The job involves catering service to our guests and assisting in the kitchen if needed - around 5 hours. 
 Are you are new Raw Team member? Please send a message to rawhouseibiza@gmail.com ". 
 This is the link to Raw house Ibiza fan page on Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/RawHouseIbiza?fref=photo 
 
 Good luck and don't forget for ear our disclaimer!
