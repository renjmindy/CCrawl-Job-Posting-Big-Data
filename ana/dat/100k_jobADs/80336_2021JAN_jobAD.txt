 Multi Ticketed Civil Labourer 
 Job description 
 Multi Ticketed Civil Labourer Required 
 
 
 Competitive Hourly Rate 
 
 
 Work with well known Civil Contractors 
 
 
 Immediate start in January 2021 
 
 
 About Our Client: 
 Our client is a one of Australia's largest infrastructure companies that completes numerous Sub-divisions and Industrial Parks in Metropolitan Melbourne and surrounds. 
 About the Role: 
 WorkPac are seeking multi ticketed civil labourers to complete Sub-divisional works across Metropolitan Melbourne. To be success in this role, the ideal candidate will possess a combination of the following: 
 Skills & Experience: 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 Working at heights ticket 
 
 
 Roller ticket 
 
 
 Electrical spotters ticket 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Demonstrated experience working on Sub-divisions 
 
 
 Duties & Responsibilities: 
 
 
 Pit and pipe installations 
 
 
 Electrical conduit installations 
 
 
 Road creation 
 
 
 Bulk earth works 
 
 
 Working with machines 
 
 
 General site cleaning 
 
 
 About WorkPac: 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
