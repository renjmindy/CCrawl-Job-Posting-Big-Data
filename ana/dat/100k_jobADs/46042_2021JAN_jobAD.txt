 Care Giver Jobs Malaysia 
 Posted on  December 9, 2010  by  caregiver 
 Malaysian employers are currently seeking to employ individuals who are qualified for  Care Giver Jobs Malaysia . Through a POEA job order that was approved last December 3, a POEA accredited recruitment agency located in Quezon City with an office branch in Tarlac City is tasked to begin accepting applications for the said care giver jobs Malaysia. For the said care giver jobs, 50 applicants will be selected for employment in care giver work abroad. 
 To apply for the said Care Giver Jobs Malaysia, proceed directly to the office of Prime Opportunities Inc. and submit your resume together with your application. For any questions regarding the said care giver jobs Malaysia, you can ask Prime Opportunities thru their landline number or email address provided below. 
 Prime Opportunities, Inc. 
Unit 3045 Royal Midway Plaza 
Tandang Sora Ave., Quezon City 
Tel No: 9524556 
Email Add: po_apply@yahoo.com 
Website: http://primeopportunities.net 
 BRANCH OFFICE 
530 MH Del Pilar cor. Panganiban Street 
Sto. Cristo, Tarlac City
