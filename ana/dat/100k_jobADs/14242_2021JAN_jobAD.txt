 Head of Clinical Services/Matron 
 An outstanding new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Head of Clinical Services/Matron to work in an exceptional clinic based in the Bingley, West Yorkshire area. 
 You will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This clinic provides treatments such as knee cartilage surgery, knee arthroscopy, knee ligament surgery, cataract surgery and shoulder muscle repair 
 An ideal Head of Clinical Services/Matron must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Experience in elective surgical environment
 
 
 
     Previous senior clinic leadership position
 
 
 
     Proven ability to successfully lead and motivate a clinical services team providing strong strategic direction and positive leadership
 
 
 
     Proven experience working at a senior management level within the health care industry
 
 
 
     Proven strategic planning, workforce analysis and policy development skills
 
 
 
     Demonstrated ability to lead Continuous Improvement processes in a Health Care facility and achieve sound outcomes
 
 
 
     Knowledge and understanding of contemporary clinical risk management strategies that are effective in promoting and ensuring high quality patient care
 
 
 
     Sound computer skills using the MS Office Suite and Web based reporting systems
 
 
 
     Aware of the relevant legislative requirements and governing body reporting responsibilities of hospital/ clinical services departments
 
 
 
 **The successful Head of Clinical Services/Matron will receive an excellent salary from £55,000 to £70,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week.
