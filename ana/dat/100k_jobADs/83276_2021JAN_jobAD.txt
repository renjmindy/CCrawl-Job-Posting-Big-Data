 Transactional Finance Manager 
 
 Location 
 
 Birmingham, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £35000 - £40000 per annum + 25 days holiday, flexible working 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Ryan Doolan 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 ryan.doolan@gleeson-rg.com

 Published:

 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-03-19 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson are currently recruiting exclusively for a Transactional Finance Manager for our client based in Central Birmingham. The role is paying up to £40k with benefits including flexible working, 25 days holiday and lovely offices with a restaurant and gym on site. 
 You will be looking after a team of 7 made up of Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable and Treasury. Key responsibilities include:- 
 
 Directly manage 7 members of staff and take ownership of their development 
 Manage and own all income and payment transactions 
 Ensure the correct accounting and control of back office policy systems into the financial ledgers 
 To maintain effective, auditable controls over transactions, minimising risk and ensuring that monies received and paid are reconcilable to the nominal ledger. 
 Manage the month end transaction process. 
 Build strong inter business unit relationships to ensure process issues are resolved on a timely basis and improve processes. 
 Monitor cash-flow and chaps payments and liaise where issues are identified. 
 
 Personal Attributes:- 
 
 Previous experience of managing a transactional finance team. 
 Excellent interpersonal skills. 
 Solid understanding of AP and AR. 
 Strong IT skills. 
 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
