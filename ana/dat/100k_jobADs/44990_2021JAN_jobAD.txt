 FUEL & OWNER OPERATOR SETTLEMENTS ADMINISTRATOR (12-15 Month Contract) 
 Midland Transport Limited has become a leader in the transportation industry by providing superior quality service to our customers. Our services include LTL, T/L, Courier Services, and Third Party Logistics/ Brokerage which provide the convenience of one-stop shipping. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Weekly processing of fuel invoices; 
 Fuel audits; 
 Management of fuel cards and transponders; 
 Period end reports; 
 Processing of Owner Operator Settlements; 
 Reconciliations. 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 Must possess strong analytical skills; 
 Post-secondary education considered an asset or two (2) years’ experience in the transportation industry; 
 Ability to work in a fast paced environment; 
 Bilingualism an asset; 
 Able to meet multiple strict deadlines; 
 Ability to work in a confidential environment; 
 Possess a high degree of accuracy and attention to detail; 
 Problem solving skills; 
 Functional working knowledge of Cognos, AS400 and Microsoft Office including: Word, Excel & Power Point. 
 
 To Apply for this Career Opportunity: 
Please apply online by December 11, 2020. 
 We appreciate your interest in our company however only those candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.
