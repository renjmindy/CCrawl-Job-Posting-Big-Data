 An exciting opportunity has arisen for an experienced HR Business Partner to join this organisation at a time of change. Salary up to £44k + Benefits. 
 As HR Business Partner, you will partner with Directors within your Clinical Client Group, supporting and driving the people agenda and driving the People aspects of Change Programmes. You will provide full generalist support, working closely with colleagues within Recruitment and Learning and Development. 
 As an experienced HR Business Partner, you must have experience and understanding of working with a medical/ clinical client group. The role will cover two sites within the North Midlands and require travel to both. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
