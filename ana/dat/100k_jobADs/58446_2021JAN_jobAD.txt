 Labor Unions 
 We have worked to develop strong, constructive relationships with the organizations that represent our employees, including the following labor unions:

 United Food and Commercial Workers Union (UFCW) Local 1000 for commissary and post office at Sheppard Air Force Base 
 Office and Professional Employees International Union (OPEIU) Local 4873, American Federation of Labor and Congress of Industrial Organizations (AFL–CIO) Industrial, Technical, and Professional Employees Union (ITPEU), food service at Dyess Air Force Base, and food service at Goodfellow Air Force Base
