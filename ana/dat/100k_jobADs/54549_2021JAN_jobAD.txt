 Senior Finance Systems Analyst 
 Senior Finance Systems Analyst 
 
 Location 
 
 Leeds, West Yorkshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £50000 - £55000 per annum 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Arshi Thind 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 ArshiThind@workwithglee.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH20010_1581680128 
 
 Published: 
 
 11 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-03-15 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Engage confidently with Finance stakeholders in multiple locations to identify the system and data structure changes required to produce the appropriate business outcomes from an accounting, reporting and FP&A perspective 
 Influence decision making by working in conjunction with:
 
 Group and Business Finance colleagues 
 Other Finance Systems Support colleagues 
 Developers 
 
 
 Systems support - help users in the business with support queries relating to the finance EPM system 
 Troubleshoot systems and data related issues on the EPM platform, and investigate any upstream/ downstream related issues 
 Proactively identify quality and user-experience issues and remediation actions required and passing them to the relevant Finance teams 
 Focus on continuous improvement. 
 Review Change Requests made through global change control system 
 Participate in projects, upgrades and new implementations of global tools and systems 
 
 Requirements  : 
 
 Thorough understanding of accounting principles and concepts and what these mean from a functional systems perspective:
 
 P&L, balance sheet, cashflow 
 Forecasting and planning 
 
 
 Super-user skills and experience in the use of Finance systems:
 
 SAP Finance ERP: GL, AP, AR, FA 
 Excel (advanced) 
 Forecast, Planning, Analytics and BI tools
