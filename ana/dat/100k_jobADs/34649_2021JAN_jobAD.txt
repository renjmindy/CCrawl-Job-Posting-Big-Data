 Boilermaker/Welder 
 Job description 
 Boilermaker / Welder 
 WorkPac is currently seeking experienced Boilermakers to commence work at the Sun Metals Zinc Refinery Upgrade Project in Townsville. 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 6-10 months work 
 
 
 Monday - Saturday 
 
 
 58 hours per week 
 
 
 $42.00 per hour flat rate 
 
 
 Daily $27.50 travel allowance 
 
 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 MIG, TIG and Flux Core 
 
 
 Cutting, Shaping and Assembling 
 
 
 Welding steel to construct or repair metal products 
 
 
 Pipe welding 
 
 
 Skills & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Confined space certificate 
 
 
 Working at Heights certificate 
 
 
 Construction White Card 
 
 
 Trade Certificate 
 
 
 Pipe welding experience preferred 
 
 
 About the Client 
 NWEC is an engineering construction company responsible for the upgrade of sections of the Refinery in building structural, mechanical & piping requirements on tanks and pipes. 
 Apply now by calling or hitting the apply button to your right! 
 Sam Watson - 07 4744 6906 
 About Workpac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leaving companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
