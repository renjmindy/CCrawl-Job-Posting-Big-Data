 Deputy Manager 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for dedicated Deputy Manager to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the Belfast, Northern Ireland area. 
 As the Deputy Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This is a purpose built, modern nursing home caring for people with dementia and physical disability 
 As the Deputy Manager your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     To line manage a team of staff and have knowledge, understanding and experience of relevant legislation ensuring they comply with the requirements of The regulatory quality improvement authorities care standards and other statutory regulators
 
 
 
     You will take pride in providing the highest standards of care to all residents and manage the Service to the highest standards, ensuring the people who use our services can be supported to live as independently and comfortably as possible
 
 
 
 **The successful Deputy Manager will receive an excellent salary of £22.50 per hour and the annual salary for this position is £45,045 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 38.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     A competitive salary
 
 
 
     Enhanced disclosure cost coverage
 
 
 
     Comprehensive induction and commitment to ongoing training
 
 
 
     28 days annual leave inclusive of bank holidays
 
 
 
     Online benefits and cashback rewards
 
 
 
     Cycle to work scheme
 
 
 
     SMART Childcare Vouchers
 
 
 
     SMART Pension options
 
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse either RGN, RMN or RNLD with a valid NMC Pin
