 Permanent

 2020-10-24 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group are working exclusively with a fast growing privately owned Business, which has gone through substantial investment in the search for a Financial Controller. You will be a key member of the leadership team, growing the finance function and board level reporting. 
 Role 
 
 Support senior leadership financial advice & strategy 
 Produce full management accounts on a monthly basis 
 Leadership and management of the finance team, including training, development and coaching to improve month end processes, including variance analysis. 
 Oversight of budgeting, cashflow forecasting and commercial analysis 
 Generate monthly based financial statements in an accurate and timely manner. 
 Work with the company department to maintain current budgets, forecasts of sales, expenditures, and cash flows as well as reporting on budget to actual results 
 Responsible for IT strategy and its development to support the growing business and implementation of systems and other systems upgrade and replacements. 
 Ownership of Year End and managing the audit process. 
 Ensure compliance with Vat, Paye, Nic and Corporation Tax reporting requirements. 
 
 Person 
 
 Fully Qualifed ACCA, CIMA or ACA 
 Dealt at board level 
 Preferably prior experience in an SME 
 
 On Offer 
 Up to £75,000 + Package
