 Scrub Nurse/ODP 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Scrub Nurse/ODP to work in an exceptional clinic based in the Bingley, West Yorkshire area. 
 You will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This clinic provides treatments such as knee cartilage surgery, knee arthroscopy, knee ligament surgery, cataract surgery and shoulder muscle repair 
 An ideal Scrub Nurse/ODP must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Your registration should not have any restrictions or conditions
 
 
 
     Experience in paediatrics desirable
 
 
 
     ALS/EPALS certificate desirable.
 
 
 
     Excellent written and verbal communication skills
 
 
 
     Flexibility and adaptability to meet the changing needs of the business
 
 
 
 **The successful Scrub Nurse/ODP will receive an excellent salary from £33,000  to £35,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Competitive Salary plus enhancements where eligible
 
 
 
     Contributory pension scheme
 
 
 
     Access to genuine opportunities to grow, develop and specialise in your career
 
 
 
     25 days Annual Leave + Bank Holidays (pro rata)
 
 
 
     Private Healthcare and Life Assurance
 
 
 
     Free onsite parking
 
 
 
     Access to our employee Discount Programme
 
 
 
     Wellbeing centre and access to 24/7 employee assistance line for free advice
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a valid NMC Pin or qualified as an ODP with an HCPC registration
