 Full-Time Mechanical Designer 
 Mastsystem -  Muovilaaksontie  , FI 
 Job Description 
 Cobham Mast Systems (Mastsystem Int'l Ltd) is an English Cobham Group of the company, which operates in Joensuu Heinavaara. We design, manufacture and sell composite telescopic mast systems. Our main customer is an international defense industry and defense forces. 
 With a staff of about 32 and a turnover of € 8,7M (2013). To strengthen mekaniikkasuunnittelutiimiamme, we are seeking served by a mechanical designer / project engineer and the telescopic mast systems design and product development. We expect you to: - experience in the mechanics of the design, as well as 2D / 3D design software, we use AutoCAD and Inventor - the capacity for independent, long-term and self-initiated work, as well as taking responsibility - enthusiastic and positive attitude as well as good communication and collaboration skills Education: - Mechanical Engineer / M.Sc. or equivalent 
 We look at the benefit of training: - experience in the strength calculation and simulations - electronics and electro-mechanics knowledge - knowledge of the different methods of preparation - experience in project management and international operating environments - readiness to act in the customer interface - experience in product development projects in the international business environment due to require a good knowledge of English and willingness to travel. 
 We offer you interesting and challenging tasks in a successful and prestigious company as well as a specialist in the work community. ,
