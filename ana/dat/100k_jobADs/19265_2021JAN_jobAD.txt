 Patient Administrative Associate - Covid-19 Assessment Centre - Temporary Part Time (201544) 
 Sunnybrook Health Sciences Centre 
 __Toronto, ON 
 Apply Now 
 We currently have a Temporary Part-Time opportunity for a Patient Administrative Associate in the Covid-19 Assessment Centre at our Bayview Campus. 
 Summary of Duties 
 The PAA is responsible for staffing, scheduling, payroll and the administrative support to the COVID-19 Assessment Centre. 
 Qualifications/Skills 
 One year of Community College 
One year of Administrative/Business experience required, in a health care setting preferred 
Advanced computer skills 
Demonstrated knowledge of ESP staff scheduling system 
Keyboarding at net 40 wpm or net 106 kspm 
Knowledge of software packages such as Word/Windows 
Patient and Family focused care philosophy required 
Ability to organize work effectively, and work at a fast pace in an unpredictable environment that requires the ability to manage multiple tasks 
Excellent problem-solving and communication skills 
Excellent telephone/customer service skills 
Demonstrated ability to work in a team environment 
 Qualified candidates are invited to submit their cover letter and resume (in one document) quoting 201544 to:
