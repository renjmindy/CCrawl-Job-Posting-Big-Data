 Consultant: 
 
 
 Senior Financial Accountant 
 Reporting to the Financial Controller of this acquisitive and innovative client based in Reading. 
 You will ensure that group and statutory reporting requirements are met in a timely and robust manner. Manage the implementation and reporting of newly acquired product. 
 Monthly Reporting Process  :  Plan, organise and manage own workload to ensure the companies monthly financial reporting processes are achieved in a timely and accurate manner, including but not limited to: 
 
 Managing implementation of newly acquired product, working with Financial Controller (FC) to integrate new markets and processes into reporting. 
 Preparation of stock valuation, and necessary stock provisions, ensuring early identification of stock risks 
 Monitoring of goods received not invoiced account ensuring timely reconciliation 
 Preparation of Accruals and Prepayments 
 Maintenance of fixed asset and intangible asset register 
 Reconciliation of Intercompany balances 
 Processing of timesheets for required departments 
 Participate strongly in financial reviews, challenging and providing commentary on significant transactions and variances 
 Preparation of group submissions, including management notes, annotation and analysis 
 Preparation of robust balance sheet reconciliations ensuring that all carrying balances are adequately supported 
 Preparation of Actual and forecast cash flow submissions 
 Preparation of Functional Reporting Commentary for Actuals and submission to Head Office 
 Updating of BI tool to enable review of Actuals 
 
 Statutory Returns 
 Assist the finance team in ensuring statutory reporting obligations are correctly recorded, reconciled, authorised and submitted in a timely manner: 
 
 Preparation of VAT returns, EC Sales list and intrastat returns 
 Preparation of National Statistical Office returns 
 Assist in the preparation of quarterly and annual PPRS returns 
 Assist in the preparation of Corporation Tax returns 
 Assist in the preparation P11d and PSA returns 
 Assist in the preparation of Statutory Accounts Business Partnering 
 Working closely with budget holders and Commercial Finance to ensure accuracy of financial information and fair and robust forecasts and budgets for specific area of P&L: 
 Conducting regular meetings with cost centre owners, to review financial transactions, to ensure understanding and accountability for figures 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 Experience of working consistently to tight deadlines, whilst maintaining high level of accuracy 
 Challenging and analytical in nature, confident to challenge until underlying reasons are identified 
 Ability to work as a team and as an individual 
 Able to provide meaningful analysis of data using Excel, able to utilise pivot tables and macros where appropriate 
 Qualified accountant (ACCA, ACA, CIMA etc.)
