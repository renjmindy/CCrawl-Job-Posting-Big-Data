 Control System Engineer 
 Job description 
 
 
 Join a global leader in the mining sector 
 
 
 FIFO 8/6 roster to Brockman 4 
 
 
 February 2021 commencement 
 
 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are seeking a Control Systems Engineer to support the site asset owner teams in maintaining and improving production assets at RTIO Brockman 4. This is a great opportunity to gain site exposure and work across a multitude of electrical & control system assets. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Develop scope and provide technical support and oversight for complex instrumentation, control system and electrical assets. 
 
 
 Execute small to medium control system changes. 
 
 
 Escalation point for control system and electrical asset fault diagnosis and recovery. 
 
 
 Monitor and maintain advanced control system schemes. 
 
 
 Execute machine protection and safety related control system audits. 
 
 
 Provide operational site support for control and electrical systems issues and improvement. 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Tertiary qualification in Electrical & Electronics Engineering. 
 
 
 Mining experience required, ideally within iron ore processing. 
 
 
 Experience working with Unity Pro for Modicon (Schneider PLCs). 
 
 
 Good knowledge of electrical assets such as switchgear, drives, VSDs & protection relays. 
 
 
 Technical document development experience including engineering drawings, scopes of works, functional specifications, procedures and commissioning plans. 
 
 
 WA C Class DriverÃ¢s license.
