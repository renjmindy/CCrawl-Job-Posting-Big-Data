 A fantastic Head of CRM role working for a Midlands based consumer business. 
 My client, a market leader in their field, is looking to recruit a Head of CRM to own the customer marketing strategy across all touchpoints. 
 The Head of CRM role will encompass: 
 
 Overseeing the overall customer marketing strategy, building a view of the CLV 
 Develop an overall CRM strategy across all touch-points 
 Utilise data to better understand the 'customer' and develop campaigns accordingly 
 
 The ideal candidate for this senior CRM role will have a strong CRM background, ideally in a digitally first environment. You have operated at a senior level, working strategically, however you will also be capable of getting involved in campaigns operationally. You will have demonstrable track record of building CRM strategy - delivering growth and sustainable results. You will be commercial but also customer centric. 
 Highly competitive package.
