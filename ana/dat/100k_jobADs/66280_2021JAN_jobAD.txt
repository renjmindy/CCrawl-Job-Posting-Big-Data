 Live-in Caregiver Jobs 
 Posted on  March 13, 2013  by  caregiver 
 Kan-Ya International Services Corp., a duly licensed manpower agency from Malate in Manila, is now accepting qualified applicants for  Live-in Caregiver Jobs . These live-in caregiver jobs is for an employer in Singapore who needs 30 caregivers with the following qualifications: 
 
 Female 
 23 to 35 years old 
 College level 
 Graduate of Caregiving Course 
 With 2 years experience as a caregiver 
 Physically fit 
 Good communication skills 
 
 If you are qualified to apply for the said Live-in Caregiver Jobs, kindly send photocopies of the requirements listed below to aiza.kanya@gmail.com for evaluation. 
 
 Resume with job description 
 Certificate of Employment 
 College Diploma/TOR 
 Training Certificates (TESDA, etc.) 
 Valid Passport 
 Whole body picture (in scrub suit) 
 
 For more details or inquiries about the said live-in caregiver jobs, you can call the telephone numbers specified below and look for Miss Aiza.
