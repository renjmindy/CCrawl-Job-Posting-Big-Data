 Are you an experience HR Advisor? Do you have strong HR generalist experience? If so my client, a UK wide blue collar organisation, is looking for a HR Advisor to join them at their Ipswich site for a period of 12 months. 
 Working closely with the General Manager, you will provide advice and guidance to the site, including reviewing HR processes, performance, capability, absence, recruitment and the coaching and developing of Managers to improve management capability. 
 As an experienced Interim HR Advisor, you will have worked autonomously within a Blue-collar environment. You will have hands-on experience of providing full generalist support to site.
