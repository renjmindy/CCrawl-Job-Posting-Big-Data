 Property Project Coordinator / Junior Project Manager 
 Birmingham 
 Initial 6 month contract but likely to extend. 
 £250 - £300 per day - start ASAP 
 My client is a FTSE 250 retail business based in North Birmingham They are going through a major business wide transformation programme and within the change programme includes the reconfiguration, refurbishment and rationalisation of their property portfolio. The estate is vast with close to 1000 properties across the whole country hence this role has been created to provide additional support to the existing property transformation team. 
 Day to day you will be developing project plans and proposals in line with requirements; liaising with senior managers to drive property activities and projects; tracking, managing and updating project and programme progress though various operational reports; tracking the project finances, updating the property RAID log and managing the property database records. 
 In order to succeed in the role the successful candidate will have gained experience of large scale property project management; whether it be refurbishments, relocations, acquisitions or disposals. You will have a broad understanding of project planning and management and must be strong on excel and generally good with data. Another key to success will be your ability to communicate effectively along with excellent organisational skills. 
 There is a good mix of project management and coordination and the role will be very hands on and involved in the development of projects whilst maintaining strong governance and administration of the property programme. 
 Please don't hesitate to apply to apply for the role with your CV if you the relevant skill set as detailed above. All relevant applications will be contacted within 72 hours from receipt of their applications so if you do not hear back please assume your skill set is not quite right. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
