 SaleSource is looking for an ambitious and self-starting Digital Marketing Specialist to join our fast growing team. Having launched our new commercial and being in the ecommerce space during the pandemic, we have seen incredible growth. 
 About The Company 
 SaleSource is a startup profitable within the first 2 years of inception, established in 2018 with over 50,000 lifetime subscribers. We are transforming the ecommerce space by providing detailed analytics to people who want to sell online all the while fostering financial independence and realistic entrepreneurship opportunities online. 
 SaleSource is present in multiple domains, including a Shopify App, Web App, Chrome Extension and are featured on several key publications, including Mediums The Startup and Forbes. 
 To find out more, please visit our website: https://salesource.io 
 To get a quick overview of our product and website, please watch our commercial: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gJ_SRjl6nGA 
 About You 
 As a Digital Marketing Specialist, your responsibilies will be: 
 
 Manage Social Media, including Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, TikTok and YouTube 
 Facilitate user growth by increasing retention and brand awareness 
 Understand and prepare reports using Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Facebook Pixel 
 Drive creative marketing campaigns on Google Ads, Facebook Ads - including remarketing 
 Design UI/UX experiences and funnels for new customers using Figma 
 Provide detailed insights into customer journey, drop off points, and landing page optimization using HotJar and LuckyOrange 
 Coming up with creative ways to market SaleSource 
 Implement basic HTML and JavaScript tracking and scripts (all taught to you by our experts) 
 Create email marketing flows in SendinBlue / SendGrid 
 
 Preferred Experience 
 
 Minimum 3-5 Years Marketing experience, preferably in the ecommerce / web-app space 
 Knowledge of Shopify & Dropshipping - having run or worked on a Shopify store 
 Experience in content marketing 
 Experience in marketing for user retention 
 Email marketing and copywriting experience - particularly building email workflows 
 Background in subscription-model based growth 
 
 Key Benefits of working at   SaleSource 
 
 Beautiful, expansive workspace in the heart of central London 
 Large marketing & self development budget 
 Front End development training by leading experts to enhance your digital marketing skills 
 Provided Specialist Equipment to get the Job Done (iMac Pro, MacBook Pro, RED Cameras etc) 
 Friendly, sociable coworkers who love working in a driven startup environment 
 Video game, anime & movie nights 
 Casual Dress 
 Work From Home & Flexible Working Hours 
 Weekly free lunches 
 Subsidised transport scheme
