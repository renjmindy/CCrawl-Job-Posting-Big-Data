 Deputy Nurse Manager 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for a dedicated Deputy Nurse Manager to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the North Kensington, London area. 
 As the Deputy Nurse Manager you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 The team pride themselves on creating a welcoming and homely family environment and do everything they can to make the transition to service as smooth as possible for residents and those closest to them 
 An ideal Deputy Nurse Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Previous deputy manager experience
 
 
 
     Work together as a team to enrich residents' lives
 
 
 
     Give the highest quality of personalised care to residents in line with their individual care plans
 
 
 
     Supportive in every way to residents, their families and your colleagues
 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
     Previous experience in a residential and/or nursing setting
 
 
 
 **The successful Deputy Nurse Manager will receive an excellent salary of £50,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 40 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     25 days annual leave (rising to a maximum of 30 days) plus bank holidays
 
 
 
     Life assurance
 
 
 
     CQC performance bonus
 
 
 
     Excellent training and development opportunities
 
 
 
     Loyalty Award available
 
 
 
     Refer a friend scheme with payment of up to £750 available
 
 
 
     Contributory pension
 
 
 
     Access to a free 24/7 support service providing support including legal, financial, emotional and medical advice
 
 
 
     Season ticket loan available (Oystercard)
 
 
 
     Discounts on shopping, holidays, cinema, dining, days out and much more via 'My Rewards'
 
 
 
     Criminal Records Checks are funded by company
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a valid NMC Pin
