 Have you been waiting for the opportunity to "step up" to  Head of Property ? Are you a technically outstanding Property professional with the natural ability to lead a team? 
 The Opportunity 
We have a rare and exciting opportunity for a high calibre property professional to take up the position of Head of Property responsible for a team covering sites running the length of the M1 (Sheffield down to St Albans) corridor and East of the UK for a leading organisation in the hospitality sector. Within this area you will lead a team of 4 Property Managers looking after all sites within this region. 
 The Ideal Candidate 
This role will suit a technically strong Head of Property who is a natural leader and team builder. You will have experience in either retail, hospitality or leisure and experience of both maintenance and running major CAPEX programmes across a large multi-site estate. 
As Head of Property you will manage, mentor and develop your team and are likely to be degree qualified in Building Surveying or another relevant discipline. Our client is willing to consider both those currently operating at this level and those looking to "step up" with the capacity to do so. 
 What's on offer? 
On offer to the successful Head of Property is the opportunity to progress within a market leading organisation. You will join a successful business reporting into an exceptional leader in the property sector. You will receive a salary of up to £70,000 plus car allowance (£8,000) or company car with a fantastic pension and bonus scheme.
