 Job Description 
 Are you a qualified Occupational Therapist? 
 Do you have a minimum of 2 years’ experience? 
 Do you dream of living and working in New Zealand and enjoying a great work-life balance? 
 Extensive opportunities exist in public health and private practice throughout New Zealand and we are welcoming overseas applications now. 
 You will bring your holistic skills to work with patients from diverse social and cultural backgrounds and work collaboratively alongside multi-disciplinary teams and take a patient centred and goal focused approach. 
 With access to world-class facilities and ongoing professional development opportunities, it’s a great time to advance your career in NZ. 
 To apply, you will need: 
 
 Relevant tertiary qualification (BHSC OT or equivalent) 
 All applicants must be registered OR eligible for registration with the Occupational Therapy Board of New Zealand (OTBNZ)* 
 Minimum of 1 years’ post-graduate clinical experience 
 Fluency and comprehension in English to be able to communicate effectively (written and verbal) with patients, colleagues, and other health professionals 
 
 *Guidance with your NZ Registration is available from our team. 
 **APPLY NOW AND START YOUR JOURNEY TO NZ! 
** 
**How to apply: 
 **Please click on the apply button and complete the short application form. To speed up your application, please upload a copy of your CV. 
 **_Working In NZ Health are international healthcare recruitment and immigration specialists. We are partnered with a large healthcare network throughout New Zealand. As the preferred agency for overseas applicants, we guarantee your application will be considered and prioritised.
