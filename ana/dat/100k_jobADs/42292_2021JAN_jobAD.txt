 Senior Theatre Practitioner - Plastics and Breast 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a Senior Theatre Practitioner - Plastics and Breast to work in an exceptional hospital based in the Chelmsford, Essex area. 
 You will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This hospital provides a wide range of services including Surgery, Medicine, Oncology, Plastic and Cosmetic surgery, and Paediatrics. They are continually introducing new technology to ensure they provide the best experience for patients and support for their hard working teams and have just opened 2 additional purpose built theatres 
 An ideal Senior Theatre Practitioner must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     A minimum of  2 years, post registration experience as a Scrub Practitioner including Plastics and Breast surgery
 
 
 
     Experience in leading a team is desirable
 
 
 
     Surgical First Assistant certificate is desirable
 
 
 
     ALS/EPALS certificate desirable
 
 
 
 **The successful Senior Theatre Practitioner will receive an excellent salary up to £40,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 ** 
 
 
 
 *£1,500 After Probtion 
 
 
 
 
 
 
     25 days annual leave plus the option to buy more
 
 
 
     Contributory Pension Scheme
 
 
 
     Access to genuine opportunities to grow, develop and specialise in your career
 
 
 
     Private Healthcare
 
 
 
     Life Assurance
 
 
 
     Free parking and subsidised staff restaurant
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN or an SFA with a valid NMC or HCPC pin
