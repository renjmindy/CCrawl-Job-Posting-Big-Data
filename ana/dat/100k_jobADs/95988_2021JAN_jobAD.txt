PRODUCTION INTERN(S): 
**Are you passionate about film and looking to gain some experience within the film production world? We at Greenlight Pictures have always believed in growing local talent and providing the best mentor experience that we can. Being a small company, you will get the chance to experience all aspects of film and television production from pre-production to hands on working experience on film sets. You will even be exposed to post-production and administrative work. This has been helpful in guiding some of our past interns in discovering where their true passion and interests lie. However, if you are already certain of your path, feel free to speak to us and we will do our best to assist you in achieving your goals. 
 Requirements: 
 
 Background in film, mass communications, broadcasting or any related courses 
 Motivated team player who is able to multi-task and problem solve 
 Strong interest in learning more about films and the film industry 
 Strong communication skills 
 Some knowledge of videography and editing would be preferred 
 Must be able to drive and have own transport 
 
 If any one of these positions appeal to you, please email us your CV at  film@greenlightpictures.co .
