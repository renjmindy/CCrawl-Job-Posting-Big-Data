 Consultant: 
 
 
 An organisation that thrives for excellence and customer satisfaction, are looking for an Analytics Manager to take responsibility across their extensive data sets. 
 Not only will you develop a small team of Analysts, but you will also take a hands on approach to improving their data quality and provide statistical reports and analysis. 
 
 What are we looking for? 
 
 Advanced experience in Power BI/Advanced DAX/Power Apps 
 Track record of analysing and reporting on large complex data sets 
 Improve analysis policies and processes through findings 
 Experience in coding - Python/R 
 Ability to support projects, producing KPI reports 
 Commercially savvy, with the ability to translate to senior stakeholders 
 An interest in Data Science with mining experience and a proactive problem solving ability 
 Mentored or managed a small team 
 
 What's in it for you? 
 
 Competitive basic salary + bens (Private medical care, gym flex) 
 Be a part of a collaborative culture, whilst putting customers first 
 Lead from an analytics perspective, contributing ideas and solutions 
 Flexibility
