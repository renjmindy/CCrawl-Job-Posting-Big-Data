 Kuwait Jobs in Caregiver 
 Posted on  November 12, 2010  by  caregiver 
 Employers in Kuwait are currently looking to employ Filipino caregivers who are qualified for  Kuwait Jobs in Caregiver . This Kuwait jobs in caregiver was given the approval by the POEA last November 5 for a POEA accredited recruitment agency operating from Malate, Manila. For the said Kuwait jobs in caregiver, 50 applicants will be selected for employment in caregiver work abroad. 
 To be included in the list of possible candidates for placement in the said Kuwait Jobs in Caregiver, all interested applicants must personally submit their resume and application directly to the office of Spring Resources Management & Promotion Inc. For any inquiries regarding the said Kuwait jobs in caregiver, you may dial the agency’s landline numbers provided below. 
 **Spring Resources Management & Promotion Inc. ** 
Unit 205 Marbella II Building 
2071 Roxas Boulevard, Malate 
Manila 
Tel Nos: 5259148 / 5213484 / 5213492 
Email Add: springresources@att.net
