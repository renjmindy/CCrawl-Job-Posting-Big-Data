 Are you looking for your first management role in Accounts Payable? Or are you an experienced Accounts Payable team leader looking for your next step? 
 Ideally we are looking for an inspirational, hands on Accounts Payable team leader for our client based in Dudley. This role will offer great opportunities and work life balance for the right candidate. 
 Job Purpose 
 
 Manage the Accounts Payable Team 
 Diligently process payment runs 
 Assist in the preparation of the Cash-flow summary 
 
 Aims and Objectives 
 
 Leading the Payment team, ensuring efficient and timely processing of payment runs and maintaining a positive relationship with the vendors. 
 To ensure compliance with appropriate HMRC regulations, particularly in respect of VAT 
 
 Key responsibilities 
 
 Prioritise among the Payments team, with regular team meetings and 121s 
 Contact point for supplier payment queries 
 Liaise with Treasury regarding payment allocation & Direct Debits 
 Responsible for the processing of payments through BACS 
 Control and process manual payments in accordance with Internal policy 
 
 This role is a permanent position & salary £27,000 - £30,000 dependant on experience.
