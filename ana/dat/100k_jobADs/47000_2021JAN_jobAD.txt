 Remote FULL STACK developer to work with Hong Kong agency needed! REMOTEPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 We are a lean, expert, independent digital agency based in Wong Chuk Hang, Hong Kong. We create beautiful and engaging websites and digital experiences and execute highly targeted performance-driven marketing campaigns. 
 Who we're looking for: 
 - Looking for a strong full-stack web developer, with frontend coverage (LAMP) 
 - Front-end: We're coding in Vue.js / ReactJS, HTML / CSS (Bootstrap), and JavaScript (React Native experience in addition would be a big plus) 
 - We're generally designing in Sketch, sometimes Photoshop, so the focus is on front end templating from these designs 
 - Back-end: PHP (Mainly CraftCMS) 
 - Looking for someone who loves tackling hardcore back-end issues, such as site structure design, API development, and Content Management System integration with frontend. 
 - LAMP stack experience, able to do non-complex deployments of websites + troubleshoot server issues (on Digital Ocean). 
 You'll get to: 
 - Work as part of a lean, talented, agency team that covers strategy, UX / UI design, technical development, and marketing activation for websites, e-commerce, and applications 
 - We have a broad range of client projects across multi-national brands, SMEs, and funded startups 
 - Have exposure to broader Butter business services, eg analytics consultancy, conversion optimisation, marketing activation, etc - Overtime is extremely rare, due to strong project/client management 
 How to Apply 
 Head over to  https://www.butter.com.hk/jobs  to find out more about us and apply directly!
