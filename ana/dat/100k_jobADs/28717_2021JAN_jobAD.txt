 High frequency trading firm are looking for a software engineer to join their team in Amsterdam. You'll be working on some of the fastest systems and most advanced technology out there so they're only looking for the best IT professionals to develop, optimise and support their tools. You'll be working in a fast, but exciting atmosphere where you'll find that expertise, innovation and fun go hand in hand. 
 As part of the trading technology team you'll be responsible for applications required to run the business. Day-to-day activities range from developing ultra-low latency exchange protocol decoders and encoders, to helping the monitoring and analysing effort by creating and developing UIs, to helping the traders with more control and information. You'll frequently sit on the trading floor working alongside the traders so you'll receive immediate feedback on how successful your work is. 
 Requirements 
 
 An excellent Computer Science degree from a top tier university 
 Professional expertise in one or more OOP languages 
 An entrepreneurial attitude, with a strong interest in the financial industry 
 A team player and exceptional communicator who thrives on independence and enjoys creative freedom 
 The ambition to grow through experience, training and on-the-job development. 
 
 Benefits 
 
 Great compensation package 
 Profit-sharing structure 
 Commuter costs paid for 
 Friday afternoon drinks 
 Sports and leisure activities 
 Relocation assistance
