 Commercial Financial Analyst 
 
 Location 
 
 Reading, Berkshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £45000.00 - £55000.00 per annum + Bonus, Pension, Flexi bens 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Rob Anderson 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 robanderson@workwithglee.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH19499_1579687357 
 
 Published: 
 
 12 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-02-21 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Reporting to the Head of Commercial Finance with the provision of analytical support to drive profitability improvement for this international pharmaceutical client based with easy access to the M4. 
 This is an opportunity to develop your skills within a fast paced environment. You will be rewarded with a competitive salary, flexible benefits and the opportunity to develop your experience in an exciting and growing sector. 
 Accountability 
 Support the Commercial Finance Dept in delivering the annual budget, current year financial re forecasts and monthly forecast flash reporting 
 Responsible for managing budget and forecast data upload process ensuring strong understanding of data flows and impacts on Long Term Solution management reporting tools 
 Generate monthly Management Accounts and provide 1st class summary on trends & performance vs budget/forecast 
 End to end sales reporting collaborating with stakeholders in operational and commercial teams 
 Provide support to wider commercial team to produce weekly / monthly quarterly financial reports to budget holders 
 Support the Financial Accounting team on budget and forecast insight 
 Key responsibilities: 
 
 Business Partnering 
 Commercial Analysis 
 Performance review 
 Data analysis 
 
 Acting as a key business partner, you will be exposed to all levels of the business providing in-depth data analysis 
 You will possess: 
 Educated to degree level and possessing a full finance qualification (ACCA, ACA, CIMA) 
 Strong numerical analyst skills gained from a commercial environment with high levels of accuracy and attention to detail 
 Excellent organisational skills, able to balance competing priorities in order to meet expectation 
 Intermediate to advanced Excel - Power BI tool (Tableau) preferable 
 Motivated, productive individual driven by results, deadlines and with an enthusiastic and can-do attitude 
 Big 4 trained preferred 
 Good written and verbal communication skills 
 Experience in a pharma / medical / FMCG sector preferred but not essential 
 Team player, who will work for the success and positive team spirit of the whole Finance team 
 Superior communication skills
