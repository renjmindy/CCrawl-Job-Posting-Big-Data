 GOLDING JOBS 
 ​  WorkPac is proud to partner with Golding to deliver exciting career opportunities.  Here we provide you with some valuable information about whatit is like working as part of WorkPac's Golding team and our current vacancies. 
 COMPANY PROFILE 
 Golding has 70 years' experience in delivering complex construction, mining, civil and marine projects which meet our clients' expectations on time and to budget. Today Golding is able to deliver projects for our clients in a broad array of contracting forms including joint ventures, alliances, ECI's and partnerships as well as through more traditional delivery mechanisms such as project management, design and construct, lump sum, schedule of rate and hourly hire arrangements. The Golding business includes: mining services; civil infrastructure; and industrial infrastructure. 
 MINING SERVICES 
 Golding has been a key player in the Queensland Mining Industry for many decades in both coal and metalliferous sectors. We offer a comprehensive, expert service from construction of mine-site infrastructure and removal of overburden and topsoil to open cut mining on both short and long-term contracts. Golding offers specialist mine site rehabilitation works including environmental dam construction and reclamation earthworks. Golding has developed specific IT systems to support mining operations by enabling us to plan mining activities, optimise circuit performance, and make management decisions during a shift to optimise resource utilisation as well as providing comprehensive client reporting. 
 CIVIL INFRASTRUCTURE 
 Golding has undertaken major infrastructure works throughout Queensland for more than 39 years. Our expertise has been employed to build a diverse range of projects from power stations, processing plants, wharves and jetties to industrial subdivisions and prestige developments such as resorts and championship golf courses. Our civil services include: 
 
 
 site development 
 
 
 major structural concrete 
 
 
 all site services 
 
 
 wharf and jetty construction 
 
 
 industrial and residential subdivision 
 
 
 road and rail infrastructure 
 
 
 industrial buildings 
 
 
 INDUSTRIAL INFRASTRUCTURE 
 Golding has the capacity and experience to provide prompt turnaround, care and use of appropriate technologies to ensure effective maintenance of industrial plant. Our services include: 
 
 
 specialist wharf repairs 
 
 
 concrete remediation 
 
 
 kiln refractory lining works 
 
 
 road repairs 
 
 
 supply of industrial maintenance personnel and associated plant 
 
 
 cathodic protectio
