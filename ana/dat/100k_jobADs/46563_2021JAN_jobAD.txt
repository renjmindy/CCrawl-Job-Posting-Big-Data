 Are you an experienced Manufacturing Engineer that would like to work with some of the most advanced manufacturing technology around? Are you looking for a motorsport environment that puts its employees first? If so, this opportunity could be ideal for you. 
 This organisation design and manufacture for the motorsport industry. They have recently also gained AS9100 accreditation and will be looking to progress into the aerospace industry. 
 As a Manufacturing Engineer you will produce CNC Programs and support with any technical issues within the machine shop. 
 The ideal candidate will have previous experience of 5 axis machining and programming Hypermill / Open Mind. You will ideally have previous experience of cylinder heads, engine sumps and cylinder blocks. 
 In order to carry out this role successfully, you will need the following skills and experience: 
 
 Hypermill / Open Mind 
 Castings 
 5 axis machining 
 Programming machinery 
 Strong understanding of engines 
 Continuous Improvement 
 3D CAD - Catia V5 
 Designing fixtures and bespoke tooling 
 Excellent problem solving and analytical skills 
 
 Please note this is a contract role with an immediate start based in Milton Keynes . The duration is likely to be a minimum of 6 months. Hourly rate is negotiable depending on experience.
