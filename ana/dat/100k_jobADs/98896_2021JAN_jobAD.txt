 Are you an experienced Senior HR Business Partner, who is looking to work for an output focused organisation, who can offer flexibility and home-working? Based from the Bristol area, with travel to a couple of sites. Salary £55-58k. 
 As Senior HR Business Partner, you will work closely with the Senior Leadership Team, understanding the business, and where HR can support around commercial decisions. You will coach and develop the Managers, to help improve their capability, and the capability of their teams. You will look at the organisation structure, and work with Managers around business transformation. 
 CIPD Level 5 minimum, or equivalent you will be used to challenging and influencing stakeholders. Coaching and developing Management capability will be second nature to you. You will have supported business transformations/ restructures etc from a People perspective. 
 The role is home-based with travel. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
