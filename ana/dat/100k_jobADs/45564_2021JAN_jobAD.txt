 Full-Time Shift Foreman Kitchen 
 Restaurant Vespa -  HELSINKI  , FI 
 Job Description 
 Restaurant Vespa is like a little piece of Italy in the middle of Helsinki. Our kitchen chefs prepare mouth-watering Italian cuisine and the menu is again delicious. A varied wine list is second to none - vini italiani, certo! Restaurant Vespa KITCHEN is looking for Shift Foreman. 
 Shift Foreman you have managerial experience in a restaurant kitchen, stress tolerance and flexibility, as well as solid experience as the restaurant industry. You will lead the work of his own example, you guide and control the cooks per shift and you have a burning desire to get high-quality results, both financially and with regard to customer satisfaction. 
 Language level is at least fluent Finnish and English  , other language skills would be an advantage. Work is an absolute prerequisite for success in managing for results, robustness, and ability to create a motivated work environment atmosphere. We offer you an interesting and varied work in a happy and comfortable working environment, as well as financially sound great advantages of the employer. You also have the opportunity to participate in Restel Business School managerial training. 
 The action is full-time and pay as agreed. Work begins on a suitable candidate is found.
