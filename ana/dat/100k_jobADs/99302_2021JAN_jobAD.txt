 Concrete Finisher 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Immediate start 
 
 
 Competitive pay rate 
 
 
 On-going work 
 
 
 About the Role 
 Our client is a leading pre-cast panel manufacturer based on the Gold Coast. They require an experienced Concrete Finisher to commence in a position based in Yatala. The successful candidate with be responsible for the below duties, working in a yard-based environment. You must be able to use a Trowling Machine. 
 Ongoing work into the new year for the right candidate with an immediate start. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Pouring, spreading and levelling concrete using screeds and templates 
 
 
 Tamping, smoothing, shaping and sealing concrete 
 
 
 Steel Fixing concrete placement 
 
 
 Using a Trowling Machine 
 
 
 Finishing of precast concrete panels 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Strong concreting background with finishing experience 
 
 
 Concreting and finishing experience ideally with precast 
 
 
 Exposure to working in and around a yard environment - work safely around overhead cranes etc 
 
 
 High Level of Attention To Detail and presentable 
 
 
 Minimum White / Blue Card
