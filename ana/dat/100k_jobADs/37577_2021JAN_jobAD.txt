 Full-Time Production Supervisor 
 HRV-palvelut/Porin area of ​​Employment and Economic Development Office -  Satakunta  , Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 FB Ketjutekniikka is Northern Europe's leading manufacturer of industrial conveyor chains. Our company is focused on the sale of their sister companies in Sweden, Norway, Germany, Austria and England. We are part of the Swedish listed company Addtech Group, which employs about 1,700 people. Koylion factory employs 75 people. More than half of our sales are for export. 
 We are now looking for confirmation of our organization Koylio production supervisor. Your mission is to control the production team to design and ensure the daily running of the production. You are responsible for the smooth running of the production process, as well as the steady utilization of machines. It is your duty to ensure adequate quality and delivery to the customer. Under the auspices of a skilled production staff and closest to the superior of the production manager. We look forward to technical training, good interpersonal and leadership skills, organizational skills, as well as shift work readiness. We appreciate your understanding of ERP systems, and  proficiency in English . 
 We offer permanent employment group of financially sound, responsible and challenging task, professional support, and a good working atmosphere.
