 Consultant: 
 
 
 One of Gleeson's long standing clients are looking or a Financial Accountant to join their team on a 12 month FTC basis. This is an opportunity that will offer up to £42k per annum, depending on experience. 
 This role is a key role and will be an instrumental part of insuring the continued success of the finance function. 
 Main responsibilities will include: 
 
 Overseeing all day to day finance operations 
 Balance reconciliations 
 Resolving queries and other outstanding issues for month & year end requirements 
 Preparation of financial accounts 
 VAT 
 Overseeing the ledgers 
 Managing the P&L 
 Provision of information for budgeting & forecasting purposes 
 Supporting the Financial Controller with various tasks for reporting purpose 
 
 The candidate: 
 
 Qualified Accountant (ACA, ACCA or CiMA) or equivalent 
 Ability to produce high quality work within agreed deadlines 
 
 If this role sounds of interest to you please wither click apply or call Emily Mitchell at Gleeson Recruitment Group for more details
