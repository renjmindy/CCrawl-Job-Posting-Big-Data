 Stockless Inventory Attendant (CUPE), (1) Part Time - Markham Site 
 Markham Stouffville Hospital 
 __Markham, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Who we are: 
Markham Stouffville Hospital is a leading community hospital with two sites – Markham and Uxbridge sites and our new Reactivation Care Centre that has a reputation for excellence in progressive community care. The hospital is uniquely positioned with cutting edge technology, expansive new facilities and a supportive and welcoming community. With a focus on patient experience, safe, high quality care and innovation, our dedicated team of physicians, staff and volunteers provides care to over 300,000 patients a year. The hospital has capacity to grow a number of programs and services as we evolve to meet the needs of our growing community. We are dedicated to providing access to the right care, at the right time, in the right place by the right people and at the right cost. Are you ready to join us? 
 Who you are: 
You are an innovative and patient centered individual who provides excellence within their profession 
 You are a team player with excellent communication, critical thinking and customer service skills 
 You have vision, flexibility, transparency, honesty and practicality 
 You are organized, accurate, able to multi task and meet deadlines 
 You support the patient experience, your colleagues and others cultural and spiritual beliefs 
 You encompass MSH’s core values and live the words of Respect, Trust, Commitment, Compassion and Courage 
 What we need: 
 Your responsibilities will include: 
Ordering and distributing medical/surgical supplies hospital-wide 
Having an understanding of where all the just-in-time carts are located throughout the hospital and how this storage system allows for the stocking and storage of supplies 
Design, build and revise layout of carts by using shelf dividers, drawer dividers and plastic bar code label holders 
Picking third party medical/surgical supplies and delivering them to units 
Checking carts for outdated medical/surgical supplies and replacing as necessary 
Maintaining individual carts to a regular state of order and cleanliness along with keeping them product specific to each corresponding bar code label on a daily basis 
Conducting daily audits of delivered items to ensure accuracy 
Reviewing and communicating with departments and Coordinator, Linen and JIT any important back orders from the just-in-time provider 
Breaking down all cardboard and returning plastics neatly inside the empty totes to be returned to just-in-time provider 
Uploading of par level cart orders into a handheld device by logging on and docking the handheld into the Pathways Material Management system 
Other duties as assigned 
 Work Schedule: 
The successful candidate must be available to work 0730-1530 , including weekends and statutory holidays. Please note that shifts are subject to change based on the operational needs of the department. 
 Physical Demands: 
 Frequent moderate and heavy exertion:  lifting up to 40 lbs., pushing and pulling carts 
Intense visual concentration required due to computer work 
 Compensation: 
 BAND CU02:  $22.53-$24.48 
 What you bring to MSH: 
Minimum grade 12 education and good mathematical skills 
Comprehensive knowledge of medical/surgical supplies from a patient care or stores setting is essential 
Previous experience or working knowledge of stockless inventory systems 
Excellent communication, interpersonal and organizational skills 
Proficient computer and typing skills 
Proven abilities to work effectively with minimal supervision and as a member of a team 
Demonstrated good attendance and performance records with the ability to maintain these same standards 
Demonstrated commitment to a healthy and safe workplace for self and others (staff, patients, families, etc.) by taking all reasonable precautions and working in compliance with hospital related policies, health and safety legislation and best practices and completing relevant mandatory education as required.
