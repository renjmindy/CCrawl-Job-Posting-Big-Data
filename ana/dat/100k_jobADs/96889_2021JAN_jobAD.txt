 My client, a well established retail business based in Birmingham, is looking to recruit a Head of Marketplace. 
 My client has experienced a record year so far and is on track for continued growth. 
 The position of Head of Marketplace will lead on all marketplace activity across amazon, eBay and other appropriate marketplaces. You be responsible for driving incremental sales growth. 
 The ideal candidate for possess the following skills/expertise: 
 
 Proven record, of successfully managing and growing marketplace revenue, within the UK and Europe 
 Extensive understanding of marketplaces, eCommerce, marketplace tools and advertising concepts, and marketplace industry trend. 
 Strong understanding and demonstrable experience of Amazon & eBay marketplace - with Amazon seller central 
 
 Highly competitive package. 
 Remote working with weekly office attendance (when safe to do so).
