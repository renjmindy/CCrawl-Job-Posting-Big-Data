 SALES AND MARKETING REPRESENTATIVE – CLIENT TELUS 
 Overview: 
 Working with TELUS’ and their Premier Vendor Partner, this exciting position will challenge your sales abilities and give you the opportunity to gain valuable experience with the potential for career progression. We have an exciting atmosphere and eligible applicants will possess a strong desire to succeed in a sales-driven environment. You will be expected to meet and exceed personal sales objectives by pursuing new customers within assigned territory and developing a network of contacts through which prospective customers are obtained, thus leading to future sales. 
 This opportunity is for people living in and around VICTORIA, BC 
 What We Offer: 
 
 Competitive compensation 
 Recognition of top performers 
 Fun and supportive team environment 
 Enhancement of sales and leadership skills 
 Open opportunities for advancement into leadership and management roles! 
 
 Major Duties and Responsibilities: 
 
 Manage the day-to-day responsibilities of an independent Sales Representative to generate new customers in an assigned high-volume territory 
 Direct, face to face communication with customers through various sales methods 
 Ensure productive and positive relationships with prospective customers 
 Execute promotions and meeting and exceed sales goals 
 Client acquisition, relationship building, and follow-ups 
 Meet performance targets for sales and quality 
 Document customer orders in an accurate and timely manner 
 
 Skills and Qualifications: 
 
 Knowledge of and experience in applying field sales techniques 
 Self-driven with an entrepreneurial edge 
 Desire and dedication for a long-term professional career 
 Passion to learn and thrive in a competitive environment 
 Excellent interpersonal and communications skills 
 Ability to work both independently and in a team environment 
 Professional, confident, and well presented 
 Able to work  FULL TIME  hours throughout the week 
 
 Job Types: Full-time, Commission

 Bonus pay
