 Dump Truck/Watercart Operator 
 Job description 
 
 
 3:1 FIFO Roster 
 
 
 Immediate starts 
 
 
 Day shift only 
 
 
 About Our Client 
 Our Client is a leading provider of contract services to the resources and infrastructure sectors in Australia, with extensive operations all over Australia. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac is currently seeking experienced Articulated Dump Truck Operators with Civil, Construction or Mining experience. Successful candidates must have operated Articulated Dump Trucks. This project is for a mine site extension. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 Commit to 3:1 Roster 
 
 
 Day shifts 
 
 
 Able to commit to min 3 months work 
 
 
 Ability to pass a pre-employment medical D&A; screening 
 
 
 Right to work in Australia 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Minimum of 12 months experience on Articulated Dump Trucks 
 
 
 Construction awareness certificate 
 
 
 Current Australian drivers licence 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels.
