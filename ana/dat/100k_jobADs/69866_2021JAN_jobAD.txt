 Career Description 
 Observe all safety regulations and practices. Be mindful of the safety of others. Willing to perform any duties required of plant maintenance team. Troubleshoot all facility and manufacturing electrical/electronic problems. Ensure all maintenance duties and repairs are performed according to prescribed specification. Practice good stewardship relative to safety and cost. Participate in the establishment and performance of preventative maintenance duties on all facility and manufacturing equipment relative to electrical/electronic applications. Maintain clean and orderly work area. Work in cooperation with all departments to maintain facility and equipment. Perform all other tasks as assigned by management.
