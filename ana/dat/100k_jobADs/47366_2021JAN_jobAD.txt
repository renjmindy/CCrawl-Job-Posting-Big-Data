  [Career Reality] 4 Most Common Career Mistakes  
 Work It Daily 
 June 20, 2011 
 As a Career Coach, Author, Speaker and Founder of the  first  Career HMO, J.T. O’Donnell has worked with literally 1,000s of people. Watch her in this episode of  Career Reality  on CAREEREALISM TV as she shares the four most common mistakes she see’s people make in their careers on a daily basis. Better still, she’s going to tell you how to avoid making them too. Learn from the mistakes of those before you. This advice could change your career forever!  On  Career Reality  we dive deeper and get much-needed answers to some of the pressing challenges faced by our workforce today. From job search to career development, we tackle the tough subjects that need to be addressed so you can find professional success and satisfaction on your own terms.
