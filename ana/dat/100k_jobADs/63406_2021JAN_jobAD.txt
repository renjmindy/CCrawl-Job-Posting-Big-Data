  Selfish Vs. Self-Satisfying: Career Success 101  
 J.T. O'Donnell 
 February 01, 2013 
 Want to know the difference between you and people who are insanely happy in their careers? That’s easy. They recognized the goal of pursuing a career is to be self-satisfied. Meanwhile, you were too busy trying to NOT be selfish to realize you were ruining your chances at  career happiness . Watch this video to understand the difference between the two. It will also teach you how choosing to find  career success  on your own terms will make you a better person!
