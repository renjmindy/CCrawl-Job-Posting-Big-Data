 Financial Modelling Manager 
 
 Location 
 
 Atherstone, Warwickshire

 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £60000.00 - £70000.00 per annum + car + package

 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson are currently working with a market leading FMCG business to help recruit a Financial Modelling Manager. Our client's brand and reputation has stood strong for decades and as a business they offer fantastic development, career progression and ambition. This is a truly progressive organisation and as a result they are looking to appoint an experienced finance professional (CIMA, ACCA or ACA) to take on this new role and department within the business. 
 The successful candidate will have: 
 
 Ownership of the operating model for the business 
 Responsible for providing commercial value to the business from data 
 Own modelling for forecasting and long-term planning. 
 Involved in monitoring of financial and non-financial performance indicators, in order to highlight trend and analyse variance. 
 Analyse results, quantify actuals vs budgets/forecast. Ensure cost controls are in place to monitor spending vs budget. 
 Reporting solutions and insight through data analytics 
 Prepare weekly KPI reporting 
 Business partner with Heads of departments. 
 Prepare financial reporting and analysis. 
 Identify and drive process improvements. 
 Ad hoc analysis as required by the business - drive insights to assist in business making decisions. 
 
 In terms of you… 
 
 Qualified Chartered Accountant (ACA, CIMA or ACCA) 
 Previous experience in financial analysis and modelling 
 Experience within FMCG highly advantageous 
 An inquisitive mind-set where you continuously seek process improvement 
 Strong team player and efficient communicator 
 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
