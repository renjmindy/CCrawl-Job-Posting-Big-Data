 Southeast Texas job tips 
 By  hjonasson  on August 10, 2015 
 While watching TV in Southeast Texas, there was a public service commercial to help people get jobs through handy job interview tips. 
 Among the usual, "Dress nice, be polite," there was also, "Don't wear flip flops." 
 Apparently this is a big problem in the Texas area.
