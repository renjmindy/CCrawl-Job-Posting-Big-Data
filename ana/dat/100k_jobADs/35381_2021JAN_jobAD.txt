 West Pier Studio are currently looking for an experienced Project Manager to join our Unity team to help develop, manage, and deliver projects to our clients. 
 You will be responsible for managing and delivering multiple software projects throughout the project lifecycle in a client facing environment. The projects vary from architectural applications, VR/AR, mobile gaming, and educational games. Applicants must have excellent experience of Agile software development and JIRA for tracking bugs and providing project updates. This is a highly autonomous role and a fantastic opportunity to join a growing company in the vibrant city of Brighton. If you're sharp-minded and passionate about technology, we'd like to hear from you. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Manage the complete life cycle and the update of interactive software projects 
 Liaise **** with clients to manage expectations and to gather technical guidance 
 Manage multiple projects in parallel within an Agile development environment 
 Track and report on project status in conjunction with Jira 
 Directly manage the software development team 
 Run daily team scrums and sprint retrospectives 
 Identify and act on obstacles that might prevent a team's ability to achieve sprint targets and business targets 
 Manage milestone delivery in line with user stories and client expectations 
 Manage scope and development of back end services 
 Continually gain an understanding of different development technologies 
 Continually gain an understanding in design solutions and transpose into development backlogs 
 
 The person: 
 
 Would have managed creative and technical teams working in Unity or similar 
 Experience managing the production of interactive experiences, games and educational products, would be desirable but not essential 
 Passionate about games and delivering a fun and good user experience 
 A dependable person, who takes responsibility 
 A lateral thinker, who can solve problems using creative reasoning 
 You will collaborate well within a team environment and develop relationships with the other teams and stakeholders 
 Proactively seek out areas of inefficiency within the scope of the role, taking action to make continuous improvements 
 A curious person who learns and improves their professional skills 
 Technical background or technical understanding would be desirable 
 
 What West Pier can offer you: 
 
 Flexible working hours 
 A competitive salary which is reviewed on an annual basis 
 Company pension scheme provided by NEST 
 Ongoing training with career development 
 A friendly, creative and focused studio atmosphere 
 Regular in-house events and social outings, such as board games, poker nights, cheese board Friday's, escape rooms and much more 
 Weekly fruit delivery with healthy snacks and locally roasted coffee 
 Free eye tests + standard glasses for display work 
 Flu vaccinations are offered on a yearly basis 
 A West Pier t-shirt is provided on the first day 
 Enviable location in Brighton city centre, two minutes from the beach and five minutes from Brighton rail station
