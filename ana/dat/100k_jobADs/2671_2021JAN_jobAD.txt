 Current Job Opportunities 
 We understand that our community, the country, and the world are experiencing exceptionally difficult and uncertain times-especially as it relates to employment. Even with Job Center closure and resource adjustments, WSM is still here working for job seekers. Our team is working with  multiple employers that are currently hiring . In addition, some employers are scheduling virtual recruitment events through WSM. To make sure you have access to all virtual opportunities during this time, register on  Maryland Workforce Exchange (MWE)  and stay updated on all offerings using our  calendar .
