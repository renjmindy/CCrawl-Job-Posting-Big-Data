 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs December 2011 
 Posted on  January 17, 2012  by  caregiver 
 For the month of December 2011, nine Job Orders were approved by the POEA allowing nine POEA duly licensed and accredited recruitment agency to recruit caregivers for placement in caregiver work abroad. These  POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs December 2011  are for the countries of Bahrain, Kuwait, Canada, UAE, Qatar, and Israel and has a total of 103 caregiver jobs. Listed below are the names of the recruiting agencies, the country of destination, and the number of available caregiver jobs for each of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs December 2011. 
 BAHRAIN 
Ortiz Agency & Employment Services Inc. --------------- 1 caregiver job 
 KUWAIT 
A & C International Resources Inc. ---------------------- 5 caregiver jobs 
 CANADA 
Luzern International Manpower Services Corp. --------- 8 caregiver jobs 
 UAE

 QATAR

A-M Philippine Professional Services Corp. ------------- 5 caregiver jobs 
 ISRAEL 
HRD Employment Consultant and Multi Services Inc. --- 26 caregiver jobs 
CPM Manpower Services Inc. --------------------------- 15 caregiver jobs 
Provident Overseas Placement Agency ----------------- 13 caregiver jobs 
 If you wish to apply for any of the said POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs December 2011, proceed directly to the office of your chosen recruiting agency and personally submit your comprehensive resume.
