 A FTSE250 business based in Birmingham, are looking for Data Analysts to join a newly created function to support business wide decisions. 
 As part of a business critical project to transform the way business users both financial and non-financial data, you will be pivotal in ensuring data quality, identifying key trends and consistency, along with working as part of a fast paced dynamic team. 
 You will need: 
 
 Ability to analyse complex data and create new reports to support business critical decisions 
 Track record in SQL - Microsoft and Cloud environment (Power BI) 
 Experience in identifying data quality issues and trends, with incorporating consistency 
 Ability to extract data from various sources, along with cleansing and improving 
 Worked in an Agile environment - Azure devops 
 
 Please note, this will be a FTC with a view to go perm depending on demand and performance. 
 Please apply here with your CV or contact Keeley for further information. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
