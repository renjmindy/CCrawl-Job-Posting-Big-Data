 Consultant: 
 
 
 An exciting opportunity has come up within an FMCG client of Gleesons. They are looking for an Interim Financial Controller who will report directly to the General Manager on a 6 month contract. The ideal candidate will have experience within the FMCG industry and have a commercial finance back ground. You will be responsible for the P&L of the site and therefore will need to be very hands on. 
 The ideal candidate will have the following skill set: 
 
 Oversee financial performance for the site & ensure robust business controls and risk management are in place 
 Provide the leadership team with an appropriate understanding of financial performance 
 Support the drive of Corporate, Commercial & Operational initiatives 
 Drive sustainable margin enhancement/cost reduction programmes through annual PIP's 
 Support and execute where appropriate business change/improvement projects 
 Capital proposals, planning, execution and risk management 
 
 Experience required: 
 
 A good understanding of all elements of the P&L. 
 Previous experience within an FC role/Qualified Accountant +5 years 
 FMCG background or experience 
 Able to demonstrate an understanding of the whole business from a commercial, innovation and manufacturing point of view. 
 Able to influence at a senior level, internally and externally. Possessing the gravitas & emotional intelligence to enable effective challenge within a strong & determined team. 
 Experience of leading project delivery & instigating change 
 
 If you are interested in this role please either contact Emily at Gleeson or click apply and I will be in touch
