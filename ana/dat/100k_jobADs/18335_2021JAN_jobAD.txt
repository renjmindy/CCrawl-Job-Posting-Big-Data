 Are you an experienced Project Executive or Junior Project Manager with a PRINCE2 qualification? How would you like to be a key part of one of the most exciting projects within the Midlands? 
 Working as part of a project team, you will be responsible for supporting a programme management team to plan, manage and deliver this high-profile project. You will develop project goals, sequencing of activities to meet delivery dates, produce project schedules and create relevant project documentation including trackers, risks and milestone plans. You will also be responsible for monitoring progress against deliverables, updating risk and issue registers and track progress against project timelines and budgets. 
 Ideally you will have a PRINCE2 qualification, have experience of producing project documentation and supporting and facilitating the procurement of goods and services and good stakeholder engagement skills. It is essential that your communication skills - both written and verbal - are second to none. You will be a self-starter, with the ability to manage multiple projects at the same time. 
 To apply for the Project Executive position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
