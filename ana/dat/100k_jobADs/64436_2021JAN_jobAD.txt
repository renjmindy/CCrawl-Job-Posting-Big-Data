 Responsibilities include, but not limited to- 
 
 maintain daily operations of property 
 successful conversion of telephone, internet and walk-in inquiries into rentals 
 sales of boxes, locks, and packing material 
 manage account receivable process through pursuit of collections, posting of payments, and processing of daily bank deposit 
 ensures the company standards of cleanliness & appearance 
 marketing to local businesses and apartment managers 
 maintain accurate records for each customer including lease, addendums, and correspondence 
 perform light repair and maintenance duties, including but not limited to: clean units and interior buildings, wiping down doors, pickup up trash, cleaning hallways, office,and bathrooms 
 perform preventive maintenance such as but not limited to: change HVAC filters as required, grease door springs on roll-up doors, oil gate chain as needed, routine maintenance of golf cart monitors the physical condition of the property and reports any maintenance issues 
 oversee contractors and third party vendor's work 
 keeps foreclosure documentation organized and accurate, following company foreclosure checklist and set guidelines 
 assist with the auction process 
 manage U-Haul transactions, rentals, reservations, truck/trailer parking and routine maintenance 
 
 Job Requirements: 
 
 Excellent communication skills both on the phone and in person 
 Works well in a team setting or independently 
 Has good organizational skills and basic bookkeeping 
 Knowledge of computers is a must (Microsoft Office, Outlook, Etc.) 
 Valid driver's license and insurance with access to reliable transportation 
 Open work availability, including weekends and some holidays 
 Ability & willingness to perform light maintenance such as sweeping, mopping, wiping, lifting, etc. 
 Willing to travel/work in multiple locations if needed (temporarily) 
 Must be eligible to work in the United States 
 Must have a High School Diploma/GED equivalent and at least two years of related experience (sales/customer service/leasing/rentals) 
 Must be a team player and have strong leadership skills 
 Must be profit driven 
 Job requires constant movement and must be able to lift and move 35 lbs. regularly and occasionally up to 50 lbs. 
 Must have clean employment history, criminal history, driving record and pass a drug screen 
 
 Positions: 
 Property Manager: Fulltime $35,000 per year 
 Assistant Manager: Parttime 24 hours per week $12.00- $14.00 per hour
