 Your brand color is used to emphasize actions and highlighted information (buttons and links) on candidate-facing pages, like your Basic Careers Page. 
 Ensuring accessibility 
 To ensure your page is easy to use for everyone, you should make sure the color meets accessibility guidelines for contrast - at least a 4.5 ratio with white.  Changing the color in Workable 
 
 Navigate to the  Careers Page  section in your account   (Super Admin access required). 
 Click  Brand Color  to open the color picker. 
 Use the options to select a color that matches or complements your brand. If you want to use an exact hex color code (like  from above), find the area to paste your code and click Enter.
