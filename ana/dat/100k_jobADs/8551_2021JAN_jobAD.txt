 Business/Functional Analyst - Tester - Qa 
 Published on the March 02, 2018 in IT & Programming 
 
 Project 
 Project Insights New 
 
 About this project 
 it-programming / web-development 
 USD 250 - 500 
 Youll probably enjoy working with us if 
• Youre a systems thinker 
• You prize logic and reason 
• Youre restless when not learning something new or stretching yourself 
• You understand that at a fast-growing startup, priorities may shift rapidly. You value exposure across functions and are prepared to work on whichever team or goal is most impactful 
• Youre willing to open-mindedly challenge your own and others ideas, but also commit to the ultimate direction when the time comes 
• You feel accountable and hold others to a high standard 
 Were looking for a Quality assurance tester and also business analyst 
 As an qa tester, you will develop and execute a rigorous qa testing schedule with our web development team, to ensure each release of our portal meets our client and internal standards. 
 What youll be doing: 
• Perform high-level/critical validation and exploratory test coverage using different testing strategies to guarantee full coverage 
• Document QA processes and work with Production lead to integrating testing protocol into release process 
• Organize bug reports and systematize communication with development team 
• Develop testing plan and define exhaustive set of user paths and test thoroughly 
• Perform issue reporting, research, analysis, confirmation and support resolution on assigned projects 
• Perform a variety of QA related support tasks for internal Web development team 
• Maintain a strong knowledge of the project design and scheduling changes to support QA test effectiveness, focus and reporting. 
• Adapt the testing plan to new project developments 
 Category  IT & Programming 
Subcategory  Web development 
 Deadline: Not specified

 JavaScript   MySQL   PHP
