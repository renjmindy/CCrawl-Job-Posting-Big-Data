 I have a fantastic opportunity for a Marketing Campaign Manager to join a fantastic B2C focused business in Birmingham, who are well-known for their excellent working culture and who are going through extreme growth at the moment. If you've got plenty of experience of managing TTL campaigns for a multi-site business, have experience of running these campaigns from start to finish and are confident in reporting back on these campaigns to those further up the food chain, then this might be the perfect role for you. 
 This Marketing Campaign Manager role will involve: 
 
 Delivering integrated marketing campaigns with the intention of building brand awareness, using social media, traditional advertising techniques and branded content 
 Ensuring all campaigns are delivered within the set deadlines, within the set budget and to the set KPIs 
 Agency management - ensuring they produce good quality content and marketing collateral in a timely manner and within budget 
 Work closely with other internal departments to develop briefs and produce creative content 
 Manage the online reputation of the business through social media 
 Create a content calendar, with a focus on video and photography 
 
 The position requires a candidate with the following skills: 
 
 Experience of managing marketing campaigns within a multi-site B2C/retail environment 
 Proven experience of managing ATL and BTL advertising campaigns 
 Proven experience of reporting back on marketing campaigns 
 An understanding of the printing process 
 Excellent and professional communication skills, both written and verbal 
 Outstanding stakeholder management skills 
 The ability to manage a number of projects at any one time 
 
 To apply for the Marketing Campaign Manager position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
