 About Get Online NOLA 
 Get Online NOLA was born out of love for New Orleans, and a huge respect for the entrepreneurs who keep the economy churning. Our mission is to help New Orleans businesses utilize the internet to gain visibility and ultimately grow their bottom line. We know that a great online presence is a key factor when customers decide what products or services to buy, and we think every New Orleans business deserves a reasonably priced, easy to use website that can generate leads. At Get Online NOLA we know that happy employees are better employees! We’re a small team so staying connected and supporting each other is a must. We want all of our employees to grow, learn, and excel both in and out of work. Having a fun and comfortable office and a focus on work-life balance is key to our company culture.
