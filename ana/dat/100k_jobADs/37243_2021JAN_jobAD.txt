 Care Giving Jobs Israel 
 Posted on  August 21, 2011  by  caregiver 
 Two POEA accredited recruitment agencies, thru the POEA approval of their Job Orders, are now accepting applications for their available  Care Giving Jobs Israel . These care giving jobs Israel was approved for recruitment by the POEA last August 1, 15, and 17 and has a total of 73 care giving jobs. Listed below are the names of the recruiting agencies and the number of available care giving jobs for each agency. 
 HRD Employment Consultants & Multi-Services, Inc. --- 58 vacancies 
CPM Manpower Services, Inc. -------------------------- 15 vacancies 
 To apply for any of the said Care Giving Jobs Israel, proceed directly to the office of any of the recruiting agency listed above and submit your updated resume.
