 Customer Experience Leader - Gift Shop - Casual (192323) 
 Sunnybrook Health Sciences Centre 
 __Toronto, ON 
 Apply Now 
 We currently have a Casual opportunity for a Customer Experience Leader in the Gift Shop at our Bayview Campus and Holland Centre. 
 This is a casual position, and requires flexibility to work days, evenings, and weekends. The position rotates between the gifts shops located at the Bayview and Holland sites. 
 Summary of Duties: 
Meet or exceed sales goals by providing exceptional service to all customers, suppliers, vendors, volunteers, and hospital staff 
Create and maintain displays to drive sales and provide a visually attractive shopping experience 
Receive, price, and merchandise retail goods in a timely and efficient manner 
Prepare on-line and telephone orders for delivery 
Administrative duties including answering email, telephone inquiries, balancing daily paperwork, and maintaining inventories 
Provide leadership, support, and guidance to gift shop volunteers 
Strive for excellence in all areas of retail operations 
Contribute to the success of a large and vibrant retail operation by playing a key role in raising funds for patient comfort & care through the operation of a successful hospital gift shop 
Maintain a safe and clean work environment 
Other duties and projects as assigned 
 Summary of Duties: 
Minimum 2 years’ experience in a retail environment 
Demonstrated superior retail sales and customer service skills 
Excellent interpersonal and communication skills 
Experience and interest in retail merchandising 
Interest in and awareness of fashion and décor trends 
Ability to deal with multiple and conflicting priorities 
Ability to work as team member in a busy environment 
Proficiency with MS Office and POS systems 
Excellent organizational and time management skills 
Previous experience working with volunteers an asset 
Previous experience with on-line retail is an asset 
Ability to work at two locations; Bayview & Lawrence and Yonge & Wellesley 
Ability to work days, evenings, weekends 
Ability to work standing and lift boxes, climb ladders 
 Qualified candidates are invited to submit their resume and cover letter (in one document) quoting 192323 to:
