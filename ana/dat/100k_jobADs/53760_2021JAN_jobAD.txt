 WEEKEND WORKER - NURSING 
 St. Joseph's Villa, Dundas 
 __Hamilton, ON 
 Apply Now 
 St. Joseph’s Villa Dundas is   currently seeking WEEKEND WORKERS (PSW, HCA, NURSING STUDENTS ENROLLED IN RPN AND RN PROGRAMS) to join our growing and dynamic team. We are looking for Pandemic Support along with permanent job opportunities. 
 St. Joseph’s Villa is one of Ontario’s largest and most innovative long term care homes. Located in historic downtown Dundas, we are proud to be the second largest organization in the St. Joseph’s Health System. Continuing the mission and legacy of the Sisters of St. Joseph of Hamilton, we are proud to serve our community. 
 We are the largest employer in Dundas. Our campus is conveniently located on a city bus route and offers free parking. We offer a range of challenging, rewarding careers and advancement opportunities for dedicated professionals in a health care environment. 
 KEY DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES: 
 Perform all duties in accordance with the Mission Statement, Organizational, Philosophy and administrative policies and standards of St. Joseph’s Villa. 
 · Provides or assists the Registered Nurse and Registered Practical Nurse with personal care of the residents as per position description. 
 · Ambulates, mobilizes and transfers residents according to care plan. 
 · Performs selected nursing duties as per position description. 
 · Participates in Continuous Quality Improvement activities in Nursing. 
 · Other related duties as assigned/required per position description. 
 FORMAL EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS: 
 · Grade 10 High School Education 
 · Community College Health Care Aide Diploma (or equivalent) 
 · Experience in long-term care facility or Community College Health Care Aide program, which included a supervised clinical component similar to that offered by Mohawk College. 
 Shifts: Days/Evenings/Nights WEEKEND COVERAGE
