 Administrative Assistant in Limassol 
 Full Time 
 
 __Post Date: November 22, 2020 
 __Apply Before: January 31, 2021 
 
 Job Description 
 On behalf of our clients, we are seeking to recruit a  Administrative Assistant . Our clients’ company is considered as one of the leading developers of software applications for brokerages, banks, and exchanges. The company's representative offices are located in many countries around the world. 
 Job Responsibilities 
 
 Communication with clients via emails and telephone regarding invoices, receivables and compliance matters; 
 Carrying out basic KYC and due diligence procedures; 
 Assisting with any other tasks that may be requested from time to time by various departments. 
 
 Job Requirements 
 
 University Degree in Business Management, Administration or related; 
 Fluent spoken/written English, other languages will be considered an advantage; 
 Working experience in a similar position; 
 Good writing skills; 
 Very well organized, with great attention to detail; 
 Ability to multitask; 
 No visa/work permit problems (EU citizens only). 
 
 How to Apply 
 If interested and you match all the requirements, please send your CV to andriana.p@work-channel.com, including the title of the position in the subject line 
 Please note  that due to the high volume of applications received, only successful candidates will be contacted
