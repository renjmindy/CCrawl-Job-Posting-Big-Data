 Build infrastructure for the Internet of Blockchain 
 Role overview 
 Job Description 
 Chorus One runs validators on cutting edge Proof of Stake networks such as Cosmos, Solana, Celo, Polkadot and Oasis. We also provide user analytics and a wallet for users at: https://anthem.chorus.one/ 
 We are seeking a Blockchain Engineer with proficiency in Rust. The engineer will build inter-protocol blockchain systems to serve infrastructural needs of application level developers. 
 This position is a unique opportunity to work with an experienced team on cutting-edge blockchain networks and financial services around cryptocurrency. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Interact frequently with application developers and product stakeholders to best understand their needs. 
 Design and develop software systems on top of blockchain protocols - to best serve the infrastructural needs of the above. 
 Work with DevOps engineers at ChorusOne to deploy, monitor, secure and maintain these systems. 
 
 Our Offer 
 
 Competitive salary + equity 
 Quarterly retreats at exotic destinations. Past retreats took place in Cairo, Serbia, Kenya and Korea 
 Unlimited leave 
 Full-time position 
 Remote work 
 Work on cool technology and interesting problems at the intersection of finance, cryptocurrency, information security and blockchain networks
