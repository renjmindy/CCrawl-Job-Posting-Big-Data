 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson Accountancy are seeking a temporary to permanent Management Accountant, to start as soon as possible from home, then based in Birmingham City Centre. The initial contract is approximately 6 months before transferring to permanent. 
 This is an exciting role for a bright, driven and commercially astute Accountant. You will perform an all round Management Accountant role, from transactional finance, right through to month end accounts. 
 Reporting into the Finance Director, duties include, month-end accounting processes; cost control and analysis; group reporting; working closely with non-finance stakeholders; variance analysis and ledger processing. 
 You will be ambitious and driven, with all round accounts experience up to Management Accounts level. You will possess the ability to build excellent working relationships with both finance and non-finance stakeholders. You will be a fully qualified accountant, or in the latter stages of study, and be available immediately or on very short notice. 
 In return, you will receive a salary of up to £40,000 plus bonus, pension, 25 days annual leave and full flexible working, based on 37.5 hours per week. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
