 Labourer 
 Job description 
 About Our Client 
 Our client is a Solar Energy Company involved commercial installations with work located in Plainlands and the Lockyer Region. 
 About the Role 
 We are seeking Labourers and Trades Assistants for a role located in Lower Tenthill with an immediate start. Previous experience installing or assisting in the installation of Solar Panels. You must be available for an immediate start and be physically fit for the role with the ability to work in heat and potentially at height. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Carting and carrying Solar Panels 
 
 
 General labouring duties 
 
 
 Climbing ladders 
 
 
 Working around machinery 
 
 
 Clearing and preparing sites 
 
 
 Assisting Tradesman on site 
 
 
 Assembling and disassembling temporary structures 
 
 
 Utilising hand & power tools 
 
 
 Skills & Qualifications 
 White or Blue Card 
 Drivers Licence and reliable Transport 
 Current Resume with minimum 2 x work reference's 
 Availability for an immediate start 
 Reliable and strong work ethics 
 Culture & Benefits 
 
 
 Full time hours 
 
 
 Ongoing work for the right candidate 
 
 
 Wages paid weekly
