 About Us 
 Cannabis on Bloor is an AGCO-licensed recreational cannabis store operating on Bloor St. E in Toronto. 
 We are looking for responsible, hardworking, and dedicated retail store associates with a positive attitude who thrive on delivering excellent customer service and educating the public about cannabis. 
 Roles and responsibilities include, but are not limited to: 
 
 Greeting customers as they pick up their curbside pickup orders 
 Operating the POS terminal and adhering to all cash and loss prevention procedures 
 Providing an educational and informative experience for all customers 
 Assist in opening/closing at the beginning or end of shifts, executing to high operating standards 
 Ensure the store is clean and well-stocked, including regular surface sanitization and floor sweeping/mopping 
 Any other duties as assigned by Store Manager
