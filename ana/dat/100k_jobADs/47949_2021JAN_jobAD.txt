 Real Estate & Mortgage 
 DE Underwriter Direct Hire 
   
   Workway, Inc.   
 __Website    __@workway 
 Currently, we have an opportunity to work with one of the leading independent mortgage retail lenders in the country! Build a role with an organization that boasts excellent product offerings, a premier client experience, exceptional underwriting efficiencies and turn times, and best-in-class technology, marketing and support.  A DE Underwriter will underwrite simple to moderately complex mortgage loan applications and evaluate loans in order to maximize organization profit and minimize risk. 
 Your specific duties in this role will include: 
 
 Determines eligibility of borrowers based on credit findings. 
 Makes underwriting decisions based on Client, FHA, VA, FNMA and FHLMC guidelines. 
 Responds to customer inquiries relative to declination reasons according to federal regulatory guidelines. 
 Reviews credit packages that fall outside of the automated underwriting parameters. 
 Reviews loan applications submitted by borrowers and makes credit decisions based on lender criteria. 
 Underwrites government/conventional files and clears conditions. 
 Works with underwriting assistants and account executives. 
 
 Specific qualifications for the DE Underwriter position include: 
 
 MUST have CHUMS ID. 
 VA LAPP/SAR preferred 
 A minimum of 5 years mortgage underwriting experience required. 
 Experience with web based approval systems (DU/LP) required. 
 Strong knowledge of FHA and VA guidelines 
 Exceptional customer service skills.
