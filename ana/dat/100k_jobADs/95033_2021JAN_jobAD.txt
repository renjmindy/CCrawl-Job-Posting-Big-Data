 Part-Time iOS (back end) developer 
 Zappy -  HELSINKI  , FI 
 Job Description 
 We are looking for a talented iOS (back end) developer for our soon to launch new mobile application Zappy. The selected candidate will be a part of our talented and dynamic team as a part-time developer (5-20h/week). The position is suitable also as a secondary, part-time project. 
 The position requires flexible schedules as you will be responsible for implementing new features and updating the application. What you should master to succeed: REST, Json, good knowledge of network protocols Databases: Neo4j or MongoDB Java, Java EE, Spring Framework Experience with build Spring Data and Spring Security (OAuth2) is a plus Basic knowledge of HTML5, JavaScript, jQuery Also: Good spoken and written English, Finnish is a plus. You are based in Helsinki/Tallinn or nearby. 
 If you are looking for new iOS challenges with a new and truly exciting app project, apply right away by sending us your CV and application. After the successful completion of a six-week trial period, the selected candidate will have a chance to join us as a partner.
