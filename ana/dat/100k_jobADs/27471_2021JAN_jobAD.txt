 Position Summary... 
This position is an expert in Replenishment systems, forecasting best practices, and optimal flow of goods. This position is responsible for working with domestic and international stakeholders to implement the most up to date technology and processes across the Supply Chain. This position provides support to the Replenishment team in identifying best practices to get the most out of the technology. Leading a team of Technology Managers, this associate must have a well-balanced skillset including strong technical ability and enhanced leadership and influencing skills.
