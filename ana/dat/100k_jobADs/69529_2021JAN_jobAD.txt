 Lunchtime Lesson: Leverage LinkedIn to Build your Business and Career - FRI April 1st 2016 
 Social media is an indispensable tool for navigating today's rapidly shifting economic landscape. As the world's leading social network for professionals, with over 400 million members, LinkedIn should be at the heart of your professional social media strategy. 
 In this session, Ernest Courant, Program Manager at LinkedIn, will go over the fundamentals of creating or upgrading your LinkedIn profile and other key techniques for leveraging the platform to create a dynamic and effective social media presence. 
 We will cover: 
 • How to craft a compelling headline and summary 
 • Creating a rocking profile that will get attention 
 • Maintaining an active presence on the network with minimal effort 
 • Best practices for writing blog posts on LinkedIn 
 • Other insider tips and tricks for getting the most out of LinkedIn
