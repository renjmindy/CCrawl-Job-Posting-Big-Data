 Caregiving Jobs Israel 
 Posted on  January 13, 2011  by  caregiver 
 Thru a POEA approved job order, a POEA licensed recruitment agency is currently accepting applications for those who are looking for  Caregiving Jobs Israel . The said job order was approved last January 6, 2011 for the said recruitment agency whose office is located in Makati City. With the said job order approval, the recruiting agency is allowed to recruit 30 qualified applicants for employment in caregiving jobs Israel. 
 If you are interested in pursuing an application for the said Caregiving Jobs Israel, you may personally submit your resume and application to Star World International Manpower & Placement Agency, Inc. You may also apply for the said caregiving jobs Israel by filling up the Online Application found at their website. For any question regarding the said caregiving jobs Israel, you may contact Star World thru their landline numbers or email address stated below. 
 **Star World International Manpower & Placement Agency, Inc. ** 
203 Orina Building, J.P. Rizal Street 
Barangay Tejeros, Makati City 
Tel Nos: 4038070, 4038071, 4038075 
Telefax: 4038066 
Email address: 
info@starworldmanpower.com
