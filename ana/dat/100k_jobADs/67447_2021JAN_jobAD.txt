 Tax Analyst 
 Inter Pipeline 
 __Calgary, AB 
 Apply Now 
 We are a major energy infrastructure business that has experienced tremendous growth over the past decade. This growth has provided our employees with stability, positive challenges, new career paths and opportunities to work in a dynamic team environment. As one of Alberta’s Top 75 Employers we are proud to say no matter where your role at Inter Pipeline may lie, you are provided ample opportunities for valued contribution and innovation, with a strong emphasis on employee growth. 
 Located out of our Calgary office, the individual in the Tax Analyst will report to the Manager, Taxation. The successful candidate will be responsible for tax compliance, tax provisions, tax forecasting and other tax related responsibilities within Inter Pipeline. 
 Qualifications: 
CPA designation is an asset 
Completion or enrollment in the CPA in depth tax course 
 Experience 
 2 – 4 years of applicable experience in a tax advisory and tax compliance role 
Strong understanding of the preparation of Financial Statements 
Experience in dealing with complex contracts 
 Skills : 
Advanced MS Office skills 
Working knowledge of SAP is an asset 
Excellent communication skills (oral and written) 
Ability to work independently manage multiple deadlines 
 Key Activities and Responsibilities 
 Ensure Inter Pipeline’s compliance with the Canadian Income Tax Act by following the appropriate procedures and practices. This includes, but is not limited to, the preparation and filing of the Federal and provincial tax returns for Inter Pipeline and all its subsidiaries 
Prepare required monthly and quarterly accounting entries for current and deferred income tax provisions 
Prepare monthly tax instalment remittances 
Assist with of all dealings with Canadian taxation authorities 
Liaise with external auditors and tax advisers 
Research and advise the Manager, Taxation with regards to various tax accounting and tax compliance matters 
Assist in updating the income tax forecast on a regular basis for the Corporate Planning group 
Assist in responding to the investor relations group with tax related matters 
Maintain up to date records regarding Inter Pipeline’s adjusted cost base of its assets and liabilities 
Ad-hoc internal tax reporting (management reports, queries, etc.) for all levels of staff throughout Inter Pipeline 
Work with a focus to analyze financial results for tax related reasonability, recommend efficiencies, and provide tax advice 
Thinking analytically to evaluate complex business and tax issues 
 Hours: 
Full time, Monday – Friday 
 Employment of a final candidate will be conditional upon completion of an employment verification with satisfactory results.
