 Sam Bombay is an Indian film director, editor, and producer. He has worked with many Bollywood celebrities in his career graph. Sam recently made the headlines when the news of him marrying his long time girlfriend, Poonam Pandey surfaced on the net. Today we will get to know more about the film director and his lifestyle.

 Sam Bombay Wiki/ biography: 
 Sam Bombay was born on the 27th of January 1984. He is 36 years of age, as in 2020. He was born and brought in a well-settled family in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Sam Bombay did his early Schooling from Jebel Ali School in Dubai and later enrolled for a graduation course in the Dubai University. And ever since Sam was a kid, he was keen to work as a director or work in entertainment. Which is precisely what he did after his graduation. 
 Sam Bombay career: 
 Sam Bombay started his career as a producer and director in the Bollywood entertainment industry. In 2017, he directed his first short film “king of the Ring” with Akshay Kumar in a lead role. Apart from this, he has also worked as a Regional executive creative director in VMLY&R. And he was also a part of a firm that provides branding strategies to global products. Sam Bombay has also handled Vice-chairman and chief creative officer in Rediffusion Dentsu Young and Rubicam Private Ltd. Sam has also worked in many advertisements for big brands like Oppo, Amazon, Sparx, Shyam Steel, etc. Hit music videos like Gal Ban Gayi and Befikra are directed by Sam Bombay. Watch the video Befikra directed by Sam Bombay. 
 Sam Bombay, Girlfriend, family, and relationships: 
 Sam Bombay belongs to a well-settled family from Dubai, United Arab Emirates. There is not much known about his family members and other details. He currently lives in Mumbai, Maharashtra. And he is married to his long time girlfriend and model Poonam Pandey. On the 10th of September 2020, they shared the pictures of their engagement. They got hitched in a private ceremony in Mumbai, Maharashtra. The couple has been dating each other for a very long time now, and they finally got married. 
 Sam Bombay Facts: 
 Sam Bombay has always wanted to work in the entertainment industry. He married his longtime girlfriend, Poonam Pandey. Sam made the headlines when Poonam Pandey posted on her social media bout their relationship. He loves photography and traveling. Sam is very active on Social media and posts random pictures on his social media profiles. And he also likes to post photos of his girlfriend, Poonam Pandey. The couple's Instagram is full of images from their wedding. Check their Instagram handles to watch their adorable pictures.
