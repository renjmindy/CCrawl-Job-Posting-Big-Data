 WORK  **  PERMITS ** 
Students looking to be employed in California must have a Permit to Work, issued by Northgate High School. Students can  print out the form here , fill it out, and return it to Student Services.* Once approved, the student may begin working. More information about Work Permits can be  found online here . 
 CLICK HERE FOR THE WORK PERMIT FORM 
 _  SUMMER PERMITS: From June 29th - August 3rd students will go to Loma Vista Adult Center (1266 San Carlos Ave, Concord, CA 94518) to submit any/all work permits to be processed _
