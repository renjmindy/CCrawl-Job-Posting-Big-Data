 Ongoing Support and Advice Throughout your Career 
 Posted by  Super Yachting South Africa  on  3rd Oct 20187th Nov 2018 
 We are always happy to provide ongoing support and advice throughout our graduates' careers. Email us anytime! We are very proud of all our graduates and we also enjoy a friendly update, even if it is just to say 'hello'. We keep abreast of all regulatory changes and new yachting legislation, and we always provide the most recent and up-to-date information to our entry-level and advanced Steward/ess graduates.
