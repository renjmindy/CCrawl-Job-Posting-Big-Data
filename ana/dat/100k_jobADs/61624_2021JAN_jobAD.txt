 Weekly Work at home Job Leads Posted Today 
 Published by  aw1219  on May 3, 2018 
 We may earn money or products from some of the companies mentioned in this post. 
 Thank you for visiting my blog to find out the latest work at home job leads you can apply to online. The jobs mentioned in this post are from the most recent listings made available to the public on their career page, job boards and elsewhere online. Hopefully this list will save you some time in your job search and hopefully lead to a work at home job. If you have been hired recently, please leave me a comment below to share your good news. 
 Make sure you also have your home office ready for taking customer service calls, doing web research, completing tasks from your smartphone by going to our Amazon page for all your home office needs and essentials. Please also visit the weekly job section, Friday freebie page, check out my blog at Homebasedmommie and subscribe to my YouTube channel for even more work at home job opportunities. 
 If you are looking for a community that caters to others like you that want to work from home, have advice on legitimate work at home employers, can provide payment proofs and more, join our official FB group here. Please come back to this blog Friday for more work at home job leads posted on the Friday Freebie pages. 
 Weekly Work at home Job Leads Posted Today 
 Contact Customer Service Associate (Temporary Assignment) 
 Academy Sports & Outdoors is seeking those with 6 months customer service experience to answer incoming customer contacts in a professional manner. You will research and troubleshoot problems providing resolutions and resources. You will need a PC, keyboard, mouse, good communication skills and be proficient with computer applications. TX residents only. 
 Home Agents 
 Sitel is hiring remote workers to service inbound calls that involve billing inquiries, account or product inquiries, product or service orders, installation, troubleshooting, etc. Requires prior customer service and sales experience, good verbal and listening skills, and a professional voice. Paid training, hourly pay with benefits. 
 Customer Service Rep 
 Nations Info Corp is seeking experienced customer service reps to work from home full time handling incoming requests from customers, gathering customer information, troubleshooting issues and other related duties. You will need experience with Skype, internet connection, excellent listening and verbal skills. Pays $12 per hour plus commission with paid time off. CA residents only. 
 Language Expert 
 I Soft Stone is looking for people to judge natural language patterns. Must have a Bachelors degree, be a Windows user and have excellent writing and editing skills. 
 Baby Registry Consultant 
 
 Babylist is seeking candidates to work from home part time answering customer support requests. You must be comfortable and excited about working with expectant parents and their families, can work weekends and are very compassionate and empathetic. 
 Telephone Data Collectors 
 Westat is seeking data collectors to conduct phone interviews on a variety of subjects. You must be able to work 15 up to 40 hours per week including weekends and evenings, have a land line phone, Windows computer and a webcam. 
 Event Ticketing & Sales 
 Working Solutions is seeking agents to work from home taking inbound customer service calls talking to buyers and sellers answering questions to provide a great entertainment experience. You will need an interest in sports, music and theatre. You must be fast thinkers who can clearly answer questions. 
 Part Time Deal Scout 
 The Krazy Coupon Lady is seeking individuals to hunt deals and take pictures for their Retail team. This requires good communication, iPhone 7 or newer, ability to use DropBox, access to a car and possess good picture taking skills. Pays $13 per hour. Mostly morning work. 
 Customer Happiness Specialist 
 TheraNest is seeking candidates to work from anywhere in the United States responding to and resolving customer questions through chat, emails and telephone calls quickly and efficiently. You must have previous experience and be comfortable speaking on the phone for long periods of time. Benefits provided. 
 
 Online Teachers 
 VIPKID is currently looking for online teachers to teach young students in China via video-conferencing. Teachers will earn on average between $14 to $22 per hour. You can pick your own hours. 
 Savvy Connect 
 Participate in market research simply by surfing the web with SavvyConnect. Earn up to $180 for enabling digital tracking on your internet connected devices. New US members will be automatically invited after signing up. Membership is FREE, signup today. 
 Slicethepie 
 Get paid cash weekly to listen to new music and write a short review. Only takes a few minutes of your time. Can work whenever you want. 
 Ibotta 
 
 Get $10 cash instantly to join ibotta plus cash back on your everyday purchases. Get started by downloading this free app on Google Play or the App Store. 
 Deliver good food for good money at DoorDash 
 Get paid weekly. No car needed.
