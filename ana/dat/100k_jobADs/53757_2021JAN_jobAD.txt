 Overview 
 "Customer engagement in B2B is all about customers experiencing a real partnership." 
 Edgify is looking for an organized, personable and tech savvy Customer success engineer to join our sales and customer success team in escorting our clients through their entire customer journey, from initial conversation to full deployment! You will serve as the primary technical point of contact throughout the customer's onboarding stages and growth stages. This role requires a love for people and conversations as you'll build and maintain long term relationships with our clients' tech teams and advocate their needs internally across our departments such as Product, R&D and Sales. 
 What you'll do? 
 Engage our Customers Tech Teams 
 
 Deep-dive technical assistance which includes conducting screen share sessions with customers to guide them through installations, tech evaluations as well as resolving complex technical issues with high customer satisfaction. 
 Ensure customers are getting the most out of our solution and that they are adopting new features as they are released . 
 Prioritize and manage incoming support tickets both via our customer chat platforms and other support channels. 
 
 Pre-sale tech support 
 
 Train, empower and support the sales team with product knowledge and technical Q&A. 
 Take on a pro-active role in potential client evaluation based on their technical specs and our tech requirements. 
 Validate potential clients based on our capabilities. 
 Work directly with key technical stakeholders from the clients' side and agree on objectives, milestones and deliverables with regards to initial integration and rollout plans. 
 
 Inside Edgify 
 
 Deliver client insights and request client needs from all internal teams (sales, marketing, product, engineering and research). 
 Communicate highly technical concepts to a variety of internal audiences. 
 Manage the solution demo system, ensuring that our team consistently delivers great product demos to potential new clients. 
 Develop miscellaneous coding projects that support demos, proof of concepts, integrations and tech support as needed. 
 
 Skills - Must 
 
 
 * Minimum 2 -4 years of full-time work experience at tech companies or a tech startup as a sales engineer\Customer success\Pre-Post sales
 
 
 Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or Engineering or equivalent. 
 
 
 
 Intermediate-level knowledge of a broad range of technologies such as: Docker environment, Understanding client server architecture, Network infrastructures 
 
 
 Excellent verbal and written communication skills in English 
 
 
 Hands-on experience with troubleshooting and problem solving 
 
 
 Ability to travel 30% of the time (Covid permitting) 
 
 
 An ability to simplify complex topics, and always relate technical explanations back to the business value of the product 
 
 
 Quick, on-your-feet thinking, especially in meetings where you might be asked rapid-fire questions 
 
 
 An intrinsic curiosity and desire to always learn more in order to keep up with our rapid pace of development and the pace of the industry 
 
 
 Must be pragmatic, realistic, and build strong, respectful relationships across levels of seniority both inside Edgify as well as the clients' organisations. 
 
 
 A positive, team-first attitude, and a desire to help your colleagues and peers. 
 
 
 Comfortable working day-to-day with cloud-based workflow tools such as Monday, Google Apps, and Slack 
 
 
 Available for a full-time position within a fixed schedule 
 
 
 You don't wait for an answer, you go and find one. 
 
 
 You're well organised, good at prioritising and juggling multiple work streams. 
 
 
 You are comfortable with working through problems of a fast-growing company. 
 
 
 Skills - Nice to have 
 
 Intermediate-level knowledge of a broad range of coding technologies & APIs 
 experience in customer success or customer-facing roles, for a technical product or services company. 
 Experience in working with machine learning based products 
 You are familiar with developer workflows and standard tooling e.g. GitHub 
 Familiarity with the Retail industry and its ecosystem 
 Knowledge of open source, cloud, and containers.
