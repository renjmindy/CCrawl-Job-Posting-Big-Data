 EEC Careers 
 Thank you for your interest in working for the Energy and Environment Cabinet. 
 If you have a passion for public service, we encourage you to apply for jobs within the Cabinet. You can view additional information or apply for any of the job openings listed by clicking the link for each position. 
 Questions regarding a specific position should be directed to the agency contact listed on the position detail page. 
 The process for hiring state merit employees is defined by personnel laws. Visiting the  Personnel Cabinet Employee website  will provide an overview of the employment system and other helpful information related to finding a career with the Commonwealth of Kentucky. 
 You can search all state jobs  here.
