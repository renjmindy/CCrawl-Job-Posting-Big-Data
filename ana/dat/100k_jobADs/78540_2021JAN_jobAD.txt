 We're looking for someone who wants to bring a game-changing solution to an industry ripe for reinvention -- and help us connect with those who can use our platform to change the way we build. 
 This is a high-energy sales role with a focus on finding and converting new potential customers across the UK and European region. You will contribute to Disperse hyper-growth by helping to acquire new clients. 
 We are looking for driven professionals who are willing to strongly contribute and ready to work in a fast build & learn environment. 
 You will gain experience interacting with prospective clients of all levels and in an industry ready for transformation. 
 In this role, you will: 
 
 Drive revenue generation for Disperse through qualifying and closing new and existing businesses 
 Establish and develop a strategy for identifying and closing net new sales opportunities - Account mapping and planning 
 Understand lead qualification and work with sales development reps to refine processes and accuracy through data driven decisions 
 Be able to execute in short-term sprints while maintaining a vision for long-term sustained success 
 Work closely with Marketing to provide essential feedback on lead generation quality and Marketing operations 
 Develop strong relationships with key clients that will generate revenue as new features go-to-market 
 Identify prospect needs, understand business drivers behind requests, and explain the Disperse value proposition 
 Build and maintain a strong sales pipeline and forecast 
 
 Requirements 
 You should apply if you have: 
 
 Previous 4+ years experience in the SaaS or Construction industry, in an Account Executive role or similar 
 Ability to be recognized as a true team player internally in the company, and trusted business advisor externally. 
 You have strong written and verbal communication skills when dealing with different stakeholders, from line managers to C levels. 
 Proven track record of growing a customer base and consistently exceeding revenue targets and company goals 
 Have a competitive edge and thrive in a fast-paced team environment 
 Strong use of insights and data-driven decision making to inform the sales process 
 Ability to problem solve, adapt, and grow quickly as we build the company and sales organization 
 Extremely proactive and upbeat with strong organizational skills 
 
 Benefits 
 
 Experience firsthand what it takes to scale up an AI startup 
 Competitive compensation 
 Friendly, open and transparent culture 
 Health insurance (Vitality + Gym membership discounts) 
 Pension scheme 
 Flexible working hours / ability to work from home 
 Choose your work equipment / devices: your local office will provide you with options to choose from to help you set up the systems and work environment that will keep you happy and productive 
 Central London location 
 Regular socials, and team retreats to amazing destinations (Tbilisi, Kiev... Where next?)
