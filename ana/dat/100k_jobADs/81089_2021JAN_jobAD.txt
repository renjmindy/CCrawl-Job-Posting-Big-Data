 Apply for the position, Assistant Cook - O'Loughlin Elementary, with USD 489 today or contact Ashley Minear for more information. Posted Dec 1, 2020.

 Description 
 **To Make Application, apply online:  ** 
 ** ** https://usd489.applicantportal.com 
 
 QUALIFICATIONS:    Must have a high school diploma or equivalency.  Selected candidate must have a completed certificate of health and satisfactory background check before first day of employment. 
 HOURS:    2.75 hours per day, Monday through Friday, only on days when school is in session. 
 STARTING SALARY:    $10.88 per hour 
 Beginning date:    January 5, 2021 
 **  Closing date for application:** Open until filled 
 Position contact  :           Jessica Younker 
 [email protected] 
 (785) 623-2400 
 General Duties 
 
 
      Assist in preparation of lunch (Usually items such as potato products, salad, vegetables, fruits, etc.)
 
 
 
     Help set up the choice line.
 
 
 
     Act a runner for main serving line and choice line.  If rotation is used, may help serve, act as runner or have duties in dishwashing area, including pot and pan washing, and dishmachine duties.  
 
 
 
     Help with general put away of food and general cleaning of kitchen.
 
 
 
 Requirements 
 
 
     Must be able to read and write English.
 
 
 
     Must be able to do simple arithmetic.
 
 
 
     Must be able to follow oral and written direction.
 
 
 
     Must have the capacity to grasp and adjust to new and changing situations.
 
 
 
     Manual dexterity and ability to work under pressure are desirable.
 
 
 
     Ability to lift objects of 25 pounds.
 
 
 
     Must be neat in appearance, clean, have good personal hygiene habits.
 
 
 
     Must wear appropriate clothing, hairnet and comfortable appropriate shoes.
 
 
 
     A health certificate is required stating healthy status and TB test or chest x-ray.
 
 
 
 Tools and Equipment 
 
 
     Dishmachine
 
 
 
     Holding equipment, stove, oven, steamer
 
 
 
     Portion control equipment
 
 
 
     Choice bar
 
 
 
     Refrigeration equipment.
 
 
 
     Slicer
 
 
 
 Working Conditions 
 
 
     Number of hours and days are specified in work agreement.  Monday through Friday; except for school holidays.  Exceptions are stated in school calendar.
 
 
 
 Nine months per year. 
 
 
     Kitchen is well lighted, ventilated and comfortable (although it may be hot in late August and late May).
 
 
 
     Much standing on feet required and some lifting.
 
 
 
 Supervision 
 
 
     Responsible to Base/Satellite Manager.
 
 
 
 Personal Requirements 
 
 
     High School Diploma or GED preferred.
 
 
 
     References required.
 
 
 
 Term of Employment 
 
 
     At Will
 
 
 
 Evaluation 
 
 
     Performance effectiveness evaluated in accordance with USD #489 Board of Education Policy.
