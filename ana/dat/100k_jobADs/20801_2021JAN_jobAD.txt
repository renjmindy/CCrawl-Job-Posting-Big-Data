 Work from home jobs Austin TX, USA  - Apply for the ongoing Work from Home Contact Center Representative Job at All Web Leads, Inc. See job description, requirements and the link to apply. 
 Job Summary 
 AWL (All Web Leads, Inc.) is one of the most successful customer acquisition marketing companies in the US. Simply put, we help our customers acquire customers. In the heart of AWL we have our Award Winning Contact Center Team that matches consumer requests for insurance quotes with the agents who can offer the lowest prices – and it’s all done in real time! There is NO insurance license required because you are  not selling ! 
 This position gives you the  flexibility  to earn competitive hourly pay while working  part time FROM HOME!  You can expect paid on site training in our state-of-the-art facility in Austin where a laptop computer will be provided. This position also opens  growth opportunities  that allow you to turn your skills into a career with AWL. 
 Qualifications and Skills 
 
 Highspeed internet (at least 15 mb/s download speed) 
 Hardwire connection 
 Reside within the state of Texas 
 No background noise 
 Bilingual in Spanish not required, but good to have 
 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Engage in inbound/outbound calls (no sales), ask qualifying questions, encourage a talk with an agent 
 Engage in follow up calls, inquire about the experience, ensure needs were met 
 Communicate with management on a regular basis – 2-way feedback & coaching opportunities 
 Select a shift that you can commit to on a long term basis
