 About the Company 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking a Civil Operators with either or all experience on -Skid steer, Bobcat and excavator. The successful candidates will commence work on a project in Tom Price. On a 3/1 roster you will do all 12 hour day shifts while meeting the clients requirements. 
 Candidates must possess the below: 
 
 
 HR Class Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Bobcat Ticket 
 
 
 Excavator ticket 
 
 
 Skid Steer ticket 
 
 
 White Card
