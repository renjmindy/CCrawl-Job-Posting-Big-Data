 Hikurangi Cannabis Company currently employs around 25 people, we expect to recruit for a number of specialist roles in 2019 and expand further in 2020. 
 Any current vacancies are posted on the Seek  website  here .

 While each role will have special skills and experience relevant to the purpose of the role, all the candidates we employ are likely to have: 
 
 Proven experience and skills related to the position applied for 
 Sound judgment and decision-making 
 Demonstrated commitment to quality, safety and reliability 
 Analytical abilities and problem-solving skills 
 Excellent communication and interpersonal skills 
 
 Some science, quality control, engineering and finance jobs do require specialist qualifications, but most of the roles do not require formal qualifications - selection will be more about pukenga/skills, experience, attitude/ahua and best fit with the wider team and community. 
 **If you are interested in a position with Hikurangi Cannabis Company then sign up to our email updates and watch for jobs listings on our jobs page at seek.co.nz  **
