 Irrigation Technician 
 Victoria Contracting & Municipal Maintenance Corporation 
 __Langford, BC 
 Apply Now 
 Division: Parks and Landscaping 
 **Job Description & Responsibilities: ** 
 Victoria Contracting & Municipal Maintenance is looking for an experienced Irrigation Technician to join our team. This position requires problem solving skills and a good understanding of irrigation systems. 
 Victoria Contracting provides maintenance services to several municipalities in the Capital Regional District and as such the Irrigation Technician may have a diverse range of responsibilities including but not limited to: 
 - Assisting with installation, maintenance and repair of irrigation systems 
 - Operating basic hand tools such as saws, shovels, rakes, etc. 
 - Operating job-related equipment in a safe and efficient way according to all relevant policies and procedures 
 - Assisting in other physical labour tasks as required 
 - Participating in and recording pre-job safety meetings 
 Qualifications  : 
 - Valid driver’s license 
 - Ability to safely operate a variety of equipment 
 - Strong communication skills, specifically the ability to convey information effectively 
 - Strong active listening skills, specifically taking directions and putting them into action 
 - Landscape and/or horticultural experience is considered an asset 
 Working Conditions: 
 This job is physically demanding and requires working outdoors in all weather conditions. The applicant must be able to stand/kneel for extended periods of time, lift and carry heavy objects, and do repetitive tasks. 
 Desirables: 
 - Commercial Experience 
 - Low Voltage Electrical troubleshooting 
 - Backflow Assembly Tester Certification 
 - Inventory Management 
 - Ability to read construction drawings 
 Compensation: 
 Salary will be based upon experience and competitive to the industry standards.
