 Apply for the position, In-Home Caregiver - Collyer, KS, with Guardian Home Health, LLC. today or contact Timothy Miller for more information. Posted Dec 18, 2020.

 Description 
 Part-time position in private duty homecare to include light housekeeping, some personal care services, laundry and related tasks. We are looking to hire compassionate and trustworthy people to serve a home-based client in Collyer. If you live in or near Ellis or Gove county, KS and have some personal care experience, we want to talk with you. This is a great fit for CNA's or even experienced non-certified caregivers looking to pick up some extra hours each week. Some physical demands may be required. 
 Guardian Home Health, LLC., based in Norton, KS currently serve 18 counties in NW Kansas as we have for the past 22 years. If you are interested in a flexible part-time job with the potential for additional hours, we invite you to fill out an initial application under the  "contact us/job application"  tab on our website:  www.guardianks.com  or call us at  785-622-4254  (select option 1 for Ruth or call Shelly at 785-567-6466).
