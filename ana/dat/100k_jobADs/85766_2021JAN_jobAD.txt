 Responsibilities: 
 
 Manage system development, release, and support of the software platform which provides cloud monitoring and optimization. This role is also responsible to define project scope/schedule, resource planning, and risk mitigation across the projects. 
 Lead the development team across the entire SDLC across requirement collection, analysis, system design, development, SIT, UAT, implementation, and support. 
 Work with the architecture and infrastructure team in technical design and development. 
 Manage and ensure system quality, compliance, and security controls. 
 Define the technology standard in monitoring and logging. Setup a CoE and provide standards to other technology teams. 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 At least 12 years of software development and team management experience across large-scale enterprise projects. 
 Development management experience in enterprise web platform technology. 
 Experience with application performance monitoring and log management solutions for large scale application platform written in C/C++, Java, .Net, React Native and NodeJS. 
 Experience with monitoring and log management tools such as BMC TrueSight, SolarWinds, AppDynamics, Dyntrace, Splunk, ElasticStack, etc. 
 Cloud Infrastructure environment exposure, such as Azure / AzureStack, VMWare Cloud Foundation, etc. 
 Experience with CI/CD, Continous Monitoring (CM), and Infrastructure Automation. 
 Sold SDLC management experience, with experience in Agile / Scrum would be an advantage. 
 Excellent command of written and spoken English and Cantonese. 
 Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering, Computer Science from a reputable University. 
 Leadership skill and team management experience. 
 
 If this outstanding opportunity sounds like your next career move, please send your resume in Word format to  Danny Kwan  at  resume@pinpointasia.com  and put  Cloud Reliability Development Lead  in the subject header.
