 Senior Architectural Technician 
 Based in the West Midlands our client is seeking a highly experienced Senior Architectural CAD Technician to join their expanding team. The position would suit someone with experience, knowledge and passion to work within a creative architectural practice. 
 COMPANY PROFILE - Senior Architectural Technician: 
 This multi-disciplined Architectural Practice was firm formed on the basis exceeding client expectations and providing a complete service. They take huge pride in their work and continue to provide a leading service to their clients across the UK. 
 DUTIES/RESPONSIBILITIES - Senior Architectural Technician: 
 
 Produce details Revit & CAD drawings 
 Line/team management 
 Develop 3D Structural models 
 Working closely with the wider organisation and design teams 
 Carrying out technical reviews of the design drawings 
 Supporting projects where required 
 Attending project meetings 
 
 PERSON PROFILE - Senior Architectural Technician: 
 
 High level of experience in Revit Software 
 High level of experience in a variety of CAD packages 
 Experience in working with Projects from £20M+ 
 Good knowledge and working experience of RIBA 4-6 management process. 
 Working knowledge of JCT contracts essential 
 Experience of working on High Density Residential, Commercial and Mixed-use Developments. 
 Self-motivated, punctual and analytical. 
 Multi-tasker with excellent follow up skills. 
 
 PACKAGE - Senior Architectural Technician: 
 Salary guide: Competitive 
 Please contact us for a confidential chat on this role as a Senior Architectural Technician.
