 Exciting opportunity for an enthusiastic Insight Executive to join a leading services brand operating in South Birmingham, on a 12 month fixed term contract. If you have a genuine passion for market research & the enthusiasm to constantly drive business improvement, this might just be the one for you. 
 Working within the insight team, you will be responsible for the execution of precise Market & Customer Insight whilst constantly taking steps to improve the customer experience. 
 Your day to day will involve: 
 
 Reviewing customer feedback to identify patterns & driving change and amendments where needed. 
 Analysing data from multiple sources & communicating results with clarity 
 Supporting the execution of internal research projects 
 Maintaining relationships with third-party suppliers 
 Leading the customer insight across the business and communicating the values of a customer-centric approach 
 
 Profile: 
 
 Strong analytical and data skills 
 Proven experience in a similar role 
 Exposure within a service brand 
 Strong understanding of Microsoft Packages (Excel and PowerPoint) 
 Ability to analyse and identify customer trends, context and relevance 
 
 To apply for the Insight Executive position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
