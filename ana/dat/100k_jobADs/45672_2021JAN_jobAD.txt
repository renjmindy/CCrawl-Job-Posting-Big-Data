 Assistant Store Manager 
 Starbucks (operating under Webers Coffee Company Ltd.) 
 __Orillia, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Job Summary 
 The Starbucks Assistant Store Manager reports to the Store Manager and assist in leading a team of associates in providing a unique coffee experience to customers by providing prompt service, quality beverages and products while maintaining a clean and comfortable environment. This individual is responsible for assisting the Store Manager and supervising the daily operations of the store, ensuring customer satisfaction and product quality, managing the store's financial performance as well as safety and security within the store. 
 Responsibilities and Duties 
 - Assist with leading day-to-day operations of Starbucks store 
 - Plan, identify, communicate and delegate appropriate responsibilities and practices to associates to ensure smooth flow of day-to-day operations 
 - Ensure the prompt and efficient service of food and beverages 
 - Enforce sanitary practices for food handling, general cleanliness and maintenance of store 
 - Establish, communicate, train and monitor store procedures 
 - Investigate and resolve complaints concerning food quality and service 
 - Utilize interpersonal and communication skills to lead, influence and encourage others 
 - Assist store manager with managing daily, weekly and monthly budgeting and expenses 
 - Assist store manager with hiring, training and coaching in order to develop a successful and enthusiastic team of Starbucks associates 
 - Manage scheduling in accordance with employment policies and budget targets 
 - Ensure that all time management and payroll processes are carried out accurately and timely 
 - Assist in development and implementation of department orientation and training 
 - Complete scheduled inventories, stock and requisition necessary supplies 
 - Build relationships with customers and deliver excellent customer service by engaging and connecting with them and responding to their needs 
 - Follow all cash management procedures and ensure proper cash practices are followed 
 - Monitor and manage labour to achieve and maintain store operational requirements 
 - Manage efficiently inventory, cost of goods control as well as labour costs 
 - Convey new product information and details to associates 
 - Train and prepare team to demonstrate Starbucks client service and operating standards 
 - Provide Starbucks service experience consistently for each customer 
 - Compliance with all safety regulations of assigned tasks and ensure a clean and safe working environment with active particpation in the health and safety program 
 - Other duties as assigned 
 Qualifications and Skills 
 - University degree or college diploma 
 - 3 years of leadership experience within a customer focused environment 
 - Previous Starbucks experience an asset 
 - Must possess excellent customer service skils 
 - Must be computer literate. 
 - Preferred candidate will have previous experience with point of sale (POS) software Oracle, Microsoft Word and Excel 
 - Strong verbal and written communication skills 
 - Detail-oriented and works with a high degree of accuracy 
 - Ability to multi-task in a fast paced environment 
 - Must be extremely responsible with integrity, honesty and punctuality 
 - Must be able to work flexible hours including evenings, weekends and holidays 
 - Required to move, lift, carry, pull and place objects weighing 50lbs or less without assistance 
 - Stand, sit or walk for an extended period of time or for an entire shift 
 - Reach overhead and below the knees, including bending, twisting and pulling
