 Live in Caregiver Jobs UAE 
 Posted on  February 11, 2011  by  caregiver 
 A Malate based recruitment agency accredited by the POEA is in need of caregivers for their available  Live in Caregiver Jobs . These live in caregiver jobs are for the middle east country of UAE and was approved for recruitment by the POEA last February 3, 2011. For the said live in caregiver jobs UAE, 5 applicants will be selected for placement in live in caregiver work abroad. 
 If you wish to apply for the said Live in Caregiver Jobs UAE, proceed directly to the office of Philemploy Services & Resources Inc. and submit your resume and application. If you have any question you wish to be answered regarding the said Live in Caregiver Jobs UAE, you may contact Philemploy Services & Resources Inc. thru their telephone numbers provided below. 
 Philemploy Services & Resources Inc. 
S605, A.P. Building, 
1563 F. Agoncillo cor. P. Gil Street 
Malate, Manila 
Tel Nos: 5253705 / 5671989 
Email Add: philemploy828@philemploy.com
