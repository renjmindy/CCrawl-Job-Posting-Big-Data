 Sweet Baby Jason! it's a Freelance job 
 Byte New York is looking for a Freelance CRM Copywriter in New York, NY, USA. 
 About 
 
 Three words to describe this job are  interesting role, weekly deliveries, fast paced. 
 This is a great opportunity because  you will be working on a global brand in a fun and cutting edge environment. 
 The ideal personality for this role is  hardworking, driven, grace under pressure. 
 You need to be well-equipped to handle  writing emails, newsletters, headlines, post copy, push notifications. 
 You 'd be working on  each stage of the customer lifecycle, including: Activation/Onboarding, Engagement/Retention and Re-activation/Winback. 
 The best way to describe our culture is  we work hard, no ego, talented, friendly, fun, challenging and rewarding. 
 You 'll love our office because  it's a friendly environment where music and conversation weave through a fun day of hard work.
