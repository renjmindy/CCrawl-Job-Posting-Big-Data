 FULL-TIME EXPERIENCED CABINETMAKER 
 The Custom Shop at Cardinal Fine Cabinetry is growing. 
 If you are an experienced CABINETMAKER interested in joining our team of craftspeople, we encourage you to apply. 
 WHAT WE OFFER 
 A STIMULATING ENVIRONMENT 
 Our Custom Shop is London’s premier furniture and cabinetmaking facility. Design is central to the Cardinal vision, and it’s in the Custom Shop that skilled cabinetmakers help bring custom woodworking projects to life. 
 STABILITY 
 Cardinal Fine Cabinetry is London’s oldest and most established cabinetry shop. We’ve been in business at the same location for 60 years. You can plan your future with us. 
 A GREAT WORK-LIFE BALANCE 
 We value family and personal time at Cardinal Fine Cabinetry and believe there’s more to life than work. Our shop hours are Monday to Friday, 7:30 AM to 4:00 PM. Evenings and weekends are your own. 
 COMPETITIVE WAGES AND BENEFITS 
 Cardinal Fine Cabinetry offers a competitive hourly wage, based on your years of experience and skill level. Our benefits package can be extended to include family members. 
 ON THE JOB TRAINING 
 Cabinetmaking is a life-long learning process. At Cardinal Fine Cabinetry, we are committed to keeping craft alive by offering our cabinetmakers on-the-job training. 
 WHAT YOU NEED TO APPLY 
 A MINIMUM OF 5 YEARS EXPERIENCE 
 To work in our Custom Shop, you will need at least 5 years of experience with a wide range of woodworking machinery and hand tools. You will also need to know how to read and interpret cabinetmaking drawings. 
 TEAMWORK & ATTITUDE 
 At Cardinal Fine Cabinetry, we believe that teams are made of talented individuals working together toward a common goal. Cabinetmakers are an integral part of the team, and they are expected to be detail oriented, creative, open to constructive criticism, and willing to ask questions. 
 TOOLS 
 We will supply you with all the tools you need, but you are free to bring any tools you like to your bench. Cardinal Fine Cabinetry will guarantee your tools and take care of their maintenance.
