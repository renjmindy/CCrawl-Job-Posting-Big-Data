 Broker / Customer Service Representative 
 DARSON INSURANCE SERVICES 
 __Stony Plain, AB 
 Apply Now 
 At Darson Insurance Services, we take pride in providing insurance protection for individuals and businesses in Stony Plain, Spruce Grove, Edmonton and all surrounding areas. As an insurance broker you will be responsible for providing professional, accurate and efficient service to all our clients. We are a small town brokerage firm looking to join our team with the same like minded individuals. 
 What you will need to apply : 
 
 Minimum Level 1 General Insurance Licence (Level 2 preferred) with minimum 3 years broker experience 
 Experience using TAM (The Agency Manager) broker management system, Compu-Quote and Insurance Company Portals would be an asset 
 Experience within a fast paced sales and service oriented environment 
 Exceptional customer service skills with positive attitude and commitment to finding insurance solutions to meet client needs 
 Adaptability and willingness to learn new systems, rules and coverages as the industry changes 
 Strong work ethic with ability to work alone and within a team environment 
 Working knowledge of MS Office Suite (Word, Excel, and Outlook) 
 
 What duties you will be expected to perform: 
 
 Quote and sell insurance policies to new and existing clients 
 Manage and service client files - maintain contact with clients, process renewals and endorsements, ensure client's insurance needs are being met 
 Handle incoming calls, referrals, and walk-in clients 
 Process new applications and changes on Insurance Company Portals 
 Ensure confidentiality of information and maintain client records 
 
 Expected start date: 2020-12-01
