 Dump Truck Operator Komatsu 785 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 8 month project 
 
 
 Permanent position 
 
 
 FIFO from Perth 
 
 
 2:1 roster day and night shift 
 
 
 $45.50 for days plus $4 additional for nights 
 
 
 $2500 one off payment once VOC'ed 
 
 
 $250 per swing retention payment 
 
 
 About the Role 
 Reporting to the Earthworks Supervisor, you will form an integral part of a project team responsible for developing an open-cut pit at the new Koodaideri iron ore mine-site. In this role you will be required to safely operate Komatsu 785 dump trucks. 
 The scope of works includes initial mining pre-strip and earthworks infrastructure to enable the commencement of mining operations such as access and haul roads, in pit facilities, bulk earthworks and drainage also construction of rom pad and skyway. 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Possess a high regard for the safety of yourself and others 
 
 
 Minimum 12 months+ Komatsu 785 dump trucks operating experience in either a mine-site or civil construction environment. 
 
 
 Manual C class drivers licence (minimum)
