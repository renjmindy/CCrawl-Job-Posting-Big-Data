 Coal Supervisor With S123 & G2 
 Job description 
 SUPERVISOR 
 
 Roster 7/7 D/N 
 Meals and Accommodation supplied 
 12 hour shifts, Super paid on all hours 
 
 WorkPac currently have a number of opportunities for experienced Coal Supervisors to commence work immediately. This work could start as early as next week. You will form part of and contribute to the Mine Operations Department of a Bowen Basin Mine near Collinsville. 
 An absolute commitment to working safely, complying with all site safety standards and ensuring a safe and environmentally healthy workplace is essential. It is imperative that you possess the ability and aptitude to maintain high standards of safety, equipment serviceability, housekeeping and compliance with mine working procedures. 
 To be successful in these roles you will have the following: 
 1. Minimum 2 years experience in a Supervisory role 
2. S1,S2,S3 and G2 
3. Current Coal Board Medical 
4. Current Standard 11 
5. A willingness to work rotating rosters 
6. Ability to contribute and work positively in a team environment 
7. Sound Work History 
 Candidates please note that a medical including drug and alcohol testing is required as part of the recruitment process. 
 Interested? APPLY NOW! 
 Join a company that considers you its most valuable asset. If the above sounds like an exciting challenge and you possess the required skills, click to apply.

P: 0747639704 
E: morgen.schneider@workpac.com 
 You can also check us out on our local Website: www.workpac.com/page/47639722
