 Home Manager 
 Our client is a top 15 UK Care Home provider, is recruiting for a full time Registered Home Manager to manage  a very reputable nursing and Residential Home . 
 This is a large size  home in Clydebank, Glasgow which provides palliative care, Parkinson's care, Dementia nursing and convalescence. 
 As a Home Manager you will be responsible for ensuring the highest quality of care is provided to Residents by developing and leading a caring and motivated team. 
 The successful manager will be offered an excellent salary  and great benefits. 
 Essential skills: 
 - You must be a Registered Nurse with an up to date PIN-Experience in a Nursing and Residential Home is essential 
 - Passionate about Dementia care. 
 - Experience in providing and reviewing care assessments and plans in accordance to CI requirements 
 - Experience in recruiting, developing and managing a team 
 - Experience of managing and working towards budgets 
 - Capable of increasing occupancy and marketing the home as a brand 
 -Excellent communication skills-confident working with local authorities and building new contacts 
 For your commitment and hard work, we will offer you: 
 - Competitive Salary 
 - Free PVG 
 - Professional training and ongoing career development 
 - Comprehensive benefits package 
 Must have NMC pin and previous experience in management
