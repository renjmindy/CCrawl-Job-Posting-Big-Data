 Description 
 Kukula Capital (KC), a leading Zambian venture capital firm, and the Danish Institute for Human Rights (DIHR), a global leader in the field of human rights, business and development, have launched a 3-year innovative project focusing on how business can support improvement of quality of life for Zambian employees and increase motivation and productivity. 
 Apply 
 Requirements 
 Relevant degree within the field of Business Management, Public Administration, Development Studies, Human Rights, Organizational Development or Human Resources 
Interest in business development, human rights, human resources, administration and communication 
At least 5 years' relevant work experience 
Fluency in English. Proficiency in local languages will be considered an advantage. 
Excellent skills in Microsoft Office 
Exposure to investment management will be considered an advantage 
Experience with administration of international development projects will be considered an advantage 
 Responsabilities 
 Project cycle management, including work plans, progress reports, statistical analysis, budgets and financial reports Provide logistical and technical support to implementation of project activities as well as ad hoc strategic implementation of project-related activities in KC portfolio companies Provide background research and analysis on human rights and business in the Zambian context, in close cooperation with KC and DIHR Contribute to development of manuals, guidelines and strategies to be implemented at KC portfolio companies Ensure the project activities are in compliance with human rights standards and best practice Ensure that the results of the project are communicated effectively to stakeholders in Zambia and abroad
