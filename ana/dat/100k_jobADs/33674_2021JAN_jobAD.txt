 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson Finance are delighted to be working exclusively with this Global brand, based in Warwick, to recruit a finance coordinator. The main purpose of the role is to control all process flows relating to the maintenance of pricing conditions and rebate agreements, ensuring all information and data is accurately recorded. You will ensure that rebate payments are made in line with contracts and the internal compliance standards are adhered to, and you will provide customers with efficient and accurate service levels. 
 In order to be considered, you will have a finance background, understanding balance sheet, accruals, profit and loss and month end processing. You will have Advanced Excel skills, and SAP ideally. The ideal candidate will be able to demonstrate a structured and logical approach and way of working. As part of the role, you will question mindset and be willing to learn and develop own skills for a fast track career. 
 You will ideally have experience within a multinational company, with exposure to process and systems change environments an advantage. Strong communication skills, both verbal and written, with the confidence to express opinions clearly. 
 In return, you will receive a salary of up to £25,000 plus fantastic benefits including: study support, free on site parking, 35 hour week, flexible working, staff discount, childcare vouchers, medical scheme, generous pension, 25 days holiday, life assurance and the opportunity to evolve quickly in this dynamic business. 
 Please apply direct, or call Sophie Evans, Gleeson Recruitment, for further information, on 07812 079718. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
