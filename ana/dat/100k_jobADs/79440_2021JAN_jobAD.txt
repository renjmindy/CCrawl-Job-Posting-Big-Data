 My client, a well established B2C business based in the West Midlands, is looking to recruit an SEO Executive. 
 The SEO Executive will join a well established Digital and Marketing team and will report directly into the Digital Marketing Manager. 
 The role will encompass: 
 
 Supporting outreach and content projects across website and other platforms 
 Improve SEO performance across the website through reporting, regular reviewing and enhancing the data 
 Daily monitoring and management of SEO campaigns 
 Implement, deliver and improve an SEO strategy, including campaign work 
 Develop link building/outreach campaigns 
 Keyword research and mapping of categories and themes 
 Work with the wider teams on campaigns and to improve other channels 
 
 The ideal candidate for this SEO Executive role will possess the following skills/expertise: 
 
 In-depth knowledge of SEO execution 
 Experience with Google Analytics 
 SEO keyword research and page mapping experience 
 On-site-side optimization experience including on-page and off-site content optimisation 
 Knowledge of technical SEO items 
 Knowledge of local SEO optimisation techniques 
 Experience with SEO monitoring tools such as analytics platforms, Search Console, SEMrush, aHrefs, Moz and positioning tools
