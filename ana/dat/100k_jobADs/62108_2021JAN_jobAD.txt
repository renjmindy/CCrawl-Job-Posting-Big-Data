 Mental Healthcare Assistant 
 Mental Healthcare Assistant (HCA) Weston Super Mare 
 I am looking for a highly motivated Mental Healthcare Assistant (HCA) to join an experienced team at the Long fox Unit in Weston Super Mare. 
 The Long fox Site is situated in the Grounds of Weston General Hospital offering care to service users with a serious mental illness who are in crisis. 
 Your responsibilities as a Mental Healthcare Assistant would be to work alongside the Nursing staff to implement patient care plans, you will also be responsible for performing observations with a specific focus on spotting deterioration 
 The Limited Company rates of pay are up to £17.00 per hour (Uplifts for Nights and Weekends) This would be paid through an Umbrella Company 
 Requirements for a Mental Healthcare Assistant are as follows: 
- 6 Months experience in a Healthcare / Mental Health Setting. 
 - Enhanced DBS Certificate 
- Mandatory Training Certificates 
- PMVA / MAPA Training (Preferred but not essential, training can be provided) 
- Vaccination History (Hepatitis B, MMR, TB and Varicella / Chicken Pox) 
 The Benefits of Service Care Solutions for a Mental Healthcare Assistant: 
 - £150 sign-up bonus for qualified workers 
- Dedicated and experienced one-to-one consultant support 
- Competitive rates of pay for temporary and fixed contract work 
- FREE enhanced DBS check 
- FREE occupational health checks and immunisations 
- Approved supplier to over 400 organisations nationwide 
- Loyalty reward scheme 
 If this role is something that you are interested in applying for please forward your CV to Jake.Nuttall@servicecare.org.uk
