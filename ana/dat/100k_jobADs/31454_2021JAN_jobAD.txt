 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs September 2011 
 Posted on  October 9, 2011  by  caregiver 
 For the month of September 2011, the POEA approved seven Job Orders allowing seven POEA duly licensed and accredited recruitment agencies to recruit caregivers for placement in caregiver work abroad. These  POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs September 2011  has a total of 139 caregiver jobs and offers employment opportunities in the countries of Israel, Qatar, and UAE. Specified below are the country of destinations, the names, addresses, and contact details of the recruiting agencies for the said POEA approved caregiver jobs September 2011 and the actual caregiver job vacancies for each agency.
