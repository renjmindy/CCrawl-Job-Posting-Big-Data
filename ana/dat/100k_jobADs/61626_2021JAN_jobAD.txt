 Night Manager 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Night Manager to work in an exceptional nursing home based in the Burscough, Ormskirk area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 The nursing home is a superb refurbished building situated on a corner plot enjoying views over picturesque scenery, as well as providing more than ample car parking space. 
 The service is truly a home for life due to being dual registered home catering for service users with both residential and nursing needs 
 As the Night Manager your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     You will be responsible for assisting the Registered Home Manager in managing and developing high quality services for all our residents and the management and development of the staff team particularly the night staff
 
 
 
     To instil a culture of care and continuous improvement across the service
 
 
 
     Ensure the promotion of high standards of care
 
 
 
     Ensuring the home runs in accordance with CQC guidelines and that the team are managed appropriately and that the home is run in a smooth and efficient manner on night shifts
 
 
 
 An ideal Night Manager must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Experience of working in a similar environment, you will be able to demonstrate a track record in leading, motivating, managing and developing staff
 
 
 
     You will have a clear understanding of leadership responsibilities in elderly/social care with excellent communication and interpersonal skills
 
 
 
     Ensure all care plans, risk assessment, audit and other agreed outcome measures are an integral part of the residents care and that these are monitored and updated on a regular basis in accordance with regulatory requirements
 
 
 
     Undertake regular audits to assess the quality of care given to the residents and present those findings at managers meetings
 
 
 
 **The successful Night Manager will receive an excellent salary of £17.50 per hour. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role and in return for your hard work and expertise you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 Comprehensive induction and on-going training 
 Career development opportunities 
 Excellent work environment 
 Free parking on site 
 Free uniform 
 5.6 weeks of paid annual leave 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse either RGN or RMN with a valid NMC Pin
