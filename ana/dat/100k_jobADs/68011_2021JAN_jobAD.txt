 Full-Time Engineering Sales Representative 
 Talent Search People -  Helsinki  , FI 
 Job Description 
 We are looking for an Engineering Sales Representative for a Global Leader in specialty chemical distribution. Work will be performed in home-office in Finland. 
 Job offer: - Sales and technical support/ material specification. - Field active with 10+ customer visits/week. - Territory responsible for pricing on a daily basis. - Market research and plan for implementation. - Drive projects booth locally and cross borders. - Develop/manage existing customer base. - Vendor contacts. - Keeping up to date with materials and machines development. - Supporting Customer Service ans Inside Sales at need. 
 Requirements: - Gymnasium of Higher education. - English + Finnish. - Technical sales, inside sales/customer service or technician/service technician with interest for sales. - Experience in chemicals field. - Common technical understanding. Conditions: - Multinational Company. - Professional growth. - Competitive salary. Travel cost will be compensated. Suomi.
