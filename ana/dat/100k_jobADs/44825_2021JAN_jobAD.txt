 Internship position 
 Do you want to put your marketing skills as well as your business skills to the test? Would you like to gain deep marketing experience, especially in SEO and PPC? Are you looking for challenging, interesting and varied work with a high level of responsibility? 
 And if you want to experience a dynamic tech start-up environment, then the Marketing Internship position at TidyChoice is perfect for you. 
 Role 
 We are looking for an undergraduate/graduate who wants to build broad marketing experience. You will join our team in London and work directly with the founders. You will be a critical member of the team and have a high level of responsibility. You will be involved in: 
 
 Managing pay-per-click Google, Bing and Facebook accounts 
 Analysing PPC performance and implementing changes on Google Editor 
 Using SEO tools like Semrush, Ahrefs and Surfer to analyse and improve SERP 
 Managing external link-building outreach 
 Creating content for on-page SEO 
 Implementing social media strategy and managing social media accounts 
 Creating copy for customer email marketing, newsletters and social media 
 Analysing marketing effectiveness and carrying out A/B testing 
 Supporting business projects by conducting research and analysis 
 
 Requirements for this position 
 
 Strong desire to learn PPC, SEO and social media best practices 
 Good understanding of Social Networks (Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus, etc.) 
 Excellent written and spoken English (being native speaker will be a plus) 
 Intellectually curious and willingness to take on new challenges 
 Comfortable using technology systems and carrying out analysis on Excel 
 Previous experience in marketing and social media will be also be positively considered 
 
 Benefits 
 At TidyChoice we offer challenging work, responsibility and flexibility. The terms (start, end and conditions) of the job are flexible and can be adapted to the schedule and needs of the candidate, should the candidate be ideal for this position. 
 This post is suitable for undergraduates or graduates seeking meaningful commercial experience to enhance their career prospects. 
 About TidyChoice 
 TidyChoice is a young and dynamic start-up. We will revolutionise the way people find trusted help for their homes. We are a lifeline for help at home. 
 Our website and mobile app allow customers to easily book available professionals for domestic services. Professionals market their services and use our tool to manage their time. 
 We are the AirBnB of home help with Amazon's customer service.
