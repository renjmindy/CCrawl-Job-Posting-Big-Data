 Are you an experienced Sales Support Administrator looking for your next opportunity? Want to work for a global manufacturing company? If so, this could be the perfect role for you. 
 In this permanent role, you will be providing full administrative support to the commercial team. The working hours are 8am - 4.30pm, Monday - Friday and based in new offices in Birmingham. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 To process a high number of sales orders 
 Provide product specific data sheets as requested 
 Resolving any customer queries 
 Working in line with the company to ensure 
 Inputting all information onto the system ensuring 100% accuracy at all times 
 Producing reports 
 
 About you? 
 
 Previous experience in sales support administration 
 Sales order processing experience 
 Good mathematical skills 
 Competent in production reports on excel 
 Proven ability to deliver exceptional customer service 
 Excellent communication skills 
 Be able to develop and maintain relationships 
 Meticulous attention to detail 
 Problem Solving and ability to use own initiative 
 Self-motivated and enthusiastic 
 Ability to work under pressure and priorities work 
 Ability to build strong working relationships 
 IT Literate - all Microsoft applications 
 System savvy - Salesforce experience preferred
