 WE ARE... 
 UK Car Glass  is a young, passionate web startup operating in London and Copenhagen. Our mission is to make it super simple to replace car glass and windscreens with unmatched customer care. 
 The platform provides a "on location" car glass replacement service in the UK. 
 Our goal is to provide instant and easy online service with a fast reaction time. 
 Our Goal is to deliver a five star service through a simple ordering process and high level of service in client management and communications. Together with a delivery of high quality products. 
 YOU ARE... 
 We're looking for a multi-talented all-rounder in administration, marketing, business operation and some SEO (we will invest in teaching you that if needed). You have an entrepreneurial mindset, excellent communication skills, an eye for detail, at least 5y + experience in the workforce and you are hands on and keen to make our combined vision a reality.
