 Credit Control Administrator required for my service-sector client - a challenging opportunity within an interesting and rapidly-growing business. Salary £18-20k per annum, depending on experience. 
 The Role 
 I am seeking a Credit Control Administrator for my client with their Head Office based near Wolverhampton. 
 This is an opportunity for someone to really make an impact in a dynamic environment. 
 Specifically we seek a candidate with the following skills and attributes: 
 - experienced in all aspects of chasing overdue accounts via phone, email and other legal correspondence. 
 - account query resolution 
 - cash allocation 
 - dealing with customer queries in a timely and professional manner 
 - liaising with internal managers to ensure billing issues are resolved quickly and accurately 
 - updating customer accounts 
 - good Excel knowledge 
 This is a great opportunity for a candidate with a passion for credit control and query resolution, who enjoys enjoys building strong customer relationships. 
 It is a permanent role within a busy team. 
 Please call Jo Payne ASAP to discuss on 07813 608579.
