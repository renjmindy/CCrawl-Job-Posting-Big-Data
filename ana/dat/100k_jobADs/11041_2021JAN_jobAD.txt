 My client, a well established and rapidly growing retail business based in Worcestershire, is looking to recruit a Digital Marketing Executive - Email. This role will have a strong focus on CRM and Retention. 
 The role will encompass: 
 
 Playing a  leading role  in the definition, creation and evolution of customer segmentation - measuring impact and dynamic changes across segments 
 Playing a  leading role  in the development and delivery of customer journeys, as well as the regular communications (emails) and adhoc requests, ensuring customers receive well-timed, relevant and helpful content which, where applicable, results in business-benefit 
 Play a  leading role  in the introduction of additional comms channels (SMS etc) 
 Play a  supporting role  in the development of the loyalty programme 
 
 The role may also encompass: 
 
 Playing a  supportive role  in the creation of website content. 
 
 If you have experience in line with the above - please apply below.
