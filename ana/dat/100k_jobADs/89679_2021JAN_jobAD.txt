 Gleeson Recruitment Group are recruiting for a Management Accountant, ideally CIMA Part Qualified for an exciting opportunity based in Cheltenham. 
 Role Purpose: 
 Support the Regional Business Controller with day to day financial management and analytics.. 
 Accountabilities: 
 
 Provide day to day finance management and analytics support to the regional team 
 Produce standard reporting, insightful variance analysis and support ad hoc information and analysis requests 
 Support forecasting and budgeting incl assumptions, modelling and commentary 
 Support with ad hoc commercial projects e.g pricing 
 Develop and standardise European reporting working across multiple regions and closely with the Finance team 
 Identify and deliver improvements and efficiency's within our financial processes 
 Build strong relationships across finance and regional teams 
 
 This role is available on a contract basis - Paying £25-£35k for the right candidate, dependant on experience. 
 You must be studying either CIMA or ACCA and looking to progress into your next opportunity. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
