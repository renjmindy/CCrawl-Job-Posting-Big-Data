 Merchant Banking Engineering - Software Engineer 
 Goldman Sachs Engineering is a leader in developing cutting edge systems to solve complex business problems. Working with the firm's trading, operations, finance, sales, banking and investing businesses, engineers use their scientific training to create financial products, advise clients on transactions, manage risk, identify market opportunities, and engineer technical solutions to facilitate all business activities. 
 Merchant Banking, within the Asset Management Division, raises capital for companies by directly investing in their corporate equity and debt, real estate equity and debt, and infrastructure-related assets and companies. It operates on a global platform and works in a fast-paced, exciting environment with individuals with versatile skills and a passion for investing. 
 RESPONSIBILITIES AND QUALIFICATIONS 
Merchant Banking Engineering - Front-End Developer 
 Merchant Banking within the Asset Management Division, is a world-class private equity investor, raising capital for companies by directly investing in their corporate equity and debt, real estate equity and debt, and infrastructure-related assets and companies. It operates on a global platform and works in a fast-paced, exciting environment with individuals who have versatile skills and a passion for investing. 
 Why work with Merchant Banking Engineering? 
Merchant Banking has committed to dramatically scale its Investing business, which means a significant investment in Engineering and exciting new development channels. As an Engineer in Merchant Banking you will have direct business impact and exposure, collaborating with investing and finance teams to analyze and develop solutions to their key challenges. This means you will have the opportunity to contribute to many different types of projects including: 
 
 Developing tools for financial workflow management and data collection 
 Developing data search, analysis, and visualization tools as well as formal report generation (internal and external) 
 Developing portfolio management and risk monitoring tools for our investments 
 Developing integrations with external parties and cloud based solution providers 
 Developing tools that work on desktop and mobile devices 
 
 Technology Stack; 
React, Angular, Java, Spring Boot, RabbitMQ, Attivio, Elastic Search, Kubernetes, Mongo, Sybase ASE 
 We are interested in individuals who have strong analytical and coding skills and a passion to learn finance. 
 Qualifications: 
Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Math, Physics, or other related field 
Proficient in React 
Software system design and software architecture skills 
Strong written and verbal communication skills 
 Preferred: 
Passion to learn finance - although the work performed by our team is financial in nature, applicants need not have specific financial knowledge or experience to apply 
Proficient in Java programming
