 Description : 
Self-storage is one of the fastest growing sectors of the retail sales industry. StorageMart is one of the largest independent providers of self storage for business and domestic customers in Canada, the United States and the U.K. 
 StorageMart is currently seeking an energetic and proactive Assistant Site Manager. 
 Responsibilities : 
Provide outstanding customer service by assisting customers with their storage needs 
Assist reservation and walk-in customers in renting storage units (no cold calling!) 
Sell merchandise, moving and packaging supplies 
Ensure the property is customer ready - making the property appearance safe and welcoming 
Manage, verify, balance cash drawer and prepare deposits 
 Qualifications : 
Experience in retail sales, hospitality, customer service or casual dining 
Excellent customer service skills 
Detail oriented with strong organizational and time management skills 
Willingness to work outside, clean units and maintain grounds 
Valid driver's license and vehicle/insurance required (mileage paid!) 
Reliable transportation to, from and during work 
Basic computer skills and physical ability to do the job (lifting up to 50 pounds, walking large sites, etc). 
 About StorageMart: 
StorageMart is a family owned business that sets the bar high for self-storage. Easy. Clean. Service. We live by those words. Come join our team and give your career a jump-start to a bright future. 
 We offer a competitive total compensation package that includes a base hourly rate ($13.50 - $14.00), commission opportunities, medical insurance, rich employer contributions to your retirement savings, and paid time off. 
 Committed to good work/life balance with flexible schedules. 
 StorageMart is an equal opportunity employer.
