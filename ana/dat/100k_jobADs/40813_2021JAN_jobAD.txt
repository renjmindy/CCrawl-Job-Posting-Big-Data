 Senior Project Leader 
 Job description 
 WorkPac is currently seeking an experienced Project Engineer based in Brisbane for an immediate start to lead Storm Water projects from engineering design through to execution. 
 This position would suit an experienced Project Lead with TSS and pH experience with 10-15 years demonstrated expertise across managing brownfields projects. Please note the successful applicant will require practical and implementation exposure and a history of managing people. 
 To be successful within this position you will be an experienced Project Engineer or Supervisor. You must be comfortable presenting and communicating at executive level and writing approval documentation, be an effective communicator with strong stakeholder engagement experience. You will need project controls process knowledge to manage the project effectively and be able to manage different streams of work in parallel. 
 Skills and Experience: 
 
 
 10 - 15 Years Experience Managing Brownfields Projects 
 
 
 Civil or Mechanical Engineering Qualification 
 
 
 Must have a strong people management background 
 
 
 TSS and pH experience 
 
 
 Batter stabilisation experience 
 
 
 Good interpersonal skills, stakeholder management, ability to progress multiple projects in parallel, manage risks, manage costs, manage external engineering service providers, review engineering deliverables 
 
 
 Current "C" Class Driver's License and Own Transport 
 
 
 Must be either an Australian Citizen or a holder of an Australian permanent residency visa to be eligible to apply for this position 
 
 
 Job Reference Number: BSINDJ2021106374 
 How to Apply: 
 Click on the Apply button or for more information please contact: 
 WorkPac Recruitment Co-ordinator: Ilse Rall on (07) 3621 2915 quoting the above reference number.
