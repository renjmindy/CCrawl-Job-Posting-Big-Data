 Data Analyst , Investigation and Forensic-MON17463 
 Description 
 BOMBARDIER 
 At Bombardier, our employees work together to evolve mobility worldwide - one good idea at a time. If you have a good idea, we’ll provide the environment where it will thrive and grow into a great product or customer experience. Your ideas are our fuel. 
 In your role, you will: 
- 
Develop, improve and implement the department’s data analysis roadmap; 
 - 
Identify and subsequently act on opportunities to analyze and visualize data at every phase of the investigstion process; 
 - 
Identify relevant data for current and future needs; coordinate data extraction and updates in the source systems; 
 - 
Expertly contribute to investigation and legaland advisory engagements by putting forward and executing data analysis scripts designed to increase the quality and efficiency of forensics, in accordance with latest industry standards and changing practices; 
 - 
Analyze and interpret outcomes of data analysis scripts; 
 - 
Develop data visualization reports (e.g. Power BI) to present information in a credible, compelling way; 
 - 
Help with other team tasks, as requested. 
 Qualifications 
 As our ideal candidate, 
 - 
You have Bachelor’s degree in IT, or a related field; 
 - 
You have knowledge of Excel, and data analysis and visualization tools (e.g.Power BI); 
 - 
You have knowledge of SQL, ACL, Alteryx, an asset; 
 - 
You have knowledge of SAP, Concur, Salesforce systems; 
 - 
You have two to five years of experience in business intelligence; 
 - 
You have and understanding of industry best practices, especially those related to data modelling and standardization; 
 - 
You have a keen sense of initiative, agility and adaptability in a constantly changing environment; 
 - 
You have an analytical mindset with a talent for solving complex problems and formulating recommendations; 
 - 
You have in-depth knowledge of Microsoft Office 365 tools (SharePoint, OneDrive, OneNote, Teams); 
 - 
You have curiosity and business sense; 
 - 
You have excellent written and spoken French and English; 
 - 
You are a team player with strong interpersonal skills; 
 - 
You are meticulous, organized and self-motivated; 
 Bombardier is an equal opportunity employer and encourages women, Aboriginal people, persons with disabilities and members of visible minorities to apply. 
 Whether your candidacy is moving on to the next step of the hiring process or not, we will keep you informed by email or by phone. Join us at careers.bombardier.com
