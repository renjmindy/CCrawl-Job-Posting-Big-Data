  Want Career Success? You Need To Own Every Career Experience!  
 J.T. O'Donnell 
 Work It Daily 
 November 15, 2019 
   
 When you fail to own your past career experiences (especially, the bad ones!), you give the control of your future away. Owning a past experience doesn't mean you were wrong or are accepting blame, it means you want to use the experience to your advantage in the future. 
 
 In this video,J.T. O'Donnell proves why the most successful people own all the things, good or bad, that happen to them in their careers - AND, why you should too!
