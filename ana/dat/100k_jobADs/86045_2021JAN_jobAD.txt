 __   Sports Career Game Plan Introduction 
 The sports industry is a multi-billion dollar global phenomenon, with thousands of people working daily to support its function. As part of your journey to a career in sports, you will learn essential skills in sales, facility management, operations, marketing, journalism -  whatever your desire. 
 This is different. 
 This course will teach you the steps you need to start taking right now to get hired in the sports industry. 
 Sports industry employers receive hundreds of resumes for each opening - hundreds! If you wait to start thinking about your future, you will be just another piece of paper in the pile. If you follow the recommendations of this course, starting today, you'll be one of the candidates on top. 
 These modules contain the actionable, real-world steps you need to take the sports world by storm. 
 Let's get started.
