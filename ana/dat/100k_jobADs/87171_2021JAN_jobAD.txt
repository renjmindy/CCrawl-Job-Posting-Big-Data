 At Backbase our aim is to improve the lives of people around the world. By making finance easier and more accessible, we’re helping millions of consumers and businesses manage their money in a more meaningful way. 
It’s a big mission — one that we accomplish by helping financial institutions become digital-first. On our Engagement Banking platform, banks can deliver powerful, people-centric solutions and superior digital experiences to their customers. 
As Field Marketing Manager DACH you are a driving force of this digital banking revolution in Germany, Austria and Switzerland. You lead the way by defining a marketing strategy that positions Backbase as the thought leader in Engagement Banking and as the innovation partner for banks. You will continuously keep Backbase top of mind and drive demand for our solutions. You will work closely with the DACH leadership and sales team as well as with the Amsterdam HQ marketing team. 
 
 
 __You bring 3 years of experience in Account Based Marketing, preferably for an Enterprise Software Solution; 
 
 
 __You have had success in PR and social media as a means to position the brand; 
 
 
 __You have experience with managing partner marketing; 
 
 
 Knowledge of the modern marketing stack: SalesForce.com, Marketo, LinkedIn and various PPC channels relevant in reaching B2B audiences; 
 
 
 __You have a bachelor’s degree in marketing; 
 
 
 __You are fluent in German, both written and verbal; 
 
 
 You will thrive if you have… 
 
 
 __A positive, self-starter attitude!; 
 
 
 __A hunger to make an impact; 
 
 
 __Strong analytical skills with a focus on details; 
 
 
 __Motivation to educate stakeholders; 
 
 
 __Ability to work as an integral part of a geographically-dispersed team that requires you to work a flexible schedule and occasional travel. 
 
 
 Excellent verbal and written communications, including ability to explain analysis to business audiences and effectively communicate at multiple levels in the organization

 THE JOB AT BACKBASE 
 
 
 __Based on the annual Go-to-Market-Strategy, you will create a Marketing plan to support the ambitious revenue goals for the DACH region; 
 
 
 __You will create awareness and build a thought leadership position for Backbase by creating relevant and credible stories that resonate in the press and influential Financial Services and Fintech (social) media landscape; 
 
 
 __You run integrated (account-based) demand generation programmes and own the forecasting, measurement and analysis of your campaigns; 
 
 
 __You create content based on the local market and customer needs which you uncover together with the sales and partner team; 
 
 
 __On a daily basis, you will work the partner team and partner ecosystem to build joint marketing campaigns; 
 
 
 __You will execute customer and partner communications campaigns such as webinars, user conferences, round tables; 
 
 
 __Working closely with the global marketing team you will improve conversion of all PPC channels as well as engagement and conversion of the website.
