 Security Guard (Security Cleared) – Petrolia, ON 
 Reporting to the Facilities Manager, the Security Cleared role will be responsible for the safety and security of all High Park employees and company assets while fulfilling the role of Head or Alternate Head of Security for High Park Farms. 
 Role and Responsibilities 
 Patrol premises to prevent and detect signs of:  intrusion, unauthorized access, suspicious occurrences, diversion of product and other security related issues 
Responsible for investigating and acting as security agents on behalf of Tilray 
Responsible for the monitoring of the Security Management System (video surveillance, intrusion and access controls) 
Responsible for responding to all security and first aid events 
Follow established policies and protocols to ensure regulatory compliance. 
Respond to Facility alarms, investigate and record disturbances 
Identify theft/diversion and take the appropriate action required by law and company policy 
Monitor Facility access to prevent unauthorized trespasses on Company property 
Provide 24 hour First Aid as an initial responder for all Facility patrons 
Participate in scheduled Security meetings 
Must adhere to the protocols and guidelines of Ontario Private Security and Investigative Services Act 
Monitor and identify all incoming and outgoing employees and visitors to the site. 
Participate in training seminars in all operations areas 
Act as the Security Cleared person on site as scheduled and in accordance with all expectations of security cleared personnel 
Qualifications and Education Requirements 
Must have Basic Security Training License/ valid Security Worker’s License along with a valid Occupational First Aid (OFA) Level II and CPR certification 
More than one year experience as a Security Guard 
Experience in a customer service setting is an asset 
Attention to detail in all areas of security operations 
Must be able to effectively follow and give directions 
Highly effective teamwork skills in conjunction with strong conflict management skills 
Effective communication skills with individuals at all levels of the organization 
Strong work ethic and positive team attitude 
Ability to effectively communicate verbally and in writing 
Proven ability to effectively use Microsoft Office Applications and a working knowledge of security management systems and applicable operational programs, including access controls, intrusion systems, and video surveillance systems would be an asset. 
Must have the ability to obtain Security Cleared status
