 About Edge  ** ** 
 Edge is the accessible, AI-powered, end-to-end solution that allows our users to create campaigns, track data, and automate payments, using data to power influencer campaign planning - all in one place. 
 Edge is a well-capitalized startup with more than 50 years of experience in technology and gaming. It was founded by a former sports lawyer, a former Nintendo executive and a 17-year-old tech-wizard. Edge is London based but has team members in the US, Middle East and Asia. 
 Check out our landing page for more around what we do: https://edge.gg/ 
 What you 'll do 
 
 Understand the product in-depth, the reasons for its existence, and the role it plays for our users 
 Grow with the team, you'll be a vital piece of the puzzle and help mould the future of the company, as we grow, we'll expand the team around you 
 Clearly articulate how existing and future features and services tie into the overall corporate strategy 
 You should be passionate about design and user experience; you will collaborate with our Creative/Design team on wireframes to ensure that their work meets the needs of our users 
 Guide the work of our engineering team by clarifying the product team's top priorities and areas of highest customer value 
 Drive entire product life-cycle from initial planning to release strategies 
 Define and prioritise the features of the product in collaboration with all stakeholders 
 Adjusting the features and priority dynamically based on user feedback and realignment of business priorities 
 You'll be a primary nexus of decision making, and thus must process correspondence quickly and completely whilst avoiding being the bottleneck. 
 Identifying minimum viable features to validate ideas 
 
 What we require 
 
 Experience in a similar role OR the ability to prove a passion for product management 
 Experience or a proven interest in influencer marketing 
 A passion for what you do and you're nice to hang out with 
 Self-motivated and hold a proactive attitude 
 Excellent proficiency in English 
 A penchant for asking lots of questions all the time about the things that matter most to the business 
 An understanding of industry trends relating to continuous delivery practices 
 Familiar and comfortable with agile development methodologies 
 
 Nice to haves 
 
 Experience working remotely 
 Understanding of UX/UI best practices 
 
 What we offer 
 
 Fully remote work 
 Flexible working hours 
 Unlimited holiday 
 Working with a set of passionate, driven individuals who are all about the team mentality 
 The opportunity to be early into an exciting startup and help build some really innovative tech 
 Weekly social events
