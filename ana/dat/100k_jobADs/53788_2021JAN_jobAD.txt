 Are you a Buyer with experience of procuring professional services? Have you had exposure to OJEU procurement? Are you looking for a new challenge within a hugely successful procurement functions? If so, this role could be ideal for you. 
 As a Buyer you will build strong relationships with stakeholders and have end to end management of projects in the professional services arena. You will identify opportunities to involve procurement and will be heavily involved in sourcing projects and managing contracts. You will research suppliers and market trends and run tenders to meet the needs of the business. 
 In order to carry out this role you will have the following skills and experience: 
 
 Experience of sourcing and category management 
 Ideally you will have had exposure to running OJEU tenders 
 Strong negotiation skills 
 Excellent relationship building skills 
 Be results orientated with the ability to work well under pressure 
 CIPS Qualification or working towards 
 Experience of drafting contracts 
 
 In return, you will receive a salary of circa £30,000 to £32,000 plus bonus, 25 days holiday + other excellent benefits and the opportunity to join an amazing procurement team. If you meet the above criteria, please do not hesitate to apply. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
