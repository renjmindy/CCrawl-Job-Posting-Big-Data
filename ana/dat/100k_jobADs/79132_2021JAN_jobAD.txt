 [Jobs] Current Openings at Zoomcar, a self-drive car rental company 
 Zoomcar  is the first company in India to focus on self-drive car rental, in contrast to earlier companies in India that focused on chauffeur-driven rentals. Zoomcar was founded by David Back and Greg Moran in February 2013 in Bangalore, India. As of July 2016, the company operates a fleet of over 2,000 vehicles in 9 cities across the country including Bangalore, New Delhi, Gurgaon,Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune and Chandigarh. 
 Zoomcar raises $24M in series B funding round led by Ford, to strengthen its marketplace model ZAP
