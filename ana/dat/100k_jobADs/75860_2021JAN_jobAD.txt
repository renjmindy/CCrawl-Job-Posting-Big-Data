 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently supporting a fantastic well respected Healthcare business based in Walsall who are recruiting for a Finance Manager. 
 The individual will perform an integral role in supporting the CFO with the growth and development of the Finance function. This will initially include revising/improving of the accounting and month end processes to make them more efficient and the development of weekly/monthly KPI reporting. The role will be responsible for all aspects of the accounting function and preparation of the monthly management accounts and will support in preparation of annual budgets & forecasts and weekly/monthly KPI's along with other ad hoc projects. 
 Key Duties, Finance Manager, Walsall 
 
 Month End Close Process (including preparation of management accounts pack and balance sheet reconciliations) 
 Monthly Payroll 
 Sales Ledger, including billing and credit control 
 Accounts Payable 
 Finance processes (including policies & procedures) 
 Management Information (including weekly/monthly KPI's) 
 Budgeting & Forecasting 
 Preparation for year end statutory audit 
 Undertake additional project work as required 
 Assist in completion of Monthly Management Accounts, within prescribed deadlines 
 Support development of reporting weekly/monthly key performance indicators (KPIs) 
 Assist in preparation of annual Budget and longer term Plan/Forecast 
 
 Key Skills & Experience, Finance Manager, Walsall 
 
 Qualified Accountant (ACA, ACCA, CIMA) or QBE Finance Manager 
 Able to take on all aspects of accounting function 
 Experience in a small to medium sized business with first hand experience of accounting systems (preferably Sage) 
 Ability to develop (finance) systems and processes 
 Must be a strong and effective communicator. 
 Must be highly IT literate with advanced spreadsheet skills.
