 Internship Swiss German speaking Delegate Marketing interns 
 Management Events Oy -  Uusimaa  , Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 Management Events offers exciting INTERNSHIP OPPORTUNITY FOR SWISS GERMAN SPEAKING STUDENTS OR GRADUATES at our Swiss delegate marketing team located at our Helsinki office. 
 Our internship period begins at the end of January 2013 and we are now looking for a pro-active and effective future talent. The ideal duration of the internship is 5-6 months and it includes a monthly salary. 
 Your role: 
 Our team at Delegate Marketing is responsible for pre marketing our events as well as inviting and committing top decision makers from event target companies to our invitation only events taking place in the Switzerland. You will help and assist the Delegate Marketing team in various customer service and database tasks during the event process. 
 Requirements: 
 An ability to learn and enjoy the internship at our company will require a good working moral, an interest to take responsibility over your tasks, positive attitude, good basic skills with Excel and Word, attention to detail as well as good service attitude. We expect you to speak and understand Swiss German and  have good working English . We expect from each other and thus from all the interns a positive spirit, customer service attitude, proactive working style, good social skills and ability to prioritize your tasks. 
 We offer: 
 Interesting and challenging role in an international environment where we enjoy working together, share multiple benefits and common success 
 In addition to monthly salary, lunch and sports vouches and a bonus model based on your performance 
 In the beginning of your internship, we provide you a throughout tutoring 
 Possibility to continuous development and learning.
