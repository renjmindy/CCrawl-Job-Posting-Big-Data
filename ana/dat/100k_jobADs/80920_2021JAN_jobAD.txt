 General Hand/Labourer 
 Job description 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Register or apply with us for ongoing access to a wide range of temporary and permanent jobs for all skill levels. 
 About the Role 
 This role is a great opportunity to join a large-scale Rail Operation???s team and contribute in the safe and successful delivery of operational activities. Working a 4 days on, 4 days off, 4 nights on, 4 days off roster and reporting to the Superintendent Operations, you will be responsible for the following and more: 
 Transport personnel as required by light vehicle. 
 Assist with minor maintenance activities to support the safe operation of the rail network 
 Assist application and release of handbrakes for trains on the mainline (in addition to any other instructed securing requirements) 
 Liaise with Asset Health for fault finding and train recovery (within qualifications) Maintain the condition of the response depot or hub. 
 Drive a light vehicle to inspect areas along the designated area of the network. 
 Role Requirements 
 Literacy skills (min WA grade 10 or equivalent 
 Basic mathematics 
 C class drivers licence (manual) 
 Strong Safety Awareness 
 ??? Good communication skills 
 ??? Understanding of safety and environmental principles governing the work environment 
 ??? Demonstrate a high level of risk perception 
 It will also be beneficial if you have: 
 Previous Rail Operations experience 
 Heavy haulage experience 
 Mechanical Aptitude 
 Experience in high risk work 
 Why work for WorkPac? 
 Looking after our employees is important to us. Our Employee Benefits program offers benefits across health, private insurance, financial services, travel and more. 
 We have a team of dedicated recruiters, most of which have in field knowledge of the industry, who make continuous contact to ensure you are happy with not only WorkPac's service but our clients. We are committed to ensuring you are happy and safe, you are our most valuable asset! 
 WorkPac have over 45 offices throughout Australia, this means you have the flexibility to work anywhere in Australia with us. As WorkPac has such a large footprint, we know our local areas and are always helping our local community. 
 Apply Online Today! 
 At WorkPac, we recognise we are strengthened by diversity. We are committed to providing a work environment in which everyone is included, treated fairly and with respect. We are an Equal Opportunity employer and we encourage applications from women and Indigenous people.
