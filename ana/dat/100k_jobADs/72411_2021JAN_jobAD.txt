 Claudia Friedlander Publishes Innovative Career Guide For Classical Singers   Reply 
 by  peabodyinstitute  •  News  • Tags:  alumnni ,  clarinet ,  voice 
 Claudia Friedlander (  MM ‘95, Clarinet, Voice  ) released her second book,  The Singer’s Audition and Career Handbook , which was created as a comprehensive guide to the training, audition technique, and professional development essential for launching and sustaining a rewarding career in classical singing. The book includes insights from nearly seventy prominent performers, educators, and opera industry professionals, as well as a Forward by acclaimed mezzo-soprano Joyce DiDonato.
