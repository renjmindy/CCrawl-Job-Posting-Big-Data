 Experienced Dump Truck Operator 
 Job description 
 
 
 
 Job Highlights $47.68/hour Flat Rate 7/7 Roster ONGOING POSITION About the Role WorkPac are currently seeking skilled and experienced Dump Truck Operators to commence at a Mine near Dysart. The successful candidate will be on a 7/7 Roster (Straight Days, Straight Nights). This client has CAT 793s and CAT 797 on site, the successful candidate would require to be passed out on a minimum of a CAT 789. Duties & Responsibilities Operation of CAT 793 or CAT 797 Completion of Risk Assessments Complete Pre-start checks on machinery 12 Hour Shifts. Follow Company as well as Site Procedures & Policies to a safe standard.
