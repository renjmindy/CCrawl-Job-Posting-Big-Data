 Caregivers Job Kuwait 
 Posted on  November 2, 2011  by  caregiver 
 A Kuwaiti employer, thru the assistance of a POEA accredited recruitment agency in Pasig City, is seeking to employ female caregivers who are qualified for  Caregivers Job Kuwait . These caregivers job Kuwait have a Job Order which was approved by the POEA last October 21, 2011 and has vacancies for 10 caregivers who will qualify for the said caregiver work abroad. 
 To apply for the said Caregivers Job Kuwait, you may submit your application and resume directly to the office of A.D.M. International Manpower Services Co. or you may send your COVER LETTER and COMPREHENSIVE RESUME (in .doc or .docx format)  to recruitment.adm07@gmail.com , and put the correct subject in the subject bar (ex. applying as nurse? subject: rnAPP). If you have any question regarding the said caregivers job Kuwait, please contact A.D.M. International Manpower Services Co. thru their telephone or mobile numbers specified below.
