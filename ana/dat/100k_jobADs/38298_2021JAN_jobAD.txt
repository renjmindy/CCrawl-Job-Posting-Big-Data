 Outpatient/ACU Nurses 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Outpatient/ACU Nurse to work in an exceptional hospital based in the Croydon, South London area. 
 As a Outpatient/ACU Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This hospital offers services to the local community such as Outpatient Consultation, Day Case Surgery, Pharmaceutical Services and Diagnostics - Imaging and Endoscopy 
 An ideal Outpatient/ACU Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Sound written and verbal communication skills
 
 
 
     Proven ability to work effectively in a team environment and independently as required
 
 
 
     Surgical Experience would be advantageous
 
 
 
     A warm, considerate and empathetic character
 
 
 
     The ability to make decisions and use your initiative
 
 
 
     Strong communication skill
 
 
 
     Passion to deliver excellent care in a rewarding and what can be emotionally challenging environment
 
 
 
     A flexible and positive attitude
 
 
 
 
 **The successful Outpatient/ACU Nurse will receive an excellent salary from £30,000 to £33,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     25 days Annual Leave + Bank Holidays
 
 
 
     Private Healthcare
 
 
 
     Contributory Pension Scheme
 
 
 
     Life Assurance
 
 
 
     On-going Training and Development
 
 
 
     Free off-site parking
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as an RGN with a valid NMC pin
