 Title: Digital Specialist 
Department: Community Engagement & Marketing 
Reports To: Sr. Manager, Marketing 
 
 Sunnybrook Health Sciences Centre is inventing the future of health care with a focus on trauma, brain, cardiac and women and babies. Sunnybrook treats the most critically ill patients in Ontario, pioneering life-saving innovations that are changing patient outcomes around the world. Sunnybrook Foundation exists to raise the awareness and funds required to ensure that Sunnybrook Health Sciences Centre achieves its mission. 
 The Digital Specialist supports the Foundation’s marketing, fundraising and stewardship initiatives across all digital platforms including website, email, social media and digital advertising. Reporting to the Senior Manager, Marketing, the Digital Specialist will collaborate with internal teams especially the Digital Fundraising Specialist and external partners to design, develop and deliver an exceptional digital donor experience. 
 Key accountabilities: 
 
 Design, develop and implement digital solutions that meet business objectives including email, website, donation pages, and social media 
 Report on digital results and provide recommendations for improvement 
 Manage and optimize Foundation website 
 Provide insights to assist with the optimization of the CMS for email, web and digital assets 
 Work with internal and external partners as required to troubleshoot technical issues 
 
 Website 
Includes Foundation website, campaign and event microsites 
 
 Update existing sites and landing pages 
 Design and build new sections, landing pages and microsites 
 Implement Google Tag Manager(GTM) on all new pages and ensure GTM standards are maintained 
 Liaise with hospital digital team and external partners for development needs when required 
 Design and build donation forms as required 
 Analyze, track and report on performance 
 
 Email 
 
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop email campaigns 
 Design, build and deploy emails through Luminate, Mail Chimp and Sales Force Marketing Cloud 
 Analyze, track and report on performance as required 
 Track and report on usage rates for email deployment software to meet annual quotas 
 
 Digital advertising and organic social media 
 
 Design and build creative assets for digital advertising and organic social media 
 Apply UTM codes based on established parameters 
 Liaise with hospital digital team and external partners as required 
 
 Miscellaneous 
 
 Respond to digital requests, confirming expectations and deliverables as necessary to meet requirements and deadlines 
 Provide digital design and development support for new initiatives 
 
 Skills and abilities required: 
 
 Excellent time management skills with ability to juggle deadlines and self-manage 
 Collaborative, enjoys working with teams or independently 
 Strong communicator: ability to explain technical issues to team and enjoys sharing knowledge 
 Is open to feedback, seeks additional information and clarification and acts upon recommendations contributing to individual and organizational success 
 Embraces change and looks for opportunities for continuous improvement personally and for the organization 
 Committed to learning; stays informed on best practices, new tools and technology in digital design 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 2-4 years of experience in a digital role 
 Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite: Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver 
 Strong experience working with email deployment platforms; preferably Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Luminate, and Mailchimp 
 Experience with Formstack , NetCommunity and Raiser’s Edge would be of benefit 
 Working knowledge of HTML5, CSS and CMS, Google analytics and Google Tag Manager, JavaScript 
 Experience creating content for social media platforms and familiarity with Hootsuite, Sprout Social or social media management platforms. Familiarity with Adobe After Effects would be an asset 
 Understanding of the effective use of typography, layout and photography in digital channels 
 Experience working on digital marketing campaigns would be an asset 
 Working knowledge of accessibility standards
