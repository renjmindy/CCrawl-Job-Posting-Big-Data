 Female Caregiver Job Abroad 
 Posted on  February 9, 2011  by  caregiver 
 Two Job Orders were recently approved by the POEA that allows two POEA accredited recruitment agencies to recruit Female applicants for Caregiver Job Abroad. These  Female Caregiver Job Abroad  are for the Middle East country of Kuwait and was approved by the POEA last February 1 and 3, 2011. Specified below are the number of vacancies in each recruiting agency for female caregiver job abroad. 
** 
Mayon International Trading Corp.** --------------- 50 caregiver job abroad 
 Global Care International Manpower Services  --- 20 caregiver job abroad 
 To apply for the said Female Caregiver Job Abroad, proceed directly to the office of either Global Care International Manpower Services or Mayon International Trading Corp. and submit your resume and application. If you have any question regarding the said Female Caregiver Job Abroad, you may contact the recruiting agencies thru their telephone numbers provided below. 
 ** 
Mayon International Trading Corp.** 
2nd Floor, Building 1 
M.H. del Pilar Street, Malate 
Manila 
Tel No: 4684203 
Mobile Nos: 09157414295 / 09228644685 
** 
Global Care International Manpower Services**

Tel Nos: 5247911
