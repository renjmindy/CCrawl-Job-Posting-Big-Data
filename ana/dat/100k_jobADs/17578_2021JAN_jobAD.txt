 Call Center Representative - Financial FT Temp-To-Hire 
   
   Workway, Inc.   
 __Website    __@workway 
 As a Call Center Rep, you will be assisting members with their financial transactions and/or needs over the telephone as well as cross-selling products and services that will benefit the member.  In this position, you have the power to create an outstanding member service experience simply by the conversation that you have with the member.  Your specific duties in this role will include: 
 
 Answers calls in a fast and attentive manner 
 Cross sells Credit Union products and services 
 Assists customers in a friendly manner; performs administrative tasks and cross trains in Teller duties based upon business needs. 
 
 Job Requirements 
 As a Call Center Representative, you must be motivated and reliable, with strong analytic and problem-solving skills. It is also important that you display excellent communication, interpersonal, and customer service skills and the ability to articulate and explain information clearly.  Specific qualifications for the position include: 
 
 High school diploma or equivalent 
 Previous financial institution experience 
 Previous call center experience helpful but not required 
 Strong sales/service skills 
 Previous loan application experience 
 
 Let one of our recruiters assist you in finding and preparing for interviews with our clients. Whether you are interested in a direct hire, temp-to-hire, or temporary position, Workway can help you to put your best foot forward. Contact us today!
