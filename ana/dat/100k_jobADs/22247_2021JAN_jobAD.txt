 About Figments 
 We're building the next version of the WWE, for esports. We're doing this by creating brand new virtual characters with fictional storylines who play esports on Twitch and YouTube. 
 About the role 
 Skills:  Unreal Engine 
 We're looking for a  Character Animator ! You'll partner with our cofounders to design and animate our characters, bringing them to life in the most fun and exciting ways across multiple media platforms. This is an incredible opportunity to make a meaningful impact on the future of real-time animated livestreaming. If you dream of directing original characters that connect deeply with a large global audience, we can make this happen together. 
 Your responsibilities will include: 
 
 collaborate with story team to develop and launch new characters and content formats 
 pose and animate characters for the daily and weekly creation of content on platforms like YouTube, Twitch, Instagram and TikTok 
 clean, solve, retarget and edit data for delivery including motion editing 
 assist with the operation of mocap system, constructing virtual sets, streaming realtime data, prepping actors, and aiming virtual cameras when needed 
 basic character rigging, texturing and surfacing 
 writing scripts to maintain and advance the mocap pipeline 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 expertise in Unreal Engine, Motion Builder and Maya 
 portfolio of character animation including short(s) you've directed 
 you're a pop culture fanatic and love video games 
 
 Bonus: 
 
 expertise in Photoshop, After Effects, graphic design and/or comics 
 you've created viral content on YouTube, Twitter, Instagram, TikTok, and/or Snap Location: Los Angeles, CA or remote 
 
 Technology 
 We're a team of problem solvers specializing in character-driven storytelling. We're building tools that empower actors, writers, video editors, graphic designers and animators to tell amazing fictional stories on new media platforms. These tools combine esports broadcast and livestreaming video/audio pipelines with next generation motion capture technology.
