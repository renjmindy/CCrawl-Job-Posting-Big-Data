 Cash/Banking Assistant 
 Cash/Banking Assistant 
 
 Location 
 
 Birmingham, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Temporary 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £20000 - £24000 per annum 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Paul Steele 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 paul.steele@gleeson-rg.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 7467887_1600782595 
 
 Published: 
 
 4 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-10-22 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group is currently working alongside a Servicing Organisation based near Birmingham City Centre as they look to recruit a Cash/Banking Assistant to join them on a Temporary basis. 
 Principle Accountability's are as follows: 
 
 Daily cash flow forecast 
 Processing and allocating payments inc BACS, CHAPS, Cheques, & Direct Debits 
 Processing petty cash for centres 
 Processing and reconciling corporate card statements 
 Daily bank journals and reconciliation 
 Centre banking reconciliation 
 Phone payments & reconciliation 
 Chip & Pin reconciliations 
 Payments and reconciliation 
 Treasury month end reporting 
 Balance sheet reconciliations 
 Queries from centres and other departments 
 Ensuring all controls are maintained 
 Providing input to continuous improvement within the department 
 
 Requirements 
 
 Proven experience of bank recs and cash management 
 System skills including intermediate excel. 
 Excellent communication skills and ability to identify issues and use initiative to solve problems 
 Self-motivated team player with willingness to help others and go above and beyond their formal role. 
 Exercise the up most discretion when dealing with confidential information 
 Good arithmetic skills 
 Organised and methodical approach to work 
 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
