 Adjunct Faculty - (MLT) 
 Apply for the position, Adjunct Faculty - (MLT), with Barton Community College today or contact Mary Jane Felke for more information. Posted Dec 4, 2020.

 Description 
 The  Barton Community College  Medical Laboratory Technician Program is seeking applications for a replacement full-time, 9-month faculty position.  Reports to the Director of the MLT Program and is responsible for teaching assigned courses, the assessment of student achievement, and selected program duties.  Qualifications: current MT(ASCP) or MLT(ASCP) certification or equivalent.  Experience in clinical microbiology preferred. Review of completed applications begins immediately. Position open until filled. EEO/AA.
