 Have You Been Injured on the Job? 
 You need an Experienced Admiralty Attorney on Your Side! The Experienced George Law Firm LLC - Maritime Law Group attorneys will Fight Hard to get You the Compensation that You Deserve. 
 Whether you are a Jones Act sailor, a Longshore or Harbor worker, or Work Offshore, if you have been Injured on the Job you may be Entitled to Compensation. The George Law Firm LLC - Maritime Law Group attorneys will help Protect Your Rights with Aggressive Representation to get you the Compensation that You Deserve. Call now for a free consultation to find out what we can do for you! 
 Call the George Law Firm - Maritime Law Group 888-240-8510 
 Are you Worried you may lose your Mariners License? 
 If you have Failed a Drug Test, Refused to Submit to a Drug Test or Been Involved in an Accident or Have Been Charged with the Use or Addiction to Dangerous Drugs or Alcohol under 46 U.S.C. 7703, the U.S. Coast Guard will seek to revoke your License and Merchant Mariner's Document. Don’t’ face the USCG Administrative Judges alone! 
 The Experienced George Law Firm LLC - Maritime Law Group attorneys know how hard it is to obtain your Mariner’s Credential because we are Licensed Mariners Too! The George Law Firm LLC - Maritime Law Group attorneys understand how to navigate the USCG Administrative Law Process and that knowledge can make the difference between a successful license recovery, or a permanent loss. 
 Call the George Law Firm - Maritime Law Group 888-240-8510 
 Let us put our experience to work for you! 
 We serve the Entire United States and US Territories
