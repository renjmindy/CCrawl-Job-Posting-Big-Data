 Animal Care Attendant 
 Small Animal Veterinary Hospital 
 __Scarborough, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Join an inclusive and supportive team!  Milliken Animal Clinic is a full service veterinary hospital for dogs and cats. We provide empowering care for pets and their human families to live better lives. We are looking for an Animal Care Attendant (ACA) for a consistent full time or part time position. We hire based on a positive attitude. The rest comes later. We are flexible on finding a full time OR part time ACA, so we can focus on finding the right person first. 
 ACAs are a vital part of our support team! As an ACA, you are responsible for a major portion of the care of our patients, including feeding and exercise. Throughout your day, you will maintain the cleanliness of the hospital. You will play a key role in creating a welcoming atmosphere for our clients. 
 We offer you the opportunity to learn about veterinary medicine, pet nutrition, client service, and how to operate a hospital effectively. We do that with teamwork, continual learning, and mutual support and teaching. We have fun at work! 
 Are you interested in making a difference while receiving mentorship? We offer pet parent discounts on medical care and products, continuing education, vacation and sick days, and career growth opportunities.
