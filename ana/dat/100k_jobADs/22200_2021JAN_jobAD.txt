 Caregiver Jobs Abroad for Israel 
 Posted on  December 7, 2010  by  caregiver 
 For the start of December 2010, the POEA approved two job orders that will provide employment opportunities to Filipinos who are qualified for  Caregiver Jobs Abroad . These caregiver jobs abroad is for Israel and were approved for recruitment by the POEA last December 2 and 3 for two POEA accredited recruitment agencies located in Malate, Manila and Paligsahan in Quezon City. Specified below are the number of vacancies for each caregiver jobs abroad for Israel. 
 Rise Manpower Services  ------------------- 15 vacancies 
 JAO International Manpower Services  --- 60 vacancies 
 If you wish to apply for any of the said Caregiver Jobs Abroad for Israel, visit the office of Rise Manpower Services or JAO International Manpower Services and forward your resume and application. For any questions related to the said caregiver jobs abroad for Israel you may contact the recruiting agency directly through their landline numbers or email address given below.
