 Job description 
 Rigger / Labourer 
 About The Company 
 Our client offers one of Australia's most complete shoring, propping and traffic product ranges, with high quality equipment and components fit for any construction undertaking. 
 About The Role 
 We are currently seeking BASIC Riggers to join the team on a 3-month contact with the potential to move into a permanent position. 
 The successful applicants will be motivated, enthusiastic team players with a strong work ethic and the right abilities and attitude to succeed and progress. 
 Duties include (but not limited to) 
 
 
 Rigging operations 
 
 
 Yard duties such as clean ups, pre-starts and product manufacture 
 
 
 General labouring duties 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 BASIC Rigging Ticket 
 
 
 Forklift Ticket 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 Civil Construction Background 
 
 
 Physically Fit 
 
 
 Why work for WorkPac? 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
