 Are you currently studying toward your CISI or IOC? Are you looking to join an innovative, forward thinking Financial Services organisation with a great office atmosphere? 
 A Financial Services firm in Birmingham is looking to recruit an Investment Administrator on a permanent basis. (Full job spec can be provided) You will be providing crucial administration support to the senior members of the business, with opportunity for further development. Depending on experience you can expect a salary between £26,000 - £30,000 per annum. 
 The Investment Administrator will be responsible for: 
 
 Production of client statements and valuations 
 Processing investment instructions as per client request 
 Liaising with third party providers 
 Reconciliation of stock and cash positions 
 Settlement of investment instructions 
 Preparing presentations for senior members 
 Organise client meetings and travel requirements 
 Market research 
 
 An ideal Investment Administrator will be able to display: 
 
 Motivation, eager to learn and show a good use of their own initiative 
 Excellent problem solving abilities 
 Ability to learn quick in a fast paced environment/ industry 
 Clear communication skills 
 Ability to pick up duties quickly 
 
 Ideally you will be working towards or have obtained a CISI investment operations certificate or IOC with a minimum of 3-5 years industry experience. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
