 Electrical and Instrumental Technician 
 AFIMAC Canada 
 __Ontario 
 Apply Now 
 AFIMAC Canada is actively hiring experienced Electrical and Instrumental Technicians for assignments throughout Ontario. 
 This position may require you to work 12 hour shifts, 5 working days per week when assigned to a project. 
 ***Please read the full job posting and apply on Indeed. Calls to our AFIMAC Head Office will result in your application being denied. After reviewing your resume, we will reach out to qualified individuals VIA email to schedule an interview. *** 
 You will earn: 
 
 $40.00 per hour before overtime 
 $60 per hour during overtime (after 44 hours a week) 
 $30 per day non-taxed meal allowance 
 Paid Travel (up to 4 hours) 
 Hotel Accommodations Provided (when required) 
 
 **Duties & Responsibilities: ** 
 
 Troubleshoot, repair, maintain, calibrate, alter / modify and make installations to the facility’s electrical and instrumentation systems, in a professional and timely fashion, as required. 
 Perform service functions as directed by Management for all electrical equipment on site that is at or below 600V. This includes but is not limited to: 
 Install, repair and maintenance of various types of electrical equipment and components including AC. motors, DC motors, Control valves, Pressure sensors, Level sensor, temperature sensors, limit switches 
 Maintenance of above listed equipment is for both electrically classified locations (Class I, Division 1&2) and non-classified areas, maintenance of rated enclosures and purge systems 
 Maintain and trouble shoot electrical power distribution systems, up to 600 Volt AC, Maintain Motor control centers, change fuses, perform lockouts 
 Maintain high Housekeeping standards at all work areas 
 Recording schedule and work order feedback 
 Recording preventive maintenance and equipment history data 
 Communicating job status upon completion or end of shift 
 Use of man-lift on occasion 
 
 **Skills & Qualifications: ** 
 
 Licensed Ontario Industrial Electrician with over 5 years in trade experience 
 Good Computer operating skills 
 Familiarity with logic solvers and controllers and HMIs. 
 Familiarity with robotic equipment is an asset 
 Familiarity with HVAC controls is an asset 
 Familiarity with CMMs is an asset 
 Possibly required to operate a mobile platform man-lift or work at heights 
 Strong verbal and written communication skill 
 Ability to work to established priorities and meet deadlines 
 Valid G Class Driver's Licence. 
 The position requires a self-starter and highly motivated person capable of working with minimal supervision. 
 
 Tool Requirements: 
 
 Hand Tools 
 Multimeters 
 Testers
