 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for May 2010 
 Posted on  May 31, 2010  by  caregiver 
 For the month of May 2010, the POEA had approved four job orders that offers caregiver employment opportunities to Filipino caregivers who are qualified for caregiver jobs abroad. The said  POEA approved caregiver jobs for May 2010  are for four POEA accredited recruitment agencies and carries a total of 185 caregiver jobs vacancies for abroad.  The said caregiver jobs vacancies are for the country of Israel which has a total of 88 caregiver jobs vacancies and the remaining 97 caregiver jobs vacancies are for the European country of Cyprus. Specified below is the list of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for May 2010 which includes the countries of destination, the names of the recruiting agencies, the date the job order was approved, and the number of applicants to be selected for each POEA approved caregiver work abroad. 
 Israel --- CPM Manpower Services Inc. -------------- 5/5/2010 -- 30 
Israel --- Provident Overseas Placement Agency ---- 5/7/2010 -- 28 
Cyprus -- Unlimited Manpower Resources Inc. ------- 5/13/2010 -- 97 
Israel --- Mariposa International Services Co. Inc. --- 5/21/2010 -- 30 
 If you are interested to apply for any of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for May 2010, you should visit the office of the recruiting agency and submit your application and credentials. For inquiries on any of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for May 2010, you can contact the recruiting agency involved through their contact infos which will be provided upon request.
