 My client, a market leading retail business based in the West Midlands, is looking to recruit a Group Senior Content Manager. 
 This role is initially offered as a 12 month contract. 
 A fantastic opportunity, the Group Senior Content Manager will lead on a broad range of content activities, including: Social, Video, Copy, TV and Radio. A key leadership role, this position will manage a team of seven. The team produces output across web, social, in-store and advertising. 
 The ideal candidate will be able to demonstrate a strong track record of delivering engaging and effective content campaigns across the channels outlined above. You will be highly commercial and results orientated, as well as demonstrating a strong understanding of data and analytics. Strong man-management and stakeholder management skills are essential. 
 Role located in the West Midlands - whilst weekly attendance is essential, significant flexibility will be considered. 
 Highly competitive package.
