 Canada Jobs in Caregiver 
 Posted on  September 29, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This agency for Canada jobs in caregiver provides placement of live-in nannies and caregivers, live-out nannies and caregivers, housekeepers, companions, nurse's aide's and resident care aides. They also specialize in the placement of qualified foreign Live-In Nannies and Canada jobs in caregiver applicants under the Citizenship and Immigration Canada and Service Canada's Live-In Caregiver Program. 
 This agency for Canada jobs in caregiver is a family owned and family oriented establishment. It was established and incorporated in British Columbia in 1997 and Ontario in 2007 and is a Canadian nanny and Canada jobs in caregiver referral service. 
 To apply for the said Canada jobs in caregiver, kindly submit photocopies of the following documents: 
 
 3 Photos that include the following: (1 full body, 1 photo with the person you are caring for and 1 passport size); 
 Passport; 
 Transcript of School Records; 
 Diploma, if applicable; 
 Certificates, if applicable; 
 Letters of Reference, if applicable; 
 Employment Letter of current or previous employers, if applicable; 
 Dear Family Letter (A letter that introduces yourself to potential employers in Canada. This would be the best way to showcase to prospective employers the reason they would employ you. In addition, this would the best tool to showcase your English language skills.) 
 
 If interested in the said Canada jobs in caregiver, kindly submit photocopies of the above mentioned documents to  apply@aclassnannies.com . For more info on the said Canada jobs in caregiver, you may log on to their website at  www.aclassnannies.com  or send your inquiries to  care@aclassnannies.com
