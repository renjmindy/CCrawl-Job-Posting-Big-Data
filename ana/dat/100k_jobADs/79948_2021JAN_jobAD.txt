 Professional, Engineering - Specialized Aircraft-MON17483 
 Description 
 BOMBARDIER 
 At Bombardier, our employees work together to evolve mobility worldwide - one good idea at a time. If you have a good idea, we’ll provide the environment where it will thrive and grow into a great product or customer experience. Your ideas are our fuel. 
 In your role, you will: 
- 
Act as Project Engineering and Integration focal for a challenging missionized aircraft program 
 - 
Act as the representative of engineering with respect to Programs as well as the interface with the client technical/PMO representatives and suppliers 
 - 
Create schedules, milestones, budgets for technical activities 
 - 
Plan and monitor tasks to achieve project objectives; from technical activities to estimating levels of effort, to schedule tasks and their interdependencies 
 - 
Lead and drive the involvement of multidisciplinary stakeholders to find solutions to technical roadblocks, risks and challenges 
 - 
Ensure that all technical activities are harmonized and that the delivery of all contract requirements are done as per the schedule 
 - 
Coordinate engineering resources to execute on the plan as well as identifying risks and facilitating the resolution of issues 
 - 
Communicate with stakeholders (customer, management, team, resources, supplier) such that there is solid alignment and coordination 
 - 
Be accountable for the submission of all deliverables 
 - 
Manage all technical requirements documentations 
 - 
Manage schedule, facilitate and coordinate key milestones activities in support of program/project (internal and external) 
 - 
Responsible to coordinate the supplier activities. 
 Qualifications 
 As our ideal candidate, 
 - 
You hold a Bachelor degree in Engineering; a Masters would be considered an asset. 
 - 
You have more than 5 years of project management experience 
 - 
You have more than 5 years of technical engineering experience; experience in avionics, electrical, or RM&S would be considered an asset 
 - 
You possess an excellent understanding and relevant experience in the aircraft development environment from conceptual design to aircraft certification 
 - 
You have an excellent understanding of aircraft systems, structures and their interactions 
 - 
You demonstrate strong leadership and communication skill such that you are able to communicate, align and influence stakeholders 
 - 
You are able to manage time and prioritize tasks effectively while working toward aggressive deadlines; 
 - 
You have a strong technical knowledge of aircraft, aircraft systems and special mission equipment; 
 - 
You have knowledge of Controlled Goods Regulations (CGR), ITAR and previous experience in government and military flight operations exposure would be considered an asset; 
 
 You may be required to travel (post-Covid) and work extended hours when required; 
 You are eligible to view and handle CGR material.
