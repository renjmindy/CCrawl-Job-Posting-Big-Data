 German Speaking Marketing Assistant 
 
 Location 
 
 Telford, Shropshire

 Marketing and Digital

 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £20000.00 - £30000.00 per annum

 Consultant: 
 
 
 My client, a leading B2C business based in Telford, is looking for a German Marketing Assistant to support the growth in new territories. Due to recent expansion, this is a newly created role and this is a really exciting time to join the business. You will be responsible for assisting the wider marketing team with developing localised marketing campaigns across a number of channels to drive sales and brand awareness in Germany. 
 The Role: 
 
 Assist the Marketing team with translating marketing campaigns into German, ensuring blogs are SEO optimised, and content is localised. 
 Assisting with the content creation and copy for the German website, email campaigns and social media. 
 Assisting the Paid Media Manager with translating paid search campaigns into German. 
 Be responsible for the German Customer Service, ensuring all customer queries are dealt with. 
 Ensuring all product information is translated correctly, and assisting with content maintenance and upload for company website. 
 
 The Candidate: 
 
 Fluent in German and English. 
 Excellent communication skills, written and verbal. 
 Previous Marketing experience is required. 
 Good team player, flexible and pro-active. 
 Excellent organisation and prioritising skills.
