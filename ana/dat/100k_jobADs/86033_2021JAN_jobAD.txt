 A Credit Control Supervisor is required for large professional services organisation based in Birmingham City Centre. Managing a small Credit Control team effectively, ensuring that targets are met, reporting and hands on Credit Control. 
 The role sits within the Accounts Receivable function with a friendly working environment. 
 Description 
 
 Manage the credit control team effectively ensuring that collection targets are met 
 Reviewing team and individual performance 
 Reviewing processes to ensure effective credit management 
 Ensuring accurate recording of all credits 
 Reporting and analysis 
 Ensure the team is meeting tight deadlines 
 Account reconciliation 
 Excel reporting 
 
 Profile 
 The successful candidate should have experience working within a Credit or Collections environment, having managed a team. Ideally having experience in training and supporting a team to ensure targets are met consistently.
