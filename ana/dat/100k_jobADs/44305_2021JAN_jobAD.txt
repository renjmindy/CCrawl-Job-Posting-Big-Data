 Deputy Ward Manager - For The Male Adult Ward 
 One of our client  provides CAMHS Tier 4 Low Secure services for young people aged 13 - 18, Transitional services for young women aged 18 -25 with personality disorders and acute mental health services. 
 We are currently recruiting for a Deputy Ward Manager to work in highly reputable service . 
 Our Deputy Ward Manager role involves: 
 
 
     To deputise for the ward manager when required
 
 
 
     To have an assertive and unrelenting focus on the performance of the nursing team and take definitive action when performance falls below acceptable levels
 
 
 
     To support the ward manager in ensuring the allocated ward achieves excellence across the full range of relevant quality and compliance criteria's
 
 
 
     Effectively work with other members of the Multi-Disciplinary Team (MDT) and Senior Management Team (SMT) to ensure effective care in delivery to patients and all members of staff are appropriately managed
 
 
 
     Promote and maintain a professional nursing environment, ensuring a high standard of nursing care at all times, assisting with evaluation through clinical governance and audit
 
 
 
     To assist the ward managers in coordinating the work of the nursing team including staff training, duty rotas and making adjustments to manage short term cover to manage emergencies
 
 
 
     To support the Ward Manager in the planning and implementation of service developments by developing a highly skilled and motivated nursing workforce through regular supervision, appraisals and probationary meetings
 
 
 
     Support and lead regular patient feedback mechanisms, e.g satisfaction surveys, community meetings as well as coordinating complaints and incident investigations and management cases
