 Endoscopy Nurse 
 An exciting new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Endoscopy Nurse to work in an exceptional hospital based in the Milton Keynes area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This is a purpose-built day care unit. 
 The centre has been designed to provide outpatient facilities for the assessment and treatment of patients 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Sound written and verbal communication skills
 
 
 
     Proven ability to work effectively in a team environment and independently as required
 
 
 
     Flexibility and adaptability to meet the changing needs of the business
 
 
 
     Demonstrated customer service skills
 
 
 
     Basic computer skills
 
 
 
     Endoscopy experience desirable
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will receive an excellent salary from £26,000 to £34,000 per annum. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role working 37.5 hours a week. 
 In return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     Contributory pension scheme
 
 
 
     Access to genuine opportunities to grow, develop and specialise in your career
 
 
 
     25 days' annual leave plus the opportunity to buy/sell more
 
 
 
     Flexible shift options
 
 
 
     Private Healthcare and Life Assurance
 
 
 
     Free onsite parking and a subsidised staff restaurant
 
 
 
     Free uniform
 
 
 
     Access to our employee Discount Programme
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse with a valid NMC Pin
