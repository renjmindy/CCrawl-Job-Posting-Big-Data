 MORE ABOUT THIS JOB 
In Finance Engineering, you'll find an exciting confluence of computer science, finance and mathematics being used to solve for what our shareholders would like from us - a high return for the right risk taken. 
 The Corporate Treasury Strats team is looking for world class quantitative programmers to work closely with Corporate Treasury partners to employ quantitative analytics to drive optimizations of firm liquidity, cash and collateral management, funds transfer pricing and trade execution strategies. This is an integrated group which both explores new ideas for optimizing the funding management of the firm and also executes trading strategies, all in one team. 
 Corporate Treasury lies at the heart of Goldman Sachs, ensuring that businesses have the appropriate level of funding to conduct their activities, while also optimizing the firm's funding costs and managing liquidity risks. As part of the Corporate Treasury Engineering team you will be exposed to securities division and banking initiatives, to new business activities, and to critical strategic programs Goldman Sachs pursues to maintain its leadership among global financial institutions. 
 RESPONSIBILITIES AND QUALIFICATIONS 
 What we do and offer: 
Corporate Treasury plays a central role in the firm's overall strategy with responsibility for providing appropriate funding to support all firm wide activity while maximizing net interest income. The division allocates financial resources, raises funding and capital to support firm activity, and dynamically manages the firm's asset liability risk and liquidity portfolio. Corporate Treasury actively engages in public markets and with businesses across the firm, investors, ratings agencies and regulators. 
 In Corporate Treasury Engineering, you'll find an exciting mix of engineering, mathematics, and finance to support Corporate Treasury in all its functions and also drive Engineering-led projects. 
 
 We help treasury analyse our funding and liquidity position by providing quantitative analysis on our positions 
 You will drive commercial impact by optimizing our trading positions and usage of assets and liabilities 
 You will ensure that our profit and loss calculations are correct and timely. 
 You will ensure that our risk is correctly captured. 
 Engineers and non-Engineers closely collaborate so you will have the opportunity to interact with treasury on a regular basis 
 We use a variety of technology such as Java, Python, and proprietary solutions. Quantitative developers and software developers work closely together. 
 You will join our global team 
 You will have a unique opportunity to learn and gain experience from a leading financial firm 
 
 
 Culture/Flexible working: 
At Goldman Sachs, our culture is one of teamwork, innovation and meritocracy. We often say our people are our greatest asset and we take pride in supporting each colleague both professionally and personally. From collaborative work spaces and mindfulness classes to working from home and flexible work options, we offer our people the support they need to reach their goals in and outside the office. 
 
 Who we look for: 
 
 Bachelor, Master or PhD with a background in financial engineering/financial math; quantitative sciences, e.g. physics, statistics, applied math, electrical engineering, or other quantitative discipline. 
 Strong analytical skills and mathematical fluency 
 Programming experience for example in Java, Python, Matlab or similar 
 Interest in solving financial business problems with broad engineering methods but prior financial knowledge is not essential. 
 Drive to commercial outcomes. 
 Excellent communication skills, including speaking to technical and non-technical audiences. 
 Desire to take responsibility for own work and projects.
