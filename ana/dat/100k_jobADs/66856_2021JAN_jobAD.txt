 A well established medium sized business require a year end accountant for a minimum of three months to start ASAP. 
 This role will require a qualified accountant with working experience with IFRS and UK GAAP. 
 Group consolidation & subgroup consolidations. 
 Preparing group statutory accounts. 
 Deal with auditor queries. 
 Manage the production of group statutory accounts. 
 Prepare balance sheet reconciliations.
