 Casual Bus Drivers 
 Elk Island Catholic Separate Regional Division No. 41 
 __Sherwood Park, AB 
 Apply Now 
 We are accepting applications for casual School Bus Drivers in the communities of Sherwood Park and Fort Saskatchewan. Casual positions are on an on-call as needed basis. Individuals will be required to operate up to a 72-passenger school buses while maintaining a level of discipline that is conducive to safe and orderly operation. Drivers must possess a valid Alberta Operator's License and class 1 or 2 certification. Training for Class 2S MELT may be provided. 
 Applications will be received through jobs.eics.ab.ca or applytoeducation.com 
 By virtue of the submission of an application, the applicant agrees that the principal or designate can contact previous employers for the purpose of conducting confidential reference checks whether or not the applicant has listed a reference for that employer. 
 We thank all applicants for their interest but only those being considered to advance in the selection process will be contacted.
