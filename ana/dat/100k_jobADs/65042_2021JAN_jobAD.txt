 Update Statuses 
As Work

 Your team can easily indicate the stage their work is in, by changing the status dropdown. This can save hours of back and forth communication. A tech or dispatch can update the status and within seconds your entire team is aware of their availability.

Schedule 
 No need to clutter up your schedule with jobs that are waiting for parts or responses. At one glance, your office users can see how jobs are divided and know how long have they been in the same status.

Customize

 Want to save even more time? Add color coded sub-statuses to each status. Workiz can be customized to fit your workflow as you need it. Try adding statuses like: 
 “In progress – In route” or “Canceled – Declined estimate“.
