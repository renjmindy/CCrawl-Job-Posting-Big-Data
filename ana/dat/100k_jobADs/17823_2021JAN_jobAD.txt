 Jobs in Caring 
 Posted on  May 2, 2009  by  caregiver 
 An agency for jobs in caring that’s been in the industry for 10 years is looking for qualified individuals to provide caring services to the agency’s clients who are mostly elderly and disabled individuals who are confined in their own homes. Both live in and live out jobs in caring are available for the jobs in caring vacancy and previous experience is very essential for live in jobs in caring. 
 For the chosen applicants for the jobs in caring who will do live out work, the jobs in caring agency will help the applicants find accommodation and full training for jobs in caring will be provided. The duties of the chosen applicant for jobs in caring will vary depending on each client’s special needs but will most likely include washing and dressing, bathing, toileting assistance, domestic duties, meal preparation, and shopping for some groceries of personal items. All applicants for the said jobs in caring vacancy must have the right to work in the UK like EEU citizens or those who possess holiday maker visa or similar. Other job in caring that are available through the agency are Au Pair, Nursing, and Social Work. 
 Previous jobs in caring experience or volunteer experience in care work will be considered an advantage. Ability to drive or ride a bicycle is also essential. The jobs in caring applicants should also have a good command of the English language both written and oral. The salary being offered for the jobs in caring applicants is GBP 6.40 per hour. The application process involves: 
 
 In-Person Interview Required 
 Letters of Reference 
 Resume 
 
 For more info regarding the jobs in caring vacancy you may log on to their website at www.carewatch.co.uk/
