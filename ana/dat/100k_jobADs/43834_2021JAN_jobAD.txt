 Are you an experienced HR Shared Services Manager? Are you looking for an opportunity to join an organisation at a time of change? Do you have a strong HR Operational background? If so, I have an opportunity for an Interim HR Operations Manager to join a growing business based South of Birmingham for a period of 12 months. 
 Reporting into the Head of HR, as HR Operations Manager you will be responsible for the day to day management of a small HR Operations team to ensure that all employees receive a highly efficient and consistent HR service across all aspects of their employee life cycle. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Challenge and influence current ways of working to drive a positive change and lead improvements 
 Partnering with the wider HR team and other stakeholders to ensure that an appropriate HR Operations framework is in place to guarantee consistency across the business 
 The design, setting up and implementing of internal SLAs for HR Operations aligning processes and Workday system usage 
 Running process mapping exercises to produce bespoke optimal processes 
 Develop, implement, manage and monitor the continuous improvement agenda within the services team 
 Tracking and monitoring of all people KPI's to improve the performance of the HR practices 
 Dealing with system updates, and improving transactional processes, promoting operational efficiencies regularly 
 Ensuring all employee lifecycle events and key documentation are recorded and up to date 
 Acting as an escalation point for any complex cases and queries received by the operations team 
 
 Required Skills: 
 
 Experience of Workday HRIS is essential 
 Proven experience of managing a team 
 Highly analytical and data focused 
 Experienced at setting, tracking and reporting KPIs and SLAs 
 Skilled at process mapping and end to end process improvement 
 Excellent written and oral communication skills 
 Excellent interpersonal and stakeholder management skills 
 MCIPD qualified or equivalent
