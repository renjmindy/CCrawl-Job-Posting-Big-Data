 Boilermaker, Welder 
 Job description 
 Job highlights 
 
 
 Karratha and Dampier residential applicants only - No FIFO on offer 
 
 
 4:5 - 5:4 - 5:5 roster 
 
 
 Casual ongoing positions 
 
 
 About our Client 
 Rio Tinto operates the world's largest integrated portfolio of iron ore assets. These assets include a world-class, integrated network of 15 iron ore mines, four port facilities, a 1,700 kilometre rail network and related infrastructure. Rio Tinto are expanding operations in the Pilbara to epic proportions while introducing next generation technologies to deliver greater efficiency, lower production costs and improved health, safety and environmental performance. 
 About the Role 
 Using your Boilermaker experience, join the Plant Shut Maintenance teams to perform planned maintenance activities at Rio Tinto's Dampier Ports Operations. These port facilities consist of shipping terminals with each terminal having facilities for train unloading, iron ore stockpiling, product blending and ship loading. 
 Reporting to the Maintenance Supervisor, you will be required to: 
 
 
 Maintain infrastructure through repairing and overhauling the fixed plant equipment. 
 
 
 Complete preventative and corrective maintenance for all equipment in the fixed plant area 
 
 
 Conduct routine inspections of fixed plant assets and determine repair action when required 
 
 
 Utilise SAP to ensure that maintenance tasks are recorded, assigned correctly and completed 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Relevant Australian recognised trade certification 
 
 
 Manual C Class Australian issued Drivers Licence (minimum) 
 
 
 Fabrication and repair experience within fixed plant facilities 
 
 
 Welding and oxy cutting 
 
 
 Basic understanding of SAP maintenance systems or similar highly regarded
