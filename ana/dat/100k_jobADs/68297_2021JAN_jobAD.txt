 Excavator Operator (Roster Rate) 
 Job description 
 We are currently seeking an experienced & ticketed Excavator Operator for a FIFO project in far Northern Queensland. 
 Must have current Excavator tickets & at least 2+ years of civil, earthworks or main roads experience. 
 Construction white card is mandatory for this position. 
 Suitable persons will be required to work away from home on a 3 weeks on / 1 week off roster. 
 Project is based in Torres Strait Island (Boigu Island), so must be able to work in a remote community. 
 Travel, meals & accommodation will be supplied. 
 Must be physically fit & able to perform manual tasks as required. 
 Project is expected to last 4-5 months with potential for ongoing work for the right people.
