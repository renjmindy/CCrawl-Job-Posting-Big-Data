 BookNook 
 
 Overview 
 BookNook is on a mission to ensure every child has access to a world-class reading teacher. We offer easy-to-use tools for educators and caring adults, that empower them to provide powerful reading instruction that gets great results. At BookNook, we believe that the best programs are designed by people who have their roots in the classroom. Both our instructional strategy and our curriculum come from experienced educators who have seen firsthand what works best for students. 
 Job Description 
 BookNook is a late seed stage startup that is using technology to change how kids learn reading, with a particular focus on helping those students who are having a hard time. We believe that the best programs are designed by people who have their roots in the classroom. Both our instructional strategy and our curriculum come from experienced educators who have seen firsthand what works best for students. 
 We are currently looking for a Head of Partnerships who can take our partnerships and sales strategy to the next level so BookNook becomes a necessary solution for schools and other partners. The Head of Partnerships will be responsible for ensuring BookNook sets, meets, and exceeds aggressive sales targets. This position will build and oversee our sales pipeline and will create a strategy to position BookNook as a solution for existing and new markets. The ideal candidate has a strong history of leading sales for education solutions designed for schools, and is able to develop partnerships with a diverse pool of stakeholders. 
 The Head of Partnerships manages a rapidly growing sales team and is responsible for the performance management and hiring of those employees. The Head of Sales reports to the CEO and the position is geo-flexible. Some travel is required. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Develop and implement strategic sales plans and forecasts to meet company wide business objectives 
 Develop and manage sales operating budgetHire and supervise a team of regional account executives and sales development representatives and motivate them to achieve the best results possible 
 Set activity and revenue targets for all members of the sales team 
 Personally manage a set of high value accounts 
 Partner with marketing and CS on lead generation strategies 
 Design and implement strategy for product positioning and pricing to produce highest possible long-term market share 
 Position BookNook as a unique and effective solution for schools and other partners 
 Direct sales forecasting activities and sets performance goals accordingly 
 Represents BookNook at conferences and events targeted towards potential customers 
 Work closely with the Head of Marketing to oversee and evaluate market research and adjust strategy to meet competitive conditions 
 Develop customer handoff strategy with the Head of Customer Success 
 Prepare weekly sales reports to share with company leadership team 
 Participate on weekly leadership team meetings 
 
 You are a good fit for the role if you 
 
 Have significant experience in K-12 education, preferably including a B2B SaaS sales operation 
 Know how to develop, implement and lead a sales strategy and team 
 Have a desire for autonomy, exhibiting a high degree of ownership and accountability 
 A substantial track record of meeting and exceeding quarterly sales targets 
 Thrive in an environment based on the principle of High Standards and High Fives 
 Are interested in working in a mission based company with a dual bottom line, both being financially successful and also helping kids overcome educational obstacles 
 
 It would be extra great if you also 
 
 Have experience working with educators, nonprofits, or schools, whether professionally or as a volunteer 
 Have been part of a startup during rapid growth and have seen what it takes to go from having a great sales team to an effective sales operation
