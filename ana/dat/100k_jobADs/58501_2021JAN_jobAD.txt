 Consultant: 
 
 
 A client of Gleesons based in North Birmingham is looking for an Internal Auditor to join their business on a 12 month FTC. This will be a international role therefore travel to Europe and the USA will be expected. Interviews will take place mid-end of August. 
 The role: 
 
 To perform internal audit work on the overall internal control and risk management framework - this will include, Financial Controls, Financial Reporting Controls, Operational Controls, Risk and Compliance and Underwriting. 
 To prepare reports on the finding of internal audits 
 To build and develop relationships with internal stakeholders 
 To follow up on findings from audits and put management actions into place 
 Maintain appropriate and up to date knowledge of FCA regulations and other relevant legislation and regulation 
 
 The ideal candidate: 
 
 Degree Educated 
 Qualified (ACA, ACCA or CIMA or equivalent) or Qualified Internal Auditor 
 Have internal audit experience in a FCA regulated entity 
 Happy to travel overseas 
 Previous experience in working with multi-site and multinational businesses 
 Experience in complex audit and risk based management processes
