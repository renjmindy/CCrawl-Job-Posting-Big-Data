 Forensic Secure Healthcare Assistant - Horley 
 Are you a Mental Healthcare Assistant or Support Worker looking for extra shifts? 
 Service Care Solutions Recruitment Agency are looking for a experienced Mental Healthcare Worker to pick up ADHOC (Agency Bank Shifts) within a Medium Secure Hospital in Horley, Surrey. 
 The position will involve assisting adults between 18 - 65 years of age aiding the recovery of the patients¸ monitoring their wellbeing and assisting with on-going treatment. 
 The hospital looks after men with mental health disorders, learning disabilities, challenging behaviour and complex needs. 
This is an excellent opportunity either for anyone looking for work to supplement your current job / studies/ family life or as an opportunity for flexible part T time/full T time employment. 
 The Limited company hourly rates of pay are as follows: 
, £10.50 ltd hour for weekdays 
, £11.50 ltd for nights 
, £12.00 ltd for Saturdays 
, £12.50 ltd for Sundays 
 Service Care Solutions are specialist providers of ad-hoc shifts to the NHS and private sectors for both nursing and healthcare assistants. 
 Essential: 
, Recent experience working as a Health care assistant or support worker in the mental health sector. 
 , Must be able to engage with patients/clients 
, Flexible 
 Desirable experience: 
, 6 months experience 
, Any form of Control and Restraint training (PMVA/MAPA/MAYBO) 
, Experience of working within Secure Units
