 Registered Practical Nurse (RPN) 
 QCareKWC is recruiting RPNs to provide support to patients within our newly opened ALC unit and the community Part-time and full-time employment opportunities are available with this position. 
 QCareKWC is focused on creating a team of qualified nurses to enhance the health of the community and our clients. We are open to Nurses with a variety of backgrounds and training expertise. 
 We are searching for candidates that would be available for: 
 
 Consistent hours 
 Full-time and Part-time hours available 
 
 The QCareKWC Difference: 
 
 Competitive starting wages (based upon experience, education and responsibilities) 
 Continuing education and paid training programs 
 Benefits upon hiring 
 Bonuses for additional training ( Trach, Vent, ASL etc) 
 Matched RRSP 
 Use of Company Vehicle 
 Pandemic pay 
 Registered Practical Nurse Role: 
 Completing client intake visits and any follow up visits as needed 
 Conduct assessments and implement a plan of care 
 Assisting in skills training as needed 
 Provide direct skilled nursing care and monitoring of patients 
 Conduct direct communication with patients and/or clients, families, and other team members to provide optimal health care 
 Develop thoroughly written reports, reporting to the Director of Nursing and Operations Team coordinators 
 Medication oversight and administration 
 Practice within the scope of practice set out by the College of Nurses of Ontario (CNO) 
 Registered Practical Nurse Role Requirements: 
 Graduate of an accredited Registered/Licensed Practical Nurse program 
 Current registration with the CNO, candidate must be in good standing 
 Valid work/study permit (if applicable) 
 Current CPR/First Aid Certification 
 Complete immunization record (MMR, TDAP, Varicella, Hep B, 2-Step TB ) 
 Clean Vulnerable Sector Screen (within 3 months of hire) 
 Strong documentation skills 
 Strong clinical skills 
 Ability to work with little supervision 
 High level of interpersonal skills 
 New graduate applications are welcomed. While we thank all candidates who apply, only qualified candidates will be contacted.
