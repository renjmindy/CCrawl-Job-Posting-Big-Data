 Disability Care Job in Canada 
 Posted on  February 16, 2009  by  caregiver 
 The client, a young mother who recently suffered a stroke and is paralyzed on one side is in need of someone to provide her with live in disability care and to assist her with mobility, daily activities, and help her in raising her two kids age 5 and 7. She is willing to be a caregiver work abroad sponsor. 
 Job Qualifications: 
 
 Minimum of 1 year experience as a disability care provider or in the same capacity. 
 Possesses a bachelors degree. 
 Compassionate, hardworking, and is also a mother. 
 Fluent in English. 
 
 For more info, interested applicants may visit:
