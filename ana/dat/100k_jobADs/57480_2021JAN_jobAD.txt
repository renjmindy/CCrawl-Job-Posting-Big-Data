 Systems Accountant - Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) 
 Systems Accountant - Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) 
 
 Location 
 
 Leeds, West Yorkshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Contract 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £45000.00 - £55000 per annum 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Arshi Thind 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 ArshiThind@workwithglee.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH18419_1578562795 
 
 Published: 
 
 about 1 year ago 
 
 Duration: 
 
 12 months 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-02-08 
 
 Startdate: 
 
 ASAP 
 Overview: 
 
 To be a key player in the finance systems team, delivering support and changes to the current EPM Planning, Forecasting and Management Reporting application; 
 Ensure the effective and efficient processing of financial and non-financial data and to ensure that the range of management tools and reports used are adequate and fit for purpose. 
 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Support projects to implement new, and update existing, models for the processing of financial and management reporting, planning and forecasting data; 
 Provide training and insight to business users and others in the wider organisation who need to use the systems; 
 Work with sponsors, stakeholders and developers to design and develop management reports to better inform the business 
 Test internal processes and procedures for the EPM toolset, ensuring that all policies, procedures and guidance documentation are up to date; 
 Ensure data consistency across multiple reports and systems. 
 
 Qualifications & Experience: 
 
 Qualified Accountant (preferred), or qualified by experience; 
 3-5 years' experience of working in a Finance FP&A function, supporting the planning, forecasting and reporting (EPM) applications and processes; 
 Experience of designing, implementing and managing finance reporting, planning and forecasting processes and policies on an Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) platform; 
 Experience of delivering month end reporting processes and experience of administering Finance EPM applications - relational SQL Server-based; 
 Advanced and demonstrable SQL experience (construction, data manipulations and ad-hoc queries); 
 Experience of acquiring and transforming data from ERP (Finance, HR) systems - ideally SAP ECC, FICO and HCM.
