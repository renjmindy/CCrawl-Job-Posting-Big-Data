 Canowindra, New South Wales

 Share: 
 
 10 Vegetable Farm workers are required for six months work from around September/October 2020 through until March/April 2021 on a large irrigated vegetable operation in the Canowindra District of New South Wales. Work will involve assisting with seed planting, general crop management and harvest work. Workers will need to be physically fit and self-motivated and capable of sustained heavy field work harvesting pumpkins, melons and cauliflowers. Hours of work will vary according to harvest requirements. Wages will be paid at standard casual award rates and work will be over 5-6 days per week, depending on weather conditions with average minimum weekly work hours exceeding 30 hours per week. Accommodation and transport may be provided at cost.
