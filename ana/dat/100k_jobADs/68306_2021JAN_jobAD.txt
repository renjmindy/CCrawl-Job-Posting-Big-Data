 The Role 
 Want a job that combines the excitement of working in a high-growth tech startup with the fulfilment of creating life-changing social impact? Then this is the role for you! 
 As an Operations Executive, you're onboarding the amazing people referred to Beam, supporting them through their remarkable journey out of homelessness. 
 You're an operationally-minded problem solver, directly helping us to iterate and bring our award-winning platform to true scale. 
 You're ambitious, excited by the prospect of working with super smart teammates in a startup founded by someone that's scaled a business to millions of users and raised funding from investors in the likes of Facebook and Dropbox. 
 You're friendly and empathetic and excel at building strong, respectful working relationships. 
 You're excited by the prospect of getting a little nerdy with the latest apps and working in a data-driven way. 
 You're process-driven and solution-orientated, able to juggle demanding workloads in a fast-paced startup environment that works to ambitious KPIs. 
 Click  here  to see one of our Ops Execs in action! 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Safely assessing and onboarding homeless people referred to Beam by our extensive list of partners 
 Working face-to-face with people to create content for their crowdfunding campaign using our proprietary in-house technology 
 Working to ambitious KPIs that increase our social impact 
 Brokering relationships to streamline pathways into work for homeless people 
 Building a strong, respectful and professional working relationship with people using Beam to skill up 
 Producing detailed risk assessments and case notes 
 Generating ideas and processes that iterate our model of support services towards scale 
 
 Requirements 
 
 
 
 1 year high-quality operational experience in a start-up, social enterprise, or highly-regarded company 
 
 
 Experience working with vulnerable individuals - or huge enthusiasm to excel in that field (don't worry, you'll be enrolled into a thorough training programme when you join) 
 Entrepreneurial, get things done attitude 
 Passion for innovation and social impact 
 Thrives under pressure and in fast-paced environments 
 Highly detail-orientated and risk-aware while working with integrity and openness 
 Highly adaptable, personable and excited to work with people from diverse backgrounds 
 Committed to promoting equality of opportunity in London - and far beyond 
 
 We're especially interested in hearing from you if you speak Polish or Romanian! 
 Compensation & Perks 
 
 £25-35k - we're open to hearing from a broad range of experiences 
 Additional EMI-qualifying share options in Beam 
 MacBook Air or PC (brand new/ recycled) 
 Work phone (brand new/ recycled) 
 Cycle Scheme 
 £250 annual wellbeing budget 
 £200 annual learning and personal development budget 
 £80 monthly counselling budget 
 Beam books & stash 
 Perkbox perks - discounts and freebies at top brands 
 'Beam team talks' with some of the UK's leading tech entrepreneurs 
 Work-hard, play-hard culture with an annual mini-break (subject to social distancing rules) 
 
 
 Application Process 
 Pre-interview: Answer 3-4 questions over email 
Round 1: Video call (30 minutes) 
Round 2: Assessment (2.5 hours) followed by 30 minutes debrief 
Round 3: Presentation in role play & interview (up to 1 hour) 
Round 4: 90 minute session comprising of 3 separate meetings with the founder and 2 other Beam team mates 
 All our interviews are currently conducted remotely and done over video calls and it usually takes us a week to get back to you after each round. The whole process from Round 1 should take between 2-4 weeks.  Please note that only successful applicants will be contacted before Round 1. Please apply directly on our website. 
 We wish you the best of luck!
