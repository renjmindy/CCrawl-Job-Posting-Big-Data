 Location 
 
 West Midlands, England 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Built Environment

 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 Up to £40000.00 per annum + company car

 We are working with a leading specialist contractor who are going from strength-to-strength even in these difficult times. 
 They are seeking the expertise of an experienced Window & Door Surveyor to join their team in a national role where you will be responsible for surveying new jobs and being the link between site foreman and the Head Office. Although our client is based in the West Midlands this role can be based from anywhere. 
 As the successful Window & Door Surveyor you will have window and door surveying experience and the ability to travel around the UK as and when required. You will be able to prove your experience as a Window & Door Surveyor with the ability to measure up new works. 
 This role is available straight away for the right candidate and is offering a salary of up to £40,000 plus a company car. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
