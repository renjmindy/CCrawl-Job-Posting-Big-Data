 Operator Coal Mining Level 3 
 Job description 
 Mobile Plant Operators 
 
 
 6 Monthly Incentive Bonus 
 
 
 Ongoing position 
 
 
 Superannuation paid on all hours 
 
 
 About Our Client 
 Our client is Australia's largest coal miner and exporter, the world's largest supplier to the sea borne coking coal market and largest coal producer and supplier of metallurgical coal. The client has several mine sites across the Bowen Basin consisting primarily of open cut mines. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking a skilled and experienced Dump Truck Operator to commence at a mine near Moranbah. You will be working a rostered shift with successful candidates starting as soon as possible. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Operate CAT 793 and 797 Haul Trucks 
 
 
 Dump waste material 
 
 
 Operate machinery safe and competent 
 
 
 Assure workplace hazards are reported and identified 
 
 
 Meet production target requirements 
 
 
 Pre-start checks on machinery 
 
 
 Skills and Experience: 
 
 
 
 RII/ Training Transcript CAT 789, 797 
 
 
 
 Minimum 6 Months Experience 
 
 
 Current Standard 11 or equivalent 
 
 
 Current Coal Board Medical 
 
 
 Current Driver's Licence and Right to work within Australia 
 
 
 Benefits of role: 
 
 
 Accommodation and Meals provided 
 
 
 Bus service available Mackay 
 
 
 Ongoing positions
