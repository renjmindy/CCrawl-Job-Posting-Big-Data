 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently recruiting for a Financial Accountant to join a fantastic finance team within a well known utilities business based in Stoke On Trent. 
 Key Skills: Financial Accountant: Stoke on Trent 
 
 Preparation of external statutory and regulatory financial reporting obligations and external reporting requirements to shareholders 
 Assist the preparation of the annual budget and business plan submission for the Executive and Board 
 Assist in the preparation of timely and accurate monthly financial reporting (actuals and revised forecasts vs budget) to the Executive and Board 
 Assist with production of monthly cash flow forecasts and management of the organisation's liquidity position 
 Ensuring all taxation, interest and debt statutory and group reporting calculations are prepared in line with agreed deadlines and assisting with audit queries 
 Management of the HMRC online system to ensure correct entities/periods stated and relevant users have the correct access 
 Management and payment of corporation tax, consortium group relief and trust tax payments within statutory/agreed deadlines 
 Preparation of year-end tax packs and analysis to support tax advisors completion of corporation tax returns 
 Support Financial Reporting and Analysis Manager with day to day treasury operations 
 
 Qualification & Experience: Financial Accountant: Stoke on Trent 
 
 Full professional accountancy qualification (e.g. ACA, ACCA) 
 Statutory and management accounting with experience of producing taxation disclosures 
 Audit background would be advantageous 
 Regulatory reporting experience would be advantageous 
 Utilising financial applications such as Sage 
 Financial controls and governance 
 Balance sheet management and cash forecasting 
 
 This is a fantastic opportunity for a qualified accountant (ACA,ACCA,CIMA) to join a business that offers structured and demonstrable progression to a Finance Manager and then Finance Controller role 
 For more information on this role please do get in touch
