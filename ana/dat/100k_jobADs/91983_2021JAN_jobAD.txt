 I have a fantastic opportunity for a Head of PR & Communications to join a fantastic retail focused business in Birmingham, who are well-known for their excellent working culture and who are going through extreme growth at the moment. 
 This Head of PR & Communications role will involve: 
 
 Managing and setting clear objectives for the external PR agency, for both crisis management and PR support 
 Building and delivering on a successful PR and communications strategy with the intention of driving top line growth 
 Building and delivering on a clear internal communications strategy across all sites around the UK 
 Budget management, reporting and ROI delivery 
 Management of a team of three PR and communications specialists 
 Building a social media strategy for the business, with a focus on paid social 
 
 This Head of PR & Communications position requires a candidate with the following skills: 
 
 Experience of in-house management of both external and internal communications 
 Proven experience of building and managing PR and communications strategies for a multi-site organisation 
 Experience of managing and building a team of PR and communications specialists 
 Excellent and professional communication skills, both written and verbal 
 Outstanding stakeholder management skills 
 The ability to manage a number of projects at any one time 
 
 To apply for the Head of PR & Communications position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
