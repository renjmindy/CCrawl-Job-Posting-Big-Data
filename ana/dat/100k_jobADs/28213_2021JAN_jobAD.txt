 Back-End PHP & DevOps Engineer (d/f/m) LOCALPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 About MYMY catering 
 We are building the first global and singular catering brand for every occasion, everywhere. 
 Founded in Berlin, active in Germany, Austria and the Netherlands, this business has raised millions from reputable investors to date and is on the cusp of a broad international expansion. Currently with more than 20 employees, we are on the lookout for talented individuals to construct the foundations and continuous accelerate the growth of the business in volume, quality and innovation. 
 We aspire to a culture defined by humility, integrity, empathy, trust and an appetite for excellence - starting, growing and scaling a business can be stressful, especially in the food space! However, when accompanied by smart, positive and genuinely ‘good’ people, it makes for a phenomenal journey ahead and great opportunity to learn. If you want to learn more about our ambitions and values, feel free to have a look at our  Culture Deck. 
 Once here, you will be... 
 
 doing back-end development (PHP & Twig/HTML) within our Craft CMS setup (built on the Yii 2 framework). 
 creating and maintaining custom features for our site as well as building integrations with other systems/APIs. 
 improving our build tools and deployment systems to ensure best practices and optimal delivery of code with zero downtime. 
 managing any needed database customisation using MySQL. 
 
 What you will bring along... 
 
 7+ years experience in web development using PHP 
 MySQL 
 Nginx (and/or Caddy) 
 Docker 
 JS ES6 
 Git/Git workflow 
 AWS 
 Integrating with REST APIs 
 Ideally experience with Yii 2, Twig, Webpack and SCSS. 
 Ideally experience with Craft CMS, Craft Commerce, Vue.js. 
 A team-oriented mindset that is more interested in doing things the right way instead of the easiest way. 
 Positive, solution-driven mindset and ability to collaborate with others and brainstorm ideas 
 Fluent English language skills 
 
 What we offer… 
 
 Competitive remuneration, with room for growth 
 Diverse and international environment in the heart of Berlin 
 Autonomy and responsibility, surrounded by caring and talented peers 
 Flexible dog policy, lots of coffee, and plenty of opportunities to celebrate!
