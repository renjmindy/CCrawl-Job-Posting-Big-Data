 About Torch 
 Torch is a leadership development platform that combines software and experienced coaches to help high performers reach their full potential. Our service helps our clients build self-awareness, and integrates ongoing feedback from colleagues so clients can track their progress. We are a mission driven company made up of people who believe strongly in human potential. Torch is a YC company (winter '18 batch). This is an early opportunity at a fast growing startup that's helping professionals lead more meaningful, purpose-driven lives. 
 About the role 
 Skills:  React, TypeScript, Ruby on Rails, Elasticsearch, PostgreSQL, Amazon Web Services (AWS) 
 Objectives 
 
 Collaborate with product teams to design and build creative solutions, enabling the personal growth of our clients 
 Help to create a scalable, high performance, media-rich web application for consumption across various device form factors 
 Work across the stack, creating reusable components with modern, maintainable and testable code 
 Identify opportunities for rapid innovation using PaaS or Open Source components wherever possible 
 Take part in peer code review processes 
 Design and communicate coding standards and contribute to architecture plan 
 Become a contributor to various technology communities and meetups 
 
 Competencies 
 
 A degree in Computer Science, a related STEM field or equivalent professional experience 
 Server-side programming skills with a modern language, preferably Ruby on Rails - Python, Java, or C# a plus 
 High competence with ES6 (preferably using React/Redux) and Unit Testing 
 Expert knowledge of CSS styling and layouts 
 Familiarity with front-end and back-end performance optimization techniques 
 Intimate knowledge of RESTful web services 
 Knowledge of distributed application design patterns and asynchronous programming models 
 Experience with several database technologies (both RDBMS & NoSQL) 
 Experience with cloud technologies is a plus! (preferably AWS, Containers and Serverless) 
 
 Benefits

 Flexible vacation and remote work policies 
 Generous professional development stipend 
 Commuter stipend for public transportation
