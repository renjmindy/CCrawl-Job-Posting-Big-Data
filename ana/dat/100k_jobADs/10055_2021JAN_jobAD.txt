 A fantastic opportunity for a Digital Marketing professional with international experience to join a market leading retail business. 
 This role, International Digital Marketing Executive, will encompass be leading on all digital marketing activity across multiple brands. 
 The ideal candidate will possess: 
 
 Experience in digital marketing across all key marketing channels with a good understanding of both paid and organic channels. 
 Strong experience of B2C Ecommerce 
 Knowledge of different marketing channels including PPC, Affiliate Networks, SEO, Social Media and Retargeting. 
 
 The successful candidate will posses international experience, ideally within the US market. 
 Highly competitive package. 
 Currently remote - this role will remain largely remote with limited requirements to attend the East Midlands office.
