 Are you super creative? Do you have a polished graphic design portfolio? Would you like a career move that allows you to put your creative skills at the heart of your professional development. 
 If so, an opportunity for a talented Graphics Designer has arisen to work at one of the most established financial services brands in the Midlands. Based in the outskirts of Birmingham offering a salary of up to 25k. 
 Working as part of an in-house design team on a range of creative and innovative projects for business product lines. Responsible for helping drive the creative direction of campaigns and producing impactful design solutions. Delivery of high quality and forward-thinking work and ideas. 
 
 What You'll do… 
 
 Assist with the creation of digital design work, including display banners, creative emails and infographics. 
 Collaborate with other designers in creative meetings, offering input and sharing ideas. 
 Support the creation of internal and external video production. 
 Production of creative and impactful presentations. 
 Maintain a high level of informed design knowledge. 
 
 
 What You'll Need… 
 
 Recognised qualification in Graphic Design or related subject and experience in graphic design role, supported by a strong portfolio of recent work. 
 To show a passion for design, creativity and client service. 
 Excellent accuracy and attention to detail. 
 Experience with leading design software packages. 
 Strong verbal and written skills. 
 Determination to achieve and succeed. 
 
 
 What You'll get… 
 
 Competitive salary of up to 25k. 
 Support from a well experienced team of designers. 
 Progression and professional development opportunities. 
 Share save scheme. 
 Annual Bonus. 
 Great working culture.
