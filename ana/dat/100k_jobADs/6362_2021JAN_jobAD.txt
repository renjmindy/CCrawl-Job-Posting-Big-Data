 Specialty Doctor 
 An amazing new job opportunity has arisen for a committed Specialty Doctor to work in an amazing hospital based in the Charlwood, Surrey area. 
 As the Specialty Doctor you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This is a low and medium secure hospital specialising in treating male patients aged 18+ who have been detained under the Mental Health Act (1983) and who will benefit from extended treatment and rehabilitation 
 As the Specialty Doctor your key responsibilities include: 
 
 
     Clerking in new patients
 
 
 
     Writing reports for ICRs, CPAs and hearings
 
 
 
     Attending and chairing said meetings in the consultant's absence
 
 
 
     Assessing physical health and requesting appropriate tests
 
 
 
     Prescribing medication and keeping medication charts up to date
 
 
 
     Liaising with external providers to ensure smooth transition when moving patients to and from the hospital, completing ICR actions and keeping care notes dashboard up to date
 
 
 
     To assist on the forensic side if covering for the forensic associate specialist
 
 
 
 An ideal Specialty Doctor must have these skills and experience as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Excellent communication skills (both verbally and written)
 
 
 
     Ability to build effective relationships with service users, colleagues and other professionals
 
 
 
     Good IT skills are essential
 
 
 
 **The successful Specialty Doctor will receive an excellent salary up to £85,000 per annum DOE. 
 This exciting position is a Full Time role and in return for your hard work and commitment you will receive the following generous benefits:** 
 
 
     30 days annual leave plus bank holidays (at commencement of employment)
 
 
 
     Highly competitive salary
 
 
 
     A Group Personal Pension Plan (GPPP)
 
 
 
     Life Assurance
 
 
 
     Free meals while on duty
 
 
 
     Employee Assistance Services
 
 
 
     Continuous learning and development
 
 
 
     Childcare vouchers
 
 
 
     Career development
 
 
 
     Voluntary benefits
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must have MRCPsych and a full GMC registration
