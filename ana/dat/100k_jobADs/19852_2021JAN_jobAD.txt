 Hot Damn! it's a Full-time job 
 Visual/UX Designer. Shaper. San Francisco, CA, USA. BOOM! Nuff said. 
 About 
 Founded in 2012 and based in San Francisco, Shaper is developing a revolutionary line of computer-augmented power tools, starting with a handheld CNC router called Origin. We’re looking for a Visual / UX Designer to evolve how we present our products and services to customers and help us reach new audiences in North America and Europe. 
 About the Role: 
You'll be responsible for teaming up with the Head of Marketing to produce stand-out creative for our marketing website, campaigns, and advertising initiatives that span digital and print. You’ll help us bring clarity to the Shaper brand and our product/service offerings with well crafted visual communication and considered user experience design. 
 Where you’ll work: 
You’ll join our team in our new workspace in San Francisco's SoMa district. You’ll have access to our 5000 square foot workshop which is fully kitted out with all the woodworking machinery you could ever wish for (including Origin).
