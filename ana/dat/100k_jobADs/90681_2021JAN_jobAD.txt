 Design, build, and maintain the core data computation infrastructure used by data scientists 
 Role overview 
 Andalus is a start-up aiming to utilize the latest technologies to help clients solve their data needs. We are hackers by nature and like to think of innovative solutions to solving issues that have long been considered status-quo. We aim to empower our clients with state-of-art infrastructure components allowing them to be a data-enabled organization. 
 You will be working first-hand on developing the first major release of our Andalus platform targeted to empower important organizations in the MENA region. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Design, build, and maintain the core data computation infrastructure used by Andalus 
 Debug issues across services and levels of the stack 
 Think and suggest the right hardware specs and components that would run well with the developed software and adhere to clientsâ€™ requirements 
 Develop systems that proactively capture the health status of various software and hardware components. 
 Build a great customer experience for people using your infrastructure 
 
 Qualifications: 
 
 Familiar with the latest computation and storage architecture and components 
 Experience with managing distributed systems within computing clusters 
 Experience in configuration and maintenance of applications such as web servers, load balancers, relational databases, storage systems and messaging systems. 
 Knowledge of system design concepts 
 Ability to debug complex problems across the whole stack 
 Demonstrated understanding of container networking and security 
 Comfort working with network protocols, proxies and load balancers 
 Experience building highly available services 
 Experience with Kubernetes or other container orchestration systems 
 Technical writing skills 
 Interest in or experience with systems languages, such as Go 
 Strong communication skills and willingness to engage with your teammates in group problem-solving in a remote-work environment 
 
 Preferred Qualifications: 
 
 Knowledge of data governance tooling and principles 
 Working experience in organizations with large databases 
 Dealt with data integrity and cleaning issues in the past 
 Few years of experience as a tech lead
