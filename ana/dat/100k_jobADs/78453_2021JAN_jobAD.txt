 If you are an individual that likes a challenge, this could be the opportunity for you. We are a custom home builder that is seeking a team member who is outgoing, ambitious, energetic, trustworthy and enjoys a demanding and rewarding position. Experience within the construction industry would be preferable. 
 Responsibilities include: 
 
 Process full cycle bi-weekly payroll; 
 Process labour reports for billing; 
 Process new hires, terminations, ROEâ€™s; 
 Print pay cheques and distribute; 
 Process monthly government reports and remittances (source deductions/GST/PST/WCB); 
 Process accounts payable invoice, includes coding and PST self- assessment; 
 Process bi-weekly A/P cheque runs; 
 Process accounts receivable, includes bi-weekly invoicing for all current projects, snow removal, sweeping & misc.; 
 Process general accounting entries; 
 Process all other accounting tasks as needed. 
 
 Qualifications preferred: 
 
 At least 2 years payroll experience or actively pursuing the Canadian Payroll Association Certificate; 
 Experience with A/R, A/P and general accounting functions; 
 Experience with GST/PST in multiple provinces, including exemptions and self-assessment; 
 Ability to multi-task, prioritize, manage time effectively, and work independently or as part of a team; 
 Strong ability to organize tasks and work within defined timelines; 
 Strong analytical skills with a keen attention to details; 
 Advanced Microsoft Excel skills; 
 Knowledge of Quickbooks, Windward & Buildertrend would be an asset; 
 
 The ideal candidate will have Quickbooks, Windward & Buildertrend software experience. However, experience with other accounting software packages and/or a variety of administrative tasks will be considered an asset. As well, construction industry knowledge/experience would be preferable. 
 If this opportunity interests you and you have the skills and experience necessary to be successful in this role, please apply with your resume.
