 Full-Time front-end developer for small creative agency in Seattle WA LOCALPERMANENT 
 Job Description 
 Transom is looking for an experienced front-end developer with a passion for building beautiful, engaging, robust websites. 
 
 Job Title:  Lead Developer 
 Available:  Jan 3, 2019 
 Expert-level Skills Required:  HTML, SCSS, JS, Craft CMS, Git 
 Conversant-level Skills Recommended:  Grunt/Webpack/Node, Plesk, Sketch/Figma, Git, Shopify, Expression Engine 
 Compensation:  $60-80K DOE 
 Experience:  3+ years 
 Type:  Salaried Position 
 Benefits:  Medical, Public Transit Stipend 
 Vacation:  3 wks 
 
 Primary Responsibilities: 
 
 Work with lead designer and creative director to translate designs from Sketch/Figma/Illustrator into fully functional websites 
 Support existing client websites 
 Explore new web technologies and build techniques and processes to utilize them 
 
 About Transom 
Transom is a small creative agency located in Seattle’s historic Pioneer Square neighborhood, specializing in branding and websites for the food and beverage industry. We used to be under the viaduct but now we’ve got a pretty great view (even with the construction on Alaskan Way). We take the third Friday of every month off. We focus on building beautiful, emotionally resonant things for our clients.
