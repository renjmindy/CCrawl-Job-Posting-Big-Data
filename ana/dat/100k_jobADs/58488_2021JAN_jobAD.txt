 DevOps Engineer Azure 
 DevOps Engineer Azure 
 
 Location 
 
 Nottingham, Nottinghamshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 IT 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 Up to £50000.00 per annum 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Adam Ali 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 adamali@workwithglee.com 
 
 Job ref: 
 
 BBBH20018_1581720535 
 
 Published: 
 
 11 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-03-15 
 Your new company based in Nottingham are looking to add a DevOps engineer to join the expanding team. 
 What we are looking for 
 The post holder's responsibilities include: 
 
 Manage & maintain IT infrastructure as per the requirement of the supported software code in a hybrid multi-tenant environment 
 Monitor the health and stability of services within the platform 
 Provide operational support to internal and external customers across the Enerlytics platform and peripheral systems 
 Investigate and resolve technical issues 
 For own work, take ownership and be accountable for providing customer satisfaction through high quality work and meeting target deadlines, including timely delivery of customer reports. 
 Manage a portfolio of projects, both within own team and across the Department. 
 Present proposals to customers thus being able to generate much of own workload. 
 Develop and maintain a wide network of contacts. 
 Promptly raise any work concerns that impact your "well-being" to your line manager to affect a positive outcome. 
 Undertake any other duties from time to time as appropriate to the Business needs. 
 
 Who we are seeking 
 
 Working knowledge ofDevOps tools including:
 
 Azure DevOps, Kubernetes, Docker 
 
 
 OS Administration - Windows, Linux 
 Scripting - PowerShell, Bash 
 Database querying skills - NoSQL & SQL 
 Programming skills - Python or similar 
 Sufficient educational or vocational qualifications to carry out the role safely and effectively. 
 Ability to plan and manage a portfolio of jobs to cost, quality and time. 
 Ability to work under pressure to meet challenging targets. 
 Communication & interpersonal skills to build excellent customer relationships (internally & externally) by understanding their issues and adapting style and approach. 
 Ability to work effectively and flexibly as a member of a team and across teams. 
 Commercially aware & understands the environment in which they operate. 
 Positive approach to change and contributes to the culture of continuous improvement. 
 Effective written communication skills including report writing. 
 
 The role is paying a salary of £50k plus company benefits. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
