 Construction Project Accountant 
 The Position: 
 Avenir Construction Group has been developing and building award winning residential & commercial buildings since 1980 across North America. We are looking for an experienced Project Accountant to join our team in Victoria, BC. This position would be ideal for a positive, self-starting individual that has a minimum of 5 years relevant experience. 
 Job Summary: 
 · Setup of project and monitoring its progress 
 · Managing and accurately reporting on project transactions 
 · Working directly with the project team to coordinate approval of invoices, release of payments and provide reports and analysis as requested 
 · Full cycle of accounts payable for the project including issuance of payments 
 · Track and monitor project-related records, including contracts, contract documents and change orders 
 · Review invoices and project costing to ensure posted to the correct budget and cost codes 
 · Analyze and investigate project variances 
 · Review and monitor project profitability and cash flow 
 · Ensure billings are prepared, compiled and submitted in accordance with contract terms 
 · Understand and enforce the governing holdback policy and lien act including issuance of documents and payments. 
 · Respond to requests from suppliers and subtrades including account reconciliations 
 · Manage all documents and payment compliance requirements 
 · Note and track any special contract requirements for application of costing management 
 · Reconcile and close out project upon completion 
 · Compile information for internal auditors as required 
 · Support Project Accounting team when required 
 About you: 
 
 A Motivated person with a positive attitude 
 Very good verbal and written communication skills 
 Ability to work in a fast-paced and goal-oriented environment with very tight timelines 
 The ability to adapt quickly and to implement new workflows to improve efficiency 
 Strong problem-solving skills 
 Ability to work independently 
 
 Qualifications: 
 · Enrolled as a Chartered Professional Accountant (CPA) or have a Degree in Accounting. (Preferred) 
 · Proven construction industry track record with at least 4-5 years of experience in Project Accounting for large scale projects (20+ million)
