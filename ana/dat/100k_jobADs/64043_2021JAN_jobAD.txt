 Weekend Roundup of W@H Jobs for 2020! 
 Published by  aw1219  on January 13, 2020January 13, 2020 
 We may earn money or products from some of the companies mentioned in this post. 
 This is the first weekend edition of the latest  work at home jobs  for the year 2020. I am excited to share with you some of the jobs that I found so that you can have as many options as possible to select the best job for you. You can also browse the  weekly job section  and  Friday freebie pages  of this blog for additional work at home job leads. 
 Until you actually get a  work at home job , you could try to pursue other money-making ideas that are in demand. Delivering food for companies like  DoorDash  and  UberEats  will help you to serve a need in your community and enable you to get your first paycheck working whenever you want. Working on the weekends and peak hours will increase your pay, so sign up for  DoorDash  and  UberEats  today. All you need is a  smartphone  and access to a car, bike, motorcycle or scooter. 
 Customer Service Reps 
 TeleDirect is now hiring reps to handle calls for a variety of clients that may involve improving the design or fixing problems with the accounts. This company requires that you have 1-year customer service experience, good spelling and grammar skills, a private workspace with a door, a USB headset and a laptop or desktop computer that is less than 2 years old. Full and part-time openings. Hiring in several states. 
 Remote Tech Support 
 ABC Financial is hiring reps to handle inbound and outbound calls while providing quality service to customers. Requires 2 years tech support call center experience or equivalent, good verbal and basic math skills, can multitask and are dependable. Job starts in February with 100% paid training with full benefits. 
 Customer Care Reps 
 OneSupport is seeking reps to provide general support and light troubleshooting for clients and their phone services. You must be able to interact with customers to provide, process, and prioritize information and other duties. Must have proper phone, chat, and email etiquette, can type 40 wpm and have a webcam. Pays $10 per hour with benefits. 
 Center Sales & Associate 
 CenturyLink is looking for associates to respond to phone calls from existing customers who have concerns with billing, products, and services. You must have good communication skills, be computer literate, and have a sales mindset. Pays $14.25 per hour with a commission. The company will supply you with a laptop. 
 Teach English 
 Education First is seeking teachers to enjoy the ability to choose their own work-from-home schedule who have the ability to bring their own teaching style to our custom-designed classroom and receive local, US and UK-based support and payment. Teachers with EF earn up to $20 per hour. 
 
 Hotel Sales & Catering Caller 
 Hospitality Softnet is hiring people with strong organizational skills to contact hotel sales and catering sales managers to generate documents to forward to Report writers that will evaluate the managers selling skills. Requires a quiet work environment, familiar with the hotel sales process, be reliable and have daily access to the internet and email. 
 Speech Recognition 
 Teamwork.ai is seeking people to create short sentences to reflect the communication with a car virtual assistant. Requires good spelling, reading, and writing skills with basic computer knowledge and access to a PC. Full training provided. 
 Happy Givers 
 Happy is an app that is looking for compassionate listeners to provide support to those who need it. You must listen with understanding and empathy. You will need a smartphone. Pays $15 per hour. 
 Transcription 
 Daily Transcription is looking for people who can type 50 wpm with excellent listening skills to transcribe audio and video files. You will need excellent listening skills and can meet strict deadlines. Work whenever you want. Training provided. Weekly pay. Earn from $250 to $950 a week. 
  Customer Service Support Rep 
 
 PNC Bank is looking for people to work from home on a daily basis performing customer service duties for a broad range of products and services across multiple channels. You will resolve inquiries, issues, recommend solutions and other duties. You must be tech-savvy, accurate, and have good communication skills. 
 InstaGC 
 Get instant gift cards to download apps, watch cool videos, search the web, take surveys and more. You can also get a check in the mail, PayPal cash and direct deposit. Must be 13 to join. Open in several countries. 
 Conversationally 
 Get paid $5 a month to use your smart speaker. You can also win up to $100 every month! 
 OHM Connect 
 
 Get paid up to $300 a year to save energy in your home! You will get $10 when you join today. 
 Swagbucks 
 Get a $5 signup bonus plus free gift cards from your favorite stores to watch entertaining videos, shop online, answer questions and more. Open worldwide.
