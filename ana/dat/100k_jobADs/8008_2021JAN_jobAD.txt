 POEA Approved Caregivers Jobs 
 Posted on  March 26, 2013  by  caregiver 
 Two of the countries leading caregiver recruitment agencies recently had their Job Orders approved by the POEA allowing them to recruit qualified applicants for placement in their  POEA Approved Caregivers Jobs . The said POEA approved caregivers jobs are in Israel which has a Job Order balance of 60 caregivers jobs. Listed below are the names of the recruiting agencies and the actual number of POEA approved caregivers jobs for each agency. 
 Mariposa International Services Co. Inc. ----------------------- 30 caregivers jobs 
Star World International Manpower & Placement Agency Inc. --- 30 caregivers jobs 
 Those who interested in applying for the said POEA Approved Caregivers Jobs, please visit the office of any of the recruiting agencies and formally submit your updated resume. If you have any question you need to ask regarding the said POEA approved caregivers jobs, feel free to contact the recruiting agencies by way of their telephone numbers specified below.
