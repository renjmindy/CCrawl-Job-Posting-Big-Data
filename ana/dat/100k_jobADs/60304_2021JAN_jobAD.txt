 Job Description 
 PHZ.fi seeking the service of an experienced PHP software developers in our customers projects. 
 Experience Minimum of 3 years. PHZ.fi is sustainable IT project business focused on agile software company that sells Web software development services for corporate customers, implement IT projects, as well as total solutions to the development of their own products. The activities of the strongest are the web-sites in addition to payments and e-commerce systems.We invest heavily in Lean and Agile process models (Extreme Programming) and we have been operating in TDD principles since 2004. We want to recruit a software industry best factors that will reward staff with good benefits and a better than average wage level. 
 PHP MySQL Ajax / jQuery Self-test (phpunit) 
 Other competencies, which have additional benefits Zend CakePHP Symfony NoSQL Node.js / Javascript My tasks are mainly customers' premises throughout the metropolitan area. Technical know-how, we also require good teamwork and communication skills with the client is running. PHP-knowledge but also a great plus is also other web programming language skills, which are in use in the development of our own products.
