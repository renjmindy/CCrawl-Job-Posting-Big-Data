 Full-Time Solution Manager, Business Reporting (EDW) 
 Wärtsilä Finland Oy Vaasa  -  Ostrobothnia  , Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 The Finance and Reporting Systems team is responsible for defining and delivering IT services in its domain. The team manages solution and application lifecycle of Wartsila's finance and controlling systems as well as business reporting, travel and facility systems. 
 We are looking for a Solution Manager for the business reporting area. The Solution Manager is accountable for Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW) related design and full lifecycle management with regard to data management, integrations and data quality. This requires close cooperation with the specified businesses in order to ensure that business requirements are fulfilled. Intensive cooperation is also required with the Solution Architect to foresee the EDW related roadmap needs what comes to growth and performance. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Manage and be responsible for development and total delivery of data warehousing solutions as per customer needs 
 Requirement management 
 Specifications created together with the customers 
 Data modelling 
 Data integrations design, data flow management 
 Participate in multi-year solution roadmaps creation, ensuring alignment with business strategy and priorities 
 Support, consult and participate in operational development projects 
 Manage the documentation and stakeholder communication related to the own functional area Requirements 
 Experience of global data warehousing solutions 
 Excellent understanding of database technologies (e.g. Oracle) 
 Understanding of data modelling in general and ability to evaluate existing solutions. 
 Understanding of ETL tools, especially Informatica (Cognos Data Manager, CTRL-M) 
 General understanding of reporting tools (e.g. Cognos, QlikView, MS BI) 
 General understanding of (application) integrations 
 Experience of gathering and analysing customer requirements and turning them into solution designs 
 Customer orientation and ability to effectively market solutions to businesses 
 Very good documentation and presentation skills 
 Good leadership, collaboration and change management skills 
 Proactive, development-minded, independent and goal-focused attitude to work 
 Fluent written and spoken English 
 
 Possible locations for this position are Vaasa and Helsinki.
