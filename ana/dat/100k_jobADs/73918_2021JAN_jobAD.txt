 Gift of Education 
 __Mississauga, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Mississauga Residents Only 
 Gift of Education is a non-profit organization that aims to provide quality education for children at home and abroad. We offer affordable, high-quality tutoring services for children in our community. Our approach to tutoring encompasses more than homework help, as we work with parents to provide strategic lesson plans catered to their child’s learning preferences. 
 We donate 100% of the proceeds to charities that provide education for financially struggling students. 
 **  Please apply online at www.giftofeducation.ca and complete the Tutor Logistics Form. ** 
 https://giftofeducation.ca/apply/tutor-application/ 
 **  ONLY applicants that have completed the Tutor Logistics Form will be considered. ** 
 Responsibilities 
 A Tutor is responsible for: 
 
 Working with families/students to provide quality educational support to students of all ages 
 Maintaining timely records of all lessons given, milestones and setting goals 
 Keeping students engaged by motivating and encouraging them along the way 
 
 Key qualifications 
 
 Currently in University pursuing a degree or has completed their undergrad 
 Strong understanding of a core subject at any grade level – (Math/Science/English/French) 
 Must possess the ability to accurately compose, translate, edit and interpret written documents and instructions 
 Excellent computer proficiency skills in Microsoft Office products 
 Team player, quick learner and works well independently 
 
 Additional (preferred but not a requirement) for: 
 
 Patient when speaking with new families/students 
 Prior tutoring or babysitting experience 
 
 Some skills that we are looking for are: 
 
 Excellent verbal, written and communication skills 
 Responsible – Someone who takes ownership of their tasks and their role 
 Organized/Timely – proven experience to meet deadlines 
 Initiative – Although we will provide guidance, the role is what you make of it. Pitch new ideas, speak your mind, we’d love to hear your take!
