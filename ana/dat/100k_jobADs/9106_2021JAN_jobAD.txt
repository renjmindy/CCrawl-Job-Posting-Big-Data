 Full-Time Quality Assurance Specialist 
 Suomen Nestlé Oy Turengin tehdas -  Janakkala  , Tavastia Proper, Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 Nestle is a leading international food company with a worldwide turnover of  76 billion employing approximately 280 000 people. In Finland the best-known brands include Piltti, Bona, Pingviini, Nescafe, Maggi, Nesquik, Fitness, Friskies, and Latz. Nestle has two factories in Finland: an ice cream factory in Turenki and a baby food factory in Turku. 
 We are now looking for a: Quality Assurance Specialist to our Ice-cream factory at Turenki for a temporary contract to replace maternity leave. As QA Specialist You will play an important role providing professional support in order to enforce Nestle quality requirements to fulfill. You will work closely with your colleagues in other functions in the factory. 
 As a QA Specialist you need to: Perform gap assessments, use problem solving tools, establish, maintain and improve quality processes, have knowledge and understanding to ensure legal and internal compliance, work on projects and lead them, participate in factory HACCP studies, provide training and awareness programs to the plant personnel and execute and follow internal audits. The job requires: relevant educational background, at least bachelor's degree, preferably 3-5 years of experience in Quality Assurance, preferably in food industry, advanced Microsoft skills (especially excel) and knowledge of SAP is an advantage,  fluent Finnish and English are required. 
 We appreciate proactive and dynamic working style, problem solving and decision making skills. You need to have professional curiosity and courage to learn a lot. For further information, please contact Factory QA Manager Sari Vesala, 0400 189 323 on 6th of March at 15:00-16:00 or 8th of March at 14:00-15:00.
