 LTC - CMA/CNA - Full-time Nights 
 Apply for the position, LTC - CMA/CNA - Full-time Nights, with Trego County Hospital today or contact Neva Hendrix for more information. Posted Sep 21, 2020. 
  Apply Online  __    (785)-743-2182  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 Trego County Lemke Memorial Hospital -  Long Term Care  is looking for  full-time Nights  CMA/CNA. 
 Shifts 6pm - 6am 
 RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 Performs all aspects of resident care in an environment that optimizes resident safety and reduces the likelihood of medical/health care errors. 
 Administers medications and follows the five medication rights and reduces the potential for medication errors. 
 Provides general nursing care such as positioning residents, lifting and turning residents, applying/utilizing special equipment, assisting in use of bedpan or commode and ambulating the residents. 
 Provides evening care which includes hands/face washing as needed, oral hygiene, back rubs, peri-care, freshening linen, cleaning overbed tables, straightening room and other general care as needed. 
 
 QUALIFICATIONS 
 
 Graduate of an accredited CMA program. 
 Current Certified Medication Aide in State of Kansas. 
 Current BCLS certification 
 Intermediate computer knowledge and ability to learn system used. 
 
 INSTRUCTIONS ON HOW TO APPLY: 
 Our application must be completed to be considered for this position. Please send application, resume, education, licensure/certifications (if applicable) to Human Resources, 320 N. 13th St., WaKeeney, KS 67672. They may also be dropped off at the hospital business office, emailed to  [email protected]  or faxed to (785) 743-6317. To request an application by mail or email, please email or call (785) 743-2182. **  Applications also available on our website: www.tclmh.org ** 
 
 Salary commensurate with education and experience. We offer a negotiable nationally competitive salary and excellent benefits. 
 We are a progressive CAH hospital with in-patient, swing bed, out-patient, home health, assisted living, long-term care, and clinic services. 
 Candidates must be energetic, possess strong leadership skills, willing to work as a team, have a positive attitude, friendly and compassionate toward patients. 
 For hospital information: www.tclmh.org
