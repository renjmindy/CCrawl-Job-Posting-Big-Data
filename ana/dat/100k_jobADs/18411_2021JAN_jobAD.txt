 Office Administrator/Receptionist/Sales 
 Seeking a multitasking candidate who is highly motivated, has a strong work ethic and a helpful attitude. Ideal candidate would be looking after the entire office, from reception to sales, from customer service to operations. 
 Job duties include: 
 
 Create a fun, hospitable, and professional work environment for members 
 Engaging with members, acquiring feedback, and communicating with management to improve member experience 
 Greet potential members and assist in tours 
 Assist with member on-boarding 
 Resolve member requests and complaints across all touch-points 
 The main point of contact for billing inquiries and issues 
 Managing and distributing incoming mail and packages 
 Opening and closing the space 
 Tidying of common areas 
 Make sales call, prepare and manage the daily call log, follow-up on leads, and close deals 
 Daily reporting to the management 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 
 Previous experience in a co-working space or real estate office is an asset but not necessity. 
 Microsoft Office experience. 
 Great organizational skills, fluent in English. 
 Good communication skills. 
 Very organized. 
 Self-starter and a problem-solver. 
 Punctual and reliable. 
 Willing to learn. 
 
 This position is a part-time position, currently weekdays only. You will receive competitive hourly pay. Resumes only accepted by email. Only those selected will be contacted. No phone calls please.
