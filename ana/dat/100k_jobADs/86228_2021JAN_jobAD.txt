 Who is this course for? 
 This course offers an in-depth look at all aspects of online job searching from basic email to using Linkedin for job-seekers with basic computer and internet experience who want to improve their web-based job searching. 
 Completing this course will help you: 
 
 __ Start a professional email profile specifically for job-searching 
 __ Understand how to navigate and use national, state, and local job search engines to find employment 
 __ How to create a job seeker profile that will showcase your best skills and experience 
 __ All about Linkedin, including how to optimize your profile, making connections, and participating in professional groups in your industry
