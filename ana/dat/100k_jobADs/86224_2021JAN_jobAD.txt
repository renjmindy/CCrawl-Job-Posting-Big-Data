 Logistics Coordinator 
 Aurora Cannabis 
 __Edmonton, AB 
 Apply Now 
 Aurora is recruiting for a Logistics Coordinator to be responsible for coordinating the logistics at our Medical distribution center at our Polaris location at Edmonton International Airport, AB. With operations excellence and continuous improvement as our end goal - this role will work within our fulfillment centers to perform the following: 
 MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES: 
Liaison for 3rd party fulfillment/warehousing operations and corporate departments 
Ensure timely/accurate fulfillment of client orders 
Monitor and resolve Client Care requests 
Oversee and coordinate packaging consumables inventory 
Responsible for inventory accuracy in the ERP/WMS system 
Provide direction/coordination to resolve inventory inaccuracies 
Coordinate finished goods inventory counts per defined schedule 
Investigate and resolve inventory count discrepancies 
Ensure adherence to inventory reporting, adjustments, investigative and control policy procedures and regulatory-compliance requirements related to Finished Goods 
Monitor/Audit 3rd party fulfillment/receiving operations for adherence to the documented Standard Operating Procedures 
 QUALIFICATIONS: 
Minimum 5 years’ experience in inventory management and order fulfillment 
Fully understands Inventory Control management 
Previous experience in Shipping/Receiving 
Ability to respond to dynamic and rapidly changing environment 
Comfortable working independently with minimum supervision 
Experience at responding to complex/ stressful situations and resolving issues positively 
Able to follow and uphold standard operating procedures precisely and consistently 
Understanding of quality management principles 
Strong documentation skills and attention to detail 
Computers and technology oriented, experience in ERP and WMS systems 
Proficient with Microsoft Office (Outlook, Excel) 
Excellent communication skills (English both written and verbal) 
 We would like to thank all applicants for their interest but only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
