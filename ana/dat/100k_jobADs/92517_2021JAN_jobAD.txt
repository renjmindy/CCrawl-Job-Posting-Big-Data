 Safety Advisor 
 Job description 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities. 
 About the Role 
 To ensure company operations are undertaken safely in compliance with statutory requirements and in accordance with the company WHS system requirements. The road construction project is in the APY Lands in remote South Australia on a 3:1 FIFO roster. Accommodation is camp accommodation on site which is a self-sufficient camp on the project site. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 You will be responsible for: 
 
 
 The implementation and ongoing maintenance of the Safety Management Systems established on specific project sites. 
 
 
 Participating in Project Risk Assessments and risk mitigation methods. 
 
 
 Ensuring compliance to the company WHS Policy, WHS Management Plan and Safety Procedures. 
 
 
 Implementation of the company Fitness for Work Policy and procedures 
 
 
 Ensuring all personnel, including visitors are site inducted in accordance with company procedures. 
 
 
 Reporting and investigation of all incidents and accidents 
 
 
 Leading Site Safety Inspections 
 
 
 Providing internal training in Safety procedures to employees as required, 
 
 
 Maintaining and managing project Safety records 
 
 
 Complete monthly project Safety Reports 
 
 
 Attend Internal Project Safety Audits 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 To be successful in this role you will have the following: - 
 
 
 Certificate IV WHS 
 
 
 Senior 1st Aid 
 
 
 Internal Auditor Training 
 
 
 Proven experience in the Civil Industry 
 
 
 Ability to work closely with Clients, Subcontractors and Suppliers 
 
 
 Proven communication & organizational skills 
 
 
 Current Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Industry Induction White Card
