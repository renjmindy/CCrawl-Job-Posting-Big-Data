 Operator 
 Job description 
 Client seeks experienced multi-skilled operators for work on Amrun Haul Road 
2/1 Roster FIFO roles, residential applicants also considered 
 Suitable applicants must have experience in all 4 the following qualifications (or the ability to acquire); 
 - Service Mine plant and Equipment. 
- Bulk water cart or watercart operations 
- Dozer 
- HR licence 
 Applicant to meet minimum site standards: 
Rio Tinto Induction - contractor card, including a D valid for 2 years 
Applied Risk Management - no expiry 
Pre-employment medical prior to placement and subsequent periodic medical every 2 years after
