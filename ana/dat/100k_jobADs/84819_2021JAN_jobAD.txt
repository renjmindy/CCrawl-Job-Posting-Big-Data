 Carpenter / Formwork Carpenter - Expression On Interest 
 Job description 
 Formwork Carpenter - Expression on Interest 
 
 
 $36.00 per hour, plus penalties, Daily Travel Allowance 
 
 
 Roles to commence early 2021 
 
 
 Adelaide CBD Location 
 
 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are seeking Expression of Interest for Formwork Carpenters within the Adelaide area for a street upgrade project, this work is expected to go for a 4 -5 month period. 
 Duties & responsibilities: 
 
 
 General formwork for concrete pours, to erecting signage, installing bins, bollards, etc 
 
 
 Maintaining a safe work environment 
 
 
 Ability to work in a team environment 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Previous Formwork Carpentry experience 
 
 
 Dogman ticket 
 
 
 MR truck licence an advantage, but not essential 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Demonstrated previous experience working on a construction site 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
