 Registered General Nurse or Registered Mental Health Nurse 
 An exciting job opportunity has arisen for a committed RGN or RMN to work in an exceptional care home based in the Wakefield, West Yorkshire area. 
 As a Nurse you will be working for one of UK's leading health care providers 
 This is a large size care home and provides palliative care, convalescence, dementia nursing and Parkinson's care. 
 The home is divided into six units, and each unit has spaces to relax and spend time in, as well as access to the garden which is well maintained. 
 There is a dedicated quiet lounge for residents to visit if they prefer 
 As a Nurse your day to day duties include: 
 
 
     Leading, supervising and training the care team
 
 
 
     General Nursing duties-Vital signs, injections
 
 
 
     Being responsible for ordering, storing and administering medication
 
 
 
     Keeping precise, up to date records on residents and reporting any changes.
 
 
 
     Maintaining the Client's dignity and respect at all time
 
 
 
 An ideal Nurse must have these skills and experiences as they are beneficial for this position: 
 
 
     Able to show a can-do attitude always
 
 
 
     A flexible attitude and wholly adopt a person centred ethos
 
 
 
     Show empathy and a desire to make a difference
 
 
 
     Be up to date with current practices, show leadership qualities and be willing to take ownership for the delivery of quality care
 
 
 
     Must have strong Dementia experience
 
 
 
 **The successful Nurse will be offered an excellent salary of £15.61 per hour. 
 We currently have vacancies Night Shifts available. 
 In return for your hard work and dedication you will receive the following generous benefits: ** 
 
 
     Paid Breaks - At selected Homes
 
 
 
     Free Meals On Long Days
 
 
 
     Free DBS
 
 
 
     Ongoing Career Progression
 
 
 
     Pension Scheme
 
 
 
     Assistance With Revalidation
 
 
 
 To be considered for this position you must be qualified as a Nurse either RGN or RMN with a current active NMC Pin
