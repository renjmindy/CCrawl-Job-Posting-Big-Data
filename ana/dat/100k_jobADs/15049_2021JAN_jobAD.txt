 OTR Driver 
 Apply for the position, OTR Driver, with Hess Services, Inc. today or contact Joe Leroux for more information. Posted May 3, 2020. 
  7856214075  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 You would operate as an over the road truck driver to deliver and help set up products our customers have ordered from us.  Destinations vary but include anywhere in the continental United States.  Our drivers enjoy a nice balance between OTR work and home time as our drivers are usually home weekly for their time off. When needed, you would haul sand and other materials locally for our concrete (ready mix) plant. When needed, you would operate a concrete truck locally. Class A CDL with a current DOT medical card required. Those with a Class B CDL and a current DOT medical card would be welcome to apply for a concrete truck position. 
 This is a key position in a successful, busy department with a great company! Applicants must be 18 years or older. We have an amazing benefits package! Benefits include Competitive Wages,  Paid Health/Dental Coverage  , Vision Coverage, Paid Life Insurance, Retirement Plan with an Employer Match, a wide range of supplemental benefits you can pick and choose from to customize your coverage, Uniforms (we even clean them for you) and Paid Time Off. Pre-Employment Drug Testing is required. For questions, please contact the Human Resources Department at (785)621-4075. To apply, you may fill out an application online at www.hess-services.com, stop by our office located at 2670 E. 9th Street, Hays, KS 67601 or visit your local Workforce Center. EOE.
