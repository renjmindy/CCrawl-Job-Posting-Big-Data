 About Us 
 Here at Gousto, we are on a mission to become the UK's favourite way to eat dinner! 
 Delicious recipes, precise ingredients & a dollop of adventure, delivered to your door. We have invested into our technology, data capability and our product to ensure the customer is at the heart of what we do. Every aspect of our business is powered by our passion. 
 Whether it is innovation through our recipe development, building algorithms to optimise our recipe box journey or devising strategies to reduce plastic and food waste. All our teams play a part in our amazing growth in the UK! 
 Our DREAM? To be the most-loved way to eat dinner, and for every meal to leave the planet better off. Gousto is changing how people shop, cook and eat food at home. It’s an incredibly exciting time to join our team! 
 Junior Recipe Developer 
 We have an opportunity within Food Proposition for a Junior Recipe Developer to join our Recipe Development team, based in Shepherd’s Bush, London. Junior Recipe Developers are responsible for creating, with the support of the recipe developers, quality recipes that keep customers coming back. They are part of the Food team and report to our Senior Recipe Developers or Senior Product Development Nutritionist. 
 If you’re a food-obsessed creative looking for an exciting career in recipe development, this is the role for you. 
 Recipe Development 
 
 Support the Food team through a combination of NPD (new product development) and EPD (existing product development) 
 Follow our Recipe Development Process, ensuring all development gates and requirements are adhered to 
 Concept recipes to a brief and budget incorporating target customer, range criteria, nutritional guidelines, seasonality and business constraints 
 Use recipe popularity, NPS and all other Gousto data that we have available to inform recipe development for both NPD and EPD 
 Facilitate new ingredient testing to ensure we always pick the best ingredients at our disposal 
 Delivering top quality recipes in full against output targets each quarter 
 Be a part of driving increased creativity & innovation in the team through researching trends in new cuisines, ingredients and healthy eating 
 Work with our tech solutions to develop recipes that add maximum value to our recipe library 
 Be creative to get the best out of our ingredients and budgets, avoiding unnecessary ingredients to keep costs low without compromising on quality 
 Deliver outstanding easy to follow recipes including step photos, written method, recipe description, all validated by our thorough checklisting process 
 Follow recipe card conventions for methods, naming and formatting and influence changes to these as our product evolves 
 Ensure 100% accuracy with prep and cook timings by sense checking them with a Recipe Developer colleague and testing them independently as part of test cooking 
 Act on customer feedback, redeveloping recipes with low RPS to make them even better without adding cost 
 Have a good working knowledge of company systems, procedures and be confident to challenge, improve and evolve them 
 
 Kitchen Management (when back in office) 
 
 Ensure strict health and safety standards are adhered to 
 Lead by example by keeping work area tidy and clean, following our H&S manuals from our food process team to deliver a safe working environment 
 Let our Kitchen Assistant know if an ingredient is running low so that they can keep the kitchen well stocked for development and shoots 
 
 Key Stakeholders: 
 You will work closely with your team as well as having key stakeholders across Gousto, including: 
 
 Food Process 
 Menu Planning 
 Brand Marketing 
 
 Requirements 
 What skills and experience you will need? 
 
 Be able to demonstrate your passion for all things food 
 Have a creative flair 
 Be a whizz in the kitchen, able to deliver high quality food across multiple dish, diet and cuisine types 
 Excellent written and verbal communication 
 Ability to work in a highly efficient way on projects with fast turnarounds 
 Incredible attention to detail 
 Be able to showcase your experience in written recipe copy 
 Strong organisational skills 
 
 Nice to haves: 
 
 Previous recipe development experience 
 Level 2 Food Hygiene Trained 
 A portfolio of recipes to showcase your breath of food knowledge
