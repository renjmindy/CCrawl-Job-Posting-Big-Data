 Consultant: 
 
 
 Would you like to be a part of the Ecommerce team for a leading global retailer who have a commanding position here in the UK market place. 
 Due to ever expanding business and the development of their Ecommerce team, they now require the addition of a talented, motivated and solutions led Fulfillment Assistant is required to work within the eCommerce Fulfillment team as a key support to Operational Project Management and Consumables Account Management. 
 The individual will have a wide range of scope, dealing with day-to-day activities (such as ordering, stock management, and invoicing) whilst also delivering business improvements and process change through the operation. They will also analyse daily and weekly performance data levels, and monitor supplier KPIs, to ensure the highest level of customer experience is delivered efficiently. As the department continues to grow and develop support on new Projects will be a key requirement the role. 
 The Fulfillment Assistant will collaborate closely with the wider eCommerce, Fulfillment and third party supplier teams to ensure the highest of standards are maintained. 
 This is a great opportunity to blend high levels of data analysis, support of account management and a hands-on operational approach to drive real business change via process improvements and large scale project implementation. 
 The ideal candidate will have experience of Recent and current data management as well as working within eCommerce Fulfillment Operations. Strong Microsoft Excel and Access skills will be a daily requirement as well as a creative flair, with the ability to openly suggest new ideas. You will need the ability to manage day-to-day operational planning and analysis while maintaining strong relationships with suppliers and colleagues alike. 
 This is a highly visible role with outstanding career development opportunity for the ambitious and driven individual so if you feel that you can add value to this position please get in contact with Matt Harris for a confidential discussion. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
