If you don't have either of these items, you can still get a work at home job. How? Believe it or not, there are a few companies that will give you a computer and/or provide you with equipment in the form of a keyboard, mouse, USB headset and will even pay for your internet charges.  Work at home Jobs & Freebies for 12/8/2017  -  Enjoy! 
 Online Tutors 
 VIP Kid is is need of teachers to work from home to teach young students in China via video-conferencing. Teachers will earn on average between $14-$22 per hour. This opportunity is ideal for teachers looking to supplement their income. 
 Reservations Support Agent 
 Princess Cruises is currently looking to hire reservations agents to develop ongoing relationships with travel consultants and guests to assist them with their vacations. You must have 1 year customer service or sales experience, have excellent communication skills and live in the Arizona area. Paid training. Pays $12 per hour. 
 Call Center Reps 
 Windy City Call Center is currently hiring dependable individuals to make and take calls from students who have applied online to receive information on furthering their education. You will have strong listening skills, be able to multitask and type at least 35 wpm. You must live in Texas, Florida, Georgia or Illinois. Company will provide headset. Pays $9 per hour. 
 Home Improvement 
 Windy City Call Center is also hiring individuals to set up appointments for their clients with well known national home improvement companies. A script will be provided. Pays $9 per hour for the first 90 days. 
 Phone Customer Service Rep 
 
 CDS Global is is seeking reps to receive inbound calls and provide service to customers in the magazine and products industry. You must be passionate about helping others, can type 25 wpm, speak in a pleasant tone and can read, write and speak the English language. Pays $12 up to $13.50 per hour. Paid training. Iowa residents only. 
 Multimedia Judge 
 Lionbridge is seeking those who use the Internet daily and have strong communication skills to review, evaluate and provide feedback of online images and videos based on a set of guidelines using their online tools. You will need a PC with Windows software installed. Part time. 
 Call Reviewers 
 Humanatic is looking for people to listen to recorded calls and answer one question. No experience is required. You will not have to speak with anyone. Weekly pay via PayPal. Open worldwide. 
 English Transcription 
 Pactera Technologies is looking for remote workers to listen to audio files and write what you hear. You will need high speed Internet. 
 Crowdsourcing 
 
 Appen is seeking individuals to complete a variety of tasks at your own pace in transcription, online user experience and website evaluation. You will need good English spelling skills. 
 Hotel Booking Customer Service Agent 
 Working Solutions is seeking agents to take inbound calls to book reservations for leisure travelers. You must be comfortable upselling and closing the reservation sale. Background check required.
