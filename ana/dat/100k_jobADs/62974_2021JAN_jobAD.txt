 A large manufacturing organisation is seeking a hands on Financial Controller, to join their highly successful Business in North Staffordshire on a permanent basis. A salary in the range of £60,000 to £70,000 is on offer + pension, PHI and company bonus scheme. 
 This is an exciting time to join the company as it goes through a period of growth. The role will suit a dynamic individual who can work closely with the Managing Director in ensuring the company's financial targets and objectives are met. There is also the need to initially get into the detail and resolve some challenges they are having with their management Reporting / Accounting process, so the desire to 'get your hands dirty' at time is essential. 
 Financial Controller - Responsibilities 
 
 
 Managing a team of 5 Finance professionals 
 
 
 Guides and oversees the preparation of financial reporting, including the development of financial analyses, statements and forecasts 
 
 
 Acts as a key finance business partner in support of the MD by developing and driving strategies that support long-term growth 
 
 
 Generates business plans in line with strategic and operational objectives of the business unit 
 
 
 Monitors, tracks and drives performance, seeking corrective action where required to ensure delivery of the business plan 
 
 
 Challenges and supports the business unit head and Group Executive including taking a lead on investment appraisals (products, new business) 
 
 
 Oversees the performance of financial and regulatory compliance and governance 
 
 
 Drives the business unit to maintain all internal and external financial controls, accounting policies and procedures 
 
 
 Creates and maintains strategic relationships with stakeholders outside of the business unit Develops people, skills and succession plans, and manages the Finance team's performance 
 
 
 Financial Controller - Who are we looking for? 
 
 Will consider either a Chartered Accountant who trained with a recognised Audit firm ("1st movers" at Manager or Senior Manager grade are encouraged to apply), or ACMA and ACCA Qualified Accountants with relevant experience 
 Strong financial control skills 
 Minimum of 3 years post-qualification experience 
 The natural ability to influence and develop relationships 
 Track record of people management 
 Previous Large Company experience preferred 
 
 To apply for the Financial Controller position in North Staffordshire, please click on the "apply now" button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter (Jamie Carter). We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
