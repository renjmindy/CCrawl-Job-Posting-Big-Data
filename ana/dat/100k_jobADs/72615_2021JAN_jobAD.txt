 Conveyancer 
 Service Care Legal are currently recruiting for a Conveyancer for a law firm based in Hackney region of London. 
 The role has become available due to expansion, so please find below further details regarding this position. 
 This position is available for full time or part time hours. 
 ROLE: Conveyancer 
LOCATION: Hackney, London 
SALARY: £30,000 to £40,000 
 The Role 
 , To work a full caseload of Residential Conveyancing matters from inception to completion 
, Matters will include freehold and leasehold sales, transfer of equity and remortgages 
, To provide legislative advice to more junior members of staff 
, To undertake the occasional case of Commercial Conveyancing matters 
 The Person 
 , A qualified Solicitor or Licensed Conveyancer with a minimum of 2 years' PQE 
, Previous experience in progressing full caseloads through to completion with minimal supervision 
, Someone who has strong time management skills and ability to work well within a team 
 If this sounds like an opportunity of interest, please feel free to reach out to Lloyd Stanley on 01772 208969, or email Lloyd.stanley@servicecare.org.uk. 
 We also welcome referrals for this position, where a successful recommendation would be worth £250.
