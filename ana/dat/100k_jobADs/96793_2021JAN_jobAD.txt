 Project Advisor 
 Job description 
 
 
 Join a global leader in the mining sector 
 
 
 Initial 6 month contract with potential extension 
 
 
 FIFO 8/6 to Brockman 4 
 
 
 About The Role 
 WorkPac are seeking a Project Advisor to join a construction project implementation team for Rio Tinto Sustaining Capital on a FIFO 8/6 roster to Brockman 4. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 
 
 Assist with the implementation and delivery of project plans in line with construction schedules. 
 
 
 Supervise major structural upgrades and refurbishments for mine plant. 
 
 
 Provide interface between SMP and Electrical works. 
 
 
 Responsible for completions and handover to commissioning team. 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Tertiary qualification in Engineering or Construction. 
 
 
 Previous Rio Tinto project construction experience. 
 
 
 Current WA C Class Driver's license. 
 
 
 Strong decision-making capability along with ability to work autonomously. 
 
 
 Excellent interpersonal, written and oral communication skills. 
 
 
 Ability to lead, influence and motivate. 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
