 Support Worker 
 Service Care Solutions are recruiting for a Support Worker to work full time temporary agency contract within the NHS. 
 The role is based in Birmingham. 
 Pay (you will be paid weekly through an umbrella company) 
Days £9.10PAYE an hour 
Nights/Saturdays- 10.30 PAYE 
Sunday/Bank Hols- £11.50PAYE 
 You will be working with patients aged 0-25, must be a car driver and have your own car with business insurance. 
 Job Role 
, To provide physical and psychological care to service users in accordance with the specified plan of clinical care and in line with the recovery model to service users who live in any part of the catchment area. 
, To carry out duties as designated by the team leader. 
, To provide support to the person responsible for the clinical care of the service user. 
, To accurately record service user information. 
, To provide support to relatives/carers. 
 Must have 
, Be a car driver 
, Experience within the NHS or private care setting 
, Worked within Mental health 
 If this role sounds of interest please do send your CV to andrew.wiles@servicecare.org.uk
