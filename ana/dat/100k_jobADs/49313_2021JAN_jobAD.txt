 WHY DO WE SHOW EXPIRED LISTINGS? 
 We are currently seeking experienced couples or individuals to demonstrate and sell 100% American Made stainless steel, healthy cooking systems at well-known trade shows, expos and festivals. 
 Our Reps perform informative, energetic presentations on a healthy food preparation and stimulate sales. We provide complete on the job training. Our support team schedules and pays for all of your events, advertisements, processes orders, ships and even services your orders. You set up the display, tell our story, write the orders and earn the profit. You are in business for yourself, but not by yourself! 
 Having experience in outside sales, entertainment, teaching, trade shows or demonstrating is desired but not required. 
 Serious applications should: 
 
 Enjoy travel and public speaking 
 Be independent, reliable and friendly 
 Dress and present themselves professionally 
 Be able to work weekends 
 Be physically fit, and able to stand comfortably for extended periods of time 
 Have a valid driver's license 
 Have a vehicle or trailer that can be used to transport the exhibit (SUV, pickup truck, van, small enclosed trailer) 
 Be able to lift 25-30 lbs.
