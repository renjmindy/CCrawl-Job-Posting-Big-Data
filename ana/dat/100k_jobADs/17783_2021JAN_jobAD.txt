 We're looking for a driven, enthusiastic and talented Android developer to become an integral part of our growing, young and ambitious team - We're speedy and strive for fast-change to meet our user demand and to be regarded as the best micro-investing platform out there. The ideal candidate will fit seamlessly into what is a very ambitious and growing business, taking responsibility for the development and maintenance of our Android mobile product. We're at an integral part of our growth and need the best people around to keep up the significant traction we are building. 
 As an Android Developer you will drive and contribute to the development of features, fixing bugs and issues, reviewing code from others in the team and making a key contribution to the entire development lifecycle, from conception and discovery through development and delivery whilst maintaining a conscious effort to keep aligned to agreed priorities and set development windows, helping the business achieve its strategy in a timely and cost effective manner. 
 Requirements 
 
 We use Git as our main VCS. A basic understanding of Git is required. 
 Self-sufficiency: initiative, time management, multi tasking 
 Minimum 2 year's experience using Kotlin 
 Experience consuming RESTful APIs in Android apps 
 Able to create complex layouts which are maintainable and scalable 
 Comfortable with TDD 
 Experience with continuous integration / continuous deployment 
 Ability to demonstrate previous apps or projects you have worked on 
 Ability to handle multiple projects and maintain oversight to deliver all to on time completion 
 Proactively manage your workload, feeding back regularly in the event of any delay or change to delivery timeframe or scope of work 
 Huge attention to detail, curious and full of ideas 
 Understanding QA and testing environments 
 Knowledge of working in Agile 
 
 Big Plus 
 
 Experience working with and mentoring more junior developers 
 Previous experience on App first businesses 
 Startup experience / FinTech desirable 
 Self-starter who enjoys pushing the boundaries 
 
 The Perks 
 
 Great holiday allowance excluding bank holidays 
 Auto-enrolment pension 
 Training with extensive access to online courses and a set progression structure 
 Attend conferences and have the opportunity to represent the business at leading industry events 
 A genuine collaborative working environment where you will be supported, tested, and given meaningful responsibility 
 
 We are accepting UK based candidates only, please only apply if you meet this criteria.
