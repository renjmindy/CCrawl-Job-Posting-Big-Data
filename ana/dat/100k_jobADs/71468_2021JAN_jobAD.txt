 Groundsman for Residential Tree Company 
 Flying Dutchman Tree Services 
 __Duncan, BC 
 Apply Now 
 Flying Dutchman Tree Services is a young, active, and exciting tree company that provides services in the Cowichan Valley including hedge trimming, tree pruning, and tree removals. A groundsman will assist the climber and primary groundsman by keeping the work area clear, fetching items as needed, and running the chipper. For hedge trimming jobs, a groundsman might develop skills to trim, or can work to clean up all trimmings. Other duties can include small tree falling, bucking logs, and rope work. 
 As our company is growing, there is a lot of opportunity to work hard and increase your standing in the company as well as your wage! 
 Our company meets at the yard on Westholme Rd every morning for a 7:45 departure time. We take the company trucks to the clients homes to complete requested jobs. At the end of the day we will return to our yard, after approximately an 8 hour work day. 
 This job requires you to be physically fit, punctual, and logical. We are looking for a long-term, reliable employee who enjoys this work and will fit well with our team.
