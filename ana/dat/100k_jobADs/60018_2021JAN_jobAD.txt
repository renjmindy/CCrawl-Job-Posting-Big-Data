 Virtual Customer Service Professional 
 Virtual Customer Service Professional 
 We are seeking an experienced Customer Service professional to support our Executive team. 
 This is a long term role for someone interested in growing with us for a long time! This is a part-time position with flexible hours with the potential to increase hours and responsibilities. This is a virtual position. However, we desire a candidate within the same time zone as the Executive team. This position is based in the Pacific time zone. 
 This position will support members of the Executive team. The role will: 
 
 Serve as first impression for Work The System, with seasoned interpersonal skills 
 Portray professionalism that represents our brand and culture 
 Demonstrate exceptional customer service orientation 
 Bring expertise in email management 
 Organize calendars for the Executives 
 Schedule and coordinate meetings and events 
 Be able to handle sensitive and personal matters for each Executive 
 Possess outstanding written and verbal communication skills 
 Be interested and comfortable learning multiple computer technologies 
 
 Interested? Take the next step to become a part of our great culture - apply to our talent pool now. 
 Send us an email with your resume attached at:  info@workthesystem.com
