 Practice Accountant 
 
 Location 
 
 Stratford-upon-Avon, Warwickshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Accounting and Finance 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £25000.00 - £35000 per annum + Flexible Working 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Sophie Evans 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 sophieevans@workwithglee.com

 Published: 
 
 6 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-08-23 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson finance is delighted to be working with this growing practice in Stratford Upon Avon, to recruit an Accountant. 
 For this role you will be a either part of fully qualified accountant who has worked within a practice setting before. Due to continued growth and expansion you will be assigned a portfolio of SME customers and you will manage all of their accounts. Duties include: 
 
 Working on a shared portfolio across the business 
 Accounts preparation for Ltd companies, sole-traders and partnerships, up to £500k 
 Collaborating with the senior management team on a number of client accounts 
 Liaising with clients 
 Liaising with the HMRC 
 Submission of Vat and Tax Returns 
 Off-site client meetings 
 
 Due to Covid-19 you will initially be working from home, but with the intention of returning to the office when you are able. 
 This role is going to be an excellent opportunity for career development and a full study support package will be offered. 
 In return, you will receive a salary of C £25,000 - £35,000, plus fully flexible working, some working from home, pension, plus other benefits. If you have some experience of practice, and would like to learn more, please call Sophie Evans at Gleeson, on 07812 079718 for further info, or apply direct. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
