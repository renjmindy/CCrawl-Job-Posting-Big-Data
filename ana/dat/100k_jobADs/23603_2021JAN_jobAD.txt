 Find Your Career At World Cat! 
 A company's success is not determined solely by its marketing efforts or its product line—success begins with PEOPLE. World Cat is committed to its team members—those individuals who help shape the company through their personal commitment to excellence. 
 At World Cat we build an array of boats in many configurations. World Cat is the largest manufacturer of power catamarans in the world. Located in Tarboro, North Carolina, our boats are precision crafted in our state-of-the-art facility. 
 If you enjoy a challenging career opportunity producing a top-quality product, then a career with World Cat may be for you. 
 We have exciting career opportunities in the following areas: 
 
 Manufacturing 
 Engineering 
 Materials Management 
 Sales 
 Marketing 
 Quality Assurance and Quality Control 
 
 In addition, we offer a benefits package that includes Medical, Dental, Vision, Life Insurance, and 401K. 
 If you have interest in exploring exciting career opportunities with the World Cat team, please fill out this form here. Thank you!
