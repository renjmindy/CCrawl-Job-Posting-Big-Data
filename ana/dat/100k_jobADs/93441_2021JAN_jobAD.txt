 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently partnering with an established Sales & Distribution Business in Wolverhampton who are looking for a Senior Finance Business Partner. 
 The Role 
 As Senior Finance Business Partner you will report into the FD and is responsible for the following: 
 
 Business partner the Operational team, analysing and reporting on financial performance 
 Provide financial support, analysis and challenge to the business including financial performance reports and decision support analysis 
 To assist in the production of the company's management accounts to agreed deadlines 
 Investigate costs and provide recommendations on how to improve/reduce cost 
 Provide input to key commercial and analytical issues and decisions, attending business meetings when appropriate. 
 
 The Requirements 
 
 Qualified accountant - ACA/ACCA/CIMA - Minimum 2 years PQE 
 Strong ability to business partner & influence stakeholders. 
 Experience with dealing with non financial stakeholders 
 Strong commercial acumen 
 
 What's On Offer? 
 
 Salary of up to £70,000 + Bonus 
 Excellent career progression opportunities 
 Opportunity to work with a market-leading brand
