 TTC SCREENING SECURITY GUARD (12hr shifts 7am-7pm or 7pm-7am) 
 Are you passionate about Safety & Security and seeking opportunity to join a team of Experienced Security Professionals? 
 A.S.P. Incorporated has provided security and customer service solutions for over 20 years to Canadian clients. We employ in excess of 2000 employees and are a subsidiary of ICTS EUROPE, which is operating in 22 countries and employing more than 17000 professionals. A.S.P provide services to some of the largest airports in Canada and has a significant presence in the Commercial and Residential spaces in Ontario and Alberta. A.S.P. is looking for a Security Guard, who take pride in becoming ambassador of TTC and make every interaction professional and positive. Work with integrity, respect, and take responsibility of the security and safety.

 Providing Security Guard COVID-19 Screening Services to TTC. 
 Asking a series of covid-19 screening questions to all TTC Employees that enter through the site and document their responses 
 Observing employees for COVID related symptoms and providing feedback to TTC Management Team 
 Acting as an ambassador of the client site by providing customer service, responding to guest inquiries.

 Minimum High School diploma or Grade 12 education.
