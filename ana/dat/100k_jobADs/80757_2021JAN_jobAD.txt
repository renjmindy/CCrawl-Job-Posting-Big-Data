 The Finance Director is a key part of the executive management team based at their head office just outside the city in Birmingham. Reporting to the Managing Director, the role will have overall responsibility for all aspects of finance and to lead the Group's commercial finance activity. 
 Responsibilities will include: 
 
 Providing commercial challenge, support and advice to the senior team and partnering the MD in the delivery of performance 
 Key member of the executive team to support acquisitions and taking a lead with a post-acquisition integration work. 
 Leading and developing a high performing finance team and instil a culture of continuous improvement 
 Full ownership and accountability for the integrity and accuracy of the P&L, Balance Sheet and Working Capital requirements 
 Overall responsibility for cash management with a demonstrable track record in cash flow forecasting and working capital management 
 
 The successful candidate will be: 
 
 A qualified accountant (ACA, CIMA, ACCA) with strong commercial experience 
 Have experience of financial leadership roles and broad finance experience to include financial control, financial planning and partnering 
 Demonstrable experience in growing revenues, EBITDA and managing working capital and cash 
 Post-acquisition integration work 
 Experience of driving a performance culture 
 
 If this role is of interest, please apply with your full CV and current package.
