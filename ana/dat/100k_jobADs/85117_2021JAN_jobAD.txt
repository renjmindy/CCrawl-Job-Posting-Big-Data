 Maintenance Worker (Multiple) 
 Apply for the position, Maintenance Worker (Multiple), with City of Hays today or contact Human Resources for more information. Posted Jan 7, 2021. 
  Apply Online  __    7856287320  __    [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 The City of Hays is accepting applications to fill maintenance positions in the Service and Solid Waste Divisions. Duties involve manual labor, equipment operations, snow removal, and minor construction projects. Specialized duties may include concrete, street and alley maintenance; and collecting and processing recyclable materials and special collection items. 
 Working days include Monday-Friday.  These positions may be required to work a flexible work schedule as needed. 
 REQUIREMENTS 
Valid driver’s license required.  Paid on-the-job training for the right candidate. A good working knowledge of general construction; ability to read and interpret written instructions, maps, and service manuals; ability to operate a variety of equipment; a working knowledge of concrete, and asphalt preferred. Exposure to hazardous chemicals, machinery, excessive noise and all types of weather conditions is expected. Both positions require a CDL Class B within 6 months of employment. 
 COMPENSATION 
Starting wage $13.39 with room for advancement to a Maintenance Worker II and Senior Maintenance Worker position. Guaranteed raises every year.  Starting wage based on experience.  The City of Hays offers great benefits including health insurance, KPERS, Paid Time Off & Holidays. Visit  www.haysusa.com/employment  for more information. 
 HOW TO APPLY 
Applications will be accepted in City Hall, 1507 Main St, Hays, KS or online at  www.haysusa.com/employment . Positions opened until filled. Questions about the positions may be sent to Human Resources at  [email protected]  or by calling 785-628-7320. EOE
