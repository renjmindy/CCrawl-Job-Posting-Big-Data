 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs November 2011 
 Posted on  December 5, 2011  by  caregiver 
 For the month of November 2011, 10 Job Orders were approved by the POEA allowing 6 licensed and accredited recruitment agencies to begin recruitment of applicants for their  POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs November 2011 . These POEA approved caregiver jobs November 2011 are for the countries of Israel, Kuwait, Canada, and Bahrain whose total caregiver job vacancies is 154. Specified below are the names of the recruiting agencies, the country of destination, and the actual caregiver jobs for each recruiting agency. 
 ISRAEL 
CPM Manpower Services, Inc. --------------------------- 78 caregiver jobs 
Mariposa International Services Co. Inc. --------------- 24 caregiver jobs 
HRD Employment Consultant and Multi Services Inc. --- 30 caregiver jobs 
 KUWAIT 
A & C International Resources Inc. --------------------- 10 caregiver jobs 
** 
CANADA** 
Luzern International Manpower Services Corp. --------- 9 caregiver jobs 
 BAHRAIN 
ABCA International Corporation ------------------------ 3 caregiver jobs 
 To apply for any of the POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs November 2011, submit your resume directly to the office of recruiting agency listed above so you can also fill up their application form. If you have any question regarding any the POEA approved caregiver jobs November 2011, you can direct them to the corresponding recruitment agency by way of their landline numbers specified below.
