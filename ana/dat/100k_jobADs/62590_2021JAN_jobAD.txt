 We are looking for ... 
 Friendly bike taxi drivers to expand our team. If you are at least 18 years old, and enjoy having a job connecting lots of movement with people, Velotaxi is definitely the right choice for you. 
 
 Flexible working days 
 Good earning potential 
 Variety and lots of fun 
 Great team and super support

 Work & pedal! 
 Your tasks 
 We offer you a job with lots of fun in the fresh air with flexible working times. Within our daily business you drive passengers from A to B with our mobile Bike taxis. 
 As a leaser of our vehicle you keep 100% of your passenger income as an independent driver. We also offer you the possibility to participate in events such as sightseeing tours, wedding tours and corporate shuttles.
