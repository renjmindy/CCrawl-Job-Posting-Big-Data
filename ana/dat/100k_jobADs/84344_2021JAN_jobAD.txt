 Dutch Speaking Credit Controller (Home Based) 
 Are you a Dutch speaking Credit Controller, and available to undertake a superb 6 month temporary position? Our impressive, International business services client who operate their EMEA Shared Service Centre in Bracknell are looking to recruit a Dutch speaking Credit Controller to join their team in supporting and delivering to clients across EMEA, initially on a 6 month temporary basis. 
 As a Dutch speaking Credit Controller you will be responsible for a variety of different areas, around credit management, credit collection, cash application and account reconciliation and handling queries, and handling a ledger of around 1,000 customer accounts. 
. 
 Duties: 
 
 Collection of outstanding debt from across EMEA customer base (around 1,000 - 1,500 accounts) 
 Customer risk assessment and credit analysis 
 Query resolution 
 Various reconciliations 
 Cash allocation, credit note and debit note processing to customer account 
 Reconcile disputes promptly and professionally across EMEA 
 Liaise with all necessary stakeholders (internal and external) to ensure customer accounts are fully reconciled 
 
 Profile: 
 
 Experience within Accounts Receivable or Credit Controller 
 Experience of chasing debtors across EMEA 
 Experience of working with foreign currencies would be beneficial 
 Strong knowledge of Microsoft packages, Excel, Word etc 
 Excellent customer service skills across multiple locations 
 Excellent interpersonal skills 
 Industry or shared service experience 
 Fluency in written & oral English 
 Team Player 
 Accurate data entry skills. 
 Fluency in Dutch, and potentially one other European language 
 Experience with ERP systems, JD Edwards/Oracle/SAP experience a plus 
 Ability to work and deliver collaboratively across a complex global matrix 
 
 This is a unique opportunity to join an award-winning business. Please contact me for further information of this superb Dutch Credit Control opportunity based in Bracknell. Initially this will be hired on a 6 month temporary basis, with the potential for this to be extended, subject to performance and sign-off. Our client can offer superb training, and this role will be a home based opportunity, due to the COVD restrictions, so their current offices are closed.
