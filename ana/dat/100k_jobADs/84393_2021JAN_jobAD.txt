 Electrical Fitter 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 - 4:5, 5:4, 5:5 roster 
- Residential (Dampier or Karratha) 
- 12.5 hour shifts 
 About our Client 
 Rio Tinto operates the world's largest integrated portfolio of iron ore assets. These assets include a world-class, integrated network of 15 iron ore mines, four port facilities, a 1,700 kilometre rail network and related infrastructure. Rio Tinto are expanding operations in the Pilbara to epic proportions while introducing next generation technologies to deliver greater efficiency, lower production costs and improved health, safety and environmental performance. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are currently seeking experienced Electricians for work on the Dampier Ports. The role is open to Residents of Karratha or Dampier working on a 4/5, 5/4, 5/5 roster. 
 Duties: 
 Perform complex troubleshooting, fault rectification duties, repairs, modifications and preventative maintenance procedures to meet the operational requirements of the specific location 
Service, inspection and breakdown repairs 
Inspection and repairs of compressors 
Equipment isolations 
Test and tagging 
Schneider Unity, Citect, Wonderware) under PLC experience. 
 Skills and Experience: 
 2 years + experience working in a Mining environment 
Electrical Fitter Trade Certificate 
WA Electrical License 
Cert IV Electrical Instrumentation- highly desirable 
Current Australian 'C' manual class driver licence 
HR Drivers Licence (preferred) 
High Risk Licence - WP (preferred) 
Confined Space (preferred) 
Working at Heights (preferred)

 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities.
