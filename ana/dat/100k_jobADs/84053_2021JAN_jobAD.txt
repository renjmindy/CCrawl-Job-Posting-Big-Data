 Consultant: 
 
 
 Gleeson Finance are delighted to be working in partnership with this corporate, forward thinking, well-known business in Northampton, to recruit a permanent Financial Accountant in the risk department. 
 Working closely with the Financial Controller, you'll be joining a business going through a period of change. Some of your key responsibilities include statutory accounts, group reporting, year end, financial planning and analysis, presenting information to senior management and audit. This is a great opportunity for any qualified accountant looking for their first move from practice or an experienced financial accountant from industry. 
 You will be a strong technical accountant, with a relevant professional qualification, advanced Excel skills, and proven hands on experience. 
 In return, you will receive a salary of circa £45,000 with an excellent benefits package including 27 days holiday (+ 8 statutory), discretionary bonus, pension, sharesave scheme, flexible working, free on-site parking and lifestyle benefits. 
 If interested, please apply direct, or call Sophie Evans, Gleeson Recruitment, on 07812 079718 for further information. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
