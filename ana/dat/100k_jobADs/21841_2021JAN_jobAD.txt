 Caregivers Jobs Hiring 
 Posted on  September 25, 2010  by  caregiver 
 Last September 14, the POEA approved a job order allowing a POEA accredited recruitment agency to recruit applicants who are qualified for  Caregivers Jobs Hiring . These caregivers jobs hiring are for principals in Israel and was approved by the POEA for Jao International Manpower Services. For the said job order, 30 applicants will be chosen for placement in caregiver work abroad. 
 To apply for the said caregivers jobs, hiring applicants must submit their resume and application directly to the office of the recruiting agency. For more details or inquiries regarding the said caregivers jobs hiring you can contact the agency’s landline numbers provided below. 
 JAO International Manpower Services 
Unit E 3rd Floor, Jacinto Building 
380 Scout Reyes corner Quezon Avenue 
Paligsahan, Quezon City 
Tel Nos.: (632) 774-0995 / (632) 332 4234 
Email address: admin@jaointernational.com 
Website: http://jaointernational.com
