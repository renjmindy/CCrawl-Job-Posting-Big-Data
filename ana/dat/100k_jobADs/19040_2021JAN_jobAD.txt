 At Wells Fargo, we want to satisfy our customers' financial needs and help them succeed financially. We're looking for talented people who will put our customers at the center of everything we do. Join our diverse and inclusive team where you'll feel valued and inspired to contribute your unique skills and experience. 
 Help us build a better Wells Fargo. It all begins with outstanding talent. It all begins with you. 
 Wells Fargo Technology is a team of more than 40,000 information technology and security professionals who help keep Wells Fargo at the forefront of America's diversified financial services companies. Employees execute an engineering-led IT strategy to deliver stable, secure, scalable and innovative services that provide Wells Fargo global customers 'round-the-clock' banking access through in-store, online, ATM, and other channels. Wells Fargo Technology plays a critical role in the company's customer and employee experience, business and risk management transformation, and growth agenda. 
 This Software Engineer role will support the Wealth & Investment Management Technology team with joint core responsibilities of software design, development and testing as a key team member on an agile product development team. This team member will drive the SDLC process for several critical mainframe applications and some distributed applications within WIMT and define robust testing strategy and help implement automation solutions to oversee overall systems testing and the migration of platforms and applications to production. The team member will be expected to perform development activities included, but not limited to the following listed below: 
 
 Partner closely with the business to understand requirements, business functionality and test execution needs from release to release 
 Creates, prepares, and implements systems quality assurance reviews for numerous applications 
 Will play the role of software designer, developer, reviewer, and tester on a cross functional agile team 
 Collaborate with business and the development team to identify test automation opportunities 
 Identify and Script net new test cases that will support new functionality in the Brokerage - WIMT area 
 Provide technical expertise in system architecture, automation architecture, integration, debugging, troubleshooting errors 
 Plan and strategize the automation approach to scripting and development of new tools as it pertains to new functionality and customer demands 
 Be responsible for individual coding task assignments as well as assess impact to integrating code 
 Act as a mentor for other team members, provide training as required to other resources on following SDLC processes, business process, test script creation, maintenance, debugging and troubleshooting 
 
 Required Qualifications 
 
 5+ years of software engineering experience 
 5+ years of information technology experience 
 5+ years of quality assurance experience 
 5+ years of mainframe experience 
 
 Desired Qualifications 
 
 An industry-standard technology certification 
 Strong verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills 
 Knowledge and understanding of automated regression and maintaining scripts in regression 
 Knowledge and understanding of project management and implementation in a QA testing environment 
 5+ years of SDLC (System Development Life Cycle) experience 
 5+ years of experience drafting use cases and test scripts 
 2+ years of TSO (Time Sharing Options) experience 
 5+ years of JCL (Job Control Language) experience 
 5+ years of Cobol experience 
 2+ years of leadership experience 
 3+ years of JIRA experience 
 Experience with Agile Scrum (Daily Standup, Sprint Planning and Sprint Retrospective meetings) and Kanban 
 Knowledge and understanding of brokerage industry/operations 
 
 Other Desired Qualifications 
 
 Proven experience with continuous integration and deployment 
 Experience working with keyword and data-driven automation frameworks 
 Proven experience in implementing In Sprint Automation within Agile teams

 Wells Fargo & Company (NYSE: WFC) is a diversified, community-based financial services company with $1.9 trillion in assets.
