 Forensic Mental Healthcare Assistant in West Sussex 
 Service Care Solutions require a Healthcare Assistant/ Support Worker to work as and when shifts for a private Secure mental health Hospital in Hassocks, West Sussex. 
 The role will involve working within various adult patients with mental health, challenging behaviours and learning disabilities. 
 Your role with include observing their wellbeing and assisting nurses with on-going treatment. 
 We have a mixture of long days, night shifts and Weekends 
 Essential: 
, Recent experience working as a Health care assistant or support worker in the mental health sector. 
 , Must be able to engage with patients/clients 
, Flexible 
 Desirable experience: 
, 6 months experience 
, Any form of Control and Restraint training (PMVA/MAPA/MAYBO) 
, Experience of working within Secure Units 
 This is an excellent opportunity either for anyone looking for work to supplement your current job / studies/ family life or as an opportunity for flexible part T time/full T time employment. 
 The Limited/ umbrella hourly rates of pay are as follows: 
, £10.50 ltd hour for weekdays 
, £11.50 ltd for nights 
£12.00 ltd Weekends
