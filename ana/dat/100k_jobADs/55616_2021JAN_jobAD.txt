 Gleeson Finance are delighted to be working in an exclusive partnership with this SME business in Coventry, to recruit a Management Accountant, based in Coventry. If you are looking to be part of an ambitious and growing company, in a role that combines project and management accounting, and you are ACA or ACCA qualified, we want to hear from you. 
 Reporting in to the Finance Director, your role will involve monthly management accounts; 
group reporting and liaison with external stakeholders, balance sheet reconciliations and some day to day processing, hands on projects, cashflow and profit & loss analysis and journals. 
 In order to be considered for this role, you will be a fully qualified ACA or ACCA. You will get the chance to be part of a forward thinking, changing company in Coventry, with a fantastic culture and continuous opportunities. This role would suit someone from a practice environment, and would be an excellent first or second move. 
 In return, you will receive a full time equivalent salary of circa £45,000 - £50,000, full flexible working, attractive hours with a half day on a Friday, and reduced hours will also be considered, plus other benefits. 
 To hear more about what this role can offer, please call Sophie Evans on 07812 079718. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
