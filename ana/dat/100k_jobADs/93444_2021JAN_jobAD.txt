 Finance Supervisor - new position for a growing business based in Telford. 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group is currently recruiting for a market leading business to appoint a Finance Supervisor to their team. 
 This is an excellent opportunity to work with a rapidly growing organisation that is undergoing a period of very positive change. 
 The Role 
 Your focus will be to support the ledger & treasury functions of the business. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Overseeing the team in the day to day operations of the sales, purchase and nominal ledgers 
 Ensuring the team are up to date with reconciliations and other outstanding issues for month/year end requirements 
 VAT returns 
 Fixed asset register 
 Supporting the team in the more "hands on" tasks, including invoice processing, matching, coding, and any associated query resolution 
 Cash position reporting & balance sheet analysis 
 Managing all related stakeholders across the business, ensuring all required information is accessible for reporting purposes 
 Creating and maintaining strong cross-functional working partnerships with the wider business 
 
 This is a permanent opportunity that will offer a fantastic challenge for a candidate with hands-on ledger skills, with a desire to use their additional skills in cash management and other areas of financial accounting. Salary ranges from £26-30k per annum plus benefits. 
 The Candidate 
 The ideal candidate would have the following: 
 
 Extensive experience and a proven track record in a similar role with ledgers and treasury exposure 
 An AAT qualification 
 Personable, supportive and customer-focused attitude 
 A strong communicator, able to motivate and mentor key members of staff 
 Hands on capabilities, comfortable performing more repetitive tasks where necessary 
 Strong Excel skills 
 
 Please click apply, or call Jo Payne on 07813 608579 for more details.
