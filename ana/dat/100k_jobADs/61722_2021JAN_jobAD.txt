 Head of Environmental Health 
 Head of Environmental Health required ASAP based in Stroud. 
 This role is on an ongoing contract. 
 The purpose of the role is to provide overall leadership and management of the Environmental Health service. 
 Duties include: 
 To lead the district's environmental health response to the Covid-19 pandemic and public health emergency. 
To provide effective leadership and overall management of the Environmental Health team and services comprising: food safety, health and safety, environmental protection, licensing, pest control, animal welfare, renewable energy, home energy efficiency, private sector housing renewal and the land drainage responsibilities. 
To contribute to the development and delivery of comprehensive and new approaches to delivering the corporate priorities of the Council. 
 Candidates will need: 
 To be educated to degree level or equivalent, ideally with a postgraduate qualification in a relevant subject. 
 Experience and strong track record at a senior level within the Environmental Health field. 
To be able to communicate effectively with a variety of different stakeholders and audiences within and outside the Council 
 If this position sounds of interest, and you have the relevant qualifications and experience, please don't hesitate to contact me at jack.windsor@servicecare.org.uk, or call Jack at Service Care Construction on 01772 208967.
