 Senior Front-End Developer in Limassol 
 Full Time 
 
 __Post Date: December 1, 2020 
 __Apply Before: March 31, 2021 
 
 Job Description 
 On behalf of one of our clients a company that offers a unique approach based on integral solutions of geolocation systems, data fusion and operations from diverse sources in a single platform and interface, we are seeking to recruit a Senior Front-End Developer to join their team in Limassol. 
 Job Responsibilities 
 
 Develop efficient and reusable front-end systems & components 
 Refactor and Optimize existing codebase for responsiveness, speed and scalability 
 Provide tech guidance and mentorship to other front-end developers 
 Collaborate with UI/UX team, product managers and other stakeholders in delivering the ultimate 
user experience 
 Perform code reviews and engage in pair-programming sessions 
 Collaborate closely with other cross-functional team members 
 Actively participate in knowledge transfer 
 
 Job Requirements 
 
 At least 5 years of experience as a Front End Developer 
 Hands on experience with Angular 2+ or React 
 Working experience with HTML5, CSS, SASS, JavaScript ES6 and/or TypeScript 
 Experience with map-centric applications 
 Experience with RESTful Services and APIs 
 Comfortable with Responsive and Material Design 
 Understanding accessibility and security principles (OWASP) 
 Version Control (preferably Git) 
 Experience with Linux O/S is desirable 
 
 What You’ll Enjoy: 
 
 Competitive salary based on skills and experience 
 Yearly bonus 
 Working from home policy & Flexible hours 
 Free daily lunch voucher 
 Udemy unlimited membership 
 Sport/Gym membership 
 Engage with new technologies and innovative products 
 Internal hackathons and technical seminars 
 
 How to Apply 
 If interested and you match all the requirements, please send your CV to  andriana.p@work-channel.com  , including the title of the position in the subject line 
 Please note  that due to the high volume of applications received, only successful candidates will be contacted
