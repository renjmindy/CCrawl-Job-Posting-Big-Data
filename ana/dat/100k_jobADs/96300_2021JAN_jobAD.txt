 Account Executive 
 New York, NY / Remote 
 Company Description 
 Workestra makes work and team management smart & easy so teams can focus on the work that matters. With a rapidly growing user base, we’re on a mission to create a better way of working. Workestra is headquartered in New York, NY, and has a large remote working culture. 
 The Team 
 Our Sales team shares Workestra with businesses around the world and helps them understand the power Workestra to teams at scale. 
 Role Description 
 As an Account Executive, you’ll help our future customers understand the power of Workestra for their business. We’re growing quickly and are just getting started. You’ll join a talented team focused on one thing: simplifying their work using technology. We’re looking for superstar, ambitious candidates who are passionate about helping people with the way they work. We’re building the next great technology company, and we need world-class talent to achieve our vision. 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Full-cycle sales with responsibility for building and maintaining a pipeline of prospective and current customer deals 
 Be the voice of the customer to the product team 
 Evaluate opportunities to increase customer satisfaction, sales, and retention within a book of business 
 Deliver product demos, provide insightful technical answers, and recommend creative ways to get the most out of Workestra 
 Work cross-functionally with Lead Generation, Account Management, and Solutions Architecture teams 
 Help scale processes including lead generation, presentation, negotiation, recruiting, and more 
 Find comfort in working in a fast-paced environment 
 Believe no task is too small, and no task is too large 
 
 Requirements 
 
 1-3 years of inside sales experience 
 Top performer in previous roles 
 Prior experience at Internet or SaaS companies is a major plus 
 Passionate about revolutionizing the way people work 
 
 Benefits and Perks 
 
 Market competitive total compensation package 
 Free Workestra space for your friends and family 
 Generous vacation policy 
 
 Workestra is an equal opportunity employer. We are a welcoming place for everyone, and we do our best to make sure all people feel supported and connected at work.
