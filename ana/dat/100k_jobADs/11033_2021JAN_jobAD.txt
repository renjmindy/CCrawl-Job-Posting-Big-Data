 Utility Worker / Kitchen Hand 
 Job description 
 
 
 
 Join a global leader in the mining sector FIFO 2/1 to the Pilbara with travel time paid Ongoing casual assignment About The Role WorkPac are seeking a Utility Worker / Kitchen Hand to join Rio Tinto Exploration on a FIFO 2/1 roster to the Paterson region. Duties & Responsibilities Join a remote, site based team on a FIFO 2/1 roster to the Pilbara. Assist with on site utilities and kitchen hand tasks as required. Work within small teams on remote exploration sites. Skills & Experience Must have experience working on remote sites. Previous experience working in utilities and as a kitchen hand in mining or exploration. Able to pass a pre-employment medical and D&A.; Unrestricted rights to work in Australia. WA C Class Driver's Licence. About WorkPac WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors.
