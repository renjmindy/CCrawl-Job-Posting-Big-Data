 Consulting Job Preparation Bootcamp 
 Posted by  Jamel Meslamani  on  2016-04-192016-04-19 
 The Tri-I Consulting club will be putting on a 2-day Consulting Job Preparation Bootcamp held on  Saturday, May 21st 2016  and  Sunday, May 22nd 2016. 
 This 2-day bootcamp is designed to help you prepare for a job in management consulting. The first day will be a series of lecture style presentations held by the company, Management Consulted. It will be dedicated to information about the consulting interview process, networking, how to write a resume and cover letter and some business basics. The second day will be dedicated solely to preparing you for the case part of the interview. This part will be in smaller interactive groups and presented by people who are experienced in casing. A breakdown of each day is listed below: 
 Day 1: Saturday, May 21st (Caspary Auditorium at Rockefeller University) 
 
 9-10AM - What is Consulting - Really? 
 10-10:45AM - The Consulting Recruiting Process 
 10:45-11AM _ - Quick break (coffee)_ 
 11-12PM - Networking for Consulting 
 12-1PM - The Consulting-Focused Resume 
 1-2PM - Lunch (Lunch will be provided) 
 2-3PM - Consulting Cover Letters 
 3-4PM - Business Basics for Consulting 
 
 Day 2: Sunday, May 22nd (CRC Rm 206, 306, 406, 506 at Rockefeller University) 
 
 
 10:00-10:30 Part 1: Casing Overview 
 
 
 10:30-11:00 Part 2: Receiving and Opening a Case 
 
 
 11:00-12:00 Part 3: Frameworks 
 
 
 12:00-1:00 Lunch Break 
 
 
 1:00-2:45 Part 4: Cracking the Case 
 
 
 2:45-3:00 Part 5: Closing the Case 
 
 
 3:00-3:45 Part 6: Full-case 
 
 
 3:45-4:00 Part 7: Final thoughts and next steps 
 
 
 There are two options available for payment. You can either buy a ticket for each day individually for $30 each, or get a combo deal of both days for $50. The event will be advertised for the first week to only the Tri-I area, but afterwards will be advertised to other schools in the NY area. Space is limited, so please RSVP quickly if you would like to attend. I look forward to seeing you all there! Please RSVP and pay with the eventbrite link below: 
 https://www.eventbrite.com/e/2-day-consulting-job-preparation-bootcamp-tickets-24718922964 
 Location:  Caspary Auditorium, Rockefeller University - 1230 York Ave, New York, New York 10065
