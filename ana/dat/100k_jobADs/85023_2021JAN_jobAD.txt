 About the Role 
WorkPac is currently seeking an experienced Qualified Builder/Carpenter with a positive team and culture focus to carry out carpentry duties including formwork and fitout. 
 Willingness to assist with other duties onsite and not just work within their trade qualifications. 
 Skills & Experience Required 
 
 
 Carpentry Trade Certificate/Qualification 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 Mining experience preferred but not essential 
 
 
 Hourly rate subject to experience 
 
 
 MR/HR Licence 
 
 
 Commitment to safety in everything you do
