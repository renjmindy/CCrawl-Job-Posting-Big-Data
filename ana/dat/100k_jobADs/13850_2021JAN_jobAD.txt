 Finance Assistant - Telford - £19-21k. Great opportunity to join a vibrant team of people in a busy environment. 
 Our Client 
 We are working with an organisation that boasts an enviable reputation in its sector. We are now seeking a Finance Assistant to join their finance team. 
 The Role 
 This is a great opportunity to join a business that is experiencing an exciting trading period. It will be a busy and challenging time to work with them. The team operates with positivity and energy, and it is a well-organised set-up within lovely offices. 
 Reporting into the Financial Controller, the role will involve the following tasks: 
 
 Processing purchase orders and generating invoices 
 Resolution of supplier queries 
 Maintaining cash book 
 Intercompany reconciliations 
 Assisting with month end processes 
 Bank reconciliations 
 Processing of paperwork to ensure prompt deliveries and payments 
 Ad hoc support tasks for the team during busy periods 
 
 This is a permanent opportunity, and will offer variety and challenge to the successful candidate. 
 It will suit someone with variety in their skill base in accounts, with a supportive, positive attitude. 
 Please call Jo Payne on 07813 608579 to discuss further.
