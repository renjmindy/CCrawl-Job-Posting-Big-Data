 Company description 
 With over 35 years of service to our local community, Aden Footwear offers its customers the complete shoe shopping experience. As one of the flagship retail stores in Woodstock, our team members need to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment. 
 Job description 
 We are looking for an enthusiastic self-starter who will help our team by providing administrative support in an office setting. Experience using a variety of social media platforms is a plus! 
 Responsibilities will include: 
 
 managing social media engagement initiatives 
 corresponding with suppliers 
 arranging couriers 
 scanning, printing and organizing files 
 ordering merchandise and supplies 
 processing bank deposits 
 maintaining POS inventory and pricing 
 
 The ideal candidate should possess: 
 
 superior multi-tasking skills and versatility 
 excellent computer skills (MS Office, email, POS) 
 the ability to work under minimal supervision 
 creative problem solving skills 
 an eagerness to learn 
 punctuality 
 significant attention to detail 
 interpersonal skills 
 a can-do attitude! 
 
 In turn we offer competitive wages, paid vacation time, generous store discounts (after probation period), and an opportunity to grow within our organization.
