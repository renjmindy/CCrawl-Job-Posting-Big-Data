 Build educational experiences students & teachers love 
 Position:  UX Design Lead 
 Contract:  3 days a week, permanent 
 Start date:  February 2021 
 Location:  London, UK (office-based with possibility for remote work) 
 " Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."  Steve Jobs. 
 We are looking for a brilliant UX Design Lead, who will work in our growing Product Team to craft our teacher and student-facing applications. Part of being on this team is a love for building a product you're proud to share with your friends and family. 
 We strive for simple, delightful user experiences. You'll be a part of a team that's tackling difficult questions of usability, architecture and experience design within education. This is a fantastic opportunity for someone who enjoys working within a fast-paced start-up environment as part of a phenomenal team working together to grow Lyfta into a formidable global education company. 
 About Lyfta 
 Lyfta  makes awe-inspiring digital learning experiences to foster empathy and global understanding. We have developed an award-winning, subscription-based digital platform where teachers and pupils can access stunning immersive storyworlds and curriculum-based lesson and assembly plans. 
 Our theme-based learning experiences are ideal for teaching many subjects in primary and secondary school, including but not limited to Literacy, PSHE, Global Citizenship, Design Technology, Geography, Science and Art, as well as fostering 21st Century skills and values such as empathy, self-direction and critical thinking. 
 We are a positive, talented and hard-working group of people with a big goal: to contribute to a better world through education. Diversity and inclusion are at the heart of everything we do with Lyfta - from the immersive stories on our platform, to our team and the way we behave and operate as a company. We aim to nurture a culture that respects and values each other's differences, that promotes dignity, equality and diversity, and supports individuals to develop and maximise their true potential. 
 Purpose of the role 
 The UX Design Lead is a new and exciting role that will be instrumental in Lyfta's continued growth and success. The UX Design Lead will be an integral part of the cross-functional Product team, consisting of Product Owner, Head of Engineering, Creative Designer and a team of developers, working to improve and build on Lyfta's existing learning offering, as well as design new products for new market segments. S(he) will be leading the user experience design of all Lyfta's products and services, supervising the Creative Designer. 
 Role description & responsibilities 
 The UX Design Lead is responsible for the user experience design of all Lyfta services and products. (S)he participates in weekly product meetings and shares her/his research insights and user stories with the team, creates wireframes and task flows for feedback and actions the final designs based on spec. The UX Design Lead collaborates with the Product Owner to build the best tools and processes to gather user feedback and closely monitors usage data to better understand user behaviour. S(he) will conduct user research through existing channels and propose new ways to engage in user research. 
 The UX Design Lead is tasked to communicate design ideas through wireframes and click-through prototypes, find creative ways to solve UX problems (e.g. usability, accessibility), and to implement attractive designs. S(he) is involved with the entire product cycle starting from research and data analysis, concept and usability testing, defining design requirements, providing feedback to developers, iteration and evaluation of the product's success. 
 Additionally, s(he) keeps abreast of industry trends, best practices and tools and contributes with product feature suggestions that give Lyfta a competitive edge in the market. 
 **Skills and experience  ** 
 
 5+ years of commercial experience in UX/UI design 
 Experience leading a team and working with colleagues in senior roles 
 Proven experience in user research and testing 
 Strong portfolio of design projects 
 Self-starter with a can-do attitude 
 Expertise in developing wireframes, task flows and click-through prototypes 
 Experience with design systems, such as Abstract, Figma or Sketch 
 Experience with Adobe Creative Suite 
 Experience collaborating with other designers and developers to create intuitive user-friendly software for both web and mobile interfaces 
 Proficiency with interaction design, especially in 360° and VR environments is desirable 
 An understanding of the EdTech sector or working on EdTech products is desirable 
 An understanding of HMTL/CSS is a plus 
 
 Remuneration and benefits 
 
 30,000 GBP p/a (50,000 pro-rata) 
 15 days annual leave + UK bank holidays 
 Work laptop 
 Annual team retreats and regular team outings 
 Lovely, bright office space in Islington with good coffee :) 
 
 How to apply 
 Please apply by sending a CV along with a covering letter, stating how your skills and experience match the description for this role, and why you would like to work at Lyfta. 
 Recruitment timeline 
 The role will be filled when the right candidate is found. We look forward to receiving your application :)
