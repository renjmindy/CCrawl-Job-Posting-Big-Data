 Electrical Fitter/Instrumentation 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Casual ongoing position - Potential for full time WorkPac opportunity after 3-6 months 
 
 
 Role open to the residents of Karratha and Dampier Only 
 
 
 12.5-hour shifts 
 
 
 Day Shift/Night Shift 
 
 
 About the Role 
 Ongoing opportunities exist for Qualified Trade Persons working a family friendly residential roster. 
 Skills and Experience: 
 
 
 Trade Qualification 
 
 
 C Class Australian Drivers Licence essential 
 
 
 2 years + experience working in a Mining environment 
 
 
 Electrical Fitter Trade Certificate 
 
 
 WA Electrical License 
 
 
 Cert IV Electrical Instrumentation- highly desirable 
 
 
 About our Client 
 Rio Tinto operates the worlds largest integrated portfolio of iron ore assets. These assets include a world-class, integrated network of 15 iron ore mines, four port facilities, a 1,700 kilometre rail network and related infrastructure. Rio Tinto are expanding operations in the Pilbara to epic proportions while introducing next generation technologies to deliver greater efficiency, lower production costs and improved health, safety and environmental performance.
