 Full-Time Accountant 
 EPV Energy -  Ostrobothnia  , Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 EPV Energy applies for financial accountant with knowledge of EPV Energy Authority (EBA) is a Finnish electricity and heat production and acquisition of specialized energy. The company supplies about 4 TWh of electricity, which is equivalent to about 5 per cent of all years, the electricity consumed. EDA is investing heavily in the development of emission-free energy production. 
 The company is currently undergoing a major wind power investment. EPO - the Group's turnover in 2011 was EUR 175 million. The company's head office in Vaasa. We are now looking financial accountant with knowledge of Vaasa period. 
 An accountant in a diverse and challenging task include general ledger, monthly reporting, and payment functions. In addition, your work may include payroll, travel and expense billing-related tasks. We expect a strong accounting and payroll knowledge and experience in the electronic financial management systems. 
 You must have appropriate training (eg, Bachelor of Business Administration), and a few years of work experience in similar tasks. You are also interested in seeing behind the numbers and to be involved in the development of financial electronic processes. The task we are looking for motivated, spontaneous, and positive team players. Benefit includes payroll knowledge and experience of the Group companies' financial management tasks. 
 We offer a varied and interesting work at the prestigious Finnish companies. You will be there with a large number of financial professionals, as well as wide range of opportunities to develop your expertise.
