 Boilermaker/Mechanical Fitter/Fitter & Turner (delete as applicable, Casual Rate) 
 Job description 
 Client requires a trade qualified and highly experienced Boilermaker to support their maritime fleet. The requirements will be varied and may involve the fabrication of landing gear, hinges, doors, panel repairs, patching, etc. of vessels. Applicant to have a high regard for safe work practices, be environmentally aware, particularly in relation to marine pollution, have own basic tools of trade and appropriate PPE. Hours are 9.5 per day, Mon-Fri and a half day Saturday.
