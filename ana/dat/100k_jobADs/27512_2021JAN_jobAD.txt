 C. Cole features labelmate Ejoba in "Pop Am" music video 
 Dec 10 
 Posted by  Christine Hartley 
 Ocean Wide Records' own C.cole hooks up with label mate and Edo state rave of the moment Ejoba for Pop Am visuals. These multi-talented young artists bring a whole new style and swag to the fast growing Nigerian music scene as demonstrated in the unique and refreshing video from the young minds. you surely want to follow them up after watching this video. another one from super director Lecture. 
 The song is produced by The doppest producer JayPaulBeatz (@callme_jaypaul), Video directed by Lecture. Probably, you must have listened to lots of songs produced by same JayPaulBeatz…
