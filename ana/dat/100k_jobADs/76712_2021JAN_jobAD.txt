 A language center in Long Gang district, Shenzhen, Guangdong Province(Near Hong Kong) is looking for English teacher to start immediately. Holding a Bachelor degree, teaching experience is required. The location is near Longcheng square metro station. 
 Working schedule:  The working schedule is from 15: 00pm to 21: 00pm during weekdays, 9: 00am-18: pm in the weekends, two days off per week. The class size is 8 students per class, the students are aged from 3-15 years old. 
 Benefits:  The base salary is 15000 RMB/month with three months probation including 40 teaching hours per week. After probation, the salary is 16000 RMB per month including 40 working hours per week; 120 RMB per hour for over 40 teaching hours per week. 
 Z visa is provided for qualified native English speakers (Holding a Bachelor degree or higher education; TEFL/TESOL certificate). 
 Accommodation is not provided, school offers the help with finding an apartment. What is more, you can benefit for the public holidays, free Chinese lessons, free accident insurance and traveling once a year with the company. 
 Requirements: 
 - Teaching experience 
 - Accepted age range: 18 to 45 
 - TEFL Certificate or Higher 
 - Degree in any field 
 - British, American, Canadian, Australian, New Zealand, Irish or South African Passport holders
