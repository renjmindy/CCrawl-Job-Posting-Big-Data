 Working Supervisor 
 Apply for the position, Working Supervisor, with Developmental Services of Northwest Kansas today or contact Christina Peterson for more information. Posted Jan 7, 2021. 
  Apply Online  __  [ (785) 625-5678  __](tel:(785) 625-5678)   [email protected] __    View Employer  __ 
 Description 
 One part-time position available, 25 hours per week. 
 Supervise individuals served employed on work contracts: yard crew, snow removal, and moving crew. Provide training on basic skills, ensure work behaviors are appropriate for the business atmosphere and assist in the completion of contracts.  Responsible for the quality of work performed. Assist in establishing productive and quality standards for each contract and/or production area. The treatment of persons served with dignity and respect is a prime responsibility. 
 Education -  High School diploma or equivalent preferred. 
 Experience -  Prior personal or job related exposure to people with developmental disabilities or background related to contract needs preferred. 
 Benefits include paid sick, vacation, and holiday.
