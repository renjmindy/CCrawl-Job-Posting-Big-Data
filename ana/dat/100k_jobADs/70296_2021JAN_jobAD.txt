 Israel Caregiver Jobs 
 Posted on  October 6, 2009  by  caregiver 
 This is an excellent opportunity for all Filipino caregivers who are willing to be employed in Israel caregiver jobs. Just recently, the POEA approved a job order that would allow this Philippine based recruitment agency to recruit qualified applicants for various Israel caregiver jobs. The job order for the said Israel caregiver jobs was approved last October 1, 2009 and has a corresponding job order balance of 30 Israel caregiver jobs vacancies. 
 To apply for the said Israel caregiver jobs, all interested applicants are advised to proceed directly to the office of the recruiting agency. The name and office address of the recruiting agency for the said Israel caregiver jobs are indicated below. 
 For more info, all interested applicants for the said Israel caregiver jobs may contact the recruiting agency through their telephone numbers and Email address which are also indicated below. 
 Provident Overseas Placement Agency 
Unit B1 DNS Building 
Filmore cor. Arellano Avenue 
Makati 
 Tel Nos: 2329948 / 5517741 
Email Address: provident@hotmail.com
