 Boilermaker 
 Job description 
 
 
 Monday - Saturday 
 
 
 Immediate start 
 
 
 Somerton location 
 
 
 The Opportunity 
 Our client is located in Melbourne's Northern suburbs that has been operating for over 50 years, specialising in steel pipe manufacturing. 
 Currently seeking a boilermaker to weld pipe using a MIG and flux core welding and submerged-arc welding and repair work to x-ray testing standards. 
 
 
 6.30am start 
 
 
 $42.59 base rate + penalties + superannuation 
 
 
 Northern Suburbs of Melbourne 
 
 
 Large workshop / manufacturing environment 
 
 
 The Role 
 
 
 MIG and Fluxcore welding for penetrating steel pipe to repair defects and welding pipe jig 
 
 
 Submerged arc welding, pipe welding, grinding 
 
 
 Repairing pipe 
 
 
 Welding steel pipeline systems for the transportation of water and wastewater 
 
 
 Perform submerge arc welding on pipe from 6mm to 12mm thickness with minimum250mm to 1750mm outside diameter 
 
 
 Metal fabrication 
 
 
 The Ideal Person 
 
 
 Proven experience fluxcore welding 
 
 
 Submerged arc welding experience 
 
 
 Must have full working rights to work in Australia 
 
 
 Two work references for previous welding roles 
 
 
 Will have to undertake a weld test and pre-employment medical 
 
 
 Able to read and interpret structural plans 
 
 
 Successful candidates will have to pass a weld test, drug and alcohol and full pre-employment medical
