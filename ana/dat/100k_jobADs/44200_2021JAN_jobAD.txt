 Caregiver Job Vacancies Abroad 
 Posted on  June 4, 2010  by  caregiver 
 Employers from the holy land of Israel are again offering new opportunities to Filipinos who are looking for  Caregiver Job Vacancies Abroad . And to facilitate the recruitment process for the said caregiver job vacancies abroad, a job order was approved by the POEA last May 28 for a POEA accredited recruitment agency based in Salcedo Village in Makati. For the said caregiver job vacancies abroad, a job order balance of 30 applicants will chosen for placement in caregiver work abroad. 
 If you are looking forward to applying for the said POEA approved caregiver job vacancies abroad, proceed as soon as possible to the office of the recruiting agency and submit your resume and application. For more details or inquiries on the requirements or qualifications, you can dial the agency’s landline numbers indicated below. 
 CPM Manpower Services, Inc. 
8F Act Tower Condominium 
G. Puyat Avenue cor. H.V. Dela Costa Street 
Salcedo Village, Makati 
Tel Nos: 8181464 / 8181592 / 8181593 / 8173314 / 8178419
