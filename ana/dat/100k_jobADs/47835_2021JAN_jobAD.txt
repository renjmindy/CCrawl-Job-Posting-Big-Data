 Job Description 
 At PPC our mission is to provide outstanding customer service and safety to our customers, while providing a genuine opportunity for personal growth and advancement to our team members. We offer full benefits (Health, Dental, Vision, Life Insurance, PTO and EAP) 
 _  Are you experiencing a slow down at your place of business, reduction in force or layoffs due to current world events happening _? PPC is busier than ever and we are looking for experienced team members, as well as those that have a general interest in the security industry. PPC has current opportunities for those that may be looking for full or part-time work. This is also a great opportunity if you are interested in a career in law enforcement or military, as you will gain hands on experience, report writing and interacting with local law enforcement 
 ** 
**We currently have career opportunities for Armed and Unarmed Security Specialist in the Puyallup Surrounding area. You will receive excellent hands on training with our National Certified Training Instructors to include; Defensive Tactics, OC Foam, Use of Restraints, AVADE, Taser and Firearms. This is a exciting opportunity if you are looking for stability and experience. [less] 
 Job Description 
 Responsibilities:  - Perform security patrols of designated areas on foot or in vehicle - Maintain site security and watching for irregular or unusual conditions that may create security concerns or safety hazards - Permit authorized persons to enter property and monitors entrances and exits - Prepare reports on accidents, incidents, and suspicious activities - Provide assistance to customers, employees and visitors in a courteous and professional manner - Additional duties as assigned  You   Provide:  - Genuine desire and interest in the security industry - Ability to stand 100% of shift and physically restrain others - Reliable transportation - Basic computer skills to include word and outlook - Great communication/writing skills - Ability to obtain a Washington State Security License (must not have any felonies or misdemeanors) - Flexible working schedule to include weekends - 18 years of age and older - DRIVERS LICENSE - Pass pre-employment drug testing - Starting Wage $13.50- $15.00 based on experience and potential.
