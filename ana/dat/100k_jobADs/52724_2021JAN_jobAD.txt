 Senior Digital Marketing Manager 
 
 Location 
 
 Birmingham, West Midlands 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Marketing and Digital 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £50000.00 - £55000.00 per annum 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Ben Williams 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 benwilliams@workwithglee.com

 Published: 
 
 13 days ago 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 Ben Williams 
 Looking for something dynamic, forward-thinking? Passionate about B2B Digital Marketing? Would you like to work on international campaigns? 
 We are working with the West Midlands Growth Company exclusively, assisting with the hire of a Senior Digital Marketing Manager, in a transformative role, leading the digital function within the organisation. 
 Play a pivotal role in driving the West Midlands region forward. A unique opportunity to work for the region's Investment Promotion Agency, as it leverages the economic opportunities and global profile created by the Birmingham 2022 Commonwealth Games to attract investment and bring prosperity to the region. 
 We are looking for someone with a strong commercial focus and a huge passion for marketing who will bring bags of initiative. 
 Key duties and responsibilities: 
 
 Creating, planning and launching multi-channel digital marketing campaigns 
 Measuring and reporting the performance of all digital marketing campaigns 
 Support attraction and sales conversion across a number of digital channels, such as email marketing, social media, display advertising and SEO 
 Uplifting traffic to the company website, delivering leads and improving UX 
 Creating and implementing an inward investment social media strategy for the business 
 
 Minimum requirements: 
 
 Substantial experience within digital marketing within a B2B environment 
 Hands on experience of all digital channels, including email marketing, SEO, PPC, social media etc 
 Degree in marketing or equivalent (CIM, IDM). 
 The ability to keep calm under pressure and work to deadlines. 
 Strong people management skills, verbal and written communication and to be able to work effectively as part of a team. 
 Be creative and have an innovative approach.
