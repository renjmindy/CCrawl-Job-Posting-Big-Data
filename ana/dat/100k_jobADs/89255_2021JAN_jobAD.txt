 Full-Time Special Kindergarten Teacher, Consultant 
 Espoon kaupunki -  Espoo  , Uusimaa, Finland 
 NOTE:  This job listing has expired and may no longer be relevant! 
 Job Description 
 Swedish daycare in Espoo systematically develops its activities in order to provide a safe living with high quality for each child at the beginning of the child's life-long learning. For workers, this interesting and varied tasks in a dynamic and multi-professional working relationship. 
 Swedish daycare as part of the common organization of Swedish education offers opportunities for everyone to participate and develop themselves in a Swedish environment. 
 Description of work: 
 The Consultative special kindergarten teacher visiting kindergartens in a given area with the guidance and support for staff working with children in need of intensified / special support and perform certain administrative tasks in the Swedish-language specialty daycare. 
 The consultative special kindergarten teacher is part of the team of experts in the Finno resource. The person hired will show criminal record according to § 6 subsection 2. in criminal law. Entry requirements: Under the eligibility requirements for professional staff in social care (272/2005) § 9. Furthermore, we assume Swedish as their mother tongue or equivalent knowledge of Swedish.
