 BI-LINGUAL COURT SERVICES/COLLECTIONS CLERK 
 
 MUNICIPAL COURT 
 
 **** 
 
 Salary: $13.78 - $17.61/hr. plus benefits (additional 3% for Bi-Lingual, and other incentives). Oregon City Police Employees Association (OCPEA) union position. 
 
 **** 
 
 Application: All required information is located on our website at: 
 
 http://www.orcity.org/humanresources/open-job-bi-lingual-court-servicescollections-clerk . 
 
 All materials are due by 4:00 p.m. on Monday, July 11, 2011. 
 
 **** 
 
 Position: The City of Oregon City Municipal Court is searching for a bilingual Court Services and Collections Clerk. This position performs a wide variety of receptionist and clerical duties for Municipal Court located at 320 Warner Milne Road. It requires a heavy emphasis on customer service and the ability to multi-task. The ideal candidate for this position must have the following competencies: Accuracy, dependability, ability to multi-task, and the ability to deal with difficult customers in a polite and courteous manner. Collection and Court experience are a plus. 
 
 The required skills of the position are: use of computers and associated software packages, computerized cash register, copiers, fax machine, postage machine, and facsimile equipment. Use of 10-key adding machine by touch is a plus. Accuracy in handling/balancing of cash/credit cards/checks, and experience in processing the collection of money and information on unpaid accounts are essential functions of this position. Must have the job skills and ability to learn policies and procedures for tasks and carry out recurring office procedures independently. 
 
 **** 
 
 Requires: A high school diploma or equivalent GED certificate supplemented by course work in general office skills. Must have at least 2 years of recent office work experience. Candidates must speak, write and read fluently in both English and Spanish languages. Must be a member or have close affiliation, knowledge of or extensive experience with the non-English spoken language 's community, demonstrated by knowledge of the traditions, values and customs of the community being served. 
 
 **** 
 
 Process: Will include all application materials being reviewed with the most qualified candidates from that screening process being invited to an oral interview. Employment is contingent upon passing an extensive criminal background check, pre-employment drug test and certification of language proficiency by the Clackamas Community College Foreign Language Department. 
 
 **** 
 
 Special accommodations and/or assistance will be gladly provided, when requested, for any applicant with sensory or non-sensory impairments. The City is an AA/EOE/ADA employer.
