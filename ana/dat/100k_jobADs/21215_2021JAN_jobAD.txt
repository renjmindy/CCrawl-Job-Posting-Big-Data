 Registered Occupational Therapist 
 Arvan Rehab Group 
 __Hamilton, ON 
 Apply Now 
 We are currently looking for an Occupational Therapist for a Long Term Care client in Hamilton,Ontario. The Occupational Therapist will work closely with residents to help improve their activities of daily living. (5 Hour Bi-Weekly) 
 Job Duties: 
Conduct OT/ADP assessments for residents per College standards, develop individualized treatment plans, and document all progress notes in the facility’s health record system 
Complete feeding assessments for residents 
Complete assessments for falls prevention and Least Restraints, including reviews of bed rails 
Work with the Physiotherapist and Nursing staff in regards to wound management (ex: positioning, lifting and transferring 
Work with multi-disciplinary team to make recommendations with respect to other interventions or treatments (ex: Restorative Care, Pain Management, Contingence Management), and ensure the interventions follow best practices 
 Qualifications: 
Must be registered and in good standing with the College of Occupational Therapist of Ontario, and must have ADP Authorizer 
Must have Professional License Insurance 
Excellent interpersonal and listening skills while interacting with residents or their families 
Strong written and verbal communication skills 
Experience working in a Long Term Care home would be a definite asset 
Current police check for the Vulnerable Sector is required
