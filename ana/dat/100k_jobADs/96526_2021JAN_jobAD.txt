 As a Senior Software Engineering (Vice President), your mission is to help lead our team of innovators and technologists toward creating next-level solutions that improve the way our business is run. Your deep knowledge of design, analytics, development, coding, testing and application programming will help your team raise their game, meeting your standards, as well as satisfying both business and functional requirements. Your expertise in various technology domains will be counted on to set strategic direction and solve complex and mission critical problems, internally and externally. Embracing leading-edge technologies and methodologies inspires your team to follow suit. Collaborating with our network of global technologists will enable you to deliver diverse and inclusive software solutions. 
 The role: 
 The project is hiring for a Senior/Lead Software Engineer to contribute to the constant evolution of our Financial Forecasting platform, which provides a combination of cutting-edge solutions for strategic business processes. A blend of forward-looking domains and technologies, which include advanced software engineering, bigdata, data science, cloud computing, and more, are used to build future-proof tools for our business users. 
 As a Senior Software Engineer, you would contribute to, and lead, the day to day work in the project with your architectural, design and implementation skills, by: 
 
 
 Expanding the system and implementing new features, 
 
 
 Leading, coaching and mentoring other contributors, 
 
 
 Participating with other leads to the design and planning of the project activities 
 
 
 Being a technology partner for our business users 
 
 
 Supporting the business in the execution of their functions 
 
 
 Helping translating business requirements into optimal technical solutions 
 
 
 Fostering constant improvement and modernization of the platform and the project 
 
 
 This role requires a wide variety of capabilities, including: 
 
 
 Senior-level experience in Python (equivalent experience in Java, C# or similar enterprise-level languages/platforms is also welcome if you look forward to expanding your skillset) 
 
 
 Expertise in application, data and infrastructure architecture disciplines 
 
 
 Advanced knowledge of architecture, design and business processes 
 
 
 Coaching and Mentoring skills to help growing the talent and improving the work in the team 
 
 
 Familiarity with Agile-oriented processes and methodologies 
 
 
 Expertise in automation, security, and optimization 
 
 
 Ability to work collaboratively in teams and develop meaningful relationships to achieve common goals 
 
 
 BS/BA degree or equivalent experience 
 
 
 Practical experience in one or more of the following areas is desirable: 
 
 
 Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) 
 
 
 Microservices and Cloud computing 
 
 
 Database-related programming (both SQL and No-SQL) 
 
 
 UI/UX expertise (including contemporary JS frameworks, for instance: React and Angular) 
 
 
 Bigdata tools and platforms
