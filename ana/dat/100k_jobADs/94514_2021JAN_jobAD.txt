 Junior Account Manager (m/f/d) - Information technology 
 With the ISMS and data protection software "QSEC", WMC GmbH is a leading producer of information security management systems. The company is specialized in the areas of Governance Risk Compliance (GRC) and holistic information security management for organizations and companies in all industries. 
 In addition to our software services, we support our customers globally in the analysis, conception and implementation of ISMS, data protection and GRC projects. 
 For further extension of our team we are looking for a qualified 
 **Junior Account Manager/in (m/f/d) 
** 
 Your challenges and tasks include: 
 
 Sales of a complex software product, including IT services in the area of information security, to major customers directly and via sales partners 
 Identification of customer potential and acquisition of new customers 
 Support, maintenance and further development of national and international key accounts and partners
 
 Independent contact and offer tracking 
 Organization and implementation of mailings, participation in customer events and trade fairs 
 Master data maintenance in our customer database 
 Creation of a presentation to prepare customer meetings 
 Preparation of offers and tenders 
 
 
 Participation in the drafting and completion of contracts 
 
 We are expecting from you: 
 
 A university degree in (business) informatics, business administration, economics or a comparable education 
 Knowledge of IT infrastructures, information security management, risk management, business continuity management are desirable 
 Know-how about the entire commercial process of preparing offers and contracts with a focus on the sale of complex IT products and services 
 Good communication and presentation skills at the C-level, high customer orientation and pronounced decision-making skills 
 Willingness to travel 
 Good written and spoken English skills 
 
 We offer you: 
 
 Performance-based salary, good opportunities for further training 
 A pleasant working environment in a highly motivated team, in an attractive location in Hamburg 
 Long-term employment perspective with varied tasks for complex key account projects 
 Flat hierarchies
