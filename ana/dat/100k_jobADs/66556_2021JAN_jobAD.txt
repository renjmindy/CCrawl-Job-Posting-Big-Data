 My client is looking to recruit a Business Development Manager. This a permanent role. This is a remote, home-working position. 
 My client provides healthcare services and solutions to NHS and healthcare providers; this role will focus on providing primary care services. 
 The role will encompass: 
 
 Working with clinical, operational and mobilisation teams to design innovative operating models, products and services 
 Supporting growth and development in existing services 
 Managing the bid process, from horizon scanning through to submission 
 Developing tenders and proposals 
 
 The ideal candidate for for this role must have: 
 
 Healthcare/NHS experience 
 Understanding the bid/proposals/submission process within the NHS 
 Knowledge and experience of public sector procurement and commissioning procedures 
 Demonstrable track record of managing business development initiatives within the healthcare market 
 
 Highly competitive package. Remote.
