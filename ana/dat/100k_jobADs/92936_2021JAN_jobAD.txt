 Homecare Jobs in Canada 
 Posted on  February 26, 2009  by  caregiver 
 A Canadian based company engaged in local and international recruitment of individuals categorized as skilled and lower skilled workers by the Canadian government is seeking qualified applicants for the available homecare jobs in Canada. They will provide assistance to candidates in securing work permits to Canada. They will also assist applicants with their travel arrangements to Canada. 
 Should you wish to apply for a position, please feel free to submit via E-mail the following documents. 
 1. Resume (Indicating position applying for) 
 2. University-college transcript of records and diploma 
 3. Copy of Birth Certificate 
 4. two (2) photos (Whole body and passport size) 
 5. Copy of valid passport 
 6. Copy of police clearances 
 7. Reference letter(s) from previous employer(s) 
 8. Current copy of country residency permit 
 Email requested documents to:  info@mcewenstaffingservices.com 
 Mailing Address 
 MCEWEN STAFFING SERVICES 
 141-6200 McKay Avenue 
 Suite 724 
 Burnaby, British Columbia 
 V5H 4M9 
 CANADA
