 My client and Portfolio Manager is looking for a dynamic Senior IT Project Manager to join their highly skilled team. 
 As a Project Manager you will be accountable for the day-to-day management of projects, products or services, under the remit of the Portfolio Manager. You will be the go-to individual to oversee all change (this includes responsibility for Projects, Changes and Fixes), in-line with established processes and using an accepted project management framework. You will also be responsible for mandatory changes such as security and platform upgrades for a Project, Product or Service. 
 You will lead your project team to ensure their projects are delivered on time, to quality and within budget. You will also ensure that any type of change relating to the project, product or service is managed and communicated and that your projects comply with departmental standards. 
 Key Tasks/Areas of Responsibility 
 
 Identifying and managing risks and issues throughout the life of a project, product or service Ensuring all change follows the agreed methodology and adopting robust governance standards including compliance with data security regulations 
 Working with stakeholders to define expected benefits and to create plans for their realisation 
 Working with nominated business representatives &/or experts to help qualify and put into context, any contact made directly to IT Service Desk, or raised via other comms channels, to ensure work is prioritised effectively 
 Ensuring all necessary change applicable to the project, product or service are prioritised, planned and communicated in line with the agreed portfolio priorities 
 Liaising with the Portfolio Manager to ensure the required resource is scheduled in line with the agreed portfolio priorities 
 Creating, issuing and gaining sign-off on all project, product or service documentation and plans 
 Managing and reporting progress to the business against the baselined project as well as key milestones for all change planned, linked to the project, product or service 
 Delivering expected outputs to high-quality standards 
 Engaging, communicating and presenting to SMT members on all changes for a project, product or service 
 Managing external suppliers to ensure they deliver within contractual obligations 
 Overseeing any allocated budget to deliver change associated to the project, product or service, within the portfolio, ensuring the correct process for quoting, invoicing & approval of spend are followed 
 Capturing and reporting on lessons learnt 
 
 Key Measures of Performance 
 
 Achieve a minimum of 90% of project methodology compliance per project 
 Stakeholder / Sponsor satisfaction surveys 
 Implementation of priority changes/fixes resulting in a reduction of open incidents and problems 
 Budget & financial management of spend against a project, product or service 
 
 About You 
 To be considered for this role you must have extensive and demonstrable experience as a Project Manager ideally within a large scale, geographically dispersed multi-layered organisation. You will be Prince 2 or equivalent qualified and have an excellent understanding of project management methodologies. You will also be able to evidence successfully leading and inspiring your teams to deliver exceptional results. 
 
 
 You will be a confident communicator/presenter with the skills to lead and manage your project teams to ensure their projects are delivered on time, to quality and within budget 
 
 
 A person capable of decisive and effective decision-making, in a time pressured environment 
 
 
 A passion for building and motivating high-performing teams 
 
 
 Have the ability to effect and successfully implement change. 
 
 
 Someone that really enjoys resolving problems and demonstrates high levels of resilience, self-motivation and flexibility. 
 
 
 Job Specifics 
 Full clean driving licence with a flexible approach towards travel is essential 
 Remuneration 
Your package will include an excellent basic salary, company car or cash alternative, attractive bonus scheme, based on individual and company targets and Private Medical Insurance 
 As well as this you have access to some other fantastic optional benefits such as Cycle2Work Scheme, Dining Cards, Holiday trading and discounts and savings on 1000's of retailers.
