 Business Analyst 
 The Aurum Group is Canada’s largest provider of premium dental manufacturing, distribution and laboratory services. Aurum has an extensive network of high quality, full service dental laboratories across Canada and the US and a rich tradition of creating the future of dentistry now – advancing innovations in service, education and technology through our commitment to excellence in health care. 
 We are seeking an experienced financial and business analyst who welcomes the challenge of building and integrating data systems that contribute to upholding Aurum’s position as a leader in the growing and rapidly evolving dental industry. The ideal candidate will be a self-starter that can be a strategic contributor while working in a dynamic and challenging role. 
 Job Description 
 · Work closely with the senior finance and accounting team to build, maintain and integrate data from various systems into effective analytics and accurate financial reporting 
 · Develop, track and evaluate key performance indicators which align with the firm’s corporate objectives 
 · Identify and drive process improvements through the creation of standard and ad-hoc tools, including automated reports and dashboards 
 · Guide the cost and revenue analysis process by establishing and enforcing policies and procedures and conducting internal and external comparative analyses 
 · Develop detailed financial models to support valuation, forecasting, and aid in the capital budgeting and expenditure planning processes 
 · Evaluate investment and capital allocation decisions through the use of multiple techniques including DCF, market comparable and strategic analysis 
 · Assist in the evaluation of strategic transactions including mergers and acquisitions and debt and equity financings 
 · Research industry trends and compile market data to make cohesive recommendations 
 · Prepare presentations for and make recommendations to senior management and executives 
 · Maintain up-to-date knowledge of analysis tools and market conditions 
 Preferred Qualifications 
 · 3-5+ years of accounting, research, finance or other relevant corporate experience 
 · Undergraduate degree in finance, accounting, economics, or statistics 
 · MBA/CPA/CFA/CBV/FMVA considered an asset 
 · Advanced knowledge of ExcelTM and PowerPointTM 
 · PowerBITM / Power QueryTM, VBATM or other relevant database management and computer programing skills considered an asset 
 · Familiarity evaluating company filings and conducting third-party financial statement analysis 
 · Strong analytical competency and proven work experience in a quantitatively-heavy role 
 · Preference will be given to candidates with meaningful corporate finance, management accounting, investment banking or sell-side research experience 
 Personality and Interpersonal Skills 
 · Self-starter with excellent interpersonal communication, strong attention to detail and creative problem-solving skills 
 · Passion to learn and grow 
 · Thinks strategically and connects initiatives to broader Aurum corporate objectives 
 · Handles ambiguity, streamlines functions and prioritizes multiple demanding initiatives 
 · Excellent written and verbal communication skills; comfort interacting with executive-level management 
 · Willing to work flexible hours as required
