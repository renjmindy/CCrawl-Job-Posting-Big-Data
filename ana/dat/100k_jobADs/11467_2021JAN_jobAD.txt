 Cleanskin Dump Truck Operator 
 Job description 
 
 
 
 Role Highlights Entry level mining role Immediate Start- 2/1 day shift only Contract until April 2021 About the Role We are currently looking for Cleanskin Dump Truck Operators for a FIFO role ex Adelaide. The role will be based at Olympic Dam and will form part of the civil project team on site. The successful candidate will be required to have experience operating machinery such as tippers, moxys or articulated dump trucks within civil construction or a quarry environment. This is a great opportunity for those looking for a foot in the door to mining! Duties & Responsibilities Operate CAT 740, 745 and 777 dump trucks within a mining environment to a civil capacity Complete pre start checks on equipment prior to operating Work within a fast paced team environment Follow a 2/1 day shift roster working 12 hour days FIFO from Adelaide with camp and food supplied Skills and Experience Previous experience operating plant machinery RII Ticket or current MR  Job title:
