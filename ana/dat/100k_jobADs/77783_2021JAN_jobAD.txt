 Site Lab Technician 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 Approx 6 Months of work 
 
 
 Perth based role 
 
 
 Attractive salary 
 
 
 About the Role 
A great opportunity for a Lab technician/Site Technician to work on a civil construction site, sampling asphalt cores. Ability to work day/night 3 week roster with occasional weekend work. 
 About Our Client 
 Our Client designs, builds and sustains assets, infrastructure and facilities and we are the leading provider of integrated services in Australia and New Zealand. 
 Skills and Attributes 
 
 
 Reliable and well-organized 
 
 
 Positive attitude 
 
 
 Flexible work schedule (day/night 3 week roster, occasional weekend work) 
 
 
 Fit and capable to sample asphalt cores from site (training will be provided) 
 
 
 Comfortable to work on a civil construction site 
 
 
 Essential Requirements 
 
 
 C Class Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Right to Work in Australia 
 
 
 Must be able to pass full medical & DAS 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
