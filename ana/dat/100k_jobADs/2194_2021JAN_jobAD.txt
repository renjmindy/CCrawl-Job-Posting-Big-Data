 POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for June 
 Posted on  July 7, 2010  by  caregiver 
 For the month of June 2010, the POEA approved several job orders that offer employment opportunities for Caregiver Jobs in Israel and Canada. The job orders for the said POEA Approved Caregiver Jobs for June were approved for five POEA accredited recruitment agencies. Indicated below is the list of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for June which includes the name of the recruiting agency and the number of remaining vacancies for each POEA approved caregiver work abroad. 
 **Star World Int 'l Manp. & Placement Agency, Inc. **---- 13 vacancies for Israel 
 CPM Manpower Services, Inc.  --------------------------- 24 vacancies for Israel 
 Luzern International Manpower Services Corp.  ------ 13 vacancies for Canada 
**HRD Employment Consultant & Multi-Services, Inc. **-- 28 vacancies for Israel 
 International Progress, Inc.  ---------------------------- 25 vacancies for Israel 
 If you are interested to apply for any of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for June 2010, proceed directly to the office of the recruiting agency and submit your resume and application. For inquiries on any of the said POEA approved caregiver jobs for June, you can contact the recruiting agency involved.
