 About 
 We are seeking Senior Product Designers, with at least 5 years of experience, who are available and ready to start working on a new freelance opportunity starting in January 2021. 
 You will be working REMOTELY for the duration of the project. As long as you are based in the U.S., it doesn't matter where you work! 
 EXPECTATIONS: 
Hours: 40 per week 
Start date: Jan 2021 (flex) 
Duration: 1-3 months, depends on the project 
 Requirements: 
At least 5 years of experience 
Experience building design systems and pattern libraries 
Experience creating high fidelity designs, animations, modular components and/or prototype walkthroughs 
Experience creating workflows 
Experience redlining designs for developer handoff 
Experience in Figma 
Principle, Framer & After Effects experience is nice to have 
Agency experience preferred but not required 
Experience working on digital products - whether for web or app 
 If you are interested in learning more about the opportunity, please apply ASAP and list your availability for a call this week.
