 PHP Developer in Limassol 
 Full Time 
 
 __Post Date: September 6, 2020 
 
 Job Description 
 On behalf of one of our clients, a regulated Forex broker, we are seeking to recruit a Middle-range PHP Developer to join their team in Limassol. 
 Job Responsibilities 
 
 Work and communicate as part of a team of developers and system analysts 
 Write effective, optimized and well documented code related to CRM functionality, financial transactions, blockchain (advantage), API integrations with payment gateways and other third-party systems as well as database structure and optimization 
 Clearly understand and process requirements from System Analysts 
 Test the quality of the code before submitting for deployment 
 
 Job Requirements 
 
 Greek or English language 
 At least 3 years development experience 
 Analytical, quick-thinking mind and ability to quickly grasp requirements 
 Excellent knowledge of PHP and MySQL 
 Very good knowledge of either Code Igniter or Laravel frameworks 
 Experience working with projects on Meta Trader 4 or 5 (advantage) 
 NodeJS (advantage) 
 Big Data like Apache KAFKA (advantage) 
 Very good understanding of security practice OWASP 
 Experience in developing REST APIs.
