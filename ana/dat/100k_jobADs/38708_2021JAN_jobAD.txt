 ABOUT WORKSTATION CONSULTANTS 
 Workstation Consultants is a full-service office furniture dealership located in downtown Albany NY. We offer pre-owned, re-manufactured, and new furniture options. We also offer space planning, installation, liquidation, and move management services. Our multi-floor showroom highlights great examples of all our commercial furnishings, as well as ergonomic accessories and seating. We have new arrivals in our showroom daily. 
 Workstation Consultants serves the Greater Capital Region locally including Albany, Saratoga, Schenectady, Troy and Clifton Park, New York with services extending through the entire northeast and shipping available nationwide.
