 Social Media Specialist 
 Calendar Club of Canada 
 __Paris, ON 
 Apply Now 
 JOB PURPOSE 
 As part of our Marketing team, the Digital Content Specialist will use a range of media to communicate with customers online, in store and, well – everywhere! The successful candidate will help us to increase brand awareness, while defining and delivering a brand voice that impresses and engages customers while attracting new ones. 
 KEY RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 Interact, and play with our customers across digital platforms like Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn and more 
 Use all manner of media (such as video) to tell the story of Calendar Club Canada in store, and online 
 Plan and create a first-class content strategy for a variety of channels that drives sales and customer engagement 
 Research, analyze data, draw conclusions, and create compelling content based on raw data 
 Regularly report on KPIs and metrics 
 Participate in marketing team meetings, brainstorming sessions, and fulfill other departmental requests, as needed 
 Assist with internal marketing initiatives and company communications 
 
 REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE 
 
 An energetic people-person with a solid sense of humour 
 High level of creativity, and the ability to bring vision to life in video, graphic design and the written word 
 Proven success in creating retail/product-focused marketing content 
 Strong video production and graphic design abilities 
 Excellent written communication, and a passionate storyteller 
 Enjoys being in front of a camera 
 Skilled in Adobe Suite 
 Proficiency with email marketing tools like Mailchimp 
 Proven experience in growing social media followings 
 Strong knowledge of SEO 
 Highly organized, and familiar with project management tools and concepts 
 Experience executing consumer focused advertising campaigns 
 Hands-on experience with content management systems 
 Proficiency in MS Office 
 Post-secondary marketing, communications, public relations or relevant field 
 Retail experience 
 Someone looking to make their mark!
