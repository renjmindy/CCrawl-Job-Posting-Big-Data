 Mechanical Assembler 
 Aerotek - Spokane Valley, WA 
 Posted:  1/13/2021  - Expires:  4/13/2021 
 Job ID: 223516745 
 
 Apply Now  
 I have already applied 
 Save Job  
 __Print  __Email  __Share 
 
 Job Description 
 Need to be mechanically minded and have a the ability to work from blue prints. Have the ability to work long shifts and be on their feet for long periods of time and be available for overtime. Must have their own tools. Having a hydraulics background is desired. Must know the diff. between metric and standard bolts. They use all standard hand tools i.e. screw drivers, sockets, wrenches and a power screw driver to assemble the units. They are doing basic mechanical assembly of corrugated box machines, which come in a variety of shapes/sizes, depending on the shape/size of the cardboard box the machine will be making. Some of the machines are smaller like a cutting board, or microwave and some are bigger like the size of a car.
