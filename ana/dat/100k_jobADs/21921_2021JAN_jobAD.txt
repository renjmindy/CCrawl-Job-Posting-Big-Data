 About the Role 
 We are currently seeking an experienced Civil Construction Supervisor for a major project for Palm Island, North Qld.

 Overseeing a crew of 5-6 staff on site 
 
 
 Maintain open channel of communications with Management Team/Site management.

 Proven experience in a Civil Supervisor role minimum 3-5 years 
 
 
 Strong Background in Civil Construction 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 MUST be available for an immediate start
