 Gleeson Recruitment Group are supporting an industry leading manufacturing firm in Bilston who are currently searching for a Finance Business Partner to join their team. 
 The organisation now needs an FBP who can support internal management along with the Commercial Director with relevant financial analysis and other business functions as required and appropriate. 
 The role would suit a Finalist/Part Qualified Accountant who is confident preparing management accounts and would like to expand on their interaction with other departments. 
 Finance Business Partner in Telford responsibilities: 
 
 
 Be instrumental in the development of reporting processes, systems and routines and Improve quality and efficiency of periodic reporting based on group reporting guidelines 
 
 
 Work with the business to create a suite of readily available, consistent and accurate management information to assist local management with running the business, including marketing, sales, departmental overheads etc ensuring compliance with group reporting requirements above 
 
 
 Assist the business in establishing and assessing revenue and cost performance processes actions 
 
 
 Prepare and present budget, forecasts, financial analyses and ad hoc projects as required 
 
 
 Participation in projects and contribute with perspectives on profitability, innovations, new business and other matters as may be appropriate from time to time 
 
 
 All other duties as requested
