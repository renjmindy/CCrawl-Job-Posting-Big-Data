Workday may from time to time also use third parties to act on Workday's behalf. You agree to the fact that to the extent necessary your personal data may be transferred and/or disclosed to any company within Workday group of companies as well as to third parties acting on Workday's behalf, including also transfers to servers and databases outside the country where you provided Workday with your personal data. Such transfers may include for example transfers and/or disclosures outside the European Economic Area and in the United States of America. 
 Workday collects your personal information for the purposes of managing Workday Careers related activities. Workday may use your personal information in relation to the evaluation and selection of applicants including for example setting up and conducting interviews and as is otherwise needed in the recruitment process. The information you enter into your account will be retained and may be used to match you with additional job opportunities. Click  here  to review our Recruitment Privacy Statement. If at any time you have questions about how your information is being used or you would like to request access to or deletion of your personal information, please contact us at  privacy@workday.com .
