 Caregiver Job Hiring Abroad 
 Posted on  March 27, 2014  by  caregiver 
 A POEA accredited overseas employment agency in Paco, Manila is currently offering caregivers a chance to apply for their ongoing  Caregiver Job Hiring Abroad . The said caregiver job hiring is for an employer in Riyadh,  Saudi Arabia  who is looking for caregivers who have the following qualifications: 
 
 Female 
 Must be 22 to 40 years old 
 Must have at least Vocational Diploma / Short Course Certificate 
 Minimum of 2 years working experience in the same field 
 
 If you meet the qualifications specified for the said caregiver job hiring abroad, please proceed to the office of M.G.M. International Recruitment Services Inc. and forward your resume together with photocopies of all your credentials. For more details or inquiries about the said caregiver job hiring abroad, you may call any of the telephone numbers we have specified below.
