 Investment Banking Vice President, Life Sciences 
 ATB Financial 
 __Toronto, ON 
 Apply Now 
 Job Number:  REQ1149 
 Location:  Toronto, ON 
 Apply by:  December 11, 2020 
 Position:  Full time or Contract 
 ATB Capital Markets is a wholly owned subsidiary of ATB Financial, an Alberta-based institution with offices in Toronto, Calgary, Edmonton and Denver. Together, we represent a highly specialized team of experts who provide a full suite of financial service solutions including: 
 Advice on mergers and acquisitions, divestiture and private equity 
Equity capital, Debt capital and financial markets 
Corporate banking, loan syndications and project finance 
Institutional sales and trading 
Institutional research to leverage industry and sector insight 
 ATB Capital Markets is looking for an Investment Banking Vice President in our Life Sciences group. Life Sciences covers both established and emerging companies across the global cannabis sector in addition to other healthcare and Growth & Innovations companies. 
 This position is based out of Toronto, ON. 
 Requirements 
 Primary duties consist of, but are not limited to: 
Primary point person for deal flow on financing and M&A transactions 
Maintaining and developing databases and spreadsheets 
Monitoring capital markets and sector activity 
Researching firms and industry transaction trends 
Constructing financial models and performing pro forma analyses 
Developing presentation materials and transaction documents 
 Role Qualifications/Requirements: 
A university degree with a focus on Finance, Business, Economics, Accounting or Engineering 
MBA, CFA, CA, CPA, CBV will be considered an asset 
Highly proficient in Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word, etc.) 
Knowledge of Bloomberg and Factset a plus 
Strong work ethic with an appreciation for schedule uncertainty 
Exceptional attention to detail and commitment to excellence 
Team oriented with the ability to work in an open and unstructured environment 
Excellent written and verbal communication skills 
Clear interest in capital markets with experience being considered a plus 
 At ATB, we know that highly talented people can readily transfer their skills. If you believe your skills and experience are transferable, please consider putting your name in the running. 
 Online applications are preferred, and please let us know if you require any accommodations.
