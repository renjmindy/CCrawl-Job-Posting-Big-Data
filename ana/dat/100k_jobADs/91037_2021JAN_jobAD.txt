 Are you an experienced senior property professional in the South East looking for a new opportunity? Do you want to have overall responsibility of the strategic development of a £1billion London-centric estate? If this is you then please read on… 
 You have the opportunity to join a market leading business in the leisure sector as Head of Property with responsibility for nearly 1000 assets in and around London. Leading a team of 5 you will implement the property vision and objectives through development, maintenance and the execution of the property strategy. 
 As the Head of Property you will have control of the annual £20mln capital investment programme delivering a measured return on investment. You will develop a contractor network and partnering agreements for the delivery of all construction and maintenance activity whilst managing health & safety issues. You will also provide expertise and support to the commercial letting, disposal and acquisition process. 
 As the successful Head of Property you will join a market leading and well established team responsible for sites within a defined area - the London and surrounding area. On offer to the successful candidate is a basic salary of up to £85,000 plus car/ car allowance, pension and bonus scheme. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
