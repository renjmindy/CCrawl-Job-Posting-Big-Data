 Job description 
 Reinforcement Operator 
 
 
 $26.25 per hour + penalties 
 
 
 Tamworth Location 
 
 
 Monday to Saturday 
 
 
 
 About the Role 
WorkPac are seeking Reinforcement Operators to join our clients production plant in Tamworth, our client who is a leading manufacturer of reinforced concrete pipes based in Tamworth area, must be willing and able to commence as early as 4am & work Monday through to Saturday. 
 Duties and Responsibilities 
 
 
 MIG Welding 
 
 
 Ability to follow repeated reo manufacture processes under instruction 
 
 
 Assisting with manufacturing a diverse range of precast concrete products 
 
 
 Use of a general range of tools such as drills, grinders etc. 
 
 
 Able to maintain a high level of housekeeping in their work area 
 
 
 Skills and Experience 
 
 
 Previous experience MIG Welding 
 
 
 Interpret REO manufacturing process 
 
 
 Right to work in Australia 
 
 
 White Card 
 
 
 Must be reliable and keen to learn 
 
 
 Demonstrated focus on safety of themselves and workmates 
 
 
 Must have a good knowledge of how to use a general range of tools - drill, grinder, etc. 
 
 
 The ability to read product and reinforcement designs is preferred however not mandatory. 
 
 
 Must be a team player, yet also have the ability to work independently. 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
