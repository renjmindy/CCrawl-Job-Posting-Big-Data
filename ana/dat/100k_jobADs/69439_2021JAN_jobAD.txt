 QA & Customer Service Officer in Nicosia 
 Full Time 
 
 __Post Date: January 11, 2021 
 __Apply Before: March 28, 2021 
 
 Job Description 
 On behalf of one of our clients specializing in Web Design and Software Development we are seeking to recruit a QA & Customer Service Officer to join their team in Nicosia. 
 Job Requirements 
 
 Preference for an IT or Computer Science degree or a degree in a related field 
 Strong verbal and written communication skills 
 Must be fluent in English 
 Ability to write and execute test scenarios and user cases 
 Ability to write and execute test scripts will be considered an advantage 
 Ability to write bug reports and present findings to software development teams and other team members 
 Quality Assurance 
 Ability to perform accurately, effectively and methodically under tight deadlines 
 Competent technical / programming skills is a plus 
 A passion for working with technology 
 Ability to communicate effectively, answer customer inquiries and direct customers to online resources 
 Be able to maintain and update customer records 
 Make recommendations to management to improve customer experience and customer care 
 
 What our client offers 
 
 Competitive remuneration package 
 Private medical insurance 
 Discretionary bonus at the end of the year 
 
 How to Apply 
 If interested and you match all the requirements, please send your CV to  andriana.p@work-channel.com  , including the title of the position in the subject line 
 Please note  that due to the high volume of applications received, only successful candidates will be contacted
