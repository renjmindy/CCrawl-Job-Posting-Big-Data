 Digital Marketing Manager - based in KENT 
 A famous retailer is now looking to hire a Digital Marketing manager to take charge of their digital marketing strategy. 
 The purpose is to create, optimize and grow the brand's name through all digital channels 
 Strategic responsibilities : 
 
 Plan and execute a digital marketing startegy and business plan 
 keep the company at the forefront of developments in digital marketing. 
 Design, build and maintain digital and social media presence 
 Managing online brand and product campaigns 
 Measure performance of all digital marketing campaigns and assess against goals 
 Responsibility for planning and budget of all digital marketing 
 
 What we're looking for : 
 
 Demonstrable experience leading and managing SEO/SEM, PPC, marketing database, email, social media and/or display advertising campaigns 
 Proven experience identifying target audiences and devising digital campaigns that engage, inform and motivate. 
 Strong grasp of current marketing tools and strategies to lead integrated digital marketing campaigns from concept to execution. 
 Natural hands-on leader with an ability to inspire performance and lead by example 
 Strong analytical skills and data-driven thinking 
 Works at pace used to making decisions and producing results in a fast-moving environment, achieving tight deadlines
