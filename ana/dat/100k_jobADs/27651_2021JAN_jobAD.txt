 MOXY Operator - 2020 
 Job description 
 
 
 Local work in Townsville 
 
 
 $39.62/hr flat rate 
 
 
 12 months project work 
 
 
 About the Role 
WorkPac are currently seeking experienced Moxy Operators with Marine Construction experience for an upcoming project work based in Townsville. Primary role includes delivery of various size and grades of rocks from stockpile to Bund walls. Candidates must be able to reverse long distances with limited manoeuvring space. 
 Skills/Experience 
 
 
 Must have over 3 yearsâ experience operating a Moxy Volvo A40 machine 
 
 
 Previous experience with delivery of various size and grades of rocks from stockpile out onto Bund walls OR similar 
 
 
 Experience in Marine or Coastal Construction is highly regarded 
 
 
 RII - Conduct Articulated Haul Truck Operations is ideal however will take good experience also 
 
 
 Ability to work autonomously and in a fast pace environment 
 
 
 QLD Construction White / Blue Card 
 
 
 Ability to pass a Pre-Employment Medical / Dug & Alcohol 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australia's largest, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding career opportunities. Looking after our employees is important to us.
