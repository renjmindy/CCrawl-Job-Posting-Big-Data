 Are you immediately available? Are you experienced in both Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable? Do you enjoy a varied role? 
 An opportunity has arisen for an Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable Assistant to join an impressive business in central Birmingham for a period of 9 months. You will be welcomed into a stable and friendly team and supported by great management. You can expect a salary of between £20,000 and £23,000 depending on experience. 
 As the Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable Assistant you will be responsible for: 
 
 Process supplier invoices, credit notes and ensure cash is allocated correctly 
 Reconcile supplier statements 
 Reconcile customer accounts 
 Create customer invoices in SAP 
 Manage supplier queries and investigate and solve discrepancies 
 Company expense processing 
 Credit Control 
 
 The ideal candidate: 
 
 Previous experience in Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable 
 Available immediately 
 AAT L2 achieved (desired) 
 Intermediate Excel skills 
 Confident communicator 
 A team player 
 
 For more information please do get in touch/ apply!
