 We are one of the largest after hours doctors housecall services in Ontario. We are looking for call centre agents to help take calls from patients and schedule and dispatch them to doctors. We have a range of shifts from 4 to 8 hours each and can be flexible with days and times. We are open from 8 am to midnight 7 days a week, 365 days a day including all holidays. We are looking for people who are available during the day, evenings until midnight and during the weekend. 
 We are looking for staff who are polite, patient and have a strong command of the English language. We are looking for strong communicators as this is a large part of the position. We work as a team and support each other, both within the office and within the large network of doctors that we work with. 
 Previous experience in call centre would be an asset but is not required. 
 Required skills are: 
 
 Strong work ethic 
 Dedication to the position 
 Accurate 
 Timely - not late and works each shift 
 Computer skills is necessary as our system is highly automated 
 Strong knowledge of the GTA - regions, districts, cities, major intersections, etc
