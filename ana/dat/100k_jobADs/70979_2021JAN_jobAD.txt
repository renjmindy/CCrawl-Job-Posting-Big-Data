 Legal Assistant/Paralegal Direct Hire 
   
   Workway, Inc.   
 __Website    __@workway 
 Legal Assistants/Paralegals, are you looking for an opportunity to work with one of the leading Law Firms in Florida and the nation? Let Workway open the door for you! We are a professional staffing firm, working with organizations across the country to place exceptional candidates. Since 2005, our primary staffing specialization has been in Legal industry, and we have great assignments for all levels of Legal Assistants with our clients, both large and small. 
 Let one of our recruiters assist you in finding and preparing for interviews with our clients. Whether you are interested in a direct hire, temp-to-hire, or temporary position, Workway can help you to put your best foot forward. Contact us today! 
 Currently, we are seeking experienced Legal Assistants/Paralegals in the practice area of Personal Injury, Medical Malpractice, Insurance Defense, Commercial, Civil Litigation, Family Law, Probate & Estate and Real Estate for positions in Miami, Ft. Lauderdale and Boca Raton, FL. 
 Job Description 
 
 Ability to handle Pre-Litigation and/or Litigation files. 
 Able to work in a fast-paced environment. 
 Scheduling initial consultations 
 Conduct Discovery 
 Assist with file organization 
 Prepare pleadings and motions. 
 Prepare files for hearings, depositions, mediations and trials 
 E-filing 
 Calendaring 
 Communicate with clients, witnesses and other parties involved 
 Paralegals responsible for billing time 
 
 Job Requirements 
 _ _ 
 
 2 years experience in a Legal Assistant/Paralegal role. 
 Good verbal and writing communication skills required. 
 Good computer skills 
 Bilingual/Spanish a plus 
 
 As a Legal Assistant/Paralegal you will be working through an established staffing agency with the networking power of a national organization and the personalized family feel of a smaller firm. With almost a decade of specific focus in Legal, our recruiters have a deep understanding of both your needs and those of the industry. You will be assigned a dedicated recruiter who will provide you with assistance in crafting an effective resume, preparing for interviews, and searching for jobs outside of our organization. 
 Employment with Workway, Inc. also comes with some exciting benefits:  medical coverage, life insurance (with medical plan enrollment), direct deposit, and weekly pay.  Further details regarding these benefits can be provided by your recruiter. 
 To receive state and federal compliance posters, e-mail   hr@workway.com   or call 972.514.1515.
