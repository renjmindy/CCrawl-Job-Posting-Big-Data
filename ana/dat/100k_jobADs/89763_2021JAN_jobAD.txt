 Senior Unity Developer 
 Lean Node Apply for this Job 
 Posted 
 December 8, 2020 
 Full Time 
 Anywhere 
 Remote 
 How will you grow with us? 
 We build MVPs for startups, so you will be able to deliver a few projects (software products created from scratch) within a year! 
 You will own your work and decide on the project’s direction. 
 Our fast-paced work environment guarantees rapid development of your skills. 
 ‍ 
 Responsibilities 
 
 Meeting with the design team to ensure understanding of the different game systems. 
 Establishing a development pipeline. 
 Planning and implementing game mechanics. 
 Debugging your code and making sure it runs smoothly on target devices. 
 Maintaining proper code architecture. 
 Working closely with other developers to deliver high-quality mobile games. 
 
 ‍ 
 Requirements  ‍ 
 Experience: 
 
 3+ years creating games using Unity. 
 Experience leading teams of developers. 
 Released at least one mobile game. 
 
 Tech skills: 
 
 Experience using PUN or other networking APIs (within Unity) in creating multiplayer experiences (preferably real time). 
 In depth knowledge of Unity’s components and scriptable objects. 
 In depth knowledge of mobile platform limitations and issues. 
 Experience with DI systems. 
 Knowledge of scripting, animation, session management, and textures. 
 
 Soft skills: 
 
 Good communication skills. 
 Excellent English language skills 
 Excellent attention to detail. 
 You’re close to the GMT + 1 timezone 
 
 ‍ 
 About Us 
 Lean Node startup studio leads the way when it comes to digital innovation by creating exciting startups in partnership with top-notch market leaders and entrepreneurs. The studio offers a variety of services including a comprehensive six months program focused on helping entrepreneurs, and companies create outstanding products 
 An example of one of our gaming partners is Spoilz -- a gaming studio aiming to empower local cultural outreach in global markets.
