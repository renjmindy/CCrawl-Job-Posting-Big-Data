 Customer Support Rep 
 Starry is hiring reps to respond to customer emails, phone calls, and live chats resolving device and network issues. You must be able to work quickly, have 1-year help desk or troubleshooting experience, and a quiet workspace. Full time with benefits provided. 
 Overnight Customer Support Rep 
 Starry is also hiring reps to perform the same job duties listed above with the same requirements with the exception of working from home full time overnight from 11 pm to 7 am. Hiring in several states. 
 Internet Analyst 
 KarmaHub is seeking creative thinkers who enjoy browsing the web to express their opinion on the content and quality. You must be fluent in English, have strong comprehension skills, and have a laptop with internet Explorer 10 or higher with antivirus software. Pays $10 per hour.

 Freelance Writers 
 
 Internet Brands is seeking freelance writers to work from home who can write from a creative brief or SEO strategy. Must be able to quickly research, understand and write about legal topics. Previous experience required. 
 Interpreter 
 Telelanguage is seeking over the phone interpreters to assist millions of limited English proficiency individuals needing language services. Must be fluent in English and one other language, have previous interpreting experience, and a reliable phone to take calls. Can work your own schedule. 
 Live Chat Agents 
 The Chat Shop is looking for people to interact with others providing support, selling products, discussing holiday vacations, etc. You must be able to multitask, can work 20 to 40 hours a week, and type 65 wpm. Hourly pay. 
 Bilingual Digital Engagement Specialist 
 ModSquad is seeking those who are fluent in English and either Korean, Japanese, or Chinese to moderate content, chat with customers, manage communities, etc. Requires basic PC skills, be interested in video games and online communities including social media. Full time. 
 Customer Support Agent 
 Icanotes is seeking those with solid education and computer skills to work from their home office as a customer support agent. 
 Transcribers (Global opportunity) 
 
 Focus Forward is seeking people from all around the world to work from home as an English language or Spanish language transcriber. You can choose your own schedule. Pays $0.40 per audio minute.
