 Role: Procurement Specialist 
 Based near Fleet Hampshire 
 Salary £25 - 30k 
 An exciting role in a young organisation, leading on outsourced procurement support and collaborative buying. We are looking for a candidate who is enthused by a varied role with the opportunity to work with a number of different clients from across diverse industries and charities. 
 Benefits: Support for CIPS qualification, 23 days holiday and NEST Pension 
 Key Responsibilities 
 This is a customer focused role to provide our clients with high quality Procurement Support covering the full spectrum of procurement services, including: managing and driving down total acquisition costs, strategic sourcing, developing supporting documentation, contract negotiation, supplier development, supplier management and help to improve supplier performances. Customer and supplier meetings will be part of the role in the medium to long term so an enthusiasm for meeting people and driving stronger relationships is essential. 
 Skills desired: 
 
 Procurement/Sourcing experience 
 Good negotiation skills 
 Track record of achieving cost savings 
 Experience of negotiating keen eye to identify areas and opportunities for cost saving 
 Strong supplier relationship skills with the ability to manage and ensure suppliers deliver effectively on time, cost and quality. 
 Building strong relationships with Internal Stakeholders in order to effectively serve their needs, often with shorter than expected timescales. 
 Must be a proactive and confident individual, eager to develop their skills within the purchasing department and display enthusiasm for career progression within procurement. 
 Strong organisation skills. 
 Fantastic people and communication skills with a strong ability to collaborate with people at all levels of a business 
 
 Talents required - more important 
 
 Innovative - creative and interested in finding solutions to overcome challenges 
 Excited and positive outlook - success motivated 
 Growth Mindset - always learning and ambitious 
 Tenacity - A desire to make things happen 
 Self-Motivated and team orientated - capable of working with a varied and often geographically dispersed team 
 Thrives on change in a fast-paced business 
 
 Experience 
 
 Minimum 2 years' procurement experience with businesses 
 Must have a proven track record of achieving results with the ability to perform to demanding deadlines 
 Degree Level or Equivalent or relevant work experience 
 Demonstrable examples of achieving cost savings on behalf of employer(s) and/or customers
