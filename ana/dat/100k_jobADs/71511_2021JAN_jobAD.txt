 Support Engineer in Nicosia 
 Full Time 
 
 __Post Date: November 24, 2020 
 __Apply Before: February 28, 2021 
 
 Job Description 
 On behalf of one of our clients specializing in Web Design and Software Development we are seeking to recruit a Support Engineer to join their team in Nicosia. 
 Job Requirements 
 
 Degree in IT or Computer Science, or a related field 
 Great understanding of customer care is vitally important 
 Super-strong English verbal and written skills are essential 
 Strong problem-solving skills 
 Experience in Web Development, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and/ or Bootstrap, or when it comes to working with standard web application programming languages such as PHP, ASP, JSP, or Adobe Coldfusion, this is definitely considered to be a plus. 
 Have to be flexible and open to working with shifts 
 Having worked for a Forex broker is also a plus. 
 
 What our client offers 
 
 Competitive remuneration package 
 Private medical insurance 
 Discretionary bonus at the end of the year 
 
 How to Apply 
 If interested and you match all the requirements, please send your CV to andriana.p@work-channel.com, including the title of the position in the subject line
