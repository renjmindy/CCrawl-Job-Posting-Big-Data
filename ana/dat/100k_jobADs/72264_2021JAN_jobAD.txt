 Job Description 
 HR Yhtiot is a temporary employment agency with 9 years of experience covering the whole of Finland and from 2012 also in Poland. Currently, the Group employs approximately 650 people. 
 Job Location: Helsinki, Finland Requirements for candidates to work Level of education: lab technician / laboratory technician 
 Experience: Prior work in laboratory is not required but the work in the microbiology laboratory or chemical is an asset Languages: English on communicative level, the base of the Finnish language certificate required: Diploma Other important requirements: Basic knowledge of the Finnish language and completion of language training, cultural training and preparation for the work in Finland. 
 Salaries and conditions of employment guaranteed gross salary: 2333 euros per month remuneration system: Pay Monthly Extras: For his work in the evening (18.00 - 22.00) 15%, additional benefits: Annual leave, 9% holiday allowance to the salary 
 Type of Contract: A written contract of employment dimension of time Occupation: Work 2-shift employment period - the duration of the contract: Indefinite, the possibility of permanent employment Starting date: November - December 2013 schedule of working time - number of hours: 163 hours a month Other information: The employer helps in finding accommodation and arranging all the formalities
