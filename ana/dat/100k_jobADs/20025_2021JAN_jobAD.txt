 We are looking for an intern (m/f/d) in our education management department. 
 **Who are we? 
** 
 Vivat Lingua! is ... 
 ... learning languages in a lively and personal way 
... making learners linguistically fit and accompanying them on their learning journey 
...  building bridges between people whose mother tongues are different 
... a place of welcome 
... a professional and warm team 
... language training experience since 2003 
... a company with short and direct communication channels 
... a place where everyone can contribute their strengths and ideas 
 Your tasks 
 - Sitting in on and observing German as a foreign language (DaF) courses. 
 - Sitting in on and observing various other foreign language courses. 
 - Additional tasks related to the observation 
 - Cooperation in organizational processes in education management 
 **Your profile 
** 
 You are looking for a mandatory internship and your studies focus on education management. 
 You should be able to work in a team as well as independently. You have very good Microsoft Office skills. In addition, you work carefully and accurately, are motivated and acquire the requisite knowledge quickly and effortlessly. If so, then you've come to the right place. 
 We are looking forward to your application. Please indicate your possible starting date. Please send applications and questions to  bewerbung@vivat-lingua.de . 
 We are looking forward to meeting you. 
 We are looking for an intern (m/f/d) in our online marketing department with a focus on social media. 
 Who are we? 
 Vivat Lingua! is ... 
 ... learning languages in a lively and personal way 
... making learners linguistically fit and accompanying them on their learning journey 
...  building bridges between people whose mother tongues are different 
... a place of welcome 
... a professional and warm team 
... language training experience since 2003 
... a company with short and direct communication channels 
... a place where everyone can contribute their strengths and ideas 
 Your tasks 
 - Prepare, develop and post target group-specific content for  various social media channels and post it (focus: Instagram, Facebook and LinkedIn) 
 - Create content and feed it into a social media management tool 
 - Collaborate on the support and further development of the social media channels 
 - Co-plan and coordinate online events and implement for content generation 
 - Co-create  Facebook and Instagram ads and short texts for the homepage 
 Your profile 
 Are you looking for a mandatory internship and your studies focus on marketing and media? Then we are the right place for you. You should be able to work in a team as well as independently. You have excellent IT skills, are experienced in managing social channels and can operate them according to B2B or B2C requirements. In addition, you have an above-average ability to express yourself verbally and in writing in both German and English and are very creative and  passionate about social media. 
 Are you an enrolled student and still looking for the right practical partner to complete your mandatory internship? Then get creative now and convince us of your skills with your application. Please include your possible start date. 
 Please send applications and questions to  bewerbung@vivat-lingua.de . 
 We are looking forward to meeting you.
