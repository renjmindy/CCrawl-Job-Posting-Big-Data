 About this project 
 sales-marketing / others 
 Less than USD 50 
 Our hiring process includes two weeks of testing to determine whether you are the right person or not. 
 At the end of the testing period of two weeks you will be paid $50 USD as a token of appreciation if during the two weeks you showed full dedication and determination during the two weeks of training. 
 Once the testing period ends you will be notified via email of our decision to hire your services or not. 
 We are looking for people who meet the following five requirements: 
 
 
 Able to speak and write English in a professional manner 
 
 
 People with integrity and honesty (People who perform well whether they are being watched/supervised or not) 
 
 
 People who are responsible (People who don't leave until the work at hand is completed) 
 
 
 People who have time flexibility since we work with clients all over the world (We are not offering the usual 8 to 5 job opportunity. We offer an opportunity to have a job from home, (getting fully trained by us) with the possibility to become an international logistics consultant, available to work as needed during the day or night depending on the logistics/needs of our worldwide clients) 
 
 
 People who understand and appreciate the great opportunity we offer in the attached section number seven of the contract. (The full contract will be shared with those who pass the two weeks of testing) 
 
 
 Please confirm that you have carefully read the above instructions and that you meet the above five requirements. 
 Upon receiving a positive response from you, we will analyze your response in order to determine if you should be considered for the two weeks of testing.
