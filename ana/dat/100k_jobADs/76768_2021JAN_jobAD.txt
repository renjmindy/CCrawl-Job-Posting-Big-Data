 IF YOU LIKE THE WORK, YOU WILL LOVE THE JOB – COMING SOON 
 Category:  Job Retention Skills 
 The workbook If You Like the Work, You’ll Love the Job assists readers in how to go about choosing a job or a career that will be conducive to their everyday happiness. Jobs that are in high demand are also provided.

 Description 
 
 Many people work at a job just because it pays well. There is no passion for the type of work that they are doing. The workbook If You Like The Work, You’ll Love The Job covers many topics that affect your work life. It helps the reader understand what type of work they are qualified for as well what type of work they truly enjoy. 
 Also covered in this workbook is a section on good jobs vs. great jobs as well as what type of jobs are in high demand. Tips are given on how to go about choosing a job where you can be happy and be more productive.
