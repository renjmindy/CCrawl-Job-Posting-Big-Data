 WHY DO WE SHOW EXPIRED LISTINGS? 
 We are an industry leader in the hotel maintenance industry. Our crews stay at some of the nicest hotels in the country while they work. We maintain carpet, tile, furniture, drapes, and service air conditioner units in hotels nationwide. No experience necessary. You must be able to lift 50-75 lbs every 15-20 min when servicing the air conditioners. Work in a team after a short separate training period. Our couples work together and may be asked to help train additional staff. A Couples crew would consist of one customer service rep and one technician/lead. You can expect pay ranging from 900-1200 biweekly on average per person and $22.50 per day food allowance per person. Along with staying in nice accommodations while you see the world and work hard with you significant other. Benefits offered include: Health, Dental, Vision, 401K, paid vacation and road warrior bonus. 
 If this sounds like it might be an opportunity for you or you would like to find out more information about our company and what we do please visit our website.
