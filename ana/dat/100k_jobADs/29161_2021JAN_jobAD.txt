 Plant Fitter 
 Job description 
 
 
 $45/ hour 
 
 
 7/7 Roster 
 
 
 Day Shift Only 
 
 
 ABOUT CLIENT 
 Our client is Australia's largest coal miner and exporter, the world's largest supplier to the sea borne coking coal market and largest coal producer and supplier of metallurgical coal. The client has several mine sites across the Bowen Basin consisting primarily of open cut mines 
 ABOUT THE ROLE 
 Seeking experienced Fixed Plant Maintenance Fitter to commence mid -late January at Ranger Mine. This position will join our decommissioning mechanical team, and report to the maintenance supervisor on a 7-7 days only roster, 12hr days and is approx. 6 months work. DIDO Darwin 
 DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 
 all aspects of mechanical experience required for decommissioning including removal of some assets 
 
 
 general labouring duties as part of a larger decommissioning crew 
 
 
 SKILLS & RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 
 Fixed plant maintenance fitter or suitable trade qualification 
 
 
 Confined space entry, Gas test atmosphere (within 2 yrs) 
 
 
 Working at heights required for this role (within 2 yrs) 
 
 
 Relevant experience in mining or similar heavy industry and a commitment to safety 
 
 
 Ability to interoperate P&IDs; 
 
 
 Accurate record keeping 
 
 
 Highly regarded: 
 
 
 Forklift, Non Slew crane, EWP High Risk Work licence 
 
 
 Relevant mining or heavy industry experience
