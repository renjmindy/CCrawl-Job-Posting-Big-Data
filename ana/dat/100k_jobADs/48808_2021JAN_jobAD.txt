 Commercial Finance Manager - Warwick - 55 - 65k + Bonus 
 Do you enjoy interacting with the commercial teams to provide critical business information and analysis? Have the drive and ambition to drive change within an organisation that are lacking support from their commercial finance teams? 
 Gleeson Recruitment Group are working with an International manufacturing organisation in Warwick who are looking to recruit a Commercial Finance Manager with a specific focus of partnering with Marketing and Sales. 
 Reporting to the Finance Director, you will be responsible for commercial analysis and planning of the Direct Sales channels. You will provide business partner support to the Direct Sales team including development and ownership of Gross Margin and ROI calculation, alongside managing a small team. 
 As the Commercial Finance Manager you will be involved in: 
 
 Support the Commercial Director and wider UK & Ireland Sales team with sales and margin analysis and reporting 
 Validate Direct Sales assumption within business case proposals 
 Validate and reconcile monthly sales and margin forecasts 
 Support the Commercial Director with the on-going product and customer rationalisation project 
 Carry out reviews with Sales and Forecast teams to understand and challenge forecasts at account and SKU level 
 Responsible for managing and collating the Sales forecast and budgets by channel / customer/ product type as input to the Financial forecasts/budget 
 Prepare monthly Sales analysis for inclusion in the monthly review presentation 
 Communicate financial measures to non-financial stakeholders 
 Challenge business's cases and sales promotion plans 
 Be the "Finance" contact for the Sales and Marketing 
 Any other ad-hoc duties as requested by the Head of Finance 
 
 Is the Commercial Finance Manager role right for you? 
 The organisation are a well-known manufacturing organisation with an International presence and a legacy for creating iconic products. The role requires a Qualified Accountant with a commercial background to support the Marketing and Sales teams by analysing large volumes of data and then communicating your findings to the wider business. 
 The role would suit somebody who is Qualified ACCA, ACA or CIMA with knowledge of the digital retail industry and a willingness to investigate and provide recommendations on how to improve. This a very commercial position which will see you interacting with Sales Managers, Commercial Finance teams and Accounting functions to ensure accuracy.
