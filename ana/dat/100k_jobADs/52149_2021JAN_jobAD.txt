 Caregiver Job Hiring for Israel 
 Posted on  April 19, 2010  by  caregiver 
 A POEA accredited recruitment agency is currently looking for Filipino caregivers who are qualified for  Caregiver Job Hiring for Israel . The said caregiver job hiring have a job order that was approved by the POEA last April 8 for a recruitment agency located in Paligsahan in Quezon City. For the said caregiver job hiring, a corresponding job order balance of 30 caregiver job hiring applicants will be selected for placement in caregiver work abroad. 
 If you have the qualifications and interested to apply for the said caregiver job hiring, proceed directly to the office of the recruiting agency and submit your application. For inquiries relating to the said caregiver job hiring, you can contact the agency’s landline numbers and email address specified below. 
 JAO International Manpower Services 
Unit E 3rd Floor, Jacinto Building 
380 Scout Reyes corner Quezon Avenue 
Paligsahan, Quezon City 
Tel Nos: (632) 774-0995 / (632) 332 4234 
Email Address: admin@jaointernational.com 
Website: http://jaointernational.com
