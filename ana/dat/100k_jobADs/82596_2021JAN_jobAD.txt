 A International Digital Product Manager position working on a hugely exciting internationalisation programme. 
 My client is a marketing leader retail business with fantastic global growth plans. This role will play the leading driving this from a digital perspective; bridging technical and business requirements. 
 The International Digital Product Manager will sit within a well established Product team but with a particular focus on this internationalisation plan. The role will encompass: 
 
 Shaping the Internationalisation 
 Define and manage the Internationalisation roadmap and backlog 
 Bring together stakeholders throughout the development process 
 
 The ideal candidate for this role will possess a strong digital product background and will have worked across international markets. 
 Highly competitive package. 
 This role is currently remote. My client is based in the Coventry are, in the future significant flexibility will be considered.
