 JOB SUMMARY 
 The purpose of this position is to provide Electrical BIM Management for a large technology client building data centers. 
 ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES 
 
 Serve as the owner BIM manager to provide general support and guidance on BIM workflow and processes for internal and external stakeholders; BIM onboarding for new vendors; hold BIM kickoff meetings; provide BIM standards and templates to external partners 
 BIM360 - Coordinate BIM360 work space setup with BIM360 support team; provide access to owner team & AE admin to BIM360; act as owner BIM360 project admin for electrical products 
 BIM Execution Plan - collaborate with internal and external stakeholders to develop, review and approve product BEP 
 Interface management - manage BIM interface tagging, tracking, and validation 
 Clash detection - clash detection check at design milestones 
 Model health check:review & verify design models (live Revit models, BIM360 published models,MCAD formats) for CAD/BIM compliance at design milestones, escalating known design issues that have construction impacts 
 CMMS asset list creation - coordinate with DCIO CMMS team to review and finalize product CMMS asset template in the model and asset list extraction 
 Model change management - Maintain model change management post product development exit 
 BIM coordination -coordinate with other project teams on model linking and coordination 
 Support product model posting to document management system 
 Assist in the development of the overall Revit shared parameters and reusable mechanical/electrical families maintained by CAD/BIM as needed 
 Research & assist in the implementation of automation tools to reduce the production time internally and externally 
 Support minor in house model update as needed 
 
 Deliverables: 
 
 BIM Execution Plan review report 
 BIM QC check report 
 Clash detection review report 
 Minimal Electrical Revit families modeling as needed 
 Product interface audit report 
 Document of vendor products and configurations 
 Minor in house electrical model updates as needed 
 Reports on CAD/BIM process improvements 
 Implementation & feedback reports on CAD/BIM initiatives 
 Data input for business intelligence reports / dashboards 
 Written documentation regarding all meetings and correspondences. 
 Written monthly status reports outlining work that has been performed by Contractor 
 Other duties as assigned. 
 
 SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES 
 No formal supervisory responsibilities in this position. Provides informal assistance such as technical guidance, and/or training to coworkers. May lead project teams and/or plan, and supervise assignments of lower level employees. 
 QUALIFICATIONS 
 To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. 
 Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions. 
 EDUCATION and EXPERIENCE 
 Bachelor's degree (BA/BS) from 4-year college or university. Minimum four years of related experience. Prior Supervisory experience preferred. 
 ** 
** 
 Preferred Qualifications 
 ** 
** 
 Minimum 5 years hands on electrical design production experience 
Minimum 5 years hands on BIM experience in design and engineering 
MCAD experience is preferred (Solidworks or similar) 
Knowledge of the principles and practices in the BIM environment 
Knowledge of the principles and practices of electrical engineering design 
Ability to read and interpret design standards and specifications, blueprints, drawings, redlines, etc 
Ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously 
Must have excellent communication and organizational skills 
Ability to communicate effectively, both orally and in writing 
 CERTIFICATES and/or LICENSES 
 PgMP (Program Management Professional) or PMP (Project Management Professional) certifications preferred. 
 COMMUNICATION SKILLS 
 Excellent written and verbal communication skills. Strong organizational and analytical skills. Ability to provide efficient, timely, reliable and courteous service to customers. Ability to effectively present information. 
 FINANCIAL KNOWLEDGE 
 Requires advanced knowledge of financial terms and principles. Ability to calculate intermediate figures such as percentages, discounts, and commissions. Conducts advanced financial analysis. 
 REASONING ABILITY 
 Ability to comprehend, analyze, and interpret complex documents. Ability to solve problems involving several options in situations. Requires advanced analytical and quantitative skills. 
 OTHER SKILLS and ABILITIES 
 Strong process skills, negotiating, decision-making and analytical skills are necessary. Demonstrated ability in project management processes, tools and techniques. 
 Ability to build and maintain effective professional/client relationships. 
 Intermediate skills with Microsoft Office Suite including MS Project. 
 SCOPE OF RESPONSIBILITY 
 Decisions made with thorough understanding of procedures, company policies, and business practices to achieve general results and deadlines. Responsible for setting work unit and/or project deadlines. Errors in judgment may cause short-term impact to department.
