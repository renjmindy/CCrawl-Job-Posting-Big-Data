 Commercial Electrician 
 Job description 
 Electrician 
 Highlights 
 
 
 Immediate Start 
 
 
 On-going Work 
 
 
 Weekly Pay 
 
 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac are seeking experienced local Commercial Electricians for upcoming works in Yeppoon commencing late November until April 2021. Working on a new build project you will be working on installation of lighting and power on a Monday to Friday roster. 
 Duties of the role 
 
 
 Cable running 
 
 
 Lighting and power installation 
 
 
 Testing 
 
 
 Working in a small Electrical crew 
 
 
 To be successful in this position 
 
 
 Qualified Electrician 
 
 
 Demonstrated experience within Commercial Electrical 
 
 
 Current Electrical Licence 
 
 
 LVR & CPR 
 
 
 White card 
 
 
 Drivers Licence 
 
 
 Based locally in the Yeppoon 
 
 
 Ability to start work immediately 
 
 
 What can we offer you? 
 
 
 Long term local work 
 
 
 Weekly wages 
 
 
 Opportunity to work for a reputable company 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
