 We are looking for a new client from us; 
 
 
 Fitter MAINTENANCE & SERVICE 
 
 Our client is a major supplier of motorized Ground Support Equipment for the airport industry. An international organization that takes care of the service and maintenance of freight and baggage tractors, loaders, conveyor belts and pushback tractors used at the airport. This company is characterized by short lines of communication and personal contact, where work safety, quality and fast switching are of paramount importance. 
 Do you have an eye for quality & detail and is 100% service in your genes? Then we are looking for you! 
 
 
 What are we looking for for the client? 
 
 
  We are looking for a full-time engineer to perform inspections, maintenance and breakdown work on the various fixed installations in the warehouses. Think of roller tracks, truck docks, doors and storage systems. 
  You also install new systems. 
  The work is located partly in the workplace and partly on location at the customer. 
 You perform the checking & repair work in the workshop, but also on location! So one moment you are working on the platform and the other moment at a cargo handler. To go to the several clients you have a bus. The bus is equipped with all work conveniences so you have all the tools to do it work! And you will always have an experienced colleague next to you. The job always gets done! 
 What do we expect from you; 
 
 
  That you have technical work experience in the field of Welding (electrodes); 
  You can bend, press and grind; 
  You are in any case in possession of a driving license B, driving license E is a Pre; 
  You have a VCA & a valid forklift certificate; 
  Good command of the English & Dutch language; 
  You know your responsibilities and can do a job both independently and in a team; 
  Because critical systems are often involved, you must be able to perform well under time pressure! 
 What do we offer; 
 
 
  A varied job at one of the largest airports in Europe; 
  You will be linked to an experienced technician who will teach you the tricks of the trade; 
  A good & competitive salary; 
  A growing and stable company; 
  A full-time job with a prospect of permanent employment! 
 Are you ready for a new challenge and is tinkering a passion of yours, are you focused on quality and service?
