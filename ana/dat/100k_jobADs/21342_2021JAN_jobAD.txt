 Full-Time Director / Program Manager in Wireless System Project 
 ALFA Henkilöstöpalvelu Oy  -  Uusimaa  , Finland 
 Job Description 
 Reports to: Director of PMO (Project Management Office) 
 Job Summary 
 Program Manager is responsible for planning, executing, and finalizing projects according to strict deadlines and within budget. This includes acquiring resources and coordinating the efforts of team members and third-party contractors to deliver projects according to plan. 
 Key Responsibilities /Accountabilities 
 
 Defining and establishing PM role and work - process, R&R of relative organization 
 develop management items and assessment index 
 develop improvement plan, and coach - prepare standard manuals and train 
 assess project result/progress and PM 
 support to projects execution - diagnosis and work out for improvement plan 
 develop best Practices for Project execution. 
 
 Qualifications 
 
 University degree: BS / MS - 15 years with 7 years direct working experience in a project or program management capacity including all aspects of process development and execution. 
 Competent and proficient understanding of telecom systems business. - Wireless Infrastructure Business - Project Management - Installation, Commissioning, and Optimization 
 Logistics, Quality Management, Operation 
 
 ALFA Henkilöstöpalvelu Oy co-operates with Samsung Electronics and represents Samsung's recruiting business in Finland and Sweden. All candidates will be presented for Samsung solely by ALFA Henkilöstöpalvelu Oy. Samsung don't want direct contacts.
