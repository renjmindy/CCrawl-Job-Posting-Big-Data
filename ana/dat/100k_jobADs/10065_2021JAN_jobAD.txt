 Domestic Helper / Caregiver Jobs Cyprus 
 Posted on  January 1, 2011  by  caregiver 
 The POEA recently approved a job order that will provide employment opportunities to those who are willing to work in  Domestic Helper / Caregiver Jobs Cyprus . This domestic helper / caregiver jobs Cyprus was approved for recruitment last December 28 for a POEA licensed recruitment agency located in Lapaz in Iloilo City. The said domestic helper / caregiver jobs Cyprus have a total of 100 vacancies which was also approved by the POEA. 
 If you are interested to apply for the said Domestic Helper / Caregiver Jobs Cyprus, submit your resume and application directly to MY International Manpower Corp. For any questions related to the said domestic helper / caregiver jobs Cyprus, you may contact MY International Manpower Corp. thru their telephone number or email address specified below. 
 MY International Manpower Corp. 
2nd Floor, Doyo Building 
Huervana Street, Lapaz 
Iloilo City 
Tel Nos: (033) 3207471 
Email Add: myintlcorp@skyinet.net 
 BRANCH OFFICE

Koronadal City
