 Gleeson Recruitment Group are delighted to be working exclusively with our high-profile, multi-national, impressive Pharmaceutical client, who's Head Quarters are in Maidenhead, who seek a Qualified Financial Accountant for their growing finance team. 
 You will ideally be Qualified (ACCA/CIMA/ACA), combined with recent. hands-on financial accounting experience, ideally from a multi-site, or Shared Service Centre background. This role initially will be hired on a 6-9 month basis to cover maternity leave and our client is able to offer an immediate start. 
 You will report into the Head of Financial Accounting, and working (remotely initially) with a close-knit Financial Accounting team - the majority of which are Part Qualified and Qualified. 
 Your responsibilities as the Financial Accountant will be varied, and will include: 
 - Accruals, Prepayments 
 - Fixed Assets 
 - Month-end Reconciliations 
 - Balance Sheet Recs and Bank Reconciliations 
 - Multi-currency transactions 
 - Business Partnering with the wider finance and non-finance business 
 - Daily advanced use of Excel 
 The successful profile as the Financial Accountant will ideally be: 
 - Proactive, and be a good problem solver 
 - Be able to cope with large volumes of work, whilst being able to cope under pressure 
 - Be focussed with a high attention to detail 
 - Add value in all areas of the Financial Accounting team and be prepared to step up when required 
 - Be Qualified (ACCA/CIMA/ACA) 
 - Demonstrable experience of working in a similar fast-paced role 
 This is a great Financial Accountant opportunity to join an established, well recognised business - and although initially working from home, and on-boarded at home, it may be in Spring 2021 when you would return to the office, so it's essential you are able to commute easily to the Maidenhead site. Free parking on offer and close to the main station. My client can offer superb training and on-boarding, flexibly and a friendly working environment. 
 Please contact me for a full job spec for this Financial Accountant position and for further details. My client can offer an immediate start but will wait up to 2 weeks for the right person. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
