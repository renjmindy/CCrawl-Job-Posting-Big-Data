 Consultant: 
 
 
 Are you looking to develop your career by working with one of the best brands in the market, using cutting edge technologies, as well as being exposed to highly challenging IT Strategy and Digital Transformation projects on a global scale? 
 Venquis is currently searching for multiple Managers and Senior Managers for one of our Tier 1 consultancy clients. 
 You will work hand in hand with clients to develop high level IT Strategy solutions, using your technical knowledge and business acumen as well as your highly analytical and conceptional skills. You will have the opportunity to work on both national and international client projects at the very highest level and across multiple sectors. 
 You structure your projects and will take over resource planning. Together with customers, you will develop convincing IT strategies, concepts and solutions, using your in-depth technical know-how, your extensive expertise in the management of IT projects and your analytical and conceptual skills. Youwill present concepts and solutions to decision-makers and agree on the further course of action. 
 Typical tasks will include: 
 
 IT project management and quality assurance 
 Development of IT target landscapes tailored to the requirements of the customer 
 Planning and implementation of IT transformation projects 
 Analysis and evaluation of IT architectures 
 IT product evaluation and selection 
 Control of sub-projects of mission-critical IT transformation projects 
 IT due diligence 
 Analysis and Optimization of IT Core ProcessesRestructuring of IT Core Processes 
 
 Knowledge and experience: 
 
 A degree in Computer Science, Business Informatics, Business Administration or a related field, graduating with above-average success 
 At least 6 years of relevant professional experience in consulting and in the implementation of operational and strategic IT implementation and transformation projects 
 Extensive project management experience 
 Experience in resource planning and leadership 
 Experience in IT organization and process development 
 Sound knowledge in the planning of IT initiatives and the development of robust business cases 
 Exceptional analytical and conceptual skills as well as good organizational skills 
 Business fluent German and English (spoken and written) 
 Team spirit, communication and presentation skills, and intellectual agility 
 High resilience and flexibility as well as willingness to travel 
 
 For consideration please send your CV/ Project list as well as your academic certificates and salary expectations to  tomf@venquis.com  and we will be in touch for an initial discussion. Feel free to call me on  +49 (0)89 2109 3906  for more information.
