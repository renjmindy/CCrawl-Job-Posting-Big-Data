 Look At This! it's a Full-time job 
 Ana Luisa is looking for an Art Director in Brooklyn, NY, USA. 
 About 
 Ana Luisa is an online direct-to-consumer jewelry brand based in Greenpoint, Brooklyn. Our mission is to change the way sustainable jewelry is designed, made and enjoyed across the world. 
 "In 2020, Top 15 Fastest Growing E-commerce brands in NYC." 
 ▻ Your Golden Mission 
 ■ Envision, develop, and lead the creative direction at Ana Luisa for multi-platform, multi-channel brand communication. 
■ A previous experience with paid marketing is a must. 
■ Hire, build and manage your dream team of 6 to meet all creative needs across our various organic and paid marketing channels. 
■ You have previous experiences in the world of e-com. 
■ Direct weekly in-house photo and video shoots (in our own Greenpoint studio and on location). 
■ Overview post-production selects, retouching/editing notes and all else involved to bring creative direction to life. 
■ Research new visual ideas and concepts to stay on top of new channels and social trends.
