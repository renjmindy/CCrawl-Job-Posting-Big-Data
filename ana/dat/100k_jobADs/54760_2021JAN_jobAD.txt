 Career: 
Miche worked in the store environment from 1994-2003 holding various roles from stock guy to district manager and most jobs in between. Miche spent significant time with leaders in the retail industry including WalMart, Gap, Aeropostale and American Eagle. 
 Miche moved into phase 2 of his career working in buying, merchandising, product development and marketing starting in 2003. Miche spent time on both sides working in a corporate retail setting and working as a supplier. Miche has created a resume that is strongly based in ecommerce, where he currently works for the world's largest retailer. Miche is considered an expert in ecommerce merchandising, new business development, screen printing and product development. 
 In 2007 Miche launched his own brand GrowndFlore which has sells in over 50 stores worldwide and online at  http://www.GrowndFlore.com . In 2010 Miche launched Poindexter Delicious, a limited edition t-shirt line based on a childhood friend that sells exclusively online at  http://www.PoindexterDelicious.com . In 2012 GrowndFlore launched a kids line called Awesome Enough in a partnership with Kiddie Shack, with mini versions of the GrowndFlore line, exclusive tee designs and swimwear. Miche also founded a start up company t-shirt showroom with other industry veterans and as showroom grows Miche remains on board as an advisor. 
 Freelance & Consulting: 
As Miche has grown in his career many companies have looked to him as ways to best utilize his resources. Projects from graphic design, packaging design, web design, font design, logo design, web merchandising, new business development and product production. Miche has worked with companies like Rousch Brands, Quiksilver, Roxy, Delias, CCS, Pac Sun, Polo and various indie brands. 
 Education: 
Miche has degrees in both Fashion Merchandising and Home Ec Education from The Ohio State University.
