 Exploration, Senior Field Assistant 
 Job description 
 
 
 Ongoing Position 
 
 
 Travel Time Paid 
 
 
 FIFO ex Perth or BIBO ex Hedland 
 
 
 About the Role 
WorkPac is currently seeking experienced Field Assistants for an ongoing role on a 15 days on, 6 days off roster, FIFO ex Perth or BIBO ex Hedland. Our client is heavily focused on safety and they invest a lot of time in to training and upskilling current employees. This role includes travel time in the first and last working day and which means all hours are paid for. You will be working with our exploration client, staying in various camps in the Pilbara and living in modified caravans. As a key member of a high performing team, you will be required to perform general field technician work such as refuelling generators, general camp maintenance, assisting with the set up and demobilising of camps, assisting with the transportation of RC and diamond drill samples and supporting Geologists as directed. 
 Skills & Experience Required 
 
 
 12+ Months Field Assistant experience 
 
 
 Unrestricted C Class drivers licence 
 
 
 Ability to pass a pre-employment medical 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities

 At WorkPac, we recognise we are strengthened by diversity. We are committed to providing a work environment in which everyone is included, treated fairly and with respect. We are an Equal Opportunity employer and we encourage applications from women and Indigenous people.
