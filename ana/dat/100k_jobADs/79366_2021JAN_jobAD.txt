 Can work independently with minimal supervision towards defined tasks within a team environment. 
 Able to thrive in a highly agile environment where priorities and tasks change frequently. 
 We are looking for someone with excellent software design and implementation skills. The ideal person is a seasoned developer, able to work productively modifying an existing codebase, managing exotic hardware devices, occasionally in a multi-threaded context. 
 This project spans multiple disciplines, so the ability to communicate well with other team members and support overall team goals is crucial. 
 
 Typical day in the role 
 
 A day will usually involve some mixture of: feature design work, writing code, modifying existing code, testing changes. Talking to other team members to clarify requirements and coordinate over the use of equipment. 
 Remote work will be available, indeed, encouraged given the current pandemic.
