 CRO & User Experience Manager 
 Paying £35-45k, A fantastic opportunity for a CRO & User Experience Manager to join the growing digital marketing team for an established and digital lead service organisation based in Warwickshire. 
 As a Conversion Rate & User Experience Manager, you'll combine elements of data-driven testing and campaign optimisation through the full sales and marketing funnel, improving the UX and Customer Journey whilst accelerating digital performance. 
 What You'll do... 
 
 Building the first in house UX & Conversion function 
 Building tests in Site Gainer or similar tool 
 Driving optimisation across lead gen for services and applications online 
 Managing end-to-end optimisation testing process, including prioritisation, requirements gathering, test implementation, measurement, and next-step recommendations 
 Conducting Quantitative and Qualitative analysis 
 Own an ongoing testing roadmap to optimise onsite user journeys 
 Creating and track goal flows to identify pain points in the user journey 
 Identifying opportunities for A/B/MVT testing 
 Creating and develop tests using CRO tools 
 Designing wireframes, prototypes and building tests 
 Working closely with Content team to drive new landing page tests and campaigns 
 Working closely with the PPC & Brand teams to improve ROI 
 Research industry leading market trends for future campaigns 
 
 What you need… 
 
 Proven ability of successful CRO campaigns showing an increase in Conversion Rate/Revenue 
 Proven knowledge of UX principles 
 Strong hypothesis-based tester with A/B and MVT optimisation experience 
 Experience building and launching tests using CRO tools and platforms (Site Gainer, VWO, Optimizely, Google Optimise etc.) 
 Excellent Analytical and Reporting Skills (using Google Analytics/Excel/CRO Tools) 
 Knowledge of HTML/CSS/JS (using CRO tools to create tests/experiences) 
 BSc/BA in Marketing, Digital Media or a related field 
 
 What you'll get... 
 
 Flexible working hours 
 An employee assistance programme 
 Extensive staff discount programme 
 Performance related pay rewards 
 Comprehensive tailored induction plan 
 Ongoing personal development and career progression
