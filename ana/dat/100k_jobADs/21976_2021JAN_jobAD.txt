 Consultant: 
 
 
 An exciting opportunity for a talented digital designer to join a leading business! You will work collaboratively with the wider marketing team to design high quality and vibrant graphics for the website, social media, email and other digital marketing channels. This role is perfect for an individual with a creative flare, an eye for design and a good understanding of engaging and relevant content. 
 Remote Working will be considered for this role, but West Midlands would be preferential as there will be occasional travel to the office. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Follow set briefs to produce online artwork for all digital channels, including new website banners, landing pages, social media, email marketing and digital advertising. 
 Produce design work inline with brand guidelines and tone of voice. 
 Design and Code HTML email campaigns and landing pages. 
 Ensure all artwork produces is on brief and delivered within set timescales. 
 Produce printed material when needed, including brochures and PR material 
 
 The Ideal Candidate Will: 
 
 Experience within a similar digital design role. 
 A good knowledge of HTML and CSS. 
 Have a good eye for detail and experience creating customer focused artwork with a 'call to action. 
 Be deadline focused, and ability to prioritise workload. 
 Have the ability to make creative decisions and solve technical issues.
