 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently partnering with a global manufacturing business who are recruiting for a qualified accountant (ACA,ACCA,CIMA) to join them as a UK Finance Manager based in Birmingham. 
 This diverse role covers many aspects of Financial Control Management & Accounting, including complex technical aspects AND team management. Main responsibilities include financial control, month end close lead, UK stats and reconciliations. 
 Key Responsibilities, Finance Manager, Birmingham 
 
 Be responsible for, and support, general, critical, and specific Accounting and Control processes, procedures, schedules and tasks. 
 Lead closing process - Planning, communicating and ensuring adherence to month end deadlines. Includes SAP journal entry management, journals including Non-controlling interest and European consolidation work and Oracle checks. Requires working with local and Division finance teams. 
 Fixed Asset accounting 
 UK statutory accounting completion & audit(s) leadership & other local compliance - such as HMRC, duty, VAT etc. 
 Account reconciliations including blackline system - Monthly corporate control process, done to an audit standard. 
 Intercompany accounting management - including queries resolution. 
 Key project work e.g. SOX control, improvement, R&D tax credits 
 Weekly Divisional Cash Forecasting & Reporting: Presenting to Division leadership team on weekly call (generally as cover) 
 Covering Finance Controller work 
 Treasury matters e.g. review of cash forecast sent to Corporate 
 Forecasting - monthly forecast and review with budget holders. 
 Support plant control (accruals, prepayments etc) and other plant control as required 
 
 Skills & Experience, Finance Manager, Birmingham 
 
 Previous finance experience required 
 ACCA/ACA/CIMA qualification 
 Ideally SAP knowledge or other ERP system 
 Multi currency experience (beneficial) 
 Advanced excel skills
