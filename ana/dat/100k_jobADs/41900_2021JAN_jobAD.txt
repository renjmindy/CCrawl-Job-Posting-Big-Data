 Are you a dynamic digital marketer in search of a new opportunity? 
 Our client is a market leader in their field and has been experiencing rapid growth over recent years, and so is looking to take on a Digital Marketing Manager to join their team. 
 This an exciting and fast-paced organisation, and is looking for an individual who has a passion for all things digital! Reporting to the Head of Marketing, the successful candidate will be responsible for the day to day running of all digital marketing efforts - using multiple different digital channels to assist in the promotion of products. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 
 Create and implement innovative marketing strategies to achieve business targets and continued growth across multiple channels. 
 
 
 Manage the creative development of website content, display advertising, SEO activity, PPC, Cost Per Click, Email Campaigns and other marketing-related projects. 
 
 
 Manage the department budget, and develop cost-effective marketing plans for the business. 
 
 
 Identifying trends and insights, and optimizing campaigns based on insights. 
 
 
 Regular analysis and reporting on marketing campaigns, to ensure they are in line with set KPI's. 
 
 
 The ideal candidate will: 
 
 
 Have proven experience in a similar Digital Marketing role. 
 
 
 Have solid knowledge of website analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics). 
 
 
 Be creative, and have a love for great content. 
 
 
 Be results driven, and always striving to improve. 
 
 
 Have excellent communication and organisation skills, with the ability to prioritise a busy workload. 
 
 
 Has previous experience in producing digital marketing reports, tracking KPIs and feeding back to the relevant sources 
 
 
 Salary is negotiable, DEO + Excellent Benefits.
