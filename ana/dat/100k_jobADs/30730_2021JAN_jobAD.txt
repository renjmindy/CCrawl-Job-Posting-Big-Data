 Fire Technician 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 2/2 roster 
 
 
 Day shift only 
 
 
 Potential for ongoing work 
 
 
 This role will predominantly involve testing of fire safety equipment on Barrow Island. The successful candidate must be qualified in the following system: FM 200 EEHL and both mechanical and electrical experience is required. 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 WA Electrical License 
 
 
 Cert II in Fire Protection, Inspection and Testing
