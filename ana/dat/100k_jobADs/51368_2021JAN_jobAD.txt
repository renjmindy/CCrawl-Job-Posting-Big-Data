 About us 
 Patchwork is an award-winning digital healthcare solution that was built by doctors, for doctors. 
 We are revolutionising flexible working in the NHS and are helping NHS organisations save millions (and counting), by connecting them to an increasing number of flexible bank workers. We help  grow their staff banks, remove reliance and spending on locum agencies, and aim to improve the general experience of flexible bank working. 
 The Role 
 You'll be the first full-time DevOps engineer to join our growing team. We're looking for someone to work closely with the wider engineering team and within our cross functional teams to help us ensure that we provide reliable, secure and high quality products for our clients. 
 Key Responsibilities 
 
 Maintaining and improving core AWS based infrastructure as the business scales 
 Ensuring we have useful alerting and monitoring 
 Automate currently manual processes using Terraform, Ansible etc 
 Working with wider engineering teams on continuous deployment process 
 Ensuring our infrastructure meets the ISO 27001 standards for security, reliability, redundancy and recoverability
