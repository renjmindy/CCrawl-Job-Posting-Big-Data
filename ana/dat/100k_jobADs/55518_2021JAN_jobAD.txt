 Remote National Title Curative/Title Clearance Specialist Direct Hire 
   
   Workway, Inc.   
 __Website    __@workway 
 We are looking for a  Remote National Title Curative/Title Clearance Specialist  responsible for handling large volumes of files for residential real estate transactions, nationally. The Title Curative/Title Clearance Specialist must have excellent customer service skills to communicate with clients, salespeople, attorneys and underwriters. 
 Your specific duties will include: 
 
 Read and clear purchase and refinance transactions; for residential transactions 
 Examine abstracts, leases, contracts, and other legal documents to determine title insurance and legal issues in connection with title insurance commitments/reports 
 Works with title agency, clients and attorneys representing parties to a real estate transaction to clear title exceptions and prepare the title for closing 
 Ability to prepare and/or review both Owner and Loan Policies for appropriate exceptions 
 Reviewing and knowledge of Title Bills/Invoices. 
 Experience with Surveys, Survey Readings, Real Estate Taxes, Recordings and Escrows 
 Experience with both New York and National transactions. 
 
 Specific qualifications for the position include  : 
 
 5+ years of Title Curative/Title Clearance experience - Required 
 Experience working residential real estate title files on a national basis - Required 
 Experience with SoftPro Select - Preferred 
 Bachelor's degree - Preferred 
 Ability to work with minimal supervision, in a high production environment 
 
 Let one of our recruiters assist you in finding and preparing for interviews with our clients. Whether you are interested in a direct hire, temp-to-hire, or temporary position, Workway can help you to put your best foot forward. Contact us today! 
 To receive state and federal compliance posters, e-mail hr@workway.com or call 972.514.1515.
