 Ecommerce Trading Assistant 
 
 Location 
 
 Atherstone, Warwickshire 
 
 Sector: 
 
 Marketing and Digital 
 
 Job type: 
 
 Permanent 
 
 Salary: 
 
 £30000 - £32000 per annum 
 
 Contact: 
 
 Darrah O'Sullivan 
 
 Contact email: 
 
 DarrahO'Sullivan@workwithglee.com

 Published: 
 
 8 months ago 
 
 Expiry date: 
 
 2020-06-14 
 
 Consultant: 
 
 
 This is an exciting opportunity for a results-driven and digitally savvy ecommerce Assistant to join a leading retail business in Atherstone. Joining a fast-paced and busy ecommerce team, your role will be responsible for planning and executing the trading strategy with a key focus on defining the ecommerce product portfolio, optimising products for the web, and other merchandising activities. 
 The Role: 
 
 Work with other departments to ensure products are ready for upload. 
 Help define the future ecommerce product portfolio. 
 Analyse and report on sales and orders, and forecast repeat order quantities. 
 Complete price checks on the website weekly, and perform any price reductions necessary. 
 Work closely with the wider marketing team, fulfilment and third party suppliers. 
 Respond to general buying and ecommerce queries from the wider team. 
 
 The Candidate: 
 
 Experience in a similar ecommerce role. 
 Stakeholder management and buying experience. 
 Results driven, and the desire to continued self development. 
 The ability to prioritise and organise busy workloads. 
 
 This is a challenging opportunity for a driven and proactive individual with excellent communication and organisation skills who enjoys working in a fast paced environment. This is a role with excellent growth opportunities, and a clear progression plan within the business.
