 About the role 
 People.ai accelerates enterprise growth through the power of AI. With the industry’s only Revenue Intelligence System, People.ai frees all customer-facing teams, including sales, marketing, and customer success, from manual data entry by automatically capturing all contact and customer activity data, dynamically updating CRM and other systems of record, and providing actionable intelligence across management tools to realize the full selling capacity of the enterprise. Some of the world’s best brands are leveraging People.ai to transform their business, including Lyft, New Relic, Okta, Tanium, and Zoom.

 As our product leader for data platform and machine learning, you drive the vision, mission, and strategy of the data platform that powers the entire People.ai product portfolio. 
 The People.ai data platform is an enterprise game changer: It provides best in class activity capture, classification, and matching to provide AI-based business insights to some of the most successful and fastest growing companies in the industry. 
 As the product lead, you own the roadmap for the data platform, identify and resolve blockers, partner with cross functional teams across the company, and build relationships with customers to make them successful. 
 Responsibilities: 
 
 Provide the vision and roadmap for building an ai-powered data platform that thrives in complex enterprise environments 
 Push the envelope to build the best in class business activity capture and matching engine 
 Deliver a performant, enterprise level business activity capture pipeline 
 Design and build machine learning models that accurately classify and match business activity data to provide actionable insights 
 Partner with design, engineering, sales, and support to build products that make our customers successful 
 
 Requirements: 
 
 5+ year(s) of product management experience 
 Experience working with big data and machine learning 
 Experience working with enterprise scale products 
 Experience working with CRM systems (Salesforce, Dynamics CRM, Hubspot, and others)
