 Dragline Cleaners 
 Job description 
 
 
 $44.90/hour 
 
 
 Ongoing roster for 2021 
 
 
 Expand your mining experience 
 
 
 About Our Client: 
 Our client is based locally in the Queensland Central Highlands Region and supplier of support services to the mining and construction industry. 
 About the Role: 
WorkPac are currently seeking experienced Dragline cleaners for work in Ensham and Gregory Mine for ongoing work. 
You will be willing to complete duties that include but not limited to cleaning in and around dragline and on shut down pad. 
 Duties and Responsibilities: 
 
 
 Vacuuming, removal of black jack other cleaning duties as required 
 
 
 Ability to follow roster - Every Wednesday 12hour Day Shift only 
 
 
 Skills and Experience: 
 
 
 Current Standard 11 
 
 
 Current CBM 
 
 
 Current Ensham and/ Gregory Induction 
 
 
 Proven experience as labourer/industrial cleaner 
 
 
 Ability to provide a clean drug and alcohol test 
 
 
 High standard and commitment to safety 
 
 
 Must be reliable and available every Wednesday for the next couple of Month 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
