 MANAGER 
 CARIBOU MOUNTAIN HOMES MANUFACTURING 
 The Little Red River Group of Companies based in High Level, Alberta is COR certified and operates businesses in civil, marine and home construction, forestry, firefighting, equipment rentals, propane distribution and retail. 
 Our expanding operations require the services of a certified and highly experienced building professional to join our management team and manage our plant 40 KM from High Level. 
 The position reports to the Chief Executive Officer of the Corporate Group. 
 **Duties & Responsibilities: ** 
 · Forecasting, scheduling and project costing; ensuring houses and related tasks are delivered on time 
 · Overseeing all aspects related to production including but not limited to safety, housekeeping and quality of workmanship 
 · Train new employees and existing employees as systems change 
 · Regular management reporting 
 · Purchasing & control of materials inventory 
 · Provide technical expertise to overcome production challenges 
 · Negotiate and resolve internal and external conflicts 
 Education/Experience Requirements: 
 · Certified Journeyman carpenter mandatory 
 · Red Seal endorsement an asset 
 · 5+ year's residential construction as lead/foreman 
 · 2 years minimum supervisory experience 
 · Experience running large crews an asset 
 · Prior experience in manufactured home plant an asset 
 · Knowledge of SIP construction methods 
 · Computer skills including Word, Excel, MS Office, MS Projects 
 · Conversant in reading/interpreting shop drawings and blue prints 
 · Knowledge of National and Provincial Building Code requirements 
 · Strong knowledge of manufacturing & logistics operations 
 · Strong knowledge of plumbing & electrical as applicable to residential housing 
 · Demonstrated strong interpersonal skills 
 · Thinks outside the box; focused on mutual objectives 
 · Understands accountability 
 Compensation: 
 A competitive base salary, incentive pay and comprehensive paid benefits package including medical, dental, pension and life insurance will be offered. Relocation expenses negotiable.
