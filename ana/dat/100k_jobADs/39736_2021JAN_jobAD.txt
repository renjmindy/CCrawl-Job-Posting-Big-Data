 Consultant: 
 
 
 My client, a well established B2C business based in the West Midlands, are looking for a Retail Marketing Specialist to join their team. This role will report into the Head of Marketing, and will play a key role in the development and delivery of engaging marketing campaigns, and will manage the production of all promotion materials- with a key focus on retail stores. 
 The Role: 
 
 Responsible for planning and executing creative marketing campaigns to engage existing customers, and reach new and potential customers. Ensuring all content is on brand and inline with strategy. 
 Continuously look to improve customer spend, shopper acquisition, and member retention via promotions (online and offline). 
 Plan and execute the delivery of in-store promotional materials, visual marketing and POS, for existing stores and store launches. 
 Ensuring all campaigns run within a set time frames and are within budgets. 
 Understand the customer in detail and their shopper trends- and then translate findings into new promotional plans. 
 Marketing via digital channels, such as Social Media and Email Marketing Campaigns. 
 Work closely with insights and research teams to understand the key customer demographic to ensure all campaigns are targeted/segmented. 
 
 The Candidate: 
 
 Experienced in a similar retail marketing role. 
 Experienced of omnichannel marketing 
 The ability to make effective decisions and continuously strive to improve. 
 Customer focussed, with ability to engage people. 
 Ability to establish relationships with key stakeholders 
 Experience with budget management
