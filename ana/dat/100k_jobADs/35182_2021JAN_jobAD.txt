 Are you an Product Manager experienced in developing a product portfolio? I am currently working with a market leading manufacturing business, who looking for a B2B Product Manager to join their growing, Birmingham based, marketing team. You will be responsible for defining the product portfolio, road map, and the full product life cycle- including development, launch, phase in-phase out, pricing and analysis. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Regular customer and competitor market research to identify customer issues and gaps in market for new product development. 
 Drive the NPD, and manage existing products within the portfolio with a view to growing revenue in line with the business targets. 
 Continuously monitor and assess the strategic positioning and margin profile of current product lines. 
 Work with the marketing team to develop marketing plans for existing product ranges and NPD. 
 As product manager you will have to communicate effectively with internal and external stakeholders, as well as being trusted as a expert on the product portfolio you manage. 
 Be the 'go to' person within the business for your product range and act as a technical champion for both internal and external queries. 
 Define product goals and metrics, and monitoring portfolio performance. 
 
 The Ideal Candidate: 
 
 Ideally a Degree in Engineering, or any other qualification in Engineering. The ability to confidently communicate to to technical engineers is crucial. 
 Be able to demonstrate a track record of developing implementing product road maps. 
 Be confident putting business cases forward to senior management for NPD. 
 Have previous product management experience within the B2B sector. 
 Have strong stakeholder management skills, with the ability to discuss technical information confidently.
