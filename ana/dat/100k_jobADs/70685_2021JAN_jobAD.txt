 Home Care Jobs in Australia 
 Posted on  March 18, 2009  by  caregiver 
 A leading provider of Aged Care in the Hunter and Central Coast Regions is seeking motivated, energetic, and dedicated person to join their Community care team in Lake Macquarie. At present, they provide care for over 700 residents across 7 facilities and 200 community care clients. 
 If chosen for the home care jobs, your duties will consists of assisting their community care clients by delivering nursing care, provide home assistance and social interactions within the Lake Macquarie City Council Area. 
 Home Care Jobs Requirements: 
 
 Certificate III in Aged Care Work (CHC30102). 
 Current First Aid Certificate. 
 Experience in providing personal care to the aged in   community/residential environments. 
 Current drivers’ license with registered, insured and reliable car. 
 Eligible to work in Australia 
 
 What they offer: 
 
 Above award wages 
 Vehicle allowance (when used for business use) 
 Flexible working conditions 
 Paid parental leave 
 Career development opportunities 
 Salary Packaging available 
 
 Interested applicants may call the number below or send their resume to: 
 Jessica Meadows 
HR/Recruitment Officer 
Anglican Care 
 Ph: 02 4958 0093 
Fax: 02 4958 0064 
Email:  jobs@anglicancare.com.au
