 Gleeson Recruitment Group are working with a superb retail business based in Coleshill who are recruiting for a Financial Accountant. 
 The Financial Accountant is a key role which will focus on supporting the development, streamlining, documenting and embedding of updated financial processes to eliminate & mitigate process risk. 
 Key Accountabilties: Financial Accountant, Coleshill 
 
 Assisting in the day to day activities to include:Review of financial data, Statutory and external reporting, Month End activities 
 Supporting the preparation of external reporting matters required by legislation and listing rules 
 Ensuring that high quality, timely and consistent reporting is produced Key tasks and responsibilities 
 Review and follow-up of issues identified during monthly management accounts reviews, including identification and resolution of potential issues or exposures 
 Assisting with the preparation of the annual and interim plc Statutory Accounts, and subsidiary accounts under FRS101 
 Assisting with the delivery of financial information to the external auditors 
 Support on tax compliance matters and liaising with external tax advisors 
 
 Qualifications & Experiences, Financial Accountant, Coleshill 
 
 Newly qualified or part qualified Accountant (ACA or ACCA) 
 Knowledge and Experience of IFRS and external reporting requirements 
 Experience and knowledge in dealing with and concluding on technical accounting queries 
 Computer literate, with good financial modelling/spreadsheet skills 
 Attention to detail is essential 
 Self-confident with strong interpersonal skills, able to communicate at all levels within the organisation as well as with external parties 
 
 For more information on this superb Financial Accounting opportunity in Coleshill please do get in touch
