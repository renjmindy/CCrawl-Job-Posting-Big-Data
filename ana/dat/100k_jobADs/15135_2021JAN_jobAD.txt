 Consultant: 
 
 
 A client of Gleesons, based in Nottingham are looking for an Interim Management Accountant to join them on a 6 month contract. The successful candidate will be responsible for: 
 
 Core responsibilities include budgeting, forecasting, financial reporting, cost control and variance analysis 
 Generate, analyse and comment financial statements and multidimensional financial reports 
 Assists in month-end, quarterly, and year-end close process 
 Collaborate with various functional and operational teams 
 
 They are only looking at candidates commutable from Nottingham and available to start within next 2 weeks, there will be a mix of remote working on on-site working, (will consider 2 days on site and 3 days from home for the right person). 
 The ideal candidate will have: 
 
 Experience in using SAP & Navision 
 At least 3 years experience in a Management Accountant role 
 Strong reporting skills 
 Negotiation and influencing skills 
 Strong excel skills 
 Financial Professional Qualification(ACCA, ACA , CIMA or QBE)- desirable not essential
