 REGISTERED NURSES – MEDICAL 
 
 Working In Australia 
 Australia 
 Dec 07, 2020 
 
 Full time Healthcare 
 Job Description 
 There are extensive opportunities in Australia for registered nurses with experience in working in a medical ward. **** 
 Registered nurses are required to work in an Inpatient Medical Services Department that provides acute/elective services. Applicants should be committed to the provision of excellent care to patients of varying ages and a variety of medical conditions. 
 Essential Criteria: 
 A minimum of 1 years’ experience as a registered nurse in an acute secondary care facility, ideally with medical ward or other acute experience e.g. Operating Theatre/Emergency/ICU. 
 Working In have 20 years’ experience in helping overseas professionals live and work in New Zealand.
