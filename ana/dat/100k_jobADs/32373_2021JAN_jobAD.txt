 A client of Gleeson's have an exciting opportunity for a Finance Business Partner to join them on a FTC for up to 12 months. They are looking for someone who can liaise with both senior finance and Operation heads within the business. They are looking for individuals who are happy to get stuck into reporting but also be able to present to the Finance Director. The successful person must be qualified (ACCA, CIMA or ACA). They are looking for someone who is immediately available and able to start this side of Christmas. 
 The successful candidate will have the following: 
 
 CIMA, ACA or ACCA qualified is essential 
 Previous business partnering experience 
 Good reporting and analytical skills 
 Business services or financial services would be desirable but not essential 
 Proven experience of budgeting & forecasting 
 Previous experience of working with varied and cross-functional teams 
 
 I am looking to organise 1st stage interviews for next week so if you are interested please apply. Homeworking initially with the hope of being back in the Birmingham City Centre offices from early next year. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
