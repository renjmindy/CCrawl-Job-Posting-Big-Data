 Heavy Duty Fitter 
 Job description 
 Job Highlights 
 
 
 8 / 6 : 7/7 roster 
 
 
 FIFO ex Perth, Broome and Meekatharra. Busselton subject to availability 
 
 
 Ongoing position with possibilities of full time employment in the future 
 
 
 About our Client 
 Rio Tinto operates the world's largest integrated portfolio of iron ore assets. These assets include a world-class, integrated network of 15 iron ore mines, four port facilities, a 1,700 kilometre rail network and related infrastructure. Rio Tinto are expanding operations in the Pilbara to epic proportions while introducing next generation technologies to deliver greater efficiency, lower production costs and improved health, safety and environmental performance. 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac has employment opportunities across various Rio Tinto Iron Ore sites located in the Pilbara region of Western Australia. 
 Reporting to the site's Mobile Equipment Maintenance Supervisor, you will repair and conduct preventative maintenance, fault diagnostics and breakdown repairs on mobile fleet out in the field and workshop. You will also inspect and determine equipment condition and assess its suitability to return to service. The fleet of heavy mobile equipment of which you will need to have experience on are: 
 
 
 CAT 789 Dump Trucks 
 
 
 Komatsu 730E, 830E, 930E Dump Trucks 
 
 
 CAT 992, 994 Loaders 
 
 
 Komatsu WA900, WA1200 Loaders 
 
 
 CAT D10, D11 Dozers 
 
 
 CAT 16, 24 series Graders 
 
 
 Hitachi 3600, 5500, 5600 Diggers 
 
 
 Komatsu PC4000, PC5500 Diggers 
 
 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Relevant Australian recognised trade certification 
 
 
 2 + years post trade experience in a similar role 
 
 
 Manual C Class Australian issued Drivers Licence (minimum) 
 
 
 Experience driving around open pits 
 
 
 Experience working on autonomous haulage sites (advantageous) 
 
 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's leading, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, light industrial and healthcare sectors. We are proud to partner with some of the country's leading companies to deliver rewarding permanent, contract, temporary and traineeship recruitment opportunities.
