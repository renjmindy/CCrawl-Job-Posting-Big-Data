 Industrial Maintenance Mechanic 
 Apply for the position, Industrial Maintenance Mechanic, with Cross Manufacturing, Inc. today or contact Keshia for more information. Posted Sep 9, 2020.

 Description 
 **Overview: 
** With 70 years experience, Cross Manufacturing, Inc. exists to glorify God by helping feed the world physically and spiritually. We design, manufacture, and market hydraulic components to meet needs worldwide. Our Core Values are to do the right thing, live within our means (debt-free), and be dependable. Cross is seeking an Industrial Maintenance Mechanic at our Hays, KS manufacturing facility. 
 **Job Responsibilities: 
** The Industrial Maintenance Mechanic services and repairs production equipment, and safely maintains the plant facility and grounds. This position is responsible for making fixtures, components, parts, and alterations to equipment, building, and grounds. 
 **Requirements: 
** A successful candidate is highly motivated to conduct maintenance as required with quality workmanship. The candidate is detail-oriented, works well as part of a team, dependable, and supports the Guiding Principles of Cross. 
 Desired Qualifications: 
 
 Maintenance, mechanical, or technical experience required 
 Basic electrical and plumbing experience preferred 
 Basic computer operation experience preferred 
 
 **Compensation and Benefits: 
** In return for these requirements and responsibilities, Cross offers competitive compensation and an excellent benefits package.
