 Are you a part-qualified accountant looking to work for an industry leading business in an exciting commercial position with future career development? 
 Do you want to work with the best of the best, reporting into highly knowledgeable and high achieving qualified accountants? We are looking for highly ambitious and driven individuals for a Junior Finance Business Partner position. 
 Based near Birmingham, this role is a permanent full-time position for a part-qualified accountant who is currently working their way through their ACCA or CIMA. 
 You will be taking a proactive approach management accounting and finance analysis. There will be a lot of communication with internal and external stakeholders so you must be confident in your communication and with a natural flair to explain financial information to non-financial professionals in a clear and concise way. You will easily be able to engage with stakeholders and effectively influence business decisions. 
 The successful individual will be supported in their studies and professional development to become the next generation of Finance Business Partners. 
 You can expect a salary up to £38,000k + study package.
