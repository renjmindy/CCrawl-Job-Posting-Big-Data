 Are you a strong HR generalist? Do you have HR Advisory experience? Have you worked within the manufacturing industry? 
 If so, then I am currently looking for a HR Advisor to join a global business who are currently going through a period of change. The role will be based at their site in Barton but will require the flexibility to travel to other sites if needed. 
 As the successful HR Advisor, you will be accountable for providing support to the HR Manager, Line Managers and Business Units at and advisor level across a full HR generalist area. 
 Please note that you must have HR experience from within the manufacturing industry. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Supporting HR Manager and HR team. 
 Providing advice and guidance to line managers. 
 Involvement in general ER issues and queries. 
 Supporting absence management processes. 
 Compiling weekly/monthly reports and required data in a timely manner. 
 Updating and maintaining HR databases. 
 Assisting in recruitment process, e.g., co-ordination of interviews, feedback, administration and inductions. 
 New starters and leavers processes and associated paperwork. 
 Supporting grievance and disciplinary meetings. 
 General HR adhoc administration duties. 
 
 Skills / Experience: 
 
 Previous experience of working in an HR Advisory role. 
 Experience within the manufacturing industry. 
 SAP experience advantageous. 
 Microsoft Office, good understanding of Excel. 
 Organised and ability to prioritise workload across various work projects. 
 Develop excellent working relationships with internal and external stakeholders. 
 Able to work autonomously and within strict deadlines to a high standard. 
 Strong IT skills especially confident using Microsoft Excel for reports.
