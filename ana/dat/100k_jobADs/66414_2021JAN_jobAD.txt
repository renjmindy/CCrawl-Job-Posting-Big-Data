 Caregivers Job Singapore 
 Posted on  May 18, 2013  by  caregiver 
 Asian International Manpower Services Inc., a POEA accredited recruitment and placement agency with offices in Manila and the province of Iloilo, is now accepting applicants for  Caregivers Job Singapore . This caregivers job Singapore offers employment to 50 caregivers job applicants who have the following qualifications: 
 
 Female 
 25 to 40 years old 
 At least vocational graduate with caregiver course 
 Good in English communication skills 
 Must have at least 1 year experience with elderly people 
 Patient and kind when dealing with elderly people 
 
 If you possess the above specified qualifications for the said Caregivers Job Singapore, you may apply online by filling up the  Online Application  found at www.asianintlmanpower.com. Or, you may apply personally to the office of Asian International Manpower Services Inc. by submitting the following requirements: 
 
 Updated Resume 
 Two (2) pcs 2×2 & whole body pictures 
 Passport (photocopy) 
 School Records (TOR/Diploma) 
 Proof of Proficiency 
 Certificates (Employment, Seminars, etc.) 
 For ex-abroad – old passport & travel documents used overseas 
 
 For more details or inquiries about the said caregivers job Singapore, please contact Asian International Manpower Services Inc. by way of their telephone or mobile numbers listed below.
