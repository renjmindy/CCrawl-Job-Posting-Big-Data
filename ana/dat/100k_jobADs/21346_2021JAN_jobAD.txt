 Work Campers 
 Do you own an RV and enjoy working together? So do We !! Are you good at  WOWing  guests? We are too !! Are you looking to join an fun and energetic team of work campers? Look no further - you've landed on the right job posting. 
 Located just 11 miles north of Nashville,  **spend your summer experiencing "Music City" **and the Nashville area, from tours to museums, to music venues and the Grand Ole Opry. Positions are available April 1st through October 31st. We pay for all hours worked-most positions are 30-40 hours per week.   Work campers must have RV and live on-site. 
 **Job Descriptions:  **We are looking for awesome Guest Services Front Desk and Maintenance/Safety couples to join our team to work 30-40 hours each per week who are  passionate  about ensuring our campers have the best time, every time. 
 Guest Services/Front Desk  - This position is responsible for, but not limited to, handling camper issues with respect by listening and acknowledging their concern. Completing registrations, reservations and store sales while following procedures. Careful and accurate money handling, processing cash and credit card transactions while utilizing the computer, credit card terminal, and other front desk equipment. Knowledge of KampSight is a plus. 
 Maintenance/Safety  - Skilled maintenance knowledge is a plus. Providing exceptional guest services is a must, as this position also requires working as an ambassador escorting guests to their site, filling propane, delivering firewood and ice. Other duties include park maintenance, grass cutting, weed eating, help with pool maintenance and other duties as assigned. Projects are to be completed properly, safely and timely. 
 **Compensation:  **Couples are paid for all hours worked, the hourly rate is dependent upon experience. Benefits also include the following:  free full hook-up site  (30/50amp), (water/electric/sewer & cable),  two consecutive days off a week with your partner  (I know, who offers that?) uniform shirts, and employee discounts on laundry, propane and store merchandise.
