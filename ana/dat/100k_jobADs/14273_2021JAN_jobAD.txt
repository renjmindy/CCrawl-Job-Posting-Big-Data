 Job Description 
 Class/Bus Aide 
Methow Head Start 
 Are you someone who enjoys making a difference with young children and their families? Are you flexible and detail oriented? OCCDA is excited to announce an opening for a Classroom Aide in Okanogan. 
 About the position: 
 To assist the teacher and cooperate in a supportive role carrying out developmentally appropriate activities. To promote the health, nutrition and education of the children and to attend to their safety and welfare. 
 The ideal candidate will have the following: 
 
 Flexibility 
 Willingness to learn 
 Ability to find joy in small children and different cultures 
 Passion to make a difference 
 Open-minded 
 
 Required Education and Experience: 
 
 High School Diploma or equivalent 
 
 Preferred Education and Experience: 
 
 Bilingual/Spanish 
 
 The position is 34 hours per week for 38 weeks per year, with a starting salary of $13.77 /hour to $13.92 hour, depending on education. 
 To Apply: 
 Current OCCDA employees submit a Cover Letter, stating what qualities you would bring to the position in regards to this position. 
 All outside applicants must submit an OCCDA Employment Application, Cover Letter and Resume'; along with Proof of Education. 
 Submit by one of the following: 
 Mail or Drop off at OCCDA  Main Office  : 127 N. Juniper St., Omak WA 98841 
 Fax to: Julie Hurlbert at 509-826-3829 (Bring in the original application, if interviewed) 
 Email:  hr@occda.net  (Bring in the original application, if interviewed) 
 A copy of the full  Job Description  is available at the  Main Office . 
 The required  OCCDA Employment Application  is available at the  Main Office  or can be downloaded from our website at  www.occda.net  . 
 Equal Employment Opportunity
