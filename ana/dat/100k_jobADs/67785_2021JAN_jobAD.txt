 Enhancements 
 TLC Professional Landscaping 
 __London, ON 
 Apply Now 
 An Enhancement Team Member at TLC Landscaping is primarily responsible for installing and providing services that improve multi-residential properties. Including installation of new soft and hardscape, renovation of existing landscaping, turf improvement programs (Fiesta and Slit Seeding) and other external improvements. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 Hardscaping 
 Lift and Relay of existing interlock brick. 
Installation of new interlock driveways, walkways and patios. 
Construction of retaining walls. 
 Softscaping 
 New planting and garden beds. 
Mulch installation and garden bed refurbishment. 
Pruning and tree management. 
 Turf Improvements 
 Weed control applications. 
Slit seeding applications and overseeding to turfed areas. 
Fertilizer applications. 
Sod installation. 
 Additional Duties 
 Load and unload materials, tools, and equipment required to complete projects 
Inspect vehicles and equipment daily to ensure safe operation 
Collaborate with small teams to effectively and efficiently complete tasks 
Qualifications & Required Skills 
1+ years of landscaping experience preferred 
Strongly motivated, detail-oriented, and able to work collaboratively with colleagues 
 Skilled in one or more of the following trades: 
Outdoor woodworking (i.e. decking, fencing, small structures) 
Hardscaping (i.e. stonemasonry, interlock, concrete, natural stone, pathways) 
Softscaping (i.e. garden improvements, sod, mulch, etc.) 
Must have a valid Class G Driver’s License
