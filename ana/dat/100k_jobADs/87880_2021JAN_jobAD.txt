 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently recruiting for a Financial Controller on an exclusive basis to join a rapidly growing business based in Staffordshire. This is a newly created role will play an integral part of the Senior Management Team, and is a fantastic opportunity to deliver financial support and information in line with the needs and requirements of the business. 
 Role 
 
 Delivery of financial and operational controls and business processes 
 Preparation of monthly management accounts and cash flow management 
 Oversee the finance function 
 Business modelling including budgeting and forecasting 
 Financial analysis, highlighting variances and identifying risks and opportunities to improve financial results 
 Leading the statutory audit process and preparation of quarterly VAT returns 
 Ad hoc project support 
 
 Profile: 
 
 ACCA/ACA or CIMA Qualified 
 Experience working at a senior level in an SME environment 
 This is a varied and extremely hands on role which requires flexibility, enthusiasm, accuracy and attention to detail at all times. 
 
 Package: 
 Up to £70,000 
 The Company offers a friendly and flexible working environment and excellent benefits package
