 Gleeson Recruitment Group are currently working with an entrepreneurial multi-company business in Central Birmingham (turnover £70m) as they look to recruit a Bookkeeper on a permanent basis. This role is paying between £25,000 and £35,000 depending on experience. 
 Reporting into the FD you will be responsible for the accounting up to trial balance of different individual businesses within the Group. 
 Key responsibilities include: 
 
 Acting as first point of contact for junior team members 
 Month end management accounts and variance analysis 
 Maintenance of the fixed asset register 
 Accruals and prepayments 
 Review of monthly journals 
 Preparation of VAT returns 
 Maintenance of purchase and sales ledger 
 Involvement in year-end audit process, including preparation of statutory financial statements 
 Monthly reporting 
 
 As a Bookkeeper, you will have: 
 
 A recognised accounting qualifications (AAT, ACCA, CIMA), will be studying towards one or will be QBE 
 Experience of double entry bookkeeping and accounting up to TB. 
 Strong IT skills including Excel 
 Knowledge of accounting processes, procedures and controls 
 Candidates must be immediately available or on a weeks notice. 
 
 Please click apply if this sounds like something of interest!
