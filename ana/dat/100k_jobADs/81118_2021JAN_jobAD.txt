 Customer Service Attendant - Photo Booth Activation 
 GTA Event Group 
 __Barrie, ON 
 Apply Now 
 GTA Event Group is looking for energetic, fun-loving individuals with lots of Christmas spirit for a unique and interactive photo with Santa experience! 
Staff will assist with directing families as they visit our location at Park Place Mall in Barrie to have their photo taken with our virtual Santa. This is a completely touchless experience for guests and staff. PPE will be provided. 
 Specific duties will include directing families as they wait to have their photo taken while making sure that social distancing protocols are followed at all times as well as the processing of electronic payments for photo purchases. 
 Dates are December 7th-23rd. Hours will vary between 11 a.m. and 8 p.m. 
 Job Requirements - 
No experience is necessary however, candidates should be comfortable and patient while working with families and children (LOTS of children!). 
Some basic knowledge of photography and DSLR cameras would be an asset but not necessary. 
 Paid training will take place the week of November 30th. Successful candidates will be required to have a clean Criminal Reference Check.
