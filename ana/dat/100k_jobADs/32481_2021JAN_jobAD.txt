 THE BUSINESS: 
 Feastr is one of the first UK multi-brand cloud kitchen businesses, delivering restaurant quality food to your door in 30 minutes, or less. 
 Operating from cloud kitchens within densely populated areas allows us to exploit the booming food delivery market without the huge overheads associated with bricks and mortar restaurants - cheaper rents, rates, and no front of house. 
 We currently have 3 delivery-only restaurants: SoBe Burger, Chick'N'Bun and Mutha, operating via aggregators such as Deliveroo. 
 THE ROLE: 
 As an Operations Manager you'll be working closely with the co-founders and will be responsible for maintaining a high level of operational performance across multiple sites in London 
 It's an opportunity to join a young, dynamic team and help build, deploy and scale an operating model that is poised to disrupt the restaurant industry. 
 RESPONSIBILITIES: 
 
 Hire, train and manage the kitchen staff 
 Rota management 
 Optimise kitchen performance (rating, prep time, etc.) 
 Maintaining high hygiene standards across multiple sites 
 Manage stocks according to internal procedures & tools (waste, portioning, etc.) 
 Guarantee the smooth running of kitchens (facility maintenance) 
 Responsible for the launch of new sites 
 Ensure implementation and use of the training tools 
 Fill in for kitchen staff in case of absence 
 Expected to work occasional weekend hours when needed 
 
 YOU 
 
 Experience in a fast pace kitchen environment and leading a section/team to deliver great food 
 Ambitious, driven and keen to learn very quickly, in a rapidly growing industry 
 Problem solver with ability to think on your feet and take decisive action 
 Excited by start-up culture and development 
 Passion for food 
 English language (written and spoken) 
 
 WE OFFER: 
 
 A competitive salary 
 28 days annual leave 
 A company vehicle 
 Burgers, a lot of burgers
