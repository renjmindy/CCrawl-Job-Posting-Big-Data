 Cookie from PHP (programming language), PHP data identifier. Contains only a reference to the current session. No information is stored in the user's browser and this cookie can only be used by the current website. This cookie is mainly used in forms to improve the user experience. Data entered in forms is stored for a short time, for example, if there is an input error by the user and the user receives an error message. Otherwise all data would have to be re-entered.

 Career 
 Career at Wieland Roessler 
 Reliable, precise, high-quality - that's how our products are, that's how our service is, that's how we are. 
 We offer a working environment in which you can feel comfortable. Nice colleagues, fairness, respect for each other, short decision-making processes and secure jobs are things that make this working environment attractive. 
 The fascinating material copper, being part of the Wieland Group and dealing with business partners from different industries (including the electrical and electronic as well as the jewellery and metalware industries) are other aspects in favour of a career with Wieland Roessler. 
 Our activities focus on the reliable and fast supply of copper and copper alloy strip to our customer base. For this we need people who keep our customers and us on the road to success through outstanding quality and service. 
 Is this you? 
 Then you will find challenging tasks here at our company, in a working environment where you can get things done.
