 Mobile Cleaner - Sittingbourne 
 Service Care Construction are currently recruiting on behalf of a local Housing Association based in Sittingbourne who require a mobile cleaner to join their team on a temporary basis. 
 Previous cleaning experience and a Full UK Driving License is essential for this role. 
 This is a full time role working 8am - 4pm Monday to Friday with a total of 35 hours per week. 
 The role involves cleaning of void properties and carrying out deep cleans where necessary. 
 To carry out general cleaning duties of communal areas and report on any health and safety issues/repairs that need attention. 
 If you are interested in this fantastic opportunity, please send your updated CV to lauren.marlor@servicecare.org.uk or call Lauren on 01772 208967.
