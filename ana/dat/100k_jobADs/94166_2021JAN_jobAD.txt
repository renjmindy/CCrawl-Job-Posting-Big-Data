 Health Records Clerk - Health Information 
 Women's College Hospital 
 __Toronto, ON 
 Apply Now 
 
 Filing and retrieval of health records according to terminal digit order 
 Performs scanning related functions of the paper health record in accordance with established guidelines including preparation, scanning and indexing 
 Performs quality assurance of scanned documents to ensure accuracy and legibility 
 Assembling health records in accordance with established assembly order 
 Sorting and insertion of loose reports into health records according to terminal digit order and approved order of filing 
 Merging of hard copy charts to be centralized in Health Information 
 Updating of departmental electronic Central Patient Index Signing records in and out using record locator software and bar codes 
 Requests charts from off-site storage facility (Iron Mountain) and processes the received charts 
 Submits workload measurement stats on a weekly basis 
 Provides coverage to other Health Information clerical employees as required 
 
 As a role model and champion you will work to identify and integrate safe, best practices into daily activities to foster the delivery of safe and exemplary care. 
 The responsibilities described above are representative and are not to be construed as all-inclusive. 
Company Bio 
Women's College Hospital (WCH) is an academic, ambulatory care hospital in Ontario with a focus on health for women, health equity and health system solutions. If you're ready to be part of a team that is revolutionizing the future of healthcare, then you will want to join an institution where the possibilities for innovation, new discovery and system thinking are limitless. Women's College Hospital is committed to equity, quality and patient safety as key professional values and essential components of daily practice. 
 With the mobilization of our 2018-2022 Strategic Plan , WCH has entered an exciting phase of its history – strengthening its bond with partners and introducing a new and inspiring vision: Healthcare revolutionized for a healthier and more equitable world. 
Job Description 
Qualifications 
 
 High School Diploma 
 Certified Health Information Management professional, preferred 
 Experience working in a Health Records Department, Clinic or Hospital, an asset 
 Experience working with Health Information departmental systems related to Chart Tracking, Chart Locator and Scanning 
 Commitment to comply with regulations governing privacy and confidentiality of personal health information 
 Ability to work as part of a team in a fast-paced environment 
 Excellent communication, problem solving and customer service skills 
 Demonstrated record of good performance and acceptable attendance will be considered as part of the selection criteria 
 Professional behaviour and communication that meets the standards of the professional regulatory college or association, as applicable, and the standards of Women’s College Hospital 
 This position plays a critical role in acting as an advocate for safety and will demonstrate principles, practices and processes that will optimize a safe environment for all
