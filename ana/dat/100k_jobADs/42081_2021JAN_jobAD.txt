 Fantastic opportunity for an individual looking for a role that will develop them into a Management Accountant. You will make an instant impact in a within a very successful business based near Wolverhampton. This is a chance to join an organisation operating in a wide-variety of markets, with a solid growth plan over the next 3-5 years. 
 This role will offer a rare chance to gain valuable exposure to the complexities of a multi-faceted organisation, with close links to the wider business. It is a broadly-based role, opening up a full range of challenges for the successful candidate. 
 Responsibilities will include: 
 - all aspects of month end 
 - assisting with the preparation of monthly management accounts 
 - provision of performance, cost & variance information 
 - balance sheet reconciliations 
 - assisting with the budgeting & forecasting process 
 - accruals & prepayments 
 - assisting with the development of a suite of financial reports for Heads of Department 
 - ad hoc tasks as required 
 This role will suit someone with an all-round background in ledgers and general finance, perhaps studying AAT, who has a genuine interest in a role that will develop their skills in management accounts. 
 This exciting role will be based near Wolverhampton, and will pay up to £23,000 plus benefits, including support with your studies. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
