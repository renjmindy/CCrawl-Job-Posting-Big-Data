 VPWE Job Opening Video 
 Work Type: Video Production & Animation 
 VPWE Rents was growing and was in need of mechanics for their service department. 
 I created a promotional video for the openings that showed types of machinery applicants would service and benefits associated with the position. 
 The video was posted on their Facebook page and ran as an advertisement. The post linked to a page where interested individuals could learn more details about the position and apply online. 
 How I Served THEM: 
 
 Created a short, eye-catching video that quickly communicated current employment opportunities. 
 Turned video into a Facebook post and ad that attracted users interested in applying for the positions.
