 Are you an experienced HR Shared Services Manager? Are you immediately available and looking for your next challenge? Do you have a strong HR Operational and Strategic background? If so, I have an opportunity for an Interim HR Shared Services Manager to join a growing business based in North Worcestershire for a period of 7 months. 
 As the HR Shared Services Manager, you will be responsible for the day to day management of the HR team to ensure that all employees receive a highly efficient and consistent HR service across all aspects of their employee life cycle. 
 Key Responsibilities: 
 
 Challenge and influence current ways of working to drive a positive change and lead improvements 
 Partnering with the wider HR team and other stakeholders to ensure that an appropriate framework is in place to guarantee consistency across the business 
 The design, setting up and implementing of internal SLAs for the HR SSC 
 Co-Lead on ER support on any restructure activities, TUPE or redundancies 
 Running process mapping exercises to produce bespoke optimal processes 
 Tracking and monitoring of all people KPI's to improve the performance of the HR practices 
 Ensuring all employee lifecycle events and key documentation are recorded and up to date 
 Acting as an escalation point for any complex cases and queries received by the HR SSC team 
 Support wider Shared Services agenda/projects 
 
 Required Skills: 
 
 Proven experience of managing a team 
 Highly analytical and data focused 
 Experienced at setting, tracking, and reporting KPIs and SLAs 
 Excellent written and oral communication skills 
 Excellent interpersonal and stakeholder management skills 
 Comprehensive and thorough knowledge of employment law and practice. 
 Ability to present and resolve complex issues 
 Able to travel throughout the UK when required 
 Fully CIPD qualified
