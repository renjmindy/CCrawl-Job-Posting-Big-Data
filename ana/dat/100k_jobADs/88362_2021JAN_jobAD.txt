 Canada Caregiver Job 
 Posted on  June 1, 2010  by  caregiver 
 This is an excellent opportunity for Filipinos who are seeking employment in  Canada Caregiver Job . Last May 27, the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration or POEA approved a job order that will allow a POEA accredited recruitment agency to recruit applicants for its available Canada caregiver job. For the said Canada caregiver job, a job order balance of only 5 applicants will be selected for placement in Caregiver Work Abroad. 
 If you think you have the qualifications and interested to apply for the said Canada caregiver job, proceed to the office of Luzern International Manpower Services Corp. and submit your application and resume. For more details or inquiries regarding the said Canada caregiver job, you can contact Luzern International Manpower Services Corp. through their landline numbers or email address specified below. 
 Luzern International Manpower Services Corp. 
1695 Unit 3 Town Homes, F. Benitez Street 
Malate, Manila
