 Fantastic opportunity for an individual looking to really push their skills towards becoming a Management Accountant, working within a very successful business based near Wolverhampton. They have a strong history in the local area, with massive growth plans. 
 This is a role that will offer a rare chance to get involved in the operations of a dynamic and complex business, supporting the Financial Controller during a period of growth. Key areas of responsibility will include: 
 - assisting with the preparation of monthly management accounts & financial reports 
 - overseeing the process of supplier payments 
 - accruals & prepayments 
 - supporting the production of budgeting & forecasting information 
 - cost, volume & trend analysis using Advanced Excel 
 - supporting the budgeting & forecasting process 
 - engaging with senior managers across the business to offer insightful analysis and information for commercial decision making 
 - supporting the day to day tasks including the ledgers, payroll and purchase order functions 
 This exciting and visible role is based just outside of Wolverhampton, and will pay up to £28,000 plus benefits, including support with your studies if required.
