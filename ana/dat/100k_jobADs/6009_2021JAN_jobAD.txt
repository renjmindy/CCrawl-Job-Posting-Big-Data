 Boilermaker - Local Mt Isa 
 Job description 
 Boilermaker Position 
 About the Role 
 WorkPac Mount Isa is currently seeking several Boilermakers for ongoing mine site work in Mount Isa. To be successful, you must be residing or willing to relocate your self to Mount Isa. 
 Duties & Responsibilities 
 Your work will be 100% mine site based, carrying out general maintenance, repair and shutdown work in various lead and copper areas of the mine. You must be willing to work in lead environments, and wear a respirator for your shifts. 
 Skills & Experience 
 
 
 Boilermaker Trade qualification 
 
 
 Confined Space and Working at Heights, both completed within the last 2 years 
 
 
 Be physically fit & capable of working in extreme heat conditions and lead stream environments 
 
 
 Be reliable, proactive and hard working 
 
 
 Mine Site Requirements 
 
 
 Pre-Employment Medical, Drug Alcohol & Blood Lead 
 
 
 Fire & First Aid 
 
 
 Manual Handling 
 
 
 Glencore Surface Induction 
 
 
 This role is a Monday to Friday position, however during a shutdown hours the roster will vary

 Sam Watson

 About our Client 
 2PH Contracting provides services and expertise in the fields of Civil Construction, Structural and Mechanical Construction, Scaffolding, General Construction, Mine Maintenance and Construction, Shut Down Supervision and long term projects. They are dedicated to providing quality construction, fabrication, scaffolding and project management services to our customers. 
 About WorkPac 
 WorkPac is Australia's largest, privately owned recruitment provider to the mining, construction, engineering, oil and gas and healthcare sectors.
