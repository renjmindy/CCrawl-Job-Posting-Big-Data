 Marketing Executive 12 Month fixed term contract

 Marketing and Digital

 Contract 
 
 Salary:

 Consultant: 
 
 
 I have a fantastic opportunity for a Marketing Executive to join a fantastic service focused business in the Solihull area, on a 12 month fixed term contract, who are well-known for their excellent working culture. 
 This Marketing Executive role will involve: 
 
 Working on a variety of different marketing campaigns at any one time 
 Helping to deliver the marketing strategy 
 Assisting with a range of marketing activities, both traditional and digital 
 Managing external creative agencies and briefing design work into the internal Graphic Designer 
 Support with internal communications across the group 
 Assist with the organisation and management of a number of annual events and exhibitions 
 Representing the marketing function in departmental meetings and in front of key stakeholders across the business 
 
 This Marketing Executive position requires a candidate with the following skills: 
 
 Experience of working in a full-mix marketing role within a commercial environment 
 Evidence of managing marketing campaigns from cradle to grave, on time and within budget 
 Excellent and professional communication skills, both written and verbal 
 Outstanding stakeholder management skills 
 The ability to manage a number of projects at any one time 
 
 To apply for the Marketing Executive position, please click on the apply now button and your CV will be sent directly to the responsible recruiter. We will respond to successful applicants within 7 days, however please feel free to get in touch if you would like any further information about your application. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
