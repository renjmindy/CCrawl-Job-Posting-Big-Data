 Over-stressed and under-appreciated? Ready to "clean up" working for a privately-owned manufacturer that runs their business with a real "soft touch"? 
 A genuinely amazing opportunity has arisen for a skilled Maintenance Engineer to join a rapidly-expanding manufacturing business based in Birmingham. Having moved to a new site, and currently leading a significant programme of investment, the business needs a Maintenance Engineer who can take their engineering capability to the next level. 
 We met the Site Manager recently. He's happy. He's happy because he works for a great business, who are of course focused on delivering profit and continuing to grow, but also on providing an amazing working culture that allows their people to thrive. The chances are, joining this business will improve how you feel about work. 
 The successful Maintenance Engineer will join a market-leading manufacturer of products for leading UK consumer brands. You will be responsible for the maintenance and repair of a wide variety of fast-paced manufacturing equipment and machinery. You will be predominantly be involved with repair and maintenance of electrical systems, however you may also be required to tend to mechanical faults & repairs. 
 You will clearly be a qualified Maintenance Electrician with the following skills & experience: 
 
 17th edition is essential 
 Experience in a fast-paced manufacturing environment 
 Ability to fault-find and repair faults, minimising downtime 
 Working to and improving PPM schedules 
 Team player who works hard to deliver for a great employer. 
 It is essential you have a strong background and understanding of PLC's. 
 
 The successful candidate will working continental shifts consisting of 2days 2nights 4 off, with an industry leading salary dependent on experience. With further expansion upcoming the company requires driven employees and can offer career progression, where sought, for high-performing candidates. 
 By applying you will be registered as a candidate with Gleeson Recruitment Limited. Our Privacy Policy is available on our website and explains how we will use your data.
